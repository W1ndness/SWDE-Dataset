Player News (last updated: December 6, 2010)
News: Anthony hit just 4-of-20 field goals during Denver's win over Memphis on Sunday night.
Spin: Anthony finished with 13 points, six rebounds and three assists. Outside of the Phoenix game on Nov. 28 in which he played three minutes and finished 1-of-1 from the field, Anthony has not shot 50 percent or better for nine straight games. During that span he's 62-of-173 (35.8%) from the field, including 4-of-20 from 3-point range, and has also shot just 78-of-97 from the foul line (80.4%). We expect he'll come around eventually, as he does have a lifetime 45.8 FG percentage, but he's clearly struggling to find his range at the present time.

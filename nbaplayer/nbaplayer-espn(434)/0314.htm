Player News (last updated: December 4, 2010)
News: Mohammed recorded a nice 14-point, 11-rebound double-double in just 28 minutes of Friday's overtime win over the Nets. He added two blocks in the win.
Spin: For all of you fantasy owners that have picked up or held onto Mohammed since his 22-point, 20-rebound explosion on November 13 in hopes that he would do it again, this is likely the closest you will see. He has the ability to have decent nights, but will not see the opportunities or playing time to put up huge statistical nights routinely. A double-double every two or three weeks should be the best you can expect if you're a Mohammed owner.

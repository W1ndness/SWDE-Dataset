    Impact: Kapono missed the Sixers' last four games due to personal reasons. Though he started the year as the team's starter at small forward, Kapono's role quickly diminished. Even without family issues taking him away from the court, Kapono would be a candidate to be inactive for the Sixers. He won't have much fantasy value unless he starts playing about 25 minutes a night and burying three-pointers at a good clip.

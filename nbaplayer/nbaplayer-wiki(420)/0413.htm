Ben Uzoh (born March 1988 in San Antonio, Texas) is a American professional basketball player. He played for the University of Tulsa Golden Hurricane over his four year collegiate career. After going undrafted in the 2010 NBA Draft, Uzoh signed a partially guaranteed contract with the National Basketball Association's New Jersey Nets.

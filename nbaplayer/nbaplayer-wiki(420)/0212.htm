Alexis Ajinça (born May 6, 1988 in Saint-Étienne, France) is a French professional basketball player with the Dallas Mavericks of the NBA Development League. The 7 ft 0 in (2.13 m), 220 lb (100 kg; 16 st) center was selected with the 20th overall pick in the 2008 NBA Draft by the Charlotte Bobcats.[1]

On October 29, 2009, the Bobcats exercised their third-year team option on Ajinça, thus keeping him under contract through 2010-11 season.[2]

On November 30, 2009, the Charlotte Bobcats assigned Ajinça to the Maine Red Claws, the NBA Developmental League affiliate. He was recalled on February 2, 2010.[3]

On July 13, 2010, Ajinça was traded to the Dallas Mavericks along with Tyson Chandler for Erick Dampier, Matt Carroll, Eduardo Najera, and cash consideration.[4]

Ajinça has played internationally for the French national basketball team.

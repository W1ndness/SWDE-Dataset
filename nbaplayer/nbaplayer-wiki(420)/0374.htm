Willie Warren (born October 22, 1989 in Dallas, Texas) is an American professional basketball shooting guard with the Los Angeles Clippers of the National Basketball Association.

However, he decided to return to Oklahoma for his sophomore season. Warren was selected in the second round in the 2010 NBA Draft.[1][2]

Warren was drafted 54th overall by the Los Angeles Clippers in the 2010 NBA Draft. On July 13, 2010, he was signed by the Clippers.[3]

Eugene "Pooh" Jeter III (born December 1, 1983 in Los Angeles, California[1]) is an American professional basketball player for the Sacramento Kings. He plays the point guard position.

  • Hapoel Jerusalem - BSL (Israel) - 2009–2010

[edit] NBA

On July 23, 2010, Jeter signed with the Sacramento Kings.[2]

Paul George (born May 2, 1990) is an American basketball player with the Indianapolis Pacers of the National Basketball Association. George was drafted by the Indiana Pacers as the 10th overall pick at the 2010 NBA Draft after playing two years with the Fresno State Bulldogs.

[edit] NBA

George signed his rookie contract with the Pacers on July 1, 2010. The contract is a two-year guaranteed deal worth $3.9M.[1]

On October 27, George made his NBA debut against the San Antonio Spurs, playing 23 minutes and scoring 4 points on 1-5 shooting.

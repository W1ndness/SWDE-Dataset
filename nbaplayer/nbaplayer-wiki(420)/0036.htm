Timofey Pavlovich Mozgov (Russian: Тимофей Павлович Мозгов, born July 16, 1986 in St. Petersburg, Russia) is a Russian professional basketball player for the New York Knicks. He is 2.16 m (7 ft 1 in) tall and he weighs 270 lbs. He plays at the center position.

[edit] Pro career

Mozgov began his professional career with LenVo St. Petersburg, in the Russian second tier division, during the 2004-05 season. In 2006, he moved to CSK VVS-2 Samara, the second level club of Samara. Before the 2006-07 season he joined Khimki Moscow Region, where he played through the 2009-10 season.

[edit] NBA career

On July 10, 2010, it was reported that Mozgov would sign a three year, $9.7 million dollar gross income contract with the New York Knicks of the NBA.[1]

[edit] Russian national team

Mozgov has also been a member of the senior Russian national basketball team and he played at the EuroBasket 2009.

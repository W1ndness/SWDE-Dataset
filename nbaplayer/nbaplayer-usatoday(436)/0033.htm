Danny Granger F Indiana Pacers
Height: 6'9'   Weight: 228   DOB: 4/20/1983  Age: 27
Dec. 1, 2010 - 2:45 a.m. ET
Danny Granger played through the flu in the Pacers' 107-98 win in Sacramento on Tuesday, scoring a season-high 37 points on 12-of-19 shooting (3-of-5 from downtown, 10-of-11 from the foul line) with seven rebounds, two assists, two steals, and a block.
His shot attempts have been down with the new focus on feeding Roy Hibbert, who scored 16 points with eight rebounds, four assists, and a block tonight, but Danny has scored 30 or more points in 2-of-3 games and that trend could be reversing. Darren Collison also looked sharp with 17 points, four boards, and six assists.

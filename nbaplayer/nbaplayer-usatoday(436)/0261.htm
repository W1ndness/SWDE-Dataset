Oct. 17, 2010 - 3:35 a.m. ET
Willie Green probably locked up the backup PG role for the Hornets on Saturday, scoring 10 points on 4-of-8 shooting with two rebounds and two assists in 20 minutes.
He didn't have a turnover tonight and made some impressive plays, while Mustafa Shakur looked shaky at best. Given Chris Paul's injury history, Green will be well worth monitoring to see if could get starter's minutes in the event of a CP3 injury.
Source: Hornets247.com
Sep. 23, 2010 - 10:46 a.m. ET
The Sixers have traded guard Willie Green and center Jason Smith to the Hornets for forwards Darius Songaila and Craig Brackins.
Green's arrival hurts the potential value of Marco Belinelli (who is backing up Marcus Thornton), although they may plan on using him at back up point guard, as Mustafa Shakur is currently the first line of defense behind Chris Paul. We're not sure what Philly got in the deal, but the loss of Green should clear more minutes for Jason Kapono and Jodie Meeks, and could be another sign that Evan Turner is the starting shooting guard in Philly.
Source: Adrian Wojnarowski on Twitter
Apr. 13, 2010 - 2:25 p.m. ET
Willie Green does not plan to opt out of the final year of his contract.
Green has a player option that will pay him $3.98 million next season. His role is completely up in the air as the Sixers' biggest need is a playmaking shooting guard.

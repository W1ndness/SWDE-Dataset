JaVale McGee F/C Washington Wizards
Height: 7'0'   Weight: 250   DOB: 1/19/1988  Age: 22
Dec. 8, 2010 - 2:54 a.m. ET
JaVale McGee didn't look good in the box score on Tuesday, but he looked great on the court against Pau Gasol and the Lakers, finishing with just five points on 2-of-4 shooting with six rebounds, one steal, and one block in 21 minutes.
McGee made Gasol work for everything and bothered him into a 1-for-6 mark from the field to start the game, before getting into foul trouble and missing much of the second half. Yes, he's been struggling in the past two games, but it's easier to swallow when he looks like he did tonight.

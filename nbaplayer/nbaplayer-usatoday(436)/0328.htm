Beno Udrih returned to the starting lineup and the Kings' offense returned to form on Saturday, as he scored 16 points on 6-of-11 shooting (including two threes) with three rebounds, six assists, and a steal in 40 minutes.

Dec. 4, 2010 - 9:28 p.m. ET
Coach Paul Westphal said he would make changes to the starting lineup after Friday's blowout loss to the Lakers, and decided on the lineup of Beno Udrih, Tyreke Evans, Donte' Greene, Jason Thompson, and DeMarcus Cousins for Saturday's game against the Mavs.
Udrih needs to be owned in all leagues, Greene gets a reprieve after having a rough Friday night, Thompson gets rewarded for his nice game Friday, and Cousins gets a chance to perform and try to hang onto the job. Cousins should also be owned in all leagues for his upside, and Dalembert should be reserved until we see him get back up to speed following his knee injury.

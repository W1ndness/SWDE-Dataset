Free agent Roger Mason hinted in a video on Youtube.com that he could soon be joining the Knicks.
"It's Roger Mason Jr. of the San Antonio Spurs, and quite possibly very soon the New York Knicks," he says in the video. Aug. 3, 2010 - 9:59 a.m. ET
The Bulls are still looking at free agents Roger Mason Jr. and Keith Bogans, according to beat writer Mike McGraw.
The Bulls are trying to fill out their roster with a perimeter player and still have Tracy McGrady in the back of their minds. They'd probably be better off with Mason, who can play both guard spots, shoots the trey well and has big-game experience from his time with the Spurs.

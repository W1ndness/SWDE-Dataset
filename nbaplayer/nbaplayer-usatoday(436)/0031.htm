Nov. 27, 2010 - 1:31 a.m. ET
Roy Hibbert bounced back from a couple poor performances and a headache for 19 points, 10 rebounds, a steal and a block on 9-of-21 shooting in 37 minutes Friday.
The Pacers blew a 14-point third-quarter lead, and it's partly because Hibbert disappeared late in the game. Kevin Durant blocked two of his shot attempts, but the bottom line is that Hibbert is back. Now let's see if he can do it again against Pau Gasol and the Lakers on Sunday, which should be a much tougher test.

Dec. 6, 2010 - 10:13 p.m. ET
Jose Calderon felt a bit better in Monday's game after dealing with the flu over the weekend, scoring 21 points on 8-of-12 shooting (including two threes) with one rebound, four assists, and a steal in 30 minutes of action.
There was some thought that Jerryd Bayless (18 minutes tonight, five points) could play his way into a timeshare, but it was revealed Monday that Calderon has been playing with the flu and since the prior two outings were blowouts there was reason to be skeptical. Bayless has some skill but there's a reason he has been traded twice, and Calderon should be treated like the starter until Bayless threatens his time in a meaningful game.

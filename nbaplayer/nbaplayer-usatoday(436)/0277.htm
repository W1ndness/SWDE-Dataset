Brandon Roy G/F Portland Trail Blazers
Height: 6'6'   Weight: 211   DOB: 7/23/1984  Age: 26
Dec. 8, 2010 - 2:35 a.m. ET
Brandon Roy, who may lose his starting job at some point and may not play in back-to-back games going forward, took the reigns with Andre Miller (suspension) out and scored 20 points on 5-of-15 shooting (including a three) with five rebounds, five assists, one steal, and one block in 38 minutes of the Blazers' 106-99 win over the Suns.
This was a big game at home not just for the team, but also for coach Nate McMillan, whose job may be at risk right now. McMillan is letting Roy make most of the decisions regarding his knee, but the writing is on the wall and Roy is probably a risk to himself with a history of pushing himself too far and too fast. Make no mistake, this could easily be owners' last chance to sell him while he's a starter in name.

Nov. 23, 2010 - 3:56 a.m. ET
As expected, Monta Ellis played on Monday despite a sore hip, scoring 20 points on 7-of-22 shooting (2-of-8 from downtown) with four rebounds, four assists, and a steal in 41 minutes as the Warriors lost to the Nuggets by the score of 106-89.
Stephen Curry added 17 points on 8-of-21 shooting (1-of-6 from downtown) with seven rebounds, five assists, and three steals. We're not sure if Monta's hip was hurting him or not, but what we can say for sure is that the W's offense is nowhere near the same without David Lee (elbow). Since he hurt himself, the team is shooting 40.9 percent from the field and is 1-5.

Monta Ellis confirmed after Sunday's game that his hip is fine -- he has a "gym burn" on his left hip and will "definitely" play on Monday.

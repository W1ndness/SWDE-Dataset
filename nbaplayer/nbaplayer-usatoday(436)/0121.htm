Nene Hilario F/C Denver Nuggets
Height: 6'11'   Weight: 250   DOB: 9/13/1982  Age: 28
Dec. 5, 2010 - 10:50 p.m. ET
Nene dominated the Grizzlies in Sunday's thrilling victory, piling up 27 points (10-of-13 FGs, 7-of-8 FTs), 11 rebounds, six assists, two steals, two blocks, and just one turnover.
This is far and away Nene's best game of the season, and one savvy play in particular helped preserve the victory -- he hustled the length of the court for an easy dunk with just 5.9 second remaining. This was just the second time that he's grabbed double-digit rebounds all year, but he's shooting career-best percentages from the field (60.8%) and the FT line (77.6%). The window to buy low may have just slammed shut.

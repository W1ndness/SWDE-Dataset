Dec. 3, 2010 - 12:35 p.m. ET
Channing Frye may continue to start for Phoenix even after Robin Lopez (knee) returns from injury this month.
This is terrific news if you were quick enough to grab him when he was named a starter -- a 7'0" PF/C with a sweet shot from downtown, he's scored double-figures in 10 straight games and just grabbed his first double-double of the season. The 'twin towers' approach also gives Phoenix a better chance at improving their (by far) league-worst -6.2 rebound differential.
Source: AZCentral
Nov. 25, 2010 - 1:16 a.m. ET
Channing Frye hit 6-of-14 shots and two more 3-pointers for 16 points, seven rebounds and two blocks in Wednesday's double-OT loss to the Bulls.
Frye has been hot and extremely consistent over the past two weeks and should be owned in all fantasy leagues as long as he's starting at center. And with Robin Lopez out for another three weeks or so, that could be quite a while.
Nov. 23, 2010 - 12:16 a.m. ET

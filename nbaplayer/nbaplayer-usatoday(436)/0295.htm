Oct. 8, 2010 - 11:24 a.m. ET
Antonio McDyess, who is now 35, said recently he is fairly certain this will be his final NBA season.
"I didn't know what to expect when they signed me (in 2009), and I'm glad to be back," McDyess said, "but I think this is it. I'm the oldest player (on the Spurs) now, and I feel like it. I'm not trying to see any more years. I'm pretty sure this is it." McDyess made a nice comeback from early injury problems, but no longer has any gas left in the fantasy tank.

Cavaliers signed forward Christian Eyenga to a four-year contract on Friday.
Eyenga played in Europe last season, but is very athletic. He was taken with the final pick of the first round in the 2009 draft by the Cavs, and he's expected to attend training camp and play for them this season.

Jul. 22, 2010 - 10:35 a.m. ET
The Cavaliers may soon offer a two-year contract to Christian Eyenga, their first-round choice in the June 2009 draft.
The 21-year-old forward has impressed new coach Byron Scott, who "loves the way he's progressing" after a season with the Spanish League last year.
Source: News-Herald
Jun. 6, 2010 - 11:47 a.m. ET
The Cavaliers aren't likely to sign 2009 draft pick Christian Eyenga this summer.
Eyenga played in Spain last year, but "he was held back after getting quite sick early in the season ... and he averaged just 12 minutes, 3.9 points and 2.0 rebounds a game." He is expected to participate in Summer League this July.

Nov. 28, 2010 - 5:12 p.m. ET
Rodney Stuckey poured in 29 points on Sunday, going 9-of-16 from the field and 11-of-12 from the charity stripe. He added four rebounds, two assists, two steals and two turnovers.
Stuckey, who notched his first 20-plus point game since Nov. 7, was uncharacteristically efficient from the floor, as he came into the contest shooting just 44.8 percent. Meanwhile, Ben Gordon played his worst game of the year, as Pistons beat writers noted that he looked especially lethargic on his way to three points on 1-of-4 shooting with two rebounds, one assist, one steal and three turnovers in 29 minutes.

Dec. 7, 2010 - 3:12 a.m. ET
Eric Gordon scored 29 points on 10-of-17 shooting, including the go-ahead three pointer late, and also added five rebounds, two assists, two steals, and two 3-pointers in the Clippers' 98-91 win over the Kings on Monday.
It's his ninth straight game scoring 20 or more points, which is a career-high for him. One point of note, he was wearing a large ice wrap on his right (shooting) wrist after the game, which may just be for maintenance but it's worth pointing out.

Eric Gordon hit 8-of-17 shots, a 3-pointer and 11-of-12 free throws on his way to 28 points, four rebounds and six assists in Thursday's easy win over the Kings.
He's scored 20 or more points in 11 of his 14 games this season and appears to be one of the biggest fantasy steals in this year's drafts. Sit back and enjoy the ride if you own him.
Nov. 18, 2010 - 12:36 a.m. ET
Eric Gordon had 30 points, three rebounds, seven assists, a steal, a block and two more 3-pointers on 9-of-19 shooting in Wednesday's loss to the Timberwolves.
Michael Beasley is getting a lot of hype as the surprise of the season, but Gordon is probably right there with him. Outside of a couple missed games and one dud, Gordon has had a great start to the season, averaging nearly 23 points, four rebounds, five assists, a steal, 0.6 blocks and almost a 3-pointer per game. The downside is he came in shooting just 17 percent from downtown and was committing 3.6 TOs a game (with five more tonight), but we expect both of those numbers to level off in the near future. Let's just hope he stays healthy.

Job Description

Based in Atlanta, Georgia, Crawford & Company (www.crawfordandcompany.com) is the world's largest independent provider of claims management solutions to the risk management and insurance industry as well as self-insured entities, with a global network of more than 700 locations in 63 countries. The Crawford System of Claims SolutionsSM offers comprehensive, integrated claims services, business process outsourcing and consulting services for major product lines including property and casualty claims management, workers' compensation claims and medical management, and legal settlement administration. The Company's shares are traded on the NYSE under the symbols CRDA and CRDB.




Crawford & Company is ranked in the top 100 in the 2009 InformationWeek 500, reflecting the Company's innovative use of information technology in its global operations.

Summary of Role:
Under the general direction of the Vice President-ICT Administration, Compliance,& formulates and executes both strategic and tactical information security plans supporting Crawford's US operation. Coordinate and communicate with global ICT regarding risks, trends, products, and solutions related to information security. Supervises, participates in and reviews the work of the ICT Security group. Provide security consultancy and technical expertise to facilitate the development of security policies, standards, and process improvements. Implement effective information security measures in accordance with agreed upon information security policies and standards. The Information Security Director serves as the process owner for all ongoing activities providing appropriate access to and protection of corporate data in compliance with organization policies and standards.

Responsibilities:
* Define plans to improve information security strategically and tactically.
* Collaborate with global ICT to document and communicate global information security standards.
* Assistance to ICT in the deployment of agreed upon information security technology solutions.
* Manage security incidents to minimize business impact
* Administration of security process checks internal to ICT
* Develops and monitors budget for security unit; address variances to budget.
* Operation and administration of security monitoring and control process
* Serves as an internal information security consultant to the organization
* Implements information security policies and procedures for the organization
* Reviews all system-related security plans throughout the organization's network, acting as a liaison to application systems
* Monitors compliance with information security policies and procedures, referring problems to the appropriate department manager
* Plans, organizes, controls, integrates and evaluates the work of staff engaged in ensuring stable, reliable computer operations supporting the availability of mission critical systems and applications and providing problem resolution and support.
* Supervises and participates in developing, implementing and evaluating plans, work processes, systems and procedures to achieve annual goals, objectives and work standards.
* Plans, organizes, directs and evaluates the performance of assigned staff; establishes performance requirements and personal development targets.
* Regularly monitors performance and provides coaching for performance improvement and development; recommends compensation and provides other rewards to recognize performance; subject to management concurrence, takes disciplinary action to address performance deficiencies, in accordance with corporate personnel policies and procedures.
* Establishes and maintains a high performance customer service-oriented environment by ensuring systematic and responsive troubleshooting techniques.
* Manages and participates in the acquisition, configuration and deployment of security appliances and applications.
* Conducts product and technology research and recommends technology approaches and direction; evaluates and makes recommendations for design or revision of operational procedures of systems and computers.
* Establishes and evaluates operating procedures and standards.

Job Description

ORGANIZATIONAL OVERVIEW:

CaridianBCT is a global leader in the blood bank and transfusion
medicine industry, investing in research and development focused on
enhancing blood quality, safety and supply. It provides technology,
products and services to blood centers, hospitals, and scientific,
clinical and biotech researchers.

Driven by its vision, For Better Blood and Better Lives,
CaridianBCT's mission is to improve lives through innovation, quality
and services delivered by our people, products and processes in blood
component technologies.

It is the largest medical device manufacturer headquartered in
Colorado. Based in Lakewood, Colorado (just west of Denver),
CaridianBCT employs approximately 2,500 people in 32 countries and
has sales in more than 96 countries. The company is privately held.

Learn more about the company on its website at
( http://www.caridianbct.com/ ) www.caridianbct.com.

Job Summary

Position provides technical expertise in an existing or
changing/emerging environment. Performs a broad range of tasks which
may include research, architecture planning, analysis, design, coding
and testing, installation, maintenance, support, training, evaluation
and selection, and implementation of information technology
solutions.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Within the context of the ITS/Business Strategic Plan,
    operates independently in various activities such as research,
    selection, and implementation of technology solutions.
  • Applies highly professional business and/or technical
    knowledge when working with clients, other ITS groups, and
    vendors in activities such as identifying options, selection,
    analyzing, designing, installation, coding, maintenance, and
    testing of information technology solutions.
  • Responsible for providing technical assistance, problem
    resolution, support, and training for clients and other ITS
    groups.
  • Shares responsibility with team members for 24-hour on-call
    problem resolution.
  • Responsible for recommending, implementing, creating,
    maintaining, interpreting, improving, summarizing, and
    reporting on the performance indicators for the technology
    area.
  • Responsible for establishing, interpreting, and executing
    departmental procedures for the technology area.
  • Dedication to quality client service and proactive and
    responsive to client needs.

    Minimum Qualification Requirements

    Education:

  • Knowledge required to perform at this level is generally
    acquired through completion of a Bachelor's degree in the area
    of Computer Science Engineering or Business.

    Experience:

  • Generally a minimum of 8 years experience in a related field.
  • A minimum of 4 years experience as SQL Server DBA.
  • Experience in client/server distributed databases required.
  • Extensive administration experience required with SQL Server
    2000, 2005 and 2008.
  • Experience with SAP desired.

    Skills:

  • Demonstrated ability to conduct research and recommend changes
    in an existing, changing, or emerging environment.
  • Demonstrated ability to analyze business/technical problems
    and select, design, or implement information technology
    solutions in accordance with business needs.
  • Demonstrated effective oral and written communication skills
    with the ability to communicate technical information to
    non-technical personnel.
  • Demonstrated working knowledge of multiple business
    disciplines.
  • Requires an in-depth knowledge of a specific technology
    discipline.
  • Demonstrated fiscal awareness.
  • Requires ability to lead projects.
  • Requires ability to educate and train others.
  • Requires in-depth knowledge of [insert database name] database
    construct, dictionaries, and operation.
  • Requires ability to translate data models into physical
    database structure, construct the physical database, and work
    with systems programmers and/or system administrators to
    implement a physical database.
  • Requires the ability to monitor [insert database name]
    performance indicators and implement changes to improve
    performance.
  • Requires the ability to diagnose and implement solutions for
    most database problems.
  • Ability to create and maintain operational, security, and
    change control procedures for a variety of platforms highly
    valued.

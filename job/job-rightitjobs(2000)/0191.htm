Senior IT Network Engineer

We have an outstanding career opportunity for a Senior IT Network Engineer to join a leading Manufacturing Company located in the Fullerton area.

We are searching for a Senior IT Network Engineer whose primary responsibility is providing technical support for the enterprise computing infrastructure on a 7 by 24 basis. This includes: Maintaining availability, monitor performance, plan and implement changes, manage and coordinate vendor support activity.

The infrastructure consists of TCP/IP backbone network which includes Window based technology, Oracle ERP running on HP/UNIX, VMWARE ESXi, vSphere, Hyper-V and other associated technology. Critical enterprise applications include Oracle Manufacturing systems, EMAIL and Internet accessibility

Responsibilities include:

• Providing support and leadership for small to medium special projects related to infrastructure products involving the various manufacturing and staff groups. Routine troubleshooting and consultation regarding infrastructure product and services are also provided.
• providing general technical direction to junior staff members and operating unit IT support personnel regarding infrastructure projects and support activities.
• Being the focal point for dealing with outside vendors providing DBA and Data Center Services and contracted maintenance. Maintains the various contracts associated with these vendors
• Preparing yearly plans and budget numbers for the infrastructure.
• Performing System Audits, Risk Assessments and vulnerability reviews on various Windows, Email and UNIX servers and document remediation plans as needed. Monitor Anti-Virus, various notifications and logs for possible security threats
• Designing, implementing and maintaining appropriate security and backup/ recovery processes.

Requirements:

• A Bachelors or Masters Degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, a similar related field, or equivalent professional experience is required.
• A minimum of 5 years of experience supporting high availability TCP/IP infrastructures. Working experience managing Windows Exchange, ISA and Juniper Netscreen Devices in an enterprise environment.
• Experience with the design and day-to-day support of UNIX based Oracle ERP and database software + hardware
• Working practical knowledge of HP UNIX RISC-based server hardware and Intel x86-based server hardware.
• Working knowledge of UNIX shell scripting and Microsoft Office suite including MS VBA is a strong plus
• Team player with good interpersonal skills and an ability to communicate with a diverse workforce and various levels of management.

We offer an above average compensation and competitive benefits package including medical, dental, life insurance, long term disability, 401(k), Employee Stock Purchase Plan and profit sharing.

Job Description

 

 Manager of Network Administration & Call Center

 

My client in the Healthcare Services industry is seeking a Manager of Network Administration be responsible for providing overall leadership and direction for the Network and Telephony infrastructure and related operational processes.  The selected Manager of Network Administration will collaborate with IS Leaders, PMO, SPO, and business stakeholders to identify, prioritize and implement technology solutions that support the Enterprise Architecture, processes, and business goals. 

 

Responsibilities: 

 

  • Planning, design, implementation and support of the LAN/WAN (wired/wireless) and Telephony infrastructure supporting internal and external customers; voice, data video and paging
  • Interfacing with key operational groups within Information Systems including Project Management Office (PMO), Security Program Office (SPO), Service Desk and others as necessary to ensure alignment with IS processes and IS priorities
  • Establishing and maintaining data networks that protect the integrity of the network from unauthorized users including but not limited to toll-free lines, calling credit cards and computer hackers.  Work closely with vendors and suppliers to identify potential abuses, actions to be taken, resolution and avoidance of breaches in network security
  • Contributing to the specification, implementation and on-going management of the corporate Incident /Change Management processes and Disaster Recovery Planning
  • Managing the specification and documentation of LAN/WAN and Telephony infrastructure technology standards in support of the strategic plan
  • Proactively managing LAN/WAN and Telephony capacity and performance through proactive monitoring, management and forecasting
  • Directly managing the appropriate data, voice, network service desk requests on a daily basis, ensuring timely follow-up and resolution on all support requests to the end user community.  Explaining variances and support issues which are outstanding.
  • Preparing and maintaining a LAN/WAN and Telephony technology "roadmap" on behalf of the organization
  • Preparing and delivering periodic status reports and project updates.
  • Providing leadership in the evaluation/selection of LAN/WAN and Telephony technology solutions as required
  • Providing day-to-day leadership of Network and Telephony staff, coaching/mentoring, career development and discipline as required.
  • Performing skill and competency assessments to support implemented and planned infrastructure and facilitating resolution of staff related issues as necessary
  • Preparing/reviewing Network and Telephony staff project and annual reviews as required.  Creating development plans and sets individual goals
  • Interacts with company vendor and business partners as required to ensure business and financial interests are represented appropriately
  • Maintaining company Network and Telephony hardware and software assets including, licensure and support contracts where appropriate
  • Conducting due diligence and planning necessary to prepare the annual Network and Telephony capital and operating budgets
  • Proactively stays abreast of new technology trends and investment opportunities so that informed recommendations can be made in support of business needs.

Senior Network Engineer

About the Job


Job ID: 9726
Position Description:
Objective of Position
The Citrix Online network consists of a large number of routers and switches spread across the globe in various datacenters and offices. These devices support tens of thousands of ports and a large amount of Internet traffic every day. Additionally, the network also supports the internal workforce and their communication needs. The purpose of the Senior Network Engineer is to design, extend, operate and maintain all of these components while ensuring an uptime of 99.99%. This position will be tasked with larger projects such as the expansions and capacity upgrades, as well as be involved deeply with the day-to-day operations of the network. A senior network engineer is expected to collaborate with the Network Architect to go over product modifications. A person in this position will be required to make tactical and strategic decisions on their own, may specialize in Routing and Switching, Voice technologies, or WAN infrastructure and may be responsible for specific subsystems from one of those classes.

Principal Responsibilities
• Translates group strategy into day-to-day tactical decisions
• Documents and applies guidance on industry standards to improve current environment and practices
• Maintains high network availability build for redundancy and resilience; periodically tests the resilience by active testing
• Calculates capacity requirements; understands and accounts for peak load; manages peak load events
• Defines Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for operational efficiency
• Continuously investigates and recommends alternatives to bring down operational costs
• Works with the Network Architect to define requirements around network performance such as QoS and traffic shaping
• Evaluates and oversees implementation of a carrier grade monitoring system
• Performs security audits of the subsystems to continuously improve the security posture; defines day-to-day procedures to achieve this end
• Ensures compliance with various audit requirements such as SOX and PCI
• Maintains operational relationships with service providers, and equipment vendors
• Is the highest level of support for escalated end-user issues
• Configures routers, switches, load balancers and other network devices in accordance with best practices; codifies these best practices as templates and publishes to the group
• Plans for disaster recovery both on a global and on a site-by-site basis
• Creates reports in system-wide performance metrics
• Ensures configuration management and reviews it is in place and being rigorously followed

Other Duties and Responsibilities
• Is available for on-call rotation 2 weeks a month
• Attends conferences and expos in the field
• Inculcates and imparts BCPs as part of every project
• Guides and instructs junior members of the NSG

Supervisory Responsibilities
• None at this time

Position Requirements:
Knowledge and Skills
• Bachelor's degree in CS or ECE or equivalent experience; prefer candidates with Master’s in the same field
• At least 5 years experience with Enterprise or service provider Networks
• Experience building and maintaining a 100person or 1000+ server network
• 3+ years of on-call technical operations experience a plus
• Must be fluent in multi-area OSPF and BGP, MLS and stateful firewalls
• CCNP or equivalent certification required
• Fluency with Cisco IOS (JunOS a plus)
• Experience with the Cisco 65xx, nexus, 72xxx platforms
• Experience with billing and CDR management systems
• Experience with Cisco Call Manager, UCCx, Avaya CM/IC, Nortel PBXes a plus
• Fluency with Basic Networking: Ethernet, TCP/IP, HTTP, SMTP, NTP, SNMP
• Advanced knowledge of VoIP: SIP, SCCP and H.323
• Understanding of voice encoding methods and codecs (G.711, G.729, G.722, iLBC, iSAC)
• Basic programming experience a plus (Perl, SQL, JAVA)
• Ability to be very customer-oriented and willing to engage
• Able to solve complex problems by successive decomposition
• Experience juggling multiple responsibilities and prioritization of conflicting requirements

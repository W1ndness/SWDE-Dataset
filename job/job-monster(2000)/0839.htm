Network Engineer - Desktop

About the Job

Visory Group, a leading IT solutions firm serving Upstate New York, continues to grow and we are searching for a Network Engineer - Desktop.  Visory Group partners with leading industry companies like Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, and many more.  If you are looking for place to work and grow professionally, Visory Group is for you.

 

The successul candidate will have the following expertise:

 

  • Install, configure and support Windows 7, Vista and XP
  • Assist with creating desktop images and deploying those images
  • Troubleshoot PC and server hardware 
  • Troubleshoot network connectivity issues
  • Install, configure and support Microsoft Office applications, including Outlook
  • Provide PC application support and resolution
  • Administer Windows Server 2008 and 2003
  • Manage and support printers and other peripherals
  • Provide basic Active Directory administration
  • Knowledge of Mac's a plus

 

The ideal candidate will be skilled at:

 

  • Delivering a high level of customer service
  • Working with clients in a variety of environments
  • Troubleshooting issues effectively for timely resolution
  • Interfacing with other 3rd party vendors, i.e. hardware, software and service providers
  • Operating individually and as part of a team
  • Communicating with all parties
  • Interfacing with clients remotely, over the phone, and on site
  • Developing product and solution recommendations

 

Visory Group offers a very attractive compensation package and terrific benefits, including health insurance, 401K plan, bonus plan, parking, travel expense, phone expense, and more.

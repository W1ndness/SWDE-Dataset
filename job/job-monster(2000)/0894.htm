Sr. CRM Marketing & Business Analyst

About the Job

Thompson Publishing Group, Inc. seeks an experienced CRM Business/Marketing Analyst (Super User) with a proven track record in Marketing and solid experience with CRM systems and performance measurement tools that improve Marketing results.

 

This position will work closely with all departments and divisions. This role will be the central marketing lead for our integrated customer and prospect databases, use customer and other performance data to provide deep customer insights, and answer key business questions - playing an integral role in the company’s future success. 

 

Responsibilities

  • Ensure consistent promotion/campaign set-up
  • Drive attribute and target segmentation strategy through customer/behavior analysis
  • Create, maintain and analyze key reports, dashboards and analytics utilizing SAP Business Objects (Webi, Xelcius)
  • Develop best practice approaches for analyzing user behavior through CRM
  • Analyze customer/business metrics and performance and support the marketing leads in maximizing customer life-time value
  • Working with integrated data sets, lead the development of a customer segmentation and lead scoring mechanism to aid in maximizing customer LTV
  • Work with cross functional groups and departments to ensure the integrity of the data
  • Provide ad hoc and campaign specific reporting in order to help the marketing managers understand traffic patterns, site metrics, marketing performance, and opportunities for improvement
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and A/B testing efforts and assist in the implementation and ongoing usage of new A/B and multivariate testing programs  
  • Work with Finance and give input to pro forma and budget analysis
  • Train and support the Marketing users of SAP CRM system

Requirements & Qualifications

  • 5-7 years experience in Marketing Analysis or a similar function
  • Strong analytical and communication skills with the ability to teach/train others
  • A team player who excels in juggling multiple projects and working across department lines
  • General facility with technology and databases
  • Knowledge of large enterprise systems (ERP) and or CRM systems, especially, SAP CRM.  Ideal candidate will have CRM experience (SAP CRM and/or Business Objects experience, a plus)
  • Experience with Business Objects
  • Knowledge or experience with campaign suppression and modeling prospects
  • Experience with smart forms and campaign automation
  • Ability to multi-task and change priorities
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Experience with A/B, multivariate testing and optimization
  • Experience with modeling, analysis, campaign performance measurement, and forecasting a plus
  • BS/BA required, MBA is a plus.


Please send resume and cover letter, with salary requirements to careers@thompson.com

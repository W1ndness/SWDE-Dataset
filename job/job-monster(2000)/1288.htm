SharePoint Developer

About the Job

Founded in 1999, Highmark Companies is a leading provider of information technology and professional services to public- and private-sector clients. Each day, we build our reputation through our employees’ expertise and a consistent professional integrity that forges lasting relationships with our customers through mutual trust and respect.

Our enjoyable work environment, management vision and attention, compensation and benefits package, and opportunity for professional growth offers an attractive setting for our employees to succeed, teach, and learn. This reduces employee turnover and, in turn, provides more value to our clients.

Highmark Companies is looking for a strong SharePoint Developer for a minimum 3-4 month project. 

Description:
The candidate would complete the development of our SharePoint Extranet authentication and role-based management framework utilizing SharePoint alternative access mapping services. This approach would provide forms-based authentication externally and Windows A.D. authentication internally. The extranet solution that runs on top of this framework is a custom grant management application consisting of application forms, multi-part workflows and integration with Microsoft Dynamics CRM via web services and SQL.
Minor mentoring of custom application development in SharePoint is requested to help Senior ASP.Net developers get up to speed quickly. Candidate will work with these developers and a SharePoint designer to complete the initial grant application deliverable.
The team will utilize the Agile SCRUM process (Stories have already been created and the Sprint Backlog tasks have been developed for many of the features).

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Requirements:
• A college graduate possessing 5-7 years of work experience
• Experience in SharePoint MOSS 2007 environment with forms-based authentication, developing advanced custom webparts and custom application pages, and content management and publishing
• Experience in SharePoint MOSS 2007 designer is a major plus
• Experience building public corporate websites and collaboration solutions using MS SharePoint MOSS 2007
• Experience with designing and deploying public-facing websites – Internet, Extranet
• Demonstrated experience developing web sites using the latest technology, for example, Dynamic HTML and JavaScript required
• Experience converting existing content, sites, services into SharePoint 2007
• Knowledge of .Net C# & object oriented design concepts
• Experience with Agile (SCRUM) is a major plus
• Experience with ASP.NET website development
• Knowledge of relational database development concepts
• Understands taxonomies, navigation principles and corporate portal design best practices
• Meets project deadlines and likes working in a fast-paced environment
• Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills
• Communicates with both technical and non-technical staff to support analysis, design, testing, and troubleshooting of project issues as related to usability and design
• Must have strong attention to detail and be able to work as a team player
• Outstanding interpersonal skills, including the ability to work effectively in a team environment, handle internal clients with tact and diplomacy, and maintain composure and production quality under deadline pressure

Technical Support Specialist

About the Job

AV Concepts, a privately held AV staging company in Tempe, AZ seeks a Technical Support Specialist to work with our IT Director in our two person IT department.  To be successful in this role, candidates need to be self starters that can quickly assess and solve problems and work with little or no supervision.  The ideal candidate will have worked in a similar role with limited resources and possess the ability to work with employees at all levels.  An overview of the position is as follows: 


DESCRIPTION: 

Provides technical support in a Windows Networked environment This includes troubleshooting hardware and software issues, including MS Office 2003 (W2K3), w/ Adobe Acrobat Std. v6, & v9 Pro, Sage CRM, Exact-Macola (Accounting Software) and AVAIL (Event Staging Rental Booking Application).

 

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·   Primary emphasis of this role is to manage servers, maintain & upgrade IT systems and support end users:  45% remote users, 55% local base in Tempe.  This position will handle an average of 15-30 trouble tickets per day.

·   Assist in rollout of strategic corporate initiatives and system changes, which may include office moves, company-wide system upgrades and special projects.   IT Project Management experience required.

·   Participate in PC integration activities; this includes imaging and deploying desktop and notebook equipment to associates while ensuring minimum loss of associate data.

·   Enforce and uphold all Corporate and IT Policies and Procedures regarding software, hardware, and equipment use and allocation within the company.  Uphold the IT policies and procedures that enforce the policies of the corporation as it pertains to the management of assets and enforcement of copyright laws.

·   Work with Inventory Control in asset management to include acquisition, identification, recording, tracking and disposing of equipment.

·   Maintain and uphold the accuracy of records in regards to equipment, software, license counts, inventory counts, user ids, access codes and utilization statistics as required.

·   Assist in ensuring a 99.99% planned availability of all network and systems components.  This includes the proper escalation and notifications to management and user communities.

·   Participate in, support and enhance daily operations; opening and closing procedures.

·   Create documentation and perform technical training to assist team members, the end user, and audio visual technicians as needed.

·   Secondary support for departmental personnel and rental audio visual equipment, where it overlaps with IT.  At company warehouse locations only, no show site support for rentals.

 

KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS REQUIRED:

·     Five years experience as a Tech Support Specialist in a Windows Networking environment reporting to various levels of management while servicing a large user base.

·   Must have experience installing/supporting/troubleshooting the following:

o         OS: Server Windows 2003, XP Pro, Windows 7

o         Applications: Office 2k3, 2010 w/ Outlook, Exchange 2003/2007/2010

o         Exact Macola/F9/CMI’s “the System”/Crystal Reports

o         VPN Connectivity – Juniper SSL VPN (see below)

o         HP Procurve Layer 2 & 3 switches/Cisco Routers

o         Sage CRM, Off-site hosted, Outlook integration

o         Desktop and laptop equipment  - Intel Processor based, Win XP Pro/Win 7

o         HP Printers and multi-purpose FAX machines (Some networked, some single user, some stand-alone FAX), HP networked plotters.

o         Canon multi-purpose Copier (Networked Scan and Print)

o         Experience supporting CAD software a plus (AutoCAD - LD Assistant, Vectorworks Spotlight, Auto CAD LT)

·   Must be familiar with PC imaging (experience downloading, transferring, and creating images).

·   Knowledge of PC hardware/software principles and operating systems such as Windows Server 2k3, XP Pro, W2k.  Knowledge of Theos, UNIX, and/or LINUX in a multi-user IP/Serial environment recommended.

·   Experience working within Active Directory:  must know how to navigate within Active Directory, be able to locate, add, remove, and maintain users and resources and identify areas of impact.  Integration of recent Desktop Authority installation.

·   Maintain multiple Mitel VOIP phone systems, including Move, Add, Change, for Internal & Teleworker users.

·   IIS maintenance and troubleshooting, for in-house Back End Web Sites, Trend Micro, rDirectory, WSUS, etc.

·   General Scripting experience, CMD scripts, PERL, etc.

·   Deploy, load, troubleshoot, maintain, Compsee Bar Code readers (Serial Data)

·   Maintain, Upgrade & Troubleshoot Juniper Firewall and VPN Networking equipment

·   Knowledge of PCS & Nextel wireless phone and data equipment, PDAs and Smartphones, and PC and Exchange synchronization; required.

·   Knowledge of installation of cabling & termination requirements for SOHO networks and associated routers, modems, wireless access points, etc., required.

·   Excellent professional interpersonal skills are required to interact with a wide variety of internal and external personnel.

·   Excellent communications skills with an emphasis on follow-through, tracking and meticulous attention to detail are required.

·   Ability to work effectively in a high-pressure environment is required as is the ability to multi-task and coordinate problem resolution management.

·   Ability to take initiative and plan effectively; anticipate and prevent potential user problems.

·   Ability to work off-peak hours to respond to customer needs and ensure availability of PCs and critical systems.  Will serve as “on-call” on alternating weeks.

·   Ability to travel within AZ and CA approximately 2-4x’s monthly.

Interested parties please submit resume for immediate consideration.  This is an urgent need.  AV Concepts offers PTO, medical, dental, vision, life, disability, 401k, and select retail discounts.  Candidates must be able to successfully complete drug screening and criminal background check.  EOE.  We thank all candidates for their submissions, but only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.

Data Base Coordinator - 02416

About the Job

We are a dynamic, growing Commercial Real Estate Investment Company.  We currently have an opportunity in our Torrance, California Office for a Temporary Data Base Coordinator.


Grubb & Ellis Company is one of the world's leading full-service commercial real estate organizations, providing a complete range of transaction, management and consulting services. By leveraging local expertise with our global reach, Grubb & Ellis offers innovative, customized solutions and seamless service to owners, corporate occupants and investors throughout the globe. For more information, visit the Company's Web site at www.grubb-ellis.com.

 

Job Summary

Implements and maintains an efficient database system that provides accurate, timely market data and analysis of commercial real estate trends impacting the local market.  Also assists the Manager, Client Services and /or Research Analyst with the overall functioning of the research department on a daily basis.


Essential Job Duties:

 

  • Database Management and Integrity

·         Maintains and updates existing databases, including Web-based data warehouses, with and overall goal of achieving data quality and accuracy.

·         Responsible for all areas of database information including inventory, classification and ownership, lease/sale properties, lease/sale comparable, tenant information, proposals and related information (taxes, tax id numbers).

·         Required to conduct phone surveys attend specialty meeting and stay in constant communication with brokers / sales staff.

·         Conducts final review of data used for research reports, broker projects, media requests and forecast efforts.

 

  • Responsible for understanding all research related database programs and communicating their capabilities to brokers / sales staff.  This includes the enforcement of the use of research department forms in an effort to maintain quick turnaround time.  Also, assist brokers / sales staff with requests for queries and reports.
  • On a quarterly basis, assists the Research Analyst and/or Manager, Client Services with the overall compilation of market statistics for reporting purposes.  The primary responsibility during this process will be to initiate the data gathering process and solicit broker / sales staff input for verification.
  • Coordinates contact management programs for specialty groups.

 

  • Data Interpretation, Presentation and Marketing
    • Contributes analytical opinions and interpretations of market activity for quarterly studies and various special projects.  Using this knowledge develops client-oriented interpretations and graphics indicating market trends.  Will be responsible for interpreting this data through various graphic formats.

 

  • Computer Software and Hardware

·         Understands all software programs used within the research function including purchased data sources. 

·         Provides training to users on such programs (Metroscan, demographic software).

·         Responsible for developing and modifying database and report forms to meet broker / sales staff requirements.

 

  • Company Programs and Policies

·            Assists the Research Analyst in reporting statistics for local, regional and national publications.  Follows up with brokers / sales staff to endure participation in requests for local information.

·         Responsible for coordinating requests and responses between the office and Company resources (demographic coordinators, Corporate accounting, National councils).

 

  • Holds the decision making power to make modifications, report design changes and format of graphics to meet the requirements of sales people.  Must consult with Manager, Client Services in matters regarding information distribution outside the Company.

 

Other Job Functions:

  • Maintain market information and periodicals most commonly found in the research library. 
  • Consistently update building and brochure files which include building photos, listing sheets, brochures and marketing packages.

 

Skills, Education And Experience:

 

  • Bachelor’s degree (marketing, business administration or similar) or comparable work experience.
  • Minimum of 2+ years previous work experience with database systems.
  • Strong organizational skills.
  • PC familiarity, preferably in spreadsheets, database management or graphics packages and highly proficient with Websites and Web related searches.
  • Initiative and self-reliance
  • Ability to work well with others in a fast paced environment.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.

Position: .Net Technical Project Lead

Location: Horsham, Pennsylvania

Join an award-winning Fortune 1000 company!

Toll Brothers, Inc., America's Leading Luxury Home Builder, seeks a .Net Technical Project Lead to join our team in Horsham, Pennsylvania.

 

This position is responsible for leading the development and support of various .Net Toll Brothers' internal applications; designing hands-on development to code, test, debug, document, implement and support all aspects of the Application Life Cycle; management of third-party software including vendor relationships and internal development projects consisting of 3+ team members; and reporting project statuses to all levels within Toll Brothers.

 

Responsibilities

-          Manage Third Party Vendor Software implementations and Vendor Relationships

-          Management project teams in the support of TBI .Net applications, production support, day-to-day maintenance, application enhancements and upgrades

-          Design and develop .Net applications to meet business defined requirements

-          Fill the role of Toll Brothers’ Technical Lead

-          Help to develop and enforce Portal Solutions standards and procedures

-          Provide regular status updates on projects, including progress updates and identification of technical risks & issues associated with assigned tasks

-          Provide technical guidance and training to other analysts/ programmers within the organization.

-          Stay abreast of changes in technology as it relates to software development

-          Evaluate project requirements, determine and design the best solution to a problem or feature, develop detailed specification, code, test and implement the solution when required

-          Application performance monitoring and tuning

-          Analyze, identify and troubleshoot issues encountered in production and/or reported by customers/users, by investigating and conducting in-depth research on the nature of these issues

TOLL BROTHERS AT A GLANCE

With 3,000 employees and FY 2009 revenues of $1.76 billion dollars, Toll Brothers is the nation's leading builder of luxury homes. Currently building in 6 regions, 20 states and 44 markets, we are the only national builder to focus primarily on the luxury move-up and empty-nester markets. We build custom and semi-custom single and attached homes - principally on land that we develop ourselves - at an average price in backlog of approximately $550,000. Toll Brothers builds luxury single-family and attached home communities and master-planned luxury multi-product residential golf course communities principally on land it develops and improves. The Company operates its own architectural, engineering, mortgage, title, land development and land sale, golf course development and management, home security, and landscape subsidiaries. The Company also operates its own lumber distribution, and house component assembly and manufacturing operations.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

                

Why Work For Florida Career College?

FLORIDA CAREER COLLEGE has been changing the lives of Floridians since 1982 with career programs designed to prepare our students for success. If you are looking for a position that can be rewarding both financially and emotionally, we may be your answer.

FLORIDA CAREER COLLEGE is an established and well-respected industry leader in private post-secondary education. Our nine Florida campuses include Miami, Hialeah, Pembroke Pines, Lauderdale Lakes, West Palm Beach, Boynton Beach, Clearwater, Brandon, and Jacksonville. The work environment is very friendly and modern. We offer Diploma, Associate and Bachelor Degrees and specialize in programs in Business, Cosmetology, Health Sciences, Information Technology, Wellness and School of Nursing. (Not all programs are offered at all locations.) The College is licensed by the State of Florida, approved by the Department of Education and nationally accredited.

FLORIDA CAREER COLLEGE is seeking qualified individuals to become adjunct faculty members. Classes are conducted Monday through Thursday 9am-1pm and 6pm-10pm.

We offer a competitive salary and 401k with company match.

Job Purpose:

·  Promotes and nurtures the education of students by planning and tailoring courses of study according to curriculum guidelines and state requirements; monitors, assists with, and corrects academic efforts; provides a safe environment conducive to learning.

·  Develops and presents lesson plans by using a variety of techniques, including lectures, projects, exhibits, field trips, audiovisual and library resources, computers, and the Internet.

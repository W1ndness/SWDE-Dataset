We are currently interviewing for an Account Consultant – Staff for Investment Services in our San Francisco office.  In this position the primary role of the Client Account Consultant is to be a member of a team responsible for 1st tier support for a specific group of clients.  This person must have Managed Accounts Experience.


 


Job Description:


The Client Account Consultant is responsible for establishing a relationship with users at Client organizations, assisting clients with use of the APL application, owning the troubleshooting and resolution of client application and production issues, identifying opportunities for efficiencies within Client operations, and the working on projects and initiatives helping clients achieve their strategic objectives as identified in the Client's Strategic Account Plan.  The Client Account Consultant is responsible for a thorough understanding of the complex APL application and a general understanding of each client’s operational workflows/processes and trading environment.


 


The successful candidate should be competent to work with clients directly and as part of a team. The Client Account Consultant owns the inquiry through resolution, whether directly handled or escalated through other channels.  The Client Account Consultant is responsible for ongoing internal and external communication of the status of daily client issues. This includes, but is not limited to, the day to day management of tickets to seek resolution and/or responses to client requests and initiatives. The implications of these inquiries could impact trading and other significant operational workflow, so great care must be taken in providing accurate and timely responses.


 


Additional responsibilities included, but not limited to:


·         Providing 1st tier support by taking direct calls from Clients, owning, troubleshooting, and resolving client application or production issues. 


·         Understanding client’s strategic business needs and tactical requests along with FIS initiatives.


·         Research and analyze client's objectives/requests and utilize the company’s work intake process and tools to enable and aggressively shepherd towards a proposed recommendation and/or resolution.


·         Become familiar with client's production environment and system utilization to optimize efficiency and system processing. 


·         Some travel required to visit clients and participate in training at headquarters location in Jersey City, NJ.


 


Skills Required:


·         Minimum 5 years financial services experience.


·         Ability to be the on-call support escalation path outside of normal working hours as assigned by the Supervisor or Manager. 


·          Ability to interact professionally with clients to understand business needs and goals.


·         Ability to write business requirements through research and fact-finding, combined with an understanding of applicable business systems and industry requirements.


·         Ability to interact with client and internal resources to develop or modify moderately complex information systems. Includes analysis of business and user needs, documenting requirements and revising existing system logic as necessary. Guide and advise less experienced client account consultants.


 


Skills Desired:


·         Understanding of the financial services industry


·         A general understanding of the managed accounts business (SMA, UMA, mutual fund advisory, etc.) and APL's role in this space


·         Ability to work effectively independently as well as part of a team


·         Investment management experience


·         APL hands-on experience


 


Education Required:


·         Bachelor’s degree or equivalent

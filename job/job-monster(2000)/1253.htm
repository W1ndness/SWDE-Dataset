Project Manager

About the Job

The AIM Consulting Group is looking for several strong, process oriented Project Manager's to join our client on a 2-3 year project.  These people must have great communication, drive and experience working in a rapidly changing environment.  They must be able to make a commitment to continuous improvement and process control.  Duties will include increased quality of work performed and the decrease of cycle time and associated costs.

Requirements:

-Experience as a process Project Manager or team Project Manager.
-Strong understanding of business and software development flow.
-Strong project management and organizational skills.
-Ability to take outstanding meeting notes and document details very well.
-Good understanding of technical and functional documentation.
-Ability to develop processes that support the business.
-Ability to manage multiple projects with changing scope.
-Ability to handle situations that don't sit still and adapt to changing deliverables.
-PMP certification preferred.
-Six Sigma and ITIL certification or training a plus.

This position includes a full-time salary along with medical benefits and accrued vacation time.

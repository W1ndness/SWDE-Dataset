About the Job

Position: Senior Java Tech Lead NYC (Full-time & contracting opportunities)

Practice: Content Solution

Date:      November 12, 2010

About NortPoint

At NorthPoint, we lead with experience.  Over the years, we have made a concerted effort to attract the best in the business to build our team.  We take great pride in the caliber of people who work here and the collective track record and expertise they bring to the table.  Please visit our company website for additional information www.northps.com

 

Join our growing team of talented Java Technical Leads. Our client list includes Rodale, Pearson & HFMU.S. publishing, Martha Stewart, BET.COM, NYU, Time Inc., ZogSports, Equinox, CHOP (Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia) and Lifetime requires us to hire “top shelf” candidates for our NYC office.

Requirements

·         All candidates must possess a strong foundation in computer science and have demonstrated a proven track record of sound code design, reuse, and maintenance.

·         Ideal candidates possess end-to-end knowledge of the content management and web application space including performance and scalability design and tuning, data migration, SEO strategy execution, CDN implementation, metrics and third party web services integration, and general knowledge of open source products and frameworks. 

·         In addition, the successful candidate will have expertise in general system administration in both Windows and Unix/Linux environments, database administration and tuning, and web server configuration. 

·         Java J2EE Object Oriented Programming skills with Content Management experience

·         Unix/Linux commanding line scripting

·         Third-party search engine configuration and integration

·         Experience with Apache and IIS web server configuration, tuning, and extension modules & HIGH traffic sites

·         Understanding of algorithms as they affect performance and memory usage

 

Experience with Java frameworks such as Struts, Spring, Hibernate, and Lucene are a plus.  Experience with Memcached, Google Search Appliance, MySQL clustering a plus. Python and Ruby are a strong plus. PHP and other scripting languages a plus.

Opportunities:

·         The candidate will work closely clients and team members to develop dynamic, interactive websites that drive revenue for our clients. 

·         Using a broad array of web technologies, candidates must appreciate both the User Experience and Technology aspects of a successful online operation and work with clients to define their requirements and shape their vision for development.

·         NorthPoint projects are delivered with 3-8 person teams so you are not just a lead, you are an active developer on each project.

Competitive Pay and Benefits Include: 

Annual compensation, Medical, Dental, Vision, Long and Short Term Disability, 401K Plan with Company Match, Flexible Spending Account, Public Transportation Incentives, Company Paid Training Programs

About the Job

AllRegs, located in Eagan, MN, is the leading provider of on-line information to the mortgage industry. Founded in 1989, we are the exclusive publisher of mortgage guidelines for 3 of the top 4 lenders in the country as well as Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac and the FHLBC. We enjoy an unbroken record of 21 straight years of profitability and increased revenue growth. Our workforce is comprised of highly skilled, hard working, dedicated professionals who enjoy a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment. We offer a great benefits package and hire only the best. For a better understanding of who we are and what we do, please visit us at www.allregs.com .

AllRegs is looking for two experienced software engineers with at least 5 years experience developing high performance web applications using .NET technologies. This position requires that you be driven to help all stakeholders succeed. You will participate in teams striving for outstanding results and depending on your contributions from your well-developed skills and experience in analysis, development and quality assurance. The position requires the ability to handle multiple priorities with ease and be adaptable as business priorities change. You should have a proven track record in development of reliable, effective web-delivered software products. Besides software development you can expect to have an important role in the evolution of the company's products and use of technology. To handle this responsibility you should have significant experience working with Web 2.0 technologies such as search, collaboration, Content Management systems and practices and XML and its related standards. Do you crave the opportunity to use your skills to get the job done well and help a company grow? Do you want to test your skills to provide important technical contributions in evolving the core technology of the business? Do you want to work for a company that values its employees and its customers?  If your skills and experience significantly match our requirements below, send a resume and cover letter to jobs@allregs.com.  We look forward to hearing from you.

Qualifications
  • Undergraduate degree in Computer Science, or related field or equivalent professional experience. An advanced degree is a plus.

Responsibilities
  • Contribute significantly to product development projects from inception to successful conclusion
  • Prepare task estimates
  • Work with outside consultants and vendors
  • Communicate progress and results to development teams and company leadership
  • Research, evaluate and recommend technologies to improve the business
  • Provide third level technical support for AllRegs subscribers
  • Collaborate with the Software Engineering Manager, CIO and other department leaders, develop and implement processes including appropriate tools that results in quality, cost-effective products and services

Experience
  • 5 to 7 years experience in internet application design and development
  • 3 years minimum experience in application development using C#
  • 3 years minimum experience creating
  • SQL Server stored procedures
  • Experience using TFS and TFS Deployer is a plus
  • Knowledge of Microsoft CRM Development is a plus
  • Strong analysis and problem solving skills.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Thorough knowledge of software development methodology and practice.
  • Superior organizational skills and ability to manage multiple priorities successfully.
  • Must be motivated, professional, and able to work with minimal supervision.

Benefits

  • 85% Employer paid Medical Premium
  • 90% Employer paid Dental Premium
  • Liberal Personal Time Policy

Software Engineer – Level V

About the Job

 

Pac-West is a 30 year old, private equity backed wholesale CLEC/ IXC headquartered in Oakland, California, that offers domestic termination, international termination and domestic origination. Pac-West has developed a hosted telecom infrastructure platform called “Telastic” which will drive growth in the legacy voice business solutions segment and software as a service targeted on the domestic and international telecom market.  Pac-West’s customer base is broad, encompassing blue chip telecom companies across the U.S. as well as well known large global players throughout the world.

 

Software Engineer – Level V

In this senior level position, you will lead the design, development and integration of a next-generation telecommunications platform. This position will require strong experience in VOIP and cloud-based computing technologies. Additionally, we will also rely on you to perform the following duties:

·          Design and develop software systems for large scale, telco-grade environments.

·          Act as team lead for development and implementation of multiple projects.

·          Provide support and guidance to other software engineers.

·          Produce software documentation, including design, user guides and admin guides.

·          Gather business requirements for software development and interact with internal customers to refine those requirements.

·          Design and document requirements for software development.

·          Supply technical and software support to internal and external customers.

·          Provide tier 2/3 support for customer service requests.

·          Develop software according to design documents.

·          Provide maintenance and support for existing software applications. Identify and correct coding as required.

·          Troubleshoot and fix reported defects, release patches and service packs.


Qualifications:

The ideal candidate will possess a Bachelor's degree plus ten years or more related experience and/or training. The successful individual will also possess a strong understanding of Front-end or Back-end development with the following technologies.  

·          Linux

·          Java

·          C++

·          PHP / Perl

·          Postgres / MySQL

·          Flex

·          Web Services (SOAP, REST)

·          VOIP (SIP, H.323, RTP, Asterisk, Sofia-SIP)

 

Benefits:

In exchange for your hard work and dedication, we offer:

·          Competitive salary, depending on experience

·          Medical, dental, vision and life insurance

·          Continuing career education options

·          Paid time off

·          401K

 

Plus, we work to maintain the best possible environment for our employees, where people can learn and grow with the company. We strive to provide a collaborative, creative environment where each person feels encouraged to contribute to our processes, decisions, planning and culture.

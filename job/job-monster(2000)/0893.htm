About the Job

Are you an organized and dedicated individual who is eager to learn and contribute to the overall health of our production systems, satisfy clients, and ultimately contribute the success of a small business?


Then Fieldware may be the right company for you!


Who is Fieldware?

We are a web-based application service provider (ASP) for government clients. The company maintains a casual work environment in downtown Chicago convenient to Metra, the CTA, and local highways.

Our product is an online case management system serving government that integrates a web-based user interface (ASP.NET 3.5/C#), interactive voice response (IVR) system, MS SQL Server 2008 database, and supporting processes.


We are a small company (28 employees) with an expanding development group. This position will be focused on supporting, configuring and deploying updates to our production environment.

Who are we looking for?

We are looking for a solid production support engineer.  As a member of the Production Team, you will be responsible for the setup and maintenance of a highly configurable hosted application, translating the general capabilities of the product into specific client configurations.


Job Responsibilities:

* Directly responsible for all production support Tier-II services on and off hours.
* Primary responsibility to ensure timely response and resolution of production outages.
* Document and perform process recovery procedures for our hosted application.
* Perform production deployments to an IIS/SQL environment.
* Generate configuration SQL scripts for client installations.
* Document deployment and configuration procedures.
* Primary technical resource for our customer support group.
* Generate SQL scripts for testing and AdHoc reporting.
* Investigation of bug reports.
* Secondary responsibility to test software builds before they are deployed to production.
* Configuring environments for sales demos.


Should have solid SQL skills including debugging, writting and troubleshooting.

 

The following qualities would be considered ideal:

* Should have good understanding of software QA and Production Support.
* Bachelors degree in related discipline (e.g., Software Engineering, Computer Science)
* 1-2 years experience with Microsoft SQL Server.
* Demonstrable working knowledge with T-SQL (SQL Server 2008, hands-on querying skills).
* Experience with .NET web applications (configuring and deployment, ASP.NET programming is also a plus).
* 1-3 years of experience supporting or developing Web based applications.
* Experience with all phases of SDLC.
* Excellent written and oral English language skills.
* Strong analytical and problem solving skills.
* Good planning and prioritization skills required.
* Strong documentation skills.
* Scripting language knowledge (Powershell, Perl, etc) is a plus.
* Automated testing tools and strategies.


*If you are new graduate and interestsed in learning to complete these work functions, please apply as well! 

Compensation based on experience. 100% of medical insurance paid by employer, company paid public transportation, employer contribution to 401K, applicable education/certification allowances and bonus opportunities.  We do NOT offer relocation expense packages.

You may obtain information about our company by visiting our website at http://www.fieldware.com .


Please apply directly via this posting.  Phone calls will not be accepted.

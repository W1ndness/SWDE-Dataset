Software Engineer

About the Job

We are looking for a Software Engineer to join our Research & Development team at our world headquarters in Perrysburg, OH!

POSITION SUMMARY:
This position will develop vision software and algorithms for automated vision based inspection systems.  The successful candidate will be expected to possess and use the disciplines and procedures required to develop software in a team environment.  They will also have experience programming in C for a real time environment and developing X Windows or MS Windows applications.  In addition, they must demonstrate a thorough knowledge of structured programming techniques and have excellent written and verbal communications skills.  Activities will include development of new systems as well as modifications to and support of existing systems, assisting with operational testing of new and modified systems, creating specifications and documenting system operation.  Domestic and international travel to glass plants, customer and supplier locations will be required.



REQUIRED SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS:
1. Bachelor of Science - Electrical Engineering or Computer Science or Computer Engineering
2. One or more years structured programming experience – preferably in a real-time environment
3. Windows programming experience – preferably both MS Windows and X Windows
4. Experience in electronic hardware design for embedded systems
5. Operating Systems: Windows XP, Windows XP Embedded, UNIX/Linux.
6. Applications: Microsoft Office. Networks: TCP/IP, Switches
7. Must have excellent written and verbal communication skills.  
8. Must be able to work independently and as a member of a cross functional team.


PREFERRED SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS:
1. Programming expertise in C, C++
2. Experience programming VxWorks and/or PSOS applications
3. Experience programming UNIX and/or Linux applications
4. BSEE/BSCS dual degree
5. Experience and/or understanding of Glass Container Inspection Systems
6. Experience in developing image processing applications

Millions of times a day, O-I glass containers deliver many of the world's best-known consumer products to people all around the world. With the leading position in Europe, North America, Asia Pacific and Latin America, O-I manufactures consumer-preferred, 100 percent recyclable glass containers that enable superior taste, purity, visual appeal and value benefits for our customers’ products. Established in 1903, the company employs more than 24,000 people with 81 plants in 22 countries. In 2009, net sales were $7.1 billion. For more information, visit http://www.o-i.com .

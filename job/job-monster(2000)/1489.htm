Desktop Engineer

About the Job

 

 

Founded in 1989 with headquarters in Reston, Virginia and an office in San Diego, California, CALNET provides a full complement of Information Technology, Intelligence Analysis, Language and Aviation Support services. CALNET provides services in software/application development and testing, web services configuration management, network engineering, systems engineering, information assurance, enterprise service desk and systems administration to Federal, state and commercial customers. CALNET was nominated to the INC 500 list of the fastest growing companies in 2006, 2007, 2008, and 2009. We are an ISO 9001-2008 certified company.

We are currently searching for a Desktop Engineer to join our team! 

About the Job

Individual will be responsible for providing desktop support for end-users.  Individual should be a hard-working and fast-learning individual with the ability to effectively troubleshoot and resolves issues in a timely manner.  Client is a fast-paced environment which requires a professional and customer service-oriented attitude.  The Desktop Engineer will perform a variety of tasks including the following:

 

·         Provide third-level desktop support to end-users running Windows XP workstations and laptops

·         Track/report support issues through a ticketing system

·         Assisting with setup, configuration of desktop client images and builds

·         Provide desktop application support and resolution

·         Provide remote assistance for users across the globe

·         Performing root cause analysis for reoccurring problems and implementing a corrective action

·         Writing knowledge base articles for end users after resolving complex or common problems

·         Maintain hardware and software inventory

·         Coordinating and collaborating with computer specialists at the client site to resolve technical problems

·         Involved in short-and-long term special projects with the end result of creating technology-related improvements for the client workforce (i.e. client image modifications, application virtualization, desktop virtualization, SMS 2003 upgrade to SCCM 2007)

·         Actively researching new technology innovations improving desktop user’s experience and proactively preventing future problems

·         Following ITIL V3 best practices for IT services and providing feedback for continuous improvement

 

General Requirements

·         Bachelor’s or Associate's degree in a related area required

·         3 to 6 years of IT experience; minimum 2 years of Desktop Engineering/Technical Support experience required

·         Minimum of 2 years of Experience with Lotus Notes Client and troubleshooting

·         Extensive knowledge on Desktop troubleshooting  XP Pro/Windows 7

·         Ability to set priorities and be flexible in a fast-paced environment

·         Experience supporting VPN Clients

·         Experience with remote desktop applications (RDP, VNC, SMS Remote)

·         Experience supporting Windows-based operating systems and Microsoft-based products

·         Act as a team player with the ability to work independently

·         Must have excellent work ethic

·         Excellent communication and customer service skills

·         A plus for the following skill sets:

o   Experience with application and/or desktop virtualization

o   A+ Certification

o   Experience with software management and distribution with Microsoft SMS and/or SCCM

o   Experience with VMware products

 

CALNET, Inc. offers a competitive salary and a generous benefits package.  This package includes medical, dental, vision, life, short and long term disability insurances, a 401(k) retirement savings plan, and generous leave time.

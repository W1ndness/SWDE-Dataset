HL7 Software Integration Engineer

About the Job

Omnyx’s vision is to transform the scope of pathology through leveraging medical imaging research and developing a novel, ground-breaking and fully digital pathology solution.  At Omnyx you will make a difference in healthcare and apply every ounce of your experience to design, build and market solutions that break through the current limitations in pathology.  Our products improve patient care by increasing pathologists’ productivity, efficiency, as well as patient safety.  We offer an entrepreneurial atmosphere with rich opportunities for self-directed people to thrive and grow professionally.

 

At Omnyx, we embrace a highly collaborative culture, working very closely with our partners and luminaries to fully understand and embrace the customers’ needs.  Together we will deliver the best digital pathology products available.  We are a high-tech startup with solid financial backing and support from GE Healthcare and University of Pittsburgh Medical Center.  Recently covered by CNBC, Bloomberg, and PBS Nightly Business along with a host of print media Omnyx is the company to watch as the digital pathology market takes shape.


The successful candidate will join the Omnyx SW R&D group for six month to one year contract and act under direct supervision from senior engineering staff to perform general SW engineering  tasks including:

Your primary responsibility on the team will be to design and implement the integration of out Digital Pathology products with Anatomic Pathology Laboratory Information Systems (APLIS) and lead other integration efforts with existing Healthcare Information Technology (HC IT) products. The ideal candidate would bring strong technical skills, domain expertise & experience, and be an excellent team player. In addition, the candidate would be a strong communicator with good analytical skills who is looking for the ultimate next challenge of transforming pathology.

  • Design and Implement the APLIS integration for our products
  • Lead and contribute to other integrations such as EMR, ADT, HIS, RIS, etc.
  • Ensure compatibility of our products with Healthcare IT standards
  • Work in cross-functional project teams that include Development, Marketing, Usability, Software Quality Assurance, Customer Learning, and Support.
  • Develop detailed requirements, functional specifications, and design documents
  • Understand and follow IT quality system procedures applicable to the job

Qualifications/Requirements

  • This position is open to internal and external candidates
  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent
  • 5+ years experience with C#/.NET and/or C++ and OOD
  • Experience implementing or integrating with CoPath (Cerner or Sunquest) or other APLIS systems.
  • 3+ years of experience in HC IT

Desired Characteristics

  • MS in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Knowledge of clinical data, architecture, patient management systems, controlled medical vocabularies, DICOM, HL7, CDA, CCOW, IHE workflows
  • Understanding of HIPAA, identity management, auditing, and access control
  • Understanding of patient reconciliation and related identifiers
  • Experience with interface engines: Mirth, CloverLeaf, OpenEngine, ConnectR, BizTalk
  • Experience with FDA-regulated software systems, Agile software development
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, demonstrated initiative and analytical thinking style, "can-do" attitude and strong team player

 

All candidates must be willing to take a drug test, submit to a background investigation and must be legally authorized to work in the United States without work sponsorship at the time of hire.


Qualified candidates:  Please send resume and salary history for consideration to yolanda.belli@ommyx.com

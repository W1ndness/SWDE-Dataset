Position: Document Management Analyst

Location: Horsham, Pennsylvania

Join an award-winning Fortune 1000 company!

Toll Brothers, Inc., America's Leading Luxury Home Builder, seeks a Document Management Analyst to join our team in Horsham, Pennsylvania. This role will support all aspects of document management system with a focus on providing a high level of client service to Toll Brothers’ multiple departments, divisions, and ancillary businesses.

 

Responsibilities:

  • Implement capture devices (standalone scanners, multi-function devices) and various capture methods for the document management system (DMS)
  • Support document management system and resolve technical issues within outlined SLO (service level objective) in tandem with DMS team and departmental resources
  • Primarily responsible for all DMS user administration
  • Develop DMS file cabinets and bins to support client requirements for document retention and collaboration
  • Work directly with clients to gather requirements and perform business analysis as inputs towards document management system development
  • Assist in developing, testing, implementing, and supporting new document management solutions that increase business efficiency and productivity, streamline business processes, and fulfill document retention requirements
  • Assist in developing customized multi-function device capture interfaces and menus using VBScript and NSI AutoStore
  • Train users and create training documentation for new document management solutions
  • Participate in system architecture projects and actively work to improve the document management system and its surrounding processes
  • Collaborate with resources within IT and third parties (vendors, solutions providers) for advanced solutions, issue resolutions, and DMS system improvements
  • Assist in administering and developing application integrations within DMS and external applications

This is an excellent opportunity to join a billion-dollar Fortune 1000 company!

Qualifications:

Skills

  • Excellent client service skills are required
  • Excellent capacity to learn in a hands-on work environment and apply acquired knowledge to projects, support tasks, and training initiatives
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, and organizational skills are essential
  • Ability to create technical documentation that is clear and concise
  • Ability to work creatively, independently, and efficiently to provide new document management solutions to clients with a high level of customer service delivery
  • Familiarity with SDLC practices
  • Exposure to Oracle, HTML, VBScript, and Perl
  • Exposure to document management capture devices and systems
  • Familiarity with Oracle databases a plus
  • Experience with Feith Systems or document imaging/management methodologies a plus

TOLL BROTHERS AT A GLANCE

With 3,000 employees and FY 2009 revenues of $1.76 billion dollars, Toll Brothers is the nation's leading builder of luxury homes. Currently building in 6 regions, 20 states and 44 markets, we are the only national builder to focus primarily on the luxury move-up and empty-nester markets. We build custom and semi-custom single and attached homes - principally on land that we develop ourselves - at an average price in backlog of approximately $550,000. Toll Brothers builds luxury single-family and attached home communities and master-planned luxury multi-product residential golf course communities principally on land it develops and improves. The Company operates its own architectural, engineering, mortgage, title, land development and land sale, golf course development and management, home security, and landscape subsidiaries.

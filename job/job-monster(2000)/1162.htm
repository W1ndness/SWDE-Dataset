Business Intelligence Architect

About the Job

Come join one of the fastest growing market leaders specializing in the exciting field of Revenue Management and Profit Optimization. The Rainmaker Group (www.LetItRain.com), a privately-owned, entrepreneurial company, is seeking an energetic and highly motivated individual to expand our talented and accomplished Development team.

Our sophisticated product line and our dedication to client care have made us #1 in our market. Our software solutions and consulting services are used by many of the biggest names in both the gaming-hospitality and multi-family housing industries. We maintain a small staff focused on high-quality products and exceptional client services, where each and every team member’s contributions are visible and important to our continued success.

Position Overview

The Business Intelligence Architect will work with the development team on design and construction of business intelligence solutions and data architecture for Rainmaker product suite.

Duties and Responsibilities

·         Design, develop, test and support Business Intelligence solutions including SQL Server datamart and data cube.

·         Review, design, develop, test and support application databases supporting Rainmaker products. 

·         Evaluate current database architecture and operations environment for operational improvements.

·         Work closely with the development team to ensure timely completion of deliverables.

·         Alert colleagues to emerging technologies or applications and the opportunities to integrate them into operations and activities.

·         Mentor team members, including operations DBA in proactive database methods.

·         Be actively involved in and contribute regularly to the development team.

·         Participate in the introduction of new technologies into the application.

·         Work within the team to evolve our technical guidelines and coding standards.

Programming/Computer Skill requirements

·         3-5 years development experience using Microsoft  SQL Server 2005 required, SQL Server 2008.

·         Development experience with SQL Server Analysis Services.

·         Expert experience in development of stored procedures and database optimization techniques.

·         Development experience with SQL Server Reporting Services, Integration Services.

·         Must possess excellent consultive, analysis and communication skills.

·         Ability to organize and manage multiple priorities

·         Experience of working in a group environment

·         Net framework, ASP.NET, C#, C++ experience a plus

 

The Rainmaker Group supports flextime and telecommuting, requires occasional travel and offers excellent benefits including medical, dental and 401 (k) matching. Our offices are located near North Point mall in Alpharetta, Georgia.

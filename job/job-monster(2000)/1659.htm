About the Job

 

The National Rural Electric Cooperative Association (NRECA), with offices in Arlington, VA and Lincoln, NE is the trade association for over 900 consumer-owned electric cooperatives serving more than 40 million people.

 

NRECA is committed to harnessing the strength of America’s electric cooperatives into a single powerful voice.

  • 50 Best Places to Work - The Washingtonian
  • 100 Best Places to Work - ComputerWorld Magazine
  • CARE Award Recipient - Recognizing organizations that promote a positive work-life balance

 

We are growing our BizTalk development team in Arlington, VA! 

 

NRECA is integrating all major applications utilizing BizTalk and web services.  In support of the work,  this position will play an integral role in the full life-cycle development of applications on the BizTalk Server environment and related technologies (Data Direct, InfoPath, .NET Framework, and SharePoint), and will be involved in the critical processes of integration, design, development, implementation, and production support. 

 

Purpose and Mission:

 

·         Development includes requirements analysis and definition, design, testing, and implementation of BizTalk integration projects in support of business goals

·         Participate in code reviews and production monitoring – as part of the team, you will be instrumental in ensuring processes can support the existing service oriented architecture (SOA), defined guidelines, and coding standards

·         Resolve problems and identify process enhancements to increase operating efficiencies

·         Provide comprehensive documentation of BizTalk, SOA, and integration processes

·         Seek out leading-edge knowledge of industry trends related to BizTalk, SOA, and web services through a variety of training venues

·         Build enduring partnerships with end users and internal clients as a trusted business advisor

 

Requirements and Qualifications:

 

  •      Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or Information Systems Technology (or 4-6 years of equivalent experience)
  •      3+ years of progressive application development experience utilizing a full System Development Lifecycle (SDLC)
  •      Hands-on experience directly related to BizTalk tools and related technologies for more than one year, combined with two to four years of experience in relational database development, MS SQL, and web development technologies (HTML, JavaScript, and CSS); solid understanding of C# and .NET 3.5 Framework preferred

 

In addition to your technology skills, bring your ability to be effective on multiple projects simultaneously, and the motivation to collaborate as part of the team to resolve problems and discuss solutions.

 

Qualified candidates must apply on-line at www.nreca.jobs or http://tinyurl.com/2u5ynjp

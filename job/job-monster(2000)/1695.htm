Engineering Architect (Job Scheduling)

About the Job

Fiserv, Inc. (NASDAQ: FISV) is the leading global provider of information management and electronic commerce systems for the financial services industry, driving innovation that transforms experiences for financial institutions and their customers. Ranked No. 1 on the FinTech 100 survey of top technology partners to the financial services industry.

Fiserv (NASDAQ: FISV), is a Fortune 500 company and the leading global provider of financial services technology solutions, bringing information management and electronic commerce systems and services to the financial services industry. Leading services include transaction processing, outsourcing, electronic bill payment and presentment, investment management solutions, business process outsourcing (BPO), software and systems solutions. Headquartered in Brookfield, Wisconsin, with significant operations in Norcross, Georgia, and other locations.  Fiserv is the leading provider of core processing solutions for U.S. banks, credit unions and thrifts.


 


With Fiserv, you will create and deliver the Fiserv solutions that drive client satisfaction. You can grow professionally and personally in an environment where intelligence, innovation, and leadership are valued and rewarded. Fiserv holds true to two essential principles: the client comes first, and people make the difference. Fiserv invites you to join us in building on those principles to lead our clients and our company to success. 


 


We are currently recruiting for an Engineering Architect to work in our Norcross, GA. facility or.


 


Responsibilities:


The Engineering Architect - Tools Engineer (Architect)   – will be a part of the Tools Engineering team in Fiserv’s FET-ETS Organization. This role is a result-oriented individual to support FET’s vision and mission of consolidation and Fiserv 2.0, to be able to provide efficient and quality services to Fiserv customers. A Tools Engineer (Architect) will lead the tool evaluations, integrations, interfaces, upgrades, and deployments, with a focus on Monitoring tools for a data center. This role will also be responsible for requirements gathering and building/maintaining of roadmaps for these tools.

Tools Engineering team has dedicated focus on Tools (3rd party Applications) that are or will be under the ownership of FET. This team is responsible for consistent approach and process for evaluation, deployment, maintenance and retirement of Tools (3rd party applications) in FET. The charter of this team includes ensuring a holistic approach to engineering of Tool solutions to maximize the leverage from each tool, and encompass the needs comprehensively for the engineering solution in question, operations, support & maintenance of the tool, audits etc. This team is an enabler for productivity, efficiency and synergy benefits from ongoing standardization and centralized Life Cycle management of Tools to accomplish Fiserv 2.0 vision.

The initial set of standardized tools spans over the areas of Systems Management, Provisioning, Automation, Job Scheduling, CMDB and ITIL Processes (Change Mgmt, Config Mgmt, Service Desk).  The primary focus for this engineer will be the Job Scheduling strategy and implementation.

This role will need working closely with other teams in ETS and in FET (Platform Engg, Core Engg, ISS, Service Management, National Operations, Enterprise Architecture, IT Optimization, and Finance) to effect timely identification, standardization and deployment of Tools across identified primary data centers in Fiserv.

This position requires an extensive background in evaluation, architecture, and deployment of 3rd party applications/tools.

Skills & Experience Requirements
7-10 years of leading the technical aspects of multiple IT/Infrastructure projects ranging in various degrees of complexity. Formal 3rd party application/tool architecture experience is preferable.

This role will be required to:
¾ Demonstrate deep understanding of needs, technology and tool interfaces for Job Scheduling
¾ quickly understand the comprehensive tools deployment plan and inter-dependencies over the next 12-18 months for an initial set of identified tools to meet the imminent needs of other FET initiatives (e.g. Data Center Consolidations)
¾ lead 1-2 tool deployments concurrently, and managing these engagements with vendors
¾ prepare test strategy, plans and data for testing a new deployment and/or enhancement
¾ produce documentation on standards and guidelines for deployment and use of tools
¾ assist and/or lead the root cause analysis, vendor liaison, and resolution to production issues for tool implementations
¾ interface at various levels of FET organization and Fiserv Business Units to present/evangelize FET Tools standards and their benefits, including the industry trends & best practices, and TCO to the company
¾ build and own rolling multi-year roadmaps for assigned standardized tools
¾ lead the evaluation and selection efforts for 3rd party tools. This would include defining the evaluation/POC requirements and selection criteria, identifying key technical and business inter-dependencies and constraints, doing cost estimates and financial analysis, preparation and presentation of the business case
¾ have solid experience of dealing with 3rd party application vendors, for evaluations and deployments
¾ provide inputs to refining the strategy and charter for Tools Engineering Team to align with long-term FET strategy
¾ work in a fast paced environment with the ability to adapt and change priorities as the business needs change

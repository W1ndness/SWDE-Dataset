Business Analyst

About the Job

Headquartered in Melbourne, Florida, Amovius is a software delivery organization, providing project based solutions at the customer site and in our US based development center. Our 11,000 sq. ft. software development center is located in Melbourne, Florida. We offer aggressive blended rate models for software development and quality assurance. Our customer engagement model is similar to offshore companies, but we have a clear advantage from a risk standpoint. Our ability to be at the customer site when needed, be on the phone with the customer during business hours, provide a perfect cultural match and truly understanding your business gives us a clear advantage over an offshore option.


Our main client base is in Atlanta, GA and we are seeking skilled individuals in that area.


Job Description
  • Interview Customers, Gather business requirements and help formulate product direction.
  • Document the customer requirements and functional specification using standard templates.
  • Outstanding business acumen and an ability to adapt to working in multiple business environments.
  • Create a Technical specification including the following:
    • Actor Catalog
    • Actor Definitions
    • Use case model
    • Use cases
    • Process Model
    • Object Model
    • Data Model
    • Infrastructure design
  • Ability to communicate and direct the Application development team based on the technical specifications.

Standards:

  • Maintain an awareness of appropriate industry and vendor standards.
  • Develop software development and technical standards that are consistent with the company quality standards and application frameworks.
  • Recommend and implement policies and procedures to safeguard company and customers’ information and information systems including security requirements, backup and recovery, etc.

Quality:

  • Maintain a consistent and high quality approach by following the systems development life cycle.
  • Ensure the quality of the technical implementation through validation of the technical design and the completeness of documentation and procedures for the architecture, design, development, installation, and maintenance of the IT infrastructure and projects.
  • Assist the product teams with the implementation of procedures and certification requirements for compliance with industry, data administration, system development, commercial software releases (version control, packaging of releases, etc.) and software distribution (source control and change management) standards.
  • Ensure systems perform in accordance with requirements, applicable industry standards, regulations, laws and company practices.
  • Assist in the development and execution of tests to demonstrate the performance, reliability, scalability and recoverability of production systems

Job Experience

 

The ideal candidate will have:

  • Minimum 3-5 years experience developing requirements and performing business analysis for medium and large application development projects.  Full life cycle experience required. 
  • Ability to communicate effectively verbally and written with all stakeholders including developers, business people, analysts, and senior management
  • Experience working in a global software development team, onshore and offshore.
  • Understanding of software design and development including programming languages (Java, C++), Object oriented analysis and design, and application architecture, application integration (e.g. WEB Methods), and relational databases
  • Initiative leadership capability.  Ability to drive issues through to completion.
  • Ability to review and evaluate functional and technical design
  • Experience on WEB Based software development projects.   
  • Should have a general knowledge of some of the following technical areas:
    • Business Processes Re-engineering
    • Development Environments
    • Hardware/Network
    • Messaging and Inter-process Communications
    • Systems Infrastructure
    • Release management
  • Must be hands-on, detailed oriented and have the ability to quickly adapt to changes and new ideas.
  • Must be experienced in the domain of the application, and in software development
  • Must have risk analysis and management, estimation, planning and decision analysis skills
  • Must have presentation and communication and negotiation skills
  • Must have a history of making sound decisions quickly under stress
  • Must have good interpersonal skills and show sound judgment
  • Must be focused on the delivery of customer value, in the form of executing software that meets (or exceeds) the customer's needs.
  • Must have excellent communication skills with the ability to communicate effectively and clearly, in writing and/or orally, both in one-on-one and group presentation situations.
  • Must be able to interact and communicate with all levels of staff and project management in both the business and the technical areas, with the ability to keep discussions at the appropriate level of abstraction.
  • Possess excellent analytical, problem solving, organizational, interpersonal, and motivational skills, with the ability to move cleanly from theoretical to implementation thinking.

Business Analyst - Procurement

About the Job


Job ID: 337

Position Description:
PURPOSE OF JOB:
To provide leadership, demonstrated processes, methodology, direction, coordination and proper integration with the business related to the functional best in class practices, system design, management of configuration changes, trouble shooting, testing, training and support for Procurement and PM systems. In addition this role will ensure proper integration is maintained related to the financial integration touch points with all other modules, including the development of business processes and tactics required to support costing, financial information systems and accounting practices. This position is essential for supporting best in class practices and will pro-actively identifying all potential process related issues in deliver required system functionality. The position will support any functional re-engineering efforts to improve the functional processes by leading functional decision-making efforts and gaining consensus on open functional issues, gaining overall functional involvement and support for the functional area, leading functional implementation, and serving as a functional Subject Matter Expert. This position must provide the infrastructure to timely and accurately support the month end closing process.

DIMENSIONS OF JOB:
To provide the leadership, direction, coordination and proper integration of the business processes involved in the functional areas of Procurement, Discrete Inventory and CMMS systems. This includes all related global support and trouble-shooting capabilities for all users covering the Procurement and PM activities related to Oracle Procurement (Including Receiving) and Tabware, while supporting the financial and supply chain integration points as they other related Oracle modules. Serve as backup to support AP functionality.

Position Requirements:
JOB DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:
The Oracle Application Analyst is responsible for maintaining the application and integration of business processes to support the functional areas assigned. They also must participate all required development efforts within HEM. This includes the design, deployment and ultimately the support once deployed. The business processes will be owned by a Functional Lead within the organization.

• Accountable to recommend and implement business processes and solutions which resolve functional user needs. Meet with functional or cross functional users to determine information systems solutions necessary to achieve identifiable business benefit.
• Key architect for system configuration and delivered functionality.
• Develop specific system requirements including time and resources needed to complete project.
• Develop technical and economic justifications for the proposed systems solutions.
• Develops project specifications, insuring that all design aspects are appropriately addressed and that detailed specifications are provided for programming; may be involved in development of more complex programs.
• Provide integrated solutions to meet end user needs.
• Schedule and execute system and integration tests to insure effectiveness in meeting user needs.
• Directs and handles the preparation of complete, standard systems documentation and user operating procedures. Ensure all support Global standardized processes.
• Insure the continuing effectiveness of in-place systems through appropriate enhancements, testing and modification.
• Oversee and performs the training of user personnel in new or modified systems.
• Serve as primary support for AP functionality.

MAJOR CHALLENGES:
- Ensuring that the new functional processes and systems will delight our external customers while accurately and effectively supporting the business analysis needs of our internal customers.
- Pro-actively leading the functional organization through a series of major cultural and strategic changes intended to standardize our processes, procedures and systems in accordance with industry best practices.
- Prioritizing multiple tasks; maintaining equanimity while driving hard to meet project-specific milestones and regular monthly, quarterly, and year-end deadlines; executing projects on-time and within budget.
- The unpredictable schedule of diverse tasks associated with this position will require working outside of normal business hours, will require overnight business travel, and will involve some stress in meeting tight deadlines which will arise from time to time.
- Influencing decision-making across business segments with solid analysis and personal communication skills.
- Raising the level of understanding within the areas of Procurement, Manufacturing and Finance in areas Procurement and PM methodologies and best in class practices.
- Diagnosing problems based on user-provided symptom information, and providing technical problem resolutions to non-technical users.
- Keeping up to date on the knowledge of all aspects of owned Oracle modules to provide support in the form of troubleshooting and repair on short notice. Being able to make recommendations for using additional functionality of applications as they relate to areas of support.

Position Attributes:
SPECIALIZED OR TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE/SKILL:
The technical skills required:
• Technical and Functional understanding of how Oracle 11i applications are integrated;
• Minimum five years of experience in information systems development with strong Oracle Tools knowledge;
• Capable of writing reports and views for Discover & Business Objects based Reporting -
• Generic Oracle Application skills (e.g., running reports, navigating);
• Oracle System Administration;
• Oracle tools (e.g., Alerts, FSG).
• Experience with business process design or ERP implementations
Functional Skills required -
• BS/BBA Degree with a major in Supply Chain Management or technical field with 3-5 years job experience. CPM or CPIM a plus.
• Process manufacturing experience with at least 5 years progressive experience supporting supply chain solutions. Requires functional knowledge of Procurement, Payables, PM, and Inventory Management. Knowledge of Manufacturing process a plus.
• Solid general financial knowledge with a strong grasp of the Oracle financial related to general ledger integration points from Oracle Procurement (Including Receiving) and Tabware subsystems.
• Broad knowledge of functional best practices in the mineral/chemical industries as well as the global business environment.
Personal traits
• Demonstrate the ability to partner with functional end user community in the area of support
• Strong leadership capabilities
• Ability to thrive in a team environment
• Ability to work independently across all levels of the organization
• Demonstrate excellence in managing projects
• Outstanding facilitator and communicator; lead innovation by teaching, consensus-building, and project execution.
• Solid decision-maker with excellent analytical skills.
• Proven ability to solve problems and influence people across all functional areas..

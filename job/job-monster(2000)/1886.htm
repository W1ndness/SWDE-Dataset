About the Job

Celebrating Over 50 years of Discovery!

Grand Circle Corporation has offered over fifty years of leadership in international travel, adventure, and discovery for Americans 50+.  Over 1.5 million Americans have joined us on an incredible journey, visiting countries we never dreamed of exploring a decade ago. Our travelers visit destinations such as the Great Wall of China, Africa, Antarctica, the Red Sea, and the shores of Normandy. 

 

We have offices around the world, and are widely recognized for our innovative philanthropic efforts worldwide. We help change people's lives! Visit our websites at www.oattravel.com and www.gct.com
 

We are seeking a Senior Business Intelligence Developer to join our Information Systems team. In this position you will be responsible for developing and implementing tools/applications for accessing and analyzing data and metadata.   You will design and develop training for end users and coach team members.  Our ideal candidate will have a Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 6 years of Information Technology development experience, specifically supporting Data Warehouse environments. Experience working with Hyperion v9 or higher, including Hyperion Interactive Reporting, Studio and/or Designer, Essbase and a minimum of three years experience in Oracle, Toad, SQL and Java Scripting is preferred.

 

Position Responsibilities:

  • Under minimal direction, develop and implement tools or applications for accessing and analyzing data and metadata
  • Customize tools and data sets for specific use by different types of data consumers
  • Develop business-oriented metadata definitions
  • Design and develop training for end users
  • Prepare documentation that defines and prioritizes end-user requirements
  • Provide data expertise and a mastery of relevant business processes
  • Contribute to technical and architectural design of database and mapping specs
  • Adhere to and drive report development and usability standards
  • Coach and mentor team members.
  • Interact with diverse populations from user level to first-line management level
  • Lead medium to large projects: estimate and manage project tasks

 

Position Requirements and Qualifications:

  • Bachelors degree required with at least 6 years of IT development experience supporting Data Warehouse environments and at least 4 years in Business Intelligence.
  • At least 3 years experience working with Hyperion v9 or higher including Hyperion Interactive Reporting, Studio and/or Designer, Essbase.
  • Similar experience with Cognos, Business Objects, Microstrategy is a plus.
  • Experience with data visualization and in-line memory BI tools also a plus.
  • At least 3 years experience working with Oracle, Toad, SQL and Java Scripting
  • Experience with training users on new Hyperion features and functions.
  • Proven individual contributor and team player with high communication skills
  • Ability to thrive in change by having a fast, flexible, cooperative work style and ability to reprioritize at a moment’s notice.

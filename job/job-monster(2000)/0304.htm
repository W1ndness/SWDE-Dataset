JOB TITLE:                                  C++ Application Developer               

LOCATION:                                  Houston, TX

POSITION TYPE:                         Direct

COMPANY PROFILE

Work for a leading provider of energy and related financial services to its customers and trading counter parties.  This investment bank has a market capitalization of $17.3bn, total assets under management of $153.4bn, and almost 10,000 staff in 24 countries. Our client encourages their employees to realize their potential in an environment that values creativity and innovation. They’ll offer you the freedom to make your mark with your team, clients, the organization and the industry. This company’s success is built on its people. So don’t miss out on this amazing opportunity.

RESPONSIBILITIES

Our client is looking for a mid to senior level C++ developer. The developer should have the following skills:

  • C++, data processing & business logic;  (not a systems programmer or someone that’s worked on testing hardware)
  • SQL skills (Sybase or others is a plus)
  • Python scripting
  • Business skills with Trading or Energy Trading

REQUIRED SKILLS

  • Self directed.  Needs to be able to handle the full life-cycle of work associated with Software development.  (analysis, design, construction, testing, deployment)
  • Willing to work with strong internal standards & processes.
  • Communication skills, some user interactions will be required.
  • Team work.  Will be working with teams domestically and abroad.

Senior Business Systems Analyst, Oracle Financials

About the Job


Job ID: 909

Position Description:
Concur (NASDAQ: CNQR) is the world’s leading provider of on-demand Employee Spend Management services. Trusted by thousands of organizations to reach millions of employees, Concur’s award-winning solutions streamline travel booking, expense reporting and invoice processing – delivering rapid ROI by helping companies increase efficiency, control employee spend and drive down operational costs.


Concur was recently ranked third on Seattle Business Magazine's 100 Best Companies to Work For 2010 - results are based on the independent survey results of our own employees. Concur's employees ranked the company highly in virtually every survey category, from hiring and retention to corporate culture. More information about Concur is available at www.concur.com .


Job Overview:
The Business Systems Analyst Senior is a functional analyst supporting Concur’s IS-Application Services department. A key to this role’s success will depend upon the candidate’s ability to maintain strong working relationships with our internal business partners which encompasses finance, accounting, and others as necessary. Additionally, this individual will partner with internal clients and users to provide support and recommendations through requirements gathering, documentation, analysis, functional design, and implementation plans in order to meet corporate goals and objectives.


This position requires an analytical mindset, accuracy, attention to detail and creative problem solving abilities. You will assist in coordinating and assembling cross functional teams, creating project plans, managing scope and resource planning, leveraging proven standards within a project management framework to execute daily work activities. This position will report to the Senior Manager, IT Application Services.
Responsibilities:


  • Become cross-functional subject matter expert for internal systems and applications.
  • Review, analyze and recommend process efficiencies.
  • Gather and document business and functional requirements.
  • Partner with business process owners in the organization to meet corporate objectives.
  • Identify, track and analyze key metrics that impact the business.
  • Execute special projects as assigned.
  • Work with key stakeholders and executive sponsors to define project deliverables, key measures of success, and estimated ROI.
  • Maintain excellent communication regarding project status and priorities with project sponsors, driving planning and tracking of tasks, schedule, resources, dependencies and success criteria.
  • Maximize productivity of existing resources, increase throughput and utilization and plan for capacity changes.
  • Serve as liaison between technical and non-technical departments in order to ensure that all targets and requirements are met.
  • Interaction with end-user community, business applications team and operations personnel throughout the process of selecting, designing developing, implementing and maintaining application software to ensure customer needs are met an appropriate capacity, controls and system integrity are maintained.
  • Comply with corporate policies


Position Requirements:
Education, Experience & Training required:

  • Bachelor’s or graduate degree in a business, accounting/finance, technology or equivalent preferred
  • 5+ years working with enterprise software, financial systems, billing systems or BI reporting solutions required.

    • 3+ years Business analysis experience required.
    • 2+ years professional experience with Oracle Financials, including GL, AP, and AR modules.

  • Proven experience in IT business analyst / functional analyst role(s)
  • Software or related industry experience preferred.
  • Project management experience preferred.
  • Ability to pass a background check.


Job Specific Specialized Knowledge & Skills:

  • Experience writing business requirements, functional specifications, detailed design documents, technical requirements, and test plans.
  • Must demonstrate a solid understanding of the software development lifecycle.
  • In-depth knowledge of Oracle (Financials) especially Oracle 11i/R12 and how various modules integrate with each other
  • Strong knowledge of Oracle’s core financial modules: GL, AP, AR
  • Experience designing interfaces and creating specs for development.
  • Ability to run and analyze basic SQL scripts
  • Ability to retrieve and analyze information from various client data systems
  • Extensive experience with financial applications and transaction flow
  • General accounting knowledge and understanding of business and accounting processes, including debits & credits, billing, general ledger and expense accounting
  • Demonstrated experience with project execution in one or more of the following areas: application life cycle processes; projects to improve customer and/or business relationship management, or projects to deliver quality or cost reduction through improvements to operational processes.
  • Experience working with functional business owners.
  • Solid understanding of Project Life Cycle (Requirement, scope, test, etc)
  • Solid understanding of business and accounting processes
  • Strong understanding of change management for business processes and systems
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, including strong communication and influence with senior level management.
  • Experience with reporting tools, such as Cognos, Discoverer, or similar end-user reporting tool is a plus.
  • Familiarity with enterprise data systems
  • Experience with MS Office (Power Point, Visio, MS Project, Word, Excel)

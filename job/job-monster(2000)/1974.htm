PC Technicians/Desktop Engineers Needed!

About the Job

NMR Consulting is a service-disabled, veteran-owned small business (SDVOSB) that delivers managed business services from a unique risk-based perspective. Customized solution sets in risk management, information technology, and logistics support consider our federal and commercial clients’ unique assets and missions and deliver solutions that efficiently meet their needs, while mitigating the impacts associated with defined threats and vulnerabilities. 

Job Title: PC Technician 
Location: Falls Church, VA 
Hours: Part-time. Thursdays during the day. Flexible scheduling. 
Compensation: $20-$24 per hour 
Clearance: Top Secret or above 

RESPONSIBILITIES/DUTIES: 
• Assess workstations and identify project priorities 
• De-assemble PC’s and networks according to NMR processes 
• Organize PC parts and place in secure packaging 
• Re-assemble PC workstations according to client map 
• Connect parts which includes monitor, keyboard, tower, mouse, and cords 
• Troubleshoot PC’s to assure that they are running correctly and are able to connect to the network 


REQUIREMENTS OF POSITION: 
• Knowledge of PC’s, workstations, and IT networks 
• Ability to disconnect, reconnect, and handle PC’s and PC parts 
• Top Secret Clearance is required 

E-mail your resume to Recruiting@NMRconsulting.com , include "PC Technician/Falls Church" in the subject line. 

Visit www.NMRconsulting.com and click on "Careers" for more details. 

*Referral bonuses available!!!

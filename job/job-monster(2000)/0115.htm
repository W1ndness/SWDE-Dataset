CampusVue Senior Administrator - Phoenix

About the Job

Job Summary

The CampusVue Senior Administrator/Senior Business Analyst will be required to work independently to resolve business needs through advanced administration, work with assigned functional areas to understand problems and needs, prioritize, determine solutions, and implement assigned projects. Additionally this position will work closely with Systems Administrators, Database Administrators, Database Developers, Application Super Users, Business Analysts and, End Users in configuration and troubleshooting the CampusVue student information system.


Key Job Elements

·         Interact with user departments to understand business environment

·         Assist users in translating concepts to written requirements.

·         Define application refinements at a high enterprise level.

·         Detail specific user-required modifications, and modify software if applicable.

·         Evaluate test results with change requestor(s).

·         Schedule enhancements for production environment.

·         Obtain user sign-off at completion of project.

·         Other duties as assigned.

·         Understand key business processes and recommend CampusVue enhancements and configurations to optimize them.

·         Understand and influence CampusVue release roadmap and correlation to EDMC's current processes and configurations.

·         Assess and optimize EDMC's current usage of the CampusVue information system and key integration points for relevancy, optimization, change, and defects

·         Configure and test development and testing environments for proof of concept demonstrations.

·         Communicate the current status of releases, major projects, and ongoing CampusVue activities to relevant stake holders


Reports To:
Director, Information Technology

Interacts With:
All levels of IT and user departments.

Job Requirements

·         Bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems, Computer Science, or equivalent experience required. 

·         Five years of experience interfacing with various layers of a business organization to identify possible areas of improvement or risk, perform advanced system administration, develop use cases including business, data, functional and technical requirements directly related to the CampusVUE Student Information System  (ERP).

·         Three to Five years of solid experience in a similar role analyzing business software needs.

·         Experience in a wide area of IT platforms and protocols as well as software training.

·         Current knowledge of hardware and software options.

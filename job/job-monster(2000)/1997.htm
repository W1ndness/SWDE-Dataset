Full-Time, Senior HP LoadRunner Support Engineer

About the Job

About Genilogix:

 

Founded in 2001, Genilogix is an international information technology consulting and software engineering/development firm that optimizes quality, performance, and availability in mission-critical applications. As an HP Software Platinum Business Partner and VMware Enterprise Partner, Genilogix provides services such as support, training, and implementation to help customers maximize their return on investment.  Genilogix is a global organization with headquarters in Pittsburgh, PA and offices in 10 major US markets.

 

We are currently seeking a Full-Time, Senior HP LoadRunner Support Engineer for our corporate headquarters located in Pittsburgh, PA.   

 

Position Summary:

 

The Senior HP LoadRunner Support Engineer provides advanced Genilogix support services to various clients utilizing HP Performance Center products especially LoadRunner, Performance Center, SiteScope Business Availability Center/BSM, End User Management, and Diagnostics. The position requires the desire and ability to learn and provide additional support for HP Unified Functional Testing and ALM. Attention to detail and strong communication skills are a must.

 

Essential Job Functions:

 

Provide guidance and resolution of technical issues related to LoadRunner, Performance Center and SiteScope.

 

Provide technical support using any combination of telephone, email, and remote desktop software.

 

Able to display consistent professionalism in communicating and working with support customers.

 

Able to track several issues on an ongoing basis using our online support website and email.

 

REQUIRED SKILLS:

 

Excellent verbal and written communications skills.

 

Understanding of HP Software BTO Solutions and integration points.

 

Experience and/or expertise with HP LoadRunner.

 

Expertise in implementing performance validation and optimization solutions utilizing HP LoadRunner in web, client/server, and/or ERP and CRM environments.

 

Familiarity of ERP and CRM applications including SAP, Oracle E-Business Suite, Siebel and PeopleSoft.

 

Familiarity of applications developed in Java (J2EE) and .NET frameworks.

 

Familiarity with web and web 2.0 technologies.

 

Knowledge of databases, networks, 3-tier architectures, with a preference given towards working experience against any of the following:  DB2, SQL Server, Oracle, F5, Cisco, Check Point, Citrix, WebSphere, WebLogic,  VMWare, Cloud computing.

 

Strong C programming experience.

 

PREFERRED SKILLS:

 

Consulting experience with multiple clients and technical environments.

 

HP Certifications – AIS and/or ASE in Performance Center (LoadRunner).

 

Experience with a variety of LoadRunner protocols for WinSock, AJAX, web-services.

 

Expertise in defining and estimating service engagements.

 

Working exposure to a number of operating systems, e.g. Windows, Unix, Linux.

 

Ability to identify software and service opportunities.

 

Project management skills and experience.

 

Expertise in software testing in multiple software development methodologies including waterfall, iterative (e.g. RUP) and agile (e.g. XProgramming, SCRUM).

 

Ability to manage projects and teams composed of Genilogix consultants and other client resources.

 

Experience and/or expertise with any of the other HP Performance Center tools, i.e. SiteScope, Performance Center, Business Availability Center (BAC) and Diagnostics.

 

Experience and/or expertise with any of the other HP tools, i.e. Quality Center and QuickTest Professional.

 

EDUCATION:     

 

Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems or Information Technology

 

EXPERIENCE:

 

Min. of 3 years of experience in a comparable position and 5-7 overall years industry experience.

If extended an offer of employment, you may be required to submit to the necessary pre-employment background checks and screenings.

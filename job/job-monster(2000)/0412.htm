About the Job

Opportunity – Senior Java/J2EE Developer, Senior Java Web Developer in an Agile SCRUM eCommerce environment  

 

Newark, a subsidiary of global distributor Premier Farnell plc (LSE:PFL), is the leading small-quantity distributor of electronic components and test equipment in the Americas, and its #1 source for RoHS compliant parts, services and information. 


In addition to offering the broadest electronics selection from 440+ top manufacturers for same-day shipping, Newark provides a unique range of value-added services at no extra cost.


Making A Difference

At Premier Farnell, we are driven to deliver results by being totally reliable, resourceful, customer focused and conducting ourselves with integrity.  These values translate into every aspect of our business – from creating a responsible organization in which our employees are proud to work and our stakeholders have confidence… to supporting our customers with element14, an industry-leading technical portal and community that enables design engineers to research technology solutions and collaborate with colleagues early in the design process

(www.element-14.com).


With multiple sales channels, customers do business with Newark through contact centers, a local branch network in the US, Canada, Mexico and Brazil, a dedicated field sales force and a world-class website at www.newark.com. The holder of a General Services Administration schedule (#GS-06F-0044M), Newark also supports federal government customers.

SHAPING THE FUTURE WITH AN UNIQUE ORGANIZATION!


Premier Farnell is looking for a highly motivated and competent Senior Web Developer.


Job Purpose:

Responsible for design and development of web technologies in support of PF websites..


Job Responsibilities:

  • Day-to-day design, development, unit testing, and maintenance of web related components across Premier Farnell global websites
  • Feature elaboration working with relevant teams
  • Design and development of POCs.
  • Perform design reviews and code reviews
  • Participate in Design Authority functions
  • Collaborate with test teams to ensure quality deliverables
  • Ensure non functional requirements are met.
  • Ensure knowledge transfer of developed features to appropriate team members
  • Establishing and maintaining high performance web delivery function as code steward for key components.
  • Regularly being dev lead on increment teams.
  • Research, selection and implementation of appropriate tools, templates, libraries for the creation of quality web components.
  • Ensure clear and consistent communication at all times.
  • Act as a technical point of contact for the business in web related technical matters.
  • Responsibility for coaching, mentoring, giving direction to junior web developers. 
  • Work closely with User Experience team, and Global Development manager to deliver innovative solutions that will continue to positively differentiate our global eCommerce offering.

 

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related field of study The job requires at least 5 years experience working in IT, with at least 3 of those years spent on J2EE, HTML, XML, JavaScript, SQL, Messaging, and design patterns.
  • Working knowledge of JUnit, JMeter, HTTP test, and selenium
  • Working knowledge of eCommerce platforms such as ATG, IBM Commerce, and Hybris.
  • Working knowledge of Application Servers such as ATG Dynamo, IBM WebSphere, JBoss, Tomcat, and BEA Weblogic
  • Working knowledge of Web Servers such as Apache, Tomcat, and iPlanet
  • Working knowledge of databases such as Oracle, MySQL, DB2, and SQL Server
  • Working knowledge of AJAX libraries is preferred
  • Experience running regression tests, stress testing, and methodologies and best practices
  • Experience in dealing with nuances of multiple browsers and their versions (IE, FireFox, Netscape, Chrome, etc)
  • Strong analytical and conceptual skills with the ability to work hands-on in a fast paced environment
  • Strong experience of online industry standards and terminology
  • Willing to learn new processes and apply to daily work
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills including ability to work with Global Architects
  • Demonstrable ability of self learning and self motivation
  • Experience working in global teams
  • Experience with Agile process (SCRUM is preferred)

 
If you are qualified and interested in applying for this position, please submit an updated word version resume to: jkim@newark.com

About the Job

THE OPPORTUNITY

We are ready to hire NOW a Cloverleaf Level 2 or above Consultant with strong TCL skills as a full time salaried W2 employee with bonuses and full benefits. Santa Rosa Consulting and Noesis Health have merged so the decades’ worth of Cloverleaf expertise now resides with Santa Rosa Consulting! We are ready to begin interviewing Level 2 and 3 Cloverleaf (HealthVision, Quovadx) integrators. Successful candidates will:

·         Have 3+ years of Cloverleaf full cycle integration experience

·         Be an expert with TCL and HL7

·         Be willing to be on-site as much as weekly with our clients Monday to Thursday though remote work is common

·         Any experience with High Availability or Training other integrators will be an added plus

 

Most of our current employment opportunities can be found on our jobs site by going to www.santarosaconsulting.com, to the “Join Our Team” link, then following the prompts. You can also apply directly through our website.

 

WANT TO JOIN A 21ST CENTURY COMPANY?


 At Santa Rosa Consulting, we believe that the traditional model of healthcare consulting has fallen out of alignment with consultant and customer needs and interests. Too much emphasis is placed on historical processes. Too many simple requests require significant time and expense. Too little attention and innovation is applied to the pursuit of the most painless and rapid realization of successful customer outcomes. This is our cause at Santa Rosa Consulting and we are meeting this challenge through the use of consultants with unique and deep expertise, flexible service offerings, and an advanced technology platform.

 

MORE ABOUT SANTA ROSA CONSULTING

 

At Santa Rosa consulting you will enjoy a new level of healthcare consulting and Information technology consulting. You will be able to maximize your choices for W2 work or 1099 - you decide what relationship you want to have.  You will customize your benefits to meet your needs.

Leadership at Santa Rosa Consulting is Rich Helppie (previously CEO of Superior Consultant, Inc.), Managing Partner; he is a seasoned CEO and board chair with over 28 years experience in business services and healthcare and over 34 years in information technology. He founded Superior Consulting in 1984 and ran it until it was purchased by ACS in 2005.  Thomas Watford (previously COO and CFO of First Consulting Group which was purchased by CSC), President and Chief Operating Officer, brings over 23 years experience in the healthcare industry, where he has delivered Professional and IT services to clients in the Healthcare Provider, Health Plan and Life Sciences sectors. To view our services and read more about our leadership team please visit our web site at www.santarosaconsulting.com .

Company Description
--------------------------
Our client is a worldwide systems integrator and managed network service provider. They provide end-to-end managed services to meet the  communications needs of organizations around the world.  The Firm makes one vital commitment to our customers: that the solutions they provide will work, no matter what. Founded by engineers, our client provides services and products supported by one of the industry's largest in-house engineering staffs.  They deliver systems and services anywhere in the world, under any conditions. From secure military applications to wireless networks and broadcast origination centers, the worldwide team gets it done.

The team is a group of exceptional individuals who are committed and passionate about the future of the Company.

As they continue to grow; they look for talented individuals like you to join the team

Required Qualifications
---------------------------
- * Solid knowledge of TCP/IP addressing/subnetting
- * Experience with Routing protocols, especially EIGRP. OSPF and BGP would be a big plus
- * Experience with LAN switching concepts (Spanning tree, 802.1q, ISL, VTP, bridging concepts, etc.)
- * Knowledge of Cisco router and switch configuration (familiarity with any of the following hardware types: 26xx, 36xx, 72xx routers and 35xx, 45xx, 65xx Catalyst switches)
- * Good troubleshooting skills (i.e., analytical thinking, problem solving) 
- * Knowledge/experience with IPSec (tunnel, transport, ESP, AH, IKE)
- * Basic Knowledge of security encryption algorithms/standards (DES, 3DES, AES, etc.)
- * Nortel Connectivity experience (600, 1100, 1700, 2700, 5000)
- * VoIP experience would be a plus (Cisco/Nortel)
- * Certifications are not needed, but would be a plus (CCIE/CCNP) 
- * Must have current and active Top Secret Clearance

Benefits - offers an exceptional compensation package and a world-class range of benefits!
Compensation Base salary from $150,000 - $180,000 plus excellent benefits

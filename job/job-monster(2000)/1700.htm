Oracle HRMS Functional

About the Job

The consultant selected for this position will be taking a Functional role in Oracle HRMS project.

Primary responsibilities include functional analysis, design, recommend, implement solutions and best

practices, training and coordinating the project development lifecycle activities. The Contract term will be

for six (6) months.

Requirements

The candidate must possess the expertise and knowledge to perform the following duties:

1. Results oriented professional

2. Must possess excellent Functional and knowledge in Oracle HRMS (Core HRMS) with five (5)

years of minimum experience

3. Experience in Setting up the positions, position control, position transaction, and supervisor

hierarchy in a public sector environment

4. Experience in Public Sector Budgeting will be a plus

5. Must possess excellent knowledge in upgrading from 11.5.10 to R12 and new features in R12

6. Understand and analyze the Business Requirements and assist customers in mapping/gapping

their business needs to Oracle and walking them through Conference Room Pilot (CRP).

7. Prepare relevant documents for functional design, technical design, Setup and Develop

Training Manuals including test plans

8. Possess necessary expertise and knowledge to be able to advise customers on best practices

according to industry standards including working knowledge of Position Control practices in

government entities, particularly IT industry practices, procedures, and policies.

9. Know how to set up required security controls, by which relevant data can be restricted for

operations

 

Skill Requirements

Government Oracle experience in:

1. Application Implementation Methodology (AIM) implementing Oracle Core HRMS

2. Develop application design and architecture components

3. Develop process and functional/technical design documents

4. Coding, testing of the technical components to meet the functional requirements

5. Developing the user test plans and conducting the application build, test, and deploy activities

6. Planning and executing data conversion activities

7. In-depth knowledge of software development methodologies and lifecycle

8. Excellent communication skills

9. Technology evaluation and research skills. Ability to log and track SR’s

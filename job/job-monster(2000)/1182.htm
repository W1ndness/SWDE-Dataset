About the Job

We are a dynamic, growing Commercial Real Estate Investment Company.  We currently have three opportunities for Business Analysts in our Santa Ana, California office.


Grubb & Ellis Company is one of the world's leading full-service commercial real estate organizations, providing a complete range of transaction, management and consulting services. By leveraging local expertise with our global reach, Grubb & Ellis offers innovative, customized solutions and seamless service to owners, corporate occupants and investors throughout the globe. For more information, visit the Company's Web site at www.grubb-ellis.com.

 

Job Summary:

 

The Business Analyst is responsible for reviewing, analyzing and documenting business process, systems and user needs for the purpose of implementing and improving business efficiency and productivity with specific assigned applications. The BA also provides analysis and project management on assigned client facing applications and projects including creating and managing to the project schedule; creating, tracking and resolving issues that arise on the project and provide periodic status reports. This position will also assist in end user training as well as day-to-day support of assigned systems.

Job  Duties  and Responsibilities:

 

·         Reviews and analyzes current business processes.

·         Conducts user needs analysis and develops business requirements matched to current systems to design IT solutions to improve business efficiency and productivity.

·         Responsible for creating business requirements, specifications, use cases, flow charts, process diagrams and Scopes of Work required to develop or modify applications to business line specified process workflows and functions.

·         Facilitate the translation of business requirements to technical requirements with the development resources and/or Third Party SAAS providers.

·         Create and manage project and or change request proposals and schedules relating to the implementation, integration, new development or modification of assigned applications.

·         Responsible for costing, estimate task/schedule duration, establish deadlines, track project progress, track issues and coordinate actions to resolve them.

·         Provide regular status reports on the progress of assigned projects, tasks and client interactions.

·         Functions as subject matter expert in assigned technologies and function as primary contact for facilitating development, configuration, and implementation efforts.

·         Primary Quality Assurance lead during the development and testing periods of all projects and improvement cycles. Provide primary on-going support in the use, maintenance, troubleshooting of assigned applications and participate as an active member of the business applications improvement process.

·         Work with the staff Technical Writer to develop training, support and online help materials. May conduct training for users, as needed. Provide status reporting to management as required.

·         The BA will be in contact with all assigned internal and external clients; functioning as the technology group’s point of contact for assigned areas.

·         Act as liaison to assigned vendor contacts providing technical and business solutions in support of the development of the business lines Scope of Work.

Qualifications:

 

·         Bachelors Degree in Information Systems, Engineering or Business Administration or a related field or related experience is preferred.

·         4 - 8 years experience within a Real Estate Services Industry.

·         Business analysis experience with superior skills in requirements gathering, business process analysis, translating client/business requirements to technical specification, process flow diagrams, issues lists management, project status communications and team reporting.

·         Experience in project management, quality assurance, management status reporting, working with all levels of staff and executive management.

·         Demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to self-manage and work closely with management, staff, vendors and consultants.

·         Excellent multi-tasking and time management skills to manage tasks with competing priorities.

·         Hands-on experiences in design, implementation and support with the following commercial/corporate real estate services systems is a plus: Facility Management, Work Ticket Management, Preventative Maintenance, Space Planning, Project Management, Sustainability, Property Management, Lease Administration, Financial and Operational Accounting systems, Asset Management, Transaction Management, and Research and Customer Relationship Management (CRM).

·         Knowledge of relational database concepts as it relates to Commercial Real Estate Services Industry.

·         Superior technical skills and knowledge of PC application software including: MS Windows, MS Office, MS Visio, software development, maintenance, and project management tools (i.e. MS Project).

 

We offer a competitive salary and benefits such as medical, dental, vision, PTO plan, 401k and paid holidays. To be considered for this opportunity, please submit your cover letter and resume with salary history/requirements.  Please include title in subject line.

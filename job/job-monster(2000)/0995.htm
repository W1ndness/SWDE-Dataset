Job Description

Do you think you have what it takes to impress a client?  Can you work in a team environment and be the person others trust to give the right answers?  Easy Dynamics is a small company focused on building solutions for clients by bringing in top talent.  In return Easy Dynamics provides its employees with flexible and personal options to make the workplace interesting and challenging.  We don’t lock employees into a set path but actively work to promote diversity and creativity.  If this sounds like something you are interested in please reply to the job posting to hear more about us and the work we’re involved with.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·        Recommend technology strategy by understanding key client objectives; diagnosing and mapping client requirements; articulating solution risks and barriers; recommending delivery approaches; preparing time and cost estimates; planning full project life cycle

·        Lead and facilitate information gathering sessions to validate business cases, gather and document business and functional objectives

·        Implement architecture by building components and custom designs; prototyping; data migration; maintaining technical integrity and consistency; documenting system

·        Validate system performance by developing and conducting test scripts; completing bug fixes

·        Prepare client to use IT infrastructure by developing and conducting training programs

·        Manage customer relationship by communicating architecture standards and frameworks; answering questions; resolving concerns and issues

·        Increase organization effectiveness by identifying opportunities to leverage solutions across multiple engagements

·        Improve information sharing through awareness of emerging technologies and; evaluating their applicability to business goals and operational requirements

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

·        Degree in Computer Science or Engineering or equivalent work experience

·        Consulting skills such as interviewing stakeholders, conducting architecture reviews, and consensus building

·        Experience leading large group meetings/information gathering sessions with senior-level executives

·        Excellent written and oral communication skills

·        Professional experience with n-tier application architectures development

·        Strong analytical and conceptual skills, with ability to function at both the detail and conceptual levels

·        Knowledge of Data Modeling, Data Integration

·        Experience with service-oriented architectures and XML

·        Solid understanding of Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007 (MOSS) product and Windows SharePoint Services technology platform. Strong understanding of Microsoft Information Worker infrastructure and architecture.  Understanding of related products such as Project server, Forms server, InfoPath, Dynamics CRM, etc would be plus

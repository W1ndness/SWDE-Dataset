About the Job

Web Application Developer

Company Information

Approgence Technologies provides IT services and solutions to clients globally.​ With its Corporate Headquarters located in Irvine, CA. Approgence provides a globally optimized delivery model to its worldwide customers. Approgence primarily focuses on driving business value to its customers by providing end-to end solutions that leverages technology, client's IT assets and best of breed products.

We are an IT consulting services and specialized recruiting firm for Mobile engineering and Telecom/Electronics companies in the US. We have been retained by a large electronics/telecom global company for recruiting full-time engineers for their R&D lab in Irvine & San Diego, CA

We are currently hiring Web Application Developers for Irvine, CA

Job Description:
Develop world-class applications for the digital-TV, Blu-ray players and other devices
• Create system and software requirement, architecture and design, implement, and debug and test the system
• Work with internal and external customers to explore product requirements and resolve challenging issues
• Contribute to the organization’s strong drive to be on the cutting edge of technology through the generation of patentable ideas
• Effectively communicate with global engineering teams
• Improve software quality by following a software engineering process

Skills / Experience requirements:
• BS in Computer Science/Engineering; MS/PhD preferred
• 5 years (after BS) of related industry experience
• Working knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript
• Development experience with C/C++, Java
• Solid understanding on various software development tools and environments: IDE, debugger, source control, bug tracking, etc.
• Ability to multi-task effectively under considerable pressure of deadlines and easily adapt within a fast-paced, start-up like environment
Candidate must have at least two of the following experience with track records.
• Development experience of web applications with extensive HTML and JavaScript
• Development experience with Adobe Flash 8, 9 and 10 with ActionScript 2.0 and 3.0
• Development experience with web services (HTTP, XML, JSON, SOAP, REST, WSDL)
• Development experience with web server and software (Apache, CGI, SSI, JSP, ASP, PHP, .NET)

Preferred:
• Extensive knowledge and understanding of streaming protocols (RTSP, MMS, HTTP, RTMP)
• Development experience with C#, .NET, Silverlight
• Industry knowledge of new technology and standards: HTML5, WebKit

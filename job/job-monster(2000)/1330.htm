Database Architect (DB2 & .Net)

About the Job

Fiserv, Inc. (NASDAQ: FISV) is the leading global provider of information management and electronic commerce systems for the financial services industry, driving innovation that transforms experiences for financial institutions and their customers. Ranked No. 1 on the FinTech 100 survey of top technology partners to the financial services industry.

Fiserv (NASDAQ: FISV), is a Fortune 500 company and the leading global provider of financial services technology solutions, bringing information management and electronic commerce systems and services to the financial services industry. Leading services include transaction processing, outsourcing, electronic bill payment and presentment, investment management solutions, business process outsourcing (BPO), software and systems solutions. Headquartered in Brookfield, Wis., Fiserv is the leading provider of core processing solutions for U.S. banks, credit unions and thrifts.


 


With Fiserv, you will create and deliver the Fiserv solutions that drive client satisfaction. You can grow professionally and personally in an environment where intelligence, innovation, and leadership are valued and rewarded. Fiserv holds true to two essential principles: the client comes first, and people make the difference. Fiserv invites you to join us in building on those principles to lead our clients and our company to success. 


 


We are currently recruiting for a DB2Database Architect to work in our Norcross, Ga. facility.


 


Responsibilities:


The DB2 Database Architect for Genesis Transformation and designs and supports all aspects of DB2 - .NET Data Services Driver which is now newly in production. This is new technology for the Industry and Fiserv and is the cornerstone of Genesis Transformation .NET enablement for SOA services developed by Architecture Vision. It is also a significant cost reduction in IBM MLC (Monthly License Charge) because the Data Service Driver is used to off load MIPS processing from General Purpose to Specialty Engines. Savings for Genesis Transformation was estimated at $60,000 per month. Position will also lead DB2 Database upgrades, new technology, analyze what DB2 PTFs to apply, perform vendor technical relationship management and architect the .NET to DB2 Distributed environment. Position  requires in depth technical knowledge of current DB2 Data Sharing environment (nine way data sharing in production) for performance tuning and application troubleshooting. Performs support for Development for DB2 Connect and DB2 Connect gateways for applications and is responsible to lead Development and FET upgrades from DB2 Connect Type 2 to Type 4 drivers (Data Services Driver.) Must represent Fiserv with IBM labs and industry conferences and support the business for any reason.


 


Requirements:


z/OS DB2 Version 9, IBM Data Sharing on z/Series with DB2, DB2 Connect on distributed and DRDA, IBM Data Services Driver, .NET and Java skills, Entity Relationship modeling from Logical to Physical data models, DB2 performance tuning, CA DB2 Suite (High Speed Loader, Unloader, Log Analyzer), DB2 Omegamon, BMC DB2 monitoring suite. Must have excellent problem identification and solving skills, ability to work closely with Development and technical leadership skills, ability to present technical concepts to a diverse audience, and ability to lead DB2 upgrades and perform DB2 PTF analysis to determine PTF applicability to current production environment.


 


Building trust and displaying leadership in working with Development. This position requires a high degree of interaction with Development. IBM Data Services Driver for .NET and Java – very new technology, Fiserv is an early adopter of the .NET Data Services Driver, develop a close relationship with IBM Toronto Labs which support DB2 Connect


 


Education Required:


 


·         Bachelor’s degree in information technology.

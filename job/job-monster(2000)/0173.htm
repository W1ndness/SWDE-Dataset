Title: UMTS RF Optimization Engineer

No. of positions: 3

Location: Naperville, ILUSA

Duration: 12 months

 

Job Description: 

Required: 2+ years experience as UMTS RF Optimization Engineer. Openings: Minneapolis: 1, St Louis: 1, Detroit: 1RF Optimization Tasks:• Work with RF design engineer to understand the RF design, cluster definition, customer expectation, and network configuration.• Study RF design coverage maps and identify problem areas in terms of coverage, interference, or pilot pollution.• Plan and execute tasks to optimize assigned RF clusters in the customer network during the deployment phase.• Confirm spectrum clearance, integration testing, power calibration, and call-through tests were carried out, and all entrance criteria are met before start of optimization for assigned clusters.• Participate in providing input to the RF lead for RF sections in the MOP, deployment plan, CATP, and site logistical needs.• Generate script files necessary for RF optimization.• Perform sanity check on antenna configuration.• Keep up-to-date documentation of cluster optimization-related actions; also maintain archives of all collected data.• Ensure all assigned clusters meets agreed-upon Key Performance Indicator criteria by launch date scheduled with the customer.• Troubleshoot areas with poor RF performance. Request drive tests, analyze data, and recommend network changes as applicable.• Perform Layer 3 analysis of dropped calls and access failures. Present the RF optimization results to customer and answer questions and perform follow up actions as requested by the customer. Assist RF Lead in troubleshooting of migration/rehome-related issues.• Support KPI monitoring before and after RNC rehome/migration, identify issues, analyze and troubleshoot and escalate to RF Lead, as needed, for resolution.

 

SKILLS: Familiarity with RF Optimization Handbook procedures, Familiarity with RF Optimization tools and test equipment, Familiarity with RF Optimization tunable parameters, Knowledge of antenna configuration, Knowledge of ALU system architecture, Understanding ALU UMTS Basic Call flow, mobile operations, Ability of using Network Performance tools.

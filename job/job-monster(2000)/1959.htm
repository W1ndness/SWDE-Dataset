Manager, Enterprise IT Security

About the Job

Currently, we have an opening for a high-performing Manager, Enterprise IT Security. This vitally important position will manage policy and procedures to ensure the protection of sensitive customer and corporate data, as well as define and audit appropriate user access levels to enterprise applications and systems.  This position supports the overall security goals of the organization ensuring compliance and the safeguarding of the organization’s reputation through adherence to industry standard security practices. 

 

This position will work with the Infrastructure team to provide regular monitoring of all in-scope equipment, systems and processes to ensure compliance with the Payment Credit Industry Data Security Standards (PCI-DSS).  In addition, this position is responsible for leading the IT team’s ongoing efforts in developing, maintaining and enforcing security policies, and remediating all existing and future compliance exceptions. 

 

The Manager, Enterprise IT Security will need to possess and maintain proficiency and in-depth knowledge of the PCI-DSS, and be skilled at educating fellow IT members, as well as the overall end-user community who utilizes IT services. This role will serve as the subject matter expert for IT Security. You must live for challenges, possess a high degree of energy and strive for operational excellence. This critical position will be responsible for the following:

 

·       Perform gap analysis, information security audits, risk assessment of IT infrastructure, and corporate personnel and contractors

·       Review network scans and vulnerability tests, identify issues, and devise and deliver solutions to prevent future problems

·       Collaborate with IT infrastructure team to refine monitoring, access control mechanisms, firewall rules, wireless policies and retention policies

·       Establish and verify consistency of security standards baselines

·       Remain current on evolving standards within the industry (new rules & vulnerabilities identified)

·       Recent experience managing security for infrastructure supporting high-revenue/heavy traffic e-commerce sites

·       Recent experience managing security within the framework of current virtualization technologies

·       Conduct assessment of all information systems including but not limited to network architecture, security and controls, patch implementation management, security device management

·       Perform forensic analysis of security violations and create and maintain incident response reports

·       Experience with network security design and best practices

·       Solid understanding of standards in strong encryption and cryptography, as well as secure communications protocols

·       Working knowledge of security and compliance issues, techniques and implications across current major computer platforms

·       Proficiency working with electronic payment processors and payment card issuers, and providing required compliance documentation

·       Analyze assessment data, prepare reports and make recommendations

·       Review and evaluate application responsibilities and user access levels for enterprise applications hosted internally as well as vendor-hosted applications; provide gap analysis and refine appropriate user access levels to minimize risk

·       Thorough knowledge of the PCI DSS, and experience enforcing and maintaining organizational PCI Compliance

·       Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities

·       Able to effectively prioritize tasks and collaborate with technical resources during high-pressure situations

·       Experience working with internally and externally managed technology systems and vendors

·       Strong interpersonal, written, and oral communication skills

·       Strong customer service orientation

·       Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative and dynamic environment

 

The ideal candidate will possess 7-10 years of work experience in Information Technology with a broad range of exposure to enterprise applications, systems analysis, networking fundamentals and 5+ years working in an Information Security capacity with increasing levels of responsibility. A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field is highly desired. IT security certifications such as CISSP, CISA, CISM strongly preferred.

 

Beachbody.com offers a highly competitive salary, benefits, bonus potential and the opportunity for you to help us achieve our mission which is to help people achieve their goals and enjoy a healthy, fulfilling life. Beachbody.com is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

 

 

About Beachbody

Founded in 1998 by Carl Daikeler and Jon Congdon, Beachbody, LLC, operates Beachbody.com, TeamBeachbody.com, and BreakthroughInBeauty.com. The company is based in Santa Monica, California, and is the creator of the nation's most popular in-home fitness and weight loss solutions including P90X*, Insanity*, Brazil Butt Lift*, Slim in 6*, Turbo Jam*, Hip Hop Abs*, and Power 90* among many others.

Beachbody programs combine challenging DVD-based workouts with easy-to-follow diet guidelines, nutritional supplements (including its breakthrough health shake called Shakeology*, AKA "The Healthiest Meal of the Day"), and an unparalleled customer service and peer-support system. In fact, Beachbody has expanded this support system through Team Beachbody.com, which includes a network of thousands of independent "Coach" distributors as well as a dynamic online support and fitness community. The company's beauty business includes brands Timeless Secret(tm) and DermExclusive(tm).

The corporate mission is to help people achieve their goals and enjoy healthy, fulfilling lives.

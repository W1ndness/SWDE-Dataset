Senior Security Consultant

About the Job

Senior Security Consultant - Consulting Firm
(Work From Home – 50+% Travel)
Ideally seeking candidates who are located in DC, MD, VA, PA, NJ, NY.

About Us
Since our start, we have focused exclusively on IT security, operational risk, and regulatory compliance. Our corporate mission is to be our clients’ long-term, trusted security and risk management advisors. Providing expert technical assessments, we ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of client data and systems through our world-class, enterprise-wide information security services and solutions. We offer consulting, audit, vendor management, and human capital assistance.

We help organizations in a variety of industries identify, assess, remediate, and effectively manage their operational risks. The majority of our clients are in regulated industries or have clients in these industries. Our consulting services enable these organizations to showcase to auditors, clients, partners, and investors their conformance to IT security regulations and standards. Our personnel are experts with regulations, standards and frameworks that include but are not limited to ITIL, PCI-DSS, ISO/IEC 27002, FFIEC Handbook, SOX (COBIT), and HIPAA.


Description
As Senior Consultant, you will be expected to manage complex projects for multiple clients including: Operational Risk Assessments, Payment Card Industry (PCI) Assessments, Penetration Testing, Vulnerability Assessments, and Governance Consulting including Policy Development.


Specific Job Duties include but not limited to the following:

Conduct IT Risk Assessments using ISO 27002 as a primary benchmark for objectivity
Conducting PCI Assessments
Knowledge Transfer on cobiT, GLBA, HIPAA, NIST and others
Risk and Compliance Management Programs
Develop Policies, Procedures, Standards & Guidelines
Develop Change Management procedures


Qualifications
• Candidates must have a strong project management background with experience in a broad range of information security disciplines.
• Candidates must have a minimum of 3-7 years experience in project management, consulting and/or auditing. Candidate must be able to complete contracts effectively and possess the ability to communicate and instruct others to complete tasks.
• Prefer candidates that have a current/valid 2010 PCI QSA and/or PA QSA, but not an absolute must.
• CISSP and PMP preferred.
• This position requires a mature, confident personality.
• Candidate must be able to balance numerous tasks and maintain his/her own schedule.
• Candidate will be responsible for developing and delivering effective deliverables and successful executive presentations.
• Candidates must have excellent communications skills and be able to write quality technical reports.
• Candidates must have tangible assets, perseverance, high self-esteem, well-developed sense of humor, willingness to take risks, and creativity and innovation.
• Must be willing to travel nationally 2.5 weeks per month, although much travel will be nearby.

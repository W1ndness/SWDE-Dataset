Information Security Analyst III (Identity & Access Mgmt)

About the Job

The 1199SEIU Benefit & Pension Funds provide comprehensive health, pension, and quality of life benefits to unionized workers represented by 1199SEIU United Healthcare Workers East.  We are among the largest labor-management funds in the nation, covering 430,000 members and their families.

 

If you’re ready for the brightest career future, join us in this excellent opportunity to showcase your talents.  We are currently seeking an experienced Information Security Analyst III (Identity & Access Mgmt) for our Information Security department. 


Responsibilities

  • Develop strategy and approaches to address the implementation of Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions as part of the Information Security services the 1199Funds; access, plan, test, report and recommend appropriate remediation measures to ensure Information Security processes and standards are maintained
  • Design and conduct access validation of all Fund applications;  work directly with various business units to discuss access issues, explain access rights and manage process
  • Conduct IAM review for new applications; ensure applications are compliant with identity and access guidelines
  • Oversee and monitor access rights and coordination of policy and controls; provide updates to Compliance Director, Information Security Director and Chief Information Security Officer to ensure business units are taking effective remediation steps for access management issues
  • Manage the addition of applications to the Funds electronic identity management suite
  • Follow up on deficiencies identified in risk reviews, self-assessments, risk assessments, internal and external audits; ensure appropriate remediation measures are implemented
  • Design, implement and maintain Role Based Access Controls (RBAC) across all system applications; ensure applications remain structured based on role based access controls
  • Assist in the development of security architecture, policies, principles and standards; ensure architecture meets best practices based on industry and organizational needs
  • Perform additional duties and projects as assigned by management


Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Security or equivalent years of experience required; plus
  • Minimum five (5) years identity and access management experience in Information Security or related discipline (i.e. Information Technology, Privacy, or Compliance) required
  • Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) certification required
  • Knowledge of identity access technologies and practices, IAM and Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) standards
  • Knowledge of Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) and certificate based authentication a plus
  • Experience in Oracle Identity Management (OIM), Oracle Role Manager (ORM), and other tools in the Oracle Identity Access Management Suite a plus
  • Demonstrated experience with risk management, access rights and identity management required
  • Knowledge of common information security management frameworks such as: International Standards Organization (ISO) 17799/27001, Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL), Control Objectives for Information and Related Technology (CobiT) and National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) frameworks
  • Experience troubleshooting information security related problems and incidents; knowledge of security architecture including encryption, firewalls, VPN’s, anti-virus systems and vulnerability management preferred
  • Excellent troubleshooting and analytical thinking skills
  • Superb communication, interpersonal skills and collaborative skills a must
  • Self-directed, self-starter, and motivated with the ability to work with minimal supervision
  • Must meet performance standards including attendance and punctuality

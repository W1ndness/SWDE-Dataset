Director of Client Engineering

About the Job

iPass changes the economics of enterprise mobility and revolutionizes the connectivity experience. Founded in 1996, the company’s mission is to be the partner of choice for enterprise mobility services. iPass delivers simple, smart and open solutions that transparently facilitate every connection, regardless of underlying network or device; provide the enterprise with carrier independence and reduce lock-in costs; and give the enterprise a new level of choice and control to drive down costs and maintain security in a world where consumers drive enterprise IT.

With 3,500 enterprise customers worldwide, including more than 370 of the Forbes Global 2000, iPass is a leading provider of enterprise mobility services.  Headquartered in Redwood Shores, Calif., iPass (NASDAQ: IPAS) is a profitable publicly traded company with 2008 revenues of $192 million.

We are currently accepting applications for a Director of Client Engineering.  This position will be based at our corporate headquarters in Redwood Shores, California.  **Only Local Candidates Will Be Considered**

Position Overview:

The Director of Client Engineering will report to SVP of Product Development and will have the following key responsibilities: 

·         Lead a team of engineers to build a set of Windows based services to provide universal connectivity solution for iPass’ enterprise customers. These services are delivered to customers as a desktop client with an easy to navigate user interface giving a single control point for connectivity across the combination of mobile networks and devices defined by the enterprise

·         Directly and successfully grow, lead, and develop a talented team of high-performing professionals in a high velocity environment. Mentor and coach these groups of engineers and rely on their technical expertise in reaching decisions.

·         Partner with Product Management to define and deliver new capabilities in the desktop client

·         Work very closely with the Director of Platform Engineering to understand desktop client’s dependencies on the platform and vice versa for efficient execution of the roadmap

·         Be a strong partner with other key functions in the business – Product Management, QA, Network Operations, Customer Care

·         Promote a culture of test driven development by emphasizing unit tests and building testability into the product


Minimum Qualifications:

Possess at least 8+ years of full product lifecycle experience in building multiple versions of enterprise grade client software.  Familiarity with Windows Networking technologies covering various access methods – Wi-Fi, 3G/4G, Ethernet,, firewalls, security, DNS, IP etc.  Familiarity and hands-on experience with Windows technologies: .Net/C#, Windows Presentation Foundation, Windows Communication Foundation, Windows 7, Vista, XP.  Ability to work with diverse development teams across several different geographical environments.  Prior experience leading projects covering variety of platforms (Windows, Macintosh and Smartphones) a strong plus.  Demonstrated experience as a true self-starter that can quickly and independently assess business requirements and become a trusted and integral part of the leadership team.  Must be familiar with Agile development methodology.  High level of energy as well as strong communication, interpersonal and negotiation skills.  Proven ability to gain trust, negotiate business objectives, and work directly with a broad group of senior executives.  Demonstrate the ability to manage and quickly gain respect among subject matter experts within the company.  Possess complex project management skills and the ability to effectively manage high-performing teams by being a charismatic motivator and thought leader in the engineering organization

iPass offers a competitive salary and benefits package. If your qualifications are a match for this position, please email your resume and salary requirements to jobs@ipass.com

iPass is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

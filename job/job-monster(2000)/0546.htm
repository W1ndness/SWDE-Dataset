About the Job

Computer Network Managed Services Administrator

 

About Us:

Paradigm Computer Consulting, Inc. is a growing New England computer network service provider. We provide outsourced IT management and managed services to the SMB market.  We seek a motivated applicant to work directly with customers, providing IT support, administering networks remotely, and monitoring network problems and scheduled events.

 

Daily Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Take calls from clients and assist with computer problems
  • Provide computer network support using remote control tools
  • Monitoring systems for critical events and alerts
  • Using scripting tools to automate system maintenance and administrative tasks
  • Document and report on systems and networks
  • Bench repair of systems and servers

 

Qualifications
 

Strong knowledge of:

  • MS Windows workstation and server environments
  • TCP/IP in LAN/WAN environments
  • PC / Server hardware platforms
  • Internet technologies and services
  • Knowlege of Kaseya Remote Management System

 

Must have and/or be able to:

  • Excellent communications skills.
  • Work well in a fast paced, dynamic environment, with limited supervision.
  • Provide an outstanding customer experience.
  • Excellent troubleshooting and problem solving skills.
  • Some College or equivalent technical education, with two or more years of experience is required.
  • IT Certification such as MCSE. MCP, A+, Network+ or CCNE is required.

 

Benefits: 

    • Salary range is 30,000 to 50,000+ per year based upon experience and performance incentives.
    • Health Insurance
    • 401K Retirement Plan 
    • Paid vacation / Personal / Sick days

Director, Technology Solutions

About the Job

 

 

Amergent is a diverse group of marketing, creative and database specialists. We fuse information technology and our marketing experience to devise real world solutions to our clients. Effective solutions. Measurable solutions. Evolving solutions.


And our clients are as diverse as our staff. For 30 years, Amergent has been successfully serving regional and national organizations in the nonprofit sector. Our creative and innovative approach has helped hundreds of organizations raise hundreds of millions of dollars.


The people at Amergent and the clients we support have two things in common. We are highly motivated and results driven. We are looking for professionals with a 'get the job done' attitude to join our winning team.  Currently, we are recruiting for a Director of Technology Solutions.

 

Reporting to the Chief Operating Office, you will direct teams of professionals responsible for technical product development and applications support, including database design, segmentation, reporting & analysis, file processing and internal training (Applications Development & Support), as well as internal and client network administration.  Ensure all departmental work is delivered accurately, on time and on-budget

 

Responsibilities of this position include:

 

1)                Direct staff on all Client Database development and support,  Agency technical applications development and support and network administration.

2)               Take leadership role as Systems Analyst and Project Manager for Agency technical infrastructure development and transition:

·         Evaluate existing Agency technical infrastructure, including all hardware/software platforms, third party applications and internally developed applications

·         Consult with Business Development, Account Services and Technical Services to determine current requirements and future vision

·         Develop project specifications, budgets, schedules and documentation for Agency technical infrastructure development

·         Provide on-going Client Technical Applications Support

3)               Develop a methodology/process for implementation of new Database clients

4)               Provide Sales Support

5)               Implement Technical Training for internal staff

6)               Oversee Client Database & Network Installations

7)               Develop and implement quality assurance checks for projects

8)               Perform employee performance/salary evaluations and review

 

 

The successful candidate must be able to manage multiple client and production deadlines, have   excellent analytical, verbal and written skills and possess strong interpersonal  skills. Some travel required by automobile and airplane.

 


We are looking for someone who has 10+ years related business experience with a strong background in Database development and Open System Architecture and applications.  Exerperience with CRM required.  Excellent written and verbal communications, along with excellent client, people and project management skills are a must.

 
If you're interested, please submit your resume, along with your salary history/requirements to jobs@amergent.com or by fax to 978.531.0400 to the attention of Human Resources.

Telecommunications Specialist

About the Job

International SOS is the world’s leading provider of medical assistance, international healthcare and security services.

Every day, millions of people put their trust in us to help them wherever they live or travel. For over 20 years, we have been helping our clients manage the health and safety risks facing their international travelers, expatriates, and global workforce. This includes setting up and managing operations on their behalf in remote locations.

By providing access to our global network of alarm centers and the expertise of our multilingual staff, we also offer companies professional, outsourced customer care and added value services to differentiate their service offering from competitors.

With over 6,000 professionals operating in over 70 countries, we serve clients and members across every sector.

 

We currently have an opening for an Telecommunications Specialist located at our office in Trevose, PA.  The Telecom Specialist will provide technical support to the management in formulating policies with respect to telecommunications functions. This role will be part of a team in charge of planning the installation and maintenance of telephone and communications systems and services. The Telecom Specialist will ensure the TRICARE line of business is trained to use the most cost efficient communication while in the office or traveling. Vendor relationships with our Global Telecommunications providers will be essential as this role must manage global vendor relations.

 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

·         Ensure laws and regulations are followed in the licensing, operating and technical performance

·         Prepare the budget of the telecommunications systems, operation and maintenance

·         Work with TRICARE Operations Team Leads to ensure we are providing quality customer service

·         Prepares activity, progress and billing reports

·         Process and audit all Telecom related invoices for accuracy and opportunity for cost savings before approving for payment

·         Manage vendor relationships and negotiations

·         Create and distribute daily, weekly and monthly billing and operational reports

 

Requirements include:

·         Bachelor’s degree in electronics, electrical or telecommunications engineering

·         Two plus years of telecommunications work experience

·         High proficiency with Microsoft Excel

·         Basic telecommunications knowledgeable and the ability to analyze and troubleshoot telecommunication issues

·         Knowledge of telecommunications industry and the inner workings of Telecommunication Providers

·         Must be able to work accurately under stressful situations

·         Must be a team player and coordinate with people from different departments

·         Ability to interact in a positive manner with consultants, vendors, team members and senior management and to keep a positive mental attitude at all times

·         Excellent organizational skills and time management

·         Excellent communication skills – verbal and written

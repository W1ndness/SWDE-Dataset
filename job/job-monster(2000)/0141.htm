About the Job

Principal Systems Engineer   

The Principal Systems Engineer is responsible for effective provisioning, installation, configuration, documentation, operation, monitoring and maintenance of web hardware and software environments supporting web services we deliver to our Clients in infrastructure primarily located at 3rd party hosting providers.

 

This individual participates in technical research and development to enable continuing innovation in the services we offer and ensures ongoing service delivery within SLAs.

 This individual will assist project teams with technical issues in all project phases. These activities include the definition of needs, benefits, and technical strategy; research & development within the project life-cycle; technical analysis and design; facilitating hardware and software provisioning by service providers, and support in executing, testing, deploying and rolling-out the solutions. Participation on projects is focused on smoothing the transition of projects from development staff to production staff by performing operations activities within the project life-cycle. 


This person must be able to communicate with project teams and participate emergency calls immediately if the problem area lies within their time shift. The successful candidates will also have strong technical skills and an understanding of both Linux and Windows environments. Given that we frequently are required to deploy the Clients’ preferred technology, knowledge of web and application servers is essential.

A working knowledge of the following platforms / servers is highly desirable: Apache, Apache Tomcat, Caucho Resin, JBoss, BEA WebLogic and Microsoft's IIS). A good understanding of FTP, sFTP, DNS, SMTP, Load Balancing, Backup/Restore procedures and Configuration Management frameworks (example, Puppet) is also required.

 

Please refer to the list below for typical responsibilities: 

Identify appropriate hardware and software solutions to support Client project needs

Coordination of hardware and software provisioning with hosting service providers

Building solutions for Client projects and operational needs

Recommend solutions/technologies in the computing environment

Fine tuning of the servers

Manage load/stress testing projects

Installing SSL certificates

Registering and configuring domains/vanity URLs/sub-domains

Perform Dev/Stage/Prod Server deployments

Create automated deployment scripts (i.e. ANT and NANT)

Install and configure Web/App Servers and related application software (e.g. Java, .Net)

 

Operations and Support:

Perform daily system monitoring, verifying the integrity and availability of all hardware, server resources, systems and key processes, reviewing system and application logs, and verifying completion of scheduled jobs such as backups.

Analyze log files

Perform regular security monitoring to identify any possible intrusions.

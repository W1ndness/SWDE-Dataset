About the Job

Windows System Administrator

Washington, DC

 

Description:

 

The Windows System Administrator will support U.S. Navy Space and Naval Warfare System Command AtlanticProvides server support for Air Force Medical Systems.

 

Responsibilities include:

 

·         Maintaining connectivity and availability of systems for users around the globe.

·         Support, design and implement server technologies.

·         Administration of Windows 2003 and 2008 Active Directory domains with physical and virtual servers, virtual desktop infrastructure, SANs, and blade technologies.

·         Configuration, troubleshooting and maintaining Windows and VMware ESX servers. Monthly patching of all systems including Microsoft Windows servers, VMware ESX server, and Symantec Antivirus.

 

Windows technologies supported include:

 

·         Remote Desktop, Terminal Services, Citrix, and VMWare Desktop.

·         Troubleshooting, repairing, and refreshing systems hardware and software.

·         Routine monitoring of all systems and log review.

 

Requirements:

 

·         Three years experience in an enterprise environment.

·         Operational experience with configuring and troubleshooting Windows 2000, 2003, and 2008 servers.

·         Working knowledge of Windows Active Directory, Group Policy, and Windows XP

·         Working knowledge of server virtualization

·         Working knowledge of blade technologies and architecture

·         Working knowledge of VMware highly desirable

·         Working knowledge of desktop virtualization infrastructure highly desirable

·         Working knowledge of Storage Area Networks (SANs) highly desirable

·         Working knowledge of Citrix technologies highly desirable

·         Experience with Cisco switches, Cisco PIX firewalls, and Juniper VPN devices highly desirable.

·         Effective communication skills

·         Team player who can work under pressure

·         Ability to work independently as well as part of a larger team consisting of several government and contractors team members.

 

Education:

 

BA/BS in Computer Science preferred, or equivalent work experience. 

Microsoft Certified Professional Windows Server required

 

Security+ or CISSP required.

 

CCNA or Network+ desirable.

 

COMMENTS: 

Other Considerations:

·         Physical Demands: This job may require lifting objects up to 50 pounds. It may involve some walking, standing, bending or carrying of light items such as files, computer reports or office supplies.

·         Work Environment: Performed in a normal data center setting where basic safety precautions are required. On call and weekend duties may be required for down systems and\or scheduled maintenance

 

Additional:

 

WareOnEarth Communications, Inc., an SMS company, leverages our vast background in Information Assurance (IA) and Security (InfoSec) for cutting edge, mission critical government network solutions (e.g. Department of Defense [DoD], Department of Treasury, DEA). WCI provides a unique blend of extensive experience, exposure, knowledge and expertise that puts us ahead of the pack and our clients ahead of the curve when it comes to infrastructure protection.

 

We enable our clients to mitigate risks before incidents occur and interrupt business. WareOnEarth provides a wide range of Information Assurance and Security services including Vulnerability Assessments, which are designed to evaluate the effectiveness of our clients’ infrastructure protection so they can discover and address any vulnerabilities before someone else does.

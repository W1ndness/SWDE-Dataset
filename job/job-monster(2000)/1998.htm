Systems Administrator

About the Job

Our ideal Systems Administrator is a strong technical leader with in-depth and extensive expertise in a broad discipline of systems and technologies, including, but not limited to:  operating systems management and monitoring; application hosting platforms; virtualization products and best practices; physical computer and networking hardware; network design, management, and security; systems troubleshooting and problem resolution; and, process modeling and engineering. The Systems Administrator will leverage their expertise to: perform daily monitoring, and maintenance of a system; design and implement upgrades or changes to a system; and provide process and systems feedback with recommend feasible solutions to stakeholders. The Systems Administrator will collaborate with project stakeholders to build, manage, and maintain systems in accordance with specified stakeholder demands and business processes.

The Systems Administrator will be highly familiar with common networking and software terms, systems management and performance optimization, and providing high availability redundant systems.  The Systems Administrator will apply his or her knowledge of networking, systems, technology, security, and processes to develop solutions that satisfy customer requirements aligned with the time and cost aspects of the project.  The Systems Administrator will provide strategic technical expertise for system integration efforts, security and resource monitoring for systems, regular system maintenance, immediate response to system failure events and detailed documentation of system design changes, upgrades, and repairs..

Other areas of responsibility include the following:

  1. Technology Oversight: Maintain an ongoing awareness of emerging developments and trends in technology that could impact current projects and influence new projects.
  2. Vendor Tool Analysis: Lead evaluation of hardware and software vendors targeted for possible integration into the systems or environment, including strategic applications, tools, and utilities.  Provide oversight and guidance of new product initiatives ensuring adherence to architectural principles.
  3. High-level technical consultation with and mentoring of development staff.
  4. Customer Technical Resource: Serve as the technical resource and reference for customer integrations, help define appropriate customer support responses, and work directly with customers on high-level issues.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Experience designing and maintaining an enterprise level information system
  • Experience with Oracle Solaris Operating System and virtualization technology
  • Experience with x86 and SPARC hardware architectures
  • Experience troubleshooting and resolving network and system issues under pressure (hardware and software)
  • Experience supporting the deployment of J2EE rich and/or web client applications in a high-availability, clustered environment
  • Exemplary general IT knowledge (applications development, testing, deployment, operations, documentation, standards, best practices, security, hardware, networking, operating systems, DBMS, middleware, etc.)
  • Experience with lightweight, rapid development, agile methodologies
  • Experience interacting with legacy systems and phased application integration
  • Attention to detail
  • Ability to work as a team member and leader in a diverse technical environment
  • Extensive knowledge of networking protocols and systems security
  • Must possess effective verbal and written communication skills
  • Able to work independently with minimal supervision
  • Bachelors Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Must be a U.S. Citizen;  Active DoD Top Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Operating Systems: Solaris, Linux, Windows Server 2003, Server 2008 XP, Vista, & 7
  • Virtualization Technologies: Solaris Containers, VMware vSphere, Microsoft Hyper-V
  • Java Middleware: JMS, Apache Tomcat, Glassfish, Weblogic, Websphere, JBoss
  • Web Services: SOAP, REST, XML (DOM, SAX, XSL, XSD), WSDL, WS-Security, WS-Signature, WS-*
  • Semantic Web: OWL, RDF, SPARQL, Jena, Sesame
  • Relational Database Management Systems: Oracle, MySQL, MS SQL Server, Sybase, Postgres
  • Development Environment and Methodology: Agile, Waterfall, Subversion
  • Cloud Computing: Hadoop, Pig, Amazon Web Services, Cassandra
  • Programming / Scripting language experience
  • Advanced Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Experience or familiarity with DoD Security Standards
  • Top Secret Clearance / SCI Preferred

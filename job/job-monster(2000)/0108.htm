Network Administrator/Database Analyst

About the Job

Quality Data Services, a respected Clinical Research Organization, is currently seeking a Network Administrator/Database Analyst to join our team at our King of Prussia, PA location.  In this exciting, fast-paced, full-time position, you will be a key component in providing superior support to our internal and external clients using a wide range of technologies and skills.

In this split role you will provide Network Administration support including the following responsibilities:

  • Sets up, configures, and supports internal and/or external networks, workstations and servers.
  • Provides technical support and guidance to users.
  • Provides primary Help Desk and troubleshooting support via the Service Ticket System.
  • Responds to Help Desk inquiries and addresses issues in a timely fashion.

 

Following training on our eClinical Technology Suite- QDRx, you will also provide support as a Database Analyst including the following functions:

  • Develops and tests diagnostic edit-check programs to support clinical trials.  
  • Performs database transfers and ensures validity of data prior to release of data to clients.  

 

Experience/Education/Degree(s) Required:

·         Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field.

·         Experience supporting Microsoft Active Directory, Exchange 2003, and Microsoft SQL Server, minimum of one year experience.

·         Excellent customer relationship skills. 

·         Demonstrated programming experience in SQL code; minimum of one year. 

·         SAS experience and Microsoft certifications preferred, but not required.

·         Attention to detail and organizational skills required.

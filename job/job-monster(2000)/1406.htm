PHP Web Developer

About the Job

Reusser Design is looking for a talented and experienced PHP web developer to join our team.  This position requires a masterful ability to code flawless, efficient, accessible, standards-compliant front-end interfaces for web sites, and web-based applications.


Responsibilities:

  • Lead & assist in the development of high performance, highly scalable web sites and web applications
  • Be involved in all aspects of development — playing a critical role in design, planning, development and deployment
  • Use technical knowledge and creative thinking to provide the most innovative solutions
  • Be self motivated to meet aggressive deadlines in a rapid development environment



Experience:

  • Expert in PHP, MySQL, Javascript/jQuery, XHTML, AJAX
  • Experience with CodeIgnitor Framework (or strong desire to learn)
  • Extensive knowledge of LAMP (Linux-Apache-MySQL-PHP) based development and server management
  • Experience working and managing Source Control (e.g. Subversion, Git)
  • Must have at least 3+ years of professional web development experience



Skills:

  • Strong fundamental understanding of web application technology
  • Strong understanding of basic web skills (HTML, CSS, PHP, and Javascript)
  • Good communications skills and organization skills
  • Ability and desire to learn new applications/processes/environments



Why Work at Reusser Design?


Reusser Design is a growing fast-paced, strategic and creative web development company based in downtown Roanoke, Indiana. If you are interested in joining an energetic team of young professionals, and you are excited to learn new technologies that push the envelope forward, then Reusser Design is perfect for you. 


In addition to being part of a close-knit team where constant learning is encouraged, some of the benefits of working here at Reusser Design include:


    •     Competitive salaries
    •     Benefits Package
    •     2 weeks paid vacation
    •     Gym Membership
    •     Challenging projects that require an array of skills
    •     Brand new equipment and software
    •     Creative environment


We don't want you to just fit in, we want you to bring something new to the table. We want you to expand our capabilities and our thinking. Prove to us that you're a fun person with an incredible skill set as described above.

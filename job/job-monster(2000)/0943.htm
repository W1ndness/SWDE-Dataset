Localization Software Engineer - Permanent

About the Job

Welocalize was founded in 1997, and is a privately held, venture backed company. Our offices are located in the USA, Germany, Japan, China, the Netherlands and Ireland. For the past five years running, Deloitte and Touche has ranked Welocalize as one of the fastest growing technology companies in North America in their annual Fast 50 ranking. In 2005 Welocalize earned a place on the Inc 500 list of fastest growing private companies in the US.

Welocalize offers Integrated Globalization Services for faster international time-to-market and specializes in the eLearning, Data Storage, Supply Chain Management, Life Sciences and Data Security industries. Our services include globalization consulting, internationalization, localization, translation and testing solutions.

We work with our clients to create a framework and methodology for expanding globally that produces scaleable, predictable results and with the integration of our product and service family into the development environment our clients are able to achieve much faster international time-to-market.

Our framework combines globalization consulting and internationalization with a well organized, localization methodology required to translate software, eBusiness applications, web sites, documentation and other electronic content and business systems into foreign languages.

Job Title: Software Localisation Engineer - Permanent - East Coast 

 

 

Role Profile:

 

In this role, the candidate is required to have strong technical skills and be proficient in all aspects of Software localization in a multi-platform environment. It will be necessary to adapt a flexible approach to assigned tasks, work with little supervision, adapt to new technologies and possess excellent problem solving skills.

 

Core Accountabilities:

 

  • Demonstrate a clear understanding of all aspects of the Software localization process.
  • Define Software guidelines for individual projects and client process.
  • Excellent trouble shooting skills.
  • Thorough understanding of different localization bug types.
  • Team Lead/Language Lead responsibilities.
  • Identify project requirements at initial stages of a project.
  • Adherence to schedule and proactive input to schedule.
  • Develop software localization plan and perform localization analysis.
  • Proactively report in a timely manner on project team status and any other issues or concerns, suggesting possible solutions.
  • Provide clear, concise and objective feedback to wrap-up reports. Compile the wrap-up reports from other team members.
  • Demonstrate ownership for your assigned work tasks and also for team tasks, highlighting likely issues and solutions.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of the Quality Localization Process and associated tasks. Provide input into Quality Assurance process development to improve standards.
  • Provide objective feedback on team and individual performance of team members.

 

 

Essential Skills & Experience:

 

Technical

  • Degree, diploma, or equivalent in a computer related discipline.
  • 5 years localisation engineering experience
  • Expert in more than one operating system.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of server-based platforms. Work towards professional certification.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of Infrastructure and Networking Protocols and supporting Architecture– achieve certification where appropriate.
  • Understand code page functionality and how it interacts with operating systems, browsers and applications (locale, character handling, Unicode) – supply doc/training.
  • Internationalization – actively building up knowledge in internationalization methodologies.
  • Be capable of scripting to automate engineering processes where appropriate, e.g. Perl, VB.

Soft skills

  • Team work - be attentive, listen, act, give relevant and constructive input.
  • Provide mentoring for more junior members of the team and lead by example.
  • Eager to self improve/ demonstrate ambition.
  • Communication skills – have excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Demonstrate an organised approach to work and processes.
  • Demonstrate a conscientious, responsible approach to your work and be a reliable, committed, dedicated member of the team have initiative and be proactive.
  • Meticulous attention to detail.

About the Job

Information Security Analyst


About us

Are you looking to be part of extremely talented team that is building a world class business? BEW Global is a fast-paced, growth oriented business focused on information security.  With a growing customer base of Fortune 1000 firms and the acquisition of a brand new facility, BEW Global is positioned for continued success.


We are looking for some exceptionally talented people to join the team and help drive growth and achieve our goals. People are the key to our success and we value our team members like no other organization.  BEW Global strives to provide our team members the opportunity to grow and develop professionally, while also enjoying life outside of work in the great state of Colorado.  The most important attribute we look for in a team member is the drive for greatness. Our business is built-to-last, good is not enough, we want to be great! 


The work environment is unique.  No cubes, private offices and natural light keep us in a good frame of mind as we attack each and every day.  No suits unless needed on client site, we prefer jeans, shorts and t-shirts, with mountain bikes and skis often seen on the cars in the parking lot. 


Our starting salary rates are industry average because everyone needs to prove themselves in our environment. Talk is cheap and you are only as good as your last performance in this fast paced world of high-tech security. That being said, with success comes quick compensation advancement in our environment.  Bottom-line, if you contribute to the success of the business, we firmly believe in sharing the wealth and have implemented a unique profit sharing plan for our employees that vest them in the success of the business on the same pace with ownership.


We are looking for people who want to build something significant with us, not work for us.


Description of Role:


The Information Security Analyst is responsible for the daily monitoring and management of DLP, SIEM, Web and Email Security Gateway solutions for small, medium, and enterprise class clients.  You will be working in a uniquely designed operations center (private offices) making system policy changes and working with customers obtaining critical information regarding potential data theft from outside sources and potential malicious insider employee or contractor behavior. Analysts are required to answer inbound calls, address emails from client contacts, authenticate callers, and create tickets for all work as well as provide full management support and monitoring of the DLP, SIEM, Web and Email Security Gateway systems. Analysts also develop reports and through system analysis deliver valued business intelligence to our clients. The position is fast paced, team oriented, and requires adept multitasking abilities during busy times.  The following types of DLP, SIEM, Web and Email Security Gateway solutions will be the primary focus of the position:


DLP:    Symantec, McAfee, Websense and RSA 


SIEM:  RSA, NetIQ, Archsight, TriGeo, LogRythm


Email Gateway: Cisco Ironport, McAfee, Proofpoint

Web Gateway: Cisco Ironport, McAfee, Bluecoat and Websense


Essential Functions:


·         Answer inbound client emails phone calls and triage client requests and issues.

·         Daily monitoring and maintenance of customer systems.

·         Clearly communicate to clients both written and verbally so client can easily understand.

·         Develop systems policies that map to incident triage and event management process.

·         Ability to analyze events and determine severity and appropriate recommended response.

·         Respond to escalations providing technical expertise with external vendor support.

·         Review system architectures for technical correctness and operational suitability.

·         Expert guidance with event source collection, configuration and reporting needs.

·         Troubleshoot technical issues and systematically resolve network or application issues.

·         Work with clients to derive business intelligence from the supported solutions.

·         Work closely with sales and field services to identify customer support needs.

·         Create scripts and tools to automate a variety of configuration and update tasks.  

·         Perform and review policy, workflow and system settings following change process.

·         Work within the Numara Track-it CRM ticketing system to document all client interaction.

·         Work closely with peers in a team environment and assist others by with assignments.


Knowledge, Skills, & Abilities:


·         Background in IT infrastructure management and monitoring. 

·         Help desk experience will be essential.

·         Adept networking and system administration abilities.

·         Demonstrated organizational and scheduling skills, rigorous attention to detail.

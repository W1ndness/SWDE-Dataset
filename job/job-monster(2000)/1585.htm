About the Job

Global Client Services Functional Support Specialist

Job Description:

The Elemica Global Client Services Functional Support Specialist is a critical role supporting Elemica's hosted supply chain applications and solutions.  The specialist role participates as member of the Global Client Services functional support team, working with external client and internal Elemica teams to ensure the reliability of the network’s, services, applications, solutions, and environments, as well as the business continuity of our clients supply chain.

Company Overview:

Elemica is the premier global neutral network for supply chain process integration and collaboration within the Chemical and Rubber Industries. The company offers an integrated, end-to-end system, enabling buyers and sellers to streamline their business processes for contract sales and improve negotiation, contract management, order fulfillment, supply chain management and payment.

Responsibilities:

·         Develop a functional and technical understanding and knowledge of Elemica tools, applications and solutions.

·         Develop working knowledge of supported business processes including, Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Procurement, Demand Planning, and Order to Cash Management.

·         Assist clients with resolution of issues and service requests in a manner that ensures customer satisfaction.

·         Work with senior team members to document and optimize processes, procedures, and solutions to ensure quick and efficient identification and resolution of client concerns

·         Assist with administration of internal CRM and Case Management tools and documentation

·         Prepare client facing communications, documentation and training materials suitable for international consumption

·         Organize and prepare meetings and conference calls for the purpose of resolving internal and external production related issues and concerns.

·         Provide off-hour/on-call support in accordance with rolling schedule

Required Skills:

·         Strong client facing interpersonal and communication skills.

  • Fluency in English and/or German (written and verbal skills).
  • Degree in Management Information Systems, Management Science and Information Systems, Computer Science, Business Administration, or related major.
  • Microsoft Office Suite
  • Ability to analyze complex functional and technical problems

Helpful Skills and Experiences:

·         Exposure or experience to Project Management methodologies and tools

·         Exposure or experience to Supply Chain Management and Logistics business processes and tools

·         Exposure or experience to B2B eCommerce and EDI messaging formats

·          Exposure or experience to Case Management and CRM tools

·         Software testing and quality assurance experience

·         Exposure or experience to scripting and programming languages

·         Exposure or experience to standard SDLC

·         2-5 years of IT consulting or eCommerce experience

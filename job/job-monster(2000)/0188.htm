About the Job

Software Engineer in Test – Third Party Content

Classmates is developing a significant offering of digitized nostalgic content. In order to accomplish this we are creating a new Third Party Content team to work with content vendors to obtain the content, process it and make it available for viewing on the site. Content includes digitized magazines, photos, audio, video and more.

The Opportunity:

The Third Party Content team is looking for an eager SDET with experience testing web 2.0 features. This role will be responsible for developing test plans, test cases, manual testing, as well as automation for testing feature changes. Additionally, work with developers to ensure that content collected from third parties is reviewed, preprocessed and ready to be ingested.  Validate changes to the meta-data done by content ingestion or content management tools.

Responsibilities Include:

·         Plan, design, and implement test plans and test cases

·         Work closely with both product managers and software engineers to help clarify acceptance criteria and to ensure the reliability of our application

·         Test our products with relentless passion for quality

·         Maintain and extend existing automation scripts

·         Report defects and follow up on them through resolution

·         Report on release readiness

Basic Qualifications:

·         Minimum of 3 years of experience in QA white/black box testing

·         Experience with test automation

·         Experience with testing cross browser / cross platform

·         Experience with testing rich media content (flash video & audio, AJAX, HTML5)

·         Prior experience with test framework such as JUnit, Selenium, Grinder

·         Bachelors degree or higher in Computer Science or another relevant field

Preferred Qualifications:

·         Experience developing & testing object-oriented code in Java.

·         Experience working with Apache and Tomcat. 

·         Experience developing for Linux-based deployment platforms

·         Able to excel in an Agile/Scrum environment

·         Ability to troubleshoot complex systems

·         Excellent written and verbal communication skills.


Who are we?

Since 1995, Classmates.com has understood the value of memories and the power of human connections.  Consumer interest in reconnecting with nostalgic memories has helped Classmates.com grow to more than 50 million registered accounts across its social networking Web sites, and we continue evolve our services to further expand our value proposition. Central to our success is our continuing ability to attract high achievers, creative talent and proven leaders who are excited about the opportunity to redefine social networking. Our culture is all about collaboration, high energy, integrity, and growing our business.


Join us today and see your ideas come to life!

This position is located at our headquarters on Seattle’s waterfront. We offer a competitive salary and benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision, 401(k), employee stock purchase plan, flexible spending accounts, and much more!

Senior SharePoint Developer

About the Job


About Adventos www.adventos.com
Adventos LLC is a leading Microsoft technology consultant to government and select private sector organizations. The firm differentiates itself by assisting clients in fully realizing Microsoft product potential with a dual focus on client staff effectiveness and excellence in technology implementation. Adventos’ focus on client staff mirrors its commitment to its own staff as reflected in unique approaches to hiring, staff development, role definition, work assignments, coaching, career path and remuneration.


Adventos Values
• Our success is measured by the impact we have on client organizations
• We are focused on driving profitable growth
• We are always honest, candid, respectful and above reproach in our dealings with clients and each other
• We are dedicated to building a performance-driven organization
• Our people are our greatest asset: we strive to create a culture that provides challenge, fun and balance
• We are committed to continuous improvement


Primary Duties and Responsibilities - This is a Senior Consultant Position
• Design and create complex and sophisticated solutions utilizing Microsoft SharePoint 2007/2010, Windows SharePoint Services, and SharePoint Foundation, Visual Studio and SharePoint Designer as well as various other .NET components
• Using extensive SharePoint knowledge and experience; provide clients with high quality insight, and consulting services
• Provide guidance in all areas of the application lifecycle including: requirements engineering, project planning and management, architecture and design for service oriented architecture, development patterns and practices, software configuration management, testing and quality assurance, as well as deployment & operations
• Successfully initiate, deliver, implement, and support Microsoft SharePoint installations
• Support the delivery of comprehensive consultancy services, including supporting the work of colleagues
• Work with the Adventos team to strengthen the company, deepen existing client relationships and identify and develop new business opportunities
• This is a traveling consultant position with focus in the Denver, Phoenix, and Washington DC areas. Travel is expected to be 50% on average, and would be based in the Phoenix area for several months.


Essential Skills
• Demonstrated consulting skill with a track record of not just implementing technology but also advising senior decision makers on technology and business decisions
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills; strong presentation skills
• Strong team orientation
• Skilled at tackling unusual and complex challenges; can guide or advise others


Essential Attributes
• Passion and commitment to delivering value for clients
• Flexible and reliable
• Sets an example for others
• A willingness and ability to share knowledge
• Effective in politically sensitive, high profile roles
• Business acumen; understands the linkage of business context and priorities to IT strategy
• Integrity and high ethical standards
• Strong listening skills that allow deep understanding of client/staff needs
• Comfortable and effective in both leading and supporting roles
• Attention to detail
• Positive attitude; a “force for good”
• Strong work ethic


Required Professional Qualifications 
• Minimum of Bachelor degree
• Ideally, professional certification(s) in SharePoint administration and/or development such as SharePoint TS or MCPD, or willingness to obtain them
• Demonstrated experience with Microsoft Office SharePoint Server, as well as ASP.NET, C#, XML Web Services and IIS. The ideal candidate will have hands on experience with SharePoint (MOSS), Microsoft .NET, Visual Studio and SQL Server.  Highly recommended, hands on experience with Visual Studio Team System, Team Foundation Server, BizTalk Server, Dynamics CRM and SQL Server Analysis Services.
• At least 2 years of SharePoint Development and/or Administration experience
• At least 5 years of .NET development


Adventos will offer the successful candidate
• A culture in which employee contributions make a direct and visible impact on the company’s bottom line
• An opportunity to work on challenging, rewarding and “cutting edge” technology projects with leading client organizations
• Opportunity to earn Microsoft certifications and re-certifications
• A dynamic, supportive environment where technology is valued as a way to achieve company growth
• A culture that uniquely values formal and informal staff development with structured approaches to knowledge transfer and coaching
• Annual bonus based on individual and company performance
• Competitive compensation with an opportunity to share directly in company growth and success
• 401k with employer match, medical and dental /vision benefit options available for employee and family, ST/LT Disability, Life and Accident Insurance, 15 days of PTO accrued in first year and seven company holidays

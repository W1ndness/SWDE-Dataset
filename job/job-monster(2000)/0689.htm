Web Developer - Junior

About the Job

 
 
 
 

CTA is seeking a JUNIOR full-time recent college graduate for an entry-level Web Developer in Colorado Springs, CO to work on web-based application development and data warehouse efforts.  The successful incumbent to this position will be responsible for:

 

  • Full life-cycle development and support of web-based applications using a wide variety of languages and tools
  • Integration of web-based applications within larger systems
  • Working with data engineering team members to integrate and leverage data infrastructure in the application layer
  • Direct and indirect client support as needed
  • Provide support to management and the solution team in their efforts to bring the highest value to the client
  • Analyzing and systematizing and structuring client business processes
  • Design and implementation of effective, efficient supportive solutions to meet client needs
  • Exploration, evaluation and integration of new technologies
  • Effective participation, communication and collaboration in a dynamic team development environment

Work within these areas: 

·        Requirements gathering and analysis

·        Contribution to solution design

·        Writing and documenting application code

·        Application testing

·        Troubleshooting and response to emergent issues

·        New technology integration

Required qualifications and skills for thisposition include:

 

• Bachelor of Science degree in Engineering, Mathematics, Computer Science, Science, or other related discipline

 

• 0-2 years experience in web development

 

• Previous experience in web technologies, platforms, and tools, including .NET languages, JavaScript,AJAX, SQL, Active Server Pages (ASP), Visual Studio, Internet Information Services (IIS)

 

• Familiarity with genera lweb application architecture and deployment

 

·        Familiarity of basic software design principles and practices, including object-oriented programming, graphical user interfaces, and performance-oriented SQL

 

The candidate will be required to perform other duties as requested, directed or assigned.  Job descriptions are subject to change at anytime.

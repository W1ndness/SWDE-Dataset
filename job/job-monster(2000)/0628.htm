Global Security Operations Center Supervisor

About the Job


Join a dynamic, forward thinking organization which is looking for a Global Security Operations Center (GSOC) Supervisor to provide oversight of more than 50 office locations around the world.  The GSOC also provides emergency travel support and safety assistance to over 14,000 workers scattered across multiple nations, cultures, and languages. 

 The GSOC Supervisor’s primary responsibility is the coordination of tactical dispatch, operations, both locally and globally, while ensuring the delivery of best in class customer service by GSOC staff.  This position requires a detail-oriented individual with the ability to quickly follow and provide direction while continuing to make logical decisions in a crisis situation.  This position also requires an individual who is performance driven to achieve success, one who can meet deadlines as assigned, provide a positive and challenging work environment for team members, and can offer leadership and problem-solving abilities to support projects assigned by the Operations Management teams.

 The GSOC Supervisor must remain proficient in their performance of all GSOC duties, which include but are not limited to; Alarm Monitoring & Assessment, Dispatch & Communications, Emergency Response, Security Systems Troubleshooting, Incident Management, and Notification & Escalation for corporate-wide major events.

Primary Responsibilities

-          Manage day-do-day performance of GSOC operation across all shifts

-           Initial escalation level for crisis management and/or medical, weather, or facilities related emergencies

-          Manage employee performance standards and evaluations

-          Assist in the development of quality controls and monitor performance

-          Ensure compliance and adherence to all company processes and procedures

-          Support and develop complete understanding as new technologies are rolled out

-          Continually identify potential opportunities for program improvements

-          Receive, review, and communicate all changes to company policies and procedures to team members and ensure team compliance with any changes

-          Partner with members of Operations Management and/or Systems Management teams

-          Maintain constant communications both inside and outside the GSOC to ensure service and resolution of all issues

-          Attend and support meetings and events as needed

-          Coordinate with system administrators to ensure system integrity by testing and troubleshooting alarm and access control devices

-          Develop, implement, monitor and maintain GSOC training and certification programs.  

Skills and Knowledge

-          Strong ability to understand security systems

-          Provide initial systems troubleshooting

-          Strong working knowledge of the Safety and Security Industry

-          A minimum of 7 years experience in a Supervisory/Management capacity, preferably in the high-tech sector

-          Excellent verbal and written communication skills

-          Ability to maintain professional demeanor under stressful situations and be comfortable in a public speaking role. 

-           Strong problem-solving and multi-tasking abilities,

-          Excellent computer skills (MS Office, OS,  etc.),

-          Strong knowledge of state-of-the-art security technology, to include, alarm and access control systems, CCTV systems, communications technologies, software programs, and database and networks

-          Good organizational skills, with an emphasis on multi tasking. 

-          Flexibility and adaptability are critical to success, given the company’s dynamic corporate culture.

Education and Experience

-          Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience

-          7-10 years of security experience

-          Strong understanding and troubleshooting of physical access control systems and CCTV

-            5+ years of security supervisor, program management, or equivalent related experience

-            Strong control center operations experience

-          Knowledge of project management techniques and tools

-          Proven experience in people management, strategic planning and risk management

-          Proficient in relevant software applications

About the Job


Highly Interactive/Nationwide IT Support

Who Are You?
-Do you have a passion for technology?
-Would you want to work everyday in an atmosphere of intrigue and problem solving?
-Are you willing to learn and can you bring knowledge to the table?
-Do you like virtualization, different operating systems, VoIP, FLAC, gadgets, and A/V?
-Do you have a strong, almost compulsive, desire for everyone to use the technology that is truly best for them?
-Do you possess the verbal skills to translate your knowledge and build a client base?

What Is Zebis?
-Zebis provides high level Managed IT Support. To us, IT is our life: what we do when we are not at work! 
-This is not your normal boring IT helpdesk. We get to support and promote innovative end user solutions, including those based on GNU/Linux, virtualization, great applications, cloud computing, open standards, Symbian, and more.
-The beauty of Zebis? We cant wait until tomorrow to bring our customers the products of the future. We are excited to do it today!

Who Are We Looking For?
1) You really enjoy providing IT service and the problem solving process.
2) You can speak to non-technical people and explain the advantages of products they may not completely understand.
3) You come up with your own ways to better the lives of people: you don't wait until THEY give YOU tasks.
4) You understand the use of IT and how it can help your customers.
5) You want to personally succeed and grow in the fascinating IT world.
6) Most importantly, you enjoy business, are confident with your skills and how to help people and you are willing to explain and sell new services and products.

What Will You Need To Qualify?
1) Download this file: http://www.zebis.com/questions201010.pdf
-If you can answer most of these questions out of your general knowledge, we are anxious to talk with you!
2) First complete the questions and send to the e-mail address in this ad.
-We will evaluate your responses and be in touch. 
-Feel free to call us if you feel you are perfect for Zebis!

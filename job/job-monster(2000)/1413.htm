IT Analyst

About the Job

JOB SUMMARY:
The Application Analyst position is responsible for the analysis, installation, development and maintenance of corporate information systems. This role will directly support the information systems within Meridian Bioscience Corporate Headquarters and assist in the support of the information systems globally. The analyst will provide/coordinate technical support of the design, development, installation, operation, modifications and maintenance activities of Meridian's computerized information systems.

KEY DUTIES:

  • Administer, monitor and maintain MS SQL Server(s).
  • Administer and maintain the OLAP and ETL processes and cubes
  • Evaluate, install, develop/integrate and maintain all MS Windows based and IIS based information systems at the corporate headquarters.
  • Support all corporately shared applications.
  • Serve as project coordinator for in-house development and/or implementation of vendor-based information systems.
  • When needed, write programs (SQL, .NET, Visual Basic, ASP, Java Script) to integrate information systems.
  • Maintain advanced knowledge of Microsoft's product line and methodologies.
  • Use, build on, and improve existing software tools and systems.
  • Manage software revision upgrades and software license agreements.
  • Learn and understand the business practices and information systems of MBI, including the Quality System QSR) and Sarbanes Oxley (SOX) regulations that impact MBI operations.
  • Develop and maintain a professional relationship with end-users, peers, management and vendors.
  • Act as a key technical liaison between computer systems users, vendors and the MIS/IT Department.
  • Ensure users are kept informed of all system updates and ensures those updates are fully tested before moving to the live environment.
  • Educate users on the functions of installed applications.
  • Daily operational support and maintenance of software and computerized information systems including Microsoft operating systems, Office and server systems, Quality System Applications including Document Management System, Financial Applications such as payroll and financial reporting and budgeting, Sales/Marketing including Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and mass e-mail, help desk software.
  • Other duties as assigned.

QUALIFICATIONS:

Education/Experience:

  • Bachelor's degree, Computer Science or related major is preferred.
  • Minimum of seven years of experience in an information technology role.
  • Minimum of five years of system integrating experience.
  • MCSE Certification strongly desired.

Skills, Specialized Knowledge and Abilities:

  • Advanced knowledge of MS SQL 2000, 2005, 2008.
  • Advanced knowledge of the computer system market (especially Microsoft server and PC systems), SQL database organization and management including Microsoft, SQL Server, SharePoint Portal, Office Systems - Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Publisher, InfoPath, Visio and Project, Active Directory, Crystal Reports/SQL Reporting Services, Programming Languages-.NET, Visual Basic (VB)/C#, Active Server Pages (ASP), Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Extensible Markup Language (SML), JAVA.
  • Highly motivated, self-directed individual capable of working under pressure within tight time frames. Sets priorities to ensure tasks, multiple projects are prioritized and completed on time.
  • Possess a high level of initiative and energy, an enthusiasm for learning, the capability to work with minimal supervision and have a client service approach to supporting users.
  • Experience with creating and maintaining computer system security.
  • Experience working with PC communication protocols such as TCP/IP.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to train others in the use of technology.
  • Solid technical, analytical, problem solving and troubleshooting skills.
  • Strong ability to complete research, recommend and implement new applications, with new or current vendors of information technology systems.
  • Detail-oriented, but efficient approach to work assignments.
  • Deep knowledge of personal computers and PC application software.
  • Ability to read, understand follow all company Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and guidelines.
  • Detail-oriented with the ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team setting with peers and colleagues in a cross functional setting.
  • Excellent interpersonal, organizational, written and oral communication skills along with excellent customer service and satisfaction skills and ability to build rapport with all levels of internal users.
  • Strong analytical problem solving, troubleshooting skills and the ability to train others in the use of technology.
  • Must be self-starter, able to prioritize and handle multiple tasks and ability to meeting strict deadlines and perform under pressure in a fast-paced work environment.
  • Ability to maintain regular attendance and punctuality requirements.
  • Ability to provide technical support on non-standard shifts and weekends to resolve programming issues or project work that would otherwise interfere with production.

Requirements: (Physical, Mental, Environmental Demands)

  • Ability to go up and down several flights of stairs throughout the work day in a multi-location facility.
  • Must be able to concentrate, analyze and solve complex issues throughout the course of the work day.
  • Ability to stand and sit for long periods of time, stoop, reach, bend throughout the course of the work day.
  • Ability to manually lift up to 40 pounds including PCs and other peripheral hardware.
  • Ability to travel by air/car to other locations of MBI.
  • Must be able to work outside normal business hours including evenings, weekends and emergency calls services.
  • Will carry cell phone to facility intra-department communications both during and after normal business hours.
  • Ability to work on a PC including repetitive use of a keyboard and mouse for long periods throughout the course of the work day.

Creative Designer & Customer User Experience Manager

About the Job

The E-Commerce Department of the LifeWay.com Division of LifeWay Christian Resources is seeking a Creative Designer & Customer User Experience Manager.  As the leader of our User Interface & Creative Design team, this individual will improve the appearance and usability of websites used by tens of thousands of faith based organizations and millions of consumers worldwide. We are seeking an experienced manager to drive our customer experience initiatives as well as to lead a staff of designers in creating highly engaging visual designs that are both informative and useful. The Creative Designer & Customer User Experience Manager  will  function in a business team lead role, guiding the usability testing, and being responsible for  the ongoing development of a compelling online user experience, including the Home Page.  This Strategist will coordinate the site A/B testing to determine customer preferences and usability of site features.  This individual will function as a business team leader, providing web designers with creative direction, design processes, and graphic elements of the online format.

Responsibilities:
Selects and conducts appropriate user-centered design techniques throughout an iterative design process.
Directs the creative efforts of a young but maturing GX team.
Guides usability testing.
Coordinates site A/B testing to determine customer preferences of site features.
Serves as the business team lead role for the ongoing development of a compelling online user experience.
Provide suggestions to re-purpose/update existing creative materials
Serves as the business team lead role for the online creative direction, design, and graphic elements.

Requirements:
Bachelor's degree in digital or graphic design, human-computer interaction, interactive media, or a related field
Expertise in Photoshop and familiarity with: Illustrator, InDesign, Fireworks, CSS, JavaScript, HTML5, Flash
Experience with formal usability analysis techniques
Ability to work independently and collaborate with cross-functional teams

If interested in this position apply at www.lifeway.jobs, Requisition 3178

Sr. Manager, Oracle ERP Applications

About the Job

Specialized or Technical Knowledge/Skills: 


·         Experience delivering reporting systems and ecommerce solutions with Oracle EBS Applications - 11i and / or R12 - Manufacturing and Financials. 

·         BS degree in related field required, advanced degree preferred

·         Successfully managed design and implementation of enterprise-wide, multi-location, cross-functional transaction systems (preferably Oracle)

·         Outstanding project management skills

·         Communicate and relate well at all levels, especially senior levels

·         Strong business acumen coupled with exceptional IT skills


Purpose of Job:

 

Reports to the Director, Business Systems and you will be responsible for managing and supporting the firm's Oracle EBS stack.  You will provide leadership in expanding the use of the Oracle EBS through implementing new modules in Oracle Applications and upgrading and enhancing Oracle EBS (E-Business Suite) as necessary. You will provide a high level of technology and application service and will also implement and support the Global Information Technology strategic plan required to meet the overall business objectives of the corporation.

 

 

Dimensions of Job:  
 

Ø  Manage 4 to 5 people, including contractors. 

Ø  Geographic scope is companywide.

Ø  Plan and develop budgets for Oracle EBS specific initiatives 

Ø  Build and manage successful relationships with Applications managers, Leaders of the lines of business (GM’s and Manufacturing/Finance Directors)

Ø  Signature authority for approval of capital and expense budget items within the financial guidelines set for the Sr. Manager position

 

 

Principal Duties and Responsibilities:  
 

1.     Leads and manages the development, implementation and on-going maintenance of Oracle E-Business Suite (EBS). 

2.     Manages the enhancements and upgrades of Oracle EBS based on business requirements/business decisions. 

3.     Coordinates and manages the functional business area requirements from key stakeholders for Oracle EBS solution development and implementation.  

4.     Liaise with the key business managers to align the IT initiatives with the business initiatives. 

5.     Identify and organize development and training needs (in conjunction with the Functional Leads) such that personnel are equipped with the proper skill set to carry out the successful completion of all program required activities. 

6.     Will put in place the proper processes to manage and track the programs progress as it relates to schedule, cost and technical compliance. 

7.     Will ensure that Senior Management is informed with regard to the current program status on a regular basis so that issues with program performance can be dealt with in a timely manner. 

8.     The ERP Systems Manager will ensure that customers and Stakeholders receive effective and timely communication as to the status of the program. 

9.     Perform analysis and documentation of as-is capabilities related to business processes, people, technology, etc.

10.  Manage and support Oracle EBS applications and the bolt-on applications closely associated with ERP Applications.

11.  Perform GAP analysis to determine customization and project planning needs and scope.

12.  Champion improvements in practices by recommending changes to business process templates, systems configuration guides and implementation procedures.

 

 

 

Major Challenges: 

Ø  Implementation and on going support of Oracle EBS. Specifically building a culture of “self help” reporting

Ø  Coordinating work among multiple business units

Ø  Need for strategic thinking coupled with acute cost consciousness

Ø  Coordinating and prioritizing resources across the organization

 

 

Decision Making Authority: 

·         Determine staffing levels, budget requirements for Europe IT, communicates/achieves buy in from the CIO and European General Managers.

·         Controls all organization development activities including hiring, dismissals, performance reviews, promotions, and disciplinary action.

·         Plan and control department budget

·         Select consultants and contractors, negotiates with vendors

·         Manage capital for strategic business system applications

 

***Local candidates to the metro Atlanta area are encouraged to apply as no relocation assistance is available at this time.
***Minimal travel is required in this position.

Director of M&A Technology Due Diligence

About the Job

Responsibilities

The Director for M&A Technology Due Diligence will be responsible for performing Technology due diligence on acquisition targets.

Key responsibilities will include:
- Develop, enhance and institute M&A Technology Due Diligence tools used in technical assessments
- Execute Technology Acquisition Due Diligence
- Direct internal and external resources to perform Technology Due Diligence
- Coordinate with other groups (e.g. Global Security, CISO, BSDL teams etc.) to conduct Acquisition Due Diligence under very tight deadlines
- Coordinate with Corporate Development to perform and report on Acquisition Due Diligence
- Perform gap analysis on the Technology infrastructure of acquisition targets and assess time, effort and cost required to address gaps
- Present Due Diligence Assessment results and recommendations to senior leadership
- Facilitate the transition and integration of newly acquired target companies into AET

Department Name

Strategy and Innovation

Required Qualifications

 

- Excellent interpersonal, communication and judgment skills
- Be a known and effective collaborator and influencer at all levels
- Strong thought leadership capabilities and innovative thinking
- Drive, enthusiasm, commitment and a positive outlook
- Must have Technology Due Diligence experience
- Strong broad based technical background in multiple technologies ranging from infrastructure, operations, security, data management, architecture, and application development
- Demonstrated experience in technology due diligence in one or more of the following areas: payment processing, data insights, POS, travel, e-commerce, mobile, web 2.0, and enterprise systems
- Demonstrated working knowledge of AET and AXP
- Previous experience with M&A is preferred

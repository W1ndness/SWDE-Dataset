About the Job

 

Senior Product Portfolio Manager – Energy Critical Information

Englewood, CO

 

IHS (NYSE: IHS) is a leading global source of critical information and insight dedicated to providing the most complete and trusted data and expertise. IHS product and service solutions span four areas of information that encompass the most important concerns facing global business today: Energy, Product Lifecycle, Security and Environment. By focusing on customers first, IHS enables innovative and successful decision-making for customers ranging from governments and multinational companies to smaller companies and technical professionals in more than 180 countries. IHS has been in business since 1959 and employs approximately 3,800 people in more than 20 countries.

 

Summary:

Drive effective leadership and decision making for Critical Information product management teams through business analysis, reporting, and recommendations. Partner with product leads to ensure  process improvement utilizing analysis to facilitate change through communication and mentorship of cross-functional, cross-segment, and cross-regional product teams. Partner with product leads and Regional Sales Support and Marketing Organization to ensure customer delight for response to Customer First surveys and communication of product changes and enhancements.

 

General Job Duties:

·         Business Analytics for effective Critical Information Portfolio and Revenue Management - including revenue reporting, forecast gap analysis and action planning, product profitability, product rationalization, product pricing Program Management -

·         Guide and direct the product planning process within the context of the business strategic plan and product/marketing strategies, manage the Product Prioritization process and follow-up to include the tracking, reporting, and analysis of results against plans for product development projects; review project status and facilitate the establishment of priorities and resource allocation; ensure status of Product Roadmap is current and concise for prioritization decision making at the executive level.

·         Customer First - provide analysis of results and facilitate coordinated response between Product Management and Regional Sales organizations

·         Manage the CI area on the Source

·         Special Projects - Identify opportunities for improvement in processes and work with teams to implement effective solutions; Manage special projects with cross-segment/cross-product impact.

 

Qualifications:

 

Required Education:

·         Bachelor’s degree in Business, Information Systems or equivalent work experience.  PM certification preferred.

 

Required Experience:

·         7-10 years experience of project or information management experience

·         Excellent verbal/written communication and interpersonal skills

·         Facilitation skills

·         Ability to collect and analyze data and make recommendations

·         Ability to manage multiple and varied projects

·         Experience with Excel and PowerPoint essential

·         Working knowledge of statistical analysis with a background in operational analysis, process management, or workflow evaluation preferred

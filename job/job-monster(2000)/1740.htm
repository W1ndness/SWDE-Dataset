About the Job

 

Senior .NET Software Applications Developer


As our Senior C# /.NET Applications Developer based in Austin, TX, you will use your experience in .NET and C# to help us evolve our back-end software and service layer platform. You will work in an agile development environment, enhancing and extending our in-house software-as-a-service (Saas) platform responsible for processing hundreds of thousands of dollars in revenue each and every day. You will be working closely with senior engineering leaders to design robust, scalable software that meets both the immediate and future needs of our constantly evolving business environment.

All Web Leads, Inc. is one of the most successful online lead generation providers to the US Insurance industry. We use internet marketing to turn consumer interest in insurance products into policy sales for many of the largest insurance carriers (and their agents) in the world. We recently placed 2nd in the Austin Business Journal’s 2009 “Fast 50”. We are a tight-knit team with a fast paced, energetic, and entrepreneurial company culture. We are highly profitable; provide competitive pay, excellent benefits, and a fun and collaborative work environment.


Responsibilities will include:

  • Designing and implementing high reliability components for our business critical .NET service layer and database platform
  • Designing efficient algorithms and data-structures to encapsulate key business logic and data
  • Working closely with the engineering leadership team and key business stakeholders to develop
  • new features to address business critical needs
  • Translating high level business needs into actionable engineering requirements
  • Working closely with our QA team to ensure on-time release and product quality
  • Participation in the full software development lifecycle process

    •  

Requirements:

  • Minimum of 5+ years experience in a back-end C# /.NET software development role with increasing responsibilities
  • Deep familiarity with Object Oriented programming and design
  • Experience building and supporting large scale, high-availability software-as-a-service platforms
  • Strong experience building and consuming web services (SOAP, REST, WCF, IIS, XML, JSON)
  • Experience writing and debugging Windows Services applications
  • Solid RDBMS and ORM experience (MS SQL Server, MySQL, SQLite, and NHibernate)
  • Experience working in a test-driven development environment (NUnit, MbUnit, etc.)
  • Enjoy working within a small, fast-paced team environment
  • Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related field

    •  

Preferred But Not Required:

  • Experience with message-based applications (MSMQ, nServiceBus)
  • Basic familiarity with OLAP database concepts, and the MDX query language
  • Basic LAMP systems administration/development experience
  • Experience with ASP.NET web applications (Web Forms, MVC, HTML, CSS, AJAX, jQuery)

About the Job

Description

IT Analyst

  • Provide computer hardware and software support to servers, clients and the technical staff as required.
  • Monitor system communications to ensure continued service to remote sites.
  • Assist in problem determination procedures and help develop and implement corrective actions where possible.
  • Perform general hardware or software maintenance to keep company servers, computer hardware and software in good working condition.
  • Perform the collection of information related to the procurement of server, computer, printer hardware and software.
  • Monitor computer hardware inventory and maintain established inventory in good working condition.

 Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following:

 

  1. Complies with legal requirements by adherence to requirements of existing and new regulations and legislation.
  2. Complies with all company policies and procedures.
  3. Works in a manner that supports team work.
  4. Completes work to assure customer and results oriented.
  5. Ensures a safe and healthy environment by complying with all company safety policies and procedures.
  6. Assist in all tasks associated with the design, implementation, and management of Local and Wide Area Networks using Microsoft Windows Operating Systems.
  7. Registering of users and granting appropriate access to data and peripherals.
  8. Creating network resources such as network shares, print queues, and print servers.
  9. Assist with installation and support of server hardware and software as directed.
  10. Provide hardware and software technical support to clients as required. Perform diagnostic procedures on hardware to determine failing components and replace as necessary.
  11. Contact appropriate service personnel, if required.
  12. Assist in network related tasks as directed.
  13. Install hardware and software on local computers.
  14. Install network related components and configure appropriate network software.
  15. Report outstanding hardware or software issues for resolution.
  16. Assist in the implementation and testing of the IT disaster recovery plan to be utilized in the event of building/location disaster or network hardware failure.
  17. Assist in the documentation of the plan and testing for accuracy.
  18. Assist in the monitoring and support of data collection systems to determine status of data collection network and devices.
  19. Take appropriate action to ensure all devices in working order or report problems for further action.
  20. Perform other duties as assigned.
  21. Maintain ServiceDesk.
  22. Follow Corporate IT policies and procedures.
  23. Maintain telecommunications as required.
  24. Other duties as required.

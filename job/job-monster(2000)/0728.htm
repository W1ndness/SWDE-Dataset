About the Job

INFORMATION SECURITY OFFICER

 

The University of Alabama in Huntsville is accepting applications for the full-time position of Information Security Officer. This position requires a Bachelor’s degree in Business, MIS, Information Security, Information Technology, Information Systems Management, Public Policy or a related field, 5 years of progressive full-time work experience managing information security, policy planning, prioritizing, defining activities associated with identity management, access management and enterprise security. Candidate must have knowledge of FERPA, PCI, HIPPA and GLBA regulations as well as current CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) or CISA(Certified Information Systems Auditor). Certifications such as CPP (Certified Protection Professional) or GIAC (Global Information Assurance Certification) will be considered in lieu of a CISSP or CISA certification. Candidate should have an in-depth knowledge of information management and practice, such as firewalls, intrusion detection, intrusion prevention and forensic analysis. A Master’s degree is preferred and ERP experience is desired. The approximate starting salary will be between $70,000 - $80,000. Please apply electronically by submitting an application and resume to http://uah.interviewexchange.com and apply for the position of Information Security Officer for I.T. Solutions (RT1663).

About the Job

I-many, Inc. is the leader in Contract Management for the Enterprise. Our customers include Global 2000 companies from over 21 industries worldwide. Our software products are brought to market in our ContractSphere family of products.  ContractSphere includes software for contract authoring, compliance software, demand management and analytics.  Product functionality includes end user, client server and web based applications that provide contract management for the enterprise.  Please refer to www.imany.com for more information.

Are you an enterprise application development expert? Do you have Internet and RDBMS development experience? Do you want to build, best-in-class, service oriented, enterprise business solutions as part of a growing and collaborative engineering team? Do you want a chance to be part of the new, new thing; a chance to participate and help create the next generation enterprise applications by delivering Contract Management Suite for Enterprise?

If you answered yes to these questions, you are ready to join I-Many. We are now seeking talented, self-motivated and team-oriented Senior Software Engineers to join our world class software engineering team.

You will be working on next-generation enterprise software applications based on a J2EE enterprise services platform. You will contribute to design brainstorming sessions, design one or more software subsystems and spend most of your time developing code to realize a web-based business application. We are looking for talented individuals who are self-motivated, quick learner, and good team players. This position reports to the Development Manager.

Responsibilities:

  • Build back-end database components in PL/SQL for web based applications
  • Design and develop modules for a high-volume multi-user application suite  
  • Create packages, procedures, functions, triggers and views to encapsulate business rules
  • Automate unit tests for PL/SQL packages
  • Document implementation specifications
  • Participate in feature implementation reviews

Requirements:

  • Education: BS/MS in Computer Science, Engineering or related fields
  • People and Project Management Skills
    • Excellent verbal and written English communication skills
    • Ability to lead and mentor heterogeneous engineering teams in complex projects
    • Incredible work ethic that thrives in fast paced, dynamic work environment
    • Ability to multitask between projects with minimal loss in productivity
    • Proven ability and desire to deliver projects on time, on quality
    • Able to cope with and thrive in uncertain and changing project requirements
    • Ability to lead technical projects and code at the same time
  • Technology Skills:
    • Demonstrated experience with Oracle (3+ years)
    • Strong programming and debugging skills in PL/SQL, SQL
    • Experience in database design, performance tuning
    • Working experience with Oracle 10g and 11g environments
    • Experience developing backend code for web based applications
    • Experience tuning large database environments
    • Strong knowledge of development methodologies, standards and procedures
    • Working experience with Application servers is a plus
    • Oracle certifications is a plus

 

Interested candidates, please email your resume with “PLSQL-09” in the subject line to resumes@imany.com

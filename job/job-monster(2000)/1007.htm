About the Job

SPINE is growing. We're a marketing communications firm in Stamford, CT seeking new talent to join our skilled creative team. In our open atmosphere, every member - whether designer, writer, programmer or production - contributes to our tireless pursuit of a better idea. The result is top-caliber work that excites us and our clients. Some of them are household names, while others are simply wildly successful. Their needs vary widely, so our projects do, too. If you crave a steady stream of media diversity and opportunities to innovate, SPINE provides the setting you're searching for.

We are seeking a full-time developer/programmer to develop dynamic websites and web-based applications. Professional experience of 3-5 years working with SQL, ASP, and JavaScript required. Responsibilities will focus on working directly with internal and external clients on new development initiatives. Position offers the potential for management-level responsibilities, and the ability to define service offerings. Successful candidate to begin work ASAP.


RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Lead and manage projects
- Serve as a strong liaison with clients from a service and tactical standpoint
- Act as a subject matter expert in meetings with new clients/prospects
- Work both independently and collaboratively
- Produce fast, efficient, error-free code
- Monitor web technology and trends
- Debug, diagnose, and fix problems
- Develop and monitor reports on usage of client sites
- Analyze website activities using tools such as Google Analytics
- Analyze and implement social media technology to enrich dialogue


QUALIFICATIONS:
- Expert knowledge and solid working experience designing efficient, cross-browser user-facing websites; extensive experience handling variations across different browser versions including Safari, MSIE 6/7/8, Chrome, Firefox, and Opera
- Expert knowledge of CSS, XHTML, HTML 5, DOM, JavaScript, and JQuery to create tableless layouts
- Knowledge of developing dynamic web applications using C#.NET
- Adherence to web standards and object oriented programming
- Experience translating comps and wireframes into flexible and usable code templates

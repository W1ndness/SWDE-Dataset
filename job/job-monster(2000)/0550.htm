Senior Product Manager – Enterprise Solutions

About the Job

The Sr. Product Manager will be responsible for defining and executing the strategy and development of Enterprise opportunities for the Stickam platform, including target markets, business models and customer requirements.  You will then drive implementation of this strategy to revenue and strategic goals.  

Today, Stickam is the leader in live online communication in a social-media format.  Your job will be to introduce that platform to the Enterprise buyer.  

In this role, you will be directly involved in the strategic direction of the leading livestreaming communication product for enterprise solutions.


Responsibilities: 

  • Serve as a business leader and product champion by deeply understanding which markets are the best enterprise opportunities based on market size and customer requirements.  
  • Contribute to the product vision, strategy and roadmap. 
  • Develop and execute strategy around product packaging, pricing, and go-to-market activities.
  • Participate in engaging customers/prospects, sales, and technical team to define and prioritize product requirements and implementation details. 
  • Document requirements to drive software development, including functional designs, and collaborate with Engineering for successful implementation & delivery. 
  • Ensure product that is developed maps to customer and market requirements;  proactively identify, prioritize and drive resolution to project issues 
  • Engage with salespeople to train, build sales pipeline and provide deal support. 
  • Manage cross functional projects by providing project leadership and daily management throughout the project from inception to delivery 


Qualifications Requirements: 

  • Self-starter with proven ability to successfully kick-start new initiatives and to drive complex issues through analysis and resolution. 
  • Excellent written and communication skills and equally able to talk to engineers, senior executives, and corporate customers. 
  • Strong analytic skills and ability to qualitatively and quantitatively evaluate alternatives and make compelling recommendations based on solid business case analysis. 
  • Able to easily transition between high level strategy to day-to-day implementation. 
  • Results driven, attention to detail and follow-through, and ability to work effectively at all levels internally and with partners. 
  • Able to influence others and drive consensus on market prioritization, product requirements, etc. 
  • Solid understanding of Internet video or livestreaming markets.  Perfect candidate will have understanding/experience in enterprise video use cases. 
  • Working knowledge of Product Management operations with at least 5 years Product Design / Product Management experience.  
  • Must have experience in enterprise software industry or SAAS models.  Preferable to have at least two years in the internet video or social networks.
  • Technical development experience is a plus.  
  • BA/BS.  MBA preferred

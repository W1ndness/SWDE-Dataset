Unix/Linux Administrator

About the Job

HealthPlan Services is a leading managed health care services company, providing distribution, enrollment, billing and collection, claims administration, and risk services for health care payors and providers. HPS customers include insurance companies, HMOs and other managed care organizations, and organizations with self-funded health care plans. Based in Tampa, Florida, the company serves over 100,000 businesses, covering over 1.6 million members in the United States.


Job Title

Linux Administrator

 

CHX101

Primary Function

This position reports to the Team Leader, Open Systems Support and is responsible for the UNIX and Linux Operating System environment and related hardware and software.  Responsibilities include:

  • Installation, configuration, and maintenance of RedHat Linux 5 and Sun Solaris
  • Perform the duties of Systems Administrator for the UNIX/Linux environment. 
  • Monitors the performance of the UNIX and Linux systems,
  • Perform tuning as necessary to ensure optimum performance;
  • Provide guidance and assistance to the programming and technical staff relating to the UNIX and Linux environment. 
  • Assist the Team Leader Open Systems Support to investigate new computing concepts;
  • Provide direction for future growth.
  • Support of LAMP environment.

 

 

Minimum Education & Experience

  • Bachelors preferred. 
  • 5 years experience with Linux.
  • Must have technical knowledge of both hardware and software

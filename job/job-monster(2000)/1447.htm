Senior Systems Analyst

About the Job

GENERAL DESCRIPTION

Review National, DoD, and Air Force directives, instructions, manuals, standards, strategies, visions, mission statements, goals and objectives.  Recommend changes to update and/or create new roles, responsibilities, policies, and procedures for those governing documents.  Develop policies and procedures for the Air Force 33 - 200 series directives and Air Force System Security Instructions (AFSSI) and manuals (AFSSMs).  Incorporate specific issues, lessons-learned, and official Air Force policy interpretations in new and/or updated policies and procedures.  Update and maintain Air Force Information Assurance Community of Practice.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

-    Interpret, Develop, Prepare and Publish Information Assurance (IA) Policy

-    Review, Update, Revise and Recommend National, Federal, DoD and AF IA Security Policy

-    Provide guidance for all IT and IA personnel across the Air Force regarding IA policy

-    Update the IA Community of Practice (CoP) content daily, Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ), discussion forums, and other applicable CoP content.

 

WORK EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS

-    Minimum of 3 to 5 years work experience and in-depth working knowledge of:

-  National, DoD, and AF Information Assurance (IA) policies, procedures, and objectives. 

-  Experience in MAJCOM or Wing-level IA office and/or computer security is highly desirable

-  Security Plus Certification required

-    Must be a United States Citizen and possess a NAC,  must be eligible to obtain a Secret clearance

 

EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS

-    Bachelor’s in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, or other scientific or technical discipline. 

-  Four years of applicable professional technical experience may be substituted for degree requirements 

-  Combination of relevant job experience, industry certifications (e.g., MCSE, MCSA, MCDBA, CCNA, CCIE, BICSI, A+), and education may be given consideration in lieu of the degree.

Skill Requirements

-     Strong verbal and written communication skills as well as exemplary presentation skills

-    Excellent computer skills to include proficiency in MS Office

-    Must possess proficiency with HTML programming language and knowledge of java

-  Web content management a plus

-    Strong aptitude for problem solving, decision making, and active learning skills

-    Must have strong work ethic, sense of urgency and a high energy level

-    Self-starter: hands-on, bias for action, results oriented

-    Ability to work effectively to meet deadlines

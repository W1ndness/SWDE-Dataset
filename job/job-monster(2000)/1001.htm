SAP Specialist - FI/CO

About the Job

BSH Home Appliances Corporation


Operating in the United States under the brands, Bosch, Thermador and Gaggenau, BSH Home Appliances Corporation is highly regarded as a market leader for innovation in home appliance technology.  As an international group with over 12.2 billion in annual sales, we continually strive to engage our dedicated customers around the world through intelligent engineering, streamlined design and a distinct ease of use.


Our Huntington Beach, CA headquarters is currently seeking applicants for:


SAP Specialist – FI/CO


We are seeking an IT professional to provide first and second level technical SAP support for all BSH-NA production plants, in the areas of Financial Accounting and Controlling.  Work closely with the Accounting/Finance and Controlling departments to support current functionality, process improvements, and new projects.  Provide user training for users of SAP.


 


 


Responsibilities:


 


      Support of the following areas in SAP:


 


 


  • General Ledger Accounting

  • Account Payable

  • Fixed Assets

  • Travel Management

  • Cash Management

  • Cost Element Accounting

  • Cost Center Accounting

  • Profit Center Accounting

  • Product Cost Controlling

 


      Knowledge:


 


  • Accounting Principles

  • Accounting Systems

  • Financial Reporting

  • Financial Reconciliation

  • Financial Closing

  • FI integrations with other SAP modules (primarily CO, PP and MM modules)

  • SAP FI/CO Module knowledge

  • SAP User Exits

  • Business Transaction Events/Open FI

  • SAP Report Painter

  • SAP Queries

  • Purchase to Pay Process

  • Sales and Use Tax

  • Validations and Substitutions

  • Technical understanding of SAP (programs, function modules, tables, ABAP code, user exits, etc.)

 


 


Requirements:


 


 


  • B.S. in Computer Science, Accounting, or related field or 5-10 years of equivalent IT experience.

  • 3+ years working with SAP FI and CO modules

  • Strong English oral and written communication skills.

  • Willingness to work occasionally outside of normal business hours.

  • Self-motivated with the ability to prioritize, meet deadlines, and manage changing priorities.

  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills.

  • A results oriented individual who thrives working in a fast paced environment.

  • Proven ability to be flexible and work hard, both independently and in a team environment.

  • Excellent interpersonal skills, computer skills, and organizational skills.

  • Good business acumen.

  • Good documentation skills.

  • Ability to work with and apply defined methodologies.

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

  • Bi-monthly travel to New Bern NC.

 


Benefits:


 


We offer a comprehensive benefits package including paid vacation, holidays, medical, dental, vision, life insurance, disability insurance, educational assistance and 401(k) plan.

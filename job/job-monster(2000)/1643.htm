About the Job

FalconStor Software, Inc. (NASDAQ: FALC), #5 in Forbes 2008 list of America´s Fastest-Growing Technology Companies, is the market leader in disk-based data protection. We deliver proven, comprehensive data protection solutions that facilitate the continuous availability of business-critical data with speed, integrity, and simplicity. Our TOTALLY Open™ technology solutions, built upon the award-winning IPStor® platform, include the industry leading Virtual Tape Library (VTL) with Deduplication, Continuous Data Protector™ (CDP), Network Storage Server (NSS), and Replication option for disaster recovery and remote office protection. Our products are available from major OEMs and solution providers and are deployed by thousands of customers worldwide, from small businesses to Fortune 1000 enterprises. Founded in 2000, FalconStor is headquartered in Melville, NY, with offices throughout Europe and the Asia Pacific regions including Paris, Tokyo and Taiwan.


UI ENGINEER
SUMMARY

We are looking for UI Engineer who will be instrumental in building http based management console to FalconStor Storage products, leveraging the latest and greatest tools as well as developing flexible and usability-friendly complex reports from scratch. You’ll be responsible for creating the look and feel for our web interfaces, as well as defining the next generation of our visual language.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

-          Build performance minded front end web applications for storage products

-          Work closely with various groups for requirement analysis and designing

-          Work closely with back end and database engineers to create a working end-to-end solution

-          Work with QA to ensure the code delivered is of high quality

QUALIFICATIONS

Skills

-          Energetic, inspired, dedicated, passionate about software development

-          A self starter who has the ability and willingness to learn new material quickly and perform in a fast paced environment

-          A great team player, sociable, with good communication skills

-          Ability and willingness to take ownership of projects, and help drive them to effective implementation

-          Attention to detail

Knowledge & Experience

-          5+ years of experience in the design and implementation of web front ends in a complex enterprise environment

-          Expert knowledge of and current hands on PHP experience.

-          Expert knowledge of JavaScript and HTML.

-          Knowledge of CSS.

-          Knowledge of browser/platform compatibility issues

-          Knowledge of javascript libraries, such as JQuery or Prototype, is highly desirable.

-          Knowledge of Java

-          Comfortable in a Linux environment and Apache web server

-          BS in Computer Science or equivalent


Software Engineer

SOFTWARE ENGINEER

SUMMARY

We are looking for well-rounded developer with experience in Linux development, including familiarity with Linux based application servers. This person will be responsible for developing data management applications and integrating existing products into FalconStor storage appliances.

 

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

-          Modify existing applications to integrate with FalconStor appliances, taking advantage of Linux as well as virtualization platforms (e.g. VMware, Xen, etc.)

-          Participate in the development of new Linux based data management applications and low-level systems software

-          Work with QA to ensure the code delivered is of high quality.

 

QUALIFICATIONS

Skills

-          Excellent Linux programming skills

-          Excellent communication skills.

Knowledge & Experience

-          Minimum of 5+ years experience in a Linux/Unix environment, including:

o   Multi-threaded system level development

o   Familiarity with Linux  CLI

o   Shell scripting, PHP, Perl

-          Experience with low-level development on Linux platform including device driver, file system, or virtualization development

-          Experience with data and/or storage management (including tiered storage and NAS), policy-based content classification and categorization, and caching

-          Must have proven CIFS or NFS file system development knowledge

-          Experience with RDBMS/MySQL applications

-          Experience with TCP/IP networking, socket development

-          5+ years of hands on experience in C or C++

-          BS/MS degree in Computer Science or equivalent

-          Demonstrated ability to have completed multiple moderately complex technical tasks

-          Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment


The ideal candidate must have experience within the storage industry and at least five years of job related experience and must be an independent, motivated self-starter able to present technical information in a strong compelling manner.

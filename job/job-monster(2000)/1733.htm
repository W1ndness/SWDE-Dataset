Software Architect

About the Job

Overview


The Software Architect provides technical leadership throughout the lifecycle of a project, with specific focus on large scale website redesign solutions. This individual is responsible for leading the design, user interface, and technology integration of projects. The successful candidate will become a trusted partner to the internal team and will interface with client technology and management teams. This individual is a key contributor to the overall proposal and architecture, and a driver of the implementation of practical technical solutions that are in accordance with client business requirements.


Responsibilities 

  • Architect solutions to our client’s digital marketing problems taking a lead role in the technical requirements definition and the technical design and architecture processes
  • Lead the design and development of software and infrastructure for robust websites and web applications
  • Translate client’s business requirements into systems design 
  • Work closely with a cross-capability staff from Account, Creative, and other groups on all aspects of digital projects
  • Engage with clients’ technical staff to ensure solutions integrate smoothly with existing infrastructure and standards.
  • Own the successful delivery of our projects on-time and within scope
  • Use effective listening skills to react to and resolve issues, and identify opportunities


Qualifications/ Skills

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Minimum 6 years of experience in software development, enterprise architecture, systems integration, and custom and package implementation
  • Ability to architect solutions with multiple logical and physical tiers using design patterns and best practices
  • Expert, hands-on knowledge of Microsoft technologies: .NET, ASP.NET, C#, WCF, WWF, MVC, LINQ
  • Strong relational database background and knowledge of Microsoft SQL Server, T-SQL, and writing complex stored procedures
  • Proficiency in Web development technologies: HTML, XHTML, CSS, XML, JavaScript, AJAX, Web Services, SOAP, REST, JSON
  • Strong background in Web services, SOA, and 3rd party data integration
  • Ability to grow and change with existing and emerging technologies
  • Solid written and verbal communications skills
  • Capacity to identify creative solutions to difficult problems
  • Positive and enthusiastic attitude
  • Experience with content management systems, application servers, portal frameworks, CRM, and ecommerce servers
  • Experience with mobile site and application development
  • Knowledge of other development platforms (e.g. Java, LAMP)
  • Understanding of Web Analytics solutions such as Omniture Site Catalyst, Google Analytics, Webtrends, or Coremetrics
  • Experience at an agency/consultancy
  • Microsoft software developer or architect certification

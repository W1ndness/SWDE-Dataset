PRINCIPAL WEB UI ENGINEER - ART.COM

About the Job

Art.com Inc. is the worldwide leading art and wall décor company; our workplace is exciting, stimulating and as vibrantly diverse as the art we sell.

 

Founded in 1998, Art.com has served over 10 million customers in over 200 countries around the world.  Our passion for what we do has helped us to be consistently profitable, with over 600 employees worldwide and facilities in the U.S. and Europe.

 

You may already know us as “The World’s Largest Print and Poster Store.” There are two core brands in our corporate portfolio, Art.com & AllPosters.com, and with a goal to change the way people buy and discover art online.

 

We value innovation, creativity and results from our employees. You can expect challenging, rewarding work, competitive salaries, great benefits, and tremendous opportunities for career growth.

We have some serious growth plans and if you have the same in your career plan, we’d love to talk with you.

PRINCIPAL WEB UI ENGINEER

If you are a talented engineer looking to contribute to an established, profitable, high-growth,  pre-IPO, eCommerce company reaching a world-wide customer base, this opportunity is for you.

We are building the technology that is transforming the way art is experienced, shared, and purchased. By assembling the world’s largest collection of art images and prints we are bringing customers, artists, publishers, and decorators together to create a new social and commercial online experience.

You will join a team that is using the latest Web 2.0 and Web2 frameworks, creating new online destinations and user experience paradigms to change the way the world discovers, designs, visualizes, and purchases art online.

As the Principal Web UI Engineer in our core engineering team, you will play a key role in the definition, design, and implementation of our platform, web sites, and eCommerce business applications. You will be part of a passionate, high-energy team creating compelling, new and innovative online paradigms.

The ideal candidate must be extremely proficient and demonstrate a track record in leading the design, development, and production of modern, high-traffic and high-volume Web sites. 

Working in a demanding, agile environment, you will collaborate daily with product and project managers, creative and user experience designers, architects, and peer software and production engineers.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·  Contribute new and unique ideas to the UI design of a Rich Internet Application

·  Create standards-compliant, cross-browser-compatible representations of visual designs  with pixel-level accuracy and attention to detail

·  Work with User Experience and Engineering teams to define, maintain, implement, and  enforce style guides, standards, reusable templates, and best practices for client-side coding

·  Monitor best practices, trends and new technologies, and evaluate for possible  adoption

·  Assist development teams with client-side coding and bug-fixing as needed

·  Work closely with other Web Developers, Flex Developers, QA, product  management and  creative/user experience teams to resolve problems

·  Mentor other mid-level Web developers on RIA best practices and other matters as required

REQUIREMENTS:

·  7+ years experience in web application development

·  Advanced sense of User Experience when creating features and widgets

·  Must have experience with dynamic, real-time, content-rich, customer-facing Web sites 

·  7+ years with JavaScript and AJAX, JQuery and JSON interaction

·  Advanced knowledge and experience in DHTML, CSS and  XML, XHTML

·  Experience with RESTful Web Services

·  Must have experience in Microsoft .Net and/or J2EE/LAMP

·  Understanding of web-caching techniques

·  Familiarity with frameworks like  UIZE, Scriptacolus, Prototype, MagicZoom, or other  dynamic Web site frameworks

·  Experience with eCommerce solutions, SEO fundamentals, and PCI compliance

·  Experience with troubleshooting Web site and JavaScript performance

·  Proven track record of identifying and championing new technologies (HTML5) that  enhance  the end-user experience, software quality, and developer productivity

·  Strong skills in supporting CSS across major browsers

·  Strong experience in one or more MVC architectures

·  Creative UI design and strong sense of usability

·  BS in Engineering, IT, or Computer Science or equivalent required (MS  preferred)

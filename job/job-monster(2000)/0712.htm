About ICF International:
ICF International (NASDAQ:ICFI) partners with government and commercial clients to deliver professional services and technology solutions in the energy and climate change; environment and infrastructure; health, human services, and social programs; and homeland security and defense markets. The firm combines passion for its work with industry expertise and innovative analytics to produce compelling results throughout the entire program life cycle, from research and analysis through implementation and improvement. Since 1969, ICF has been serving government at all levels, major corporations, and multilateral institutions. More than 3,500 employees serve these clients worldwide. ICF's Web site is www.icfi.com.
 
Job Description:
 

ICF Jacob & Sundstrom is seeking a Unix Systems Administrator to join one of our client support teams currently located in Northern Virginia with plans to move to Quantico, Virginia mid-year 2011. You will be joining an experienced ICF/JASI team that is responsible for the operating system and network administration of government systems critical to our national security.
 
The candidate will provide Unix Systems Administration support for a large-scale multi-shift production data center. The operating environment utilizes Sun hardware platforms, clustered for high availability; Veritas File System and Volume Manager for disk management; Veritas Netbackup Data Center and Tivoli Storage Manager for backup and recovery support. The computing environment also includes a virtualized implementation of Solaris on Intel-based platforms



Key Responsibilities:
  • Perform software installation and maintenance
  • Perform file system and space management 
  • Support backup and recovery operations 
  • Perform problem diagnosis and re mediation 
  • Perform tasks related to the certification and accreditation of all systems

 


Basic Qualifications: 


  • U.S. Citizenship.
  • Active high level government security clearance.
  • A Bachelor's degree or equivalent total 10 years working experience.
  • 6 years of working Unix experience.

Professional Skills:
  • Excellent verbal, interpersonal and written communication skills
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving and decision making capabilities
  • Team player with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment

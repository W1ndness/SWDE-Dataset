About the Job

 

Company:                                              Cygnus Business Media

Location:                                               Multiple locations with headquarters in Fort Atkinson, WI and strong Technology teams in MD and WI

Company Background:  Cygnus Business Media is one of the nation’s leading business-to-business multi-platform media companies, with expertise in print and digital publishing, as well as event management.  Cygnus delivers content to 14 major vertical markets, including emergency services, technology, aviation, and contracting industries.  Its magazines mainly rank number one or two in their categories and have 1.5 million subscribers; its robust Web sites attract 2 million unique visitors monthly.  Cygnus Exhibitions’ nearly three dozen events attract tens of thousands of attendees annually. 

 

A New Cygnus Business Media

Cygnus Business Media is committed to transforming its business and will dedicate the needed resources to achieve success.    The company recently emerged from a pre-packaged bankruptcy with a rational capital structure, a new, experienced Board of Directors, CEO, CFO and EVP of Digital, and a strong shareholder group led by GE Commercial Finance and Goldman Sachs. The Company feels it has an advantage in having been the first among its principal competitors to restructure its balance sheet to reflect the new operating environment. 

 

Job Description:

 

 

WEBSITE MANAGER

 

 

Cygnus Business Media, www.cygnusb2b.com, a very dynamic, growth-oriented leader in business-to-business media is currently recruiting for a Website Manager.

The Website Manager will work with sales, editorial and digital associates to implement the goals and facilitate the changes of internal corporate websites.

Job Requirements

-       Maintain web site code and basic functionality

-       Process web site change requests

-       Schedule site advertising within ad management program

-       Troubleshoot/ issue resolution

-       Implement new site features

-       E-blast/e-newsletter builds/sends

-       Work with design department to implement design needs

-       Work with Digital Intelligence department to collect and analyze report data

-       Work with Digital Intelligence department on tactical SEO implementation

-       Provide technology support through new product initiatives

-       Assist with special projects, as needed

 

Core Competencies

Communication

-          Resolve and/or escalate issues in a timely fashion

-          Understand how to communicate difficult information tactfully

-          Effectively communicate within a team atmosphere

 

Technical Understanding

-          Possess general understanding in the areas of web development, database design, and web design

-          Understand internet principles and standards

-          Thorough knowledge of web languages/concepts

 

Professional Qualities

Teamwork:

-          Consistently acknowledge and appreciate each team member’s contributions

-          Motivated to work as a team player

-          Effectively deal with team conflict and communication problems

 

Client Management:

-          Manage day-to-day internal client interaction

-          Set and manage internal clients day-to-day expectations

-          Communicate effectively with internal clients

-          Continually seek opportunities to increase customer satisfaction

 

Qualifications:

-       Experience in managing websites with varying degree of content offerings

-       Strongly versed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (jQuery, XML, JSON, and AJAX a plus)

-       Working knowledge of web-centric programming languages (direct experience with PHP a plus)

-       General understanding of SQL tables and queries and the relationship between databases and websites

-       Experience working with a CMS

-       A strong passion for learning new technologies and expanding skill sets

-       Ability to translate technical terms and communicate with people of varying

-       technical knowledge is a must

-       Ability to work independently and deliver on deadlines

-       Ability to work with a team

-       Ability to work under shifting priorities and a fast paced environment

-       Strong written and verbal communication skills

About the Job

Business Analyst- Direct

 

The Direct Business Analyst will provide direction, guidance and judgment on new project initiatives across our multi channel selling environment focused on being a liaison between Direct Merchandising and Planning, Brand Creative, Direct operations and IT for new initiatives in support of our customer facing ecommerce systems. The position requires an understanding of Direct Merchandising & Planning, Brand Creative, and Direct Marketing, a strong understanding of ecommerce site usability and design and a solid technical understanding of online customer interfaces.

Duties and Responsibilities:

Analysis and Solution Definition

 

·         Quickly identifies and understands business issues and opportunities

·         Assists business partners in identifying and prioritizing new capabilities

·         Identifies system, process, and organizational strengths and weaknesses and suggests areas of improvement.

·         Defines and documents project scope, business requirements, software specifications, business processes and recommendations related to proposed solution.

·         Defines business Use Cases and associated high level test scenarios

·         Develops functional specifications and system design specifications for new capabilities.

·         Identify, define and deliver user training

 

Strategic Planning

 

·         Monitor and understand leading practices and industry trends and make recommendations to business partners as appropriate

·         Work with business partners to define the vision for future state capabilities (processes/technology/organization)

·         Work with business and IT leadership to develop the strategy to achieve the vision

·         Translate the strategy into projects and programs on the IT Strategic Roadmap

 

Technical Recommendation and Testing

 

·         Works with business partners to identify and manage required changes.

·         Communicates needed changes to development team.

·         Assists in the creation of project estimates

·         Participates in QA testing efforts

·         Leads User Acceptance Testing efforts

 

Project Execution

 

·         Understand and assist in the creation of project business cases

·         Assists PMs in management of project deadlines and schedules.

·         Communicates and applies project standards.

·         Manages deliverables and business partner participation in accordance with project schedule.

·         Consistently delivers high-quality services to the business.

·         Understands the drivers of running an on time and on budget project.

 

Communication

 

·         Leads project team and business requirement/scoping meetings.

·         Delivers informative, well-organized scope documents and business requirements.

·         Understands how to communicate effectively with both business partners and the development team.

·         Organizes meetings and deliverables to make the most efficient use of business partner and development team members’ time.

 

Technical Understanding

 

·         Possesses strong understanding of application development and system design.

·         Understands system limitations and boundaries and applies these in solution definition.

·         Identifies which components of a new capability are best defined as process or system based

 

Problem Solving

 

·         Identifies and addresses critical issues with ease.

·         Identifies the right person(s) to provide the solution to critical issues

·         Manages and coordinates varying points of view to solve a problem

·         Exhibits confidence and an extensive knowledge of emerging industry practices when solving business problems.

 

Education and Experience:

o        Hands on experience in Direct user experience analysis, design, development projects

o        Experience identifying site usability opportunities and coordinating creative, IT and operational teams in defining opportunities

o        Successful track record in project delivery and benefits realization

o        +1 years experience in multi-channel product management/business analysis roles

o        +3 years working for a Retail Apparel company

o        +4 years of experience in a product management/business analysis role

o        BA or BS in related Retail or Technical competency or higher

o        Fine arts background a plus

o        Business Analyst or Project Management Professional Certifications a plus

o        Experience developing and/or support IT Strategic Planning

o        Experience with Blue Martini/Escalate application implementation

o        Experience with Flash, Ajax, javascript development projects

o        Ecommerce operational experience (website publishes)

o        Experience managing Creative and IT personnel in successful project delivery

o        Experience researching market trends, best practices and competitive analysis in online experience

Skills:

 

o        Effective communication - communicates effectively and at the right level based on the audience

o        Problem Solving - Defines business solutions with a diverse set of input for problems that may not be clearly defined

o        Cross Functional Coordination - Coordinates input and participation from diverse set of business stakeholders

o        Business Relationships - Grows and strengthens relationships with business partners

o        Professional Growth – Continuously seeks opportunities to expand area of expertise

User Experience Research Manager

About the Job

Advantis Global has an outstanding permanent opportunity available for an User Experience Manager.  This position can be based in San Francisco, CA or Minneapolis, MN

This person will drive research projects and initiatives for a group of 3+ user experience researchers and help create the next-generation of interactive experiences for our customers: best in class transactions and tools that will offer contextual guidance, preemptive support and recommendations.

You will be a leader in helping teams understand how to meet and anticipate the needs of customers, and be adept at setting research directions and goals, as well as translating research results into conclusions to set the direction for design. You will work in conjunction with the Interaction Design Manager to create new products and overhaul existing ones in accordance with brand guidelines while maximizing the user experience.

 

Functions and Responsibilities:

 

  • Have a passion for improving the user experience.
  • Bring knowledge of user research methods, devise research strategies and create a research plan that addresses the needs of the UX leads.
  • Oversee all UXR projects from planning to completion: setting priorities, managing budgets and inputs from Platform and Program Management.
  • Set high level research goals and lead user research efforts on mission-critical initiatives.

·         Coordinate UXR activities with other key departments, ensuring accurate and highly professional communications and collaboration at all times.

·         Introduce user-centric processes in a very fast-paced environment.

·         Define UX performance metrics (error rate, abandon rate, average number of clicks, perceived ease of use, etc.) for strategic projects in order to measure its success.

·         Ensuring UX solutions comply with guidelines and principles.

·         Serve as a steward for team documentation and guidelines. Disseminate best practices across the UX organization.

·         Be a key catalyst in driving a culture of teamwork and innovation centered around the needs of our customers.

·         Managing and mentoring UX researchers; writing performance reviews; determining compensation and career growth.

 

 

Requirements:

 

  • College and/or advanced degree(s) in Computer Science, HCI, Cognitive Science, Cognitive Psychology, Anthropology, Information Science or related field.
  • Must have a minimum of 7 - 10 years of proven, hands-on experience as a lead usability analyst and/or user-experience researcher on significant web-related projects.
  • Thorough knowledge of user research methods, including data analysis, results interpretation, inferential statistics, site visits, interviews, card sorts, focus groups, user flows, user profiling and tasks analysis; has demonstrated ability to adapt the appropriate method to specific design questions.
  • Able to effectively communicate, coordinate and integrate research efforts and data from other research organizations (marketing, data analytics, etc.) to create coherent understanding of our users.
  • Proven experience creating and executing research plans in response to overall product strategy.
  • Excellent management skills to provide leadership skills and mentoring career growth for researchers.

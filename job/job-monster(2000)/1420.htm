Project Manager

About the Job

 

Reporting to the company’s VP, Project & Product Management the Project Manager will be responsible for enabling successful project delivery through disciplined and accepted project management processes. The project manager will work closely with all the associated business areas to ensure projects successfully achieve their objectives on schedule. The candidate would be expected to manage multiple projects at the same time. The candidate will participate as needed in leadership or as part of a project team to develop business workflows, research and develop solutions, specify requirements, and manage relationships with business teams and IT. The project manager will also be responsible for managing the product life cycle and developing process designs and methodologies and ensuring continuous improvements. 

 

ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES include the following.  Other work-related duties may be assigned.

 

  • The position is expected to function with considerable independence while operating within a team and project structure. Leads and provides expert technical leadership to highly visible, sensitive and multi faceted projects. Sets strategic direction of projects.

 

  • Manage project plan and other projects as assigned in terms of scope, schedule, cost and quality including resource requirements and estimates. Team with internal IT and Operations Project Managers to manage technical project into overall Project Implementation plan.

 

  • Manage initiatives related to Medicare and Medicaid implementations and provide solid documentation of all key deliverables and due dates. Responsible for organizing assigned work, managing the work to completion, and managing relationships required to accomplish the work. Manage the day-to-day details of the project including the plan, schedule, resources, scope, budget and risks.

 

  • Liaise between business units and IT units to provide support, resolve problems, identify key deliverables and escalate to management as appropriate. Track progress and monitor all creative, business and technical development activities to ascertain and control progress.

 

  • Report on project status of due dates met and deliverables pending the next due dates. Active problem solving in engagement of project managers in solution decision making are required. Monitors projects form initiation through delivery and monitors for continuous improvement. Completes cost/benefit analysis.

  • Establishes team membership and negotiates time commitments and resource allocation. Provides staff leadership to multi disciplinary teams, as well as manages work of outside consultants. Motivates team members and facilitates team meetings.

 

  • Provides guidance to team on performance and productivity issues. Actively participates or may lead in analyzing data and developing recommendations and action plans.

 

  • Accountable for the development of project documentation for senior executives and other key clients to facilitate sharing of project outcomes and best practices. Makes formal presentations to various senior level audiences.

 

  • Adheres to applicable federal, state and local laws and regulations, accreditation and licenser requirements (if applicable), and SSC's policies and procedures.

 

  • Experience –– Experience in health care business or related field, plus 5-7 years progressive experience leading project teams, preferably in health care and 3-5 years experience working on IT or IS related projects is required. The ideal candidate must have a strong analytical background with report development experience, database knowledge, and ability to translate data into meaningful information into report or presentation formats. Ability to assemble cross functional project teams and leading them through concept development and business case creation through execution. Ability to communicate at all levels within the organization, build consensus and influence others is essential. Candidates must have strong analytical and operations skills. Project management certification (PMI, six sigma), industry experience are a plus.

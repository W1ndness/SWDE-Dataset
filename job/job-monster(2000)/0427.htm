About the Job

SharePoint Architect

Are you looking for a rewarding consulting career supporting our nation’s top priorities?  INTEGRITYOne Partners (IOP) is a professional services firm specializing in business consulting and technology solutions primarily to the U.S. Federal Government.  Recently selected as one of the top five small consulting firms to work for in the nation by Consulting Magazine, IOP is recognized as a leader in the consulting industry and public service sector.  At IOP, we endeavor to create an environment where intelligent and talented people at various experience levels thrive.  While we value experience and education in candidates, we also look beyond just the skill set and try to find individuals with the right mix of experience, skill, values and, most importantly, potential and desire to perform at high levels.

 

We have a new opportunity for a talented SharePoint Architect to join our team.  Successful candidate will:

  • Advanced knowledge of creating applications to run at Optimal performance with high availability and minimal maintenance,
  • Advanced knowledge of authentication and authorization in a shared multi-tenancy environment
  • Act as liason between engineering and development team
  • Experience architecting,  implementating and configure Search capabilities within SharePoint (e.g., FAST, Google appliance, etc.)
  • Will develop multiple advanced custom solutions for Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007/10 (MOSS)
  • Advance knowledge, with skill and ability using the SharePoint Object Model, .NET Framework, C#.NET, VB.NET, ADO.NET, ASP.NET, XML, XSLT, JavaScript, T-SQL, Visual Studio, Microsoft Visio, Microsoft Project, to develop workflows, web features, web parts, web services, Business Data Catalog definitions, custom site definitions, advanced customizations and complete custom solutions for MOSS and WSS
  • Prepare technical specifications, design documentation, code walk-through, test plans, and documentation of completed code.
  • Individual should be a fast learner of new technologies, be a creative thinker, share skills and information freely.

To be successful as a SharePoint Developer with IOP, candidates must have the following skills and experience:

  • This position requires candidates have the ability to obtain and maintain a DHS Secret Clearance which requires Citizenship in the United States and you will be subject to a government background investigation
  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • Understanding of OO programming Concepts, MVC, software design patterns
  • 4+ years experience using the SharePoint Object Model, .NET Framework, C#.NET, VB.NET, ADO.NET, ASP.NET, HTML, XML, XSLT, JavaScript, AJAX, SOAP and T-SQL
  • 2+ years experience using Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007 (MOSS) and Windows SharePoint Services 3.0 (WSS)
  • 2+ years experience developing features, web parts, workflows, web services, Business Data Catalog definitions, advanced customizations and complete custom solutions for MOSS and WSS
  • Proficient in the .Net framework, C#, ASP.NET using Visual Studio and SharePoint Designer
  • Hands on experience with the Personalization components of MOSS
  • Hands on experience with BDC, Excel Services, Forms Services, Performance Point Services, KPI, and InfoPath
  • Strong experience packaging and deploying SharePoint solutions
  • 5+ years experience performing as a project team leader
  • Experience tasking others
  • Experience with SDLC
  • Must be creative and flexible in order to respond quickly and positively to shifting demands and opportunities; ability to work under tight deadlines and handle multiple, detailed tasks

Demonstrated skills in the following while not required are desired:

  • SharePoint 2007 or Windows SharePoint Services Development certification
  • MCSD (Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer) is a strong plus

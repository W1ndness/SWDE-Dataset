Senior Programmer

About the Job

We are a multi-million dollar retail, internet, and catalog powerhouse with strength and stability rivaled only by the exceptional products we sell.  Our motivation to become the dominant force in serving our customers’ tool and equipment needs is driven by innovative products, out-standing customer service, growing sales and a highly-motivated work force. For more than 25 years, Northern Tool + Equipment has been the ultimate resource for contractors, automotive shops, grounds maintenance professionals, homeowners and serious do-it-yourselfers. 

 

Join our expert workforce as a Senior Programmer at our Corporate Headquarters in Burnsville, MN where some of our core values include empowering the individual, acting with integrity and having fun.

 
 

Major Tasks & Responsibilities

 

*Works closely with business champions to analyze business needs and develop and implement the written specifications for all retail systems enhancements.

 

*Designs and revises system specifications and programs technical solutions for retail system software.

 

*Collaborates, develops and implements solutions to continually improve retail operations.  

 

*Works closely with the Retail Systems Manager to manage and maintain relationships with retail system software vendors.

 

*Trains end-users, responds to their questions and brings user problems to resolution. 

 

*Reviews software enhancements (either written internally or externally) to ensure changes are properly tested and meet the stated requirements before the enhancement is implemented.  Assists with system, testing and programming language questions as required. 

 

*Develops, maintains and updates system auditing procedures to protect data integrity and reliability. 

 

*Evaluates, identifies and recommends improvements in data processing operations and services as appropriate.

 

*Provides on-call, after-hours support on a rotating basis.

 

*Keeps manager informed of important developments, potential problems and related information necessary for effective management.  Coordinates/communicates plans and activities with others to ensure a coordinated work effort and team approach.

 

Requirements

 

*High school education or equivalent, plus 1-3 years of training in computer programming or related area.

*1-3 years of related programming experience.

*Complete knowledge of hardware and program language.

*Must possess strong organizational skills and attention to detail.

*Solid experience in system design and analysis. 

*Demonstrated ability to keep up-to-date on new software/hardware technology and other advancements relating to the state of the art.

*Demonstrated ability to effectively develop, write and test programs.

*Ability to effectively work with operators, end users and others.

*Experience with AS/400 (iSeries) Application Development Skills: RPG 400, RPG ILE, CL, SQL, Lotus Notes Development, Trusted Link ED, PHP and Java Script. SAS, Encore POS system, Aldon BRMS, ROBOT and Hawkey experience preferred.

*Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing, with a wide variety of customers, Company personnel, and others.

 

 

We offer competitive pay, an excellent benefits package and opportunities for growth in a team environment.  If interested and qualified, please submit your resume.

 

For more information on Northern Tool + Equipment, visit: www.northerntool.com

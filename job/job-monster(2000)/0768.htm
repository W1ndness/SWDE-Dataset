Senior Business Analyst

About the Job

 

An opportunity to get onboard with a successful company on the leading edge of the growth curve is just around the corner. 

Infinite Computing Systems provides advanced IT consulting, custom software application development, and staff augmentation to develop enterprise solutions for our clients in .NET, Java and mainframe environments. 

We are seeking a Sr. Business Analyst to be responsible for the elicitation and development of requirements and other key information for project delivery. Key responsibilities include establishing and maintaining requirements documentation standards, serving as requirements owner for multiple project development teams, writing and editing requirements documents, and ensuring that requirements are completed and kept up to date. The Senior Business Analyst may also prepare related documents, such as functional specifications and end-user documentation, and assist in the creation and execution of test plans.  This is a senior level position and is responsible for establishing and maintaining the requirements management processes followed by other Business Analysts.

 

Essential Duties and responsibilities
  • Participates in the development of project plans to ensure that all technical writing activities and dependencies are properly reflected in project plans.
  • Drives the facilitation, elicitation and documentation of technical requirements.
  • Works with business owners and project teams to identify and quantify the depth of the written deliverables (e.g., help files, user manuals, etc.) required by the project.
  • Clarifies the business jargon or terminology to a level that readers easily understand given their context to the project.
  • Develops and publishes all assigned user documentation.
  • Applies documented standards and practices for requirements analysis.  Assists the identification, implementation and maintenance of standards and best practices as they relate to the competency area of technical writing and requirements analysis.
  • Assists with the development of test plans and the execution of test plans.  Ensures that requirements are adequately traced through testing phases.
  • Addresses and resolves defects identified during reviews and testing.
  • Follows the documentation, migration and deployment standards defined in the configuration management approach for the project.
  • Accurately reports completion status and actual hours worked against the project work plan.
  • Communicates progress, issues and risks to the project team and management as they are determined/discovered throughout the lifecycle of the project.
  • Assists Infinite Project division management with the establishment and oversight of the requirements management capabilities of the organization’s business analysis center of excellence.  Facilitates knowledge-sharing and the development of a sense of community within the business analysis staff.
  • Drives the development of departmental standards, policies and procedures for requirements management.  Advocates the use of best practices on projects, and coaches business analysts in their use.

Maintains an active understanding of current issues, trends and tools affecting requirements analysis.  Evaluate, recommend, and assist in implementing new tools for managing the requirements analysis process.

 

Other Duties

·          Perform other duties as assigned and required by management.

 

Job Qualifications

Education: 

·         BS or MS in Computer Science or related technology field

·         Formal training in structured software development processes and techniques

·         Professional accreditation in business analysis, requirements analysis or business process engineering is desirable

Experience: 

·         Minimum 5 years experience in requirements/business analysis documentation or an equivalent field of related experience is required

·         Experience with documentation and source code repository tools such as MS SharePoint is required

·         Experience with formal software development processes/procedures, and configuration management is required

·         Prior experience in managing requirements in an onsite/offshore engagement model is highly desirable.

Other Knowledge, Skills, Abilities or Certifications: 

·         Exceptional written and verbal communication skills

·         A strong ability to quickly understand and articulate technical information and product concepts

·         Thorough understanding of new product development and software development lifecycles

·         Thorough understanding of requirements process, practices and methodologies

·         Working knowledge of Use Case Analysis and product requirements documentation

·         Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

·         Must be able to work independently and prioritize work effectively, and function as an effective team member in a local and virtual team development environment

 

TraVEl requirements

·         Occasional travel to client sites – no more than 25%

 

 

This position is located in Cedar Rapids, Iowa and physical relocation is required.  Infinite Computing Systems, Inc. provides an excellent benefit package including health, dental, vision, STD, LTD, PTO and 401(k).  If you are interested in this opportunity to be a part of our company’s expansion efforts, please send an updated resume and cover letter to Nikole Tutton, ntutton@infinite-usa.com

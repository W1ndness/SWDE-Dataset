Leadership and Equity Position

Director, Technology Product Support and Logistics

About the Job


We are a young and rapidly expanding Healthcare Technology firm based in New York City in need of a seasoned Professional with Telecommunication Product experience and a Strong Logistics, Inventory Management and Quality Assurance background.
 
We are quickly becoming a leader in one of the hottest and fastest growing industries in the country and are searching for a Hands-On ‘Director of Product Support and Logistics “to join our team.
 
The successful candidate will have expertise in the IT / Telecommunication Product, Service and Support Lifecycle, (Design, Build, Test,  Deploy,  Support)  National Logistics Management,  Asset Management,  Vendor Management, QA,  Product Testing, Repair and Refurbishment.
 
The Director of Product Support and Logistics must be strategic thinking, innovative and “Hands-On” in approach, while also driving a methodical and process oriented environment with a relentless passion for quality and customer service. 


Description

Works closely with the executive team to determine logistics, procurement and product   strategies, needs assessments, performance reviews, planning, business alignment and cost/benefit analysis; identifies and   evaluates state-of-the-art and emerging technologies;  establishes technical specifications, implementation, production,   quality, and performance standards; Contributes information and analysis to organizational strategic plans and reviews.

Serves as a Subject Matter Expert and manages all aspects of the department · 

Plans, manages and oversees the day-to-day activities of our product support and logistics teams and partners across the US.
 
Monitors performance of individuals and departmental objectives, and develops efficient processes and procedures to ensure continuous improvement and the delivery of world class products.
 
Measures, Evaluates Product Performance in Production and Repair, Analyzes, Trends, Reports and makes Recommends for improvement
 
Manages our Manufacturer, Carriers,  and Partner relationships around the globe for telecommunication access points, biometric peripherals and equipment
  
Models, Forecast and Managers inventory, logistics and workflow throughout  the U.S. according to business and service needs to ensure the optimum balance  of meeting SLA’s, the customer's experience and profitability·           
 
Develops workflows, company wide documentation, wiki’s and knowledge base to help support current and new services and products
 
Tracks, analyzes, trends and reports Service Level performance against established objectives
 
Assists in the implementation of new technology, tools, products and processes


Partners with our Clinical and Sales teams to develop and procure products that enhance our product and service portfolio·   

Supports our field delivery teams in the implementation, repair and testing of our end-user equipment and peripherals.
 
Participates in Customer and Partner meetings and conference calls
 
Develops and Maintains Contingency, Disaster Recovery, Risk Mitigation and Business Continuity Programs
 
Demonstrates knowledge of laws, regulations and policies and ensures such compliance
 
Maintains professional and technical knowledge by tracking emerging trends in logistics, technology management, Healthcare; attends educational workshops; reviews professional publications; establishing personal and professional networks; benchmarks state-of-the-art practices and industry standards; participates in professional organizations


 

Qualifications and Education
 
1. Bachelor's degree in business administration, technology or related field preferred
2. 5 or more years of experience within a similar role and environment
3. 4 Years of Management/Leadership experience
4. Telecommunication, Information Technology, Desktop  Experience
5. Strong Business Acumen and ability to manage a budget
6.  Experience optimizing Process and Work Stream (procurement to delivery) 
7.  Excellent Lab, QA, Technical skills and Machine to Machine technology experience
8.  Experience working with Asset management and ERP systems
9.  Strong PC, Desktop applications, telephony, video, multimedia and IP technology
10.Superior communication and presentation skills a must

Additional Preferences
11. Participation in the start-up operations or experience with an early stage company
12. Self starter, with sound judgment, entrepreneurial spirit and strong work ethic
13. Superior motivator with a relentless focus for quality and operational efficiency
14. Wireless (carrier, Bluetooth) and Wireline equipment experience
15. Product Development Experience
16. Knowledge of Healthcare Industry and Biometric Peripherals
17. ITIL certified a plus

Description

Everything Channel (www.everythingchannel.com, www.channelweb.com )

Everything Channel is the one-stop-shop for accessing, enabling, managing and accelerating technology sales channels.  From branding and recruiting to marketing and sales, Everything Channel offers technology marketers the unmatched breadth and depth of global brands and market intelligence combined with an unparallel audience loyalty and credibility serving all technology sales channels.  Through innovative sales and marketing solutions, Everything Channel arms the sellers of technology with the resources they need to achieve measurable and significant results.

Junior Developer/Support Analyst

This person will be working on the SharedVue technical team with multiple specialists.  Their time will be divided between product development/support and HTML content development.

The Junior Developer will work closely with the entire development team and tech management on a daily basis.  The open and collaborative environment rewards hard-working, independent thinkers who are always looking for ways to do things more efficiently while treating existing commitments and deadlines with the utmost respect.



Required Skills

•Expert: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
•Extremely Proficient: Classic ASP, SQL
•Knowledgeable: Version control (Subversion, Git), Photoshop, .NET (C#), PHP,  MySQL
•Bonus: .Net MVC, DotNetNuke, Sharepoint, ColdFusion, various CMS systems

•Meticulous and detail oriented
•Excellent problem-solving skills
•Self motivated and able to prioritize requests
•Knowledgeable with web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, ASP, web hosting)
•Comfortable with small teams
•Very good communication skills (email, phone, documentation)

•Ability to take ownership of tasks and work independently through resolution
•Manage dozens of simultaneous tasks, understand how to effectively prioritize and communicate status updates to coworkers and customers alike
•Take initiative to fill time with productive work, including ideas for new projects
•Engage in feature brainstorming and product development discussion
•Testing, QA and user experience analysis for the platform on an as-needed basis



Required Experience

Sample Work that would be done in the last two weeks:

•Build out HTML pages from templates and Word documents.
•Monitor support tickets in the queue from account managers.
•Troubleshoot 2 client implementation support issues – one requiring some assistance from  a tech team specialist.
•Provide response on 6 support tickets for the account managers.
•Work with tech team member to enhance classic ASP reports for large clients.
•Update new-partner initialization process in classic ASP.
•Assist a content team member with quality review of a set of content updates.

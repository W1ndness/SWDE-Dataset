About the Job

Picerne Real Estate Group, a national real estate owner/apartment manager has an exciting opportunity for an individual seeking a career as a Network Administrator.


Job Function
The candidate will be responsible for the company’s Microsoft Windows-based network and general end-user support. The position has responsibility for maintenance, upgrades and support to all server, desktop and technology operations for the Northeast Region, including the corporate office and remote sites.  The candidate will coordinate with and provide additional support as needed to the Florida and Arizona regions.


The Network Administrator is responsible for supporting the company’s network. This position includes installing, configuring and maintaining all network hardware and software.

Duties and Responsibilities
Sense of urgency to resolve issues independently

Strong oral and written communication skills
Strong customer (internal) focus
Planning and organizational skills
Ability to work on multiple projects
Self motivated and directed
Analytical and problem-solving capabilities procedures

Vendor management Picerne Real Estate Group, Rhode Island’s largest full service Real Estate

Office communication and other technical systems


Technical Skills
Windows Server 2003 & Active Directory

Microsoft Exchange Server 2003

Working knowledge of PC / Server / Network / Printer hardware

Knowledge of DENS, WINS, DACAPO, TCP/IP & networking protocols
Yardi Property Management Software

Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office products
Experience with Veritas Backup (or comparable backup solution)
Understanding of Network topology
Basic understanding of Cisco Routers and Switches

SQL 2000


Experience and Education
At least 5 years of hands-on IT experience in a Microsoft Windows environment and degree in Computer Science.

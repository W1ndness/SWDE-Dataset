Structured Cabling / Low Voltage Designer

About the Job

Responsibilities
The role of the Structured Cabling / Low Voltage Designer is support other Design Engineers and Project Managers in the design, acquisition, implementation, and administration of structured cabling solutions for our clients. The ideal candidate will specialize in the design of voice and data cabling systems, associated pathways, communications rooms, outside plant engineering and project implementation in the following areas:

·         Complete Surveys & Needs Analysis for critical rooms, data centers, trading floors, and general office spaces

·         Apply Product Specification, new product awareness & research

·         Present best practices and advise clients where conditions require decisions on trade-offs

·          Bid Leveling, Product Comparison, and Price Evaluations to clients

·         Apply knowledge of best practice and industry standards to voice and data inside and outside plant designs and gain experience in TIA/EIA/ANSI Standards, best practices supported by BICSI, as well as NEC and local code requirements

·         Learn to apply Electrical, Cooling & Spatial requirements to design for Telecommunications Rooms

·         WAN Services Design / Coordination

·         Assist in performing Disaster Recovery Analysis & Redundancy Planning

·         Work with Carriers to Procure Circuits

Qualifications

·         Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, MIS, Information Technology, Management, or related field 

·         Minimum 2-5 years relevant experience

·         Proficient in AutoCAD

·         Expert skill-set with Office productivity tools, including MS Office, MS Excel, email, etc.

·         Working knowledge of structured cabling standards and best practices such as EIA/TIA, BICSI, etc

·         RCDD certification a plus

·         Excellent verbal and written communication skills

·         Ability and desire to learn clients' businesses and cultural factors

·         Ability to conform to shifting priorities, demands, and timelines

·         Ability to react to project changes quickly and appropriately

·         Highly analytical, detail oriented, persistent, and not afraid to ask questions

·         Capable of prioritizing and multitasking efficiently in a fast-paced environment

 

The Company
Labrador Technology is a technology consulting company that provides project management and design services to financial services companies, law firms, consultancies, media companies and educational institutions across the Northeast and other key metropolitan areas.  Since 2000, Labrador Technology has assisted clients with a diverse range of technology design and implementation projects, including, but not limited to:  Technology relocations, Data Centers, office renovations, trading floors, phone and network upgrades, carrier and circuit diversity, new platforms and upgrades and environmental sustainability projects. 

Our team is defined by credentials, integrity and professionalism. Our clients are integral to our success and we take the time to listen and partner with them to make sure we are delivering the best quality we can for them. We provide both in-house and external training to help keep up-to-date on changing standards and emerging technologies. We value teamwork and information sharing, working in an open and inclusive environment where people are encouraged to ask questions and support one another.

Labrador’s employees benefit from 401K, medical, dental and many other available benefit options.

*We are an equal opportunity employer*

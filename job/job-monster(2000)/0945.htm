Network Administrator - Senior and Entry Level Positions Currently Available

About the Job

Our IT consulting company, based in downtown Denver, provides a variety of professional IT services to small and medium size businesses with the goal of helping them use technology to achieve a business result. Product offerings include managed services, systems integration, and Microsoft CRM and SharePoint development. Ongoing learning, commitment to excellence and personal development is imperative to remain competitive in this ever-changing technology consulting industry.

 

POSITION OVERVIEW:

The Network Administrator position is responsible for proactively monitoring and managing client networks and for providing Tier 1, 2 and 3 support (based on level of position) for multiple clients in diverse industries; skillfully supporting current technologies; contributing to profitability; maintaining strong client relationships; and ensuring that quality work is done in a timely manner.

        Solid experience in the following:

·         Server 2008/2003 including DNS, Terminal Server, Active Directory, Group Policy, & Remote Access, etc.  

·         Small Business Server 2008/2003

·         Windows 7/XP, Office 2010/2007

·         Exchange 2010/2007 including OWA, OMA, Active sync

·         Wide array of backup solutions

·         Firewall/Router configurations

·         TCP/IP 

·         Line of business application and end user support

·         MSP tools and ticketing systems

 
The Network Administrator is responsible for managing all aspects of client network infrastructures including:

·         Proactively monitoring and tuning networks for optimal performance and security

·         Effectively and efficiently implementing hardware and software installations and redeployments

·         Advanced troubleshooting of network, application, connectivity issues

·         Deploying technology according to business priorities and long-range plans

·         Thoroughly and accurately document configurations, procedures, and work performed

·         Providing input to management for network design and procurement of equipment/supplies

·         Interacting with clients professionally, and acting as a technical expert to educate clients and make
 approved recommendations

·         Troubleshooting and resolving end user support requests remotely with some onsite (HelpDesk is a primary role of one of the positions currently available)

 

Job Requirements:

·         Microsoft Certification:  MCP, MCSE, MCTS or equivalent an advantage

·         Prior consulting experience desired

·         Prior network administrator experience of 5-8 years

·         Position requires the ability to lift up to 40 pounds and is largely (60%) a sitting position with phone and
 computer usage

·         Excellent verbal and written communication skills

·         Valid Drivers License and reliable transportation required to travel to a variety of client sites

·         Periodic weekend and evening work and on-call responsibilities required

 

Job Status:

Full-time, exempt, salaried position

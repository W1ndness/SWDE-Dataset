Software Development Manager

About the Job

We are a mid-size company in the online marketing space growing like crazy
 
We are looking for a Software manager to manage 5/6 JAVA Engineers to help create a highly transactional JAVA/J2EE Web application to handle over 10k transactions per second!!!

Ranked as the 14th fastest growing company on the New England list.

Flexible and collaborative Environment

You are a technical leader and motivator who owns and drives technical projects and provides leadership for groups of engineers in an innovative, productive and collaborative Agile environment. You are responsible for the overall planning, execution, and success of complex technical projects.

In this role you will support your team in designing and implementing new back end and front end architectures for a core system that spans across our entire product line.  

Actions and Results

  • Manage complex technical projects and teams
  • Live the Agile principles and create a high performing team through them
  • Hire, mentor and grow passionate individuals who work well in strong teams
  • Strong leader capable of motivating the very best software engineers, as well as gaining their respect
  • That rare mix of intelligence, initiative, integrity, domain knowledge, verbal agility, and diplomacy which allows you to rapidly earn the trust of astute teams and individuals across the company

Skills and Experience

  • B.S. / M.S. in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Proven track record leading successful software teams
  • 10+ years of industry experience with 5+  years in a management role
  • Experience with Agile methodologies including Scrum or Kanban
  • Familiarity with a broad mix of technologies including a strong subset of: Java,, JSP, JBoss, HTML, XML, Spring, UI frameworks, DB2, NoSQL (Cassandra),  SOA, Web Services, Solaris, Windows, Linux, and a range of open source libraries
  • Desirable:  SaaS experience and a track record of design and implementation for scaling large systems

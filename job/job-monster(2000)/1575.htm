About the Job

Hachette Filipacchi Media U.S. (HFM U.S.)  www.hfmus.com is an innovation-driven company with a heritage as strong brand builders, experts and producers of credible content on all platforms including print, web sites, mobile, television, radio, events, books and other brand extensions.  We look to the future and create it, by developing new ideas and directions to deepen relationships making them more rewarding for our communities.  Our engaged audiences of over 40 million people and editorial environments deliver rich opportunities for advertisers and partners. We produce brands in the following categories: Fashion, Beauty and Design (ELLE, ELLE DECOR, and ELLEgirl); Women & Wellness (Woman’s Day, Woman’s Day Special Interest Publications); Automotive (Car and Driver and Road & Track as part of Jumpstart Automotive Group); Motorcycling (Cycle World) and Entertainment (Premiere). HFM U.S. is part of Lagardère’s (www.lagardere.com) media division Lagardère Active, a producer of special-interest content in more than 40 countries.

We have an exciting opportunity for a Microsoft Dynamics CRM Administrator. The candidate will design, implement and support corporate wide Microsoft Dynamics CRM installation and be involved in entire CRM implementation lifecycle, including requirements gathering, data cleansing and migration, development, testing and support.  The candidate will work closely with both IT and end-user communities to ensure effective and efficient use of developed solution and examine existing business models and the flows of data within the business in order to design an appropriate solution. 

 Responsibilities:

  • Provide implementation support to end-users and IT
  • Perform installation, upgrades and support of the server, workstations (Outlook Client), and e-mail integration
  • Understanding of MSCRM data structures and functionality
  • Understanding of end-user system usage (e.g. screens, tables, business processes and procedures)
  • Provide technical guidance in data analysis, data gathering and system design
  • Oversee (or perform) changes to database fields and screens
  • Provide technical guidance re: integration with other in-house systems
  • Perform (or oversee) user administration (e.g. security)
  • Develop queries and/or SQL Reporting Services (SRS) reports
  • Import data on an as-needed basis
  • Liaison between HFM U.S.  and/or Microsoft
  • Assist users with day-to-day questions, problems and application usage
  • Manage change control (i.e. receiving business requirement, report requirement, change or enhancement requests from Users)
  • Document training, policy and procedures, and user manuals
  • Conduct end-user training
  • Oversee changes to system fields, screens and picklists
  • Perform data administration (data integrity, cleansing data for imports, etc.)
  • Perform user administration (e.g. security)
  • Oversee Report generation, maintenance and testing
  • Define and communicati3 project direction and scope with management
  • Coordinate project technical requirements and direction with IT
  • Review requests from User Administrator (enhancement and/or fixes, data changes, functionality issues, etc.); analyzing for appropriateness; determining whether the requests are consistent with current business practice, business system rules or system design
  • Assign, prioritize and schedule the tasks to appropriate resources
  • Ensure that enhancements or fixes are properly tested, implemented, and appropriate training and/or communication is accomplished 

Requirements:

            Bachelors Degree.  Degree in Computer Science or MIS preferred.

  • Minimum 2 years relevant experience.
  • Experience in full cycle CRM installations or knowledge of CRM relating to sales, marketing and/or back office operations
  • Strong knowledge of salesforce automation solutions
  • Strong understanding of the relational nature of business applications.
  • Experience with Installing and configuring MS Dynamics CRM
  • Experience with modifications: iFrame, JScript
  • Strong knowledge of Web Services architecture, Web Service Management tools and experience in developing and consuming Web Services
  • Strong knowledge of SQL, Exchange, IIS, .NET, SRS - SQL Reporting Services
  • Experience with Microsoft CRM.  Must know Transact SQL language, .NET 2.0 or 3.0, be comfortable working on SQL 2000 and 2005 servers. 
  • Knowledge of Sharepoint and SaaS is a major plus.
  • ETL processes, DTS and SQL Integration Services (SSIS) design experience a must.
  • Experience in Object Oriented Programming, Dot Net, VB, ASP, T-SQL (Stored Procedures, Views, Triggers), C#, Windows Forms,  COM+, Windows Server Security, XML
  • Must have done at least half a dozen CRM installations
  • Knowledge of third party products and ETL’s such as Scribe
  • Ability to interact with customers to understand technical requirements. 

Our organization offers a competitive salary, a comprehensive benefit package, and opportunity for advancement. Please e-mail resume with salary history to careers@hfmus.com . We will only contact candidates whose experience matches our needs. EEO

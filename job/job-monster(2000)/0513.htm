Blink - Graphic Designer

About the Job

We envision a future where fitness is part of everyone’s daily routine.  We believe everyone deserves access to an easy-to-use, affordable fitness club. 

                                          

Our goal is to bring great design, integrated technology and accessibility to an otherwise intimidating and boring experience.  We aim to make every interaction fast and easy.  Our approach is straight-forward: Blink offers the best of the basics – a convenient location stocked with premium cardio and strength equipment in a safe, clean and well-designed space – all at an affordable price.

 

We are committed to engaging our members in fitness at their own pace and we consistently share fitness tips to use both inside our clubs and in your daily life. 

 

Ultimately our mission is to bring fitness to every city, every community and every body and in so doing, to build a brand that stands for good health, accessibility, value, ease-of-use and the highest standards in design, cleanliness and safety.

 

Blink is seeking a Graphic Designer. Ideal candidate will have 2-3+ years experience in a fast-paced, deadline driven studio or agency environment, with an emphasis in traditional graphic design and marketing communication.


Specific Responsibilities and Duties:

 

The responsibilities and duties of this position described here are representative of those an employee must perform.  This is not a comprehensive list and other duties may be assigned.  Reasonable accommodation may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

 

STRATEGIC

 

  • Thorough understanding of sales driven marketing and its relationship to effective design execution
  • Knowledge of creating relevant direct marketing campaigns with a clear understanding of emphasizing key business drivers (i.e., headline, call to action, pay-off)
  • Expert knowledge of print, direct mail, outdoor, poster/signage, magazine and multi-page layout a must
  • Concepting with creative director on high profile marketing projects and ability to disseminate information to team

 

TECHNICAL

 

  • Creating press-ready mechanicals
  • Collecting files for output
  • Resizing signage and various other forms of print collateral into pre-existing brand standard templates Image sourcing and retouching
  • Mounting and trimming artwork for presentations Working knowledge of layout, off-set printing, typography and color
  • Experience with the process of print production (knowledge of online production a plus)
  • Experience with Mac platform
  • Expert knowledge of the latest Adobe Creative Suite and Quark Xpress Multimedia (primarily HTML email) design experience preferred
  • Solid understanding of graphic design is essential

 



Position Requirements and Qualifications:

To perform this job successfully, an individual must meet the following minimum requirements and qualifications.

 

  • Complete a BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) program from a reputable Art School
  • 2-3+ years experience in a fast-paced, deadline driven studio or agency environment, with an emphasis in graphic design and marketing communication
  • Ability to meet non-negotiable deadlines in fast paced environment
  • Strong attention to detail Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience directly interacting with "clients" either internal or external while maintaining a positive attitude
  • Self motivation and accountability for every aspect of project management to exceed client and Creative Director expectations is essential
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced, creative environment
  • Can-do attitude is a must

 

Blink offers competitive compensation, benefits and more in an atmosphere defined by energy and professionalism.

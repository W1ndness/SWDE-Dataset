NetApp Storage Engineer

About the Job

DSCI is seeking an experienced NetApp Storage Engineer with our Viatech group, located in Kuwait to act as a subject matter expert in the design, implementation and support of NetApp storage and VMware Virtualization technologies. The successful candidate will be an expert in designing and implementing complex system configuration and standards in diverse, demanding customer environments.

Job Description

  • Enterprise level NetApp SAN/NAS storage design, networking, configuration, capacity management, troubleshooting and support.
  • Solid understanding of NetApp SAN/NAS technology and how VI3 is implemented with shared storage (eg. fiber channel, iSCSI, NFS or CIFS. RDM vs VMFS) strategies for performance and optimization across logical and physical network topologies.
  • VMware Infrastructure design and planning from storage perspective involving large scale clusters. Understanding of various methods to back-up virtual systems.
  • Experience in large scale data migration involving SAN, NAS and D2D data from older storage clusters to new storage cluster.
  • Storage Utilization reporting Technical documentation.

Experience & Skills

  • FAS31x0 and V Series experience. Filer installations & Hardware upgrades and additions. Data ONTAP and firmware upgrades and recommendations
  • Extensive experience with SnapManager, Snapmirror, Snapvault.
  • MultiStore/vFiler experience would be desirable.
  • VMware on NetApp experience
  • Multiprotocol implementation - NFS, CIFS, FCP, iSCSI, FTP protocols
  • Ethernet experience; ability to work with network Admins to configure virtual interfaces (vifs) on NetApp
  • Operations Manager (DFM) experience. Operations Manager and Protection Manager installation and upgrades
  • Experience working with DBAs for storage provisioning and data migration.
  • Experience in data migration from older Netapp Clusters to new Netapp clusters for primary storage and secondary storage.

Required Skills:

NetApp, SAN/NAS Storage, SnapManager, Snapmirror, Snapvault

Bachelors degree; Must be a US citizen with Ability to acquire and maintain a clearance

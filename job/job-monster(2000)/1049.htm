About the Job

Sonalysts, Inc., is seeking entry-level software engineers/programmers.  Work includes utilizing analytical and problem-solving skills to program in modern programming languages and operating system environments.

 

Qualifications: 

·      BS in Computer Science (or related field) OR show comparable programming/software experience.

·      Familiarity with developing multi-threaded applications using C# using the .NET platform (C++, Windows development experience and MFC a plus). 

·      U.S. citizenship and eligibility for a U.S. Department of Defense security clearance.

 

Pluses include experience with .NET forms and controls, Visual Studio 2008 IDE, and C# application development or other PC based related experience.

 

Preference will be given to local candidates.

 

Please verify U.S. citizenship in your resume or cover letter.  Resumes without a statement verifying U.S. citizenship will not be considered.


Founded in 1973 and headquartered in Waterford, Connecticut, Sonalysts is an EMPLOYEE-OWNED company known for its first-class technical capabilities precisely aligned with communication expertise, creativity and an understanding of the "business" of both government and commercial projects.

 

From the beginning, Sonalysts sought to provide the best in professional services to its customers while creating an environment of accountability and partnership among its employees.  This strategy has proven indispensable in Sonalysts' cultivation of a loyal clientele resulting in steady corporate growth for over 30 years.

 

Today with approximately 350 highly skilled professionals in more than a dozen offices around the country, Sonalysts provides solutions to the wide variety of complex challenges facing the government, corporate and entertainment industries.

 

Benefits include health, dental, life, and disability insurances; retirement plans; tuition reimbursement; and flexible working schedule.

 

Visit our website at www.sonalysts.com for more information about our unique company and other exciting employment opportunities.

Network Security Engineer

About the Job

ZS Associates is a global management consulting firm specializing in sales and marketing strategy, operations and execution. ZS is recognized for its expertise in go-to-market and sales force strategies and its downstream implementation capabilities. ZS helps companies achieve sales success by delivering both issue-specific solutions and large-scale business transformations.

 

With 19 offices and 1400 people worldwide, we have concentrated our resources on creating competitive advantage in sales and marketing for our clients in selected industries. Our focus has enabled us to develop an unmatched depth of knowledge and base of experience.  ZS also has a unique working environment.  Our work is challenging, intense and highly quantitative, yet our interactions are direct and informal.

 

We are currently seeking applicants for a SaaS Network Security Engineer to join our Evanston office. Qualified candidates will possess the skills detailed below and relevant work experience (minimum 3 years).

 

The SaaS Network Security Engineer supports managing, monitoring, data integrity, and security in an 24/7 SaaS environment. As a Saas Network Security Engineer, you will:

  • Monitor and manage network security of a SaaS LAN/WAN infrastructure
  • Monitor and manage network / server security infrastructure
  • Monitor network security and investigate security issues
  • Communicate and coordinate network security updates
  • Establish and maintain network users and related security
  • Establish and Maintain IT compliance requirements
  • Be organized and be able to prioritize tasks
  • Stay current on security technology and advisories
  • Provide 24/7 on-call support as required for all network and technical issues
  • IT team projects as assigned.

 

What are we looking for?

Successful SaaS Network Security Engineer possess the following characteristics:

  • BS/BA with record of high academic achievement
  • 3-6 years of previous experience in a SaaS environment
  • MCSA or MCSE preferred
  • CCNA, CCSP preferred
  • ITIL certification preferred
  • CISSP preferred
  • Eagerness to contribute in a team-oriented environment
  • Ability to work methodically and analytically in a quantitative problem-solving environment
  • Excellent leadership, communication and organizational skills
  • Strong customer service skills
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Active Directory, Microsoft Windows 2003/2008 Server, Linux and Unix
  • Working knowledge of Cisco Routers, Switches, Firewalls
  • Cisco Security Experience – VPN,ASA, MARS, and ACS
  • Working knowledge of NetIQ AppManager, Security Manager, Secure Configuration Manager
  • Working knowledge of Data Loss Prevention – Trend Micro, McAfee.

 

 Education and certifications:

·         Bachelor's degree in related field or equivalent work experience

·         Advanced training and certifications in security related topics and technology

Candidates must be authorized to work in the US on a permanent basis. No relocation provided.  EOE.

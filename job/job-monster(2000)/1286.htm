Underwriting Solution Manager - Business Systems Analyst (BSA)

About the Job

CRICO / RMF, a world leader in medical malpractice claims management, loss prevention education and patient safety initiatives has an opening in our Cambridge office for a Underwriting Solution Manager - Business Systems Analyst (BSA). 

Acting as the IT Solution Manager for CRICO/RMF, this position is responsible for performing the role of BSA, Project Manager, and IT SME for the Underwriting department. Candidates must have experience in an environment supporting a formal, documented Project/Program Management methodology. Candidate must possess the ability to confidently interact with individuals at all levels within the organization related to technical and business content.  


Duties include but are not limited to:

·         Responsible for business analysis, systems analysis and project management for the Underwriting Department and general IS projects that serve the entire organization

·         Manage technology projects through an SDLC methodology

·         Translate business processes into user requirements for information solutions

·         Offer recommendations that align to our overall corporate strategy and present tradeoffs of various solutions

·         Demonstrate solid understanding and working knowledge of  custom applications and business processes and knowledge

·         Provide excellent project and customer management skills

·         Define project direction, scope, risks, contingencies, resource needs and sequencing of activities

·         Ensure solution that is delivered meets requirements for our customers

·         Communicate effectively and efficiently at all levels both verbally and in writing

·         Rely on extensive experience and judgment to plan and deliver IT projects collaboratively and successfully

·         Leverage business/technical interpretation skill set; translate technical complexity into business context

·         Utilize Microsoft Project Server for task and resource management

 
Experience:

·         3+ years experience in insurance underwriting

·         4-6 years progressive experience with Technical Project Management and/or Business Analysis

·         Exceptional written and verbal communications skills.

·         Strong understanding and proficient use of industry standard project management tools – i.e. MS Project

·         Experience defining requirements and providing general and technical project management to system development projects that follow the SDLC process

·         Bachelors Degree in Computer Information Systems, Management Information Systems or related field


Desired:

·         4+ years progressive experience with Project Management in the healthcare and/or insurance industry 

·         Knowledge of insurance industry best practices

·         Quality program experience such as Six Sigma

·         Advanced degree preferred

·         PMP Certification 

·         Working Knowledge of Business Objects and SQL

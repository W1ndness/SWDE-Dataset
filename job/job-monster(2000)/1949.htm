Scrum/Agile Coach

About the Job

The candidate will be responsible for maturing and improving the existing Agile processes within the team, applying agile principals within real-world constraints and dependencies, ensuring consistent and tactical application across the teams, and helping the team to maximize its benefits. The candidate would coach the various Release teams (both functional and technical) in proper Agile Release and Iteration planning, Agile Estimation and Iteration execution activities. This person would bring thought leadership to the team in terms of leveraging existing or new tools for planning, estimation and collaboration within and across team.

 

Specific responsibilities include:

·         Be the Coach and Trainer for development and business teams and facilitate the
introduction of proper Agile Processes and culture within the team.

·         Advocate and evangelize best practices in Agile development within the team.

·         Work with Task leads and team members to develop proper Release/Iteration

·         Plans, decompose high-level client requirements to user stories, define and deliver iterations or sprints.

·         Coach and mentor other Agile/Scrum practitioners within team.

 

As an Agile Coach and Trainer, the candidate will be responsible for:

·         Driving planning activities for a defined suite of Website tasks/releases

·         Work with task leads and team members to develop and deliver proper Release/Iteration

·         Plans Collaborate and communicate with project leads, project managers, and team members to coordinate cross-project initiatives and activities. Includes, but not limited to working with project leadership to introduce improvements in form of process and tools to facilitate Agile/Scrum based SDLC

·         Identify and communicate dependencies, issues, and risks and developing and driving identification and implementation of appropriate mitigation strategies.

·         Implement scope control procedures.

·         Work with technical and business leads to actively manage the work efforts of multiple technical and functional resources through the project plan.


Required skills include:

·         Experience with Web Content Management technology a plus

a)      4+ years experience working on AGILE implementations and organizations

b)      Experience tracking and managing multiple releases

c)       Ability to coach the team to reach their highest potential

d)      Experience tracking progress in terms of Team velocity and sprint burn down charts, etc. Ability to leverage agile metrics to discover and mitigate schedule and performance risks

·         Expertise in all phases of system development methodology

·         Experience working in an Agile development environment

·         Familiarity with web-development technology

·         Ability to successfully manage delivery on projects in a matrixed resource environment

·         Strong communication and relationship skills

·         Solutions focused and capable proactively recommending solutions when scope, schedule, and resource availability are out of balance

·         Desire to be active in operations and drive process improvements, along with strong customer service skills

About Market America

 

Market America is a product brokerage and Internet marketing company that specializes in One-to-One Marketing. With more than 3 million Preferred Customers and 180,000 Distributors and UnFranchise® Business Owners worldwide, Market America has generated over $3 billion USD in accumulated retail sales. In that time, individuals have earned over $1.8 billion USD in commissions and estimated retail profits. Headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina, the company employs over 550 people globally with international operations in the United States, Australia, Canada, Hong Kong, the Philippines, and Taiwan. Through One-to-One Marketing, Market America combines the Internet with the power of people, creating the ultimate online destination. As a result, we have revolutionized a brand-new industry, setting a standard by which all other businesses are measured: Built on Product. Powered by People.

About the Job

HighPoint Solutions is a premier provider of specialized IT services with vertically focused solutions for Pharmaceutical and Healthcare companies. HighPoint is a place where the work is challenging, where innovation and initiative are not just valued, they are rewarded, and where diverse individuals are encouraged to unite their strengths.

 

Job Title: Sr. / Mid Level .NET Developers Needed

Job Description:

Seeking a Sr. / Mid level .NET Developer (C#) for a full-time opportunity at HighPoint with the opportunity to learn SharePoint. This role will involve development and application enhancements and maintenance. The right candidate will enjoy engaging with the business side, enjoy learning, can adapt to change, and has very good communication skills.

Requirements:

  • BS or BA degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 3 to 7 years of relevant experience
  • Display drive, energy, enthusiasm, and confidence
  • Microsoft .NET C#
  • Basic knowledge of SQL 2005, 2008
  • Visual Studio 2008 .NET 3.5 and 4.0 is a plus
  • Agile development experience is a plus
  • Experience using source code and version control tools
  • Understand Web-based technologies and programming

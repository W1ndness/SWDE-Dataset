EDI Programmer Analyst

About the Job


Nice-Pak Products, Inc./Professional Disposables International, Inc. was founded over fifty years ago, and is the global leader of pre-moistened wipes for cleaning, sanitization, healthcare, cosmetics and personal hygiene.

 

Essential Function:
Responsible for design, development and maintenance of EDI communication systems.
Responsible for leading and implementing computer related customer requirements.

Scope:
Communicates and coordinates with Customer Service, Sales, Marketing, etc… in all divisions on the customer requested EDI initiatives. Drives the Nice-Pak’s vendor EDI initiatives based on the vendor requirements set by NP Purchasing. Manage all computer related customer requirements such as UCCNet, RFID, etc.

Position Responsibilities:

1. Responsible for maintaining/upgrading/adding trading partners on the AS/400 Gentran system.
2. Responsible for maintaining/upgrading/adding versions, standards and transactions on the AS/400 Gentran system.
3. Responsible for maintaining/upgrading/adding communications on the AS/400 Gentran system including but not limited to by-sync, internet AS2, HIPAA compliant FTP, etc…
4. Responsible for upgrading the AS/400 Gentran system to most current software.
5. Responsible for all RPG and CL programming involved in EDI processing.
6. Responsible for the redesign and development of existing EDI transaction processing to a more generic approach rather than the current customer specific approach.
7. Responsible for the design and development of new EDI initiatives including but not limited to Vendor ASN, Carrier Shipment Info, etc…
8. Responsible for monitoring all EDI activities and applying fixes/changes and/or cleansing data accordingly.
9. Responsible for complying with customer requests.
10. Responsible for creating and distributing Nice-Pak standards to our vendors.
11. Responsible for maintaining EDI project list and addressing projects in priority order.
12. Responsible for keeping Director of IT up to date on all EDI activities.

Minimum Requirements:

Equivalent Education Level Requirement:
College Degree preferred

Experience Required:
3-5 years in EDI mapping, design, analysis, and programming on the AS/400 utilizing RPGLE and CL. Gentran experience a plus.
Must have excellent communication and organizational skills.

Knowledge Required
1. EDI transaction mapping
2. AS2 Internet EDI
3. FTP
4. AS/400 RPGLE and CL

Nice-Pak Products, Inc. offers competitive benefits & wages including health & dental insurance, paid vacation & holidays; plus immediate participation in our 401(k) retirement plan with matching funds. Other benefits are company paid disability & life insurance. EOE

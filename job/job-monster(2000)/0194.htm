.NET Web Developer (50 to 90K)

About the Job

We are looking to hire two Jr. Level .Net Web Developers and 1 Senior .NET to our in-house solutions team. Salary range is $50-60k for Junior and 70 to 85K for the Senior.  This is a strong .NET Developer that has worked on Websites and preferably has CMS experience.

 

Developer

 

Horizontal Integration is looking for talented development professionals to join its in-house technical development & integration team. With an increasing client roster that includes some of the leading companies in the US and a portfolio of services that delivers business value across disciplines and business units, Horizontal Integration employees continue to work on cutting edge technologies and innovative projects for our clients in the web development, custom application development, CRM, web content management and digital marketing design and execution. 

To continue to support its growing client needs and demands, Horizontal Integration is currently looking for Junior – Mid Level Web Developers that have a passion for developing front end web based solutions, demonstrates the desire and aptitude to learn new technologies quickly and have the foresight to know when, why and how best to apply them to client business problems.

 

Key skills for this position are:

 

·                2+ years plus demonstrated experience designing, developing and managing the deployment of Microsoft .Net based software solutions REQUIRED

·                2+ years hands on programming experience in Microsoft C#.Net (v2.0 – v3.5), ASP .Net, ADO.Net, XML, DHTML, HTML, HTTP Protocol, Java script, and Web Services REQUIRED

·                Good understanding of software development methodologies, object oriented programming, database design and development.

·                VB.Net, Flash, PHP, ColdFusion and other programming languages a plus

·                Required experience working on web development on a wide range of web properties (web sites, commerce sites, intranets, extranets).

·                Experience with web content management systems such as Sitecore, Umbraco, or Sitefinity is a plus

·                Exposure to database design and development on SQL Server 2000

·                Strong programming and problem solving skills, analytics skills and communication

·                Motivated, Energetic and demonstrates a strong aptitude to learning new technologies in a fast paced environment 

·                Self Starter

Bachelors degree and/or Masters in computer science preferred

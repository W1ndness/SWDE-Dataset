Network Integrity Analyst

About the Job

Just One Look… We’re Michigan’s best-kept secret!

 

Take a closer look at Consumers Energy and you’ll find a major Midwest company with over 7,000 employees providing electric and natural gas service to 6.5 million of Michigan's 10 million people. As Michigan’s best-kept secret, our opportunities are not just jobs, but careers that often last a lifetime. We are currently seeking an individual who is looking to grow their career.

 

Do you possess keen analytical skills with the ability to work independently, performing complex infrastructure tasks?

 

At the most senior level, this position performs the most complex analytical tasks associated with IT Infrastructure design and deployment. Tasks are performed at an independent manner with some direction as to the general results expected. At this level, the individual possesses and applies an in-depth knowledge of principles, practices, and procedures within an IT infrastructure setting, and often handles multiple assignments concurrently.

 

Responsibilities include:

  • Providing design, configuration, testing, implementation and management of Firewalls, VPN Concentrators, Proxy Servers, Load Balancers, Log Correlation Appliances, FTP Servers, Intrusion Prevention Appliances, and Network Admission Control Systems
  • Internet Application support and diagnostics
  • Network consulting, intrusion investigation, and 3rd level client support

 

Position Requirements:

Required Education, Training and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in an Information Technology field preferred, in one or more of the following: Computer Engineering, Computer Info Systems, Computer Science, Computer Science Engineering, Computer Technology, Systems Engineering, Information Technology, or related field
  • 2-10 years applicable working experience
  • Knowledge of firewalls, LAN/WAN, Network, TCP/IP, and
  • CISSP, CCNA, Posting band SG13-17 Certifications desired

 

Additional Required Skills and Traits:

  • Analytical, reliable, logical thinker, attention to detail
  • Able to perform research, investigations and trouble shoot
  • Must be security conscious
  • Must be customer and results oriented, self-motivated, and have excellent problem solving and decision making skills
  • Good communication, planning and organization skills with ability to multi-task
  • Proficient Microsoft Office and Internet skills

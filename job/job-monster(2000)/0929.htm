About the Job

Cervalis is a premier provider of IT infrastructure solutions including business continuity / rapid recovery, managed hosting, managed security, managed storage, networking & telecommunications and co-location.  We have state-of-the-art operations centers totaling more than 280,000 square feet of space in NY, CT, and NJ.   We offer attractive compensation and benefits packages.  This is a fast-paced environment with a focus on integrity, teamwork, and superior customer service. We are looking for energetic, ambitious and talented individuals to join our team.

Feel free to visit our website to learn more about our company at http://www.cervalis.com .

Position Summary: 

Strong customer service, technical aptitude and attention to detail are required.  Technicians are responsible for responding to customer incoming inquiries via phone, email and web mail within a defined timeframe. Performing initial troubleshooting and using proper communication and escalation is critical. Technicians are also responsible for monitoring our infrastructure, alarm panels, network and customer hardware and adhering to procedures as defined.  Daily operational tasks include data center walk through to check critical areas, remote hands requests, working the ticket system and completing projects as assigned.

 

Essential Functions: 

·         Be Initial point of contact (phone/emails/web mail) for all customer inquiries.

·         Verify all contacts against data base to ensure that contact is authorized to open a trouble ticket.

·         Acknowledge all incoming email and web ticket inquiries within 15 minutes of receipt.

·         Provide superior customer service to all customers.

·         Adhere to customer-specific procedures and Service Level Agreements (SLA).

·         Monitor all alarm conditions; follow escalation policies and procedures and SEVERITY Level processes.

·         Complete shift turnover at the onset and end of each shift.

·         Provide hardware configuration and support including server builds, rack and stacking, equipment and LAN cabling as needed.

·         Monitor all sites utilizing on-line tools and systems such as Solar winds and System Center Ops Manager.

·         Understand facility infrastructure specifically to the electrical and mechanical systems used in a data center environments including: Generators, UPS systems, ATS/STS units, PDU units, Chillers, air handling units, CRAC units.

·         Check Data Center critical areas as defined and report/document issues found.

·         Respond to all Data Center alarms per policies and procedures.

·         Perform daily remote hands including tape swaps, DNS changes, RFCs and other customer requests.

·         Generate weekly customer web and network reports.

 

Non-essential functions:  Other duties as assigned by supervisor or Cervalis management.

 

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities: 

Customer service is a key component of all jobs at Cervalis.  This position requires a higher level of customer service and commitment to excellence.  Cervalis customers expect a response to their inquiries within minutes and the appropriate follow through on open tickets.

This position also requires a basic understanding and the ability to identify, diagnose, escalate and/or resolve moderate technical issues pertaining to Internet protocols, TCP/IP, FTP, HTTP. Experience with HP Proliant servers, Windows 2000/2003 server preferred.  Knowledge of RIS a plus. 

Technicians are expected to be subject matter experts of all monitoring tools.  They are responsible for understanding our Severity Levels and associated escalation procedures.

General experience and knowledge of data center/server room configurations experience preferred. Candidates should show initiative in their career and demonstrate further educational goals such as pursuit of MCSE, CCNA, RHCE, or some other technical certification.

Knowledge of ticketing system.  Initiates, updates, tracks, escalates and close trouble tickets.


Knowledge of Commvault Qinetix, Veritas NetBackup and Acronis suite.

 

Work Environment and Physical Demands: 

General office and data center environment. High stress may occur at times. May involve lifting of equipment and supplies (up to 50 pounds). Job may include long hours of sitting or standing. Exposure to a climate controlled environment for extended periods of time.


Ability to work holidays is mandatory as this is a 24/7/365 operation.

Senior Media Technician

About the Job

New York Institute of Technology (NYIT) offers undergraduate, graduate and professional degrees to approximately 15,000 students through seven academic schools, at campuses in New York, at a number of global sites and campuses, and online. NYIT is a leader in developing creative approaches to the technological and global challenges in higher education today.

NYIT seeks a full time Senior Media Technician to provide technology and audio visual support to all educational and classroom activities and events.    Candidate will be responsible to staff the events and operate all media and audio visual equipment.  Candidate will provide technology and audio visual support to all meetings conferences and client events, and meet with event staff and owner to assess  and coordinate the technology  requirements needed.  Candidate will also provide technical leadership and staff supervision when applicable.  Responsibilities also include but are not limited to:  media conversion and basic editing, maintain and trouble shoot all related equipment, plan, design, install, configure and upgrade hardware, operating system, and software for all of the media services  equipment ;  apply patches/fixes manage security and system performance, file systems and local network and improve availability and reliability.  Technician will serve as a first point of contact for both clients and the ITI staff, act as a liaison to other technical groups, evaluate related hardware and software products and make appropriate recommendations.  Candidate will prepare, maintain and publish equipment inventory and provide on-call coverage outside business hours on a rotational basis.

Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Electronics, Engineering or related field and equivalent work experience with at least 6 years of related experience is required.  Demonstrated project management experience and ability to handle multiple projects and deadlines is preferred.  Experience and knowledge in all aspects of audio visual equipment, including lighting, sound, projection  and presentation, knowledge of media conversion and editing is required,  as well as strong experience in video conferencing technologies and equipment as well as familiarity with digital streaming and broadcasting technologies and hands on experience with audio visual control systems  e.g. Extron Familiarity and knowledge with hardware/software vendors and strong vendor relationships is preferred. Excellent decision making and problem solving skills and effective in getting things done independently and collaboratively .  Previous experience in a higher education environment is strongly desired.  Excellent communication, presentation and customer service skills are required as well as the ability to work on holidays, weekends and evenings.       We offer a competitive benefits package including tuition remission, along with a professional environment designed to enhance your career development.  For immediate please submit  resume to Judith Roback, Human Resources  at jroback@nyit.edu . EOE.

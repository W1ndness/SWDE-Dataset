Content Management Specialist

About the Job

eXalt Solutions, Inc. (www.exaltsolutions.com) provides on-demand, web-based services for solutions sales enabling suppliers of complex technical products and their channel partners to sell more product more profitably.

eXalt Solutions is looking for a full-time Content Management Specialist who will be responsible for:

 

Design

  • Becoming an expert (Domain Specialist) in our customers' cutting edge server, networking, and VoIP products.
  • Writing Requirements documents for customer signoff
  • Designing data structures and information models for structured content stored in eXalt’s application database


Implementation

  • Creating and/or acquiring content for customer’s mission critical web sites
  • Ensuring project documents are complete, approved, current, and stored appropriately
  • Coordinating workflow with other Content Specialists for Graphics, Software and User Interface Design

Project Management

  • Directing Offshore resources for creation of specialty content
  • Developing and Driving Delivery Schedules
  • Tracking project progress at all times.
  • Actively manage risk and accelerating delivery wherever possible
  • Communicating current project status to Customers and Internal Management including any constraints or assumptions that could impact future progress of the project

Account Management

  • Managing Customer Relationship and negotiating delivery milestones. 
  • Obtaining Customer Sign Off on Releases.

Quality

  • Working closely with Quality Assurance Team to Validate Software and Content
  • Following and improving internal delivery processes and standards

Support

  • Managing Deployment of site updates to mission critical websites during off business hours
  • Managing automated ticketing system for Change requests
  • Providing Level 2 support in case of  Incidence impacting site availability

 

We are looking for an enthusiastic, motivated individual with a BS in Business, Engineering Technology, Math, Science, Information Systems or related technical discipline and the following skills:

Must have:
- Strong Microsoft Excel skills, including advanced formulas, pivot tables, and macros
- Excellent communication skills

- Strong Interest in Project Management

- Exceptional problem solving skills providing the client with a long lasting solution to their business problems.

- Proven record of ability to learn new products and technologies quickly

Preferred, but not required:
- Experience building and working with a relational database

- Some knowledge of Access and/or SQL
- Basic understanding of HTML and/or XML
- Basic understanding of CSS
- Customer support experience
- Some programming experience

- Software and/or Data testing experience
- Technical Support experience

 

The successful candidate must be a self-starter with the ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines in a virtual office environment. Applicants must be comfortable working in a start-up, entrepreneurial atmosphere with minimal supervision. Candidates must be located in the Boston metropolitan area.

This position is a salary position ranging from $35,000 to $45,000 with pay based on experience and offers health insurance.

US Residents Only

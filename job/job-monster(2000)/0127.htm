Customer Technical Support Manager

About the Job

The position is responsible for managing the Aastra’s Customer Technical Support (ACTS) organization.  ACTS provides Tier 1 and Tier 2 technical support 7x24 365 days a year for a broad range of telecommunications products, solutions, and services.  ACTS is also responsible for escalation and tracking of issues with both internal technical resources, the Escalated Technical Support (ETS) organization and/or other Aastra development centers, and with external OEM vendors.

  

Minimum Qualifications:

t       Bachelor’s degree (B.A. or B.S.) in Business Management, Telecommunications, Technology Management or related field required.  Equivalent related experience and/or training or equivalent combination of education and experience may be substituted for degree requirements.

t       Minimum of two (2) years in a supervisory/management role in a technical support call center environment.

t       Advanced understanding of all aspects of a support/services business in a high tech environment, from a supplier, integrator, and operator’s point of view.

t       Advanced understanding of IP infrastructures and protocols, especially in the area of VoIP and SIP.

t       Advanced understanding of system monitoring and performance monitoring tools, including SNMP and VoIP.

t       General understanding of secure remote access solutions for IP networks.

Skills, Abilities, Special Licenses or Certificate: 

t       Be a self-starter, with initiative, energy, commitment and eagerness to learn.

t       Ability to prioritize and troubleshoot multiple issues, as well as adapt to new challenges quickly

t       Ability to work with internal and external customers, draft technical documentation and training to facilitate support of new product introduction and cross training.

t       Ability to work independently and effectively as part of a team.

t       Analytical skills to support troubleshooting and debugging.

t       Demonstrated leadership abilities and be achievement oriented.

t       Excellent organizational skills.

t       Excellent interpersonal skills.

t       Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office products with an emphasis on Word and Excel.


Preferences:

t       Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification preferred.

t       Experience with Remedy Case Management System and/or Oracle Project Accounting preferred.

t       Experience with various Aastra platforms such as Clearspan, MX-One, Pointspan, Centergy and SIP terminals.

 

 

We offer a competitive salary commensurable with experience and excellent benefits, which include Health/Dental/Vision Insurance, 401(K) plan with company matching, FSA, Short Term Disability, Long Term Disability, Life Insurance, 15 Days Vacation, 10 Paid Holidays, 2 Paid Personal Days, just to name a few. 

 

If you meet the requirements, you may apply online at www.aastrausa.com/careers/ or email your resume with salary history to: hr_recruiter@aastra.com .  Please reference the Job Title and Job No. 2010-0208 in the subject line.

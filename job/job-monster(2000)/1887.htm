Senior Software Engineer, Consumer Tools
Are you looking for a fast moving, creative environment where you can use your expertise to develop and market cutting-edge interactive and digital products?
AT&T Interactive , a subsidiary of AT&T Inc, is an industry leader in creating local search products that encourage consumers to discover and engage with local businesses across three screens - online, mobile, and TV. YP.com attracts over 36 million monthly online unique visitors [i] . YP.com is also accessed by millions of users each month via the mobile web. AT&T Interactive consumer apps have a pre-activation presence or have been downloaded through digital app stores on over 40 million mobile phones. [ii]
Helping advertisers grow their business, AT&T Interactive's local ad network, one of the industry's most robust, distributes ads to a distinguished list of top publishers whose presence spans online, mobile and TV platforms. AT&T Interactive's wide range of local ad products such as premium listings, performance-based advertising, and search engine marketing provides advertisers an opportunity to reach consumers who are looking for a local business.
AT&T Interactive products are supported by more than 5,000 media consultants from AT&T Advertising Solutions.
Join our dedicated and talented team of individuals all focused on creating the best products in the marketplace. We currently have an opportunity for an experienced Senior Software Engineer – Consumer Tools in our either our Glendale, CA offices.
Responsibilities:
  • Design, implement, and test leading-edge Web-based Consumer Tools features and functionality using Ruby On Rails
  • Create robust large-scale production applications to provide insight into consumer team
  • Be the TDD and RSpec/Cucumber subject matter expert/opinion leader
  • Create prototypes very quickly and re-architect them for production readiness
  • Take research projects and work collaboratively to move them to engineering
  • Provide platform support and maintenance
  • Document code and system designs
  • Play an active role in the evolution of Consumer Tool products
  • Develop deep expertise in multiple areas including APIs, Web Monitoring and Web based Technologies
  • Keep abreast of latest technologies and trends
  • Work in a team environment and be a team player
Requirements:
  • Broad development experience, preferably related to building web tools Using RoR and Java.
  • Must have significant understanding of and experience building within MVC frameworks either in RoR or java (samples required)
  • Must have significant understanding and experience with TDD, TestUnit, RSpec, Cucumber or equivalent testing framework (samples required)
  • Knowledge of Agile development methodology
  • Minimum 3-5 years of hands on experience and expertise in writing Ruby on Rails, Ruby, SQL, Java
  • Significant hands on experience developing data-driven applications using Oracle, MySQL, or other RDBMS
  • Solid understanding of web technologies
  • Must be self-motivated, have great communication skills (work with product, qa and other dev teams), innovative and engineering excellence, and the ability to adapt to change
  • Should have an understanding of CSS and an opinion on the benefits/trade-offs between the different JavaScript frameworks and the ability to use Prototype anyways
  • Must be opinionated yet flexible
  • Active open source member/contributor preferred
Education:
· BS in Computer Science or related engineering field required. MS and/or PhD a plus.
We offer competitive salaries, excellent benefits including a matching 401(k), bonuses, and a casual yet high-energy work environment. Jobs are located in Glendale, CA near Downtown Los Angeles, CA. Send us your resume today.
AT&T Interactive is an Affirmative Action/Equal Opportunity Employer, and we are committed to hiring a diverse and talented workforce. EOE/AA/M/F/D/V
Principals only please. Agency resumes are not accepted and will be considered unsolicited resumes that are not subject to placement fees .

About the Job

TITLE:  SENIOR FLEX DEVELOPER  (SME)

LOCATION:  Southern California, Orange, California. (Moving soon to South Orange County, Irvine Spectrum area.) 

 

M86 Security is the global expert in real-time threat protection and the industry's leading Secure Web Gateway provider. The company's appliance, software, and Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions for Web and email security protect more than 24,000 customers and over 17 million users worldwide. M86 products use patented real-time code analysis and behavior-based malware detection technologies as well as threat intelligence from M86 Security Labs to protect networks against new and advanced threats, secure confidential information, and ensure regulatory compliance. The company is based in Orange, California with international headquarters in London and development centers in California, Israel, and New Zealand. For more information about M86 Security, please visit: www.m86security.com.

 

This is an outstanding opportunity to join a company with tremendous growth potential.  We need highly motivated developers / engineers that thrive on creating and developing cutting-edge security applications. You will understand business processes, design solutions, make decisions, and provide estimates of effort. You will have a direct impact on the design and development of the company’s products.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • This position will entail design and coding of new features and maintenance on a cutting edge dynamic web and email reporting application.
  • This role will work on the Agile Scrum team as a SME (Subject Matter Expert) for UI design and implementation.    
  • The individual will work as a senior team member to accomplish goals as a group and help define the future vision for the user interface.

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

·         Bachelor’s degree or higher in Computer Science or related discipline.

·         9 + years of hands-on related Software Development experience.

·         SME (Subject Matter Expert) experience in designing web applications interfaces

·         4 + years of experience extending and creating Flex components, familiarity with exporting component libraries, and working on a modular application.

·         4 + years of experience coding / designing applications that use the AMF protocol.

·         Experience generating imagery with Photoshop / Fireworks for web applications.

·         3+ years of experience building with JAVA middle-tier applications.

·         Extensive knowledge of BlazeDS, and AMF3.

·         Strong familiarity with Tomcat and Linux Servers .

·         Strong experience in at least one or more modern MVC Web Application Frameworks.

·         Proficiency with Object-Oriented design Methodology and multi-tier architecture.

·         Team oriented, works well in a fast-paced environment.

·         Ability to work with varied stakeholders to solidify design and meet dead-lines.

·         ActionScript 3 as a second language.

·         Desire to engage in extremely challenging assignments. Strong interpersonal and collaborative skills.

·         Experience analyzing, designing, and querying relational databases is a plus.

 

WE OFFER:

·         Competitive salary commensurate with experience

·         Medical, Dental, Vision Benefits

·         401K Plan

·         Casual attire work environment.

·         Tremendous opportunity for professional growth.

·         Flexible schedule, with manager approval

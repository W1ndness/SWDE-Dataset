Technical Business Analyst

About the Job

For more than 30 years, libraries have used SirsiDynix technologies to bring the power of their collections to their communities. We are firmly committed to our library customers and the communities they serve, but that commitment extends beyond software and technical support – it means genuine service, to our libraries and to the industry. Together with libraries, we help communities thrive by connecting people with library resources that educate and inspire.


Summary


A Business Analyst is responsible for acting as the bridge between a Product Manager and Development staff. This position is required to make the business strategy more effective through a proper use of technology and relevant processes within the Development organization. This position is a blending of business and technical skills.


The Business Analyst works closely with Product Managers to review market requirements and detailed product requirements in order to accurately schedule and manage the interactions and needs of a product release. The Business Analyst is responsible for verifying that requirements are correctly implemented, tested, and documented by members of the Development staff according to the needs of the end-user. The position involves close interaction with product managers, engineering, software architects, software test, client care and key customers.


To be truly effective, the Business Analyst has to understand his/her customer's business, know their pain points, their needs, and their strategic plans. A full understanding is necessary to be able to adequately convey this to the Development staff.


Primary Responsibilities
  • Translate requirements provided by Product Manager into User Scenarios against which engineers can design, estimate, and implement. Work with Product Manager to refine requirements based on experience with product users.
  • Understand in detail how new features are being implemented. Identify situations where specific design or implementation decisions may not meet business requirements. Work with Engineers to evaluate the costs and benefits of different implementation approaches.
  • Understand in detail how users configure and interact with product. Identify situations where specific design or implementation decisions may not meet user needs or expectations.
  • Track day to day implementation progress against release milestones. Communicate risks immediately to Product Manager.
  • Help engineers engage with product users by coordinating and managing usability testing. Help engineers translate the results of usability testing into effective product design.
  • Do research in support of Buy, Build, or partner decisions. Research new technologies that may provide more efficient solutions to the user problems the product aims to solve.
  • Work with Product Marketing to conduct competitive analysis, create white papers, document success stories, coordinate launch plans, and occasionally conduct special projects for customer acquisition or retention.
  • Provide Documentation staff with technical information as necessary.
  • Work with Sales to conduct win/loss analysis.
  • Be the liaison between the business units, technology teams and support teams.
  • Work with Engineering to Provide Operations staff with all necessary information to successfully install, upgrade, configure, troubleshoot, and otherwise support customers using the product.

Qualifications

Knowledge and Skills
  • Basic knowledge of the specific technologies used in products (Java, SQL, HTML, etc…)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Must be able to manage large projects and break them down into workable units
  • Must be able to negotiate with Management, Product Managers, Business Analysts and engineers on new functionality as well as enhancements
  • Ability to articulate principles of effective interface design.

Education
  • BA/BS Degree; preference for a Technical or Business field of study
  • Industry-specific experience a plus
  • MLS/MLIS or professional library experience highly desired
  • Programming experience a plus.
  • Experience with web-based and desktop user interfaces.

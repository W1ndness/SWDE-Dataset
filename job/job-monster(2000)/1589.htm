SAP DELIVERY FI/CO FUNCTIONAL ANALYST

About the Job

 
 

Satisfy our appetite for SAP FI/CO Tests, Specs & Training Materials!  

Translate your understanding of business processes into SAP FI/CO solutions at Tate & Lyle, a world-leading producer of sweeteners, food and industrial ingredients!  Providing Level-3 support in Decatur, IL, this SAP savvy FI/CO will interact closely with Business Relationship Management and SAP Technical/Development teams to deliver results and enhancements on a timely basis.  Duties involve developing functional/technical specs, test plans and training materials as needed. Coordinating with business process owners from around the globe, the SAP Delivery FI/CO Functional Analyst will provide Unit & System Testing plus Regression Testing on FI/CO changes.  

In addition to impressive technical knowledge and superior customer service skills necessary to work with a diverse business community, the qualified candidate must possess:  

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Business, Finance, Logistics or related discipline.  
  • Minimum 5 years SAP FI/CO configuration/support experience and experience developing functional/technical specifications, tests, and ASAP/RunSAP methodology.  
  • Hands on experience with 2 full life-cycle implementations including Post-Go-Live support.  
  • Excellent interpersonal, customer service and communication skills (oral and written).  
  • Exposure to other SAP products, Financial Supply Chain Management, Travel and Expense Management, etc., is a plus.     
  • ITIL certification, desirable.  

In return for your proven track record and desire to exceed expectations, Tate & Lyle offers attractive compensation plus many outstanding benefits-- including medical, dental and prescription coverage, company-paid life insurance, 401(k), and much more!  For immediate consideration, please apply online at www.tateandlyle.com

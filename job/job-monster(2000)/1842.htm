Oracle PLSQL Developer

About the Job

 
 

SSCI is a dynamic company providing high-end technical consulting services with a focus on database engineering.  We value hard work, honesty, quality, and getting the job done efficiently with minimal overhead.  We are seeking highly motivated candidates who take initiative and are looking to learn, contribute, and grow with the company.

 

We currently have an opening for an Oracle PL/SQL Developer on a project for the Consular Affairs Bureau of the Department of State.  This is a full time position located in Arlington, Virginia and is available immediately. Experience with Oracle PL/SQL development is key. Please note that this is a Department of State contract and all candidates must be U.S. Citizens to be considered.


General Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Design, development, testing and tuning of web-based applications using Oracle PL/SQL and MOD_PLSQL.
  • Design, development, testing and tuning of data transformation, loading processes and data delivery using Oracle PL/SQL
  • Full life cycle tasks including systems analysis, system design, application development, unit testing, data staging, and deployment.​ 
  • Ability and interest to have an impact and input to systems architecture and solution design
  • Investigate and resolve system-wide technical problems
  • Design new features to meet new requirements
  • Development and maintenance of processes to ensure data quality

Required Skills:

  • Database programming experience utilizing Oracle PL/SQL
  • Good understanding of database design concepts
  • Strong conceptual and analytic skills
  • Experience w/ medium to large-scale systems
  • Good oral & written communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal/team skills
  • Well organized and detail oriented

Additional Desired Skills:

  • MOD_PLSQL development
  • Advanced Oracle SQL and PL/SQL techniques
  • XML Web services development
  • Experience with developing services in a Service Oriented Architecture/ESB
  • Understanding of Enterprise Messaging Models
  • Understanding of SOA or SOA 2.0

Clearance:

  • Candidate must be a US Citizen
  • Security clearance is required to start work & will be obtained by employer prior to start of employment

Certification: 

  • Related certification desired

Education:

  • Technical degree or equivalent work experience

Job Type:

  • Full-time
  • Position available immediately

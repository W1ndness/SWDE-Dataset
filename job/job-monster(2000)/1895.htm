Software Developer (Java EE JPA Hibernate JSF SOA Maven)

About the Job

Wide-scale customer adoption of FairWarning® healthcare privacy monitoring solutions is driving a high-growth environment.  FairWarning® employees are world-class information security and compliance professionals.  The work environment is open, exciting, dynamic and demanding.  Only highly motivated professional candidates wishing to further their career need apply.  No travel is necessary.  The FairWarning® team is located in our office in Tampa Bay Florida.  Read more at About FairWarning®

 

Apply via Monster or send an email to Software-Jobs@FairWarningAudit.com  

 

Responsibilities

·         Candidate will provide hands-on software development of a Java EE application built using Spring 3.0, JPA, Hibernate, JSF 2.0, JSP, and AJAX running in Apache Tomcat

·         Candidate must have a strong software developer background and keen sense of judgment allowing him or her to extract custom components and make them standard within the software solution.

·         Candidate must be able to work closely with the Product Team to assess product requirements, help define the visual interface design (look and feel), and create the screen designs based on the UI models.

·         Contribute to the system architecture, design, and development of the Java EE Application

·         Implement and improve current system design based on the mix of products and environment constraints

·         Participate in design and code reviews, documentation of design, and implementation of methodologies to ensure high quality deployments.

·         Integrating customer clinical and non-clinical application data files into the FairWarning® privacy surveillance solution.

·         Work independently on and in collaboration with customers to provide solutions that address their needs.

 

Experience, Knowledge, Skills

·         Computer Science or Computer Engineering degree.

·         Mastery of the English language with strong verbal and written skills.

·         Experience in a business environment working in a software or technology company.

·         Developed with JPA, Hibernate, JDBC

·         Developed with Spring framework, preferably 3.0

·         Experience with UI implementations using: JSP, JSF preferred

·         Experience with Tomcat, deploying WARs, debugging deployment issues

·         Experience with Maven, SVN for build and code repositories

·         Familiar with MySQL (data modeling and database admin skills, configuration, indexing, sql optimization).

·         Working knowledge with a Linux/UNIX operating system and shell scripting preferred (Perl, bash, kornshell). Knowledge of SELinux.

·         Familiar with OOP and SOA.

·         Experience with design patterns.

·         Understanding of UML.

·         Understanding of the full software development life cycle with experience writing unit-tests.

·         Data privacy knowledge; Health care EHR systems; Information Security.

·         Understands the importance of testing and developing a quality product. 

·         Strong interpersonal and communication skills. 

·         Problem solver with attention to detail. 

·         Self motivated with strong time management skills and able to handle multiple assignments.

Attitude and Environment

·         Willing to apply technical and business acumen to new challenges in a fast paced environment with an entrepreneurial-spirited team

·         Highly motivated to see FairWarning® achieve its vision as “The world’s leading supplier of solutions that protect patient privacy” while maintaining and expanding on our position as a leader in the industry and committed to supporting the FairWarning® customer-centric business model

About FairWarning®

Founded in 2005, FairWarning® invented and introduced privacy breach detection to the healthcare industry and has grown 100 % + every year since. The company’s customers represent nearly 400 hospitals and over 1,800 clinics across the United States, Canada, and United Kingdom. FairWarning® is poised for growth in 2011 and beyond having accumulated a wide-range of household name, reference-able customers and has formal go-to-market partnerships with the leading brands of healthcare and information security. The company has been profitable since 2008 and is headquartered in Saint Petersburg, Florida with offices in London, England and Paris France.  www.FairWarningAudit.com

About the Job

United Nations Foundation (UNF) / Better World Fund (BWF)

Systems Engineer

Vacancy Announcement          

 

Overview

 

The Systems Engineer will manage the UNF network/systems infrastructure and applications; monitor and troubleshoot issues with regards to network/server security and outages and gather business/system requirements. The Systems Engineer will work with the IT Director, other members of the IT team and vendors to evaluate technology needs, and recommend improvements and modifications to existing infrastructure.

 

Working Relationships

 

The Systems Engineer is a member of UNF's Executive Office Department and reports to the IT Director.

 

Major Duties and Responsibilities

 

The Systems Engineer serves as one of the high level technical experts in regards to computer/network systems and associated issues. Essential duties include:

 

·         Assist to design, plan and implement network infrastructure and applications including Microsoft products, VMware ESX, SAN storage and other technology solutions.

·         Collaborate with the IT Director to develop, plan and implement system preventative measures, maintenance, enhancements and upgrades.

·         Analyze and assess computer/network architecture requirements and determine optimum, cost-effective solutions.

·         Perform troubleshooting analysis of servers and associated network systems and escalation tickets while monitoring and maintaining application environment stability.

·          Assist to develop service specifications, internal standards, and policies and procedures to adapt to evolving technology and business needs.

·         Coordinates and executes large-scale network operations, such as: critical maintenance, large-scale migrations, global technology changes/additions, major hardware/software upgrades with other members of the IT team or technology vendors.

·         Assist with the forecasting and managing of server technologies and growth, coordinate client/server disaster recovery planning.

·         Operate and maintain local area and wide area networks, tracks significant problems, monitors performance, and performs upgrades to hardware and software as required.

·         Install or modify existing installations of networked computer hardware, software, and other components.

·         Secure the network infrastructure and set up wireless networks.

·         Participate in long- and short-range technology planning.

·         Assist to analyze utilization information and plans network capacity.

·         Configure and maintain routers, switches, firewall/security devices, and other types of networking equipment.

·         Produce clear and concise documents and diagrams capturing networking and operational procedures and LAN/WAN topology using MS Visio, MS Project, MS Excel and MS Word.

·         Must be available for occasional weekend and after hours work.

·         Other duties as assigned and/or requested.

 

 Selection Criteria:

 

·         2 – 4 years of experience as Systems/Network Engineer.

·         Experience in implementing networking solutions in a dynamic/growing environment.

·         Working experience supporting Windows 2003/2008 environment, VMWare, Active Directory, Exchange 2007, DNS, IIS, DHCP, SMTP, Blackberry Enterprise Server and Dell EqualLogic SAN storages.

·         Perform technical analysis of IT infrastructure and applications to support remote connection and replication of physical and virtual resources

·         Experience with application performance and latency problems for physical and virtual environment.

·         Experience implementing and supporting enterprise system monitoring tools

·         Solid network and systems troubleshooting experience.

·         Excellent written and verbal communication skills and client focus a must.

·         Ability to multitask, work well under pressure and prioritize tasks to meet numerous operational deadlines.

·         Excellent customer service skills, positive attitude and the ability to communicate well with employees at all levels.

·         Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field required

·         MCP or higher certification

·         Microsoft SQL 2005/2008

·         VMware's Install, Config, and Manage Course for vSphere4

·         Network+, Security+  or equivalent

Compensation & Benefits

Salary commensurate with experience.  Actual salary will depend on qualifications and anticipated contribution to the Foundation.

 

UNF pays 100% of medical, dental, vision, life and disability insurance premiums for its employees and 75% of medical and vision for employees' dependents.

 

In addition, UNF provides 20 days of paid vacation per year, 12 paid holidays, three personal days per year, an immediately vested 150% matching 403(b) contribution up to a limit of six-percent of employee pay, Metro Pass benefits, and flexible spending accounts for health and dependent care.

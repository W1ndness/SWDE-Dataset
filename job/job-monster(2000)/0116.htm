Technical Architect - Salesforce.com Consultant

About the Job

Job Title:  Salesforce.com Technical Architect

 

This year, Innoveer was named CRM Magazine’s “One to Watch” consultancy for the second straight year.   Join our International CRM Consultancy and be a part of the next wave of success.


World class leadership and strong “down economy” demand for CRM services have enabled Innoveer to thrive through two major recessions.


One of the secrets to Innoveer's success is its people.   We work hard to hire the best and the most talented for each position and our "no jerks" hiring policy ensures that all of our teams get along well internally and with our clients.   Innoveer values its employee's individuality and continually invests in their professional growth. Our culture is collaborative, pragmatic, and client oriented.


From a technology perspective, while you will work with some of the best CRM products in the world, we are   ‘vendor neutral’.  This means that you are always working with the latest and best technology but you are not tied up with any one technology or platform; we always do what is best for the customer.  Salesforce.com, Oracle’s CRM On Demand, Cegedim Dendrite, or Siebel On Premise – whatever works for the client is what we implement.


Sound too good to be true?  During the interview process you will talk to our consultants -- ask them how this works.   After thirteen years, thousands of projects and over 500 happy customers, you'll be part of the best CRM consultancy out there.


We are looking to hire a  Salesforce.com Technical Architect:


Location:  Boston area preferred.  New York City area would be 2nd preference.  Open to other east coast locations, e.g., New Jersey, D.C., and areas near major airports.

 

Responsibilities/Position Description:

  • Customer and Project Responsibilities
    • Lead SalesForce.Com implementation engagement
    • Project management including development and maintenance of project plans, status & budget reports, escalation of issues to client project sponsor(s)
    • Manage resource requirements and project team members.
    • Ensure the successful delivery of client engagements
    • Lead business and functional requirements definition and prioritization/scoping of requirements
    • Map functional requirements to SalesForce.Com features and functionality
    • Maintain and coordinate communication with the Customer throughout each project                                                                                                         
  • Corporate Responsibilities:
    • Disseminate best practices on solution positioning and delivery.
    • Mentorship of more junior team members in business analysis, project management, and SalesForce.Com best practices
    • Support presales activities inclusive of participating in sales calls, proposal development
    • Manage multishore projects
    • Adapt to our dynamic work environment and be creative within the team


Qualifications – Looking for a well-rounded candidate that is both functionally and technically oriented.

 

Must Have:

  • 2+ years of experience in full lifecycle SalesForce.Com engagements
  • 5+ years of experience in project management, business analysis, and technology implementation for full lifecycle enterprise  software projects
  • 2-5 years CRM domain and application experience
  • Experience in SFDC technical customizations using Force.Com
  • Well rounded in both functional and technical aspects of a CRM engagement
  • Demonstrated excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Combination of both business analysis and technology implementation experience.
  • Must be willing and able to travel up to 100% of the time (Mon-Thurs  - home F,Sa, Sun)

Nice to Have:

  • Sales Support Experience; i.e., Proposals, Sales Presentations
  • Experience in other CRM technologies such as Oracle CRM On Demand or Siebel On Premise
  • Experience managing at least 1 multi-shore project

DB2 Database Administrator

About the Job

Established in 1990, HTC is an Inc. 500 Hall of Fame with headquarters in Troy, Michigan. HTC is a leading global Information Technology (IT) solution and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) provider. HTC offers dynamic solutions that utilize proprietary tools, methodologies and processes, increasing the speed and reducing the cost of software development and business processes. HTC assists clients across multiple industry verticals in software development, systems integration, implementation and support, application management, software training and technical support. Staffed by a team of highly qualified subject matter experts, HTC offers quality management systems, e-business, data warehousing, electronic content management (ECM), supply chain management (SCM), customer relationship management (CRM), and enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions. HTC offers Connect IT, our Global Delivery Methodology that enables us to seamlessly deliver outsourced IT services and help achieve superior business results for our customers at very competitive price. HTC has global delivery centers across the globe. HTC works with clients under professional services and/or projects outsourcing Agreements.

 

 

HTC is looking for a DB2 Database Administrator with the following skills.

 

 

Required Skills:

 

·          Understanding of technical environments for DB2 on Z/OS

·          Host, utilities, SQL performance and Replication experience

·          Provides physical design work and systems database tuning

·          Consults on database designs and assists in facilitating database and application design reviews

·          Establishes database system business resumption rules (back-up and recovery).

·          Minimizes and manages data redundancy

·          Using the logical model, build physical database and associated metadata

·          Coordinate and manage the organization in the physical database for current and future database environments.

·          Development and maintenance of the database

·          Work with project efforts to assist and consult on database design and implementation

·          Experience with DB2 utilities such as IBM and BMC

·          Experience working with Queue Replication, SQL Replication, and SQL Performance Tuning

 

 

Preferred Skills:

 

·          Experienced with IBM DB2 tools such as IBM DB2 Administrator Tool, IBM Omegamon for DB2 PE, IBM Optimum, IBM Optimization Service Center, IBM File Manager for DB2, IBM Query Monitor and IBM Pure Query

·          Experience in an enviroment that runs DB2 z/OS that was accessed by CICS, Batch COBOL and WebSphere on z/OS

·          Knowledgeable in the design and implementation of stored procedures

·          Knowledgeable in the design and implementation of data warehouses

·          Proficient in the use of Embarcadero ER Studio

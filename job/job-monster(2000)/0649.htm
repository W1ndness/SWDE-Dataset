About the Job

 
 
 
 
 
 

HPC Architect, Sr. Manager

 

MSC Software, for decades market leaders in tools for simulation and virtual prototyping, is in the midst of a transformation. Our HPC strategy is an important element in our new vision and product roadmap.


The Research and Development Architect will be responsible for design and development of new parallel, highly-scalable algorithms for new and existing and new products capable of running on high performance shared and distributed memory systems as well as GPUs.


Duties and Responsibilities:

 

·       Directly supports research by contributing in the areas of cyber infrastructure systems architecture and design. Is responsible for analyzing, specifying, designing, implementing and coordinating large HPC Simulation projects. Works with the Data Storage Manager to support and integrate the associated HPC local and wide-area, multi-hundred terabyte file systems with the HPC systems

 

·       Develop HPC architecture, conduct design and performs code reviews for team’s development.

 

·       Develop software programs/proof of concepts; identifies and implements opportunities to enhance customer solutions with software components.

 

·       Manage software projects, and author project documents. Provide technical oversight for multiple software projects

 

Qualifications:

 

     ·       Comprehensive knowledge of C++, mastery of numerical and parallel computing methods.

 

·       Advanced understanding of the HPC market segment, HPC solutions (platforms and architectures) and its software/hardware technologies. Hands-on working and programming experience with (large) HPC cluster systems (Linux/Windows)].

 

·       Moderate skill level using MPI, Open MP, MPICH, PVM, GPU and other parallel tools

 

·       Advance knowledge of large scale complex networked research computing clusters and systems (especially grid computing), including development, programming, and use of regional and national distributed HPC environments such as NYS grid, SURA grid, Open Science Grid and the Tera Grid.

 

·       Comprehensive engineering and problem solving skills

 

·        Commercial simulation package experience preferred; Maxwell, HFSS, SIWave, etc.



 

  Software Development:

 

·       Advanced knowledge of software engineering methodologies and practices

 

·       Advanced knowledge of C++, Java, .net, FORTRAN & C.

 

·       Moderate knowledge of UNIX, Linux and Windows platforms.

 

·       Ability to author clear design documents technical presentations using leading productivity tools, including Microsoft Office applications.

 

·       Advanced technical leadership skills, proven ability to mentor others

 

·       Advanced project management skills

 

·       Advanced written and verbal communication skills.

 

·       Ability to effectively interact in a diverse team environment

 

·       Moderate managerial skills: ability to effectively motivate, coach and train employees.

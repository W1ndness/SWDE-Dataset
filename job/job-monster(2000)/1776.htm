About the Job

Company Summary

Lumeris™ offers provider performance management solutions to health plans and other health care delivery organizations. Through a unique combination of technology, content and services, Lumeris helps its clients identify and solve care and utilization problems by engaging their contracted providers with actionable information and decision support proven to positively influence behavior, lower costs and improve outcomes.



Position Summary

This person serves as the subject matter expert for the Essence Development staff including the web development team related to the architecture and topology of the custom written application for Essence, including the Essence customer facing web sites.   This person will provide guidance to Development as to best practices, administration of the Windows, Web and TFS Servers, upgrade and release support and day-to-day support of the production, development, QA environments.

They will work with other Infrastructure team members, developers and management as required to meet project requirements, adhere to standards and performance criteria and meet deadlines

Position Requirements

  • Investigate user problems and needs, identify their source, and determine best possible solutions
  • Work with the web development team to push code to production servers and work with them on best practices for web server configuration
  • Push code to production from staging.
  • Configure web servers as required from the web development team.  These changes will need to be tested and confirmed as best practices.
  • Ensures issues are identified, tracked, reported on and resolved in a timely manner
  • Monitor system(s) performance and capacity
  • Develop and maintain Windows related standards, policies and procedures according to established standards.
  • Implement operating system patches and upgrades as necessary; participating in the patch and change control review process.
  • Analyze development project proposals to include identifying potential problem areas and recommend optimum approaches for project path
  • Partners with customers to determine requirements and identifies opportunities for improving/resolving issues
  • Actively participates in sprint meetings with the development group.
  • Can work with outside consultants on projects
  • Other duties as assigned


Position Qualifications

  • Must have expertise in Windows administration, Active Directory administration, Firewall knowledge and VM environments.
  • Must have expertise in web technologies and web server administration such as IIS
  • Knowledge of application programming, database and system design
  • Knowledge of structured analysis and technical design techniques
  • Knowledge of database management and file access methods.
  • Knowledge of SQL statements and databases.
  • Knowledge of Microsoft development technologies (.NET, Silverlight, etc.)
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving, plus strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Communicates with team members and business owners with professionalism and in a way to foster teamwork
  • Delivers informative, well-organized presentations
  • Conceive and deliver innovative, robust solutions within agreed delivery timelines
  • Motivation to research and resolve technical issues, your own bugs or someone else’s
  • Eagerness to learn new technologies, work around their early glitches, and use them to create excellent customer experiences and winning business advantage
  • Pushes creative thinking beyond the boundaries of existing industry practices and business owners mindsets 
  • Ability to work well in a team environment
  • Flexibility
  • High Level of accuracy
  • Must be able and willing to work a flexible schedule including nights and weekends.
  • Excellent organization skills and attention to detail



We offer competitive salary and excellent benefits, including 401k, EOE

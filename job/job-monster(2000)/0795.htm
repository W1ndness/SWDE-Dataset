Internet Video Support Engineer

About the Job

Open Text™ (Nasdaq: OTEX; TSX: OTC) is the world's largest independent provider of Enterprise Content Management software. The company's solutions manage information for all types of business, compliance and industry requirements in the world's largest companies, government agencies and professional service firms. Open Text supports approximately 46,000 customers and millions of users in 114 countries and 12 languages. For more information about Open Text, visit www.opentext.com .

Job Description:

Working for the Open Text Video Services (OTVS) team, Product Applications Engineers support cutting-edge technologies to help deliver world-class Web Media Solutions to customers as they deploy and maintain the OTVS hosted platform.

You will serve as a project manager and technical support resource as new clients integrate the OTVS solution within their environment or with existing clients as they extend their use of the platform. Responsibilities include assisting customers as they deploy and configure our products within their environment, answering technical questions about the platform, helping customers determine the best way to integrate, demonstrating capabilities of the product, sharing best practices, helping troubleshoot issues, and generally providing consultative support to our clients.

As a spokesperson, you will help create a positive impression of Open Text and ensure our clients are satisfied.

Responsibilities:
• Consult directly with customers to gather needs, define development of solutions to meet these needs, respond to and track technical issues to their complete and thorough resolution, and confer with clients to provide clear communications during development of these solutions to ensure user acceptance
• Provide escalated technical support for other team members, and actively participate in our online technical documentation and knowledge database review to maintain accurate product information
• Manage customer projects from concept to launch, providing project management and technical support during the launch phase and after to ensure a quality customer experience
• Work as part of the team to pursue new business, complete project proposals, RFPs, project scoping, scheduling and pricing estimates
• Provide all levels of technical support and serve as liaison between customers and Development teams
• Contribute to product management, development, and quality assurance testing of new product releases

Qualifications:
• 3+ years of support or development experience with web-based applications
• Strong customer service, communication and interpersonal skills
• Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously.
• Advanced troubleshooting skills
• Knowledgeable in Internet video, encoding and streaming media technologies
• Strong knowledge of J2EE, Linux, Apache, XML, HTML, JavaScript, PHP, AJAX technologies
• Hands on experience with relational databases like MySQL
• Advanced understanding of networking technologies a plus
• Experience with content management systems a plus
• Ability to be a self-starter in a dynamic environment


Academic:
• The position requires a BS in Computer Science (or a related field), or equivalent industry experience.

Six - Sigma Certified Change Agent

About the Job

HERE’S WHAT WE NEED…

One of Resolvits major manufacturing (CPG) clients has an immediate need for a Six - Sigma Certified Change Agent with experience in the Consumer Package Goods industry.

This position require 95% travel through the region.

The primary objective of a Continuous Improvement Change Agent - Logistics/Advanced Planning and Scheduling (APS) is to coordinate and strategically lead supply chain performance improvement across the Transportation Network and within the APS department and cross functional partners in the business, distribution centers, and, if needed, at suppliers. This will be accomplished through effective working relationships with several Change Agents and leadership teams, lean production system design, effective problem-solving techniques, the design and management of performance improvement initiatives, training and coaching of personnel in all of the above, and advising the senior supply chain executives on the nature and progress of network wide improvement activities.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

-Establish meaningful metrics across a number of sites to monitor performance and identify the nature and cause of problems through effective problem solving methods.

-Lead root cause problem solving process, once the issue/opportunity has been identified, to ascertain the best solution/improvement for that site and/or Supply Chain as a whole. Solutions will need to be communicated and coordinated with other Continuous Improvement Change Agents.

-Keeping total Supply Chain in mind, diagnose and evaluate performance (cost, quality, delivery) challenges stemming from demand management and forecasting, inventory management, capacity and production planning, and production process design and execution.

-Develop and implement solutions based on Lean Production system design and transformation principles, tools, and techniques (also the tools of Six Sigma).

-Work with sites to design common work standards, quality control charts and systems, and other elements of superior manufacturing practice to aid plant improvement.

-Create alternative methods of production control and scheduling that use best practice techniques and better connect the plant and its suppliers with customer and seasonal demand patterns.

-Train and coach personnel in these and other capabilities. Direct and oversee the rollout of these activities in APS/Logistics.

Qualifications

-Bachelor's degree in a relevant business discipline preferred, Master's degree is desirable or related experience.

-Ten plus years of Continuous Improvement (Lean/Six Sigma) experience as a primary function of your responsibilities required.

-Experience in a functional area of the business preferably APS and or Logistics including progressively more responsible management roles a plus.

-Toyota Production System or equivalent experience highly preferred.

-Consulting industry experience implementing LEAN a plus.

-Ability to learn and apply lean concepts and principles within functional area are critical.

-Influence, Listening and problem solving skills are critical.

-Leadership abilities are very important.

-Conceptualization skills are important.

-Software applications such as Lotus Notes, Word, Excel, Access, Power Point, etc. are desirable.

-Self motivated/driven and be able to create enthusiasm for improvement initiatives.

-Ability to travel up to 100% to suppliers, supply chain plants, customers, etc. to fulfill responsibilities.

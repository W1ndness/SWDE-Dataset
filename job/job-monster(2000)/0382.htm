About the Job

   Burrtec Waste Industries, Inc. is a well established refuse and recycling collection company serving over fifteen municipalities throughout Southern California and employing over 1200 full time employees. Burrtec continues to grow through company acquisitions and successful bid proposals. Burrtec is a family owned company that firmly believes the key element to our success is knowing how important each employee is in the organization.  We have the following position at our Corporate Fontana location:

Job Title:        .Net Developer/Analyst

Location:        Corporate-North

Reports to:     Regional Manager of IT

Status:            Exempt

 

Position Summary:

 

 

Serve as a programmer/analyst for the company’s ERP system currently being developed by a third party vendor.  Expected to deliver high-quality Business Requirements, System Requirements and System Design documentation as well as verify and report to upper management on the design and requirement documents that have already been submitted by the third party vendor.  Provide input into Business Systems product solution and road maps.  Must possess a strong level of business knowledge and business process best practices.  Experience in planning, development, implementation and maintenance of Business applications.  Develop and aid in the development of the company’s  software applications programming from written specifications.

 

 

JOB REQUIREMENTS

 

KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS:                   

·         7+ years of experience in .Net development.

·         Experience working with and developing for ERP applications.

·         A solid understanding of accounting principles and an understanding of accounting workflows (invoicing, ledgers, etc.)

·         Solid object-oriented design and implementation skills.

·         Experience with Object Relational Mapping tools is a must.

·         .NET Windows Forms development experience using C# (Windows Forms as opposed to ASP.NET) using Visual Studio 2008.

·         Strong knowledge of SQL Server 2008, stored procedures, functions.

·         Experience with functional programming languages including F# is a must.

·         Sense of innate urgency to complete projects on time and with high quality.

·         Open communicator and can jump in (when appropriate) to help other team members.

·         Excellent communications skills – (oral, written, and presentation).

·         Excellent interpersonal skills in areas such as teamwork, facilitation and negotiation.

·         Remains up-to-date on current industry trends through courses, reading, etc.

·         Ability to work independently when needed.

                                                               

RESPONSIBILITIES                                  

·         Help the company evaluate the current development path for its ERP software system being developed in house in conjunction with a third party vendor.

·         Have a strong ability to interface with a third party vendor reviewing ERP development plan.

·         Help develop, publish, and implement an annual and a long-term development plan for review and approval by senior management. Update the plan as the business requires or as directed by senior management.

·         Develop timely and standard methods to report to executive management that show development progress in relation to plan.

·         Learn software applications and provide applications support – Investigate into the application issues or failures and help develop fixes for them.

·         Develop or aid in the development of the company’s ERP software where needed and as directed.

·         Other job duties as assigned by management.

PHYSICAL:                                              

  • Position requires ability to stand, walk and sit for long periods at a time.
  • Position requires ability to stoop, bend, climb and lift

  

                                                               

WORKING CONDITIONS:                 

  • Must use company required safety equipment
  • Will drive to different sites on as needed basis

 

We provide competitive salary commensurate upon experience, and EXCELLENT Benefits package including 401K and 100% paid medical/dental/life insurance and holidays/vacations/PTO’s.

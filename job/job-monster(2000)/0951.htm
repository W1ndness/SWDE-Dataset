About the Job

Sr. Software Engineer / Technical Lead – Java and Healthcare Technologies

A.    Role Description

As part of the client’s Professional Services team, to design and develop programmatic data interfaces for clients leading healthcare interoperability product, to meet client’s integration needs. Such interfaces can be both inbound and outbound and will be based on a wide range of technologies, from flat file batch processing to real-time web service calls. The candidate in this role will work individually or as part of a team, supporting one or more client engagements. Interaction with end clients may also be required.

B.     Necessary Skills/Experience/Qualifications

o   Software programming experience of at-least 5 years in Java technologies

o   High proficiency with J2EE.

o   Experience working within complex custom-built Java-based frameworks

o   High proficiency with Oracle DBMS and SQL

o   Understanding of Internet concepts such as protocols and security aspects

o   Working knowledge of Linux

o   Knowledge of Weblogic application server

o   Good communication skills

o   BE or MCA degree or equivalent

C.    Desirable Skills

o   Experience working in setting up services

o   Experience developing data feeds and data conversions

o   Knowledge of database administration and data modeling

o   Experience working with commercial business workflow and/or ETL tools

o   Understanding of Agile methodologies


D.    Location:  Boston, United States of America


CitiusTech – Quick Company Profile:

CitiusTech is a leading provider of healthcare technology consulting, software engineering services and professional services across North America and Europe.   CitiusTech’s development centers and offices are located at Mumbai (in Powai and in Navi Mumbai) and in Florida, Washington and Singapore.

In addition to healthcare product engineering and interoperability, CitiusTech has strong practice areas in healthcare business intelligence, disease and care management, and consumer health.  CitiusTech clients include leading healthcare ISVs, provider organizations, and healthcare services organizations. Our clients include some of the biggest names in healthcare – Microsoft, 3M, Carestream, Fuji Medical Systems, Kaiser Permanente, TELUS Health, Walgreens and United Healthcare.

CitiusTech has a strong technology team with significant experience in software development and testing technologies, together with strong knowledge of healthcare workflows and standards.   CitiusTech’s team is drawn from leading global and Indian companies, as well as best-in-class educational institutions from India and abroad.  In fact, CitiusTech has amongst the largest number of certified HL7 professionals worldwide. CitiusTech also has strategic relationships with several large technology organizations. CitiusTech is also very active in industry participation and has representations on several healthcare standards committees (including HL7-X.12 standards organization) as well as regular presentations at leading healthcare technology conferences. 

CitiusTech’s ability to provide cutting-edge technology expertise coupled with our strong knowledge in the healthcare domain and our ability leverage our global presence provides us with a unique competitive advantage in the marketplace, and enables us to sustain our highly profitable business model.

CitiusTech has over 400 professionals worldwide and has a consistent track record of growing over 50% per annum over the past 4 years.

Learn more about CitiusTech on our corporate website – www.citiustech.com.

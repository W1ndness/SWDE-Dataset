Information Architect & Usability Testing Specialist

About the Job

Paladin has a great opportunity for Senior and Mid level IA’s – Information Architect & Usability Testing Specialist for a client located in Chicago.  This is a great opportunity to get your foot in the door with a growing team. These opportunities may be direct hire or temp to hire.  Please be sure to review the positions requirements and apply if you are a fit.   

 

Location- Chicago

Start- ASAP

Mid level 3-5 years
Senior level 5+ years

 

Job information

Our client is in need of a senior and mid level IA’s that can do eCommerce or transactional work.  The role will require the background and experience to create, analyze, produce and critique the standard user experience designs and deliverables associated with the role of an IA.  There is also analysis of use cases, the creation of use cases based on requirements and the ability to integrate such documentation with subsequent IA and usability test artifacts. 

Job Skills

▪ A practitioner of commerce web site user experience arts and sciences
▪ Analysis of commerce web site requirements and use cases
▪ Development of artifacts germane to that of information architecture (eg., simple use cases, wireframes of varying fidelity, flow diagrams, business proposal treatments etc.)
▪  Development of usability testing strategies, cases, scenarios and processes
▪  Test subject interviewer and test session moderator


Usability Testing   (Primary function in this multifaceted role)
This is the prime function of this role.  While information architecture (IA) background is essential to this role, the candidate must have experience developing usability testing strategies and execution methods for e-commerce sites or sites with equivalent complex tasks.  By analyzing use cases, wireframes and screen flows, this candidate will be able to develop usability test cases, scenarios and test scripts for end customer interactions in a controlled setting.  Conducting interviews with business, creative and test subjects and moderating test sessions will be a key part of this multifaceted role.  The candidate must have the ability to read the test subjects responses, verbal and non verbal; know when to intervene in the test to get the subject back on tract and when to step back and let the subject struggle through the test; must have an easy style able to put test subjects at ease; must be able to model how a test subject is to participate in the test etc.  The candidate must be able to manage detailed logistical events with many people on precise schedules yet be able to adapt when schedules and priorities change.  At least 1-2 years experience.

Information Architecture   (Secondary function)
The role will require the background and experience to analyze, produce and critique the standard artifacts associated with the role of an information architect.  At least 3-5 years experience.

Business Analysis   (Tertiary Function)
The role will include analysis of use cases, the creation of use cases based on requirements and the ability to integrate such documentation with subsequent IA and usability test artifacts.  At least 5-7 years experience.

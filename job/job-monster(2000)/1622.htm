LEAD WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPER

About the Job

Lead Web Application Developer
Arizona State University
University Technology Office-Web Consulting Services
Job Id# 25316

Duties and Responsibilities:
The Web Consulting Services team in ASU's University Technology Office is looking for a lead Drupal developer with experience managing a small team.

Applicants for for this position should have experience seeing projects from requirements gathering and analysis to through to site building, development, testing, quality assurance, delivery, and follow-up support. You should have a solid foundation of PHP and MySQL skills, including experience developing Drupal modules and themes. Ideal candidates will have experience working with the full LAMP stack and be able to troubleshoot issues in all layers of the web application infrastructure.

As lead of the team, you will communicate directly with customers and vendors to identify and resolve issues, plan projects, and coordinate a team of Drupal developers, site builders, themers, and support staff. Excellent customer service skills are essential.

DAYS AND SCHEDULE:
Monday-Friday 8:00AM-5:00PM; may include evenings and weekends based on project needs

Department Statement/Gen Info:
The mission of the University Technology Office is to provide technology leadership for the New American University. Through its three divisions--Operations, Development and Customer Care--the UTO helps ASU create and maintain innovative technology solutions and services that advance learning and discovery at the University.

Standard Statement:
Arizona State University is a new model for American higher education, an unprecedented combination of academic excellence, entrepreneurial energy and broad access. This New American University is a single, unified institution comprising four differentiated campuses positively impacting the economic, social, cultural and environmental health of the communities it serves. Its research is inspired by real world application blurring the boundaries that traditionally separate academic disciplines. ASU serves more than 70,000 students in metropolitan Phoenix, Arizona, the nation's fifth largest city. ASU champions intellectual and cultural diversity, and welcomes students from all fifty states and more than one hundred nations across the globe.

 

Minimum Qualifications:
Masters degree in Graphic Design, Business, Information Technology or closely related AND eight (8) years web, database or related experience with three (3) years full project management experience within an IT environment; OR, Any equivalent combination of experience and/or education from which comparable knowledge, skills and abilities have been achieved.

Desired Qualifications:
Demonstrated Customer Service experience fostering partnerships, developing understanding of needs, issues, problems resulting in a win/win relationship. Evidence of leading strategic initiatives to develop solutions and processes to meet needs by identifying unique innovative approaches; evidence of information gathering to obtain thorough knowledge of objectives. Experience with project management which includes planning and organization that clearly articulates deliverables, objectives, results and outcomes and establishes milestones and timetables. demonstrated experience with principles, methods and techniques of process/quality improvement ; Experience working with a team, providing leadership skills and conducting performance coaching and feedback.
1-3 years of Drupal experience, including module (PHP & MySQL) and theme development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). Understanding of network architectures, request and security models. Working knowledge of version control systems and change and issue management methodologies. Experience with performance optimization of a LAMP architecture, including the use of additional, non-traditional technologies. knowledge of web services methodologies. Effective verbal and written communication skills.

Fibre Channel/NAS Storage System Administrator

About the Job

Become part of our industry leading virtualization architecture team utilizing state-of-the-art technology with EMC, Cisco, and VMware as the cornerstone of CareCore National’s cloud infrastructure.

 

CareCore National is a fast-growing, successful healthcare management company providing services to over 25 major health plans nationwide.   Headquarters located between Hilton Head Island, SC and Savannah, GA, CareCore National offers a unique combination of corporate challenge, career opportunity, technical advancement and coastal lifestyle.

 

CareCore National is currently seeking a Fibre Channel/NAS Storage System Administrator to assist with design, implementation, and maintenance of current large scale physical and virtual consolidated infrastructures as well as with the design, test and implementation of future technologies within virtual space. The successful candidate will be a part of leading edge technology only implemented by 2% of world’s top corporations.

 

 

Responsibilities include:

·        Architectural design, testing, implementation, and maintenance of a large scale EMC SAN and NAS infrastructure as well as its replication between multiple data centers.

·        Systems integration and deployment with Cisco, VMware, Microsoft, and AIX support engineers.

 

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree or technical equivalent.
  • Minimum 5 years proven EMC Storage Administration in complex enterprise environments.
  • Proven capability with EMC ControlCenter, Symmetrix Manager, Celerra Replicator.
  • Experience with multiple OS (Microsoft, Unix).
  • EMC Networker, EMC DataDomain, EMC Replication manager experience a plus.

 

To apply, send a resume with salary requirements to careers@carecorenational.com with the subject line:  Storage Administrator. EOE

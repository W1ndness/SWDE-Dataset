Network Engineer

About the Job


Do you thrive in an environment where innovation and change are encouraged? If you are a big-picture person who is not afraid to make decisions, take responsibility for them, and strives to complete things on time - we want to talk with you!  Equivalent Data stands above the rest...we continue to raise the bar and seek people who want to make a real difference.

 

Equivalent Data, a leader in the electronic document discovery and management industry, is seeking a Network Engineer.

Position Overview

Candidate will be immediately responsible for account administration, PC and server configuration, hardware and software installation, backups, Internet connectivity, systems security, problems resolution, network expansion planning and implementation.

Additional responsibilities include: remotely monitor, administer and troubleshoot web, LAN, and WAN systems including, but not limited to, IIS, SQL 2000/2005/2008 R2, Exchange 2003/2007, Microsoft Networking Services, CISCO Firewalls, routers, switches and VoIP.  This position will require overtime and travel (Approximately 5%-10%).

The responsibilities of the position include:

 

Daily Responsibilities

·         Provide 1st level computer/technical support to all locations.  If an appropriate/satisfactory resolution of problem isn't identified, elevate the problem to 2nd level support or contact alternate support providers.

·         Check previous evening’s backup jobs for completion and take appropriate action if the job has failed.

·         Check Citrix/Terminal Services disconnected connection status and clear when appropriate.

·         Check virus status and definitions on all domain AV servers.  Take corrective steps if viral activity is discovered.

·         Check and monitor Anti-Virus systems on the Exchange Server(s) and distribute warning E-mails when new substantial risks are released/discovered.

·         Check Nagios Server and correct any down services/servers.

·         Monitor performance and troubleshoot as required.

 

Periodic Responsibilities

·         Create/modify/delete users, groups, distribution groups, rights, permissions, and membership on the appropriate domain controller or global catalog.

·         Install, configure, and administer file and print services on local/remote F&P servers.

·         Create/modify/delete users on mail servers when required.

·         Provide support for the creation of new web servers both internally and on the internet.

·         Configure and manage routers, switches, firewalls and other miscellaneous infrastructure hardware pieces when changes are required.

·         As required, problem solve any PC issues, both local and remote using the appropriate tool(s).

·         Recommend new network architectures to improve performance, storage and reliability.

·         Assess software/hardware that will aide in the administration of the network.

·         Assess software/hardware that will improve our product offering/service to our customers, both external and internal.

·         Install and configure VoIP phones & devices as they are required.

·         Create/delete Unity Voicemail boxes as user’s accounts are created/removed on the network.

·         Order and/or build new servers, networking hardware, and desktop systems when requested.

·         Warranty, service, and upgrade hardware as needed/requested.

·         Provide training to management, production, and technical personnel as required.

·         Perform software & hardware upgrades and OS migration as necessary.

·         Travel to remote sites and perform maintenance that can't be provided over the WAN links.

·         Setup and assist remote users in IPSec & PPTP connections.

·         Monitor software and hardware changes on all PC based equipment.

·         Track software changes (Licensing) and perform corrective actions to guarantee compliance (either through purchase and/or un-installation).

·         Utilize Global and Local Policies to improve pc performance and/or to protect the network from both malicious and inadvertent actions.

·         Diagnose and repair Spyware, malware, virus, and Trojan issues as the present themselves.

·         Perform upgrades/modifications in a test lab to determine feasibility and identify problems before affecting production.

·         Test and research emerging technologies, report findings, and document when appropriate.

·         Plan and coordinate multiple activities simultaneously and respond effectively to a wide range of business technology needs.

·         Any other duties as assigned by ownership and/or management.

 

Requirements

·         Experience in a Network Administration environment.

·         Knowledge of principles, standards, functionality, operations, methods, processes, tools and equipment pertaining to LAN/WAN administration.

·         Internal and external network configuration and maintenance.

·         Security Protocols.

·         Active Directory Management.

·         Backup Strategies.

·         Symantec Endpoint Protection.

·         Exchange Server 2007, 2003 and 2000.

·         Windows 7, Vista, XP and 2000 Professional.

·         MS Office 2010, 2007, 2003, XP and 2000 Professional.

·         Desktop and server hardware and diagnostics.

·         Network services and protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, etc.).

·         Printer setup and administration.

·         Familiarity with switches, routers and firewalls.

·         VPN remote access with Windows PPTP and Linux IPSec.

·         Heavy lifting of fifty (50) pounds or more. 

 

Other Skills/Abilities

·         Excellent written and verbal communication skills (English).

·         Functionality, application and operation of client/server technology in a network environment.

·         Excellent interpersonal skills, communications and overall dependability are essential.

·         A bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field, or the equivalent combination of education and experience that would demonstrate the capability to successfully perform the essential functions of this position.

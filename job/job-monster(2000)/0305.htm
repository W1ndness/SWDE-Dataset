Maintenance Renewals Manager - San Mateo

About the Job

Job Description:



RESPSONSIBILITIES:


  • Lead a team of Maintenance Renewal Representatives.
  • Hire, train, motivate, and develop Maintenance Renewal Representatives through coaching, feedback, and performance management
  • Implement metrics based management approach to ensure individual production standards are achieved
  • Carry team revenue quota, and drive team to consistently meet and exceed individual quota goals and performance objectives. Develop and maintain automated management reports that provide visibility into key departmental performance indicators
  • Work with Marketing to design and execute demand-generation strategies and campaign priorities to achieve consistent pipeline growth objectives
  • Define and implement training programs and automated systems infrastructure to streamline processes and ensure maximum effectiveness and productivity of team
  • Conduct regular calls with field sales reps and management to validate lead quality and program results
  • Conduct weekly lead activity and pipeline reviews with individual team members
  • Stay abreast of new telesales/web technologies to ensure that organization sets “best of breed” standards
  • Work with Sales and Marketing VP’s to set quarterly and annual goals that align with corporate objectives
  • Regularly review and update metrics management indicators, goals, and department objectives
  • Promote the success of the group throughout the organization


QUALIFICATIONS:


  • Minimum 7 years experience with 3-5 years experience managing inside sales/ Maintenance Renewal Representatives organization in technology industry. Enterprise software sales experience preferred.
  • Proven track record establishing and driving teams to achieve measurable goals in a highly automated sales development environment
  • Experience managing multiple initiatives and projects simultaneously, including:
  • Hiring
  • Training
  • Systems and process implementation
  • Compensation and incentive plans
  • Individual skills development
  • Professional presentation skills and demeanor on the phone and in person
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Committed to personal and professional success of individuals. Ability to cultivate sales talents, identify strengths and weakness and provide disciplined process approach to business.
  • Excellent organizational skills with ability to work on multiple assignments and to complete them within established deadlines.
  • College degree

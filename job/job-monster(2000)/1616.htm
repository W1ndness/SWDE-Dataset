About the Job

Quality Assurance Analyst (Business Analyst)

 

Company Description:

 RTTS is the premier professional services organization that specializes in providing software quality for critical business applications. Based in New York City and serving Fortune 500 since 1996, RTTS has worked with over 300 clients on hundreds of projects and forged strategic partnerships with IBM, Microsoft and Hewlett Packard, which provide us access to the most comprehensive enterprise software solutions in the industry. 

 

·                Entry-level positions available – an ideal fit for recent graduates and career changers!

·                Work on assignments at our list of Fortune 500 client sites and gain valuable exposure to many areas of business and technology.

·                The most comprehensive up-front training program in the industry. 

·                eLearning courses and one-on-one mentoring provided.

·                Competitive salaries, excellent benefits, and emphasis on work-life balance.

·                Our work environment is challenging, supportive, educational and fun!  Our office culture naturally fosters innovation and professional growth. 

 

Job Description: 

Using your strong analytical, interpersonal and technology skills, you will be responsible for delivering software quality solutions to our Fortune 500 customers.  Assignments may last for a period of 1 week to 12 months and beyond, depending on our clients’ needs.  

 

Responsibilities include:

·                Perform job function at client site. 

·                Schedule project tasks and maintain timely delivery of service.

·                Extract and reverse engineer business and technical requirements  

·                Design use cases and test cases outlining application work flow.

·                Execute, evaluate and track project results.

·                Analyze results and provide feedback.

 

Job Qualifications:

 Must possess an undergraduate, graduate level or certificate degree.

·                Analytical, logical thinkers.

·                Strong technical skills and basic knowledge of computer hardware and software.  

·                Professional appearance and exceptional communication skills.

·                Customer service experience a plus. 

·                The position is available at our midtown Manhattan office location.   You will be required to travel to client sites in and around the metro area.  Travel outside of the metro area is also possible but not the norm.  

·                Live within 40 commuting miles of our Manhattan office location.     

·                Applicants for employment must be authorized to work in the U.S. without the need for sponsorship

 

Compensation:  $40-50K depending on experience.

 

Benefits include:  Medical, dental, 401k, healthcare flexible spending accounts, dependent care flexible spending accounts, short and long term disability, life insurance, TransitCheks, bonus plan programs, tuition reimbursement and more. 

Applicants must submit a cover letter with their resume to Monster-NYC-QAA@rttsweb.com

 

To learn more visit our website at www.rtts.com

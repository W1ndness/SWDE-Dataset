PC-Lan Support Tech

About the Job

Job Description:

Uses basic diagnostic tools to troubleshoot desktop, laptop, and peripheral hardware, software, applications and related network problems for individual users and resolves basic issues or escalates as needed. Completes routine work orders and move/add/change requests including patches, upgrades, software deployments, installations, repairs, and preventive maintenance on desktop hardware and related systems and completes related documentation. Provides routine training and technical assistance to users at the desktop level. Learns LAN design and configuration concepts. Assists in testing, implementing, maintaining and controlling PC/LAN systems.

 

Major Job Responsibilities:

·       Maintain all hardware assets within the organization. (Workstations, Laptops, Wireless Devices, etc.)

·       User account maintenance including account creation, email setup, access control.

·       Assist with remote site setups.

·       Support enterprise level virus, spyware and malware application.

·       Provide support to project implementation teams.

·       Maintain high volume scanning equipment.

·       Maintain desktop operating systems (Windows XP/Vista/Windows 7)

·       Provide application support to end users

·       Monitor help desk.

 

Required Skills:

·       Experience supporting workstations and peripherals in an enterprise environment.

·       Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

·       Excellent problem solving skills.

·       Basic networking knowledge (Active Directory, DNS, DHCP, TCP/IP, Routing and Remote Access).

·       Ability to support various hardware platforms (HP, Dell, Apple).

·       3+ years of hardware support experience.

·       Help desk experience.

·       Some travel will be required.

·       Flexible hours with overtime potential. 

 

 

Education and Experience Preferred:

·       Microsoft Certified Professional certification.

·       CompTIA A+ Certification

·       Implementing and supporting VMware View

·       Experience supporting Blackberry and Windows Mobile Devices

·       Document imaging experience

·       Experience with Digitech Systems (Paperflow and Papervision)

·       Healthcare related business experience.

·       Experience supporting high volume scanning equipment (Bell & Howell, Canon)

·       Familiarity with HIPPA and ARRA guidelines.

 

Super;visory Relationships:

 

  • Supervisor:     Reports to IT Director
  • Supervises:     None

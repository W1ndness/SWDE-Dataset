About the Job

This position is open to U.S. citizens by birth or naturalization and U.S. nationals.

Rochester Electronics, headquartered in Newburyport, Massachusetts with a campus of corporate, manufacturing and distribution facilities, is licensed and authorized by over 50 semiconductor manufacturers to provide a continuing manufacturing source for mature/discontinued products. Manufacturers that have authorized Rochester include AMD, Analog Devices, Fairchild, Freescale, Intel, Intersil, National, Texas Instruments, and many others. Rochester manufactures thousands of devices in a variety of package styles from commercial through space manufacturing flows.

For industries with long life cycles, Rochester provides a continuing manufacturing supply of critical devices. Inventory includes the world's largest supply of silicon wafers, over 10 billion die and finished devices, over 5 billion. Rochester's design and technology office, located in Rockville, Maryland, provides complete silicon re-creation. Certified to ISO-9001:2008 and QML MIL-PRF-38535, Rochester provides the world's most comprehensive solution for mature/discontinued products.

Our continuing mission is to provide a professional, dynamic, energetic and family oriented environment. Since 1981, Rochester has constantly improved and developed our services, capabilities and products.

Our excellent employee benefits program includes many tremendous features:

  • 401K Employer Matching Program.
  • Company funded Retirement Plan.
  • Comprehensive and low-cost Health, Dental and Vision Plan.
  • In-house state-of-the-art Fitness Center (Newburyport, MA). Top of the line equipment from Nautilus, Precor, StarTrac and more. Multi-media sound and video systems.
  • Company sponsored wellness programs (Weight Watchers, Anti-Smoking, etc.).
  • Company fun days.
  • Paid vacation and sick days.
  • A company you can feel proud of and enjoy coming to work for.



Summary Of Position
Responsible for analyzing and researching all business systems and related processes for best possible use and solutions.

Essential Job Requirements
• Work with each department to analyze key systems, operations, functions and processes. Research best practices, solutions and systems for the greatest productivity, efficiency and accuracy.
•  Research business system capabilities and needs for best productivity and process.
•  Maintain standard operating procedures for all systems.
•  Provide technical support and training.

Required Education
•  Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in Information Systems, Computer Sciences, Engineering, MIS, etc.

Required Experience
•  5+ years of business system and process analysis, development and integration.
•  In depth understanding and capabilities of business operations and systems, including ERP, MRP and distribution.
•  Demonstrated abilities of working with others in a collaborative, professional and effective manner.

Required Skills
•  Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.
•  Extensive business system and operations knowledge.
•  Ability to collaborate in a friendly and positive manner.
•  Exceptional computer and application skills.

Physical Requirements
Must be able to sit at a computer workstation for extended periods of time. Ability to move about the office freely.

Mental Requirements
Must have strong analytical and problem solving skills.

Equipment Used
Computer, telephone, fax machine, scanners.

Supervisory Responsibilities
None

Working Conditions
Works primarily in an office environment, but may at times be required to work in the warehouse or test area.

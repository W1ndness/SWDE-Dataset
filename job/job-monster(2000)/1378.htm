SDS IT- VoIP Engineer (Cisco)

About the Job

The VoIP Engineer will be responsible in enhancing, configuring, maintaining and supporting our existing and future Global Cisco VoIP Infrastructure. Our network includes Cisco UCCM 7, UCCX 7 and Unity 7, Cisco ASA Firewall, Cisco WAAS, Cisco Routers, and Cisco Switches, Cisco MARS, Cisco Wireless  and nonetheless Microsoft ISA Server. She/he will work as a team to upgrade and extend our Cisco VoIP System, perform maintenance, IOS/Software updates and backups of all the networking equipment worldwide. This position requires on-call rotation duty, off-hours for network maintenance & scheduled downtime and some travelling (4-6 times per calendar year). This person will be assisting the Information Technology group with daily support of ongoing Voice, Network and System projects.

 

Required Skills
  • Ability to function independently with minimal supervision and within a collaborative team environment.  The candidate must be approachable, diplomatic and adaptable to a fast-paced, high-volume, deadline-oriented atmosphere.  The candidate must be a team player with a positive 'can do' attitude who must be able to assist team members when required.  The candidate must set aside time for proper documentation and project planning.
  • In-depth knowledge of Cisco VoIP configuration and troubleshooting is required. This includes:
    • Cisco Unified Communication Manager 7.X, Cisco Unity Unified Messaging 7.x
  • In-depth knowledge of Cisco Call Center configuration, troubleshooting and scripting. This includes:
    •  Cisco Unified Contact Center Express7.x 
  • In-depth knowledge of Cisco IOS configuration and troubleshooting across multiple platforms, including: Cisco routers, switches, security devices, and wireless access points is required. This includes:
    • Cisco Switching 3750, 65XX, 45XX - Cisco Routers 28XX, 26XX - Cisco Wide Area Application Services - Cisco Voice Gateway
  • Good knowledge of IP, IP Routing, and IP Multicasting, including MPLS, HSRP, VRRP, BGP, EIGRP is required.
  • In-depth knowledge of LAN Switching, including VLANs, VLAN Trunking, and Spanning Tree is required.
  • Good knowledge of MS Windows Server 2003/2008 systems required.
  • Excellent documentation skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple complex projects simultaneously.

 

Optional Skills

  • Knowledge of Security, including Cisco ASA Firewalls.
  • Experience with networking tools and applications, such as Syslog, MARS, TACACS knowledge is desired.

IT ANALYST

About the Job

About us:  Spectrum, one of the country’s largest and most successful athletic club operators in the upscale segment of the industry, has been helping members meet their health and fitness goals for nearly three decades.  With $100M in revenues and more than 100,000 members in 23 locations clustered  in Southern California and San Antonio, TX,  we take pride in creating a sense of community whereby members are offered numerous options to achieve results while experiencing exceptional personalized service via resort-like amenities at best-in-class facilities.  www.SpectrumClubs.com

►The Opportunity:   As a member of our Information Technology team, the IT Analyst will provide end user IT hardware and software technical support for the Texas Region.  The primary mission for the position it to assure user satisfaction while maintaining the integrity of the Spectrum IT systems.

►If you are:

·         A well organized, “customer-centric,” trustworthy team player who is genuine in his/her approach to dealing with people, and engenders trust and commitment from those around him or her.

·         One who employs sound and accurate judgment and exhibit a high level of personal integrity.

·         An intelligent and articulate person who can relate to people at all levels of the organization, has good written communication skills and verbally exhibits an open and collaborative communication style.

·         Great with attention to detail, and will be very thorough with his or her daily duties.

 

►Who Can:

·         Provide first and second level end-user technical support, including hardware (computers, printers, phones and other devices) and software support (Microsoft Windows, Microsoft Office, Membership System (RCM). This includes face-to-face and phone help-desk computer support.

·         Travel to Texas club locations to provide on-site hardware and software support to end users.

·         Install and replace technology devices, including computers, monitors, printers, network switches and firewalls.

·         Work with other IT personnel to develop and maintain a “knowledge base” of information technology systems including installation, configuration and troubleshooting procedures.

·         Create and generate customized end user reports utilizing tools such as Excel, Access, RCM Results.

 

►And have:

·         High School degree or equivalent.

·         2-4 years experience in Information Technology or related field.

·         Knowledge of server processes, interactions and management.

·         Proficient with all versions of Microsoft Windows and Office (including Word, Excel, Access, Power Point, and Publisher).

·         Computer hardware component knowledge (computers, hard drives, memory, monitors, printers, network switches, firewalls, etc.).

·         Microsoft Visual Basic Script experience (preferred, but not required).

·         Computer support help-desk experience (preferred, but not required).

·         Knowledge of Citrix Presentation Server/XenApp (preferred, but not required).

·         Ability to learn new things quickly in a fast paced environment.

·         Exceptional organizational skills including planning, prioritizing, and follow-through.

·         Ability to work in a team environment as well as work independently with minimal supervision.

 

►Consider a career on the Spectrum team! 

We offer a competitive salary and benefits package, including family Club membership, and an opportunity to join a team that is passionate about  achieving results while ensuring exceptional membership and team member experiences!

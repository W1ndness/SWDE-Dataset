About the Job

IS SECURITY LEAD

(Information Security Analyst Level III)

Enterprise Resource Planning

 

Job Opening ID# 3639

 

Hennepin County Medical Center is repeatedly named one of America's Best Hospitals by U.S. News and World Report. Located in the heart of the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul, Minnesota, we're a dynamic academic medical center with 469 hospital beds (more than 100 in ICUs) and 16 operating rooms. We're Minnesota's first and largest Level 1 Trauma Center, and offer career opportunities in a setting where teamwork is not just talked about - it is practiced every day.  Our mission as "a public teaching hospital that provides outstanding health care services in an environment which promotes excellence in education and research," is the inspiration for the care we provide.  Our diverse workforce creates an environment that fosters collaboration and commitment to our patients.

This full time opportunity is to act as the point of contact for all Information Services security related activities. This will include initial analysis, research, and triage of information security events as well as incident detection, containment, eradication, remediation, and prevention. Individual will work closely with the Information Security and Privacy officer as well as many other IT and application teams to form a cohesive monitoring and response function.   This position will report to the Director of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP).

 

Interested applicants may apply online by visiting www.HCMC.JOBS.  

 

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Lead the implementation of audit recommendations
  • Work closely with the Information Security and Privacy to ensure adequate protection of IT assets and information
  • Assist in the remediation of critical risks, threats, and vulnerabilities pertaining to IT systems
  • Development and execution of a robust user provisioning system
  • Represent IT Security at various meetings, committees, and task force
  • Work on the development and revisions of existing and new IS policies
  • Lead operational elements of IT security
  • Manage daily operations as it pertains to IT security
  • Lead IT projects as they relate to IT Security
  • Application ownership of user provisioning
  • Participate in the Cyber Emergency Response Team

 

 

The Qualifications for this position are:

·         Minimum of Bachelors degree, ideally in an IT related discipline plus 8- 10 years of Information Technology industry experience with at least 5 years experience in Information Security Operations and Management.

·         Knowledge of security standards (ISO 27000 series, NIST)

 

·         The following certifications are preferred:

o    Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP);

o    Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA);

o    Certified Information Security Manager (CISM);

o    Cisco Certified Network Engineer (CCNE);

 

The ideal candidate should possess the following:

·         Proven professional experience evaluating IT infrastructure and applications, and server configuration, database security, relevant physical security, and other security devices and applications, with a goal of eliminating or mitigating security risk

·         Healthcare industry experience preferred

·         Knowledge of HIPAA, ARRA and Meaningful Use

·         Experience with EPIC desired

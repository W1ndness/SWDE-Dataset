SAP Materials Manager

About the Job

Title                    : Materials Manager

Department         : Operations

Reports To            : Vice President of Operations

FLSA Status           : Full Time - Exempt

 

Job Summary :

 

The Materials Manager will manage the day-to-day operations of the materials function to promote efficiency throughout the entire supply chain.  One is to plan, execute, and finalize projects according to strict deadlines and within budget.  This includes acquiring resources and coordinating the efforts of team members and third-party contractors or consultants in order to deliver projects according to plan.  This role will also define the project’s objectives and oversee quality control throughout its life cycle.  One will supervise the buyer and the shipping/receiving supervisors.

 

Duties & Responsibilities :

 

  • Project management of operations with ERP implementation lifecycle experience.
  • Lead mentor as required all project team members on ERP implementation.
  • Lead Materials Planning, Master Scheduling, Procurement, Production Control and Logistics processes.
  • Responsible for improving the materials management function relating to the planning, procurement, storage, control, and distribution of materials and products to meet company objectives.  This role is critical to the company’s strategic vision for growth and culture change.
  • Develop a highly effective materials organization by providing overall direction, supervision, coordination and evaluation of staff.
  • Develop streamlined logistics processes to minimize cost and optimize resource utilization.
  • Manage the process of accurately controlling the business’ inventories, from procurement to manufacturing and through distribution.
  • Develop and maintain a supplier base sufficient to support current and future operational needs.  Interview and confer with current and prospective suppliers to determine capabilities, and manage suppliers and logistics.
  • Utilize project management tools to estimate projects and monitor planning and progress techniques.
  • Support management in the development, planning and execution of a lean transformation strategy and metrics.
  • Serve as a key driver of change within the business, supporting management decisions by communicating company changes to employees.

 

Job Requirements :

 

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related field.
  • Minimum of 5 years experience as a Materials Manager or Program Manager.
  • Must have experience with full lifecycle SAP or ERP implementation.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment with executives, shop associates, purchasing professionals, quality managers, production managers and team members.
  • Lean Manufacturing experience (Kanban)
  • Track record of driving improvement with quantified results.
  • Six Sigma experience a plus (DMAIC, DMADV, DFSS)
  • Ability to work with minimal supervision in a fast-paced environment.
  • Intermediate skills in MS Office and spreadsheet programs as well as use of the Internet.
  • Knowledge in analyzing technical data in suppliers’ proposals
  • Effective communications and strong negotiation skills.

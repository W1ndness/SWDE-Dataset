Programmer/Analyst - SQL, Oracle

About the Job

 

CySolutions, Inc., a reseller and publisher of Electronic Medical Records and Practice Management Software is looking for qualified Programmers and Data Analysts for its fast growing and diverse customer base and product offerings. Our customers include physician offices and Community Health Centers located in over 45 states. 

Candidates should have a working knowledge of .NET languages, (C# or VB.NET) and a thorough knowledge of MS SQL (all versions), including data transformation services, stored procedures, DB schema management, views, etc. Knowledge of MS SQL Reporting Services, Oracle, OLAP development and maintenance, SQL Based Ambulatory Care PracticeManagement and Electronic Medical Records software, or other widely used applications is highly desired.  

We provide extended services to our customer base including application customization, data conversion and custom reports services using MSSRS and Crystal Reports. Employees are based in their homes. We presently have a solid development team and are looking to expand it in order to meet the increasing demand for our products and services, particularly in the areas of data migration and transformation between applications and databases. Proximity of the applicant to the Dallas Metroplex is an important factor but not determinative to employment. Occasional travel may be required.

 

Position requires an aptitude for problem-solving, strong interpersonal and telephone communication skills as well as proficient database management skills (SQL). Working knowledge of legacy languages including Visual Basic 6, ASP 3.0 and Visual Fox Pro languages is a plus but not required. 

 

We offer a friendly, team oriented working environment as well as competitive compensation and benefits. We provide employees with the opportunity to grow in their career advancement in the medical software industry.

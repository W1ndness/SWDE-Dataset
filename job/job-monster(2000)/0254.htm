About the Job

Interested candidates should send a word copy of their resume with geographic preferences, salary/rates, availability to christinemo@kleinmgmt.com

 

Oracle Clinical Support Manager

Groton, CT

Contract Position

 

Provide technical consultation for Oracle Clinical/Oracle Onsite/Thesaurus Management System (OC/RDC/TMS) implementation, including new initiatives and solutions, and business as usual (BAU) work.

 

New Initiatives and Solutions:

  • Work closely with Company Application Architect and Application Engineer in Solutions Delivery group to implement long term OC/RDC/TMS reliability.
  • Work streams in this area may include upgrading infrastructure/platform that hosts the application, improving Oracle product quality (technology stack and business functions), and data warehouse solution in stabilizing the transactional system.

 

Continuous Improvement Projects:

  • Manage and implement system availability and performance improvement.
  • Work streams in this area may include system monitoring, performance/load testing, system simplification, capacity enhancement, and any identified work effort in raising OC/RDC/TMS uptime and performance.
  • Provide insight on application strength and weakness, their implications on IT maintenance and business use, and recommend remediation/improvement.
  • Technical coordination of all work streams and BAU routines to ensure their synergy and benefits; foresee risks and provide mitigations.
  • Technical planning and preparation of environment required by each work stream and BAU major version upgrades.
  • Quality oversight of all technical work to warrantee system integrity and data integrity. The work includes, but not limit to, infrastructure configuration changes, application customizations, and upstream and downstream data integration. The work can be performed by Company internal resources or external vendors.
  • Monitor system growth and engage related IT groups in capacity planning/increase for BAU growth and other source of growth such as acquisition integration.
  • Liaison to IT service groups, downstream applications owners, and external vendors in technical engagement and collaborations.
  • Technical contact to business unit in presenting technical solutions and addressing questions/concerns; Involve and work with business effectively to deliver, e.g. develop business processes for performance testing.
  • Technical communication with Oracle in explaining issues, understanding Oracle*s solutions, and their impact or benefit to Company; Participate in OC/RDC/TMS focus group discussions for product improvement efforts.
  • Accountable when called upon in resolving production crisis, although not responsible for day to day application support.
  • Participate internal system audit and answer questions.
  • Timely reporting to management

SAP MM / WM Application Analyst

About the Job

Exciting opportunity to join a terrific company with excellent benefits.  If you have a few years of hands on SAP MM / WM experience working as a Technical / FunctionalAnalyst then look no further.  This is an opportunity to expand your career and SAP skills! 

SUMMARY

Responsible for developing / configuring software applications to meet strategic business needs. Assist in the entire life cycle of the solution process, including analyzing user and business needs, building consensus on solutions, customizing /configuring software, and implementing the solution.

 

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

 

§         Work with management to develop strategy for business process improvement opportunities

§         Perform ongoing analysis of existing processes to identify systems gaps / better utilization of current systems

§         Follow Six Sigma / Project Management process for all projects

§         Follow Six Sigma / Project Management process for all projects which includes:

§         Collaborating with project champion to define project scope, goals, and deliverables

§         Developing detailed project plans and associated project documentation

§         Identifying and managing project dependencies and critical path

§         Using appropriate tools to plan and schedule project timelines and milestones

§         Tracking and reporting project status to management

§         Responsible for detailed project planning, tracking and reporting to management.

§         Responsible for leading assigned projects and providing detailed project planning, tracking and reporting to management.

§         Perform analysis to determine scope and objectives for business and/or technical requirements.

§         Initiate the review of existing systems to evaluate performance and functionality effectiveness; conduct root-cause analysis to identify problems areas and risks in systems or application design.  Identify improvement opportunities in line with best business practices.

§         Conduct feasibility studies pertaining to the acquisition or development of new information systems; i.e., cost-benefit, ROI, SWOT, Pro/Con, etc.

§         Effectively communicate the functional requirements and technical alternatives to business customers and other technology groups.

§         Research and evaluate alternative solutions and recommend the most efficient and cost effective solution considering existing systems, available technology, the capacity and limitations of equipment, operating time and form of desired results.

§         Develop / implement software solutions based on requirements and design specifications.

§         Design and conduct a detailed systems testing plan to ensure an accurate and effective systems/application implementation.

§         Effectively communicate with all levels of management

§         Provide guidance to lower level analysts.

 

 

 

SKILL LEVEL GUIDELINES

Performs work requiring an extensive degree of innovation, usually in collaboration with other professionals to address new or improved methods, products, systems, or services.

 

QUALIFICATIONS  To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

 

EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE

Bachelor's degree (B. S.) in Computer Science or  related education from four-year college or university; with five to seven years of related experience or equivalent combination of education and experience. SAP configuration / programming experience is required.

 

Successful candidates must have at least 3 -5 years experience with Logistics functions within the SAP WM and MM modules to support and develop solutions to address business issues and/or improve business operations and usage of the systems.  Activities may include:

·         Interacting with functional areas to gain deep understanding of business processes and challenges

·         Identifying opportunities to improve system functionality and business processes

·         Customizing SAP Warehouse Management inbound (receiving, putaway), internal (transfers, cycle counts), and outbound (picking, packing, staging, shipping) processes

·         Customizing SAP Materials Management processes (Procurement, Material Master, Inventory)

·         Defining test conditions and test scripts

 

Knowledge of Handling Units, Warehouse setups, RF, Materials Planning, and Invoice verification is preferred.

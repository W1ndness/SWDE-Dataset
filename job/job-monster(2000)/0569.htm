Position Overview:
The successful candidate will be responsible for the design, development, implementation and ongoing maintenance of all components surrounding the BizTalk interface.  This position requires a candidate with 3-5 years of experience with the Microsoft BizTalk package - 2006 or later release. The software will be deployed as middleware between an instance of the MS eCommerce package and a mainframe-based ERP system.


Basic Requirements:
·         Knowledge equivalent to the completion of a degree in Computer Science
·         Minimum of 3 years of BizTalk experience, and proficiency in the surrounding .net languages, including VB and C#
·         Working knowledge of DB2 and SQL Server
 
Additional Requirements:
·         SAP a plus
·         eCommerce a strong plus
·         Experience in Web Services with BizTalk Orchestrations
·         Solid written, verbal, and interpersonal skills.
·         Understanding of project management practices with the demonstrated ability to successfully deliver assignments on time and on budget.
·         Comfortable using a disciplined development methodology and adhering to development best practices, standards and procedures.
·         Strong problem resolution, judgment and decision making skills.

Drupal Developer (mid Level)

About the Job

iQuasar is an Information Technology Company based in Washington DC Metropolitan area (Northern Virginia). We specialize in IT consulting; Staffing; Software Development; Managed Consulting; and Software Products and Applications

 

One of our clients in Washington DC needs DRUPAL developer. With strong skills in PHP and Drupal development

 

Location:              WASHINGTON, DC US

Job Type:             Regular Full Time

Job Level:            Mid-Level

Education:           Bachelor’s Degree or Equivalent

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Drupal Developer will be responsible for understanding functional and technical requirements as documented in the specification documents and perform design tasks within a Drupal, PHP and MySQL context. The developer will draw from prior experiences and identify missing or ambiguous requirements and escalate these to the attention of the technical lead and the requirements engineer. The developer will follow industry standard PHP and Drupal coding practices and write efficient PHP/MySQL code that can be unit tested using frameworks such as Http Unit and PHP Unit. The developer will have experience working with various content types, the CCK and experience in or knowledge of writing custom Drupal modules. The developer will demonstrate a high degree of initiative; ability to deal with ambiguity; excellent communication skills; drive to get things done ahead of time and attention to detail.

Overall the Developer work effort will include:

15% solution design and documentation

65% solution implementation, unit testing

10% configuration management, build automation and release engineering

10% meetings, code reviews etc.

 

REQUIREMENTS

 

Education:

Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field a must.

 

Qualifications:

5+ years of software development experience with at least 2 full years in a Drupal/PHP developer role

Domain experience in LAMP

Full software lifecycle experience software development experience

5 years experience with MySQL or equivalent

Experience must include at least one enterprise scale Drupal implementation

Knowledge of and integration with enterprise search engine such as Google will be an asset

Knowledge of social networking APIs such as facebook, blogger and twitter will be an asset

Experience with CMMI processes desirable

Experience with Federal customers desirable

 

Security Clearance:

Candidate must be able to obtain 6C (Public Trust) Clearance

Business System Analyst/Developer

A position has been declared open in the Weber School District for a Business System Analyst/Developer.  This position is for 8 hours per day, 12 months per year.  Weber School District employees and applicants outside the district may contact the Human Resources Office.  Files will be reviewed before interviews are scheduled.

This position will remain open until filled, after a minimum of 5 working days.

 

Purpose
The job of Business System Analyst / Developer was established for the purpose/s of working as a liaison among multiple departments to identify solutions which will help to bridge the needs of the organization with the technology infrastructure provided. This Analyst will work to deliver timely and accurate data to end users, develop and enhance applications which provide meaningful information and work to improve business processes through sound IT decisions and recommendations.

Essential Functions

·        Create SQL queries for the purpose of extracting, transforming and loading various data.

·        Customize and Develop IFAS screens (e.g. Development work will typically focus around .NET technologies-primarily C#-as well as HTML and XML.) for the purpose of meeting the needs of the District.

·        Develop custom applications outside of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software for the purpose of improving end users' processes.

·        Develop and Enhance WorkFlow models for the purpose of routing critical pieces of data such as purchase requests, invoices, etc. and adding additional functionality to support items such as document progression, image attachments, e-mail interaction, etc.

·        Develops SQL reports using CDD (Click Drag & Drill) inside of existing ERP software, Sungard Public Sector’s Interactive Financial and Analysis Software (IFAS), and improve existing reports for the purpose of providing additional functionality, speed and accuracy.

·        Enhance and Improve District self-service employee portal (Employee Online) for the purpose of adding additional functionality to aide employees in benefit enrollment process, EFT configuration and other areas important to this service.

·        Maintain Existing and Develop new database tables, stored procedures, views, queries and triggers (e.g. enhancing ERP software, analyze queries and other functions) for the purpose of ensuring data integrity, accuracy and speed.

·        Model And Design solutions accurately and thoroughly (e.g. Modeling should include high-level design which includes detailed documentation and characteristics of the algorithm presented.) for the purpose of development.

·        Use database analytics (e.g. link potentially useful information) for the purpose of identifying trends and relationships across different areas of data.

 

Job Requirements: Minimum Qualifications
Skills, Knowledge and Abilities
Specific skill-based competencies required to satisfactorily perform the functions of the job include: operating pertinent software and hardware; staying current with pertinent technology advances; and working in a team environment.

KNOWLEDGE is required to utilize theoretical mathematical concepts; review and interpret highly technical information, write technical materials, and/or speak persuasively to implement desired actions; and analyze situations to define issues and draw conclusions. Specific knowledge-based competencies required to satisfactorily perform the functions of the job include:

o   Knowledge of the following languages is preferred: C#, JavaScript, VBScript, XML, SQL. Willingness to learn additional languages as needed is required.

o   Knowledge of MS SQL Server or similar environment.

o   Strong SQL skills and the ability to analyze queries, tables and other aspects of the database thoroughly to highlight problems or possible areas of improvement.

o   Thorough understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle and the application of these principles in daily duties.

        ABILITY is required to schedule activities, meetings, and/or events; routinely gather, collate, and/or classify data; and consider a wide variety of factors when using equipment. Flexibility is required to work with others in a variety of circumstances; analyze data utilizing a variety of complex processes; and utilize equipment under a variety of conditions for multiple purposes. Ability is also required to work with a wide diversity of individuals; work with data of widely varied types and/or purposes; and utilize a wide variety of types of job-related equipment. Some problem solving may be required to identify issues and select action plans. Problem solving with data frequently requires independent interpretation of guidelines; and problem solving with equipment is moderate to significant. Specific ability-based competencies required to satisfactorily perform the functions of the job include: being attentive to detail; working with detailed information/data; communicating effectively with others; maintaining confidentiality; problem solving.

Chief Technical Architect and Subject Matter Expert (SME)

About the Job

Experienced the satisfaction that comes with successfully impacting a high Visibility Client’s Critical Operation?

Well come join a Team that prides itself on doing just that every day!

TeraThink Corporation is a leading edge information technology and management consulting firm specializing in solution development, enterprise software implementation and maintenance, and program/project management. Ranked #96 fastest growing company in the country on the 2009 Inc. Magazine, Inc. 500 list, #24 on Washington Technology's 2009 Fast 50 list as well as recently named to the SmartCEO Future 50 as one of five of the Emerging Growth Winners in the Future 50 listing, we are a small business that proudly provides services delivered through proven methodologies that focus on customer needs to optimize business processes and maximize return on investment through enabling technologies as we support both our Private and Public Industry Clients. Our vision is to partner with our customers from project conceptualization, through implementation and beyond solution deployment, providing the highest level of support and guidance.

TeraThink's mission is to provide sound solutions to our customers, not sacrificing quality or efficiency, while maintaining the highest degree of customer satisfaction. We ensure our customer objectives are met to enable solutions that ultimately enhance business processes and increase operational efficiencies through enabling technologies.  As such we have an exciting opportunity for a Chief Technical Architect to join our Team who will also serve as a Subject Matter Expert (SME) for one of our DoD Clients! 

In this highly visible role our Chief Architect will serve not only as the Subject Matter Expert (SME), possessing in-depth knowledge of a particular area, such as business, computer science, engineering, mathematics, etc., but will also provide technical knowledge and analysis of highly specialized applications and operational environments, high-level functional systems involving analysis, design, integration, documentation and implementation advice on exceptionally complex problems that need extensive knowledge of the subject matter for effective implementation. Our selected Individual will participate as needed in all phases of software development with emphasis on the planning, analysis, testing, integration, documentation, and presentation phases coupled with applying principles, methods and knowledge of the functional area of capability to specific task order requirements, incorporating advanced mathematical principles and methods to exceptionally difficult and narrowly defined technical problems in engineering and other scientific applications to arrive at automated solutions.

 

We seek an Architect that thrives on providing technical/management leadership on major tasks and technology assignments coupled with establishing goals and plans that meet project objectives via proven domain and expert technical knowledge. Directing and controlling activities for this highly visible client, encompassing having overall responsibility for financial management, methods, and staffing to ensure that technical requirements are met while successfully interacting in client negotiations and interfacing with senior management are all part of this critical role. Your decision making and domain knowledge may have a critical impact on overall project implementation as well.  Our ideal candidate can also expect to do the following:

 

·         Serve as technical advisor to the Enterprise Engineering Division.

·         Provide overall technical oversight for all work under this task including supervising others.

·         Conduct research and evaluation on technical areas of interest to our DoD Client.

·         Assist the Government in project planning, strategic planning, and technical evaluations.

·         Attend meetings and represent the Enterprise Engineering Division as required.

·         Develop technical briefings and reports for written and oral presentation as required, hence excellent written and verbal communication skills are mandatory.

·         Review and approve project plans produced under this task, as well as review and approve System Change Notifications produced by the Client's Engineering Division.

 

 

A completed Bachelor’s degree from a four year college or university is mandatory as is an active DoD Security Clearance, hence U.S. Citizenship is required.

Why not become part of an elite team of Consultants leading the transformation of how our Clients do Information Technology Management?  TeraThink employs only the finest professionals, provides quality service and strives for customer satisfaction. We invest heavily in career development and instill a positive culture and discipline, as our professionals serve as the foundation and future of the company.


TeraThink is an EEO/Affirmative Action Employer who proudly supports Diversity in the Workforce.

Application Developer

About the Job

POSITION SUMMARY
Under general supervision of the Technical Lead or Support Supervisor or other management team member, the Application Developer performs work assignments in one or more of the following areas: providing technical guidance and direction to other staff members; participating in project planning and requirements definition; system/application design, program/system development, unit testing, support during QA and user testing, implementation and integration; customer support for and technical expertise in end-user decision support software tools and end-user computing technology; conducting all activities in accordance with standards and procedures.


MAJOR JOB RESPONSIBILITES
• Implement modifications and perform maintenance to existing web-based computer systems. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to
- Research problems that arise in business computing systems.
- Troubleshoot technical issues in a variety of application environments.  This includes being able to research issues related to system configuration, application & database code, and problems with data.
- Debug programs, applications, and systems to determine the root cause of issues.
- End-to-end testing of systems for proof of concept work or for troubleshooting purposes.
- Provide on-call support on a rotational basis to ensure business commitments and SLA’s are met.
- Participate in annual Disaster Recovery tests and other infrastructure-related initiatives.

• Perform assigned software development tasks as part of a project team assigned to enhance existing web-based computer systems or develop new web-based computer systems.  Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
- Design, code, & test new programs and changes to existing programs
- End-to-end testing of systems for proof of concept work or for troubleshooting purposes
- Compliance with established change management procedures, company standards and .NET/T-SQL/CLR best practices

• Compliance with established change management procedures, company standards and T-SQL/CLR best practices.
• Ensure the smooth implementation of the data tier of a project or enhancement, including creating required change management documentation and developing implementation scripts and procedures.
• Provide customer support for end-users related to web-based computer systems.
• Undertake other assignments as directed by management.


 
EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE / QUALIFICATIONS
• Five or more years work related experience, B.S. in computer science or information systems fields required.
• Minimum of 4 years experience in application development in the Windows web environment
– Strong experience developing & supporting web applications with ASP.NET, VB.NET, C#, XML
– Strong experience with web programming/scripting languages and environments
– Ability to troubleshoot issues with web sites (IIS, .NET framework, Windows Services, Windows Server)
• Minimum of 2 years experience in database development in the MS SQL Server environment
– Experience in T-SQL development, data warehousing and data driven web applications
– Experience writing T-SQL Stored Procedures
– Pluses would be:
o Experience programming & implementing MS SQL Server database objects, including SSIS packages, SSRS reports (i.e. RDL files), T-SQL functions,  T-SQL Views, etc.
o Ability to deliver high-performance data access objects on very large databases (VLDB).
o Experience interfacing with data on an IBM system i (i.e. iSeries, AS/400)

• Experience working with Agile and/or Lean Manufacturing development techniques
• Technical certifications related to .NET are a plus
• Demonstrated experience with configuration management tools (e.g. Team Foundation Services, Clearcase, SourceSafe, CVS)
• Strong application & database analysis and troubleshooting skills
• Experience with BizTalk mapping, orchestrations, pipelines is a plus
• Ability to deal effectively with individuals at all business levels and a variety of situations  requiring tact, judgment, and composure
• Ability to organize tasks and time to ensure timely completion of all assignments.
• Ability to maintain cooperative relationships with those contacted in performance of duties.
• Ability to work as a member of a team and conforming to uniform work practices.
• Ability to understand business processes and how they are expressed in the technical implementation
• Self-motivated and strongly oriented to problem solving with emphasis on root-cause analysis and quick resolution of issues

Production Control Manager

About the Job

 

Production Control Manager for custom-house electronics manufacturing firm.  This is a full-time exempt position, for mid-sized thin-film circuit firm located in Fremont.  Company is profitable, over 15 years in the same location, and has about 110 employees.
 

Responsible for production control and stockroom operations. Position reports to the Manager of Operations and has six direct reports.  Accountable in daily production meetings and involved in significant interface with Sales/Marketing, Operations and QA departments.

 

Required Qualifications/Can Do Factors:

  • Minimum five years management experience.
  • Minimum 10 years working in a production/manufacturing environment.
  • Knowledge in inventory control procedures.
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent.
  • Proven capability of creating and implementing improvements with procedures and personnel.
  • Experience in distribution, warehouse traffic, or production planning is a plus.
  • Vantage experience is very desirable; other software experience (such as Oracle, SAP, ASK) may substitute for experience.

 

Desired Qualifications/Will Do Factors:

  • Change agent with a track record and experience in leading, developing, and implementing changes in operations that create efficiency.
  • Assertive and collaborative self-starter who can coach and organize the staff and hold them accountable.
  • Ability to communicate effectively with subordinates, both verbally and in writing.
  • Individual with good organizational skills who can create clear paths and stay on top of the department.
  • Problem solver who can collect information, determine root cause, and create a solution or plan for change.
  • Someone who can present messages and ideas in a confident and convincing manner.
  • Individual who follows through to complete tasks in a timely manner and delivers on action items without prompting.
  • Team player with ability to work collaboratively with supervisor, peers, subordinates, and other departments.
  • Also, someone who is willing to "get their hands dirty".

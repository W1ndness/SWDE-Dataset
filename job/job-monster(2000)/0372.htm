About the Job

JAVA Web Developer

 

Company Overview

Curriculum Advantage is an Atlanta based company that specializes in educational software.  They have been in the business for 11 years developing software for schools all across the country. Curriculum Advantage offers a competitive salary and great benefits including 401K. We are a growing company that is in the need of a web developer that is able to work with little supervision and achieve deadlines put forth by senior management. This position will report directly to the Director of Product Development. Visit our website at http://www.classworks.com /.

 

Job Description:

Primary duty will be working with a fast paced development environment to create an AJAX web application using the Google Web Toolkit. You will be working with a team of other developers and quality assurance testers.

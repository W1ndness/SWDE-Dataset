About the Job

Network & Application Security Specialist


About us

Are you looking to be part of extremely talented team that is building a world class business? BEW Global is a fast-paced, growth oriented business focused on information security.  With a growing customer base of Fortune 1000 firms and the acquisition of a brand new facility, BEW Global is positioned for continued success.


We are looking for some exceptionally talented people to join the team and help drive growth and achieve our goals. People are the key to our success and we value our team members like no other organization.  BEW Global strives to provide our team members the opportunity to grow and develop professionally, while also enjoying life outside of work in the great state of Colorado.  The most important attribute we look for in a team member is the drive for greatness. Our business is built-to-last, good is not enough, we want to be great! 


The work environment is unique.  No cubes, private offices and natural light keep us in a good frame of mind as we attack each and every day.  No suits unless needed on client site, we prefer jeans, shorts and t-shirts, with mountain bikes and skis often seen on the cars in the parking lot. 

Our starting salary rates are industry average because everyone needs to prove themselves in our environment. Talk is cheap and you are only as good as your last performance in this fast paced world of high-tech security. That being said, with success comes quick compensation advancement in our environment.  Bottom-line, if you contribute to the success of the business, we firmly believe in sharing the wealth and have implemented a unique profit sharing plan for our employees that vest them in the success of the business on the same pace with ownership.


We are looking for people who want to build something significant with us, not work for us.


Description of Role:


The Network and Application Security Specialist will be responsible for the scope, design and implementation of enterprise security solutions for our clients on a national basis.  The following types of security solutions will be the primary focus of the position:


·         Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

·         Email Security Gateways and Secure Mail Applications

·         Web Security Gateways and Proxy

·         Security Information Event Management (SIEM) 


We anticipate the Network and Application Security Specialist position will spend 80% of their time designing and implementing these types of solutions for our clients. Travel is anticipated to be 60% - 70%.  Previous experience is preferred in software or appliance solution based implementations. The appropriate training will also be provided on each of the security products described above by the specific product vendors and our current team members. The position will also work with BEW Global sales staff in regards to pre-sales support. This will include participating on project scope calls and attending meetings with senior decision makers for current and prospective clients in regards to security solution integration projects.


Essential Functions:


·         Background in IT Security preferred, preferably at least 3 years in a related field.

·         Experience with Windows and Red Hat Operating Systems and/or other Linux OS.

·         Networking experience with routers, switches, firewalls and traffic shaping highly desirable.

·         Scoping and implementing applications in customer environments using statement of work.

·        Presents and educate clients on key product features during implementation.

·         Experience working with Exchange or other mail systems extremely desirable.

·         Experience with SQL and Oracle databases also preferred.

·         Collaborate with the sales team in regards to technology decisions for our clients.

·         Maintain in-depth knowledge of products, technologies, competitors and industry trends.


Knowledge, Skills, & Abilities:
 

·         Aggressive self starter with a great attitude and strong organizational skills.

·         Ability to work under pressure and adapt to a changing environment.

·         Manage/overcome client internal issues during an engagement.

·         Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

·         A passion for technology and ability to relate technology to business processes.

·         CISSP, CISA and CISM would be a plus but not required.

·         We prefer a 4-year degree in Computer Science or Electrical Engineering.

·         Work experience will be heavily weighted.


Personal qualities:


·         Energetic, enthusiastic and reliable.

·         Exceptional organizational abilities.

·         An aptitude for learning is a must.

·         Team player is a must.

Our culture is critical to our success.  You must be willing to be accountable for your actions, work beyond your potential, and set the standard for the way business should be conducted.  We will not accept anyone who does not display these traits.

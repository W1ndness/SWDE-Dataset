About the Job

Software Engineers - NEW Large Scale Rails 3 Development (Ruby experience *NOT* required) Well funded, early stage!!

Ruby on Rails experience is not required! ~ "Doesn't have to be a ruby person, just a good engineer that is willing to work in ruby." - hiring manager. Targeting experienced software professionals with 3-10 years of experience. Would also consider high potent recent graduate with some side personal development experience and visible passion for creating useful software solutions for consumers.

Well funded, early stage startup - ground floor opportunity with lots of New Design and Development, practically no maintenance in the near term. Small team (around 5) looking to add several more talented software engineers. Majority of the work will be back-end focused building systems to handle large scale data in the cloud. Lots of very interesting problems to solve here – dealing with LOTS of data from numerous on-line services being used by consumers. New Engineers should be comfortable with doing some front-end work, as there will be some, but again, majority is back-end focused. Very cool stuff working with the latest and greatest: Rails3, Git, Cassandra….

Experience/Background should include:

- Computer Science (or closely related) degree with sound software engineering skills.
- Developing applications / data driven websites in any object oriented language. Ruby on Rails experience is desired but NOT required.
- Java MVC and/or any experience with other Open Source web application frameworks (Django, Symfony, CodeIgnitor etc.) is also a plus.
- Large scale, server-side programming
- Developing in an Agile friendly environment (Sprints, TDD, Scrums daily)
- Strong Linux and/or OSX

Salary: in the 80-110k range, with room for great talent with relevant experience.
Location: Cambridge, MA. Just opened up brand new office.

This is a fast paced, very early stage startup with funding already in pocket…now they just need their team! If you think you’re up for the challenge, send your resume to Jobs @ Bivium

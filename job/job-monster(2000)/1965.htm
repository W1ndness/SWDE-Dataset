Senior IT Auditor

About the Job

We are currently recruiting for an IT Audit Senior for our Fortune 500 client.  This position reports into the Manager of IT Audit and is a key member of the Internal Audit organization responsible for planning, executing and reporting on IT and other department projects.  The IT audit senior is responsible for working on multiple, concurrent engagements which primarily consist of IT related audits and/or special projects but may also include other non-IT related special projects, investigations, and internal department initiatives.  In this capacity, the IT audit senior is responsible for various engagements to ensure the key objectives are accomplished.  The role is also responsible for assessing various technologies to identify significant IT risks, determining the adequacy and effectiveness of the controls in place to address the risks and to verify compliance with IT related policies, standards, and procedures. 

 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

 

1.        Information technology general control audits focusing on infrastructure and application risks.

2.        Non-IT related Operational and Compliance Audits as needed.

3.        Fraud Investigations as required focusing primarily on Information Technology risks.

4.       Interact with senior levels of management (CIO, CFO, VP HR, COO, etc.) to communicate audit risks, issues and related corrective actions.

5.       Work on initiatives within the Internal Audit Department as needed (Time Management, Expense Reporting, Work Paper Peer Review, etc.)

6.       Assist on the annual risk assessment of the information technology environment and rank the risk appropriately.

7.       Assist in the annual IT Audit Plan based upon risk assessment and discussions with IT Leadership.  Ensure that the audit plan covers significant IT risks and allocates resources appropriately while ensuring that the audit approach is effective.

8.       Supervise staff to ensure the audits are executed in accordance with department and professional standards while providing guidance on identifying and prioritizing key risks related to IT exposures.

9.       Assist in the administration of Internal Audit systems and website (Auto Audit, ACL, Sharepoint)

10.    Participate on the continuous audit approach which monitors all key transactions across the Corporation. 

 

Should you meet the requirements below and would like to be immediately considered for this position, please e-mail your resume in a Word document attachment to Neil Rosen, CPA at nrosen@the-rosen-group.com   Also, indicate your current salary and any bonuses. 

 

The Rosen Group, Professional Recruiting Consultants, LLC. is a Hartford, CT based permanent placement agency.  Many of our client assignments are exclusive opportunities to our firm.

 

Candidates must be either U.S. citizens or Green Card holders in order to be considered for this position.

 

Education

  • BS Degree required (IT Degree a plus)
  • Masters Degree (MBA or Masters in IT a plus)
  • 5-7 years experience in auditing with at least 2 years of experience in managing an IT Audit Group
  • Professional designation (CISA, CIA, CISSP or CISM)

Competencies

  • Effective project management skills with the ability to lead multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Proactively strives to improve the department and the company’s key IT controls and processes (focused not only on “compliance” but on adding value).  Partners with the business to improve processes and increase productivity.
  • Understands the internal organization and operations; demonstrates awareness of business changes and priorities.  Continuously seeks to assess and improve an adequate internal control environment.
  • The ability to effectively communicate with senior levels of management.
  • Ability to plan, implement, monitor and control a project; manages human and material resources to product specified results

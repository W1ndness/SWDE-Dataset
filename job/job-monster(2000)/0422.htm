Senior Application Architect

About the Job

Blue Shield of California is a long-established health care firm which is
embarking on a major program to reengineer its core business and to modernize
the technology that supports it. The Shield Advance program will provide the key
technology and business processes which are fundamental to delivering on the
firm's strategies for future growth. Technology opportunities in this program
offer very talented individuals both the stability to be found in an
established, successful organization, and the excitement to be found in a
start-up environment.

BSC is seeking a Senior Architect to be part of the systems architecture team.
This person will work with business solution architects and the Director of
Architecture to establish the overall applications architecture framework in
this domain and will be responsible for architecting solutions needed to support
the business. The Architect is responsible for creating the analysis and design
models for architecturally significant use cases and providing risk mitigation
strategies. The Architect will also work closely with the Director of
Architecture to ensure overall alignment and consistency of the Applications
Architecture across multiple domains. He/She will also partner with the Director
of Architecture to produce Blueprints including multi-year strategic technology
roadmaps for the domain he/she will be responsible for.

The Architect will be a critical part of the senior team that drives the vision
of information technology architecture and strategy. (S)he will support the
Director of Architecture in influencing and partnering with the various
application development teams and various business units within BSC to
facilitate the delivery of best practice solutions and experiences for BSC's
business partners and customers. The role combines strong technical architecture
expertise, understanding of core business functions and a broad knowledge of
hardware/software products and platforms to deliver and maintain solutions to
meet business needs.

(S)he will be accountable for the architectural design and quality attributes of
the group's technical assets. (S)he will be accountable in ensuring that
applications within his/her domain support BSC's short and long term business
objectives for that technology.

Another major role for the Architect will be to work closely with the Enterprise
Architecture team to ensure that the Shield Advance program both conforms to
existing BSC architectural standards which apply to the program, and drives
adoption of new firm-wide standards where they are needed for the success of the
program. This person will work within BSC's enterprise architecture governance
process in order to meet these goals.

The initial tasks and deliverables for the Architect will be to produce analysis
and design models and complete the Software Architecture Document (SAD) for the
architecturally significant use cases as part of the inception and elaboration
phases.

Excellent Business Systems Analyst Opportunity

About the Job

Business Systems Analyst Job Description:

There are several upcoming projects in need of a sharp Business Systems Analyst.  This person will:

 

    *Facilitate requirements gathering, business process design, solution design, and integration strategies

    *Act as a liaison between business facing groups and technical groups

    *Aid technical group in analyzing, troubleshooting and problem solving

    *Provide input for project proposals and statements of work

    *Manage multiple requests in an organized manner according to the priorities established by the business

    *Establish trusted relationships within the business group and effectively manage those relationships through 
    open and honest communication

    *Demonstrate/develop expert knowledge of systems currently in use

    *Act as liaison between the supported department and system vendors for technical issues

    *Participate in the development of business technology requirements and specifications

    *Experience with Citrix, Active Directory, and Access Management would be a plus

    *Must be assertive, analytical and demonstrate initiative

    *Must possess excellent documentation skills both for solutions and work processes

    *Excellent oral and written communication skills in both technical and non-technical situations

    *Must be able to deal logically and effectively with senior management, departmental employees & outside 
    personnel

    *Must be a self-starter and have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment, set priorities, work under 
    pressure and follow through with assignments under limited supervision

    *Must have excellent interpersonal and customer service skills

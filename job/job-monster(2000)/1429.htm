Job Summary

Applied Systems, Inc., the worldwide leader in insurance technology, is currently searching for a Business Analyst to be responsible for the market analysis, business requirement, and design documentation for all of our Integration Services solutions. In this role, you will work closely with the insurance agencies currently using our products to document business needs and workflows combined with prototype screen shots built in Visual Basic. This will include researching project requests, evaluating their potential demand, create ROI documentation, and helping to communicate that information to management. You will work closely with both our Development department and our clients to present design documentation for acknowledgement, acceptance, and approval.


Additional responsibilities include:

  • Identify and gathers information required to produce complete Business Requirement documents
  • Create representative user-interface in Visual Basic that supports the Business Requirement documents
  • Participate in development meetings surrounding Business Requirement documents
  • Work with project planning tools to organize and maintain project statuses
  • Participate in Technical Architecture document review process
  • Create test plans based on Business Requirement documents
  • Monitor overall feature quality and accuracy based on Business Requirement documents.
  • Monitor and research all defects reported for a product prior to assigning to a developer

Required Skills
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, MIS, CIS or related field; or equivalent work experience
  • 3+ years experience in a business analyst (or similar) role
  • Experience designing and documenting software solution screen workflows in Visual Basic
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Organized and reliable; an independent, self-started who takes initiative
  • Knowledge of or the ability to learn the entire suite of Applied Systems’ software offerings
  • Possess an understanding of the Insurance Industry
  • Possess an understand of basic software development concepts and practices


Corporate Profile

At Applied Systems, Inc., we develop, sell, and support the automation tools and processes that insurance agents and brokers use to run their businesses at maximum efficiency and profit. Founded in 1980, Applied Systems owns a legacy of success and stability embodied in quality products and services as well as industry leadership. Our technology is used worldwide by independent agents, brokers, and financial institutions that sell insurance. Our ongoing and vigorous growth of development, sales, and service functions reflects our dedication to stay ahead of agents’ needs in a dynamic and competitive marketplace.


Employees feel appreciated at Applied Systems. In addition to excellent benefits and paid time off, our staff enjoys a variety of extra benefits and social events, including holiday parties, picnics, and frequent company-wide mixers. Rounded out by our business casual dress code and open communication policy, the atmosphere is professional and conducive to productivity, yet comfortable and fun.

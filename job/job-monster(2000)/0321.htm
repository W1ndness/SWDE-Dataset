Sr. Network Engineer

About the Job

The Sr. Netwrk Engineer’s role is to plan, direct, and coordinate the design, installation, and connectivity of computer and network systems to ensure the stable operation of Surescripts’ IT assets. This includes developing, configuring, maintaining, supporting, and optimizing all new and existing network hardware, software, and communication links. The Network Services Manager will also schedule and direct the activities of a team of network specialists to resolve Surescripts workforce members’ hardware and software problems in a timely and accurate fashion.

 

Responsibilities:

·         Support of Production, Certification Datacenter networks as well as Quality Assurance, Development and Corporate networks.  Accountable for network technologies including Intrusion Detection, Firewall, load balancers, desktop/laptop LAN support, Microsoft Exchange servers, PBX & Cisco VoIP, WAN management & capacity planning, digital certificate PKI management, Anti-SPAM, DNS, disaster recovery planning & testing of the network.

 

·         On-call support for emergency after-hours issues.

 

·         Design and implement short- and long-term strategic plans to make certain network capacity meets existing and future requirements including input to the annual budget process.  This includes both new products/services as well as maintaining/refreshing existing infrastructure.

 

·         Develop, implement and maintain policies, procedures and associated training plans for network resource administration and appropriate use.

 

·         Develop and deploy methodologies for testing network performance and providing network performance statistics and reports.

 

·         Establish best practices and policies for installing, configuring, maintaining, and troubleshooting end user workstation hardware, software, and peripheral devices.

 

·         Practice network asset management, including maintenance of network component inventory and related documentation.

·         Plan and conduct performance appraisals of staff, administer disciplinary action, raises, bonuses and promotions when necessary.  Ensure cross-training of staff.

 

·         Conduct new product & services research, negotiate with vendors for the procurement of new products & solutions, plan, acquire and oversee installation, and administration/operation of the product/solution.

 

·         Provide high quality support for participants on connectivity issues by attaining a high level of expertise regarding Surescripts network interfaces.  Assisting with customer and vendor troubleshooting and support for network issues.

 

·         Attend training seminars, conferences, and trade shows to broaden skillsets and technologies.  Train, coach, and mentor Network Technicians and other junior staff.

 

·         Establish and maintain regular written and in-person communications with the organization’s executives, decision-makers, stakeholders, department heads, and end users regarding pertinent network activities.

 

·         Approve and administer user accounts, permissions, and access rights.

 

Qualifications:

Required:

·         College diploma or university degree in the field of networking, IT, computer science, information sciences, or related field and/or 6 years equivalent work experience.

·         Eight to ten years experience in a position where telecommunications network skills were applied and enhanced and four years experience in a telecommunications related leadership position.  

·         Confidence working in a strong, fast-paced team environment.

·         Excellent verbal and written skills

·         Excellent organizational skills

·         Ability to manage multiple tasks

·         Knowledge of TCP/IP networking concepts & products

·         Demonstrated aptitude for problem solving & analytical thinking.

 

Competencies/Skills:

Required:

 

·         Experience and thorough knowledge of network and PC operating systems, including TCP/IP, Windows, UNIX, Linux, IOS.

·         Experience and thorough knowledge of LAN/WAN concepts & products (e.g. Frame Relay, ISDN, DSL, MPLS, Routers, VPN, VoIP)

·         Thorough knowledge of firewall & load balancer concepts & experience with products.

·         Microsoft Exchange Server Administration

·         Solid relationship management and performance management skills.

·         Ability to motivate and direct staff members and subordinates.

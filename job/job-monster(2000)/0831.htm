Technology Consultant

About the Job


Job Title:                    Technology Consultant

Location:                    New York, NY 10016

Type:                          Full-time

Compensation:          Based upon Experience



Position Summary:

Our client is a nationally recognized pharmaceutical consulting firm and has turned to Sound Advice to help them identify, qualify and present qualified individual for this critical position (see below).

 

Responsibilities

·         Support and eventually manage implementation projects

·         Meet with clients and developing business requirements

·         Manage relationship with external vendors (developers, infrastructure, etc.)

·         Manage and take accountability for sections of projects

·         Responsibility for project deliverables, from requirements through testing and training

·         Assist as a technical resource in traditional consulting projects and sales efforts

·         Assist with internal IT strategy and implementation

·         Serve as an internal technology expert (assist other consultants with applications, etc.)

·         Assist with identifying and developing new technology offerings

·         Develop relationships with clients and identify future opportunities

·         Support in growing the organization, assume additional office responsibilities

·         Other duties as assigned

 

Qualifications and Experience

·         Minimum 2-4 years of work experience, preferably within IT consulting (Big 4/5 experience preferred, but not required)

·         Undergraduate degree or equivalent experience.

·         Experience with full lifecycle system implementations (requirements, build, testing, training)

·         Specific technologies (preferred, not required): ASP.NET, SQL, Java, Windows Architecture [IIS, Windows Server], Microsoft Office Expertise, SAP/Oracle Apps

·         Entrepreneurial, well organized, self-motivated, team player

·         Client facing skills such as advanced communication skills and presentation skills

·         Project management experience/interest

·         Technology focused, but interested in business and traditional management consulting

·         Willingness to have a different opinion, ability to articulate thoughts and concepts well

·         Inform management of downtime and look for additional tasks or to assist others

·         Strive to attain subject matter expertise in all Client’s business, products, and services

·         Keep up on current and emerging technologies

·         Strive to improve skills related to the Client’s Core Competencies

·         Experience or knowledge of pharmaceutical industry a plus

 

Education: Bachelors in Computer Science or equivalent

About Sound Advice:

 

Sound Advice Consulting Services (SACS) is a boutique talent acquisition firm that provides clients with Recruitment and Staffing (Process) Consulting Services, Recruitment and Staffing Services (direct-hire and contract consulting), and Recruitment Process Outsourcing Services (RPO).  SACS is dedicated to satisfying our clients’ requirements for qualified talent that permit business managers to achieve optimal results through the use of technology. 

 

We are amongst the industry leaders in providing Agile/Scrum Professionals in support of product development and project management initiatives. We specialize in providing resources for such functional roles as Project Managers, Business Analysts, Quality Assurance (QA) Analysts, User Interface (UI) Designers and Engineers, Software (SW) Developers/ Engineers, and Database Administration (DBA).   Our industry focus is primarily in Life Sciences (Health Care and Pharmaceuticals), Financial Services, and Government Services. 

                                                          

Located in the heart of mid-town Manhattan, Sound Advice professionals have nearly 100 years of combined industry experience serving clients throughout the United States.

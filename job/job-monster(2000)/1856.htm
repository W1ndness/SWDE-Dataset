About the Job

If you are seeking a long-term Programming career....you know how to hand-code HTML....and you know how SQL databases function....please read our job description!
 
**Interviews are being conducted now for qualified candidates. Successful candidates must be in Tampa Bay and be able to accept employment to start between now and 1/3/11**
 
We are a software development company located in Palm Harbor, Florida (Tampa Bay). We are the nation’s leading provider of software solutions for workforce development, employment, and training. We have developed state-of-the-art systems for governmental workforce development agencies in more than thirty states. Our software is available to over seventy-five percent of the job seekers and employers in the country.

Check us out on the web at www.geosolinc.com and one of the live websites that we host www.EmployFlorida.com . This will give you an idea of the customization and functionality of our software.

Job Summary:

The Entry Level Programmer develops, administers, maintains, and supports policies and procedures for ensuring the functionality and accuracy of the programs used to spider job data from external sites.  Relies on direction and good judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Works under general supervision.


Duties & Responsibilities:
  • Supports and documents new development of spidering robot programs (bots) to quickly and accurately extract data from external websites
  • Ensures all bots meet full QA standards
  • Able to maintain the performance metrics as prescribed for the role and reviewed on a weekly and monthly basis
  • As experience grows will have a basic understanding of the bot library, robo-servers, and balancing issues

    Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  •  
  • Understanding of computer programming methodologies
  • Good analytical skills
  • Able to quickly learn and adapt to new software
  • Basic knowledge of web technologies and programming languages
  • Basic SQL knowledge
  • Capable of writing basic SQL queries to check on the results of assigned bots and basic tables used to store job data

    Candidates with Associates or Bachelors degrees in an IT specialty are preferred (GPA must be 3.0 or above)

    Compensation/Benefits:  Full benefits package including PAID health insurance for individual employee, dental, vision, 401K (after 1 year), accrue 18 PTO days per yr, 7 paid Holidays per yr, and a fast-paced, casual-dress work environment!.

    All candidates must successfully pass a criminal history background check. No 3rd party or agencies. LOCAL candidates preferred. No Relocation allowance offered. You must be authorized to work in the US without sponsorship.

    Geographic Solutions is an Equal Opportunity Employer. M/F/D/V

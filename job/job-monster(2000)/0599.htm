UNIX/Solaris System Administrator

About the Job

UNIX System Administrator

Overall Responsibility

Provide top level Solaris administration, security, and be able to accurately document your setup for Government Information Technology (IT) systems.

Areas of Responsibilities

• Administrating Solaris 10 operation systems

• Provide IT support for over 50 users at government facilities in multiple offices

• Building, auditing, and securing Solaris machines both on SPARC and x86 platforms

• Maintain Solaris network which includes routing, tunneling, and firewalls

• Author design documentation for the system to support the System Development Life Cycle

• Support Government Security Manager enforcing AIS security regulation pertaining to the Solaris network. \


Requirements:

• Candidate must be a US citizen and have a current SSBI

• This position requires the candidate to have at minimum a High School Diploma with Associates Degree, or two years post secondary equivalent with professional experience of at least 5 years with Solaris administration.

• Candidate must be willing to attend & complete training courses that are specific to the customer’s hardware, and software needs.

• Desired candidate should be familiar with government certification and accreditation procedures and able to document systems and complete system accreditation papers as needed.

• In addition to experience, Industry Certifications such as CISSP, MCSE, MCP or AS or BS is preferred.

• Candidate must have experience administrating Solaris systems. We desire a candidate that is familiar with the following Solaris technologies: zones/containers, virtualization, backups, networking, and central accounts.

Network Engineer Lvl IV

About the Job

Moja has been providing Information Management solutions since it’s conception in 1995.  We concentrate on the customer’s specific needs to ensure our client receives full business solutions as well as excellent products.  Moja has experience and expertise to integrate software, hardware and communications technologies.  Our clientele includes:  National Geospatial Intelligence Agency, National Security Agency, Department of Treasury, Defense Intelligence Agency, Department of Navy as well as a host of others. 

 

Position Title:  Network Engineer Level IV

 

Location:  Ft. Meade, MD

 

Job Description:

Leads network design, development, and testing activities for large-scale network infrastructureProvides consultation, support, and guidance to organizations and clients.Leads the resolution of complex network problemsRecommends voice, video, and data network solutions to management and customers based on results of independent assessment of current and future network performance, stability, and network management issues. Performs cost analyses and vendor comparisons to ensure cost-effective and efficient operations; measures feasibility of various approaches, makes recommendations as appropriate.

 

Minimum Requirements:

Knowledge of networking principals, protocols, and routing/switching. Knowledge of Blue Coat hardware systems to include ProxySG and ProxyAV. Understanding of Proxy Automatic Configuration (PAC) file logic.  Understanding network architecture that includes proxy devices, PAC file configuration, and browser setup. Hands-on experience with network equipment and systems. Should possess good analytical and problem-solving skills to troubleshoot network problems.  Should be comfortable before an audience of peers and/or seniors. Should possess exceptional verbal and written communication skills. Should demonstrate leadership developing network solutions to meet customer requirements.

Education Requirement:

BA/BS degree in related field. Degree may be substituted with an equivalent combination of education/related work experience.

 

Clearance Requirement:  Active Top Secret/SCI clearance is a MUST.

 

Additional:

Immediate dayshift position available. Ability to work extended hours as needed. 

 

Moja recognizes the importance of it’s employees and therefore offers a comprehensive benefits package which includes:  Medical and Dental coverage, Supplemental Vision coverage, Life and AD&D benefits, Disability Income insurance (Long-Term and Short-Term), Flexible Spending Accounts, 401(k) Plan, Credit Union membership and Paid Time-Off (PTO).  The company also offers employee referral bonuses, tuition reimbursement.  Visit our website to learn more about our company at www.moja.net   Find out how your talent can lead you to a dynamic career at Moja, today!

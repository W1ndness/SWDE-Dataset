Strategic Product Planning

About the Job

Position


The Strategic Product Planning Manager develops and proposes innovative, high growth, portfolio-, product- and partner-level strategies, translates those strategies into product roadmaps (in conjunction with Product Management) and formulates strategic positioning and messaging content for internal and external communication (in conjunction with Marketing). The Strategic Product Planning Manager will focus on strategic planning, positioning and strategic partner activities for the entire Companies product portfolio, working in unison with Product Management, Marketing, Sales, Business Development and Engineering.

  • General
    • Develop an on-going set of key strategic planning deliverables across the product lifecycle such as market analyses, competitive analyses, customer analyses, competitive responses, positioning, messaging, strategic proposals, business cases, product marketing plans, end-of-life plans and the like -- as a proactive player in a collaborative, multi-function, team environment that includes Product Management, Sales, Marketing, Engineering, Manufacturing, Professional Services and Support. Base outputs on insightful market segmentation and detailed knowledge regarding industry solutions, technologies, etc. Communicate conclusions/recommendations to appropriate internal and external audiences
    • Proactively engage customers through Sales to identify market trends and priorities, to communicate Companies’s vision, value proposition and solution set, and to resolve high profile customer issues
    • Proactively engage industry analysts and media through Marketing to communicate Companies’s vision, value propositions and solution set
    • Develop and promote strategic planning best practices, templates, etc. to appropriate coworkers and teams
    • Operate by the Companies values and perform other responsibilities as requested.
  • Product Portfolio Strategy
    • Develop, propose, and champion a globally applicable, multi-year product portfolio plan to profitably grow the business and meet target P&L metrics, strategic objectives and the like. 
    • Identify innovative and differentiated portfolio growth opportunities (e.g. new product lines, extensions, strategic partnerships, etc.) that align with existing Companies promotion and distribution capabilities
    • Identify, evaluate, propose and execute strategic product alliance/technology partnership opportunities in accordance with Companies’s strategic direction. Evaluate the product, portfolio, business model possibilities and technical fit for each prospect. Ensure potential product alliances enhance or are compatible with Companies’s channels and go to market strategies
    • Develop strategic proposals and business cases and present findings to senior management and the appropriate Planning Forums (PPF, CPF, SPF)
  • Work with Marketing and Product Management to prioritize, justify, integrate and drive product opportunities, strategies, roadmaps and tactics
    • Work with Business Development to initiate discussions with approved prospective alliance partners and team with other function owners as appropriate. Assist with the creation and negotiate a “terms sheet” with product alliance prospects and drive to a final legal agreement
    • Act as the strategy focal point within Companies to all existing and prospective companies collaborating with Companies to jointly develop standard products
    • Assist with managing the life cycle of all existing product alliances.
  • Marketing and Sales/Channel Support
    • Act as the focal point for consolidating and prioritizing Companies’s global positioning and messaging for the portfolio and collective products per target audience, and act as the liaison between Product Management and Marketing to deliver the product portfolio positioning and messaging to the worldwide supply chain market. Team across Marketing to ensure that all sales organizations on a worldwide basis, including all channels, can effectively communicate the product portfolio positioning and messages
    • Consolidate and analyze competitive intelligence and develop positioning and messages against Companies’s voice and non-voice competitors. Team across Marketing to deliver the competitive messages and positioning through all sales organizations, including partners, on a worldwide basis. Ensure all sales organizations on a worldwide basis have access to the latest competitive intelligence and positioning.

Experience


  • BS in an Engineering discipline; BSEE or BSCE preferred
  • 7 or more years product line management or strategic planning experience managing >30% CAGR for multiple software or embedded firmware/hardware systems products targeting enterprise customers
  • Willing to travel, domestic and international, up to 30%
  • Communication Skills and Experience
  • Demonstrated high degree of initiative, creative problem solving, attention to detail, and coolness under fire
  • Proficiency in word-processing and spreadsheet PC applications
  • Proven Writing Skills: business plans, proposals, value propositions, marketing collateral RFPs, etc.
  • Proven Verbal Skills: succinct and to the point in various formats, including one-on-one, small group and public speaking events for a variety of deliverables (e.g. product presentations, proposals, strategies, etc.)
  • More than 3 years experience in the socialization of business cases, strategies, and the like, both internally and externally. Proven experience of persuading both internal and external people on the value of a solution
  • More than 5 years Field/Customer experience as appropriate for targeted products and market
  • Market/Technology/Competitive Knowledge
  • Demonstrated technical understanding of enterprise software, mobile computers and network management systems (e.g. architectures, applications, hardware, etc.) for both current & emerging technologies
  • Knowledge of enterprise mobile computing device competitors 
  • Knowledge of enterprise customer needs, uses, etc. regarding voice-enabled workflow devices & software
  • Proven track record of strategic product planning, including product conceptualization, strategic proposals and business cases, for high tech enterprise solutions that returned the forecasted value, including financial objectives. Must show experience of translating customer requirements into innovative, differentiated and valued solutions and product feature/functions. Must also demonstrate awareness of the business value and implications of the proposed solutions in the marketplace
  • Proven track record of market segmentation, customer analysis, competitive analysis, positioning, etc. -- complemented by proven experience in providing logical, objective, and defendable claims/arguments
  • Proven track record of team building, leadership and achievement in cross-functional team settings
  • Proven experience in providing leadership and deliverables to Product Marketing, Marketing and Sales via collateral, training, support and the like in an effective, constructive environment
  • Proven track record of increasing product revenues and unit sales >30% per year while maintaining or increasing product margins
  • Demonstrated project management skills
  • MBA with a technical undergraduate degree
  • Product management, strategic product planning, product line management and marketing experience
  • Other broad or unconventional experiences, such as working in an overseas assignment
  • High degree of curiosity, creativity, intuition, and street smarts
  • Familiarity with the voice recognition, auto ID and/or PC Peripheral Industry

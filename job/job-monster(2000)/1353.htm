Java Developer

About the Job

Lake 5 Media is seeking a Java Developer to join our team.  This is an opportunity to join an exciting company that is quickly gaining traction in the media services market.  The ideal candidate is a creative self-starter who enjoys a challenging fast-paced environment.  This individual also has working knowledge of Java software development methodology.  He / she must always put the team first, and continually strive for technical excellence.

 

Duties

  • Develop Java/J2EE components and JSP pages, use best practices to document at different stages of development cycle
  • Work under the direction of project leader, effectively communicate with team members
  • Develop SQL queries through JDBC and apply performance tuning
  • Expert coding skills, debugging and application profiling, Java performance tuning
  • Ensure development requirements are met and unit tested. Design and develop product verification tests
  • Review and research new technologies, provide suggestions about 3rd party components integration

 

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or Information Science
  • 2 years experience in software development
  • Mastery of object-oriented design, familiar with web technologies and MVC design pattern, good knowledge of relational data modeling and design
  • Proficiency in Java, Servlet, JSP, SQL, JavaScript, HTML. Knowledge of Ajax, Custom Tags and Actionscript is a plus
  • Experience with UNIX, Linux and Windows operating system platforms
  • Excellent problem solving and analytical skills as well as good verbal and written communication skills
  • Familiarity with open source development tools like ANT and Eclipse
  • Ability to document development including application design and ongoing documentation of changes and modifications
  • Superior bug fixing and testing skills
  • Ability to succeed with little management direction

 

About Lake 5 Media

Lake 5 Media is a provider of advanced software and systems to the media industry.   Our customer list includes national television networks and media service agencies.

We are committed to providing our clients increased operational effectiveness and superior reaction time to the media markets via our suite of tools.  Our offerings include media marketplace analysis tools, competitive analysis tools, optimization applications, media planning products as well as inventory management and allocation systems.

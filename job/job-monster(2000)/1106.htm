Senior Principal Java Developer

About the Job

 

Exciting Opportunities for Motivated Senior/Principal Java Developers at Litle & Co.!

 

Are you an energetic, enthusiastic senior- or principal-level Java Engineer?  Do you want to deliver high-quality software that gets installed into production on a monthly basis?  Litle & Co. is seeking motivated senior and principal level software developers who want to create robust, high-volume production services and take pride in solving real world business problems.

 

Litle & Co. is the fastest growing player in the payment management industry and is located north of Boston in Lowell, MA.  Founded in 2001, Litle & Co. provides payment processing and consultative merchant services for card-not-present and alternative payments.  The key to our quality of service is our underlying technology platform, which is developed and supported in-house.  Using superior technology combined with practical thinking, we are able to rapidly develop the features our clients need without compromising on reliability.  If you want to gain experience expanding a production platform and maintaining a high-volume transaction processing system, this is the team for you.

 

Our company has experienced tremendous growth over the past several years, and we have just doubled the square footage of our office space to accommodate this growth.  Excellent benefits, competitive compensation packages, and the use of an on-site, state-of-the-art fitness center free of charge are just a few of the perks offered to Litle & Co.'s employees.  Submit your resume to hr_resumes@litle.com , and explore all that Litle has to offer!

 

Job Description:

 

Our Java Software Engineers develop back-end and front-end applications for all aspects of our payment processing platform in the ever-expanding field of e-commerce. If you have worked in a transaction processing industry or would like to experience the design and coding excellence that the industry demands, consider joining Litle & Co., the leading provider of payment management services to direct marketing and e-commerce merchants.

 

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate on distilling business requirements into technical solutions
  • Design, implement, document, and test software components and systems
  • Utilize agile software development procedures and practices, including paired programming and test driven development
  • Perform unit level and integration level testing of software components and systems to ensure a high degree of quality
  • Gain domain expertise in all aspects of e-commerce payment processing

 

Requirements:

  • Prior professional software development experience
  • Superior problem solving and analysis skills
  • Team focused with strong collaboration and communications skills
  • Emphasis on attention to detail and quality
  • Fluency in OO design and development with Java or C++
  • SQL experience wiith ability to create complex and efficient queries a strong plus
  • Experience with J2EE, XML, JDBC, JMX, RMI and Web UI frameworks and application servers is a strong plus
  • Test driven development experience a strong plus, both unit and integration level
  • Agile and pair programming experience a plus
  • Linux/Unix experience a plus

 

 

Please email resumes to hr_resumes@litle.com .

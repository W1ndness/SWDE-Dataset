Technical Architect (HP Networking)

About the Job

Check out the Top 5 Reasons to Work at Softchoice!

Softchoice is a leading North American provider of IT solutions and services and one of Canada’s Best Workplaces™ five years and running. With a network of more than 40 local sales offices supported by five regional call centers, we work with partners like Microsoft, HP and IBM to manage the technology needs of more than 19,000 small, mid-market, enterprise and public sector organizations.

Our commitment to service and to delivering strategic advice in person has resulted in consistent, above-market growth. Whether providing efficient, low-cost technology fulfillment, or advanced solution design and implementation, we’re helping organizations everywhere harness the power of innovation.



Position Objectives:

To increase HP revenue and gross profit, with specific emphasis on HP ProCurve/E-Series Products. Provide HP program, pricing, product, and promotion expertise in support of our sales organization. Execute marketing campaigns, strategies, and tactics to drive business results.

Key Job Responsibilities:

1. Provide Pre-Sales Support to the Telesales & Outbound sales organization

  • Provide top notch, end to end Phone, e-mail and customer facing support on deals within prescribed thresholds
  • Drive field sales understanding and awareness of HP products and sales process
  • Act as a primary point of contact for connecting Softchoice and HP resources

     2. Possess high degree of HP product knowledge

  • Understand how to build/configure HP solutions, specifically around ProCurve/E-Series technologies
  • Deep understanding of HP products at an advanced functional and mid-level technical level
  • Formulate and drive increased attach rates, services selling and up-sell tactics
  • Functional understanding of non-focus HP product lines

    3. Knowledge of HP Pricing programs

  • Bid pricing policies and procedures
  • Product promotions
  • Deal registration – Volume and Value Programs
  • Other discount pricing programs

    4. Pipeline Management Activities

  • Ability to manage, track and forecast sales opportunity pipeline for HP on a weekly basis
  • Proactively follow up on sales opportunities
  • Track and summarize transactional (Quote + Order) activity

    5. Business Analysis & Reporting

  • Analyze sales participation and proactively suggest opportunities for improvement
  • Ensure accurate, up to date, relevant information from HP is contextualized and communicated internally at Softchoice
  • Use of internal reporting tools to transform data into insight and drive educated decision making

    6. Training & Certification

  • Maintain Up to date HP sales certifications
  • Possess HP technical certifications as required
  • Know the HP training process & methodology of their products
  • Understand the certification process for HP products
  • Conduct sales rep training on HP products
  • Attend HP and Softchoice meetings, training, and events where required (some overnight travel required)

    7. Internal Softchoice Process & Procedures

  • Understand how to work with internal departments (Finance, Product Master) to facilitate sales process
  • Identify areas to improve efficiency and effectiveness of internal processes with respect to sales, bid desk and supply chain teams.

    8. Sales Communication

  • Proactively suggest and create new sales tools to increase product awareness
  • Leverage existing sales communication vehicles such as Playbooks, internal portals and newsletters to ensure HP messaging is included and relevant

     



    • Experience 

  • Minimum of 2-4 years direct sales experience in the technology industry with strong focus on Networking products and sales cycles
  • University degree or College Diploma with Networking focus strongly preferred
  • Experience working with a technology reseller preferred
  • Technical Certifications for ProCurve/E-Series and UCS technologies strongly preferred

    *****Please include a list of current and previously held HP certifications with your application

    Key Performance Indicators (How you will be measured)

  • Achieve assigned revenue & gross profit plans
  • Top line revenue growth
  • Top line gross profit growth
  • Pipeline growth
  • Quotes and Order growth
  • Big deal activity
  • Customer buying account growth
  • Retention
  • Acquisition
  • Vendor engagement and relationship
  • Level of vendor field engagement with Softchoice sales organization
  • Completion of assignments on time
  • Execution of marketing programs, campaigns, strategies and tactics

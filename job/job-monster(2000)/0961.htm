UI Designer (Living)

About the Job

 

Our client is celebrating their 25th anniversary earlier this year and reflecting on the impact their brand has had on revolutionizing the Internet, they are now charting the next 25 years. This company is pioneering a new standard for high quality content on the Internet and reinventing how content is distributed to new and existing communities. They are a world leader in this space.

 

Core to the company's DNA is innovation and creativity. They have recently launched a year-long "Project on Creativity" with iconic artist Chuck Close to create an Originals collection, and have collaborated with artists from around the world to showcase work and build community

 

The Media Design team brings our company's commitment to innovation and creativity to our suite of 80+ sites and brands. The responsibilities include: brand development, large scale web platforms, mobile experiences, Flash development, video experiences, and advertising support (pitches/concepts, custom integrations). Our client is focused on building groundbreaking interaction design and world-class visual design. As a member of the media design team, you would join a team of top designers, and be a direct contributor to the products that they develop. This client hires from top creative powerhouses like AKQA, Big Spaceship, Code and Theory, and Fantasy Interactive.

 

They are seeking User Interface Designers with a passion for creating the best interactive experiences with experience in Living. The successful candidate will be collaborate with a team of designers, product managers, and developers to create forward-thinking, user-focused designs while meeting business objectives and needs.

  

Job Responsibilities:
• Create user-focused designs by considering market analysis, usage data, site metrics, and usability findings

• Participate in user testing and research

• Develop taxonomy structures, site and navigation maps, user flows, and wireframes

• Work closely with design and technical leads to establish and maintain functional and aesthetic product consistencies

• Present work to design, product, and development teams and senior executives for review and feedback

 

Job Requirements:

• 6-8 years of directly related experience in information design for a large scale living channel, magazine, website, or related on line media.

• Proficiency in industry standard UI design software (OmniGraffle, Illustrator, Photoshop, Visio, Dreamweaver, Fireworks)

• Excellent communication, presentation and interpersonal skills

• Ability to work in a collaborative team including development, product, and programming

• Ability to define, articulate, and evangelize the role of UI design within the company

• Fluency in best practices for web-based information architecture and design

• Familiarity with development practices

Education:

• Bachelor’s degree (Masters, Fine Arts preferred) or equivalent experience

About the Job

Join our growing team as a Programmer/Developer


FirstLab, is a leader in drug and alcohol testing program administration for 20 years. Our diversified healthcare services extend to various Fortune 200 & 500 companies, law enforcement agencies, energy companies, state and municipal governments, airlines, professional recovery programs, hospital and outpatient facilities, and sports organizations.

Our IT Department, the hub of our company, recognized for their innovation, skill and ability to meet the challenges of our growing company, is seeking several experienced Programmer/Developers. Your responsibilities will include development and testing of web based applications and writing accurate and complete technical and end user documentation.

Requirements

The ideal candidate will have

·  Bachelor of Computer Science

·  Minimum of two years of development experience

·  Working knowledge in the software development process and project life cycle

·  Good communication skills and the ability to work across multiple teams

·  Good documentation skills

·  SQL, C#, ASP.NET, Web, HTML/CSS, Javascript, Ajax, Visual Basic

·    Experience with Microsoft GP (Great Plains) a plus

·  Ability to multitask

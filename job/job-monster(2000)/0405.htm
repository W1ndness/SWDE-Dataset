About the Job

Field Operations Technician

 

 

About Us:

Alpheus Communications, a well established provider of TDM, Sonet, and DWDM telecom services to carrier and enterprise customers, is seeking an experienced and motivated Field Operations Technician to join our Dallas area team.

 

Job Description:

  • Responsible for surveillance, installation, circuit test/acceptance, and network outage repair for the deployed Alpheus Communications production transport and IP/Ethernet networks
  • Works closely with NOC, OSP, Engineering and customers to ensure maximum network operation, integrity, and availability
  • Works closely with customer and provider fault management teams to ensure efficient problem resolution
  • Performs test and turn-up of DS1 to OC192 circuits, DWDM Waves, Ethernet over copper circuits to 20Mbps, Ethernet over DS3 to 90Mbps, Ethernet over Sonet to a GigE, including direct customer interface
  • Configures and troubleshoots routers, switches, and Ethernet CPE/NIU network elements
  • Other duties as assigned

 

Knowledge and Skills:

  • Familiarity with ILEC telecommunications testing methods, IEEE RFC2544 Carrier Ethernet testing procedures and all associated equipment
  • Prior experience in the telecommunications, military electronics, and/or data networking industry
  • Prior experience successfully managing external customer relationships
  • Knowledge of the technical aspects of wireline, microwave, data, and fiber optic networks standards
  • Strong customer service orientation
  • Inventory management experience
  • Ability to deliver results in cross-functional and matrix management environment
  • Strong working knowledge of MS Office platform and PC communications protocols
  • Resourceful and creative problem solving experience
  • Proactive and detail-oriented
  • Strong aptitude for creativity
  • Good written and communication skills

 

Special Technical Knowledge:

  • Strong focus on operations efficiency and productivity
  • Must have excellent analytical and problem solving skills, as well as excellent communication, decision-making and organizational skills
  • Ability to prioritize and manage multiple issues simultaneously
  • Strong technical skills in the transport / fiber / IP telecommunications field
  • Must be able to work under stress and in a fast paced customer driven environment

 

Special Administrative Knowledge:

Personal Computer

  • Trouble Ticket software, Network Management software, Word, Excel, and Microsoft Project programs
  • Fiber optic, IP test methodology knowledge, operating/understanding OTDR test results, and Broadband Test Systems

 


Special Skills and Abilities:

  • Assists with new site turn up and development. Tasks may include resource planning, scheduling, and adherence to set deliverables
  • Provide field support for new construction in the following areas: on site supervision, equipment delivery coordination, equipment installation, turn-up, and optimization
  • Coordinate or perform maintenance of sites which involves the emergency generator, batteries/power equipment, the AC units, locks and fences, coax/fiber cabling, batteries, high voltage systems, and etc
  • Maintains optic transport equipment, circuits, and facilities by routine or fault testing and repairing as needed
  • Assist where needed to identify customer system and equipment problems
  • Determines the cause of equipment failure using various types of complex test equipment
  • Ensures market compliance with accepted maintenance procedures and policies
  • Ensures compliance with written operating plans and procedures, company policies, labor laws, and OSHA, FAA, DOT, and Hazardous Materials
  • Provide support for customer complaints, suggestions, and concerns

 

Physical Requirements and Working Conditions:

  • Be able to lift up to 75 lbs of weight
  • Must be available to work after-hours and weekends as need as on-call support in the assigned market
  • Be able to travel up to 25% of the time within the state of Texas
  • Be able to travel up to 75% of the time within the locally assigned market
  • Stand for extended period of time
  • Be able to work assigned shift work as required for Production Network support and Engineering project implementation

 

Education/Experience: 

  • Technical two-year degree or four years military experience in electronics or telecommunications
  • 4+ years of industry experience in a field tech, engineering, or operations capacity

 

Compensation:

  • Competitive salary, commensurate with experience

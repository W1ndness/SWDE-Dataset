Network Systems Analyst

About the Job

Software Engineering Services is a premiere defense contracting organization servicing multiple industries, as well as local government and private sectors.  Due to the sustained superior performance of high performing team members, the SES Team has been awarded the opportunity to serve the mission of Ft. Sam Houston by providing a Network Analyst.  To accomplish this mission, we are seeking an Analyst.  Job Description and requirements are as follows:  

Reviews, evaluates, plans, and implements network architecture and systems. Responds to outages and other service deficiencies by troubleshooting existing network systems and architecture and resolving issues in a timely manner. Installs hardware and software updates, maintenance releases, system upgrades, and resolves any resulting software and hardware problems. Based on industry standards, experience, and troubleshooting results recommends improvements to existing systems. Designs, develops, and maintains network layout documentation to support growth, enhance performance, and facilitate technology advancements. Provides documentation/project tracking and management reporting to supervisory personnel at multiple levels. Familiar with a variety of the field’s concepts, best practices, procedures, and security guidelines. Has understanding and experience with Department of Defense (DoD) standards and a wide variety of networking protocols. May be responsible for managing / supervising the work of others.

Requirements:  Must have CCNP or CCDP, demonstrates knowledge of wide, campus or local area networks(WAN, CAN, LAN). Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in the IT field of 6-9 years; familiar with IT standards, concepts, practices, and procedures; performs a variety of tasks. Security + / Network + highly desired.


Project Description

The nature of this mission is processing incoming and outgoing personnel and equipment, ensuring their user account is opened or closed properly, and all other requirements are met within a timely manner.  Additionally ensuring equipment moved from BRACKED location to new location, is Army compliant, up to date and able to be put on the network


To learn more about how you can join this high-performing team and support this critical mission by providing outstanding technical services, send your RESUME and COVER LETTER to www.SESsolutions.com TODAY! 


This position will start immediately and end on September 29, 2011


*An Active Department of Defense Secret Clearance is REQUIRED for all positions

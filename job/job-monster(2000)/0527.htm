About the Job

  Holloway Sportswear, Inc.

     

We are currently seeking a resourceful team player to join our IT team.

 

Desktop Support Technician

 

Required Qualifications:

·          Ability to install, configure, diagnose, maintain and repair hardware and software.

·          Experience supporting Windows XP, Office 2003, and IBM iSeries Access.

·          Excellent customer service, communication, organizational and problem solving skills.

·          Ability to manage and prioritize multiple tasks.

·          Analyze, design and implement effective end-user solutions.

·          A+, or MCTS certification, or equivalent experience.

·          Self-motivated, able to work with minimal supervision, and a strong team player.

·          Worked in an Active Directory environment and possess a basic understanding of networking.

 

Desired Qualifications:

·          Experience supporting Dell desktops and notebooks, HP and Lexmark printers and other peripherals.

·          Experience supporting Windows 7, Office 2010.

·          Network+ certification or equivalent experience.

·          Experience supporting MAC desktops.

·          Experience using Track-IT Helpdesk and Symantec Ghost, or similar products.

·          Experience supporting thin clients using Terminal Server.

·          Basic cabling skills.

VP, Motion Solutions - Adams


MSC Software is seeking a leader, a General Manager for it’s very successful multibody dynamics simulation product, Adams.

Reporting to the CEO, and based in Ann Arbor, MI., the VP of Motion Solutions will be responsible for driving MSC’s motion business worldwide. In addition to being the primary visionary and chief salesperson this leader will have responsibility for product management, partner management, product development of vertical solutions and, in matrix fashion, worldwide motion business development specialists, services and technical support. This is a unique opportunity to own and guide a product already #1 in its markets.

 

Direct Reports:  Product Management Marketing and Product Development

Responsibilities:

·       Product Development – Initial focus on Adams as it is applied for the automobile industry and on a dedicated application for office and industrial machinery.  Additional solutions to be developed based upon market need. 

·       Education  – Ensure that MSC motion products and Adams specifically become the standard in academic institutions worldwide. 

·       Sales Enablement– Working with product marketing, develop material required for the field organization to effectively excite prospects and customers and beat the competition.

·       Professional Services– Ensure that the field services organization for motion products are well trained to sell and deliver high value services that also lead to additional product sales. 

·       Partner development – Motivate current and future ISV partners to create a broad range of stand alone and embedded applications and assist them in the promotion of their products on a worldwide basis.

·       Brand development – Work with the global marketing team to ensure that MSC and Adams are recognized as the global leader in motion simulation.  

·       Acquisitions – Seek possible solutions that will strengthen the MSC motion product portfolio.

 

 

The successful candidate will possess the following skills and qualifications:

·       10+ years of CAE/CAD industry experience, preferred. 

·       Combination of business, marketing and technology skills and keen decision making ability for best strategic and product tradeoffs.

·       Proven ability to author product/solution business plans, business models and requirements documents (at the market/customer requirements level)

·       Demonstrated experience in identifying new product/solution opportunities and leading/translating these into measurable revenue and EBIT growth

·       Track record of success in development of strategic roadmaps and initiating/driving/integrating small/medium acquisitions to success.

·       Demonstrable experience in leading viable commercial products to improved market share and increased revenue.

About the Job

 
The Company
Established in 1996, Visionary Integration Professionals (VIP) is a global information technology solution provider. VIP’s unwavering commitment to understand the business needs and goals of its customers allows the company to partner with clients to increase business performance, mitigate risk and resolve challenges. The VIP family of solutions includes business intelligence and data integration, human capital management, technology integration and operations, quality management, management consulting, and SAP professional services. For more than 11 years, VIP has delivered quality solutions nationwide to more than 900 clients that include various industries in the commercial sector as well as federal, state and local government. VIP maintains its headquarters in Folsom, California and was named the Best Place to Work in Sacramento. For more information, visit www.vipconsulting.com or email us at moreinfo@vipconsulting.com .


This position reports to the product training manager. This position is responsible for the following:
- Providing delivery and support for LMS software application training for a broad spectrum of clients
- writing, reviewing, and presenting training materials
- creating an agenda for the specific training for each client
- being the lead trainer or co-trainer for the training
- traveling to client sites to perform training, or providing remote delivery (by Webex etc)
- communicating with the training manager or the internal project teams, to determine the requirements for each client’s training and the number of training hours budgeted (to include travel and training)
- writing and distributing a training summary document/email, after training is completed
- communicating with the Product team about potential issues found and/or product suggestions from clients

Bachelor’s degree in a related area, or a minimum of 5 years in a related field
3-5 years experience with developing and delivering training (ideally software training)
Knowledge of LMS software products desired
Ability to travel 50% or more


Travel is required. Costs are usually covered by the client or Meridian.

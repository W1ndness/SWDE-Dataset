CCNA Network / Server / PC/ Break Fix Technician

About the Job

We are seeking a well qualified, detail oriented PC/ Break Fix Technician with Network/Voice experience to work in the Oxnard, CA Area, for a rapidly expanding nationwide company.

 

Job Duties Include: 

 

Successful candidate should have 5 years enterprise support or equivalent experience. The candidate must have good written and verbal communications skills with professional appearance. Past experience with customer relations a must. Must be punctual and a team player. Must be able to troubleshoot and resolve issues with wiring, and cabling to ensure connectivity and functionality. Candidate must be able to identify and resolve network and telecommunication system malfunctions and connectivity issues.

 

Candidate must be able to perform routine system administration to include but not limited to: addition / deletion of users and hardware/software, defining access rights, implementing and ensuring compliance with security procedures and policies and Daily Maintenance Sheet (DMS) for compliance. Candidate will be purchasing hardware/software as required by the site. Candidate will be planning and implementing computer migrations and maintaining inventory controls.

 

Due to the nature of this position the candidate should also have the following expertise.

 

Successful candidates must be able to prepare workstations by configuring hardware, software, network cards, printers, and peripherals. Must be able to troubleshoot and resolves desktop and laptop issues, printer problems. Candidate will need to keep inventory of all systems and equipment. The candidate must be able to troubleshoot software problems. The candidate must be proficient in Windows XP, Windows NT, Novel Netware and Norton Anti Virus.

 

Candidates must be able to install upgrades by planning and implementing desktop rollout projects; installing hardware and software. Serves existing client account by analyzing work order; planning daily installation / repair schedule; investigating complaints; conducting tests; and resolving problems.

 

Candidate must have effective communication skills and be able to provide customer service through a central help desk to resolve and ensure resolution of problems.

 

The successful candidates must pass a background check and drug testing before placed on this assignment. We offer excellent benefits including medical, dental, life, vision, and 401K plans with competitive wages.

 

Candidate must have a CCNA certification.

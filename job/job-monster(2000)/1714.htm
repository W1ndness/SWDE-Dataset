Senior Test Engineer $75K -$95K

About the Job

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

·         Manufacturing and Production Test strategy and ATE design and implementation

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

 

·         Minimum BS Electrical Engineering education with 7+ years experience in ATE SW design and development, Aerospace and Defense preferred

·         Strong knowledge of test executives like TestStand

·         ATE development using SW like LabVIEW, VB .NET, C#, C++

·         Working knowledge of ATML (Automated Test Markup Language)

·         Experience using databases like SQL

·         Familiarity with QA standards such as ISO17025 and ANSI Z540

·         Mixed signal knowledge, understand schematics and specifications

·         Statistical analysis of data and measurement uncertainty analysis

·         Experience using test equipment like oscilloscopes, signal generators, logic analyzers, etc

·         Excellent debugging and troubleshooting skills

·         Excellent interpersonal and communication skills

 

 

 

If qualified and interested, please submit your resume in MS word format
via the *Apply* link.

Or send to resumes@techounds.com

All responses will be treated with the utmost in confidence.

Only those closely matching the qualifications of the position will be contacted.

We offer a competitive salary along with a comprehensive benefits package to include: Health, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, 401(k)

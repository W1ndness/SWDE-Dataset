Web Developer

About the Job

Overview:

Axis41 is a marketing communications agency that combines strategic consulting, brand design, and leading technologies to deliver complete marketing solutions for our clients.

 

We are seeking a Web Developer to join our technical services team.  As an agency, our employees are required to work with multiple clients on multiple projects simultaneously.  An ability and commitment to deliver projects on time is a MUST.  The nature of our business requires excellent communication skills with both clients and the internal project team.

 

As a Web Developer for Axis41, you will be responsible for the fulfillment of web development and integration projects for our clients in a variety of web technologies.

Key Areas of Responsibility:

-Develop and maintain corporate web sites for Axis41 clients using PHP/HTML/CSS and other web technologies

-Implement a variety of site architectures and navigation structures 

-Construct templates and understand project structure 

-Write code to overcome cross browser compatibility issues

 

Knowledge Base:

-2+ years web development experience

-Excellent understanding and proven experience of Internet technologies including web programming languages, databases, web services, multimedia, content management, web browsers, and search engines

-Knowledgeable of marketing automation concepts, tactics and issues

 

Skill Set:

-Expert HTML and CSS skills

-2+ years experience in PHP including OOP in PHP5

-Experience with JavaScript

-Basic understanding of programming using an API

-Basic knowledge of Linux and Apache

 

Compensation:

Axis41 offers a competitive compensation package including medical, dental, life, and long-term disability insurance, 401k, and profit sharing. Salary range will depend on experience and other variables.

Communications Engineer - Voice Recorder System

About the Job


Position: Communications Engineer with primary responsibilities for Voice Recorder System (VRS) Installation/Administration.


Strongly Desired Skills:

·         Thorough knowledge of digital voice recorders (NICE)

·          Familiarity with telecommunications procedures and physical cross connection

                       

 

Preferred Skills:

·          Certification in Microsoft Server OS

·          Understanding of Servers, Databases, Security, Networks, and related terminology

·          Understanding of PBX switches (Avaya, Centrex)

·          Understanding of two way radio systems

·          Understanding of dispatch consoles (Safetran DTX, Avtech VoIP)

·          Prior railroad experience

 

 

Required Experience:

·          5 years managing Microsoft Server OS

 

 



Additional Required Skills:

·          Experience using Word, Excel, Visio, and other Microsoft Office Suite products

·          Strong verbal and written communication skills

·          Strong interpersonal skills

·          Strong teamwork and customer support focus

·          Ability to maintain focus on multiple projects concurrently

·          Ability to analyze, interpret, and simplify technical information, and clearly communicate this information to others

 

 

Preferred Education:  4-yr College Degree preferably in Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

 

 

 

 

Responsibilities:

·          Assist in the management of all current and future Voice Recorder Systems. This includes but is not limited to the Operating System, SQL Server, user accounts, and application specific software

·          Work as a member of team responsible for the installation and/or upgrade of Voice Recorder Systems.

·          Perform some project management, and supervision of work and sub contractors. 

         

 

 


Additional Responsibilities / Requirements:

·            Domestic travel is anticipated, possibly 2 weeks per month

·            Current state issued drivers license

·            Proof of citizenship or authority to work in the US

·            Available for calls after hours

·            Assist with training of other employees as necessary

 

 

Your primary responsibility will be project support of system installations, verification of those installation, and administration of the entire VRS. The actual installation of VRS and associated equipment will be handled by multiple groups including this position and additional personnel.

About the Job

Sr RoR Software Engineer – Denver, CO


Overview:
This is a startup website that needs to be built from the ground up. Candidate must be well experienced with Ruby on Rails. We are looking for an all star Sr. Software Engineer to take the lead driving this new business to success, with the support and resources of the parent organization. Working in our Denver office, you'll find yourself a dynamic work environment and will join a brilliant, young team of entrepreneurially driven talent. Everyone is given the freedom to do what they do best. We're passionate about what we do, we work hard and play hard. We reward creativity and talent.

Summary:
The Lead Developer will take full responsibility for leading the technical development and day to day operations of the website. Responsible for managing and leading development efforts, scaling a consumer focused web business, managing the back end and front end.


Responsibilities:
• Manage and lead technical development of complete web stack.
• Working with the company owner to provide input and direction on features and functionality (and then implementing them!)
• Responsible for hosting, management of backups, scalability etc..


Ideal skills:
• Start up mentality and ideally experience - you are going to be operating in a multifaceted and pivotal role in the growth of a new online business
• 5-7 years web / software development experience
• 3 years RoR, PHP, Javascript, HTML / CSS experience
• MySQL and experience with LAMP or other stack preferred
• Experience with search server technology (e.g. Solr, Lucene) a plus
• Experience with web analytics package such as Google Analytics ideal
• An understanding of SEO best practices
• Experience with high volume consumer web systems a major plus


This is a fantastic opportunity for a true self starter to make a huge contribution to a development and success of a new online business - you will be operating in a highly entrepreneurial role, but with the support, resources and knowledge of the parent organization. We are offering a competitive base salary, bonus plan, equity, and relocation assistance.

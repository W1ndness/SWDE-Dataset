About the Job

Provider of satellite entertainment programming to U.S households

 

EDITORIAL/GRAPHICS COMPUTER SUPPORT TECHNICIAN
 
DIRECTV needs a Post Production Support Technician. This highly-skilled and knowledgeable individual will oversee and assist in the management of all design and editing systems, servers, and software within a dynamic and fun creative group.
 
Responsibilities include:
 
  • Troubleshooting day-to-day technical issues with production servers and workstations, including XSAN, as well as Video and Print Production workstations

  • Maintaining the archiving system

  • Interfacing with DIRECTV IT and facility divisions for troubleshooting and technical synergy

  • Managing the FTP site

  • Supervising and maintaining shared servers





The successful candidate will have at least 3 years of experience in the IT/broadcast arena, ideally working for very busy and highly technical production facilities.
 
Your abilities should include: advanced knowledge of and hands-on experience with servers (XSAN in particular), Final Cut Pro editing systems, archiving practices, Macintosh graphic systems (both motion graphics and print), practical knowledge of popular creative software (editing as well as 3D and 2D).
 
You'll also be the kind of person who stays up to date on software and can organize our current software for quick reference. You're creative, organized, detail-oriented, with excellent communication skills. You also have a solid awareness of products, services, and systems that are currently used throughout our industry. 
 
You should be flexible, energetic, highly motivated, and open-minded. You're able to solve problems quickly and work well under pressure. Finally, you must enjoy working in a team environment and interacting with a wide range of people and personalities.

About the Job

JOB DESCRIPTION
Application Engineer
The Application Engineer at Cayuse is responsible for understanding, developing,
modifying and supporting a broad range of information system solutions both inside and
outside the company, often performing required duties with minimal supervision. The
primary focus is the analysis, specification, development, deployment and support of
production software technologies as a member of our Professional Services team. This
team deploys and operates software products for large research institutions via SaaS
delivery models. The Application Engineer reports to the Manager of Professional Services.
As a technical professional, the Application Engineer is expected to understand diverse
business processes within our customer organizations as well as within Cayuse itself, and
then participate with multi-disciplinary teams to creatively and deploy robust IT solutions
to supporting those processes. The person in this role must exhibit high levels of
professional initiative in determining appropriate priorities, determining when escalation of
issues is required, and productively interact with both internal & external personnel.
Responsibilities
Develop and maintain a deep understanding of how the entire suite of Cayuse products is
developed, deployed, operated and maintained. Quickly understand how to meet unique
needs on a customer by customer basis, develop solutions to those needs, and educate
other personnel within Cayuse about how to integrate those solutions into the overall
customer satisfaction model.
· Evaluate, respond, prioritize, resolve and document challenges presented to the
Professional Services team as a part of deploying and maintaining customer
solutions..
· Act as a technical lead for all work within the Professional Services organization.
· Work with the Manager of Professional Services to continuously define and improve
a wide spectrum of processes utilized by the Professional Services team. The
Application Engineer holds primary responsibility within the organization for defining
and improving technical business processes for the rest of the team
· Continuously exhibit the highest levels of professional behavior & ethics. Assist the
Manager of Professional Services in setting high expectations for professional
behavior across the entire Professional Services team, and demonstrate these
expectations by example to less-experienced members of the team.
Professional Experience
· At least 2 years of experience in production software product development and/or
operations environments, including a combination of enterprise-scale systems
adoption and integration, 24x7 high-availability operations, and significant levels of
direct end-user assistance.
· At least 2 years of demonstrable hands-on technology experience, including
significant software tool development and systems administration (Linux and MS
Windows).
· At least 2 years prior experience analyzing, defining, creating and implementing
business process automation solutions utilizing information technology systems,
with a focus on data-driven solutions, web-based solutions and enterprise-scale
system integration projects..
· Experience creating, deploying and maintaining technology solutions for institutions
of higher education will be given preferential consideration.
Technical Skills
· Deep familiarity with Linux, MS Windows, Tomcat, Apache, Javascript, Ajax, Java,
Perl, Oracle, MS SQL Server, PostgreSQL, PostFix. Some familiarity with PHP,
ColdFusion, SSO architectures (Shiboleth, CAS).
· Direct hands-on experience with highly available, multi-tenancy applications.
· Deep familiarity with various web-services integration technologies.
· Demonstrated autonomous problem solving abilities.
· Demonstrated experience delivering clear, effective and timely communication to
both technical & non-technical personnel, in both oral & written forms.
Education
Bachelors degree in Computer Science or a related technical discipline required;
equivalent experience may be considered.
Environment
Cayuse, Inc. is a web-based software company in Beaverton, Oregon, just outside
Portland. We have an informal, energetic and invigorating culture built around assembling
smart people and letting each person focus on what they do best. Our core product is an
enterprise software system utilized by research organizations to prepare, submit and
manage grant proposals seeking funds for important scientific research. We are planning
for strong growth in our customer base, revenue, and product mix, while working diligently
to keep all our customers delighted. This all adds up to Cayuse being a great place to grow
and work!
Other Information
Full-time, on-site, salaried position, with generous medical, dental, and vision benefits as
well as paid vacation.
Contact

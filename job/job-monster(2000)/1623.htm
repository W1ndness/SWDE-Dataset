SR UX ARCHITECT

About the Job

Our client is seeking a contract to possible hire SR UX ARCHITECT in the Atlanta area.

 

Position Description:

 

The Sr. UX Architect will work closely with editorial and design staff to help turn content into intuitive experience designs, page designs, application designs, navigation flows, and interaction models through the use of user flows, sitemaps, user scenarios and wireframes.

 

Candidates should be experienced working with design and content teams to whiteboard, brainstorm, and concept solutions for complex storytelling, organization, and navigation challenges.

 

This role will work closely with other departments including editorial, television production, product development, web development, video, project management, ad sales, marketing and others.

 

Projects will include designing the UI and interaction guidelines for special reports, custom interactives and next-generation, forward-thinking projects across multiple platforms. 

 

Qualifications

 

o Creating sitemaps, wireframes, and user flows

o Creating intuitive and innovative interaction and navigation models

o Gathering & documenting front-end requirements

• Experience leading workshops and presentations on high-level concepts and strategies with and to stakeholders

• Ability to do light project management, including coordination of small teams and projects  (with internal stakeholders and external agency partners.)                                                                                                       • Communication - Excellent oral and written communication skills

• Multi-Task - Demonstrated ability to multi-task and manage priorities

• Collaborative -- Works well with others to support shared goals

• Analytical - Effective problem solving and analytical skills

• Creative - Demonstrated ability to develop creative solutions

• Presentation - Meeting facilitation and presentation skills

• Job Knowledge - Good mix of editorial, design, business, and technical understanding

 

Minimum Requirements:  

 

Minimum: Bachelor's Degree

Preferred (but not necessary): Master's Degree in Cognitive Psychology, Industrial Design, Anthropology, Product Design, HCI, Information Design or similar discipline

 

 5+ years of experience that includes activities related to one or more of the following: interaction design, content strategy, information architecture, human computer interaction, digital product design. 

Experience approaching design challenges collaboratively with visual designers, writers, technical engineers, and business stakeholders.

Experience applying user research and usability testing methodologies to derive user insight and test the effectiveness of design work.                                                                                                                                  

Desired:  Editorial experience.  Media and entertainment (working in Television, Radio, Print, or other related media field.)

 

If interested, please send resume, detailed cover letter, desired W2 hourly rate, and portfolio URL to norma@paladinstaff.com

 

Only candidates authorized to work in the US will be considered.

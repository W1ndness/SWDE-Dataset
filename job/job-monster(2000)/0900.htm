About the Job

OPCO Description

 AirMagnet is the leader in security, performance and compliance solutions for wireless LANS. The company's innovative products include (AME) AirMagnet Enterprise, the leading 24x7 WLAN security and performance management solution, and AirMagnet WiFi Analyzer — which is known as the "de facto tool for wireless LAN troubleshooting and analysis." Other products provide WLAN site survey and design, RF interference detection, remote diagnostics, and the world's first voice over WiFi analysis solution. AirMagnet has more than 8,500 customers worldwide, including 75 of the Fortune 100. The company is based in Sunnyvale, California. AirMagnet,Inc. is a part of Fluke Networks. Fluke Networks provides innovative solutions for the installation and certification, testing, monitoring and analysis of copper, fiber and wireless networks used by enterprises and telecommunications carriers. Both AirMagnet and Fluke Networks are part of the Danaher Corporation.

Danaher Overview

 Danaher Corporation designs, manufactures, and markets professional, medical, industrial, and consumer products, which all generate approximately $13 Billon in revenue. Danaher’s business activities encompass four reporting segments and are comprised of six strategic platforms: Medical Technologies, Professional Instrumentation (Environmental, Electronic Test), Industrial Technologies (Motion, Product ID, and Focused Niche Businesses) and Tools & Components (Mechanic’s Hand Tools). Danaher operates globally with about 50% of its revenues derived from outside the United States. The Company has significant operating businesses headquartered in Europe and has greatly expanded its operating presence in Asia over the past several years. Danaher is a well-capitalized business, which has historically used available cash flow to fund acquisition activity. Culturally, the company operates in a highly de-centralized model with an extremely lean corporate structure. Danaher has a proven system for achieving performance: the Danaher Business System (DBS). It drives every aspect of the group’s culture and performance. DBS is a system of continuous improvement and is used to guide and measure everyday activities, which has enabled it to double in size about every 5-years with impressive financial performance.

Description

 Senior level software engineer to provide strong technical abilities in design, development, and implementation of the AirMagnet WLAN solutions. The successful candidate will join our efforts to design and develop innovative technology solutions that enhance our strong product suite. You will be a key contributor to the effort to empower our valued customers by providing industry leading WLAN Network assurance solutions.

  • Working closely with Technical Management team, to understand product requirements, design technical solutions.
  • Driving detailed technical design documents, and active participation in design reviews.
  • Implementation of new features for AirMagnet products using C++, VC++ in windows environment.
  • Maintenance and enhancement of existing features.
  • Employ technical expertise in helping team members overcome technical problems.
  • Participate and be actively involved in the team during all phases of the project.
  • Work with QA/Technical Support and/or Outsourcing teams.
  • Adhere to release/project schedule and milestones.

 

Qualifications

  • 8-12 years experience in developing windows applications, thick clients/engineering applications.
  • Very good C++, Windows UI development skills.
  • Ability to work in a dynamic environment on multithreaded UI applications with charts/views/controls and other UI features.
  • Analytic in approach, understand and solve user problems.
  • Should be very hands on and need to have very good debugging skills.
  • Eager to learn and enthusiastic to understand and solve any problems.
  • Team player and should have good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Experience working with QA/support/outsourcing teams.
  • Any exposure to C# and .Net platform is a plus.

 

Education: Bachelors Degree or Equivalent

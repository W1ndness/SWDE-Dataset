About the Job

MASSACHUSETTS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (MIT)

Information Services and Technology

 

SENIOR WEB DEVELOPER

 

This position represents an opportunity to serve as a senior web developer within a talented development team working with a wide array of the latest web technologies through the full scope of architecture, design, and implementation, and provides broad engagement with the MIT community (faculty and students in a variety of departments, labs, and centers).

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

•  Develop and test major platform components

• Support backend development of platforms/services across Education Systems/IS&T

• Support and improve user workflows, interactions, and information architecture

• Collaborate closely with application support personnel in deploying and maintaining services and platforms

 

REQUIRED:

 

• At least three years of development experience with complex, tiered, high-availability, Java-based Internet applications; Java and Perl expertise; experience with JavaScript/Jquery; broad experience with a range of programming languages

• Strong background in web development and web services utilizing J2EE technologies (XML/XSL/XPath/XML schemas, Spring, Hibernate or other SQL database and Java object-relational DB mapping, SVN, Maven, Junit, and Tomcat)

•  Experience with Ajax, JavaScript, XHTML and CSS;

• Strong troubleshooting and debugging skills in a distributed/multithreaded environment.

• Strong software implementation and problem solving skills.

• Strong technical and communication skills

• Comfort working in a test-driven environment and with a diverse community of colleagues and customers

 

DESIRED:

 

• JQuery experience

• Experience with PHP

• Familiarity with agile development and object-oriented design

• Hands-on experience with learning management systems (particularly Blackboard and Sakai-based solutions)

 

Interested candidates may apply on-line at http://web.mit.edu/jobs .  Please reference job number mit-00007307 and indicate where you saw this posting. 

 

MIT is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer.  Applications from women, minorities, veterans, older workers, and individuals with disabilities are strongly encouraged.

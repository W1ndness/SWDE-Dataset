Sr. Systems Administrator

About the Job

Position purpose

The Senior System Administrator position is responsible for day-to-day operations of 24x7 web/digital media environment.  This position will assist in the plan and build-outs of new data centers or expansions.  The Senior System Administrator will be responsible for maintenance and configuration of servers, networks and other data center equipment.  This position will aid in designing and implementing redundancy, disaster recovery and system failovers.  This position will monitor and respond to system alerts and outages on a 24x7 basis.  This position will establish/recommend policies on system use and services.  The senior system administrator will troubleshoot data center issues from inception to remediation. 

Responsibilities/Duties/Functions/Tasks

 

·         Assist in plan and build-outs of new data centers or data center expansions

·         Maintain and configure servers, networks and other data center equipment

·         Design/implement system redundancy, failover and disaster recovery

·         Monitor and respond to system alerts and outages on a 24x7 basis

·         Establish/recommend policies on system use and services

·         Troubleshoot data center issues from inception to remediation

·         Travel to data centers may be required

Qualifications

Required:

 

BS in Computer Science, related field or equivalent experience

5+  years experience in a high-availability web/digital media environment

5+  years experience in a high-availability production data center

Deep working knowledge of TCP/IP, DNS, SMTP. IP Networks, Firewalls, VLANs, VPN Tunnels

Linux and shell scripting skills

Experience working in a rapid response 24x7 environment

Self-starter and independent worker

Good verbal and written communications skills

Deep experience with Windows and Linux servers and operating systems

Experience with SAN, NAS, RAID

Experience with load balancers a plus

Experience installing rack-mounted devices

Some travel (to data centers) required

 

Work Requirements

May have to lift equipment up to 50 pounds.

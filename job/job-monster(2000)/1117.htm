About the Job

IntraPoint is growing fast and we want to increase our development capacity! We are looking for 2 clever developers in our Reston, VA office! Our product Crisis Manager has been built and improved over the last several years, and we believe it is a world class application. It is written in PHP5, and runs on the LAMP-stack (Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP).

About us: IntraPoint is a software company based in Trondheim, Norway and Reston, VA. We create and deliver the product "Crisis Manager" to customers in many countries around the globe: Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Iceland, Russia, Brazil, Italy and USA, mainly to the oil/gas/energy, trains/shipping/aerospace and telecom vertical markets. Our solutions are web based applications for incident-, emergency-, crisis- and business continuity management that we host from our datacentres for most of our customers. We also supply integration with the product to the customers' applications. "Crisis Manager" has been built and improved over a number of years, and we believe it is a world leading application. It is written in PHP5, and runs on the LAMP-stack (Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP).


The typical tasks and projects for our developers cover these areas:

  • Customer projects: IntraPoint has many demanding customers, and we typically implement many customizations and extra features based on their needs.
  • Integration projects. We continuously work to make data from Crisis Manager available to other systems as well as integrate data from other systems into Crisis Manager.
  • Maintenance and Optimization of our production environment. As we grow in size, our systems need to scale well, have high availability and be rock solid and very stable. Together with "IT Operations" our developers work to improve the architecture and design as well as providing mainentance systems, reports and automation.
  • Maintenance, test and bug fixing: No product will maintain itself! IntraPoint developers share these responsibilities too.
  • Long term development and innovation projects, building the next generation of our product


If you have work experience, expertise or a good education covering some or all of these areas, we're very interested in hearing from you:

  • PHP5. Our code is object oriented, so if you feel short on PHP skills, you need a background in Java, C# or other OO language.
  • Databases: We mostly use MySQL. We use database procedures, and we integrate Crisis Manager with all major databases. It is a bonus if you have DBA skills or database optimalization experience.
  • JavaScript: Extensive experience with JavaScript is a must. It would also be beneficial if you have experience with a JavaScript-library like jQuery, Dojo or Prototype
  • HTML/XHTML and CSS: You need to keep up with the web standards and the development of the browsers. We need you to create good looking, easy-to-use web-pages that just work!
  • XML and XSLT: All our developers need a basic XML understanding, but we'd like to find people who know their XSLT, XPath and XQuery
  • Ajax: Good web apps don't refresh without a good reason!
  • Apache with modules: You need to be familiar with web servers and their configuration
  • Linux-experience is a bonus (as all production servers are Linux)
  • Unit tests: We'd like you to be familiar with PHPUnit or a similar framework.


All applicants need at least 3 years higher education, i.e., a bachelor's degree or higher.

We'd like to see the following traits/personal skills:

  • Have good command of the English language
  • Is not afraid to speak your mind and show initiative
  • Takes responsibility for assigned tasks (and completes them)
  • Is well-organized
  • Is not afraid of having customer contact
  • Is willing to take part in our duty schedule
  • Is a fast learner, show ability to understand new code, new APIs and solve new challenges
  • Can write, e.g., take meeting minutes, write estimates and document what you do and have done

We work in project teams, and is in the process of adapting and learning Scrum as well as test-driven development. We have nice and central offices at Solsiden in Trondheim and Reston Virginia. IntraPoint has several social arrangements, and we'd like you to join us in our common activities.

About the Job

ABOUT ATLAS

Established in 1989, Atlas Development Corporation is a rapidly growing best of breed solutions provider to some of the most recognized names in health care. Atlas Medical connects doctors to leading national and regional laboratories for diagnostic test ordering and results reporting, and Atlas Public Health provides mission critical bio-terrorism and infectious disease surveillance systems to state and local governments. We are the best at what we do.  Our web-based, database-driven applications utilize the latest software technology. We are currently seeking talented individuals to join our team of dedicated professionals.

Atlas Development Corporation is an Equal Employment Opportunity employer.

Job Description

The Product Analyst is responsible for working directly with clients to gather functional requirements and specifications and converting them into written documents for the development team to use during application engineering.  Additionally, Product Analysts are also responsible for assisting Sales with product functional issues as well as provide industry specific knowledge and expertise during the sales cycle.

The Product Analyst is responsible for working directly with clients to gather functional requirements and specifications and converting them into written documents for the development team to use during application engineering.  Additionally, Analysts are also responsible for assisting Sales with product functional issues as well as provide industry specific knowledge and expertise during the sales cycle.

 

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

 

§         Gather Functional Requirements and Specifications from client and convert them into written documents that are used by the development team for application engineering

§         Conduct regular meetings with the Project Team to review Functional Design Requirements, assign\coordinate implementation and design tasks, track progress, and clarify design issues pertaining to functionality and the application’s user interface

§         Verify project development is consistent with Functional Requirements and Development documents

§         Assure that the development release complies with product functionality and is in-line with product\client requirements

§         Track and manage issues that are “GAP” on product release between both the client and development team

§         Assisting the Development Manager in prioritizing and assigning development activities that are in-line with client’s expectations

§         Verifying that the technical release notes are properly formatted and available for public consumption for each client

§         Participating in analysis and design meetings for the Product

§         Assist Sales Engineering as a product or subject matter expert

 

SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES The Analyst has no direct supervisory responsibilities.

 

QUALIFICATIONS  To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

 

EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE

 

§         Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Business, Healthcare or related field.

 

§         Must have an aptitude for complex problem solving and ability to manage changing priorities.

 

Clinical background a plus.

 

TECHNICAL SKILLS

Must be able to interact with basic Windows applications and understand how to translate conceptual ideas into a visual user interface in a mockup created in Microsoft Visio

About the Job

Applications Systems Manager/Analyst

As the leader of a small team of Applications Systems Analysts/Programmers, you will be responsible for the review, analysis, and modification of programming systems, including encoding, testing, debugging and installing, to support the organization's application systems. You will consult with users to identify current operating procedures and to clarify program objectives. You will coordinate the creation of documentation to describe program development, logic, coding, and corrections, including manuals for users to describe installation and operating procedures.

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Lead team of Applications Systems Analysts/Programmers in the operation, maintenance, and improvement of Ecometry Direct Commerce applications suite and related systems.

·         Collaborate with department heads, managers, and supervisors, to understand their processes and principles, determine direction, priorities, objectives, and deliverables.  

·         Analyze and resolve complex problems and evaluate the impact of potential solutions from a business and technical perspective.

·         Plan and manage projects, including requirements, specifications, estimation, scheduling, allocation/tracking of resources, testing, issue logging, analysis, documentation, and reporting.

·         User education and training.

REQUIREMENTS

·         Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent, with 5+ years related experience. Working knowledge of/experience with relational databases and client-server concepts.

·         Experience with Ecometry, Hyperion, Taurus, and direct marketing/retail industry preferred.

·         Excellent planning, organizational, analytical, and problem solving skills.

·         Ability to work independently, take initiative, and manage competing priorities.

·         Excellent verbal and written communication skills, strong interpersonal skills, presentation skills, and ability to work productively with all levels in the organization.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

Specialty Catalog Corp. is one of the nation's leading multi-channel direct marketers. With a modern, efficient and finely-tuned infrastructure we serve the women's hair fashion, medical hair loss and apparel markets as well as providing continuing education materials and tests for healthcare professionals. With easy off the highway access and ample on-site parking, West Bridgewater, MA is home to our corporate offices, Distribution Center, and Contact Center. We have operations in the U.S. and the United Kingdom and our catalogs, websites and retail stores serve a multinational cross section of customers around the world.

 

As part of being an Equal Opportunity Employer, we are culturally diverse and celebrate the unique qualities of all of our employees.

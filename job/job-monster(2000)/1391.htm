Help Desk Administrator

About the Job

Constant Contact, Inc. helps small businesses, associations, and nonprofits connect with their customers, clients, and members. Launched in 1998, Constant Contact® champions the needs of small organizations and provides them with an easy and affordable way to build successful, lasting customer relationships. Constant Contact's leading email marketing and survey products—supported by our expert personal coaching and service—help all types of small businesses and organizations create professional-looking email newsletters and insightful online surveys, and begin a dialogue with their customers. Today, more than 300,000 customers worldwide trust Constant Contact to help them connect with their audience.

Headquartered in Waltham, Mass., with an additional office in Loveland, Colo., Constant Contact was named Massachusetts Technology Leadership Council "Company of the Year" in 2008 and is ranked as the 14th fastest growing company on the Deloitte Technology Fast 50 for New England list.

As a result of our continued growth and the success of our business model we are searching for a Help Desk Administrator. Participating as a member of a team, this newly created position will be responsible for all aspects Constant Contacts daily IT support needs. This role will report to the IT Help Desk Manager.

Responsibilities Include:
  • Provide desktop technical support to local and remote end users (600+ users)
  • Resolve software and/or hardware issues as they arise
  • Conduct individual adds, moves, changes of desktop systems and printers
  • Evaluate and analyze new desktop technology including hardware, software, and peripherals; document test plans and make recommendations for areas of improvement
  • Assist with new employee orientations and provide computer education related to our corporate systems to users
  • Interact with computer vendors on warranties and returns
  • Manage inventories of desktop systems including consumables
  • Ensure all corporate assets are properly tracked
  • Research, recommend and implement technologies to help achieve efficiencies in the Help Desk environment


Position Requirements Include:

  • Excellent customer service skills with focus on delighting the customer
  • Strong ability to engage, interact with, and assist internal customers while building lasting, beneficial relationships.
  • Minimum, two (plus) years experience working in a help desk and/or operations environment
  • Minimum, two (plus) years experience with Microsoft Windows operating environments (XP, Vista, W2K or W2K3)
  • Minimum, two (plus) years experience working with Microsoft Office suite
  • BS in CS, CIS or equivalent; relevant work experience may substitute for formal education
  • Strong background in desktop integration and support in a high-availability environment
  • Ability to effectively communicate with technical and non-technical audiences
  • Ability to handle a fast paced environment and stressful situations while maintaining a professional attitude and appearance that reflects positively on the organization and the team.
  • Strong focus on innovation and problem solving; ability to learn new technologies quickly
  • Good writing, strategic thinking, analytical, and creativity skills
  • Able to physically lift/move systems and monitors as necessary
  • High energy team player with the ability to develop and execute a plan, manage time, and prioritize tasks in order to meet assigned schedules
  • Strong desire to participate and add value to a team environment


Constant Contact offers a competitive compensation package that includes base salary and stock options. Our benefit package is comprehensive and includes Medical Insurance; Dental Insurance; Vision Insurance; 401(K); 529 College Savings Plan; Flexible Spending Accounts; Paid Vacation/Holidays; Short Term Disability; Long Term Disability; Tuition Reimbursement; Adoption Assistance; and, an exciting Stock Option Plan!

If you desire to make a difference, are committed to bringing the highest quality of service to our customers and are looking for a "once in a lifetime" opportunity, send us your resume and salary requirements for consideration.
We are not accepting employment agency referrals for this position.

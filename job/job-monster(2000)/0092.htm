About the Job

COMPANY

Spawn Labs is a private company headquartered in Austin, Texas. Company leadership has built and sold multiple companies to multi-billion dollar well-known Web/tech leaders such as Google, eBay and Cisco. The company is backed by a Series A venture financing, and was recognized as a Best of Show company by CNET at the prestigious 2009 TechCrunch50 event in San Francisco.

 

Spawn Labs is a leader in the emerging cloud gaming market. The company’s first product – the Spawn HD-720 – gives gamers the freedom to play their console video games, alone or with friends, at home or on the road from any network-connected computer. See  http://spawnlabs.com/products / for more information.

 

The company also sells a private enterprise version of this solution to the world’s leading game developers for remote, real-time game play access to console development kits. See  http://spawnlabs.com/products/gamedev / for more information.

 

See  http://www.spawnlabs.com/buzz / for some of the news/buzz we have generated to date. We shipped products in late January 2010, and are now ramping sales, and accelerating growth of our staff and business.

 

POSITION

This position is for an engineer on our server team. This is not your usual Java development job. We are building several distributed and replicated database driven services. There is a strong focus on operations and management features with some customer facing web page. The successful candidate will possess a passion for the craft of programming highly efficient and scalable services. The candidate should have 2+ years experience and a BS in Computer Science  or equivalent experience.

 

REQUIREMENTS


  • Experience/exposure to Java systems programming and service oriented architecture (SOA).
  • Experience in data modeling and interacting with databases (Derby, JDBC)
  • Experience with Apache Etch is a plus
  • Passionate about reliability, performance and test-based development
  • Web 2.0 technology experience is a plus (HTML, jQuery, javascript)
  • Experience with Jetty, Eclipse, Maven and Subversion, Junit, Clover is a plus
  • Experience working in an agile development environment
  • Experience with building RPMs, deploying and configuring services on Linux is a plus

 

COMPENSATION

This is a full-time salaried position that offers a competitive salary, great stock options, and competitive health insurance benefits. Beyond these essentials though, this opportunity is an excellent one for a very talented individual who values the chance to play a key role in building a major company.

 

LOCATION

Candidate must be located in Austin, Texas, no relocation is available.


Please email:  jobs@spawnlabs.com   to apply.

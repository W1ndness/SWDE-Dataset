SW Integration Eng

About the Job

DSCI is seeking qualified applicants for an Experienced Software Integration Engineer position located in White Sands, NM.

Successful Candidate will have a minimum of 5 years of experience (7-10 years preferred) and a Bachelors Degree Degree, CS or Engineering Discipline preferred.



US Citizenship and DOD SECRET clearance preferred.

Technical Requirements:

Software development (Java, C++, Linux); Software integration and troubleshooting experience preferred; previous knowledge of and experience with DOD C4ISR systems and message standards preferred. Familiarity with information assurance architecture and implementations. Network configuration and Linux system administration experience.



Responsibilities:

Development and testing of standalone software applications to support DOD C4ISR systems design and experimentation.

Integration and troubleshooting of complex, multi-application systems.

Independently design and drive forward flexible solutions for meeting complex integration needs on site.

Interface with customer and partners to represent customer`s needs and creatively define and implement solutions.

Up to 25% travel expected.

ABOUT US:
DSCI is an employee-owned products and services company that links the following disciplines: Systems Engineering, Simulation, Modeling, Maintainability, Analysis, Availability, Research, Repairability, Training & Testability as an integrated process to provide unique solutions to complex problems within the Command, Control, Communications, Computer, Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (C4ISR) space.

DSCI is actively seeking talented individuals for openings at various locations in the United States and abroad.

As a rapidly growing employee-owned company (with headquarters in Eatontown, New Jersey), DSCI offers an outstanding employment package, including highly competitive salaries, medical, dental, and vision insurance and stock options to full time personnel.

DSCI is an Equal Opportunity Employer and Participates in E-Verify.

Technical Requirements:

Software development (Java, C++, Linux)
Software integration and troubleshooting experience

Compliance Specialist

About the Job

 

 

VantageLinks is dedicated to Information Technology (IT) staffing through permanent placement, contract-to-hire, and contract services.   We also offer Team Services and Information Systems Development Solutions.  We specialize in finding Information Technology professionals ranging from help desk specialists to programmers including; systems analysts, designers, architects, database professionals, ERP specialists, and project managers.  We support a wide range of environments including but not limited to .NET, Java, UNIX. For additional information and complete list of openings, please visit our web site at www.vantagelinks.com .

 

We are looking for a Compliance Specialist

 

Job Requirements

 

Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities: 

·         Provide day-to-day ongoing support for the compliance program. Provide updates to the Compliance Officer and management through reporting and regular reviews. Provide feedback on a quarterly basis or sooner as required by the compliance program.

·         Analyze and prepare monthly monitoring reports as required by the compliance program.

·         Under general supervision and acting on own initiative at the direction of the Compliance Officer, but in line with established policies and procedures, perform a variety of duties of a complex and confidential nature, including but not limited to regulatory compliance and business continuity planning.  This job responsibility requires the use of tact, discretion and excellent judgment.  Contacts company personnel at all organizational levels to gather information and prepare reports.  Is responsible for the accurate, timely completion of assigned duties and maintaining the confidentiality of sensitive information. 

·         Responsible for maintaining the compliance review program, to test the accuracy and completeness of department process as they relate to compliance regulations and laws. Provide recommendations and assist with the implementation of process improvements to ensure efficiency and compliance within business units.

·         Provides guidance and acts as a resource to business units

·         Participate in and conduct as directed by the Compliance Officer training with colleagues to ensure competency with compliance laws and regulations.

·         Demonstrate organizational advocacy through positive teamwork, trust and mutual respect for customers and employees.  Drive initiatives that reinforce the mission, value and advocacy model of CLIENT.

·         Understand and comply with bank policy, laws, regulations, and the bank’s BSA/AML and Compliance programs.

·         At the direction of the Compliance Officer, performs all actions necessary to ensure that the Company’s policies and procedures are in compliance with the multitude of consumer legal and regulatory requirements.

 

 

Key Performance Indicators: 

·         Ability to apply commonsense understanding to carry out detailed written or oral instructions independently. 

·         Ability to read, analyzes, and interprets the complex documents.  Ability to respond effectively to the most sensitive inquiries or complaints.  Ability to write reports and presentations using original or innovative techniques or style. 

·         Must have a strong regulatory compliance background and be familiar with and comply with laws, regulations and internal policies and procedures that are applicable to the compliance department and CLIENT.

·         Ability to define problems collects data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.  Provide a high level of commitment to excellent performance. 

 

Core Competencies: 

Analytical Skills/Problem Solving

Business Objectives

Planning and Organization

Customer Services/Team Work

Community Relations

 

Essential Job Skills and Knowledge: 

·         Must be proficient with MS Word, Excel, Outlook and Internet usage for research purposes

·         Processes information logically and is able to use the result in appropriate business applications to meet the requirements of the job. Handles normal and extraordinary situations well.

·         Reliably organizes work; works effectively and efficiently, prioritizes work and anticipates business need.  Manages multiple projects simultaneously with good results.

·         Must possess excellent communication skills and work effectively without direct supervision.

·         Must have a high level of accuracy and problem solving skills.

·         Must be able to cope with pressure resulting from meeting required deadlines and reporting requirements.

·         Ability to handle sensitive situations in a confidential and professional manner.

 

Essential Qualifications: 

·         High School Diploma or equivalent required with a minimum of 5 years banking or lending experience in the areas of branch operations or consumer and/or business credit or a minimum of 2 years related compliance experience required.

·         Certificates of completion from nationally recognized compliance schools are preferred.  Regulatory Certifications a plus (CRCM, CAMS)

·         Working knowledge of banking regulations required.

·         Must be willing to travel on an irregular basis to various offices and to attend self-education.

·         While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to sit and talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to stand; walk; use hands to finger, handle, or feel objects, tools, or controls; and reach with hands and arms. The employee is occasionally required to stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl.  The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 10 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, and color vision.  The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

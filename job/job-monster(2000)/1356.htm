Cisco Voice Specialist

About the Job

Technology Consulting, Inc. (TCI) was formed in 1988 to provide information systems and consulting services to a variety of clients. In the twenty two years that have followed, a strong growth pattern has evolved, based largely on the professional quality of services and the results that our clients have received. TCI concentrates on IT staffing and project delivery solutions across all technology platforms (mainframe, midrange, client-server, and internet based).

TCI has an immediate need for a Cisco Voice Specialist in New Albany, IN. This is a direct hire opportunity.

Please Note: US Citizens are encouraged to apply. This particular position requires US Citizenship or permanent resident.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Performing client consultation including supporting the voice infrastructure, designing, planning, and implementation
  • Development and implementation of physical design and the technical architecture of the network by analyzing existing data and voice networks and recommend solutions
  • Setting up, configuring, and testing Cisco Unity, Unity Express, Call Manager, Call Manager Express, Cisco LAN switches (core, distribution and access), Cisco firewalls, routers and voice gateways, etc.
  • Troubleshooting and maintaining hardware/software products as well as upgrading and providing solutions to complex problems
  • Assisting Sales Executives and Business Development Managers during the sales process by educating the customer about new technologies and IP Telephony methodology
  • Assisting in the management of projects
  • Ensuring a stable performance environment for enterprise computer applications
  • Participating in various IT projects intended to improve/upgrade converged network solutions

Required Skills:

  • Minimum of three years related experience in infrastructure/network environments, experience in network engineering (hardware and software), network security practices and designing, planning and implementing LANs and WANs using the latest technology
  • High-end experience in PBX and/or Call Center systems engineering
  • Minimum of one year experience in programming or analytical work
  • At least one year of consulting experience preferred
  • CCVP or CCIE certification preferred
  • Experience with router configuration, knowledge of voice mail, unified messaging, ACD and IVR systems
  • A thorough understanding of not only the industry, supplies, suppliers and customers but also competitors, partners and capabilities of the business will be required.
  • A professional business attitude and courteous manner toward clients, partners, and co-workers
  • Ability to work weekends and/or off hours as necessary to meet clients' needs
  • This position may require travel, locally and nationally
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills which include demonstrated experience presenting technology recommendations from a business perspective

Instructional Systems Designer

About the Job

Responsibilities:  Define, design and develop instructional program materials.

·         Design instructional materials and programs by assessing customer-training needs.

·         Define TTO training goals, training objectives, and determine appropriate training delivery methods.

·         Determine instructional evaluation and assessment strategies.

·         Develop course content and modifies as needed, and recommend course delivery strategies

·         Gather and analyze applicable instructional data to include statistics.

·         Write, edit, review, plan, prioritize, organize, and coordinate tasks.

·         Respond to Customer Service inquiries, communicating and presenting information orally

·         Produce design for learning content and determine the size and arrangement of illustrative material including selecting style and size of type, and arranging layout based upon available space

·         Conduct analysis, design, development, implementation, and evaluation of solutions for learning

·         Apply instructional systems design (ISD) theories and learning principles

 

 

Minimum Requirements:

·         Six (6) years experience applying instructional systems design (ISD), learning technologies, education, training or adult learning.

·         Two (2) years working with instructional design or adult learning or training methodologies

·         Two (2) years working with communication practices, principles and procedures

·         Three (3) years experience creating graphics and animation to support learning content

·         Two (2) years experience using Adobe Macromedia products (Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash, Fireworks)

TS/SCI Clearance required

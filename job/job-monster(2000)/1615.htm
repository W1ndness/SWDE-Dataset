Sr. Network Infrastructure Engineer

About the Job

DSR attributes its success to our associates who are encouraged to be client centric, which results in over 90% of our revenues coming from existing customers. Our associates are our greatest brand ambassadors and take great pride in being *owner operators* which results in one of the lowest attrition rates in a hyper competitive industry. Over past 10 years, DSR has experienced dramatic growth and has been honored 3 years in row on the Inc 500 fastest growing privately held companies.


We are looking for a  Infrastructure Engineer with Telephony ability for a long-term / contract-to-hire position for a Chicago/Western Suburb client. 

Infrastructure Engineer
Plan, design, develop and implement infrastructure technologies including
Unix / Wintel / Citrix servers, ACDs / PBXs, storage and data archiving
systems. Provide subject matter expertise around specific technologies.
Provide project management for the deployment of infrastructure projects.
Act as primary vendor interface for third-tier support for applications and
technologies provided and supported by vendors.

Role Specific Responsibilities
· Identify changes needed in the infrastructure configuration to achieve the technology organization goals
· Define aggregate demand data in support of budget plans and make recommendations for ways to improve performance and / reduce costs
· Supports the infrastructure strategy reviews and development by performing applicable analysis
· Provide project management oversight to infrastructure implementation projects.
· Implements technology and infrastructure changes for platforms, performance and scoring.
· Analyzes system response and determines tuning recommendations to improve performance.
· Manage effective issue identification and resolution process; serve as the focal point for infrastructure-related issue / crisis resolution
· Identify opportunities to save costs and more effectively manage assets and escalate the opportunities
· Ensure communication of process and procedure changes to key stakeholders
· Design an effective test plan for any new / changed infrastructure technology and work with Project Managers, QA / Testing Managers and Release Managers to ensure its implementation before the client takes possession of the system
· Support identification and collection of metrics and performance reporting processes
· Monitors delivery against SLAs and reports exceptions to management
· Participate in process improvement initiatives, when applicable

Qualifications
· College degree in related technical / business areas or equivalent work experience
· Understanding of the technology organization and / or business and technology
· Re-engineering / process improvement experience, leading and coaching quality improvement projects
· Strong change management skills; change agent
· Excellent problem solving / analytical skills and knowledge of analytical tools
· Excellent written and verbal communication skills
· Ability to create / define metrics that accurately reflects the current state of a given process
· Knowledge of Six Sigma, CMM and other relevant methodologies



We stay on top of technology and recruit the best. You will always work with professionals skilled in advanced techniques.

DSR's empowers each of its team members to be entrepreneurial and at the same time adhere to our strict value system of Intellect, Integrity and boundless energy. The DSR Team represents a spectrum of cultures and technologies.

Technology changes daily, but our core values remain the same.

Senior Java Developer

About the Job

The Nielsen Company is a global leader in measurement and information, we believe providing our clients a precise understanding of the consumer is the key to making the right decisions -- decisions that can lead to profitable growth. At Nielsen, we're always innovating to keep pace with emerging market trends and the increasingly diverse, demanding and connected consumer.
 
The Engineering team has the exciting role of developing new technologies to measure the ever changing consumer viewing and purchasing environment.  These include unique technology solutions around the use of television, mobile phones, internet and consumer purchasing. Many of the research and innovation methods of measuring consumer behavior have come from the Engineering team. The team works on a number of initiatives that are highly visible and provide a large impact on the organization. 
 
This position is responsible for providing technical expertise and guidance to project team members in determining plans and strategies for developing highly complex applications.  This position is responsible for independently analyzing, developing, testing, and supporting highly complex application software.  These software applications must be independently developed and maintained ensuring high-quality standards with minimal defects.  

Requirements:
  1. Bachelor's degree in computer science or related field
  2. 4 plus years of software development experience
  3. At least 2 years experience designing scalable systems with high throughput
  4. Expert level knowledge of the Java Programming language, including common frameworks and toolkits
  5. Well rounded knowledge of C and C++
  6. Knowledge of one or more file access methods (NFS, SAMBA) and SQL
  7. Working knowledge in Unix / Linux and Windows operating systems
  8. Working knowledge of network programming concepts (TCP/IP, Sockets)
  9. Working knowledge with the following products and API's would be preferred:  Tibco EMS, JMS, PMD, findBugs, JUnit, Ant, ivy, HTML, XML, JDBC, Hudson, protocol buffers, GWT, Guava, Glassfish
  10. Expert understanding and the ability to apply software design and testing principles.Expert ability to apply analytical and problem-solving skills to solve complex design and coding problems

Responsibilities: 
  1. Analyzes, designs, and documents software applications:
    1. Coordinates across multiple applications and project teams to investigate and analyze verbal and written requests for application software, interpreting application requirements to construct highly complex applications.
    2. Evaluates options to determine the best design approach for highly complex or critical software code and applications ensuring test plans are created that meet application requirements.
    3. Writes comprehensive technical documentation for applications and systems, facilitating their maintenance.
  2. Constructs, tests, installs, and maintains software applications:
    1. Develops highly complex software code, which is maintainable, easy to use, and satisfies application requirements.
    2. Constructs application software by incorporating reusable software components.
    3. Coordinates across multiple applications and teams to determine interfaces and to develop the appropriate testing approach and test levels (integration, acceptance, end-to-end).
    4. Determines optimal implementation plan for new software and performs supporting activities to ensure that internal clients have the information and assistance they need for a successful implementation.
    5. Develops and executes system and routine test plans, analyzes test results, and makes appropriate revisions to ensure the applications function as specified.
  3. Participates on project teams:
    1. Provides leadership to project teams and may function as a project coordinator on special projects, enhancement releases, or complex implementations.
    2. Performs technical reviews of highly complex work submitted by other developers.
    3. Develops proposals for improving the information technology (IT) software development lifecycle (SDLC) and mentors other team members in adhering to SDLC standards.
    4. Conducts and participates in technical reviews of highly complex software, evaluating proposed technical solutions and recommending appropriate revisions for efficiency and maintainability.
    5. Provides input into the project work plan by investigating and developing application impact and cost estimates for new systems and enhancements.
    6. Contributes to measuring application size and effectiveness by participating in function point counting, milestone tracking, and defect tracking initiatives, ensuring assignment completion by the required dates.
    7. Contributes to the evaluation of job applicants, determining the level of their interpersonal and technical skills.
  4. Performs configuration management tasks:
    1. Provides leadership to IT teams to follow IT standard processes for securing applications and components to ensure data and application integrity.
    2. Creates, maintains, and enforces programs, standards, and procedures that reflect state-of-the-art techniques and ensures the quality, integrity, security, maintainability, and cost-effectiveness of the application systems built on the architecture.
    3. Ensures application software components are archived in common repositories according to IT standards with appropriate documentation that reflects the progression from development to implementation.
  5. Researches, assesses, and facilitates the adoption of new technologies:
    1. Investigates new technologies and development techniques and recommends their application across project and IT teams.
    2. Performs in-depth technical analysis and provides alternatives, impact analyses, and cost estimates to prepare and plan for application and/or software releases.
    3. Contributes to proposals and recommendations involving emerging technologies or technical innovations that could benefit our organization. 
    4. Leads the evaluation of new methods and technologies.
    5. Maintains a high-level of awareness of new technologies and, where appropriate, develops a working knowledge of those compatible with businessobjectives.
  6. Provides support to internal/external clients and support staff:
    1. Investigates application incidents for missing or incorrect functionality.
    2. Assesses incident resolution and estimates application changes.
    3. Applies resolutions, leading to timely, error free revisions in applications.
    4. Communicates effectively when dealing with clients. 
 Major Problems/Challenges: 
  1. Resolving highly complex technical problems that may have a far-reaching and broad impact on business plans or profits.
  2. Determining and recommending appropriate use of new technologies to solve business needs.
  3. Resolving highly complex data and product-related problems using knowledge of Nielsen products and application systems.
  4. Assisting management in developing and implementing technical objectives from a broad perspective with a long-term impact.
  5. Communicating with external clients using sound judgment.

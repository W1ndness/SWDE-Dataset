Information Technology Administrator

About the Job

Job Description for
Information Technology Administrator

Pay Grade:                Salaried
Work Schedule:        Normal Business Hours / As Required
Reports To:               Director of Process Improvement & Technology
FLSA Status:             Exempt

 

The Information Technology Administrator is responsible for the administration of computer and network hardware, enterprise and desktop software, equipment, and resources.

Job Specification

Education

·         Associate’s Degree in a computer-related field required

·         Industry-recognized technical certification strongly preferred

Experience

·         Five or more years’ progressive experience in an IT-related position required

Knowledge and Skills

·         Advanced understanding of computer hardware systems and related software, including:

    • Network Hardware
    • Server Hardware
    • Workstation Hardware
    • Server Operating System Software
      • Microsoft Windows 2003+
    • Desktop Operating System Software
      • Microsoft Windows XP+
    • Server Application Software
      • Microsoft Exchange, SharePoint, Office Communications Server, Forefront, WSUS, SQL Server
      • VMWare
      • Citrix
    • Desktop Application Software
      • Microsoft Office, Visio, Project, InfoPath
      • Adobe Acrobat

·         Strong analytical aptitude

·         Able to visualize, design, and manage complex solutions to business problems on an enterprise scale

·         Able to assist in the administration of enterprise-level MRP/ERP software such as Microsoft Dynamics NAV

·         Excellent written and verbal communication skills

·         Excellent customer relations skills

·         Organized and detail-oriented, responsible, self-motivated, and able to work independently

Job Description

General Tasks and Responsibilities

·         Manage assigned computer/network hardware, related software, security, and services

·         Analyze enterprise computing system needs and recommend and evaluate appropriate computer hardware and related software

·         Assist in the development and maintenance of corporate information technology strategy, policies, and procedures

·         Assist in the development and maintenance of corporate network, hardware, and related software standards

·         Assist in the development, maintenance, and execution of corporate disaster recovery procedures

·         Identify, recommend, and implement solutions for continuous improvement opportunities

·         Assist in design, development, implementation, and maintenance of technical solutions to enterprise business problems

·         Provide technical support for end users

·         Train and mentor end users on desktop application software

·         Identify and secure sensitive corporate data

·         Other duties as assigned

People-Related Tasks and Responsibilities

·         Serve as a positive role model for fellow employees

·         Conduct business with the highest level of honesty and integrity

Physical Requirements

·         Ability to sit or stand as needed to perform essential functions of the position

·         Ability to periodically walk to other areas of the plant throughout the day

·         Ability to use keyboard, reach above shoulder height, below the waist, and lift and file documents throughout the day

·         Ability to legally operate a motor vehicle in the state of North Dakota, as some travel is required (less than 25%)

·         Ability to lift up to 50 pounds as required while performing equipment maintenance

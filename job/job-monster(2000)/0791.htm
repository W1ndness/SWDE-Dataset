About the Job

WEB DEVELOPER



Peapod is an e-commerce grocery shopping/delivery service. We are a wholly owned subsidiary of Royal Ahold and a partner with Ahold USA supermarket companies. As our business continues to grow, we presently are seeking a Web Developer for our Corporate Office in Skokie, IL.

Peapod is a fast-paced environment that heavily embraces open source technologies in our business solutions. This position would be within the e-commerce team focused on the building and supporting applications/features across the various consumer web sites Peapod manages.

Responsibilities include:

  • Convert image mockups and Functional Requirements into fully-functional static/dynamic pages with minimal direction.
  • Work with Product Managers and Business Analysts to resolve functional, flow or design issues.
  • Maintain and support current code base, including some content changes.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve cross-browser issues in a timely manner.
  • Work with other developers and Content team to implement new functionality and projects, as needed.
  • Code to current web standards and follow best practices, including techniques for web accessibility.
  • Set up and maintain personal development environment.
  • Budget time to effectively balance new development with maintenance tasks.
  • Participate in support rotation.
  • Troubleshoot production issues.

Qualifications

  • In-depth experience with open-source tools and technologies. We really can't stress this one enough. Almost everything we do, from front to back, is built around open-source applications and tools.
  • Solid experience with Servlets and JSP, including EL.
  • Experience with a Java-based MVC framework, ideally Spring.
  • Solid experience with hand-coded CSS, XML, XHTML, Schema and DTD. This includes complex, table-less layouts and inheritance. No WYSIWYG editors.
  • Solid knowledge of Javascript, including AJAX techniques and technologies, DOM, JQuery and JSON.
  • Solid experience with SQL and a RDBMS. MySQL and/or Informix a plus.
  • Basic knowledge of Web Services.
  • Experience with SCM tools such as Subversion or CVS.
  • Familiarity and some experience with scripts and scripting languages. We use Ant, Bash and Perl, so any or all of those would be a plus.
  • Experience configuring and troubleshooting Apache and Tomcat servers in a *nix environment.
  • Java experience a definite plus. There's an opportunity to move into heavier development if desired.
  • Familiarity with SEO concepts.
  • Possess a sense of design, an eye for detail and an innate desire to produce quality work.
  • Have the desire and ability to trace issues to their source- even when those issues lie outside the boundaries of your code.
  • Able to follow established practices where they exist, and recognize where new ones would be beneficial.
  • 1-4 years of professional experience.


Peapod offers a competitive starting salary, with excellent full-time
benefits including health, dental, vision, life insurance, 401K with match,
holidays, vacation/sick days, employee stock purchase plan, flexible
spending account, direct deposit, credit union and short/long term
disability.

Job Description:
--------------------
Our team is looking for an enthusiastic, experienced, and talented program manager to help us design, implement, and maintain first class contact center technologies. This is your chance to work with a focused team dedicated to driving innovation worldwide in company centers and our contact infrastructure. With your strong project management skills you will have the opportunity to drive development efforts while communicating to business owners, technical operations, and product development members

Responsibilities:
--------------------
* Work with business representatives to gather cross-functional requirements and develop detailed functional specifications 
* Manage the development, test, and implementation of new technology and incremental application releases 
* Manage project schedules, budget, issues and risks, as well as regular status reporting 
* Review company website feature designs to assess impacts to CRM applications and plan accordingly 
* Assist as needed to resolve production issues on CRM applications (troubleshooting, coordinating hot-fixes, etc.) 

Must Have:

* 5+ years of experience as Program manager
* Experience with contact center/ Call center applications
* Workforce mngmnt Systems, Speech IVR Systems

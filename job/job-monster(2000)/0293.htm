Sr. Oracle DBA Systems Architect & Developer

About the Job


JOIN A MARKET LEADER.
Exciting times lie ahead at Velocity Technology Solutions. With double digit growth over the past few years and even more expected on the horizon, now is the perfect time to join us as we rise even higher in the IT consulting and hosting marketplace.



You can learn more about Velocity at: www.velocity-inc.com or, submit your resume via Monster.com for an opportunity to start on the path to success with Velocity!


POSITION OBJECTIVE

Velocity is seeking a Senior Oracle DBA, Systems Architect and Technical Developer to serve as a technical leader and key individual contributor on an existing team that has responsibility for the overall systems architecture (Architecture & Engineering Group). Candidate will be responsible for establishing and documenting architectural guidelines, establishing best practices, and providing automation facilities to deploy and manage large scale database and application deployments.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Experience as a senior Oracle DBA, Systems Architect and Technical Developer.
  • Experience being measured for success based on the availability and performance of mission critical highly available production environments with stringent SLAs.
  •  Experience creating and disseminating standard build documentation and best practices as well as refining these based on experience and new findings.Experienced in planning and communicating such deployments and upgrades in an environment with formal change control processes and compliance processes (e.g., SOX, PCI, and SAS70).
  • Experience working with Oracle Support Services to resolve issues and familiar with Oracle's escalation procedures.
  • Experience in ongoing support of production database environment - space projection and reclamation, query performance tuning (of existing applications), etc.
  • Experience in designing, implementing, and certifying disaster recovery (DR) strategies.


REQUIREMENTS

  •  7-10 years experience in database technology and systems architecture
  • Demonstrated Oracle experience with applicable certifications (see Certifications below). Specific experience with RAC, Data Guard, ASM, OEM/Grid, RMAN, and Tivoli Storage Manager (TSM) for Databases - in production environments.
  • Demonstrated experience in dealing with large-scale, highly available, and SLA managed environments
  • Demonstrated data modeling experience. Experience with tools like Toad Data Modeler a plus
  • Demonstrated programming experience in PL/SQL, Perl / Python, and UNIX scripting
  • SQL tuning and PL/SQL debugging/tuning
  • Demonstrated experience with Oracle tools including 3rd party products (e.g. Toad)
  • Demonstrated leadership and mentoring experience
  •  Effective oral and written and communication skills
  • Effective time management and organizational skills

PREFERRED
  • Experience with databases in support of ERP/ HCM/CRM applications (e.g. Lawson, PeopleSoft, and Oracle E-Business Suite)
  • Experience with Microsoft SQL Server
  •  Full lifecycle application development (of technical and systems management tools) - designing, coding and debugging applications in various software languages, on various operating systems, and with various databases. Target languages include: Python, Java, Jython, Perl, Shell Scripting, JavaScript, HTML, XML, and SQL. Target Operating Systems include: AIX, Solaris, HP-UX, Linux, and Microsoft Windows based platforms. Target Databases include: Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server, and MySQL.
  • Experience with ITIL discipline - Planning To Implement Service Management



EDUCATION
BS Degree in computer science, engineering or equivalent experience

Certifications

·         Oracle Database 11g Administrator Certified Master

·         Oracle Database 11g Performance Tuning Certified Expert

·         Oracle Database 11g Certified Implementation Specialist

·         Oracle Enterprise Manager 11g Certified Implementation Specialist

·         Oracle Database: SQL Certified Expert

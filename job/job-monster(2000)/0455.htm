Executive Level Opportunity with Online Glossaries Company

About the Job

Type: Full-time
Experience: Mid-Senior Level to Executive
Functions: Project Management, Strategy/Planning, Sales, Business Development, Management
Industries: Internet, Educational
Compensation: Annual salary plus performance bonus

WebFinance, Inc. has a world-class career opportunity with an entrepreneurial environment for an individual who has a strong desire to plan and implement the future strategy of the company and its family of financial and glossary websites, including InvestorGuide.com, InvestorWords.com, and BusinessDictionary.com. You'll lead a profitable, fast-growing company where ideas matter and where you will lead an accomplished, driven team of technology and business experts who thrive on performance and results in a collaborative environment.

Responsibilities:

Are you an entrepreneur or self-starter who is motivated to drive strategic development and achieve revenue goals?  Are you tired of being lost in the revolving workforce of a large company? Can you identify needs and develop them into actionable ideas/concepts that can be transformed into successful online products and services?  Do you want to see your projects grow directly? This career opportunity is designed for a leader who possesses creativity and innovation through idea generation and conceptual thinking.  The Vice President leads and manages the development and implementation of strategies designed to grow and advance our entrepreneurial and profitable online services company.
  • Develop an overall strategic vision designed to take our business to the next level
  • Develop a long-term plan to manage risk while being open to change, challenges and experimentation
  • Clearly communicate the overall strategic vision and obtain buy-in and ownership from the team
  • Leverage internal talent to brainstorm/identify/develop large-scale ideas for improvements to existing websites and potential new ones
  • Manage and delegate the day-to-day operations from an administrative and a financial perspective, including but not limited to hiring, retention, policy administration, outsourcing, P&L, etc.
  • Provide support and guidance to ad sales staff
  • Develop strategies and processes to identify and communicate with our core users in order to solve some of their problems
  • Identify new markets/niches/verticals where our existing expertise can be applied
  • Manage execution on a wide variety of strategic and tactical projects while coordinating with our team of energetic, intelligent individuals
  • Develop strategic alliances and partnerships with firms and/or individuals whose business objectives are similarly aligned
  • Report directly to the company founder/owner

Ideal Candidate Qualities:
  • BA/BS degree preferred with strong academic record from accredited University
  • Ability to think strategically in regards to Internet technologies and online business models, including, but not limited to, SEO best practices, quantitative and qualitative analysis
  • Experience and strong interest in online content creation, management, and monetization
  • Proven track record of successfully creating growth opportunities, managing the growth process from inception to completion and leading a team in a small to medium-sized organization
  • Familiar with web based glossary industry and computational lexicography
  • Ability to use problems and challenges as ‘ladders’ to achieve stakeholder (both internal and external) symmetry
  • Ability to assess internal talent to focus on driving revenue by effectively challenging and motivating employees
  • Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to construct new, ground-breaking ideas in the field of online glossaries and beyond
  • Desire and ability to locate problems/bottlenecks and conceptualize products/services aimed at solving them
  • Experience in taking complex, ambiguous tasks/ideas and researching/developing a plan to deliver on them
  • Possess the determination to overcome potential obstacles and identify potential key revenue generators for the future
  • Programming skills are a plus but not required

About WebFinance Inc.

Founded in 1996 and based in Fairfax, Virginia, WebFinance Inc. is an established Internet company which designs and builds a family of educational content websites, such as InvestorGuide.com, BusinessDictionary.com, InvestorWords.com, and a collection of 30 niche-specific glossaries. Our goal is to provide free educational content to help individuals improve their knowledge and achieve their educational goals.

About Our Team

Every member of the WebFinance team is a top graduate of an accredited university, hand-picked for his or her academic and career success, problem-solving creativity, and a proven track record of setting aggressive goals and then achieving them. We understand that the success of a company depends primarily on the quality of its employees, therefore we hire only the best and the brightest.
 
Compensation and Benefits

We offer a competitive performance-based compensation package commensurate with experience. Benefits include health and dental insurance with 100% paid premiums, semi-annual performance-based bonuses, and paid vacations/holidays.  Our team environment stretches beyond the workplace with frequent team outings and post-work activities, including an office kickball team and trivia nights at local restaurants.

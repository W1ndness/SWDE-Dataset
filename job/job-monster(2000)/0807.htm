About the Job

ASR Analytics (ASR) is a small, dynamic and rapidly growing management consulting firm with headquarters located in the Washington, DC metro area. ASR has deep expertise in the fields of business intelligence, predictive modeling, and data mining. We have an immediate need for an SQL Server Programmer to support engagements in Washington, DC. Successful candidates will possess the following qualifications:

 

Required Qualifications

1.    At least 3-5 years of Transact-SQL programming experience

2.    Experience developing and implementing data warehouses and BI environments, including dimensional data modeling, OLAP, and ETL

3.    Proficiency in Transact-SQL and scripting with experience in developing advanced queries, stored procedures, and views

4.    Ability to work with complex data structures and understand data relationships in OLTP and/or OLAP based databases

5.    Ability to analyze customer requirements and implement best fit solutions using available technologies

6.    Ability to develop and document standards, guidelines, and processes as required

7.    Experience with database performance analysis, query optimization, and table space planning

8.    Exceptional communication and problem solving skills

9.    Ability to be at a client site when / if necessary

 

Desired Qualifications

1.    3-5 years of SQL database administration experience, version 2005 or higher

2.    Knowledge of installation, configuration, clustering, and mirroring SQL Server

3.    Experience using other SQL-based databases such as MySQL, Oracle, etc

4.    Experience using other BI platforms such as Cognos, Business Objects, Actuate, MicroStrategy, or SAS

5.    Experience with advanced analytic methods (i.e.., regression, statistical sampling) is a plus!

6.    Experience in a customer support help desk role, or

7.    Experience in an application development environment

Our team of successful analysts is smart, self-motivated, and eager to take on new challenges. Our rapid growth rate (over 50% year-over-year since our founding) ensures that there is plenty of room to grow. ASR provides competitive salaries, health insurance, 401(k) from day one and company-wide profit sharing as part of our overall compensation package. We look forward to hearing from you.

IT Business Analyst-Consultant

About the Job

We are currently seeking an IT Business Analyst to assist our client with their ERP implementation.  The Business Analyst will work directly with the ERP implementation team to create a solid and stable implementation environment.  More specifically, the IT Business Analyst will be responsible for the following Pre ERP Implementation, ERP Implementation and Post ERP Implementation activities:

·         Identify and assess business needs for the ERP system.

·         Gather and document technical requirement and functional business requirements.

·         Perform needs analysis.

·         Design and write functional specifications.

·         Utilize appropriate tools to analyze and resolve ERP application issues.

·         Support functional configuration, implementation, and production activities of the ERP system.

·         Team with Subject Matter Experts (SME) to deliver strategic business capabilities.

·         Perform gap analysis.

·         Provide user with technical and functional support.

·         Train users and super users.

 
The ideal candidate for the IT Business Analyst position will have the following qualifications:

 

·         Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Business, or related degree.

·         At least 5 years experience in application support and configuration for large ERP systems such as Oracle, SAP, PeopleSoft and Microsoft Dynamics AX.

·         Experience with Microsoft Office Suite including Word, Excel, and Outlook.

 
Other Required Skills:

 

·         Demonstrated experience working with Oracle databases as well as Oracle application tables and data structures.

·         Ability to interact with diverse functional and technical groups, spanning all organizational levels.

·         Demonstrated knowledge of technology processes and legal requirements.

·         Ability to troubleshoot complicated issues across multiple systems, regardless of architecture and driving the effort to a successful conclusion.

·         Ability to explain complicated technical solutions clearly to non-technical individuals.

·         Demonstrated ability to partner effectively with organizational units outside one's own reporting structure (i.e. internal clients and/or support organizations).

·         Ability to grasp new concepts and learn new skills quickly.

·         Demonstrated willingness to assume new responsibilities, and initiative.

·         Ability to deal effectively with internal employees and external business contacts while conveying a positive, service-oriented attitude.

·         Ability to work with minimal supervision, managing own time and the time of others effectively.

·         Demonstrated communication, presentation, and written skills.

·         Ability, willingness and flexibility to travel frequently as required.

·         Demonstrated logic, problem solving, and decision making skills.

 

Accretive Solutions is a national consulting and executive search firm that delivers business solutions to help companies manage and improve their financial, operational and IT performance.  We have made a name for ourselves based on our ability to deliver results that consistently exceed our client’s expectations.
 
Our primary goal is to build long-term relationships with our consultants, while providing challenging long-term opportunities with a competitive compensation and benefits package.
 
Our benefits package includes PTO, holiday pay, 401(K), health, vision, dental insurance and generous referral/sales bonus plans.

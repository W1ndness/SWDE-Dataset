Firmware Engineer

About the Job

Do you feel under appreciated or under utilized?

 

B&W Tek, Inc., is a Newark, Delaware based advanced instrumentation company producing optical spectroscopy and laser instruments for biomedical, physical, chemical, and research communities.  With a strong vertical integration capability, B&W Tek, Inc. also provides custom product development, design, and manufacturing. 

 

We are seeking for another talented individual to join our family!

 

As our Firmware Engineer you will make significant contributions to our products.  Are you ready to use your creativity and educational background to its fullest extent?

 

In the next 12 months would you like to do the following and more for a growing company?

 

3 months:

1) Learn the requirements of software/firmware for a medical laser device design.

2) Team up with other engineers, including electronic, firmware, and software, for the development of a portable WinCE based medical laser device.

3) Become familiar with B&W TEK’s software development design control and document the design.

4) Train other software/firmware engineers on WinCE.

 

6 months:

1) Transfer the design to production.

2) Production support, for example, troubleshooting.

3) Work on a human version software/firmware for the same laser device.

 

12 months:  

1) Choose a more powerful WinCE based hardware platform for increased video capability.

2) Programming on that platform for an upgrade version of the portable WinCE based medical laser device.

3) Gradually be involved in other projects and products.

 

REQUIREMENTS:

  • BS or higher in Physics, Electrical Engineering or related fields
  • > 3 years experiences on Windows CE based firmware/software coding and development in using Visual Studio.net platform
  • Familiar with various ARM CPU based control hardware
  • Familiar with firmware coding protocol involving RS232, SPI, I2C, GPIO, etc.      
  • Knowledge of medical laser or related device is a plus
  • Analog and digital electronics understanding preferred
  • Good communication skills and team work

 

Position Location: Newark, DE

Hours of Operation: 8:00 – 5:00 M-F

          

Make a difference and join B&W Tek, Inc.

 

Would you like to join the B&W Tek family?  Please forward your resume (in Word) AND salary requirements to:  HR@bwtek.com .  IMPORTANT- In the email subject line include the following: Your Last Name, First Name, Firmware Engineer, where you saw the ad. 

 

We are looking forward to hearing from you!  Thank you for your interest in B&W Tek, Inc.

About the Job


Senior Software Engineer (C / Embedded Programming)
Westford, MA

About NetScout Systems, Inc.:
NetScout Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ-NTCT) is the leading provider of integrated network and application performance management solutions for organizations worldwide. Through the combination of Sniffer® and nGenius® technologies, NetScout enables customers to maximize the performance and efficiency of applications and content delivered across globally distributed networks. In today's highly complex and rapidly changing IT environments, NetScout's solutions play an essential role, providing the operational intelligence required to assure that critical networked applications and services run efficiently and reliably, meeting or exceeding user expectations, and increasing return on investment from the infrastructure.  
 
Headquartered in Westford, Massachusetts, NetScout has approximately 800 employees and offices across the globe. NetScout serves the Global 5000 enterprise community as well as government agencies and telecommunications service providers.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented, Senior Software Engineer to join our Development Team. This role will be responsible for the development, testing, documentation, and analysis of software applications in an embedded environment. Projects involve design and development of applications for a network monitoring product. Technology used will be embedded Real Time software development using C on a proprietary as well as Linux OS.  
 
The position is based at our office in Westford, MA.  
 
Essential Job Responsibilities:  
-Design, develop, document, test and support modules for a network monitoring agent  
-Participate on a team of engineers involved in development of Infrastructure software  
-Analyze complex issues, then develop and deploy appropriate solutions using judgment within defined practices and procedures  
-Develop and execute testing and debugging routines, and document work and results  
-Contacts are intra-organizational, multi-site and will include external customer contacts

Requirements:
-5 to 7 years of software development experience  
-Solid C programming skills  
-Good understanding and knowledge of algorithms and data structures  
-Experience with Linux OS development environment (development, debugging, profiling)  
-Familiar with usage of Linux Tools (GNU tool chain etc)  
-Experience or Knowledge of Linux Internals (Threading and IPC mechanisms etc) is must  
-Knowledge of network protocols such as TCP/IP application protocols a must  
-Experience in studying protocol specifications  
-Should have worked on producing Functional Specifications based on Customer Requirements  
-Excellent analytical skills  
-Experience in embedded real time Software Development environment and ClearCase source control preferred  
-Experience with network management test tools and products is a plus  
-Minimum level of education: BS in CS, EE or equivalent. MS preferred

Additional Information:

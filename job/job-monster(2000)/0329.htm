About the Job

Senior SQL Server DBA/Developer

About the Job

Mediaspace Solutions (MSS) is a rapidly expanding media company. We simplify the planning, buying and placement of media for advertisers and leading media agencies. We live to break the rules of advertising and strive to be relentless in our commitment to shatter the status quo. We’re looking for people who share our passion.

We seek a Senior SQL Server DBA/Developer who’s ready for an exciting career with a top-notch media placement agency. At MSS we connect with thousands of newspapers and dozens of big-name brands such as American Express, Mohegan Sun, Dunkin Donuts, SC Johnson and many more.

 

Position Location

Norwalk, CT

 

Title

Senior SQL Server DBA/Developer

 

Qualifications:

·         6+ years of SQL Server experience required

·         Proficiency in database design, implementation, and performance tuning of SQL Server 2005/2008 environments

·         Experience with SQL Server replication technologies

·         Experience with creating and maintaining SSRS reports and SSIS packages

·         Expert knowledge of Transact-SQL

·         Expert knowledge in creating, optimizing, and managing stored procedures, functions, views, triggers, and indexes

·         Solid knowledge of Object Oriented Design and Programming

·         Strong .NET development experience is highly desired

·         Excellent planning, time management, communications, decision-making, and organizational skills

·         The ability to identify and analyze a problem, propose solutions, anticipate future problem areas before initiating action and implementing solutions

·         Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including ability to interact with Business Analysts and/or end user community to gather and analyze technical requirements; ability to explain technology to a non-technical audience; and ability to work as part of a team to develop large and complex software features

·         College Degree within computer science or relevant work experience

You must be able to work in the US – no sponsorship provided. Only principals apply – Recruiters, please do not contact this job poster.
 
No phone calls please.

 

 

Benefits

In exchange for your smarts and expertise, we offer a dynamic corporate culture, competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package!

To Apply

Think you have what it takes? Along with your resume and salary requirements, send us three (3) accomplishments that prove your track record of delivering results, innovative problem solving ability and genuine curiosity. Make me say, “Wow, why aren’t you working here already?” Applicants who exclude this information will not be considered. 

Please email your three accomplishments and resume to APLAYERS@mediaspacesolutions.com

Reference “Senior SQL Server DBA/Developer” in the subject field of the email.

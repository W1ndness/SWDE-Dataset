Linux Systems Engineer

About the Job

Our client is seeking a Linux Systems Engineer experienced in maintaining high-transaction, high availability Linux based Web systems. The environment contains F5 load balancing, failover, clustering and VMware virtualization.

 

Outstanding opportunity to join our clients growing WEB Based K-12 education services division. Our client is well known and offers premier content in this space. This is a LONG TERM contract of 1 year or more. This is an urgent need with an immediate start.

 

 

**Qualifications**

* BS Degree or equivalent experience supporting RHEL (Linux) based systems

* 3 to 5 years, Linux Engineering experience

* 3 to 5 years Web based Systems Support

* 1 or more years supporting VMware, Virtualized environment

 

**Technical Skills**

* Linux Operating System

* Web based (Public Facing)

* High-transaction / High-traffic systems

* High Availability (Failover, Clustering etc)

* Load Balancing (F5 is a BIG PLUS)

* Shell Scripting (Perl, CSH or KSH)

* WEB Application, Server and Network, Reporting, Availability and Management

* Splunk, Nagios and Coradiant or equivalent management system experience

* VMware Virtualization

* Automated managing the configuration of LINUX Systems

* Puppet (Software) or Equivalent

 

**Personal Attributes**

* Excellent documentation skills

* Articulate complex technical implementations

* Detail oriented

* Available via phone/email on a rotating 7/24 basis

 

 

**Responsibilities**

* Ongoing infrastructure work and also be involved with future planning

* Maintain high availability website operations

* Manage and maintain systems and software

* Support server software/hardware systems

* Implement tools to provide analytic trend analysis, capacity planning and performance data

 

**Additional Tasks**

* Document existing systems architecture and technology portfolio

* Design, configure and deploy networking facilities throughout the company

SQL Server DBA / Data Analyst - Internal Staff Position

About the Job

Job Description

Provide technical consultation and support in support of “all things data” in relationship to the Econometrix application.  The primary purpose of this job is to design, develop, enhance, and administer standard practices pertaining to data administration. We are looking for a unique individual that manage our application data as well as develop ETL packages.

 

This individual will have an excellent understanding of SQL Server in order to function as the DBA and specialize in optimizing the RDBMS environment to support the application.  Ownership of the SQL Server environment will belong to this individual.  ETL will be within the domain of this individual.

 

The candidate will develop new documentation and participate in the development of department technical procedures.  As an escalation point for application support and troubleshooting, provides guidance and direction to less experienced staff in resolution of escalated issues and/or complex production, application or system problems.  Assures quality, security and compliance requirements are met for supported area and oversees updating and testing of the business continuation plan.

 

This position works in collaboration with members of his/her team, the IT Infrastructure team, our business partners, application clients and business management. 

 

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to the following

·        Monitor SQL Server usage and processes, as well as monitor performance and recommend strategies for improvement

·        Test and update SQL Server service packs and patches

·        Monitor, and improve as needed, current SQL Server backup processes

·        Production support duties as needed

·        Process and monitor data exports and imports.  Work with business teams as appropriate to resolve issues with imports/export files

·        Develop new ETL processes for new and existing clients

·        Review database changes for new releases

·        Perform data analysis for ad hoc and recurring requests

Required Qualifications & Experience

·        At least 2 years of proven track record of supporting and maintaining SQL Servers

·        SQL Server Administration

·        T-SQL scripting, writing, testing and debugging

·        Formal SQL Server training preferred

·        At least 1 year of C# development

·        At least 1 year of SSIS development

·        Excellent technical, logical, analytical and problem solving skills

·        Exceptional expertise and detailed knowledge of industry trends

·        Demonstrated ability to analyze technical information, evaluate products, services and vendors, and make recommendations

·        Comprehensive understanding of the data processing environment, its impact on business functions and the client’s business

·        Strong planning and organizational skills

·        Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills

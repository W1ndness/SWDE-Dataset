Duncan Massey, Inc. is an innovative force in the IT Staffing Industry. Based in St. Louis, but working nationwide, we work with professionals whose skills are "... at technology's edge"(tm). * 3+ years Java development experience

* 3+ years experience with Spring MVC

* 3+ years experience with Hibernate

IDEAL SKILLS:

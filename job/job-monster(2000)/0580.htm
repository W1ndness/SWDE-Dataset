White Box Test Analyst

About the Job

Our client currently seeks experienced White Box Test Analyst candidates to join a brand new technology center opening up in Columbus, Ohio.  This is a 3-month contract to hire opportunity.

Summary

Provide automation, administration and development of performance tests that support the Development, IT departments and commercial online web-based products. Perform identification of product use cases, research, evaluate and use of industry lead tools to verify and validate product performance. Provide Load testing best practices, tool administration and appropriate development support and be a key player in sharing the techniques used to verify and validate of proper performance of our products..

Duties and Responsibilities

1.     Performs automation test function design, tool analysis and programming activities which may require some research.

2.     Performs test tool evaluation, tool administration and support for tool deployment and operation.

3.     Work to optimize the effective utilization of test tools and reusability of performance testing in the products’ verification and validation cycle.

4.     Perform performance audits on products in order to recommend changes that facilitate automation and improve quality.

5.     Work with the product development team to identify performance opportunities as well as ensure testability issues are addressed during design and implementation.

6.     Ensure the integrity of the system performance from release to release.

7.     Recommend, install and support add-on products and interfaces that have a positive impact on performance efficiencies, coverage and depth.

8.     Maintain and enhance the performance scripts required to support new versions of our products.

9.     Create and maintain automation scripts to support functional and performance assessment

10.   Keep abreast of the latest application functionality, identify key performance characteristics and proactively assess performance.

Job Qualifications

1.     5+ years experience in structured software testing environment for web based applications

2.     3+ years experience with Load and Performance testing

3.     2+ Performance testing experience in ASP.NET & SQL applications, Web services, C#, .NET and WCF

4.     Use of the following tools/systems: Visual Studio Test edition, Visual Studio Team Foundation System

5.     Strong individual contributor

6.     Have experience in working with cross functional departments

7.     Solid analytical skills/work experience

8.     Strong knowledge of Web based technologies and products

9.     Strong experience in performance scripts and automation tools

10.   Strong Performance test background

11.   Experience in day-to-day operations of development and test environment

12.   Ability to explain performance assessment concepts to cross functional teams

13.   Effective communication skills

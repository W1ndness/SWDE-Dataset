About the Job

Java Developer

 

At Impac, we solve our clients’ information technology challenges with a unique blend of innovation, passion, and fun. As a professional services firm we specialize in implementing and supporting Oracle Technology and Oracle Applications.

 

As our business continues to grow, we are seeking a Java Developer to join our dynamic team supporting our multi-state and Canadian operations.
 

Responsibilities include

·         software development in support of a variety of Oracle Applications (e.g., Financials, Purchasing, Project Accounting, CRM, HRMS, and Manufacturing);

·         custom development work based upon client needs;

·         integrating Oracle applications with other applications;

·         extensively interacting with clients as well as the entire implementation team.

 

Qualified candidates will possess

·         Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or Information Systems;

·         2- 5 years of Java experience

·         demonstrated proficiency with Oracle relational databases and Oracle development tools (Java, JDeveloper, Eclipse, SQL, PL/SQL, Forms, Reports, XML Publisher, Workflow);

·         experience with Portal Technologies

·         experience with Unix and Windows operating systems as development platforms;

·         exposure to SOA Suite and BPEL; familiarity with the Oracle Applications Framework;

·         strong understanding of systems implementation lifecycle;

·         strong problem-solving, communication and presentation skills;;

·         ability to work effectively with diverse teams;

·         self-motivation and desire to excel in a fast-paced and dynamic environment. 

Ability/desire to travel throughout the Pacific Northwest and Western Canada is required. 

 

In addition to a great working environment, Impac offers a competitive salary and bonus program as well as employer-paid benefits.

Applicants must have legal authority to permanently work in the US.

This position is not available to individuals needing Visa sponsorship.

Impac is an equal opportunity employer. No agencies please. www.impacservices.com

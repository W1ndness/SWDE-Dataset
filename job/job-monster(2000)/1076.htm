About the Job

About Us

StoneTurn Group LLP is an international independent dispute-consulting firm providing specialized services in Complex Business Litigation & Consulting, Forensic Accounting, Forensic Technology and Intellectual Property matters.

 

StoneTurn’s Forensic Technology Practice assists attorneys and corporate clients with all of their information technology needs in forensic and litigation environments by utilizing its state-of-the-art technology laboratories to stay ahead of the technology curve. Our national practice consists of certified computer forensic experts (EnCE & CCE), including former “Big 4” specialists and strategic relationships with former law enforcement professionals skilled in obtaining and analyzing forensic images, restoring data obtained through forensic technology, performing in-house, third-party and incident response investigations, and assisting its clients in the electronic discovery (“eDiscovery”) process from the initial planning stage through data retrieval and production. 

 

We recognize that our ability to provide high caliber service to clients relies on attracting and retaining seasoned practitioners. In today’s competitive talent market, we have placed a strong emphasis on building a firm culture of collaboration and enthusiasm, in which our practitioners are invested in the success of our clients, our colleagues, and our firm.

 

StoneTurn seeks motivated individuals who are flexible, possess creative problem-solving skills, and have relevant experience working on computer forensic investigations. We offer competitive compensation and benefits packages.

 

StoneTurn is seeking a qualified consultant to join our Boston Forensic Technology practice.

 

Job Summary

In this position, you will work in a state-of-the-art technology laboratory alongside a team of highly skilled forensic technology analysts in examining and interpreting data and forensic images, and assisting in third party investigations. In addition, you will apply financial, statistical, accounting, and computer forensic knowledge in offering advice to both counsel and corporations. You will be asked to research, analyze, and report on artifacts and participate in eDiscovery investigations.  This position also overlaps with the duties of our team, where you will work with Managers and Partners in the Forensic Accounting and IP practices to deliver coordinated services on investigations, and aid with the planning and implementation of the discoveries.

 

Responsibilities

-          Perform forensic data analysis in conjunction with highly sensitive corporate investigations and other matters including intellectual property theft, regulatory investigations, accounting fraud, and general litigation support

-          Analyze and implement the use of best practices applicable to the computer forensic field

-          Provide support for engagement managers who are overseeing the process from collection, extraction, processing, hosting, review and production

-          Integrate with other staff in StoneTurn’s Forensic practice to deliver coordinated services on investigations

-          Interact with clients and other external sources to obtain relevant information and document findings

-          Maintain chain of custody documentation and the evidence inventory system

-          Foster a positive, efficient and team-oriented environment

-            Execute multiple priorities and projects on a daily basis

 

 

Required qualifications

-            Bachelors Degree in computer science or related field

-            Experience in the area of computer forensics and complex data analysis is NOT required

-            Strong understanding of Windows based systems including installing, troubleshooting, and networking

-            Strong technical background. Previous experience in help desk, systems administration, or other technical support role helpful.

-            Experience with various forms of electronic media (hard drives, tapes, optical media, etc.)

-            Flexibility with the potential travel requirements of client engagements

-            Professional demeanor and strong written and oral communication skills

-            Excellent prioritization skills

-            Commitment to exemplary client service delivery

-            Experience in a professional services firm preferred

-            Certification in the computer forensics field (CCE, ACE, EnCE, etc.) helpful but not required

-            Understand networking concepts and experience working with networked systems

-            Familiar with data collection and preservation principles

 

Software Skills

-          Experience with digital forensics tools such as EnCase, FTK, Paraben helpful but not required

-          General networking and system concepts

-          General database concepts

-          General knowledge of backup tape technologies, software and hardware preferred

-          Knowledge of mainstream operating systems and applications

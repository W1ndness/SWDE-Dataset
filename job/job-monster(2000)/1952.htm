Network Specialist - Voice Specialist
15905
Oregon-Portland



Meridian Technology Group is looking for a Network Specialist - Voice Specialist

In this role, you will be responsible for the management and ongoing support of the organization's IT networks on a daily basis. Duties may include the design, security, installation, implementation, and monitoring of the IT network. The Network Specialist will possess a high level of specialized knowledge in one or more areas of client's technology environment.

Additionally, the Network Specialist:

  • may direct the complex technical activities required for the technical support needed by internal IT clients and/or users throughout the company.
  • may be asked to provide technical training and staff mentoring where appropriate and should strive to spread an understanding of the Network Services technical roadmap throughout the IT department.
  • may be a key contributor and may lead the development of enterprise-wide standards, processes, methodologies, and the long term IT strategic roadmap.

    Essential Job Responsibilities and Expectations:

    Performs the following functions with moderate to limited supervision, engaging appropriate technical resources or management when needed to review and provide recommendations or to escalate problems.

    • Administer and manage the enterprise voice network infrastructure.
    • Provide technical knowledge and consulting services to internal clients and members of the team, including the analysis of system and network performance.
    • Analyze system performance to identify deficiencies and recommend solutions for improvement, based on trending information, business requirements and operational issues.
    • Emphasis is on proactive monitoring and the implementation of measures in the enterprise network to maximize uptime and to provide timely response to problems affecting internal, as well as external clients.
    • May research new network related technology trends and make recommendations or develop plans for the future of the enterprise network infrastructure.
    • Meet with appropriate IT and Business Units to determine requirements and expectations.
    • Participate in and may take the lead of technical discussions impacting aspects of the enterprise network design.
    • Design and document outcomes from technical discussions including:
      -Physical Network Architecture diagrams depicting the enterprise network.
      -Virtual Network Architecture diagrams depicting the enterprise network.
      -Application, as well as systems attached to the enterprise network including network ports, processes, and bandwidth requirements if known, including virtual interfaces between systems
    • Develop and document the configuration details necessary to support the installation of the network and the network management systems.
    • May assist other team members and assigned project managers in defining project scope, tasks, and milestones as necessary to ensure successful completion of project.
    • Lead small to medium projects from concept to completion using defined project management processes.
    • Develop and document the configuration details necessary to support the installation of the network and the network management systems, including patch cable installation details.
    • Install and configure network systems in accordance with internal design documents and industry best practices.
    • May assist in the research of new technology trends and make recommendations/plans for the future of the enterprise network infrastructure.
    • Participate in an on-call duty rotation, with late night and weekend work as required.


    General Responsibilities:

    • Maintain awareness of system issues within the Network Services team, as well as other IT Departments to assist with the development of client/business discussion points and internal upgrade considerations.
    • Perform site walkthroughs and client interviews for information gathering to assist with the development of estimates and project kickoff.
    • Develop a generalist level of understanding of all network systems to assist in a support role, if required, as well as a subject matter expertise in specific systems as identified.
    • Understand and adhere to current Corporate and IT standards, policies and processes (change control, computer security, user access, system use, etc.).
    • Develop an understanding of business issues and goals in order to provide appropriate technical solutions.
    • Maintain and enhance proficiency with tools and technologies typically used by the Network Services team.
    • Develop a working knowledge of network technology, concepts, practices, and procedures.
    • Maintain familiarity with the network systems.
    • Develop a working knowledge of budget and accounting.
    • Accepts assignments and new responsibilities from management.
    • Complete assignments with strict attention to timeline and budget.
    • Seek out mentoring and training as the opportunities arise.


    Experience and Skills Requirements:

    • A degree in Computer Science or equivalent work experience in a related discipline.
    • Typically Five to Seven years of previous network experience.
    • Working knowledge of one or more networking topologies, routing and switching protocols, wireless networks, firewalls, remote and site-to-site access VPNs, unified communications, network management software and cable plant solutions.
    • Working knowledge of installation and maintenance of Windows 2003 Server and associated server hardware, including but not limited to: RAIDs and mirroring; dual NICs; RAM; Partitions; and hard drive replacement, setup and restore with server in an online or offline state.
    • Working knowledge of best practices relating to network design and implementation including network protocols, sub netting/address schemas, DNS, DHCP, ARP and Proxy services.
    • Working knowledge of installation and maintenance of software applications including but not limited to: Backup software, Anti-virus software, Cisco Unified Communications Manager, Cisco Unity, Cisco Emergency Responder, Cisco Unified Contact Center Enterprise, Cisco Interaction Manager, and Cisco Customer Voice Portal.
    • Demonstrated skills in VoIP technologies such as: IP PBX, IP telephony, handset, call control, voicemail solutions.
    • Ability to install, operate and troubleshoot Unified Communication Systems, quality of service (QoS), gateways, gatekeepers, IP phones, voice applications, Cisco routers and Catalyst switches.
    • Ability to install, operate and troubleshoot Unified Communication systems, including phone, user Moves, Adds and Changes in Communications Manager and Unity.
    • Ability to monitor system event logs and backups of all Unified Communication servers and applications with the ability to quickly troubleshoot and resolve complex problems based on severity.
    • Ability to function as a technical lead for small to mid-sized projects.
    • Ability to perform analysis to determine user requirements for handoff to development resources.
    • Scripting experience XML, VXML, TCL and/or Java a plus.
    • Familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite including Visio.
    • Documented training or professional certifications including CCENT (Cisco Certified Entry Networking Technician) CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate), CCNA -Voice or equivalent a plus.
    • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.


    Miscellaneous Requirements

    • There may be required travel within the area and to facilities. Occasional overnight stays may be required. Expected travel would be no more than 10% of work time.
    • Valid driver's license required.
    • Other duties as assigned.

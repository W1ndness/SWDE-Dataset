About the Job

 

Linux Systems Engineer

Charleston, SC

 

Description:

 

The Senior Windows Systems Engineer will play an integral role on the SPAWAR TIMPO Information Assurance Sustainment team.

The Senior Linux Systems Engineer:

 

·         Must be able to provide support for emergent network issues, be adaptable, and well versed in all areas of Network Engineering. 

·         Will have systems administration skills on the Linux server platform, and should have experience with VMWARE, Cisco routers and firewalls. 

·         Will focus on systems and application stability, security, performance and capacity management and provide documentation.

Experience with the medical and HIPPA compliance programs is a plus.

Required Experience:

·         Responsible for the sustainment of systems with enterprise-class medical environment.

  • Proven technical expertise in relevant state of the art technologies and systems, accompanied with expert systems design understanding.
  • Minimum 5 years overall experience, heavily versed in Red Hat Linux (RHCE a plus) and Pearl Scripting.
  • Linux server administration experience required.
  • Expertise in system installation, configuration, administration, troubleshooting, performance tuning, preventative maintenance, capacity planning, monitoring and security procedures.
  • Experience in Puppet configuration management a plus.
  • Experience in VMWare ESXi a plus.
  • Understanding of internet standards such as - DNS, FTP, SSH, HTML, XML, JDBC, SNMP and other protocols.
  • IP communication and packet structure, with focus on HTTP protocol and experience with network protocol analyzers.
  • Network hardware architecting including experience with load balancing equipment, switches, routers, and network troubleshooting.

Education Requirements:

 

Bachelor’s degree preferred.

Preferred certifications: CCNA, CISSP or CISM

 

Additional:

 

WareOnEarth Communications, Inc., an SMS company, leverages our vast background in Information Assurance (IA) and Security (InfoSec) for cutting edge, mission critical government network solutions (e.g. Department of Defense [DoD], Department of Treasury, DEA). WCI provides a unique blend of extensive experience, exposure, knowledge and expertise that puts us ahead of the pack and our clients ahead of the curve when it comes to infrastructure protection.

 

We enable our clients to mitigate risks before incidents occur and interrupt business. WareOnEarth provides a wide range of Information Assurance and Security services designed to evaluate the effectiveness of our clients’ infrastructure protection so they can discover and address vulnerabilities.

 

Due to the nature of work performed in our facility, U.S. citizenship is required.

Candidate must have active DoD Secret Clearance or ability to obtain an Interim Secret Clearance.


WareOnEarth Communications, Inc. offers outstanding compensation and benefits including matching 401K plan, up to $4,000 annual Tuition Reimbursement, 15 PTO days plus 10 paid holidays per year.

Replies will be sent only to those candidate meeting position requirements.

 

WareOnEarth Communications, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity, Affirmative Action employer and strongly encourages qualified women, minorities, Vietnam-era veterans, and individuals with disabilities to apply with our company.

About the Job

IT Systems Analyst
EASTCONN is seeking a Systems Analyst responsible for effective provisioning, installation/configuration, operation, and maintenance of systems hardware and software and related infrastructure. This individual participates in technical research and development to enable continuing innovation within the infrastructure. This individual ensures that system hardware, operating systems, software systems, and related procedures adhere to organizational and departmental standards.
Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Perform a wide range of system administration duties on database, application, and other servers including install, debug, maintain, upgrade, and general support.
  • Evaluate, test, and deploy software and hardware.
  • Proactive monitoring of servers, applications and services
  • Perform regular and on-demand system backups and recoveries.
  • Manage & troubleshoot networks, intranet & internet connectivity throughout the organization
  • Work closely with the IT team to provide end-user support and issue resolution for EASTCONN and for external customers
  • Maintain up-to-date knowledge of emerging server tools and technologies.
  • Research, evaluate and recommend system technologies to support educational/business requirements.
  • Create and maintain documentation for all processes and procedures.
  • Other duties as assigned by IT Director 

Required Skills:

  • Cisco routers and firewall appliances, Windows Server 2003/2008, Microsoft Exchange server, IP networking, Mac OSX, X-Serve,
  • B.S. Degree in Computer Science or Management Information Systems
  • Minimum 2-3 years experience in Systems Administration
  • Advanced knowledge of installations, upgrades and troubleshooting of Windows Server 2003, 2008 & Web application Servers. Experience with IIS and Apache servers is highly desirable
  • Strong technical aptitude and ability to research & solve complex issues independently.
  • Ability to analyze system and make recommendations

Other Desired Qualifications:

  • Advanced database administration skills including backups/restores, monitoring DB performance, running SQL scripts to monitor the system and modify tables when required.  Knowledge of MySQL server administration and troubleshooting highly desirable. 
  • Familiarity with school network infrastructures and procedures a plus

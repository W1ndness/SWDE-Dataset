About the Job

Company Overview:


Headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, Liberty Power is one of only four national retail electric providers (“REP”) licensed in 16 or more states. Currently serving tens of thousands of customers in New York, Texas, Maryland, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Maine, Massachusetts, Connecticut, Illinois, Delaware, Rhode Island, California, and the District of Columbia, Liberty Power provides businesses, government entities, and residential customers with low-cost, reliable electricity and outstanding customer service. The Company is the largest privately held, non-residential REP in the United States according to KEMA, an international consulting firm.  Liberty Power is further differentiated as the only REP with a national presence that is a certified Diversity Supplier.  KEMA currently ranks Liberty as the 17th largest energy retailer in the nation, and the company is on track to grow to more than $1B in revenue in 2012.  Additionally, Liberty’s national presence and diversity supplier status make the Company a unique and attractive energy solution for Fortune 500 companies and government agencies. 

As an entrepreneurial company, Liberty embraces a culture that is “Humble, Hungry and Smart.” People at Liberty Power are low ego and willing to chip in and do whatever it takes to grow the company into being one of the Top 10 players in the industry.   There is also an on-going quest for knowledge and willingness to challenge the status quo to continuously improve.  People do what they say they will do and have a strong sense of urgency and responsibility because they understand that accountability builds trust, which is one of the Company’s core values.  People that self identify with these qualities tend to be a strong match for the company.  


SUMMARY:
 
The .NET Developers to help design and build Object Oriented libraries and applications for parsing, importing, managing, and analyzing transactions. The Senior .NET Developer will design and build a scalable, robust multi-layer architecture in C# as well as web applications in Visual Studio 2008 and SQL Server 2005/2008. Additionally, you will work with a team of developers and serve as technical experts and consultants to other groups.

The Project Manager will drive the development and implementation of the Siebel CRM with Energy Vertical system across the organization. This individual will work closely with business units and IT to deliver a solution that meets the organizations needs and is aligned with the business vision and strategic objectives. Proven experience in delivering complex projects to completion on time and within budget is required. 

The Project Manager will interact with many levels of staff in the organization; up to and including the executive management level. Ability to manage teams through all phases including concept, development, implementation, and closeout. Ensures that the project plan, scope, work breakdown structure, schedule, risk, budget, and deliverables are in alignment with the project goal and objectives as defined by the project statement of work. Achieves the project goals and objectives without compromising quality. Works independently and as part of a team to guide project efforts while developing stakeholder relationships and maintaining confidentiality.

PROJECT MANAGER FUNCTIONS:

  • Develops and prepares written project plans addressing tasks, roles and responsibilities, risk, resources, budget, communications, quality, and organization.
  • Develops work breakdown structure. Performs critical path analysis and develop time-lines for completion of tasks, measuring the deliverable work packages of the project against the project plan.
  • Drives aggressive implementation schedules while maintaining high quality standards.
  • Manages and updates project activities throughout the project life cycle, schedule, budget, and required reports. Maintains and updates project documentation to include project plans, work breakdown structures, schedule budgets, risk plans, communication plans, table of organization, change control log, and reports.
  • Demonstrates an understanding of tactical goals and effectively participates in the development and implementation of business solutions.
  • Manages project risk through comprehensive mitigation assessment and planning techniques.
  •  Leads an effective change control process within the project. Obtain approvals for changes to scope, budget, and schedule that effect the critical path.
  • Ensures that scope, budget, and schedule targets are within variance and project delivery dates are met.
  • Identifies and eliminates obstacles to solution plans, business goals, or implementation. Assists in the development and implementation of contingency plans.
  • Assists in the development of project budgets in compliance with the company’s financial practices.

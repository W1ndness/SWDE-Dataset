Seeking Seasoned PeopleSoft Technical Developers!

About the Job

Why not become part of an elite team of Consultants leading the transformation of how our Clients do Information Technology Management? 

TeraThink Corporation is a leading edge information technology and management consulting firm specializing in solution development, enterprise software implementation and maintenance, and program/project management. Ranked #96 fastest growing company in the country on the 2009 Inc. Magazine, Inc. 500 list, #24 on Washington Technology's 2009 Fast 50 list as well as recently named to the SmartCEO Future 50 as one of five of the Emerging Growth Winners in the Future 50 listing, we are a small business that proudly provides services delivered through proven methodologies that focus on customer needs to optimize business processes and maximize return on investment through enabling technologies as we support both our Private and Public Industry Clients. Our vision is to partner with our customers from project conceptualization, through implementation and beyond solution deployment, providing the highest level of support and guidance.

TeraThink's mission is to provide sound solutions to our customers, not sacrificing quality or efficiency, while maintaining the highest degree of customer satisfaction. We ensure our customer objectives are met to enable solutions that ultimately enhance business processes and increase operational efficiencies through enabling technologies.  As such we have an exciting opportunity for several seasoned PeopleSoft Technical Developers who will be instrumental in providing support for all of the technical aspects of a PeopleSoft Federal HRMS upgrade (from v8.8 to v9.1 PeopleTools 8.50) for one of our prestigious Federal Government Clients.  The highly visible role will be based in Washington, D.C. 

This exciting opportunity requires current and proven experience with at least 2 of the following HRMS PeopleSoft 9.1 modules:  

·         Core HR Federal Product using Position Management,

·         Job Profile Management,

·         ePerformance,

·         Base Benefits,

·         Payroll Interface,

·         Self-Service Functionality,

·         Talent Acquisition Management. 

·         In depth knowledge of the Person Model also strongly preferred.

 

Our selected candidates must have prior technical Federal Government upgrade experience encompassing evaluation, development, and unit testing of reapplied custom code (PeopleSoft and external objects), as well as the proven ability to document and unit test all changes. Knowledge of Configuration Management practices, packaging, and migration of PeopleSoft projects across various environments is required, coupled with the expertise and ease necessary to work closely with the functional resources to map functional design to technical design. 

 

TeraThink employs only the finest professionals, provides quality service and strives for customer satisfaction. We invest heavily in career development and instill a positive culture and discipline, as our professionals serve as the foundation and future of the company.

Come join us! 
We invite all qualified candidates to offer their current resumes to: Jkreuter@TeraThink.com !

U.S. Citizenship is required as selected Individuals must be able to pass an agency background check.


TeraThink is an EEO/Affirmative Action Employer who proudly supports Diversity in the Workforce.

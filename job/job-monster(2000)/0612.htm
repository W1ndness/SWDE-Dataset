Network Engineer-IP

About the Job

Primary responsibility of the Network Engineer-IP is to ensure the continued integrity, stability and security of the customer network. The Network Engineer - IP will also be the primary interface with the Network Design and Central Office groups, to facilitate fluid transfer of information for both implementations and upgrades. The Network Engineer-IP also performs engineering level support for the Network Operations Center. 

 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned:

 

·         Network Operations Center troubleshooting support of system issues

·         Ability to adjust role to support the Network design group in IP design

·         IP infrastructure implementation and upgrades

·         Server system implementation and upgrades

·         NOC network/server security

·         Operational Network and Server Administration

·         Customer Web and Email Administration

·         Product review and recommendations

·         Preventative maintenance program

·         Capacity Management

·         Change Management

·         Incident Management

·         Problem Management


Technical skills: 4 years experience with IP Networking at a professional level, WAN/LAN experience, Network administration, Linux Server administration i.e.; (rack and blade, backup and restore, high availability clusters, DHCP, DNS, Apache, MYSQL, Proficient in PERL to the level of creating/maintaining scripts and PERL modules from scratch and CPAN. Proficient in MySQL, must be able to design and implement a new database schema. Must be proficient in data normalization standards, including SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE AND JOIN functions. Must be able to perform routine work on RPM’s including disassembly and building from scratch. Must be familiar with Red Hat Satellite server. Should be able to build and maintain a yum server. Good understanding of XEN with the ability to deploy, maintain and troubleshoot in the environment, Must be familiar with “bare metal” system restores. Knowledge of Cisco equipment, Knowledge of web hosting, Knowledge of email administration, OS knowledge in Redhat, Linux, UNIX, Microsoft and AS400, Basic understanding of telephony and cable TV systems.

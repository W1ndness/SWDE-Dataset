Technical Writer/Software Documentation

About the Job

Junction Solutions is a rapidly growing, vertical-specific, extended ERP and eCommerce solutions’ company that is revolutionizing the way Food & Beverage, Chemical and Retail organizations manage their entire value chain. Junction Solutions’ eCommerce on Demand solution, JunctionEOD™, is a full featured, hosted, next generation eCommerce platform. It provides integrated eCommerce for cross-channel retail and direct-to-consumer initiatives. Powerful management tools offer complete management of merchandising and website storefronts. JunctionEOD can be sold stand alone or integrated to any of Junction Solutions for Retail applications including point-of-sale, cross-channel retail, consumer demand planning, and business intelligence.

 

We continue to experience strong growth in our eCommerce On Demand group, JunctionEOD™, and are currently hiring a Technical Writer/Software Documentation Professional for our North Tustin (Santa Ana) office.

 

The Technical Writer will be responsible for writing technical documents and communications deliverables, creating document templates and providing quality assurance editing on documents.  The ideal candidate will have excellent technical writing skills and the ability to manage priorities to meet immediate team objectives.  As part of your duties, you will:

Software Documentation Responsibilities:

·        Create and maintain system features and functions document

·        Create and maintain help doc for online and desktop apps.

·        Create and maintain internal and customer facing technical documentation

·        Review and edit customer facing functional specifications.

·        Review and edit marketing docs.

 

 

Required Qualifications:

·       3+ years of software industry experience in functional design or quality

·       3+ years of technical writing experience

·       Exceptional technical writing skills and organizational abilities

·       Excellent communication, interpersonal and organizational skills, as well as a strong command of grammar and vocabulary and attention to detail

·       Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively

·       Ability to learn new tools, technologies and processes quickly and independently; can understand complex software quickly

·       Detail oriented

·       Ability to multi-task

·       Demonstrated experience eCommerce applications, highly desired

 

 

Preferred Skills:

·        SQL Experience

·        Development experience (.net c# preferred)

·        SVN experience

·        Computer Science or equivalent degree

 

All candidates must be client-focused, team-orientated, and have superior communication skills.

 

At Junction Solutions our product is our people. You'll be part of a team of smart, engaging, client-centric professionals who understand that our success depends on the measurable success of those we serve. You'll find an opportunity to balance personal life with professional growth in an environment that rewards creative problem solving. We believe that great people, all focused on a shared and powerful vision, can achieve tremendous results. There's a real sense of empowerment when you know your contribution really matters.

These are full time salaried positions with full company benefits. Junction Solutions offers a comprehensive benefits package including, health, dental, vision care, life insurance, short-term disability, long-term disability, pre-tax flexible spending accounts, and 401K plan.

Wireless Circuit Path Designer

About the Job

Axcent Networks is an independently owned and operated Telecommunications Database Engineering firm. Our portfolio of services includes years of skilled experienced in the areas of Network Resource Management, Optical Network Integration and Installation, Project Management, and Wireless Operations.

Core Values

Axcent Networks is seeking individuals that possess hands on 3G, UMTS and Ethernet Circuit Design experience. Candidates will be responsible for a variety of activities which includes MSN port assignments, IMA/PVP circuit design, continued Granite documentation, and UMTS/3G Network support.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·         Spare select ports/elements MSN, RNC, DACS and CFA

·         Initiate MSN or RNC rebalance when required

·         Update User Defined Attributes for Granite paths and segments

·         Complete Granite circuit revision, circuit add, or circuit decommission path design

·         Coordinate and design core elements for RNC or Node B re-homes

·         Support cell site re-homes by providing before and after designs along with cut-sheets

·         Work closely with project teams and complete requirements by project timelines

·         Complete all IMT circuit designs between all CORE elements, including GIGE circuit designs and all signaling circuits.  (Gb, SS7, F-Links)

·         Follow all engineering circuit design guidelines (to include Standards, Diversity, and all Policy Letter directives)

·         Support DS0 mapping for E911 for all New Site Builds

·         Initiate MSN or RNC rebalance and re-grooms when required

·         Complete all designs and requirements by SLA timeline

·         Prepare and forward reports when required

Requirements:

·         2-5 years experience as a Transport Engineer/Circuit Designer

·         Thorough understanding of the 3G & UMTS Networks is imperative

·         Expert understanding and knowledge of Granite

·         Must be able to work flexible hours and overtime as needed

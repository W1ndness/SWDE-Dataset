 Engineering Architect - Tools 



The Engineering Architect will be a part of the Tools Engineering team in Fiserv’s FET-ETS Organization. This role requires a result-oriented individual to support Fiserv's vision and mission of consolidation and Fiserv 2.0.  The incumbent needs to be able to provide efficient and quality services to Fiserv customers. This position will lead the tool evaluations, integrations, interfaces, upgrades, and deployments, with a focus on Monitoring tools for a data center. As well, be responsible for requirements gathering and building/maintaining of roadmaps for these tools.

Tools Engineering team has dedicated focus on 3rd party applications. This team is responsible for consistent approach and process for evaluation, deployment, maintenance and retirement of Tools (3rd party applications). 

The initial set of standardized tools spans over the areas of Back-up, Monitoring, Provisioning, Job Scheduling, Transmissions, ITIL Processes (Change Mgmt, Config Mgmt, Service Desk), and Capacity Planning. The primary focus for this engineer will be Monitoring tools for network, servers and storage.

This role will work closely with other teams in ETS and in FET (Platform Engg, Core Engg, ISS, Service Management, National Operations, Enterprise Architecture, IT Optimization, and Finance) to effect timely identification, standardization and deployment of Tools across identified primary data centers in Fiserv.

This position requires an extensive background in evaluation, architecture, and deployment of 3rd party applications/tools.


Skills & Experience Requirements
7-10 years of leading the technical aspects of multiple IT/Infrastructure projects ranging in various degrees of complexity.
Formal 3rd party application/tool architecture experience is preferable.

Responsibilities Include:


·         Demonstrate deep understanding of needs, technology and tool interfaces for Monitoring of servers, network, storage, databases etc. in a data center, including monitoring of Virtual environments – to be able to define and deploy the architectures for comprehensive and scalable 3rd Party Tool solutions

·         Quickly understand the comprehensive tools deployment plan and inter-dependencies over the next 12-18 months for an initial set of identified tools to meet the imminent needs of other FET initiatives (e.g. Data Center Consolidations, WAN Consolidation)

·         Lead 1-2 tool deployments concurrently, and managing these engagements with vendors

·         Develop block diagrams and logic flowcharts

·         Prepare test strategy, plans and data for testing a new deployment and/or enhancement

·         Produce documentation on standards and guidelines for deployment and use of tools

·         Assist and/or lead the root cause analysis, vendor liaison, and resolution to production issues for tool implementations

·         Interface at various levels of FET organization and Fiserv Business Units to present/evangelize FET Tools standards and their benefits, including the industry trends & best practices, and TCO to the company

·         Build and own rolling multi-year roadmaps for assigned standardized tools

·         Lead the evaluation and selection efforts for 3rd party tools. This would include defining the evaluation/POC requirements and selection criteria, identifying key technical and business inter-dependencies and constraints, doing cost estimates and financial analysis, preparation and presentation of the business case

·         Have solid experience of dealing with 3rd party application vendors, for evaluations and deployments

·         Understand and work with FET processes of Portfolio and Demand Management, Project Management, Change Management, Release Management, Governance and EEP; At the same time, be a part of Governance and EEP review, as needed, to ensure alignment of other solutions with Tool standards and architectures

·         Provide inputs to refining the strategy and charter for Tools Engineering Team to align with long-term FET strategy

·         Work in a fast paced environment with the ability to adapt and change priorities as the business needs change

Required Tools and Platform Knowledge & Skills:

Knowledge and /or experience of 3rd Party applications and tools:

Primarily, CA tools suite for network and server monitoring: (NSM, eHealth, Spectrum, Cohesion, CMDB), Orion Solarwinds, and Microsoft MOM/SCOM.

Other tools for monitoring across various platforms: IBM Director, Prognosis/HP Offender, Introscope, Nastel, Oracle Enterprise Manager, Dell Open Manager, HP Insight Manager; LogManager, Sighline and SysManager for Unisys, and Virtual Center for VMware envs.

HW Platforms/OS: Primarily Distributed platforms - Windows/Linux/AIX e.g., IBM p-Series, IBM i-series, Dell servers, ESX servers, Sun, HP Non-stop, SAN/NAS storage (EMC, IBM)
Databases: Oracle, DB2, UDB, SQL* Server

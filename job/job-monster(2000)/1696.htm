About the Job

Grow your SAP Career with Jason Roberts LLC- SAP Supply Chain/ Contract and Contract to hire roles.

 

Due to the expansion of our U.S. SAP Practice Jason Roberts LLC is growing again.  Mutiple National locations available. Travel will be required for some roles.

 

We are currently looking for additional SAP Team Members to fill multiple roles within our SAP Supply Chain division.  Prior SAP experience is required within the SAP Supply Chain.  If you have SAP expertise in Supply Chain areas, please forward your resume and further investigate our opportunities.  We are looking to staff multiple national locations, and many career levels.

Basic Qualifications:

a. Education: BS degree in Accounting, or Physical Sciences, or Mathematics, or Computer Science, or Finance, or Business, or Management Science or Information Management Systems. (Or business experience equivalent).

b. Experience: A minimum of five (5) years of experience in design, development, implementation, and deployment of ERP supply chain systems and not less than three (3) years of direct SAP specific module / focus areas - WM, EWM, APO, GATP, SCM, SRM, PLM, Transportation Management, Supply Network Collaboration (SNC), Service Parts Management, Procurement & Inventory Management - MM, SD, Logistics Execution System (LES) as examples. 

 

We will be utilizing both technical and functional Senior SAP resources for our projects.

 

About our team:

 

As a brief introduction to our firm, Jason Roberts LLC is a national provider of business services and enterprise application solutions.  Established in 1986 and headquartered in Berwyn, Pennsylvania, Jason Roberts continues to operate as an SAP Premier Services Partner.  The ability to deliver successful enterprise solutions to a diverse customer base has brought Jason Roberts national recognition.  Our solution offerings combined with our industry specific management expertise deliver an unmatched value proposition.

Jason Roberts offers a comprehensive consulting, staffing, and support program which translates into greater productivity and faster implementations.

Proven Success - Satisfied customers attest to Jason Roberts' success. Jason Roberts prides itself in consistently delivering essential business solutions to its clients within private industry and the public sector. 

Core Values - Integrity, Quality Workmanship, Teamwork and Trust are the core values that will guide our partnership. 

Methodology - Our Global Sustainable Deployment Framework is certified by SAP and embraces both ASAP and Run SAP. This methodology ensures that risk is mitigated, costs are reduced, and value is increased.

 

SAP Partnerships - Jason Roberts maintains a number of SAP partnerships including;

 

•SAP Consulting Premier Services Partner

 

•SAP Active Global Support Partner

 

•SAP Training Partner

 

•Certified Run SAP Implementation Partner

 

 

These partnerships ensure alignment to SAP solutions and services and carry numerous benefits including constant knowledge transfer, tools, methodologies, and experts ensuring that our teams have access to the latest SAP content. SAP supports their partners to ensure long-term business success.  

Expert SAP Professionals - Our service teams are comprised of project managers, platinum and senior level consultants. Our teams are backed by industry and practice managers skilled in various vertical markets and methodologies.   

            Jason Roberts provides the most talented SAP consulting resources in the industry.  Jason Roberts will engage subject matter experts, as well as, application and technology consultants to support project initiatives.  Our consultants bring expertise within a wide range of SAP solutions.

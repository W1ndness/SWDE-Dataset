Senior Oracle Applications Business Analyst

About the Job


Nuance is the leading provider of speech and imaging solutions for businesses and consumers around the world. Our technologies, applications and services make the user experience more compelling by transforming the way people interact with information and how they create, share and use documents. Every day, millions of users and thousands of businesses, experience Nuance by calling directory assistance, getting account information, dictating patient records, telling a navigation system their destination, or digitally reproducing documents that can be shared and searched. Making each of those experiences productive and compelling is what Nuance is all about.
As a contributing member of Nuance, you will work within a dynamic team environment to develop, support, market and sell our award-winning software applications. We offer competitive compensation packages; including stock options, Employee Stock Purchase Plan, 401(k), full health and welfare benefits and a challenging technical but casual work environment. Join our dynamic, entrepreneurial team that operates worldwide (Europe, US, APAC). Be a part of our fast growing track of continuing success.
For more information, please see www.nuance.com.

Nuance is an equal opportunity employer.

To be considered an applicant, please submit your resume to this job at: http://www.nuance.com/company/careers /

Primary responsibilities of this position include providing Techno functional expertise of Oracle Applications 11i application modules in ANY of the following areas: Financials, Order to Cash, Procure to Pay, Project accounting, Service Contracts, Install base, Inventory, Teleservices, isupport, OTL and Human Resources. Act as techno functional area expert within Oracle Application 11i. Seeking team player, outstanding communication skills and customer service, able to stay on task when working independently.

Provide process leadership in driving and implementing solutions, Techno functional support to business on Oracle modules. Assist in the implementation of solutions, monitor and document post-implementation problems and revision requests.

Understand what functionality is available as well as what has already been implemented. Advise on new solutions for enterprise business applications integration and functionality activation. Understand the impact a process change might have on the system and be able to guide process definitions accordingly.

Write Functional design documents, write/ review technical design documents, solution architecture with cross functional / cross modular knowledge along with ability to work on micro level details as an individual contributor when needed.

Consult with end-users to analyze and understand user needs, objectives, desired features, and input and output requirements. Provide end-user instruction and support to resolve application issues. Provide resolution to support issues, and responsible for escalation procedures while retaining ownership of the issues. Manage Technical Assistance Requests (TAR) with Oracle Support.

Write user documentation for new applications. Develop training materials and provide training to new users. Guide users in the implementation of business processes. Provide detailed business requirements specification for developers, process definition outlines and flows, maintain and update application specific process documentation and end user training documentation. Ability to provide high level application design, architecture with cross functional / cross modular knowledge along with ability to work on micro level details.

Develop, coordinate, and implement plans to test business and functional processes and quality assurance testing. Lead functional acceptance testing of patches and upgrades and train and document processes.

  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance or Information Systems or equivalent
  • 5 plus years of Techno functional experience with Oracle Applications (11i a must, Release 12 experience is a plus) - must state module specific experience. Cross module experience is a plus.
  • Strong working experience in ANY of the following application modules; Exposure to 2 or more of the following preferred:
    • Financials (Payables, Receivables, GL, Cash Management, Fixed Assets, iexpenses)
    • Projects ( aka Project accounting, now called as Projects costing, billing, management)
    • Order to Cash ( quoting, OM, Shipping, INV, BOM, Installe base, service contracts)
    • Purchasing (iprocurement, Purchasing, Receiving, Quality)
    • CRM ( isupport, Teleservices, Incentive compensation) etc
    • Human Resources ( Core HR, OTL, Advance Benefits etc)
  • Demonstrated experience performing business analysis for Oracle Applications 11i, in the areas of implementation and support/upgrade/development projects with very good understanding and experience of relevant methodologies like Oracle AIM.
  • Ability to organize and manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Ability to analyze and solve complex problems, provide documentation, guidance and instruction to users, at all levels.
  • Strong Customer Focus, Relationship Management skills, Learning Agility, communication skills, execution skills

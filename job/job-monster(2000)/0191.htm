IT Business Analyst (Make & Procurement)

About the Job

Sensata Technologies is the world's leading supplier of sensors and controls across a broad range of markets and applications. Sensata manufactures innovative engineered sensors and electrical protection devices for the automotive, HVAC, aerospace and industrial marketplaces.  We focus on new product development for high growth segments within these market spaces. Our sensing and protection solutions improve safety and efficiency for millions of people every day. Sensata has over 9,000 employees world-wide, and is headquartered in Attleboro, MA. 
 

The IT Business Analyst will act as the strategic and tactical focal point supporting the Global Manufacturing (“Make”) and Procurement Organizations of Sensata Technologies for the development of world class IT tools and solutions.  Sensata’s Make organization comprises the global manufacturing organization for Sensata, including 8 manufacturing sites around the world, logistics, and planning.  The business analyst will provide technical assistance in identifying, evaluating, and developing systems, processes, reports and procedures that meet user requirements.  This position will plan, direct and complete analysis of business requirements and act as a liaison between the user community and internal IT resources.

 

Position Responsibilities

1.      Manage projects from inception through completion, ensuring that requirements are gathered, project plans are created and maintained, and ultimate solution is deployed as required.  Coordinate the completion of tasks with team members and the management team.

2.      Together with Make business managers, define and analyze system needs and requirements, and communicate those needs using the proper tools to the IT organization.  Insure that the needs of Make are reflected in the IT Strategic Planning and Project Management Office process, and the Annual Planning processes.

3.      Act as Global Liaison between Make business teams and IT teams, with the ability to present technical information to a non-technical audience, and an ability to understand and interpret business requirements.

4.      Develop and deliver system specifications and user-training documents.

5.      Manage tracks of global full life cycle Oracle implementations.

6.      Provide support as needed, in coordination with the overall IT M&A program, for mergers, acquisitions and divestitures that the company may undertake. 

7.      Anticipate and identify issues and develop recommendations and courses of action which are consistent with business objectives and functional requirements.

8.      Define and deliver effective business reports to support Make strategic business objectives.  Insure that the needs of Make are addressed in the overall business intelligence and reporting solutions created for Sensata.

 

Position Requirements/ Skills

  1. Knowledge of and experience with a global manufacturing organization.
  2. Knowledge of Material Requirements Planning (MRP), Production Planning, Logistics, Inventory Control, Supply Chain Management, Order Management, and Purchasing within an Oracle environment.
  3. Strong negotiation skills to reach agreement on realistic requirements scope, schedule, and priorities.  Demonstrated consensus building skills.
  4. Strong computer skills and experience and proficiency using Oracle and Microsoft Office tools, including MS Project.  Plusses include experience with Oracle upgrade processes and knowledge of Oracle R12 Advanced Supply Chain Planning.
  5. Proven, strong Project Management skills.
  6. Confident communicator, with advanced written and oral communications skills.  Comfortable presenting to senior management.  Confident in making recommendations for solutions to business issues.  Ability to set priorities.  Demonstrated ability in documenting functional specifications.
  7. Ability to work with a global perspective. 

 

Education – Bachelor Degree in Information Systems/Technology or Business/Engineering with professional experience in an IT environment.

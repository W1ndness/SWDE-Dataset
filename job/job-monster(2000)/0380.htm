About the Job

QUALITY MANAGER
 
World Wide Technology, Inc. is currently seeking a Quality Manager located in Austin, TX.
 
RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Conduct Internal Audits at WWT facilities across the country
  • Interpret international requirements for WWT business lines to aid in compliance.
  • Responsible for technical writing of Work Instructions for Procurement, Lab and Manufacturing Departments.
  • Maintain all current Quality Management System certificates
  • Maintain the Quality System documentation.
  • Train Management staff in Quality concepts
  • Lead Lean projects
  • Collect, trend and analyze internal performance metrics.
 
QUALIFICATIONS:
  • Bachelor’s Degree with 2 years of experience supporting a mature Quality Management System preferred.
  • Fluent in Spanish language preferred
  • 2 years experience with ISO certification
  • 2 years experience in Closed Loop Corrective Action resulting in process improvements
  • 2 years experience in process design using of in process controls and sample plans
  • Proven track record of utilizing a Process Improvement Program
  • Well developed oral and written communication skills
  • Completed Lean and Value Stream Mapping training or comparable Quality training IE Six Sigma Green Belt training
  • Ability to work with minimum supervision
  • Proficient in Visio, Microsoft Word, Excel and Power Point.
  • Certified Internal Auditor



***Must be willing to travel.

 
WWT offers excellent benefits, training, and a competitive compensation package. Visit our web site at www.wwt.com for more information.

Resource Manager

About the Job

At Tektronix Communications, we have earned our lead position in high tech by continually staying one step ahead. We deliver the test, measurement and monitoring solutions that our customers need to push the boundaries of technology. We invite you to join us at the forefront of innovation.



NO CALLS/RESUMES FROM STAFFING AGENCIES WILL BE ACCEPTED

Purpose of Position:

As a Resource manager, the candidate will be responsible for design, develop, and deliver quality products on time all the time. He/she manages a team of engineers working in an Agile s/w development environment.



Essential Functions:


* Leads team of engineers to design and develop quality product that meets internal and field quality metrics; ensures product features meet customer needs
* Delivery of quality products on-time all the time
* Drives product architecture that is scalable, flexible, and easy to use and build new application/protocol suite
* Drives creativity and product innovation
* Manages daily work in the business such as agile daily scrum, weekly reporting, content planning, product release management, resolution of business and technical product and customer satisfaction issues
* Manages s/w release content by working with PLM and provides visibility to the PLM and exec team on staffing availability
* Leads continue improvement in development processes within the Engineering Champion and leads change where needed to support the strategy
* Articulates the product architecture and solutions to internal stakeholders (PLM, marketing, sales) and customers
* An accepted proven leader and role model for the organization, who constantly enhances morale, creates followership, facilitates continuous learning and continuous improvements at all levels
* An effective champion and communicator of the vision, goals, and values of the business

Education required for this position:


* B.S. Undergraduate degree in engineering (CS or EE is preferred)
* MS or MBA is preferred but not essential (may be substituted with equivalent work experience)
Experience required for this position:


* 7+ years of experience in software development as designer and as an architect
* 3+ years of experience in managing engineering resources to successfully deliver products with quality on time
* Previous experience working in Agile s/w process is a strong positive
* Good written and verbal communications skills; inter-departmental communications is also very important
* Must be a driver, but also a consensus builder
* People management, including developing and top grading talent
* Strong technical background on software technologies and telecomm technologies

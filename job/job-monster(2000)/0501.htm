About the Job

IT 2nd Level Support Specialist

 


West Point, GA

 

 

 

Knology is currently seeking an individual to join our Corporate IT Team based in West Point.  This individual will be responsible for providing technical support for Knology’s internal network of end-users.

 

Key duties will include:

 

1.                   Provide end-users with technical support and resolve technical issues via E-mail, phone, onsite, or ticketing system. 

 

2.                   Install, configure, and troubleshoot laptops, desktops, and computer peripherals as needed. 

 

3.                   Escalate application or system outages to appropriate personnel. 

 

4.                   Upgrade hardware and software components as required.

 

5.                   Must be willing to be in an on call rotation and some overtime is required.

 

 

Attributes we seek in a candidate include the following:

 

High School diploma or GED required.  Some college would be a plus.  Previous experience with current Microsoft Operating Systems, basic networking components and replacing PC parts.  Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to interface with the end customer in a courteous, effective manner are required.  Must be willing and able to learn new technologies.  Must be able to function as an effective team member. Experience with Windows based applications, Active Directory, and creating user accounts.  Must be able and willing to travel (up to 25% on a short term basis).

 

This is a full time role with benefits that include:

  • Group Insurance (Major Medical, Prescription Card, Dental, & Vision coverages available)
  • Paid Time Off
  • Flex Spending Plan
  • 401K Retirement Savings Plan
  • Tuition Assistance
  • Discounts on Knology Services are available to our Employees who reside in our service areas 


To be considered for this opportunity, you must apply online via the Knology website at www.knology.com/page/careers.  When you reach our careers page, click on the jobs list link to locate this position and find information on other opportunities.  After selecting the job link, follow the prompts to complete the employment application. 

 

Knology Inc. (NASDAQ: KNOL), headquartered in West Point, Georgia, is a leading provider of interactive communications and entertainment services in the Southeast and Midwest. Knology serves both residential and business customers with one of the most technologically advanced broadband networks in the country. Innovative offerings include over 200 channels of digital cable TV, local and long distance digital telephone service with the latest enhanced voice messaging features, and high-speed Internet access, which supplies IP architecture with segmented voice and data bandwidth, and Managed Integrated Network Solutions (MATRIX), and integrated IP-based technology which converges data and voice. For more information, please visit www.knology.com.

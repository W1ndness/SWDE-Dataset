Senior Network Engineer

About the Job

Fiserv, Inc. (NASDAQ: FISV) is the leading global provider of information management and electronic commerce systems for the financial services industry, driving innovation that transforms experiences for financial institutions and their customers. Ranked No. 1 on the FinTech 100 survey of top technology partners to the financial services industry.

Fiserv (NASDAQ: FISV), is a Fortune 500 company and the leading global provider of financial services technology solutions, bringing information management and electronic commerce systems and services to the financial services industry. Leading services include transaction processing, outsourcing, electronic bill payment and presentment, investment management solutions, business process outsourcing (BPO), software and systems solutions. Headquartered in Brookfield, Wisconsin, with significant operations in Norcross, Georgia, and other locations.  Fiserv is the leading provider of core processing solutions for U.S. banks, credit unions and thrifts.


 


With Fiserv, you will create and deliver the Fiserv solutions that drive client satisfaction. You can grow professionally and personally in an environment where intelligence, innovation, and leadership are valued and rewarded. Fiserv holds true to two essential principles: the client comes first, and people make the difference. Fiserv invites you to join us in building on those principles to lead our clients and our company to success. 


 


We are currently recruiting for a Senior Network Engineer to work in our Philadelphia, PA. or Dallas, TX.facility. 


 


Job Purpose


Under general supervision, exercise judgment within defined standard practice and procedures to analyze, test, design, debug, modify and implement network systems and associated configurations.


 


Job Responsibilities


  • Test and modify vendor-supplied operating systems and the associated configurations as needed.

  • Develop network designs as identified and plan implementations with other local team members.

  • Develop block diagrams, logic flowcharts, detailed schematics and associated documentation.

  • OS review, prepare test data, test and debug and manage implementation of OS upgrades.

  • Produce documentation for all network components and architecture created within the department.

  • Assist operational staff with moderately complex support issues.

  • Assist the operational teams with root cause analysis and solutions to moderately complex production issues.

  • May proactively identify potential issues in the production environment and help recommend potential solutions.

  • Conduct analysis of systems specifications and use analysis/diagramming tools to represent business/technical processes. 

  • Recommend changes in maintenance and systems standards, and train support personnel on the conversion and implementation of the system. 

  • Will  provide guidance and training to less experienced analysts/programmers as needed. 

 


Job Requirements


 


Education Required

Bachelor’s degree in Business, Computer Science, Information Technology or equivalent.


Experience Required


Minimum five years experience in network design and implementations.


 Skills and Knowledge Required


  • Good working knowledge of network and IP designs for data centers including Internet and secure zones, redundancy & dynamic routing protocols. 

  • Good working knowledge of Cisco IOS for switching, routing and VPN platforms.

  • Good working knowledge of load-balancing technologies.

  • Good troubleshooting skills and working knowledge of packet decoding tools.

  • Good communications, writing and editing skills needed to transfer information to individuals involved in the development process.

  • Good project leadership or team-lead skills.

  • Acquire/enhance network expertise, keeping up with evolving technology.

 


Working Conditions



              


                        May require some lifting of systems in equipment racks


                        Will require working some Sunday maintenance windows.


                        Occasional travel (less than 10%)

Additional Responsibilities and Requirements


 


Based on the technical competency being recruited (Open Systems, MVS Systems or Networking), use the appropriate concentration below for job posting.


 


Network Systems:


Cisco IOS for switching, routing and VPN platforms. Load-balancing technologies. Authentication systems such as TACACS+. TCP/IP design, addressing, sub-netting & NAT. HA designs for data center environments. WAN technologies such as Frame Relay and VPN.


 


Certificates, Licenses, Registrations - Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) and/or Cisco Certified Design Professional (CCDP), Microsoft certifications are desired but not required.


 


Skills and Abilities - Experience in design, implementation and support of data center networks that include both secure and Internet interfacing zones and associated routing designs. Experience with data center connectivity to external 3rd party and/or customers.


 


Experience with network redundancy, diversity and associated dynamic protocols, including HSRP, VRRP, BGP and EIGRP.


 


Good working knowledge of Cisco Switching IOS, router IOS and VPN concentrator IOS, both operationally as well as being able to research OS for problems, bugs and provide recommendations.


 


Good working knowledge building and supporting VPN connections using Cisco VPN concentrators including tunnels, encryption methods, key exchange and NATing.


 


Good logical, troubleshooting skills with the ability to isolate latency in an environment, leveraging packet decoding tools if required.


 


Working knowledge of support and diagnostic technologies such as logging, central authentication, SSH, SNMP and associated management platforms such as MRTG.


 


Preferred experience - Cisco switch and routers, TCP/IP design & sub-netting. Cisco and Foundry load-balancers. Network Instruments and/or Network General packet decoders.

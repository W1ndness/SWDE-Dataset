Sr. Technology Services Specialist

About the Job

Title Resource Group (TRG) is a full-service title, settlement, underwriting and vendor management services company serving real estate companies, affinity groups, corporations and financial institutions in support of residential and commercial real estate transactions. TRG is currently seeking a Sr. Technology Specialist to support the Client / Server IT function located in Mount Laurel, New Jersey.

 

Job Description:
The Sr. Technology Specialist will primarily be responsible for providing Level 3 support for Client/Server issues and responsibilities.  Additionally, this position will assist with the design and architecture of the Client/Server infrastructure and will be responsible for acting as the Client/Server department lead on numerous IT projects - including identifying tasks necessary for completion of projects and working closely with other departments or third parties to ensure the tasks are completed as scheduled.

Functions include:

§         Troubleshoot complex issues, determine root cause, develop solutions, and own the issues through resolution.

§         Analyze current processes and procedures and propose/implement more effective, efficient, and supportable methods for accomplishing TRG’s goals.

§         Work closely with other IT departments and team members to design and implement technical solutions that meet business requirements/needs and develop detailed project plans used to implement these solutions.

§         Assist in setting the technical direction for the infrastructure to support the TRG business.  Actively participate in strategic and tactical planning process covering capital projects as well as lights-on support.

§         Assist with management of over 300 Microsoft Windows 2000 and Windows 2003 Servers –including file/print clusters, SQL Server clusters, IIS servers, and Citrix / Terminal Server servers.

§         Provide Third-Level support for end-user issues associated with all Windows/Citrix servers, Active Directory, Group Policies, and scripts.

§         Participate in the Client/Server On-Call rotation.

§         Act as the Client/Server Lead on numerous larger, capital projects and ensure that Client/Server’s tasks are identified, tracked, and completed on schedule.

§         Completely manage/lead “mini-projects” that are not as large in scope as the larger capital projects.  This will require working with team players in other departments to ensure that tasks are identified and completely on schedule.

 

Qualifications:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or Information Systems Management, or equivalent combination of education and work experience is required. Work experience must include at least seven (7) years with system administration and/or infrastructure analysis and design in a distributed, mission-critical, corporate environment. Experience in managing IT projects is also required, although this position is not a Project Management position. 

 

This position is for a highly technical individual with the ability to ensure that project tasks are identified, scheduled, and completed on schedule with extensive knowledge and experience implementing/managing Microsoft technologies. Must have hands-on experience with the following:

·         Windows Server 2003/2008

·         In depth IIS 6 knowledge

·         Server Virtualization (VMWare ESX Server preferred)

·         High Availability technologies – including Load Balancing (F5 BigIP experience preferred), Microsoft Clustering, and SAN technologies

Experience with Electronic Document Management a plus, as is experience with Microsoft BizTalk and the ability to script server administration solutions. Will need to have a working knowledge of LAN/WAN technologies: including routing, firewalls, proxy servers.

 

This position will require after hours work as necessary and participation in a 24 x 7 on-call rotation. Occasional, as-needed travel may also be required.

 

The ideal candidate must demonstrate excellent decision-making skills, exceptional analytical skills to identify and resolve technical issues or problems, a capability and desire to continuously learn new technologies, tools and methodologies, excellent oral/written communication and presentation skills, as well as an ability to work well with team members from all departments within IT and the business leadership.

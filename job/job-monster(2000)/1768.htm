About the Job

Systems Engineer

Technician Professionals, LLC is seeking a multi-talented Senior Systems Engineer to provide planning, implementation and deployment support of server and data center hardware and software.   Significant emphasis will be placed on the design and support of back-up operations and virtualization technologies using CommVault and VMware products.  Qualified candidates can expect to perform the following duties in a dynamic customer driven environment;

·       Design, implement and validate new Windows systems and associated hardware

·       Develop and plan for process improvement to the Windows Server Infrastructure

·       Develop plans for process improvement to the virtualization environment

·       Develop plans for business-continuity /​ disaster-recovery capabilities

·       Provide recommendations and technology-based solutions to business requirements

·       Configure, deploy and redeploy servers and related equipment

·       Create and maintain documentation of systems, operational procedures and hardware /​ software inventories

·       Maintain and coordinate servicing of network connected servers and peripherals

·       Provide technical guidance and mentoring of junior team members

·       Troubleshoot, analyze and resolve system and user problems

·       Plan, organize and coordinate work assignments and prioritize workloads

·       Provide customer service via telephone and interpersonal contact

·       Provide backup coverage for colleagues Admin/​Engineering Teams, if needed

·       Rapidly deploy fixes to systems in response to newly identified stability and security threats

Candidates should possess a combination of familiarity, knowledge and expertise in the following areas;

·       System documentation, Visio diagramming, statistics summarizations and report writing

·       Virtualization technologies focusing on the VMware/​ESX family of products

·       Disaster Recovery technologies focusing on CommVault

·       Microsoft systems including Windows OS (2K thru 2K8), SQL and Exchange

·       Microsoft best practice for design of Active Directory and DNS

·       Windows server administration, including system stability, integrity, and troubleshooting

·       Monitoring practices for Windows technologies and hardware

·       Network protocols including MS Networking, DHCP, DNS, WINS

·       Scripting including PowerShell, VB Script and CScript

·       Storage technologies including Fibre Channel, iSCSI and NAS devices

·       Hardware and software upgrades, planning and roll out of servers

·       Backup integrity and recovery coordination along with disaster preparedness planning

·       Commonly-used concepts, best practices and procedures

·       Local and remote computer system diagnostic tools

·       Current versions of Microsoft Word, Excel, Visio, and PowerPoint

Work locations vary by position.  Up to 50% travel is required for some openings.  Must be able to work effectively, both independently and as part of a team and have access to broadband connectivity for telecommuting.  Salary is negotiable and based on experience.


Qualified candidates will hold a Secret Level Clearance or be eligible for an Interim Secret.

Ideal candidates will have a 4 year degree in a related field or equivalent combination of education, training and experience with 3 or more years’ experience as a Windows 2K thru 2K8 Systems Administrator / Engineer providing systems support/​integration/​design in large (1000+​user) environments.  Additional consideration for candidates who currently hold or are working towards the following certifications;

·       VMware Certified Professional           

·       Certified CommVault Administrator/Engineer                       

·       Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer


Technician Professionals, LLC is an Equal Employment Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer

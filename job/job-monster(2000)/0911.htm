Technical Practice Director/PM for Travel or Energy/Utilities Industry

About the Job

Two Roles:          1)Practice Director for Energy & Utiliites

   2)Practice Director for Travel Industry

Location(s):  London (UK), New York (NY)

Travel: Approximately 50- 70%

Job Description:

 

Luxoft is a leading European provider of software development, R&D and system engineering services. Headquartered in Moscow (Russia) the company operates a number of development centers in Russia, Ukraine, Romania, Poland and Vietnam that host more than 4000 engineers. In 2009 Luxoft was selected as the #1 ITO provider in the Eastern and Central Europe in the Black Book of Outsourcing independent ranking. We are looking for an experienced Director for our Energy & Utilities Practice and a Director for our Travel Practice. Both practices currently have more than 100 qualified SW, HW and PS engineers and provide a wide range of services to the major E&U and Travel end-to-end solution providers and OEMs. This is an excellent opportunity for a high-caliber IT professional looking for new challenges.

 

Principal duties:

Practice Director is accountable for managing the key pillars of practice: business development, portfolio/delivery management and people management. Practice Director is also expected to manage and oversee strategic accounts. Reports to VP of Technology Services.

 

 

Job Responsibilities:

a)      Business development

·         Develop and execute new approaches and opportunities for expanding customer base

·         Continuously identify and develop service offerings for existing and potential customers in E&U or Travel sector

·         Cultivate, maintain, and grow relationships within the existing accounts that will increase ongoing business, ensure excellent client relations and satisfaction

·         Initiate and oversee partnerships with key technology vendors in E&U or Travel sector

·         Ensure proper knowledge management

b)      Delivery and Operations

·         Accountable for operational/financial metrics and overall business results of practice

·         Manage/oversee multiple delivery teams (Delivery Centers) to ensure client satisfaction

·         Provide risk assessment and reliable forecasting

·         Cultivate cross-functional communication with other Lines of Business/practices

·         Provide mentoring for the practice senior technical management (Delivery Managers) on the subject matter as required

 

 

Job Requirements:

·         Extensive experience of selling complex solutions to the companies from E&U or Travel sector in EU/US

·         Hands on project management experience in software development or system engineering

·         Extensive experience in E&U domain: AMI/AMR, substation automation, T&D systems, SCADA, billing, related standards (IEC, ANSI, CIM) or Travel domain: booking/ticketing, GDS, cross selling, inventory management, fares management etc. Understanding of airline operations is a plus.

·         Good understanding of main technology players in EU/US

·         Good understanding of contemporary IT technologies and platforms, product development lifecycle, software development methodologies

·         Experience of working in multi-cultural international environment

·         Ability to build and maintain executive-level client relationships in complex hierarchical corporate environment

·         Articulate, excellent presentation and communication skills as well as strong influencing and negotiation skills to drive results

·         Willing to travel

·         Fluent English. Russian/Ukrainian/Romanian is a plus

 

Education: 
      ·        A degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Power System Engineering or similar is required for this position.

Senior Applications Designer

About the Job

Our client's Design Studio in New York is looking for a Sr. Application Designer to work on its industry-leading sites. Our client's sites have been top-ranked in their respective categories and we are looking for someone who can continue this success, while working to take them in exciting new directions. We are seeking talented people to help redefine our content experience and platforms for the future.They are looking for a top quality application designer to lead the development of new programming platforms.  We’re looking for a consumer facing application designer with an expert understanding on how to develop simple, focused experiences.  Someone who has both web application experience and software experience from top quality brands or agencies.  Examples are: Apple, Adobe and Flickr.

Job Responsibilities: 
• Provide creative direction and vision to your team of visual and interaction designers and deliver world-class products
• Inspire, excite, and motivate your team and stakeholders
• Foster and support a collaborative, innovative culture
• Oversee multiple projects simultaneously from planning to launch
• Define priorities and manage resources as appropriate across projects growth Manage and mentor designers: performance reviews, compensation and career
• Mastery of design fundamentals such as color theory, typography, and composition. Possess excellent knowledge of information architecture, user interface essentials, interaction design, style guide creation, and motion design.
• Demonstrates ability to articulate design concepts to a broad audience including non-designers.
• Works to create exciting visual design that supports and enhances the overall experience.
• Constructively challenges current practices or approaches.
• Identifies trends that will have an impact on the work unit or particular product and articulates implications.
• Originates new concepts, strategies, or innovative approaches that have significant impact on the company.
• Demonstrates ability to develop new processes and approaches.
• Acts as a recognized and active resource on design issues for others on the design team.
• Identifies and addresses technical problems as they relate to visual design.
• Regarded as expert and possesses in-depth knowledge of CSS and Flash.
• Handles questions about complex material easily and persuasively.
• Communicates complex design information in a strategic context.
• Ability to serve in a leadership role in the development of the look and feel of the assigned project.
• Gains cooperation in a leadership role in the development of the look and feel of the assigned project.
• Manages projects efficiently and effectively.
• Ability to estimate projects tasks, deliverables, and milestone task assignments.
• Maintains sold relationships with key internal and external clients.
• Uses advanced skills to indentify, develop and adapt to new initiatives.
• Actively solicits client feedback to ensure that all activities and deliverables meet expectations.
• Establishes and cultivates relationships with key contacts across the business units.


Job Requirements:
• Leadership: Must know how to inspire people, motivate and reward them, and be able to make tough decisions and address issues in a timely manner.
• Possesses complete understanding of the overall vision of the project and is able to quickly take in project information.
• Possess the ability to “think outside of the box” in order to provide high level client service.
• Possesses strong screen design skills, executing clean, tight, and exciting designs.
• Possesses a thorough understanding of interface design and expert knowledge in web design.
• Design experience: Background in product design, interaction design, Human Factors, HCI, information design, or related field. Solid understanding of the design of Web applications and services. Need to have superb information design sensibility and a sound understanding of how to leverage user research effectively.
• Communication: Strong analytical and oral communication skills; excellent interpersonal and leadership skills; able to collaborate effectively with others in a cross-functional team
• Scope: Must have experience shipping products for large consumer audiences and be used to working on many products simultaneously.
• Process: Must understand user-centered design and product development processes, and use these effectively. Must be organized and able to switch rapidly between different projects in a fast-paced and exciting environment.

Education:
• Bachelor's or Master's degree in Graphic Design, Information Design, Interaction Design, Product Design, or similar degree.

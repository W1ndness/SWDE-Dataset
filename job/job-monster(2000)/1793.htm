IT Security Manager

About the Job

STL Professional Services is currently seeking an IT Security Manager for a client in Bloomington, IL.

The Manager, Information Security and Privacy will provide leadership for ensuring information security and privacy protection.  Manages the planning and implementation of people, process, and technology defenses against threats to information assets.  Manages the development, implementation, and enforcement of policies, procedures, standards, and technology for protecting company information assets in compliance with industry, regulatory, and contractual requirements.

 

Essential Functions and Responsibilities:

  1. Manage internal information security and privacy staff.  20% of work time.
  1. Develop, implement, and enforce information security and privacy policies, procedures, standards, technology, and employee awareness and training.  20% of work time.
  1. Serves as the primary information security and privacy point of contact and manages information security incidents.  Server as subject matter expert for responses and reviews of customer, vendor, and prospect contracts and proposals.  15% of work time.
  1. Ensure that information security and privacy practices are in accordance and compliance with all applicable local, state, federal, and industry-specific regulations and laws.  Monitor trends and proposed regulations and legislation to anticipate future information security and privacy requirements.  15% of work time.
  1. Develop and influence information security and privacy strategy and governance.  Member of corporate Risk Management Committee.  10% of work time.
  2. Develop and monitor Information Security and Privacy metrics and budgets. 10% of work time.
  3. Perform information security and privacy risk assessments. 10% of work time.

 

The above statements are intended to indicate the general nature and level of work being performed by employees within this classification.  They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties and skills required of employees assigned to this job.  Employees in this job may perform other duties as assigned.

Minimum Job Requirements (Education, Experience, Skills):

 

§  Four year college degree and two to five years of relevant security management experience.  

§  Five (or more) years related information technology experience and/or training is highly preferred.

§  CISSP, CISM, and other /or CISA certifications are highly desirable.

§  Knowledge of project management principles, practices, techniques and tools is required. 

§  Exceptional interpersonal skills are required.

§  Working knowledge of common information security and privacy standards, laws, and regulations such as PCI DSS, HIPAA, GLBA, and ISO 27002

§  Working knowledge of information security tools and ethical hacking techniques

§  Ability to plan and conduct information security assessments.

§  Ability to handle multiple tasks with deadlines simultaneously is required.

§  Ability to organize resources and establish priorities.

§  Knowledge of system development methodologies is highly desirable.

§  Advanced verbal and written communication skills are preferred.

§  Ability to analyze complex problems and develop creative solutions is preferred.

§  Excellent group facilitation, mediation and conflict resolution skills are preferred.

§  Skills in examining and re-engineering standard operating procedures, formulating policy, and developing and implementing new strategies and procedures are preferred.

§  Balances business needs with information risk management strategies

§  Exhibits good listening skills.

§  Knows how and when to engage management to move initiatives forward.

§  Integrates complex or conflicting information to arrive at clear and timely decisions. 

§  Seeks alternatives to the status quo.

 

 

Working Conditions and Environment:

Incumbent works in a temperature controlled, office environment.  Incumbent sits at a desk during regularly scheduled work hours; answers and makes telephone calls using a standard telephone; types on a standard keyboard; reads and comprehends information from a computer terminal and/or written resources. Incumbent is on call 24/7. May be required to work beyond normal business hours to meet internal and external customer requirements. Evening and weekend work may be required.  Some overnight travel may be required.

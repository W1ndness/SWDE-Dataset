Sr. Cisco Voice/Network Engineer

About the Job

Job Title:            Sr. Cisco Voice/Network Engineer

Location:           Shelton, CT

Job Overview:

This position is responsible for planning, implementing and supporting VoIP in a corporate environment. Although the primary focus of this position is to implement and support VoIP installations, the candidate may also be tasked with general network support and build-outs including routing, switching and other network related duties.

 

Reports To:      CIO / Enterprise Architect

Duties and Responsibilities:

·         Maintain and support Cisco’s Unified Contact Center Enterprise and related Cisco IP telephony infrastructure.

·         Administer and troubleshoot Contact Center systems, IVR, voicemail, outbound dialing, and supporting infrastructure.

·         Assist with the design, control, and analysis of inbound and outbound campaigns to optimize resource management and contact center efficiency.

·         Generate and analyze contact center metrics to ensure adherence to defined SLA’s.

·         Work with carriers and vendors to order services and equipment as needed.

·         Provides technical support and guidance on network design issues.

·         Performs setup, configuration, and testing of Cisco Unity, Unity Express, Call Manager, Call Manager Express, Cisco LAN switches (core, distribution and access), Cisco firewalls, routers and voice gateways, etc.

·         Designs appropriate testing routines or scripts; may conduct more complex testing to meet specified requirements.

·         Designs, implements and maintains complex LAN, WAN, or Voice Systems in accordance with specified requirements.

·         Analyzes data, voice or video network documentation and assists in communicating to management regarding the current operational status of networks.

·         Determines methods and procedures for new data, voice or video technologies to ensure performance.

·         Provides consultation, support and guidance to organizations and remote users. May develop and conduct training as needed or appropriate. Participates in discussions regarding project decisions and project direction.

·         Recommends data, voice or video network investment decisions to management based on results of independent assessment of current and future network performance, stability, and network management issues.

·         Performs cost analyses and vendor comparisons to ensure cost-effective and efficient operations; measures feasibility of various approaches, makes recommendations as appropriate.

·         Provides leadership and work guidance to less experienced personnel.

 Credentials and Experience

·         Expert knowledge of Cisco’s Unified Contact Center offerings including, but not limited to, UCCE, Unity, Outbound Dialing option, IVR and speech recognition technologies.

·         5+ years of experience in data and voice networks

·         3+ years of experience working with Cisco Contact Center solutions.
3+ years UCCE/UCCX a MUST. 

·         CCVP Certification strongly desired

·         CCNP Certification desired

·         Prior technology VoIP implementation and support experience in large,  enterprise environments

·         Experience working with network systems, data, voice (including PRI’s) and video

·         Experience working with network software and hardware, data, voice or video

·         Experience working with proprietary software and hardware

·         Must have recent, extensive experience working with Cisco Routers, Switches and Firewalls

·         Good network performance modeling or benchmarking skills

·         Skilled in time, priority and task management

·         Interpersonal skills to interact with customers and team members

·         Good communication skills to interact with team members and support personnel

·         Good leadership skills to guide and mentor the work of less experienced personnel

·         Ability to anticipate problems, resolve ambiguity and take decisive action

·         Ability to work independently and as part of a team

·         Good ability to analyze and solve complex problems using analytical and creative problem solving skills for design, creation and testing of networks

·          Familiarity with common Office applications such as Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Visio is essential

Other Considerations

·         Occasional travel may be required

·         On-call as needed

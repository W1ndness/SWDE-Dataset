Sr. System Administrator / Analyst

About the Job

DSD Laboratories Inc has an immediate need of hiring a Sr. System Administrator / Analyst who will provide technical and operations support and analysis for the National Data Exchange (N-DEx) program in Clarksburg, WV.

Administrator / Analyst will join the program's Operations and Maintenance team to provide technical programming and operations support and sustain engineering for a new system that is being delivered in a series of incremental releases

 

Responsibilities include:

 · Interface with state and local agency representatives to extract, test, and prepare legacy data for ingest into the new N-DEx system

 · Install, configure and maintain the testing system environment and related business applications ensuring that the system is available as needed to support off-site data extraction activities

 · Perform system configuration documentation, configuration management, document procedures, create process documentation, as well as perform property and license management tracking as required

 · Assist the data prep, ingest, and training teams with status presentations, issues and progress reporting

 · Represent the N-DEx program in a professional manner when interacting with local, state, and federal agencies

 · Other duties, as required by the contract

 

 Required Skills:

 · Must have a BS degree in Information Systems/Technology, Computer Science or related technical discipline or possess equivalent experience in lieu of a degree

 · Must have a diverse Information Technology background and have a minimum of 6 + years hands-on experience combining one or more of the following areas:

 o   Enterprise systems administration including Commercial Off-the-Shelf (COTS) products and Blade Server Technology

 o   Red Hat Linux installation, configuration, and maintenance

 o   WebLogic installation, configuration, and maintenance

 o   Oracle 11g installation, configuration, and maintenance

 o   Bash and Shell scripting

 o   VMWare experience

 · Must have experience with the software development life cycle, software documentation processes, and software configuration management

 · Must have the ability to operate in a team environment consisting of both internal and external members, co-workers and customers

 · Must possess excellent communication skills with the ability to interact with the customer to express complex technical concepts in terms that are understandable to a non-technical audience

 · Must have ability to travel up to 10% of the time

 

 

Qualified applicants may be subject to a security investigation and must meet minimum qualifications for access to classified information. U.S. Citizenship is required.

DSD is an independent company with excellent benefits and opportunity for growth.
DSD is an AA and EOE. Qualified candidates please submit resume via email to humres@dsdlabs.com or fax to 978 443 1725

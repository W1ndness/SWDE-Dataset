About the Job

IT Professional 3 (Software Developer - Nevada Business Portal)

Position Description

This Software Developer position with the Office of the Secretary of State for the State of Nevada is available for applicants with strong software development skills along with some Java development experience. The selected applicant analyzes, develops (design, program, and construct), implements, maintains, and modifies various platforms of information systems. The applicant must have the ability to design, implement, and unit test programs that meet established coding standards and State security requirements. Skills and prior experience with SDLC, coding standards, and using documented requirements to develop and application are essential Candidates must be self sufficient, work independently, be very intuitive and have the ability to react quickly and manage change. The State of Nevada is an equal opportunity employer.

 

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to: 

The selected applicant will participate in the Web Services expansion of the Nevada Business Portal. Knowledge of Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Web Services, Business Process Execution Language (BPEL), Business Process Management (BPM), Portal Technology, Content Management Systems (CMS), Identity and Access Management (IAM), and Business Intelligence (BI) products is recommended.

Minimum Qualifications

 

·         Bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university with major course work in computer science or closely related field.

·         Three years of progressively responsible professional IT experience, which involved analyzing, designing, developing, testing, and deploying enterprise applications.

·         Applicants must have professional level experience in an application development capacity.

·         Applicants must have experience or training with at least one programming language.

·         Software Development Life Cycle

 

Special Requirements:

 

·         A pre-employment criminal history check and fingerprinting are required. Persons offered employment in this position will be required to pay for these items.

·         A State of Nevada/FBI background check will be required of the selected applicant.

·         The selected applicant may be required to work on evenings, weekends, and holidays.

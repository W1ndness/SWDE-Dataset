About the Job

Cherokee Nation Entertainment is the retail, gaming, entertainment, hospitality and cultural tourism entity of the Cherokee Nation. CNE operates casinos in Tulsa, Claremore, Roland, West Siloam Springs, Fort Gibson, Sallisaw, Tahlequah and Ramona, as well as the Cherokee Travel Plaza, two Cherokee Outpost convenience stores, six retail tobacco shops, four gift shops, two golf courses and three hotels. The company recently completed a $155 million expansion at their resort property in Tulsa which is now branded as Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Tulsa and a $125 million expansion of Cherokee Casino West Siloam Springs. 

Responsibilities 

Responsible for custom application development.  Analyzes, develops and modifies application programs utilizing core and non-core technologies in support of enterprise systems and/or complex applications.  Designs, develops, modifies, debugs and evaluates programs for functional areas.  Develops conversion and system implementation plans.  Prepares and obtains approval of system, programming, and implementation documentation.  Recommends changes in development, maintenance and system standards.  May interact with users to define system requirements and/or necessary modifications.

*Codes, tests, debugs, modifies and troubleshoots all application programs in accordance with industry standards.

*Analyzes complex application processes and integrated computer systems and their data structures.

*Tests business processes and monitors system test procedures.

*Documents database tables and data structures according to generally accepted principles.

*Develops technical documents pertaining to system design and programming requirements.

*Develops user manuals.

*Helps define internal software development standards, methodologies and processes.

*Plays a lead role on small development projects and participates as a team member on larger projects

*Trains users in the conversion, implementation, and use of application systems.

Qualifications

Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, MIS, an equivalent four year degree from a college or university or an equivalent in-depth specialized training program or work experience that is directly related to the type of work being performed.

Requires a minimum of five years experience in a similar environment.  Requires proficiency in the use of C#.Net 3.5 (including, WCF and WPF), MOSS 2007 (including, website development), SQL Server 2005/2008, common design patterns, and object-oriented programming fundamentals.  Experience using Windows Server 2008 Hyper-V technology helpful.  Demonstrated understanding of contemporary information technologies, including database, programming environments, systems integration and operating systems.

Benefits Information

We offer competitive pay and a comprehensive employee benefits program including medical, dental, vision, 401(k) savings plan, life insurance, long-term disability, education assistance, and paid time off.

CNE Work Schedule

M-F 8-5 or as needed to fit the needs of CNE

Other Information

Cherokee Casino and Resort is a Native American Preference Employer

Conditions of Employment 

Potential employees must be 18 years of age or older.  The potential employee must be able to pass a drug test and background check and must not have a felony conviction, whether expunged, annulled or sealed by the court.   If you meet the minimum qualifications and are motivated to provide outstanding customer service, we hope you will apply for a position with us.

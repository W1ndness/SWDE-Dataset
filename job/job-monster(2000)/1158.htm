About the Job

Lead PeopleSoft Applications Developer

 

The job holder leads the technical design, development and documentation of cross-functional, multi-platform distributed application systems. S/he analyzes, defines and documents requirements for data, workflow, and logical processes and interfaces with other systems. S/he also ensures that expected application performance levels are achieved by coordinating, coding, testing, implementation and documentation. 

 

Responsible for development and maintenance of PeopleSoft (v8.9) HCM modules (HRMS, Payroll and Self Service). Duties include supporting upgrade, enhancement, testing and system implementation activities.

 

Candidate will also be responsible for interacting with the HR business partners to perform requirements gathering, systems analysis, development of functional and technical  specifications and development of test plans - including Use Cases.

  

Testing experience in a highly customized PeopleSoft HCM functionality

Hands-on experience with the PeopleSoft HCM v8.x+

Thorough understanding of development and testing methodologies

Front End Interactive Developer
If you describe yourself as inquisitive, curious, strategic, and passionate about user experience…

And your approach is to work collaboratively, be accurate, adapt to change & meet deadlines...

...then this job could be the right fit for you, so read on...
The gist of it:
We are looking for someone passionate about front-end multi-media development with a creative eye for design. You are incredibly good at HTML/CSS, but realize there is always more to learn and are likely already pushing the limits of HTML5 and CSS3. You should be constantly devouring new information and techniques from the online community and you likely consider yourself more of a creative than a pure developer because you really care about the quality of the work you produce.

You have an in-depth knowledge of JavaScript frameworks such as jQuery, Prototype, scriptaculous, and YUI -- when you see something done well in Flash you are instantly envisioning ways to reproduce it in Javascript.

You are always conscious of accessibility when developing, and are conscientious in developing unobtrusive code that gracefully degrades for older browsers and platforms. You realize your work isn’t done until the designs are implemented into a CMS.

You will be heavily involved in the user interface and experience of high-traffic websites. It would be ideal if you have experience working with server-side languages, in particular PHP. An understanding of Section 508 guidelines is great and experience working with WCAG guidelines is a plus.

Job skills/requirements:

     

  • Experience: 5+ years direct experience
  • Education: Relevant four year bachelor's degree preferred
  • PHP/LAMP development experience
  • Extensive front-end web development experience (Photoshop, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS)
  • Experience in consumer facing web development
  • Understanding of UI and UX, and are comfortable doing IA when needed
  • Understanding of cross-browser capabilities
  • Knowledge of SEO best practices
A Collaborative Environment
You will work closely with our designers and developers and will be our designers’ partner in saving the world from bad design. When working with our designers you will collaborate on bringing sound interactive design principles and usability issues to the forefront so designs are correct from the get go. Our back-end developers will rely on you for template and content implementation, they will work with you to make sure their code is set up for success.
Your Team:
Our staff has as a committed work ethic and loves creative technology; they chat, joke & tell stories. Relationships are strong. Your co-workers will probably ask you on your first day what your favorite music group, favorite beer/wine, and/or if you have pets - their names, you know the important things in life!
Commitment:
This position is a salaried full-time position. You will need to be a self-thinker who is self-motivated and can self-manage to meet challenging project milestones. Project deadlines will dictate your schedule to a large extent, so it may take long hours and/or weekends to meet a few deadlines. As we mentioned up front, you need to be passionate about what you do.
About Us:
LIFEBLUE MEDIA is an Interactive Agency specializing in the planning, creative, development, and implementation of interactive products for our extensive client base. We strive for excellence and to be an innovative force for our clients. We have over a decade of experience in providing the right solutions for global brands and organizations.
Your Working Environment:
Fun and casual. Your team is hard working and focused upon doing great work for the client – but we never forget to have fun in the process. It will be fast paced and deadline filled and we all enjoy coming to work... most days.

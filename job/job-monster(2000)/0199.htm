SAP FICO Project Manager

About the Job

DC&M Partners is seeking an experienced SAP Project Manager/FICO Consultant for a 6 month contract engagement.  The Project Manager/FICO Lead will expertly guide the client company in a chart of accounts conversion effort for their European operations.

Responsibilities include:

 

* Scope and plan the project and resources

* Create and manage project WBS and documentation standards

* Set, manage, and control project scope, deliverables, milestones, and deadlines

*Resolve issues that create barriers to success

* Foster collaboration between project team and business owners

*Host frequent and consistent project meetings with agendas and expected outcomes

*Establish project communication standards

*Mentor project team in SAP FICO and consulting approaches such as preparing gap analysis and options presentations

* Lead the project team in translating business requirements into project scope.



Skill Requirements:

 

* Deep understanding of SAP FICO software

* Application of  Run SAP, Solution Manager, and relevant implementation methodologies
* At least 10+ years of SAP Functional Analyst experience

* At least 5 full cycle SAP projects as a FICO Lead or Project Manager

*At least 5 upgrade, conversion, migration projects as FICO lead

* Strong leadership skills

**Experienced in the use of Sharepoint

* Excellent communication skills are a must.

* Bachelors degree required.

* Big 5 or similar experience preferred.

*Development of presentations using PowerPoint

*Skilled in using MS Project

MBA and CPA desired

 

Work requires that the Lead be on-site 5 days a week and an all inclusive rate.   Therefore, local candidates or those who will relocate for the duration of the project are the best fit.

 

*******************US Citizenship  or Green Card authorization Required****************

Solution Architect Sr TSG

About the Job

Agilysys is a leading provider of innovative IT solutions to corporate and public-sector customers, with special expertise in select markets, including retail and hospitality. The company uses technology — including hardware, software and services — to help customers resolve their most complicated IT needs. The company possesses expertise in enterprise architecture and high availability, infrastructure optimization, storage and resource management, identity management and business continuity; and provides industry-specific software, services and expertise to the retail and hospitality markets. Headquartered in Cleveland, Agilysys operates extensively throughout North America, with additional sales offices in the United Kingdom and China.

 
Agilysys has an opportunity for a Solutions Architect to support Account Executives in the local Chicago and on occasion, Wisconsin territory.


The Solutions Architect is responsible for supporting the Account Executives in designing, documenting, and presenting complex solutions for new and existing customers. Design current and future technologies that are consistent with operational best practices. Required to maintain a strong knowledge of emerging technologies and to provide input to the sales team and customers regarding continuous improvement opportunities. Committed to developing loyal and successful partners by clearly identifying and understanding both customer and supplier requirements and specifying services and products to meet or exceed their expectations. This role is reserved for an individual who has attained a level of distinction in technical expertise and seniority within the pre-sales Solutions Architect career path and/or IT industry.

 

REQUIREMENTS:

 

  • Associates Degree in an IT related field of study and
  • 7 years of relevant work experience or work experience equivalent.
  • Advanced certification in an information technology discipline
  • Demonstrated ability to guide conversation with customer/peers/sales/vendors
  • Hands on technical work in Enterprise storage and data management
  • Expertise architecting complex technical solutions that have been successfully deployed
  • Deep working knowledge of Hitachi (HDS), Sun/Oracle, Brocade, STK, and HP storage
  • Regular territory travel up to 50%


ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Executive level written and verbal skills
  • Excellent Project Management skills
  • Working knowledge of a broad range of IT systems and architectures in Enterprise environments
  • Strong capability in scoping Professional Services briefs and Statements of Work
  • Vendor certifications that lead to expertise, product access, and rebates
  • Entrepreneurial, "can-do" attitude

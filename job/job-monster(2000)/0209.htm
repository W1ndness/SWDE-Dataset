About the Job

Headquartered in Irving, Texas, NEC Corporation of America is a leading provider of innovative business solutions drawn from one of the industry’s broadest portfolio of IT and network technologies, expertise and professional services for service carriers, Fortune 1000 and SMB businesses across multiple vertical industries, including Healthcare, Government, Education, Retail and Hospitality. NEC Corporation of America’s solution expertise and product offerings span unified communications, wireless, voice and data, managed services, server and storage infrastructure, optical network systems, microwave radio communications and biometric security. NEC Corporation of America is a wholly-owned subsidiary of NEC Corporation, a global technology leader with operations in 30 countries and more than $38 billion in revenues. For more information, please visit www.necam.com.


Job Description: 

Participate in development, implementation and customization of a biometric system.  Perform database customizations based on the customer’s requirements, including tables/indexes customization and PL/SQL code customization.  Test and verify the solutions, analyze impact of system changes.  Create, maintain and monitor Development Databases on Windows and Unix/Linux environments.  Install, patch and manage all Oracle software.  Establish and manage a backup & recovery system for Development Databases using RMAN.  Storage Allocation and Planning.  Tuning/optimizing databases.  Administering users and ensuring data security.  Following up with future Oracle trends for optimizing hardware better performance. Maintain documentation of systems and standard operating policies and procedures.

Job Requirements:

B.S degree in Computer Science.  Experience with at least one full lifecycle of a database application.  A minimum of 3 years of Oracle RDBMS 9i/10g development as a primary job function (PL/SQL developer).  A minimum of 3 years of Oracle 9i/10g PL/SQL programming experience, including BULK operations, EXPLAIN PLAN.  Excellent SQL development experience with performance tuning and optimization of SQL statements & PL/SQL code.  Experience with Oracle built-in PL/SQL packages and types, specifically DBMS_LOB, DBMS_UTILITY,UTL_RAW.  Experience with processing large objects (BLOBs,CLOBs) in Oracle database is a huge plus. Experience in data conversion and data load processes including batch processing in OLTP environment.

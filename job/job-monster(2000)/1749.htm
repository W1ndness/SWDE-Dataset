Programmer Analyst

About the Job

 

POSITION TITLE:  Programmer Analyst

LOCATION:  Avon, Ohio (no relocation)

WORK HOURS:  8:30 - 5:30 pm 

REPORTS TO:  IT Director

SEND YOUR RESUME TO: careers@jenne.com


Come see what working for Jenne can do for your career!


Jenne, Inc. is a leading North American distributor of Business and Computer Telephony products. Since the company’s founding in 1986, Jenne has been committed to providing our Dealers and Value-Added Resellers with a broad product selection, competitive pricing, fast delivery, outstanding customer/technical support, plus ongoing sales and technical training.

You can read more about our products at www.jenne.com


RESPONSIBILITIES

§  Implement process improvement changes through modifying existing software and implementing new software solutions.

§   Integrate new software packages into existing ERP environment by building the appropriate infrastructure connectivity

§  Test and document the developed software.

§  Identify process improvement opportunities across the Organization

§  Develop proposals for implementation of  improvements

§  Work with the Web Marketing Manager to enhance the value of the existing web site for both our Customers and for departments within Jenne by developing new features and functionality.

§  Stay informed of new technologies and changes in existing technologies with a keen eye on how these changes may improve Information Systems deliverables.

§  Analyze key system metrics, create reports, and formulate conclusions and recommendations for improvements.

§  Identify and champion new ideas including new technologies, methodologies, and processes.

§  Provide support to existing ERP, Web, office automation, and warehousing systems.


REQUIREMENTS

§  Drives the completion of projects and initiatives; follows through on the execution of chosen strategies, and demonstrates an ability to “stay the course” in spite of obstacles.

§  Ability to focus on and manage multiple objectives simultaneously

§  Flexible, able to respond immediately to often changing business priorities

§  Excellent problem solving skills

§  Must be an exceptional communicator who can immediately grasp concepts at the systems strategy, technology change, and user requirements levels and convert them into action plans.

§  Ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing required.

§  A team player that understands how to work effectively in a fast paced, evolving organization.

§  Bright, conceptual and a quick learner – able to quickly assess a situation and generate new insights and usable ideas.


QUALIFICATION/EXPERIENCE

§  Bachelors Degree preferred in Computer Science, Business or Related Field and two (2) years professional work experience in technology industry; MBA preferred.

§  Ability to develop software using; HTML, Visual Basic Scripting, SQL, MS Commerce Server, MS Access, Crystal Reports, and Microsoft Office Tools; familiarity with .Net a plus

§  Understanding of the principles, procedures, and standards of project management and systems methodologies.


BENEFITS

Jenne offers a competitive hourly wage and a competitive benefits program which includes: Medical, Dental, Prescription Drug, 401 (k) Plan, Life, AD&D, LTD. Voluntary STD, Voluntary Employee, Spouse, Child Life Insurance.

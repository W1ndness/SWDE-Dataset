About the Job



 

Software Engineer ASP.NET VB.NET c# / SQL Server / Azure

Job Description and Requirements


Software Progressions Corporation is seeking an experienced Software Engineer with demonstrated hands on expert level skills in ASP.Net and SQL Server.

Windows Azure and SQL Azure Web application Development experience is a plus.

This person will have deep and broad technical skills in .NET, SQL Server and related Microsoft technologies/products as well as solid architecture and design skills.

 

Technical Skills Required:

  • Expert-level expertise with ASP.NET, XML and Web Services
  • Experience with large-scale applications in a leadership role
  • Expert-level competency in VB.NET
  • Broad experience with .NET Framework 3.0/3.5
  • Experience with ADO.NET
  • Demonstrable experience developing solution architecture 
  • Leadership experience throughout the software lifecycle

 

Technical Skills Desired:

  • Windows Azure and SQL Azure development experience
  • Training or speaking experience (MCT or similar)
  • WCF, WPF/Silverlight, or WF experience
  • Security, including CAS and WCS
  • Familiarity with Agile development methodologies

 

Additional Skills and Competencies:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, or equivalent work experience
  • Must be a self-starter who requires minimal supervision
  • Experienced in problem solving, and able to follow a methodical implementation process
  • Excellent interpersonal and organizational skills, ability to handle diverse situations, multiple projects and rapidly changing priorities
  • Ability to communicate with clients at all levels
  • Work directly with business development teams during project envisioning to come up with the best solution for given requirements, with the ability to provide reasonably accurate estimates for projects.

About Software Progressions Corporation


Software Progressions Corporation develops, markets and supports the Unified Enterprise Suite™, a high-end suite of enterprise software solutions that are used by fortune 1000 companies around the world.


Our Unified Enterprise Suite™ platform is based on our patented semi-structured Unified Enterprise Database™ technology and includes the following enterprise business solutions:

  • Web Application Generator WAG™
  • Tracker Total Relationship Management TRM™
  • Enterprise User Management
  • Corporate Central SaaS Portal

 
From the innovative design and the quality of our products to the delivery of customer service and support, we constantly exceed expectations.

Located in the San Francisco Bay Area, our headquarters in Walnut Creek, California radiate the excitement and vitality of a region that has given birth to countless technological innovations and discoveries.

Our offices are a short walking distance from the Walnut Creek BART station (bay area metro).

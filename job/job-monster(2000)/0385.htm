Clinical Content Manager

About the Job

The Clinical Content Manager will collaborate with the RHCs to lead and implement the various transformation components of CareLink including adaptation of the enterprise design for care delivery processes and workflows, CPOE and order sets, patient assessments and interdisciplinary plans of care.  The Manager is responsible for leading and supporting the management and maintenance of a system-wide library of current medical and clinical content and knowledge in an executable form.  He/she will utilize evidence based content in an easy-to-use form that will help teams progress.  He/she will embrace the critical importance of relevant data and meaningful information in order to ensure that there is informed decision-making.  The Manager will understand and incorporate the principles of change management and clinician adoption into all initiatives.  

 

The Manager will ensure that transformation initiatives are implemented proficiently to result in superior clinical and operational performance in all regions across CHE.

 

§  Provides hands on experience in a leadership role with large scale transformation initiatives including CPOE, process and workflow design, adoption, adaptation, and facilitation in the clinical arena.

 

§  Assist with the facilitation of clinical content design sessions, both in person and virtual meetings.

 

§  Leads the incorporation of updates and design decisions utilizing all aspects of the Zynx Health tool.

 

§  Track requests for order set development, and changes to existing order sets.

 

§  Track requests for IPOC development and changes to existing IPOCs.

 

§  Track clinical content use on a quarterly basis.   

 

§  Maintain the timeline for updating, maintaining and editing of clinical content at least quarterly, synchronized with Zynx Health.

 

§  Coordinate actions taken on change requests for existing clinical content and reconcile active content that is in production relative to versions.

 

§  Assist in transformation activities related to CareLink, incorporating process redesign, clinical content, change management strategies, benefits realization and technology enablement.

 

§  Provide status reports on content inventory, update requirements, work plan status, resource requirements, and evidence review to Clinical Services leadership.

 

§  Educate clinicians and team members on Zynx (View Space and Author Space) use as appropriate.

 

§  Support the CareLink implementation and go lives at each RHC.

 

§  Manage the scheduling, track decisions, and provide documentation for clinical content meetings.

 

§  Coordinate and integrate regulatory requirements into clinical content development.

 

 

Requirements

Education, Certifications or Licenses:

 

 

§  Bachelor’s degree in a health care related field: Nursing, Information Systems, Pharmacy, or Therapies. Informatics training desirable.

 

Skills

 

§  A broad knowledge of multidisciplinary clinical processes and practice issues as well as a basic understanding of pathophysiology.

 

§  A broad knowledge of Informatics & Nomenclature Standards

 

§  A general familiarity of peer-review medical and clinical literature and basic web-based information retrieval strategies.

 

§  A successful track record in leading teams of physicians, nurses, clinical and administrative staff to accomplish change within a healthcare delivery organization.

 

§  Personal sense of ministry and commitment to CHE Mission and Values.

 

§  The position requires a high degree of self-motivation, initiative, innovation, creativity and independence

 

§  The position requires experience with and understanding of various clinical information and technology solutions and their ability to enable and support the transformation.

 

§  He/she must be comfortable facilitating groups and managing both simple and complex improvement projects.

 

§  Advanced understanding and use of Microsoft© applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, and Outlook.

 

§  Understanding of Zynx Health TM.

 

Travel:

 

§  The position will require travel estimated at 10-50% of his/her time.

About the Job

Heery International, Inc. is a progressive, multi-disciplined management and design firm with more than 1000 employees in offices throughout the United States.. Founded in 1952, Heery has been recognized annually by such industry publications as Building Design & Construction and Engineering News-Record as one of the nation’s top ranked firms.  Further background can be found via our website at www.Heery.com. 

 

Heery is currently experiencing a period of significant and exciting growth and is seeking to identify highly qualified candidate for Heery’s Help Desk, Level One opportunity. Heery has a need for the following position in our Knowledge Management Resources Group, in our Midtown, Atlanta, Georgia location.

 

HELP DESK LEVEL 1

 

Ideally this candidate will have some college and preferably a related college degree.   This position requires the skill and temperament required to assist our user base with computer related issues.  Also needed are basic PC knowledge and networking skills, including MS Windows, Office 2007, Plotting/Network Printing, CAD and Autodesk Revit. This position requires 2-5 years of experience.

 

Heery’s client base includes: Vertical Construction, Healthcare, Schools, Aviation, Corrections, Judicial and Municipal to name a few.  There is continual growth and opportunity within Heery.  If you are looking for a career with one of the Nation’s top, most respected firms, involvement with exciting and challenging projects, respond today!

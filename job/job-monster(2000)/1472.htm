Program Manager (SAP CRM)

About the Job

Global Information Services – Program Manager (SAP CRM)

Full time Hire / Located in West Michigan / Relocation assistance

Job is located in Southwest Michigan / Relocation Assistance Provided!


Currently, we are seeking qualified candidates for a Program Manager opening to join our clients Global Business Solution organization.

As the Program Manager, you will be responsible for and lead the program responsible for the conversion or transition from Siebel CRM to SAP CRM for this Global Manufacturing Company.  In order to be considered, candidates must bring great experience in a large global enterprise leading complex programs and projects focused on Services to the customer or consumer (Call Center, Service Management, Warranty, Parts, etc).   

Day to Day (what a typical day or week look like in this role)

 

•       Support CIO and Business in ideation and conceptualization activities,      including: definition of strategic objectives, project charter, stakeholder analysis and business case

•       The Program Manager oversees various system projects of a highly complex nature

•       Drive consistent project delivery, including: definition of project plans, resource allocation, and management of project risks, scope, schedule, cost, and benefits

•       Manage working relationships with key stakeholders, including, business management, project sponsors, suppliers, and technology management

•       Oversee and manage execution of day-to-day project activities.  Build effective teams.  Drive performance and talent management, development, and mentoring

•       Direct activities of third party suppliers in support of projects within area of responsibility

What You Will Do

 Role-Specific Responsibilities

•       Support CIO and Business in the following pre-ideation tollgate activities:

o    Determine project objectives, initial project plan, schedule, and budget

o    Plan resources (initial / high-level)

o    Develop project business case

•       Direct and manage multi-disciplinary teams in the development of plans, goals, objectives, policies, and procedures for completion of a project

•       Adapt project specifications, plans, and approach to the different concerns and expectations of stakeholders

•       Balance the competing demands for quality, scope, schedule, and cost

•       Develop and maintain detailed project plans

•      Update monthly project forecast, maintain project budgets (starting in Conceptualization phase)

•      Review project proposals or plans to determine effort and schedule requirements and allotment of available resources to various phases of the project, arrange for assignment of project personnel from internal and external sources

•      Confer with project staff to outline work plan and to assign duties, responsibilities, and scope of authority

•      Direct and coordinate activities of project personnel to ensure the project progresses on schedule and budget

•      Review status reports prepared by project personnel and modify schedule or plan as required

•      Submit weekly project status report

•      Chair weekly project management meetings

•      Prepare project reports as required (e.g. Priority Council one-pagers)

•      Coordinate intra- and interdepartmental matters necessary to expedite projects

•      Facilitate and troubleshoot the solution of problems associated with designing,  implementing, leading, and completing projects

•      Support Business Analyst in identifying and managing requirements

•      Support  PMO, and others in developing or continuously improving policies and procedures to support project excellence and continuous improvement

•      Develop and prepare special customer surveys and information gathering tools; coordinate survey instruments; direct staff in conducting survey; report survey results; monitor survey success

•      Prepare and present clear and concise oral and written communications

 Business Partnership

•      Establish and maintain contacts with internal and external stakeholders, to provide information, garner acceptance and support of the project, and ensure that the project meets business needs

•      Serve as the principal point of contact for the business and the project team, responding to inquiries and providing information pertaining to the status of assigned projects

 People Management

•      Provide functional supervision over consultants and contractors, and supervise (dotted line) technical staff

•      Coordinate the selection of project staff; evaluate performance; and provide feedback to departmental managers regarding staff performance.

•      Assess training needs of project team members; develops, coordinates, and conducts specialized training as required

•      Establish and maintain effective working relationships

What You Need To Succeed

•       Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Computer Science, Information Systems or related discipline and equivalent work experience and training

•      Minimum of seven years experience with technology-related projects is required, including a minimum of 5 years of direct project management experience.

•      Solid understanding of project management methodologies and hands-on experience in managing all aspects of project delivery, PMI PMP certification desirable.

•      Work Experience managing geographically distributed and culturally diverse work-groups

•      Supervisory experience in the management of information systems

•      Experience in working with Outside Service Providers

•      Strong analytical, organizational and decision-making skills

•      Demonstrated strong verbal/written communication skills with the ability to communicate complex issues to multiple audiences

•      Process engineering and re-engineering techniques and practices

•      Customer Services Process knowledge is a plus

•      Complex Program Management experience with SAP CRM is a plus

•      Complex Program Management experience with WebSphere eCommerce is a plus

About the Job

Job Description:

Infinata, Inc. is currently looking for highly motivated, entrepreneurial full-time candidates who have a solid understanding of Oracle architecture with a strong knowledge in SQL Plus scripting.

 

The primary responsibility is to aid in the maintenance and enhancement of a data warehouse environment.  Duties include extraction, transformation, and loading of monthly files into a data warehouse.  Creating database users and assigning the proper security privileges.  Monitoring database/user activity and performance.  The candidate will work to automate processes and tune queries to ensure the database is running optimally. The candidate must be very SQL Plus savvy and have good experience working with Oracle utilities like Sql Loader and Data Pump.  Good communication with team members and willingness to learn outside of DBA duties a must.

 

Duties (Example):

 

  • Loading and processing of large datasets each month.
  • Tune large sets of queries to run faster and more efficiently.
  • Assist clients with ad hoc requests (query writing, exporting files)
  • Manage database users and security.
  • Monitor Oracle logs every month.
  • Run backups.
  • Respond to database emergency during off hours.

 

Compensation:

·        Highly competitive base with strong upside for top performers.

Qualifications:

  • 3+ years Oracle/SQL Plus experience
    • SQL Plus tuning.
    • Extensive knowledge in SQL Plus, PL/SQL, Triggers, Functions, Procedures.
    • Database tuning.
    • Log monitoring.
    • Data warehousing experience a plus.

 

Important Skills:

  • Good problem solving skills.
  • Strong communication skills via phone and in person with current and potential clients.
  • Entrepreneurial mindset and business driven.
  • Ability to see through the life cycle of a problem.
    • Understand the problem.
    • Evaluate and develop a plan.
    • Implementation.
    • Monitor and work with users to ensure the implementation is successful.
    • Automate and document the process.
  • Willingness to take initiative and manage self.
  • Ability to learn some system administration skills.
  • Ability to automate and document processes.

 

About Infinata:

 

Infinata is a rapidly growing, leading provider of on-line market research and prospecting solutions to the Investment Management, Biotech and Software industries.  Our web-based software provides timely and accurate information and analysis to executives within these dynamic and rapidly evolving industries.  Infinata is a wholly owned subsidiary of the Financial Times Group and Pearson LLC (NYSE: PSO)

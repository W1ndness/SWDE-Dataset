Systems Analyst

About the Job

Job Details:
- Bachelor's Degree preferred


HOSPITAL OVERVIEW:

Franciscan Hospital, a pediatric center for children with special needs that is a nationally and internationally recognized model, leader, and advocate of care, we promote the integrity and well being of children through our quality of care. This allows each child the opportunity to achieve the maximum potential in an atmosphere of respect, compassion and dignity.

POSITION SUMMARY:

Reporting to the IS Director. Works closely with the Medical and Clinical Staff to analyze issues, processes and data; acts as liaison between the Medical/Clinical Staff and the Information Systems department - providing general consulting, reporting needs and problem solving services. Coordinate aspects of planning, design, development, training, implementation, maintenance and evaluation of existing or new functionality related to the Clinical Information System. Provides operational support to end users in their use of information systems. Works on and assists with all assigned projects including the analysis, design, and implementation of projects in support of the information systems needs of hospital departments.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS SUMMARY:
  • In a timely and efficient ma nner responds to calls from users and resolves problems. Facilitates problems that require intervention with the software vendor, specifying the problem and acting as the contact on behalf of the hospital. Tracks problems through to resolution. Keeps IS Director informed of significant or unresolved problems.
  • Maintains expertise with designated applications and familiarity with all IS applications. Attends seminars provided by the software vendor to stay up-to-date on application functionality and enhancements and new applications.
  • Consults with users to identify current procedures and clarify program objectives. Formulates logical plan outlining steps to develop programs to solve users needs and provide them with the requested infor ma tion in the desired for ma t.

About the Job

Entry-Level Programmer/ Test Engineer

 

Company Description:

RTTS is the premier professional services organization that specializes in providing software quality for critical business applications. Based in New York City with an office in the Atlanta Metro area and serving the Fortune 500 since 1996, RTTS has worked with over 350 clients on hundreds of projects and forged strategic partnerships with IBM, Microsoft and Hewlett Packard, which provides us access to the most comprehensive enterprise software solutions in the industry. 

 

·                Entry-level positions available – an ideal fit for recent graduates and career changers!

·                Work on assignments at our list of Fortune 500 client sites and gain valuable exposure to many areas of business and technology.

·                The most comprehensive up front training program in the industry. 

·                eLearning courses and one-on-one mentoring provided.

·                Competitive salaries, excellent benefits, and emphasis on work-life balance.

·                Our work environment is challenging, supportive, educational and fun!  Our office culture naturally fosters innovation and professional growth. 

 

Job Description: 

Using your strong programming, analytical and interpersonal skills, you will be responsible for delivering software quality solutions to our Fortune 500 customers.  Assignments may last for a period of 1 week to 12 months and beyond, depending on our clients’ needs.   

 

Responsibilities include:

·                Perform job function at client site. 

·                Create automated scripts to test client software applications and technical architecture. 

·                Schedule project tasks and maintain timely delivery of service.

·                Design test cases outlining application work flow.

·                Execute manual test cases to verify functionality, usability and interoperability. 

·                Code custom solutions using cutting edge automated functional and performance testing tools.

·                Evaluate and track project results.

·                Analyze metrics and provide feedback.

 

Job Qualifications:

 Must possess an undergraduate, graduate level or certificate degree with a concentration in Computer Science, Information Technology or a related field. 

·                Proficient in a mainstream programming language such as Java, C++, C#, C, PHP, VB.net or other mainstream language.  (Minimum two semesters of study)

·                Professional appearance and exceptional communication skills.

·                Customer service experience a plus. 

·                You will also be required to travel to client sites in and around the metro area.  Travel outside of your office’s metro area is also possible but not the norm.  

·                Live within 90 miles of our Atlanta Office and have a valid driver’s license and access to car.     

·                Applicants for employment must be authorized to work in the U.S. without the need for sponsorship

 

Applicants must submit a cover letter with their resume to Monster-ATL-TE@rttsweb.com

 

Compensation:  $40-50K, depending on experience.  Benefits include:  Medical, dental, 401k, healthcare flexible spending accounts, dependent care flexible spending accounts, short and long term disability, life insurance, TransitCheks, bonus plan programs, tuition reimbursement and more. 

 

To learn more visit our website at www.rtts.com

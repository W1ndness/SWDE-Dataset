Unix/Linux Engineer - Cleveland, OH


About Key:

Cleveland-based KeyCorp is one of the nation's largest bank-based financial services companies, with assets of approximately $97 billion. Key companies provide investment management, retail and commercial banking, consumer finance, and investment banking products and services to individuals and companies throughout the United States and, for certain businesses, internationally. The company's businesses deliver their products and services through branches and offices; a network of 1,439 ATMs; telephone banking centers 1-800-KEY2YOU® (1-800-539-2968); and a website, Key.com®, that provides account access and financial products 24 hours a day.


About the Job:

KeyBank's UNIX Server Management team is looking for a dynamic individual to join our fast-paced and challenging group. We are seeking people with strong collaborative and partnering skills to work with our customers to manage and maintain mission-critical systems for the Bank. We interact with a broad and deep range of technologies and candidates should be upbeat, energetic, and have a diverse technical background.


Essential Job Functions:

  • Architect, engineer and support KeyBank¿s distributed server platforms.
  • Environment administration (building, patching, securing, maintaining and troubleshooting servers issues, operating systems and infrastructure applications)
  • Project lead role on large size projects
  • Ability to work independently as well as to lead/guide small groups
  • Enterprise product evaluation and integration responsibilities
  • Development and enforcement of internal standards
  • Documentation of systems implemented
  • Auditing and adhereance to Sarbanes Oxley, GLBA, PCI and other Compliance/Regulatory requirements
  • Interface with other teams (internal and vendor) to maintain and support the environment.
  • Lead cross-disipline discussions and research on an Enterprise scale.
  • An in-depth understanding of distributed operating systems (Solaris, AIX, Linux, Windows, VMWare/virtualization technologies) with expert-level proficiency in at least one distirbution of UNIX/Linux.
  • Ensure consitency of server configurations across a large environment with an eye to alerting/monitring and log review techniques to assist in performance, capatcity and operational analysis.


Required Qualifications:

  • 5+ years minimum experience in an enterprise computing environment
  • Understanding of middleware technologies and computing concepts
  • Expert understanding of UNIX commands from multiple distributions
  • Good knowledge of at least one other technology field (network, distributed, systems management, application design)
  • 4 year degree or equivalent experience
  • Excellent communication and documentation skills (verbal and written)
  • Hands-on experience with the following:
  • RedHat Linux 4&5, Solaris 9&10, Novell SuSE/SLES 9 or greater, IBM AIX 5.x or greater; HP hardware/components; Sun Enterprise Servers, VMWare or related virtualization experience, IBM Power hardware/components; Systems Management Tools: Monitoring Tools, UNIX printing, Capacity Management tools, Server backup and storage.
  • Shell scripting, bash, php, python, LAMP, LDAP

Information Assurance Expert – US Citizenship Required

About the Job

Since 1994, The Squires Group, Inc. (TSGi) has been dedicated to serving the Mid-Atlantic region with expert ERP and IT staffing solutions. Our clients have continued to turn to us for our ability to attract expert local ERP and IT consultants–and to rapidly assemble regionally-based project teams with proven track records.

 

Join one of the hottest Oracle Federal projects in the DC Metro region. The Squires Group is looking for an experienced Information Assurance Expert for an upcoming long-term Federal project in Arlington, VA. US Citizenship is required.

 

Job Requirements include:

 

·         Certification and Accreditation (C&A) in accordance with DODI 8510.01, DoD Information Assurance Certification and Accreditation Process (DIACAP)

·         Experience in Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA)

·         Experience with Ports, Protocols, and Services Management

·         DoD Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)

·         Experience in Information Assurance Vulnerability Management (IAVM)

·         Understanding of Vulnerability Management System (VMS)

·         Experience Security Technical Implementation Guides (STIGs)

·         Understanding of Personal Identifying Information (PII)

·         Experience in Risk Assessments

·         Knowledge of Access Control

·         Understanding of Audit Log Review

·         Experience with Oracle Encryption Methodology    

·         Technical writing and presentation skills

·         Working collaboratively with partner Agencies

·         Understanding of the Global Information Grid (GIG)

·         Resources must be a Certified Information System Security Professional (CISSP)

·         Have an active Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) with IT Level 1 designation

·         U.S. Citizenship required

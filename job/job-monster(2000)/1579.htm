Internet Research Software Engineer

About the Job

Competitive salaries? Check. Comprehensive benefit package? Yes. Work/Life balance? Absolutely. Compelling technology? At your fingertips. Your job search stops here….

LGS is the successor to the former Lucent and Alcatel Government Solutions business units. Beginning operations on January 1, 2007, LGS is an independent and wholly-owned subsidiary of Alcatel-Lucent's North American operations. Alcatel-Lucent is the leading provider of telecommunications & networking products and services worldwide.

The Internet Research Department performs basic and applied research and prototype development in various aspects of Internet and general network security.  Most of the work requires security clearance, and is very customer focused. As this new hire you will research and design for classified programs.

Research, design, and software development for classified programs.  Hacker mentality with Computer Science training.  Able to understand advanced computer science/math  algorithms (e.g. machine learning, Bayesian networks, etc.) and apply them to solving practical computer security problems.  Extensive experience with object-oriented programming (e.g. C++/Java), familiarity with additional high-level programming languages, and broad knowledge of IP networking required.  Knowledge and experience with the latest open source computer security and penetration testing tools preferred.  Knowledge of kernel programming, software testing, and Web 2.0 security a plus.  MS/PhD in comp sci

 

The ability to obtain and hold a U. S. Government Security clearance is required.  A current TS/SCI is preferred. 

 

LGS Innovations is an Equal Opportunity Employer

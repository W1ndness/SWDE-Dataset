About the Job

Senior Project Manager

 

 

We are seeking a Senior Project Manager who will create and deliver the solutions that drive client satisfaction. You can grow professionally and personally in an environment where intelligence, innovation, and leadership are valued and rewarded. Our client holds true to two essential principles: the client comes first, and people make the difference. Our client invites you to join them in building on those principles to lead their clients and their company to success.

 

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Responsible for all aspects of the development and implementation of assigned projects and provides a single point of contact for those projects. 
  • Take projects from original concept through final implementation working with internal Product Manager and external customers. 
  • Interfaces with all areas affected by the project including end user, computer services and client services. 
  • Define system’s scope and project’s objectives. 
  • Develops detailed work plans, schedules, project estimates, resources plans and status report. 
  • Conducts project meetings and is responsible for project tracking and analysis. 
  • Ensures adherence to quality standards and reviews project deliverables. 
  • Recommends and takes action to direct the analysis and solutions of problems.
  • Plan and supervise activities for multiple, large-scale projects.
  • Develop and manage project expectations ensuring all applicable functional areas are engaged on the project
  • Initiate and lead project planning activities such as Scope Definition and Management (WBS, Project Change Control, and Scope Document), Risk Management, Communication Management, etc.
  • Create and maintain a project schedule for each project by identifying and monitoring: overall project timeline, project milestones, tasks, resources, ETAs, task dependencies, and critical paths.
  • Control and track project performance, time allocations, cost, and quality by constantly evaluating project progress and conducting regular team status meetings.
  • Coordinate with other project managers and organizational entities to identify and monitor issues, impacts and dependencies.
  • Coordinate with other line of business managers for integration and continued product advancement. 
  • Coordinate meetings, as needed, to initiate working sessions, resolve project issues, etc.
  • Promote continuous improvement by conducting, documenting, and communicating lessons learned throughout the project.
  • Provide status on progress of projects
  • Prepare and conduct informal and formal presentations
  • Mentor other project managers on PM Core Competencies
  • Influence departmental procedures and objectives
  • Display strong understanding of the organization's business strategies and apply knowledge to all project activities.
  • Build relationships with all functional areas and manage stakeholder expectations
  • Ability to author comprehensive scope and solutions documents with input from technical resources.

 

 

REQUIREMENTS

  • 5+ years of professional experience in a project management and/or consulting role.
  • Typically requires five to seven years of professional experience in a project management and/or consulting role.
  • Bachelor's Degree or equivalent Project Management work experience
  • PMI Certification is a plus
  • Experience with Six Sigma; Green Belt Certification a plus.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office applications
  • Proficient with Project Management Tools including MS Project
  • Experience working in the Information Technology and/or Financial industry (Financial Industry experience is a strong plus)
  • Ability to build relationships with and influence other functional areas
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Well-developed negotiation skills
  • Ability to build consensus
  • Ability to work in a matrix environment
  • Experience managing cross-functional projects.
  • Detail and big picture orientation
  • Organization and time management skills: Operates independently with minimal guidance by management
  • Problem-solving skills with ability to perform root cause analysis
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Knowledge of and demonstrated experience in project management processes and resultant methodologies.
  • Knowledge of and demonstrated experience in the development project lifecycle (e.g., design, development, testing, implementation, etc.)
  • Extensive experience in meeting planning, facilitation, and public speaking
  • Occasional travel might be required (less than 20%)


If you are interested and meet the requirements above, please email your resume along with a brief summary indicating how your experience and skills specifically qualify for this position to Susan Lind-Kanne, Susan@CompetitiveEdgeRecruiting.com . Only qualified candidates will be contacted.

Principles only, please.

Sr. Web PHP/DRUPAL & TECHNICAL LEADS

About the Job


Senior PHP\Drupal Tech Lead (Full-time & contracting opportunities)

Our growing Content Solutions Practice which caters to a growing list of clients such as Rodale, Pearson & HFMU.S. publishing, Martha Stewart, BET.COM, NYU, Time Inc., ZogSports, Equinox, CHOP (Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia) and Lifetime requires us to hire “top shelf” candidates for our NYC office.

At NorthPoint, we lead with experience.  Over the years, we have made a concerted effort to attract the best in the business to build our team.  We take great pride in the caliber of people who work here and the collective track record and expertise they bring to the table. 

Our goal is to help any organization that authors, manages, and delivers content of any type, for websites, knowledge management, intranets, and other large enterprise content initiatives.  We are able to leverage deep domain expertise and industry knowledge to help organizations successfully operate and grow their online business.

Requirements

All candidates must possess a strong foundation in computer science and have demonstrated a proven track record of sound code design, reuse, and maintenance. Ideal candidates possess end-to-end knowledge of the content management and web application space including performance and scalability design and tuning, data migration, SEO strategy execution, CDN implementation, metrics and third party web services integration, and general knowledge of open source products and frameworks.  In addition, the successful candidate will have expertise in general system administration in both Windows and Unix/Linux environments, database administration and tuning, and web server configuration.  The candidate will need to have the following:

Skills:

·         Object Oriented developer with PHP 5.x, Drupal 6.x/7 database and distributed computing experience

·         Full Life Cycle experience with Project Leader, Analyst, Architect, Technical Lead skills is a plus

·         Working Knowledge of XHTML, CSS, Content Management applications

·         Understanding and knowledge of web development and content solutions

·         Experience within the Media or Publishing industry is a plus

·         Experience with CDN’s and caching strategies is a plus

·         Experience administrating Linux, Apache or MySQL is a plus

·         Experience deploying web infrastructures is a plus

 

Cloud computing and storage experience is a plus. Experience with Java frameworks such as Struts, Spring, Hibernate, and Lucene are a plus.  Experience with Memcached, Google Search Appliance, MySQL clustering a plus. Python and Ruby are a strong plus. PHP and other scripting languages a plus.

Opportunities:

·         The candidate will work closely clients and team members to develop dynamic, interactive websites that drive revenue for our clients. 

·         Using a broad array of web technologies, candidates must appreciate both the User Experience and Technology aspects of a successful online operation and work with clients to define their requirements and shape their vision for development.

·         NorthPoint projects are delivered with 3-8 person teams so you are not just a lead, you are an active developer on each project.

·         These are full life cycle projects that include Requirements Gathering, Design/Architecture, Development, Deployment of business tools.

Competitive Pay and Benefits Include: 

Annual compensation, Medical, Dental, Vision, Long and Short Term Disability, 401K Plan with Company Match, Flexible Spending Account, Public Transportation Incentives, Company Paid Training Programs

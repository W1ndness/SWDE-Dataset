About the Company

-------------------------

Redmon Group is an industry-leading Web and multimedia development company located in the heart of Old Town Alexandria, Virginia. Our mission is to enable, train, and entertain. We provide custom design and development services in the areas of interactive media, e-Business, and e-Learning, and pride ourselves on our technical prowess, innovative designs, and cohesive teamwork. Since our founding in 1990, we have worked with over 100 distinguished corporate, public sector, international, and educational clients including: Arlington County (Virginia), Berlitz, Challenger Center, Discovery Channel, Smithsonian Institution, U.S. Senate, and the Virginia Department of Transportation. More information about the company can be found at www.redmon.com .

 

 

About the Job

-----------------

As a ColdFusion Web Developer at Redmon Group you can expect to find yourself working simultaneously for multiple project managers on a variety of projects.  Your specific tasks will depend both on your skill set and the needs of the projects but will include designing and building Web pages and Web applications, maintaining and improving Web sites, performing user acceptance testing, and more.  You will follow specifications laid out in design documents, scripts, storyboards, and/or graphic treatments and will work closely with others including Project Managers, Instructional Designers, Multimedia Developers, and other Programmers.

 

In this position, it is expected that you will carry out project and company duties as assigned by the Management Team; follow timelines and schedules; perform work within defined scopes and allocated hours; learn new applications or software as needed; and be an active and productive team member who helps maintain a focus on innovation, quality, and delivering client solutions that work.  Types of projects could include:  e-learning, Web sites, digital signage, kiosks, DVDs, and handheld applications.

 

This is a permanent, full-time (average of 43 hours per week) position with great benefits, a competitive salary, and many opportunities for growth.

 

 

Job Requirements

-----------------------

To thrive at Redmon Group, you must have excellent verbal and written communication skills, an ability to multi-task across projects and technologies, strong collaboration and teamwork principles, a comprehensive grasp of technology and/or design techniques, an aptitude for learning new applications and skills, and a passion for producing engaging and high-quality work.

 

A Web Developer must be competent (able to independently employ the principal features of applications or skills to produce a professional-level output) in the following areas:

 

• ColdFusion (preferably version 8 or higher)

• SQL Database Query Development

• Web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and XML

• Microsoft Office Suite of Tools

• Task Estimation

• Client Communications

• At minimum of 4 years practical experience in web development (a 4-year degree is considered equivalent to 2 years of experience)

 

Skills considered a "plus" include:

• E-Commerce Experience

• Content Management Systems Experience

• Google Maps or GIS Integration

• Experience in one or more of: PHP, .NET, C#, C++, XML, Python, Perl, Linux/Unix-specific knowledge

• Microsoft Internet Information Services (IIS)

• Apache web server administration

• SQL database administration

• Object-Oriented Programming Experience

• Knowledge of W3C accessibility standards and Section 508

• Skills with media development tools (Photoshop, Fireworks, 3d Studio, Final Cut, Illustrator, etc.)

 

 

How to Apply

Qualified and enthusiastic candidates are encouraged to send a resume to jobs@redmon.com  Please include a brief cover note explaining why you’d be a good fit for Redmon Group and for the position.

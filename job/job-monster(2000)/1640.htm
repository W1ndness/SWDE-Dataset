About the Job

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 

TextWise, a leading provider of semantic technology, is seeking a creative and energetic Principal Engineer to help implement innovative systems for understanding, organizing, and locating information.

The right person will be a seasoned developer with excellent skills in Java, Linux, Lucene, Relational Databases, product design, and product development. Experience in the development of Highly Scalable Web applications is paramount.

Requirements

  • MS in Computer Science or related discipline, or equivalent experience, with 8-12 years of experience designing and developing software.
  • Proven experience implementing search, transaction processing, machine learning, or data mining applications.
  • Experience developing and deploying software on RedHat Enterprise Linux, CentOS, or Ubuntu.
  • Knowledge of XML, Unicode and large scale text processing techniques.
  • Proficiency in object-oriented design and programming in Java.
  • Experience developing SQL databases, such as MySQL.

 

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with Amazon EC2, Solr, Hadoop/MapReduce and distributed computing.
  • Experience developing software using Eclipse, Maven, and Spring.
  • Web front-end development using HTML, AJAX, and JavaScript and application frameworks such as Spring MVC.
  • Experience with performance evaluation, benchmarking, testing of web applications.
  • Knowledge of a breath of languages such as C, C++, Perl, Python and others.

 

Company Description

TextWise developed the first scalable, automated, semantic similarity search technology enabling the web to move from matching keywords to a meaning-based foundation. Indexing content with TextWise semantic descriptions enables any application to provide highly relevant matching, concept tagging and categorization.

TextWise holds a significant patent portfolio in extraction, search, categorization and classification using both NLP and statistics. TextWise is headquartered in Rochester, New York, has 21 employees and is VC funded.

 

Additional Information

  • No third party applications
  • Applicants for employment must possess a work authorization that does not require sponsorship by the employer for a visa
  • North American residents are preferred for this telecommuting opportunity

About the Job

 

SAS Programmer

Washington, DC

 

IHS (NYSE: IHS) is a leading global source of critical information and insight dedicated to providing the most complete and trusted data and expertise. IHS product and service solutions span four areas of information that encompass the most important concerns facing global business today: Energy, Product Lifecycle, Security and Environment. By focusing on customers first, IHS enables innovative and successful decision-making for customers ranging from governments and multinational companies to smaller companies and technical professionals in more than 180 countries. IHS has been in business since 1959 and employs approximately 3,800 people in more than 20 countries.

 

Summary:

The Consultant position is based in Washington, DC and requires travel to Baltimore, MD 3-5 days per week to work on site with our client for the first year. The consultant will both be programming in SAS Enterprise Guide as well as leading a team doing the same. You must be able to envision the project scope and develop a plan for allocating work among the team members.

 

The ideal candidates will have 2-4 years of experience in a consulting environment and ready to take on the challenge of leading a 4-6 person team, work directly with the client, and work with the project manager to ensure the timely completion of deliverables.

 

Qualifications:

  • SAS Enterprise Guide Team Leader: Consultant (2-4 years experience)

 

Requirements:

  • 2 years experience with SAS
  • 1-2 years experience with Enterprise Guide
  • Bachelors, Master's  or PhD degree in an analytical discipline (economics, statistics, science, engineering,  operations research, etc)
  • Strong technical skills in database environments running on Windows.
  • Experience with troubleshooting errors in a BI environment
  • Excellent communication skills (both oral and written) and strong analytical skills are essential
  • Ability to work collaboratively and effectively with peers, business line management and staff, and end users
  • Must be a self starter and be able to work productively without requiring a great deal of supervision
  • Preferred experience working with a consulting firm

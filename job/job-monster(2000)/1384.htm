We are currently recruiting for a Solution Architect- Infristructure to work in our Norcross, GA. Facility.



Solution Architect (Technical/Infrastructure Focus )

Enterprise Technology group at Fiserv has expanded it’s value proposition by creating Solutions Architecture team, which is FET’s arm to lead the end-to-end solution strategies & definition for large revenue generation initiatives, and for the IT due-diligence on M&A opportunities, driven by Fiserv Corporate and/or other Business Units(BUs).


Solution Architect (Technical/Infrastructure focus) is a vital role on Solutions Architecture team and will be responsible for conceptualizing and defining an end-to-end infrastructure solution architecture aligned with the overall Solution Architecture defined for the opportunity. This role will also be responsible for evaluating & recommending alternatives for meeting the short-term and the long-term needs of business.


The overall solution may comprise of multiple products and services from Fiserv BUs and 3

rd parties. The Solution Architect will guide the BU IT/data center teams on methodologies, processes and best practices for identifying technical architecture foundation (primarily all of infrastructure and connectivity) of the proposed overall solution. This individual will also work as needed with the involved vendors, partners and other 3rd parties to assess their technologies and/or do POC’s.

Responsibilities

The Solution Architect (Technical/Infrastructure Focus) will be responsible for driving the definition and validation of the infrastructure footprint, interfaces, and connectivity methods for a cost-effective, scalable and robust technical architecture foundation to support the proposed overall Solution Architecture. This individual will be required to:

?

Partner with IT/data center teams of Corporate, Business Units, FET and 3rd parties/partners to lead and facilitate the definition of the technical architecture foundation of the solution

?

Work with other Solution Architects (e.g. Application, Process, Data, Security) in the team and/or in BU teams, to understand the non-functional technical, security, and operational requirements

?

Map technical/infrastructure foot prints – for short-term and long-term solution

?

Articulate and document the technical solution architecture, including a data comm/network connectivity schematic, and ‘own’ the technical solution

?

Identify key risks on the technical architecture options, and work with involved teams for mitigation strategy and plans

?

Create technical architectures that are leverage-able for future re-use

?

Provide inputs and assistance, if needed, to FET financial analysts and Solution Engg team for cost and effort estimates for the opportunity

?

Define and maintain Checklist and Metrics for Technical Architecture Definition and Validation

?

Understand the IT Governance process and standards in the company, and ensure that the suggested technical footprints and connectivity methods are in compliance. Remain available as the consultant to all the implementation project teams during the full course of the initiative – for Q&A on defined Solution Architecture, for review/approval

Skills & experience requirements:

Solution Architect (Technical/Infrastructure focus) should have over 12 years of experience in defining enterprise and system architectures. This individual should be able to think outside of the box to identify or define elegant technical solutions to improve productivity and bring simplicity to the solution for complex business problems.


Skills and experience requirements include:


Subject-Matter-Expertise on Technical Architecture definition process, methodologies and tools


Ability to analyze solution requirements and define technical/infrastructure footprint to address complex business and technology needs.


Fairly good knowledge of data centers, inter-connectivity, and infrastructure standards at Fiserv or at an organization of similar size outside of Fiserv


Fairly good understanding of IT Operational processes & post-production support


Knowledge of ITIL process framework


Excellent communications, consulting, and client relationship skills. He/she must be able to clearly communicate complex technical and business concepts, both to clients (internal or external) and to team members, and to negotiate change


Ability to interface at various levels of Customer(BU) organization, and if needed also of the Client organization, to present technical footprint options and budget needs, and guide them to the recommended option based on industry best practices, ROI and TCO aspects


Deep commitment and ability to be very responsive to the Client’s (and BU’s) business needs, constraints, and tight timelines (e.g. for an RFP), while also managing their expectations for realistic cost and timeliness for the technical architecture work


Ability to balance between the ideal technical solution and the business/ customer needs – short-term and long-term


Ability to work in a fast paced environment with the ability to adapt and change priorities as the business needs change

More on Technical skills:

Extensive understanding and experience in technology areas for Mainframe and Distributed platforms, networking, databases, HA/DR, security, PCI compliance, and other IT regulations


Experience with application architectures and standards (SOA, N-tier,.NET, Web 2.0, SDLC, RUP, J2EE, etc.)


Extensive understanding and experience with a variety of infrastructure technologies:

About the Job

Fenwal, Inc. is a leading provider of products and services to the blood industry from the collection, separation and storage of blood and blood components to our new technologies for improving the safety and availability of the blood supply.  Fenwal enjoys revenues of over $550M and has approximately 4000 employees, including five manufacturing sites throughout the Caribbean, and Europe.

 

We currently have an opportunity for a SharePoint Developer to support and enhance Fenwal’s SharePoint applications. This is a great opportunity for someone willing to continue learning and developing in the SharePoint 2007 environment.

 

This is a full-time, direct hire opportunity that will be located in our Lake Zurich, IL Corporate office.

 

Responsibilities include:

  • Developing ASP.NET user controls and assemblies for use within the SharePoint 2007 environment.
  • Designing custom templates and workflows using Designer.
  • Working with users to troubleshoot problems.
  • Developing solutions both in and out of the SharePoint environment.
  • Assisting with development of functional and detail design specifications.
  • Assisting with support of production systems.
  • Developing business or functional knowledge by responding to and resolving user questions, problems and inquiries.
  • Working with the business or function to assist in the discussion of proposed solutions and define user requirements.
  • Training users in normal SharePoint tasks, as well as custom applications.

 

Required Skills:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • 3 - 5 years of programming experience; at least 1 year of MOSS experience
  • Visual Studio 2005 or 2008
  • Strong ASP.NET and C# programming skills
  • SharePoint 2007 user interface experience
  • SharePoint (MOSS) API, programming against:

ü  Lists and Content types

ü  Document Libraries

ü  Web Parts and AJAX Web Parts

  • Use of SharePoint:

ü  Forms Based Authentication (FBA)

ü  Features

ü  Master Pages, Content Pages and Page Layouts

ü  Solutions and Packages a plus

ü  Workflows, Visual Studio and Designer

ü  Data View Web Parts (Designer)

  • Prefer experience in:

ü  SQL Server 2005

ü  SharePoint 2007 administration

ü  InfoPath 2007

ü  IIS 6.0

  • Windows Server 2008, Active Directory and SQL Server 2008 administration a plus
  • 3rd party .NET developer tools such as Telerik a plus
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to multitask in a deadline oriented environment
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Candidates must be eligible to work in the US without sponsorship.

Fenwal, Inc. offers an excellent salary and benefits package including immediate             medical, dental and vision coverage, as well as life insurance, disability, 401K with company match and profit sharing.

Fenwal is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We encourage and support a diverse workforce.

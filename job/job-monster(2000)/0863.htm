IT System Administrator

About the Job

Company Overview:

 

PhaseOne is a rapidly growing research based consultancy specializing in communications' ability to influence attitudes and motivate behaviors.  Our mission is to help some of the world’s most respected organizations gain insights into the drivers of a broad range of communications programs designed to influence behaviors.  PhaseOne’s mission is to understand what makes communications successful and apply that knowledge in helping clients' improve bottom line results by making smarter communications decisions. 

 

PhaseOne’s on-going research program is designed to take a multidisciplinary approach to communication and to continuously identify the various dynamics that impact success.  It is the application of this learning that allows PhaseOne to consistently, objectively and accurately contribute to our client’s success by ensuring their communications are strong and strategically focused across all media.

 

Our clients are interested in gaining a more thorough understanding of various target audiences in order to enhance the success of their communications.  As a researcher, you will use a wide variety of sources to conduct secondary research and assist in the development of audience profiles and market dynamics.


Department Overview:

 

The IT Department at PhaseOne exists to support the organization’s technology needs and promote the effective use of hardware, software and telephone systems.

 

Position Summary:

 

The IT System Administrator is responsible for ensuring that the installation, configuration, maintenance and day-to-day performance of the Company’s network system and resources are available to the Company’s approved users.  This individual ensures that all system hardware, software, operating systems, analysis systems, and VOIP used for processing Company sensitive data adheres to the Company’s, and its clients, high standards and values while enabling users to have the data confidentiality, availability and integrity necessary to do their jobs.

 

Responsibilities:

 

  • Using strong network / system administrator skills: Apply weekly antivirus security update to all clients and servers, apply OS and application patches and upgrades as needed with priority to security patches, maintain a network diagram, troubleshoot and repair any hardware or software downtime and contribute to and maintain security configurations and procedures
  • Provide quick and efficient problem resolution and timely password resets
  • Diagnose hardware and software issues as they arise and find cost effective solutions
  • Gather data pertaining to user’s needs and requests and use this information to identify, predict, interpret, and evaluate future or potential system network requirements
  • Install, configure, and support the Company’s LAN, WAN, and internet connectivity
  • Assist with jumpstarting the Company’s exposure to SharePoint to achieve business objectives
  • Provide technical and administrative support for a Intranet environment
  • Analyze equipment performance to determine repair or replacement timeframes
  • Support video teleconferencing needs in house
  • Create user accounts and train new users on appropriate computer system usage
  • Issue user accounts, with appropriate access, designated by management and coordinate appropriate briefings with security
  • Modify, Terminate or Suspend accounts as designated by management, ensuring data ownership rights is transferred
  • As approved by IT management, coordinate with vendors for maintenance and repairs
  • Advise ISSM/ISSO on anomalies in network
  • Validate that the addition of any updates, new software or hardware to the company network will not affect secure configuration or daily operations
  • Perform audit log reviews of print hardcopy records against automated records for disparity report to ISSM/ISSO
  • Conduct and Provide weekly audit report to ISSM/ISSO on all users
  • Act as Alternate ISSM in ISSM’s absence


Qualifications:

 

  • Solid network/system administrator skills
  • Skilled helpdesk/system administrator with a focus on security
  • Must be CISSP certified or working toward a CISSP certification
  • 4-6 years system administrator experience
  • Bachelor (4 year) degree with a technical major in Engineering or Computer Science/Information Technology preferred
  • Able to effectively manage reporting relationships and interact with all stakeholders in alignment with PhaseOne 3rd Dimension principles
  • Must be a U.S. Citizen with Security Clearance


Certifications Desired:

 

Microsoft, Linux, Cisco, COMSEC, CISM, GIAC, Secure++ desired


Direct Reports:

 

None


Travel:

 

Job may require some (infrequent) evenings or weekends work with little advanced notice due to the nature of the IT System Administrator’s job.  Willingness and ability to travel within Continental U.S. not more than once a month, with stays of less than 5 days.

IT Systems Engineer

About the Job

Plateau Systems is the industry’s premier provider of enterprise-class SaaS talent management suites designed to facilitate strategic workforce initiatives around learning, performance, compensation and career and succession management. Several of the world's most successful global organizations and government agencies including General Electric, the U.S. Air Force and Capital One Services and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) are using Plateau’s award-winning talent management solutions to power strategic talent management initiatives across their enterprises. Plateau consistently earns top industry awards and accolades for consistently delivering superior customer satisfaction and best-in-class technology. 

Plateau is seeking a hands-on IT Systems Engineer with the IT Services Organization. This candidate is expected to be a highly skilled IT generalist who will architect, build and operate systems for mission critical corporate IT services including SharePoint, Exchange, Microsoft SQL, and WebSense, as well as development and test environments for the internal organizations we support. The candidate should be fluent in configuring and operating Windows Server 200X and Active Directory. 

Job Duties include:

·         Monitoring and optimizing the performance of production servers

·         Work with systems administrators and database administrators within the company to design, build and maintain their infrastructure

·         Recovery of the systems in the event of a hardware or software failure

·         Troubleshooting and correcting network, system and  application problems

·         Monitoring and reporting on all servers (may include memory, disk space, log file analysis, cleanup, etc)

·         Maintaining system security policy, including firewalls, host and client access, file permissions, user accounts, etc.

·         Recommend and implement hardware and software solutions to support application delivery, backup/recovery, disaster recovery, business continuity, and storage/SAN architecture

 

Required Qualifications:

 

·         7 years system administration and/or network engineer

·         4 years experience with firewalls, load balancers, switches, etc.

·         4 years hands-on experience with Dell and HP enterprise servers

·         3 years experience with SAN, NAS and NFS

·         3 years experience with Exchange, SharePoint and MS SQL

·         Exchange 2010, SharePoint 2010, and MS SQL

·         1 or more years with server virtualization and VMware ESX

·         1 year with VoIP telephony

·         Thorough familiarity with DNS, DHCP, SNMP, and the other elements of TCP/IP networking

·         Skill at performance analysis and tuning

·         Strong problem solving skills

·         Exposure to MS SQL and Oracle 10g/11g databases a plus

·         Must work effectively with a minimum of oversight

·         Willingness and ability to work in a fast-paced environment and to handle fire-call duties

·         Build and manage Windows and Linux servers experience, preferred

 

·         Must be able to obtain a US security clearance

 

 

Plateau is an Equal Opportunity Employer.  We offer a competitive compensation package that includes paid time off (PTO) and paid public holidays, generous health/life/disability insurance, stock options plan, retirement plan, and an annual employee profit sharing/bonus plans among other benefits—as well as a casual work environment and the opportunity to work among the industry’s best in one of America’s fastest growing companies

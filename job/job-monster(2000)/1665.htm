Systems Analyst - Interfaces

About the Job

Sheridan Healthcorp, Inc., one of the most experienced & successful Healthcare Management companies in Florida, has an opportunity available for a Systems Analyst (Interfaces) in our corporate offices. We are centrally located in the Sawgrass Technology Park, just minutes from the Sawgrass Mills Mall in Sunrise.

If you are looking for a stable, fast paced & growing Company with more than 55 years in the industry that is committed to innovation, excellence & integrity, then this may be the next step in the advancement of your career.

The Systems Analyst (Interfaces) will maintain & support HL7 & other interfaces between clinical EHR applications & other business applications deployed across the Sheridan Enterprise.

Responsibilities will include:
  • Rolling out HL7 & other interfaces between clinical systems to end users
  • Maintaining working knowledge of current Medical Information System types, their differences & common elements including HIS, RIS, PACS, EMR, PMS, billing systems, coding engines, etc.
  • Consulting with users to identify current operating procedures & clarify program objectives
  • Writing documentation to describe program development, logic, process flows, & specifications; and user manuals to describe installation & operating procedures
  • Managing user requests according to business needs & priorities
  • Developing custom reports & monitoring performance of applications as necessary
  • Troubleshooting application modules in response to user issues or requests, providing help desk support, responding to issues reported by users or support staff, & resolving these problems with application vendor or other technical support as necessary & appropriate
  • Training end users to operate new or modified programs & working with them during User Acceptance Testing
  • On-call availability for after hours & working overtime to meet project deadlines as required
  • Some travel for off-site software installation & support may also be required

Successful candidates must have 3+ years of demonstrated expertise in the following to be considered:
  • Working experience with the following HL7 messages: ADT (admit/discharge/transfer), ORM (order), ORU (observation result), and DFT (detailed financial transaction); Also an understanding of the principles of asynchronous information exchange interfaces
  • Ability to break down & disseminate the different entities (& objects) involved in an HL7 data exchange (& a regular medical environment) including event types, patients, physicians, facilities, modalities, types of procedures, outcomes, financial exchanges, etc.
  • Familiarity with hosting environments (clinical EHR systems & other business applications) including data structures & hardware environments, & ability to use this knowledge to map fields to published HL7 standards
  • Experience analyzing & optimizing already deployed interfaces between clinical EHR applications & other business applications
  • Knowledge of network infrastructure, security and authentication processes
  • Working experience with all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
  • Prior experience interviewing end-users for insight into functionality, interface, problems, &/or usability issues
  • Very strong written, oral, & interpersonal communication skills, with demonstrated proficiency interacting with all levels of staff from IT administrators to Directors and Department Chiefs
  • Ability to conduct research into interface-related issues & products
  • Ability to effectively prioritize & execute tasks in a high-pressure environment
  • Experience working both independently & in a team-oriented, collaborative environment
  • Experience with a 3rd party tool/engine (Corepoint Health, Interfaceware, CloverLeaf, Orion Health Rhapsody, etc.) & XML is preferred

Education/Experience:
  • Associate’s Degree & 5+ years of related experience; or
  • Bachelor's Degree (BA/BS) & 3+ years of related experience as a System Analyst for clinical applications; or equivalent combination of education & experience

 

We offer a highly competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package. For immediate consideration, please apply online at http://www.sheridanhealthcare.com /. Join our team of experts! EOE

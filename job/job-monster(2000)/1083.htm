Implementation Business Consultant

About the Job

Our client located in Jacksonville, FL is seeking an Implementation Business Consultant for a contract to hire need.

Operate as liaison during implementation of new product lines and vendor integrations for existing Invoice Management clients.


-     Document and report on all issues identified during implementation. Document all correspondence, including conference calls, and coordinate with client and internal team members to keep status updated and to ensure tasks are completed timely. Track and report the status of all open issues through the completion of the project.
-    Write procedures and test scripts as necessary.
-    Provide general support to ensure completion of required tasks for client integration.
-    Assist in providing scope and direction to business development initiatives.
-    Perform other duties as assigned.

Four year college degree or equivalent combination of applicable experience and education.
Minimum of three years default-servicing experience including supervisory; preferably in a Foreclosure, Bankruptcy or REO department.
Experience working in a business analyst role would be considered a plus.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
Strong understanding of client systems and industry directives.
Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Project, Visio).
Recognized leadership ability.
Willingness to work as a team player.
Ability to multi-task and operate in a fast paced environment with focus on problem resolution.
Travel to client sites as necessary (25%-30%).

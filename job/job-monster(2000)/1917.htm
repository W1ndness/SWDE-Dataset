Senior Software Billing Business Analyst (P&C experience)

About the Job

Company: OneShield

OneShield is a leading provider of technology solutions designed to automate the sales and service of insurance products for insurance carriers. Our flagship product, OneShield Dragon®, is a tools-based, data-driven insurance underwriting, policy administration, rating and workflow engine. We are seeking to hire candidates with Insurance and Technology experience.

If you are ready for a new challenging career and have Property and Casualty insurance technology experience– Then we want to hear from you.

OneShield is located in Westborough, MA and Glastonbury, CT.   Please visit us on the web at www.oneshield.com. We provide a flexible work environment, excellent benefits and competitive compensation packages.

 

Job Description:  Senior Billing Business Analyst

This Insurance business analysts serve as the bridge between the information systems department and other departments in the company and are well versed in insurance industry standards, policies, trends and news. The analyst must remain cognizant of the changes and trends in the insurance industry and the impact of these changes on the daily activities of the departments supported and on the company.

The business analyst's prior experience often includes roles in operations areas and technical knowledge gained through formal or on-the-job training. Because of this experience, insurance business analysts move easily from operations units to information systems areas. Having an intimate knowledge of not only the department the analyst supports but also the insurance industry as a whole makes the analyst more able to research and to negotiate system changes that make sense for the supported department. The insurance business analyst position encompasses other roles in the company, including financial and trend analysis, training, reporting and project management functions.

OneShield is seeking a dynamic individual with a broad skill set in business analysis on major insurance software implementations. This individual will play a key role as Senior Business Analyst in bridging the gap between clients and implementation team members to ensure high-quality solutions are delivered. Specifically, the role involves in-depth analysis of insurance billing, accounting and reinsurance workflows and processes and overall business requirements in these areas for ISI's diverse client base of P&C insurers.

The ideal candidate will possess:

* Post-secondary education
* 7+ years of insurance industry experience, including systems implementation/support  exposure
* Strong knowledge of Property & Casualty insurance specifically in insurance billing and accounting processes and workflows
* Reinsurance knowledge is a definite asset
* Excellent inter-personal and presentation skills
* Keen attention to detail and the ability to apply your business analyst skills

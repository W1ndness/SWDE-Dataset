Advanced Antenna Designer (Infrastructure Antennas)

About the Job

Be part of a small, highly experienced team in the design and development of custom antennas for broadband data, base station, and indoor/outdoor Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS).

3 positions:  Junior, Senior, and Project/Team Lead

RF Engineer

Position:

Prioritize and schedule multiple projects, resolve conflicts, and communicate project status to the General Manager, Sales, and the global design centers. Serve as customer technical interface, and provide technical sales support including customer visits.

Team objectives are to:
  • Design advanced infrastructure antenna products.
  • Manage the transition to high volume production in international factories. 
  • Provide electrical design validation via near field radiation testing using an anechoic chamber. 
  • Prepare product specifications.

Experience:


  • BS in Electrical Engineering with a minimum of 5 years in antenna design greater than 500 MHz. Masters Degree preferred
  • Hands-on experience with antenna test equipment including Vector Network Analyzers, and near field anechoic chambers
  • Design of impedance matching networks
  • Experience using EM simulation tools (CST and IE3D preferred)
  • Solid background in applied physics and microwave design techniques
  • Knowledge of base station and DAS designs
  • Good hands-on troubleshooting skills
  • Prior working experience in commercial business enterprise; sense of urgency
  • Scope, plan & prioritize projects, ability to manage design to cost budgets
  • Aggressive, team oriented and creative problem solver
  • Effective communicator creating synergy within our global organization; diplomacy to get results; sensitivity to cultural differences
  • Demonstrates persistence - commitment to achieve goals despite setbacks, obstacles
  • Ability to travel domestically and internationally
  • Fluent in English (verbal and written)
  • Detail oriented; approaches work in a meticulous and thorough manner
  • Demonstrates professionalism at all times, and strives to improve skills
  • Is flexible and can adjust to shifting priorities
  • Ability to effectively handle escalations and time critical issues
  • Familiarity with a broad range of component manufacturing techniques
  • Mechanical aptitude and working knowledge of 3-D CAD systems
  • Understanding of high-volume manufacturing issues and strategies, including process control and automation

Senior RF Engineer
  • Must have very specific experience designing and testing infrastructure antennas (base station antennas).
  • Senior position, ready to develop product roadmap with little outside help.

Project/Team Lead
  • Experience developing DAS or base station.
  • Has been through process of testing, approval, and implementation with large carriers such as AT&T a plus.
  • Will have direct reports who are RF Engineers and support from U.S. and international offices.
  • Highly technical, very hands-on.
  • Will be point of RF technical contact with clients, but must be able to interact at corporate and highly technical level.

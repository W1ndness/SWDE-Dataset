About the Job

Software as a Service (SaaS) is fundamentally changing the economics of computing by providing software functionality on a shared, secure multi-tenant infrastructure while using a web browser and the Internet as a delivery mechanism.   Plateau Systems is a leader in providing SaaS based talent management systems.  Plateau’s SaaS offering is used by many of the world's most successful enterprises to increase the productivity of their employees and partners.  We have won numerous awards, including being named by Inc. magazine as one of the fastest growing companies in corporate America.

 

Plateau is seeking a System Engineer III to join our Hosting / SaaS Product Operations Team.  Based out of our Headquarters in Arlington, VA., you will have an integral part in the deployment of customers, products and services into our Production Data centers and ensuring superior service delivery to our customers.

 

Job Duties include:

Primary

·         Management of hosted web applications

·         Monitoring and optimizing the performance of these applications

·         Primary application engineer for customer migrations, upgrades and on-boarding

·         Primary interface between Product Ops, Services and Engineering for the application issues

·         Primary escalation point for operational web, application, database and code issues

·         Development of integrated web portal

·         Application technical installation, configuration, support, upgrades as necessary

·         Working with the other system and database administrators in the hosting group and with their customers in maintaining the hosted applications

Secondary

·         Researching, evaluating, installing, configuring and development of new tools to facilitate all aspects of hosting

·         Automation of day to day tasks

·         Installation, configuration, support, troubleshooting and recovery of applications and Web Servers

 

Required Qualifications:

·         5+ years Unix system administration and web application experience

·         Strong problem solving skills

·         Works effectively with a minimum of direction

·         Willingness and ability to work in a fast-paced environment

·         Willingness and ability to handle fire call duties

·         Must be eligible to work in the US without employer sponsorship and be eligible/qualify to obtain a US government clearance

Desired Qualifications:

·         Experience with software development in Java, Oracle, LAMP or .net

·         Experience with Oracle (10g/11g preferred)

·         Experience with Apache and Tomcat6

·         Experience with Linux and Windows

·         Experience with F5 preferred

 

 

Plateau is an Equal Opportunity Employer.  We offer a competitive compensation package that includes paid time off (PTO) and paid public holidays, generous health/life/disability insurance, stock options plan, retirement plan, and an annual employee profit sharing/bonus plans among other benefits—as well as a casual work environment and the opportunity to work among the industry’s best in one of America’s fastest growing companies.

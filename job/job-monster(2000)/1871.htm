Senior Web Developer

About the Job

Wanted: An experienced, talented web developer who can help us manage client projects throughout the development lifecycle. We’re a dynamic Philadelphia-based communications firm with a strong web team looking for a new senior member.

 

Ideal candidate will have the proven ability to manage projects and client relationships, a flair for innovative user interface development, and the requisite technical skills to pull it all off. Musts are an energetic yet collegial approach and strong work ethic—plus the demonstrated desire to work in a fast-paced, diverse and client-service oriented environment. 

 

REQUIREMENTS

·         6-8 years of experience developing standards-based websites using valid, semantic markup for structure and CSS for layout

·         Ability to produce design specifications, wireframes, and final visual designs in a collaborative environment

·         Expert knowledge of DHTML, CSS, Javascript (must have advanced Javascripting skills), jQuery (including common plug-ins) and AJAX-based front-end development

·         Expert knowledge of PHP and MySQL (or similar database query language)

·         Expert knowledge of Illustrator and Photoshop

·         Thorough knowledge of current development trends and other popular styling and scripting techniques

·         Ability to be extremely detail-oriented with strong commitment to cross-browser and cross-platform testing, troubleshooting and quality assurance

·         Extensive knowledge of UI design methodology and processes (user centered and interactive design)

·         Must be able to demonstrate, understand and be proficient in managing all phases of web product design and development

·         Excellent communication, organization and collaboration skills – person will be working within a team environment, balancing multiple projects and priorities while meeting, in many cases, extremely tight deadlines

·         Portfolio of previous design work demonstrating experience and skill level

EDUCATION

·         Bachelor's degree or equivalent

 

APPLICATION PROCESS

Please email cover letter, salary requirements, resume and sample URLs to jkrout@osiris-inc.com  This position is not open to telecommuters.  Salary is commensurate with skills and experience.

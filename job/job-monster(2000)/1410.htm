VP of Software Engineering

About the Job

AutoAnything, a San Diego-based, rapidly growing e-commerce Company, merges the high-speed lane of specialty automotive parts and accessories with cutting-edge internet technology and world class Customer Service. Our entrepreneurial style, knowledge of the competitive landscape and innovative business model has allowed us to excel in the industry since 1979.  AutoAnything is currently ranked #204 in the Internet Retailer Top 500 Guide of e-Tail companies.  Much of our success can be directly attributed to the energy, dedication, passion and inventiveness of our Team.

 

We are seeking a VP of Software Engineering to join our Team!

The VP of Software Engineering reports to the CTO and is responsible for providing key software development oversight and technical leadership for the consumer-facing and internal systems for AutoAnything. This position is accountable for project deliverables in a rapid development environment, directly overseeing several functions such as business process modeling, business rules development, and quality assurance to provide the best solutions using state of the art technology.  This is accomplished by managing and driving process into new technology through research, analysis, development and defining the system and application architecture.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·         Lead the engineering and development of products, systems, and services that are aligned with the business strategy and enable the company to leverage engineering knowledge into profitable revenue streams.

·         Provide leadership for the Technology department, which includes application development, support and R&D, and quality assurance.

·         Oversight of engineering staff including recruitment, performance evaluations, staff planning, etc.

·         Implement processes and procedures for the development and release of products/projects that facilitate high quality and rapid deployment.

·         Work closely with the CTO to ensure that business and technology strategies are properly aligned.

·         Manage and improve the effectiveness, efficiencies, and consistency of engineering and development teams.

·         Monitor and manage ongoing performance of engineering initiatives including; uptime, conversion rate, and consumer experience.

·         Further the information technology platform (architecture, systems, and people) that accommodates scalability and growth.

·         Oversees the development and execution of quality assurance test plans.

·         Plans team resource needs; allocates, supervises, and reviews work.

·         Manage engineers by providing leadership, career development and mentoring.

·         Provide mentoring to team on the implementation of best practices in software development life cycle methodologies and implementations.

·         Continuously improve processes and productivity.

REQUIREMENTS:

·         Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Business Administration, Engineering, or related discipline required.

·         Proven leadership, communications and presentation skills. 

·         7-12 year progressive record of technical management experience in software development lifecycle.

·         Proven ability to successfully grow and manage engineering teams

·         Solid understanding of the project lifecycle process, including initiation, requirements gathering, analysis and design, development tools and technologies, release and version control, contemporary testing methodologies, and deployment management.

·         Extensive experience in Agile software development methodologies.

·         Excellent technical foundation and in-depth knowledge of eCommerce technologies. 

·         Current knowledge of 3 tiered applications. 

·         Experience in software engineering involving development and testing of applications.

·         In-depth knowledge of software/online application testing methodologies and processes. 

·         Prior management of technology staff.  Ability to recruit and retain top engineering talent.

·         Able to prioritize effectively according to department needs and ability to organize a large number of changing variables.

·         Ability to manage multiple projects with rapidly changing priorities and deadlines.

·         Highly organized and thorough, with an ability to facilitate communication and scheduling among different levels of staff.

·         Strong communication, writing and inter-personal abilities, including skills such as attitude, diplomacy and persuasiveness, analytical ability, strong judgment and managerial skills.

·         Ability to anticipate problems and develop effective solutions. 

·         Desire to work in fast-paced team environment.

STAFF APPLICATIONS ARCHITECT-SENIOR OBIEE DEVELOPER

About the Job

Beckman Coulter develops, manufactures and markets products that simplify,automate and innovate complex biomedical testing. Our diagnostic systems arefound in hospitals and other critical care settings around the world and produceinformation used by physicians to diagnose disease, make treatment decisions andmonitor patients. Instruments for life science research are used by scientistsas they study complex biological problems including the causes of disease,identify new therapies, and test new drugs. Our customers include hospitals,physicians´ offices, diagnostic reference laboratories, pharmaceutical andbiotechnology companies, universities, medical schools and researchinstitutions.

Job Description:

This position is actually for an OBIEE Developer. The role of the RPD developer is required to ensure that Beckman Coulter has an internal resource with in-depth knowledge of the RPD Architecture related to the OBIEE analytics applications.

Areas of Responsibility:

• Develop subject matter expertise with the out-of-the-box RPD objects for the pre-built analytical applications

 • Design, build, test, implement and maintain any new or modified RPD objects for the analytics applications

• Prepare and maintain technical documentation for all new or modified RPD objects

• Prepare Code Migration requests using automated code migration tool

• Participate in design of new data warehouse tables as needed

• Design and conduct unit tests of new or changed RPD objects

• Interact with business units to understand their needs and design appropriate RPD objects

• Participate in performance testing and tuning

• Set up Delivers iBots to support the needs of the end users

• Develop expertise in BI Publisher development and support end user’s efforts to user this reporting tool

• Provide internal consulting assistance to the report writing teams

• Participate in the implementation of any OBIEE product upgrades

• Prepare status reports for all assigned work .

 

Skill Requirements: The person filling this role will be primarily responsible for reviewing requests for new or modified RPD objects within the analytic applications; determining the best way to satisfy the user requests; and, performing the development work necessary to deliver the required solution. The person selected for this position should have: 7 years experience designing data warehouses, particularly the meta-data 6 years experience configuring Oracle BI (RPD) 2 years experience working with a MUD environment 2 Years experience working with the OBI Apps 5 years experience configuring and tuning Informatica ETL applications 2 Years experience configuring and tuning the DAC Previous OBI Application upgrade involvement preferred Experience with Oracle Ebiz Applications perferred Experience using SQL skills to test and validate solutions Experience with Data Modeling of OLTP and OLAP data bases Experience with Star Schema Modelling Education: Bachelor’s degree in field with 9+ years exp. OR Master’s degree in field with 7+ years exp. OR Doctoral degree in field with 4+ years exp.

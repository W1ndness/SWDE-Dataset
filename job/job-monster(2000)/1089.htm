About the Job

 Email Marketing Specialist

Trabuco Media in Aliso Viejo, California

 

Trabuco Media is seeking a highly motivated candidate to fill a full-time Email Marketing Specialist position. This is your opportunity to be an integral part of a cutting edge business with a bright future. In this position you will be working directly with Trabuco Media clients to manage, consult and/or assist in execution of their email marketing programs.

 

This position is a great opportunity for a professional, articulate individual with an outgoing personality and strong initiative

 

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:

*Create and Deploy email marketing campaigns for various advertisers

*Maintain the email marketing calendar.

*Identify, monitor and explain variances to pre-defined strategy for each campaign.

*Distribute reports related to the deployment, brand engagement, open/click and conversion data associated to these email campaigns.

*Trouble shooting HTML

*Consult on email marketing best practices, trends and behaviors

*Monitor email reputation and work with ISPs to setup/manage feedback loops and resolve deliverability issues.

*Track all email campaigns via propriety analytics system, analyze performance data and recommend optimized deployment strategies.

 

Qualifications and Skills:

*Bachelors degree, emphasis in Information Technology or Computer Science or equivalent experience

*Must be a fast learner with a strong desire for continued education in email marketing and marketing best practices

*Ability to exchange information with others clearly and concisely

*Extremely high attention to detail and accuracy

*High level of patience

*Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

*Excellent time management skills

*The ability to prioritize projects to meet deadlines

*Highly organized with strong multi-tasking abilities

*Email Marketing experience from successful online B2C companies

 

Requirements for consideration:

*Expert level knowledge of HTML coding and troubleshooting

*Minimum 2 years professional experience

*Bachelors degree, in Information Technology or Computer Science or equivalent experience *Marketing Communications experience is a plus

*Extensive computer knowledge and proficiency in Microsoft applications

*Expert in navigating the World Wide Web

*Working knowledge of the following technologies and terms: HTML, Rich Text, CAN-SPAM, Deliverability, Domain, ISP, URL, Alt-tag, etc.

*Proficient written and grammatical skills.

 

Hours for the position are 9:00 am to 6:30pm Monday through Friday. This position requires applicants to be an expert in HTM.  All resumes submitted without this qualification will not be considered for employment.

 

Please send resume and salary history as a Word attachment to: hrtrabuco@gmail.com

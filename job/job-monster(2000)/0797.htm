JOBTITLE:                                  Assoc Director Application Support            

LOCATION:                                  Houston, TX

POSITION TYPE:                         Direct

COMPANY PROFILE

Work for a leading provider of energy and related financial services to its customers and trading counter parties. This investment bank has a market capitalization of $17.3bn, total assets under management of $153.4bn, and almost 10,000 staff in 24 countries. Our client encourages their employees to realize their potential in an environment that values creativity and innovation. They’ll offer you the freedom to make your mark with your team, clients, the organization and the industry. This company’s success is built on its people. So don’t miss out on this amazing opportunity.

RESPONSIBILITIES

ITG Houston is looking for an Associate Director to lead the Application Support functions for the FICC business groups across North America; primary offices include New York, Houston, & Calgary. The Director level position be responsible for ensuring the production environments are operating in an effective manner, activities are coordinated between Sydney, New York, & Houston, and a local team of IT professionals are being properly managed.

REQUIRED SKILLS

  • Bachelors+ in Technology or related degree/experience
  • 7+ years professional experience in Technology Roles
  • 5+ years of Team Management & Group Coordination
  • 3+ years of direct customer liaison work.
  • 2+ years of Trading, Investment Banking, or Energy Industry experience

In addition to the above skills, our client is looking for the following qualities:

  • An accomplished leader that has a good mix of people & technology skills that has the ability to make good decisions based on the technology management tools at Macquarie.
  • A change agent with a focused discipline on continuous improvement.
  • Ability to recruit, retain, & develop a team located in multiple offices.
  • Ability to continuously manage a dynamic work queue of tasks, ensuring the most important tasks remain the focus.
  • Strong problem management skills, especially in a heated environment, where sub-tasks are delegated, facts are communicated, and the problem is solved in fast & safe manner.
  • A manager with experience developing & managing metrics to effectively evaluate the team’s performance.
  • Strong communications skills that can project leadership 360 degrees.
    • Users
    • Senior Management
    • Developers
    • Infrastructure Technology
    • Team Members
  • Builds and maintains strong relationships with the business and other teams within IT
  • Takes proactive approach to implement preventative measures, improvements and quality controls

 

ABOUT TRIAD

Triad Resources specializes in direct hire staffing, managed contract services and comprehensive training solutions. As a company committed to the highest level of integrity, honesty and quality in service, we are unique in two important ways: First, we always operate with the utmost discretion and never submit your resume without discussing the opportunity and client with you. Second, we are committed to helping you find a position that matches not only your skills and experience, but your personal and professional goals as well. Utilizing our network of premier global and progressive companies, we are able to offer you exciting opportunities that keep your best interests at heart. We look forward to working with you.

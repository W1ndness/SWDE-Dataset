About the Job

iQuasar is an Information Technology Company based in Washington DC Metropolitan area (Northern Virginia). We specialize in IT Consulting, Staffing, Software Development, Managed Consulting, and Software Products and Applications.

One of our clients in Memphis TN needs a Master Data Management Architect with strong skills in Master Data Management Architectecture for a Long term project with possible extension thereafter.

Job Title: Master Data Management Architect

Job Description:
This person will serve as the Master Data Management Architect for a global Enterprise Business Systems implementation project for a leading company in the Hospitality industry.

This person will work with the systems integrator and project leaders develop a comprehensive Master Data Management approach and governance strategy around the following master data domains: Customer, Supplier and Chart of Accounts (DRM).

Responsibilities will include, but not limited to:

* Help define and support data governance, repository standards and core information management capabilities including, Master/Meta data management, Data Quality & Lifecycle management
* Define and identify reference data, and provide strategy and process to harmonize Master Data creation and access across the enterprise.
* Develop approaches, methodologies, policies, and standards to govern master data
* Develop data owner and data steward strategies
* Enforce standard adherence of master data architectures for data transformations
* Define roadmaps, project plans and steps to implement master data management architectures
* Master Data liaison for custom, SAP and third party application master data integration
* Analyze current Master Data business processes and participate in designing future business processes

Required Qualifications:
15+ Years Data Architecture and Data Modeling Experience for Large Enterprise Operations
Solid experience on Informatica ETL, Oracle database and a BI tool such as OBIEE, MicroStrategy or Cognos
Experience with data modeling and query tools such as Rational Data Architect, ERwin, Embarcadero, TOAD, SQL Navigator etc.
Implementation experience with Siebel UCM and Hyperion DRM
Knowledge of UML modeling tools such as Sparx EA or Rational
Knowledge of Meta Data
Solid understanding of Master data management
Solid understanding of Service Oriented Architecture
Solid understanding of Data Quality principles and techniques
Strong architectural, technical, design and analytical skills
Excellent communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience with the IBM Rational toolset
Six Sigma certification (green belt)
Hospitality Industry Experience

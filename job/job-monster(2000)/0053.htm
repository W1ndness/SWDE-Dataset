Sr Web Designer (Contractor)

About the Job

Description

Are you looking for a fast moving, creative environment where you can use your expertise to develop and market cutting-edge interactive and digital products?

AT&T Interactive, a subsidiary of AT&T Inc, is an industry leader in creating local search products that encourage consumers to discover and engage with local businesses across three screens - online, mobile, and TV. YP.com attracts over 36 million monthly online unique visitors [i] . YP.com is also accessed by millions of users each month via the mobile web. AT&T Interactive consumer apps have a pre-activation presence or have been downloaded through digital app stores on over 40 million mobile phones. [ii]

Helping advertisers grow their business, AT&T Interactive's local ad network, one of the industry's most robust, distributes ads to a distinguished list of top publishers whose presence spans online, mobile and TV platforms. AT&T Interactive's wide range of local ad products such as premium listings, performance-based advertising, and search engine marketing provides advertisers an opportunity to reach consumers who are looking for a local business.

AT&T Interactive products are supported by more than 5,000 media consultants from AT&T Advertising Solutions .


Join our dedicated and talented team of individuals all focused on creating the best products in the marketplace. We currently have a contract hourly opportunity for an Sr. Web Designer in our Glendale offices.

Do you get excited about growing revenues for emerging products? How would you like to do just that as part of an entrepreneurial company backed with the resources of AT&T?

We have a variety of Products in development that need your contribution as a User Interface & Web/Graphic Designer. Are you comfortable creating user-interfaces and designs that adhere to stylistic, technical, and schedule specifications? Are you able to work on a variety of projects in a busy Product department, all the while maintaining overall company business goals? Do you understand user interaction on the web and mobile? Do you think about the user first, and know how to design clean and slick graphical concepts that don't pose an obstacle between the users and their goals?



Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Report to Director of User Experience within the User Experience Design team.
  • Work with the HFE/IA & Usability teams to improve the user interface of company products.
  • Collaborate with other Web Designers on projects to achieve the best results.
  • Create conceptual designs adhering to YP.COM/AT&T Interactive style and standards.
  • · Work with cross-functional teams in Web, Mobile, Data, Advertising and Marketing
  • Maintain an understanding of project objectives in relation to overall business goals.
  • Complete graphic and UI projects within tight deadlines.
  • Continue to improve design skills and knowledge.
  • Evangelize for consumer-driven UI/graphic design across various media.
  • Manage brand standards and corporate Identity.


Qualifications:



To perform this job successfully, you must be able to perform each essential duty. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required.
  • 5 - 7 years of design experience in a fast-paced Internet environment
  • An associate's degree or its equivalent, Art degree a plus
  • Experience developing look and feel for large, high-visibility consumer websites.
  • Experience working on dynamic data-driven websites
  • Experience designing UI and graphics for mobile devices
  • Strong understanding of functional user-interface design, usability and accessibility
  • Expert skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash. Working knowledge of HTML and CSS. Familiarity with Mac OS.
  • Solid team communications skills
  • Self-starter with excellent eye for detail
  • Experience with branding and maintaining brand consistency
  • Able to work multiple projects simultaneously against tight deadlines
  • Excellent organizational skills

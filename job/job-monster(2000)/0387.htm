Help Desk Professional - Atlanta

About the Job

SCI Companies, a “Best in Class” provider of HR outsourcing, is one of the largest privately held Professional Employment Organizations (PEOs) in the nation. Specializing in human resource administration and consultation, benefits administration, workers’ compensation administration, and payroll and tax administration, SCI’s HR business solutions are delivered through an experienced client support team and robust web based HR technologies. 


Founded in 1985, SCI’s services enable its Client Companies to focus on their core competencies and, consequently, improve their business performance. Based in Atlanta, SCI has 1,000 client companies with 45,000 co-employees served by professionals both domestically and abroad.

To learn more about SCI Companies, please visit www.scicompanies.com.

 

JOB SUMMARY

 

This position is responsible for both on-site and remote desktop support and basic network troubleshooting in our Atlanta (Lawrenceville), Georgia office.  The position offers a rare opportunity for a self-motivated individual to learn and become proficient in a variety of technologies, and the expectations are for you to grow and develop within the Helpdesk and beyond.  Both professionalism and technical ability are essential to growth and success.

 

ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Desktop support, including laptops

·         Other hardware support, including printers/scanners/fax machines, mobile devices, phones, time clocks, etc.

·         Setting up user accounts in a Windows 2003 Active Directory and Exchange production environment

·         Basic understanding of network resources such as firewalls and routers

 

REQUIREMENTS

 

  • Strong knowledge of Windows XP and Vista operating systems
  • 1+ Year experience with High School Diploma. No experience required for candidates with at least an Associates Degree in a computer related field and/or Industry recognized Certifications  
  • Good customer service and telephone skills for support of clients and remote employees
  • The ability to work independently with limited direct supervision. 

 

The following are considered a strong plus:

  • Current A+ certification
  • Network+ or CCNA certification
  • Experience with Lawson Software, especially Human Resources/Payroll suite
  • Experience with Oracle database systems
  • Experience with UNIX shell access and command line
  • Experience with Windows and/or UNIX scripting

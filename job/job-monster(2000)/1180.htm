About the Job

NexGen Technologies, Inc. is a premier IT services and resource provider to various civilian agencies within the Federal government with ISO 9001 certified Quality Management Systems. Our partners include major software manufacturers and large corporations and small businesses in the IT industry. Our team members are currently providing mission-critical application development and support services to civilian agencies within the Federal government. 

SharePoint Developer
 

 

NexGen Technologies, Inc. is looking for a SharePoint Developer to support federal government. This individual will be required to work closely with members of the NexGen’s and client’s teams, including but not limited to subject matter experts, technical leads, and project managers.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop intranet and internet portals using Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007.
  • Assist in developing the strategy for optimal use of SharePoint and other collaborative tools within the organization.
  • Create, maintain and train as necessary on new 2007 enterprise solutions.
  • Help define requirements for new integrated service oriented solutions using MOSS 2007.
  • Assist in deploying enterprise document management, search and collaboration techniques.
  • Initiate flexible and customer focused workflow and electronic forms-driven solutions.
  • Participate in the implementation of all SharePoint Collaboration tools.


Qualifications:

  • 2-4 years Microsoft SharePoint and Windows SharePoint Services (WSS) work experience.
  • 3+ years development experience in portals and workflow solutions.
  • Microsoft SharePoint Server 2003 or 2007.
  • SharePoint Designer 2007 and electronic forms.
  • InfoPath 2003/2007.


Additional Desired Skills/Experience:

  • Experience with MOSS 2007.
  • SQL Server 2005 and MS BI components.
  • Familiarity with federal government standards, policies, and guidelines.
  • The candidate should also possess strong oral and written communication, problem solving skills and should be a team player.
  • The successful candidate will also be able to pass background screening prior to employment.
  • US Citizenship or work-authorization with alien registration number required.


To Apply:
To respond to this opportunity, please forward resumes to "careers-2010@nexgeninc.com" with the subject line "SharePoint Developer"

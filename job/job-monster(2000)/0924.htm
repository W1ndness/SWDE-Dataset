About the Job

SENIOR COGNOS ARCHITECT DEVELOPER                                         

 

About the Job:

Reporting to the Manager for Reporting & Analysis, the Senior Cognos Architect and Developer is responsible for architecting Cognos solutions, designing Cognos Frameworks and provide Cognos expertise to multiple groups of Cognos developers.  In addition the Senior Cognos Architect Developer is responsible for gathering and analyzing business requirements, planning, preparing, implementing, organizing and executing developmental tasks, developing and delivering solutions to production.  This role requires that the person be the go to expert for everything regarding Cognos technology and requires that the individual be able to lead Cognos developers.

 

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

  • Architect Cognos solutions.  Stay abreast of new/change Cognos offerings and demonstrate deep understanding of Cognos applications to partner business and developer teams and with infrastructure teams
  • Develops standards and procedures for Cognos usage.
  • Develops and implements Cognos best practices.
  • Defines solutions to support business needs, develops and implements Enterprise Cognos Business Intelligence (BI) application architecture, security plans and processes.
  • Designs, documents, deploy and maintain analytical and reporting applications and utilize knowledge of data and data manipulation techniques to implement functional requirements into reports.
  • Develops, supports and implements Business Intelligence (BI) solutions, multi-dimensional cubes, Report Template Library, batch processing and relational models as well as complex user reports and analysis.
  • Develops and supports operational and ad-hoc reports using the Cognos tools which include Framework Manager, Transformer, Query  Studio, Reporting Studio, Metrics Studio, Events Studio and Cognos SDK.
  • Creates, develops and supports complex Extract, Transform and Load (ETL) processes and Structured Query Language (SQL) procedures which transfer, load and cleanse data and develops and implements Cognos Script for automation.
  • Provides technical quality assurance, and documents the resolution of technical problems by the end-user and provides training.
  • Utilizes quantitative and analytical skills and business knowledge for the migration of applications to Quality Assurance/Staging and Production environment.   This includes monitoring applications for performance and stability, resolving potential problems and optimizing application routines. 
  • Installs, modifies and maintains Cognos Application systems and provides overall technical guidance for the usage of Cognos tools and related software products.
  • Collaborates and conducts meetings with business owners and end-users on the methodology of utilizing quantitative, analytical and business skills and knowledge to understand crucial data needs.
  • Collaborates with BI Systems Architect to design and develop efficient operational procedures and to ensure that the application environment supports the business requirements.
  • Collaborates with IT Operations in order to solve potential problems, defines and analyzes new technical requirements and software releases and performs ongoing capacity planning.
  • Performs project management activities including project documentation, business requirements and project tracking.

 

QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:

 

Minimum

  1. A baccalaureate degree from an accredited college, including or supplemented by 24 semester credits in computer science or a related computer field and four years of satisfactory full-time computer software experience in computer systems development and analysis, applications programming, database administration, systems programming, data communications, including one year in a project leader capacity or as a major contributor on a complex project; or
  2. A four-year high school diploma or its educational equivalent and six years of satisfactory full-time computer software experience as described in "1" above, including one year in a project leader capacity or as a major contributor on a complex project; or
  3. Education and/or experience equivalent to "1" or "2" above. College education may be substituted for up to two years of the required experience in "2" above on the basis that 60 semester credits from an accredited college is equated to one year of experience. A masters degree in computer science or a related computer field may be substituted for one year of the required experience in "1" or "2" above. However, all candidates must have at least a four-year high school diploma or its educational equivalent and at least one year of satisfactory full-time experience in a project leader capacity or as a major contributor on a complex project.

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Extensive experience working with Cognos BI products including Bursting, PowerPlay, Impromptu, ReportNet (Report Studio, Query Studio, Metrics Studio, Events Studio and framework Manager), Access Manager, Portal Administration, Server Administration, Content Administration, Cognos SDK, Cognos Scheduler, Macros, .Net, HTML, Java, SQL Server 200/2005
  • Strong communication and documentation skills
  • Strong quantitative and analytical skills and business knowledge.
  • Ability to perform project management activities.
  • Knowledge of databases & SQL is a must.
  • A working knowledge of SQL Server 2005, Data Staage 7.5/8.0
  • A working knowledge of SQL Server 2005 and Data stage 7.5/8.0.
  • Strong understanding and working knowledge of Relational/Star Schema modeling and design as well as Web Services (COGNOS Web Services, HTML, XML, etc.).
  • Knowledge of data and data manipulation techniques.

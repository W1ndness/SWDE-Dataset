ISR Collection Management Subject Matter Expert

About the Job

Information Systems Solutions, Inc. has an opening for an experienced Collection Management Subject Matter Experts (SME) to support collection management in a coalition environment.  The position will require 6 month rotations into Afghanistan from Tampa, Florida with the option for a one (1) year deployment to Afghanistan.

 

The primary responsibilities include:

·         Providing on-site and off-site Collection Management expertise within an ISAF Collection Group in Afghanistan.

·         Developing written documentation in support of CMMA training initiatives

·         Working with Operations personnel to resolve customer trouble tickets

·         Providing CMMA demonstrations to commands/organizations within CENTCOM

·         Submitting travel requests, trip reports, student rosters, and training feedback forms

·         Providing Weekly Activity Reports

·         Maintaining proficiency on Collection Mission Management Applications

·         Identifying and maintaining awareness of collection management issues identified by ISAF

 

The candidate must be a self-starter with a robust knowledge of collection management processes.  This position will require travel to customer sites around the world.

 

Other responsibilities include:

·         A liaison between the CENTCOM, ISAF, and Joint Program Office (JPO)

·         Coordinates support to other remote sites IN ACCORDANCE WITH JPO and CENTCOM standard operating procedures, to include periodic rotations to Afghanistan.

·         Works with JPO Engineering to model technical infrastructure characteristics using the archived information described above, in order to plan enhancements that improve the quality of service for CENTCOM and ISAF

·         Assists JPO Engineering by coordinating the use of the ISAF site as a real world test bed, as appropriate, to gain immediate customer feedback and suggestions for improving CMMA baselines.

·         Travels to the JPO facility in Suitland, Maryland as well as other areas directed by CENTCOM for familiarization, training, and updating of their knowledge as it pertains to JPO baselines and collection mission applications

·         Provides trip reports for all off-site activities

·         Maintains frequent contact with the JPO Field Engineering Manager, by telephone or e-mail, to ensure that all issues are handled in a timely manner

·         The Field Engineer position has been identified as ‘mission critical’. As such, the Field Engineer shall adhere to all DoD directives and policies, such as Anthrax vaccinations and the use of Nuclear, Biological, and Chemical (NBC) gear as directed

 

(a) Required Skills

·         Active Top Secret clearance based on an SSBI, must be a US citizen

·         BS in systems engineering, computer science, or an information technology discipline from an accredited university. Four years operational experience may be a substitute for the degree

·         Experience with one or more of the following:

o   Planning Tool for Resource Integration, Synchronization, and Management (PRISM) experience

o   Experience with Flight Control / Flight Enterprise

o   Experience with Precision Sensor Assessment System Multi-intelligence Multi-Domain (PSAS-M)

·         Training or teaching experience

·         Excellent problem-solving skills

·         Excellent written and oral communication skills

·         Ability to work independently overseas with no on-site company supervision.

 

(b) Highly Desired:

·         3 years experience with UNIX (Solaris) system administration

·         Ability to install, configure and trouble shoot basic networking services, such as TCP/IP and SMTP

·         Training/teaching experience

·         Configuration and administration experience with Sun, PC, and printer hardware

·         Prior experience with on-site operational support

·         Prior experience with end user training

·         Microsoft, Sun, Cisco certifications

·         Any Foreign Language Skills

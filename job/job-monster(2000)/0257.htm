Research and Data Analyst

About the Job

Andrew Solutions, a CommScope Company, is the foremost supplier of one-stop, end-to-end radio frequency (RF) solutions. Our Antenna, Cable, and Cabinet Group and Wireless Network Solutions Group combine to design, manufacture and deliver complete solutions for wireless infrastructure--from top-of-the-tower base station antennas to cable systems and cabinets, RF site solutions, signal distribution, and network optimization.

The Wireless Network Solutions group currently has a immediate opening for an Research & Data Analyst located in our Ashburn, VA facility.

POSITION SUMMARY:

This position provides wireless data research and support for products to internal and external customers. The successful candidate will research, design, modify, develop and implement data processes and applications supported by the Comsearch business unit. Including wireless telecommunications data and databases such as the FCC’s ULS database. The successful candidate must be proficient in MS Excel and MS Access and should have knowledge of SQL and Visual Basic. Knowledge of mapping products such as ArcView or MapInfo is preferred. Must be very detailed-oriented and able to juggle multiple assignments effectively.

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:

Design, manipulate, and implement data downloads and deliverables in support of customer deliverables and company initiatives.

Research and advise on wireless data standards, processes, and regulatory requirements/ issues.

Identify data errors and troubleshoot complex data sets.

Support and maintain all applications in development, quality assurance and production.

Conduct custom research projects for wireless consulting opportunities.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:

Education/Training : A Bachelor’s Degree is required.

Experience :

Must have a minimum of 2 years relevant experience. Relevant experience includes:

 

Proficiency in MS Excel and MS Access. Must have strong working knowledge of advanced formulas and functions and the ability to work with a relational database.

Excellent research and analytical skills.

Must be a critical thinker with an ability to work in a team environment.

Preferred Experience:

Experience with mapping products like ArcView or MapInfo.

Knowledge of SQL and Visual Basic.

Strong communications and report writing skills.

About the Job

 


Online Marketing Account Manager


We are looking for a talented online Account Manager with agency experience to join All Web Leads, Inc as our Affiliate Manager based in Austin, TX. You will manage high-volume web marketing affiliate accounts and ensure that they consistently deliver the highest quality insurance leads and qualified traffic for the right price. To be successful in this role you must be a self-starter and possess a can-do attitude! You should have a passion for relationship-building, as well as have a focus on achieving measureable results. Your ability to think strategically while also remaining very detail oriented is what will see you thriving in this exciting role.


You will develop a plan for optimizing and growing existing affiliate relationships for a program with extremely agressive growth goals. This will entail creating a feedback loop with current affiliates to achieve best results, maximize volume and quality while managing costs, and identifying new opportunities. You will also be responsible for all set-up and execution of new affiliate launches, and will work closely with in-house designers and engineering as part of this process. You will run daily metrics reports and make regular adjustments to optimize the AWL affiliate program to new heights. This role is a great opportunity to step up and take ownership of a new, rapidly growing piece of the business at our extremely successful company.


All Web Leads, Inc. is one of the most successful online lead generation provide
rs to the US Insurance industry. We use internet marketing to turn consumer interest in insurance products into policy sales for many of the largest insurance carriers (and their agents) in the world. We recently placed 2nd in the Austin Business Journal’s 2009 “Fast 50". We are a tight-knit team with a fast paced, energetic, and entrepreneurial company culture. We are highly profitable, provide competitive pay, excellent benefits, and a fun and collaborative work environment.


Responsibilities:

  • Support Affiliate Director in all tasks involved to exceed quarterly goals
  • Account management
  • Expand current affiliate relationships
  • Streamline affiliate sign-up process
  • Evaluate campaign performance and work with affiliates to develop strategies to capitalize on growth opportunities
  • Leverage analytics to effectively manage volume opportunities, costs, and quality
  • Collaborate with other Marketing team members and Engineering to push new initiatives through and achieve goals
  • Conduct market research and competitive analysis in order to identify opportunities for growth
  • Put out fires, manage crises and jump over hurdles to maintain great relationships
  • Reconcile monthly invoices and work with Accounting team to ensure affiliates get paid

Requirements

  • 2 years experience in a role which has included marketing, account management and extensive data analysis
  • Proven track record of conceiving, developing and managing online marketing programs
  • Strong relationship building skills
  • Expert analytical skills, organizational abilities and a strong attention to detail
  • Independent thinker; ability to jump in and take initiative.
  • Ability to work independently as well as part of a team
  • Strategic thinker
  • Creative problem solver
  • Extreme drive and passion
  • Precise and effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Expert with Microsoft Excel and other Office software
  • BA or BS degree in business, marketing or related field

IT Project Manager (DPPA VI #1223)

About the Job

The Oklahoma Tax Commission (OTC), a government agency with the State of Oklahoma, is seeking an IT Project Manager to join the Information Technology (IT) Division’s Project Management Office (PMO).

 

This position will have the responsibility managing multiple IT related projects. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and facilitation skills, experience with methodologies and tools used to document project efforts, and the ability to oversee multiple projects in a governmental environment.  The right candidate must have strong leadership skills and the ability to take ownership of project management resources and tasks.  The candidate will have at least three (3) years recent “hands-on” experience in a project management capacity, along with a Project Management Institute (PMI) Project Manager Professional (PMP) certification.  This position will have the responsibility of managing projects to include project initiation, planning, execution, as well as monitoring and controlling, through project completion.  This position will be responsible for conducting meetings, setting and managing to deadlines, communicating with project owners and resources, gathering and documenting business requirements, and keeping projects on track and within budget.

 

Job Responsibilities:

 

·        Create and maintain project documentation to identify, evaluate, scope, plan, monitor, and follow up on active projects

·        Maintain and update PMO Project Lists to include pending, active, closed, and on-hold projects

·        Conduct and participate in meetings to perform analysis, gather requirements, and track project progress through completion

·        Conduct and participate in weekly project review meetings

·        Facilitate problem solving sessions

·        Recognize when and determine what the appropriate corrective action is to be taken to ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget and scope

·        Collaborate and coordinate with resource managers and project resources

 

Position Requirements:

 

·        Bachelors Degree in Business Administration, Business Management, Computer Science or MIS. Other degreed disciplines will be considered if Project Management Certifications are earned and minimum requirements for experience are met

·        Masters Degree is a plus

·        Project Management Institute (PMI) Project Management Professional (PMP) certification  

·        3-5 years experience with project management and software development methodologies

·        3-5 years management experience on multiple IT projects

·        Experience with Microsoft (MS) Word, Visio and Project

·        Excellent written, verbal, and organizational skills

·        The ability to oversee multiple projects

·        The ability to oversee time and resources based on project needs and requirements

·        Excellent interpersonal skills in order to lead, motivate, and promote team building and project progression

·        Self-motivated and able to work with limited direction and supervision


Employment with the OTC offers benefits which include medical, dental, life, disability and vision insurance, medical and health care reimbursement programs, retirement plan, deferred compensation plan, paid holidays and longevity payments. Employees are provided a benefit allowance to spend toward their benefits. Employees earn three weeks annual and three weeks paid sick leave in the first year of employment.

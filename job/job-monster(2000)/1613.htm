HTTP Streaming Server Developer

About the Job

MAJOR JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

We are looking for a Software developer to contribute in the design, development and delivery of software that converge the worlds of Internet, television and mobile. You will be working on a video processing and delivery platform.

 

Besides your programming skills the ability to quickly learn and excel in new areas is vital.

 

·        Design, development, maintenance and documentation of complex computer programs in accordance with established standards.

·        Unit testing and integration testing for functionality and performance.

·        Complex problem analysis and verification, release testing and beta support for assigned products.

·        Investigation of problems discovered by QA or product support and develops solutions to the problems.

·        Prototyping of concepts to proof of concept

 

 

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:

 

Required:

·         Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or equivalent experience

·         Minimum 8+ years of experience developing complex applications

·         Expert knowledge  in the design and implementation of streaming media technologies including in-depth knowledge of MPEG, Adobe, Apple or  Microsoft Smooth Streaming

·         Experience with VOD, IPTV, mobile delivery and CDN architectures

·         Strong knowledge of Apache and lighttpd web server functionality and development

·         Familiarity and experience of the Linux operating system.

·         Good working knowledge of TCP

·         Strong understanding of OOP concepts, and design patterns, and fluency in C and C++

·         Strong analytical and design skills

·         Must have excellent organizational, interpersonal and communication skills

·         Must demonstrate the willingness and capability of multi-tasking by taking on several duties, tasks, projects and/or responsibilities at one time

·         Must be capable of working independently or as part of a team

·         Must be capable of contributing new ideas and methodologies as appropriate

 

Desired:

  • Knowledge of smooth streaming and live delivery implementations for common video formats

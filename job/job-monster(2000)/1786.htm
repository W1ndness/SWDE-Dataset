Software Developer C# - .Net - SQL Server - Multiple Positions and Companies

About the Job

My clients, several successful Software Products and Financial Products and Services organizations are utilizing my services to fill their full time software developer positions. They are exceptional companies that build long term relationships with their employees.

We are looking for individuals who want to make a long-term commitment to the company. Excellent candidates will be well-rounded individuals, with an ability to learn quickly. Candidates should be highly motivated self-starters with a passion for developing high quality software. Accordingly, strong technical and logical thinking skills are essential. In short, we are looking for people whose passion for people and software development will help us to continue to innovate and add value to the whole company.

-Bachelor’s degree or higher is strongly preferred, although we retain an open mind as to the major.

-Minimum 3+ years experience. Financials experience highly preferred.

-Experience with .NET Framework and SQL Server

-Extensive experience with object oriented design and languages, such as C#, C++ or Java

-Experience in developing stored procedures, T-SQL development, and schema design

-Excellent object-oriented programming skills, problem solving abilities and attention to detail are a must. 

-Comprehensive understanding and usage of Microsoft Technologies

-Comprehensive understanding of Programming Techniques and Practices

-A solid foundation in computer science, with strong competencies in data structures and software design

-Good written, verbal communication skills required. Developers will interact with team members in various departments. 

Preferred Experience:

-Development experience in an Agile (Scrum) environment

-Experience refactoring legacy applications.

Please email a Word doc resume with 3 working references for consideration.

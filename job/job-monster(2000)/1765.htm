Business Systems Analyst

About the Job

Skills: Experience with the Federal Information Security Control Audit Manual (FISCAM), NIST SP 800-53

 

Location: McLean, VA.

 

Tax Term: FULLTIME

 

Tasks: Provide process documentation support to the Defense Logistics Agency based upon the ITIL v3 service management framework.

 

Job Description:

Summit Technologies is currently seeking experienced business process improvement professionals to support the Defense Logistics Agency resource stewardship governance efforts and enterprise-level strategic objectives. The successful candidate will deliver recommendations, Plan of Action and Milestones, business process decompositions and other work products that will enable DLA to design, refine and implement standardized, repeatable and compliant best practices in accordance with the DLA IT Operations framework, which is based upon the ITIL v3 service management framework.

 

The successful candidate will have experience in the following areas:

 

§         Testing and reviews of logical access controls

§         Experience testing application security controls to include the Software Development Lifecycle and principles,

§         Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Planning,

§         Information Security and Risk Management to include Policies, Standards, Guidelines and Procedures

§         Risk Management Tools and Practices

§         Operations Security to include Change Control Management

 

Knowledge of other DoD Guidance (e.g, DoD Lifecycle Management, DODI 5000.2 and 5000.1) is anticipated. Preference is given to those candidates that have prior experience supporting DoD and specifically; DLA business process mapping and IT audit efforts.

 

Qualifications:

-           BA or BS degree

-           Experience using the FISCAM for technology audits of federal information systems is highly desired

-           Experience with NIST Special Publication 800-53 and FISMA

-           Familiarity with ITIL or ITIL certification is highly desired

-           Strong writing, editing and communications skills required

-           CISA, CISM, CISSP or equivalent IT security certifications is a plus

 

 

Security Clearance:

Candidates must undergo a National Agency Check with Law and Credit (NACLC) investigation.

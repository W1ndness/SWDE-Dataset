Senior Network Engineer

About the Job

Excellent Opportunity for a Motivated Senior Network


Engineer at Litle & Co.!

 

Are you an energetic, multi-talented Senior Network Engineer who has experience in engineering and administering network systems in a multi-site production capacity?  Do you thrive on solving real-world business problems and architecting high-availability solutions?  If you want to work in a fast-paced, small team environment where talents are recognized, collaboration is valued, and results are rewarded, then apply today to take your place on Litle & Co.’s dynamic Network & System Services team!

 

Litle & Co. is the fastest growing player in the payment management industry and is located north of Boston in Lowell, MA.  Founded in 2001, Litle & Co. provides payment processing and consultative merchant services for card-not-present and alternative payments.  Our company has experienced tremendous growth over the past several years, and we have just doubled the square footage of our office space to accommodate this growth.  Excellent benefits, competitive compensation packages, and the use of an on-site, state-of-the-art fitness center free of charge are just a few of the perks offered to Litle & Co. employees.  Submit your resume to hr_resumes@litle.com , and explore all that Litle has to offer!

 

 

Job Description:

 

We are seeking a Senior Network Engineer to join Litle & Co.’s Network and Systems Services team.  The projects identified for 2011 and beyond underscore Litle’s commitment to continuous technology upgrades in support of an infrastructure that requires unprecedented scalability. Systems availability and a focus on security are cornerstone ideals that Litle excels at. This allows Litle to provide a range of high-quality services that not only meet, but exceed our customers’ expectations.

 

 

Responsibilities:

 

  • Work with team members and management to scope, develop and implement strategic technical solutions to meet the business needs
  • Install, maintain and update network infrastructure components  including, but not limited to,  Firewalls, load balancers, routers and switches
  • Review enterprise performance data to proactively propose improvements to the network and infrastructure
  • Investigate and assist in providing solutions to improve overall monitoring, trending and management of the infrastructure
  • Analyze and resolve network problems, including interoperability issues with hardware and software, to ensure IT SLA’s are met
  • Manage relationships with 3rd party providers and vendors, including new product offerings and enhancements, trouble reporting and maintenance support
  • Ensure network processes and systems meet defined Litle security requirements
  • Maintain a broad knowledge of current and emerging state-of-the-art computer/network systems, technologies, architectures, and products

 

Requirements:

 

  • 6+ years experience engineering and administering network systems in a multi-site production capacity
  • Proven skills in architecting high-availability network infrastructures including design, deployment, performance tuning and maintenance
  • Experience in designing, configuring, testing and maintaining DNS, BGP, OSPF and VRRP
  • Deep understanding of best-practices for systems and network security, with successful implementations of firewall, VPN and IDS/IPS products
  • Skilled in monitoring, trending and alerting applications
  • Experience with network analyzing tools
  • Experience with Layer 2/3 switches and load balancing techniques and products at Layer 4 through Layer 7
  • Effective use of QoS techniques and bandwidth management products
  • Experience with WAN technologies e.g. MPLS, F/R, Internet connectivity and deployments with tier 1 carriers
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks within a dynamic environment
  • Keen attention to detail
  • Highly self-motivated and self-directed
  • Strong analytical skills, with a capability to resolve issues in a timely and structured manner

 

Education:

 

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer or IT-related discipline
  • Minimum of 6 years experience working in Network and Telecom, solution defining and support roles within an enterprise environment
  • Professional-level network certifications and/or training desired

 

Preferred skills:


  • Knowledge of VoIP phone systems, e.g., Cisco Call Manager
  • Strong knowledge of Linux systems and network integration
  • Working knowledge of backup and recovery strategies and best practices
  • Experience with WAN optimization solutions

About the Job

Company Summary:

 

Harman International is the worldwide leader in the manufacturing of high-quality, high-fidelity audio and electronic products for automotive, consumer and professional use.  Harman’s Palo Alto office is the home of Aha Radio, the first interactive, on-demand, personalized radio. Aha is safely connecting consumers to their favorite audio and Internet content on demand while they are on-the-go. Harman Palo Alto produces the Aha Radio iPhone application and is currently working with multiple automotive and CE manufacturers to bring personalized content directly to drivers.  The office is led by a team of executives with nearly four decades of experience developing consumer automotive and location-based services applications. Working for Harman Aha gives you the opportunity to be part of the team creating “Radio 2.0” within the environment of an exciting start-up, backed up by the resources and global reach of a multibillion dollar corporation.

 

Job Description:

 

Aha Radio is looking for a Marketing Director to create and manage the marketing and advertising strategy and programs for Aha smartphone applications. This candidate is to utilize techniques such as social media, in-app advertising, viral marketing/word-of-mouth campaigns, and programs utilizing the existing user base to enable Aha to reach and exceed its application download goals.  The candidate is to create marketing requirements documents (MRDs) for Aha products and key features; Perform and maintain a current competitive analysis and create consumer positioning; Create and manage market research program to better understand target consumers, existing consumers, Aha products, competitive products, etc. Plan and oversee (for Aha and collaboratively with partners) new product launches to generate maximum awareness and interest.

 

 Job Requirements:

 

-          8+ years of experience in consumer marketing.

-          Prior experience as a Sr. Director or VP of Marketing at a high technology consumer startup.

-          Experience marketing consumer businesses based on ongoing consumer interaction (such as a subscription business instead of a single purchase).

-          International experience preferred

-          Experience in Mobile, Audio or  Video space is a plus.

-          Team player and able to work collaboratively with Engineering and Design team, as well as Marketing teams at automotive and CE partners

-          Strong knowledge of standard market research analysis techniques.  State-of-the-art understanding of smartphone application store optimization techniques

-          Ability to promote products using awareness through word of mouth

-          Experience utilizing online advertising, social media, and programs with existing customers to drive consumer demand.

-          Metrics and results-driven individual with strong interest in measuring and improving Marketing strategies and tactics

-          Self motivated and driven with strong communication skills

-          Ability to take on the task at the beginning as developing a team

-          Willing to travel

-          Masters Preferred, Bachelors required or equivalent.  MBA is a plus.

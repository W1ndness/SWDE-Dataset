About the Job

Director of Army Programs - Aberdeen Area


We are seeking an exceptional person who is motivated and desires to be empowered, given freedom to grow, learn, and passionately contribute to the success of our customers and the corporation. We have an entrepreneurial environment where individuals can contribute to the growth of the business and financially benefit from their efforts.

The ideal candidate will be a recent (less than 3 years) retired Army O4-O6, with knowledge of Project Manager for Battle Command (PM BC), Common Software, Software Engineering Center, CERDEC (I2WD, C2D) and Army’s Battle Command Systems.  This is a senior management, Director level position with significant opportunity to grow.

Responsibilities include the following:

The candidate is responsible for increasing companys services and presence within the Army's RDECOM at Aberdeen Maryland. Candidate will be the lead for capturing of task orders under IDIQ vehicles, and for recruiting.

The candidate will be engaged in leading the activities of task order proposal teams. He/she will work through the entire opportunity life cycle, including prospecting for new customers, identifying opportunities for growth, leading in teaming decisions, and developing key customer win themes for pre-RFP and post-RFP activities.

The candidate will also be responsible for gaining an understanding of the full scope of solutions and services provided by company (including Software Engineering, SOA, Semantic, Cyber, and R&D services) and representing these services to the customer. He/she will leverage and coordinate the business development activities of company's existing staff.

Following company's Account Plan process, the Director will be responsible for managing and monitoring company's relationship with key RDECOM constituencies and reporting results. The candidate will coordinate, support, and manage customer relationships as required.

Required Skills:

  • TS/SCI clearance is required
  • Ability to seek and close business opportunities with the US Army RDECOM/PEO C3T/CERDEC;
  • Exceptional management skills, ability to lead and inspire technical staff, and the ability to coordinate and lead brainstorming;
  • Demonstrated track record of initiative, creative thinking, and ownership for attaining performance goals;
  • Negotiation skills and the ability to create and deliver convincing presentations;

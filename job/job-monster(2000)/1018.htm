Engineer, Systems Integration & Automation Testing

About the Job

Great vacations begin with great employees!
 
Royal Caribbean Cruises Ltd. is one of the world's leading and revolutionary cruise lines. The company operates under the Royal Caribbean International, Celebrity and Azamara Club brands.
Currently the fleet has 31 ships in service visiting over 180 attractive destinations.
 
Combine your experience and sense of adventure by joining our exciting land-based team of employees. Royal Caribbean Cruises Ltd. is pleased to offer a competitive compensation and benefits package, excellent career development opportunities, each offering unique ways to explore the world.
 
Join our team as an Engineer, Systems Integration & Automation Testing! 
  
The Engineer, Systems Integration & Automation Testing is responsible for the design, development, operation, and maintenance of QA Systems Testing Tools, Processes and Methodology.  
 
Responsibilities and Duties
•Execute QA activities to ensure system functionality is thoroughly tested and meets global business objectives
•Design and build efficient and reusable test cases and test scenarios for functional, regression and performance testing, using automated test suites, as appropriate
•Develop reusable functions to perform common functionality executed in test cases
•Support and enhance existing testing framework, leveraging QA software products and tools, i.e.  QC, LoadRunner, QTP
•Analyze and troubleshoot test script execution issues and test results
•Gain understanding of operating environment, business and applications
 
Financial Responsibilities
•Meets expectations for defined deadlines within budget, schedule and quality
•Tracks effort hours by activity in time management tool
•Some understanding of IT accounting rules for expense and captial tasks and projects
•Understands and participates in IT estimation process/activities


•Bachelor degree in area of speciality or equivalent experience
•3-5 years experience in Software Quality Assurance and Testing field utilizing software testing tools, i.e. HP/Mercury Load Runner, QTP and Quality Center.  Certification with these tools, a plus
•Strong understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC)
•Strong interpersonal, communication, and teamwork skills
•Analytical: highly organized with strong problem resolution skills
•Dynamic: ability to multitask and handle changing priorities, work independently with minimal direction
•Team working skills: must possess ability to interact with peers in a high-bandwidth and collaborative fashion.
•Results orientation: must be delivery focused and exercise good judgment.
•Proven ability in training and knowledge share with team members
•Proven experience using a hybrid automation framework
•Experience with C/C++, Java, multiple OS and RDBMS.  Strong knowledge with database queries (SQL)
•Experience testing in Siebel, AS400, .net, and/or J2EE technology platforms

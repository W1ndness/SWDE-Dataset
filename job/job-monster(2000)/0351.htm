About the Job

Solutions Architect

 

Description

 

As a Solutions Architect you will be the most senior technical member of software development team.  You will be responsible for managing a team of developers through the entire software development lifecycle to ensure the delivery high quality software solutions.  You will be responsible for the "big picture" and take an active, multi-disciplinary governance role on software development projects. This will involve formulating the solution for a project, oversight of design, development, and testing, as well as planning the packaging and ultimate deployment of the solution. 

 

Responsibilities

 

·         Lead numerous software development projects (product development, client implementation, and platform innovation)

·         Lead technical teams through entire software development lifecycle

·         Develop a comprehensive understanding of the Client's platform

·         Develop subject matter expertise in provider management

·         Establish yourself as a trusted technical advisor with key internal and external stakeholders

·         Forge relationships with and influence clients’ senior technical staff

·         Analyze architecture requirements and design technical solutions

·         Advise on technical architecture and deployment options

·         Collaborate on analysis, design, development, testing, and deployment decisions with Client’s consulting and engineering teams to ensure the quality and consistency of Client’s software

·         Provide technical assistance, mentorship and guidance to technical project team members

·         Accountable for software development project planning, management, and quality

·         Develop a thorough understanding of the organization’s technology goals and objectives

 

 

Position Requirements

 

Knowledge & Experience

·         Thorough understanding of enterprise software architecture, internet technologies, IT systems, and infrastructure

·         Knowledge of the health insurance industry and provider management discipline is a big plus

·         Knowledge of payer, hospital, and physician practice core systems a big plus

·         Experience designing and developing high-performance web applications

·         Experience working in and managing the entire software development lifecycle (SDLC)

·         Expertise in estimating, planning, and managing software development projects

·         Strong understanding of project management principles and processes

·         Strong application design skills (UML) and understanding of enterprise software design patterns

·         Thorough understanding of object-oriented software principles

·         Experience with markup languages (XML, HTML, CSS)

·         Experience with scripting languages (Javascript, Jython)

·         Experience with Java technologies (JSP, Servlets, Applets, EJB, Swing, JDBC)

·         Experience with relational database design and modeling

·         Experience with SQL database management systems (Oracle, SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL)

·         Experience with enterprise application integration

·         Experience installing and configuring enterprise software

·         In-depth knowledge of applicable data privacy practices and laws, including HIPAA

·         5+ years experience in full project lifecycle covering of large scale Object Oriented systems or vendor package implementations in a consulting environment

·         Bachelors or Masters degree; Computer Science, Engineering or Management Information Systems

 

Personal Attributes

·         Passion for cutting-edge software design and development

·         Strong leadership and management skills

·         Highly self-motivated and directed

·         Excellent written and oral communication skills

·         Keen attention to detail

·         Proven analytical, evaluative, and problem-solving abilities

·         Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment

·         Exceptional customer service orientation

·         Extensive experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment

·         Willingness to mentoring and train junior technical staff

·         Willingness to support sales activities, including assisting with RFPs, conference calls, and product demonstrations

·         Willingness to support resolution of escalated technical support issues

·         Willingness to travel to nationwide client locations

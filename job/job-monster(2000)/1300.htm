Senior Systems Analyst, Oracle Systems and applications

About the Job



Overview



C&S Wholesale Grocers is a wholesale distributor to grocery chains and independent stores throughout the United States. As the largest food wholesaler in the United States, we store and distribute more than 53,000 food and non-food products for our customers.



Anything else? Yes several state of the art automation and robotic engineering projects that will take C&S and our Industry into the next century. Setting a standard that our competitors will not be able to match.







C&S is committed to advancing technology in all of its operations, currently moving to Enterprise based platforms and software. Our facilities feature state of the art warehouse, Labor and transportation management systems, Voice recognition software, robotics, web-based technology and integrated logistics systems enabling C&S the flexibility to redefine the food supply chain industry.



Job Summary:

The Senior Systems Analyst for Forecast Systems is a results oriented position and is responsible for the high-value delivery of IT-enabled forecasting solutions to demand planning end-users and other related stakeholders. This is a technical "hands on" development role.



Must have multiple years of designing, building, and delivering custom applications to solve complex business problems using Oracle SQL, PL/SQL, and Apex or some other UI tool.



  • From start day, candidate will learn, support, and enhance custom Oracle PL/SQL/Apex rules engine/application.
  • Learn, support, and enhance Oracle Demantra application.
  • Work primarily focused on processing, enhancing, and normalizing large amounts of data so it can be present to down stream operational applications, Forecasting, Purchasing, etc.
  • learn and support the forecasting business process of C&S




The qualified candidate must possess strong communication skills, both verbal and written, as well as exceptional analytical and time management capabilities.







Responsibilities



Job Responsibilities:

* Follow the project lifecycle methodology on assigned projects and tasks.

* Work with all business and IT constituents in a positive, supportive, and collaborative team manner.

* Define the Functionality, Usability, Reliability, Performance, and Supportability requirements of critical software components.

* Analyze design alternatives, and make appropriate design decisions.

* Direct the preparation of functional and technical software specifications that reflect an in depth understanding of the business and end-user requirements while also considering IT standards and technical direction.

* Assess risk, cost, schedule, and resource trade offs when considering alternative IT solutions.

* Complete project and department deliverables as directed by IT leadership.

* Work closely with other IT and business personnel to execute project work plans and prepare project deliverables.

* Provide functional support for on-call related issues.

* Be responsive and sensitive to customer issues and needs.





Qualifications



Job Requirements:





* Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, technical, or analytical major.

* 5-8 years of Oracle or relational database development experience required



*Advanced SQL and PL/SQL skills are required.



*Experience working with and processing large data volumes required



*Advanced Apex development skills are preferred.



*3-5 years of consulting and/or Supply Chain experience highly desired.

*Experience with database procedure tuning and optimization required



* Experience with custom and package software systems.

* Must possess strong oral and written communication skills.

* Proven ability to deliver IT solutions through the entire systems development lifecycle (requirements, design, build, test, deploy, and support).

* Ability to fully document user requirements, process designs, functional/technical designs, and test plans.

* Knowledge / experience with a variety of technical environments including UNIX, web technologies, database technologies

* Experience with web-based architectures and service oriented architectures (SOA) a plus.

* Experience with Oracle Application Server preferred



Experience with Ascential/IBM DataStage, or other data interface processing a plus.

* Knowledge / experience with reporting, data warehousing and business intelligence tools (e.g., Teradata, MicroStrategy, Crystal Reports/Business Objects) a plus.

Software Engineer: GWT / Java Web Developer (Downtown Mountain View, CA)

About the Job

About Remilon

Remilon is a highly profitable, fast growing and financially stable web media company on a mission to make education accessible to everyone through technology innovation. We began our journey in 2002 and are entirely self-funded, so we have full control over the direction we go in. We are located in the heart of Downtown Mountain View, just a few steps from the CalTrain Station. We only hire top talent, so those who pass our rigorous code test and interview process will find themselves a part of one of the brightest and most innovative teams in Silicon Valley. We have a culture of social responsibility. As we post record profits, we give back by funding scholarships for needy students and helping education charity organizations provide open access to information throughout the poorest regions of the world.

Join our growing team as a Java Software Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, deploy, manage and maintain multiple critical high-availability GWT applications that help run our day-to-day business
  • Participate in system design, architecture decisions and lead regular code reviews
  • Work in a highly interactive, team-oriented environment
  • Actively find ways to improve software (evaluate and incorporate new libraries, tools and technologies; code reviews; refactoring; testing; etc.)
  • Find simple, robust solutions to complex problems
  • Become a key member and decision-maker in our top-notch engineering team

Requirements:

  • 2+ years experience in GWT, Swing, or related technologies
  • 2+ years experience in Java and J2EE technologies (Spring, Hibernate, Resin, JDBC, JMS, JMX)
  • Mastery of front-end technologies like JavaScript, AJAX, JQuery, etc.
  • Advanced XHTML, CSS knowledge
  • Mastery of object oriented design, design patterns, data structures and multithreaded algorithms
  • Strong background in relational database, SQL query optimization and tuning (MySQL preferred)
  • Working knowledge of UNIX or Linux
  • Self-motivated, detail-oriented and strong analytical / problem solving skills
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment while juggling multiple projects
  • Eligibility to work in the US without sponsorship

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced Resin and/or Tomcat knowledge
  • Experience with service oriented architectures (SOA)
  • Experience with content management systems
  • Paired programming and agile development experience
  • Experience with EHCache, Terracotta, Maven or Ant build scripts
  • Use of Confluence or other Wiki for documentation and specifications
  • JUnit framework, JMeter, HtmlUnit or other testing frameworks and harnesses
  • Mastery of IntelliJ and/or Eclipse IDE
  • Exposure to online marketing, search engine optimization and lead generation

Benefits:

  • Great medical and dental plans, covered 100% by us
  • Flexible PTO starting at 20 days per year (and up to 30 days)
  • Additional 10 days of paid holidays annually
  • Flexible workday schedule
  • Competitive salary and compensation plans
  • 401k retirement plan with 4% employer matching
  • Stocked kitchen with food and beverages provided
  • Fully paid for gym membership
  • Company sponsored Friday happy hour

Why work for Remilon?

  • Have your work impact 3+ million web visitors each month
  • Help make education accessible to people who need it
  • Fun, casual and collaborative working environment
  • Profitable and financially stable company
  • Visibility and input into company decisions and direction
  • See results of your work in days, not years
  • Commitment to employee training and skill development
  • Comfortable sit/stand, dual- 24 inch monitor workstations
  • Work with other talented and passionate people

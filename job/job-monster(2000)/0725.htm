Labor Management System Analyst/Programmer, Systems Analyst - IT, Lakeland

About the Job




INTRODUCING PUBLIX

Come join a company that has built its reputation on providing premier customer service. For years, Publix has been the top-ranking supermarket chain in the nation on the American Customer Satisfaction Index.

Come join a company that treats associates as family. Thanks to our founder, George Jenkins, all eligible associates are shareholders in Publix. Our annual survey of associates shows strong job satisfaction. Many in our family stay with us for more than 20 years. No wonder we have been on Fortune magazine's "100 Best Companies to Work For" list 13 consecutive years!

Come join a company whose continual growth provides ongoing opportunities for advancing your career. Publix has 143,000 associates, more than 1,000 stores in Florida, Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina and Tennessee, and continually opens new stores. Our support system of manufacturing plants, distribution centers and corporate offices are expanding to serve that growth.

Come join a company that is financially sound, ethically strong and committed to diversity in all respects.


Department: Information Systems

Position Description:
• Gathering and modeling requirements through business process modeling and use cases in a modeling tool
• Designing the application solution
• Diagnosing performance issues in UI, batch, network and database
• Providing program specifications and direction to other developers
• Planning and coordinating Change Management and Release Management functions during project development
• Providing consultation to various teams and areas
• Projecting team participation, and
• Instructing, directing, and verifying the work of other within Information Systems.
Required Qualifications:
• B.A. or B.S. degree or equivalent experience
• Minimum of three years experience in analysis, design and coding
• Minimum of three years experience using VB.Net and ASP.Net (Visual Studio 2003,2005 and 2008)
• Minimum of three years experience with SSRS (Visual Studio 2003,2005 and 2008)
• Minimum of two years experience with MS SQL Server 2005 or higher
• Minimum three years experience in XML, HTML, DHTML,CSS and JavaScript
• Minimum of three years experience with Powershell and VBScript
• Minimum three years experience implementing SOA/web services
• Knowledge of multithreading in VB.Net, and common design patterns in OO development
• Experience in producing and maintaining design documents including the use of UML and logical flows
• Experience with integrating in-house systems with commercial off-the-shelf systems and working with message queues
• Cross platform knowledge across SAN, Network, database and application tiers
• High degree of self-motivation, commitment, and integrity
• Demonstrated ability to work under minimal supervision
• Good human relation skills (the ability to work well with others and to be a team member)
• Must show enthusiasm, initiative, pride in work and a
• Commitment to Publix and our Mission.
Preferred Qualifications:
• Knowledge of Retail business.

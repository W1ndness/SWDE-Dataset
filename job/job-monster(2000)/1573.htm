Senior Network & Security Engineer

About the Job

CashEdge Inc., the leading provider of Intelligent Money Movement™ services enables financial institutions to provide a single point of access for multiple easy-to-use consumer and small business transfer capabilities.  Our state of the art technology enables inter-institution money movement, person to person email payments, instant online account opening and funding and risk management services. CashEdge also provides account aggregation for both retail end users and investment advisors. 

As we continue to grow we are seeking a highly experienced Senior Network & Security Engineer to join our highly professional Operations team in Sunnyvale, CA.  Be an integral member of a team working on high-volume, highly secure, web-enabled commerce systems in the financial industries sector. 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

·         Help define overall network and systems security strategies, policies and procedures

·         Develop and review system and network designs to ensure compliance with corporate security policies and security best practices

·         Immediate 24X7 response to critical site incidents 

·         Evaluate new and emerging security threats, products and technologies

·         Deploy, manage and use intrusion prevention systems and event correlation engines.

·         Perform internal audits and reviews to ensure compliance with all security policies and procedures

·         Work with external parties (SAS70; PCI, etc) to conduct security Audits and develop plans for addressing audit recommendations

·         Help develop and refine Disaster Recovery Plans

·         Conduct internal penetration tests

·         Design and maintain hosting and corporate network and telecommunications infrastructure

·         Develop, implement and maintain network intrusion detection systems

·         Represent security interests on system development project teams

·         Will require some travel providing support extension to other CashEdge offices

To be considered, you should possess the following:

Technical:

·         Data center experience is a must

·         Must have in-depth knowledge of high end Cisco Pix, Juniper Netscreen Firewall and IDS

·         A minimum of 8 years experience in Hosting Operations

·         More than 5 years of experience in networking with previous experience in a 24x7

·         Experience working with Cisco networking hardware

·         ASP-based production environment supporting multiple hosting sites

·         Strong understanding of network security concepts, technologies and exploits

·         Solid understanding of network protocols, TCP/IP explicitly, OSI Model

·         Traffic shaping and QoS

·         Experience in routing protocols, BGP, EIGRP, VRRP, VRRPE, HSRP and OSPF

·         Experience in WAN environments, installing and troubleshooting circuit problems (MPLS, OCxx, T1, DS3)

·         Experience in creating and troubleshooting IPSEC tunnels on Cisco IOS and Cisco Concentrators

·         Experience in F5 BIG-IP load balancers and GDM

·         In-depth understanding of internal and external DNS technologies

·         Firewalls & route filtering policy experience

·         Tunneling, IPSEC protocols through the Internet (e.g. AHP, ESP, ISAKMP), SSL VPN, ShorTel VOIP

·         IDS and IPS setup, tuning signatures and management

·         TACACS+ setup and control

·         Experience in using network tools/analyzers, Sniffer, Ethereal, tcpdump, syslog analysis

·         Review new security Vulnerabilities

·         Experience in performing internal and external network vulnerability assessments and penetration test and developing reports

·         Some scripting and programming experience.

·         Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or MIS

Non-Technical

·         Enthusiasm and Interest in Information Security

·         Strong sense of professionalism and ethics

·         Ability to communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing to technical and non-technical audiences

·         Position requires on call 7x24 hour support and immediate responses to emergencies

·         Requires effective communication with internal and external customers

·         Requires project management experience

·         Must adhere to all standard operating procedures

·         Must be able to effectively operate as a member of a cross-functional team

Additional experience in the following is helpful:

·         Security Certification such as CISSP or SSCP

·         Experience working at a high volume financial transaction or website company

·         Experience in designing, implementing and maintaining a multi-site financial transaction or Web service

·         Experience designing, implementing and maintaining a global MPLS network (VOIP, Video)

·         Experience with Avaya telecommunications solutions

·         SOX, SAS70, PCI Audit experience

CashEdge is looking for a dynamic individual who thrives in a challenging work environment and is looking for the opportunity to really learn and grow their career.  The successful candidate will receive a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package.  Just as important, CashEdge offers a stimulating and culturally rich work environment. 

For immediate consideration, please forward a cover letter and resume to USAcareers@cashedge.com .  Be sure to indicate “Senior Network & Security Engineer” as the position for which you are applying.

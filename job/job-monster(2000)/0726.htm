Position Summary:


This position is located within the Professional Services division of Fiserv. It is primarily a customer engagement based software development position that requires advanced technical, organizational, and communication skills.   The Staff Software Developer position is responsible for troubleshooting code defects with existing production systems as well as designing and developing internet based on-line banking applications.  This work generally will occur under the guidance of a lead development or project manager.


 


The following requirements apply to the Staff Software Developer role. 


 


Key Basic Requirements:


·         Bachelor’s degree required with a degree in Computer Science preferred, or equivalent combination of education and experience.


·         Minimum of 3 years of software development experience with two of those years spent designing and developing internet e-commerce or e-finance applications.


  • Experienced ASP.NET and C# developer.

  • Experienced in XML, XML Schemas, and Web Service technology.

  • Strong web UI design and development experience

  • Strong integration design and development experience

  • Strong problem solving skills

  • Experienced in designing and developing solutions for high volume internet sites

  • Strong architecture skills

  • Strong Analysis, design and build skills

  • Minimum of 2 years of project development / methodologies experience: full system development lifecycle, Agile, etc

  • Experienced in writing stored procedures within Microsoft SQL Server.

  • Experienced in developing and documenting use cases using Rational software.

  • Experienced in conducting development activities using stringent source code control procedures.

  • Experienced in developing estimates for design, development, and unit testing of application functionality for the engagement.

  • Willingness and ability to travel up to 50% of the time.

Optional:


  • Financial services industry expertise

  • 3rd party integration experience with the following:
    • Symitar

    • TSYS

    • FDR

    • FIServ

    • Summit

    • XP

  • Project management / team lead experience

 


Team Involvement and Leadership:


·         Possesses the ability to track progress against assigned tasks, report status, and proactively identify issues to the engagement management team.


·         Work successfully in a team environment and demonstrate a willingness to help team members in achieving their project goals if required.


·         Possess a keen sense of personal ownership and accountability for delivering assigned tasks and deliverables within the established schedule.


·         Facilitate a strong and supportive team environment that enables the team as well as individual team members to overcome any political, bureaucratic and/or resource barriers to participation.


 


Communication:


·         Possess advanced written and oral communication skills for producing software design specifications and conducting JAD sessions with customers.


·         Provide professional, clear, unambiguous, and complete written and verbal communication such that the intended audience receives information correctly.


·         Demonstrate the ability to present information effectively in communications with peers and senior project management.


·         Identify and collaborate with all necessary stakeholders (internal and external) in order to come to terms or reach agreement in accordance with defined project goals with little to no assistance.

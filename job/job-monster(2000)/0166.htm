Lotus Notes Administrator

About the Job

McCormick & Co., Inc., a world leader in the spice, flavor & seasonings industry, is seeking a full time Lotus Notes Administrator in its Global Business Solutions Division located in Hunt Valley, Maryland. 

 

As an employer recognized for our exceptional commitment to employees, McCormick & Co., Inc. offers a wide variety of benefits, programs and services which address the needs of employees and their families.  McCormick’s benefits include, but are not limited to tuition assistance, medical & dental, disability, group life insurance, 401(k), pension plan, paid holidays and vacations.

 

DESCRIPTION/RESPONSIBILITES:

Responsibilities of this position include providing administrative and technical support to ensure the stability of electronic communications including internal and external email, Collaboration and Domino server maintenance.  Other responsibilities of this position include:

•           Supporting employee access to email and applications

•           Troubleshooting complicated user issues, mail client issues, and basic server problems

•           Administer application deployment, server configuration and standards for Domino environment

•           Developing and maintaining written procedures for routine administration including ACL management, replication schedules, policy deployments and security.

•           Resolving problems by working with McCormick divisions and subsidiaries worldwide to troubleshoot, communicate and provide timely follow-up on problem resolutions.

•           Providing Notes/Domino technical support and assistance for customers and the application development group and assumes responsibility for related projects as required.

•           Administering the Blackberry environment and assisting with deployment of new devices and mobile applications.

•           Working with Web Services team to develop and deliver ongoing strategy of messaging and collaboration.

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

•           High School Diploma or GED equivalent required.

•           Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field preferred.

•           Lotus Notes Certification (CLP) preferred. ICSA – LN&D 7 or higher preferred

•           5+ yrs experience supporting Lotus Notes/Domino server environments with experience in advanced troubleshooting within those environments preferred.

•           Proven knowledge and experience with Notes/Domino relevant platforms and experience with alternative interfaces preferred

•           Experience working with SMTP and mobile device issues required

•           Strong customer service focus required

•           Good communication skills. Able to communicate technical information to non-technical users required.

•           Proven ability to work effectively individually or as part of a team required

•           Good analytical skills as well as evaluative judgment based on the analysis of factual and qualitative information in complicated or novel situations required.

•           Ability to exchange ideas and information in a concise and logical way, and be sensitive to audience diversity required.

•           Experience working with collaboration tools outside of message include instant messaging, wikis, forums and social networking preferred

•           Knowledge of Black berry Enterprise Server and the administration of accounts and blackberry devices preferred.

 

If interested, please visit www.mccormickcorporation.com and apply for position #21574.

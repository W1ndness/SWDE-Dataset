Entry-level Software Developer

About the Job

For more than 30 years, libraries have used SirsiDynix technologies to bring the power of their collections to their communities. We are firmly committed to our library customers and the communities they serve, but that commitment extends beyond software and technical support – it means genuine service, to our libraries and to the industry. Together with libraries, we help communities thrive by connecting people with library resources that educate and inspire.


We offer an exciting and challenging work environment with excellent benefits. We're currently looking for a sharp, energetic and ambitious individual to join our team. We have full-time, entry-level opportunities at our Provo, Utah office for a Software Engineers.

Description


Our Associate Software Engineers focus on new development and maintenance of existing applications. Principal work activities include the analysis and design, implementation, testing and maintenance of J2EE, C/C++, Java code and technologies. The engineer will be required to develop technical specifications based on business requirements and to provide estimates for assigned tasks.


Primary Responsibilities
  • Create code that meets design specifications, follows standards, and is easy to maintain. When required, makes suggestions for new designs
  • Responsible for all of the unit tests for all code components produced
  • Ensure that all enhancements, bug fixes, and new products are documented
  • Work with various teams in the organization to ensure appropriate project requirements are met
  • Teach other non-technical customers/other team members information about the code
  • Participate in a Development team that is responsible for developing quality software
  • Create product using a “test-driven design” approach including the creation and execution of identified test cases. Candidate is expected to automate test cases where possible
  • With experience, candidate will be expected to research problems, research various solutions, and devise a work plan to solve these problems.

Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Math or Physics
  • Programming course work in common languages such as Java and C/C++
  • Completion of a minimum of 3 projects where programming languages are put to use to solve problems
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to reason logically and provide solutions
  • Project oriented
  • Detail oriented
  • Ability to work well within group or individually on projects
  • Self-reliant / self-starter


For more information, please visit our website at www.sirsidynix.com or apply online at http://www.sirsidynix.com/Careers /.

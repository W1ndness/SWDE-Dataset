About the Job

 
SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT ENGINEER

Spirent Communications is where the world’s leading communications companies turn when they need to accelerate their time-to-market for next-generation communication systems. In fact, every significant industry test in the last six years has used Spirent's test systems as the benchmark. We are continually recognized by customers, industry, press, and technology associations for our innovative thinking and our ground-breaking products. The communications market has untapped potential. The boundaries of how fast, far, and accurately voice and data can be transmitted have yet to be reached, or even defined. Spirent solutions are paving the way for tomorrow’s communications.

The atmosphere at Spirent is innovative, creative, and technically challenging. We are always looking for new employees with exceptional abilities, vision, and commitment levels to join our team.

If you want to work with truly bright and motivated people—and be a part of an industry-leading company that's helping to revolutionize communications technologies—this is your opportunity. Our focus on employee satisfaction stems from a core belief that market-leading products start with one thing: market-leading people. At Spirent, we offer more than jobs, we offer unique career opportunities. We can do this because we are large enough to encompass a breadth of possibilities, while being small enough for you to be noticed and recognized. Are you innovative enough to work at Spirent?

Software development engineer with experience in system integration products using Java, C++, and a variety of tools spanning design to coding to testing and deployment. Plans, designs, develops and tests software systems or applications for software enhancements and new products.

Major Responsibilities:

1. Development of system integration products using Java, C++, and a variety of tools spanning design to coding to testing and deployment.

2. Analysis of third party connectivity and integration issues leading to creation of new interfaces between products in the fast paced telecom domain.

3. Work as part of team of Engineers working on leading edge products.

4. Development and Implementation on UNIX and Windows platforms.


QUALIFICATIONS INCLUDE:
BS Degree Required, MS Degree desirable.


Applicant must have 3-6+ years experience in System Analysis and Design, with full life cycle software development experience.

Well versed in Java and C++ with emphasis on Java, scalability, and platform independent architectures.

Well versed in web development and application server lifecycle emphasizing Java Server Pages, Java Servlets, Tomcat, Struts, JavaScript and Hibernate. Familiarity with CSS, YUI, AJAX, and Applets a plus.

Well versed in data development and tools utilizing XSLT, XML, SQL databases, Hibernate, and JDBC.

Experience with web services and services oriented architectures using SOAP, WSDL, Axis, and JAXB.

Enterprise bus experience with emphasis on JMS and MQ.

Familiarity with J2EE architecture and JBoss is a plus.

Strong developer experience including IDE, Ant, Agile and accountable, Junit, SOAPUI, Jmeter, regression, OO design, and UML.

Strong multi-platform experience including PC, emphasis on UNIX, OS lifecycle, compatibility, including shell, and Perl scripting.

Experience with system integration products such as billing, testing, workflow, ticket systems, and client/server technologies including CORBA, JMS, and SOAP.

Familiarity with software configuration management concepts.
Familiarity with OSI Model, network topology, protocols and tools including IP, protocol analysis, and HTTP application layer.

Familiarity with Rules-based tools, or Telecommunications quality assurance, or inventory tools.

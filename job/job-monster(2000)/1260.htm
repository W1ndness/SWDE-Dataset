About the Job

We have a Minneapolis client with an immediate, full time, permanent need for an experienced C++/Windows developer with solid communications experience. Key skills are C# and C++ programming in a Windows PC environment and solid Communications Protocol (TCP/IP) experience (ideally) in a regulated industry. The person in this position designs, develops, operates, maintains and documents software and firmware components and computing systems software to be applied to engineering and manufacturing requirements. Applies the appropriate standards, processes, procedures and tools throughout the system development life cycle. Assignments may not have clear and specified objectives and require the investigation of a large number of variables requiring knowledge of principals, practices and procedures in software engineering.

 

Duties Include:

·    Conducts system and software design engineering activities of moderate-to-great scope or degree of difficulty (medium software component/system associated with a project), under minimal supervision from the direct supervisor or an experienced member of the engineering staff or Program Manager.

·    Maintains communications within and outside of department in support of software design and development activities, such as project management meetings.  Actively participates in and contributes to decision-making process.

·    Prepares documentation associated with software design engineering activities such as Software Requirements Specifications, Software Design documents, and Software Validation testing procedures.

 

The skills and experiences our client would like to see this individual possess include a solid knowledge of principles, practices, and procedures related to software design engineering in order to complete assignments of moderate to great difficulty.  Knowledge is evidenced by past job progression/performance.

 


 

Our client is a world leader in the medical device field and is an excellent place to work with excellent full benefits and opportunity for career advancement.

About the Job

At United States Steel Corporation, our strength is our people. Our 45,000 dedicated, diverse and innovative employees across North America and Europe contribute to our company in creative ways every day and have helped us maintain our position as a Fortune 500 company. U. S. Steel’s operations are efficient and high tech and our customer focus intense. We’ve been making steel for more than 105 years, always with an eye to making it better, faster and more cost effectively. U. S. Steel has a Vision for the future – Making Steel. World Competitive - Building Value.



U. S. Steel has an immediate opening for a server software administrator within its IT infrastructure organization located in Pittsburgh, PA. The associated duties, involving server-based software support, would include the responsibilities listed below.

Major Responsibilities:
Duties include, but are not limited to, the following:
Supporting multiple Oracle SOA and JDA/i2 server software suite environments
Supporting other 3rd-party server software products on Unix and Windows server platforms
Installing, upgrading, and patching of designated server-based software
Interaction with application groups, software vendor support, and infrastructure support to troubleshoot problems and resolve software-related system issues
Developing test plans, system documentation, and disaster recovery planning for designated software systems
Conducting compliance reviews for proposed or changing application systems.
On-call responsibilities
 



Minimum Requirements: To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below:

Minimum of one year of experience with IBM WebSphere/Oracle WebLogic application
servers and with Microsoft IIS/Apache webservers.
Minimum of one year of experience in Windows 2003 and Unix server administration
Experience with SQL language and kornshell/perl/windows/Visual Basic scripting
Extensive computer skills with Microsoft Office products
Preferred Qualifications:
Aptitude for quickly learning complex technical systems
Strong organizational and interpersonal skills
Strong troubleshooting skills
Proficient verbal and written communication skills, including effective presentation skills
Experience in application programming (.NET and JAVA) and networking is advantageous
Minimum of one year of experience supporting Oracle SOA server environments
Education:
Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related area
Critical Competencies for Success:
The ability to form working relationships with technical and business customers, as well as the ability to work across functional areas to identify and resolve issues
The ability to understand and support complex server software systems
Possessing excellent attention to detail
Highly self-motivated and flexible
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
The ability to prioritize and handle competing work requests

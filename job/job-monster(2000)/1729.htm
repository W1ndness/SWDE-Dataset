Software Configuration Management Expert

About the Job

CGI Federal is looking for a Software Configuration Management Expert to work in our Cambridge, MA location. 
 
The Software Configuration Management Expert must possess a strong knowledge and demonstrated experience in setting up and operating a software configuration management process in line with industry best standards (Project Management Institute, CMMI, etc.)  The Expert works with managers and project staff to identify issues and to provide vision and strategic direction as required.
 
The Expert should also be knowledgeable in systems/software development life cycle (SDLC) processes in line with industry best standards (CMMI, etc).  The Expert is recognized for business and/or architectural expertise in adapting transportation systems functional business requirements and processes into technical solutions based on an understanding of the overall enterprise architecture and environment. The Expert provides insight into IT and technologies that sets the direction and establishes an approach for technical solutions and has the expertise needed to perform and resolve functional, system, and architectural gap analyses. The Expert is current in technology and industry trends that affect transportation enterprise solution sets, including technical platforms and network architectures. The Expert may lead technical design reviews,
validate enterprise approaches, define application systems that support redesigned or improved business processes, recommend technical architectures that lead to comprehensive business
solutions, and assess work products. The Expert has knowledge of and is able to apply Federal standards and other guidelines related to the development of secure systems and Section 508 compliant systems.


Requirements
Master Degree or equivalent experience and a minimum of 10 years of directly related work experience with subject matter (e.g., transportation systems, IT, security). At least three of the most recent five years of experience must be focused in senior-level SDLC activities as network architect. Twelve (12) years  of direct related experience can be substituted with a Bachelors degree and Fifteen (15) years of direct related experience can be substituted in lieu of degree.  Candidate must have set up and participated in a software Configuration Management process.
 
Additional Requirements
  • Ability to successfully complete the Stanley Background Investigation to include; 50 State Criminal, Education and Employment; Additionally, your ability to successfully complete a Credit and/or Driving Records Checks may be conducted if required by the contract for continued employment. 
  • Ability to successfully complete a Government Agency Background Investigation/Security Clearance; US Citizenship is required
 Preferred
  • Experience with existing or in development Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) Information Systems.
  • Possess and maintain currency with professional Project Management Professional (PMP) certification or equivalent professional certification as
    provided thru the Project Management Institute.

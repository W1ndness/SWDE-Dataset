C++ Design Engineer

About the Job

The position is below, Must have a heavy amount of C++ experience in a unix/linux environment (at least 5 years), large scale application development and real time development.

Position Requirements
 
Basic Purpose of Position
To interpret customer or in-house developed specifications into a detailed engineering design document that conforms to company design standards, level of detail, accuracy and completeness thereby  allowing  application engineers to perform coding and testing tasks with minimal design flaws and minimal re-work based on a detailed design document


Responsibilities

•  Develop well defined, accurate detail design documents that meet customer (internal and/or external) requirements
•  Provide thorough level of research to ensure detail design documents contain accurate, timely, and fully defined information
•  Adheres to company/department standards and processes.
•  Communicates and participates in design reviews
•  Lead discussions with application engineers in providing an understanding and reviewing design documents
•  Participates in code reviews to ensure implementation matches the design. Updates Design Document as required.
•  Work with System Engineers and Architects for completeness and accuracy of designs to meet requirements
•  When necessary perform application engineering tasks
•  Mentor lower level design engineers as required


Essential Skills
•  Excellent  problem solving, planning, oral communication and analytical skills
•  Excellent written communication skills
•  Excellent object oriented skills
•  Excellent team skills
•  Detail focused
•  Customer focused
•  Schedule/ Quality  driven
•  Excellent task estimating skills for work effort
•  Strong debugging skills – logical and analytical views
•  Ability to leverage existing designs and design patterns to promote re-use and consistency


Required Technical Skills
•  Proficient with C++ in a Unix/Linux environment
•  Large scale applications development using full lifecycle
•  Real Time development (not embedded systems)
•  3 Tier Architecture Experience


Preferred Technical Skills
•  Standard Template Library (STL)
•  Oracle Database
•  X Designer
•  Clearcase
•  Clearquest


Education and Experience
•  Minimum  5-7 years experience with OOD and C++  
•  Experience as a application engineer,  as a design engineer
•  Bachelor Degree in AS /Engineering/IT
•  Technical Courses in Designing Systems, design development.

HVAC Controls Engineer & Programmer

About the Job

Guardian Energy Management Solutions provides companies with solutions designed to reduce their operating costs through energy efficiency strategies. As a Tridium Vykon System Integrator, we are seeking an experienced system engineer/programmer to lead our HVAC control design and implementation efforts. The following requirements apply to this position:

* Experience in designing, programming, implementing and commissioning DDC systems for HVAC automation.
* Must be Niagara AX certified and 2+ years of programming experience required.
* Experience integrating Tridium Niagara into other control systems a huge plus.
* Understanding of HVAC systems, components and controls.
* Team player with 'can-do' attitude.
* Independent thinker with experience in successful DDC implementations.
* Involved in full process life cycle of a project - including presales, sales, installation and start up.
* Hands on programming and start up experience.
* Able to review HVAC prints or make onsite assessment of existing equipment.
* Strong organizational skills are a necessity.

If you are searching for an enjoyable working environment with a flexible schedule, we are looking for a key individual to help our continued growth and profitability. This position includes salary commensurate with experience plus excellent benefits.

Please send your resume with cover letter for consideration. Note: Please include salary requirements or your application will not be reviewed.

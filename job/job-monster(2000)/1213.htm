Network Analyst

About the Job

CommScope is a global leader in connectivity solutions for communications networks. We provide infrastructure solutions for wireless, business enterprise, residential broadband and carrier wireline networks. CommScope is the behind-the-scenes connection that helps the world communicate — at work, at home and on the go.

By committing to integrity, innovation, quality and performance and, by building these values into every CommScope transaction, we give our customers the power to help their customers communicate where, when and how they choose.

Founded in Hickory, North Carolina more than 30 years ago, CommScope and its worldwide team of more than 13,000 employees create infrastructure solutions for communications networks in more than 130 countries.

IT is currently recruiting for a qualified, interested and available Network Analyst located in Hickory, NC.

Must be able to relocate to the CommScope facility in Hickory, NC.

JOB SUMMARY:

Implement, manage and support communications services and systems for all CommScope facilities. Review system and network performance, take corrective actions when required and provide recommendations for improvements. Manage projects that add functionality, increase reliability and availability, and decrease time-to-repair within the corporate network.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

·         Analyze, troubleshoot and resolve problems with global communications systems and services used by the corporation.

·         Provide ongoing support for daily operation of voice and data systems, services and networks used in production, both directly and through the services of vendors. Review system performance of Wide Area Network (WAN), data systems, voice systems and associated trunks and services. Document potential changes to the corporate communications systems, while focusing on network optimization, corporate requirements, long-term network integrity and appropriate level of benefit.

·         Project manage implementations of data communications networks, voice/video services and associated equipment. Work with various departments to establish business solutions utilizing communications systems and services.

·         Participate in development and operation of voice and data network management systems, tools and methodologies. Organize and maintain integrity of data used by these systems and processes.

 

EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:

A Bachelors degree is required. Telecommunications, Computer Science or other related technical discipline is preferred.

5 years minimum working experience in the implementation and maintenance of data communication required.

Advanced certification in one or more of the following is strongly desired:

Cisco routers, switches, and firewalls

data communications

Riverbed link optimization

Avaya PBX / Voice Mail

TCP/IP protocols

BGP routing

QoS

Good working knowledge of Microsoft Office and Visio required.

Must have extensive knowledge of Wide Area Networks (WAN), Cisco IOS command line router configuration, firewalls and TCP/IP protocol.

The ideal candidate will have a high degree of diplomacy and ability to negotiate with persons at all levels of authority and technical ability.

Must have outstanding communication (both written and verbal) skills. Must be able to communicate clearly and accurately in English.

Must be self-driven and a strong team player.

Must be able to relocate to the CommScope facility in Hickory, NC.

Manager, Business Consulting - Murex

About the Job

Job Description:
Sapient Global Markets

At Sapient Global Markets, we are quite literally on the front lines of the biggest issues facing the global markets leaders today – investment banks, asset management firms, wealth managers, oil and energy firms, and government and regulatory agencies. Driving transformation and leading change within these industries requires big thinkers who bring insight, knowledge, technical acumen and passion for innovation. What we offer is the opportunity to join a recognized leader in developing and delivering groundbreaking solutions for today’s dynamic global marketplace and an award-winning culture that fosters growth and leadership.

Title: Manager, Business Consulting – Murex

JOB SUMMARY:

At Sapient, a Manager, Business Consulting leads the development of innovative strategies to improve business performance. This individual will work on a broad range of projects to develop business, operating, and IT strategies. The focus of their work includes three key areas:

Business Problem Solving – Take ambiguous and complex business problems and using research and business assessment capabilities, define the problem, drive innovative ideas, define the opportunity set, and recommend actionable next steps.
Informed Business Decision Making - Bring rigor a client’s decision making process by performing detailed cost/benefit analysis, presenting and evaluating solution options, and driving consensus among key stakeholders. In some instances, we help our clients develop new decision frameworks and governance processes that continue to deliver value far beyond a particular engagement.
Solution Planning - Define, scope and plan new solutions for clients. This includes determining how to measure business results, modeling current/future business processes, gathering business requirements and identifying the organizational changes required to successfully realize the benefits of the solution.

With a unique skills set and experience, the Manager, Business Consulting will also be expected to help drive both sales pursuits and strategies, and to support structuring of complex, long-term contracts with clients and partners.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

Essential duties and responsibilities include the following and others as assigned:
o The Business Consultant will architect, configure and setup the Murex solution such that it meets the business requirements and can successfully process the transactions from deal capture through settlements.
o Gather and translate business requirements into a solution design, modeling transactions through the system to ensure that the business requirements are met.
o Will liaise with the Murex business teams, as well as with technical development team.
o Will be responsible for the management of the configuration of the Murex solution from design through testing and deployment.
o Will ensure that the integration of the configuration with Core and Custom builds is architecturally sound.
o Responsible for problem identification, structuring workstreams and engagement planning.
o Managing key tricks of work (including other individuals) in accordance with established budget, workplans and quality standards.
o Creating overall application design and solution architecture.
o Lead customer, vendor, market analyst, and client interviews.
o Lead secondary research, data collection and market/competitor analyses.
o Lead financial analysis and business case development.
o Review, document and assess client operations.
o Develop and present solutions and recommendations.
o Lead the gathering and prioritization of business requirements based upon business and user needs and technology capabilities.
o Create strong working relationships with clients in business and IT roles.
o Support business development activities including client proposals.
o Work collaboratively on multi-disciplinary teams.
o Support practice development activities including recruiting & community building.

JOB REQUIREMENTS:

Education:
BS degree in Engineering, Mathematics, or Finance. Advanced degree preferred.

Experience Guidelines:
o 5+ years experience with end-to-end Murex package implementation as well as configuration, integration, testing and migration.
o 3-5 years of experience in trading area at a major investment bank or broker-dealer.
o Must understand business concepts and capabilities of the Murex to suggest multiple approaches to the configuration and enhancements of the product.
o Able to create business processes, functional designs and requirements for a Murex implementation or upgrade.
o Experience writing business requirements and technical specifications.
o Strong knowledge of derivatives and structured products. Products include IRS, swaptions, CDS, CDO, equity baskets, complex structures, etc.
o Create Murex trade, payment, and market operations workflows including trade status, validation queues, and user defined fields
o Provide expertise in areas of static data by asset class including generators and market conventions
o Identify, document, and create rate sheets, revaluation curves, assignment models, simulations, organizational trading desks and portfolio requirements
o Excellent data mining and analysis skills; Develop Murex custom reports using dynamic tables, datamart, and Actuate
o Must be able to analyze a derivatives termsheet/confirm.
o Hands on knowledge of SQL and relational databases.
o Superior Excel skills and experience with add-ins for derivatives pricing (e.g. FinCAD, Numerix, Quantifi).
o Professional services or Consulting experience highly desired.
o Must have experience in successfully delivering business system change which involved gaining the buy-in of business users.
o Ability to effectively interact with senior clients.
o New business opportunity identification.
o Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills including the ability to create & deliver clear, powerful & concise presentations/proposals.
o Must also have the technical aptitude to communicate with the development team and understand the application architecture.
o Ability to plan and manage time and resources, establish priorities and meet goals.
o Strong understanding of financial models; ability to create financial models.
o Creativity with an eye to developing new and innovative solutions.
o Strong problem identification and analytical skills; ability to frame problems appropriately.
o Experience leading teams and motivating others to take action.
o Highly proficient in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and MS Project.

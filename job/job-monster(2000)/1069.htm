Web Experience Senior Manager

About the Job

At Wacom, we believe that technology should be easy to use and simple to understand. With products that rely on human intuition and natural movement, we strive to bring people and technology closer together. Our patented pen and touch input technology has helped professionals around the world create extraordinary characters, digital art, films, special effects, fashions, and industrial designs. We're proud to say that some of the most celebrated companies in these industries rely on our products. No other technology company designs tools that empower creative professionals the way Wacom does. Not even close.

PURPOSE

The Web Experience Senior Manager drives the strategic direction and implementation of web related programs, including development efforts for the corporate web site, campaign sites, community, and online ecommerce capabilities.  The position provides expertise in user interface design/usability orientation, ability to move web visitors through actionable pathways, ability to facilitate content development, conceive of and implementation of cross promotional programs launched from corporate site, tie corporate site to outbound promotional campaigns, and a strong analytics skill set.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS

The Web Experience Senior Manager works closely with director of corporate marketing and other key marketing executives to plan initiatives, supervise technical development efforts using both internal web resources and contract agencies, as well as coordinate efforts with corporate IT and network administration to include:

  • Planning, budgeting and strategic counsel on web related projects
  • Manage development and evolution of Wacom web properties to accomplish business and marketing objectives to include multi-language, multi regional sites, ecommerce functionality for both U.S. and Canada currencies and integration of web properties with Wacom enterprise accounting, CRM and other systems
  • Manage technical web team and agency resources in the design, production, and maintenance of Wacom’s sites
  • Provide counsel on best practices for usability and user interface design
  • Facilitation of content development with product management resources and other Wacom point experts
  • Optimizing the e-commerce system in support of year over year online sales growth
  • Implement best practices for search engine optimization on all Wacom web properties
  • Build and implement sustainable model for web analytics focusing on refinement of practices to optimize and monetize web traffic
  • Support development aspects of Wacom initiatives in social media, adhering to Wacom brand guidelines
  • Maintain a high level of familiarity with current internet technologies

 

SECONDARY FUNCTIONS

  • Maintain and administer all web domains owned by Wacom Technology
  • Perform other duties as assigned

 

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field from an accredited college or university; or equivalent education/experience in the computer science field required
  • Applicants should have minimum of 10 years of industry experience (including specific time handling ecommerce and social media) and familiarity with contemporary programming languages
  • Demonstrated excellent written and oral communiation skills
  • Demonstrated ability to handle multiple projects concurrently, and commuicate effectively across a variety of functional groups
  • Knowledge and experience in Adobe CS and other popular graphics applications

 

PHYSICAL DEMANDS
Requires prolonged sitting, frequent standing, bending, stooping and occasional lifting up to 25 pounds.

WORK LOCATION
North American Headquarters, Vancouver, Washington (Portland, OR metropolitan area)

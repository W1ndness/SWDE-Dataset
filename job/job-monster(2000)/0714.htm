Program Director (Resolution Health expansion) - 46089

About the Job

WellPoint is the nation's leading health benefits company serving the needs of approximately 28 million medical members nationwide.

The Resolution Health (RHI) Program Director, reporting to RHI's Chief Operating Officer, will be the primary operational program manager for all RHI - WellPoint internal programs.  Requiring superior organizational knowledge and savvy, this individual will be a key leader in implementing RHI products and technology throughout the WellPoint's diverse business units.  The Program Director will take the lead on numerous high-profile and strategic projects, including work with NextRX and expansion of RHI services to WellPoint Fully Insured book of business.  WellPoint programs represent a significant portion of RHI's revenue stream and the Program Director will be instrumental to building relationships throughout the WellPoint community in multiple states, multiple business units and over multiple years.
 

Primary duties may include, but are not limited to:


  • Coordinates and manages the development, planning, prioritization, approval, implementation and compliance of on-going programs.

  • Establishes program governance when needed to assure response to issue escalation; develops program budget and ensures program meets its stated objectives.

  • Provides subject matter expertise in response to day to day business issues; researches applicable subject matter practices; and remains aware of industry trends. 

  • Manages relationships and partners with corporate and regional business areas.

  • Develops communication documents and training criteria related to program success measures.

  • Develops documentation to support meeting presentations and the tracking and reporting of the programs success.

  • Provides leadership to program managers and project managers.

  • Typically reports to an executive.

  • Program directors typically manage programs that require managing activities and resources of multiple departments or business areas of the organization.

  • Program directors typically support business strategies through an integrated portfolio of programs, projects and initiatives. 


  • Requires a BA/BS degree, 8-10 years of experience in related field; or any combination of education and experience, which would provide an equivalent background.

  • MBA or a professional designation related to the field preferred.

  • Demonstrated leadership, planning and organization skills to ensure development and maintenance of relationships across the enterprise required.

  • Thorough organizational knowledge of WellPoint. 

  • Understanding of complex business processes required.

  • Project management experience strongly preferred.

  • Proficiency in Excel, PowerPoint required. 

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills required.

  • Experience with working with finance and budgets strongly preferred.

 
The reporting location is Columbia, Maryland, located mid-way between Baltimore and Washington, DC.  Relocation support will be available.

About the Job

Kyra InfoTech is a well established information technology consulting services organization based in Florida with offices in Lakeland and Tallahassee. The company has won numerous awards; which include State's diversity business of the year, Inc. 5000, and Tampa Bay Fast 50; for its aggressive growth and quality of project execution.


We are looking for Senior IT Security Administrator for client's ongoing project in Tallahassee, FL with the following skills:

Essential Functions

 

·         Responsible for administering, maintaining, and monitoring security applications such as Checkpoint firewalls appliances, Symantec antivirus applications, Websense, IDS/IPS, SIEM appliances, ARS, forensics applications, and other security applications.

·         Mentor and lead junior members of team.

·         Set security standards and security architecture designs.

·         Take lead role in IT security projects, the security components in other IT projects, and in operational tasks.

·       Assure compliance of IT security policies and reports issues and status to IT Security Management.

·         Serve as team contact for the support of external and internal audit efforts

·         Assure security compliance on applications, databases, networks, systems, and Active Directory.

·         Assure workstation and server security.

·         Perform vulnerability assessment, patch validation, and participates in security risk assessment.

·         Coordinate security incident response, root cause analysis, and logging security incidents.

·         Perform forensic related activities such as image acquisitions and network forensics

·         Research, recommends and implements changes to procedures and systems to enhance system security.

·         Responsible for comprehensive log management and analysis and design corrective measures based on those analyses.

·         Perform problem determination, root cause analysis, and resolution.

·         Create, maintains and validates documentation.

·         Stay informed on security related laws, trends, and emerging technologies.

·         Perform periodic security system audits

·         Interface with IT staff and end-users

·         Create review, validate, and maintain accurate process and security documentation.

 

 

Required Knowledge, Skills & Abilities

 

·         Excellent communication and interpersonal for interfacing with department and corporate customers.

·         Advanced understanding of IT Security services involving networks, firewalls, Active Directory, and general IT security principles.

·         Intermediate-level understanding of MS Office Suite, Microsoft Desktop and Server Operating Systems.

·         Advanced understanding of information security theory and practices.

·         Understanding of Incident Response Technologies.

·         Advanced knowledge of application, systems, and network security administration.

·         Advanced understanding of information security forensic analysis methodologies.

 

Required Education & Experience

 

·         BA/BS Computer Science, Information Technology or relevant field OR a combination of college education, training or related IT experience in a business environment, equivalent to 4 years of college.

·         5 years experience in information security in one or more of the following domains: access control, computer operations security, network security, security architecture and models, or application and system development security.

·         Current certification in one or more of the following: MCSE, CCSP, MCITP (Enterprise Admin), or equivalent or higher

·         Current certification as CCSA (Checkpoint Certified Security Administrator) or equivalent or higher. If not current, must be obtained within 90 days of employment.

·         Current Certification as CISSP (Certified Information System Security Professional) or equivalent or higher.

 

Preferred Qualifications

·         Experience with Client's internal business processes.

·         Knowledge and understanding of ITIL.

·         Knowledge of regulatory issues such as SOX, COBIT, PCI, HIPAA.

·         Understanding of security standards and frameworks [such as ISO 17799]

·         Additional current certification as:

·         CCSP

·         MCITP (Enterprise Admin)

·         MCSE

·         Other Security Certifications (such as CEH, CPT, GCIH, etc.)

·         CCSE

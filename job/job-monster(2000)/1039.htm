SAP Quality Management Analyst

About the Job

McCormick & Co., Inc., a world leader in the spice, flavor & seasonings industry, is seeking a full time SAP Quality Management Analyst in its Global Business Solutions Division located in Hunt Valley, Maryland. 

 

As an employer recognized for our exceptional commitment to employees, McCormick & Co., Inc. offers a wide variety of benefits, programs and services which address the needs of employees and their families.  McCormick’s benefits include, but are not limited to tuition assistance, medical & dental, disability, group life insurance, 401(k), pension plan, paid holidays and vacations.

 

DESCRIPTION/RESPONSIBILITES:

The SAP Business Process Analyst – Quality is the primary contact for our business partners in the Quality and Technical process area. The analyst works with business users in the quality area to define scope and objectives for system solutions for business requirements.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·        Global business support

o       First level contact to the business for inquiries including systems support, improvement analyses, and advice

o       Providing ongoing responsibility for system failure prevention, system problem calls and user inquiries.  This includes regular system maintenance and support which will require evening, weekend and on-call support

o       Seeking constant improvement opportunities and appropriately challenges processes to ensure global consistency

·        Project

o       Responsible for evaluating and developing reliable and efficient solutions to fulfill complex business requirements

o       Responsible for system requirements, detailed functional specifications, system configuration, unit testing, integration testing, user acceptance, and deployment. Providing post-implementation process and application support. May act as work stream leader from start to finish on a project lifecycle

·        Additional Responsibilities

o       Generating ideas for products and services that provide realistic and tangible benefit

o       Providing leadership and training to less experienced Analysts and Interns

·        Domestic/International travel may be required

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

·        Bachelors degree and a minimum of 4 years of overall IT application development and project experience preferred

·        Minimum 8 years of overall IT application development and project experience – required (in lieu of Bachelor’s degree).

·        Additional 3 years experience in business system analysis required

·        In-depth experience with a minimum of two full lifecycle implementations of SAP applications in the quality management area required.

·        Experience in other areas of the supply chain application (such as manufacturing and distribution) preferred.

·        SAP experience/knowledge of integration of Quality Management with Materials Management, Process Manufacturing and Warehouse Management required.

·        Experience with SAP ECC 6.0 (R/3) preferred.

·        Familiarity with Project Management processes preferred.

·        Multi-cultural project experience preferred.

·        Professional certifications (CPIM or CSCP) preferred

 

 

If interested, please visit www.mccormickcorporation.com and apply for position #21170.

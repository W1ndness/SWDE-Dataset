LABORATORY INFORMATICS MANAGEMENT CONSULTANT

About the Job

LabAnswer is the leading laboratory informatics consultancy in North America. We work with “best in breed” Laboratory Informatics software products to provide total solutions for our government, biotech and pharmaceutical clients. LabAnswer is expert in architecting, configuring, deploying, validating and supporting laboratory systems. These systems include LIMS, CDS, Scientific Content Management Systems and Electronic Lab Notebooks.

 

As a LabAnswer consultant you will be exposed to the technologies and processes that many leading US companies and government agencies utilize. We offer a generous compensation package consisting of salary, bonuses and benefits. But LabAnswer is not for everyone, our customers are located all over the US, which means you need to plan to travel at least 50% of the time. Our consultants can live in any city with a major airport and access to broadband services. In addition you can work from home when you are not traveling.

 

Job Description:

 

Work with clients to formulate a comprehensive laboratory informatics strategic plan for the enterprise. 

 

Responsibilities:

 

Lead team utilizing the LabAnswer Methodology in:

·               Business analysis of a variety of laboratories

·               Development of KPIs for the laboratory

·               Calculation of ROI as it pertains to IT investment

·               Determine optimum knowledge management architecture for the laboratory including LIMS, ELN, SDMS, EDMS and CDS

·               Assist customers with vendor selection

·               Development of implementation strategies

·               Gain consensus with management, IT and scientists

 

Requirements:

 

·               Laboratory experience as scientist

·               Strong working knowledge of ELN, SDMS, LIMS and CDS

·               Full software development lifecycle experience

·               Prior experience implementing an ELN, LIMS, SDMS or CDS in large enterprise

·               IT architecture experience

·               Strong BA skills

·               Excellent oral and written communication skills

·               Ability to lead a team of clients, consultants and vendors with differing agendas

·               Ability to communicate effectively with technical and non-technical audiences

·               Grasp of business and financial implications of enterprise software projects

 

Education:   

 

The ideal candidate will have a master’s degree or above with a degree in science and a degree in business or MIS.

 

At LabAnswer we offer a very competitive compensation package based on experience and education.   We are looking excellent people to join our team, those who are looking to join a company for the long term.   Our consultants are team players and are the best at what they do.  Please read our philosophy statement at http://www.labanswer.com/LabAnswers_Philosophy.asp  to see if you are a match for LabAnswer.

Please contact Donna Delrieu at LabAnswer at donna.delrieu@labanswer to learn more about starting a new career with LabAnswer.

Network Systems Engineer - Cisco

About the Job

Custom Computer Specialists, Inc. is the leading Technical Consultant on Long Island. For over 30 years, we have successfully provided a wide array of technical and consulting solutions designed to drive our client’s success. Our approach is to take business goals and assist our clients in realizing those goals through a variety of solutions including IP Communications, Consolidation/ Virtualization, Security, Messaging and Archiving.

We are looking for an experienced and motivated technical professional to join our team as a Network Engineer. Our Network Engineers are responsible for creating and delivering infrastructure solutions for our clients. This includes the design, configuration, installation, integration and troubleshooting of IT network solutions. This position involves significant customer interaction and requires strong organizational, communication, leadership, and client relationship skills.

Responsibilities include:

·         Performing detailed client site surveys and develop documentation of existing environment

·         Installing and integrating hardware into existing LAN environments.  This includes the configuration of Network VLANs, routing protocols and optimizing MAC layer parameters as required

·         Participate in pre-sales activities and solution development (assist with analyzing client needs / technical requirements, recommending design/solution options, facilitate the preparation and delivery of presentations, proposals, and defining scope of work).

·         Hands on configuration, implementation, troubleshooting and problem resolution of proposed solutions Physical network troubleshooting – drops, switch ports, switches, crimping cables, terminating cables, etc.

·         Create “leave-behind” documentation of solution specifics for client use.

·         Design, implementation and post implementation support services on network infrastructure

 

A successful individual will need to have 5+ years experience delivering solutions across a broad range of technologies (from pre-sales through implementation) and /or support for external clients.  In particular, a successful candidate will possess the following:

·         A detailed understanding of the OSI reference model, with particular experience/knowledge of the data and network layer

·         A demonstrated competency in network design, configuration and support on industry leading platforms including Cisco, HP and Juniper networks

·         A thorough understanding of the operation of various routing protocols including RIPv2, OSPF, EIGRP, BGP and MPLS

·         Bachelor of Science/Bachelor of Engineering preferred in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science

·         Advanced certifications desirable including CCIE (R/S or Voice), JNCIE, CCNP, or JNCIS

 

 

We offer an opportunity that is what YOU make of it and:

 

·         A 30+ year track record of success and continued growth.

·         Unique corporate culture that is fast-paced, challenging, empowers everyone to help the company grow, encourages career development and rewards generously for success.

·         Access to the latest technologies in our on-site lab environment that allows Engineers to gain hands-on experience and build proficiency in multiple solutions.

·         Strong relationships with industry-leading partners including Microsoft, Cisco, HP, Dell, IBM, VMWare and more with the ability to leverage their resources for solution design, training and exposure to innovative technologies.

  •  “Right People, Right Results” Real Rewards Program which includes three great opportunities for you to earn additional income above and beyond your salary. These include our STAR (Service Training and Rewards) which rewards you for obtaining technical certifications, our Employee Referral Program which rewards you for referring future employees and our LEAD referral program which rewards you for referring business opportunities.
  • Company recognition programs and awards including the Custom Leadership “Game Ball” (which includes the opportunity to win an expense paid trip to the Super Bowl) and our “Service Excellence Award”
  • Employee Appreciation events such as Team Meetings, Years of Service Awards Dinner, Theatre/Sports Tickets, Summer Picnic and Halloween Party which includes families.

·         Competitive salaries, comprehensive benefits including medical, dental, vision, 401K, flexible spending, paid vacation time and tuition reimbursement programs.

This position is based in Hauppauge, Long Island, New York and will require travel to client sites located in the NY metropolitan area.

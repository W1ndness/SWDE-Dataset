Database Developer

About the Job

Baseline is the world's preeminent provider of film and television data. Our data set is the industry standard for verified information on historical film and television credits, celebrity biographies, television pilots, studio development, production schedules, company rosters, artist representation and related intelligence. Baseline provides deep industry expertise, strict editorial standards, premium-quality information products, entertainment business tools that make sense, extensive worldwide reach, and industry renowned customer service to our customers. Baseline is proud to serve the information needs of the world's leading entertainment and media brands, including The New York Times, Yahoo Movies, Variety, NBC, and FOX to name a few.


The ideal candidate will have strong SQL development experience and will function primarily as a SQL Database Developer.  This individual must be comfortable working with multiple databases within numerous tables in both highly normalized and denormalized schemas.  An ability to write SQL statements, queries, complex stored procedures and triggers with exceptional skill is a must.  He/she will also have extensive experience in MS SQL Server 2005/2008 database design, architecture, modeling, programming/scripting, and be familiar with XML. 


 


This position will report to the Database Lead.  Some of our initiatives are with major media outlets and Internet businesses in the fortune 500 so this individual must be able to handle a number of simultaneous projects, and must demonstrate a sense of urgency and ownership to drive projects to completion.  Successful candidates will also have prior knowledge and understanding of the entertainment industry.


 


Responsibilities include:


Working collaboratively with development team to create Tables, Triggers, Functions, Stored Procedures, Tuning and Indexes etc.


Working with DB Lead and Technology team to define data models, schemas and database application architectures.


Working with development team to develop new Database objects for web applications.


Having experience in writing DB scripts to move or  modify data.


Experience in query optimization.


Assisting in designing, documenting, and developing data transformation processes.


Assisting in designing, documenting, and implementing SQL databases.


Supporting Application Developers regarding database related questions.


 


 


Requirements:


3+ years SQL Database Developer experience with strong standards and performance based development skills, knowledge of secure coding standards and experience working in a large enterprise environment.


At least 4+ years of prior T-SQL development experience in Microsoft technologies such as .NET, ASP, or VB.


Bachelors Degree in computer science or related field.


Prior experience in creating Stored Procedures, SQL scripts, and DTS Packages.


Understanding and working knowledge of database normalization concepts.


Proficiency developing the major SQL Server database objects.


Prior experience working in a project oriented environment and meeting deadlines under tight time constraints.


Experience with test driven development and SDLC expertise.


Prior knowledge and understanding of the entertainment industry is preferred.


A solid working knowledge of SQL Server, Windows, and development practices for working with a team of developers.


Experience with the following a plus: Rational Unified Process, .NET, Java and Coldfusion.

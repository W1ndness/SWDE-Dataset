Senior Application Developer

About the Job

MedAssurant is offering an opportunity to join a team developing a new healthcare technology and advanced data analytic products.  We are seeking Senior Developers to work in a quasi development role and interface directly with clients.  This position will heavily focus on deep SSIS and SSRS development supporting web and windows based applications in a .Net 3.5 environment. 

 

This position requires strong technical .Net and SQL experience plus an instinctive, problem solving mentality and experience conceptualizing and creating out-of-the-box solutions.  We work in an Agile environment with multi-terabyte datasets and focused on developing solutions to address complex database design and data modeling challenges. We are engaged in continuous optimization and refactoring, while promoting an upbeat and effective team environment.

 

Responsibilities:

 

·        Develop and maintain an expert level of knowledge regarding products and services of MedAssurant to achieve product development and operation requirements;

·         Diagnose and process operational data requests;

·         Determine how new systems or system enhancements may improve process flow and business function

·         Manage the extraction, transformation, and population of data files and databases (MS SQL Server, Excel, etc);

·         Responsible for research, planning, estimating, designing, and delivering solutions that meet the business needs;

·         Research, Design, Code, Test and Debug Applications, SQL and DB operations including (but not solely) custom web system interfaces and GUIs; windows applications; custom queries using SQL and reports through various reporting media to meet specifications/needs;

·         Design and build solutions based on the Microsoft Business Intelligence platform and Microsoft .NET platform using best practices.

·         Communicate effectively within the operations/development teams.

·         Maintain compliance with MedAssurant’s policies, procedures and mission statement;

·        Adhere to all confidentiality and HIPAA requirements as outlined within MedAssurant’s Operating Policies and Procedures in all ways and at all times with respect to any aspect of the data handled or services rendered in the undertaking of the position; and

·        Fulfill those responsibilities and/or duties that may be reasonably provided by MedAssurant for the purpose of achieving operational and financial success of the Company

Requirements:

·        BS degree in computer science or the equivalent years of experience.

·        Expert level experience with SQL Server 2005/2008 and stored procedure development. Experience working with very large databases;

·         Expert level experience with T-SQL, SSIS, and SSRS development.  SSRS integration with SharePoint a plus;

·         4+ years experience developing data driven applications C#, ASP.NET, XML;  Web Services, WCF, WPF, multi-
threaded programming a plus;

·         4+ years experience developing in Visual Studio 2005 or later; and

·         Good understanding of OOAD, design patterns, and application development processes and practices with a focus on reusability.

 

Who we are:

 

MedAssurant, Inc. is a leading provider of superior healthcare quality, care management, and financial performance improvement solutions empowered by advanced data analysis, abstraction, and verification systems. Applying a unique combination of nationwide personnel and an advanced technology infrastructure, MedAssurant provides local and national health insurance plans, care delivery networks, employers, pharmaceutical companies, regulatory bodies, and government organizations with powerful, turnkey services addressing disease management, clinical outcomes, quality of care, cost improvement, revenue enhancement, risk adjustment, and healthcare data verification. Our corporate headquarters is in Bowie, Maryland. We have other primary locations in Annapolis, Maryland; Lansing, Michigan; New York, New York and Atlanta, Georgia. Additional field offices and employee clinical and data review and operations personnel are maintained throughout the country, supporting services for all 50 States, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico.

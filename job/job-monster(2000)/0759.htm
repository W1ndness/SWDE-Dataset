About the Job

If you enjoy working with highly skilled professionals in a team environment for a leading edge technology company, then Flatirons is for you!

Flatirons Solutions is currently seeking a Senior Systems Engineer to join our Government Services division in the Washington, D.C. area. The position responsibilities include, but are not limited to the following:

Essential duties and responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

·         Plan, develop, organize, coordinate and manage Software engineering resources from initiation to closing

·         Applies advanced software engineering principles and methods to perform and direct a variety of complex tasks related to software applications and related products to meet client needs

·         Advise on the development of system architecture

·         Create and manage project resources and review resource allocation

·         Manage the delivery of complex software projects as needed

·         Manage the Software Practice Area

·         Direct a group of Software Technical Leads

·         Develop and implement a Software Practice Area Training Plan

·         Mentors and guides staff professional development/assign Technical Leads to do the same

·         Develop the job descriptions for new personnel requirements within the Practice Area

Qualified applicants will possess the following Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

·         Experience defining and implementing software solutions

·         Strong knowledge of Service Oriented Architecture

·         Experienced in the use of software / SOA standards, SDLC design/delivery methodologies etc.

·         Familiarity with Department of Defense Architecture Framework (DoDAF, Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR), Acquisition Management System (AMS), or other federal acquisition regulations, etc.

·         Software Languages including Java, C/C++, javascript, Spring, Fuse and related

·         Experience with Rational Rose, ClearCase, ReqPro, ClearQuest or similar

·         Experience with full software lifecycle development

·         Practice Manager experience with a group of 20 personnel

Educational Requirements:

·         Bachelor’s in Computer Science or related field required/Master’s Preferred

·         Certifications in Management (PMP) and/or Technical Domains

 

 

Supervisory Responsibilities:

·         Manage a team of 10-20 Software Engineers

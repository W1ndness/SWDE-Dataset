Technical Account Manager

About the Job

 

individual who brings a positive attitude to the work place. You need to be a team player as you will be a part of a sales team responsible for prospecting, developing, and managing a particular US territory in accordance with a Territory Manager and Business Development Representative. This position is a diverse mix of IT Technical Support and Training while working within a Sales Dept, which creates a dynamic work environment.

 

Responsibilities

  • Ability to demonstrate each Zenith solution (Smart Style, Managed Services, Backup and Disaster Recovery/Continuity) to prospective partners in person and via the web.
  • Use Zenith’s chat support system to assist and support prospects in presales technical efforts.
  • Create a close relationship with partners in evaluation to ensure they experience a smooth partnership and move forward.
  • Host and lead group training sessions for all Zenith evaluation partners.
  • Manage and train accounts in a technical support role.
  • Use Zenith’s CRM system to track prospects and evaluation partners.
  • Submit daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly reports on time and as specified by management. 
  • Work independently to perform other duties assigned by management.
  • Participate as a technical resource at Zenith road shows, trade shows, and conferences.
  • Become partner’s trusted technical advisor during entire relationship.
  • Continually stay up-to-date on industry trends and market competition.
  • Additional responsibilities can be added at any time.
  • 50% Travel required.

 

Qualifications

  • Account Management with respect to Technical Support and Training experience is a PLUS.
  • Minimum 2 years business experience (or college equivalent) working with Information Technology at an administrative level with a solid understanding in business mechanisms, virtualizations, backup, cloud computing and data centers.
  • Exceptional customer service and communication skills along with strong interpersonal, organizational and presentation skills.
  • Must be results oriented with a high level of energy and enthusiasm.
  • Able multi-task and work independently in a fast paced environment.
  • Any prior experience working with a CRM or PSA package is a PLUS.

 

Salary Base Pay - 45k annually and incentive program.

 

Benefits

In exchange for your hard work and dedication, we offer:

  • Incentive program to compliment base salary
  • Medical, dental, vision and life insurance
  • Online career courses to further your skills
  • Paid time off
  • 401k Program

 

We work to maintain the best possible environment for our employees, where people can learn and grow with the company. We strive to provide a collaborative, creative environment where each person feels encouraged to contribute to our processes, decisions, planning and culture.

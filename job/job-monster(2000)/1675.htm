Programmer Analyst III - Java (256-113)

About the Job

Progressive Medical, Inc. is a rapidly growing managed care company that provides a wide array of healthcare related services to underwriters of workers’ compensation insurance, self-insured employers and third party administrators across the United States.



Progressive Medical Inc. has been recognized within the community, industry organizations and through national publications for outstanding business practices, company growth, charitable contributions and employee loyalty. We offer competitive salaries, an excellent benefits package that begins on your first day of employment, generous vacation and incidental time and an on site fitness facility.



Description

Develops or modifies information systems to solve complex systems problems for assigned business segment considering computer equipment capacity and limitations, operating time and form of desired results. Directs all phases of system development projects including definition, analysis and design.

Essential Job Functions

Formulates and defines systems scope and objectives through research and fact-finding to develop or modify information systems that support changes in the company's functional activities. Provides recommendations and problem resolution.

Writes well-structured, easily-maintained, efficiently operating program code from documented or verbal instructions for new and existing programs. Identifies areas where additional definition is required and obtains information.

Ensures system effectiveness by formulating test plans, and troubleshooting and repairing complex parts of developed systems. Participates in user acceptance testing.

Works directly with clients and business analysts to identify client needs and alternative solutions. Provides extensive knowledge of business to provide systems specialized support.

Serves as technical team leader (1-3 full time employees) on complex projects or multiple smaller projects to ensure work flow is progressing on schedule. Develops work team, plans and monitors the work of other team members including prioritizing to accommodate user needs and deadlines.

Mentors other Programmer Analysts in their job roles and technical abailities. 

Reports status, activities and issues to internal and external customers as appropriate in accordance with department standards.

Ensures documentation of developed programs including program and client documentation. Conducts client training, education and presentations.

Provides guidance and technical expertise to less experienced programmer analysts in resolving design problems.

Maintains a professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, and establishing personal networks.

Acts as subject matter expert for technology platforms as assigned.

Develops technical specifications to guide the development efforts of other programmers.

Delivers projects in accordance with department standards. 



Required Skills

Programming languages and technologies:
  Java: JDK 1.4-1.6+, J2EE, JEE.

Developed knowledge of business operations.

Ability to organize work, engage in a variety of tasks simultaneously and consistently meet deadlines.

Developed oral and written communication skills; strong customer service abilities.

High degree of initiative and independent judgment.

Well-developed analytical and problem solving abilities.

Strong attention to detail.

Able to create functional designs in accordance with company and industry standards.

Able to create technical designs in accordance with company and industry standards.

Technical: Data Modeling, SQL, EDI, systems analysis, design and program development skills.



Required Experience

Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field with 5 to 10 years programming or systems analysis experience, and/or equivalent combination. 

At least six months experience leading project development teams of at least 1-3 developers and previous experience mentoring developers. 

Preferred Qualifications

Previous experience in healthcare industry.

General Development: CVS, Subversion, MS Windows XP-7, Linux, Agile.

Java: Swing, Eclipse, Hibernate, JSF, JPA, EJB3, Glassfish, JBoss, Maven, Groovy.

Perl: Perl DBI.

Oracle, PL/SQL.

Experience in an “agile” development environment.

Progressive Medical, Inc. supports a drug and nicotine free work environment. We only hire nicotine free employees. Progressive Medical is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

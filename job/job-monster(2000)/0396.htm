IT Systems and Communications Manager

About the Job

Growing Company seeks IT Systems Manager to join our team.

The IT Systems and Communication Managers overall responsibilities include managing networks and routers for our offices in Norfolk, Williamsburg, and Largo, Florida; maintaining security and stability of all company networks. Maintain hardware and software maintenance programs. Provide technical support to staff. Maintain, and enhance computerized financial and financial-related systems. Coordinate planning and implementation of system improvements. Responsible for budget management. Management of communication devices, expenses, and plans.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelors degree or higher and 7-10 years IT experience.
  • Excellent communication and analytical skills.
  • Working knowledge of spreadsheet software and database accounting systems.
  • Superior time management and organizational skills.
  • Proven technical expertise in Technology
  • Strong initiative and project management skills.
  • Knowledge of networking systems. Cisco Routers.
  • Have working knowledge of  Microsoft Exchange
  • Have working knowledge of Server 2008, Terminal Server, Windows 7, Windows XP

          Blackberry Enterprise Server; SQL

The Kerner Group offers competitive compensation package, terrific benefits and a great working environment. If interested, please apply online or email Amy Hansen ahansen@kernergroup.com

SAP Business Objects Developer

About the Job

Founded in 1996, Creative Information Technology, Inc. (CITI) has continued to prove itself as a forward thinking information technology company. A company that provides its clients with real-world solutions that integrate long-term functionality and capability. With quality solutions and services provided primarily to the Federal government, CITI has grown into a diversely talented and motivated IT enterprise. We are currently looking for a SAP Business Objects Developer.


We are seeking talented, ambitious developers to support a growing Business Intelligence (BI) program for a large Federal customer. Familiarity with reporting and visualization tools depicting a variety of dimensional and relational data with reports and dashboards is required. Primary development tools are SAP BusinessObjects Enterprise 3.1, WebIntelligence, Xcelsius, Universe Designer, and Crystal Reports.

Required Experience:

* US Citizen with ability to obtain Secret clearance
* Experience working on data warehouse projects
* A minimum of 1 year experience developing with the BusinessObjects Business Intelligence tools within the last 3 years
* Experience working with users to ensure requirements are developed to specification
* Basic knowledge of BusinessObjects infrastructure configuration
* Experience with Oracle databases
* Ability to effectively communicate information, both verbally and written, to end users and executive management
* Sufficient knowledge to identify and help avoid pitfalls associated with data warehousing
* Must be self-motivated and take initiative in performing tasks


Desired Experience:

* Experience with BO Xcelsius, Universe Designer, WebIntelligence, and Crystal Reports
* Experience participating in architecture process and peer reviews.
* Experience participating in end user and technical requirement sessions

Education: B.S. in computer related field

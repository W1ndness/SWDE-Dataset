The Junior Coder of the Year - Health IT SaaS Startup

About the Job


 

The Future Junior Coder of the Year
Profitable, health IT startup looking for a junior software developer with lots of potential!



Hello candidate.  Let us first introduce ourselves.  We are Kareo (www.kareo.com), based in Irvine, California.  Our web-based software powers thousands of small physician practices in the U.S.   We’re leading our industry in the shift from traditional software to cloud computing (a.k.a. Software-as-a-Service) and helping doctors get paid by taking today’s complex healthcare system and making it simple.  This allows doctors to spend less time worrying about their business and more time caring for patients and saving lives.  Basically, Kareo is at the center of the storm, which is at the center of Obama’s policy agenda.  Healthcare.  A $2.4 trillion (yes, that’s with a “T”) industry, representing 17% of the U.S. GDP.  Technology is coming to transform and revolutionize this industry and Kareo is positioned to carry the torch for the 500,000 physicians in the U.S. who practice in small medical offices.



That’s the high level.  The details go like this… Kareo, founded in 2005, has finally reached profitability!  We’ve done this by building innovative & customer-focused technology, acquiring customers through cost-effective online marketing channels, and keeping a tight reign on expenses to make ends meet.  But now we’re generating cash flow, and that means it’s time to grow our business and expand our team.  Digging in and writing real code to enable new features and services, under the direction of our kick-ass senior engineers, is the junior developer’s job to take.  You might call this position a Junior Software Engineer, but that doesn’t do it justice.  We’re looking for The Future Junior Coder of the Year!  Based on your experience, we’re budgeting for an annual salary of $60,000 - $90,000, and you’ll be entitled to benefits and stock options.  Here’s what the position looks like:



We’ll be developing your talent and unlocking your potential by exposing you to the really awesome technology that it takes to create an amazing customer experience for our growing base of thousands of customers.  But it’s not all fun and games here at Kareo.  There is a ton of work to do, and some of it downright sucks.  After all – we solve a lot of the messy problems in healthcare so our customers won’t need to.  So you’ll need to buck up and grind through server errors, bug reports, user interfaces,  EDI transforms, forms, reports, and source code, without complaint or supervision, and come back asking for more.  What do you get in return?  For starters, you’ll get an opportunity to write real code for a cool product that will be deployed to real small business customers via frequent releases.  But even better, you’ll be mentored by a super talented team of senior engineers that really know their stuff and will help jumpstart your career in software development.



This is an entry-level engineering position for Kareo… but make no mistake; you’ll be writing a lot of code.  We are a small startup today, but we are embarking on a substantial growth ramp in terms of revenues and people, and that means there is a massive opportunity for you to grow with us.  Critical to your success will be an understanding of the fundamentals of software development, and that means object oriented design, algorithms, and data structures.  You’ll be working with Microsoft technologies so basic experience with .NET, C#, WinForms, SQL Server, and ASP.NET, will be a huge plus.  But here come the essential ingredients.  We want the smart, hard working, creative problem solver.  Sure, you may not have years of experience building commercial-grade business software, but you’re a quick study with loads of potential and you’re ready to work incredibly hard to get stuff done for Kareo so you can accelerate your career.  You’re thrilled by the chance to learn from a small team of more senior engineers that are close to customers and deliver frequent releases in a fast-based environment.  You bring energy to your work and your passion for writing code is infectious.



This high-potential engineering job is here for the taking but here’s what it’s going to take from you:

 



  • Send us your resume or a link to your LinkedIn profile. 
  • Complete the questions and homework below (this shows us you’re serious). 



It’s a lot to ask, so what will we do in return?  If you complete the application, we guarantee that our entire development team will review your application and our CEO will personally respond to you directly within 5 days or less.



Junior coders with lots of potential need-only apply.  What are you waiting for?  We look forward to hearing from you. 



Kareo Future Junior Coder of the Year Homework



1. What would you be doing on a day-to-day basis in your ideal job?



2. When did you start programming? Tell me about your first project, what technologies you used, and why you did it.



3. What’s the biggest, coolest project you ever built from top-to-bottom? Not a component; a whole self-sufficient thing.



4. Write pseudo-code (or actual code) for a function that takes in a string and returns the letter that appears the most in that string.



5. Write a C# class that can’t be instantiated more than 10 times.



Send us all relevant code. If you don’t have access to Visual Studio to test your code, feel free to use the free express versions found at http://www.microsoft.com/express/Downloads /.



For confidential consideration, please email your resume and homework to jobs@kareo.com .

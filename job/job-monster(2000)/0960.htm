About the Job

IMPLEMENTATION CONSULTING POSITION

 

OutStart Inc., a leader in the technology based learning market has immediate an opening for an Implementation Consultant to be based out of our Orlando (Lake Mary) FL office.   Position requires 75% travel.

 

Implementation Consultants will assist customers with the implementation of intended use of product, provide mentoring, gather requirements, determine technical needs, review project requirements and refine implementation.

 

Ideal candidate will have at least two years experience designing technology based training including web based training and/or computer based training;  background in instructional design, multi-media design and development and training.  Consulting, project management, facilitation, knowledge of web based technologies such as HTML, Scripting and strong customer relationship skills also required.  Additional technical skills such as database and IT knowledge are nice to have.

 

RESPONSIBLITIES

 

  • Conduct planning and discovery to provide new customers with consulting on the intended uses of the product within their enterprise
  • Facilitation of process and implementation workshops / activities
  • Provide application training to end users and administrators
  • Content workflow design and implementation
  • Metadata / taxonomy strategy design and implementation
  • Content development strategy support to support ILT, CBT, e-learning, documentation, simulations, etc.
  • User Interface customization to fit customer requirements
  • Some installation and technical tasks required.

 

EDUCATION

  • Bachelors Degree required; Bachelors Degree or Masters Degree Instructional Design / Learning Technologies preferred

 

LANGUAGE

·         Multiple languages helpful.

 

EXPERIENCE

 

  • Minimum two years experience developing technology based training
  • Instructional design, multi-media design and development
  • Technical training delivery
  • Understanding of web technologies
  • Consulting  / facilitation skills
  • Project management
  • Strong customer relationship skills
  • Experience with LMS / LCMS / KM Implementations preferred and helpful

 

OutStart offers very competitive salary and benefits package in casual work environment. 

 

 

Applications are encouraged from candidates whose race, national origin, sex, age, religion, marital status, sexual orientation, veteran status or physical disability will contribute to our diversity.  EEO/AAP

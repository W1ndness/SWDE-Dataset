Professional Services - Consultant

About the Job

Job Title: Professional Services - Consultant

PerTrac is seeking a Consultant to be based in our New York office.  The primary role of a Professional Services Consultant is to ensure that all allocated project work is completed in a timely manner, to a high quality and delivers value to our clients.  A consultant may work on projects individually or as part of a team.  Project work will cover the following disciplines:

Communication of specifications, requirements, and deliverables to clients
Product installation, configuration and troubleshooting
Data manipulation and migration

Consultants may also be involved in pre-sales meetings with prospects, post-implementation training and general account management interactions with our clients.

Role Qualifications

  • Education: BS/BA or equivalent work experience.
  • Strong client facing, written and oral communication skills.
  • Strong analytical/problem-solving skills.  A critical thinker with a “can-do” attitude.
  • Organized and detail-oriented. Ability to multi-task and manage multiple projects at once.
  • The ability to work as part of a team or individually. A motivated individual that is a self-starter that can succeed with minimal guidance. At the same time a Team Player that can work with cross-functional departments.
  • Experience in or an interest in the Alternative Investment Industry together with the desire to learn all the aspects of the clients’ workflow and business process.

Technical Skills

Position requires the mastery of the following technologies. Candidates with current experience a PLUS.

  • Microsoft products and technologies:

                Microsoft Dynamics CRM

                MS Office Suite (Outlook, Excel and Access)
                MS SQL Server, SQL Query design and SQL Report writing

MS Exchange

  • Basic understanding of programming languages:
    Object-Orientated or Scripting Languages such as VBA / VBScript / JavaScript

The successful candidate will have the ability to work in a dynamic environment and become an important part of a growing company.  PerTrac will provide the opportunity to gain knowledge of the Alternative Investment Industry, including formal training and exam sponsorship for either the CAIA or CFA qualifications.

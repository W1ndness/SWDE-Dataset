Sr. Software Engineer - Supply Chain Applications

About the Job

J.CREW

Quality People…Quality Product…Quality Service

Sr. Software Engineer - Supply Chain Systems
Distribution Center: Lynchburg, VA

Overview: Systems software engineer involved in the analysis, design, development and implementation of supply chain systems software..  Makes well-thought out decisions on complex or ambiguous software engineering issues. Designs, develops, tests, debugs and implements complex operating system components software tools, and utilities.  Ability to make complex modifications to existing software to fit specialized needs and configurations  Requires 3 to 6  years experience. Experience with Red Prairie Warehouse Management application a plus.

Responsibilities

Technical Skills:

  1. Design, development, implementation and maintenance of J. Crew's Supply Chain Applications.
  2. Assume primary design/coding responsibility for one or more functional subsets of system software. (Application, Reporting, Integration)
  3. Proven experience in life cycle application development with the ability to generate application development policies, standards and procedures.
  4. Develop utilities, procedures, and documentation upon customer requirements
  5. Maintain application release control (version control).
  6. Application installation and upgrade, including third party integration and hardware configuration.
  7. Working with varying software development and deployment packages, Radio Frequency Terminals, Specialty Printers would be a plus.

 

Interpersonal Skills:

  1. Able to work effectively both alone and in team/cooperative development efforts.
  2. Able to learn Supply Chain concepts and progress in technical and general business skills.
  3. Able to assist Project Leader and customer personnel with integration testing, debugging and installation of application and systems software.

 

Organizational Skills:

  1. Good written, verbal and non-verbal communication skills with internal and external customers.
  2. Able to learn Supply Chain concepts and progress in technical and general business skills. Actual background/knowledge in Supply Chain applications would be a plus.

 

Computer Skills:

  1. Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, MIS or related field, or equivalent experience in a technology-related field. Masters degree preferred.
  2. At least 3 years of software development experience as a programmer analyst, with 3GL and 4GL programming languages, relational databases, web tools, client-server graphical user interface (GUI), PL/SQL, and Windows technologies
  3. Experience with one or more of the following: C/C ++, SQL and/or PLSQL, UNIX, Oracle 10g, Java, Visual Basic, VB.net, Uniface
  4. Excellent problem solving skills, particularly with regard to anticipating and solving problems, issues, or concerns before they occur or become critical.
  5. Knowledge of varying software development and deployment packages, Radio Frequency Terminals, Specialty Printers would be a plus.
  6. Background/knowledge of networking and remote desktop and FTP
  7. Experience with Red Prairie applications and the MOCA language would be a plus.  

 

We are committed to affirmatively providing equal opportunity to all associates and qualified applicants without regard to race, color, ancestry, national origin, religion, sex, marital status, age, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, legally protected physical or mental disability or any other basis protected under applicable law.

We offer the successful candidate a competitive salary and an outstanding benefits package that includes medical, life, and dental insurance, 401k, Employee Stock Purchase Plan, 25 days of paid time off, and a 30% discount on company merchandise.

This position will be based out of Lynchburg, VA

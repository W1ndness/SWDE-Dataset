About the Job

Business Intelligence Technical Project Manager

 Dunn Solutions Group is an IIT solutions provider specializing in the practice areas of Strategic Planning, Web Design, Business Applications, Business Intelligence, Quality Assurance and Technical Education.

 

Dunn Solution group is looking for an experienced hands on Business Intelligence Project Manager with experience managing projects in the Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence tools, reporting into Director of Business Intelligence. This position also involves working with proposals and presales presentations and the candidate should have experience in this area.

 

Qualifications/Requirements:

 

·         8+ years of total IT experience   .

·         2+ years in a  Project Management role

·         5+ years working and leading data warehousing environments/projects,

·         MUST have demonstrated experience as a Data Warehouse Architect (Participation on the Consolidation of Data Warehouse initiative, analyzing system issues and develop solutions, back end development and processes that utilize ETL tools, development of data warehouse)

·         MUST have 4+ years of Cognos/ Business Objects/Crystal Reports and or Other BI tools experience on the entire product range as a Project Leader, but with "hands on" tool experience

·         Must be able to mentor and guide team directly using the unique background of having used BI tools and also leading and directing a team

·         Should have good data modeling experience (physical and logical)

·         Should have demonstrated Management experience over team of developers in a technical area, ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, including directing work of contracting staff

·         PMP Certification a plus (not required)

·         BS Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or other computer related degree is required

·          All candidates must have excellent communication skills and written skills with project write-ups and technical documentation. Candidates will be required to demonstrate examples of prior written work that have been completed in this area

·         Ability to travel between 10-35% annualy     .

 

Location: Skokie, IL (Chicagoland area)

 *** Position is Full-Time Permanent salaried with benefits (medical, dental, 401(K), profit sharing, paid vacation, holiday and sick days)

Applicant must be U.S. resident or currently able to legally work in the U.S. on a Full-Time basis.


If you meet these minimum requirements and are interested in working in a dynamic environment with the best people, please send a WORD copy of your resume to mail to :   jobs@dunnsolutions.com  and put “BI Project Manager - IL” in the subject line of email.

 

No agencies or phone calls, please. Only qualified candidates will be contacted. For more information about Dunn Solutions Group, please visit us at our website at www.dunnsolutions.com.

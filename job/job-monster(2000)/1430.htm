Electronic Marketing Specialist

About the Job

Job Description:  Electronic Marketing Specialist

Our client is currently looking for an electronic marketing specialist to assist in the deployment of Salesforce.com, manage existing instances, and provide support to users, as well as administer company websites and e-marketing initiatives.  This is a new position and will report to the Director of Marketing.  In this role, you will serve as the organization's primary manager of the Salesforce.com application as well as perform both routine and ad hoc administrative tasks. 


Please send your qualified resume to tkelly@corpit.com

Key tasks and responsibilities include but are not limited to:

Salesforce.com:

·        Administration of the Salesforce.com application – data management, configuration, potential workflow, training and security

·        Provide customized reporting for executives

·        Assist in planning and deployment of new salesforce instances

·        Manage ongoing support requests and administrative needs of users

·        Collaborate with sales and marketing to evaluate existing lead distribution and qualifying processes, and to provide suggestions designed to improve and automate these processes

·        Communicate regularly to user groups in order to spread best practices, details and training on new features and functionality, and other relevant information

·        Maintain user roles, security, profiles, workflow rules, etc.

·        Create and maintain updated system documentation

·        Develop and maintain custom reports, dashboards, and eventually workflow

·        Set up and train, and/or assist in the development of training curriculum

·        Recommend and implement continuous improvements


Other Electronic Marketing:

·        Administer company websites and electronic properties

·        Administer electronic marketing initiatives, like monthly newsletters

·        Collaborate with sales and marketing to track the effectiveness of electronic marketing initiatives

·        Maintain brand look and feel on all electronic properties pursuant to graphic design standards.

·        Help to ensure integrity and relevance of site content

·        Optimizing web page code and content, and marketing the website.

 

Skills / Qualifications:

·        Bachelors Degree in Business or Technology field or equivalent work experience

·        Salesforce.com administration experience strongly preferred

·        Must be a self starter

·        Proficient in Microsoft Office, especially Excel and Access

·        Familiar with database design and construction.  Working knowledge of SQL a plus

·        Proficient in CSS, HMTL and Java.  C, C++ and PHP experience a plus

·        Proficient in Photoshop (GIMP) and Illustrator (Inkscape)

·        Strong orientation to detail, process and prioritization

·        Ability to work cross-functionally and with all levels of an organization

·        Excellent customer service skills

·        Ability to translate customer requirements into specifications



Please send your qualified resume to tkelly@corpit.com

About the Job


Repair Center Supervisor

We are looking for someone with leadership and project management skills to lead our repair center functions which include in and out-of-warranty repairs, an exchange program and third party authorized repair centers. This position requires a “hands-on” individual committed to continuous improvement in a lean manufacturing environment.  The supervisor will have the following responsibilities·

  • Supervise first shift hourly personnel

  • Manage accuracy and timeliness of all repairs

  • Oversee inventory control system to track status of repair sales orders,  exchange and consignment inventory levels

  • Manage cycle counting and Kanban replenishment process

  • Maintain shop floor appearance in accordance with 5S standards.

The ideal candidate for this position has:

  • Proven ability to successfully supervise operations of an electronic repair center including the entire repair process from in-bound to out-bound.

  • Experienced in hiring, training, leading and supervising order processing and repair staff.

  • Ability to effectively work with other internal departments including Manufacturing & Engineering, and inventory

  • Inventory and parts management.  Experience with advanced exchange (hot swap) programs preferred

  • In-depth experience using Excel for data analysis and reporting.

  • Experience with tools, testing, processes and quality assurance related to electronic repair.

  • Experience with adhering to departmental budgets and proven ability to operate within budget.

  •  Experience and ability to interact with customers and resolve issues.

  • 5-10 years in the electronics repair field with at least 2 years in a supervisory role position.

  • What you will get

HME offers a friendly working environment and a competitive compensation package with attractive benefits. We are committed to building a company where highly talented, challenge-hungry people thrive. You will have the opportunity to learn and grow, while making a real contribution to our product. The benefits package includes tuition reimbursement, paid vacation and holidays, medical, vision and dental coverage, 401K match and life insurance.

We’re the leader in Wireless Quick Service Restaurant Communication.  Founded in 1971, HM Electronics, Inc. (HME) is an innovative electronics technology company focused on enhancing productivity and customer service for our customers.  Our ground breaking products, passion for excellence and commitment to service have earned us the distinction of being the leader in quick service restaurant and professional audio communication technologies.

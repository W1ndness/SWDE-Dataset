Sr. Database and Web Developer

About the Job

Lexmark International, Inc. (NYSE: LXK) provides businesses of all sizes with a broad range of printing and imaging products, software, solutions and services that help them to be more productive. In 2009, Lexmark sold products in more than 170 countries and reported approximately $4.0 billion in revenue. Learn how Lexmark can help you get more done at www.lexmark.com .

Seeking a Sr. Database and Web Developer for our next generation of Cloud Solutions. The candidate must have 10+ years experience in commercial database design and development especially with respect to web based applications. Strong experience with Oracle 11g and MS SQL server is required. Strong background in Web 2.0 technologies, eCommerce, SaaS and SOA architectures is also required. The candidate must have industry experience of developing and architecting web based solutions and interfacing with backend systems. The candidate must also be able to see through a product from its inception and requirements phase until its delivery and support phase with minimal guidance and direction.

The candidate must have strong background in web based programming languages such as HTML, DHTML, JavaScript, Java & XML. Experience with commercial and open source web servers such as Apache and Tomcat as well as network protocols and technologies would also be considered a plus.

The candidate will be responsible for developing high quality software based on best practices in the software industry. Duties may include research, evaluation, development and application of new processes and methods for current and future products. The specialist level is viewed as an expert in a given field for a business unit or department. Assignments necessitate advanced knowledge for more complex technical assignments. Candidate must be able to develop, mentor and coach others as well.

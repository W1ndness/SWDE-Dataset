Senior Architect

About the Job

The Senior Architect’s primary focus will be providing technical leadership during the pre-sales and delivery phases of cloud computing solutions, currently Salesforce.com and Force.com.  The successful candidate will be an expert in architecting, scoping and estimation for Salesforce.com custom extensions and Force.com custom solutions, that leverage APEX, Visualforce, Salesforce.com APIs, and other tools and platforms (e.g. Google App Engine and Amazon Web Services).  Responsibilities include leading technical architecture and requirements gathering workshops, architecting, designing and documenting technical solutions, developing and testing customizations and integration points, mentoring and leading other team members and providing hands-on assistance with technical issues, as needed. Strong experience managing client expectations is also critical to this role.

 

Required Skills/Experience:

·       5+ years of experience leading architecture, custom development, systems integration and package implementations, in positions of increasing responsibility

·       Experience gained with top-tier consulting organizations and strong client-facing skills

·       Thrives in a pre-sales scoping capacity, with strong estimation skills

·       A proven track record of delivering high quality enterprise solutions, aligning business and technology needs

·       Experience with Salesforce.com Sales Cloud and Service Cloud customization and custom development on the Force.com platform (i.e. APIs, APEX & Visualforce)

·       Integration experience with XML, HTTP/HTTPS, Java, C++, and ASP environments and a thorough understanding of XML, SQL, and enterprise application integration

·       Experience with ETL tools, such as Informatica, Cast Iron and Boomi

·       Knowledge of enterprise systems, CRM, SFA, and ERP

·       Excellent analysis skills and the ability to develop processes and methodologies

·       Excellent presentation and communication skills

·       B.S. in Business, Computer Science, Software Engineering, MIS or equivalent work experience

 

Desired Skills/Experience:

·       A year or more of hands on development experience with the Salesforce.com API, APEX and VisualForce, as well as completion of several Salesforce.com integration projects in a development and lead technical role

·       Salesforce.com Developer and Advanced Developer Certification

 

CoreMatrix Systems offers a competitive compensation package, including comprehensive benefits.  If you are interested exploring opportunities with us, please send your confidential resume to Kathy Coppins at kcoppins@corematrix.com .

 

CoreMatrix is a leading provider of cloud computing consulting, systems integration, custom application development and support services for mid-sized to Fortune 500 companies. With experience gained from more than 1,000 cloud computing engagements, CoreMatrix is dedicated to helping customers achieve their objectives and obtain the greatest value from their investments in technology.  Customers include: American Express® Travel Management Services, PharmaNet, P2 Energy Solutions, WebMD® and Syniverse® Technologies.

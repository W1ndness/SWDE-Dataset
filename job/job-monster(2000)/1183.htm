Senior Digital Designer

About the Job

 Company Overview 

Terralever, a Digital Marketing Agency headquartered in Phoenix, AZ is seeking highly motivated individuals for the Senior Digital Designer position.

 

Terralever has grown rapidly by adding highly experienced people who perform at an expert level, and by attracting clients who are seeking premium service and impeccably managed online marketing and interactive projects. If you want to work for a firm that values your experience, integrity and passion for the business, then we are the company for you.

 

You can find us on the web at www.terralever.com.

 

Please note: 

  • You must have 3-5 years of digital design experience in an AGENCY environment
  • Include salary requirements with your resume
  • Include the most current digital portfolio and/or (if applicable) a link to your work along with descriptions of the role played in each project

 

Essential Duties & Responsibilities: 

The ideal candidate will work closely with the Senior Art Director and other project team members in translating initial ideas into engaging digital campaigns. The designer will be expected to see the bigger picture and think about interactivity constantly. Strong design skills and the ability to autonomously deliver work of the highest quality is a must. The following are key items the Designer is responsible for on a daily basis.

§  Effective collaboration with assigned project teams to deliver finished work within designated budget and time constraints

§  Working with the art director to turn initial concepts into live campaigns

§  Work closely with the development team to ensure creative ideas are technically feasible and leverage the latest technologies and best practices

§  Create interactive design concepts based on detailed wireframes

§  Create engaging designs that meet or exceed expectations

Qualifications: 

§  3+ years working in digital advertising & campaigns in an agency environment.

§  An acute understanding of the online industry from production practices to interaction and navigational models.

§  A comprehensive knowledge of UX principles

§  The ability to ideate and articulate creative ideas and reasons clearly

§  Intimate knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Flash

§  Strong graphical and conceptual skills

§  Highly motivated and passionate

§  The desire to learn and grow with a world class agency

Compensation:

The chosen candidate will receive a base salary based on experience and track record, plus has the potential for annual bonus. Generous health and life style benefits are available in addition to a matching 401K.     

Contact Us: 

Apply by emailing monster@terralever.com . Please use ‘Senior Digital Designer for Phoenix’ as your subject line. No phone calls please!

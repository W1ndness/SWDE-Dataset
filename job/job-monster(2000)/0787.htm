Digital Media Project Manager

About the Job

TMG, based in Washington, D.C., and one of the country’s leading custom-media firms, seeks a digital media project manager to work across multiple business/technology-related disciplines within TMG.

The Digital Media Project Manager is responsible for managing custom digital media production's initiatives in a dynamic publishing environment within TMG. Using provided functional specs, the Digital Media Project Manager will work with Production team to oversee the production of custom digital media projects.

 

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:
Maintaining and meeting multiple tight project deadlines.
Creating specs and determining best plan of action to complete projects which may include managing outside vendors as needed.
Communicating with Production team regarding best practices based upon customer requirements.
Develop web-based or related multi-media titles.
 Notifying team members and stakeholders of schedule changes that will affect delivery dates.
Identifying and resolving conflict areas in priorities with management.
Performing documentation updates as needed.

Reviews all creative and technical deliverables to ensure all work remains in scope.
Other duties may be assigned as needed.

 

 

Skills:
 Exceptional written and verbal communication
 Strong problem analysis and resolution
 Proven experience in managing project risks
 Practical experience with requirements gathering and documentation
 Proven ability to manage multiple concurrent projects and people in a team based environment and to develop highly successful cross-functional working relationships
Understanding of web technologies and online software development practices
Experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript a plus

At least 3 years of Project Management experience in an ecommerce or online marketing environment

Requirements:
 Bachelor's degree or the equivalent experience.
3-4 years demonstrated experience in the publishing industry or digital media environment.


This is a great opportunity to work in Washington for a firm with a quality reputation and a fantastic staff. Our office is Metro accessible. Excellent salary and benefits.

 

Please visit our web site at http://tmgcustommedia.com and submit your application and resume (go to: who we are > working here > opportunities).

VTC Video Teleconference Technician

About the Job

Summit Technologies, Inc. has openings for VTC Video Teleconference Technicians who are experienced in scheduling with AMX, Xtron, Polycom or Tandberg experience in an enterprise VTC setting.  These full-time positions are located at multiple sites in Washington DC or  in Loudoun County, VA. Candidates will need 9+ years of related experience and a Bachelors degree or equivalent experience. 

Job Title: VTC Video Teleconference Technicians w/ Top Secret


Job Description:
The successful candidate(s) will provide technical and administrative support of video teleconference units, including scheduling of classified and unclassified Internet (IP) and ISDN video teleconferences for CONUS and OCONUS customer needs.

Job Duties: 

  • Work in a team environment to operate all video teleconference systems and facility operations
  • Provide experienced users desk-side instructions on the use of equipment
  • Responsible for training personnel on the use and operation of VTC systems
  • Utilize scheduling software to ensure proper coordination of users, participants, schedulers and facilitators to ensure uninterrupted and trouble-free transmission of conferences
  • Provide pre-conference testing and setup for video teleconferences and/or meetings
  • Troubleshoot Video Teleconference network issues (Routers, Switches, ATM, AMX, Xtron Tandberg and Polycom systems)
  • Ensure signal quality is maintained through the use of circuit and system performance tests, determine point of signal distortion and implement preventive or corrective action as required
  • Connect systems to Local Area Network (LAN) or ISDN network
  • Perform system monitoring, fault isolation and circuit restoration of communication suites in the various frequency spectrums
  • Operate crypto keying functions on equipment for secure video teleconferences
  • Support of customer sites for maintenance and circuit issues.  


    Desired experience & skills:

  • Demonstrated work experience in enterprise video conferencing with AMX, Xtron, Tandberg and/or Polycom preferred
  • 3+ years of large video telecommunications system operations, troubleshooting and support required
  • Demonstrated knowledge of TCP/IP, and ISDN network protocol
  • Knowledge of encryption software and technologies preferred
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft 2000/2003 server, especially Active Directory
  • Experience in troubleshooting network components between VTC system segments and understanding of telnet, remote login, and other related commands
  • Ability to work with Senior Executive Staff with setting up VTC meetings
  • Strong organizational skills; able to manage priorities and workflow
  • Excellent communication and customer service skills with superior troubleshooting skills

     

    Desired Education & Certifications:

  • A Cisco certification is required
  • Candidates will need 9+ years of related experience and a Bachelors degree or equivilant experience.  
  • 3+ years of experience in using AMX, Xtron, Polycom or Tandberg VTC systems
  • AMX, Xtron, Tandberg and/or Polycom Video Conferencing certification a plus
  • Associates or Bachelors degree in an IT- related technical discipline preferred
  • Working knowledge of H320 and H323 protocols preferred
  • CTS certification

     

    Clearance Requirement(s):

  • These positions require a DoD Top Secret or Top Secret/SCI clearance
  • Only United States Citizens will be considered for these opportunities
  • All qualified candidates must pass a pre-employment background investigation conducted by the government as well as the government’s clearance investigation.

     

    Location(s):  Washington DC; Loudoun County, VA

    Compensation & Benefits: 
    Salary will be commensurate with experience.

    Benefits:  Summit Technologies offers a variety of benefits, including, medical, dental, short and long term disability insurances; 401(k) retirement plan, paid holidays and time off.

Norfolk Southern - IT Department



Network Engineer

Job Description: Norfolk Southern corporation is currently seeking a Network Engineer for a permanenet position in the Technology department, located in our midtown Atlanta office.

Education: Minimum four year degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, or related field.

Responsibilities:

Candidate will work as a member of a group responsible for architecture, design and implementation of IP data and telecommunications network connectivity within the NS enterprise. Candidate should have an advanced understanding of architecture and design issues in a complex enterprise network.

Various projects and areas of responsibility include the following: WAN/LAN network design and implementation, conventional and VoIP telephony, IP addressing, mobile networking, network services (e.g. DNS/DHCP), Internet/DMZ/VPN environments, 802.11 wireless systems, videoconferencing, network security (firewalls, IDS/IPS), as well as network monitoring, management, and performance tools and methods. Depending on the project, some travel and/or overtime work may be required.

A significant part of the required daily activities involves documentation related to project management and change management methodologies, technical standards, and design.

Qualifications:

Candidate will satisfy the majority of the following list of qualifications.

  • Six years experience in an architect, design and/or project implementation role for a large enterprise network.
  • Hands-on experience with Cisco network hardware including routers, switches, and security appliances
  • Knowledge and experience with networking protocols and configurations including OSPF, BGP, HSRP, multicast, SIP, QoS, VLANs, and MPLS.
  • Experience with design and implementation of data center networks, including virtual server environments.
  • Experience with design and implementation of telephone systems and related voice technologies
  • Experience with wireless and mobile networking
  • Experience with the design of physical networks including fiber and copper cabling
  • Knowledge and experience with network management systems
  • Familiarity with operating systems (Cisco IOS, LINUX, Windows) and scripting languages
  • Ability to work effectively alone and as a member of a team to complete assignments
  • Must have the initiative to create structure and definition around a project, and to drive it through to successful completion
  • Experience in project management methodology
  • Ability to maintain focus on multiple projects concurrently
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills.

We are proud to be an EEO/AA employer M/F/D/V. We maintain a drug-free workplace and perform pre-employment substance abuse testing.

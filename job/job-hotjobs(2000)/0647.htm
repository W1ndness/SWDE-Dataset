Purpose of Role: A Systems Analyst II (SA II) interfaces with business partners (internal and external) and IT in estimating, prioritizing, analyzing, designing, testing and documenting systems applications, from requirements gathering to final implementation and maintenance. SA II’s work directly with business partners at various levels within the organization and with outside parties to clarify and define business and technical requirements with sufficient detail to allow IT development staff to collaboratively develop the solution proposal.

Clinical or healthcare experience preferred.

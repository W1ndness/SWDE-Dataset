Sales Technical Support Specialist



The Sales Technical Support Specialist will support all existing products and assist the product team in evaluating and implementing new products. They will also assist in the development of any sales related applications.

Professional who is a hands-on resource that has familiarity with Sales, all aspects of web based systems including technical requirements, configuration, user requirements and all related policies and procedures. Maintains a high-level familiarity with other IT systems/business applications that have an impact on all of our products usage and configurations.

Key Activities:
-Know and understand the sales strategies, plans and goals of the Product Management department.
-Ensure functionality meets field needs and to develop ongoing project plan and improvements.
-Lead and/or contribute to the technical side of projects, manage internal customer relationships with the business and create solutions to meet business challenges.
-Perform technical evaluations, solution and design approaches to business solution needs.
-Write technical approach and design documentation.
-Create integration and development project plans and designs as required by the product team.
-Provide technical support to all sales professionals
-Assist with the execution of all configuration changes, including new releases of all third party products.
-Hold continuous updates with the Sr. CRM Developer to ensure they receive the appropriate level of communication in regard to all third party products and any issues, programming needs or technical support that may be needed.
-Escalate issues using good discretion.
-Help identify obstacles to success and focus on continuous improvement.
-Use situational analysis to determine the best outcome.
-Anticipate and address problems outside the normal scope of the job and offer solutions.
-Adhere to company policy, Code of Ethics and assigned standards of performance and ensures staff is in compliance.


Skill Requirements:
-Must have excellent communication and listening skills.
-Excellent project management skills.
-Ability to craft solutions quickly based on situation.
-Strong technical design skills with a creative flare in devising products & solutions.
-Knowledge of enterprise systems: CRM, SFA, and ERP.
-Front-end programming knowledge including CSS, HTML, and Javascript
-Back-end Programming using OOP techniques with PHP and/or Python
-Proven ability to design and optimize business processes and to integrate business processes across disparate systems.
-Experience with Integrated Development Environments.
-Experience with supporting and managing Servers running in a Linux environment.
-Demonstrated deployments of enterprise software systems using industry standard environments including XML, HTTP/HTTPS, PHP, and Python
-Must be able to work well under pressure of multiple deadlines and assignments.
-Must have a diligent work ethic and be dependable.
-Must be able to use systematic thinking, the ability to persuade and exhibit effective training skills.
-Possess general knowledge of web applications and internet architecture.
-Demonstrated ability to provide effective feedback.
-Proficient use of Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook) and CRM systems.
-BS in Business, Computer Science, Software Engineering, MIS or Technical or comparable combination of education and experience.
-Limited travel required
-Must possess a valid driver’s license, good driving record and company’s required level of personal auto liability insurance

To apply for this position please visit us at http://www.scripps.com/careers/jobsearch.html and search for requisition #2853.

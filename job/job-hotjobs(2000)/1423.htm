IT Staffing Account Manager: FirstPRO, Inc.

Are you ready to be a part of one of the“Ten Best Staffing Firms to Work For” in the United States?



firstPRO has been named to Staffing Industry Analysts Best Staffing Firms in 2009, a company where management recognizes employees who work hard and meet goals and listens to staff suggestions. We are looking for an Account Manager with proven sales experience and relationships. Join our winning team and you will have a chance to innovate, grow, and to do what really matters: sell quality solutions that resonate with your customers delivered by a world class company.

The job of the Account Manager is to manage and prospect into a group of national/regional (Fortune 1000 or named) accounts, to build and maintain relationships from the operational through the C-level within those accounts, and to achieve an established quota.

Responsibilities:

· Develop and manage sales funnel to analyze and manage pipeline activity and monitor sales activity against assigned quotas.

· Increase channel effectiveness by establishing and implementing marketing programs and maintaining high-level relationships.

· Develop and maintain a continuous residual staffing business by both farming existing accounts and hunting for new ones and new opportunities.

· Develop and implement sales strategies leading to expansion of existing and new sales channels and markets.

· Develop strong, ongoing relationships by demonstrating firstPRO's high quality standards and genuine concern for client satisfaction.

· Provide accurate and timely sales reporting feedback to firstPRO management including but not limited to product forecasting and service forecasting based on thorough analysis and customer dialogue.

· Acquire and integrate industry knowledge related to general trends, emerging technologies, and competitors.

· Gather information on the customer's business processes, critical success factors, and competitive standing through a strategic and consultative sales approach to deliver value-added business solutions

· Create and maintain accurate Account Plans and review those plans with firstPRO management.

· Structure presentations, offerings, and contract negotiations (e.g. MSAs, SOWs, Pricing Addendums) so pursuits move to closed business.

· Ensure internal procedures and processes are followed by completing pursuit reviews, expense reports, time cards, etc.

· Collaborate with internal resources to share information and coordinate sales.

· Provide support, mentoring, and leadership to sales team members as needed.

· Contribute to market strategy by monitoring competitive products and communicating client reactions.

· Recommend new solutions and services by continually evaluating current solutions.

· Deliver on commitments in a timely manner and resolving issues promptly to ensure customer satisfaction

· Update technical, sales, and business knowledge by participating in educational opportunities.

Qualifications:

· Bachelors Degree desired

· 5-10 years business to business sales experience

· At least 5 years of experience in direct sales of IT Staffing Services

· Ability to travel as much as 20%. Must be able to attend on-site customer appointments as needed

· Core Competencies: Customer Focus; Teamwork; Communication; Accountability; Innovation

· Must have a demonstrated sales track record with industry contacts in the staffing sector.

· Must have demonstrated success in building new sources of revenue through existing and new customers.

· Highly motivated individual with excellent interpersonal, communication (verbal and written) and presentation skills and the ability to deliver convincing technical presentations and demonstrations to customer audiences of varying sizes.

· Ability to effectively communicate and interface with all clients levels, from operational through C-level, as well as peers and executive level management in firstPRO.

· Advanced knowledge, understanding, and experience in business and operational systems including experience with ATS (Maxhire), VMS, and custom solutions as well as hosting and managed services.

· Proficient in contract negotiations

· High degree of computer application proficiency with Microsoft Office Suites of products.

· Ability to work well under pressure, execute results against strategy and meet critical deadlines.

· Excellent organizational skills and ability to set priorities and expectations while being responsive to the customer

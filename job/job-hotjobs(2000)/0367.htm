A893 Sr. Business Analyst




Position Summary

Collaboratively work with the various business areas of the organization to capture business ideas for improvement/expansion, perform the necessary analysis in collaboration with Product Management to validate the ideas and if approved by management perform all of the business development activities to see the business idea to a successful launch. In the course of the business development cycle the individual will be creating the business idea, business proposal, and business plan, gathering business requirements, facilitating SME’s through the creating of the sales, marketing and launch plans and creating the necessary test plans to test the gathered business requirements. The individual will also work with Project Management to ensure proper project planning, analysis and reporting is performed based on the activities of the project.

The Business Consultant is a crucial role in creating and maintaining the strategic partnership between Business needs and Solution delivery.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

Analysis and Solution Definition – 35%

  • Quickly understand the business issues and data challenges of business issue
  • Identifies organization's strengths and weaknesses and suggests areas of improvement.
  • Develop solution definition in terms of business, marketing, sales and launch planning
  • Critically evaluate information gathered from multiple sources, reconcile conflicts, decompose high-level information into details, abstract up from low-level information to a general understanding, and distinguish user requests from the underlying true needs.
  • Identify and recommend solutions and requirements via industry standard analysis techniques such as data flow modeling, workflow analysis, and functional decomposition analysis.
  • Develop business requirements and related business rules and processes based on business needs.
  • Develop business test plans that will successfully test the gathered business requirements.
  • Solicit requirements through interviews, workshops, and/or existing systems documentation or procedures.
  • Review and edit plans, requirements, specifications, business processes and recommendations related to proposed solution.
  • Work closely with the Business Partner and Business Subject Matter Expert(s) in the definition, testing, training, implementation, and support of business plans and functional requirements.
  • Drive and challenge business units on their assumptions of how they will successfully execute their plans

In general, this phase of the role will include the creation/revision of the following analysis artifacts and deliverables in conjunction with the business SME:

  • business idea
  • business proposal
  • business plan
  • test plan
  • sales plan
  • marketing plan
  • business launch plan

Project Execution – 40%

  • Execution of the various business plans to ensure successful launch of the business solution.
  • Assists in enforcement of project deadlines and schedules.
  • Communicates and applies Business Development methodology
  • Communicates and applies project standards.
  • Successfully engage in multiple initiatives simultaneously

In general, this phase of the role will include the actual execution of the following plans in conjunction with the business stakeholders and sponsor:

  • sales plan
  • marketing plan
  • business launch plan

Communication 10%

  • Assists in the facilitation of team and client meetings.
  • Deliver informative, well-organized presentations.
  • Communicate difficult/sensitive information tactfully.
  • Participate in weekly status meetings with Business Partners to facilitate the understanding, clarification, and implementation of business solutions.
  • Manage and help communicate the process of innovative change.

Client Management – 10%

  • Develops relationships with client personnel that foster client ties.
  • Communicate effectively with clients to identify needs and evaluate alternative business solutions.
  • Continually seeks opportunities to increase customer satisfaction and deepen client relationships.
  • Manages client expectations effectively.
  • Consistently delivers high-quality services to our clients

Teamwork – 5%

  • Facilitates effective team interaction.
  • Takes input from supervisor and appropriately and accurately applies comments/feedback.
  • Provide critical feedback on collaborative efforts where others are involved

Education/Experience/Skills Required:

  • BA/BS Degree in a business related field or other technology degree preferred
  • 4-6 years experience in specifying and building business solutions
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to interact professionally with a diverse group, executives, managers, and subject matter experts.
  • Must be flexible and willing to undertake a wide variety of challenging tasks.
  • Strong communications, written and oral, email and telephone
  • Microsoft Office
  • Ability to excel in a fast paced, swiftly changing, challenging business environment
  • Strong facilitation skills.
  • Must be able to elicit requirements from various personalities across organizational and geographical boundaries
  • Proven experience at driving consensus across multiple stakeholders
  • Process driven analysis skills and a background in various requirements elicitation methods
  • Strong analytical skills required including a thorough understanding of how to interpret customer business needs and translate them into business and operational requirements.

Program / Project Manager: Talon

Our client, located in Princeton, NJ is seeking a Program / Project Manager for an immediate contract position. Details include:

Manage project managers, manage partners, major projects/project components or multiple mid/small projects within focused programs. Manage all aspects of a project, from beginning to end, with direct accountability for project execution while leading a project team, or teams, to accomplish specific objectives in a given time frame and with limited resources to return specific value to the business.

Manage all aspects of monthly project accounting and reporting. Responsible for preparation of project financial reporting and tracking. Lead and manage weekly and daily reporting, issue tracking and resolution.

MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES AND TASKS

ACCOUNTABILITIES: (Key roles and responsibilities for this position.)

-Manage major projects with overall accountability for project success while also managing one or more project managers on numerous mid to smaller projects.

-When needed, act as a Program Manager, with accountability for related series of projects, executed over a broad period of time, which are designed to accomplish broad goals of the program to which these individual projects contribute.

-Implement and follow Project Management Office standard methodologies, processes and tools, develop and maintains project documentation, work plans and project deliverables. -Guide others in the use of the methodology and tools.

-Manage and motivate project teams, planning work assignments and schedules and ensuring deliverables are completed on time and within budget. Guide and monitor work performance.

-Accountable to Spartan Program leadership for project delivery and directions while simultaneously accountable to Enterprise IT-PMO for project methodology and reporting.

-Work with program sponsor(s) and core program team to establish project performance goals, metrics and baselines.

-Establish and maintain effective communications with team members, stakeholders, senior management and contacts. Communicate project status to identified stakeholders through routine status reports and meetings.

-Initiate and maintain issues logs, taking responsibility for identifying and coordinating resolution of all technical and business issues.

-Ensure all project time is accounted for and project and team member activities are routinely collected and reported in standard fashion. Estimate resources needed on projects to ensure success.

-Where appropriate, coordinate vendor/partner activities to ensure project success. Spartan projects will have major partner team components.

-Assist in the advancement of project management competencies, including development of methodology and frameworks, development and conduct of education/training completion of quality reviews and coaching/mentoring of others engaged in project management activities.

Experience: Required:

-10-12 years of management and/or project management experience to lead projects as well as guide others on project management techniques.

-8 years of direct senior project management experience with at least 4 years in a role of direct accountability as senior project manager delivering large complex move/migration related multi-phased projects.

-Must have led major infrastructure projects in the capacity of senior project manager.

-Direct experience with delivery of at least one major application project as the primary project manager.

-Direct experience with call center build out and integration.

-Direct experience with people moves and migrations – space consolidation, restacking.

-Must have 3-4 years experience managing Infrastructure projects and knowledge of data centers, servers, storage, and networks.

-Selected candidate should have deep technical knowledge and is preferred if they have worked first in a technical tower them progressed their career into project management.

-Demonstrated capability to manage projects with budgets in excess of $1m leading these projects to successful implementation and full life cycle management.

-Proven ability to work with and collaborate with multiple departments, facilities, business, call center, etc. to co-partner solutions and resolutions that meet the combined overall project objectives for all stake holder departments.

-Excellent oral and written communication skills to provide clear messages to all levels.

-Demonstrated ability to effectively establish and maintain working relationships with peers and constituents at all levels of the organization.

-Demonstrated analysis and problems solving skills using innovative thinking.

Preferred:

-Experience with standard project management tools, methodologies and frameworks.

-Broad working knowledge and experience with healthcare applications and technology.

-Experience in managing large scale projects.

-Experience working with and negotiating with external vendors.

-Ability to lead and manage multi-related fast paced projects with dependencies and predecessors.

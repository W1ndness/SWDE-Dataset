POS Hardware and Software Installers: ESS

ESS is a nationwide single source technology provider of Point-of-Sale (POS) Systems, hardware installation, cabling, site surveys, on-site service, preventative maintenance, hardware procurement, staging, depot repair and parts and supplies for the restaurant, hospitality and retail industries.

ESS is a rapidly growing company that has immediate openings for motivated, dedicated and hard working POS Hardware and Software installers throughout the country. Our Installers perform Hardware Installation, Software Installation and Maintenance to customers in the restaurant and retail industries. All Installers work out of their homes and these positions require possible overnight work, travel to different places and possible weekend work.

Currently, we are actively looking for POS Hardware and Software Installers to work on a specific project that is going to last at least 18 months in duration. All work is performed at night and you will work in teams at a large fast food chain's locations across the country. You will be working a regular schedule of Sunday - Thursday every week and traveling extensively. Compensation for this position starts at $31,500 plus overtime (experience level is considered for higher amounts) and after some initial training and working on your own, within 90 days, you have the ability to make over 10-20% more. After that, increases are possible based upon meeting specific certification levels. All travel expenses (air, car, hotel) are pre-paid by ESS and any other expenses are paid by you and reimbursed within 2 weeks through payroll.

Strong troubleshooting and customer service skills are what we desire! Training will be provided for these positions; however, basic technical skills are very helpful.

Requirements:

  • Prior experience with Point-of-Sale is a benefit, but not required
  • Basic understanding of Network Wiring - Cat5 and Ethernet Cabling
  • Basic understanding of Computer Hardware - Hubs, Switches, Network Cards, etc.
  • A+ Certification or equivalent recommended
  • Basic knowledge of PC's
  • Windows and DOS Knowledge
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills with good problem solving and analytical expertise
  • Good communication and excellent customer service skills, "customer friendly" attitude
  • Detail-oriented with ability to work independently and handle multiple tasks
  • Good Driving Record, Background check and Drug testing are required for employment

ESS offers a competitive compensation package and an excellent benefits program for full-time employees, which includes health, dental and life insurance, STD, LTD and 401K with company matching. Resumes must be sent via email to: careers@esspos.com, attention Human Resources. For more information about ESS, please check out our website at www.esspos.com

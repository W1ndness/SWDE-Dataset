***This role is available in Newark DE or Fishers IN.***

JOB SUMMARY:
The Senior Business Systems Analyst in Technology Demand and Management (TDM), is responsible for gathering business requirements and providing business analysis guidance to Sallie Mae Customer Service Centers and as needed Servicing Operations. This position will focus on the needs of Private Credit Servicing, ED Servicing and some Sales/Originations efforts as well. The Snr. Business Systems Analyst works closely with each business area to understand its needs and challenges, identify opportunities for collaboration, and translates the business's needs and opportunities into well-defined business requirements for new IT projects and several Operational Alignment (OA) Initiatives. The Snr Business Systems Analyst will also have User Acceptance Test responsibilities that could include a range of test coordination, recommendation of planning/scenario development, defect tracking and metrics reporting. Qualified candidates demonstrate their ability to support multiple projects of medium to high complexity, interact with business management and able to translate business needs into technology requirements or provide guidance to achieve overall success. The role will support projects from the beginning of the SDLC life cycle through implementation for systems such as FDR, CARES, CIS, MYL and CLASS.

Depending on experience and other qualifications this position can be filled as a Systems Analyst (11). To be considered for the Systems Analyst position the applicant must have demonstrated experience in management/leadership, process improvement, project management, and be technically adept.

JOB FUNCTIONS:
1. Requirements and Analysis - Work closely with each business area to understand its needs and opportunities, identify opportunities for improvement and translate the business's needs and opportunities into well-defined business requirements for new IT projects. Provide subject matter expertise in servicing operations and system enhancements.

2. Project and Resource Co-ordination -- Co-ordinate with multiple business/IT participants on medium to large projects.

3. Customer Service - Actively support Customer Service Center project implementation and provide excellent customer support to Contact Centers Business Operations and other Servicing Operations.

4. Training and UAT support -- Provide project requirements and business solution content to training and UAT.

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:
Bachelor's Degree Information Technology or another related field

Additional equivalent experience above the required minimum may substitute for the checked level of education
EXPERIENCE :
MUST HAVE:
* A minimum of 5 years experience in financial services industry, preferably in education finance or the student loan industry. General experience includes increasing responsibilities in operations or information systems analysis and design.
* Experience in building, managing, and delivering Customer Service Centers or Servicing Operations related projects.
* Experience in Conversion projects.
* Experience in Process Improvement initiative and Sallie Mae Process Asset Library (SMPAL).
* Experience in SDLC requirement, Security, and Change Management.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS and ABILITIES:
* Ability to produce high quality results in a dynamic work environment that involves numerous system and business process changes, new service/product development, regulatory changes, and volume fluctuations.
* High degree of initiative and flexibility. Must personally be able to set a great example and embrace and quickly adapt to change.
* Excellent verbal and written communication skills. Must be able to communicate professionally with customers, colleagues, and all levels of Sallie Mae management and personnel.
* Ability to effectively communicate technical information to a wide audience for complete understanding is required. Must be able to make effective presentations to a wide-variety of audiences and under varying circumstances.
* Excellent interpersonal skills. Must be able to successfully interact with senior management, staff, customers, and vendors at all levels.
* Demonstrated project co-ordination and organizational skills. Must be able to effectively coordinate a team to deliver the desired results in the time frame expected. Must be able to evaluate and effectively prioritize several projects independently.
* Excellent analytical and problem solving skills. Must be able to identify the root causes to problems and develop solutions to eliminate re-occurrence.
* Excellent decision-making skills. Must be able to make good business/IT decisions using factual and sometimes limited data.
* Excellent working knowledge of the student loan industry, Contact Centers, and Sallie Mae business processes.

Sallie Mae is a federal government contractor. Should this position support a government contract, now or in the future, the successful candidate will be subject to a background check conducted by the U.S. Government to determine eligibility and suitability for federal contract employment for public trust or sensitive positions.

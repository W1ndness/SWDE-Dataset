We are currently sourcing for a position with our client

Established in 1995, Rangam Consultants is a multiple service provider in the Information Technology, Scientific, Engineering and administrative fields. We have a proven track record for successfully delivering mission critical consulting, support and recruiting services to various government and Fortune 500 clients.


The details for this position are as follows:

Job Title: Decision Support Analyst

Job Code: RCI-CON-1123

Job location:- Secaucus, NJ, 07094

Duration: Full-Time

JOB DESCRIPTION:

Primary Duties and Responsibilities:

The Decision Support Analyst primarily develops operational and analytical reports against a variety of internal and external systems. The individual must be highly proficient in all aspects of the report lifecycle: Requirements gathering, writing report specifications, designing, coding, testing, iterations/refinement, quality practices, UAT/QA deployment, production support, remediation, etc. Assignments will range from simple reports based on one database to complex information sets pulled form disparate systems.

Additional responsibilities include data integration, process automation, writing ad-hoc queries, creating validation scripts, and supporting new technology products and services.

Job Responsibilities:

· Work with the Decision Support Manager, internal business customers, and external clients to define requirements, set priorities, and plan reporting projects

· Drive reporting requests from inception through delivery within specified timelines

· Maintain, tune, and enhance existing business critical reports

· Collaborate with business analysts and stakeholders to define, document, and fully understand initial project requirements before coding begins

· Continually emphasize data quality to ensure that all deliverables are robust, accurate, and highly regarded by information consumers

· Strive for service excellence by being proactive and responsive to all client inquiries within defined SLAs

· Create and present internal Knowledgebase articles that inform all department personnel of individual technology accomplishments

Job Qualifications:

  • SQL programming: Queries, Stored Procedures, Functions, Optimization
  • Solid knowledge of MS SQL Server
  • Ability to interpret business logic and incorporate precisely into code
  • Capacity to successfully manage simultaneous requests from start to finish
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills

Education/Experience:

  • Minimum 3 years experience with business analysis and report development lifecycle
  • Demonstrated success developing complex SQL based reports in business environments
  • Understanding of healthcare or pharmaceutical industry desirable
  • Bachelor’s degree in information technology, computer science, or engineering

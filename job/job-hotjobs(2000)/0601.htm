Positions Most Attractive and Unique Qualities

§ Experienced in writing configuration management plans and documents

§ Ability to coordinate and lead simultaneous projects

§ Demonstrated organizational skills and diplomacy

§ Demonstrated written and verbal communication skills

§ Enthusiastic self-starter who is able to take the initiative and work with abstract problem statements

§ Proficiency with MS Office 2007 (Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint)

§ Proficiency with BMC Remedy V7.1 configuration management tool

Responsibilities:

Specific responsibilities include:

§ Responsible for the design and implementation of an ITIL V3-based Configuration Management Processes.

§ Responsible for documenting the processes and coordinating them throughout the Service Desk for implementation.

§ Coordinates with the Service Desk Manager to ensure helpdesk, network and blade operations functions are using a single, integrated process

§ Responsible for the end to end performance of the configuration management process

§ Support the Service Desk Manager in definition of CSFs, KPIs and Metrics for the asset and configuration management process.

§ Works with client counterparts to coordinate activities associated with the process area

§ Ensure integrity of data entered into the CMDB in Remedy

§ Responsible for development of all deliverable in the Performance Work Statement in support of the Configuration Management tasking

Required Candidate Attributes:

§ Bachelors Degree in Information Systems, Computer Science or related field

§ Five years experience working in IT related configuration management

§ US Citizen

Desired Candidate Attributes

§ Military/DoD/MHS Background highly desired

Certification(s):

§ ITIL V3 Foundations Certification is a plus

Clearance:

§ Must be able to hold a clearance

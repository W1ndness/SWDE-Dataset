About SuccessFactors

SuccessFactors is the global leader in Business Execution Software. The SuccessFactors Business Execution (BizX) Suite, which is delivered through the cloud, improves business alignment and people performance to improve results for companies of all sizes. Across 168 countries and 34 languages, more than 8 million users and 3,000 companies leverage SuccessFactors every day, up from approximately 300,000 users and 100 companies in 2003. BizX bridges the gap between strategy and success by allowing every person in an organization to execute against their goals better and faster. SuccessFactors’ recent acquisitions of Inform Impact and CubeTree supplement SuccessFactors’ core BizX strategy with integrated solutions that directly align with SuccessFactors’ mission of helping companies get work done by delivering robust business insights and improved collaboration. To learn more, visit: www.successfactors.com.

About the Role

We are looking for bright engineers from varied backgrounds including UI, Server Architecture, and Infrastructure Architecture!! The Software Engineer’s responsibilities will include designing, developing, testing, and documenting software, tools, and applications.

Duties and Responsibilities:

Ø Support the elements of the design effort and participate in multiple projects, solve complex problems, and support more junior engineers.

Ø Evaluate and analyze software products and provide sales support.

Ø Define, develop, and manage some processes of a project for partner integration, and engaging individuals across various departments or organizations to resolve problems

Ø Work jointly with clients to ensure business requirements are clearly communicated

Ø Prioritize multiple requirements, and communicate to senior management the present status and future direction of the product integration strategy.

Required Experience:

Ø Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering (any type) or a related field

Ø 5 years of experience in software development, engineering, or related field

Ø In-depth knowledge of Unix, Windows, JVM, web and application servers

Ø Strong knowledge of J2EE (EJB, Servlets, JSP, XML, JDBC, JMS)

Ø Strong knowledge of Design Patterns

Ø Strong knowledge of database systems, and proficiency in SQL

Ø Good knowledge of Object-Relational Mapping/Hibernate is a plus

Ø Experience with JSF, JBoss, Seam, Spring, and/or Hibernate would be a plus

Ø Experience with web-based financial application is a plus

Required Skills

Ø Ability to work independently as well as with a team

Ø Ability to understand and master new technologies quickly

Ø Strong focus on quality and generating results

Ø Self-disciplined with strong attention to detail

Ø Proven ability to quickly understand and solve unique and undocumented programming problems

Ø Exceptional time management and organization

Ø Excellent written and oral communications

Ø Energetic and self-starting

Ø Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists.

Ø Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.

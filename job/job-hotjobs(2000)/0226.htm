Project Specialist Contract to Hire Report to VP of IT



Job Summary:

As part of the Information Technology team, the Project Specialist will support the Visual Manufacturing ERP system and implement and support various applications interfacing with it. The project Specialist will participate at all levels of the System Development Life Cycle (SDLC), with primary focus on implementation, integration and testing, deployment and maintenance of new releases, features and applications. Extensive use of Microsoft Access with SQL Server tables is required. Project Specialist is also required to document applications and train employees at all levels of the Company. The Project Specialist is part of the IT Support Team.

I. Essential Functions and Responsibilities:

Ø Support Visual Manufacturing throughout the Company including installing patches and coordinating upgrades.

Ø Support applications (such as UPS World Ship, Fedex Ship Manager, various custom built Access databases, etc.) that interface with Visual Manufacturing.

Ø Participate in SDLC for new applications and interfaces including translating business processes and requirements into data requirements and ultimately into functioning systems.

Ø Develop, expand, maintain and document Access database applications and interfaces.

Ø Train users at all levels of the organization.

Ø As part of the Support Team, respond as needed to trouble log emails.

II. Other Duties and responsibilities:

Ø Work with outside vendors such as IT Pros and Synergy Resources, etc.

Ø Utilize software and other tools (such as Automate, Communication Assistant, etc)

Ø Other duties as needed

III. Supervisory Responsibilities:

Ø N/A

IV. Fiscal Responsibilities:

Ø N/A

V. Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

Ø Must be detail oriented

Ø Must have good problem solving skills

Ø Must be analytical

Ø Must have logical and critical thinking skills

Ø Must be able to perform effective testing

Ø Must have good written and verbal communication skills

Ø Must be able to multi-task and prioritize workload

Ø Must be able to work independently as well as with a team

VI. Credentials and Experience:

Ø Minimum of Associates Degree with 3 years IT department experience

Ø Minimum 2 yrs experience working on or supporting a computerized manufacturing or business system (experience with Visual Manufacturing or other ERP system a plus)

Ø Advanced level Microsoft Access experience including report writing, data manipulation, database programming and application development experience

Ø Minimum of Intermediate skills with Excel and Word

VII. Physical Demands and Special Requirements:

Ø Able to respond to the needs of multiple people at once

Ø Moving and installing computer equipment

If you would like to discuss please contact tbarbieri@techneeds.com or 978-562-2750 x204 This position is a contract to hire role.

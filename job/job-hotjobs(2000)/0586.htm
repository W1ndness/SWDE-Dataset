McKesson Horizon HRM Analyst

Description

Xerox Corporation is a $22 billion leading global enterprise for business process and document management. Through its broad portfolio of technology and services, Xerox provides the essential back-office support that clears the way for clients to focus on what they do best: their real business. Headquartered in Norwalk, Conn., Xerox provides leading-edge document technology, services, software and genuine Xerox supplies for graphic communication and office printing environments of any size. Through ACS, A Xerox Company, which Xerox acquired in February 2010, Xerox also offers extensive business process outsourcing and IT outsourcing services, including data processing, HR benefits management, finance support, and customer relationship management services for commercial and government organizations worldwide. The 130,000 people of Xerox serve clients in more than 160 countries.

If you meet the requirements of this position and want to work for a world-class company with a great marketplace reputation, apply today.

May perform one or more of the following:

The outsourcing team of the Healthcare Provider Applications group at ACS currently has an opening for a Client Analyst. This position is located at one of our long-term client engagements located in Farmington Hills , Michigan . Candidates must be located in the Detroit , Michigan area or be willing to relocate. This is a full time, non-travel consulting opportunity.

 

The primary function of this position is to lead and support all hospital clinical and financial systems and implementations most especially for the Financial and Human Resources departments. Some of the specific software supported by this position includes:

 

  • McKesson’s Pathways Financial Management System including General Ledger, Account Payable, Asset Management and Project Management
  • McKesson’s Horizon Human Resource Management including Human Resources and Payroll
  • McKesson’s Horizon Performance Management System including Budgeting, Cost Accounting, and Contract Management
  • McKesson’s Horizon Business Insight system
  • Ultimate Software’s UltiPro Human Resource and Payroll SaaS system

 

This is the lead support person for the above applications. This person communicates with the vendors, users, and support techs for these applications addressing all kinds of support issues, including data base issues (not technical), application software issues (not technical), application upgrades, project management, training, interfaces, security, down-time, and understanding application functionality.

 

All other duties as assigned.

 

Applicable Skills

 

Priority will be given to Payroll/Human Resource’s expertise as this person will be required to support the install of the Ultimate Software UltiPro system which will replace the McKesson HHRM system.

 

Qualified candidates will possess 3 – 5 years healthcare/IT experience, some financial and/or HR application experience and the following qualifications:

 

  • Five or more years of work experience in a hospital setting as a Systems and/or Support Analyst supporting clinical or financial systems.
  • Experience in supporting the above specified application products is a plus.
  • Must have some project management experience.
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate and interact with users, vendors and support techs in the support of hospital applications;
  • Must have strong problem solving / troubleshooting / analysis skills;
  • Experience with MS office software, and Microsoft operating environments.
  • Excellent organizational and consulting skills are requirements.
  • A customer service orientation is necessary.
  • B. S. degree in computer or information science or equivalent related experience

  Someone without Financial and/or Human Resource application experience will not be considered.

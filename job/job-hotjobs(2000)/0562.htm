SAP Product Training, Functional or Technical Consultant



Who We Are

Learn2Perform (L2P) specializes in Application Training and Learning and Performance Technology solutions. We only hire experienced consultants that have extensive backgrounds in application implementation consulting, training, learning and performance applications and corporate learning and performance. L2P has uniquely positioned itself as a leading provider of learning solutions to the Fortune 1000 and SMB markets. We provide a comprehensive consulting solution covering strategy, implementation and product training.

L2P is always looking for talent. Our team is made up of some of the best and brightest in the learning performance management industry. For the role of SAP Trainer/Consultant, we are looking for any or all of the following qualities:

  • Leadership - All L2P consultants will be able to lead their engagements. This requires the ability to communicate with all levels of an organization, oversee project issue management, relationship development and management and provide subject matter expertise as it applies to the tasks at hand
  • Professionalism - As the front-line representative of Learn2Perform, our consultants are expected to conduct themselves with the utmost professionalism. This translates to work, client relationships, and overall personal presentation
  • Collegiality - Whether you are a consultant or a contractor, you will be considered part of the L2P team. We encourage our team to constantly strive for excellence and to learn from each other. Our infrastructure has been developed to offer an ever expanding knowledge base and professional development network
  • Learning and Performance Management Skills - Demonstrated mastery of technology is important. We typically hire those who have worked in a Learning, Training or Performance Management leadership role for an organization, or have a consulting background with Learning and Performance Management Systems or, learning content development tools
  • Product Knowledge - A key to our success is the ability to convey our subject matter expertise with the applications we train on. This typically comes from hands-on implementation experience.

Job Description

Learn2Perform has an ongoing effort to develop a team of product training specialist. We are looking for experienced SAP Trainers/Consultants with subject matter expertise and implementation experience as a Functional Consultant to develop and deliver product training to our clients. We are looking for individuals with strong technical backgrounds and the ability to effectively communicate their knowledge to others in a structured learning event.

This is a wonderful opportunity for a SAP Functional Consultant who is looking to build their consulting practice by exposing their skills and product knowledge as a product training specialist.

Summary of Main Responsibilities

  • The SAP Trainer/Consultant will have SME status and product training experience for any of (but not limited to) the following SAP modules or areas below:
    • FICO, CRM , SD , MM, SEM, HCM, BI (BW/BO), ABAP, BASIS, SECURITY, PORTAL, PI (XI),
    • RWD uPerform/Info Pack
  • Researches, develops, designs, implements and supports state-of-the-art instructor led, on-line and self-study training to support employee development. Including technical training in area of expertise.
  • Facilitates and/or leads instructor led training courses both virtually and onsite.
  • Ensures consistency of design and implementation of all internally created training programs/materials.
  • Provides advice and consultation in the role of SME in areas of expertise.
  • Partners with Learn2Perform to develop custom training solutions for clients, i.e. develop style guides, on-line publishing standards and templates.

Skills and Attributes Required

Applicants must at least meet the following requirements:

  • SAP functional skills within SAP area of expertise
  • Training design, development, and delivery skills
  • Strong documentation, organizational and analytical skills
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Strong record of positive relationships with clients, suppliers, vendors & extended work teams
  • Proven track record delivering SAP training courses with high degree of professionalism.
  • Command of the English language both verbal and written

Education Needed

  • Bachelor's degree, with minimum of 5 years of relevant IT training experience.

Job Experience

  • 2+ years of experience in SAP training
  • 5+ years of SAP implementation experience as functional or technical consultant
  • Experience in all phases of training development
  • Experience in the development of ILT and web-based training courses
  • Experience in training facilitation and instruction
  • Experience with Adobe Captivate, uPerform, OnDemand or other SCORM authoring or simulation tools
  • Experience with learning management systems
  • Experience with HTML coding (preferred)

Relocation and Travel Requirement

  • Learn2Perform consultants work from their homes, which allow our consultants the freedom to live anywhere in North America. Our consulting model is a blend of virtual and on-site work depending on the needs of the client.

Citizenship Requirement

  • U.S. or Canadian Citizen, or U.S. Green-card holder (minimum)

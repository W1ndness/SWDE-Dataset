View Alloy Media + Marketing profile and job listings

Alloy Media + Marketing is one of the country's largest providers of nontraditional media and promotional marketing programs connecting with targeted consumer segments. Working with AM+M, marketers reach consumers through a host of programs incorporating Alloy's diverse array of media and marketing assets and expertise in interactive, social network marketing, display media, direct mail, and promotional and educational programming. Alloy Media + Marketing services over 1500 companies including half of the Fortune 200. We are looking to hire a Desktop Specialist for our AMP Agency group in Boston, MA.

AMP Agency is the marketing/advertising/promotional agency of Alloy Media + Marketing, an Alloy, Inc. Company. As part of Alloy Media + Marketing, AMP Agency delivers custom solutions for consumer brands targeting youth, young adult and multicultural lifestyles. AMP Agency was named 2004 Agency of the Year by PROMO Magazine, a recognition that is considered to be the Gold Standard among promotional industry professionals, and has been recognized in the Top 25 Promotions agencies for the past several years.

Requirements:

  • Beginner level IT desktop specialist
  • Understanding of hardware, Windows Operating Systems, Microsoft Office
  • Hardware and Software installation
  • Understanding of basic networking
  • Helpdesk experience, Windows Operating system administration and troubleshooting
  • MS Office/Outlook installation
  • HP printer support.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Experience with Blackberry/Smartphone devices and cellular technologies
  • Windows software update
  • Must have good problem solving skills.
  • Must be able to work independently
  • Preferred: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field or technical school certification

Alloy Inc. is a publicly traded company, NASDAQ symbol ALOY.

We offer a competitive salary and excellent benefits package including, 401(k), medical & dental benefits and others.

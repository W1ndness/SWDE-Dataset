Become the internal and external customer-facing gatekeeper to SW engineering team



Senior Software Engineer

(SecureData Appliance Team)

Voltage Security is looking for an experienced Senior Software Engineer with extensive experience in Unix and Windows environments and a passion for excellence in delivering highest quality products to customers. Join a creative team in solving complex problems and providing solutions that the world's largest companies depend on to keep their sensitive information secure.

Career Summary:

Become the internal and external customer-facing gate keeper to the Voltage SecureData Software Engineering team, resolving 3rd level customer problems and involving engineering resources as needed. Become the SecureData Guru knowledgeable about all SecureData products, CLs, SDKs, servers, etc. Serve as the release engineer for product releases. Work closely with the rest of the Voltage Engineering team to keep the organization agile using Scrum.

Requirements (must have):

· 8+ years working experience developing in one or more of C, Java, C#

· 5+ years experience scripting using Shell, Perl, Python, etc.

· 5+ years experience with the J2EE Web Container, including Servlets, Web Services, and key APIs such as JDBC, RMI, JMS, etc. and also JNI.

· Working knowledge of different build tools, compilers, linkers and loaders on Unix and Windows

· Efficient debugging and problem solving skills with demonstrable ability to quickly understand and debug code written by other developers

· Strong verbal and written skills, capable in diverse mediums such as face-to-face, conference calls, email, instant messaging and user group forums

Requirements (nice to have):

· Experience with Oracle 8,9,10, SQL Server, PL/SQL, and Transact SQL.

· Experience developing security software, working with technologies including SSL SSL-VPNs and IPSEC VPNs, X.509, PKCS, ASN.1, PKI, OpenSSL

· Practical experience in a truly agile software development organization

Education: B.S. in Computer Science or equivalent experience in a related field

About Voltage:

Voltage Security, Inc., an enterprise security company, is the global leader in information encryption. Voltage solutions, based on next generation cryptography, provide encryption that just works for protecting valuable, regulated and sensitive information persistently and based on policy. Voltage delivers power, simplicity and the lowest total cost of ownership in the industry through the use of award-winning Voltage Identity-Based Encryption™ (IBE). Voltage Security offerings include Voltage SecureMail™, the Voltage SecureData™ and the Voltage Security Network™ (VSN), a Software as a Service (SaaS) solution for the extended business network.

Voltage Security is the number one OEM provider of email encryption technology in the world with OEMs that include Microsoft, Proofpoint, Secure Computing, Sendmail, Canon, Code Green Networks and NTT Communications. Voltage Security partners with leading global technology companies including: IBM, Microsoft, RIM, Symantec and others. Customers include nearly three million licensed and deployed users at Global 1000 companies in banking, retail, insurance, energy, healthcare and government, such as Wells Fargo, American Board of Family Medicine, Diebold, Integro Financial, NTT Communications, SafeAuto Insurance, Winterthur Life UK Ltd. and XL Global Services.

Voltage provides solutions for secure communication and data at rest to leading financial services, healthcare, government and pharmaceutical companies

Voltage Security is head quartered in Palo Alto, CA. For more information please visit http://www.voltage.com.

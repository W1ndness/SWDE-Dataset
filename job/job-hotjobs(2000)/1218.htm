JOB DESCRIPTION:

RESPONSIBILITIES

We are seeking a Sr. Business Systems Analyst to contribute to functional solutions serving the Supply Chain Solutions business. A strong background with warehouse management systems and applications programming development processes is necessary. UPS Supply Chain Solutions leverages the WM for Open Systems application from Manhattan Associates.

This position works with business partnersacross multiple business functions to align technology solutions with business strategies. The primary goal of the Sr. Business Systems Analyst is to evaluate functional requirements and design, develop and link requirements to the application. This position designs user documentation, supports user development of test cases for system testing and provides the interface between I.S. and the functional organization. This position coordinates addressing and resolving issues of moderate to high complexity and works on assignments of moderate to high complexity. The Sr. Business Systems Analyst analyzes current processes and identifies system and business impacts. This position is responsible for the collection, analysis, design, configuration and documentation of business and functional requirements.

ESSENTIAL ACCOUNTABILITIES

  • Interacts with all Project Team members, support group staff, function/user reps, systems and applications managers, outside consultants, customers, vendor, and Corporate/Region/District staff.
  • Identifies and manages medium to large conflict, facilitation, negotiation efforts and engaging the appropriate help when necessary.
  • Works with business and technical partners to analyze and define solutions.
  • Provides supervision and/or mentoring to junior team members.
  • Ensures that work processes and product adhere to enterprise standards.
  • Effectively functions with a high degree of autonomy.

KNOWLEDGE & EXPERIENCE

  • Experience in business and functional aspects of supply chain management, including order, inventory and warehouse management.
  • Functional experience with WMS systems.
  • Advanced knowledge of different business implications of the application of technology to the current business environment.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Excellent planning, organizing, decision-making, problem-solving skills.
  • Broad knowledge of programs, projects and knowledge management processes.
  • Knowledge of testing strategies, plans and execution.
  • Knowledge of quality techniques, methodologies and tools.

SKILLS

  • Good oral and written communication skills.
  • Ability to independently prioritize and organize workload to meet tight deadlines.
  • Ability to explain technical information to a wide variety of technical and non-technical individuals in a clear and accurate manner.
  • Ability to analyze business and user needs, document requirements and translate into proper system requirement specifications.
  • Investigate and research multiple sources to expose details and complete information.
  • Assess and analyze client needs and help others in the project understand the requirements.

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/MIS or the equivalent of education and work experience. Relevant experience of 3-5+ years required with a broad knowledge of the client's function and systems.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Do you have a Bachelors degree in Computer Science/related discipline or the equivalent in education and work experience?
  • Do you have 1-3 years programming experience?
  • Do you have any relatives who work for UPS or any of its Subsidiaries?
  • Are you a U.S. Citizen or National of the U.S., an alien lawfully admitted for permanent residence, or an alien authorized to work in the U.S. for this employer?
  • experience

Senior Windows/VMware Engineer with Citrix, AD and Data Center Relocation Experience



Windows & VMware Infrastructure Engineer

Our client, a major financial institution, is seeking candidates for its Senior Windows & VMware Infrastructure Engineer position. Only candidates with a 4 year degree and financial experience will be accepted.

General Background:

Senior Windows Server / VMware engineer with experience that spans the following areas:

  • Windows Server hardware and OS
  • VMware ESX platform
  • Virtualization strategy and migration planning
  • Citrix
  • Active Directory
  • Information Security, Active Directory, Server and VMware “hardening”
  • Data Center relocation

Requirements / Qualifications:

Proven technical engineering and administration competency in the following Windows areas:

  • VMWare ESX 3.5 and higher
  • Citrix 4.5 and higher
  • Windows Operating Systems 2003 and higher
  • Windows Clustering technology
  • Active Directory
  • Windows Security and Patching
  • Technology Resiliency – High Availability, Disaster Recovery, etc.
  • Develops, recommends and implements technology strategy for the
  • Windows and VMware environment demonstrating clear and crisp communication to senior management and good decision taking when faced with ambiguity
  • Analytical and critical thinking skills with a proven ability to streamline existing processes that maintain infrastructure integrity and reliability with sustainable solutions
  • The ability to track and trend chronic infrastructure issues, develop easy to understand reporting metrics for senior management and providing the overall health of the infrastructure
  • Ensures continuous delivery of Windows services through the assessment of growth trends, usage and ITIL processes
  • Must be viewed as “easy to work with” and the ability to communicate effectively in both oral and written form, using MS-Powerpoint as the primary communication tool to management or the business.
  • Must have a keen sense of who the audience is and not force the audience to learn techno-speak.

Education and Experience:

  • BS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or Technology related field
  • 8 – 10 years “hands on” professional experience in the areas mentioned above
  • ITIL Certification preferred
  • Certifications in at least one of the following: VMWare 4.x, Windows Server 200x, Citrix 4.5 or 5.x

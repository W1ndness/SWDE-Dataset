Marketing Communications Manager

Intacct strives to help small and midsized businesses (SMBs) and CPA firms improve company performance and make finance more productive by providing award winning cloud computing financial management and accounting applications. We are the preferred provider of financial applications for AICPA Business Solutions and focus only on one thing – delivering the most comprehensive Software as a Service financial management system to our more than 30,000 customers across more than 3,300 companies. As a leader in this space with a strong focus on customer satisfaction, we value the individuality, ingenuity and originality each employee contributes. The talent and drive of our employees is a key to our continued success.

Imagine what it would be like to have a successful career at Intacct - working with top talent, motivated to help businesses and CPA firms achieve their business goals. Intacct is the business solution and is the future. If you would like to be a part of our success, please apply for this opportunity.

Job Description:

The marketing communications manager drives the development and execution of marketing communications programs to further sales objectives and increase mindshare and awareness. He / she has wide range of intra-and inter-departmental responsibilities communicating strategic messages and value of the Intacct solutions.

For external communication, the marketing communications manager will translate strategies into an actionable integrated communications and manage all aspects of implementation, including copy writing, collateral content, web presence, advertising, and customer and channel partner marketing. An ability to understand marketing metrics and develop a plan for meeting them is essential for success in this position.

Internally, the marketing communications manager will work closely with individuals from PR, demand generation, product management, product marketing, and sales. He / She must possess the skills to collect, consolidate, and filter content from these sources and turn this content into sales tools for our direct sales team and channel partners.

Job Responsibilities:

· Develop and execute marketing communication programs to drive awareness, support demand generation and assist sales in closing deals.

  • Provide initiative, creativity and content for marketing collateral, including sales tools, product presentations, product demos and other materials that maximize the impact of our sales and marketing efforts. Manage process from concept to completion.
  • Partner with web/online marketing manager to deliver programs to support SEO/SEM and social media objectives
  • Author, edit and proofread marketing materials and web site content.
  • Develop tradeshow strategies and execute from concept to completion.
  • Develop creative briefs and work with creative agencies and in-house resources to manage process through execution.
  • Manage MarCom plan deliverables to budget.
  • Provide timely reporting of performance metrics, analysis of results, and benchmark against established goals.

Job Requirements:

  • 8+ years of relevant experience as a marketing communications professional at a software company; SMB experience a plus
  • Demonstrated track record of excellence in marketing communications
  • Outstanding writing, editing and communications skills
  • Demonstrated delivery of world class materials – collateral, presentations, advertising, etc.
  • High level of creativity and initiative
  • Strong MS Office Skills: Word, Excel, PowerPoint
  • Comfort in a rapidly changing environment with competing and shifting priorities
  • Creative and resourceful problem solver who’s persuasive and willing to take risks.
  • Organized, with the ability to keep a lot of wheels turning at once and stay on deadline.
  • Bachelor’s degree required and an MBA is a plus.

Please apply at: http://us.intacct.com/corporate/careers.php

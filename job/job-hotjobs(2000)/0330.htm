Sr Field Service Engineer (Minneapolis, MN)*



Overview:

Agfa Graphics is a world leader in prepress solutions for the commercial, newspaper and packaging printing industries. In addition, we are quickly expanding our assortment for the fast-growing market of industrial inkjet printing with the development of state-of-the-art printers and inks for applications such as billboards, packages and decoration.

We are currently looking for a Sr. Field Service Engineer to service the Minneapolis, MN area.

The chosen candidate will be responsible for properly managing customer accounts assigned by providing the hardware/software maintenance of stand alone Agfa products and/or associated peripheral equipment. Major responsibilities center on implementing timely, effective installation and repair services as well as ensuring customer satisfaction and the active promotion of all Field Service offerings.

Leadership ability and superior knowledge on one or more series of products sold by Agfa is required to assist other equally or lesser titled employees.




Responsibilities:

Responsible for the installation and repair of assigned Agfa products, to include safety verification of electrical and environmental factors at customer site, testing of product to ensure functional operation, verification of operating performance with customer, and review of the basic operational functions with the customer to meet our training obligation at installation.

  • Performs preventive maintenance and repair functions on assigned SMA, courtesy visits as required, and time & material accounts.
  • Maintains regular communications with Regional Manager to provide prompt escalation to difficulties and to ensure customer satisfaction.
    • Documents service calls, expenses and time reports on a daily basis and submit to zone office on a weekly basis according to established procedures. All documentation must be legible and neat.
    • Responsible for parts ordering and returning repairable spare parts components.
    • Ensures the service ready condition of service aids, tools and diagnostic equipment.
    • Maintains close contact with scheduler to meet all response time and uptime commitments.
    • Promotes all Agfa offerings (i.e. System Maintenance Agreements, Professional Services, Products)
      • Understand and adhere to all safety policies on the job and while driving company vehicle.
      • Quickly ascertain customer demeanor and personality and be able to interact effectively.
  • Engage with other Agfa employees and be able to work effectively with different skill-sets, emotions and motives.
  • Demonstrate company loyalty and consciously make decisions that are fiscally responsible and prudent.
  • Be resourceful and creative but work without supervision while staying motivated.
  • Serve as higher level authority in urgent situations or where needed to assist or guide another employee.
  • Incumbent serves as onsite conduit to Agfa management and Sales for customer issues: sales needs, political environment, staff changes, etc.
  • Product expertise expands to include many offerings in various business markets: newspaper, commercial, inkjet, and/or industrial inkjet.



Qualifications:

Qualifications:

Education: Minimum of an Associate’s degree in electronics technology or equivalent.

Experience: Minimum 3 – 5 years experience in a high-tech field, printing, publishing, and/or inkjet environment.

Specific Technical Requirements

Knowledge of electronics:

Reading electrical schematics

  • Measuring and understanding of electrical readings (v-Amps-Hz….)
  • Distribution of electricity. (high voltage AC /low voltage DC)
  • Data communications and protocols

Knowledge of mechanics

  • Motors and Motor Controls (DC-AC-Steppers-Servo)
  • Drive technology: Chains, gears, belts
  • Measurement techniques
  • Rotation (RPM)
  • Translation

Knowledge of control engineering: (Flow, pressure, temperature…)

  • Valves
  • Pumps
  • Fans
  • Heating elements
  • Sensors: flow, temperature, level…
  • Compressed air

Laser technology, some plate and chemistry knowledge, and the ability to assess customer environments as it impacts equipment: temperature, humidity, dust.

The chosen candidate must have mechanical ability, can read schematics, use a meter and scope, and digest technical documentation. Strong communication skills, the ability to work unsupervised, and customer relations skills are required as well.

Ability to obtain and maintain a passport for international travel is required. Travel could be up to 95%.

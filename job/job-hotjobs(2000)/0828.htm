Senior Core Java Developer



In addition to this exciting opportunity please visit our website at www.aetea.com to see the other opportunities waiting for you.

If you meet the qualifications of the position described below, please e-mail your full resume as a Word document to sea@aetea.com. Please include all education and training completed, relevant work experience, technical skills developed and exactly where and how long they were used.

Senior Java Software Engineer.

An industry leading mobile web technology firm located in Encinitas , CA ( San Diego County ) is looking to add a Sr. Java Software Engineer and architect to its small development team. You will be joining a small team to help scale out their current resources to facilitate their continue growth. You will also be working with a talented team of start-up and industry veterans to build a best of breed platform that will scale out to massive numbers of users.

The ideal candidate will be an excellent communicator who is comfortable working closely with others in a fast-paced environment and who has already experienced the problems and challenges behind scaling out infrastructure for redundancy and load distribution. The desired candidate will have experience working in a start-up or leading in the architecture/design for hot-deployable and high traffic web-applications.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Analyze, innovate, architect, design and develop software programs for web applications, APIs and platform components.
  • Write well covered, maintainable code for new features, existing component enhancements and bug fixes.
  • Provide mentoring and support to junior developers as well as actively participate in code reviews.
  • Prioritize multiple requirements, and communicate to senior management the present status and potential future directions of the platform components.
  • Work closely with QA to produce high quality software deliverables.
  • Work closely with systems architects to design and facilitate a hot-deployable, component-oriented software platform.

Experience:

  • 7 years minimum, 10 or more years preferred of professional software development of high-volume web applications.
  • Experience creating, migrating and deploying applications on OSGi bundles.
  • Interest in and knowledge of NoSQL-type datastores such as Cassandra, CouchDB, Tokyo Cabinet, or similar (can include personal interest/research).
  • Expert knowledge of building high-volume web applications and supporting service infrastructure in a distributed environment.
  • Excellent hands-on design, implementation and communication skills
  • Expert knowledge of GNU-Linux as both a development and production environment for Java applications
  • Hands-on experience with writing software for large, partitioned MySQL installations

Experience with the following technologies:

  • J2SDK/J2EE, Servlets, JSP, AJAX , JBI/ESB, JMX, JMS, JDBC, Struts, Hibernate, Spring Framework, Maven
  • Skilled in scripting languages such as JavaScript, Perl and Linux/Unix Shell scripting
  • Knowledge of other programming languages such as Ruby, Scala or Python is helpful

Nice to Have:

  • Experience with scaling out large MySQL installations
  • Video streaming server experience
  • Experience with Java web applications and orchestrating deployment of such across multiple nodes and clusters
  • Previous experience in mobile device industry
  • Knowledge of Agile/Scrum software development process

Why AETEA Information Technology?

We are proud to be an ethical organization whose mission is to provide the highest level of service, commitment and continued growth to our clients and consultants. In addition to a standard benefits package, by working with AETEA you will gain access to job openings not yet advertised by client companies. Also, we will ask for your permission to present your resume to each and every opportunity before we submit you to any of our clients.

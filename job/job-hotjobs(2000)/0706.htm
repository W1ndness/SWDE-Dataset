Turn Inc. (turn.com) is the smart platform for digital advertising. Our mission is to deliver the most effective data-driven advertising in the world. Our focus is to create deep customer intimacy between the marketer and their targeted audiences while delivering outstanding results. Turn is powered by its real-time media buying and optimization platform that delivers custom audiences at scale, exceptional performance, and insightful analytics to advertising agencies and premium brand advertisers across all quality inventory sources. We are headquartered in Redwood City, California and we have locations in New York City, Chicago, San Francisco and Charlotte. Turn is privately funded by Norwest Venture Partners, Trident Capital, Shasta Ventures, and Focus Ventures.

Our employees work hard and play hard and are delivering year over year hyper growth. We’ve created great working environments for our employees. We value creativity, leadership, innovation, collaboration, initiative, honesty, integrity and customer service. Our employees drive the success of our company. If you would like to be part of a winning team, please submit your resume to jobs@turn.com.

As part of the Operations Group, the Sr Network Engineer will be an integral part of the infrastructure growth and operational improvements underway at Turn. This is an opportunity for a seasoned engineer/architect to apply his or her craft in an exciting, fast-paced startup environment. Our business is growing quickly, and we need to build out our network infrastructure to match that growth. The Sr. Network Engineer will be responsible for design and implementation of latency-sensitive, highly performant, fault-tolerant networks, understanding adding to documentation on the network infrastructure, communicating with the Operations and Engineering teams on maintaining high quality, and proactively proposing enhancements and improvements to the reference network architecture and existing implementations of it. A successful candidate for this position will be a generalist with experience in layered network architecture, system design, intra- and inter-domain routing protocols, name service operations, and security / threat mitigation.

Primary Job Duties include:

· Maintain and grow the operational infrastructure along with business growth.

· Primary, on-call shift responsibility for network components, responding to incidents, and communicating complex network concepts to rest of organization in an understandable way.

· Using data, metrics, monitoring and alerting mechanisms to establish a baseline and improve on it.

· Identify opportunities for configuration improvement, propose and document them, and implement them.

· Maintain and improve WAN connectivity; establish peering with appropriate partners, communicate with vendors and maintain specified uptime on network components

Requirements and expectations:

· BS degree in Computer Science or Engineering.

· 3-5 years industry experience preferred.

· Demonstrated ability to maintain complex, multi-site networks, both WAN and LAN.

· Excellent written documentation and verbal communication skills.

· Proactive leader; “get it done” mentality, while following change control methods and processes.

· Willingness to learn, be challenged, and have fun in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.

· Must have experience with Cisco and Juniper (EX) routing and switching. Strongly desired: Cisco

(CCNP or CCIE) and/or Juniper (JNCIS / JNCIE) certification or equivalent experience.

· Design experience with BGP, OSPF.

· Experience in tiered datacenter architectures, with Linux / Unix servers, shell scripting, and associated common network services.

· Understanding of spanning tree and experience configuring it.

· Excellent troubleshooting skills, a creative and methodical thinker.

· Programming experience in C/C++/Java is a plus.

This position offers a competitive base salary, bonus program, stock options, and other comprehensive benefits.



For immediate consideration, please send us your resume and cover letter to jobs@turn.com. Please put “Sr Network Engineer" in the subject field. We are an Equal Opportunity Employer.

Title: Senior Director – Downstream Engineering

Department: Downstream

Location: Sunnyvale, CA

Reports To: VP of Engineering

FLSA Status: Exempt

Summary:


Ariba, Inc. is the leading provider of collaborative business commerce solutions. Ariba combines industry-leading software as a service (SaaS) technology to optimize the complete commerce lifecycle with the world's largest web-based community to discover, connect and collaborate with a global network of trading partners and expert capabilities to augment internal resources and skills, delivering everything needed to control costs, minimize risk, improve profits and enhance cash flow and operations – all in a cloud-based environment. Whether you’re buying, selling or managing cash, you can do it more efficiently and effectively in the Ariba® Commerce Cloud. Over 300,000 companies, including more than 80 percent of the Fortune 500, use Ariba’s solutions to drive more efficient inter-enterprise commerce.

Duties and Responsibilities


· Drive planning, execution, and overall success of downstream engineering work streams.

· Utilize technical, project management, and team management experience to actively lead the distributed downstream engineering dev team

· Leading/managing the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) engineering organization consisting of Senior Manager/Director level management staff & highly technical teams of software development & QA engineers across two locations Sunnyvale (CA) and Bangalore (India).

· Provide strong leadership & mentorship to individual contributors and managers in the organization

· Closely collaborate with the CTO and Architect to provide technical direction for the downstream and platform product architecture

· Be an integral part of the engineering leadership team to focus on broader organizational topics like process, cultural improvements & people development

· Establish a strong software development model for ensuring successful, on time and high quality releases

· Successfully manage multiple schedules, work streams across On-Demand and/or CD (installed software) release trains simultaneously

· Communicate with sales, support, marketing, business development and other parts of the company to ensure the product meets their needs

Basic Minimum Qualifications

· Minimum BS CS/CE or equivalent, MS or PhD is a plus.

· Strong oral and written communication skills.

· Very high technical competence, track record of strong coding and individual technical accomplishments, and strong academic record.

· Strong project management skills, deep understanding of the product development cycle. Track record for delivering projects on time. Able to provide technical guidance when needed.

· Ability to work effectively with staff to executive level employees and employees within and outside of their function and organization.

· Strong experience in managing budget for a large organization including headcount and capital expenditure

· Extensive second-level management experience and proven record for building great engineering teams.

· Experience in managing teams across multiple global locations.

· Experience in SaaS (Software as a service)/On-Demand enterprise solutions.

· The downstream team is distributed between Sunnyvale and Bangalore so the candidate needs to be able to travel frequently to Sunnyvale during the year for various activities including but not restricted to release planning, participation in Ariba customer meets, engineering leadership offsites etc

· 12+ years of management experience & proven track record of shipping complex enterprise software products that meet the highest levels of quality, scalability & performance standards

· Proven track record of managing overseas software delivery organizations on a large scale (75+ people).

· Willingness to travel to the India Development Center at least twice a year.

· Strong technical skills with a background in developing & managing teams involved in are building underlying infrastructural layers in an enterprise software architecture

Description

Sales Executive, Total Benefits Outsourcing (TBO)

 

Company Overview

ACS is a Fortune 500 premier provider of business outsourcing and technology-related services for commercial enterprises and government agencies.  ACS provides a variety of outsourcing services to the Global Fortune 1000 including IT Solutions, Health Care Claims Processing, Finance and Accounting, Human Resources, Payment Processing, Customer Care, and Supply Chain Management.  ACS has received numerous awards and recognitions as a premium outsourcing services provider including: ranked #1 HRO vendor by The Black Book of Outsourcing, ranked #1 Best-Performing Managed Services Provider by The Global Services 100, and ranked #1 Best BPO Outsourcing Excellence Award by Outsourcing Center.  ACS has one of the highest client retention rates in the outsourcing services industry and for the past 5 years, has achieved record growth with revenues climbing over 20% annually.

Position Summary

The Sales Executive, TBO reports to the Vice President, Sales TBO and will be responsible for leading sales pursuits for new logo and existing account sales.  This sales leader will closely collaborate with the solutions architecture team and the VP Business Development for pre-sales support and marketplace development activities.  This role will encompass:

  • Leading cross-functional professionals on the sales pursuit teams for new business.
  • Provide detailed reports & analysis that guide the sales process.
  • Review sales contracts to ensure they reflect the interests of the company.
  • Strategize to drive our TBO business into a dominant leader in the industry.
  • Engage product marketing and solutions expertise to refine service offerings for specific market segments.
  • Identify, negotiate and close accounts.
  • Develop and maintain effective business relationships with clients and contacts.
  • Cross-sell and collaborate on multi-tower opportunities.
  • Produce forecasts, sales, and trip reports on a monthly basis.  Plan and manage sales support duties including proposal responses, contract updates, account management, sales reporting and presentations.

Education and Experience

  • 5 - 10 years of proven large-contract sales and outsourcing experience.
  • 2 + years of recent relevant sales and outsourcing experience in TBO.
  • Proven track record of successfully managing and working in a TBO sales environment with significant experience prospecting, negotiating, and closing sales pursuits.
  • 2 + years sales experience across multiple verticals of TBO including health & welfare, defined benefits and defined contribution
  • Requires excellent leadership skills.  
  • Experience in development, negotiation and administration of long-term contracts.
  • Ability to manage large, complex sales and long sales cycles.
  • Strong presentation, sales, negotiating and influencing skills.
  • Bachelors Degree and MBA preferred.

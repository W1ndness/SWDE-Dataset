Job Description

  • VP of Development
  • Hartford, CT
Summary of Position

This position is responsible for leading the development, implementation, and support of applications and systems for both internal and customer-facing usage. Working with partners in all business units, the 2VP Development and Support will direct and manage computing and information technology strategic plans, policies, programs and schedules for business and enterprise systems, and manage information services to meet corporate goals and objectives. This position will act as a liaison for the Information Services Department and internal business units, stakeholders, and management. The incumbent will be responsible for companywide technologies through all stages of planning, development, testing and deployment as well as ensuring requirements are met and industry best practices are complied with. The 2VP Development and Support is a thought leader, championing the use of technology to improve the revenue, profitability, and efficiency of the organization. The incumbent is also responsible for ensuring that both internal and partner development resources are leveraged across all business units.

Nature and Scope:
This position reports to the Chief Information Officer.

The 2VP Development and Support is responsible for leading teams in the development, maintenance, and support of software applications throughout the organization. The incumbent delivers IT solutions with an emphasis on speed, flexibility, and efficiency that align with the business strategies and support the needs of internal customers. A successful incumbent ensures the on-time delivery of scheduled application projects by developing project plans and driving the implementation process. In addition to managing applications developed by internal staff and contract resources, the incumbent must assess, implement, customize, and support off-the-shelf software solutions


Minimum Qualifications:

*Subject Matter Expert experience in the design and development of commercial applications
*Subject Matter Expert experience with developing strategic plans for application development, maintenance, and deployment
* Subject Matter Expert experience in a managerial role, including the management of Programmer/Analysts and Application Support Analysts
* Subject Matter Expert experience in leading a team in problem solving and troubleshooting application, connectivity, and performance issues
* Subject Matter Expert experience in managing IT projects and budgets
*Extensive experience managing 3rd party providers including hosting facilities, contract developers, and outsource support
*Bachelors degree in computer science or a related technical field or equivalent work experience required.
Preferred Qualifications:



* Experience in a highly-controlled environment with SAS 70 or equivalent certification.

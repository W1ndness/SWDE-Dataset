Job Description

  • Vice President of I.T.
  • Los Angeles, CA
Los Angeles-based Internet Brands, Inc. is currently seeking a Vice President of IT with broad experience. This senior level technology executive role requires strong, hands on technical experience within the technology infrastructure environment, providing leadership over critical technical areas including hardware, networking, system administration/engineering, and more in support of a broad range of web-based technologies. There will be many complex processes and technical challenges that you will have to solve. This is a position in support of a 24/7 production environment.

Responsibilities:
* Provide executive leadership to our IT team.
* Maintain and improve the company's technology infrastructure including the production websites, telecom systems, network systems, reporting systems, CRM, and data transport systems on a 24/7 basis. Oversee the architecture and design of this environment. Take full responsibility for prioritizing the projects presented to the group and work to help design each new generation of technology and processes.
* Implement the processes and resources in support of 24/7 production troubleshooting, such as overseeing the NOC. Provide hands-on support as appropriate.
* Lead Sarbanes Oxley infrastructure efforts. Provide recommendations for the security policy. Advise executive staff on information security practices and procedures. Provide leadership in network and firewall design in support of security options.
* Provide ongoing monitoring of critical systems for operational problems. Review our existing monitoring techniques and recommend more comprehensive fault detection and notification systems.
* Provide recommendations in support of business continuity including fault tolerance architecture and design.
* Maintain systems for highest possible uptimes.
* Work with the CTO to manage the IT budget, delivering a solid infrastructure environment cost effectively and scheduling and overseeing projects to deliver consistent enhancements.

Qualifications:
* Strong track record of leadership and technological innovation and success.
* A minimum of 5 years experience managing in an information technology environment in support of a web-based business.
* Strong Unix/Linux knowledge.
* Strong experience in information technology security.
* Strong knowledge of networking.
* Degree in Computer Science is a definite plus.

We are looking for a thought-leader, problem-solver and sharp executor to provide leadership within our fast-paced, fast-growing environment. The ideal candidate will be extremely web savvy and will have had experience with application development as well as infrastructure management.

We offer a comprehensive compensation and benefits package including a casual work environment.

About Us: Internet Brands, Inc. (NASDAQ:INET) is a unique and leading Internet media company. INET owns and operates more than 100 websites that are leaders in their vertical markets. These sides include ApartmentRatings.com, CarsDirect.com, CruiseReviews.com, DavesGarden.com, DoItYourself.com, FitDay.com, FlyerTalk.com, HealthNews.org, Loan.com, Wikitravel.org, and many more. In total, these sites organically attract (without paid marketing) more than 55 million unique visitors per month. The vast majority of these sites have very strong community participation.

INET is also unique in its ability to monetize Internet audiences. The company's proprietary platform optimizes yields from its more than 40,000 direct advertisers spanning seven vertical categories. The platform is also core to the company's acquisitions strategy, providing a cost-efficient and scalable approach to expanding the company's reach.

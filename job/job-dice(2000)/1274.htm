Job Description

  • Software Quality Assurance and Testing Manager
  • Easton, PA
Founded in 1925 and based in Easton Pennsylvania, the Victaulic Company is the world's leading ISO 9001 certified manufacturer of mechanical pipe-joining products with 3,500 employees worldwide. The company is continuing to experience significant growth and is seeking to add to its staff in selected territories nationwide. Today, Victaulic piping solutions can be found at work the world over - in small to large commercial and industrial HVAC and fire protection applications, safely moving large volumes of wet and dry mediums in industrial piping, oilfield, water and waste water treatment plants, and mining operations. What began as simply a faster, easier and more efficient way of joining pipe has evolved into a whole new approach to providing piping solutions, setting the global standard for piping, efficiency, and performance reliability.

The Software QA and Testing Manager's role is to develop, establish and enforce quality assurance standards and measures for the information technology services within the organization including defining and establishing SDLC process, monitoring the lifecycle and oversee testing phase of the software development lifecycle. The QA Manager will apply proven analytical and problem-solving skills to help validate IT processes through careful testing in order to maximize the benefit of business investments in IT initiatives.

Responsibilities:
1. Build from the ground up the QA Department. Define the department's mission, objectives, necessary roles, responsibilities, and staff department.
2. Lead the development, establishment and enforcement of quality assurance measures and testing standards for new applications, products, and/or enhancements to existing applications throughout their development/product lifecycles.
3. Help to define the strategic direction and roadmap for quality and testing throughout the SDLC.
4. Ensure that software quality measures comply with regulatory standards, industry standards, and accepted best practices.
5. Create methodologies and processes for the collection and analysis of data for ensuring software, systems, and product quality.
6. Direct the analysis of formal test results in order to discover and report any defects, bugs, errors, configuration issues and interoperability flaws.
7. Direct the analysis of documentation and technical specifications for any new application under deployment or consideration to determine its intended functionality.
8. Develop change control processes, practices, and guidelines for new and existing technologies.
9. Determine the selection and appropriate usage of automated test tools and data collection tools.
10. Develop and write test plans and scripts for tracking defects and fixes in product development, software application development, information systems, and operations systems.
11. Drive and deliver software products on time with high quality; must take responsibility for quality of projects.
12. Define/apply/utilize metrics to determine the readiness, quality and operability of software, systems, and products due for release to clients and/or within the enterprise.
13. Create and communicate test plans and scripts that will determine optimal application performance according to specifications.
14. Conduct internal audits to measure and assure adherence to established QA standards for software development, application integration, and information system performance, and corresponding documentation.
15. Coordinate testing of new software to ensure integration into company systems meets functional requirements, system compliance and technical specifications.
16. Communicate test progress, test results, and other relevant information to project stakeholders and executive management.
17. Manage all members of the quality assurance team, and to ensure that aggressive deadlines are met.
18. Responsible for working closely with development and BA teams to provide overall quality measurements for projects being tested.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's Degree in software engineering, computer science, or business administration and/or 12 years equivalent work experience. Master's degree preferred.
2. PMP certification preferred.
3. 10-12 years direct experience in overseeing the design, development and implementation of quality assurance standards for software testing.
4. 5 years direct experience as a QA Manager involving major application implementation and integration.
5. At least 3 years direct experience managing all aspects of a QA team.
6. Experience building a QA team and instituting QA practices.
7. Strong knowledge of system testing metrics, best practices and methodologies.
8. Extensive experience with ERP software applications, BCPS and Lawson M3 experience especially helpful.
9. Experience with CRM packages (such as Siebel, eCommerce platforms (such as Websphere) and data warehouse solutions (such as Oracle Analytics and Silvon Stratum) desired.
10. Direct hands-on experience with ad hoc query programs, automated testing tools, and reporting software.
11. Extensive practical knowledge in importing data for use in report software, spreadsheets, graphs and flow charts.
12. Proven data analysis, data verification, and problem-solving abilities.
13. Experience with statistical reporting and analysis.
14. Excellent PC skills and technically fluent in programming languages, such RPG, Java, Lotus Notes, scripting languages, etc.
15. Good project management skills and/or substantial exposure to project-based work structures. Capable of estimating schedules, determining project scope and driving projects to completion.
16. Experience leveraging project management tools, such as MS Project.
17. Excellent written and oral communication skills.
18. Ability to communicate ideas in both technical and user-friendly languages.
19. Able to prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.
20. Excellent test management skills coupled with ability to manage multiple testing projects, while building and communicating the cross-organizational test strategy/approach.
21. Superior people management skills, with the ability to educate/mentor/influence both team members and business stakeholders.

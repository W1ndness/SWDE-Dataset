Patelco Credit Union is one of the largest and most successful credit unions in the United States. We were charted in 1936 to serve the employees of Pacific Telephone and Telegraph Company, the organization from which the credit union derived its name. Patelco remained a telephone company credit union until 1983, at which time it expanded its field of membership to companies within the telecommunications and high technology industries, and professional service organizations.

Now with over 250,000 members and over $3.5 billion in assets, we have become an industry leader in convenience and innovation. Patelco delivers service through its many branches and a nationwide network of ATMs and shared branches.

While the position is now located in our downtown SF office, we will be relocating to Pleasanton in early 2011.  Our new corporate offices will be conveniently located near the BART station and as a benefit to our employees we offer a Commuter Check program.

Overview

This position handles accounts payable and other accounting functions using third party software, SUMMIT, and Excel, and works with other credit union staff and the managers for questions related to the A/P vouchers and employee reimbursements submitted.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Administers expense and employee reimbursement policies and procedures
  • Assigns proper G/L coding in processing expense and employee reimbursement vouchers
  • Analyzes the invoices for incorrect billings and contacts the manager and/or vendor for potential errors
  • Determines if payments should be expensed or treated as prepaid expenses or capitalized as fixed assets
  • Prepares various spreadsheets to monitor certain monthly expenses
  • Ensures the monthly recurring expense vouchers are submitted by contacting managers and SVPs. Provides the required monthly accrued expenses
  • Ensures the monthly miscellaneous income checks are received. Provides the required monthly accrued income
  • Prepares G/L account reconciliations
  • Performs balancing and reconciliation of other accounting functions

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree in accounting, finance, or business or three years of accounting experience is required
  • Financial institution experience preferred
  • Accounts Payable experience required
  • Demonstrated ability to balance speed and accuracy in a fast paced work environment
  • Ability to meet deadlines
  • Excellent computer skills MS Windows and MS Office (Excel and Word) Outstanding oral and written communication skills
  • Must be detail oriented and multi-tasked
  • Analytical skill and fast learning ability required
  • Ability to be well organized, team-player, motivated and flexible with working hours

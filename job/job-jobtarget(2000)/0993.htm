A member of Human Resources will be on hand to discuss these opportunities with you and will be accepting resumes for the following:

•TEAM LEADER TELLERS - Full TIME
4801 West Golf Rd., Skokie, IL & at 20 S. Clark, Chicago, IL
Responsibilities include servicing the needs of the customer by offering referral opportunities and processing transactions. Requires a High School Diploma, good math aptitude, a minimum one year customer service, 6 months cash-handling or Teller experience. PC experience is also required.

•RELATIONSHIP BANKERS - FULL TIME
4400 Oakton Street, Skokie, IL & 5501 W. 79TH Street, Chicago, IL
Responsible for developing, retaining and expanding profitable banking customer relationships by generating ideas, recommendations and product solutions that enhance the customer's financial position. Individual seeks out prospective new customers and sells all appropriate banking products and services. College degree, with a business emphasis, and a minimum of two years previous sales experience with proven track record of meeting goals is required. Financial Services Industry experience preferred. Must also have excellent business development skills, professional presentation and high quality customer relationship orientation. PC experience, including Microsoft Office is also a requirement.

•BANKING CENTER ASSOCIATE - FULL TIME
4400 Oakton Street, Skokie, IL
Accountable for delivering superior customer service in performing a variety of basic banking services. The banking center associate is also accountable for referring prospects/customers to a banker and other banking partners. The banking associate may perform certain administrative duties within the teller area. Job Requires High School Diploma or Graduate Equivalent Degree required 2 to 3 years of related work experience with two plus years of customer service experience

•BUSINESS CUSTOMER SPECIALIST - FULL TIME
4400 Oakton Street, Skokie, IL
Leads the support effort for the banking sales team to meet established calling and sales goals, and ensuring customer retention and satisfaction. Works with loan administration and loan processing on Commercial Loan renewals and acts as liaison between the customer and the Bank to gather necessary documentation to renew/increase and/or restructure loans. Job Requires High School Diploma or Graduate Equivalent Degree, 5 plus years of related work experience and previous experience in commercial loan documentation

•BANKING CENTER PROJECT COORDINATOR - PART TIME
Accountable for delivering superior customer service in performing a variety of basic banking services. The banking center associate is also accountable for referring prospects/customers to a banker and other banking partners. The banking center associate may perform certain administrative duties within the teller area. Job requires High School Diploma or Graduate Equivalent Degree required, 2 - 4 years of related work experience, and knowledge of systems including Microsoft Office suite and proprietary software products used in support of the business.

•TELLERS - FULL TIME
350 East Dundee Rd, Wheeling, IL
Accountable for delivering superior customer service in a variety of basic banking services. The teller is also accountable for referring prospects/customers to a banker and other banking partners. The teller may perform other certain administrative duties within the teller area. High School Diploma or Graduate Equivalent Degree required. 1 to 3 years of related work experience with 1 to 3 years of customer service experience and cash handing experience.
•BANKING CENTER MANAGER I & II - FUTURE FULL TIME POSITIONS
this position manages the overall performance of the banking center focused on servicing the needs of our Core customers. Selects, trains, coaches and evaluates Banking Center Staff, also responsible for retaining and expanding a portfolio of commercial and consumer customers. Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience and 3 to 5 plus years of banking experience; business development, sales, banking center management and customer retention beneficial

•VP BANKING CENTER RELATIONSHIP Manager - FUTURE FULL TIME POSITIONS
Responsible for developing, retaining and expanding profitable banking customer relationships by generating ideas, recommendations and product solutions that enhance the customer's financial position and minimize risk to the bank. Customers and prospects are businesses with sales under $10 million annually. Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience required 5 to 7 years experience with 5 plus years of Financial Services experience in a sales capacity with an emphasis on companies with sales of less than $10 million annually and the ability to follow a consultative sales approach. Training in non-credit (Treasury Management and Depository) product and service solutions and formal credit training beneficial


Job Requirements

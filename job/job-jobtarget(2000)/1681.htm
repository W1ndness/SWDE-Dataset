HP

Denver, Colorado, United States

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.

   

Conducts or participates in multidisciplinary research and collaborates with equipment designers and/or hardware engineers in the design, development, and utilization of electronic data processing systems software. Designing, developing, troubleshooting and debugging of software programs. Develops software for operating systems, compilers, routers, networks, utilities, databases, etc. Determines hardware compatibility and/or influences hardware design. Applies developed subject matter knowledge to solve common and complex business issues within established guidelines and recommends appropriate alternatives. Works on problems/projects of diverse complexity and scope. Exercises independent judgment within generally defined policies and practices to identify and select a solution. May act as a team or project leader providing direction to team activities and facilitates information validation and team decision-making process. Ability to handle most unique situations. May seek advice in order to make decisions on complex business issues.
 
You will be responsible for the design, development, maintenance, test, quality, and performance assurance of firmware.  You will make changes to firmware to correct errors in the original implementation and create extensions to existing programs to add new features or performance improvements.  Additionally, innovation is key as you design and develop major functional and performance enhancements for existing products and produce new software products and tools.  You will review requirements, specifications, and designs to assure product quality and will develop and implement plans and tests to ensure product quality and performance assurance. Depending on your skills and the mix of applicants, you may work on system firmware, CEC firmware, manageability firmware, or system errors handling on a variety of BCS platforms. (Please see the Qualifications section for more details on the differing qualifications to work on these different areas of firmware.)

BS plus at least 3 years relevant industry experience or MS plus at least 2 years relevant experience.

? 5 years experience using C and/or C++

? Leading and/or working with cross-functional teams (HW and SW) as well as practical experience in all phases of the software development lifecycle.

. Highly desirable to have prior experience in embedded firmware. 

? Strong analytic ability in order to research technical issues and generate creative/innovative solutions.

? Ability to learn new technical content quickly

? Comfortable working in large code base and debugging complex problems

? Understanding performance impacts of code

? Strong distributed software design skills.

? Strong programming skills.

? Strong system architecture knowledge including symmetric multiprocessor systems

? Understanding of low level HW (e.g., at the register level) and ability to understand HW specifications and translate them into designs and code.

? Understanding of own skill set strongly enough to generate accurate plans for tasks on the road map

? Strong communication skills .

? Ability to collaborate with partners in a virtual, geographically dispersed team.

? Understanding of server customers and their requirements (desirable)


 
Demonstrated practical knowledge and experience in the following areas:
?         x86 Assembly code, x86 BIOS
?         UEFI development using EDK2
?         PC architecture, ACPI, SMBIOS, PXE, TCP/IP
?         PCI express, USB, SAS, SATA, & LAN hardware devices and drivers.
?         Writing and debugging cpu boot strap code and interrupt handlers
?         OS/Firmware interaction and debugging multiprocessor code
?         AMI or Phoenix Bios experience desirable
?         Writing device drivers desirable
?         Intel ITP experience, hardware analyzer tools: pci, usb, network analyzer experience desirable.
 
Manageability Firmware Engineer
Very experienced writing distributed applications in the Linux environment using pipes, sockets, shared memory and signals and using semaphores, mutexes or other means of coordination among processes and threads. 
 
CEC Firmware Engineer
?         Very experienced writing distributed applications in the Linux environment using pipes, sockets, shared memory and signals and using semaphores, mutexes or other means of coordination among processes and threads. 
?         Experience with virtual memory, interrupt handling, and software/hardware error handling
?         Knowledge of processor design concepts
?         Experience writing Perl scripts
  
Architectural Simulator Engineer
?         Demonstrated ability to bridge the gap between hardware, firmware, and testing; being focused on all three to accomplish goals
?         Strong C++/OO design skills, knows and can implement design patterns
?         Demonstrated ability to  program in multi-threaded/multi-process environments, constructing code that is maintainable and dynamically extensible for future revisions of hardware.
?         Strong understanding of common networking protocols and routing desirable
 
 
Error Subsystem Firmware Developer
?         Familiarity with design and coding of applications for a real-time or event driven environment
?         Familiarity with error logging subsystems either at an OS or application level
?         Familiarity with software and hardware error detection and recovery techniques
?         Experience with automatic error diagnosis

Maniilaq Health Center is seeking a

Accreditation and Quality Services Specialist

Maniilaq Association is a progressive tribal organization located in Kotzebue, Alaska providing health, cosial and tribal services to the people of Maniilaq service area.

Responsible for assisting Administrative Staff,  Program Managers and village clinics with the development, implementation and compliance maintenance of  Joint Commission, CARF and other regulatory standards.  Provides training to assist in meeting compliance with Joint Commission and other regulatory  requirements.  Develops, coordinates and participates in a multifaceted regulatory standards educational program to ensure that all appropriate employees are knowledgeable of and comply with pertinent state and federal standards, such as those for Joint Commission, OSHA, CARF, HIPAA, Medicare and Medicaid.  Responsible for assisting Administrative Staff, Program managers and village clinics with implementation of  Quality Assurance, Quality Improvement and Performance Improvement.  Coordinates maintenance of  the PolicyTech Software database  Works independently and is expected to exercise a high degree of creativity in organizing and planning in carrying out the duties.  Reports directly to the Director of Compliance & Quality Services.


Job Requirements

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

1.                  1. Responsible for developing, maintaining and coordinating activities to meet the required Joint Commission and other regulatory agency standards.

2.                 2.  Coordinates and assists various Health Center Departments and Village clinics with the implementation of projects regarding Joint Commission and Performance Improvement as assigned by the Director of Compliance and Quality Services.

3.                 3.   Keeps up-to-date on  changes in standards and requirements governing accreditation.

4.                  4. Responsible for determining the degree to which the Health Center and Village Clinics meets the  Joint Commission requirements and coordinates corrective action.

5.                5.   Responsible for determining the degree to which Behavioral Health meets the CARF requirements and coordinates corrective action. 

6.                 6.  Recommends and assists with implementation of resolution strategies in regards to meeting Joint Commission, CARF and other regulatory standards. 

7.                 7.  Performs Patient and Departmental Tracers at the Health Center to ensure Joint Commission survey readiness.

8.                 8.  Coordinates the completion of the Joint Commission Periodic Performance Review.

9.                9.   Assists department managers with required Joint Commission processes such as hazard risk assessments.

10.             10.   Coordinates all activities related to obtaining a successful outcome on a Joint Commission or CARF survey.

11.             11.   Serves as chairperson of the Joint Commission and Performance Improvement Committees.

12.              12.  Coordinates regular meetings regarding CARF compliance. 

13.              13.  Implement facility wide Joint Commission programs as assigned by the Director of Compliance & Quality Services.

14.             14.    Develops and implements ongoing training and education for appropriate personnel to ensure compliance with Joint Commission, CARF, state and federal regulations.

15.             15.   Submits reports regularly to the Director of Compliance & Quality Services regarding the Joint Commission and CARF compliance status.

16.              16.  Assists department and village clinics with the development and implementation of the Quality Assurance, Quality Improvement and Performance Improvement Calendars.

17.             17.   Spends a minimum of 25% of the time working on the continuing improvement of processes and systems within the Health Center and Village Clinics. 

18.              18.   Submits reports regularly to the Director of Compliance & Quality Services on the status of Quality Assurance, Quality Improvement and Performance Improvement.

19.              19.  Assesses accuracy of CRS (GPRA) data and serves as the state liaison for GPRA activities.  

20.             20.   Provides support for the Social Services and Elder Care Services Division of Maniilaq Association as directed. 

21.             21. Cross trains and covers in New Employee Orientation in Ethics, Safety, Customer Service, and HIPAA and Compliance.

Company Overview

Ross Video specializes in the design of products for the professional broadcast market. We design, manufacture and support a wide range of innovative products for use in broadcast, distribution, live event and production applications. Ross Video products have been sold into more than 100 countries. Visit www.rossvideo.com to apply.

The Programmer Analyst, CRM Systems, is responsible for improving Ross Sales force efficiencies through administering CRM (Customer Relationship Management) tools and data.

Duties, Responsibilities and Accountability:

  • § Expert and Administrator of the entire suite of Ross Video's CRM software systems and data (Sage SalesLogix).
  • § Responsible for system and data security, accuracy, and availability.
  • § Primary MIS interface to the Sales organization. Must understand Sales operations, needs, and expectations.
  • § Database administrator and data manipulation expert on this system and advisor to other Ross systems, such as MRP and business intelligence solutions.
  • § Specifies, develops, modifies, implements, and maintains these systems to most effectively sell product and track sales
  • § Stays current on Saleslogix capabilities, flavours, and versions and able to justify and implement the most valuable improvements for the company.
  • § Ensures these systems interoperate and communicate effectively with other Ross data systems
  • § Interface with external consultants as required.
  • § Manages projects of medium complexity.
  • § Designs and implements improved business processes related to these tools
  • § Trains other MIS members on system operation and practices
  • § Resolves end user problems with these tools effectively and completely
  • § Maximize the value of these tools to all Ross employees
  • § Supports worldwide sales staff on these tools
  • § Maintains current knowledge of related CRM software tools and industry practices to best apply these at Ross
  • § Works with Ross IT group to obtain acceptable data and system reliability performance
  • § Perform other related duties as required.

Job Requirements

Job Requirements:

Knowledge/Education/Training:

  • § Bachelor of Computer Science, or equivalent experience in the field.
  • § SQL Server 2008 and SSRS
  • § Training on administration of databases and database connectivity (SQL, Oracle, ODBC, etc).
  • § Training in data manipulation and scripting (VB.NET, C++, Java, XML, Perl)
  • § Microsoft Outlook/Exchange and Active Directory training is an asset
  • § Experience with Microsoft Windows Server 2003 and 2008
  • § Deep understanding of the product sales flow and process


Traits:

  • § Enjoys a self-driven, privately owned company environment
  • § Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • § Able to distill user requests into prioritized, justifiable improvements
  • § Problem solving and reasoning ability
  • § Project management skills
  • § Strong customer service orientation
  • § Attention to detail and ruthless pursuit of accuracy

Experience:

  • § 8-10 years experience in data systems administration and operation, specifically with CRM systems including SalesLogix
  • § 2 -3 years experience in a corporate Sales environment or supporting Sales teams
  • § Practical experience with a relational database management systems
  • § Prior experience in playing a lead role in a system implementation or upgrade
  • § Experience in a product development or manufacturing environment is useful

 Other:

  • § Valid Driver's License and access to a vehicle for required travel to Ottawa or Iroquois sites for meetings
  • § Ability to travel to US and other countries for training, conference, and support of remote Ross personnel

Manufacturing facility in Lockport, Il has immediate opening for a Quality Assurance Inspector. Company culture is team oriented, with challenges and tremendous growth opportunity.

Must be technically sound in steel fabrication for multiple processes and proficient in material specifications of steels and alloys -  

Candidate must possess strong negotiation, problem solving, verbal and written communication skills, excellent interpersonal skills, and effective team building skills, with a proven ability to deliver results. Must have the ability to multi-task and prioritize and the ability to effectively manage conflict and maintain strong employee relations.

Position will:

 

  • Work with Quality Assurance Manager to ensure highest quality product to our Customers.
  • Prevent errors at inception without continuing through the production process.
  • Interpret, validate sample, production materials and finished product -  random and scheduled inspection 
  • Maintain a positive flow of finished material from the workstations to the staging area for loading. maintain a positive flow of finished material from the workstations to the staging area for loading.
  • Generation of inspection reports/forms , Database entry, corrective action requirements
  • Provide written reports submitted to the Q.A. Manager.
  • Ensure or execute product piece marking.
  • Generation of shipment documents for tracking sub-contractor or customer components from shop drawing and/or Bill of materials.
  • Attention to safety, report unsafe practices immediately.  
  • This position may require overtime at any given time without notice because of production schedule 

This job description in no way implies that these are the only duties to be performed by this position.  He or She will be required to follow any other instructions and perform any other request by his or her supervisor. The general job description is subject to review and revision at any time.

 


Job Requirements

Skills required:

 

Successful candidate will have a solid understanding of basic ERP systems and effective PC skills (Microsoft Office, ability to work in Microsoft office product to generate spread sheets and documents.), knowledge of ERP software packages for material procurement, Inventory and scheduling.

Proficient in the interpretation of structural steel blue prints.

 

Work safely with material handling equipment as hand truck, fork lift, crane operation.

Ability to interpret bill of materials, ability to generate a pack list in preparation of shipping documentation.

Effective in general mathematical calculations as well as measuring equipment.

Ability to lift 60 pounds routinely throughout shift.

Ability to use small hand tools for creation of packaging crates/skids.

Ability to use computer collection systems for reporting of labor performed.

 

Only those with impeccable references and a track record in purchasing should apply.  We check references and assess skills as part of our hiring process. We are an Equal Opportunity Employer.

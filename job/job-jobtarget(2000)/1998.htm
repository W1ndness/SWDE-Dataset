This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7368072
Position Title: Marketing Director
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Posted: November 10, 2010
Job Function: Advertising / Marketing
Job Type: Full-Time

Position Summary: Guides, directs, and manages all marketing activities including marketing planning, advertising/promotion, communications, public relations, database marketing and research.  Provides strategic leadership for sales/sales management and service quality.    

 

Job Responsibilities:

 

  • Responsible for implementing the marketing concept throughout the Bank to ensure attainment of marketing objectives and goals.
  • Formulates, implements, and maintains the marketing planning process throughout the Bank.
  • Develops, implements, and tracks advertising and promotions.  Works with appropriate resources to coordinate advertising and directs the appropriate placement of media to ensure that Bank objectives and goals are met.
  • Develops and implements communications for the Bank.  Directs internal and external communication function to ensure that needs of employees, customers, and the press are met in a timely and effective manner.
  • Directs public relations function for the Bank.  Develops and manages all community programs to increase public awareness of the Bank.
  • Provides strategic leadership in the areas of sales/sales management, customer retention, and service quality to ensure attainment of marketing objectives and goals.
  • Responsible for managing marketing data base, direct mail campaigns, market research, product management, and product development to ensure attainment of the Bank’s marketing objectives and goals.
  • Develops and manages the marketing budget to ensure the department operates within its limitations.
  • Performs additional duties and responsibilities as required.
  • Acts as web master in ensuring that information on the Bank’s web page is current and accurate.  Oversees social marketing activities.
  • Ensures that the Bank is effectively marketing to customers primarily using alternate delivery channels.
  • Participates in training and development of bank/branch staff in sales and marketing.
  • Creates and produces written marketing materials such as brochures, pamphlets, and point of sale materials in branches.
  • Writes and manages On Hold Production messages for delivery to customers on hold.
  • Writes and manages press releases for Bank events, staff changes, and other appropriate newsworthy material for the bank.
  • Communicate any observations and recommendations which would improve Bank productivity or efficiency.
  • Should communicate any recommendations for targeting new markets or delivery channels.
  • Represents and supports bank interests at outside events beyond the scope of branch authority.
  • Maintain a good working relationship with all departmental areas of the Bank.
  • Expected to maintain open lines of communication between Bank and vendors.

Job Requirements

Job Qualifications/Other Skills:

 

·         B.A./B.S. with concentration in Marketing or equivalent job experience.

·         3-5 years related experiences in developing and marketing new products and services.

 

Other Skills/Qualifications:

 

·         Ability to interact positively with all Bank employees and officers.

·         Ability to represent Bank interests outside the Bank.

·         Working knowledge of personal computers and software needed to support job functions, specifically Microsoft Word, Excel and MCIF Software.

·         Working knowledge of computer, related marketing software programs.

·         Good analytical and problem solving aptitude.

·         Written and oral command of language.

·         Exceptional organizational project management skills.

·         Ability to handle multiple tasks with minimal error.

·         Ability to work with demanding timelines and schedules.

·         Work cooperatively with all levels of Bank Management.

 

 EOE

M/F/V/H

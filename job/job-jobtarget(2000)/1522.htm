As one of the largest degree-granting higher education systems in North America, DeVry University provides high-quality, career-oriented associate, bachelor's and master's degree programs in technology, science, business and the arts.  Nearly 70,000 students are enrolled at its 93 locations in 26 states and Canada, as well as through DeVry University's online delivery.  DeVry University is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission of the North Central Association, www.ncahlc.org. DeVry University, a division of DeVry Inc. (NYSE: DV), is based in Oakbrook Terrace, Ill.  For more information about DeVry University, visit www.devry.edu.

In conjunction with the University Director of Student Finance, the Group Director of Student Finance will administer financial aid and student account programs for a designated group of campuses.  Provide leadership, direction and coordination for the organization and delivery of Student Finance programs in the designated group.  Create an environment of consistency and accuracy.  Direct and manage all Student Finance operations in the group and ensures compliance with State and Federal guidelines and laws. 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Provides oversight and supervision of the Directors of Student Finance in the group as they direct the overall operations of Student Finance programs which includes providing financial assistance to students and delivery of all student financial aid activities.
  • Provides leadership to promote and maintain integrity, accuracy and timeliness in the delivery of financial aid to students.
  • Ensures that Directors administer the various financial aid programs and that all financial aid is awarded, authorized and disbursed in a timely and accurate manner.
  • Assists the University Director in setting Student Finance goals and objectives for the group and ensures that all goals are met.
  • Ensures that programs are developed to overcome student financial aid issues in order to maintain and promote student retention.
  • Collaborates in the design and maintenance of Campuses' structures and staffing plans to effectively accomplish DeVry's student finance goals and objectives.
  • Assists Campus Presidents in recruiting, hiring, training and managing the Directors of Student Finance.
  • Directs the development of policies and procedures for Student Finance in order streamline work follow processes.
  • Reviews and approves the plans, development, and implementation of Student Finance strategies in order to meet short-term and long-term objectives.
  • Ensures the creation of processes, controls, reports, evaluation and reviews of DeVry's Student Finance policies and ensures compliance with Federal and State laws.
  • Ensures the Student Finance Departments maintain an exemplary standard of customer service which includes counseling students and parents on financial aid programs, providing detailed information, and answering questions.
  • Ensures the necessary reports regarding financial aid are produced on schedule for management.
  • Fosters and maintains a supportive student-centered atmosphere that is sensitive and responsive to students and their families.
  • Participates in committee meetings.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.

Qualifications:

To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.  The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.  Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • Bachelor's degree required and Masters degree preferred.
  • Ten plus years experience in Student Finance operations with experience directing many different locations.
  • Knowledge of principles, practices, methodology, and procedures for the provision of student financial aid.
  • Knowledge of federal and state financial aid laws.
  • Ability to supervise and direct the activities of others.
  • PC skills in Microsoft office and financial aid software packages.
  • Excellent mathematical and organizational skills.
  • Proven ability to set priorities and deadlines and ensure projects are completed.

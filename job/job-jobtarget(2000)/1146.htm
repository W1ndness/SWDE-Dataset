HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Business Climate:
Hewlett-Packard is the leader in Converged Infrastructure solutions.  The HP Converged Infrastructure provides a blueprint for the data center of the future that eliminates costly and rigid IT silos, so customers can spend much more of their IT budget on business innovation.  This is achieved by converging server, storage and networks with facilities - all managed through a common management platform.  The result is an infrastructure that delivers a new level of simplicity, integration, and automation that can accelerate business outcomes that matter most.
 
The HP StorageWorks X9000 Scale-out NAS Network Storage System is designed and delivered on HP Converged Infrastructure using a common modular infrastructure of virtualized storage resources.  The X9000 is the solution for controlling costs associated with the exponential growth of unstructured data and improving the performance of file-based applications with scale-out network storage that efficiently manages information in a single namespace.  X9000 Network Storage Systems scale-out beyond traditional network attached storage in both capacity and performance.  Central management allows the administrator to manage not just terabytes but multi-petabyte environments at the highest density possible.  The pay-as-you-grow architecture and an all-inclusive feature set, eliminates storage over-provisioning or expensive add-ons.  The X9000 is the platform for customers designing the next generation data center.
 
This is an opportunity to work with a select team of hands-on technical selling professionals who are highly competent, capable and confident.  This opening is for an X9000 Solution Architect.
 
Job Description:
An HP X9000 Solution Architect (SA) is a presales technical domain expert with in-depth hands-on technical knowledge of Scale-out NAS and Storage related solutions as well as HP storage management software.  Solution Architects assess customer requirements and recommend solutions for our customers.  The X9000 Solution Architect will frequently be asked to validate recommended solutions with our customers via on-site pilot project and proof of concept project engagements to ensure that the recommended solution effectively meets or exceeds our customers' expectations.  This position may be identified by other industry terms such as Presales Solution Consultant, Presales Subject-Matter Expert, Solution Architect, Technical Consultant, Presales Engineer, Sales Engineer and Sales Consultant.  It is an opportunity-driven position and the X9000 SA will be proactively engaged on a demand basis by multiple sales account teams.
 
As an X9000 Solution Architect, you will partner with HP ESSN sales account teams to create competitive selling advantages.  Key responsibilities include:
  • Designing and consulting on complex IT solutions
  • Executing hands-on proof-of-concept activities
  • Transferring expert knowledge to customers and reseller partners
  • Providing IT thought leadership
You will work with sales teams and Storage sales specialist to generate profitable X9000 business and exceed sales quota.  You'll support multiple accounts and opportunities as needed, and have visibility to the most challenging and exciting technical projects at HP's customers.
 
As an X9000 Solution Architect you will leverage your domain expertise to quickly overcome customer barriers to complex buying decisions and thereby accelerate sales.  You'll develop trust relationships with key decision makers and technical staff when engaged in on-site opportunities.  You'll deliver increased customer loyalty, satisfaction and sales via the successful adoption of HP X9000 and StorageWorks products and services.  And in addition to customer-facing work, the HP product engineering groups and marketing teams will seek your feedback, to ensure HP solutions meet customer needs.  Mentoring and coaching of less-experienced employees is another important part of the job.
 
This position is on a leveraged 80/20 plan and covers the US with 50% travel.  Candidates must live within the US - preferred locations are: Charlotte, NC; Tampa or Orlando, Florida, or Atlanta, GA.

FIELD TECHNICIAN
Arlington, VA
*Based in the Washington DC Metro Area*

This position is responsible for providing system support for FirstView® and PROTECT®. FirstView is a web server based video and sensor security software system that is deployed on custom off the shelf (COTS) PC hardware. Support of the system involves deep understanding of PC hardware, and operating systems, and the ability to source and specify PC and other hardware devices to support our systems. This position also requires an understanding of software installation and troubleshooting. The successful candidate would be responsible for loading and validating our software, as well as creating and implementing monitoring procedures to proactively monitor customer systems. The role also involves maintaining good customer relationships by providing professional second level technical support. The role involves helping troubleshoot hardware software and network problems for customers as well as assisting field engineering during installations.

Other responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Ability to provide remote support over the phone, through remote connections, and onsite support to customers and staff as well.
  • Repair and maintain customer hardware and software as necessary.
  • Assist in maintaining internal IT infrastructure
  • Comply with and ensure department compliance with Company health, safety and environmental policies.
  • Comply with all applicable U.S. export control and security regulations.
  • Perform other duties as assigned

Job Requirements

Requirements include:

  • A bachelor's degree or equivalent experience
  • 3+ years of direct and demonstrable experience
  • Experience with IP networking, video encoding/streaming and deployment in enterprise operations a plus.
  • Extensive computer network experience: LAN, WAN, wireless, firewalls, routers and VPN.
  • Expert knowledge of Windows XP, Windows 2003 Server and Linux including installation, configuration and deployment experience.
  • Expert knowledge of PC hardware including assembly, upgrades and physical deployment.
  • Impeccable interpersonal and communication skills.
  • United States citizenship and ability to obtain a Secret level security clearance.
  • Ability to travel and work independently to provide technical support to customers and other staff.
  • Ability to move and install computer hardware weighing up to 50lbs.
  • Ability to travel 40percent-50percent

JOIN THE LEADER!

Ralcorp Holdings, Inc

 

Ralcorp, headquartered in St. Louis, MO, is the leading producer of a variety of value brand and store brand foods sold under the individual labels of various grocery, mass merchandise and drugstore retailers, and frozen bakery products sold to in-store bakeries, restaurants and other foodservice customers.  Through a recent merger with Post Foods, Ralcorp is now also a leading producer of branded cereals. Ralcorp's diversified product mix includes: ready-to-eat and hot cereals; nutritional and cereal bars; snack mixes, corn-based chips and corn snack products; crackers and cookies; snack nuts; chocolate candy; salad dressings; mayonnaise; peanut butter; jams and jellies; syrups; sauces; frozen griddle products; frozen biscuits and other frozen pre-baked products.

 

We have emerged as a solid and powerful organization focusing on newly energized and highly motivated teams.  Today we also emerge with a heightened commitment to expand our presence through new product development and through the acquisition of allied store brand businesses.  Through operations in over thirty plants within the United States and Canada we employ about 9,000 people with sales boasting of $4 billion. Every location offers an environment that values each of its employees!

 

KEY RESULT AREAS: The Planning and Analysis – Lead serves as single point-of-contact for end users for specific module(s), functional and/or business area(s). Understands the business function and works with team to define business requirements. Communicates and coordinates with other Business Analysts, business leadership and IT leadership to ensure appropriate integration of processes and modules across Ralcorp. Supports Business community in their use of the Trade / Advertising and Consumer Promotions applications, and integrating JDE modules.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Perform problem identification & resolution for issues related to specific module(s), functional and business area(s). Provide training to prevent problem reoccurrence and knowledge transfer. Escalate issues to management when appropriate.
  • Collect, analyze, review, document and communicate business needs and requirements to IT. Translate high level business requirements into functional specifications and manage changes to such specifications.
  • Serve as module expert during implementations of ERP software. Perform module configuration and testing following control guidelines.
  • Contribute to the overall planning and execution of projects and issue resolution including the development of approaches, identifying resources, and developing timelines.
  • Mentoring and educating other staff members.
  • Involved in security/authorization set-up, user documentation, data interface design, data migration and reconciliation.
  • Assist IT management and end-users to determine feasibility of potential systems projects.
  • Perform special duties/functions which might be assigned by manager.


OTHER DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Possess leadership skills
  • Work under general direction/minimal supervision
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Proven ability to work in a successful team environment
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Good project management skills
  • Manage and adjust priorities
  • Customer focused solutions oriented
  • Ensures collaboration across functional areas


SCOPE DATA:

  • All areas within Ralcorp Holdings, Inc. which use offered services.
  • Occasional travel required.


WORKING RELATIONSHIP:

  • Works with internal Ralcorp Holdings, Inc. managerial and non-managerial personnel to accomplish job requirements
  • Works with all levels of end-users as needed to accomplish job requirements


Job Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree required.
  • 6 - 8 years experience overall, including understanding of IT Processes; work with large scale IT project such as conversions
  • Must possess an understanding of the business function(s)/process(s) supported.
  • Experience with Sales, Trade, or Advertising and Consumer Promotions application support is preferred.
  • Excellent knowledge of systems project work and business functionality in ERP applications required.
  • Working knowledge of systems project work.
  • Familiarity with JDE is preferred.
  • Experience using MS Office.

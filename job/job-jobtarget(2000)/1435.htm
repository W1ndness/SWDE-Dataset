Order to Cash (OTC) Analyst

Pitney Bowes

Stamford, Connecticut, United States

This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7238914
Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: Order to Cash (OTC) Analyst
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Industry: Information Technology (IT)
Posted: November 27, 2010
Job Function: Analyst, SAP / Java Lead, Software Engineer, Systems Analyst, Technical Analyst
Min Experience: 3-5 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Contact Person: Chris Pegnataro
Email Address:
Pitney Bowes is a Fortune 500 Global information solutions provider and has an exciting opportunity for a candidate well versed in Order to Cash processes. This position will be responsible for Order Management processes and application functionality within Pitney Bowes' (PBI) TechCentral IT organization reporting to the Manager of Customer and Order Management applications.  The position will be responsible to lead small to mid size projects and project teams supporting PBI's ERP applications.  In addition the position will play a key role in 'Strategic Transformation' projects currently underway enhancing current applications and being part of developing roadmaps for new platforms and applications.
 
This position serves as a liaison between the business community, IT organization, and technical supplier(s) in order to provide application solutions to meet user needs. The candidate must effectively partner with internal and external customers, provide fact based critical analysis, demonstrate the ability to work in a matrix environment, and act with courage and decisiveness to ensure quality delivery of project/business objectives.
 
Accountabilities:
 
  • Drive application standards and process simplifications
  • Ensure that design, development, delivery and supportability of application solutions are in accordance with business and IT objectives
  • Introduce new ways of business process modeling supporting the elicitation of business and functional requirements as part of CMMi
  • Coordinates with offshore and other technical providers to ensure requirements and functional specifications are of high quality.
The company is an EEO and Affirmative Action Employer that values diversity in the workplace.  Women and minorities are encouraged to apply.
 
American with Disabilities Act Requirements
 
While performing the essential functions of the job, the employee is regularly required to sit, speak clearly, hear and use hands and fingers.  The employee is occasionally required to stand, walk, reach with hands or arms, stoop, kneel, crouch or lift and/or move maximum of 10 pounds.
 

Job Requirements
  • Minimum of 3 years experience in IT positions
  • Demonstrate deep technical and business process knowledge around OTC applications
  • Experienced in system analysis and development, project planning and execution
  • Must have a proven track record in requirements gathering, blueprint, and implementing optimal solutions based on business requirements.
  • Experience designing, testing, and implementing functionality within the SD module in SAP.
  • Must have the ability to manage projects that include non SAP technologies.
  • Significant testing experience, including definition and documentation of test results at various phases (unit, system, user acceptance) of the project lifecycle.
  • Strong communication skills that enable the candidate to work independently with business and IT partners throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Aptitude and attitude that will allow the candidate to grow into increasingly larger management positions in due course.
  • Must be able to lead small to mid size project teams, including coordination with offshore resources.
  • Translates business needs into functional requirements that can be understood by a technical resource.
  • Must be able to manage multiple tasks simultaneously

To apply or obtain more information on this position, please contact Chris Pegnataro at chris.pegnataro@pb.com.

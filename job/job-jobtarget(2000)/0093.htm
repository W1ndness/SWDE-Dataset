Position: TRIAL TECHNICIAN / LEGAL VIDEOGRAPHY 

 Join one of the Top 100 Fastest Growing Companies.  Work in a professional atmosphere in an upscale, downtown Portland high-rise.  Our company has provided legal videography, videoconferencing, trial technology and court reporting services for 30 years to the legal community.  We are a cutting edge leader providing technologically advanced support to the legal industry.  Our company has offices in Oregon, Washington, and Idaho that provide services across the country and internationally.

 

Our Portland office has an opening for Trial Technician / Videography 

 

Must have:

 

  • Professional appearance
  • Proven leadership skills
  • Ability to make decisions
  • Positive and upbeat attitude and behavior
  • Excellent oral and written communications skills
  • Advanced computer skills
  • Excellent attendance
  • Hardworking with the drive to succeed
  • Task oriented
  • Flexibility

 

The candidate will assist attorneys with:

 

·         Shooting video depositions

·         Set up and tear down audio/video equipment

·         Process video/transcript synchronization

·         Edit, arrange and display computerized documents

·         Trial preparation utilizing the most advanced trial software available

·         Will participate in client development

 

If you are a qualified candidate and have the required 2-3 years experience working with Attorney's in the court room, a strong video background and are driven to succeed, we encourage you to email your resume and cover letter

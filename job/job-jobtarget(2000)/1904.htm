Snowed In Studios

Are you passionate about creating video games and interactive media experiences? Do you want the opportunity to bring your game ideas to life on current, upcoming and new gaming platforms? Are you looking to take the next step from programming towards creating and designing games and interactive media?


Snowed In Studios is an emerging video game and interactive media studio in Ottawa. Founded by five industry veterans with experience working with some of North America’s largest video game companies, the studio is quickly making a name for itself with a client list that includes Behaviour Interactive (formally Artificial Mind and Movement), Artech/Vitamin G Studios and Eidos Montreal as well as other developers.


Responsibilities

Reporting to the Producer and/or Senior Software Engineer, you will be responsible for:

? Interpreting client design and technical documents in order to create full Flash/Actionscript games and interactive software

? Developing end-to-end Flash/Actionscript games for custom, web, mobile and/or other interactive platforms

? Working independently on the company’s Flash/Actionscript development efforts

In addition, the chosen candidate will be asked to take on design duties. These may include:

? Creating design documents, mock-up screenshots and other supporting documentation for game designs

? Developing prototypes, video reels and/or other playables for designed games

 

To apply, please send your resume, cover letter and portfolio link to jobs@snowedin.ca with “Flash Programmer” in the subject line or apply directly through this job board.


Job Requirements

Qualifications

? Bachelor of Computer Science, Graphic Design, Information Technology or similar post-secondary degree/diploma, or equivalent work experience

? 3 years’ experience developing for Adobe Flash/Actionscript 3.0 or combination of skills and experience

? Ability to communicate technical project-related information to team members accurately and efficiently

? Strong willingness to raise the bar for coding standards and create high-quality code using object-oriented techniques

? Goal-oriented and professional problem-solver with team-spirit


Desirable Qualifications/Additional Information:

? Strong preference will be given to candidates who also possess game design experience including designing, creating and/or releasing their own games

? Preference will be given to candidates who also possess shipped game development experience

? Experience with development source control applications and related software an asset

? Experience with web-based development platforms and languages an asset (Facebook, PHP, Python, etc.)

? Experience with other rich-media development environments an asset (HTML5, Java, etc.)

? Experience with other mobile game or application development platforms an asset (iOS, Blackberry, Android, etc.)

? Skills in art: concept art, user interfaces, character design, etc. an asset

? Bilingualism (English/French, written and spoken) an asset

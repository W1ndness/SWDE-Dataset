HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. The Software Engineering team in the Bristol-Myers Squibb Account is responsible for the installation, configuration, support and maintenance of all software used by the client.  The team provides routine operational and on-call support of the software environment as well as participates in all project work requested by the client. The team is also responsible for technology roadmaps and upgrades and other related services to ensure that enterprise applications are available to the business.
 
Due to the breadth of applications and technologies supported by this team, all team members must possess the ability to meet high standards, not only in their technical skills but demonstrate versatility and flexibility in adapting to changing needs and challenges presented to them. The ability to grow and learn new technologies is a key success factor. . General Technical Skills

The Data Warehousing team within Software Engineering supports a variety of reporting tools like Business Objects, Cognos and Hyperion. The team is also involved in supporting Analytics tools such as Dataflux, SAS, Erwin and Syncsort as well as Siebel, in the customer relationship management space. The following are some general technical skills that are required in a successful candidate for this team:
 
-       Knowledge of data warehousing, reporting and CRM technologies.
-       Experience working with various relational database systems including Oracle and SQL Server.
-       Ability to analyze and support additional software or heavily customized applications developed by the clients data warehousing teams.
-       Sufficient depth and breadth of technical knowledge to design and scope
      multiple deliverables across a number of technologies
-       Has demonstrated innovation and communication of new deliverables and  
Offerings
-       Ability to develop solutions that enhance the availability, performance,
maintainability and agility of a particular customer's enterprise. Has contributed to the design and application of new tools. Ability to re-use existing experience to develop new solutions to take to market.
-       Possesses an understanding, at a detailed level, of architectural dependencies of
      technologies in use in the customer's Information Technology (IT) environment.
-       Able to communicate with internal and external senior management confidently
and demonstrate the professionalism in the job family.
-       Ability to work in a multi technology environment with the ability to diagnose
complex technical problems to their root cause
-       Demonstrates application of technical expertise in successful engagements involving multiple disciplines.
-       Able to independently complete solution implementation or application design deliverables
 
 
Specific Technical Skills:
  • Installation and configuration of Siebel 7.x or 8.x SIA application stack.
  • Gateway - Understand concepts of Gateway and Enterprise creation. Troubleshoot local database issues. Familiarity with use of administration utilities.
  • Siebel Server - Install and configure Siebel servers in clustered and non-clustered environments. Strong experience in configuring Siebel Servers via GUI and command line required. Set and adjust logging levels as required. Perform PTT and PTP activities including .srf files, .ifb files, browser scripts and other components.
  • Siebel Database- Experience in creating Siebel repository, and running a variety of database utilities. Siebel DB upgrade experience a major plus.
  • Siebel Reports - Deploy Actuate or Oracle BI Publisher reporting components. Administer report security and troubleshoot issues with support team.
  • SWSE - Generate SWSE configurations and load balancing files. Configure eapps files for use with SSO. Work with Web Engineers in configuration of IIS virtual directories.
  • Siebel Client Software - Install and configure Siebel Tools in server environments and on client machines. Install Siebel Web Client and configure to work with multiple environments.
  • Understanding of MSCS clusters a major plus.
  • Download software and patches, build install images and open cases with Oracle Technical support.
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and work with vendor and development teams to determine root cause.
  • Experience in Siebel upgrade efforts a major benefit.
  • Experience with supporting client application components such as QAS or HP Output Server also helpful.

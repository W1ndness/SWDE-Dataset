POSITION TITLE:               Assistant Category Manager - Bakery

REPORTS TO:                    Category Manager

POSITION SUMMARY:. Assists the category manager in the development, layout, and proofing of the weekly and monthly ad programs. Manages the set-up, tracking, and maintenance of new and discontinued items. Handles operational phone calls or emails to resolve store issues and problems. Responsible for the category management functions of a limited number of categories. Provide analytical support to the CM function. Maintain Plan-O Gram and integrity of Plan- O- Grams. Conduct and maintain historical research on item performance for given categories. Works with our third party suppliers and our internal buyers to communicate, track, and manage our service levels

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES:   

  • Collect, analyze, manipulate and format cost, price, margin, and other related product item data for the Category Manager.
  • Review and analyze information such as item movement, SKU assortment, sell-through, etc.  Report assessment of data and trends to Category Manager.
  • Collaborate with Pricing as needed to ensure cost, price and other item information is correct within the various automated and data base systems.
  • Review service level and inventory turns. Inform Category Manager of any significant issues or potential supply problems.
  • Update all POG’s with new items, discontinued items, resets, etc. Ensure data integrity for all POG’s within the category.
  • Compare trends within the category to the category’s defined plan and goals.
  • Review competitive sets.
  • Work with vendors to obtain specific movement and product information regarding product distributed by the vendor.
  • Work in developing and analyzing reports from the data warehouse and Nielsen from category manager direction.
  • Follow up on weekly ad performances using financial statistics.
  • Daily communication to Team on daily OOS and potential issues.
  • Comparing various types of data in order to make educated decisions about products, pricing, plan-o grams and placement.
  • Develop Category Plans using templates provided by Senior Management.
  • Data integrity must be worked daily off the collection site
  • New vendor set up working with CDM to insure accuracy.
  • Cost change forms submitted and processed accurately.
  • Period end reconciliation to include funding for performance events.
  • Develops pricing strategies for categories and retail prices for individual items.  Ensures compliance with overall pricing policy as they relate to gross profit and competitiveness.
  • Develops clear and timely communication of all strategies and programs
  • Manages all lines of the P&L as they relate to the profitability of the categories assigned
    • Sales
    • Gross margin
    • Net margin
    • special reductions
    • shrink
    • vendor funding rates
    • all types of allowances
  • Advocates for systemic or workflow process improvements.

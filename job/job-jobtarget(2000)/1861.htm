Under the direction of the Director of the Financial Aid Office (FAO), oversees the operation functions of the University and Federal financial aid programs using appropriate financial aid specific software and applying current practices of financial aid reporting; utilizes state-of-the-art approaches for calculations and data analyses of awarded amounts (both current and projected), scholarships, funding adjustments, awarding budgets, etc.; tests new, pertinent software releases and runs quantitative analyses as needed; and consults with immediate supervisor.
Please contact JoAnne Jones in Human Resources at 202/651-5352 for further information.

Job Requirements
Bachelor’s degree required in management, business administration, finance, accounting, management information systems, higher education or related field. Must have at least three years of experience in a similar type of financial aid related position with advanced knowledge of computer usage and financial aid technical applications and analyses. Demonstrated experience working with PeopleSoft or a similar software program and its Financial Aid related modules. Must be familiar with the Federal financial aid application (FAFSA) and aware of verification procedures. Knowledge of query language and of the Microsoft suite with proficiency in MS programs. Knowledge of web design (i.e., Ingeniux) and multi-media software helpful but not required. Must have in-depth knowledge of financial aid processing systems, knowledge of Federal and other higher education financial aid programs and the regulations governing those programs. Good interpersonal skills needed. Excellent analytic and organizational skills. Must be able to analyze data and provide complex reports and trend analyses. Ability to initiate projects, work and make decisions independently and collaboratively. This position requires self-direction and minimal supervision. Sign language skills or willingness to learn at time of employment. Deadline: November 19, 2010

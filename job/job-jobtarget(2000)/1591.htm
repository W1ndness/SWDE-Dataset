Mechanical Estimator

Montreal, Quebec

 

This multi-faceted role will see you prepare estimate plans and establish work breakdown structures for mechanical projects.

 

Responsibilities:

·         Prepare project estimate plans and the basis of estimates

·         Establish project work breakdown structures

·         Prepare capital cost estimates (mechanical) per industry classification for direct costs, indirect costs and other costs including escalation and contingency

·         Liaise with engineering, procurement and other disciplines to collect appropriate data for each type of estimate

·         Prepare quantity MTOs and/or check quantities provided

·         Develop labour rates and unit costs for commodities

·         Analyze estimates including life cycle costs

·         Evaluate quotations from equipment vendors

·         Estimate the cost of scope changes

·         Prepare tender check estimates

·         Assess contractor extra work and/or claims

·         Package line items in the estimate into commitment packages

·         Assist with project trending and prepare cost forecasts for input to the project cost report

·         Assist in cost control through the development of cash flow forecasts


Job Requirements

Requirements:

·         University degree in Engineering, or an equivalent combination of experience and education

·         Five to ten years of experience in a similar position

·         Membership in professional associations such as AACE International and/or PMI would be an asset

·         Proficiency with estimating software packages

·         Proficiency in a variety of estimating methods and techniques

·         General understanding of schedules

·         Fluency in French and English, both written and verbal

·         Demonstrated ability to contribute to a team and work effectively under pressure

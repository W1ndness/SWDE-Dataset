Required Education Level: Bachelor's Degree

As an Avionics Integration and Test engineer for the AH-1W platform, incumbent will report directly to task lead for AH-1W tasking, and work collaboratively as part of SOR team, to: estimate, plan, execute, and report on assigned SORs.

Essential Job Functions:

Integrate hardware/software avionics solutions into AH-1W aircraft from initial specifications, to final Fleet release and maintenance.

Develop integrated, sustainable, and high-quality H-1 products, that meet agreed upon requirements.

Support management planning by providing input, creating and/or reviewing plans (e.g., roadmap development, WSSA resource needs, SEMP, QA program).

Support program planning reviews, meetings and milestone events.

Lead and participate in H-1 WSSA SOR Team activities.

Coordinate with vendors, collect and refine requirements, and provide estimates.

Define system hardware installation, and software and operational requirements.

Ensure requirements traceability is maintained between design, installation, and test.

Manage and negotiate changes to requirements and design, and define and manage subsystem interfaces.

Oversee integration of subsystems to form the system.

Follow defined H-1 processes, and ensure product quality.

Communicate status, issues and internal or external product risks.

Provide Fleet support.

Estimate, plan and develop test requirements; and propose options for developing achievable test plans, procedures and events.

Required Skills:

Understanding of the systems engineering discipline and processes, and achievement of technical requirements and their applicability to H-1 WSSA projects.

Awareness of how today's decisions may impact outyear aircraft utilization, resources, configuration requirements and long-term stability of the H-1 aircraft.

Experience with military aircraft and their maintenance, hardware and software modifications, systems verification, and platforms utility. Prior success developing aricraft modifications, plans, procedures,and reports.

Desired Skills:

Organization and flexibility, as well as ability to work rapidly, accurately, and efficiently in a fast-paced engineering environment.

Ability to prioritize and manage concurrent tasks.

Ability to work independently, as well as with others. Understanding when collaboration and communication can result in better decisions.

A "can-do anything" attitude, and a get it done right; work ethic.

Competent using standard Microsoft products.

Excellent leadership, organizational and communication skills. Skills to promote teamwork and teambuilding throughout the organization.

Someone that thrives in an environment that values innovation, commitment and trust.

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.

The Agilaire Release Manager is responsible for the entire release of this new software suite of products, including configuration management, change management, release management and quality release control. This senior leader is responsible and accountable to insure all software modules/projects stay on track, projects that cross the organizational boundaries of Architects, Developers, and Deployment. The Release Manager works with the Product Owners to control the scope of the products for the release and with the customer Deployment engineers to control the scope of any customizations. They works with the ePMO to insure the reporting needs of the release are being met and they coordinate with the HP Account Team to insure any special work the account is undertaking for the customer adheres to the overall schedule.  Experienced in EDGE App Development processes especially Iterative and Agile Development. Strong understandihng of managing Sprints in Agile Environment.  Manages financials, staffing, status reporting,  experienced in resolving issues across multiple teams and managing  dependencies between projects. Leads customer engagement to ensure that it meets all scope, time, budget and quality expectations, through planning, controlling and managing customer projects.  Manages corporate, customer and third party vendor efforts to plan, sell and implement solutions to customer problems.  Responsible for business as well as team management

At NCCI Holdings, Inc., we realize our greatest asset is our team of more than 900 professionals working together every day to make our company the leading provider of workers compensation insurance data and research information. Read on to learn about the requirements for this opening.

Job Purpose:

Designs, integrates, and implements network, system and application level security. Defines standards to ensure the security of NCCI's information systems while meeting the needs of the business. Provides technical guidance to Development, Data Security and System Administration teams.

Major Duties:
Define and document information security principles, practices and associated metrics reporting.
Analyze existing security environment, design, and integrate new security-related technologies and tools.
Assist in training and mentoring less experienced Security Staff.
Represent NCCI as a Subject Matter Expert and lead security conversations in multi-disciplinary team settings of subject matter experts, vendors, and subcontractors.
Provide technical expertise in penetration test execution and system hardening.
Monitor security systems and define alert thresholds and remediation.


Job Requirements

Required Education, Experience & Skills:
Bachelors Degree in Computer Science or related field or equivalent work experience.
Six plus years data security experience (at least two years security database administration experience.)
Professional experience in several of the following areas is required: understanding of business security practices and procedures; knowledge of current security tools available; hardware/software security implementation; different communication protocols; encryption techniques/tools; familiarity with commercial products, and current internet technology.
Strong UNIX, Windows, Oracle & database security experience (Oracle and Oracle PL/SQL) and familiarity with web based applications. Ability to multi-task.
Organized, responsive and highly thorough problem solver. Ability to be creative and think out of the box.
Understanding of industry security best practices. Strong communication skills, both oral and written.
Knowledge of Oracle database security solutions, including Oracle E-business Solutions and encryption/data scrubbing. 
Knowledge of data exchange methods in general, including XML/SOAP and database replication.
(ISC)2 Certified Information Security Specialist Professional(CISSP)

Preferred Education, Experience & Skills:
Knowledge of other databases, including MS SQL and DB2 desirable



As key contributors to our success, our team members enjoy working in a casual, collaborative environment that offers state-of-the-art resources, advanced technologies, and a superior benefits package.

We require a drug screen and background check.

GENERAL SUMMARY: 

 

As the Sr Manager for Walmart Global eCommerce division, you’ll have the opportunity to help define and implement components for Walmart’s next-generation, global e-Commerce Platform. This hands-on position, reporting to the Director, Engineering at Walmart, will be responsible for design and implementation of backend application system components in a massively scalable, high performance, multi-tenant, international eCommerce platform in a Java EE and Oracle environment, working together with the Architects, Engineering Managers, Business and Product management teams. The position will serve as the technical lead for the distributed development teams working on this global platform.  Some of the key areas he/she will manage and contribute to will span Order Management systems, Inventory Mgmt, Fulfillment and Supply Chain. A wide degree of creativity and efficiency is required.

 

 

ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

Management Leadership (60%)

• Manage talented individuals to successfully deliver business initiatives.
• Partner with Product management to help define road map.
• Create a close working relationship with the business to identify the pain points and provide better services.
• Communicate effectively with stakeholders to manage expectations.
• Establish and manage working relationships with 3rd parties/vendors, to meet our schedules and quality standards
• Manage and develop the team, including hiring, goal setting, performance management, coaching, projects and training
• Work with team members to develop career paths and opportunities within the organization.
• Manage project timelines and budget.
• Assess training needs with team members, and develop training plans
• Understand and resolve issues and remove impediments that impact a team member’s ability to perform to his/her optimal best
• Foster growth and skill acquisition/development for his/her team

Technical Lead Back End (40%)


• Lead technical initiatives with complex interdependencies, high impact to the department or organization.
• Create vision and technology roadmap for the Back End: Order Management, Inventory, Supply Chain  areas.
• Expert problem solver.
• Responsible to see that various team deliverables for projects meet deadlines
• Participate in technical design review meetings
• Responsible for application/system, including timelines, dependencies and deliverables
• Develop effective monitors for the responsible systems
• Serve as final decision maker for technical project issues
• Be the point person for escalations
• Serve as the domain expert of the application/system. This is essential for on-call situations and escalations, as well as knowing the functionality and constraints the system when defining product specifications


Job Requirements

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

1) Knowledge, Skills, Abilities:

Functional Competencies:

·         Required:

·         Excellent system analysis and design skills

·         Solid hands-on technical understanding and grasp of supply chain systems as used in eCommerce solutions

·         Have developed a vendor/fulfillment partner integration product for high volume eCommerce sites

·         Knowledge of  Order Management, Inventory management applications/ EDI systems

·         Supply chain expertise and experience in integrating to 3rd party logistics providers/Drop ship vendors

·         Ability to manage development teams based in multiple, global locations

 

 

·         Preferred:

·         Experience with Retail eCommerce

·         Knowledge of queuing systems (JMS)

·         Experience working in a multi-tenant or “Software-as-a-Service” (SaaS) eCommerce company

·         Experience working in a multi-country eCommerce or technology implementation

·         Experience implementing service-oriented (SOA) architectures and EAI technologies like Tibco

·         Experience designing systems for extremely high traffic and transaction volumes

·         Experience in similar roles at prominent Internet companies, with detailed knowledge about scaling issues and solutions

 

·         Ability to work with business users, product managers, QA, Release, and UI and Platform Engineering teams to develop great e-commerce applications

·         Ability to work in an unstructured, fast-paced environment

 

Interpersonal Competencies:

·         Ability to communicate effectively to both technical and non-technical audiences, executive and technical, inside in the company and in our partners

·         Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal skills

·         Strong organizational and planning skills

·         Ability to anticipate potential problems and offer solutions

·         Timely and proactive communication skills

 

Leadership Competencies:

·         Persuade / influence team members, sell ideas effectively, get results and follow through, both within Wal-Mart and in our partners

·         Strong decision-making skills, and an understanding of where projects fit into development strategy

·         Demonstrate integrity and maturity, and a constructive approach to challenges

·         Operate independently given direction, set direction and bring ideas and solutions to issues raised

·         Foster and maintain an understanding of Wal-Mart's three core values:

·         Respect for the Individual

·         Service to our Customers

·         Strive for Excellence

 

2)  Minimum Educational Level:

3)  Experience:

8 to 10 years in overall software engineering design and development experience

 

4)  Physical Requirements:

·         25% need based travel to third-party partners, Wal-Mart locations worldwide etc.

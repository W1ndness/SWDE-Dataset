CLIA QA Manager / Project Manager

Asuragen

Austin, Texas, United States

This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7392969
Position Title: CLIA QA Manager / Project Manager
Job Function: Molecular - Diagnostics
Posted: November 19, 2010
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: http://www.asuragen.com
 

Key Duties:

·        Create and establish the infrastructure for an independent Quality Management System for the CLIA laboratory in the Pharmacogenomics Services Division.

·        Compliance oversight for all CLIA activities:  ensure compliance with all regulatory standards, systems, procedures and practices.  Includes ISO, GLP and other regulatory standards as applicable.

·        Oversees all controlled documentation creation, approval, modification and release.

·        Provides guidance to assist in the timely resolution of investigations, deviations and errors.

·        Oversees on and off site audit programs and training.

·        Provide support for Quality Agreements and technology transfer activities.

·        Oversee, direct and manage the Quality Assurance function, working with other members of the management team, to ensure highly disciplined compliance with all product and process quality parameters and standards including formulations, specifications, approved methods, procedures, practices and systems.

·        Provide strategic input to senior management.

·        Provide technical assistance to Research and Development in developing, testing and documenting new and improved products.

·        Provide support to Operations to continually improve product quality and line efficiencies.

·        Other duties as assigned.


Job Requirements

Minimum Education/Experience Requirements:

·        Ph.D or M.S. in relevant scientific area with a minimum of 5 years experience in a cGMP manufacturing facility.

·        Five years supervisory/management experience.

·        Medical Technologist, ASCP Certification, preferred.  

·        Excellent communication (oral and written), organization and leadership skills

·        Knowledge of cGMP, ISO, CE Marking, CLIA or other relevant skills of value in establishing internal and external quality systems and processes. 

·        Prior experience in dealing with FDA and other regulatory agency personnel and understanding of their requirements/expectations.

Preferable Skills and Attributes:

·        Skilled in the use of software programs such as Microsoft Office and database management programs.

·        Detail oriented and skilled at examining documents for accuracy and completeness.  Ability to prepare records in accordance with instructions.

·        Knowledgeable in QA principles and concepts in a dynamic manufacturing and testing facility as well as demonstrated knowledge in scientific principles.

·        Ability to set priorities and meet deadlines on a personal level and ability to work under tight deadlines in a team based environment.

·        Capability to effectively train employees on procedures, processes and QA concepts.

·        Team oriented individual with the ability to work effectively with multiple skill level employees and skilled at dispute resolution.

·        Excellent written and oral communication skills.

·        Excellent analytical and problem solving skills and the ability to work independently.

·        Knowledge of cGMP, GLP, CLIA, ISO, OSHA, CE, IVD required.

·        Previous Clinical Laboratory experience preferred.

Facility Manager - Critical Environment

Jones Lang LaSalle

New Jersey, New York, United States

This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7374512
Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: Facility Manager - Critical Environment
Min Education: Associates Degree
Industry: Facility Management
Posted: November 12, 2010
Job Function: Facility Operations & Maintenance
Min Experience: 5-7 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: http://www.us.am.joneslanglasalle.com/unitedstates...
 

We are seeking a Facility Manager to play a key role in a Critical Data Center environment in support of our client.

Primary responsibility is to support the efficient operations and maintenance of all assigned facilities including electrical, mechanical, structural, life safety, and critical back-up systems and to apprise the Senior Critical Facilities Manager of any and all conditions affecting building services and tenant comfort. The specific areas of responsibility include but are not limited to critical building services and contractor supervision and day-to-day operation of both the critical facility and administrative areas. 

? Support the Facility’s critical operations and other buildings operations, contract service and monitor performance of their assigned responsibilities. Directs the performance of assigned critical facility departmental service requests and other requests associate with the building(s), insuring that worked performed in all spaces are accomplished efficiently with a minimum amount of disruption.
? Understands the engineering design and operational aspects of the facility’s systems and equipment. Must be able to immediately recognize system shortcomings and respond to operational and emergency situations.
? Implements the preventative maintenance program.  Specific duties include but are not limited to: scheduling of preventative maintenance with a minimum disruption of building services, preventative maintenance tasks to the appropriately qualified maintenance source, orders parts and equipment required for repair, maintenance and installation of new equipment and facilities, and maintains inventory.
? Participates in facility remodeling review, new system construction, and other planning activities of the facility and monitors construction and repair projects.
? Insures the availability of critical spares inventory to operate the building. Performs periodic checks of inventory levels and equipment conditions.
? Assist with planning the budget allowing for appropriate control and utilization of clients resources. Recommends and estimates facility repairs and improvements for inclusion in the annual budget.
? Complies with departmental policy for the safe storage, usage and disposal of hazardous materials.   Maintains a clean and safe workspace.
? Performs other duties as assigned.  Serves as the day-to-day contact with the client to assure open and complete communication. 
? Interact with on-site client and landlord associated with both critical and non-critical aspects of the properties(s)


Job Requirements

? Education - Minimum of an associate’s degree in facilities management or equivalent of on the job experience directing and managing a facility engineering department.  Data Center engineering design and operating training helpful.
? Job Experience - 5+ years of experience in facility/plant engineering and maintenance supervision.  Managerial experience required.
? Strong electrical knowledge (UPS, Generator, DC power)
? Personal characteristics - Good interpersonal skills, ability to communicate well in both oral and written reports ability to work without direct supervision.
? Special knowledge and skills - Exposure to HVAC, fluid handling/pumping, fluid filtering, mechanical systems, welding, and electrical systems engineering and operation, back-up power design and data center design.
? Must be proficient in basic computer applications and software such as Microsoft Office, E-Mail etc.

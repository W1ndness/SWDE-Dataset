The Financial Analyst will assist in the development of our client's annual operating plan, forecasting, and financial analysis functions.  He/She will perform a variety of finance and accounting functions to support the maintenance of accurate financial records and financial reporting.

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:


  • Assists in the development of the company's annual operating budget.

  • Monitors budget performance compared to plan and alerts managers to potential issues.

  • Prepares mid-year budget re-forecasts.

  • Works with Sales & Operations Organizations by providing analytical support through sales forecasting, performance metrics, and ad hoc analyses.

  • Supports and administers appropriate Business Performance Management software packages.

  • Performs analyses evaluating corporate performance including sales, margin, and expenses as well as various operational analyses.

  • Analyze manufacturing operations performance.

  • Conducts customer and product line profitability as well as make-buy analyses.

  • Active member on cross-functional teams by providing the analytical support necessary to "drive" and "add value" to the business.

  • Identifies cost-saving and process improvement opportunities and recommends appropriate action steps to realize savings.

  • Works with Information Systems staff to establish corporate performance reporting mechanisms to track and report key financial metrics.

  • Maintains documentation to support internal and external audits.

  • Participates in internal and external audits - interacts with auditors and prepares reports.

  • Maintains a current knowledge of accounting practices and company operations.

  • Maintains a current knowledge of accounting operations.

Job Requirements

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree in accounting or finance with a minimum of three years related experience preferably in a manufacturing / distribution environment.
  • Knowledge of JD Edwards and E-1 preferred.
  • Strong organization and analytical skills.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.

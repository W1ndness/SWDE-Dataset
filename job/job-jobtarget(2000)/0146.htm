Security Benefit (SB), a leader in the education market, is seeking an Actuarial Assistant to work in its  Valuation Section within the Retail Retirement Line of Business.  This position will be based in Topeka, Kansas.  Security Benefit fosters strong partnerships to provide insightful and customized retirement solutions for employers and individual investors nationwide.  Through a broad advisor network and its nationally recognized money managers, Security Benefit provides mutual funds, annuities and retirement programs.  As an industry leader in service technology, our affiliates offer business processing and broker/dealer solutions tailored to the retail retirement marketplace. 

 

General Accountabilities:

Provides analysis and technical skills in support of fulfilling the strategic objectives for the Valuation Actuarial Department.  Contributes to the management and financial success of Retail Retirement by performing Statutory and GAAP valuations, analyzing inforce business and doing special projects.  Candidate must be able to successfully work with the Sales, Marketing, Finance, Legal and Product Development Departments.

 

Duties and Responsibilities:

 

  • Perform mathematical and financial calculations
  • Prepare actuarial exhibits and reports
  • Run models using actuarial software systems

Job Requirements

·         Bachelor’s degree with major or minor in Actuarial Science or Mathematics

·         Minimum of one SOA exam is required.  VEE credits would be a plus

·         Strong computer skills including Access, Excel and actuarial software

·         Good written and verbal communication skills

  • Strong problem solving, analytical and decision-making skills

·       Strong initiative and ability to work independently

·       Annuity (VA, FA, EIA) Financial reporting experience would be preferable

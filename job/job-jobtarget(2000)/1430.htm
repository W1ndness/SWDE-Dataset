PURPOSE OR OBJECTIVE:

  • Perform forensic and investigative audits pertaining to violations of company policies in accordance with established procedures. Report investigation findings through clear, concise and detailed reports including conclusions and recommendations for improvements in internal controls. Possess advanced knowledge of internal controls, accounting, business, or other technical areas.

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES:

  • Responsible for planning and execution of investigations including: collection of documentary evidence to support allegations, coordinate investigation activities with other Departments, and determine compliance with established policies and procedures. Identify and define fraud issues and root causes and provide effective recommendations and solutions.
  • Independently gather and analyze complex financial data, verify the accuracy of financial records, perform investigative research and present results in the form of written reports and/or oral presentations. The objective of the forensic analysis will be to determine if an offence has occurred, the methodology used to commit the offence, and the total financial loss to CP.
  • Prepare clear, concise and detailed investigation reports to support allegations including identifying additional findings outside the original scope of the investigation, and providing conclusions and recommendations for internal control improvements to prevent future reoccurrence of the improper activity. Reports will be of high quality and in compliance of CP and ACFE & IIA standards.

Performance Skills/Individual Competencies:

  • Ability to review and understand general business and financial documentation.
  • Ability to apply Company policies and procedures in connection with complex investigations.
  • Strong, proficient auditing and analytical skills; strong control mindset, confidentiality in all work and the ability to remain objective and independent.
  • Ability to interface with Management and work well in a team environment.
  • Possess excellent organizational skills with the ability to prioritize multiple tasks (must be efficient, flexible and able to adapt to change); demonstrated attention-to-detail skills with the ability to work independently and take initiative on department projects.
  • Possess excellent computer skills (Excel, Word, PowerPoint); experience with data mining software such as ACL would be an asset

Job Requirements

JOB REQUIREMENTS:

  • University degree plus professional designation (CA/CPA, CMA, CGA, CIA) and/or CFE; equivalent combination of education and work experience would also be considered.
  • 7 years or more of audit experience including experience involving fraud investigations.
  • Knowledge of investigation standards, methods, procedures and techniques are important. Experience in internal corporate investigations would be an asset.

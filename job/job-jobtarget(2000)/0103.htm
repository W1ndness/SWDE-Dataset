Position Accountabilities

  • Ensures accurate analysis and design of SAP MM/WM, in addition to related SAP and legacy intergration systems, that meet specifications and requirements of projects and overall business needs.   

  • Ensures accurate implementation of SAP applications and systems to support the environment that will provide up-to-date information to all areas and levels of the company.

  • Ensures timely completion of all assigned IS projects and deliverables within the project expectations.

  • Ensures consistently high quality results that meet business objectives.

    • Position Essential Functions

    • Ability to determine the business needs of application users and map to a process design document (flows and definitions).
    • Ability to design, configure, and troubleshoot SAP SCM objects.
    • Ability to design and test interfaces to SAP SCM.
    • Ability to plan and execute unit tests, integration tests, and user acceptance tests.
    • Ability to plan and execute SAP cutover tasks.
    • Ability to analyze potential gaps and recommend configuration and non-configuration solutions.
    • Ability to design and test workflows, reports, interfaces, customizations, enhancements, and forms (WRICEF) to meet user requirements.
    • Ability and experience analyzing enterprise software and make recommendations on best course of action.
    • Support data conversion activities.
    • Ability to speak English well.


    Job Requirements

    Position Specifications

    Educational Preference

    • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent industry experience
    • Advanced degree in Information Sciences or Business (MBA or MIS) preferred
    • 3+ years  minimum experience in SAP  SCM/APO configuration and programming
    • 3+ years minimum experience in SAP SCM/APO Demand Planning (DP)
      SAP SCM/APO Supply Network Planning (SNP), Supply Network Collaboration (SNC) and SAP R/3 SD/MM knowledge preferred
    • 3+ years minimum experience as a SAP Configuration Analyst during an initial SAP implementation, including maintenance and support post implementation
    • Experience with the following SCM configuration - Demand Planning & Forecasting   Safety Stock Planning, Statistical Forecasting,  Lifecycle Planning, Collaborative Demand Planning, Planning with Bills of Materials, Transfer of Consensus Demand Plan, and reporting, as well as, potential IS-AFS specific configuration
    • Functional Specification development for workflows, reports, interfaces, customizations, enhancements, and forms (WRICEF)

    Experience Preference

    • Business Process Procedure (BPP) development
    • Business Process Requirement (BPR) development
    • Integration to external partners and other internal systems
    • Integration test planning and execution
    • Cutover planning and execution
    • SAP IS Apparel Footwear Solution (AFS) experience preferred
    • SQL Server Environment experience preferred
    • SAP BI/BW specification development
    • Willingness to mentor junior staff
    • Strong conceptual, analytical, and problem-solving abilities
    • Problem solving/troubleshooting and resolution skills
    • Well-organized and able to prioritize and coordinate simultaneous tasks and projects while addressing customer needs
    • Strong interpersonal and communication skills
    • Project management experience helpful but not required

    Computer Proficiency Preference

    • Familiar with complex system languages and application development
    • ASAP Methodology for Software Development – preferred
    • Familiar with all Microsoft applications

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Who are we? An upstart team of UI designers and developers insanely focused on creating compelling new software and service products that integrate beautifully and meaningfully with a wide range of HP products that are loved by our tens of millions of customers.  We are the Consumer Applications and Services business in HP's Personal Systems Group. Our software and services are used by millions of people; on their PC's, mobile phones, and a wide range of other consumer products.
 
Who are you? A very experience UI designer, possibly a creative director who owns the overall design direction for a group of designers; possibly you are the design ninja known for delivering innovative UI for your company's compelling web product. You have been innovating UI for over a decade, and have a portfolio to back it up. Most importantly, you have ideas that were just not possible in your current role  ?  you needed more reach, more scale, more possibilities.
 
Why Move? You are ready for new challenges  on a grand scale. Something that allows a total experience only found with integrating hardware, software, and services into a killer user experience. You have a grander vision for what UI could mean in the next ten years but need a large canvas to see that vision realized.
 
How will we recognize you are the ideal candidate?
-  You are a leader who drives design strategies as a core member of business strategy teams
-  You excel at spreading "design thinking" across an organization, while educating non designers on the value of design.
-  You have a knack for interpreting customer, company and technology-focused research findings and delivering insightful and compelling design solutions.
-  You have a history of mentoring junior designers into design leaders. People want to work with you because they become better designers.
-  You are principled, and will fight for what you feel is the best design for the user.
-  You create stunning looking designs, yes visual design talent is important in this role!
-  You have been "bringing it" for 10+ years, with key UI innovations that were recognized in the industry and/or led to important UI patents for your company.
 
What will you be doing?
- Working on design strategy for the business
- Directing visual design for all our UI projects including our developing consumer UI design system.
- Directing design on software and services projects to ensure quality designs, proper use of the design system, and mentor designers in creating innovative UI's.
- Working with product teams to lead the creation of innovative concepts and prototypes.
- Driving new UI explorations based on key trends in design and UI technology.
- Push the boundaries of what is possible to create better experiences for users. Qualifications
Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Interactive Design or related field of study with a minimum of 10+ years related experience or equivalent.  Master's degree is a plus.
- Must have 8+ year's experience as key member of a design team participating in the complete product development lifecycle of a web product.
- Must have 5+ years experience leading large design efforts and coordinating project teams of 3 or more members.
- Must have 8+ years experience in interaction design for web and desktop applications, 5+ years in a lead role.
- Strong design portfolio in consumer software/web products which compete in the global marketplace
- Leadership experience inventing, driving and inspiring design.
- Demonstrated experience in designing and developing complete web applications, client software, and mobile interfaces.
- In-depth understanding and experience of user objectives, user scenarios, interaction/flow diagrams, design systems, usability principles, web applications framework, and the development of a product road map.
- Expert understanding of methodologies for designing easy-to-use interfaces.
- Ability to interpret user research and develop testable, user-centric designs is important.
- Extensive knowledge and experience in user-centered design processes.
- Leadership skills with cross-functional teams including engineering, product management, and user research.
- Expertise with tools such as Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator.
- Effective communicator, presenter, and negotiator.

Edifice is an information services company specializing in the collection, analysis and distribution of point-of-sale data used by retailers and suppliers to improve their supply chain efficiencies. Visit our website for a list of the great brands we support in growing their retailer and supplier businesses: www.edificeinfo.com

The Accountant will perform all day-to-day activities to keep the books and records current.  This includes processing daily cash receipts; vendor bills and disbursements; and customer invoicing.  The accountant will work closely with the Sr. Accountant and Controller to assist with analysis of company and departmental results and assist with account analyses.

Responsibilities:

·         Prepare and process all customer invoices; follow up with timely collection calls.

·         Coding, entry, and payment of vendor invoices.

·         Process daily cash receipts.

·         Prepare timely journal entries and account reconciliations to maintain adherence to the month-end close cycle.

·         Analyze financial data and prepare management reports, review and prepare bank reconciliations.

·         Assist with development, documentation and implementation of policies, procedures and internal controls.

·         Provide regular timely updates on work status to controller.

·         Perform special projects as needed.

·         Other responsibilities as assigned.


Job Requirements

Required qualifications:

·         Bachelors degree in accounting with minimum 3.0 GPA

·         1-2 years experience in private accounting with progressive accounting experience.

·         Solid understanding of GAAP and internal controls.

·         Experience in Daily cash Management, AP, AR, and the collection cycle.

·         Strong attention to detail, communication, analytical and organizational skills.

·         Self motivated with exceptional organizational, multitasking and written communication skills

·         Well-developed interpersonal skills to develop strong, positive relationships with clients, vendors and coworkers.  

·         Ability and desire to continuously learn and take direction from manager as well as work independently, and proceed with objectives without immediate supervision.

·         Skill to use a personal computer and various software packages, including QuickBooks, Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and outlook.

 


To Apply:

      Please submit your cover letter and resume, including salary requirements to EBaltruk@EdificeInfo.com

      No third parties, please.

Large Boston/Cambridge area PeopleSoft enterprise in the very early stages of upgrading their PeopleSoft HRIS systems to v 9.1, along with platform change from Sun Solaris to Linux based systems in a virtualized environment.

In this role you will be a key member of an innovative technology development team responsible for the technical analysis, development, and ongoing support of the HR Systems; a recruiting system, document imaging system, a training and development application and PeopleSoft HRMS (HR, Payroll, Benefits, Pension, Time & Labor, Absence Management).

Defines system requirements, conducts gap analysis, identifies feasible alternative solutions, and assists in the scope of modifications.

Responsibilities include detailed technical design, development, testing, deployment, and support of the PeopleSoft HRMS application.

Responsibilities include leading project teams of their peers in the requirements analysis, technical design and development new software modules and the enhancement of existing ones. The ideal candidate will contribute equally to the design and development of core architectural components, the development of PeopleSoft modules and the definition of back end database needs.

Strong technical development expertise with PeopleSoft HRIS along with an
ability to work closely with business owners to clarify project scope and specifications, including providing and evaluating alternative options where appropriate.

Must be flexible and able to work independently or in a team environment

Ability to independently translate business requirements analysis into technical design specifications and development. experience with full software lifecycle (requirements, analysis, design, construction and testing), strong analytical and problem solving skills


Job Requirements

Current Technical Experience to include:
• PeopleSoft HRMS Required, versions 9+ desired
• Good working knowledge of the PeopleSoft Internet Architecture, PeopleTools 8.49+; Weblogic and Tuxedo
• 3-5 years of programming experience with PeopleCode, Application Engine and SQR.
• 3-5 years experience SQL on any database (ORACLE, MSSQL)
• Development platforms: Windows, Unix and Linux.

Desired experience includes web applications development methodologies, project management, object oriented programming, HTML, XML, Java, JavaScript, PVCS, testing tools (i.e.Load Runner), and query tools.

Also knowledge of Internet protocols and standards, Internet software development standards, and web server configuration management.

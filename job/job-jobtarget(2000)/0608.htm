Regional Clinical Informatics Manager

Location(s): Chicago, IL

 

Adventist Health System an has immediate openings for a Regional Clinical Informatics Manager in Chicago, IL.

 

The Clinical Informatics Manager will coordinate regional clinical informatics and provide consulting services on clinical information management and processes for assigned hospitals.  This position will be based primarily in the greater Chicago, IL region. Occasional travel outside the region will be required.

 

 

Responsibilities:

 

  • Take a leadership role in Clinical Care Transformation, maximizing the benefits of information systems within the Adventist Health System IS Department.
  • Coordinates regional clinical informatics and provides consulting services on clinical information management and processes for assigned hospitals. 
  • Contributes to enterprise wide Clinical Informatics projects, providing supervision to Clinical Informatics Care Transformation Specialists working on project implementations in the region.

 Why work for us?

 

  • Computer World voted us one of the Top 100 best places to work in IT for 2009
  • We area development partner with Cerner.
  • We currently have 26 Hospitals on an integrated Cerner Millenium platform, fully implemented on 34 Cerner applications
  • We are a financially sound, growth company.
  • We have extraordinary support at the executive level for the growth and development of the EMR and other computer related projects
  • We choose projects that will move us toward maximum optimization of the EMR. We want our users to love the system!
  • We are a company of 500 employees and growing. Our employees love what they do
  • We have a strong team focus both within and across applications and/or departments of the company including the facilities.
  • We focus on customer service. We have a state of the art Help Desk program.

·         Finally, we have a Strong Faith Based Mission

Adventist Health System is the largest not-for-profit, Protestant healthcare organization in the US. Today, the organization provides 37 hospitals totaling more than 6,000 licensed beds, cares for nearly 4 million patients each year in inpatient, outpatient and emergency room visits, and employs almost 43,000 members of their communities in full and part-time positions.

When Seventh-day Adventist healthcare began, philosophy was translated into life-saving and life-enhancing service when mission was put into action. Today, our mission of extending the healing ministry of Christ depends not only on our commitment to Christian ideals but on our efforts to provide nothing less than extraordinary compassionate care.


Job Requirements

Requirements:

 

  • Minimum of 5 years of related clinical experience 
  • 3-5 years experience with information systems, clinical software, and other computer applications. 
  • Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills are required
  • Highly strategic, creative, and process oriented
  • Strong analytical and organizational skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative team environment
  • Ability to interface and influence at all organizational levels

 

Preferred:

 

  • Bachelor's Degree in Nursing or clinical informatics preferred
  • Prior experience with Cerner Millennium preferred

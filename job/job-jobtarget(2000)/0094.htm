Intermountain Healthcare needs one OB/GYN for expanding group in Orem and Provo, Utah.  Call:  1 in 4.  Physician will deliver primarily at Orem Community Hospital, with occasional deliveries at Utah Valley Regional Medical Center.  Employment position with the Intermountain Medical Group.  Guaranteed first year salary with transition to compensation based on productivity.  Full Intermountain Healthcare benefits.  Relocation provided.  EOE.

Orem Community Hospital is a 21 bed facility with inpatient services specializing in the safety, care and comfort of mothers and their newborns.  The hospital uses the “LDRP” model, and the 1500 mothers who choose Orem each year rate this hospital among the very top hospitals in Utah.  The surgery department is primarily outpatient, but does support GYN and other surgeries requiring some inpatient care.  

Information on Orem Community Hospital can be found at:  http://intermountainhealthcare.org/hospitals/orem/services/Pages/home.aspx.

Orem, along with its sister city of Provo, was recently voted as "the best place to live in America" by Money magazine.  It is a community of 89,517 located along the Wasatch Mountains in Utah County.  The area is known for its unabashed fertility - of its farmland, its inhabitants and its industrial development.  Orem is home to WordPerfect, one of the leading computer software industries, along with more than 80 other software companies. Unparalleled outdoor recreational and cultural events abound.  World-class skiing, summer theatre under the stars, and the eerie formations of Timpanogos Cave National monument are only samples of this region's varied attractions.  Orem is a family-oriented community combining a small town atmosphere with the advantages of a larger city and the stimulation of a university environment, yet with easy access to the beautiful outdoors and a major airport.

FREIGHT BROKER

***New Position***

 

Location: Chicago, IL – locals only – no relocations

 

About Our Client:  A financially strong, aggressive freight broker company who gets it!  You have the clients, the freight moves – no matter what!!

 

Job Summary: If you are an independent type of freight broker, then this is for you – basic freight broker TL/LTL – small company with big opportunities – endless, yes, endless commissions!  No restrictions!  You sell, the freight moves – period.

 


Job Requirements

Duties & Responsibilities – simply put:

  • Able to work independently
  • Grow the business
  • Type A personality – High energy
  • Manage Multiple Projects
  • Customer centric with a sense of urgency

 

Requirements, Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelors Degree preferred
  • Minimum 3 years experience selling within the freight forwarding industry
  • Potential 2 year customer following
  • Transportation software experience preferred
  • Excellent Negotiation skills
  • Computer literate with Microsoft Word, Excel & Outlook
  • Excellent written, oral and presentation skills

 

Benefits

  • Base salary + uncapped monthly commissions
  • 401K + medical benefits & PTO

 

 

 

For more information or to be considered, please submit your resume as Word document/attachment to:

Betsy Papamitrou – bpapamitrou@tylersearch.com

 

As a recruiter and Retail Logistics Professional, I specialize in Logistics & Supply Chain placements. Tyler Search Consultants is an Executive Search firm dedicated to the Global Trade community specializing in all levels of Supply Chain Management, International Logistics as well as Import/Export Compliance.

Job Description of an Architect

Ø  Will be part of the project core team living throughout the life cycle. Key architects/designers will be designated as part of the core team (the number may vary depending on the project need) and the rest will be ramped up and down during the life cycle.

Ø  The core architecture/design team members of a project will be part of the requirements phase, collecting/analyzing and clarifying requirements so that they will understand the business/functional/technical/non-functional aspects of the requirements

Ø  Will own the creation of architecture/design of the components required and will also own the maintenance of the architecture and architecture governance.

Ø   Will be part of change management to analyze the impact of changes, feasibility of implementing changes and estimating the impacts to project timeline and cost

Ø  Will be responsible for architecture/design validation through benchmarks, POC or other means for both functional and non-functional

Ø  Will be responsible for capacity planning or sizing of the deployment architecture.

Ø  Will be responsible for establishing architecture/design principles, establishing design/coding/interfacing or any other applicable standards that the components will be compliant with

Ø  Will be responsible for validating the products of the projects that it meets the principles, standards and architecture/design specifications etc...

Ø  Will have interaction with CTO/CIO and industry technology leaders or software providers and other IT services providers etc to the extent required to build the products of the project

Ø  Will own and deliver the required architecture/design related deliverables as per SLAs/Project plan/budget

Ø  Will own the technical quality of the product produced in the project

Ø  Will be responsible for maintaining reusability across AD projects and within the project

Ø  Will be responsible for maintaining a repository of the problem/solution, reference architectures etc

Ø  Architect along with the chief architect dedicated to the account will be responsible for maintaining the enterprise vide application/technology/COTS products/partner's products and identify white spaces, HCL's business growth opportunities, providing better solutions with this knowledge of the landscape, creating and formalizing value added projects, investments required from HCL for POC, concept creation, validation and acceptance to win the project.

Ø  Will gather, calibrate, analyze architecture/design phase metrics, rework related metrics against requirement changes and defects due to  incorrect architecture/design etc should be owned by the core team members from this competency

Ø  Will create/maintain and own the process definitions, templates, guidelines, tool selection, tool, implementation for CMMi process areas pertaining to architecture and design

Ø  Will develop proficiency in BAIT/APO and Asset transition methodology so that the architects can be part of the due diligence, transition or any pro-active solution implementation

Ø  Will prepare and maintain requirements traceability matrix, which is bidirectional  to be  used by all the ADM team members for components identification  and development

Ø  Will be part of Architecture/design competency center  that will develop technical skills at real depths so that  the resources enhance project quality, efficiency and CSAT goals

Ø  Will create Architecture/design documentation using HCL or customer mandated tools, templates. Baseline proficiency in system architect, Rational modeling tools is mandatory


Job Requirements

MS Technology Stack

C#, VB/.NET, ASP/.NET, .NET, BIZTalk, MS MQ, Web Services, ADO, ORM, Sharepoint, SQL Server, Active directory, WCF, WPF, WWF, Report server, XML parsing

Java Technology Stack

Java, SWING, JSP/Struts, Servlets, JSF, web portals, Spring, ORM, JCAPS, EJB, XML parsing, web containers, ejb containers, app servers

Information or Data

Data modeling, Financial Industry logical data models, DW/BI, Reporting, MDM, single customer view, data migration, database performance management, Information Life Cycle Management

Integration/BPM

Business process modeling and execution tools (Pega/TIBCO/Oracle/Lombardi), Financial Services domain process models, Integration styles, Web services, SOA, Connectors / Industry standard adapters

Infrastructure

Data center design, data center monitoring, performance management, capacity planning and sizing, data center security, cloud, grid, virtualization, load balancing, mirroring, clustering, configuration, administration and (Hardware and OS level) performance management

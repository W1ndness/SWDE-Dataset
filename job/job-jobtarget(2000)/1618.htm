This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7428043
Position Title: Application System Administrator, Blackboard
Industry: Education / Teaching / Administration
Posted: December 3, 2010
Job Function: Information Technology
Job Type: Full-Time

New York Institute of Technology (NYIT) offers undergraduate, graduate and professional degrees to approximately 15,000 students through seven academic schools, at campuses in New York, at a number of global sites and campuses, and online. NYIT is a leader in developing creative approaches to the technological and global challenges in higher education today.

NYIT seeks an Application System Administrator to monitor the performance and operation of all Blackboard related hardware, operating systems, databases and applications.  Under the direction of the Director of Systems & Networks, the successful candidate will document problems and create strategies for efficient repairs,  perform day-to-day system administration functions including backup, restore, user account administration, system troubleshooting and repair.  Candidate  will  also  monitor and tune Apache and Tomcat web servers for peak efficiency; report issues to Blackboard technical support and implement changes/fixes as recommended; participate in planning and implementation of upgrades and new software and hardware installations; coordinate and perform application upgrades and hot fixes; perform and/or automate data import and export procedures and work with data owners to ensure validity and integrity of transmissions and  assist with the development and implementation of disaster recovery plans and procedures.  Candidate will perform periodic course archive  procedures in accordance with data retention policies, perform routine Oracle database maintenance tasks such as analyzing statistics, correcting data errors, rebuilding indexes, performance tuning and writing backup scripts, maintain separate production and test environments and  perform periodic refreshes of the production data to test systems.  

 


Job Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in a related field and a minimum of five years of relevant experience required.  Demonstrated proficiency with Oracle RDBMS and Apache/Tomcat and knowledge of Unix operating systems (Solaris, Linux) also required.    Prior hands-on experience with Blackboard applications preferred.   Demonstrated project management experience, ability to handle multiple projects and deadlines, as well as interact effectively with a wide variety of users at all organizational and technical levels.    Previous experience in a higher education environment is strongly desired.   Demonstrated proactive approach and self-initiative is a strong plus.   Excellent decision making and problem solving skills and effectiveness in getting things done collaboratively.   Must be available to work holidays, weekends and evenings and provide on-call coverage after   business hours on a routine rotation basis and for special projects.  Excellent oral and written communication skills and presentation skills and ability to work independently are required.    We offer a competitive benefits package (at no cost to the employee) including tuition remission, along with a professional environment designed to enhance your career development.  For immediate consideration please submit resume to:  Judith Roback, Human Resources Associates at jroback@nyit.edu . EOE.

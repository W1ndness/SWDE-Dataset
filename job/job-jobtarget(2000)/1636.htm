HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Hewlett Packard (HP) is the largest IT Company on the planet (over 100B) USD revenue per annum), with a broad product portfolio that spans printing,personal computing, software, services and IT infrastructure.  HP Supply chain IT is currently expanding our logistics Practice and is looking for seasoned professionals with a proven track record of successful OTM TMS implementations, with experience in full Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) activities. 
 
The Position:
  • Hewlett Packard is seeking people across the United States who can provide immediate support to our current needs and also assist in transforming our supply chain IT landscape. 
  • The right individual will be a self-starter, who can translate their intellectual vision into successful OTM Transportation management solutions for HP's internal supply chain
  • Individuals will be required to lead implementing practical solutions that match the strategic thought leadership within HP Supply chain IT
  • Successful candidates will be required to provide Oracle TMS expertise to a variety of business challenges for the end to end transportation lifecycle.
 
We are interested in candidates with the following experience:
  • Demonstrable OTM TMS design and implementation experience. OTM package experience is preferred but candidates with comparable experience on other TMS packages like i2, Manhattan etc will also be considered.
  • Should have been part of at least 2 full lifecycle implementations for U.S Domestic/International/Multi modal transportation.
  • Five plus years of hands-on functional experience with some or all of the following OTM TMS offerings: Ready to Ship, Load planning, Freight Charge Management, Execution and Visibility, Multi Modal Planning.
  • Technical understanding of OTM platform including workflow agents or other technologies like Java, XML, SOA etc.
  • Strong and proven experience providing similar services to Fortune 500 companies
  • The ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • A creative approach to problem solving and thought leadership, with the ability to create project artifacts and associated SDLC collateral with minimal guidance
  • Proven communication skills, both written and oral
  • Capable of working with multiple business units at all levels 
  • The ability to meet deadlines and business unit expectations
  • Willingness to travel as required with relocation for long-term assignments an option

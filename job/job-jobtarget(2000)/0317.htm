At Ritter Communications, we have over a century-long tradition of providing quality residential and business communication services all across Arkansas.

A pioneer in the telecommunications industry, we offer the latest communication innovations for both your home and your business including local and long-distance telephone, customized business systems, internet and even premium cable service. We have experienced tremendous growth and success, due in large part to an innovative lineup of residential and commercial communications products and services, and an unsurpassed commitment to excellent customer service. To support our continuing efforts, we are currently seeking an experienced Maintenance Technician.

SUMMARY

This position is responsible for preventative maintenance, troubleshooting, alignment, testing and repair of the HFC plant.  Responsibilities to support all services including analog, digital, fiber and other communications mediums. Required to work on-call.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned. 

  • 1. Performs system sweeping, alignment, testing, troubleshooting and repair of the HFC RF two way plant.
  • 2. Produces and maintains documentation and records of all activities and testing as required.
  • 3. Participates in regularly scheduled and emergency on-call duty beyond normal working hours as required. Position may require non-standard working hours on a regular basis in order to perform duties at hours that are least disruptive to customers.
  • 4. Maintains thorough and current proficiency of FCC performance specifications and can, without supervision initiate and complete comprehensive testing including (but not limited to) all FCC required or recommended tests and measurements.
  • 5. Utilize computer software and test equipment for variety of measurements including: RF levels, signal leakage, signal to noise, carrier to noise, low frequency disturbances and composite triple beat.
  • 6. Perform proactive and reactive monitoring and maintenance of HFC power Supplies.
  • 7. Perform general construction duties to include splicing and activation of new cable plant and emergency restoration of the communication network.
  • 8. Maintains all equipment assigned in proper working order including regular calibration, repair, adjustment and alignment.
  • 9. Test and maintain backup powering systems- including generators, uninterruptible power supplies, and temporary feeds.
  • 10. Utilize optical time domain reflectometer to perform splice acceptance tests as well as fault location.
  • 11. Proficient with software programs for storage and archival of test equipment results.
  • 12. Performs maintenance by climbing on ladders, power/telephone poles and other structures, lifting and carrying up to 75 pounds, digging, crawling, stooping and standing. Works outside throughout the year. Works in various weather conditions, which may be extreme hot or cold conditions.
  • 13. Inspects, maintains and uses proper safety equipment issued in compliance with OSHA and Company safety regulations.
  • 14. Compliance with system operating specifications as well as applicable governmental regulations.
  • 15. Ability to keep to schedule with minimum supervision.
  • 16. Performs all the job requirements of all the technical personnel.

Job Requirements

EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE

High School Diploma or recognized equivalent.  Minimum of five (5) years of directly related experience and/or successful completion of industry related course studies.

Prior successful completion or ability to successfully complete within a given timeframe the NCTI “Test and Measurement” course.

The following certifications are preferred:

            NCTI Service Technician, NCTI System Technician and SCTE Broadband Technician.

PHYSICAL DEMANDS

The employee is occasionally required to climb or balance and stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl.  The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 70 pounds.  Specific vision abilities required by this job include ability to adjust focus and color vision.

While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently exposed to outside weather conditions.  The employee is occasionally exposed to high, precarious places and risk of electrical shock. 

 

OTHER

Must possess a valid driver's license and a satisfactory driving record as determined by the Company.

Ability to safely operate aerial lift vehicles

JOIN ÁEGIS LIVING, A NATIONAL LEADING PROVIDER OF SENIOR CARE, AS A SALES DIRECTOR!  FINALLY – THIS IS A JOB YOU CAN LOVE!

 

We are currently hiring for the position of SALES MANAGER for our ÁEGIS of SAN FRANCISCO community.

Áegis Living is a leader in the senior living industry and we invite you to join our success!  We were founded in 1997 by Chairman and CEO Dwayne Clark; we now have 35 communities in Washington, California, and Nevada.  The Áegis vision is to be the standard by which all great companies are measured, with delighted employees, a passionate culture, and sustained profitability.  We are continuously recognized as a leader in our field by prestigious organizations.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Work directly with the Executive Driector and Corporate Sales Team to develop, execute and enhance Sales strategy for our San Francisco premier assisted living community.

Develop and implement all aspects of marketing plan including advertising, community out reach, sales, and special events for community.

Monitor market competition, market conditions, and competitive products/services in the area.

Drives and supports the marketing communications and community relations programs, including but not limited to: trade shows, special events, seminars and charitable associations.

Ability to facilitate, support and develop a win/win situation utilizing the cluster concept with other communities to most effectively position Aegis.

 

 


Job Requirements

 QUALIFICATIONS:

 5+ years of sales experience required.

 

 Hospitality or Healthcare sales experience preferred. 

 

 Bachelors Degree preferred.

 

 Solid track record of consistently exceeding sales goals. 
 

Experience with revenue management and development of pricing strategies to maximize revenue.

Solid marketing background in the areas of advertising, community relations, special events and networking.

Exceptional oral communication and presentation skills, with the proven ability to effectively communicate the company's vision, competitive advantage and strategies.

Strong leadership skills and proven ability to work successfully with marketing, operations and cross-functional teams.

Drives operational excellence through the organization.

Experience using competitive analysis tools, understand what the data means and the ability to turn the information into productive action plans and solutions. 

Must be willing to learn and adapt to the Aegis marketing matrix and marketing approach and use existing software to develop reports and marketing plans.

Superior customer service orientation.

 Áegis is committed to attracting the very best employees. By “best” we mean the most caring and compassionate; the most professional and attentive; the most responsive to our residents' needs; and the most universally committed to making a difference in every community we serve.

We are proud to offer an excellent salary and benefits, great work environment and an enjoyable work/life balance.  Please visit our website at www.aegisliving.com for additional information.

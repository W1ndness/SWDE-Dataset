Berean Group is seeking a Senior SAP Business One Consultant

Job location: Greater Nashville Area

Duration: Full time

Citizenship / Work Authorization: Must be US Citizen or Perm Resident Only

 

Job Description

·         We are looking for a talented, enthusiastic and versatile Certified SAP Business One Consultant who is capable of dealing efficiently with all stages of the project life cycle including pre-sales consultancy, requirement definition analysis, project definition analysis, proof of concept, installation, configuration, training, data migration and go-live.

Skills

·         The successful candidate will have a proven track record in the full project life cycle of SAP Business One for SME's and will have a successful background within a consultancy or software house.

·         An accounting background or qualification such as CPA, MBA would also be an advantage; you will need to have a strong understanding of financials to P&L and Balance Sheet.

·         Experience with ISV’s in the following:

o    EDI, Shipping, WMS, Project Accounting, Crystal Reports

·         You will be joining an established team providing a full range of services to SME's around the SAP Business One product.

·         You will be gathering and analyzing SAP B1 business and functional requirements, implementing and optimizing SAP Business One solutions, designing system testing, identifying risk, and presenting regular project updates to the client throughout the project life cycle.

·         A lot of your work will be based on client site, but you need to be within reasonable commuting distance from Nashville, TN.

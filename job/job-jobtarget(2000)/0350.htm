Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: HR Generalist
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Industry: Legal

Job Function: HR Generalist
Min Experience: 1-2 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Contact Person: Tamara Fairhurst
Apply URL: http://selfapply.nixonpeabody.com

This position reports to the Regional HR Director.  A dynamic and energetic self-starter, dependable and highly motivated with solid problem-solving and follow-through skills sought to provide overall coordination of HR and recruiting programs for the San Francisco, Palo Alto, Los Angeles and China offices.   Flexibility for additional hours on weeknights and weekends as necessary.

Responsibilities

Coordinate regional lateral attorney, summer associate, and staff hiring

Update recruiting database and review screened resumes

Liaison with search firms, career services personnel, and attorneys/hiring managers, schedule and interview candidates

Coordinate regional recruiting committee meetings and prepare agendas

Coordinate regional summer associate programs

Assist with regional on-campus interviewing

Support new hire integration, host orientation programs, and process new hire paperwork

Support evaluation processes

Assist with implementation of compensation and bonus programs

Assist with professional development and recruiting programs, as needed

Organize and/or attend diversity outreach initiatives

Promote positive employee relations, monitor and ensure compliance with firm policies

Participate in counseling and discipline to resolve personnel matters

Coordinate departure process

Contribute to firm-wide HR initiatives by working on special projects as needed

Participate in ONE HR meetings as required

Participate in recruiting meetings as required

Participate in local and regional committees

Participate in external local and regional legal recruiting and HR organizations to network and raise firm profile


 


Job Requirements

Bachelor’s degree and minimum of two years legal recruiting experience in a law firm required

Human resources and employment interviewing experience strongly preferred

Knowledge of federal, state and local labor laws

Proficiency in Windows-based software applications including Microsoft Office Suite, document management systems, HRIS, and recruiting software preferred

Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills

Strong organizational and project management skills with ability to work in a fast-paced environment, managing multiple priorities and competing demands

Client-driven team player with strong customer service skills and strong attention to detail

To apply for this position, please visit www.nixonpeabody.com/careers.asp or go to http://selfapply.nixonpeabody.com.

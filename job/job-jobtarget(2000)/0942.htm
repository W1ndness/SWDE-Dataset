Mxi Technologies is seeking Solution Analysts to join our Ottawa location. You will work as part of a project team within our professional Services division to provide business and systems knowledge in the design, development, and testing of new Maintenix system implementations as well as the enhancement of existing systems.  You will contribute to the detailed design of a Maintenix-based solution for a given client by working under the direction of a Senior Solution Analyst or Architect.  In this customer-facing role, you will conduct analysis of client business needs and translate them to system requirements.  You will also ensure the efficient configuration of systems and provide production support and system training support to client implementations.  You will plan and execute testing to validate designs and implementations.  In addition, you'll contribute directly to our internal business improvement initiatives.


Job Requirements
  • University degree in engineering or computer science or an applicable education in aviation maintenance and operations, or equivalent
  • 1-2 years of business modeling experience (information and data flow) considered an asset
  • Proficiency in MS Office (i.e. MS Excel, MS Visio, MS PowerPoint, etc.)
  • Understanding of best practices in the software development life cycle, business and systems analysis supported by business process and data modeling; awareness of technology trends and leading practices
  • SQL programming experience considered an strong asset
  • Ability to thrive in an environment of pressing deadlines and constantly changing conditions
  • Experience working in a project team environment
  • Excellent interpersonal relations and demonstrated ability to work with others effectively in teams
  • Oracle database experience considered an asset
  • Previous experience in a customer-facing role considered an asset
  • Must be willing to travel globally, as required 

To Apply:

Please send your resume in either a Word or PDF format to careers@mxi.com.  Please be sure that the subject line clearly indicates which position you are applying for.

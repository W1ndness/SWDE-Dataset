HEC Montréal is recognized as a world leader in business education. HEC professors maintain high standards of excellence both in their teaching and in their research. The school offers undergraduate programs, as well as Graduate diplomas, MBA, EMBA, Master's degree and Ph. D. in Administration. The main language of instruction at HEC is French; however, there are also programs and courses offered in English and in Spanish.

 

The Accounting Studies Department seeks to fill one faculty position at the rank of Associate or Full Professor, to begin June 1, 2011.


Job Requirements

Candidates must have a Ph.D. in Accounting or a closely relevant discipline and substantial years of experience in teaching and research. Applicants specialized in the area of financial accounting, auditing or taxation will be given priority. Teaching excellence is required, as well as a strong aptitude for research and a marked interest for publication. They must demonstrate an ability to communicate specialized knowledge both at public conferences and in writing. Recent conferences as well as a track record of publications in top-tier journals will attest to this ability.  Finally, candidates must have a very good knowledge of technological environments and of software applications commonly used in business.  

 

Candidates are invited to submit their curriculum vitæ with a covering letter, specifying citizenship, before December 1, 2010.

HEC Montréal is committed to Employment equity and encourages applications from women, Aboriginal peoples, visible and ethnic minorities and person with disabilities. All qualified candidates are encouraged to apply; however Canadians and permanent residents will be given priority.

 

We thank all applicants for their interest. However, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

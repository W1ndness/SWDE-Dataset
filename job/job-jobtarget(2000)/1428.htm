Data Conversion Analyst (Ottawa and Calgary)

Overview of Role:

MD’s Data Conversion Analysts play a critical role in ensuring the integrity of electronic medical records (EMR) through the challenging process of migrating detailed and highly sensitive data from legacy systems to MD Physician Service’s top class Practice Solutions Suite.  Data integrity is at the top of mind for physician’s managing busy practices and who are passionate about providing the best care possible to their patients. 

At MD we want individuals on our team who are equally passionate about their contribution to this goal.  This means more than just individuals with technical skills; equally important to us is drive and commitment to quality and an understanding that each piece of data has a person’s health attached to it.  Data Conversion Analysts have a relentless approach to finding problems and robust solutions to ensure scalpel precision in the information physicians rely upon.

You should bring tremendous pride in your ability to root out issues in XML data that has been extracted from previous systems in ORACLE databases, and your ability to clearly and concisely articulate issues through a disciplined documentation processes and verbal status updates.  We want more than a data monkey!  We need a team player, with energy and drive to analyze large data files on an iterative basis to ensure the highest quality migration possible. 


Summary of Duties and Responsibilities:

·       Under the direction of the Data Migration Manager, the Data Conversion Analyst will be part of a team migrating EMR from legacy systems to the PS Suite. 

·       The Data Conversion Analyst will take data from XML based extracts received from clinics and using a proprietary data tool, import these records into MD’s software. 

·       Individuals will analyze the data to detect malformed XML data, investigate root causes of data issues, document these issues and prepare Data Assessment Reports.  This process will continue on an iterative basis using trial extracts until data integrity is assured. 

·       The Data Conversion Analyst continues to support the clinic through production import with ongoing issue tracking and analysis until final handoff to the MD Service Support Team. 

·       Throughout each migration, the Data Conversion Analyst will thoroughly document all issues and their resolution, as well as participate in team status updates.

Education & Experience:

·        Must have a strong understanding of XML-based data, including an ability to analyze and track issues during the import process

·        Have experience in identifying issues and making appropriate corrections within the conventions of solid XML formats

·        Demonstrated ability to produce detailed documentation in tracking of incidents, including examples of issues, processes followed, intentions and work done

·        Have strong organizational skills, including prioritizing work with logic and efficiency, personal time management to work through tight deadlines and to work in synch with project timelines, and written and mental maps of work completed, under way and to be done.

·        experience with standardized XML databases such as COPD and large volume data sets

·        University or college degree in information systems

·        At least 2 years of related work experience working with XML data

·        Exceptionally strong verbal and written communication skills as it relates to clarity in describing data issues and resolutions

·        Works well with a team but is also self-motivated with particular strengths in delivering quality results

This is a tremendous opportunity for individuals early in their careers or those seeking to make a career change into the high growth healthcare sector and EMR business.  Every MD employee feels a sense of pride that we are making a contribution to the quality and improvement of healthcare with innovative solutions our clients need and our competitors can’t match.  MD offers competitive compensation, generous benefits and a genuine commitment to recognizing people through reward and incentive programs.  Employees are encouraged to seek out opportunities for personal growth and development and are supported in career-long learning through diverse training opportunities and a tuition reimbursement program which cultivate and promote progression.

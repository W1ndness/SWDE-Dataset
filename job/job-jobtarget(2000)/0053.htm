HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Hewlett Packard (HP) is the largest IT Company on the planet (over 100B) USD revenue per annum), with a broad product portfolio that spans printing, personal computing, software, services and IT infrastructure.  HP Supply chain IT is currently expanding our logistics practice and is looking for seasoned professionals with a proven track record in the areas of data analysis, master data management and data quality in support of an end to end supply chain data flow, with experience in full Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) activities.
Why join the team at HP Supply chain IT:
?         We are recognized as a global leader in our sector
?         We provide business critical services to our business units and present an excellent opportunity for growth for the right professionals
?         We provide excellent growth opportunities for the right professionals and the ability to achieve new insights and learning opportunities while being part of a world class organization
The position:
Hewlett Packard is seeking people across the United States who can provide immediate support to our current needs and also assist in transforming our supply chain IT landscape. 
The right individual will be a self-starter, who can translate their intellectual vision into successful strategic and tactical solutions for HP's internal supply chain
Successful candidates will be required to provide data analytics expertise to a variety of business challenges for the end to end transportation lifecycle.
 Professional competencies:
?                    Leadership skills
?                    Customer service
?                    Team player
?                    Good interpersonal skills
?                    Well organized, process oriented
?                    Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines
?                    Good communication skills
Essential Day-to-Day Responsibilities:
?                    Setup data quality KPIs and reporting
?                    Proactively monitor data quality levels
?                    Adjust data quality reporting as required
?                    Bringing discovered data issues to the attention of the relevant business owner
?                    Regular submission of data quality reports to the business data owners
?                    Adhering to Master Data principles and other relevant policies
?                    Maintaining expert knowledge on the data quality tool and processes
?                    Assists Data Stewards in defining desired data quality levels
?                    Reports on the data quality levels to the individual stakeholders in the Data Governance Model
?                    Assist Data Stewards in assessing the impact of changes to data (caused e.g. by changes to the IT systems or changes to the meaning of data)
?                    Develop data quality analysis reporting
?                    Perform data profiling and IT system analysis
?                    Advise on data cleanse, harmonization and standardization efforts
?                    Ensure business rules from the BDM are adhered to in all manipulation/movement of data
?                    Update data quality metrics on a day-by-day basis Educational:
?                    Typically a technical Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience and a minimum of 9 years or greater of demonstrable related and relevant experience
Required Skills:
?                    Strong and proven experience providing similar services to Fortune 500 companies
?                    Ability to work with numbers, statistics, mathematical background
?                    Analytical mind which is able to process information logically
?                    Analysis of information using various statistical methods, trying to see patterns in data
?                    Good knowledge of computer hardware and software and ability to learn about new software
?                    Proficient in using analytical tools and instruments for instance Excel, Microsoft Access, SQL, JMP, SAS, experience with Informatica data quality toolset or similar products
?                    SQL skills and experience of working with relational (Oracle) and non-relational databases
?                    Large IT landscape experience including: SAP, Siebel, Oracle OMS
?                    Understanding of how integrated systems work with integration layers
?                    Data Quality Management skills (theory and technology)
?                    Excellent communication and negotiation skills
?                    Ability to control scope and negotiate where necessary
?                    Ability to work in a team and support less experienced members of the team
Required advanced skills:
?         Ability to create and program manage UAT
?         Ability to program manage issues and resolutions
?         PMI preferred but not required
?         Able to work with and mentor college hires
?         Prepare presentations for senior managers
Desired skills:
?         Previous data quality analysis in a supply chain domain (ERP, SAP, Oracle OTM, Manhattan WMS)
?         Background in Data Architecture, Data Integration and Data Modeling
?         Background in Quality Assurance, Testing, Testing methodologies

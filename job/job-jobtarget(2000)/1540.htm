Position Title: Customer Account Specialist
Industry: Manufacturing
Posted: December 2, 2010
Job Function: Customer Service
Job Type: Full-Time

As IDEX Health and Science - Middleboro, we are home of Sapphire Engineering and Isolation Technology products serving analytical instruments, biotech, and IVD markets. We recently moved into our newbest-of-class 80,000 square foot facility in Middleborough, MA, near the 495/44 intersection. We are part of the Northbrook, IL based IDEX Corporation, a $1.4 billion applied solutions company with nearly 5000 employees and operations on six continents. Together we serve a highly diverse set of customers who look to our company to provide applied engineered fluidic and mechanical solutions to our exacting specifications, anywhere in the world. Although ours are the recognized brand names of the industrial world, we produce hundreds of products that touch people's lives every day.

The Customer Account Specialist will be the customer advocate for all clients to which they are assigned.  The successful incumbent will be a highly motivated, service oriented, and customer service driven member of the sales team, responsible for ensuring that the needs of the customer and the business are proactively addressed at all phases of the product sale, delivery and service process.  Support first class customer service. Success in this role will be marked by enhanced customer retention and repeated orders.

  The Customer Account Specialist is responsible for owning and managing all aspects of customer orders.  This includes, but is not limited to, booking orders, managing customer change order requests, and setting customer expectations regarding anticipated shipment dates.  

Ability to review customer forecast information and identifying when to execute pro-actively to support the customer and business is essential.

The Specialist will set accurate customer expectations.  This demands that the commitments made by the Customer Account Specialist be based upon the accuracy of the pricing, terms, and conditions as defined by the Sapphire Engineering quotation system.   The ideal candidate will have a technical aptitude which will assist in working with customer requests.

The Customer Account Specialist is expected to build an alliance with and influence internal resources in other departments.  It will be necessary to communicate cross functionally when addressing price administration, order entry, inventory management, logistics, post shipment service and RMA's.  Additionally, regular attendance and participation at manufacturing meetings is necessary in order to highlight any delivery or inventory issues that will affect the customer deliverables.  The Customer Account Specialist must be the voice of the customer.    

The Customer Account Specialist will respond to all customer requests and resolve customer issues in a timely manner, analyze customer's Material Resource Planning (MRP) schedules, and monitor demand flow of inventory.  In addition, they will communicate with customers via electronic means as well as interface directly.  All communication methods (written, verbal and presentations) must be clear, concise and unambiguous.

This position will continually assess and improve upon the level of service provided to our customers.  New service techniques and/or tools that will continually improve our customer relationships and processes, should be investigated.

ESSENTIAL INTERPERSONAL JOB FUNCTIONS

Success in this position requires frequent communication with peers and management.  These relationships must be built on respect, integrity, cooperation, teamwork and professionalism.  Failure to actively maintain and encourage these constructive relationships will significantly impact success in this position, just as equally as weak technical skills.

Must be able to willingly shift job priorities, based upon customer (internal/external) needs.

Ensure individual accountability for meeting individual quality standards and schedule commitments.


Job Requirements

Associates Degree in a related field, or a combination of academic proficiency and directly related work experience.

At least one year of client service facilitation in a high tech or sales administration environment.  Proven and documented experience as a proactive customer advocate is essential.

Knowledge of the life science industry is a plus.

Familiar with ISO standards and Quality manual adherence practices is preferred.

Prior use of CRM and/or ERP software application. JD Edwards is preferred

Proven excellent communication (written and verbal), and listening skills are essential.  This experience should be supported by writing samples. 

Detail oriented work style is required.

Candidates must also be proficient with MS Office products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint and Outlook).

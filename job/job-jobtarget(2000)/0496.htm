CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER – NEW POSITION

 

Excellent opportunity for a dynamic experienced COO to join the management team of HCC Specialty Group (HCCS) in the Mount Kisco, NY office.  HCCS will be the newly formed division of HCC Insurance Holdings, Inc. ("HCC Insurance"), comprised of the eventual combined entities HCC Specialty Underwriters, Inc. (HCCSU) (www.hccsu.com) and Professional Indemnity Agency, Inc. (PIA) (www.piaint.com).   The combined entity projects to finish 2010 with $500,000,000 of gross written premium.

 

HCC Specialty Group will be a division of HCC Insurance with its common stock trading on the NYSE (symbol:  HCC) and is a leading international specialty insurance group with offices across the United States and in the United Kingdom, Spain and Ireland.  As of June 30, 2010, HCC had assets of $9.0 billion and shareholders’ equity of $3.2 billion.  HCC’s major domestic and international insurance companies have a financial strength rating of “AA (Very Strong)” by Standard & Poor’s Corporation.  HCC’s major domestic insurance companies have a financial strength rating of “AA (Very Strong)” from Fitch Ratings, “A1 (Good Security)” from Moody’s Investors Service, Inc., and “A+ (Superior)” by A.M. Best Company, Inc.

 

HCCS underwrites a unique portfolio of specialty and niche products.  These include:  disability insurance for professional athletes and entertainers, kidnap & ransom, event cancellation for major sporting and entertainment events, earthquake insurance, special event liability, weather insurance, prize indemnity insurance, product recall insurance, various professional liability classes (including but not limited to employment practice liability and architects & engineers), various niche programs including public risk and lumber/loggings.

 

This position is based in Mount Kisco, NY, but will require frequent travel to HCCS locations which include, San Diego, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Phoenix, Auburn Hills, MI, Richmond, VA, Boston and London.  The COO will report to the President who is based in our Wakefield, MA office.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

This full-time position will be responsible for the Company’s day-to-day operating activities, including financial review, operations, information technology, compliance, revenue and sales growth; expense, cost and margin control; and monthly, quarterly and annual financial goal management.  In addition candidate will:

 

  • Work with President and CEO to develop Group’s growth strategy, which includes identifying and acquiring new lines and businesses.
  • Partner closely with Group’s management team to accomplish the short and long-term goals established within the Group’s strategic plan.
  • Undertake direct management of staff reporting directly to him/her, delegating responsibility where appropriate.
  • Improve communication between operational and underwriting department leaders in various locations.
  • Work with management team to improve/enhance financial and operating systems.
  • Work with branch locations to ensure they are successfully running all aspects of their business, while conforming to the Group’s policies and procedures.
  • Cross sell between all divisions within HCC Specialty Group and other HCC entity products.
  • Work with all HCC Insurance subsidiaries’ business development teams to expand into new territories, including international territories.
  • Other duties as assigned by President.

 


Job Requirements

QUALIFICATIONS:

Minimum Training and Experience:

  • B.S. or Masters Degree in Business Administration, CPA would be a plus
  • Prefer candidate to have insurance industry experience and background
  • Minimum of 10 years of relevant work experience, including at least 5 years of senior leadership experience (CFO, COO preferred) for a growth oriented company   
  • Experience with financial reporting, budgeting and systems  
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook), Internet applications and financial software packages
  • Must be self-motivated and able to work well with a team or individually

Skilled in:

  • Coaching and Mentoring
  • Change Management
  • Strategic Planning
  • Effective and professional oral and written communication, with the ability to communicate and interact with people at all levels

Ability to:

  • Demonstrate enthusiasm, unity and teamwork throughout the organization
  • Lead, motivate and develop teams
  • Develop and deliver effective presentations
  • Manage multiple priorities in an organized manner
  • Work effectively under pressure and under tight deadlines
  • Organize people, time and projects for numerous divisions, nation-wide

If your qualifications match these requirements, please send a cover letter along with your resume including salary requirements/history to resumes@hccsu.com.  Please put in the subject line "COO".  Any resume without a cover letter will not receive a response.

Any offer of employment will be contingent upon positive criminal background and credit checks.

HCC Insurance Holdings, Inc. and its subsidiaries are equal-opportunity employers.  Please visit www.hcc.com for more information about our companies.

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. The Finance IT Architecture Manager is a critical position in the organization.  This leader will work collaboratively with business leaders and stake holders as well as Finance IT managers and leaders and other IT teams as required to:
?         Ensure the organization has a clear architectural timeless vision and that:
?         It is designed to enable our partners to plan their activities with limited interaction with Finance IT
?         Drives improved capability and operational efficiency to the benefit of our business partners
?         Links Finance capabilities roadmaps with the Finance IT architecture
?         Establish and/or refine the roadmaps required to achieve that vision
?         Manage interlock with our business partners to ensure the priority programs are on the Finance IT POR
?         Ensure project requests are sufficiently complete and provide sizing assumptions to the organization's delivery teams
?         Support the Finance IT delivery organizations with solution architectures on key projects as required
As a senior member of the Finance IT leadership team, this individual will have staff leadership, training, mentoring and vendor management responsibilities. The Architecture Manager will have both, "hands on" technical, functional and program management skills. The candidate will model usage patterns and transaction volumes in a multi-tier, highly-interactive, high volume distributed environment utilizing both packaged software and internally developed applications. Knowledge of business capabilities, business process, business requirements, use cases, integration, and data is relevant.
 
This leader will also facilitate serve as the architecture lead cross-functionally for ES & GF IT to ensure sharing and utilization of best practices across the organization
 
II. Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Additional duties may be assigned.
?         Maintains a high-performing team characterized by broad and deep knowledge of the environment and using that knowledge to help our delivery teams achieve our timeless vision.
?         Understands overall business operations and develops innovative solutions to help improve productivity and simplify the application landscape.
?         Develop a reusable model to demonstrate simplification of the application landscape and minimization of reference mappings - using business relevant measures.
?         Model usage patterns and transaction volumes in a multi-tier, high-interactive, high volume distributed environment utilizing packaged software.
?         Provide architectural guidance in solutioning for key projects contributing to the Finance IT timeless vision.
?         Serves as the primary interface to partner IT organizations to remain aligned on strategy and timing.
 
III. Supervisory Responsibilities
This is a management position with direct supervisory responsibilities for members of the Finance IT architecture team.
?         Establishes clear goals and maintains those in Grow@HP - including regular review and updates
?         Holds team and 1x1 meetings with the team on a regular basis to share information, review progress, and understand opportunities to improve efficiencies
?         Develops team members
?         Meets all organization and HR commits

Critical Competencies to Drive Business Results:
 
Opportunity Analysis
Applies technical/solution knowledge to understand what the customer is trying to accomplish, and the technical or business parameters involved in the opportunity
 
Technical Solution Design (Services)

Solution Costing/Resourcing

Solution Advocacy (Services)
Partners with account teams to compellingly position proposed solutions both internally and to the customer
 
Solution Readiness Support
Orients delivery to deal execution components to ensure effective solution implementation and customer satisfaction with HP
 
Technology Practice Development
Takes ownership for the practice and contributes to the development of Hp's technical standards, direction, and the technical community
 
Financial/Business Acumen
Exhibits authoritative business and financial acumen to develop meaningful business or operational recommendations
 
HPS/BU Business Context

HPS/BU Solutions

HPS Solution Interfaces

Change Management

Global Projection

Deltek, Inc.

Toronto, Ontario, Canada

This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7381982
Position Title: Functional Acctg./Finance Sr. Consultant (French Speaking)- 10PSAE021
Job Function: Application Functional Consulting
Posted: November 16, 2010
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: https://www2.deltek.com/careers/default.asp?id=409...
 

Summary of Position: Deltek (NASDAQ: PROJ) is the leading provider of enterprise applications software designed specifically for project-focused businesses. For more than two decades, our software applications have enabled organizations to automate mission-critical business processes around the engagement, execution and delivery of projects. More than 12,000 customers worldwide rely on Deltek to measure business results, optimize performance, streamline operations and win new business.

DESCRIPTION:

  • Deltek is seeking an Accounting & Finance Senior or Principal Consultant reporting to the Global Services Consulting Manager. This position will serve as a knowledgeable consultant on product implementations for Deltek Global Services clients, participating in the day-to-day operations of implementations as assigned and displaying and executing a basic understanding of the technical, functional and interface impacts of various implementation scenarios. This revenue generating position has performance metrics tied to results and utilization and will require extensive travel, up to 100%. Deltek is focused on our employees’ professional development and will provide technical and functional product training as well as consultative skills training.
  • Under general direction, the successful candidate will perform multiple work assignments including: configuring, testing, documenting, and implementing systems and/or software. In addition, this individual will apply Deltek Global Services (DGS) project and consulting methods and processes. Further, this person will be instrumental in determining work priorities in accordance with project plans, project schedules, changing work demands and DGS policies and practices and will escalate as appropriate.
  • Our consultants apply analytical and technical thinking within areas of assigned responsibility and resolve routine client issues and challenges by identifying possible alternatives, and providing recommendations. In addition, they will be asked to research software implementation issues and provide detail and preliminary recommendations to their project lead (or designated representative).
  • Members of the Deltek Global Services Team assist the entire Deltek consulting team in ensuring client requirements are met by actively soliciting, identifying, and responding to customer questions and assisting with issue resolution. We expect that our consultants take ownership of assigned issues and follows through on commitments.
  • This individual will work collaboratively and build relationships with other consultants, customer staff, and third-party consultants.
  • This individual will be assigned and responsible for other duties as assigned

Job Requirements

Education & Experience:

  • Must possess 4-10 years of professional consulting services experience
  • Must possess 5-20 years of industry experience
  • B.A./B.S. or equivalent work experience or technical and/or product knowledge comparable to six-year degree in a related area.
  • Advanced degree or professional certification(s) preferred
  • Experienced with all aspects of Microsoft Office products
  • Prior travel-related experience preferred (travel up to 100%)
  • Fluent in French or French Canadian languages Highly Preferred

Technical Knowledge & Skills

  • Successful completion of at least two, preferably numerous, full lifecycle ERP implementation of a major enterprise suite
  • Adept at designing and implementing technology enabled business solutions for clients
  • Team lead on at least two full cycle implementations
  • Must be able to consult at "C" level and influence process direction/decisions.
  • Demonstrates the ability to handle multiple tasks and priorities and work with general direction
  • Must possess evidence of the ability to develop best practices, guides and processes
  • Deltek Vision experience preferred.
  • Other relevant ERP experience preferred: SAP, BAAN, Oracle Financials, PeopleSoft, Timberline, Jamis, JD Edwards, Solomon, Lawson, Advantage, Sema4, FMS, Microsoft Dynamics, Great Plains, Peachtree, Clearview, Maconomy, Elite.
  • Cost Accounting, Project Accounting, Architecture and Engineering, Resource Planning, ERP Consultant, Controller, Assistant Controller, Accounting Manager, CPA, MBA preferred

Client Care Skills

  • Affectively sets and meets client expectations
  • Excellent relationship-building skills
  • Able to collaborate with clients, identify engagement follow-on opportunities, and have a strong desire to excel
  • Must have ability to manage multiple client assignments and work independently
  • Possesses a working knowledge and experience within one or more of Deltek’s client industries

Other Competencies

  • Strong communication, verbal and written skills required
  • Must possess a proven record of leading and mentoring junior-level consultants
  • Possesses analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Able to work as a team member or an individual contributor
  • Toronto or Montreal area locations preferred

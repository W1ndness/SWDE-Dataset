IT'S MORE EXCITING HERE

Comcast, founded in 1963, has grown into one of the world's leading communication and entertainment companies - focused on broadband cable, commerce and content. Comcast Cable is the country's largest provider of cable services, and has expanded to digital services, faster Internet and IP-enabled phone service, and innovative programming - over 250 cable channels, including popular channels like - E!, Sportsnet, Exercise TV, The Golf Channel and more.
Comcast connects with more than 24.7 million cable customers, 14.1 million high-speed Internet customers and 5.2 million voice customers - providing top consumer products and services in three key areas: video, digital cable, VOD, HD and DVR; online; and voice. We strive to be the company customers look to for the products and services that connect them to what is important in their lives.


POSITION SUMMARY: Prepare clear, complete, and accurate maps and drawings illustrating plant requirements as well as designing and implementing new RF cable and fiber optic plant.


MAJOR DUTIES: (May perform any or all of the following duties.)

? Draw maps of street layouts and drawings of apartment complexes and mobile home parks from information supplied by Engineering.
? Design and assist with node splits between Region and System. Review node splits to validate fiber deep node splits.
? Generate MDU RF system design.
? Make corrections on design maps received from outside contractors, and release to field personnel.
? Update, revise, and maintain plant maps and drawings to as-built specifications supplied by construction information.
? Prepare charts, graphs, and illustrations for presentation of equipment of cable routing.
? Assist in developing bill of materials.
? Analyze and review strand maps to determine information needed in developing new or rebuilt cable systems.
? Establish cable routing design for new or rebuilt cable systems.
? Make revisions to strand maps.
? Ability to work overtime as needed.
? Some travel may be required.
? Proficient with FocusOne or AutoCad/Lode Data, MS Excel, Word, Visio and Streets and Trips.
? Perform other related duties as assigned.



Job Requirements
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:

EDUCATION: HS Diploma plus drafting / engineering course or the equivalent of a 2-year degree in Engineering / Electronics or the recognized equivalent in work experience or self-study.

Strong drafting background (5 years work experience) utilizing AutoCAD, and/or Focus/Bentley a must. Experience with Spatial Info design software a plus.

Experience interpreting CATV or Telco construction / contractor blueprints and as-builds a must.

Working knowledge of all Microsoft Windows 2003 or newer, Windows XP based software.

Strong documentation background utilizing Visio 2003-2007, MapInfo, and OSP Insight.

Strong understanding and working knowledge of all Comcast documentation and design standards.

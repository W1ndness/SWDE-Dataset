Director of Recruiting


Company Overview
A software company focused on providing reliable, cost-effective Business Intelligence software solutions to clients within both the public and private sectors. The Company areas of experience and expertise include Intelligent Software and Professional Services.

Description
The Director of Recruiting supports the company recruiting process and will be required to build a scalable repeatable recruiting model using focused metrics and accountability. This includes identifying and attracting qualified candidates through the processes of sourcing, interviewing, and preparing candidates for the selection and hiring stages. The Director of Recruiting establishes enterprise recruiting tools, programs, systems and resources.

Educational / Work Experience 

 Candidate must be at Director Level of Recruiting and have Professional Services experience

  • Develop the company recruiting plan, recruiting vision statement and recruiting objectives and be held accountable for attaining those objectives, Monitor and update the plan as necessary
  • Four-year degree, graduate degree preferred
  • 8-10 years experience in recruiting management and leadership
  • Lead a team of recruiting managers and recruiters
  • Develop measurement systems to assess the effectiveness of recruiting efforts (metrics)
  • Responsible for talent acquisition reporting, trend analysis and reporting of metrics by period
  • Evaluate and assess effectiveness of recruiting strategies and talent retention systems to continuously improve talent acquisition efforts
  • Identify and source candidates.
  • Coordinate candidate recruiting and establish candidate pools. Meet company growth target and support AAP and EEO initiatives.
  • Effectively partner with other departments by providing ongoing communication on projects, performance and process
  • Properly utilize/create company processes including selection tests and interview guide to screen candidates.
  • Solicit internal referrals.
  • Keep employees constantly aware of the company recruiting need by leveraging the referral programs provided by the company.
  • Report on recruiting activities and results to management team by conducting regular recruiting update meetings.
  • Act as a source for the recruiting process to hiring managers, candidates, applicants, and employees.
  • Other duties as assigned by senior management.

Job Requirements

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

  • Career commitment to recruiting and talent acquisition
  • Demonstrated results in closing gaps in candidate pipelines Demonstrated results in closing gaps in candidate pipelines
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, as well as excellent presentation skills
  • Leadership skills and interpersonal skills
  • Strong analytical skills, oriented to measure performance
  • Working knowledge of recruiting/staffing policies and procedures and compliance regulations and ability to apply policies,  procedures, regulations appropriately
  • Professionalism in work environment, to reflect positively on the company and its reputation
  • Must be a self-starter and have the ability to manage own performance effectively, plans and orgnizes time and priorities to achieve business results
  • Proficient in competitive market compensation
  • Proficient in using recruiting techniques to increase potential candidate pools and the ability to persuade candidate to enter the recruiting process
  • Technical and functional expertise with ability to seek technical knowledge and skills to meet the needs of both external and internal customers
  • Strong project management and organizational skills
  • Demonstrate a substantial personal and/or professional network; has exposure to business planning and performance
  • Ability to adapt and work efficiently within a variety of situations
  • Produces results; directs action toward achieving goals that are critical to recruiting success.
  • Candidate must possess the ability to work in a high-stress, rapid paced environment requiring the provision of high-quality reports, and other products and services on short notice.

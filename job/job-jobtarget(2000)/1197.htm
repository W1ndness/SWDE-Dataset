Company Summary:

Wouldn’t it be exciting to work at a rapidly growing company in an extraordinarily high-growth industry?  ShopAtHome.com is a 24-year old company & the largest coupon & cash back loyalty site on the Web, with more than 40 million monthly visitors. We are expanding rapidly & are looking for an energetic Controller to help us manage that growth. 

 

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Oversee the Accounting team & function as a hands-on manager in the areas of financial statement preparation, reporting & analysis, accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll & benefits, cash management, & compliance with regulatory reporting
  • Prepare annual & quarterly budgets, reporting & analysis
  • Report routinely to Executive Management on budget analysis, cash flow management, financial statement analysis, etc.
  • Work with outside financial vendors such as bankers, accounting firms, benefits providers, insurance brokers & others
  • Provide routine analytical support to Company internal departments

 

Qualifications:

  • Minimum 8 years experience in corporate accounting functions, with a track record of progressive responsibility
  • Minimum 2 years management responsibility for corporate accounting functions
  • Experience in internet-related industry a huge plus
  • Bachelors Degree in accounting/finance or other related discipline; CPA desirable
  • Thorough knowledge of GAAP
  • Superb general computer skills;  SQL database expertise a plus
  • Great Plains accounting software experience preferred with experience in FRX
  • Advanced Excel skills
  • Proven management & mentoring skills
  • Unquestionable integrity

 

Employees Enjoy:

  • Competitive compensation
  • Medical, dental & life Insurance for employees & their dependents
  • Matching 401(k) savings plan
  • Flexible Spending Accounts
  • Free on-site gym
  • Working with the greatest people ever in an explosive growth company!

 

Please send cover letter, resume & salary requirements to:  hrdept@ShopAtHome.com .  No agencies please. EOE.

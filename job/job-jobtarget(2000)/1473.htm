Specifications and requirements

Individuals with appropriate experience, applicable undergraduate degree in accounting, active Florida CPA license, or professional equivalent.  Entrepreneurial ambition.  Excellent communications and teamwork skills. Ability to develop and maintain solid client relationships. Effectively supervise numerous staff, multiple engagements and firm administrative assignments. Strong commitment to the success of the firm.  Perform work on all levels of engagement with no supervision.  Must be able to travel for engagements or the firm.

 

Technical skills and professional development

Customarily and regularly exercises discretionary powers.

 Responsible for achieving engagement objectives, management of client relationship and staff supervision.

 Submit completed engagements to senior manager or partner on a timely basis and in accordance with all applicable technical, professional and Firm standards. 

 Attest engagements must be well documented and provide effective presentation of financial statements including issues researched; identify and resolve client issues, and if applicable, preparation of a well written management recommendation letter (A&A). 

 Demonstrates advanced knowledge of accounting principles (GAAP), auditing standards (AU, GAAS, SAS) and techniques to supervise staff and resolve complex engagement issues (A&A).

 Knowledgeable of and complies with all peer review requirements. Assist in firm peer reviews (A&A).

 Strong understanding of tax software and the ability to review tax returns in an effective, efficient and timely manner (Tax.)

 Determine methods and consider alternatives to increase realization on engagements from year to year.

 Keeps current on recent professional standards, technical standards and keeps responsible firm members informed.

 Strives to perform above average in accordance with firm’s Top Grading Employment.

 

 Administration of engagement and client service

Client fee negotiations, billing, collection and related client relations.

 Provides clear and direct instructions to staff to effectively implement firm policies and engagement techniques.

 Closely monitors individual, engagement and firm productivity realization and utilization. Active involvement in scheduling and utilization of staff.

 Develops positive working relationships with clients and co-workers.  Fosters environment of teamwork.

 Monitors and controls engagement process in terms of budgeted time and scheduled completion date.  Communicates to keep partners and client informed.

 Ability to earn clients’ and peers’ respect and trust.

 

Strong written and verbal communication skills.  Good listening skills.

 Effective at retaining firm clients.

 Takes responsibility for managing personal schedule for time conflicts or availability.

 Recognizes how assigned tasks and performance affect the final product and service providing. Actively seeks to promote the firm’s engagement methodology and be an effective, competent and professional Ambassador of the Firm.

 

Demonstrates quality and professionalism in all aspects of work and client relationships.

 

 Personal growth and development

Conveys a high level of enthusiasm and professionalism.

 

Owner or entrepreneurial attitude.

 Ability to serve as role model and coach to staff.

 Effective communicator.

 Develops high level of expertise in one or more specialized areas/industries.

 Identifies and delivers cross-selling opportunities to current clients.

 Resolves staffing and personnel issues.  Involves HR when applicable.

 Addresses client concerns in effective manner.

 Holds leadership roles in professional and community organizations.

 


Job Requirements

Our Melbourne, FL office is looking for an experienced public accounting Audit Manager.   Candidate must have active CPA certification and at least 5 years of recent public accounting management experience.  Excellent communication and teamwork skills, along with the ability to develop and maintain solid client relationships is required.  Government and commercial audit experience a plus.   About 25%-30% travel will be required.  Only those living in or interested in relocating to Brevard County, Florida need apply.

 Highlights of Berman Hopkins Wright & LaHam, CPAs, LLP: 

  • Berman Hopkins CPAs has been in Central Florida since 1958.  We are a group of over 50 staff with strong expertise in construction and real estate development, non-profit and government associations, manufacturing, small SEC entities.      
  • Our firm is an involved member in one of the largest international accounting associations known as IGAF Worldwide.  This active membership allows us to be a large regional firm with multi-firm national and international resources on caliber with the Big 4. 
  • We seek the top and best employees to bring into our team.  They must be self-starters, well-educated and enthusiastic professionals who enjoy having some fun while they work. We provide a stimulating environment with leading - edge technology and professional growth opportunities at all levels.   
  • We know that life outside of work is important to you.  We provide opportunities to include family and friends in many of our activities such as our busy-season party, family picnic, and holiday party.  Spontaneous activities are also planned by different employees such as beach parties, camping trips, sporting events with tailgate parties and more.

 

IT IS OUR POLICY TO APPLY RECRUITING, HIRING, PROMOTION, COMPENSATION AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT PRACTICES WITHOUT REGARD TO RACE, RELIGION, COLOR, NATIONAL ORIGIN, SEX, DISABILITIES, AGE OR OTHER LEGALLY PRESCRIBED CRITERIA.

Berman Hopkins Wright & LaHam, CPAs, LLP is a drug-free workplace and performs drug testing and background investigations.

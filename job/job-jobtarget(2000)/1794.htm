Certona Corporation is looking for an experienced Senior Software Quality Assurance Engineer to contribute to furthering the QA discipline to ensure the highest quality delivery of the next generation of analytics and merchandising tools for Certona’s Resonance® personalization platform. The successful candidate will work in an extremely fast paced environment, help provide technical leadership, and make key hands-on contributions in all stages of the software development lifecycle.

NOTE to  H1-B Visa holders: 
US citizens and Green Card Holders and those authorized to work in the US are encouraged to apply. We are unable to sponsor H1B candidates at this time. Thank you.



Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement both manual & automated tests against a 3-tier web based application.
  • Define and build strategies for implementing the automated regression testing suites.
  • Work with the software development team to implement best practices for Software Configuration Management and execute some “build master” duties.
  • Work with product management and the development team to understand requirements and create test strategies understood by technical and non-technical users.
  • Become a go-to person for technical understanding of the software platform.

Highly Desired Skills

  • Degree in CS or 5+ years of relevant QA work experience.
  • Experience with the design and implementation of object oriented (OO) QA automation frameworks using any of the following: Ruby, C#, or Java.
  • Strong SQL, PLSQL, or MySQL skills. Backend testing experience is required.
  • Proficient in using Selenium or Ruby / Watir to automate the testing of web-based applications.
  • Skilled with Microsoft Team Foundation Server (TFS) or other design and management systems such as SVN, CVS and experience with continuous integration (CI) systems such as Hudson or Cruise Control is preferred.
  • Experience using automated tools to test web services (e.g., MS Team Suite, Soap,UI, JMeter, etc.).
  • Prior exposure to testing eCommerce, business intelligence, data analytics and/or data warehousing systems is a big plus.
  • Agile experience is a highly desirable.

Preferred Skills

  • Strong critical thinking and analysis, verbal and written communications skills.
  • You are comfortable with cross-team dependencies, rapid schedules, and constantly changing business needs.
  • You take ownership of projects and assigned support issues and are accountable to seeing them through to successful completion.
  • You are a highly motivated self starter and can work in a flexible, fast paced team-oriented environment.
  • Well organized and careful attention to detail.

Please send your resume and optional cover letter to careers@certona.com

Plexxus

Plexxus provides leading edge business support solutions to Ontario's health care sector.  It is owned by 12 member hospitals:   Bloorview Kids Rehab, Lakeridge Health, Mount Sinai Hospital, North York General Hospital, Rouge Valley Health System, St. Joseph's Health Centre, The Scarborough Hospital, Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre, Toronto East General Hospital, Toronto Rehab, University Health Network and Women's College Hospital. 

Plexxus is a not-for-profit shared service organization with a mandate to provide supply chain as well as transactional finance, human resources and payroll services to member healthcare organizations. The Plexxus objectives are to improve service levels and maximize non-clinical efficiencies, resulting in savings for members that can be reinvested in direct patient care. Plexxus embodies values that reflect a commitment to excellence.  These include:  A focus on results while delivering value, acting with integrity and respect, inspiring our people, being flexible and responsive, and finding innovative solutions for our customers. 

Plexxus is now positioned to implement new efficiencies by implementing SAP financial and supply chain software applications at Plexxus and our member hospitals.  We are searching for a Data Management Lead to be part of the ITS Project Leadership Team and take responsibility for design and development of master data in support of the ITS project.  This role will report to the ITS Program Director and work closely with the Director, Data Administration.  Harmonized master data for Plexxus and its member hospitals are the primary deliverables of this role.

Accountabilities: 

  • Identify business requirements for Master Data as it relates to the scope of ITS
  • Define data governance process whereby Plexxus can manage the quality, consistency, usability, security and availability of ITS data including defining performance measures to help determine how well data governance effort is working
  • Develop and enable a master data management strategy for ITS
  • Collaborate with Plexxus business owners and hospital stakeholders to further develop and publish data master business rules, processes and systems that support product master data Establish a data governance framework and practice that spans across Plexxus and its Member Hospitals in order to ensure that the appropriate structure, rules and processes are in place to manage master data
  • Ensure accuracy, completeness, timeliness & management of item and vendor master data collection. Develop and monitor compliance with business standards and procedures as well as working to continuously improve data standards, quality, repositories and processes that include original input, change functions, auditing and cleaning
  • Define a data migration strategy for hospitals as they transition to SAP
  • Develop training documentation and conduct training on all necessary areas of master data
  • Working with Hospital IT departments, identify requirements for conversion of data from  legacy systems and ITS   It is likely that data cleansing will be included in this work
  • Define and design the Master Data Management work processes in SAP
  • Prepare and lead others in preparing master data for loading into SAP

Job Requirements

Qualifications:    

  • Experience in large scale ERP (preferably SAP) implementation projects including specific experience with master data
  • Experience in leading large scale data conversions
  • Understanding of and experience with the master data requirements of a supply chain implementation project.
  • Strong analytical, organizational and troubleshooting skills
  • Strong data analysis and data architecture skills
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to work with a broad group of stakeholders to implement common standards and best practices
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Proven ability to work in a project environment
  • 10 years of relevant industry experience, preferably in a shared service or business process outsourcing environment
  • Experience with the tools required to facilitate this work - Excel, Visio, MS-Project, other data handling tools.

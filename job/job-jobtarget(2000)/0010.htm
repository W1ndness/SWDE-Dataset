Program Manager

Olympus America, Inc.

Center Valley, Pennsylvania, United States

Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: Program Manager
Required Travel: 0-10%
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Posted: November 10, 2010
Industry: Other
Job Function: Project Management
Min Experience: 7-10 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: http://www.olympusamericacareers.com
 

Center Valley, PA - The Program Manager will be responsible for managing a multi-organizational, cross-functional team to implement the activities related to cross-functional product development, production, and launch/release of new MIT products.  The incumbent will also be responsible for managing similar activities related to incremental improvements/updates of existing products.  He/she will also manage multiple, simultaneous, complex product development projects. The incumbent will also be the primary focal point for project results and communicating project performance and issues to Executive Management.  He/she must serve as a change agent to quickly improve the delivery of new products that are simple to use, have improved performance, low cost, and reliability. EOE M/F/D/V.

Requirements and Responsibilities:

* Responsible for managing the development and delivery of new/existing products.

 * Responsible for meeting program Key Performance Indicators (KPT) of on-time, on-quality, on-cost (product and program), and on-requirements.

 * Establish direction and accountability for the program team. Direct the day-to-day activities of team members and remove hurdles so that team members can achieve desired results.

 * Manage program budgets, jointly negotiating contracts and consulting/getting timely approval from the Legal Department for respective matters.

 * Create the Work-Breakdown-Structure (WBS) in consultation with functional groups.

 * Track and present key metrics.  Create clear concise presentations of status and critical issues/mitigations to Management, including Executive Management.

 * Track and assign defects/enhancement requests by working with the Functional Manager.

 * Build rapport with peers and internal/external customers.

 * Use influencing skills to find win-win situations with internal and external stakeholders.

 * Prioritize and break down activities in a manageable way for team members.

 * Hold cross-functional core and team members accountable for achieving program deliverables.

 * Manage external design/development partners to ensure on-quality, on-cost, on-time deliverables.

 * Demand and continually improve performance from the team.

 * Create alignment among the team and between the team and product strategy.

 * Ensure that all development and support activities comply with SOPs and FDA QSR regulations.

 * Coordinate activities with peers in a global product environment.

 * Perform all other related duties as assigned.


Job Requirements

 (ONLY QUALIFIED CANDIDATES WILL BE CONSIDERED)

* A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or Electrical Engineering, or equivalent knowledge is required. A MS, MBA, or MEM is highly desired.  PMI certification is a plus.

* A minimum of 8 years of experience in system/software development is required. Must have a minimum of 4 years of experience managing new product development activities.  A consulting background is a big plus.

* Medical device, Healthcare IT, or Software industry experience is highly desired.

* Prior experience and skill in leading/driving organizational change, including systems, processes, and people, is vital to success.  Experience in implementing, applying, and continuously improving a stage-gate development process is desirable.

* Must have a demonstrated ability to lead a multi-organizational cross-functional team who report indirectly to the Program Manager.

* Experience with stage-gate development model is highly desired.

* Must be experienced in developing high-tech products with significant software content.

* A demonstrated ability to manage and complete programs/projects while meeting Key Performance Indicators is essential.

* Must be able to work with external development partners. 

* Excellent knowledge of Microsoft technologies is desired.

* Must have the ability to successfully take on new and difficult challenges, question the status quo, and take risks.

* A high degree of multi-tasking ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously is vital.

* Must be analytical and fact-based most of the time, but able to draw on intuition.  The ability to reduce an overwhelming amount of information into a few salient issues to focus on, as well as prioritize the most relevant from all other issues is necessary.

* Strong presentation skills to Executive Management is required.

* Must be highly driven to achieve results and aggressively identify and solve problems that can impact the scheduled delivery of products that meet customer requirements.  

* The ability to identify root cause issues affecting the program and define and implement corrective/mitigation actions is essential.

* Must be accustomed to effectively dealing with unforeseen crises with quick analysis and decisive action.

* Requires high energy and an unsurpassed drive to win every time.

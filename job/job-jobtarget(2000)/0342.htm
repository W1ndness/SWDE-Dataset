HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Summary: Looking for a strong web development resourse to plan, coordinate and execute team activities supporting the design, development, testing, implementation, support and optimization of information technology related programs and systems that enable and support PA MMIS healthcare claims processing business functions, client defined business needs and maintenance of account obligations.  Provide the technical expertise necessary for timely identification and resolution of application code anomalies and issues.  Assist leadership in determining technical direction.  Research, analyze, recommend and implement new technologies, standards, processes, tools, and techniques.   Ability to work in an area of specialization.  Identify, analyze and devise solutions to business and technical information technology problems.  Effectively interact with clients regarding business and technical information technology needs.  Manage projects within the scope of responsibility and effectively communicate status, progress, and items needing resolution.    
 
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
?         Analyze business requirements from both internal and external clients
?         Produce business and technical design documentation to facilitate client understanding, agreement of requirements and specifications, and approval of developed, optimized and delivered system functionality
?         Develop both single system and cross system functional and technical specifications and designs for multiple types of system components including development of new components as well as modification to existing components
?         Utilize industry standard protocols and methods for developing efficient, streamlined programming logic
?         Develop comprehensive test plans and execute to them to demonstrate the capability of system / application modifications
?         Assist business and other technical team members as needed
?         Research and analyze business conditions to troubleshoot application behavior and performance and resolve issues and anomalies quickly and efficiently
?         Mentor team members in advanced application development practices and techniques  
  Required Experience
?          Minimum of 5 years experience in a web application development environment following an application development methodology
?         5+ years experience with either  ASP, VB, ASP.NET, .NET, PL/SQL, and XML development
?         5+ years experience in Systems Development and Support including design, coding and testing
?          Ability to respond to client problems/needs timely and effectively
?         Demonstrated strong communication and interpersonal skills
?         Healthcare industry experience a plus
?          Experience working with State/Federal Government clients a plus
  • Ability to work with Web-development tools for new applications. 
  • Basic Understanding of RDBMS databases such SQL Server and Oracle. Basic understanding of modern software design and development methodologies (e.g., OO). 
  • Strong understanding of modern SCM (software configuration management). 
  • Basic understanding of testing tools and unit and integration test scripting, and testing methodologies.
  • Experience using an Integrated Development Environment (e.g., Eclipse, Visual Studio).
  • Strong understanding basic Database Administration. 
  • Able to apply consistently quality and security standards. 
  • Good verbal and written communication skills.
  • Developing general project management/team leader skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team and with clients.

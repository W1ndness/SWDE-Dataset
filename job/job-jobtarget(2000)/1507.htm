Dahill- A Xerox company; offers innovative and high performance document imaging technology from two of the world's leading manufacturers- XEROX and SHARP.

Our mission is to help organizations find more productive and efficient methods of producing and managing document workflow.

Proven Leadership. Powerful Results.

Dahill is searching for dynamic, aggressive, and career minded B2B sales professionals to join our Corpus Christi sales team.

  • Identify prospects via online resources to include CRM, directories and other methods.
  • Introduce and sell the Dahill product line to prospects and customers in designated local territory.
  • Present software and hardware solutions to solve customer document workflow issues.
  • Achieve assigned monthly sales quota by consistently performing required daily/weekly activities necessary to build robust pipeline of qualified prospects.  
  • Complete customer needs analysis, draft proposals, provide product demonstration and presentations.

We are looking for candidates who excel in fast paced, highly competitive sales environment.

  • HS Diploma/GED 
  • Strong time management skills with the ability to perform consistently with minimal supervision.
  • Successful in generating business leads via cold calling, onsite visits, and other methods.
  • Strong communication, negotation and presentation skills.
  • Valid driver's license, proof of auto insurance and have reliable transportation.

 Preferred:

  • BS/BA  and/ or 2+ years of outside sales experience.
  • Consultative sales experience with strong account forecasting, planning, and prioritizing skills.
  • Sales experience in office products, technology, medical supplies, advertising, and/or similar industry is preferred.

Dahill Perks

Competitive base salary with uncapped commission* Benefits package that starts in 30 days* 401K with company match* Ability to achieve President's Club with destinations like Cancun, Puerto Vallarta, and Florida* Promotion from within philosophy

 Ready for a career with unlimited earning potential? Dahill is waiting for you!

Qualified candidates will be required to pass a drug screen, background check and motor vehicle report.

Dahill is an EQUAL OPPORTUNITY EMPLOYER

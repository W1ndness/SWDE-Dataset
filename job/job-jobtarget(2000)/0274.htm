Systems Engineer
Canada

Due to product demand, our Sales force is expanding, which means we need to add another Systems Engineer to our Professional Services team. As a Systems Engineer, you will be responsible for providing pre-sales and post-sales support for customers in Canada and possibly across North America. You will have the opportunity to visit world-class data centers and design the data protection solutions that make them run efficiently. You'll assist sales and marketing teammates with the technical components of RFI/RFP responses. We're seeking road warriors with storage industry experience across both tape and disk. Not a PC/MCSE job.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
Seeking experience with hardware technical support or field engineering, and presales support. Qualified candidates will have the following skills and abilities:

  • Experience with hardware technical support or field engineering;
  • Strong hardware troubleshooting skills are a must;
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills;
  • Hands-on knowledge of government business cultures, sales processes, and high-tech companies in your territory;
  • Knowledge of key accounts and contacts in the territory;
  • Familiarity with tape libraries, storage devices, and storage software required;
  • Experience with VTLs, Replication and DeDuplication;
  • Experience with SCSI devices and computer storage products preferred;
  • Fully bilingual in French and English;
  • Above average RFP Response writing skills;
  • Above average presentation skills.

LOCATION:
Preferably Toronto with a possibility of Montreal.

EDUCATION:
Prefer a four-year engineering or CS degree, or equivalent technical or military experience.

TRAVEL:
Approximately 80% travel, most in territory. Some travel will be by plane. Must hold a valid driver's license. International travel will be required.

STATUS:
This is a contract position.

Are you looking for a unique vehicle to a successful career?  Are you seeking to utilize your talents to their maximum potential?   NTN Bearing Corporation has employment opportunities that will assist qualified candidates to achieve their desired goals.  NTN is one of the world’s premier bearing manufacturers serving the automotive and industrial markets for the past 90 years.  Proven stability, growing sales that are grossing in the billions, are a few of the factors that set NTN apart.  NTN has an immediate opening in our Operations Department for an Inventory Planning Manager.

 

Responsibilities Include:

  • Ensure that the inventory strategy and approach aligns and supports NBCA’s overall business plan
  • Ensure that inventory levels and inventory availability support both NTN’s financial goals for effective inventory as well as NBCA’s Sales and Marketing goals for current and future growth
  • Develop metrics, reporting, and root causal analysis at the department, supervisory, and individual planning level to measure and manage performance
  • Ensure that communication, training, and performance management processes are in place to understand and drive performance and accountability at all levels of the organization
  • Ensure a focus on continuous improvement leveraging best practices with processes and systems
  • Support day-to-day inventory requirements.

Job Requirements

Skills/Qualifications:

  • Must have Bachelor’s Degree (Supply Chain Management or Logistics preferred).
  • APICs certified
  • At least 7 years of professional work in Supply Chain.  Prefer exposure to various supply chain functions including warehousing and transportation
  • Strong quantitative and analytical skills.  Strategic thinking skills.
  • Minimum 3 years supervisory/management experience with at least 3 direct reports.
  • Leadership skills including ability to drive change, think creatively, and ability to take measured risk
  • Demonstrated proficiency in ERP and/or Inventory Planning Systems (i.e. Logility, i2, etc)  and Excel
  • At least 5 years experience in using ERP and/or Inventory Planning software.

 

NTN Bearing Corporation offers a full range of comprehensive benefits that far exceed the industry standard, including medical, dental, vision, as well as matching 401(k), paid vacation, tuition reimbursement.  NTN Bearing Corporation is an equal opportunity employer that strongly supports diversity in the work place.

About Technology Operations:

Our newly built, advanced Global Command Center in downtown Buenos Aires is looking for qualified candidates to start a promising career in Technology Operations. This innovative command center works correspondingly with its twin center in Bangalore, India and a large network of IT professionals located in our other Content, Technology & Operations locations.

 

Technology Operations at Thomson Reuters is a critical field that's relied upon by the 27,800 employees of Thomson Reuters Markets in addition to our global professional subscribers in the financial markets and consumers interested in global news and financial markets information. Our strategic data and command centers are internationally certified to the ISO 9001 Quality and ISO 27001 Information Security Management Systems standards. Not only are we a leading IT Service Management (ITIL versions 2 & 3) practitioner, but we've also partnered with several vendors including Hewlett Packard, IBM, Cisco, etc. which have assisted us with influencing cutting edge technologies such as deploying private cloud computing environments, low latency global trading platforms, and implementing advanced techniques in virtualization & IT process automation. By joining the Thomson Reuters Markets team you will be exposed to many hands on experiences, in addition you will benefit from advanced hand-on training from qualified associates and central business skills courses.  When ready, your new skills can take your career aspirations to many of the other rewarding career positions that the Buenos Aires operational center offers. Overview:

Common Platform is a multi year programme to transform the Thomson Reuters Markets business, by delivering compelling and customer driven products over a single, flexible and robust platform. The programme will deliver the next generation suite of desktop products for Sales & Trading.

Time Series (TS) for Common Platform (CP) is one major pillar of the environment, which will provide TS data for all the products within the CP environment.

The project and therefore the engineer is responsible for building, testing and supporting TS for CP.

 

Responsibilities:

Specifically this role will involve working on implementing the new TS for CP Integration and Production environments as part of projects. The candidate will either be assigned directly onto this project or integrate into the BAU support team/roster to support the existing environment. - Coorinate the installation of servers, troublshooting issues with internal tech teams. - Install, set-up and configure software, troubleshoot issues with 3rd Level Support and provide feedback for improvements. - Support and maintain the infrastructure and service by following the incident and change management processes. - Take ownership of incidents/issues and see through to resolution. - Monitoring of the service will be required at times. - Work with teams across geographical locations.

 

Additional Information:

Travel to US (St. Loius) required for the first 3 months for knowledge transfer. Implentation hours = 5 days-a-week (stagnated start time). Support = 4 days on, 4 days off, 12 hour per shifts (13:00-01:00 GMT)

Education:

Graduate level (technical based degree) Required Skills:

> Technical Expertise on HP Proliant (Windows 2003), HP Blades (Windows 2003) for implementation and support roles and thorough knowledge on Networking and Communication for Data Centre Operations for Internet, Intranet and MPLS distribution and knowledge of virtualization concepts. > Knowledge with IBM WAS (Websphere Application Server). > SQL Server knowledge. > Remote implementation and support experience. > 3 to 6 years experience on Technical Support and Implementation roles for IT infrastructure Data Centre environment. > Exposure to ISO 9001, ISO 27001 and ITIL Processes. > Experience with complex product environment for application support with thorough understanding on data flow and architecture. > Writing skills for technical/handover/support documentation.

 

Desired Skills:

IBM Blades (Windows 2003), Sun (Solaris10), Cisco products.

 

Certifications Desired:

MCSE 2k3 CCNA, ITIL Foundation.

JOB TITLE: Computer Lab Instructor Assistant

POSITION SUMMARY: The Federated Indians of Graton Rancheria’s Tribal Family TANF Program of Sonoma and Marin Counties (“the Program”) serves needy Indian families with temporary cash assistance and supportive services.  The Program provides parent or caretaker Participants with job preparation, work-related assistance and other supportive services enabling families to become self-sufficient.  It is the intent of the Program that particular attention is paid to culturally relevant services and preservation of Indian families.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

The Computer Lab Instructor Assistant, under the direction of the Instructor, will:

·         Instruct students in computer literacy training covering business software applications.  Software includes but not limited to: Microsoft Word, Excel, Access, Outlook, Photo shop, Mavis Beacon typing tutor.

·         Assist students in completion of GED preparation software.

·         Administer GED practice tests and review with the student.

·         Administer GED work book assignments and review results with the student.

·         Input daily data regarding student participation in the TANF Case Management Database System (“TAS’)  

·         Maintain an accurate computer lab sign in log documenting participation hours.

·         Administer assessments for plan development.

·         Maintain/update client EDS written file.

·         Schedule GED testing at SRJC for prepared clients.

·         Alert Dept. Supervisor of any problems related to computer lab or training activities.

·         Work with the Dept. Supervisor in an ongoing effort to improve TANFs training expanding the program to include: Quick books software, Medical transcription, Medical billing and inventory control.

·         Calling clients to schedule lab or workshop appointments.

·         Monitor all computer lab activities.

HOURS: Permanent, Full-Time, Non-exempt, 40 hours a week position


Job Requirements

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:

·         Ability to communicate effectively in English, both orally and in writing

·         Ability to read, understand and follow complex rules, regulations, policies and directives.

·         Awareness of current Indian issues with regard to employment, training, and social services.

·         Knowledge of Indian values and culture in relation to self-sufficiency and welfare dependence.

  • Strong computer skills including experience with internet research, Microsoft Office, Photoshop, Mavis Beacon Typing Tutor, Quickbooks and automated database systems.

EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:

Must have at least one year of experience, in education, social services, or any other relevant employment, or two year of relevant post high school education. Some college preferred.

SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must be willing to adhere to professional and ethical codes of conduct and to FIGR employment policies.
  • Must successfully clear a criminal history background check.

TRIBAL MEMBER AND INDIAN PREFERENCE WILL APPLY

Preference in hiring is given to:  (1) Qualified Federated Indians of Graton Rancheria members; and (2) qualified American Indians in accordance with Title 25, U.S. Code, Section 472 and 473.  Applicants asserting American Indian preference must submit verifying information, such as written confirmation of tribal membership, Certificate of Degree of Indian Blood, or other documentary evidence.

SALARY:  DOE. Strong benefits package.

Mail cover letter and resume to, Federated Indians of Graton Rancheria, Attention: HR

6400 Redwood Drive, Suite 300, Rohnert Park, CA 94928 or email cover letter and resume to jobs@gratonrancheria.com

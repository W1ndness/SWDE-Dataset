Position Overview

The Technical Writer’s role is to develop, write and maintain high quality documentation for the company’s software products. Outputs include a range of documents in PDF and Word to be read by customers, largely application programmers, and internally by the company’s technical personnel.

 

 

Responsibilities

 

Write, maintain and update user guides, manuals and technical specifications for all CrawfordTech software products. The manuals normally contain code examples and sample configuration, batch and command files that need to be explained to the users.

Take part in and monitor the process of creating and updating software reference information and periodically generate a complete set of software reference guides in PDF.

Publish the above documents internally and on the company website.

Maintain currency, accuracy and completeness of all documentation.

Collaborate with development and support Subject Matter Expert’s to collect and interpret technical information.

Develop and update documentation project plans and timelines.

Maintain and update documentation standards and communicate them as needed.

 

Salary will be based on your experience level and the amount of knowledge you bring to the company.

 

Please submit your resume and cover letter in confidence to humanres@crawfordtech.com.

 

We thank all applicants for their interest, but only those short listed will be contacted.

 

About CrawfordTech

Since 1995, CrawfordTech’s award-winning solutions have helped over 700 companies around the world reduce process costs associated with delivering bills, statements and other mission-critical transactional communications to their customers in the format they need, when they need it, including Braille, Large Print, Audio and E-text. CrawfordTech’s unique software products and services are used by four of the top five US banks, four of the top five US insurance companies and by four of the world’s top five car manufacturers. CrawfordTech has a platform-independent approach, leading system performance and superior output fidelity. CrawfordTech automates the delivery of billions of communications annually through its quality software, expert support and print business knowledge, helping clients to meet operational, service, marketing, legal requirements.


Job Requirements

College degree in the field of English or Communications or a certificate in Technical Writing or Technical Communications or an equivalent combination of education and experience.

At least five years of technical writing experience.

A strong background in business document industry and/or software development highly desirable.

Experience with authoring tools, including Word, Author-it Editor, Visio and Adobe Framemaker.

Experience with extraction software such as Autoduck and Javadoc an asset

Ability to quickly absorb and retain complex technical information.

Capable of installing and testing application software

Exceptional written and oral communication skills.

Exceptional interpersonal skills, with a focus on listening and interviewing skills.

Highly self motivated

Ability to work as part of a team and to work independently

Experience with Windows, Linux, Unix and z/OS system environments an asset

Experience with Joomla preferred

Software Sales Representative - North America

If you are a seasoned software/solution Sales Representative with experience in the COLD, ECM and document archiving market space and are looking for the opportunity to make the most of your energy and high impact skill set in a growing company, please read on.

Job Title: Sales Representative   

Position Summary

We are a rapidly growing software company looking to grow our sales revenue via the expansion of our direct sales force to augment our existing OEM and VAR sales channels.  We are seeking a Software Sales Representative to sell industry-leading software to medium and large sized companies in North America.  Your technical sales expertise will be vital to our growth plans.

You will develop a clear understanding of the business operating environments and requirements of prospective customers. You will identify customer success criteria and determine what it takes to close opportunities. You will work both independently and as part of a team. You will spend a significant portion of your time prospecting (cold-calling).

Requirements

  • 7+ years of experience in an enterprise software vendor organization
  • 5+ years of successful software sales experience (preferably in a small to medium sized software vendor company)
  • Bachelor's degree in a closely related field. Experience may be substituted for a degree
  • Successful track record of selling COLD, ECM and document archive software solutions
  • Ability to thrive in a dynamic, fast paced and high pressure environment
  • Self starter with proven experience managing multiple priorities
  • Ability to effectively communicate, both written and orally with internal and external customers
  • Spanish and French language skills a plus
  • Proven track record as territory sales person using consultative selling
  • Knowledge with High Volume Transactional Document business and applications
  • Good Computer proficiency in a MS Windows environment
  • Ability to work to deadlines and manage time
  • Self motivated with initiative, tenacity and determination, dynamic and enthusiastic
  • Good presentation and public speaking skills
  • Personal involvement in large complex deals
  • Willingness to travel regularly

What is in it for you?

  • Competitive salary
  • Excellent benefits
  • Informal working environment
  • Flexible Work Hours
  • Be a Part of small, rapidly growing software company

We thank all applicants for their interest, but only those short listed will be contacted.


Job Requirements

Responsibilities

  • Prospect within your defined territory (including cold-calling into accounts)
  • Utilize top-down selling techniques to determine key decision makers
  • Schedule and conduct face-to-face appointments with key decision makers and project groups
  • Understand the prospects' current COLD, ECM and document archive production environments and the solutions they currently use
  • Present CrawfordTech solutions to improve customer output management computing environments
  • Propose solutions and assist customers in justifying the acquisition of CrawfordTech software and services
  • Work with selected partners to transition customer support contracts from the partners to CrawfordTech
  • Build ongoing relationships with key decision makers to expand future sales opportunities within their organization
  • Proactively identify, drive and create opportunities to increase revenue
  • Actively participate in related conferences, seminars and trade shows

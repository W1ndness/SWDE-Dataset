Position Title: Product Support/Merchandising Coordinator
Industry: Retail
Posted: December 6, 2010
Job Function: Other
Job Type: Full-Time
Contact Person: Marianne DePippo
Email Address:

 

The role of the Product Support/Merchandising Coordinator is to serve as the liaison between the sales team and all vendors with relation to product issues and to support the Merchandising Department in achieving sales and profit goals by performing the following duties.

  ESSENTIAL JOB RESPONSIBILTIES

  •  Manage replacement parts for customers including acquiring pricing and orer from vendor, ski setup, field communication, etc.
  • File warranty claims with vendors for issuance of a credit memo or replacement and negotiate with vendors on warranty claims that have been denied to find a resolution
  • Resolve customer’s product issues and inquiries regarding maintenance and repair of product
  • Update internal sales software with product information including instructions, measurements, weight capacity, materials, etc.
  • Answer product questions that arise from the customer but that might not be in the internal system
  • Input product inclusion and exclusion data for all company-wide promotions, work with IT to execute, and communicate promotions to studio operations
  • Manage scheduling of cross-functional strategy session meetings, update critical dates calendar, and insure all key deadlines are being met 
  • Assist in collecting vendor and product information as needed by Buyers
  • Manage monthly webinar and conference call setup
  • Research special projects as necessary

 

 


Job Requirements

 

  • A passion for design and keen product knowledge
  • 1-2 years previous retail or merchandising experience preferred
  • Excellent communication skills, written and verbal.
  • Strong project management skills.
  • Strong Customer Service Skills and Focus
  • Excellent computer skills, MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Must be detail oriented.  Accurate data is required.
  • Excellent Organization Skills
  • Reliable, punctual attendance is required.
  • Ability to meet urgent deadlines.
  • Ability to interact effectively within Merchandising team and other departments

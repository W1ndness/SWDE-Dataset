About Us
Alliance Data (NYSE:  ADS) and its combined businesses is North America's largest and most comprehensive provider of transaction-based, data-driven marketing and loyalty solutions serving large, consumer-based industries. The Company creates and deploys customized solutions, that drive business growth and profitability for some of today's most recognizable brands.  Alliance Data helps its clients create and increase customer loyalty through solutions that engage millions of customers using traditional, digital, mobile and other emerging technologies.  Headquartered in Dallas, Alliance Data employs approximately 7,400 associates at 50 locations worldwide.



Alliance Data is a leading provider of marketing-driven credit solutions, and is the parent company of Epsilon®, a leading provider of multi-channel, data-driven technologies and marketing services, and LoyaltyOne, which owns and operates the AIR MILES® Reward Program, Canada's premier coalition loyalty program.  For more information about the company, visit our web site, www.AllianceData.com, or you can follow us on Twitter at www.Twitter.com/AllianceData.
About the Opportunity
Provide direction to the Compliance Team to identify risks inherent in Company activities, measure the Company's level of adherence to standards agreed to by Management, serve as an advocate for our business partners by seeking creative solutions to business needs, and provide compliance training for our in-business partners. Work cooperatively with Legal to propose standards that mitigate risk to acceptable levels as determined by Management.
Responsibilities
Responsible for effectively managing the Compliance Department, staffing and budgeting.  Support, motivate, and retain staff.
Provide strategic direction to Company related to compliance risk
Serve on enterprise and operational level project teams
Responsible for performing risk analysis related to Company activities and providing resources needed to address those risks.
Responsible for compliance reporting to the bank board
Develop and promote company wide initiatives for compliance training, awareness, and conformance with regulations.  Support initiatives through department staff
Review and determine feasibility of products from compliance risk perspective
Maintain knowledge of applicable laws and regulations
Analyze complex data
Communicate effectively in English (read, write and speak)
Interact professionally with clients, customers
Lead a team of associates to complete a project or other assignment
Prioritize assignments and work concurrently on multiple tasks
Proofread
Research problems and communicate solutions
Resolve customer problems through personal contact
Speak to groups of customers, clients and/or colleagues
Supervise the work of others, formally or informally
Troubleshoot problems of a technical nature

SUMMARY

PERCENTAGE OF TRAVEL REQUIRED

 Researches, writes, proofreads, and edits content for HMS Web site, press releases, case studies, product information, newsletters, and other content as needed.

PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES (OTHER DUTIES MAY BE ASSIGNED)
  • § Maintain and update content on hmstn.com, working with Director on communications strategy.
  • § Draft press releases on new HMS customers and senior-level hires; work with Business Wire or PR agency (if applicable) on distribution.
  • § Write and distribute monthly customer newsletter, the online Community.
  • § Assist with development of HMS monthly employee newsletter.
  • § Coordinate with Director and others in department on social media postings and tracking, including blog, Twitter, and others as appropriate.
  • § Assist with development and maintenance of HMS product sheets.
  • § Research and write HMS case studies.
  • § Contribute to copy/coordinate updates for HMS brochures and other collateral material as needed.
  • § Coordinate (working with Director) development of content for landing pages and other advertising response information.
  • § Assist in developing "campaigns" and message strategy across HMS communications platforms to promote various initiatives and strategies, including annual Expos, new products, and the like.
  • § Assists with department-wide projects as needed; for example, annual Expo communications.
  • § Manage outside vendors as needed.
  • § Demonstrate ability to track projects and analyze results.

OTHER REQUIREMENTS / SKILLS (EDUCATION, SOFTWARE, HARDWARE, ETC.)
  • ¡ Requires a bachelor's degree and 3-5 years of experience in the field of healthcare and/or IT.
  • ¡ A certain degree of creativity and latitude is expected.
  • ¡ Good interpersonal skills that include the ability to effectively communicate in both writing and verbally.
  • ¡ Excellent written, proofreading, and verbal communication skills.
  • ¡ Must be able to efficiently and independently manage projects and issues;
  • ¡ Must posses and have proven problem resolution skills;
  • ¡ Must be detail oriented, organized, and have the ability to multi-task.
  • ¡ Ability to demonstrate supportive relationships with peers, clients, partners, and corporate executives.
  • ¡ Must be flexible and work with a high level of initiative.
  • ¡ Must have the ability to remain professional under high pressure situations.
  • ¡ Ability to retain and protect confidential material.
  • ¡ Strong computer skills in Microsoft Office applications (i.e., Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.)
  • ¡ Negative pre-employment drug and background tests.

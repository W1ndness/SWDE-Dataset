Job ID: 7311843
Position Title: Advanced Modeling & Simulation Developer
Industry: Consumer Package Goods (CPG)
Posted: October 22, 2010
Job Function: Supply Chain Management
Job Type: Full-Time

This position performs advanced modeling & simulation with a primary objective of optimizing HbI’s administrative and manufacturing processes.  The position works collaboratively with HbI’s Lean function to contribute to the development of new principles and concepts, while solving unusually complex technical problems and providing innovative solutions; enabling HbI to consistently deliver high-quality results.  The scope of this position includes functions and processes that support $4.0 billion in sales, involve more than 190,000 products, and span the globe with more than 150 manufacturing locations.

 

Incumbent supports the AM&S team by organizing modeling activities and works both independently and collaboratively to conceive, develop, and refine models using both advanced software modeling tools and non-advanced modeling tools that include: Monte Carlo simulation, discrete event simulation, and optimization using linear programming techniques and tools.  Incumbent will apply advanced technical principles, theories, and concepts to the design and development of complex simulation and optimizations. The AM&S Developer works under consultative direction toward predetermined long-range goals and objectives, as assignments are often dictated by HbI functional area demand.

 

Specific value added services provided by this position include: development of simulations, optimization models utilizing linear programming techniques, and complex analysis.  Incumbent will assist with the development of reports that credibly communicate AM&S results to many levels of the organization, across all functional areas and include interactions with Senior Management.

On a daily basis, develop, verify, validate, document, and perform analysis on simulation and optimization models using Arena, Crystal Ball, LogicNet Plus, or other optimization and simulation software.
• Conduct optimization and simulation development, integration, verification and validation
• Develop, run, and evaluate optimization and simulation models
• Verify and validate models utilizing appropriate verification/validation techniques
• Assist with problem definition and model design
• Adhere to project timelines and milestones
• Support the development of analysis plans, performance of analysis, and report generation.
2. On a frequent basis, prepares results of optimization and simulation efforts for presentation. The ability to credibly produce the following is a critical job function:
• Statistical data: tables, charts, and graphs
• Evidence relevant to cause and effect
• Evidence relevant to decision making
• Fundamental strategy and analytical design of models and simulations

3. Works closely with AM&S Senior Manager to acquire data inputs and analyze data utilizing valid statistical methods. Critical data analyzed include variables such as sales demand, economic factors, engineering assumptions, and numerous operational KPI’s.  The ability to insure accuracy of data assumptions across this broad array of variables is a critical job function.
4. Interfaces with HbI functional areas (customers) on technical issues associated with model credibility requirements. Work is checked through consultation and agreement with customers and in conjunction with formal review by the Senior Manager AM&S.
5. Conduct advanced modeling and simulation training at Middle Management and lower levels. The purpose being to advance the knowledge of the appropriate application of AM&S techniques to improve and optimize processes.


Job Requirements

Required Qualifications 

  • Bachelor of Science from an accredited college or university in Engineering, Operations Research, Statistics, Mathematics, Management Science, or Computer Science.
  • Strong knowledge of AM&S techniques.
  • Strong knowledge of Arena and/or other discrete event simulation software.
  • Strong knowledge of statistics as relevant to simulation analysis.
  • Proven interpersonal skills and the ability to work well with others in a matrix organization are critical.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills are essential.
  • Must work effectively in a fast-paced, multi-tasking environment.
  • Ability to work in a multicultural environment.
  • Mastery of MS Office tools is required.
  • Working knowledge of relational databases.
  • Proven Data Mining capability.

 

      Desired Qualifications 

  • Masters degree from an accredited college or university in Engineering, Operations Research, Statistics, Mathematics, Management Science, or Computer Science.
  • Working knowledge of Crystal Ball and/or other Monte Carlo simulation software.
  • Lean/6 Sigma experience is a significant plus.
  • Basic Foreign Language Skills.

To qualify, applicants must be legally authorized to work in the United States and should not require now, or in the future, sponsorship for employment visa status.

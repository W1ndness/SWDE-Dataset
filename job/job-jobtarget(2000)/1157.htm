HID Global is the trusted leader in providing access and ID management solutions for the delivery of secure identity. We are focused on creating customer value worldwide with technology-based access solutions, issuance solutions, embedded technology solutions and logistics technology solutions.

Headquartered in Irvine, California, HID Global has over 1,900 employees worldwide and operates international offices that support more than 100 countries. HID Global is an ASSA ABLOY Group brand.

 

The Sales Application Engineer will provide application engineering and sales support to the Americas for the Industry & Logistics, Food & Animal, and Cards & Inlay business segments.  Engineering support will include qualifying customers to make sure HID product can achieve the customer requirements.  Assisting customers making frequency, tag, and reader choices.  Assisting customers with technical questions regarding set up, maintenance, and troubleshooting their systems.  Assist sales in the CPO (Customer Product Opportunity) and SPO (Standard Platform Option) process that required documenting changes to our existing or development of new products.  Must be a self starter with a strong work ethic. This position will initially be half application engineering support and half RFID sales.

 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

 

·          Manage and train other application engineers when the need arises to hire additional resources

·          Effectively communicate, present, and sell to existing and potential customers by himself/herself

·          Regularly update sales activities into Salesforce

·          Cold calling customers

·          Following up on leads provided by managers and HID inside sales

·          Account management and growth of existing customer base

·          Support customers by following their orders from placement to delivery

·          Assist in market research to discover new potential applications for RFID

·          Up to 50% travel to visit customers domestically and internationally

·          Provide technical support for customers in the Americas

·          Assist sales in our CPO and SPO process

·          Assist in integrating HID products into customer applications

·          Understand HID’s entire product portfolio and assist customers and HID sales on making the proper selection

·          Able to project manage solutions for customers

·          Develop application related solutions

·          Generate and present technical concepts to customers to assist in the sales process

·          Prepare and execute technical product training for customers

·          Development of application notes and support at documentation generation

·          Perform some benchmarking tests

 

 


Job Requirements

Required Knowledge / Experience:

 

·          BS in Electrical Engineering or similar

·          Five years of experience in the RFID marketplace

·          Knowledge on RFID tags & reader & antenna (HF, LF, UHF)

·          Experience in basic programming for demo software with reader

·          Flexible and highly motivated

·          Very good knowledge required in the English language.

·          Spanish or other languages is a plus.

·          Must be able to travel internationally.

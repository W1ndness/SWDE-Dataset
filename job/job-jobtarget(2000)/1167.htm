The Fluke Corporation is the world leader in the manufacture, distribution and service of electronic test tools and software. We are a dynamic market leader with sound financial health. Fluke has achieved the number one or number two position in every market in which it competes. The Fluke brand has a reputation for portability, ruggedness, safety, ease of use and rigid standards of quality. A wholly owned subsidiary of Danaher Corporation (www.danaher.com), Fluke is a multi-national corporation headquartered in the greater Seattle area. We offer an entrepreneurial environment that is team-centered, customer-driven, quality-focused, and growth-oriented. It is driven by the phenomenally successful Danaher Business System. Working at Fluke gives you access to a robust career development process and challenging, “stretch” opportunities.
We’re seeking a talented Regional Product Manager for Fluke Calibration products. The ideal candidate will have strong technical expertise with the ability to identify and recommend solutions for customers, and be responsible for achieving sales quotas for a product line.
1. Job Purpose:
  •  Resonsible for generating sales and supporting the sales efforts of others leveraging product/services, industry, and/or technology expertise.  
  • Maintains up to date technical and product knowledge and experience in order to support or close a sale, as well as to identify and close additional opportunities in cooperation with assigned sales account manager. 
  • Typically has shared quota.
 
2.  Primary Responsibilities:
  • Promotes Fluke Calibration solutions and achieves sales quota for product by building rapport with customers and selling to and supporting their needs. Identifies needs by phone and in person, performs demos during customer visits, and assists customers in understanding how our solutions can support their needs.
  • Effectively uses Fluke Calibration resources, customer relationship management system, and collaborates with marketing, engineering and others to secure orders, renew orders, cross-sell with other Fluke Calibration sales associates, or sell warranty or service programs.
  • Maintains records and develop reports to track product activity.
  • Keeps customers appraised of new products and services.
  •  Provides monthly sales projections and larger order forecasts to assigned manager.
3. Working Conditions and Basic Requirements:
Frequent travel, long hours may be required

Job Requirements
  • BS degree or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Proven, successful ability to sell as evidenced by 3-5 years related experience; technical expertise to sell and demo appropriate equipment.
  • Self motivated; good communication and relationship skills.
  • Proven ability to initiate communication and execute problem solving and sales strategies.

    Other:

    Frequent travel of up to 30%

    • All external hiring is contingent upon the successful completion of a pre-employment drug screen and a criminal background check.

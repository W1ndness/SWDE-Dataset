HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.

Monitors, oversees and guides the solutioning for designated pursuits (generally <$100 million). Coordinates the technical resourcing for opportunities to ensure the appropriate team is assigned and operating at optimal utilization. Manages Solution Leaders. Supports multiple deals simultaneously, as needed. Sets strategy and direction. Understands and helps others understand the deal/solution from multiple perspectives including technology, price, approach and delivery.

 

Effortlessly navigates internal and customer organizations. Is considered a "trusted advisor" by customers and team. Interfaces with, and influences, customer teams and leaders, as well as internal HP teams and leaders.

 

Critical Competencies to Drive Business Results

Role Excellence

 

 

Portfolio Influencing/Shaping

  • Provides input to the development and/or modification of portfolio offers based on extensive business development contacts and experience
  • Assesses regional sales for new requirements or competitive features impacting offer attractiveness
  • Shapes the planning and development of other BUs to include offers in their bundled solutions
  • Customizes global offers to ensure optimum fit with local requirements
  • Advocates optimum pricing configurations based on sound assessment of area-of-control cost variables

 

Opportunity Profiling and Qualification

  • Profiles and qualifies opportunities rigorously to ensure sound application of HP's resources on significant deals
  • Critically assess client's IT operations to identify efficiency gaps and potential cost savings
  • Develops detailed account profiles as a basis for strategy development and sales pursuit execution
  • Orchestrates development of a compelling business case detailing associated cost reductions, benefits, TCO and ROI

 

Business Assessment Discovery

  • Consultatively engages clients in business strategy and pain-point management discussions to ensure thorough understanding of requirements and challenges from a business- rather than solution-driven perspective
  • Establishes the confidence and trust essential at client executive levels for frank and open exploration of business issues or core business strategies
  • Prepares, organizes and expertly drives discovery and analysis workshops to explicate business vision, delineate opportunity scope and achieve executive endorsement
  • Translates business/IT challenges into concrete costs to the business associated with neglecting or avoiding remedies

 

Solution Requirements Analysis (Services)

  • Incorporates understanding of customer's business objectives with technical service acumen to identify solution scope & requirements
  • Assesses and narrows technical service alternatives for meeting customer's business needs
  • Determines site-specific IT parameters/constraints impacting solution
  • Determines specific solution functional requirements, based on such things as number of modules, number of concurrent users, use or proximity challenges, and uptime requirements

 

Technical Solution Design (Services)

  • Creates a solution vision and design framework for guiding and synthesizing the technical contributions of specialists
  • Demonstrates a profound understanding of design methodology - ITIL, ITSM
  • Creates a solution vision and design options as a framework to assess the technical dimension of business challenges
  • Determines a solution's fit to offers, gaps requiring customization, and the resources needed to build the solution
  • Articulates an overall design and project plan to guide the work of technical specialists
  • Synthesizes and balances the input of specialists with pricing considerations to determine the final design

 

Solution Acumen

  • Demonstrates comfort with IT and/or industry, solution, product, service knowledge -- easily integrates/applies these perspectives to solving business needs
  • Builds solution credibility with customers
  • Incorporates up-to-date understanding of IT-related innovations & trends into account activities
  • Uses IT knowledge effectively to advance a sale 

 

Solution Costing/Resourcing

  • Appropriately sizes the resources and costs involved with solutions to advance the sale, optimally position HP as the preferred vendor, and ensure HP profitability and margin
  • Develops creative strategies to bridge costs for custom requirements with standard offers
  • Engages in due diligence throughout the sales cycle to uncover root needs, hidden risks and delivery challenges
  • Assembles, validates and communicates deal costing to ensure accuracy and protect the business interests of HP

 

Solution Advocacy (Services)

  • Partners with extended teams to compellingly position proposed solutions both internally and to the customer
  • Articulates solutions in a way that translates technical benefits into a compelling case centered on business challenges
  • Authors or reviews the technical components of proposals for validity and the accuracy of associated costs
  • Collaborates with others to ensure satisfactory compliance to SOAR review requirements
  • Documents solutions and builds case collateral to advance repeatable solutions and best practices within HP

 

Pursuit Plan Coaching/Strategy Review

  • Where warranted, supports pursuit  teams in developing well-targeted pursuit plans to ensure the successful capture of business
  • Ensures the accuracy of value propositions in proposals and pursuit-related communications
  • Coaches sales teams on how to position solutions for different industries, management levels and organizational functions
  • Collaborates with pursuit leadership in developing effective client sales engagement plans and pursuit strategies

 

HP ES Business Context

  • Understands the market segments sold into, the business challenges addressed by Enterprise Services solutions, and HP's strategy for market penetration
  • Understands HP's marketing strategy for Enterprise Services - direction, priorities and major initiatives
  • Understands Enterprise Services' market segments in area-of-responsibility and typical sales opportunities
  • Understands the business challenges and drivers confronting potential Enterprise Services sales opportunities (e.g., customer business goals, market pressures, competitive landscape, service cost, contract management challenges, coverage scope)
  • Understands potential customer sponsors and other interested parties for Enterprise Services (e.g., CxOs, directors)

 

Negotiation

  • Manages contractual negotiations to protect HP's business interests while at the same time maintaining customer satisfaction
  • Assesses contractual requirements for feasibility to reflect attendant changes in client's business requirements
  • Conducts negotiations in a way that maintains the HP/client partnership by ensuring win/win outcomes

 

Negotiating/Gaining Commitment

  • Helps align all stakeholder interests to create a win/win balance that demonstrates mutual benefit, increases the probability of commitment, advances sales and contributes to future partnering
  • Handles objections by understanding what clients want and how the solution achieves these results
  • Adapts messages and builds value propositions to gain buy-in from multiple stakeholders in customer organizations

 

Business Value Proposition

  • Applies an in-depth understanding of the market, HP initiatives, goals, and portfolio/solutions
  • Maintains a sound understanding of the market realities
  • Understands business portfolio offerings to create value proposition messaging
  • Shapes value propositions to ensure that they resonate with geography- and industry-specific verticals
  • Articulates business value to clients, internal stakeholders, and team members

 

Program/Project Management          

  • Understands and applies program/project management methods and processes to define, plan, cost, resource, track and ensure the accomplishment of targeted goals
  • Effectively organizes and manages project work
  • Reconfigures project components as needed to better manage tasks and resources, and maintain clarity of purpose even under trying situations

 

Program Team Management

  • Effectively manages and develops project team to achieve project/program objectives
  • Assesses team capabilities and capitalizes on available experience to engage the right skill-sets at the right time in a project's lifecycle
  • Supervises the work of indirect reports and influences their contribution to program support and effectiveness
  • Builds group cohesion and individual commitment to business goals and personal excellence
  • Uses technology and personal skills to overcome proximity challenges to orchestrating remote team activities
  • Services Sales Fundamentals

 

Sales Facilitation

  • Applies influence and organizational savvy to advance sales opportunities externally, with clients, and internally, within HP
  • Capitalizes on corporate status/position to facilitate entry to potential opportunities, generate customer interest in HP's offerings and establish consultative relationship
  • Expedites HP internal approvals and buy-in through "internal selling" to key decision-makers  
  • Troubleshoots threats to effective selling or order fulfillment to minimize their impact on the business

 

C-Level Partnering

  • Contributes to enduring executive relationships that establish HP' consultative professionalism and promote its total solution capabilities at the highest levels of the client's organization
  • Applies understanding of C-level (CEO, CFO, CIO, COO) concerns, metrics, and decision making to sales planning
  • Ensures that proposed solutions address benefits and needs of interest to CEOs, CFOs, and CIOs
  • Ensures adequate preparation and contributions of account team members at senior-level management meetings

 

Resource Optimization

  • Applies partner and internal resources effectively and efficiently to advance sales opportunities
  • Ensures the appropriate selection and use of resources required to meet business objectives
  • Builds commitment for resources based on a clear formulation of need and the benefits that will result from such a commitment

 

HP Sales Initiatives/Goals

  • Closely monitors HP sales initiatives and goals to ensure alignment with account planning and sales activities
  • Skillfully incorporates HP's go-to-market strategies into sales activities
  • Identifies opportunities for advancing HP sales goals

 

Financial/Business Acumen

  • Exhibits authoritative business and financial acumen to develop meaningful business or operational recommendations
  • Understands what it takes to manage a business and uses these insights to gain a better understanding of business needs
  • Understands and accurately positions the financial benefits associated with business propositions or action plans
  • Develops and applies financial models to create value propositions using appropriate data (e.g., profitability ratios, operating ratios, leverage ratios)

 

HP Partner Resources

  • Effectively accesses appropriate partner resources associated with a solution and guiding account strategy
  • Demonstrates up-to-date knowledge of key solution partners, their HP management interface, appropriate partner engagement channels, and rules of engagement
  • Demonstrates profound understanding of the business relationship between HP and its key alliance partners, distributors and resellers - mutual benefits and added-value for the client
  • Optimizes partner involvement in the sales process through prudent partner engagement (e.g., builds the right skill mix to increase the probability of a win; ensures a success that can be built on/extended in the future)

 

HPES/BU Business Context

  • Understands the market segments sold into, the business challenges addressed by Understands HP's marketing strategy for BU represented - direction, priorities and major initiatives
  • Understands BU services' market segments in area-of-responsibility and typical sales opportunities (e.g., investment protection, multi-vendor coverage or service contract management)
  • Understands the business challenges and drivers confronting potential technology services sales opportunities (e.g., customer business goals, market pressures, competitive landscape, service cost, contract management challenges, coverage scope)
  • Understands potential customer sponsors and other interested parties for technology services (e.g., CxOs, directors)

 

HPES/BU Solutions

  • Understands HPES/BU solutions in area-of-responsibility- what they consist of, product roadmaps, key technology concepts, and the competitive landscape in which they are sold
  • Understands the services roadmap (e.g., what is being introduced, what phased out, what consolidated or broken out)
  • Understands the portfolio of services and how to position these solutions within this framework
  • Understands how HPES offers map into competitive service counterparts and how best to position against the competition (e.g., major and niche vendors)

 

HPES Solution Interfaces

  • Understands how services in area-of-responsibility fit within or interface with the sales of other solutions - HPES, other GBUs, or HP's partner strategies
  • Understands how a specific service opportunity may suggest alternative HPES services
  • Understands how a technology service opportunity may suggest additional, integrated, cross-BU opportunities (e.g., server, storage, software, consulting, etc.)
  • Understands HPES partners - who they are, role played in sales pursuit or implementation, and how to involve in an opportunity

 

HP Sales Productivity Tools/Processes

  • Systematically applies HP sales planning & productivity tools/processes to manage work
  • Uses HP sales processes, systems and sales automation tools appropriately to promote consistent, efficient and systematic sales processes -- planning, price quoting/configuration, order tracking & fulfillment, etc.
  • Uses sales tools, contacts and collateral effectively to gain customer commitment and advance the sale for HP  
  • Uses HP's Customer-Driven Sales Methodology

Extensive, combined experience in all of the following areas:

  • Technical (design/development)
  • Delivery/client-facing
  • Sales or sales support/pursuit
  • Business and financial acumen
  • Market (including competitors) and  industry (knowing our clients business)
  • Contract, negotiation, and legal
  • Financial modeling, Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) approach, client business economics
  • HP Portfolio and services, and best practices
  • Proven leader and motivator of people, team and organizations
  • Proven track record of customer interaction and building successful customer relationships

 

 

Key Traits:

  • Drive, commitment and passion for success
  • Work ethic - willing to do what it takes to get the job done
  • Patience
  • Open minded
  • Trustworthy

Key Responsibilities:

  • Ensures appropriate resources are assigned
  • Coordinates cross-opportunity reviews and concurrent opportunity flow control
  • Provides mentoring/coaching to solution leads, tower leads and SMEs
  • Defines new best practices and standards based on past pursuits and implements process improvements and best practices in solution development
  • Works closely with the solution leads and tower leads to identify the areas where the cost structure can be affected
  • Assists bid manager in resolving issues related to solution and costing activities
  • Assists in multiple DSC governance checkpoints such as driving the solution
  • Review and costing checkpoints through to successful completion
  • Assists in the identification of cost saving opportunities - i.e. replacing non-standard software with HP recommended and/or leveragable software
  • Attends Win Strategy Lab and appropriate Yield reviews
  • Ensures the solution lead or pursuit lead engages alliance partners at appropriate stage
  • Ensures the solution components, costs and pricing aligns with standards and best practices
  • Implements action plans to remedy any deficiencies in solution, cost or pricing models
  • Improves service offerings delivery and influences the reduction of associated costs
  • Ensures best practices are consistently applied across opportunities
  • Ensures solutions are complete and competitive
  • Assists and supports in solution development and costing of software, hardware and third party contracts
  • Highlights HP risk exposures and actions to mitigate
  • Assists and supports contract negotiations

Education Systems is a recognized leader in providing student enrollment management software solutions for recruitment, retention, and financial aid featuring interactive internet portals. We are currently seeking a Project Manager to join our team. The Project Manager works under the direction of the Product Manager with the production team to develop and maintain detailed project plans for one or more EMAS Pro solutions.

Basic Functions and Responsibilities

Project Management

  • Creates and executes project work plans
  • Revises project work plans as appropriate to meet changing needs and requirements
  • Identifies resources needed and assigns individual responsibilities
  • Manages day-to-day operational aspects of project schedules and scope
  • Reviews deliverables prepared by team
  • Effectively applies corporate methodology and enforces project standards
  • Prepares for project reviews and quality assurance procedures
  • Minimizes our exposure and risk on project
  • Ensures project documents are complete, current, and stored appropriately

Project Accounting

  • Tracks and reports team hours and expenses on a weekly basis
  • Manages project budget and associated costs
  • Publishes regular project status reports

Communication

  • Facilitates team and client meetings effectively
  • Holds regular status meetings with project team
  • Keeps project team well informed of all significant changes
  • Effectively communicates relevant project information to leadership and project team
  • Delivers engaging, informative, well-organized presentations
  • Resolves and/or escalates issues in a timely fashion
  • Understands how to communicate difficult/sensitive information in a tactful manner

Job Requirements

Qualifications

Technical Skills

  • Understands application programming, databases and system design
  • Understands client/server and SaaS architectures
  • Can quickly come up to speed on ESI technical and project capabilities
  • Maintains awareness of new and emerging technologies and the potential application to projects
  • Understands Software Development CMM models & processes
  • Knowledge of project management tools

General Skills

  • Requires a bachelor's degree and at least 2-3 years of experience
  • Ability to remain highly organized, multi-task, and meet tight deadlines
  • Experience working on projects in critical path
  • Experience working with distributed and international teams
  • Schedule flexibility for work meetings and project coordination calls outside of normal business hours
  • Ability to think outside-of-the-box to identify creative solutions to issues
  • Team player – works well with others
  • Candidate should have demonstrated skills in the following:
  • Project management experience with comparable software solution development
  • Professional work ethic and values
  • Commitment to individual outcomes & team success
  • Critical and creative thinking
  • Communications skills excellence - oral and written, math and verbal

Qualifications Preferred

  • Preferably PMO certified  
  • Demonstrated entrepreneurial aptitude
  • Higher Education software development experience
  • Awareness of Higher Education software and services industry unique factors
  • Location La Jolla, CA (San Diego area)

For consideration, submit your resume, references, and salary requirements to sdat@emaspro.com

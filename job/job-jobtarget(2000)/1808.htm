OnBase OnLine, Hyland Software’s Cloud Computing division, is seeking a passionate developer to help us create the next generation of our market leading SaaS platform. The Systems Automation and Integration Developer will be responsible for orchestrating common IT processes across a variety of technology platforms within our eight global datacenters. As a member of our Strategic Research & Development team, this position will work in a fast paced “skunk works” environment that rewards innovation, agility, and quality. We are seeking flexible candidates who are comfortable enhancing existing applications, architecting new prototypes, and delivering fully realized solutions to our employees, partners, and end users. The successful candidate will also utilize a consultative communications style to coordinate requirements gathering, “train the trainer”, and troubleshooting activities across organizational boundaries.

Position Responsibilities Include:

  • Write production quality code that is maintainable, easy to troubleshoot, and efficient
  • Develop and implement unit tests
  • Create functional and technical documentation as required
  • Conduct “train the trainer” style training presentations to other employees
  • Help to define, document, and utilize consistent coding standards
  • Solicit and document functional and technical requirements from all stakeholders
  • Learn new skills and API’s through a combination of self-study, hands on experience, and formal classroom training
  • Troubleshoot and solve production issues under the supervision of the Strategic R&D Team Leader

This position is available to qualified individuals throughout the United States and Canada. The selected candidate may work from Hyland’s headquarters in Westlake OH or any of our regional offices. Exceptional candidates may be permitted to telecommute from a home-based office.


Job Requirements
  • Proficient .NET programming in C#
  • Strong SQL and data modeling skills
  • Strong interest in Cloud Computing, virtualization, service monitoring, and/or ITIL
  • Broad knowledge of networking and Microsoft Windows® technologies
  • Proven track record of delivering projects on time and on budget
  • Passionate and positive “get it done” attitude
  • Ability to communicate effectively both in writing and verbally
  • Ability to work through a problem effectively and provide a solution
  • Ability to work independently as well as in teams
  • Commitment to lifelong learning
  • 4 year degree in computer science/related degree or equivalent work experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • UI development using WPF, Silverlight and/or ASP.NET
  • Hands on experience with one or more of the following API/Frameworks: nServiceBus, nHibernate, VMware vSphere PowerCLI/vCloud, PRISM, Microsoft Enterprise Library, Microsoft Entity Framework, MEF, Windows/Azure AppFabric
  • Hands on experience developing workflows, business intelligence solutions, and/or integrations with Microsoft Dynamics CRM and/or SharePoint
  • Professional experience with Information Technology Service Management (ITSM) best practices like ITIL, COBIT, or MOF
  • Familiarity or interest in common data center concepts such as data protection/replication, load balancing, segmentation, load balancing, service monitoring, SLA’s, data privacy, multi-tenancy, disaster recovery, and redundancy
  • Familiarity or interest in modern application architectures/patterns including enterprise service bus, durable messaging, CQRS, ORM, Smart Clients, P2P, distributed caches, computational grids, publish/subscribe, IoV, MvvM, DDD, and UI Composition
  • Polyglot programming using languages like Python, Ruby, Powershell, Lua, VBScript, VB.NET, shell scripts, and F#
  • Comfortable installing, configuring, and using Linux-based operating systems

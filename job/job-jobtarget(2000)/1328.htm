HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. The WFP Program Manager II is a part of the Global HPCO Center of Expertise team responsible for driving workforce planning and analytics. This role will lead and participate within HP's high-impact Workforce Strategy, Planning, Anaytics team where your superior analytic and communications skills will be challenged to deliver data-driven, insightful recommendations against HP's highest priority workforce opportunities. 
 

? Leads and drives to improve the process of developing workforce plans aligned to business or function needs.
? Oversees data collection, provides superior analyses and interpretation/evaluation of workforce metrics.
? Develops advanced and innovative statistical/financial analytical models and techniques.
? Monitors business progress against the execution of workforce plans and ensures plans are aligned with corporate principles.
? Applies superior subject matter expertise in internal and external workforce trends to evaluate workforce plans, identify gaps and propose solutions to Human Resource (HR) and business/function executives.
? Ensures workforce planning processes are cost-effective and efficient.
? Delivers data driven insights to support actionable strategic workforce direction.
? Collaborates with executives across the company (business, HR, finance, etc) in the strategic planning, implementation, and monitoring of workforce plans. Builds and presents presentation to HR and business executives.
? Analyzes external industry trends, competitive threats, and expansion opportunities to integrate into strategic workforce plans.. Education and Experience Required:
 
? First level university degree; advanced university degree in business preferred.
? Typically 10+ years experience in finance and/or business administration, preferably in a workforce planning or strategic Human Resource (HR) role.
 
Knowledge and Skills Required:
 
? Superior analytical and modeling capabilities including the interpretation of analysis/data to executives.
? Superior business and financial acumen.
? Superior program management, process development, leadership, influencing, and consulting skills.
? Superior communications (written/verbal), presentation, facilitation, and collaboration skills.
? Ability to make strategic decisions that are forward thinking and congruent with industry trends.
 
Critical Competencies to Drive Business Results:
 
Program Management & Optimization

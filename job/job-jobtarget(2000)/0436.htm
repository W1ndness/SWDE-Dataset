Boulder, Colorado - Only 2.5 weeks of call per year - Outpatient only

 

Rocky Mountain Family Medicine has been providing family practice services to the Denver metropolitan area since 2002.  With nine locations, over 12 physician providers, and 6 mid-level providers, Rocky Mountain Family Medicine has expanded its service line to include a weight loss management program, physical therapy, worker's compensation care and ENT services and plans to continue to expand its presence in the metro area.

 

RMFM seeks a full time BC/BE family medicine physician for its Longmont practice.  Join two urgent care physicians and a physician's assistant in an established outpatient only practice.  Monday - Friday 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.  Inpatient care provided by Longmont United Hospital hospitalist services.

 

Rocky Mountain Family Medicine offers a competitive compensation and benefits package.  Relocation assistance provided.  Partnership opportunity after one year.

 

The residents of Longmont, Colorado, located in Boulder County, won’t be surprised to learn that it was twice named one of the "Top 100 Best Places to Live" in the United States - once in 2006 and again in 2008.  The "Top 100 Best Places to Live", as designated by Money magazine, identifies small livable cities with the best possible blend of good jobs, low crime, quality schools, reasonable home prices, an abundance of open space and plenty to do.  According to the Software & Information Industry Association, the Boulder/Longmont area has the highest concentration of software-related jobs in the nation.  With a vibrant restaurant scene, excellent schools and over 1500 acres of parks, Longmont is the place for you and your family!

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Ink Services Business Product Manager
 
Job Description
 
The Inkjet Supplies Business (ISB) has committed to improve the consumer printing experience with new business models designed to drive more printing while addressing customer concerns about the cost of printing.  As part of a new "start up" organization, we have an job opening for a product manager to drive the definition and development of a new consumer ink services business model. This position will be responsible for managing and driving the marketing strategy and solutions roadmap for this new business model.
 
Key Responsibilities
 
?         Work with our marketing team to create and execute consumer ink services marketing strategy and plan.
?         Define, test and execute solution offer including pricing, positioning and messaging to make sure it delivers a compelling value to consumers.
?         Develop roadmaps, drive implementation and execute introduction of an ink services business model.
?         Develop end to end business model addressing channel and direct implementation requirements.
?         Develop and implement new "go to market" ideas for reaching consumers using social media and internet marketing technologies.
?         Leverage market research, business model benchmarking and competitor analysis to define and refine offering.
?         Lead definition and execution of market research to understand customer needs and assess viability of potential new business models.
?         Develop strategies to deliver excellent sales and after-sales services and maximize consumer sales, customer satisfaction and retention.
?         Integrate with a cross-functional team to drive the definition of the product and solution portfolio and all deliverables required to advance program through product life cycle.
?         Work with world-wide, GBU and regional partners to determine the "go to market" strategy and an introduction process that assures an effective launch.
?         Influence strategic choices with executive management.
?         Work with external partners to provide complete solutions and seamless workflows.
?         Ensure consistent implementation of solutions roadmap across the hardware portfolio.

Lead SAP Basis Administrator

First Solar

Perrysburg, Ohio, United States

Job ID: 7109114
Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: Lead SAP Basis Administrator
Required Travel: 0-10%
Industry: Manufacturing
Posted: October 18, 2010
Job Function: Administrator, Senior Administrator, Team Lead
Min Experience: 5-7 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Contact Person: Brian Smith
Email Address:
Phone: 419-662-6856

The BASIS Lead will be responsible for overseeing the BASIS team with a focus on results and deep customer partnerships. The focus of this position will be to provide both system and application administration to ensure the stable operation of SAP systems (ECC 6.0) and related components running on Windows operating systems. This person will also resolve advanced administration issues and mentor BASIS team members to improve effectiveness and technical skills.

Experience:

  • 7+ years of hands-on SAP BASIS experience with an emphasis in configuration, analysis and troubleshooting required.
  • 2+ years in management/lead capacity
  • Experience with performance tuning, support/enhancement packs and patches, system refreshes (sandbox, dev and test environments), SAP security maintenance and audit compliance required.
  • Experience with Transport Packages, job scheduling/troubleshooting, Java and ABAP stack maintenance required.
  • Familiarity with formal Software Development Lifecycle process required.
  • Experience supporting applications for medium-to-large size company, preferably with multiple locations and international footprint.
  • Experience in multiple manufacturing-related systems (Supply Chain, Inventory, Shop Floor, Quality, Warranty, CRM, etc.) a plus.
  • Experience working with global end-user base a plus.
  • Experience with the Microsoft Solutions Product Stack (Biztalk, BI tools, .NET, SQL Server 2000/2005, MOSS 2007, CRM) a plus.
  • Experience and/or ability to organize and collaborate with various resources (both internal and external) to drive resolution of issues or completion of small-to-medium sized tasks/projects.

Essential Functions & Responsibilities

 

  • Responsible for participating in the design and architecture of technical solutions. Create System Architect Diagrams as it relates to the SAP environments. 
  • Assist in creating the roadmap and strategy for SAP solutions and the key components.
  • Help create and drive processes in the SAP environments.
  • Participate in business and technology planning sessions to anticipate future business/technology changes.
  • Responsible for ownership and administration of the SAP system and all related components (ECC, Portal, NetWeaver, GRC, SCM, BOBJ, etc)
  • Responsible for supporting development and enforcing the formalized Promote to Production process.
  • o Ensure change request and other controls are met as customizations are moved into production in accordance with First Solar's SDLC and Change Request processes.
  • Create and manage process for minimizing system downtime.
  • o Schedule downtime with users
  • o Manage planned maintenance downtime, troubleshoot when unplanned downtime occurs
  • o Responsible for documenting support activities.
  • Develop, implement and enforce new release rollout process for Change Requests, regulatory patches, new service packs and upgrades for primary system as well as 3rd party tools.
  • Monitor performance and perform tuning and troubleshooting of the technical environment - ensuring maximum performance.
  • Work with IT group to ensure recoverability (DR), integrity, access controls, availability, archiving, data modeling and database optimization.
  • Support application Service Level Agreement requirements.
  • Manage system user accounts (new/modified/retired) and application-specific security roles.
  • Assist with refreshing sandbox, development and testing instances on scheduled intervals.
  • Assist with troubleshooting and project specific tasks as required.
  • Help develop clear processes and workflows as part of enhancement and upgrade cycles.
  • Maintain control systems and comply with SOX regulations/policies/controls.
  • Exercises effective communication levels and fosters team building.
  • Adheres to all safety procedures and good housekeeping standards.
  • Rotational on-call support outside of normal 8-5X5 as necessary during critical times.

Job Requirements
  • Experience with SAP GRC, Enterprise Portal, NetWeaver and SQL Server 2005.
  • Strong knowledge of integrating applications with Windows OS platform and technologies (AD, LDAP, IIS etc.)
  • Proven ability and knowledge of application support.
  • Ability to handle multiple issues/tasks/projects of varying size simultaneously required.
  • Ability to communicate effectively on phone and via e-mail required.
  • Ability to share knowledge with internal support team as well as train end users highly desired.
  • Effectively manages issue escalation, resolution and root cause analysis with a focus on continuous improvement
  • Excellent communication and organization skills.
  • Proven interpersonal skills.

Physical Requirements:

 

  • Will sit or stand for long periods of time throughout the day.
  • Required to lift up to 25 lbs.
  • Will walk and climb stairs.
  • Will reach over shoulder heights and below waist.
  • Required to use hands to lift, handle, carry or feel.
  • May be required to kneel, bend, talk and hear.
  • Specific vision abilities are required.

All associates working at a First Solar manufacturing facility may be required to wear a respirator at any given time and thus, the ability to wear a respirator is a condition of employment and continued employment (requires little or no facial hair).

Solar Research Technician - Thermal Systems

Requisition #1599BR

 

Job/Research Summary                  

A Solar Research Technician position is available in the Thermal Systems Group of the National Renewable Energy Laboratory's (NREL) Electricity, Resources & Building Systems Integration Center. NREL is the nation's primary laboratory for research, development and deployment of renewable energy and energy efficiency technologies. The worker will assist in the development of R&D test equipment and experiments and perform other technician activities in support for the concentrating solar power (CSP) program as well as other thermal systems activities.

 

Job Duties                                                                

• Assists in the development of R&D test equipment and experiments. This includes working with engineers/scientists to modify and fabricating test equipment, setup experiments (this includes setting up computers and controls and monitoring equipment).
• Performs and maintains experiments.
* Operates manual and 3-axis CNC mill and manual lathe.
• Performs machining of steel, aluminum and plastics along with TIG welding.
• Read and understand basic electrical and flow schematics. Operation, maintenance and troubleshooting of controls involving 120V and -3P 208V.
• Will frequently be involved with project milestones, reports, and Safe Operating Procedures. Attention to detail is a must.
• Special attention to all aspects of safety is a priority. This is a very hands-on position.
* Must be proficient with manual mill and lathe (able to take machining test without direction).
* Has experience operating a CNC mill.

 

Required Education and Experience                                 

High School Diploma or equivalent and 7 or more years of experience or equivalent relevant education/experience.

Or, relevant Associate's Degree or certification program of comparable scope and 5 or more years of experience or equivalent relevant education/experience.

 

Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities                            

Full knowledge of specialized focused support functions. Complete acquaintance with and understanding of the general and detailed aspects of the supported activities, and their practical applications to problems and situations ordinarily encountered.

Proficient in research techniques and practices. Solid communication, interpersonal, project management and customer relations skills.

 

Additional Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities               

• Associate’s degree or certification program of comparable technical scope or equivalent relevant experience. Advanced special training/short courses in areas relating to job responsibilities.
• Must have experience with 3D Computer-Aided Drafting and Design (CADD) software.
• Must be a self-starter with the ability to prioritize and schedule to meet the needs of multiple experiments.
• Some experience overseeing work of other technicians.
• Should be able to lift 60 lbs. Occasional work at heights using ladders and lifts will be required.
• 7 years of relevant technical experience.

 

Preferred Qualifications

• Bachelor of Science in an engineering discipline.
• Experience using SolidWorks.
• Familiarity with data acquisition set-up and programs, including experience programming in LabView.

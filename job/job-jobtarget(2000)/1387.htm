Currently, Schwan's Shared Services, LLC. (Schwan’s), a subsidiary of The Schwan Food Company, is expanding our Information Services department. If you’re a professional with interests in requirements planning and analysis, and acceptance testing of new systems within assigned business unit, take a look at career opportunities at Schwan’s.

At Schwan’s, you’ll get a competitive pay and benefits package. If you’re interested in joining a global, privately owned company on the move, this is your opportunity. To find out more and to apply for these opportunities at Schwan’s, visit our website at: www.schwansjobs.com.
REQUIREMENTS
  • Ensure that the system meets the needs of the user by collecting requirements, identifying data source, analyzing and documentation
  • Demonstrates the ability to develop prototypes, develop business processes and workflow, and prepares testing ensuring that the systems continue to meet the needs of the Business Unit
  • Conducts the appropriate reviews of technical systems including, but not limited to the design, development, testing and documentation to maintain system integrity
  • Responsible for detailed design of systems and user training
  • Supports and maintains existing software by troubleshooting processes and demonstrates ability to identify and resolve problems on a 24-hour emergency need basis.
  • Demonstrates a good understanding of System Development Life Cycle processes and is able to apply the processes of The Schwan Food Company usage need and requirements
  • Maintains corporate compliance, regulatory policies and standards requirements
  • Offers excellent customer service with end-users (customers) to ensure systems continually meet or exceed the users or customers expectations
QUALIFICATIONS
Education: Bachelor's degree or equivalent
Years of related experience: 1– 3 years SAP work related experience
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities: Intermediate to advanced knowledge of systems development principles, theories and concepts to include ABAP coding & specs, BEx and basic debugging; experience with items such as workflow, archiving, DaRT and SOx processes, interpersonal and communications skills; advance proficiency in operation of a personal computer in a Windows environment to include the Microsoft Office Suite and Outlook; advanced proficiency with numeric data analysis; advanced basic understanding of top-down development planning; and advanced ability to utilize resources to keep up with the new technologies/functions; results oriented and fast leaner; may include travel for projects, training and seminars.
Let us tell you about The Schwan Food Company. We are the largest, branded frozen-food company in the United States. Schwan's® offers home delivery to customers throughout the 48 contiguous United States as well as operating with a large presence in the foodservice channel and high visibility in the retail frozen food aisle. The company has 19 brands including Tony's®, Red Baron®, Freschetta®, Edwards®, Mrs. Smith's and the Schwan's® brand.

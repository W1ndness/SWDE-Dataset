People's United Bank

Bridgeport, Connecticut, United States

Position Title: Communications Associate
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Industry: Banking / Accounting / Financial
Posted: October 26, 2010
Job Function: Community Relations
Min Experience: 2-3 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: https://www3.ultirecruit.com/PEO1003/JobBoard/JobD...
 

This position is responsible for performing communications functions and helping to create internal and/or external communications in support of division and/or organizational initiatives and strategies. This position will participate in the planning, creation and implementation of communications that support broader communications strategies using a variety of channels. 

 

Principal Responsibilities:

Sources, edits and writes a variety of communications for external and/or internal audiences, utilizing diverse communication channels that include, but are not limited to press releases, intranet/internet, e-mails and online newsletters.

  • Collaborates with other staff members or management on revisions while working within established guidelines, including deadlines, style and key messages.
  • Chooses and manipulates content and graphics appropriately for placement on the intranet and other online and hard copy communications vehicles.
  • Serves as a resource to intranet content managers by evaluating their needs and developing appropriate intranet sites. Advises on and edits site content and design. Maintains and responds to critical client needs as required.
  • Establishes and maintains effective working relationships with key areas throughout the organization. 
  • Enhances knowledge of strategic initiatives and direction and products and services to increase the effectiveness of communications as they relate to a specific topic or area of focus.
  • Serves as social media coordinator including:
    • Coordinate implementation of social media strategy as it develops, monitoring and posting approved content to appropriate social media channels.
    • Organize internal processes to generate content for social media channels, creating and managing the needed internal procedures and cross-functional teams.
    • Work with area experts - including Web content editor - creating events, content and community conversations and interactions.
    • Coordinate social media activity with the bank’s other marketing and web activities, ensuring we have an integrated approach, consistent messages and overall tone.
    • Track and measure success of our approach to each channel and proactively evolve our messaging and content to the needs of the community.
  • Assists on special projects or other assignments as directed by management.

 


Job Requirements
  • College degree or equivalent work experience required.
  • 2-4 years relevant experience.
  • Additional Job Requirements:
  • Certification/License
  • Behavioral/Technical Skills:
  • Strong written, verbal and presentation skills are needed to interact effectively with a wide range of internal and external contacts. Ability to write clear, concise, logical, factually accurate communications. 
  • Strong ability to foster and cultivate effective working relationships with internal and external clients.
  • Strong PC skills including , but not limited to, word processing, spreadsheets and presentation software as well as industry software such as Dream Weaver and Adobe Photoshop.  Ability to learn additional software as needed.
  • Strong research, organization and analytical skills and the ability to maintain a high level of accuracy while meeting demanding deadlines.
  • Project management skills are needed to manage project phases or smaller and less complex projects from inception through completion.
  • Ability to respond to multiple priorities and assignments to support management.
  • Knowledge of commonly used communications concepts, practices and procedures.
  • Work may involve sensitive and confidential information; discretion is essential.

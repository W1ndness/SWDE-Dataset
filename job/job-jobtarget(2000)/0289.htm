Title: Senior Manager, Information Technology Quality & Release Management
Location: MD, Gaithersburg - Corporate Headquarters
Req: 02962

Major Duties and Responsibilities (including supervising others):
The Sr. Manager, IT Quality and Release Management will have responsibility for developing, maintaining and executing processes and procedures to assure the quality of IT systems and solutions. The position will provide project leadership for regulated and critical business systems to ensure compliance with all applicable regulatory and operational requirements. The position will act as a business and quality liaison to understand operational compliance requirements and establish how the IT department meets those requirements.

The position will apply knowledge of IT, applicable regulations and business operations to develop adequate IT policies, procedures and job aids for IT and business personnel and will review and approve quality plans, computerized systems validation and testing projects as necessary. The position will apply knowledge of computer system lifecycle management, system development and delivery methods and change management to ensure conformance to internal policies and procedures and the quality of deliverables. The position will develop internal procedures, establish record keeping methods and practices and provide quality oversight to IT solution delivery and change management processes. The position will have direct supervision of several IT Quality Analysts and indirect supervision of all IT projects in the area of quality.

The position will ensure that appropriate procedures and quality standards are defined for Release Management and that changes to IT systems are developed and tested in adherence to IT policies, standards and procedures.

- Liaise with various business groups to understand how they use IT systems in order to assess whether systems should be included within the scope of various compliance areas (FDA, HIPAA, SOX, EU, etc.)
- Provide guidance to service delivery staff and consultants on development of documentation, such as requirements, specifications, test scripts and change control to ensure compliance with applicable regulatory standards and with IT policies and procedures.
- Assist users in understanding the fundamentals of software testing and validation
- Oversee testing and validation activities and assure quality of documentation
- Define the validation strategies for IT and support project teams in implementation
- Approves all system changes and releases back to production.
- Produce various progress reports and metrics


Job Requirements

Special Skills/Abilities:
- Thorough understanding of the IT aspects of pharmaceutical regulatory compliance, IS security, financial compliance and risk management processes.
- Knowledge of the various regulatory requirements affecting IT systems used in the manufacture of pharmaceutical and medicinal products as well as Sarbanes-Oxley and other financial control requirements.
- Ability to define, implement, and maintain project process guidance related to quality management, tailoring organizational processes and communicating with the project team as needed.
- Ability to plan for, coordinate, and conduct reviews of project deliverables for completeness, quality, and compliance with established project standards.
- CISA and/or CISM are desirable
- Knowledge of system performance testing and experience developing performance tests
- Excellent team management skills
- Strong written and verbal English communication skills and the ability to communicate effectively with people at all levels
- Excellent presentation, project management, problem-solving, training/coaching, and conflict resolution skills
- Proficient in MS Office, Visio and PowerPoint
- General knowledge of COBIT, Sarbanes Oxley, GAMP 4/5 and the PCAOB’s Audit Standard 5.

Job Complexity: Medium to high level of complexity

Supervision: Minimum supervision required (Supervision required, level of independence)

Education:
Bachelor’s degree in information technology or equivalent

Experience:
- At least 15 years work experience with 10 years of progressively more responsible management experience in an IT organization with demonstrated skills in solution delivery, software quality assurance and testing.
- At least 8 years experience, preferably in Pharmaceutical or Medical Device industries, with emphasis in the area of Quality and Compliance or equivalent combination of education and experience.
- Proven ability to provide leadership, management and technical guidance to a culturally diverse, technically oriented staff

Hawai'i Natural History Association (HNHA) seeks a full-time I.T. Systems Administrator at its headquarters located in Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park. Travel to other locations may be required.

HNHA is a nonprofit organization and National Park Service Cooperating Association. We inspire the discovery and stewardship of the natural and cultural heritage of Pacific Island parks.

POSITION DESCRIPTION:

Duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Manage HNHA's point of sale system (presently eight sales outlets)
  • Manage all hardware, software, and network functionality in our operations at headquarters, four additional Hawai'i Island parks, and Haleakalä NP on Maui
  • Evaluate software and hardware needs; take the lead in installation and implementation
  • Troubleshoot, diagnose and resolve hardware and software issues.
  • Coordinate and oversee contract information technology specialists as needed.
  • Design, implement and maintain computer data backup plan and system; help establish policies and procedures related to corporate network/server management
  • Coordinate and implement computer-based communications technology such as FTP, VPN, etc.
  • Develop and help conduct employee computer training program
  • Work with staff and service providers to design and implement website
  • Assists with site maintenance, feature upgrades, and related e-media
  • Assist or manage other projects as needed

WAGES/BENEFITS:
Starting Wage:    $ 18.16  per hour
Starting Date:    December, 2010
Benefits:    Medical (inc. drug, dental, vision); paid holiday, vacation and sick leave; employee retirement plan

Deadline:    November 19, 2010 (deadline may be extended until suitable candidate is selected.)

Job Requirements


MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND NECESSARY SKILLS:

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science or related field
  • Knowledge of Mac platform hardware, software, and networks (LAN and WAN)
  • Three years previous experience and/or training that includes personal computer use, network administration, technical support, preferably in a Mac environment
  • Knowledge of computerized point of sale systems
  • Knowledge of website design and e-media
  • General office administration experience
  • Ability to keep current on new technologies and industry trends
  • Flexibility to respond to emergency infrastructure/network servers issues outside of standard business hours
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, and able to manage multiple priorities and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to work with a variety of people in a positive, productive manner
  • Exhibits behavior and performs job in a manner which supports HNHA's vision and values, teamwork, excellence, attitude, and mutual respect, as well as cooperation, responsibility, initiative and leadership

Are you an experienced Interactive Creative Director? Your work shows digital expertise and mastery of consumer and product insights? Clarity of thought? An innate ability to sell B2C? Are you client-side with a great Fortune 500 in-house agency and want a new challenge? Maybe agency-side and tired of burning the candle at both ends? You want challenging work, creativity and impact. But you want REAL work/life balance with one of the most stable, fastest growing financial institutions in the country? We need you. Even if you weren't looking already, send us your resume. You’ll oversee two talented interactive creative teams. And drive the creative strategy for online and digital campaigns for consumer acquisition.
  • You must have experience with corporate website marketing
  • You must have experience with B2C marketing
  • You like leading and influencing in a matrixed team environment.
  • You’re happy learning and effectively navigating complex client organizations and processes.
  • You like to build partnerships and collaboration with Brand Strategists and Internal Business Clients
  • You enjoy helping the Group Creative Director mentor and lead talented teams.
  • You Think FAST.
  • You’re composed... and present well to senior executive audiences.
  • You’re organized.
  • You build good rapport with team members.
  • You know a good headline and call to action.
  • You instinctively identify and apply compelling imagery.
  • You make irresistible creative
  • You have strong integrated thinking skills.
  • Math does not scare you.
  • You have generally positive outlook and attitude, someone who naturally builds up the people around them.
  • You are a true creative problem solver.


    Job Requirements
    Basic Qualifications:
    • Bachelors Degree
    • 5 years of industry experience as an interactive Creative Director leading creative teams designing for web.

    Preferred Qualifications:
    • Bachelor’s degree in Advertising, Design, English, Communications, or similar discipline.
    • Direct Response experience
    • Experience with B2C on the web.
    • Ability to consistently deliver quality on time.
    • Ability to overcome adversity and obstacles.
    • Financial industry experience highly preferred or Creative Director position within a Fortune 500 or highly recognized ad agency with Fortune 500 client experience.
    • Experience overseeing web designers and art directors or have personal facility with commonly used software packages with emphasis on Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Adobe CS, Adobe Flash / Action Scripts.
    • Knowledgeable working with Microsoft Office Suite

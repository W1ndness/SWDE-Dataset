COMPANY OVERVIEW

Power Efficiency Corporation is a clean tech company focused on efficiency technologies for electric motors. Headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada, the Company has developed the patented and patent-pending technology platform E-Save Technology, which has been demonstrated in independent testing to improve the efficiency of electric motors by up to 35% in appropriate applications. Electric motors consume over 25% of the electricity in the U.S. and many operate inefficiently. Power Efficiency’s products include a Motor Efficiency Controller for three phase applications, such as escalators, crushers, granulators, mixers, saws and MG elevators, and a new Appliance Motor Efficiency Controller for single phase applications, such as residential and light commercial appliances. For more information, go to www.powerefficiency.com. 

 

POSITION SUMMARY

The Chief Technology Officer (“CTO”) is a hands-on, senior management position responsible for building and leading a highly qualified engineering team as necessary to effectively, efficiently and accurately design, development, testing and integration of high quality electronic products and technologies.  The CTO is responsible for bringing hardware and software concepts and products from conceptualization to launch and throughout the product life cycle.  He/she will have a detailed understanding of the overall technology environment, and how  Company products and technologies best meet the current and future needs of the marketplace at a detailed level.  The CTO/VP Engineering will work closely with the Company’s Sales and Operations departments as well as with commercial and industrial customers, including multinational OEMs, to design products that meet their specific requirements and integrate with their products and systems. 

RESPONSIBILITIES

Strategy & Planning

·         Participate as a member of the senior management team in leading product development from initial business case through technical development and design, programming, testing, documentation and integration.

·         Collaborate with the appropriate departments to develop and maintain the product road map and technology plan that supports customer requirements in accordance with overall Company goals and objectives.

·         Develop and communicate product technology alignment plans to executive team, staff, partners, customers, and stakeholders.

·         Establish overall departmental goals and objectives that meet the requirements of the Company’s product roadmap, and regularly monitor progress against them.  Take corrective action as necessary to remain aligned with objectives, and/or advocate modification of objectives to meet changing business conditions, customer requirements and resources.

·         With the CEO and CFO, play a lead role in developing annual operating and capital plans and periodic re-forecasts of departmental expense requirements

·         Conduct research to remain up-to-date and knowledgeable in regards to industry trends and emerging technologies in anticipation of new product requirements and enhancements.

Team Building and Leadership

·         Develop and maintain specific requirements for all engineering, test, documentation, integration and support staffing needs in order to meet Company and functional objectives.

·         Oversee recruitment, development, retention, and organization of qualified technical staff in accordance with corporate budgetary objectives and personnel policies.

·         Establish individual goals that are consistent with and drive toward attaining departmental and Company goals.

·         Mentor and develop team members in a manner that enables achievement of short and long term individual objectives

·         Maintain and set an example for a highly ethical environment.

·         Promote learning and individual opportunity

Operational Management

·         Act as primary liaison for the company’s technology vision via regular written and in-person communications with the organization’s executives, department heads, OEM customers and end user customers.

·         Creatively and independently provide resolution to technical problems in a cost-effective manner.

·         Develop and maintain an organized and disciplined project management orientation with regard to product development and execution.

·         Ensure continuous delivery of technical services through oversight of service level agreements with end users and monitoring of systems, programs, and equipment performance.

·         Ensure products adhere to applicable laws, regulations and certification requirements.

·         Oversee and develop patenting of intellectual property, inventions, and business processes as well as protection of trade secrets.

Acquisitions Evaluation and Integration

·         Working with the CEO and CFO, assess and communicate opportunities, potential synergies, risks and integration requirements associated with the Company’s M&A efforts and due diligence.

·         Design and execute staffing and integration plans for acquired product lines and engineering teams.

·         Assess incremental costs as well as cost synergies resulting potential business acquisitions.

Compensation AND BENEFITS

  • Base Salary $145,000 to $175,000, depending on experience
  • Bonus eligible  
  • The Company offers stock options, vacation and sick time, fully paid health insurance for the employee and dependants, and paid vacation. 

SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES

This will directly supervise one engineering manager or director, and indirectly a team of five engineering professionals that will grow to up to ten or more over time.


Job Requirements

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS

1.     Education and Certifications: Bachelor of Science degree in electrical engineering from a national or international accredited institution.  MSEE preferred.  Project Management Institute (“PMI”) certification highly desirable.

2.     Work Experience: Strongly prefer development experience involving integrated HW/SW products incorporating power electronics.  A minimum five years executive leadership experience.  Ten years relevant hardware and/or software electronics industry experience, at least five of which must be in microprocessor based control circuitry design.  Demonstrated comprehensive project management expertise and ability to apply technology solutions to customer requirements.   Expertise in overseeing regulatory approval and certification processes (UL, CE, CSA). 

Proven track record of successfully developing and managing new products from concept through test, launch, and ongoing technology maintenance and upgrades.  Experience in strategic technology planning, execution, and policy development.  Highly referenced leadership and mentoring skills, managing internal and outsourced  teams.  Sales support experience directly interfacing with customer management and technical staff. 

3.     Technical Experience: Expert level knowledge of power electronics, induction motors, and transformer theory is necessary.  Experience designing power electronics, IGBTs, SCRs, and motor controls a strong plus.  Experience using schematic capture and layout tools is required.  Control system design and control algorithms development experience.  Experience with major embedded programming languages (primarily C) as well as industrial communications technologies and protocols (Ethernet, Modbus, DeviceNet, ControlNet and more).  Solid understanding of electric motor control characteristics, features, purpose, and operating capabilities. 

4.    Personal Attributes:  Proven leadership ability and strong executive level interpersonal skills are an absolute must, as is the ability to communicate effectively with business partner organizations, OEM and end user customers.  Strong aptitude and desire to work in a fast paced entrepreneurial environment.  Must be able to clearly demonstrate the aptitude and willingness to establish a deep, solid understanding of the Companies technologies and processes.  Ability to set and manage priorities judiciously.  Strong tactical skills.  Ability to articulate ideas to both technical and non-technical addressees.  Exceptionally self-motivated and directed.  Keen attention to detail.  Superior analytical, evaluative, and problem-solving abilities.  Exceptional service orientation.  Ability to motivate in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.  Must be willing to travel initially on a regular basis to the Company’s Las Vegas corporate office.

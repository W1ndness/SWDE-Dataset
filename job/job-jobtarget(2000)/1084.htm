Primary Purpose:                                                                                                                                

Proactively sell exhibit space via telemarketing and direct selling for the Solar Power International Conference and Expo and PV America Conference & Expo.

 

Job Responsibilities:

  • Participate in developing sales plans and setting goals 

  • Implement telephone and personal solicitation campaigns to communicate the benefits of exhibiting.  Identify prospective exhibitors through trade publications, prospect lists, cold calling and networking.  

  • Establish personal rapport with new clients and maintain relationships with repeat clients.  Respond to new leads. 

  • Proactively sell exhibit space via telemarketing and direct selling to potential prospect for the Solar Power International Conference and Expo and PV America Conference and Expo.   

  •  Maintain Sales documentation. Continually develop and maintain database for all prospects and active exhibitors.  Maintain up to date information concerning each exhibitor record.  

  • Assign exhibit space that best matches exhibitor objectives. 

  • Establish, with prospective exhibitors, the terms of the exhibit agreement including payment policies, insurance certificate requirements, display guidelines and exposition rules and regulations. 

  • Assist on programs for all on-site space sales at respective shows.  Includes, but not limited to, working with marketing to announce programs, developing selection schedule, confirming exhibitors points for schedule, and staffing on site sales office and procedures. 

  • Perform various administrative duties including:  answer and screen incoming calls; Prepare, proof and edit correspondence; send faxes and coordinate mailings; respond to routine questions and requests.  Develop and implement systems to support office activities and to increase efficiency and productivity.   

  • Insure the highest level of customer satisfaction to our current and potential exhibitors on a daily basis.

  • Knowledge of Key competing shows and ability to prospect for new leads.

 Other Job Functions: 

                                                                                                                        

Other duties and projects as assigned or directed.

 


Job Requirements

Physical Requirements:                                                                                                                     

Requires use of typical business equipment (e.g., Microsoft word, excel, database management, a2z floor plan other program equivalent to, telephone, fax, copier.)

 

Education, Skills and Experience                                                                                                     

  • College degree or equivalent work experience.

  • Minimum of 3 years prior work experience, including at least 2 years directly and specifically related to the position.

  • Computer literacy with understanding of and ability to use Microsoft Office products.

  • Ability to understand and use database software to process and maintain information.

  • Excellent telephone and interpersonal communication skills

  • Customer service and team orientation

  • Strong oral and communication, writing and editing skills

  • Ability to organize, prioritize and accomplish multiple tasks with attention to detail.

  • Strong conflict resolution and problem-solving skills

POSITION SUMMARY: 

This position is responsible for the installation and service of commercial security systems to include, but not limited to hardware and software installation, programming, access control, and intrusion detection.  The Building Security Systems Engineer will configure and program interfaces with other building software systems as well as interact with customers, vendors and other contractors on-site to properly coordinate successful installations.  Additional duties include: performs periodic reviews of overall security system architecture and design with the foresight of integrating old and new technology to insure best performance; makes inferences from building and electrical blueprints, schematics and drawings, in providing service to the Arizona State Courts Building.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:

The successful candidate will hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field and hands on experience in installing, programming and maintaining electronic security and commercial access control systems or related experience within the electronics industry, with mechanical abilities in building management.  Additional experience may substitute for education.  This position also requires an individual with strong hands on experience with P.C. based hardware, Microsoft Operating Systems, TCP/IP Networking and misc P.C. based software required to operate a complex security system.  Mechanical abilities in building management and good written and verbal communication skills are also required.

SELECTION PROCESS:
Some positions may take 4-6 weeks to fill. Only applicants whose education, training and experience most closely meet the needs of the position may be invited to interview.  Requests for a special accommodation to participate in the interview process should be made at the time you are contacted to schedule an interview.  The Selection Process may include first and/or second round panel interviews.  This is a regular full-time exempt level position. This position is open for internal and external recruitment.

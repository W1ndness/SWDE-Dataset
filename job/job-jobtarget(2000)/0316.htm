Central Michigan University

Mt. Pleasant, Michigan, United States

Position Title: SR RESEARCH ANALYST - ADVANCED ANALYTICS
Min Education: Master's Degree
Industry: Education / Teaching / Administration
Posted: November 29, 2010
Job Function: Research
Min Experience: 3-5 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: http://www.apply-for-job.net/c/jobclick.cfm?site=3...
 

General Statement of Duties: +The senior research analyst functions as a team leader on analytics projects that support the capture, collection, exploratory data analysis, and transformation of complex data sets and incorporates them into statistical, data mining, text mining, machine learning, simulation, geographic information systems and other business intelligence tools for insight and visualization. The senior research analyst shall serve as a program manager responsible for ensuring adherence to the organization's project management methodologies and help guide the development of predictive data models, optimization, and quantitative study of data that has been prepared for analysis. This will include the quality control of team member analysis, results, reports, lab book documentation, and presentations to groups or individuals. The position requires the ability to quickly understand the relationship of data to a client's business or investigative needs in a variety of fields. A strong background with statistics and quantitative data analysis is required to assist with the analytic aspects of quantifying and developing appropriate models to understand and communicate to others the nature of these complex relationships within the data. Comfort with high level programing in tools such as R, SAS, and SQL is required in order to efficiently automate routines and data process.

Wage rate: Commensurate with qualifications. Hours: Typically Monday-Friday, (8:00am-5:00pm; evenings & weekends as necessary; schedule may vary). Screening begins immediately and continues until filled.  Applicants must apply on-line at www.jobs.cmich.edu.    CMU, an AA/EO institution, strongly and actively strives to increase diversity within its community (see www.cmich.edu/aaeo/).


Job Requirements

Required Qualifications: Master's degree or equivalent experience in statistics and quantitative data analysis required to assist with analytics projects. Collaborative leadership style that fosters interdisciplinary cooperation and team work. Experience working with corporate clients and be able to formally present analytics findings to both executive and technical customers. Strong background with statistics, modeling and quantitative analysis. Ability to learn new tools, techniques, and apply them in novel ways. Excellent verbal and written communication skills. Self-motivated with strong analytical, communication and decision making skills. Well-developed interpersonal skills and ability to work in a team environment. Demonstrated ability to manage multiple concurrent projects and meet deadlines. Ability to organize material in a logical and compelling manner. Demonstrated proficiency with SQL based reporting and high level programming. Flexibility with regard to work hours and requirements. Many clients may be located in other states and time zones. Client contact may require evening hours. Travel within the State of Michigan and the U.S. is occasionally required.

Desired qualifications include Ph.D. Previous experience in data or text mining; strong background in optimization or simulation methods; conversant in business, quantitative, and information technology vocabularies; knowledge of computer software and hardware applications; experience with R, ESRI's GIS software, and SAS or other equivalent major BI tools; familiarity with Unix and Windows operating systems.  Excellent command of advanced MS Excel functions; familiarity with on-line information gathering and syndicated research sources; ability to assess changing and emerging Information Technology technologies; prior experience developing and teaching analytics curriculum; program or project management certification (PMI). Experience in the health care sector.

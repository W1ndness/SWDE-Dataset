Des Moines University-Office of Development & Alumni Relations invites qualified candidates to apply for the highly visible position of Manager of Annual Giving.  Located in Des Moines, Iowa, this development position offers opportunity for the right candidate to " do a world of good" here at Des Moines University! 

So, if you possess documented and successful experience with annual fund campaigns (especially in higher education),experience supporting direct mail marketing and phone-a-thon efforts and have good organizational and follow up skills, please read on. 

 Responsibilities for this critical position include, but are not limited to: 

·           Organizing & implementing a multi-phases annual fund campaign.

·          Assisting with writing, design, and production of annual fund letters, brochures, response vehicles, pledge cards and other materials used in the direct mail and phone

    Coordinate identification & solicitation of prospects for the President’s Society.

·         Assist with annual fund analysis and utilization of that analysis for future fund raising efforts.

·         Develop & evaluate plans for recognition programs.

·         Research, develop & evaluate plans for specific donor-centered activities.

·         Develop & manage a portfolio of donor relationships.

·         Develop, initiate, recruit & support volunteers.

·         Keep up to date on fund raising, prospect research and donor related information, technology and/or advancement issues.

·         Perform other duties as required.

 


Job Requirements

 

Qualifications include, but are not limited to: 

·         Bachelors w/ two years annual fund campaign experience; candidates with a lesser degree but possessing 5 or more years of annual fund campaign experience may be considered.

Strong preference for experience in higher education.

·         Experience in supporting direct mail marketing & phone-a-thon efforts.

·         Knowledge of software and database systems such as Wealth Engine.

·         Working knowledge of Raiser’s Edge, MS Excel and Word.

·         Excellent communications, presentation, negotiation and interpersonal skills.

·         Excellent organizational and time management skills and demonstrated follow up.

·         Capable of travel 3-4 days month (statewide or nationwide). Valid driver’s license and proof of insurance required.

 

Qualified candidates are invited to submit a CV plus *cover letter (address cover letter to attention Office of Development & Alumni Relations) detailing their interest and qualifications along with contact information for three professional references online at www.dmu.edu/employment.  No hard copy, faxed, emailed or dropped off documents are accepted.

*Resumes/CVs w/o cover letters will not be eligible for consideration.

CV/resume review and/or interview scheduling will commence immediately.

Complete job description, Fulltime Salaried benefit summary and/or other information may be found at www.dmu.edu/employment.

Application deadline is 11/15/2010 - extended to 11/30/10

Des Moines University-EOE

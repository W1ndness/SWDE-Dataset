HP

Dallas, Texas, United States

Industry: Other

Job Function: Sales
Job Type: Full-Time
Salary: Yearly Salary)

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.

The Solution Manager is responsible for making sure that HP submits a compelling financial and technical proposition while also promoting a solution that can be delivered with a manageable risk profile.

 

Key Responsibilities:

n  Provides mentoring/coaching to solution leads, tower leads and SMEs

n  Provides consultation to customers early in the process to help bring them to the transaction ("art of the possible", potential deal structures, etc)

n  Works closely with the bid manager, solution leads, tower leads and alliance partners to resolve issues with solutions or pricing models and identify areas where the cost structure can be affected - i.e. replacing non-standard software with HP recommended and/or leveragable software

n  Assists in validating consistency and connection between all elements of bid, including assumptions, technical solution, costing, pricing, service levels, commercial terms, etc.

n  Ensures the solution components, costs and pricing aligns with value proposition, client business case/TCO and applicable standards and best practices

n  Identifies, justifies and drives solution team and delivery organizations to adopt market-driven offering structures, delivery strategies/methods and competitive price points

n  Participates and leads GSS Yields for deal Phases. Owns Yield approval for Yield 1 and Yield 3 series. Drives solution review and costing checkpoints through to successful completion.

n  Assists and supports in solution development and costing of software, hardware and third party contracts

n  Assists and supports contract negotiations

n  Works closely with Delivery and Global Sales leaders in obtaining solution approvals

n  Ensures best practices are applied consistently across opportunities, defines new best practices and standards based on past pursuits and implements process improvements and best practices in solution development

n  Assists in producing client-facing documents

n  Assists in aligning the RFP requirements, Win themes, HP proposed solution and pricing structure.

n  Aligns cost and pricing models with standards and best practices.

n  Assists in implementing action plans to remedy any deficiencies in solution, cost, or pricing models.

 

General support outside of direct pursuit support:

n  Assists in pre-qualification of pursuits as needed, including performing analysis on prospective deals as part of the deal qualification process.

n  Identifies and implements standards and best practices relative to costing, pricing. and client budget analysis.

n  Provides feedback to and works with function owners to resolve solution, cost/price data discrepancies.

n  Provides ad hoc support of and advice to regional pursuits on all solution functions.

n  Develops modeling tools and performs ad hoc analysis for the continuing improvement of HP's pursuits.

n  Works with cross-functional teams that include portfolio management, delivery, and business/support units to improve HP's costing and pricing capabilities.

n  Assists in developing and deploying capabilities (tools, processes, methodologies, and materials) to support and enhance solution, costing, pricing, and deal structure.

 

Critical Competencies to Drive Business Results

 

Pursuit Plan Coaching/Strategy Review:

Supports pursuit  teams in developing well-targeted pursuit plans to ensure the successful capture of business

n  Ensures the accuracy of value propositions in proposals and pursuit-related communications

n  Coaches sales teams on how to position solutions for different industries, management levels and organizational functions; identifies comparable client situations and integrates into positioning, references

n  Collaborates with pursuit leadership in developing effective client sales engagement plans and pursuit strategies

n  Articulates new, innovative and previously overlooked presentation of value propositions

 

Team Leadership:

Effectively collaborates, builds relationships and influences team members to achieve program objectives

n  Establishes relationships of trust with team members and stakeholders

n  Represents the organization at the executive-level in partners' and customers' organizations

n  Resolves misalignment of different strategies to ensure achievement of project objectives

n  Mediates difficult situations to ensure significant business impact

 

Sales Facilitation:

Applies influence and organizational savvy to advance sales opportunities externally, with clients, and internally, within HP

n  Capitalizes on corporate status/position to facilitate entry to potential opportunities, generate customer interest in HP's offerings and establish consultative relationship

n  Expedites HP internal approvals and buy-in through "internal selling" to key decision-makers  

n  Troubleshoots threats to effective selling or order fulfillment to minimize their impact on the business

 

C-Level Partnering :

Contributes to enduring executive relationships that establish HP' consultative professionalism and promote its total solution capabilities at the highest levels of the client's organization

n  Applies understanding of C-level (CEO, CFO, CIO, COO) concerns, metrics, and decision making to sales planning

n  Ensures that proposed solutions address benefits and needs of interest to CEOs, CFOs, and CIOs

n  Ensures adequate preparation and contributions of account team members at senior-level management meetings

 

Financial/Business Acumen:

Exhibits authoritative business and financial acumen to develop meaningful business or operational recommendations

n  Understands what it takes to manage a business and uses these insights to gain a better understanding of business needs

n  Understands and accurately positions the financial benefits associated with business propositions or action plans

n  Develops and applies financial models to create value propositions using appropriate data (e.g., profitability ratios, operating ratios, leverage ratios)

 

Negotiation :

Manages contractual negotiations to protect HP's business interests while at the same time maintaining customer satisfaction

n  Assesses contractual requirements for feasibility to reflect attendant changes in client's business requirements

n  Conducts negotiations in a way that maintains the HP/client partnership by ensuring win/win outcomes

n  Identifies need for and takes appropriate actions to ensure synchronization between solution, SLAs, timelines, pricing, terms, etc of overall transaction

 

Solution Advocacy :

Partners with account teams to compellingly position proposed solutions both internally and to the customer

n  Articulates solutions in a way that translates technical benefits into a compelling case centered on business challenges

n  Authors or reviews the technical components of proposals for validity and the accuracy of associated costs 

n  Documents solutions and builds case collateral to advance repeatable solutions and best practices within HP

 

OpportunityProfiling and Qualification:

Profiles and qualifies opportunities rigorously to ensure sound application of HP's resources on significant deals

n  Critically assess client's IT operations to identify efficiency gaps and potential cost savings

n  Articulates new, innovative and previously overlooked value propositions for client opportunities

n  Develops detailed account profiles as a basis for strategy development and sales pursuit execution

n  Orchestrates development of a compelling business case detailing associated cost reductions, benefits, TCO and ROI

 

Technical Solution Design :

Creates a solution vision and design framework for guiding and synthesizing the technical contributions of specialists

n  Determines a solution's fit to standard offers, gaps requiring  customization, and the resources needed to build the solution

n  Articulates an overall design and project plan to guide the work of technical specialists

n  Synthesizes and balances the input of specialists with pricing considerations to determine the final design

 

Solution Costing/Resourcing :

Appropriately sizes the resources and costs involved with solutions to advance the sale, optimally position HP as the preferred vendor, and ensure HP profitability and margin

n  Develops creative strategies to bridge costs for custom requirements with standard offers

n  Engages in due diligence throughout the sales cycle to uncover root needs, hidden risks and delivery challenges

n  Assembles, validates and communicates deal costing to ensure accuracy and protect the business interests of HP

n  Understands basis and mechanics of cost models at all roll-up levels, and applies them to achieve appropriate, desirable outcomes as needed

 

Key Traits:

n  Drive, commitment and passion for success - hates to lose

n  Work ethic - willing to do what it takes to get a high quality job done

n  Listens to gain greatest understanding of issues, needs, drivers

n  Patience

n  Open minded

n  Trustworthy

n  Respected by peers, team and organizational leadership

n  Ability to influence peers, team, organizational leaders and clients; challenges the status quo

 

Experience:

Account management and/or Account delivery experience - 3+ years

Sales support or Sales experience - 5+ years

Technical experience - 7+ years

 

Education:

Bachelor or equivalent university degree - Preferably in Computer Science, Business, Economics, or Engineering. MBA or Masters degree in above fields is a plus. Equivalent work experience will be considered.

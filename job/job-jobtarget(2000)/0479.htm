The Business Systems Analyst, Financial Applications is responsible for the design and support of business processes, planning, testing, and configuration of the SAP R/3 Finance and Controlling modules.  The focus will be on process automation, control upgrades, efficiency improvements and return on SAP and Application Investment.  The Business Systems Analyst will work in conjunction with process leads, business owners, business analysts, business warehouse analysts, technical resources, security analysts and system users.

 

The ideal candidate will possess a solid mix of hands-on accounting/finance experience and strong SAP Configuration/Implementation know-how.


Job Requirements

A sample of responsibilities include:

- Business process analysis and decomposition of current business processes.

- Design of SAP system functionality to support business processes.

- Creation of Detailed Functional Specifications for custom programs, reports, conversions and interfaces. 

- Interaction with Technical Resources on design and testing of custom functionality.

- Configuration of SAP Finance and Controlling modules, including General Ledger, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Banking, Treasury, Fixed Assets, Special Ledger, Cost Center Accounting, Internal Orders, Product Costing and Profitability Analysis.

- Configuration of accounting integration points in MM and SD Modules.

- Creation and execution of Unit and Integration Test Scripts.

- Creation of Business Process Procedures and execution of end user training.

- Assist in the Implementation of SAP in Worldwide Business Units.

- Support day-to-day accounting functions.

- Ability to travel domestically or internationally on an as-needed basis.

 

 

Specialized skills - Technical Knowledge:

- Experience in evaluating and implementing new software and upgrades.

- Experience Configuring, Testing and End User Support in the SAP Finance and Controlling modules.  SAP Certification is a plus.

- Working knowledge of SAP ECC 6.0 preferred.

- Ability to work independently or as part of a large implementation team, depending on the project.

 

Academic/Credentials/Certifications:
- BS/BA in a business related discipline, or equivalent experience

- Accounting Degree desired

-Fluent oral and written communication skills in German and English

Length of experience:
- A minimum of 3-5 years of hands on business experience in finance/accounting.
- A minimum of 3-5 years of SAP Configuration experience in Finance and Controlling modules.  At least 1 Full Lifecycle Implementation and 1 Upgrade project desired.

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.
 
The goal of HP's Enterprise Services US Public Sector practice is to establish HP as the world's leading information technology company; to be the best at helping customers manage and transform their IT environments to optimize business outcomes; and to be the provider of choice for IT infrastructure solutions that support the mission of our clients in the Federal marketplace.  This is an exciting time to join us! We are experiencing year over year growth, outpacing our market. We continue to capitalize and grow our key Consulting solution areas:
 
1.       Data Center Transformation
2.       Enterprise Software Management (HP software, ITIL)
3.       Client, Microsoft & Workplace Services
4.       Cyber Security
5.       Platforms Infrastructure & Migration
 
The Solutions division within the US Public Sector Consulting Practice is now searching for a senior level Technology Consultant based in the Washington DC Metro region with experience managing the growth and development of programs and accounts within the Public Sector community.  This role will report to the Client, Microsoft & Workplace Services Practice.
 
  Experience Summary
 
We are looking for a Technical Consultant to work in the area of Microsoft Desktop/Windows 7.
 
The person selected for this position should have at least 5 years of increasing responsibility for and delivering of desktop solutions with a track record of participating in and leading several successful technical implementations, technical upgrades, and migrations.
 
The Windows Desktop Technical Consultant will be working "hands on" and/or leading the technical aspects of projects on-site with HP clients.  He or She will be responsible for implementing Desktop Infrastructure technologies and other related packaged solutions according to the established Statement of Work with the client.  In addition, the Windows Desktop Technical Consultant will actively ensure accurate and on-time implementation of the project deliverables, and assist the Project Manager with project planning including iteration of the tasks involved in delivery, identification of key resources, timelines, and milestones.  The Windows Desktop Technical Consultant may also assist the Business Development Lead and a Consulting Principal with creating the SOW, project plans, and other deliverables for the client.
 
 
The Qualified Individual additionally has the following skills:
 
Required:
 
  • Experience with the architecting and designing of Windows desktop infrastructure projects
  • Implementation experience with or leading a project for the rollout of desktop upgrades
  • Experience with Windows 7 and/or Vista desktop migration projects
  • Experience with application discovery and remediation with regards to desktop migrations
  • Broad understanding of networking, system management, and Windows operating systems
  • Excellent Communication skills (written and verbal) to all management levels within customer organization
  • Ability to listen carefully and extract key issues
  • Conflict resolution and negotiation skills/experience
  • Excellent interview and documentation skills
  • Ability to socialize and generate consensus for new ideas and to overcome resistance
  • Teaching, training, coaching and mentoring skills
 
Preferred:
 
  • Experience with application packaging for the desktop
  • Experience with the architecting and designing of Virtual Desktop Infrastructure projects
  • Knowledge and implementation experience of one or more of Citrix XenDesktop, VMware View, and Quest VDI products
  • Knowledge and implementation experience with one or more of VMware ESX, Microsoft Hyper-V, Citrix XenServer, and Quest Virtualization Solutions
  • Knowledge and experience with the Lakeside Software and AppSense software tools
  • Microsoft, VMware, Citrix certifications a plus
  • Solid project management skills/experience

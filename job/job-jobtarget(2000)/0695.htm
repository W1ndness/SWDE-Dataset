Argyle Executive Forum is seeking mid level professionals to join our team that have experience executing B2B lead generation and advertising campaigns for clients. You will be responsible for managing small, mid and large scale business development and marketing projects for our clients. You will be responsible for leading cross-functional teams and for delivery of all aspects of the campaign including but not limited to creating and maintaining accurate project plans, deliverable and task tracking/ reporting, risk and issue management, as well as accurate scoping, estimating and ensuring resources are allocated appropriately, as well as working with appropriate domain leads. You will acquire and maintain subject matter expertise in the industries our clients seeks to target and you will be responsible for effectively problem solving to ensure the appropriate resources are in place per campaign, that the work is conducted in timely fashion, within approved scope and budget, while also exceeding a client’s expectations.


Job Requirements

Responsibilities

• Lead cross functional teams in the development and execution of business development and marketing campaigns; including email marketing solutions, speaking roles at meetings, execution of introductions to our members, as well as other key deliverables
• Manage client engagements to ensure account profitability while client expectations are met or exceeded
• Responsible for the consistent successful execution of a project from estimation through launch and maintenance
• Champions and consistently employs, improves and shares project management processes, standards, templates and best practices
• Mentors others on processes
• Assists in defining, creating and leading project management processes, standards, templates and best practices
• Passionate about delivering the best thought leadership, insights, creative and technology, by finding creative ways of executing the project that does not jeopardize schedule or budget
• Owns the facilitation and creation of accurate project plans with clearly defined milestones, deliverables and tasks
• Ownership and facilitation of internal project status meetings
Collaborate with the account leadership to identify and analyze risks and determine mitigation plans

Skills
• 3+ years of proven project leadership experience on medium to large size and moderately complex campaigns within professional service firms (consulting, ad agencies, marketing services, etc)
• Extensive knowledge of project management concepts, methodologies and tools
• Ability to work collaboratively in a creative environment while keeping projects on schedule and within budgeted allowance
• Ability to lead well under pressure and to lead a project team to ensure deadlines and budget are met while exceeding clients’ expectations
• Experience with leading PM software packages (MS Project, Visio, Basecamp, as well as MS Excel, etc)
• Ability to effectively create project plans based on the needs of the project
• Ability to tailor communications approach based on the situation
• Strong communication skills with the ability to present information in verbal, written or visual form to a variety of audiences, from the internal team to client executives
• Must be proactive and have strong effective problem-solving skills
• Experience managing marketing, creative or technology projects
• Strong problem solving skills; responsive and flexible to evolving responsibilities and changing environment
• Experience developing Statements of Work
• PMP certification preferred

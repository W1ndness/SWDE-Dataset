Position General Purpose:
Interact in a multidisciplinary team environment to design and implement health outcomes research projects in the Center for Outcomes Research and Evaluation (CORE). Participate in all aspects of research design and analysis including: review scientific literature, design data collection instruments, develop study protocols, conduct qualitative interviews, merge data files, conduct quantitative and qualitative data analysis, and reports results. Conduct statistical analysis using a variety of software packages including SAS and STATA. Compile and analyze results and prepare written summaries for study reporting and manuscripts and presentations for professional meeting and stakeholder groups. May function as project leader or coordinator of projects.

Essential Duties:
1. Assist investigators in the review/preparation of research protocols, grant and contract proposals, and manuscripts for multiple projects (i.e. provide input on study design, develop data collection forms, conduct analyses and summarize results).
2. Extract and merge research data from multiple sources including Medicare claims datasets, Area Resource File, Census data, and other clinical and administrative research datasets.
3. Perform data cleaning, quality control and prepare datasets ready for analysis.
4. Independently carry out analyses according to project timelines.
5. Work on several projects concurrently and communicate with team leaders regarding prioritization.
6. Organize and create clear documentation of procedures employed for projects including: detailed description of cohort and sources of data, detailed data dictionary, variable definitions, statistical analysis, program code and location of files in archive.
7. Prepare written and oral reports to summarize results of data analysis, interpret findings and provide conclusions and recommendations.
8. Ensure data security and compliance with data use agreements by becoming knowledgeable about, and strictly adhering to, the policies and procedures set forth by institutions in their data use agreements.
9. Develop databases for electronic data entry using ACCESS or SQL server.
10. Communicate effectively with internal and external staff and organizations to achieve project goals with high customer satisfaction.
11. Prepare Institutional Review Board (IRB) applications and data requests.
12. Assist in the training of students and staff.


Job Requirements

Education and Experience:
1. Master's Degree in specific discipline and one year of experience, or an equivalent combination of education and experience.

Additional Education and Experience:
1. One year of related work experience in healthcare related research or similar job family, which must indicate ability to work with people, conduct structured and unstructured interviews and to work with minimal supervision and be receptive to direction.
2. Experience in qualitative data collection through interviews.
3. Experience in qualitative data coding and analysis, utilizing qualitative data analysis software such as Atlas.ti, NUD*IST, Qualrus, WordStat, NVivo or similar qualitative analysis software packages.

Skills & Abilities:
1. Proficient in ATLAS, EXCEL, ACCESS, MSWORD, POWERPOINT.
2. Knowledge of qualitative data coding and analysis.
3. Thorough knowledge of research methodologies and protocols.
4. Proficient in interviewing subjects for studies including qualitative interviewing. Extracts and compiles a range of data from written sources, from individuals by asking questions or from one or several given databases, limited interpretation of data.
5. Summarizes, revises or makes limited use of complex, technical or specialized literature.
6. Must demonstrate excellent organizational ability, attention to detail and knowledge of research process.
7. Use of one or several standard reference works.
8. Incumbent plans and schedules own work and occasionally provides work guidance or orientation for non-routine procedures and policies.
9. Preferred: Some supervisory experience of research assistants. Knowledge of SAS, SPSS or other statistical programs. Familiarity with medical terminology and cardiovascular disease.

Background Check Requirements: All external candidates for employment will be subject to pre-employment screening. All offers are contingent on successful completion of a background check.

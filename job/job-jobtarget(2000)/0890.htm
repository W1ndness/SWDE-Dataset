ECM Application Administrator  

 

This position is responsible for the operation, administration and configuration of an ECM Platform used to support ATB Financial and its subsidiaries.

The incumbent will demonstrate alignment with overall enterprise information management strategies ATB direction, purpose and value. 

 

You will find success based on your ability to:

 

  • Participate in, and help guide, the implementation and operationalization of an ECM Platform and function for ATB.
  • Day to day operations, development, administration, configuration and maintenance of the Electronic Content Management (ECM) Platform consisting of:  Documentum, Captiva, NiceLabel, TaskSpace, WebTop, ASR, RPSA, and RM.
  • Participate in the development and delivery of ECM training plans and tools.
  • Provide ongoing user support.
  • Participate in establishing and implementing enterprise-wide records and information management policies, standards and procedures and produce the reporting necessary to support the ongoing monitoring and auditing of such policies, standards and procedures.
  • Participate in the development, enhancement, implementation and ongoing maintenance of ATB’s record classification plan and retention citation schedules, including the required preservation and/or destruction of records across the enterprise..
  • Participate in efforts to administer best practices and deploy initiatives for central file collections and inactive records.
  • Provide expertise and guidance to business units regarding ECM operations, system/user configuration and related records management matters (i.e. taxonomies, metadata, retention, user configuration, groups/roles and securities).
  • Provide and assist business units with ECM and records management reporting to support retention policy services, auditing, solution development/implementation, records destruction, transfer and disposition.
  • Support the Director, Strategic Information Management.

 

The scope of ECM and Records Management is enterprise-wide.  Ownership resides within the Information Technology Services Department and aligns with initiatives that touch Business Intelligence, IT Architecture. The complexity of the work includes user support, development, implementation and expansion problem solving and creativity the job does every day.  This position will interact will with users across the enterprise to ensure needs are met and issues resolved.

 


Job Requirements

As the ideal candidate you possess:

 

  • Undergraduate degree (in a related field) with a minimum of 5 years experience in both physical and electronic records management
  • Training in, and a minimum of 5 years experience with enterprise content management systems/applications
  • Specific working knowledge of Documentum, TaskSpace, WebTop, ASR, Content Server, RPSA, RMA, DA,Captiva and various collaboration software tools in the MSOffice environment
  • Business/delivery focused, strategic thinker, with demonstrated experience in financial services
  • Extensive experience in the development, implementation and configuration of ECM Platforms used to manage records in both corporate and operational environments
  • Good knowledge of functional classification theory and taxonomy principles, document control, and business requirements gathering
  • Experience in customizing, compiling and delivering reports, training and user support
  • Demonstrated proactive solutioning for functional optimization
  • Excellent interactive and teamwork skills including negotiating, communicating and listening
  • High proficiency in computer technology and business applications
  • Familiarity with SQL, VB, OCR, Barcoding, labeling, Imaging technology and data-base modeling is an asset

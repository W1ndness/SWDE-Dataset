The Certification and Testing Division of ICC is responsible for development and administration of testing-related services: 

The Test Administrator will ensure the delivery of ICC examinations including:

  • Test production and delivery, both Computer-based and Hard-copy
    • Assembly
    • Items, plans, figures
    • Printing / Publishing
    • Distribution
    • Scoring
    • Score reporting
    • Test site coordination
    • Proctor coordination
  • Client and vendor interface services
    • Billing
    • Scheduling
  • Quality Control
    • Critical toward zero defects
  • Customer Care
    • Answer questions and address concerns for customers via phone, email, internet, fax, and other medium.
  • Perform other duties as assigned or requested.

Job Requirements

Essential Skills and Education / Experience:

  • Associate's degree (A.A.) or equivalent from a two-year college or technical school; or six-months to one-year related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Experience in a fast-paced environment with critical hard-stop deadlines
  • Requires high organization and process tracking skills.
  • Exposure to construction industry related terminology and processes desired.
  • Must be proficient with computer software programs including MS Office and be able to learn in-house programs.
  • All applicants will be subject to proficiency exams related to MS Office at time of or prior to interview.

We offer a complete benefits package including:
Competitive salary
Health, dental and vision insurance
401-K
Paid vacation, sick and holidays

This is an outstanding opportunity for the right person. Only the highly motivated need apply.

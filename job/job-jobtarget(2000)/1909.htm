HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.   The Organization
IT Services (IT for IT) is the centralized IT organization responsible for implementing a comprehensive end-to-end vision for planning, building, executing and managing IT applications within Global IT.  Within IT Services, the Common Architecture and Services (CAS) team is responsible for providing an integrated stack of software, tools, and standards to accelerate the delivery of engineered business solutions while improving quality, stability, and manageability.
The Position
 
This is a hands-on position.  Design, build, test, and support current and next generation tools, architecture, and platforms as a member of the Common Architecture and Services (CAS) core team.  Interact with cross-functional teams as a technical leader, driving innovative ways to support our primary stakeholder -- every IT development team in the enterprise.  Provide mentoring and guidance to other developers.  Be an ambassador of CAS to application development teams world-wide, exerting influence, and driving the consistent architectural vision.  Collaborate with your CAS peers and other thought leaders in the enterprise on strategy, design, process, and capabilities on a regular basis.  Apply HP and 3rd party technologies while leading the design of highly complex infrastructure and software solutions. Independently implement end user or enterprise infrastructure or services of significant complexity, while taking the time to simplify where possible.  Assign work to others, provide technical leadership, and provide time and resource estimates for assigned projects.
 
Duties include:
 
?     Draft functional specifications from business requirements
?     Developing java framework and tools software to be embedded in business solutions world wide
?     Design, prepare and execute unit tests as well was provide guidance to QA teams during system testing
?     Collaborate with team leads/architects during technical design
?     Resolve defects during QA testing and in post-release patches
?     Resolve defects on production systems

  Education and Experience Required:
 
Typically a technical Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience and a minimum of 10 years of related experience or a Master's degree and a minimum of 8 years of experience.
 
Knowledge and Skills Required:
 
?     6 or more years of experience writing code (such as, and not limited to, Java, C, C++, C#, Python)
?     Experience with testing tools
?     Experience working with multiple full release cycles
?     Deep understanding of server-side Java environment and relational databases
?     Deep understanding of object oriented programming, design patterns, data structures and algorithms
?     Strong knowledge of Linux/Unix
?     Experience with tuning applications, SQL and databases for best performance
?     Good documentation skills (excellent in written and verbal communications a must)
?     HTML experience a plus
?     Experience with any frontend MVC framework (Struts, Spring, JSF) is a plus
?     Advanced understanding of modern software development tools and Software Configuration Management (SCM).
?     Good verbal and written communication skills; influencing skills and ability to work effectively in a geographically dispersed team.
?     Demonstrated technical leadership skills.
 
Other Critical Attributes:
 
?     Passion for technology
?     Passion for collaboration
?     Energetic, inspired, and dedicated to software craftsmanship
?     Cares about the little things but can easily see the big picture
?     Ability to translate business requirements into reality
 

Ability and willingness to learn new material quickly and with little top down management

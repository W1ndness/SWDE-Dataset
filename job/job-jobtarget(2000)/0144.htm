HIM Consultant:

Position is designed for an HIM consultant with diverse skills who can demonstrate the knowledge and experience of Medical Records Systems (EMR implementation a plus) with an emphasis on Revenue Charge Capture experience within a healthcare physician practice or hospital setting.

Reports To:    E-CS Chief Executive Officer

Scope of Position:

·         This position has a key role in the planning, development, implementation and maintenance of the EMR Implementation with physician practices.  

 

·         Under the direction of the E-CS CEO, Position works in collaboration with the Northern Illinois University Regional Extension Center (REC’s) in the state of IL.

 

·         Oversees the 3 different mile stones that are designed to guide and bring along the initial stages of adopting an EMR for a physician practice.  This includes all phases of the EMR implementation process from beginning to Post-Implementation, including EMR readiness, workflow analysis, EMR vendor selection, quality reporting, E-Prescribe, Meaningful Use etc. etc.

 

·         Assists in the development of a EMR strategy for the Physician Practices that includes:


 - Development of policies and procedures for this business sector

 - Evaluating software against clinical policies and procedures.
 - Overseeing the selection, testing, and implementation of the health care EMR process.
 - Provides leadership and direction related to management of current medical records system within the organization.
 - Interacts with all levels of the organization that employ patient data in decision-making and everyday operations of EMR

·         The HIM Consultant is responsible for the assist with the implementation of at each facility and participates in all phases thru a successful go-live and follow-up.  

 

·         The HIM Consultant will also act as a liaison to the electronic medical record with the regulatory compliance support as related to all operations of the HIM department and projects. 

 

Essential Functions:

 

·         Identifies, collects, processes and manages information technologies and clinical software to support practice, administration, education, and research of the organization.

 

·         Evaluates the effectiveness of the clinical informatics programs against standards of practice.

 

·         Acquires and uses current evidence based knowledge to drive decisions making in this business sector

 

·         Assists the physician practice the selection and implementation plan for an electronic medical record ( E.M.R.).

 

·         Makes recommendations based on the preceding to senior management of the practice.

 

·         Helps implement this selection with support from the EMR team members for a smooth transition.

 

·         Creates and implements a roll out schedule for the practice(s).

 

·         Possesses the knowledge of medical, administrative, ethical and legal requirements and standards related to healthcare delivery and the privacy of protected patient information.

 

·         Reviews and makes recommendations related to current HIM policies and procedures.

 

·         Thorough knowledge of billing, coding, and reimbursement functions within a physician practice and/or hospital settings.


Job Requirements

Experience and Education

 

Experience

 

·          Must have two years HIM experience in coding, billing, reimbursement and EMR

 

·         Experience working on large cash acceleration including patient access and request management, cash collection and

posting, coding and charge capture, claims process and follow up, and the technology that support these processes.

 

 

·         Considerable experience identifying and addressing client needs: building, maintaining, and utilizing networks of client relationships and managing resource requirements, project workflow, billing, coding, and collections.

 

·         Prepares and/or coordinates complex written and verbal materials that include CMS, AMA, and AHA policies and guidelines.

 

·         Determines and identifies charge capture opportunities, including researching and reviewing applicable reimbursement rules.

 

·         Experience as a team leader: supervising teams to create an atmosphere of trust; seeking diverse views to encourage improvement and innovation.

 

·         Providing timely meaningful written and verbal feedback.

 

·         Preferable experience in a Healthcare Provider consulting environment. 

 

·         Strong interpersonal, writing and oral communication skills particularly over the phone as most all E-CS employees are remote

 

·         EMR Implementation Experience preferred

·         Have  successful management and project management experience

·         Have the ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing

·         Understanding and knowledge of billing, coding and EMR information systems preferred

·         Demonstrate ability to lead and direct projects and project teams

 

Education

·         The successful candidate will possess: RHIT or RHIA or CCS, CCS-P, CPC, CPC-H certification

·         Be knowledgeable of federal and state laws and standards of practice, and knowledgeable of CMS, AMA, and AHA policies/guidelines

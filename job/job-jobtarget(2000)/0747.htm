The Engineer II - Requirements Management will have expertise in Requirements Management using the DOORS Requirements Management (RM) Tool. He/she will define requirements structures and build standardized requirements management modules in DOORS across various projects and programs. Develop customizations in response to needs by systems engineers and programs while maintaining standardization and working towards continuous improvement of the system.
  • Act as the primary point of contact for systems engineers and other program members requiring assistance with creating or modifying program requirements in DOORS.
  • Be familiar with scripting languages for automation.
  • Have a constant drive for improving the process and automating day to day activities.
  • Work as a proactive team member within a team of systems engineers and developers.
  • Take part in conceptual design discussions for determining the best way to utilize DOORS for the current program, with an emphasis of streamlined and more efficient requirements change management processes.
  • Document standard processes and best practices for DOORS usage on programs.
  • Create or help to create DOORS related training materials. Teach or assist in teaching these materials to BD DOORS users.
  • Assist with the resolution of defects filed internally against DOORS, and possibly lead defect resolution to conclusion.
  • Use DXL to write customizations to the DOORS platform to assist users in their day to day DOORS activities.
  • Use critical thinking skills for troubleshooting problems.
  • Assist with program requirements management planning (Program RM plan, program setup, module setup, schema development)
  • Perform requirements change administration & tracking (Change proposal reports, manage change proposals and enter into DOORS)
  • Assist systems engineers and other program members with requirements decomposition into SMART requirements (singular, measurable, achievable, realistic, traceable)
  • Generation of system level requirements documents, requirement baselines, traceability matrices and traceability analysis and reports.
  • Facilitate resolution of requirements compliance issues.
  • Interface with RM and configuration management teams to develop integrated RM & Configuration Management (CM) workflow activities for the assigned program.
  • Work closely with program managers and core teams to ensure good RM practices are being followed; reducing rework.
  • Balance technical tasks while supporting program managers schedule milestones.
  • Provide coaching and/or consultation to the personnel in various programs on the RM process and tool usage.
  • Develop and report RM-based metrics to program manager and others as required.
  • Identify, communicate and work with the RM team and DOORS administrator to improve existing RM processes and tools.
  • Seek to improve the process to increase quality of work, improve efficiency and reduce rework throughout the lifecycle of projects.
  • Support various programs on requirements management activities in DOORS; including importing and exporting documents, and requirements linking.
  • Provides progress reports and performance data as needed.
  • Assist in upgrades and validation of DOORS application.

Job Requirements
Qualifications:
  • Minimum BS degree
  • A minimum of 3 years experience using DOORS Requirements Management software.  
  • A minimum of 3 years design control experience.
Assets:
  • Able to think on feet and have strong problem solving skills.
  • Experience in one or more of the following: XML/DTD/XSL.
  • IBM Rational DOORS, DXL and Windows Operating Systems proficiency.
  • Experience working in a FDA or ISO regulated environment.
  • Self-driven while seeking and integrating input from BD Associates.
  • Experience working on cross functional teams and has the capability of providing coordination among groups or functions.
  • Experienced with the system development lifecycles and requirements development for complex systems.
  • Ability to communicate effectively in a team environment.
  • Experience leading projects is desirable.
  • IBM Rational DOORS, DXL and Windows Operating Systems proficiency.
  • Effective interpersonal, negotiation and leadership skills.
  • Effective analytical, quantitative, and problem solving skills.
  • Effective organization and planning skills.
  • Demonstrated abilities to commit to and complete assigned tasks.
  • Effective meeting and presentation skills.
  • Self-management and team working skills
  • Able to influence others without direct authority.
  • Excellent communication skills (written and verbal).
  • Must be able to communicate technical issues clearly to non-experts.
  • Familiarity with safety critical requirements.
  • Acts independently to determine the plan of attack on new or special assignments.
  • Has exercised independent judgment to determine evaluation criteria for obtaining results.
  • Has resolved significant and unique issues in creative and effective ways.
  • Customer service and problem solving skills.

BD Biosciences, a segment of BD, is one of the world’s leading businesses focused on bringing innovative research and clinical tools to life scientists and clinicians.  Principal product lines include fluorescence activated cell sorters and analyzers; cell imaging systems; monoclonal antibodies and kits; research reagent systems; tools to aid in drug discovery and growing tissue and cells; and diagnostic assays.  Its customers are involved in basic research, the discovery and development of drugs and vaccines, clinical trials, diagnostic testing and disease management. This diverse customer base includes academic and government institutions, pharmaceutical and biotech companies, and clinical laboratories.

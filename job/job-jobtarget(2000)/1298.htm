About the Department:
Shared Resources are core facilities that provide services and access to specialized equipment and expertise in support of a wide spectrum of research activities based at the Center and as time permits to the broader academic and biotechnology community.  These state-of-the-art facilities provide quality service in a cost-effective manner with a deep understanding of and commitment to the customer.  The Centers Shared Resources constitute one of the main reasons why outstanding scientists choose to come to and stay at the FHCRC to conduct their research.

About Us:
Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, home of three Nobel laureates, is an independent, nonprofit research institution dedicated to the development and advancement of biomedical research to eliminate cancer and other potentially fatal diseases. Recognized internationally for its pioneering work in bone-marrow transplantation, the Centers five scientific divisions collaborate to form a unique environment for conducting basic and applied science. The Hutchinson Center, in collaboration with its clinical and research partners, the University of Washington and Seattle Childrens, is the only National Cancer Institute-designated comprehensive cancer center in the Pacific Northwest. Join us and make a difference!

Equal Employment Opportunity:
The Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center and The Seattle Cancer Care Alliance are equal opportunity employers, committed to workforce diversity.

Qualifications:
Masters degree in a management/finance or scientific discipline preferred.  Minimum of five years financial-analyst and budgeting experience. Excellent written, communication, and interpersonal skills are paramount to success in this position.  Self motivated, with strong attention to detail and accuracy and the ability to work in a team environment. Strong leadership, supervisory skills, and budgeting experience are necessary to ensure ongoing viability of shared resources.

Major Duties:
Maintain and support accounting, budgeting, and financial modeling activities in support of shared resources; delivering resource-specific financial analyses, fee and usage analyses, reporting, forecasting and budget modeling services for the shared resource department in a changing funding environment. 
Form partnerships with resource managers to ensure continued success of each resource and a user-centered focus.
Develop open dialogue and interactions with center investigators to evaluate current operations and research support. 
Work with shared resource management team to develop revenue and expenditure goals for each shared resource.  Monitor and evaluate resource progress toward established revenue and expenditure goals and work with shared resource management team to evaluate and implement methods for improved progress as necessary.
Troubleshoot and address operational problems within shared resources that compromise future viability of the resource.
Provide support and guidance to resource managers regarding Center policies and procedures and personnel issues.
Coordinate annual budgeting and core grant submittal process for shared resources,   including developing supporting evaluations of total cost of ownership, activity and impact on research. 
Direct and provide leadership over the administrative support team (currently six individuals), working closely to ensure timely delivery of services and support.
Work with Facilities in assessing immediate and long-term physical plans and architectural needs of the shared resources.  Coordinate resource-planning activities, develop equipment and facility specifications, and provide detailed information to facilitate planning processes.
Work with Center departments including Center IT, EH&S, Purchasing, Accounting, Grants and Contracts to facilitate operations and compliance with Center policies.  Establish and implement revisions to policies and procedures as appropriate to ensure efficient operations; participate in evaluation of software packages to enhance operational efficiencies and support user needs.
Participate in discussions and provide project-management support for major initiatives for shared resources including resource planning and allocation
Work with shared resource management team to coordinate development of emergency preparedness plans in support of business continuity requirements.
Perform other responsibilities as required.

Responsibilities:
The Director of Operations reports to the Centers Vice President, Shared Resources and works independently in overseeing day-to-day operations of the department.  Special skill and expertise are applied in performing the financial and planning aspects of the position while strong interpersonal skills are applied in working with shared resource management and staff, research staff and center administration.  This is a newly created position within Shared Resources.  This role may expand over time and the successful applicant has the possibility of a career path to become the Centers Vice President of Shared Resources.

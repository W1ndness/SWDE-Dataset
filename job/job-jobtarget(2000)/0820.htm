GENERAL SUMMARY: 

 

As a Manager in Walmart’s Global eCommerce division, you’ll help define and implement components for Walmart’s next-generation, global e-Commerce Platform. In this hands-on position, you will design and implement backend application system components in a massively scalable, high performance, multi-tenant, international eCommerce platform in a Java EE and Oracle environment. You will work with the Architects, Engineering Managers, Business and Product management teams. The position will serve as the technical lead cum architect for the distributed development teams working on this global platform.  Some of the key areas you will contribute span Ecommerce domains like site design and development, site browse/search, SEO, shopping cart, Pricing, Inventory, Checkout, catalog content management and publishing, product information management, site analytics, CDN networks, site performance and scalability.

 

ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

1.     System Design, architecture and development (40%)

·         Serve as a manager for architecture, design and interface decisions related to Walmart’s web platform

·         Assess, recommend, build and integrate with off-the-shelf commercial and open-source software components

·         Review and recommend technologies from global software providers, open source as well as in-country specialized products, for inclusion into the platform

·         Work with members of the management team to define and drive technical strategy and architectural direction for the platform

·         Provide guidance to Walmart’s eCommerce initiatives in various countries

·         Assess feasibility of new business requirements and design appropriate solutions.

·         Explore web technologies such as personalization, search, availability, distributed systems to enhance or introduce them to the Global ecommerce platform

·         Recommend engineering best practices, including use of design patterns, code reviews and automated unit testing

·         Collaborate, communicate with different teams, and triage technical issues across teams.

2.     Partnership with Lines of Business/Partners (30%)

·         Collaborate with engineering and business partners to define product features, architectural deliverables and project estimates

·         Support Business requests for investigation and analysis

·         Collaborate with third party implementation partners (ISV) to review the deliverables, define and enforce standards.

3.     Research and innovation (20%)

·         Research technology trends and apply the results to solve business problems

·         Develop solution prototype and be able to demonstrate value proposition of the solutions to a wide audience

4.     Ongoing Support & Maintenance (10%)

·         Provide support in troubleshooting / investigations

·         Respond quickly to critical requests

 

Job Requirements

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

1) Knowledge, Skills, Abilities:

Functional Competencies:

·         Required:

·         Experience working in ecommerce industry managing high volume applications.

·         Subject matter expertise in building large scale retail B2C web applications using:
- Cart, Inventory, Promotions, Pricing, Checkout, CMS (Fatwire, Interwoven, Vignette)
- Commerce Platforms (e.g. ATG, WebSphere Commerce, Blue Martini)
- Search (e.g. Endeca, Fast, Verity, ATG Search)
- Integration of product catalog, Web content, outbound feeds and Price Comparison Engines.
- ESB Technologies (e.g. Tibco Active Matrix, Websphere) & CDN (Akamai), Caching technologies

·         Experience with internationalization, localization & translation

·         Comprehensive SDLC experience including:
- Strong design skills – use of UML, Rational Rose, patterns, OOA/OOD.
- Agile implementation principles and practices
- Use of version control management such of SVN, CVS, Clearcase, or perforce,
- Production environment deployment and maintenance

·         Solid hands-on understanding of DB for the Web environment

·         Performance tuning and optimization for scalability and high availability.

·         Experience with Oracle DW, ATG, J2EE stack.

·         Preferred:

·         Experience working in a multi-tenant or “Software-as-a-Service” (SaaS) eCommerce company

·         W3C compliance and conventions

·         JBoss, Weblogic, or Websphere Portal Servers, Java, JEE, J2SE 5 / 6, queuing systems (JMS)

·         Ability to work with outsourced technology partners based in multiple, global locations

·         Experience working in a multi-country eCommerce or technology implementation

·         Experience implementing service-oriented (SOA) architectures and EAI technologies like Tibco

·         Experience in similar roles at prominent Internet companies, with understanding of scaling issues.

·         Ability to work with business users, product managers, QA, Release, UI and cross-functional teams

·         Ability to work in an unstructured, fast-paced environment

 

 

Leadership Competencies:

·         Persuade / influence team members, sell ideas effectively, get results and follow through, both within Walmart and in our partners

·         Strong decision-making skills, and an understanding of where projects fit into development strategy

 

2)  Minimum Educational Level:

·         B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science or equivalent

 

3)  Experience:

·         8-10 years in overall software engineering design and development experience

 

4)  Physical Requirements:

·         25% international travel.

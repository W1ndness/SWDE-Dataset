About us:  Spectrum, one of the country’s largest and most successful athletic club operators in the upscale segment of the industry, has been helping members meet their health and fitness goals for nearly three decades.  With $100M in revenues and more than 100,000 members in 23 locations clustered  in Southern California and San Antonio, TX,  we take pride in creating a sense of community whereby members are offered numerous options to achieve results while experiencing exceptional personalized service via resort-like amenities at best-in-class facilities.  www.SpectrumClubs.com

?The Opportunity:   As a member of our accounting team, the Staff Accountant will apply the principles of accounting to analyze financial information and prepare timely and accurate financial reports and statements. Staff accountants are responsible for recording the transactions of the business in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP), and generating the financial statements for each business unit, consolidating the reporting on a division and consolidated basis, and working with business unit managers on an ongoing basis to review financial performance and identify opportunities for improvement. 

?If you are:

A bright, confident, detail oriented team player with 4+ years of accounting experience; and  

A well organized accountant  who exhibits the ability to synthesize complex or diverse information, skillfully gather and research data by utilizing intuition and experience to interpret and analyze facts and problem solve.

?Who Can:

Successfully apply the principles of accounting to analyze financial information and prepare timely and accurate financial reports and statements; and

Multi-task and meet challenges with resourcefulness, effectively manage competing demands, take responsibility for fulfillment of obligations, and are willing to put in work time necessary to reach goals.

?And have:

·         Business acumen with innate aptitude to understand and demonstrate CORE values that are common in all Spectrum clubs (Community, Options, Results, Experience).

·         Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year college or university majoring in Accounting, Finance or other related field; or equivalent combination of education and experience.

·         Excellent computer skills including the MS Office software suite. Outlook, Word and exceptional Excel expertise required.

·         Proficient knowledge of Great Plains and FAS asset accounting systems and Crystal Reports experience, preferred.

·         Demonstrated intermediate level knowledge of generally accepted accounting practices, including journal entries, accruals, balance sheets, reconciliations, and accounting projects.

·         Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists and effectively communicate findings in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.

·         Specialized skills such as fixed asset accounting, lease accounting, and tax preferred.

 

?Consider a career on the Spectrum team! 

In addition to  a great location in El Segundo, CA,  next door to our flagship South Bay Club, we offer a competitive salary and benefits package, including family Club membership, and an opportunity to join a team that is passionate about  achieving results while ensuring exceptional membership and team member experiences!

Sr. Software Quality Assurance (SQA) Automation Engineer

This position can be located in....

Keene, NH
  • Dublin, OH
  • Rockland, MA
  • Southington, CT
  • or St. Paul, MN

    Experience with QTP and Quality Center required!!



    • Supported by experience in designing, building and executing automated tests against multiple applications, the successful candidate will be a team player with t he ability to lead, educate and mentor others as we integrate automated testing into our Portfolio via QTP and Quality Center. This role requires a hands-on tester using automated and manual testing, implementing best practices to ensure the software works as expected to comply with medical device regulations.

    Specifically, the Sr. Software Quality Assurance (SQA) Automation Engineer will:

    • Perform QuickTest Pro (QTP) and Visual Basic (VB) scripting to automated scripts for Quality Center application with eSignature capabilities.
    • Write reports that summarize executed testing for business computer systems implementation and upgrades in an FDA regulated environment.
    • Coordinate program tasks with ad-hoc validation team members
    • Provide technical expertise in the evaluation, design and implementation of new, and improvement of existing, software, technology and equipment for RA/QA use of business computer systems.
    • Create, execute and coordinate computer system validation testing (automated and manual).
    • Set up and mentor other users in Quality Center.
    • Utilize the most cost effective methods in blending automation and manual testing in writing scripts.
    • Schedule testing development and execution based on master plan.
    • Strategically determine cost of automation development versus manual testing.

    Job Requirements

    Qualifications include:

    • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in relevant field.
    • Minimum of 5 years experience in software testing.
    • Advanced skills and practical experience (1-3 years) in manual and automated testing: writing, updating and executing tests.
    • Practical experience with Quality Center 9.x for creating and executing tests.
    • Experience using Defect Tracking tools to log and report on defects.
    • Working knowledge of Oracle E-Business Suite.
    • One to three years of experience in a regulated industry; medical device industry preferred.
    • MS Word, Excel, database ERP and advanced keyboarding speed.
    • Familiarity with ISO 9001, FDA 21 CFR Parts 820 and 11 preferred.
    • Ability to work within a structured test environment.
    • Excellent interpersonal skills with ability to work as an individual contributor and a team member.
    • Superior attention to detail, organized, assertive with strong verbal and written communication skills.
    • Self-starter, with strong analytical skills who thrives on finding and resolving issues.
    • Ability to prioritize multiple work assignments, perform well under pressure and to deadlines.
    • Ability to work collaboratively with IT Business Analysts, Developers and business users on resolution of issues.

Santa Monica, California-based Ascent Media Group, LLC is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Ascent Media Corporation (Nasdaq: ASCMA). With more than 40 facilities worldwide, Ascent Media Group is a leading provider of fully integrated, end-to-end services for the global digital media supply chain. Guided by our entrepreneurial culture, we blend breakthrough creative with emerging technologies to deliver some of the most advanced and innovative media solutions to help film and television studios, independent producers, broadcast networks, cable channels, advertising agencies and other companies make, manage, move and monetize their digital media.

We are seeking a Director, VOD  Service Operations for our Burbank, CA facility.

Job Summary:

The position will lead the team that provides operational support for Ascent’s VOD management platform currently deployed by cable operators. This is a relatively new line of business that has strong growth potential as the industry continues to evolve VOD offerings and move towards “TV Everywhere” based on IP video technologies. The Operations team provides several key functions to clients and helps direct product/service improvements for the line of business. Key functions include: monitoring end to end content flows on a national basis, troubleshooting issues, deploying new markets and new features, analyzing overall performance for its clients, configuring various business rules that drive automated systems, providing input on areas to improve the service and value to clients.

The ideal candidate will have an established track record of running customer support operations, preferably with technical operation experience for a cable industry or broadcast related company.

 

Job Responsibilities

•           Leads the team including; hiring, training, directing, planning, setting workflows, budgeting.

•           Develops collaborative relationships with other technology vendors used by client, Ascent’s       development and infrastructure engineering departments, product development, sales/business development.

•           Develops and maintains relationships with clients at various levels of their organization ranging from NOC staff to senior management who have VOD responsibilities

•           Leads efforts for constantly improving the quality of service, support, efficiency

•           Oversees performance reporting that is both client and internally focused. Motivate team to achieve performance goals. Help drive strategies for achieving goals for both clients and internal team.

•           Engage in problem solving, trouble shooting for high priority issues.  Clear and timely communication to senior management of client organization and  within Ascent

•           Oversee the implementation and management of operational support tools used for monitoring, ticketing, reporting, communicating, etc..

 •          Defines, implements and enforces processes and procedures for the efficient, scalable and satisfactory service operations that adhere to customers SLAs and internal OLAs

 


Job Requirements

Job Requirements:

•           7+ years of operations management experience, with hands-on operations experience

•           3+ years operating software based services in particular content management services

•           Structured analysis and problem solving

•           Proven leadership with ability to manage daily operations effectively

•           Good project management skills, including planning, timeline, dependency and risk management

•           Service & customer focused, performance metrics driven individual

•           Cable or broadcast industry experience preferable

•           Excellent interpersonal and written communication skills

•           Good understanding of digital processes and file based delivery systems is desirable

•           Knowledge of media industry business and content management processes is desirable

HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. If you enjoy developing challenging user interfaces, this is the job for you!  This is a opportunity to make a difference in a world leading network security development team.
 
TippingPoint, now a part of Hewlett Packard, is the market leader in in-line Intrusion Prevention Systems with well over 6,000 global organizations trusting TippingPoint/HP to secure their networks and assets. We deliver the highest performance, lowest latency and most secure network security systems in the industry. TippingPoint products use state of the art technology - high-speed custom hardware, multicore processors, and multi-threaded software architectures in C, C++ and Java. We couple these technologies with market leading user interface design and usability to offer a intuitive and effective system that is valued and trusted in many of the top Fortune-100 companies in the world.
 
Tippingpoint is now growing its development team to enter new markets and build exciting new products. This provides a rare opportunity for a candidate who enjoys a challenge and a high performance team culture, and who wants to join a new team early in the development cycle.
 
The role is to design and develop market leading user interfaces to our product. The successful candidate must have drive, initiative, and commitment to customers.  As part of a world class and growing software development user interface team, you will be responsible for both the client and server side design of the user interface. Success in the role means understanding market requirements, considering how a customer would want to use a feature, designing the user experience, and then developing high performance web/command line interfaces. Technologies include HTML, Javascript, C, C++, XML, Apache, Linux, embedded vxWorks, server side C/C++/scripting, and much more.
 
Duties and Responsibilities:
 
?         Work in a cross functional team to develop user experience with the best technologies in accordance with design and business requirements.
?         Collaboration with the wider security software team and product management, to understand how features can best provide value to our customer.
?         Assessing usability and workflow efficiency in a user-centered design.
?         Creative design in web and command line interface using appropriate tools and technologies.
?         Software programming in the web browser and in the embedded web server environment.
?         Working on user interfaces and interactions between all TippingPoint products, producing a system with best-of-breed end user experience.
?         Defining design guidelines and styles, and driving them across the organization.
 
What we bring to the table:
?         The opportunity to shape and influence the whole user interface of major new product line.
?         Exciting technologies and product design challenges.
?         A dynamic work environment and a culture of excellence in a world class software engineering team.
?         A place of influence in a world-leading technology organization that is successful in the market, growing and has big plans.
?         All the benefits of a large company - a competitive benefits package, many training opportunities and growth opportunities both locally and in the wider company. What you should bring to the table:
?         Bachelor's Degree in Human Factors, HCI, industrial design, visual arts or related area, preferably with some Computer Science.
?         Deep knowledge of information architecture, interaction flow, user interface and visual design
?         A solid understanding of web design principles and best use of current web technologies and scalable dynamic user interfaces
?         A proven ability to work within ambiguous design constraints.
?         Proven creativity, vision and innovation in user interface design.
?         Enthusiasm and motivation.
?         Creativity and a commitment to customer quality.
?         Strong communication skills - both written and verbal.
?         Strong organizational skills.
?         Experience in cutting edge user interface tools and key tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML and CSS.
?         Strong programming in HTML/Javascript is required. C/C++ programming is desirable.
?         An open and collaborative mindset suited to a high performing team.
?         Ideally a knowledge of networking protocols and network security technologies such as Firewall, VPN and Intrusion Prevention.

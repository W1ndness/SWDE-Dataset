Are you a Relationship Expert? Client stewardship is a cornerstone of our success, and Courtesy Associates' Strategic Account Directors maintain primary responsibility for outstanding client stewardship. They masterfully establish and nurture client relationships that position us as a trusted strategic resource for convention/meeting related programs, yield excellent revenue retention, foster new business growth, and achieve financial goals. Working hand in hand with meeting planning teams, they ensure our clients receive Best in Class service on all aspects of their convention and meeting needs.

Courtesy Associates (www.courtesyassociates.com), a subsidiary of SmithBucklin (www.smithbucklin.com), is a recognized leader in delivering turnkey conference, meeting and event management services worldwide. Our clients benefit from the experience and continuity of our best-in-class, senior-level staff, who are dedicated to serving government, academia, technical and medical societies, corporations and foundations.

Strategic Account Directors play a critical role in client relationship building, business penetration, and providing outstanding customer service and are responsible for developing creative strategic solutions for our clients.  Strategic Account Directors work hand in hand with the meeting planning team.

Role/Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for account/revenue retention, generation and penetration and achieving financial goals set annually.
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients; Create strategies to retain, grow and enhance client relationships.
  • Become an expert in market knowledge, competition, trends and maintaining contacts within industries in pursuit of new opportunities.
  • Negotiation and execution of major hotel and vendor contracts.
  • Manage development, maintenance, and growth of all convention/meeting related programs for clients.
  • Manage all aspects of annual conferences, meetings and seminars (pre-planning, on-site execution, and post event wrap up, research and recommendations for future sites, and conference promotions).
  • Lead team members, and serve as key interface with other team members, internal account team members, and external clients (vendors) maximizing productivity and delivering high quality conventions/trade shows.
  • Ensure effective meetings as evidenced by early problem resolution, positive attendee and client feedback.
  • Ensure critical deadlines and budgetary guidelines are adhered to, and response expectations and quality standards are met.
  • Effectively manage complex and multiple projects, budgets, meetings, and work activities and increase profit through expense management, efficient operations, effective negotiations, and smart time management.
  • Manage crisis situations effectively. Demonstrate ability to learn and adapt to changing procedures, methods or processes and assist in teaching team.
  • Demonstrate industry knowledge by contributing effective and innovative ideas to client strategic planning and analysis processes.
  • Directly supervise staff.

Job Requirements
  • BA/BS required
  • Client relationship management role in one or more of the meeting/event, conference, hospitality industry
  • Experience in a multi-client environment
  • Financial/budget management
  • Demonstrated history of building and growing client relationships and revenue
  • PC experience with Windows operating system and a variety of software programs (Microsoft Office, and/or exhibit software)
  • Excellent knowledge of meeting & event industry terminology and trends
  • Ability to work as team leader, team member, and independently to best serve clients
  • High level of professionalism

JOB DESCRIPTION

The Society of Manufacturing Engineers (SME), the premier source for manufacturing knowledge, education and networking, is looking for a Member and Industry Relations Manager (MIRM).   The MIRM will have direct responsibility for membership recruitment and retention in the Northeast region (CT, DE, ME, MD,NH, NY, NJ, RI, VT), a Technical Community, and various member engagement projects. The Member and Industry Relations Manager has overall responsibility for regional strategy development and builds strong relationships with corporate and individual stakeholders in SME core and target industries. The manager works closely with internal staff to ensure efficient integration and delivery of value-added products and services to the members and member units of SME. This position may travel up to 50% of the time.

Product Planning and Concept Development

  • Utilize market data, industry shifts, & trends to forecast & test new product concepts for membership in assigned region & technical community
  • Working Knowledge & success in budget development, preparation, analysis & forecasting
  • Negotiation & influencing skills to align staff, volunteers & other stakeholders around concept development

Relationship Based Selling - Business Development

  • Planning and execution of projects intended to raise awareness of SME membership in target markets, prospecting & follow-up
  • High value product selling - SME Membership, long term relationship development that includes relationship & expectation management

Project Management

  • Accountable for the effective implementation and management of project based work
  • Manage inter-departmental and volunteer committee relationships to meet project goals
  • Assure staff & volunteer leadership are engaged & kept apprised of project status, & that needed approvals from key staff and leaders are obtained
  • Upon project completion, review and assess for degree of success, efficiency, timeliness, and provide recommendations for improvements

ABOUT THE COMPANY

                                                                                                             
Founded in 1932, the Society of Manufacturing Engineers (www.sme.org) is the premier source for manufacturing knowledge, education and networking. Through its many programs, events and activities, SME connects manufacturing practitioners to each other, to the latest technology and the most up-to-date processes spanning all manufacturing industries and disciplines, plus the key areas of aerospace and defense, medical device, motor vehicles, including motorsports, oil and gas and alternative energy. A 501(c)3 organization, SME has members around the world and is supported by a network of technical communities and chapters worldwide.


Job Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience in engineering, manufacturing, or technology required
  • Minimum of 6-years experience in manufacturing and/or engineering
  • Requires project management experience relevant to delivery of mission-critical projects
  • Demonstrated success in establishing and maintaining highly visible and effective interaction with team members with various reporting relationships
  • Facilitation skills including running effective meetings, decision making, facilitating group dynamics and conflict resolution
  • Demonstrated ability to work with volunteers and executives in business environment
  • Intermediate to expert level knowledge of Microsoft Office software, including Word, Excel, Powerpoint, and MS Project
  • 50% Travel Required

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Demonstrated success facilitating and managing volunteers
  • Advanced degree in business or engineering
  • Manufacturing industry knowledge specific to the specified geographic region
  • Process orientation with focus on reducing waste and increasing value
  • Ability to distill information from customer/member interactions into business intelligence via CRM system
  • Attention to detail with superior track record of follow-up/follow-through
  • SME Lean Certification preferred
  • Subject Matter Expertise in one or more of the following areas: AMA, F&F, ILC, MER, MMR, PPDM, PCC, RTAM, Lean, Quality
  • Industry Knowledge and background in SME core industries or target markets of Aerospace & Defense, Medical Device, Transportation and Energy

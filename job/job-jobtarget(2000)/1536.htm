THE COMPANY

LP Amina (www.lpamina.com) is a multinational environmental engineering company with research and development activities in the US, Europe and Asia. Created to tackle a wide range of environmental issues, the LP Amina of today was established in 2007, but its roots are built on a tradition of excellence reaching back over a quarter century. The company has over 100 full time employees and a network of over 2,000 technicians working in diverse locations around the globe. At LP Amina, we strive to be the most innovative environmental solution provider to the energy industry and to create a sustainable, low cost energy future. Our Mission is to deliver the most advanced environmental technology and solutions to support the sustainable use of coal resources for power, chemistry and other applications. Innovation and speed to market are our competitive advantages as we provide superior benefit to our customers, our society and our shareholders.

POSITION SUMMARY:

LP Amina is seeking to hire an experienced senior mechanical engineer for a short-term assignment estimated to last 6-12 months.  This position will be based, for the most part, in Beijing China on either a full time basis for the duration of the assignment or on a travel basis (fully paid by LP Amina) for significant stretches (i.e. 80% in China, 20% in home country) of time. This position will have two primary responsibilities:

  1. This engineer will be involved in the critical mechanical design of the company's systems and technology.  The successful candidate will have at least 15 years experience at a senior mechanical engineering level with large scale R&D projects, preferably in power generation projects, including ductwork and/or pressure part design. 
  2. This position will spend a significant amount of time training several less experienced mechanical engineers located in China. Training will include effective utilization and instruction of applicable software, techniques and overall engineering principles utilized in the United States and other highly developed countries.

The qualified candidate will possess the ability to work effectively in a team environment.  The individual will be able to communicate effectively with peers, supervisors and management who often will utilize English as a second language.  The successful candidate must also be able to manage multiple priorities effectively and have proven decision-making skills. This position reports to the Director of Technology and will require approximately 10-20% travel while in China.


Job Requirements

PRIMARY DUTIES: 

  • Proven experience in the design, construction and operation of large multidisciplinary projects involving large scale components and complex systems.
  • Experience in the design, fabrication, construction and operation of large scale R&D experiments, including experience commercializing R&D technologies.
  • A detailed understanding of mechanical systems coupled with a basic understanding of combustion system design. Good systems engineering skills are a definite plus.
  • A demonstrated ability to work independently as well as a member of a team.
  • Ability to work in a start-up environment where quality and timeliness are essential.
  • Following standard techniques and procedures, develop mechanical seal designs, determine the proper application of a mechanical seal, determine material to be used in the application and make necessary engineering calculations.
  • Aid in the transition of a product from development into production with emphasis on the bills of material.
  • Analyze mechanical seal design and component part design, prepare drawings, sketches and specifications required to engineer or modify existing company products to each customer's needs.
  • Build prototype models for test and evaluation.
  • Evaluate customer requirements, research the necessary data and determine cost effective materials of construction.
  • Provide guidance to designer or drafter assigned to complete layout and detail drawings.
  • Communicate orally and in writing with customers, sales and company personnel and vendors providing counseling, recommendations and technical assistance.
  • Meet with customers in plant or at customer's site to discuss, analyze and resolve field problems.
  • Analyze, evaluate and rectify mechanical seal failures internally and externally.
  • Approve/reject purchase of fabricated parts not to drawing specification.
  • Initiate new and revised current standard seal parts lists, typical drawings and master sheets.
  • Prepare material and other relevant product specifications for internal and external use.
  • Additional duties and responsibilities will vary based upon departmental needs.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE / QUALIFICATIONS

  • A BS in mechanical engineering or in a related engineering discipline.
  • At least 15 years experience at a senior engineering level in large facility design and construction. Power generation design is a definite plus.
  • Significant experience working collaboratively in foreign cultures for extended periods of time with a strong bias towards China
  • Proven track-record of instruction or instructor-related positions teaching mechanical engineering principles
  • Registration as a Professional Engineer is also a plus.
  • Good verbal and written communication skills.

Qualified candidates will be required to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and/or confidentiality agreement prior to employment

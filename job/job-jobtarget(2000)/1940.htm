Mitsubishi Electric Automation

Vernon Hills, Illinois, United States

This is not an entry-level position.

Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: SAP Basis Administrator
Required Travel: 0-10%
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Posted: October 11, 2010
Industry: Industrial Machinery & Components
Job Function: Database Specialist, Specialist, Support, Support Consultant, Technical Consultant
Min Experience: 2-3 Years
Job Type: Full-Time

Position Summary:

Mitsubishi Electric Automation, Inc, is seeking an SAP Basis Administrator to be responsible for the support of the company's SAP landscape and will execute daily operational activities related to the SAP environment.  The Administrator will also provide direction and support for the SAP Business Analyst team as it relates to minor enhancements and full system upgrades.

 Specific responsibilities include managing SAP related technical infrastructure projects, coordinating with IT and business teams to minimize impact to the user community. In addition, provide technical guidance and support to the IT and business teams, to insure successful, on-time completion of projects. Leading technical design reviews, validating enterprise approaches, and defining application systems that support redesigned or improved business processes.


Job Requirements

Position Duties and Responsibilities:  

  • Perform System Installations, System Refreshes, Client Copies, Change Management, Performance Tuning, Transport Management, Data Archiving, System Administration & Monitoring, Troubleshooting, Problem Analysis, and Client Strategy
  • Install SAP system and Oracle database software using SAP installation tools
  • Upgrade SAP and Oracle using SAP upgrade tools (Both ABAP and JAVA)
  • Install SAP and Oracle software patches
  • Perform all post installation/upgrade configuration tasks.
  • Utilize solution monitoring capabilities of Solution Manager, Review Early Watch Reports, and perform necessary changes based on recommendations from reports.
  • Keep abreast of changing technologies and their applicability to the MEAU business environment.
  • Ensure adherence to accepted project management practices.
  • Resolving and analyzing SAP problems and creating/maintaining of SAP related technical documentation, standards and policies.
  • Serve as a resource to IT Business Analysts, customers, and ABAP Developers in designing, configuring and implementing a SAP System supporting business and operational needs
  • Actively seeks ways to apply technology to business issues to effectively improve business results.
  • Provide production support and perform on-call duties (24x7 on-call support as needed)
  • Provides cross-training to other technical team members to insure back-up support expertise. 
  • Performs other job functions as assigned.

 Supervisory/Management Responsibilities:

  • None directly

Travel Requirements: 

  • Occasional travel within North America and South America. 

Essential Education, Skills and Experience:

  • BS/BA in Computer Science, Information Technology or related discipline or equivalent experience
  • 4+ years of SAP Basis Administration experience
  • Experience administering SAP ECC, CRM, BI, Solution Manager, and/or Enterprise Portal landscapes in a SAP NetWeaver 7.0 and above environment utilizing Windows Operating Systems and Oracle Databases
  • Experience performing multiple SAP system installations and managing SAP landscapes

Beneficial Education, Skills and Experience

  •  SAP Enterprise Portal and SAP Netweaver Certification preferred
  • Experience performing multiple SAP system upgrades and SAP EHP upgrades preferred
  • Knowledge of installation, design, maintenance, and Basis functions within Solution Manager
  • Experience with NetApp storage solutions a plus
  • Experience with administering, installing, updating and supporting 3rd party SAP integration software such as: TAXWARE, TAXAUTOMATION, UPS WORLDSHIP, and FEDEX SHIPMANAGER, etc
  • Strong problem solving skills; ability to use all resources available to resolve issues.
  • Strong teamwork and interpersonal skills, possessing the ability to communicate effectively with both technical and business teams
  • Strong technical knowledge and ability to express complex technical concepts in terms which are understandable to the business or non-IT individual
  • Maintain currency of technical knowledge using all available resources, including membership in a technical architecture/support organization
  • Strong Project Management Skills

We are an Equal Opportunity Employer.

We offer a business casual work environment along with an excellent benefits and compensation package including 401(k) and tuition reimbursement.

For more information on our Company, please visit us at www.meau.com

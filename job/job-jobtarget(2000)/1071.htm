Advancement Services Specialist (003474)
Office of Institutional Advancement
Non-Exempt, Regular, Full-time, Grade 9


The University of Maryland University College (UMUC) seeks an Advancement Services Specialist in the Office of Institutional Advancement (IA). Under the direction of the Director of Advancement Services, the Advancement Services Specialist is responsible for maintaining the overall biographical data in the UMUC alumni/donor database. This position will work closely with the Director and Assistant Director in assisting with the daily gift processing activities as well. Specific responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Creating and maintaining alumni and donors records in Sungard Advance Database (Advance)


  • Updating biographic/demographic information in the database


  • Managing the data import/export transfer from iModules (alumni online community) to Advance


  • Working with the office of Student Affairs, facilitate the new graduate uploads from PeopleSoft to Advance


  • Provide donor and/or alumni data request as needed


  • Maintain the daily gift log for all gifts that pledge payments received through Institutional Advancement


  • Dissemination of all acknowledgement letters and gift receipts in a timely manner


  • Assist in entering and maintaining gifts and pledges into the database


  • Represent UMUC at the monthly Advance Advisory Meeting at University System of Maryland Foundation on occasion


  • Supervise student worker’s workload


  • Other job-related duties as assigned


    • Job Requirements


    Requires a Bachelors degree in Business Management or related fields and a minimum of one year experience in data management and/or data quality assurance. The successful candidate must have: skill in performing arithmetic calculations in Excel, the use of computers and other office equipment; ability to use word-processing, database, and spreadsheet software; ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing; independent judgment, superior level of professionalism and be able to plan, organize, and prioritize assignments; ability to gather and summarize information using a variety of sources; ability to handle highly confidential information. General knowledge and/or working experience in a fundraising environment and experience using a donor management/alumni database are preferred.

    POSITION AVAILABLE IMMEDIATELY

    SALARY COMMENSURATE WITH EXPERIENCE

    ELIGIBLE FOR TUITION REMISSION 8 CREDITS PER SEMESTER

The Solutions division within the US Public Sector Consulting Practice is now searching for a senior level Manager based in the Washington DC Metro region with experience managing the growth and development of programs and accounts within the Federal Intel/Military community.  The prime focus for this Principal role will be Client Infrastructure, Microsoft & Workplace Services
 
.  Below is a description for this role and the qualifications for this position:
 
 
The Practice Principal (PP) leads the large, complex, and customized engagements and has the following responsibilities:
 
1.       Support Sales of  Practice Solutions - Client & Microsoft  Principal  is expected to run a team of architects and technologists that independently serve as content knowledge expert on HP's workplace services  & Microsoft Solutions with external Corporate, Enterprise, and Government Tier 1 customers.   This will involve activities like building credibility with clients, proposal preparation, as well as development and delivery of executive and management level customer presentations. 
2.       Serve as Lead Architect and Delivery Manager of Practice Solutions - Client & Microsoft  Principal  is expected to manage the delivery effort of multiple concurrent projects serving as Lead Architect, delivery sponsor, or team leader on projects.
3.       Maintain State of the Art Skills in Practice Solutions - Client & Microsoft  Principal  is expected to take responsibility for updating the team and leveraged staff technical, consultative and business skills, including knowledge related to Microsoft Exchange, Unified Communications, Collaboration & SharePoint, VDI, Virtualization, Application deployment, Security, Database Platforms, Desktop Deployments and Microsoft operations.
4.       Mentor & Manage nical Consultants - Client & Microsoft  Principal  will also have responsibility to mentor and manage other consultants in the solution, build practice community and leverage the cross HP technical value.
5.        Contribute to HP Intellectual Property and Knowledge.   Client & Microsoft  Principal  has responsibility to ensure re-use of current  IP during delivery  and all engagement developed collateral from pre-sales and delivery is submitted into the HP  knowledge repository and is leveraging available collateral for efficient delivery.    The Principal may also lead the development of new sales tools and delivery methodology.
 
Additional leadership may include:
  • Selling and delivering engagements in cross practice domain areas.
  • Managing a series of projects to achieve company targets including revenue, profit, growth, utilization and customer satisfaction.
  • Influencing and coordinating sales efforts by working closely with Client Principals and Managing Client Principals.
  • Proactively managing delivery projects and teams to ensure successful delivery of HP solutions.
  • Providing coaching, mentoring and performance feedback to his/her teams.
  • Actively seeking and build long-lasting relationships with key customers.
  • Performing financial forecasting and manage P&L of his/her accounts/projects.

In order to satisfy our contractual obligations with clients, the successful candidate will be required to have an active TS/SCI clearance with full lifestyle polygraph.

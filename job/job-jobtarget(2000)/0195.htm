HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Conducts or participates in multidisciplinary research and collaborates with equipment designers and/or hardware engineers in the design, development, and utilization of electronic data processing systems software. Designing, developing, troubleshooting and debugging of software programs. Develops software for operating systems, compilers, routers, networks, utilities, databases, etc. Determines hardware compatibility and/or influences hardware design. Applies advanced subject matter knowledge to complex business issues, and is regarded as a subject matter expert. Frequently contributes to the development of new ideas and methods. Works on complex problems/projects where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of multiple factors. Exercises significant independent judgment within broadly defined policies and practices to determine best method for accomplishing work and achieving objectives. Leads and/or provides expertise to functional project teams and may participate in cross-functional initiatives. May provide mentoring and guidance to lower level employees. Acts as an expert providing direction and guidance to process improvements and establishing policies. Frequently represents the organization to external customers/clients.
 

Be a leader and part of an exciting team testing Enterprise Integrity systems in HP. Our team provides user-level platform testing necessary to deliver the most defect-free product to our business units.

 

The team is homed in Enterprise Systems Lab (ESL) which develops mid-range and high-end Itanium enterprise server platforms that run HP-UX, Windows, OpenVMS, and Linux operating systems.

 

As a part of the team, you will be responsible to:

  • Lead System Validation Programs including the following activities:
    • Work as part of a 'virtual' system validation team. This includes providing test plans, schedule forecasts, prototype needs, status updates, testing, RCA, risk management, and working with remote team members.
    • Work with hardware and software partners to understand, debug, and resolve issues that come up during testing.
    • Manage prototype and lab infrastructure needs to ensure that all material needed to complete testing is available when needed. Stay within forecast budget. Keep all HW/SW updated.
    • Support CPE/customer escalation efforts as needed and requested. This may include attending PACPE meetings, SWAT team support, and RCA efforts.
    • Provide testing for all CPE releases on all Enterprise Integrity and HP9000 servers. That includes providing test plans, schedule forecasts, prototype needs, status updates, testing, RCA and risk management.
    • Continue to improve our test efficiency.
    • Create and manage test scripts and software infrastructure
  • Program Leader General responsibilities
    • Technical details of program and execution of tests
    • PMF (Project Management Fundamentals) management including good communication with leaders and the program
    • Manage (organization or product) metrics including execution metrics and defect metrics
Education and Experience Required:
 

Musts:

BSCE, BSEE or equivalent degree

Experience in a leadership role with proven execution track record

UNIX system administration, basic UNIX scripting skills

Knowledge of testing methodologies, including best in class industry QA/Test practices and tools

Strong verbal and written communication skills, including documentation for training and maintenance.

Strong organizational skills

Experience working well with internal and external partners

Flexible, team player


 

Desired:

HP-UX system administration and installation.  Network administration, SAN administration.  Server cluster administration

Experience in full software development life cycle (SDLC) and methodologies

Experience in firmware and hardware testing or development.

Experience in developing automated tests and test infrastructure, including consulting on design for testability or unit test development

Perl, C, or equivalent code development experience

Possess strong project and resources management skills

Leadership qualities (e.g., integrity, attention to detail, sense of urgency, goal oriented, motivator, etc.)

The ideal candidate has a passion for test and is recognized by others as a champion for system quality.

Hands on experience modifying hardware for fault injection and analysis


Typically a Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience and a minimum of 8 plus year's related experience or Master's degree and a minimum of 4-6 plus years experience.

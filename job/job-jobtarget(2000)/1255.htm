HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Strategic Event Manager Objectives:
  • Create a branded presence for CSBU and its offerings at events that is compelling to customer segments and differentiated versus competitors
  • Implement an integrated event marketing strategy that includes the appropriate elements of the marketing mix
  • Throughout the event campaign, engages potential customers and converts them to leads before, during, and after the event
  • Maximizes CSBU return on investment from event participation
Roles and Responsibilities:
  • Partnering with business development and sales, create and manage the event-based CSBU marketing and sales strategy, messages and operational plan
  • Ensure CSBU strategy and message is reflected into every aspect of events
  • Continually investigate and make recommendations for events that align with the CSBU business objectives and relevant to customer segments and maintain/refresh event schedule
  • Maximize acquisition and interaction with existing and potential customers pre, during and post event
  • Create content and deliverables as needed to support event strategy and execution
  • Integrate events with strategic announcements and campaign execution plans
  • Host/participate in working and review meetings with regions, core teams and executives regarding event strategy and plans
  • Capture ROI event metrics including output, outcome, impact and operational efficiency
  • Create a repeatable event process, capture best practices and identify new opportunities to increase ROI
  • Analysis of competitive event presence and event campaign programs
  • Negotiate contracts with hotels, conference centers and related vendors
  • Responsible for planning and management of budget for events and associated campaign elements
  • Select, manage, develop and motivate agencies and other business partners such as booth/event properties, booth equipment, signage, and giveaways
Requirements
  • Self Starter with 10+ years marketing/marcom experience
  • Bachelor's Degree (undergraduate) required, preference would be in business or marketing or communications
  • Proven experience leading measuring events in support of business objectives
  • Fine-tuned agency and budget management skills
  • Strong communications expertise, including messaging experience, written and presentation abilities
  • Strong negotiating skills
  • Excellent project management skills
  • Diplomacy and conflict resolution experience
  • Willing to travel 25% as part of on-site event mgmt
  • Ability to work both autonomously and as part of a team
  • Excellent people and management skills to interact with colleagues, partners, customers, vendors and cross-functional team
  • Ability to interact with all levels of an organization from Sr mgmt to technical team
  • Knowledge/experience in not only event mgmt but other marketing such as PR, social media, promotions, etc.

Disney Consumer Products (DCP) is the business segment of The Walt Disney Company that extends the Disney brand to merchandise ranging from apparel, toys, home decor and books to interactive games, food and beverages, stationery, electronics and animation art.

DCP IT is a technology service provider to Disney Consumer Products, providing a full range of IT services, including:  application development, technology strategic planning, program and project management, business analysis, training, and infrastructure planning and support.

The Sr. Manager, Application Support & Operations will assume responsibility for management of the team that delivers and operates the global Licensing applications, supporting Business-to-Business and Business-to-consumer applications around the world. 

 

The Sr. Manager provides thought leadership and subject matter expertise on the sustainment, enhancement, and optimization of the shared Licensing Applications.  The Sr. Manager is chartered with managing the performance of applications and services to the agreed upon levels.  The ideal candidate will bring both extensive experiences in application support, process optimization and IT best practices, coupled with proven customer service skills and team mentoring.     Experience supporting a licensing, intellectual rights management or financial environment is highly desirable.

 

Specific Responsibilities

Manage a team of highly skilled support professionals who are both application subject matter experts and provide Level 1/2 application support.   Provide leadership, coaching, and counseling in technical and non-technical areas. Develop a high performance staff through education, mentoring and providing continual evaluation of employee development and eliminate single points of knowledge.

  • Responsible for the availability and performance of production systems, striving to meet or exceed established Service Level Agreements (SLA).
  • Develop the application support strategy, overseeing the “promote to production” aspects of the software development lifecycle.  
  • Partner with the application development teams to ensure production issues are addressed in a timely and cost effective manner.
  • Manage the Global 7x24 application support process, working with regional IT teams to deliver against established SLAs.
  • Provide on-call support
  • Drive and deliver operational metrics which represent the key performance indicators for the Licensing applications
  • Collaborate with key business users to ensure that operational efficiencies are driven through continuous improvements in process and technology.
  • Establish and deliver key services, including data management (ETL) and management of the quality assurance (QA) environments
  • Establish best-practices and support techniques; streamline issue resolution
  • Direct the use of key application support tools, partnering with the application architecture team to strategize and implement new resources as required.
  • Direct the performance and deliverables of staff and 3rd party, domestic or off-shore service providers

·         Build strong relationships with the Licensing application development teams & key stakeholders

·         Interface with external partners, including Corporate and other business units to leverage existing solution and drive down cost

  • Develop the strategy and implementation roadmap for a holistic Application Support service.
  • Support the strategic planning process for the Application Support & Operations organization.  Ensure solutions and services are linked to business strategy with measurable benefits. Participate in budgetary planning by assessing department and customer requirements, outlining anticipated expenditures and proposing fund allocation to support the business needs of the company.
  • Evaluate and drive new technologies to further enhance Licensing IT, both strategically and operationally.

Success Criteria



  • Makes sound technical and business decisions autonomously when confronted with competing design or implementation approaches or trade-offs.
  • Translates strategy into specific architectures and actions plans; expected to act independently if appropriate; conceives creative ideas to solve problems and act on them without constant management review and approval.
  • Partners with Licensing IT stakeholders to ensure that business requirements are understood, thoroughly documented and that services levels are achieved.
  • Collaborates with other Disney organizations to leverage existing services, share intellectual capital and enable cross-business unit/business entity application integrations.
  • Drives the adoption of standards, developing both operational “playbooks” and application development requirements for production readiness. 
  • Provides process, efficiency, and operational thought leadership and problem resolution

Job Requirements
  • Advanced to expert knowledge/experience in application support, with demonstrated leadership of a global support team.
  •  Advanced to expert knowledge/experience in process optimization following industry best practices.   ITIL foundation certification at minimum; Intermediate or Expert highly desired. 
  • Demonstrated expertise in root cause problem identification and resolution.
  • Proven mastery of SDLC methodologies, with emphasis in application change management
  • Experience using application management tools such as CA Introscope, HP Business Availability Center, Gomez, IBM Tivoli, etc., and leveraging monitoring methodologies including synthetic, real-time, response time tuning, etc.
  • Strong analytical problem solving skills.   Six sigma experience is a plus
  • Excellent communication skills - both verbal and written
  • Proven team-building & leadership skills
  • First-rate interpersonal and relationship building skills



  • Experience with data management (ETL) is highly desired
  • Experience managing financial systems is highly desired
  • Experience with the Disney Consumer Products Licensing systems is highly desired

 

Education & Experience

  • BA/BS degree in Information Technology or Business related field
  • 7+ years management experience, with at least 5 years experience managing technical personnel
  • 5+ years experience managing large scale, web-based applications
  • Experience managing Informatica is highly desirable

Software Quality Assurance program/practice experience is a plus

Hyland Software is searching for an Operations Infrastructure Engineer.  An Operations Infrastructure Engineer is responsible for the servers, storage, operating systems and monitoring infrastructure that comprise the Saas Healthcare (OBOL for Healthcare) services, as well as installing and supporting customers’ OnBase and legacy eWeb solutions within the SaaS hosted environment. 


About Hyland Software, Inc.
Hyland Software, Inc. provides a dynamic work environment and is consistently recognized as a rapidly-growing employer of choice.  We also recognize that employees are our greatest asset. Thus, we strive for all employees to reach their maximum professional and personal potential by maintaining a healthy work-life balance.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Safeguard the security, integrity and confidentiality of all customer data
  • Maintain the availability of the OnBase, eWebHIM, eWeb Coding, and ChartVault  hosted services
  • Proactively monitor the health of the infrastructure components and services
  • Participate in assigned maintenance windows, including those that fall outside of normal business hours
  • Maintain verified backups of all essential systems and data
  • Rapidly and effectively resolve data loss and site failure events
  • Create and maintain documentation of the configuration details of infrastructure components
  • Complete customer service request/change requests in accordance with the procedures
  • Install applications consistent with established standards, best practices, and policies
  • Coordinate and work with Technical Services, Customer Support, Quality Assurance, and Development departments regarding Hyland Software issues
  • Provide expert support for customer infrastructure issues
  • Regularly report to the Infrastructure and Security Manager on the health and status of the infrastructure and services

 Hyland Responsibilities:

  • Conduct business in accordance with Hyland's core values and display the character traits described in the "Sweet Sixteen"
  • Faithfully and diligently work to satisfy your internal and external customers beyond expectations
  • Enthusiastically perform all business activities that are requested of you by management

Job Requirements

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, or related field and equivalent experience
  • 3-5 years experience with ASP processes and procedures delivering SaaS
  • A strong working knowledge of high-availability enterprise applications and network components
  • Strong troubleshooting and problem solving skills
  • Familiarity with MS Office applications
  • Extensive experience with Microsoft Windows® technologies, including the implementation and administration of Active Directory
  • Unix (Linux) Operating System experience
  • Scripting and/or programming experience in MS PowerShell, bash, Ruby
  • Database query language experience (Oracle and/or MS T-SQL)
  • Experience with the administration of storage devices and related SAN components
  • Experience with monitoring and automation technologies and methodologies
  • Understanding of replication technologies and methods
  • Experience with backup and restore methodologies and best practices

 Desired Qualifications:

  • Database administration experience with Oracle and/or MS SQL Server
  • Extensive experience with server and application virtualization technologies, including VMWare and Citrix products
  • Solid understanding of the audit process and risk-based IT audit activities
  • Familiarity with monitoring and system automation technologies

 Travel Expectations:

  • 5-10%  for hosting center maintenance and/or training

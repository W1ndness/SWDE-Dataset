Job Function: Information Technology

Job Type: Full-Time
Email Address:
 

New York Institute of Technology (NYIT) offers undergraduate, graduate and professional degrees to approximately 15,000 students through seven academic schools, at campuses in New York, at a number of global sites and campuses, and online. NYIT is a leader in developing creative approaches to the technological and global challenges in higher education today.

NYIT seeks a Lab Support Specialist III to work as a member of the Academic Computing team.  Candidate will support faculty and students in the use and application of the technology resources available for teaching, learning and research, as well as execute a variety of technical and end user support duties  of routine difficulty, including documentation of procedures, project implementation while supporting/troubleshooting the software, hardware and web services available at New York Institute of Technology.  Selected candidate will also participate in information technology training, provide timely solutions of all service incidents and projects assigned, and contribute to the proper operation and care of computer hardware, peripheral hardware, software programs and web services, and document problems and resolutions related to hardware, software and related NYIT protocols.  Candidate will also provide status results as necessary and manage campus audio visual needs as well as student workers assigned to facilities with emphasis on providing direction and assistance.  Responsibilities also include maintaining a safe work environment and following college safety policies, interfacing with hardware and software vendors and other related third parties, recommending products and strategies related to user needs for computing and specific problems and contributing to both the maintenance of the information technology and asset database and of the call tracking system including logging all service requests and other projects.    Candidate must be able to work under minimal direction and often independently and provide coverage outside business hours, on a routine basis and for special projects as well as perform other duties as assigned.     


Job Requirements

Bachelors Degree in Computer Science with at least 5 years of related experience is required as well as experience with computer hardware and software installation and configuration in a support related area.  Previous help desk, call center and customer service experience is desired as well as web development skills.  Solid understanding of: MAC OS, XMS Windows XP/Vista/7, productivity MS applications (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Access, and Outlook), general PC hardware, networking (TCP/IP, DNS, WINS, DHP) wireless networking and Antivirus software.  Prior experience with web services/applications is required.  Candidate should also have a fundamental understanding of and experience in networking principles, ability to interact effectively with a wide variety of users and strong organizational skills with emphasis on detail and follow up.  Candidate must also manage projects and priorities in a dynamic fluid and collaborative environment, and have excellent decision making and problem solving skills.  Previous work experience in a higher education environment is strongly desired, and able to work on holidays, weekend and evenings.  Excellent presentation, oral and written communication skills are required.  For immediate consideration please send resume to:  Judith Roback, Human Resources Associate, at  jroback@nyit.edu.

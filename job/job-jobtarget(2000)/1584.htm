ZOLL Medical Corporation is committed to developing technologies that help advance the practice of resuscitation. With products for pacing, defibrillation, circulation, ventilation, and fluid resuscitation, ZOLL provides a comprehensive set of technologies that can help clinicians, EMS professionals, and lay rescuers resuscitate sudden cardiac arrest or trauma victims. ZOLL also designs and markets software that automates the documentation and management of both clinical and non-clinical data.

We are looking for strong candidates who take initiative to continuously improve what they do in an effort to meet our customers' needs. We offer the challenge of working in a fast-paced team environment where your energy and expertise can help you to reach your full potential. At ZOLL you’ll be part of a team that Forbes.com has designated as “one of the most trustworthy companies in America” for the past two consecutive years.

Director of Global Sourcing/Supply Chain

Primary Purpose :
This highly valued Director in our Operations team will be responsible for the leadership of ZOLL Chelmsford, MA Sourcing and Logistics Team, as well as leveraging ZOLL Chelmsford, MA resources and buying power to benefit all of ZOLL’s locations. This position will manage an organization of 10-15 individuals, encompassing the Purchasing and Logistics teams. The Director will also provide significant support in these areas to other ZOLL locations. This position will report to the Vice President of Operations. 


Job Requirements

Competencies :
To qualify for this position the candidate must have a minimum of a Bachelors Degree in addition to:  

  • 10+ years of Supply Chain/Sourcing experience, with at least 5 years in a leadership role
  • International experience in sourcing and distribution to include Low Cost Countries 
  • Demonstrable skills in global Supply base development, global procurement, inventory optimization/planning, and logistics 
  • Strong track record of Strategic Sourcing, Commodity management and developing Supplier Relationships 
  • Driving Low Cost Country supplier development 
  • In depth knowledge of ERP systems- Oracle highly preferred 
  • Driving Business Process Optimization and Standardization 
  • Strong leadership and mentorship skills 
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills 
  • Strong interpersonal skills 
  • Highly skilled in problem solving/systems analysis 

Follow your aspirations to ZOLL Medical Corporation for diverse opportunities, competitive salaries, great benefits and a 401(k) retirement savings plan, all with a company providing the growth and strength to build your future. For immediate consideration, please forward your resume, including cover letter with salary requirements.

NO AGENCY CALLS PLEASE

SUMMARY:

 

Responsible for design, implementation and administration of all Association risk management programs including business continuity and disaster recovery, regulatory compliance, information security, GLBA compliance, risk management and technology policies and procedures.

 

Directly supervises Administrative Services Manager and IT Staff while providing oversight of contracted IT vendors and auditors.

 

SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITY:

 

  • Manages the evaluation, implementation, and support activity for all the Association’s internal and external automated information processing systems and services, including all software, hardware, and related equipment.

 

  • Manages the evaluation, implementation, and support activity for all the Bank’s office automation systems including, but not limited to: local and wide area networks, telecommunications systems, software, hardware, and related equipment.

 

  • Develops, administers and monitors programs that ensure the Association’s compliance with laws, regulations and rules governing operations and product offerings.

 

  • Researches federal and state banking laws and regulations, reviews the Association’s policies and procedures, and ensures financial institution compliance with laws and regulations.

 

  • Develops and prioritizes short and long-term strategic initiatives related to Risk Management and lead the planning process.

 

  • Manages the implementation and maintenance of an effective privacy and data security protection framework that includes an internal control structure, training, ongoing monitoring, and the assessment, resolution tracking, escalation and reporting of issues.

 

  • Serves as the primary contact to represent the technical requirements of the Association and negotiates contracts for all technology, communications and information systems.  May delegate responsibilities as appropriate.

 

  • Work collectively with other members of Senior Management to provide an effective Compliance strategy.

 

  • Act as Management’s liaison to the Audit Committee of the Board.

 

  • Take a leadership role with Examiners and Auditors assuring that all work is effectively delegated for submission and review.

 

  • Evaluates overall operations of computing and information technology functions and recommend enhancements.

 

  • Responsible for the continued development, review, and certification of all business continuity and disaster recovery procedures and plans.

 

  • Promotes professional and ethical standards and establish a risk-aware culture within the Association where all personnel fully understand the importance of their role in the regulatory and compliance process.

 

  • Develops department and individual goals for assigned personnel as directed to assure accomplishment of business and strategic plans.

 

  • Provides effective management and oversight to Administrative Services functions.  In coordination with the Administrative Services Manager, oversees Association facilities through long-range and annual facility planning, facility cost forecasting and budgeting, lease negotiating and management.

 

  • Annually assure proper insurance coverage for the Association and Personnel via an approved broker.

Job Requirements

Normally requires a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Business Administration or a related field or equivalent experience and training in a related field. 

 

10 years in increasing levels of IT management and leadership positions with at least three years in a Director of IT or similar role in the financial services and/or bank regulatory and compliance industries.

 

Strong working knowledge of current financial services regulations (GLBA, FFIEC, etc.) with the ability to advise on diverse compliance related financial services activities and the regulatory rule making process.

 

Possess a solid understanding of the FFIEC management guidelines.

 

Strong research skills, including the ability to interpret laws and regulations impacting the financial services industry and advise on corporate-wide compliance oversight and enforcement program policies for regulatory risk management and business line personnel.

 

Demonstrated success engaging vendors in negotiations and leveraging contractual terms such as service level agreements (SLAs) to improve quality and cost effectiveness of vendor services.

 

Ability and experience to communicate compliance and technology concepts to all levels of the Association, vendors, Board of Directors, regulators, and examiners including the ability to articulate and link technical concepts and solutions to non-technical audiences.

 

Must be bondable.

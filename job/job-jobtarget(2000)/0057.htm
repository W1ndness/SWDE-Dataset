HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Develops the architecture, design strategy, solution, and costing/pricing for small, medium and large, complex pursuits that address network services for Mid-Market Clients.
 
General Responsibilities:
 
  • Embodies "Sales" and the HP sales and sales support processes and tools
  • Translates client business needs into technical requirements and selects the appropriate HP services and methods to effectively sell and deliver them
  • Develops quality, integrated, cost-effective solutions that meet the client's requirements, including focus on:
  • Solution quality & delivery feasibility
  • Risk mitigation
  • Financials (cost/price as required)
  • Alignment of Client requirements to HP Enterprise Services cost/price models
  • Engages other solution design resources as needed to complete required technical deliverables
  • Engages business partners as needed
  • Follows Best Practices for sales support processes:
  • Builds, validates, and approves deliverables including writing technical sales proposals, creating technical drawings, sales presentation content, and contractual Statements of Work (SOW)
  • Schedules and manages pre-sales delivery meetings and reviews as required
  • Ensures that the solution is developed within the parameters set by the price-to-win
  • Ensures that solution components align with standards and best practices
  • Provides client-facing interface as requested by sales pursuit teams
  • Presents the solution and effectively answers questions in customer-facing situations
  • Prepares written responses to client questions as necessary
  • Utilizes appropriate job aids such as knowledge centre, financial modeling tools
  • Assists Marketing and Portfolio Management in developing and enhancing service offerings and collateral material
  • Operates effectively in a fast-paced environment with multiple responsibilities and short deadlines
  • Accepts ambiguity easily and effectively resolves conflicting priorities and requirements
  • Maintains flexibility and tolerance of unforeseen changes and revisions to requirements
 
Specific Role 
 
  • ITO - Specializes in Network Management solutions
 
 
Specific Responsibilities
 
Independently architects and creates solutions for Voice and Data Network management solutions using any hardware platform, operating system, or technology combination including:
 
  • HP/3Com, Fujitsu, F5 and Cisco hardware
  • Proprietary and open systems operating systems for network devices and appliances
  • Associated management and utility software products and tools
 
Collaborates as a member of a solutioning team and is responsible for the proactive and optimal integration of the network solution into the overall team solution
 
Demonstrates high proficiency in network hardware/software selection, architecting, sizing, and design; addresses high availability and redundancy and optimizes environments for sales success
Creates Migration/Implementation project plans and integrates those with the efforts of other Solution Designers and Subject Matter Experts
Maintains general familiarity with other ITO services to facilitate solution integration   Education and Experience Required:
  • Technical University degree or Bachelor's degree preferred
  • 8-12 years experience in technical consultative selling and solution/account management
  • Technical and solution experience in IT industry
  • 5-7 years experience in project/program management
  • Appropriate solution or career certifications (Cisco certification, IT Infascructure Library Certification)
  • 3 - 5 years experience in analyzing, designing, engineering, implementing, and the ongoing operations of network devices and appliances 
  • Must be knowledgeable in current voice and data network technologies and services
  • Candidate must be able to work with multiple hardware/software vendors and technologies to effectively architect, select, and size solution needed
  • Must have excellent communication skills (CxO - level presentations skills).
Knowledge and Skills Required:
 
Technical/Solution acumen
  • Demonstrates expert knowledge of HP's technology & solutions
  • Leverages HP solutions to support customer IT strategic directions, creating extensive customer business value
  • Applies deep understanding of technical innovations & trends to solving customer business problems
  • Establishes thought leadership in solution or technical specialty area with customers
  • Demonstrated ability to work as the lead for large complex projects
  • Has a high level understanding of the HP product/services roadmaps for multiple BU's, and deep knowledge in area of specialization
  • Has demonstrated hands-on level skills with some of the technology
Business acumen
  • Demonstrates skilled use of financial and capital investment concepts in justifying HP solutions that create business value for the customer
  • Utilizes deep knowledge of customer value chain and business requirements to create and propose solutions
  • Persuasively communicates the value of the solution in terms of financial return and impact on customer business goals
Understands business metrics and drivers for multiple levels of customer management and

appropriately tailors communications to demonstrate value

 

Industry acumen
  • Extensive level of industry acumen; keeps current with trends and able to converse with client on issues and challenges at multiple levels of customer management
Solution selling
  • Demonstrates strong communications skills with IT and LOB managers, as well as C-level executives
  • Leverages deep understanding of the competition - both positioning strategy and technology - to create competitive advantage for HP

Purpose of Role:

The web development manager will lead our development team of contractors and vendors to design & deliver a leading edge web environment strategy for Mars Symbioscience businesses, while still being able to operate tactically at times to ensure things are being built from the ground up in line with business requirements. This role will be responsible for managing, supporting and optimizing existing systems as well as building out new systems utilizing leading edge technology and online business practices. The ideal candidate for this role will have a solid technical background, a proven background of implementing new technologies to drive business objectives, as well as a proven ability to manage complex projects from start to finish.

Principal Accountabilities

  • Manage the build out and support of our ecommerce platforms along with ensuring proper site integration with fulfillment centers, call centers, and other systems.
  • Ensure that Mars Symbioscience is a leader in analyzing and optimization of our web sites to ensure a leading edge user experience and conversion rates. We need to know where the user is going, why, and how to convert traffic in to sales in the most efficient way.
  • Implementing leading edge technologies in to our web sites to increase product awareness, create web communities, and generate product sales.
  • Work with SEM & development vendors to ensure that our sites are maximizing our investments in SEM and working with our business partners, as well as sharing best practices & knowledge across business segments.
  • Manage the complete end to end business system, including back office systems, call center solutions and interfacing with our financial System Microsoft Dynamics NAV.
  • Through successful management of third party vendors, build a leading edge ecommerce environment that allows Mars Symbioscience to quickly and efficiently deploy new web sites for new businesses.

Mission Critical Competencies

  • Business Acumen
  • Creativity
  • Dealing with Ambiguity
  • Customer Focus
  • Learning on the fly
  • Problem Solving
  • Process Management
  • Managing and Measuring Work

Key Functional Skills/Knowledge

What does an associate have to be able to do & know to create superior results in this role?

  • Extensive familiarity with software design techniques, testing plans and procedures, and deployment practices which ensure excellent quality, performance, scalability, and availability of the web applications.
  • Ability to relate to all levels of the user community and translate technical language to lay audiences.
  • Strong foundation in Software Engineering, Operating Systems, Databases, Networking, and Web protocols
  • Solid knowledge of current SEO/SEM best practices and strategies
  • Proven ability to self-manage projects through the complete software lifecycle.
  • In-depth experience in development and administration on the Microsoft platform (MOSS 2007, SQL, IIS, .NET).
  • Experience in developing /supporting PHP coded sites.
  • Experience with Flash, AS3, ActionScript, Flex, AJAX is a plus.
  • Proven ability to work with designers to transform design visions in to working sites.
  • Architect and implement metrics systems used to track site traffic, developing web site maps outlining traffic patterns, traffic from search engines, trends in search terms, conversion, and other key traffic metrics.
  • Analyze web statistics, user paths, user feedback/surveys, and usability test results to create and refine user interface requirements that represent strategic site features and improvements.
  • Research and track new technologies in order to provide technical expertise in developing applications.
  • Identify and evaluate commercially available solutions against requirements and present recommendations
  • Analyze and report site performance data to guide development, deployment & optimization decisions.
  • Manage and direct requests for system enhancements and support.
  • Manage vendors responsible for development & Web hosting.
  • Manage all DNS, registrar info, Email, web site administration for Symbioscience sites.
  • Microsoft Dynamics NAV experience is a plus.

Key Experiences

What professional experiences (assignments/ time in roles) must they have had and for what length of time in order to create superior results?

  • 7+ years experience with web business systems development & Support
  • 7+ years leading/managing web development projects including ecommerce.
  • 3-7 years in web development / programming
  • 3-7 years working with offshore/remote development teams
  • Working knowledge of PCI requirements

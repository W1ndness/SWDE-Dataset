Unified Grocers Inc, the largest wholesale grocery cooperative in the Western United States. We offer retailers the resources they need to compete in today’s supermarket industry. We are ranked fourth among Fortune magazine’s “Most Admired in the Wholesale Food and Grocery Industry” in 2009.  We serve more than 3,000 retail stores, have recorded annual sales of approx. $4 billion in 2009, have over 3,400 employees in 11 locations, and we continue to grow! 

 

Corporate Headquarters in Commerce, Ca is seeking a highly strategic and results oriented individual with in-depth knowledge of the grocery retail and meat industry.  Individual will be directly responsible for managing and directing all procurement staff and procurement activity throughout California.  Individual will have opportunity to demonstrate expertise in competitive pricing, while maximizing sales and profits for grocery retail members. 

 

The selected candidate will also be responsible for the following:   

  1. Directly oversee selection, quality, buying, pricing and sources of supply to meet department guidelines.
  2. Direct departmental staffing; selection, training, development and performance to achieve programs for the department.
  3. Develop and maintain a strong purchasing program that will assure reliable sources at competitive prices to keep the membership competitive.
  4. Monitor the buyers' performance for the following: analyze and recommend solutions for critical items and lines; maintain budget levels of profits; selection of new items to be introduced.
  5. Direct the development of departmental efficiency and initiate changes to effect cost savings and maintenance of competitive pricing to the membership.
  6.  Attend and participate in buying committee meetings, company conferences, industry-wide conventions, etc. 
  7. Counsel with department buyers relative to key buy-ins and promotional activity.
  8. Communicate with warehouse managers to balance in-bound and out-bound inventory levels.
  9. Make retail calls to develop better private label support from membership.
  10. Chairs buying committee meetings, assists in the development of departmental efficiency, and recommends changes to effect cost savings and maintenance of competitive pricing to the membership.

Job Requirements
  • Must have a bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or a closely related field. In the absence of formal education, four to six years of directly related work experience may be substituted.
  • Must have five to six years of prior retail/wholesale experience including six or more years in the purchasing, pricing, and inventory control of various beef product line(s).
  • Demonstrated in-depth knowledge of meat products and knowledge of company operations, including available products, pricing and promotion programs, and members/retailers. 
  • Demonstrated proficiency with personal computers and associated business software (MS-Word and Excel preferred).
  • Advanced negotiation skills are essential. 
  • Must have analytical and problem solving skills demonstrated by the review of vendor/supplier proposals, evaluation of available competitive information, and selection of best alternatives.
  • Must have the ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing, with member/retailers and sales staff.  
  • Must be detail oriented with the ability to prepare, review, and evaluate purchase contracts and associated documents  

 

Unified Grocers offers an excellent compensation package, including medical, dental, vision, matching 401K, company-paid retirement plan, tuition reimbursement, vacation/sick pay, and much more!

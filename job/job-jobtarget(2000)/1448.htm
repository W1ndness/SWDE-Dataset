Clean Energy Coalition, a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization based in Ypsilanti, Michigan, seeks an energetic and tech-savvy Business Operations Manager to bring structure and organization to a busy and dynamic, project-based, work environment.  Experience with project accounting and the tracking and monitoring of grants is critical.  Applicants must be proactive and resourceful, possess exceptional Excel and cost/project accounting skills, and pay extreme attention to detail.  This individual will oversee the development and tracking of the organization’s “dashboard” as well as support operations including office/facilities, human resources, financial, purchasing, marketing, and communications systems. The Business Operations Manager works under the guidance of the Executive Director and serves as a member of CEC’s leadership team.


Job Requirements

Minimum Qualifications                                                    

  • Bachelor’s degree in business administration or public administration, accounting, finance, or any related fields, or equivalent work experience in the above-mentioned areas.
  • Five years relevant administration experience, including at least three years working with project accounting, tracking, and measures.
  • Supervisory experience preferred.
  • Demonstrated organizational and leadership skills.
  • A proactive approach to task management and demonstrated ability to anticipate and prevent problems.
  • Self-starter, very detail-oriented, enthusiastic, innovative, capable of meeting challenges and deadlines, and comfortable working in a small, flexible, and collaborative environment.
  • Team player with trouble-shooting and exceptional multi-tasking skills.
  • Superior written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to delegate responsibilities effectively.
  • Commitment to energy/environmental issues and organizational mission.
  • Strong judgment and discretion in dealing with sensitive information.
  • Solid computer skills including proficiency in using basic Microsoft and/or similar software products such as word processing, spreadsheet, presentation, email, web, database applications, and capacity to work in Mac OS environment.

Job Duties & Responsibilities                                                

Financial Management :

  • Prepare monthly financial reports for Executive Director and Board and identify areas of concern or risk.
  • Assist with development and management of annual budget and planning process.
  • Work with organization’s Division Managers to prepare division budgets.
  • Oversee cash flow and accounts receivable and follow up with outstanding accounts.
  • Assist with payroll.
  • Process donations and prepare/make deposits at the bank.
  • Assist with yearly audit and IRS forms.
  • Manage petty cash and staff reimbursements.
  • Manage grantor contracts, invoicing, and reimbursement requests.
  • Assist with monthly, quarterly, and long-range assessments and forecasts of organization’s financial performance against budget.

Office Management :

  • Develop and maintain office systems including database administration.
  • Develop and maintain organizational filing systems.
  • Lead the design, documentation, and implementation of organizational policies and procedures.
  • Oversee IT and phone systems.
  • Oversee purchasing.
  • Oversee technology related equipment acquisition, maintenance, and repair.
  • Maintain and order office supplies and organizational inventory.
  • Oversee office upkeep.
  • Process outgoing mail.

Human Resources :

  • Oversee contract for outsourced Human Resources services.
  • With contractor and management team, develop and maintain personnel policies and employee handbook. Research and coordinate employee benefits.
  • Maintain personnel files.
  • Lead the on-boarding of new employees.
  • Organize staff training and teambuilding activities.
  • Fulfill HR needs for staff and off-site contractors.
  • Assist with recruiting staff, interns, and volunteers as needed.

Executive Assistance :

  • Maintain and communicate “dashboard” metrics.
  • Provide input to the divisional and agency operating plans.
  • Evaluate new opportunities from a financial, risk, and human resources perspective.  Review all grants prior to submission.
  • Provide support and recommendations to Executive Director on strategic issues, contract/grant financial concerns, and adherence to grantor reporting and invoicing requirements.
  • Prepare financial reports and metrics for the Board of Directors.
  • Assist in preparation and planning for Board meetings.
  • Identify need for and coordinate staff communications and meetings as requested.

Donor Development and Community Outreach :

  • Oversee activities of organization’s outreach staff.
  • Prepare thank you letters and tax receipts for donors.
  • Update and maintain donor contact database.
  • Oversee fundraising plan and activities.
  • Oversee marketing function; assure all outreach is in alignment with brand and image.

Risk Management :

  • Serve as liaison to legal counsel in addressing legal issues.
  • Oversee organizational insurance policies.
  • Review all contract requirements and assure effective reporting and compliance.
  • Oversee outside contracts including HR, Marketing, IT, etc.

Organizational Leadership :

  • Contribute to short and long-term organizational planning and strategy as a member of the management team.
  • Participate in and lead (as appropriate) the streamlining and improvement of agency processes and practices.
  • Serve as a role model for teamwork and a culture of collaboration.

Salary & Benefits                                                        

  • Commensurate with experience.
  • Competitive benefits package.

Application Details

  • This position is open until filled.
  • No phone inquiries please.
  • Clean Energy Coalition is an equal opportunity employer committed to diversity and consideration of all applicants for all positions without regard to color, ethnic background, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, national origin, age, and disability status.
  • Qualified individuals should electronically submit a cover letter, resume, and professional references addressed to Sean Reed, Executive Director, and emailed to: employment@cec-mi.org .  Include “Business Operations Manager Application” in the subject line.

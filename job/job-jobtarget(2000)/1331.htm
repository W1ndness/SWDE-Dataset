Description

 
Provide communications expertise and manages or supports various communications program activities with emphasis on external communications.
 
Primary Duties and Responsibilities:
 
- Manages print and electronic communications (can literature, publications, Web site, e-marketing, video) for municipal and industrial clients and prospects
           -         Develops technical and non-technical materials to support general business activities, project development, client relations, community    relations and certain internal communications
           -         Develops corporate customer/prospect communications, internet, media/e-marketing development 
           -         Develops or supports social media components
 
- Business development support
           -         Supports select projects as needed
           -         Develops materials for both municipal and industrial markets.
           -         E-marketing program development support as needed
 
- Community relations
           -         Support community relations programs and initiatives
 
- Supports Industrial Business communications
           -         Customer and prospect communications and e-marketing programs
           -         Update and maintain various print and electronic tools ranging from literature to web site and e-marketing program
 
- Develop or support Web and social media implementation
 
- Editorial and technical writing support
 
- Supports measurement of Balanced Scorecard metrics
 
- Support select internal employee communications
           -         Participates in development of employee communications
           -         Participates in supporting materials and select Water Wiki activity
 
- Events/trade shows
           -         Participates in trade shows as needed

Job Requirements

Qualifications

 
Education:   Bachelors degree.  Some preference given to journalism or communications degrees.
Years of Experience: 7 years of communications experience in a news, agency or corporate communications environment.
Language:  Ability to speak, write and read Spanish is a plus but not required. 
 
Other Requirements:
 
-         Excellent PC skills with thorough knowledge of MS Word, MS PowerPoint, MS Excel.  Additional software knowledge is a plus.
-         Excellent oral and written communication skills.
-         Above average organization and time management skills.
-         Ability to handle diverse task simultaneously and work effectively with interruptions and meet or exceed production and quality goals.
-         Ability to work in a team environment within a large organization.
-         Willingness to readily assume additional and new responsibilities.
-         Ability to handle confidential or sensitive information or issues.
-         Positive and productive attitude.
-         Ability to pass all pre-employment screenings (i.e. - Background check and drug test)

Position Title: Manager - Internal Audit, Inc. HQ
Job Function: Manager, Internal Controls
Posted: October 18, 2010
Job Type: Full-Time
Contact Person: Kristina Larsen
Apply URL: http://baesystems.contacthr.com/17516114

Execute the Annual Audit Program across North America and lead internal audits in an objective review of business risks and opportunities. Recommend improvements to internal controls to enhance/ensure policy compliance, efficiencies and effectiveness, safety, and responsible behavior with audits ranging in scope from financial controls to operational procedures. Provide assurance to management and stakeholders that robust processes and controls are in place and being followed and facilitate change through best practices.

Coordinate logistical aspects on ongoing and upcoming audits. Coordinate the activities of internal and external resources (Auditors, Subject Matter Experts, FLDPs, etc.).

Develop Audit Programs to be executed during the fieldwork. Develop Self Assessment tools to be left with the business units for continuous improvement.

Miscellaneous (tracking audit findings, disseminating good practices amongst IA and business units, training, etc.).


Job Requirements

Required Education:

Required Experience:

Required Skills:Requires advanced analytical and problem solving skills with the ability to apply professional judgment and newly gained knowledge and skills on the job. Demonstrate conceptual and critical thinking in complex and/or completely new situations. Analyze information to identify facts, patterns and trends. Compares data, information and input from a variety of sources to make sound, well-informed and objective conclusions. Senior Project/Program team member responsible for work flow which is varied in nature with situations frequently requiring new solutions.

Responsibility directly impacts the professional/technical direction and strategy of the business. Positive role model and developed leadership skills. Inspires confidence and respect. Team oriented. Is sensitive to and values the diversity, differences, beliefs and viewpoints of others.

Skilled communicator who influences and shapes the image of BAE Systems across North America. Advanced interviewing and presentation skills exhibiting confidence and credibility. Prepares technically sound audit reports that appropriately communicate results of audit work, are informative, well organized, logical and professional in both form and content.

Possess working knowledge of Internal Audit techniques and processes.

Familiar with Audit tools and audit related software; proficient with MS Office applications.

**Must be able to travel up to 50% of the time** Work location is Reston, VA.Preferred Skills:Understanding of corporate GRC environment and fraud awareness a plus.

Master's degree with 10 plus years of related experience (ideally with some being in the government contracting arena).

BAE Systems is the premier global defense and aerospace company, delivering a full range of products and services for air, land, and naval forces, as well as advanced electronics, information technology solutions, and customer support services. With 105,000 employees worldwide, BAE Systems had 2008 sales of $34.4 billion.

Dynamic broadband company headquartered in Kirkland, WA is seeking a Senior Vice President, Engineering to join our senior management team.  Wave Broadband is a growing cable and broadband services company currently serving over 300,000 cable, high speed Internet, and phone service customers in Western Washington, Oregon, and Northern California.  Owned and operated by local industry leaders, customers are supported by decades of cable know-how.  Wave’s mission is to grow the business by making our customers happy and we strive to link customers to the services they enjoy most at competitive prices.  Wave offers a salary commensurate with position and experience as well as a comprehensive benefits package including medical, dental, paid time off and 401K.

 

Position Overview:

The SVP, Engineering is responsible for delivery of reliable network performance to  Wave Broadband customers by ensuring unfailing service delivery of all products, including video, high-speed internet, telephone and commercial business products, as well as support of the company’s headends and HFC systems. 


Job Requirements

Required Responsibilities and Qualifications:

  • 10-15 years minimum experience managing broadband services engineering groups
  • Key systems supported include companywide headends,  Video Processing and Delivery equipment, HFC plant  and design,  VOD platform, customer premise equipment  and other system equipment and services
  • Ensure field compliance with companywide engineering standards for product delivery and quality
  • Provide proactive network monitoring and support services to support the reliability of system performance
  • Support the maintenance and enhancement of networks and hardware and software systems that support the field business applications
  • Effective communications, coaching, training, and development at all levels of organization
  • Proven leadership skills w/ability to influence at all levels
  • Experience with budget and strategic planning
  • Experience with plant, field and technical operations
  • Highly functional within competitive and dynamic industry
  • Proven capability to meet timelines and implement successfully
  • Demonstrated leadership ability with motivational personality to effect team mentality and foster enthusiasm and growth of Wave products
  • Proficient at purveying company culture, mission and goals
  • Ability to work directly with peer group department heads in defining and developing business plan and resource requirements and solutions.
  • Passionate, collaborative, organized, strategic and smart
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment and willing to roll up sleeves to get the job done

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 10-15 plus years Engineering work experience
  • 10 plus years leadership experience
  • Ability to manage cross-functional teams
  • Ability to make decisions and solve problems while working under pressure
  • Ability to prioritize and organize effectively
  • Demonstrated project management experience
  • Ability to meet established timelines
  • Ability to communicate with all levels of management
  • Strong PC and presentation tool skills including MS Excel and PowerPoint
  • Ability to develop, communicate and implement strategies and tactics
  • Ability to manage and deliver against several initiatives simultaneously
  • Ability to communicate effectively orally and electronically

 

Education, Certifications & Licenses

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Information Systems, or other related field or equivalent work experience
  • MS, MBA and/or Certifications in networking, project management, quality improvement or similar disciplines preferred

Diverse Workforce / EEO:

WaveDivision Holdings (WDH) recognizes and strongly supports the benefits of a diverse workforce, and strives to provide a culture that recognizes the unique contributions of each of our employees.  WDH requires a drug test, background check, employment and education verification as conditions of employment.  WDH is an equal opportunity employer and will consider all qualified candidates regardless of race, color, religion, national origin, gender, age, marital status, veteran status, and the presence of a non-job related handicap or disability, or any other legally protected status.

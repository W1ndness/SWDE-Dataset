Senior Interaction Designer

Macadamian Technologies

San Francisco Bay Area, California, United States

This is not an entry-level position.

Job ID: 7373669
Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: Senior Interaction Designer
Required Travel: 10-25%
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Posted: November 12, 2010
Industry: Computer Software
Job Function: Design
Min Experience: 5-7 Years
Job Type: Full-Time

We're looking for a talented Senior Interaction Designer to work with our customers in the San Francisco Bay Area to create delightful software experiences. 

You are equally comfortable designing Web, desktop, and mobile software, and you love collaborating with a team. Your passion is interaction design, and you also have great visual design chops and/or a deep understanding and appreciation of the importance of visual design in creating a compelling user experience. You are a thought-leader in the community, you have senior-level experience, including working directly with clients or stakeholders, and you have a broad understanding of user-centered design knowledge, from user research to visual design.


What does that mean to you?
You will work on creating next-generation products and experiences with a wide range of technology leaders and startups – from consumer electronics to unified communications to Smartphone software. It also means that you will work with small agile teams who care about best practices and have a strong desire to deliver quality products. You will switch gears often to work on new projects and learn new industries. You will be working remotely with a team of talented designers, researchers, and developers from our headquarter office in Canada.

Most importantly, you will work in an environment that values communication, honesty, work-life balance and mutual respect - where your voice will be heard and your opinions matter. 

Your Role: 

  • Work with our customer’s product management teams and business leaders to translate business goals into design solutions, and ultimately into successful products 
  • Work with multidisciplinary design teams of user researchers and visual designers to deliver projects from concept to completion 
  • Collaborate with the usability and development team in concept design and brainstorming 
  • Design GUIs for web, mobile, and desktop products 
  • Occasionally assist with user research and usability testing efforts
  • Work effectively with remote team of designers, researchers, and developers from our headquarter office in Canada

Job Requirements
  • 4+ years of industry experience in interaction design
  • A killer portfolio showing your prowess in conceptual design, wireframes and interaction design, and visual design 
  • Bachelor’s degree in design (industrial design, human-computer interaction, or similar) or equivalent experience
  • Demonstrated thought-leadership in design (blogs, articles, presentations, seminars etc)
  • Mastery of the user-centered design process
  • Clear understanding of Web 2.0, Software as a Service, and mobile computing
  • Highly motivated, adaptable, and eager to learn new technologies and methods
  • Outstanding communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to take initiative


Interested? Please send your resume in Text, HTML, PDF, or Word format, to careers@macadamian.com. Please indicate Reference Code: SID-112010O. We do not accept resumes by fax, mail, or in person.

NOTE: Please submit a portfolio, either as PDF or a URL, with your resume.


About us
Macadamian is a global software consultancy that helps companies create successful products, and design and build delightful software experiences. Companies turn to Macadamian to develop product strategies, design compelling user experiences, and build quality software. We’re a multi-disciplinary group of user researchers, interaction designers, software engineers, and quality experts who love creating great products, and thrive on helping companies transform ideas into market-ready products that their customers will love. We value communication, honesty, fun, work-life balance and mutual respect – we’re an environment where your voice will be heard and your opinions matter.

Why join us?

At Macadamian, you can expect to work with great and talented people in a great work environment. You will be a part of a successful organization that takes great pride in ensuring our values of Responsiveness, Constant Improvement, Collaboration, Agility, and Transparency are translated in all of our activities.  

We care about our employees, and resultantly you will be working in an environment that promotes communication, honesty, career-development, work-life balance and mutual respect. We also offer competitive compensation and comprehensive benefits package.

For more information, please visit www.macadamian.com.

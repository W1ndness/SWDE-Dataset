MapleWorks is a Canadian source for experienced software development outsourcing (SDO) and consulting services located in the Ottawa region. Our clients include companies that develop cutting edge data, voice, and video. We develop telecommunication mobile and embedded products for both the service provider and enterprise markets located in the United States and Canada. We are also developing business applications for enterprises as well as the Canadian Federal Government.

 

At MapleWorks, we hire only permanent staff and we are recognized as employing only the ‘best of the best’. Our team is the most experienced software design workforce in the Ottawa area.

 

MapleWorks is currently looking for an experience GUI Designer


Job Requirements

Key Skills:

 

-         Strong knowledge of web usability principles and experience designing UI elements

-         Experience designing wireframes and HTML; converting mockups to prototypes

-         Excellent visualization skills with the ability to articulate concepts

-         Experience designing form-based end-user Web applications

-         Proficiency with GUI design layout software

-         Proficiency in GWT, Rest and PHP is an asset

 

 

Personal Soft Skills:

 

-        Highly flexible and adaptable personality

-        Strong communication skills, both written and spoken

-        Self-starter who is able to learn new skills rapidly

-        Able to interact with customers and discuss their requirements and needs

-        Able to participate and contribute to a dynamic team


Education and Experience:

 

-         BSc or higher degree in EE/CS/CE or equivalent and 5+ years experience in telecom or IT is preferred.

If you have the required skills please send your resume to jobs@mapleworks.com.

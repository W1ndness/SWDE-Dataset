Fairfax, VA based, international trade association representing the professional audio visual / information communications systems industry has an immediate opening for Exposition Sales Manager.  Duties include: sales management and customer service of new exhibitors.  Manages and sells exhibit space, advertising and sponsorship opportunities within assigned prospect list for an annual TradeShowExecutive Gold 100 exposition. Visit our website for more information: www.infocomm.org.


Job Requirements

This position requires a minimum 3 years of successful exhibit space sales experience (or equivalent)¸ preferably in technology-oriented industries.   Individual must be able to work independently as well as part of a team.  Professional telephone and written communication skills are required.  Intermediate to advanced computer skills are essential including proficiency in MS Word, MS Excel, MS Outlook, A2Z floor plans, association database management software and contact/sales tracking programs. Bachelors Degree preferred or equivalent years of industry-specific work experience. Travel required to attend annual exposition, exhibitor advisory committee meetings, site inspection visits and other industry trade shows (approx. 3 to 4 weeks out of office per year).  Salary and commission plus competitive benefits package commiserate with experience.  Fax resume and cover letter with salary req’s to Christy Jones, Human Resources Generalist, at #(703) 273-8432 or via e-mail to hr@infocomm.org. EOE.

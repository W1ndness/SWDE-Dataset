Product Manager/Director

 

Location:                    Baltimore area, MD

Our Client: 

·         Leading technology firm

 

Essential Functions:

 

The product manager is the business owner for a particular product and/or product family and ensures achievement of strategic revenue objectives for a specific product or product family. Researches market trends/needs and articulates findings in the form of product requirements. Plays proactive role in product planning and design to ensure the product is differentiated from competitors in the marketplace. Leads the product decision process for new product development and product upgrades. Involved throughout the entire lifecycle of the product, working closely with development, sales and marketing; operations and technical support teams from product initiation to delivery and to eventual product retirement. This particular position is focused on Government encryption products.

 

Responsibilities:

 

50% Defines strategic components of product marketing plans, in conjunction with executive team

·                Directs definition of marketing tactical plans for each product area in conjunction with managers and business unit directors, ensuring that tactical plans reflect strategic objectives

·                Ensures that product marketing plans are consistent with company goals and objectives

·                Implements established marketing research strategies and plans through product planning managers

 

20%  Evaluates information on new product ideas for financial and market potential

 

20% Coordinates activities of the department with employee education, sales and customer/product support to resolve issues that will affect product success

 

10%  Manages personnel activities of product management team (i.e. hires, fires, trains, appraises, rewards, motivates, disciplines

 

Education:

BS degree in a technical field (i.e. computer science or engineering or equiv); an MBA is highly desired

 

Qualifications:

10+ years of prior product management experience specifically in managing data encryption/ authentication technologies. 

 

5 years management responsibility

 

Must have a strong background and proven results in strategy and business planning for Enterprise commercial software. 

 

Strong understanding of the security marketplace especially in data encryption and/ or authentication technologies.  Data encryption background preferred.

 

This position requires extensive travel…both domestic and international.

Edwards Lifesciences is the global leader in the science of heart valves and hemodynamic monitoring.  Driven by a passion to help patients, the company partners with clinicians to develop innovative technologies in the areas of structural heart disease and critical care monitoring that save and enhance lives.  Headquartered in Irvine, California, Edwards Lifesciences has extensive operations in North America, Europe, Japan, Latin America and Asia and currently employs about 6,400 individuals worldwide.  

For us, helping patients is not a slogan – it's our life’s work. From developing devices that replace or repair a diseased heart valve to creating new technologies that monitor vital signs in the critical care setting, we help ensure that patients regain and even improve their quality of life.

Director of IT (PDM, PLM, and CAD Systems) 

Opportunity Snapshot:

This position reports directly into the VP of IT, with responsibility for enabling R&D, Regulatory Approval, New Product Introduction, Quality Assurance and Manufacturing Systems.  In this role, you will have accountability for defining, developing and deploying Product Data Management (PDM), Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) and Computer Aided Design (CAD) system solutions for the benefit of ALL Business Units in the company, across all geographies. 

The Director / Sr. Manager of IT (PDM, PLM and CAD Systems) is responsible for all aspects of information technology management and control, including supervision of information technology employees; budget preparation and management; recommendations for technical acquisitions; and development of guidelines, standards and procedures. 

Working in close collaboration with the IT organization, you will focus on the IT needs of Edwards Lifesciences for PDM, PLM and CAD systems; assist with defining, developing and leading the implementation of IT strategic plans, tactical action, and operational decision-making to fulfill the charters of the R&D, Regulatory and Quality functions across all Business Units and geographies. You must possess initiative and drive, have broad relevant business and technical knowledge with good management expertise; and have excellent written and verbal communication skills in a multi-national setting.

 

Details of responsibilities include: 

Leadership

•       Achieve excellence in defining, developing and deploying business-centric IT solutions

•       Champion change and effectively manage the implementation of new ideas, to support Edwards aspirations and R&D, Regulatory and Quality strategies

•       Provide leadership and management to members of the PDM, PLM and CAD IT team

Departmental Management

•       Define, develop, present to management, gain acceptance and approval of a R&D, Regulatory and Quality IT plan that is aligned and coherent with an overall Global IT strategy

•       Lead the implementation of a R&D, Regulatory and Quality IT plan on an annual basis

•       Work with the Head of Infrastructure and Technologies and other Value Stream Heads to ensure that IT solutions being defined are in-line and coherent with IT’s global standards and strategies

•       Hold regular departmental meetings to review strategic goals and initiatives, promote teambuilding and work to provide growth paths for staff

•       Ensure department is well informed, at all times, of changes and news-worthy events within the company

•       Effectively communicate relevant IT-related information to management

•       Handle difficult personnel situations directly, using appropriate discretion, HR advice, and respect for the individual

•       Provide day-to-day supervision, conduct performance appraisals, and delegate work assignments for all IT personnel

•       Assist in recruiting, hiring, and providing appropriate training for IT support staff

•       Administer the department budget

•       Review status reports and address issues as appropriate

•       Enforces standard company policies and procedures

•       Serve on planning and policy-making committees as required

Teamwork

•       Reinforce team approach to problem resolution, especially as it pertains to working with the global IT team

•       Support and solicit input from team members at all levels within the organization

•       Ensure that regular team and practice events take place away from the office

Client Management

•       Communicate effectively with the R&D, Regulatory and Quality management team to identify current and future needs and evaluate alternative business solutions and strategies, with always an eye toward a single, global solution

•       Continually define ways to increase internal client satisfaction and deepen client relationship

•       Maintain lasting internal client relationships

•       Provide senior level resolution to internal client issues

 

Infrastructure and Application solutions (Hardware and Software)

 

•       Work closely with other members of the global IT Leadership Team (ITLT) to establish global infrastructure and application solution standards

•       Work closely with the global ITLT to identify emerging information technologies to be assimilated, integrated, and introduced within the R&D, Regulatory and Quality fabric

•       Oversee the design, development and deployment of new application solutions and changes to existing application solutions that are (1) global in nature; and (2) localized just enough to meet the statutory and regulatory requirements in all OUS (Outside US) countries, or encompass local specificities and local business dimensions (i.e.: launch of product in Europe which would require unique support)

•       Serve as primary contact with outside vendors in the generation of RFPs, bids, contracts, agreements, and other major vendor interactions in support of the R&D, Regulatory and Quality domain


Job Requirements

The successful candidate must possess a user-centered attitude and a desire to work in a matrixed, team oriented environment that serves a diverse population. 

Minimum Education, Skills and Abilities

•       Master's degree in computer science or related field

•       A business degree (MBA) will be a strong PLUS

•       Proven skills working in a highly matrixed environment

•       Prior experience working in a multi-national business environment

•       Strong ability to manage and deal with different cultures and nationalities

•       Strong analytical and logical problem solving skills

•       Strong interpersonal communication and relational skills

•       Proven organizational and project management skills, working in the Headquarters of a multi-national corporation, preferably US-based, with responsibility for deploying localized applications on a global basis

•       Possess and display sound judgment; initiative; flexibility and detail-orientation

 

 

Preferred Experiences

•       Six to ten years management / leadership experience, with at least 3 years in a Senior IT leadership position

•       Experience with top tier CAE systems such as AutoCAD, Pro/E, Solidworks, etc.

•       Experience with top tier PLM systems such as TeamCenter, Agile, Windchill, etc.

•       Deep expertise in streamlining Engineering Change Management and New Product Development and Introduction processes preferred

•       Experience designing business processes and architecting integration of enterprise systems such as CAD to PLM, PLM to QMS, PLM to ERP, will be a huge PLUS

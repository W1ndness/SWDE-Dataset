eSight, an Ottawa based enterprise, is successfully combining breakthrough advances in digital video capture and display with proprietary image processing algorithms to create a brand new way to help people challenged with low vision.  The eSight team consists of experienced industry leaders who are on a mission to build the technology that will make a difference in peoples lives.  eSight is seeking a Configuration and Test Developer eager to share the same mission.

The Role:

The Configuration and Test Developer will review system requirements, define and manage a system test plan, develop, execute and document test cases and results.

Working closely with a team of highly skilled professionals in a collaborative work environment you will work with hardware and systems designers, other software developers, test engineers and external partners.

  • Review product requirements for system testing
  • Develop and manage a system test plan
  • Develop and execute test cases and release Acceptance Test Procedures including regression testing
  • Automate system tests where possible
  • Document tests and results
  • Manage test configurations locally and at partner sites
  • Work with software, system and hardware designers to identify product issues
  • Generate Problem Reports and manage their resolution
  • Make a difference in someone’s life

Job Requirements

Your Expertise:

  • A Bachelors degree in Computer Science or equivalent
  • A minimum of 1 year of experience in test environments preferably executing system tests for embedded systems
  • Experience with C and C++
  • Srong problem solving and root-cause analysis skills
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills

It would be great if you had:

  • Cross platform development toolkits (e.g. QT)
  • Experience with RTOS based high performance systems
  • Image processing (capture, modify and display) experience
  • CMMI-DEV knowledge
  • Experience with hardware components such as image sensors, displays, I2C devices, FPGAs, DSPs, and TI OMAP technology

To apply, please forward your resume to careers@esightcorp.com referencing the job title.

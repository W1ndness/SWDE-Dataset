The Corporate Records Manager is a key role within the Zachry Corporation, working under the direction of the Vice President of Compliance, and is responsible for developing and managing of all corporate records programs, processes including capture, storage, retention and destruction across all media.   The Corporate Records Manager is the sponsor of the Records Management Policy, and procedures, monitoring employee compliance and ensures that all information practices meet federal, state regulations and company policies.  This position requires a bachelor's degree or equivalent and at least 7 years of experience.


Job Requirements

 

  • Experience with records management and Document Control in corporate environment
  • Experience with management and compliance of electronic and paper records
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong ability to perceive and analyze problems, plan and develop alternatives and implement solutions
  • Experience with network scanning operations
  • Strong organization and leadership skills
  • Experience with SharePoint software or other document management systems
  • Experience with updating and researching retention requirements and applying organization-wide to all media, document types and records.
  • Exposure to taxonomy and file plans.
  • Intermediate skills with Microsoft Office applications
  • Formal project management experience
  • Lifting and/or moving of up to 50lbs

This job description is intended to describe the general nature and level of work performed by people assigned to this job description. The job description is not intended to be an exhaustive list of responsibilities, duties and skills required for this position.

 

Education and Experience:

  • Bachelors Degree is preferred 
  • Department level Management experience in a similar business environment (7+ years preferred)
  • Certification in Records Management (CRM) is desired

Zachry Construction Corporation performs heavy civil work throughout Texas and other states. Relocation is sometimes required for continuous employment opportunities.

 

Interested candidates should apply on-line: http://www.zachryconstructioncorp.com/career/

ASSISTANT DIRECTOR, RESEARCH DECISION SUPPORT,
DIVISION OF RESEARCH

The Office of Sponsored Research at Florida International University has an exciting opportunity for a dynamic, knowledgeable person who has strong analytical skills and wants to become a part of a team that strives to achieve and maintain a dynamic working partnership with current and future employees. 

The Assistant Director, Research Decision Support is responsible for conferring with staff of organizational units to analyze current operational procedures, identify problems, and understand specific requirements such as forms of data input, how data will be summarized and used, formats for reports, etc.

Duties include:

-Analyzes information problems, requested changes, and required modifications to develop appropriate business process solutions.

-Designs a detailed description of user needs. Works with technical resources to develop, test, and document software in order to implement and maintain an operational system.

-Assists in developing system projects, including development of the project plan, monitoring and reporting on project progress, and advising managers and stakeholders of potential project delays or resources required to complete the project on a timely basis.

Minimum qualifications:  Master’s degree in an appropriate area of specialization and two years of experience; or a Bachelor’s degree and four years of experience.

Desired qualifications:  Highly desired candidates will have a background that combines experience in accounting and business operations with extensive system skills necessary to support and optimize related research administration business processes. System skills include business process analysis, system requirements gathering and documentation, testing, production support and troubleshooting, system analysis and detailed data analysis as well as project management.

Salary:  $55,000 to $80,000 (Commensurate with experience)

For further information or to apply online visit our website at http://www.fiujobs.org and reference position #62744.

FIU is an EE/EO/EA Employer & Institution.

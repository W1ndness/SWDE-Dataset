AFG is seeking highly motivated and experienced Director of Facility Management (DFM) to lead major projects in the United States and the Middle East for Commercial, Mixed Development, Educational, Hospitality and Justice Projects. The ideal candidate will be a hands-on professional. This position involves the management of a staff of more than 150 employees. Position provides overall direction and leadership to multi-facilities company, and is responsible for planning and overall budget. Direct project management and on-going responsibilities to include: strategic planning, preventative maintenance, repair, and maintenance and capital expenditures.


Job Requirements

Duties & Responsibilities Provides direction & management assures on schedule completion within or below budget in accordance with contractual obligations. The DFM is responsible to

 ·         Directs and manages the functions and provides leadership to the over 150 staff in the facilities division including skilled trades, maintenance personnel, cleaning staff, technical, administrative support positions and planning staff.

·         Formulates strategic and tactical planning for all sites and creates performance measures consistent with mission and goals.

·         Serves as Chief representative for developing and assisting with the implementation of the company  master plan

·         Assists the acquisition of real estate to meet the demands for space and functions

·         Develops and implements protocols for managing facilities including routine maintenance, and  special projects,

·         Manages the budget efficiently to assure that procurements follow appropriate rules and regulations.

·          Collaborates with other senior executives to develop and advocate for capital budgets; upgrades and improves facilities to meet Master Plan and Performance Measures Process goals.

·          Plans and coordinates capital projects. Future capital projects may include new facilities, major facility upgrades and buildings system improvements.

·         Assures that contracts are procured and managed in an efficient manner.

·          Monitor vendor performance

·         Develops productivity measures and operational improvements to get the best operational and financial value

·          Assists in facilitating positive communications and relationships with the Company and neighborhood community.

·         Represents the Company with external parties in collaborative and negotiated relationships pertaining to planning and facilities management.

·         Implementation of CAFM system.

·         Supervise and monitor all issues related to staff performance and operating training.

·         Identifying the risk contingency factors.

·         Set up and develop recruitment plans, and skill mix,.

·         Provide direction as needed to prioritize, track and evaluate projects and requests.

·         Maintain an effective system to monitor work orders, status updates, completion of facility maintenance requests and minor construction projects.

·          Determine the frequency and content of status reports from the project teams, analyze results, troubleshoot problem areas, and provide summary status

·         Identify, evaluate and implement cost-effective operations standards and streamlined processes. Conduct post project evaluations to identify successful and unsuccessful project elements, and direct improvements in best practices.

Qualification

·         Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, Architecture , Facilities Planning or equivalent

·         Formal FM qualification

·         Excellent working knowledge of industry practices/safety/quality management systems.

·         Thorough knowledge of current technology; must be able to select and apply appropriate software for use on project.

·         High level of critical thinking skills.

·         Clear Verbal communication skills.

·         Minimum of 15 years’ experience involving large-scale residential developments, commercial office buildings, resort and business hotels, entertainment and retail complexes, marine facilities, highways, ports, aviation and/or infrastructure improvements among similar facilities. 

·         Ability and willingness to live and travel throughout the Middle East

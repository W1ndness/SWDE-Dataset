Tourism Development & Investment Company (TDIC), established in 2006, is a master developer of major tourism destinations in Abu Dhabi, the largest of the seven emirates which make up the United Arab Emirates, and a key contributor to its economic and cultural progress.

TDIC are looking to hire an experienced Property Accounts Manager.

Property Accounts Manager

  • Assess the requirements of the PM department and formulate PM accounts strategies that will assist to achieve it's goals
  • Lead the scoping, tendering, and selection of a property management software system suitable for mixed use schemes, and retail, commercial and residential properties
  • Implement the property management software in the company, incorporating the required work streams
  • Communicate closely with stakeholders to ensure successful implementation
  • Lead the integration of PM software with CRM, corporate finance, and FM software
  • Ensure the accurate and timely set up, entry and maintenance of all property, ownership, tenants, unit, rental etc information
  • Ensure all lease information is up to date and accurate eg trigger date, expiry, rent review dates etc
  • Input into the required policies and procedures that will support the department
  • Manage the AR and AP functions for property management
  • Ensure that rent is demanded and collected in a timely manner including credit control
  • Author the staffing strategy for the team
  • Recruit, train and retain team
  • Coordinate the preparation of the annual property budgets including rental and other income, insurance, service charges and other costs
  • Assist with the preparation of departmental and team budgets
  • Together with the Property Manager oversee the Service Charge Management of each asset
  • Review and sign off on all service charge reconciliations
  • Oversee the audit process for both the internal and external audits
  • Create a suite of reports for easy use by property managers and corporate finance
  • Deal with additional ad hoc requests for reports
  • Undertake regular property analysis including scrutiny of income and expenditure, property cost comparisons, debtors listings etc
  • Extensive experience in property management software applications
  • Expert in other applications such as excel and reporting tools
  • Experience in project managing the implementation of PM accounts software
  • Experience in training others to use PM software
  • Strong experience in budgeting and financial management

Job Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree
  • 10 - 15 years in property management accounting including 5 years in a management role.
  • Extensive experience in property management software applications
  • Expert in other applications such as excel and reporting tools
  • Experience in project managing the implementation of PM accounts software
  • Experience in training others to use PM software
  • Strong experience in budgeting and financial management

Associate/Director of Development
IFAS


The Office of Development and Alumni Affairs and the University Florida Foundation, Inc.  (ODAA) coordinates the fundraising program for the University of Florida. Over $200 million are received through contributions to the university each year.  The University of Florida campus is located in beautiful north central Florida in Gainesville, a vibrant community with numerous cultural, athletic, and recreational activities, enhanced by the University of Florida’s prominent intercollegiate athletics. ODAA currently has a staff of approximately 255, including over 70 dynamic development professionals.  The University of Florida has started its third comprehensive capital campaign, to successfully conclude in 2012, reaching a goal of over $1.5 billion. 

The University of Florida’s Institute of Food and Agricultural Sciences (UF/IFAS) is a federal-state-county partnership dedicated to developing knowledge in agriculture, human and natural resources, and the life sciences, and enhancing and sustaining the quality of human life by making that information accessible. While extending into every community of the state, UF/IFAS has developed an international reputation for its accomplishments in teaching, research and extension.  Because of this mission and the diversity of Florida’s climate and agricultural commodities, IFAS has facilities located throughout Florida.  IFAS provides research and development for Florida’s agricultural and natural resources industries that had a $76.5 billion annual impact in 2008.

We are currently searching for two dynamic fundraising professionals for the following positions:
Associate/Director of Development, IFAS, Requisition # 0806486
The incumbent is responsible for assisting the Senior Director of IFAS Development in the fundraising program with focus on Florida 4-H and IFAS Extension programs.

Associate/Director of Development, IFAS, Requisition # 0806487
The incumbent is responsible for assisting the Senior Director of IFAS Development in the fundraising program with focus on the College of Agricultural and Life Sciences.

The incumbents in both positions will serve as major gift fundraisers for IFAS and coordinate efforts with ODAA.  As such, he/she is responsible for the identification, qualification, cultivation, solicitation and the stewardship of the College’s major gift prospects.  The incumbent focuses on individual, corporate, and foundation prospects at the $100,000 level and above.  The working title will be determined based on experience of finalists.     

Minimum requirements: Masters degree in an appropriate area of specialization or a bachelor’s degree in an appropriate area of specialization and 2 years of related experience with proven success in major gift closure. 
The ideal candidate will possess:
•    Director level - three to five years of experience in major gift fundraising in higher education is highly preferred
•    Associate level - two to three years of experience in major gift fundraising in higher education is highly preferred
•    Excellent oral and written communication skills strongly desired
•    The incumbent should be computer proficient in these areas:  Windows, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Access, WWW browsers, preferably Internet Explorer, E-mail
•    Experience with donor database, contact management and tracking system software is highly preferred
•    The willingness and ability to travel are essential 

The positions are also titled Development/Alumni Affairs, Coordinator 3 and will close on 11/30/2010.  Please apply online at http://jobs.ufl.edu to the requisition number corresponding to the position for which you wish to be considered and submit a cover letter, resume and list of three references.   For further details about this and other development opportunities at the University of Florida, please contact Veronica Alexander or Sonya Allen at (352)-392-5408. 

The University of Florida is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer committed to diversity.  Women, minorities and individuals with disabilities are encouraged to apply.  If an accommodation due to a disability is needed to apply for this position, please call (352) 392-4621, or TDD (352) 392-7734.  For more information about employment opportunities with the UF Foundation, please visit our website at www.uff.ufl.edu

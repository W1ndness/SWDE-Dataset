To apply for this position please submit cover letter, resume, salary history and requirements, and professional references.

 

Membership

• Develop strategies for increasing and marketing member benefits*

• Create and direct first-year membership program that increases engagement and retention*

• Manage the membership database function to ensure the database is accurate and up to date at all times

• Respond promptly to membership requests for information, and important outside inquiries*

• Manage the membership dues invoicing, accounts receivable and processing functions

• Process membership applications and activate new and renewing members

• Prepare for and attend Membership Committee meetings, as well as recruitment and retention events.

• Serve as liaison to local law schools

• Assist in the development of applications, brochures and marketing materials*

• Create and maintain service records for association volunteers

 

Publications

• Manage all aspects of advertising for association publication, including target marketing, tracking of solicitation responses, processing contracts, content and payment, etc.

• Develop strategies for using publication for marketing Association membership, sponsorships, events, programs, and benefits

• Proof and edit publications and various materials/correspondence as requested*

 Website

• Maintain and update Association website, including program and event notices, registration and calendar*

• Maintain and update publication and membership information on Association website

• Assist in website update planning and implementation*
• Perform other duties as assigned*

 Programs/Meetings/Events

• Assist with planning and providing logistical support for association meetings, programs and events*

• Prepare for and attend Association programs and events as requested*

 Administrative

• Provide administrative support for the Executive Director and other staff

• Help assure that all matters requiring attention are promptly addressed as appropriate*

• File/scan documents as required

• Maintain adequate office supply inventory

• Help facilitate healthy communication within the office and with volunteer leadership*

• Support the overall goals and objectives of the team through additional responsibilities

 

* Indicates shared task


Job Requirements

To apply for this position please submit cover letter, resume, salary history and requirements, and professional references.

Education and/or Experience: Bachelor’s degree and/or five or more years of experience in an administrative position. Must be proficient in the use of all aspects of MS office software (Access, Excel, Word, Publisher, PowerPoint, and Outlook), and the ability to quickly learn new programs as needed.

Qualified candidates must be proficient in database management, mail merges, and full use of Excel capabilities, with a minimum typing speed of 40 wpm.

Membership recruitment and retention and/or marketing experience preferred.

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

• Ability to take direction, use discretion and exercise sound judgment

• Strong work and team ethic

• Articulate and personable, with outstanding customer service skills

• Superior and professional oral and written communication skills, with excellent grammar

• Ability to organize work, prioritize tasks, and meet deadlines

• Ability to work independently, handle multiple tasks simultaneously, solve problems as they arise, adapt and respond quickly to change

• Good analytical and conceptual thinking skills

Candidate must have valid California driver’s license, reliable personal transportation, ability to lift 40 lbs., and the ability to work evenings and weekends as needed.

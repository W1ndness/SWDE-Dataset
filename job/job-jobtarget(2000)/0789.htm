HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers.

Hewlett Packard's Enterprise Services US Public Sector Consulting specializes in system integration, IT strategy and infrastructure disciplines for US Federal as well as State and Local accounts. 

The Solution Capture & Pursuit Team works with large federal government accounts.  Our team is responsible for identifying and closing sales opportunities for enterprise servers, storage, software, and services.  The sales environment is fast-paced and competitive. Team members have defined specialties, but must also be capable of recognizing opportunities outside their area of expertise and engage the necessary resources  to win each deal. 

The primary responsibilities of the Solution Architect include:

  • Provide technical direction onsite for customers
  • Conducting technology workshops
  • Strategy for Proof of Concepts
  • Working directly with sales teams on opportunities
  • Coordinating with other HP resources required to meet project objectives

Opportunity analysis

  • Gathers and assesses customer needs, both business and technical
  • Works with customer IT and Line of Business management to develop an accurate understanding of business needs and related issues
  • Identification of related needs (lead generation, opportunity expansion)
  • Identification of customer wide technology parameters
  • Competitive analysis

Solution Planning and Design

  • Architect technical solution to meet the customer's requirements
  • Optimize a solution's fit to the requirements of an opportunity plus the broader customer IT strategy
  • Balance and incorporate the input of specialists in the solution design
  • Adapts solution design to new requirements
  • Anticipate and plans for competitive threats
  • Establish validity of technicalsolution and its components with both short and long term implications
  • Identify the growth path , scalability options and implications for customer IT strategy and/or related Line of Business implications of a solution and includes these in design activities and account planning

Client/customer relationship

  • Develop and manage strategic professional relationship with key executives
  • Understanding and addressing key executive issues
  • Consultative selling
  • Build customer loyalty through being a trusted advisor
  • Partner effectively with others in the account to ensure problem resolution and customer satisfaction
  • Communicate roles in a proposed customer solution
  • Account team collaborate
  • Participate with the account team in account and opportunity planning across the Region
  • Provide solution advice, drives proposals, presentations, and other customer communications during pursuit
  • Transfers knowledge to account team
  • Proactive engagement of other teams and resources within HP and partners
  • Application of technical skills to identify opportunities within assigned accounts
  • Facilitation of smooth transition from sales to implementation by orienting the appropriate teams to the solution design

Scope and Impact

Contributions have major impact across an HP Region and some BU's, and solid impact on Regional HP business Leads the presales direction for assigned account(s) or solution area and contributes to the presales direction for the Region Breadth of influence goes across multiple organizations within the Business and is sought, valued and used

Qualifications:

Security Clearance: Desirable Location:  Washington DC Area (Maryland, Virginia or DC) Education and Experience Required: Technical University degree or Bachelor's degree preferred 8-12 years experience in technical consultative selling and solution/account management Technical and solution experience in IT industry Experience in supporting clients in the US government

 

Desirable:

5-7 years experience in project/program management Technology certifications such as CISSP, ITSM, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security

Knowledge and Skills Required:

 

Applies broad understanding of technical innovations & trends to solving customer business problems Establishes thought leadership in solution or technical specialty area with customers Demonstrated ability to work as the lead for components of large complex projects Has a high level understanding of the HP product/services roadmaps for multiple Business Units

 

Business Acumen

Leverages financial and accounting concepts as well as capital investment concepts in demonstrating business value of proposed solutions to customers Creates solutions that creatively address customer value chain and business requirements Persuasively communicates the value of the solution in terms of financial return and impact on customer business goals Appropriately tailors communications to varying levels of customer management

Industry acumen

Extensive level of industry acumen; keeps current with trends and able to converse with client on issues and challenges in the Federal Government Solution selling Demonstrates strong communications skills with IT and LOB managers, as well as some C-level executives Leverages strong understanding of the competition - both positioning strategy and technology - to create competitive advantage for HP

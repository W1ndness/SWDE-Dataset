Inmar, Inc., located in Winston-Salem, North Carolina is currently looking to hire a Supply Chain Analyst to join the Reverse Logistics Team.  Inmar's technology-driven solutions deliver critical operations for leading retailers, wholesalers and manufacturers to manage reverse logistics, facilitate promotions and recover revenue. We serve over 1,200+ retailers, wholesalers, manufacturers and 32,000 retail pharmacies by processing billions of returned/recalled products, promotional transactions and pharmacy claims each year.

Our suite of services enables our clients to manage the physical, financial and information flows within the supply chain. From returns and receivables management to promotion creation, tracking and analysis, we position companies to manage risk, recover revenue and move product through the supply chain more effectively during even the most challenging economic environments.

Candidates should send cover letter outlining interest and salary requirement as well as résumé to recruiting@inmar.com  

 POSITION SUMMARY

Incumbent will report to the Senior Director of Supply Chain Consulting in the Supply Chain Analytics Department of Inmar Reverse Logistics and will be responsible for overall logistics operations analysis and evaluation of transportation information to develop supply chain models and efficient transportation networks for clients and the company. This person will work with transportation and operations personnel internally to continuously improve logistic operations.

 MAJOR JOB RESPONSIBILITES

  • Responsible for preparing data and models for proposal development and participating in proposal presentation.
  • Lead the analysis and provide statistical and cost information to business development team related to transportation and warehouse selection.
  • Consults with internal departments and clients to develop most efficient and cost effective logistics networks utilizing warehouse capacity data and transportation/network modeling software.
  • Gathers and interprets relevant data (costs, productivity, demand patterns, etc.)
  • Investigates problems, finds root causes, and develops solutions
  • Develops periodic performance reports and distributes them to stakeholders
  • Provides analytical support for projects, new business opportunities, and proposals
  • Provide analysis of current transportation and operations practices to determine process and cost improvement opportunities.
  • Other special projects as assigned.

Job Requirements

EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE / QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor's degree in related field required.
  • Approximately 5 years of related experience in logistics, transportation, traffic and sales/business interface.
  • Prior experience in domestic freight is essential along with understanding warehousing/supply chain.
  • Experience with network modeling and transportation management software strongly preferred.
  • Candidate must be a well organized person capable of handling a variety of issues and assignments.
  • Position requires strong analytical, quantitative, interpersonal and project management skills.
  • Must be able to work under pressure and meet deadlines. Some travel required.

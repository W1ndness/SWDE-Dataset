Position Title: NYC BSC LM Fall Internship
Min Education: Undergraduate
Posted: September 9, 2010
Job Function: Unpaid Internship/Volunteer
Job Type: Internship

About Seedco:

Seedco is a $60+ million national nonprofit organization that works with local partners to create economic opportunities for disadvantaged job seekers, workers and neighborhood entrepreneurs. Seedco use practical, effective strategies to address the challenges confronting families and individuals who are struggling to leave poverty and achieve economic progress. Working on these issues for more than two decades, Seedco is a leader in implementing innovative, multi-partner initiatives that foster financial advancement and security.

 

Position Overview:

NYC Business Solutions is a suite of services offered by the New York City Department of Small Business Services to help businesses start, operate, and expand.  Our services are provided at no cost regardless of the size or stage of the business.  This internship is located at NYC Business Solutions Lower Manhattan Center.  The intern will assist with the general center operation to learn about small business development, gain knowledge of basic business consultation practices, and become more experienced in general office administration and operations.  Other learning objectives may be developed to fulfill individual candidate’s professional development interests.  In the past, learning objectives have included developing knowledge of business plan writing and gaining experience in business plan review, sharpening presentation and public speaking skills, and gaining familiarity with the financing assistance.

 

Responsibilities:

The Program Assistant will work closely with staff both in the New York office as well as staff in field offices across the country to develop and implement program initiatives and strategies.  Responsibilities include: assisting in the design and development of training materials and reports; conducting research; coordinating project activities; providing program support, creating and managing databases and spreadsheets; and planning meetings, conferences, and trainings.

 

Qualifications:

  • Assist with center operations including answering the phone and taking notes;
  • Provide administrative tasks to strengthen center’s programming;
  • Conduct data entry by using customer relationship management software;
  • Provide additional assistance in course coordination and general follow up;
  • Research financing related events and other networking opportunities;
  • Junior or Senior level college student studying business or economics;
  • Interest in business development or small business management;
  • Excellent communication and research;
  • Organized and detail-oriented;
  • Ability to multi-task and work under pressure in both team settings and individually;
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to multi-task a complex workload
  • Strong computer skills, including MS Office and database management; and
  • Bi-lingual English/Chinese is preferred.

 

Compensation:
The hours can be adjusted based on school schedule.

15-20 hours/week for 12 – 15 weeks.  The internship is unpaid. Intern may also obtain school credit.

To Apply:
Forward resume, cover letter and salary requirements in MS Word format to: internships@seedco.org.  You must put “NYC BSC LM Fall Internship” in the subject line of the e-mail in order for it to be processed.  Please, no phone calls about this job opportunity.  E-mailing your resume is preferable to fax.

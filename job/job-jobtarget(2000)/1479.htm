Strategic Customer Consultant

C.H. Robinson Worldwide Inc

Eden Prairie, Minnesota, United States

Job Duration: Indefinite
Position Title: Strategic Customer Consultant
Required Travel: 50-75%
Min Education: BA/BS/Undergraduate
Posted: November 30, 2010
Industry: 3PL, Consulting, Customer Service, Engineering, Freight Forwarder, Logistics, Transportation, Other, Supply Chain Management
Job Function: Consulting, Demand Planning/Operations, Inventory Planning/Control, Logistics Planning/Management, Supply Chain Management, Transportation Management, Warehouse Operations/Management
Min Experience: 5-7 Years
Job Type: Full-Time
Apply URL: http://www.chrobinson.com/careers
 

SCOPE

To be the primary strategic resource to a specific key account, lead collaborative projects and provide ongoing strategic and tactical consulting services to meet customers supply chain goals.  Identify areas of opportunity and develop creative solutions that drive strategic supply chain decisions.   Utilize industry knowledge and best in class technology to create and implement solutions that drive efficiency and savings into the customer’s overall supply chain.

 

PRIMARY ACCOUNTABILITIES

  • Represent C.H. Robinson’s capabilities to the customer
  • Contribute ideas and knowledge that help develop and drive the customers supply chain strategy
  • Engineer complex solutions and implementation plans for realizing identified opportunities
  • Map current and future “ideal state” processes, identify gaps and define plan to close gaps
  • Effectively represent processes, methodologies, ROI and project results
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to create solutions
  • Analyze data to understand and communicate the “store in the data”
  • Identify drivers causing barriers that prevent the customer from meeting strategic objectives
  • Develop win-win strategic and tactical solutions that support joint strategic objectives
  • Communicate strategy and influence goals across all levels of the organization
  • Develop project plans with metrics, timelines and tollgates and provide regular status reports

OTHER

  • Effective self management and ability to schedule multiple active and pending projects
  • Able to multi-task and keep stake holders informed
  • Develop business plans (forecast costs, travel, team resources needs, likely projects, etc…)
  • Accountable for plan and positive performance results.

Travel:  Up to 50% (more initally)


Job Requirements

QUALIFCATION AND SKILL REQUIREMENTS

  • 4 year engineering degree with an emphasis in Supply Chain or simliar field.
  • Advanced degree preferred  
  • 7 to 10 years supply Chain experience preferred

              - Demand and Inventory Planning

              - Forecasting and Replenishment

              - Supply chain analysis and design

  • APICS certification preferred
  • Experience using a variety of Supply Chain design software and tools
  • Engineering/Process design skills
  • High degree of Analytical skills
  • Advanced Process Mapping skills
  • Skilled in Access, Excel, Word, Visio and PowerPoint
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to think strategically
  • Ability to demonstrate ROI on projects

Our achievements are measured by the people who need us.
At UCLA Health System, we define greatness by the quality of the patient experience we are able to deliver. Each and every time, to every single patient. If that’s where your ambitions lie, UCLA is where you belong. We currently have the following opportunity available:

EHR Application Manager

In this position, you will help us build on our success as one of the nation’s top health systems by overseeing the daily activities of an Electronic Health Record (EHR) application build team. Responsibilities include defining build objectives; managing the configuration, build and installation of applications; coordinating software update reviews as well as the implementation of desired changes; ensuring each new release is thoroughly tested; and providing end-user support.

THE BENEFITS OF BELONGING

As a valued full-time member of our staff, you’ll enjoy outstanding benefits which include health, dental and vision plans that begin on your first day and a retirement plan that is one of the best in the nation. You will also receive 13 paid holidays and 15 vacation days beginning your first year. Relocation assistance is also available for those who qualify. 

For more information, please contact Robin Clayton at RClayton@mednet.ucla.edu or apply online at: http://hr.healthcare.ucla.edu and reference the Job Code H54866. EOE


Job Requirements

To qualify, you must have a bachelor’s degree (or equivalent work experience), 7-10 years of experience in a healthcare-related organization, and demonstrated management experience. Prior experience with IT systems and systems development projects is also required. Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite is crucial, as are strong leadership and project management skills. The selected candidate will be required to pass the Epic certification exam in 2 attempts within 3 months of employment, therefore you must be willing to travel to Wisconsin 3 times to attend Epic certification training sessions.

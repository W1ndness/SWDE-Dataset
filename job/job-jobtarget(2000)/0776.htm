General Summary:

Performs a variety of administrative duties for the Boys & Girls Clubs of Hartford (BGCH) which require a range of skills and knowledge of organizational policies & procedures.

 

Essential Job Responsibilities:

Ø Plan, write, and prepare press releases, newsletters, scripts, articles, or any communication regarding BGCH

Ø  Write, edit, proofread and distribute all content for electronic and print communication

Ø Staff liaison for Marketing & Communication Committee

Ø Assist in the design and preparation of all BGCH promotional and marketing literature and products

Ø Coordinate production of marketing materials and manage fulfillment activities

Ø Maintain BGCH’s Website

Ø Provide administrative support for the CEO/President.

Ø Responsible for all phone lines coming into BGCH

Ø Receive and direct visitors

Ø Schedule and coordinate meetings, appointments, and/or other similar activities for administration, which may include coordinating travel and lodging arrangements

Ø Responsible for all Board Meeting correspondence

Ø Implement and maintain complete filing system

Ø Manage the front office to ensure all work areas are clean and neat

Ø Prepare daily checks deposits

Ø Sort, screen, review, and distribute incoming and outgoing mail and faxes

Ø Maintain supplies, copier/printer support, daily mail, and other services

Ø Perform miscellaneous job-related duties as assigned


Job Requirements

Ø Bachelor degree preferred; at least 5-7 years of experience that is directly related to the duties and responsibilities specified.

Ø Previous development of communications materials is preferred

Ø Highly organized, comfortable with change and shifting priorities, resourceful

Ø Eye for consistency and quality; proofreading; critical thinker

Ø Solution oriented: able to identify and resolve complex problems and overcome obstacles

Ø Effective communicator

Ø Requires advanced computer skills using MS Office software (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)

Ø Knowledge of supplies, equipment, and/or services ordering and inventory control.

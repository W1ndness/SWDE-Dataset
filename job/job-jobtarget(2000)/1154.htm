Position: Product Manager

 

Company Description:

ILSCO Corporation is an industry leader in the design, development and manufacture of electrical connectors.  ILSCO has been named to the “Greater Cincinnati 100” list more than 20 times and offers a competitive benefits package, including 401(k), profit sharing and major medical insurance.

This position reports to our Director of Channel Marketing, and is responsible for the management, development, and implementation of product marketing on the mechanical offering for ILSCO.

 

Position Description:

·         Manage mechanical product line including maintenance of product pricing, resolving quality, production and packaging issues, providing the marketing input in the ECN process and maintenance of product database.

·         Develop sales tools for the field including literature, product presentations, training programs, merchandising assistance and product promotions.

·         Generate appropriate reports to manage business, such as monthly business summary, and annual sales and expense budgets.

·         Oversee product development effort: product additions, designs, and rationalization.

·         Provide marketing input to manufacturing forecasting models.

·         Provide customer application assistance.

·         Provide support for industry trade shows.

 


Job Requirements

Required Qualifications:

·         BA/BS degree in Business Administration, Marketing or Engineering

·         Minimum 7 - 10 years experience in sales and/or marketing field

·         Knowledge of electrical terminations or similar product offerings

·         Familiarity with UL/CSA/IEC/NEC/ANSI/IEEE codes and standards

·         Proficiency with MS Office, AS400 systems, email and CRM or similar package

·         Knowledge of the electrical distribution channel

·         Technical product knowledge for phone or e-mail questions

 

Desired Qualifications:

·         Working knowledge of Microsoft Access

·         Experience in web site maintenance/construction

·         Working knowledge of EDI and E-Commerce

·         Familiarity with PIM software

·         Knowledge of the export market

·         Experience with global sourcing

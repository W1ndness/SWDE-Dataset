Assistant or Associate Director of Prospect Tracking


The Office of Development and Alumni Affairs and UF Foundation, Inc. (ODAA/Foundation) coordinates the fundraising program for the University of Florida. Approximately $200 million is received through contributions to the university each year.  The University of Florida campus is located in beautiful north central Florida in Gainesville.  Livability.com ranked Gainesville as the No. 1 college town in American in 2010.  ODAA/Foundation currently has a staff of approximately 255, including over 70 dynamic development professionals.  The University of Florida is in the midst of its third comprehensive capital campaign, to conclude in 2012, with a goal of $1.5 billion.  We are currently recruiting for an Assistant or Associate Director of Prospect Tracking. 

This position is responsible for the prospect tracking program within the Department of Research to support the strategies and initiatives of the University’s fundraising objectives and to maximize positive solicitation outcomes.  He/she will serve as the application specialist for the Prospect Tracking System within the Advance database.   This incumbent will provide support services including all training programs, user documentation, defining user needs and planning usage guidelines and policies.

Minimum requirements:  Master’s degree in an appropriate area of specialization; or bachelor’s degree in an appropriate area of specialization and two years of appropriate experience.  The successful candidate will possess:   
•    Three years experience in development and prospect management preferred
•    Experience in large complex organizations, preferably in higher education
•    Project management skills and supervisory experience strongly preferred
•    Strong analytical and decision-making skills are required
•    Excellent reading, writing and oral communication skills are essential
•    Computer proficiency in SQL (structured query language) for managing data in relational database management systems, GIS tool like ArcView, Statistical Applications, MS Excel graphs and charts, MS PowerPoint, MS Access, MS Word, WWW browsers is strongly preferred.  
•    Experience with donor database, contact management and tracking system software is highly preferred

To view complete job description, application instructions and complete an on-line resume, please visit www.hr.ufl.edu/job.  Reference number for the vacancy is: 0806257.  Salary ranges from low to mid $40s.  Both title and salary will commensurate with experience and education.  Deadline to apply is November 15, 2010.  For further details about this and other development opportunities at the University of Florida, please contact Veronica Alexander or Sonya Allen at (352) 392-5408. 

The University of Florida is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer committed to diversity.  Women, minorities and individuals with disabilities are encouraged to apply.  If an accommodation due to a disability is needed to apply for this position, please call (352) 392-4621, or the Florida Relay System at 1-800-955-8771 (TTD).  For more information about employment opportunities with ODAA/Foundation, please visit our website at www.uff.ufl.edu

Winix Inc (USA)

Product Manager (Korean Speaking)

Winix means "Pure Comfort" to the consumer of appliances for the home environment. 

 Our products are not commodities - we win because we innovate and because we design better products and offer better quality than our competitors. We "skip generations" with technology in order to lead and create the trends, and we build relationships based on our commitment to service and responsiveness to our customers. We sell to the largest retailers as well as producing private label products for the leading consumer brands.

 As a Product Manager (Korean Speaking) you will be participating in the creation of innovative products of unparalleled quality and performance. You will collaborate with your US team as well as the team of engineers and designers at our headquarters in Korea. You will work closely with large national retailers and well-known global brands to achieve their goals for cost and performance.  

  • If you are detail oriented, precise, logical and organized...
  • Enthusiastically support your team and your company and bring a positive attitude to your job...
  • Enjoy the challenges of a rapidly growing business...
  • Possess the ability to communicate clearly and calmly in both English and Korean...
  • Have exceptional problem solving skills and enjoy creating efficient systems...
  • Possess technical aptitude.
  • Are an individual of both integrity and compassion... Then we want to hear from you.

Job Requirements
  • 2+ years experience in project management or product development.
  • 4 year degree.
  • Great verbal and written communication skills in both English and Korean.
  • Energy, Enthusiasm and an Unparalleled work ethic.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Word, Excel, PowerPoint and project management software.
  • Experience in home appliances, and/or other durable consumer products  preferred.

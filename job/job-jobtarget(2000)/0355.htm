The Coding Source was founded in 2002 in response to the critical shortage and urgent need for qualified, certified medical coders. Based in Los Angeles, TCS serves clients nationwide to optimize revenue while ensuring compliance. The Coding Source, LLC is ranked #96 on the 2008 Inc. 500 list of Fastest -Growing Private Companies in the U.S and #2 on the Los Angeles Business Journal's 2007 list of Fastest-Growing Private Companies in Los Angeles County.

 

We have an immediate need for one of our clients.  I am currently conducting a search for a coding specialist to work remotely, fulltime, coding both inpatient and outpatient hospital charts.


Position Description

 

Position Title

Remote Coding Consultant

 

Reports To:

 Manager, Coding Services

 

DepartmentLocation:

Reimbursement & Coding Services - Facility

 

Positions to Manage:

None

 

Position Summary: In two or three brief sentences, summarize the purpose of the position.

The remote coding consultant provides coding services to clients as assigned by the Manager, Coding Services in a home office setting.  All coding is performed in conjunction with the TCS Compliance Plan, with focus on accuracy and completeness with an understanding that payment optimization is an outcome of correct code assignment rather than a separate process.

 

Primary and Secondary Responsibilities:
  • Provide coding services including ICD-9-CM coding, CPT-4 coding, and abstracting in a home office setting.
  • Complete online timekeeping for payroll in accordance with TCS policy no later than 10 a.m. on Monday each week.
  • Complete expense reports in accordance with TCS policy.
  • Attend continuing education classes to maintain proficiency in areas of coding that include but are not limited to ICD-9-CM, CPT-4, ambulatory surgery, ambulatory care, and physician office coding.
  • Participate in TCS coding quality reviews as deemed appropriate by the Manager, Coding Services.

·         Comply with TCS policies regarding the use and disclosure of protected health information which includes accessing and using protected health information only to the extent necessary to fulfill the above mentioned responsibilities. 

 

Behavioral Characteristics Required:

Flexible, detailed-oriented, ability to work independently, willingness to take ownership of responsibilities, quality conscious, dependable and ability to adapt well to change.

 

 

Knowledge and Skills Required:

Knowledge and 3+ years experience in inpatient and outpatient coding including but not limited to ICD-9-CM and CPT-4 coding skills. Medical terminology understanding of medical records content, format and other pertinent procedures.  Knowledge and understanding to work with Microsoft Windows and Microsoft Office products.

 

 

 

Education Required:

 

Associates degree with equivalent business experience.  Credential of RHIA, RHIT, CCS, CPC, or CPC-H.

 

Decision Making Authority:

In conjunction with Manager, Coding Services coding decisions may be made.

Working Relationships: List the titles of individuals, their departments, and organizations which an individual in this position has frequent contact.

Director, Facility Reimbursement Services

Manager, Coding Services

Director, Sales & Account Management Team

Director, Information Systems

 

 

Criteria used to assess the quality of work performance:
  • Quality review of coding projects
  • Productivity measured by  Operations Manager
  • Reporting functionality of the ValiCode Remote Coding software

 

Expected standard of performance:

Ability to meet productivity and quality standards on a regular basis.  Positive communication with UASI staff.

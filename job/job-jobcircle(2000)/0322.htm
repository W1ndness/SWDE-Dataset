  Corning Incorporated

                                                 

 
 

SQL Server Sr. Analyst

Posted:  December 2, 2010

Org Marketing Statement Corning is the world leader in specialty glass and ceramics.  We create and make keystone components that enable high-technology systems for consumer electronics, mobile emissions control, telecommunications and life sciences.  Corning succeeds through sustained investment in R&D, over 150 years of materials science and process engineering knowledge, and a distinctive collaborative culture.

Our products include glass substrates for LCD flat panel televisions, computer monitors and laptops; ceramic substrates and filters for mobile emission control systems; optical fiber, cable, hardware & equipment for telephone and internet communication networks; optical biosensors for drug discovery;  and other advanced optics and specialty glass solutions for a number of industries including semiconductor, aerospace, defense, astronomy and metrology.

To know more about Corning, please visit us at www.corning.com Department Marketing Statement The global Information Technology (IT) Function is leading efforts to align IT and Business Strategy, leverage IT investments, and optimize end to end business processes and associated information integration technologies.  Through these efforts, IT helps to improve the competitive position of Cornings businesses through IT enabled processes.  IT also delivers Information Technology applications, infrastructure, and project services in a cost efficient manner to Corning worldwide. Responsibilities Key Responsibilities: *Interact with customer to gather and define requirements from both a business and systems perspective by interpreting business needs and translating them to technical requirements and specifications to meet the need. *Review and respond to documented issues related to a project (through a help ticket or other written documentation) and define requirements to address the issue. *Train and develop junior team members in technical areas through one-on-one development *Maintain status of SME on specific area of knowledge by researching and keeping current with new and anticipated technology within their area and related areas of expertise. *Implement components (e.g. program, specifications, order, build, etc.) for complex IT solutions for new business demands and maintenance of business. *Trouble-shoot more complex applications, infrastructure problems or other IT problem that may have a significant impact on the business. *Estimate time and materials costs plus all other elements of Project Initiation process for IT or business projects. *Govern or act as a gate keeper for the quality of the Corning systems. *Serve as the technical lead on projects by participating as project resource representing their teams area of responsibility/area of expertise. *Provide input to developing and testing standards by making recommendations to the Infrastructure Review Board. *Manage and extend existing Corning SQL  Server standards and processes *Design and architect robust, scalable, fault tolerant SQL Server database environments for high availability *Develop and maintain roadmap and strategy for SQL Server database services working with appropriate IT groups *Prove concepts and introduce technologies related to SQL Server database environment and related technologies via Position Papers and Proof-of-Concepts *Manage capacity plans for SQL Server database environments.

Performance Metrics: *Overall project success and specifically the success of their area of responsibility on the project in terms of meeting project technical specification requirements. *Extent to which demonstrates technical expertise and innovative approaches to improve a process or technology by finding ways to do even better using current, up-to-date, technology. *Continuously looking at how to do things better or differently for current work or for application in other situations such as Windows applications, back up processes and responses, and application of software such as Citrix, application security, etc. *Feedback from customer satisfaction data (qualitative). *Case closure rate for resolving specific problems or issues. *Project timeline execution for specific part of project responsible for. *Extent to which meets Individual Performance measures (objectives for specific work assigned to the individual). *Extent to which makes decisions independent of supervisor as appropriate for area in which individual is a SME.

Job title: SQL Server Sr. Analyst Job number: 164781 Qualifications Required *BS in Computer Science / Systems Management / IT Related field *Relevant work experience in a corporate technical customer service environment *Experience in an enterprise setting - large sized and broad scope organization similar to Corning *Formally trained and/or certification in Microsoft SQL Server *8-10 years relevant work experience in IT ? in a large enterprise environment *5+ years Experience in Microsoft SQL Server (2000/2005) *1-2 years Experience in Microsoft SQL Server 2008 *Experience in managing highly available SQL Server Database and SSIS/SSAS/SSRS environments

Desired *Worked for some period of time and demonstrated consistency in work; counted on to work with minimal supervision with quality and not rework and can manage parts of projects and are technologically competent *Experience in a role where there was a balance of emphasis on understanding and supporting the business with technology *Extensive experience in: *Leading design and architecture efforts for SQL Server environments *Leading performance tuning efforts of complex systems.

Experience with: *Related Microsoft Server technologies like BizTalk Server, Commerce Server and IIS Knowledge of Industry best practices in Business Intelligence platforms Oracle 9i/10g/11g MS Office Suite, Windows Desktop / Server proficiency

*Global experience preferred but not essential

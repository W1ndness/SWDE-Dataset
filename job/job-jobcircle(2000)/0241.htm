J2EE Application Architect, Full Time

Posted:  December 3, 2010

FULL TIME, Lead Application Architect

Great, FULL TIME position in Piscataway, NJ for an Application Architect. Although the position is an "Architect," the right candidate will be 'hands-on' to be able to deliver and act upon a proof of concept and guide the current team of 13 developers.

This is a J2EE architecture role; WebLogic is a plus; Oracle is required (SQL queries, Stored Procedures, Triggers); SOA (Service Oriented Architecture) and Cloud Computing knowledge.

The primary responsibility of the Lead Application Architect is to provide the overall guidance and structure for creating and maintaining the whole software effort of Publishing Technologies.

The Application Architect will direct the design framework of the department's software needs, define and enforce the software development methodology, mange the software code safe, define and supervise the development-quality-assurance-production environments, and provide planning and support for all application development efforts.

The main responsibilities of the Lead Application Architect are to:
-- define the application architecture,
-- with the Manager, Software Development, coach developers in the design of the application solution, ensure component re-use
-- design and maintain Visual Source Safe
-- provide continuity in all major application solution decisions
-- take ownership of the design and development of the overall system architecture
-- future direction of our application server -- move off of BEA in favor of OpenSource products such as Tomcat or JBoss
-- Redesign Xplore architecture fromo J2EE javabeans to portlet technology
-- integration of current search engine into newly developed products that are outside of Xlpore
-- integration of RightsManagement into new products outside of Xplore
-- movement towards VMWare and the implications on our application design
-- challenges and opportunites presented by cloud computing
-- challenges presented by mobile applications especially as they pertain to Access and Authentication

TECHNOLOGY SKILL SETS:
Application server (WebLogic, Tomcat); Exposure to varied Web Development Frameworks (MVC, Grails, PHP). SQL and entity relationship moodeling (Oracle preferred); Rose or similar UML tool; Portal Technologies (WebLogic preferred); Access & Authentication (RightCare experience preferred); Information retrieval (Endeca and Lucene experience); Content Management Systems, VMWare

EDUCATION: BS Degree or Higher

REQUIRED: 5+ years SPECIFIC EXPERIENCE as an APPLICATION ARCHITECT.

If interested, please email your resume to lespenschied@aetea.com for immediate consideration.

PLEASE NOTE: this client does not sponsor - candidates must be US Citizen or Greencard holders.

PLEASE NOTE:  salary is $118k - $120k firm plus a great benefits package

Please refer to job code LNE-FTArchitect when responding to this ad.

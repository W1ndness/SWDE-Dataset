Commercial Client Admin

Posted:  November 27, 2010

Description Summary Assists Commercial Regional Managers with overall management of portfolio relating to customer service, sales and asset quality and assists loan officers in maintaining credit quality standards of portfolio by performing the following duties.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

Reviews loan documentation prepared by another area and schedules loan closings with customers

Assist customers in day to day transactions such as transfers from one account to another and advances on lines of credit

Pulls monthly reports to assist officer in maintaining account, future maturities and past dues

Assists officer in getting updated financial information and forwarding information to the credit file

Engages attorneys to do the loan closing; works closely with attorneys to make sure loan documentation on the front and back end are correct

Orders proper appraisals and phase I environmental if needed

Pulls monthly exception report to keep exceptions below standards and sends information to proper area to be cleared

Prepares loan commitments and loan agreement

Attends loan closings if needed

Cross -sells additional services to existing customers such as insurance and cash management products

Provides proactive customer service relating to problem resolution and specific customer requests

Prepares appropriate portion of loan documents with appropriate party and schedules closings for customer

Monitors loan portfolio for renewal dates and coordinates renewal documents (internal and external)

Develops a call schedule to proactively develop relationships with contact personnel of Tier I customers

Closes and completes loan packages

Assists in tracking and collecting current financial statements for portfolio

Checks for existing exceptions that need to be cleared and works with appropriate party to clear exceptions when needed

Prepares loan commitments and loan agreements

Runs required reports such as past due, maturity projection and exceptions.

Assists loan officer in obtaining necessary documents to complete loan requirements.

Conducts joint calls with the loan officer and customer

Assists loan officers in identifying and selling commercial cash management products, trust/private banking investment services, and insurance services

Prepares monthly inspection bills to send to builders and for keeping track, on a monthly basis, those who have and have not paid

Responsible for quoting payoffs to the title companies when requested

May prepare loan documents ready to deliver to the attorneys office for closing

Answers questions regarding closing requirements

Secondary Duties

Assists officer with other duties as needed

Assist the customer

Answer customer calls which may require research

Receives calls and assists customers as needed

Answers routine questions and handles problems for customers

Actively participates in identifying opportunities for cross-selling existing relationships

Assists loan officers in other duties as needed

Cross-sells depository accounts, cash management services and other bank services available for commercial customers

At certain locations, CCAs may be expected to assist Private Bankers in providing administrative support and full-service banking to existing high net-worth clients

Supervisory Responsibilities This job has no supervisory responsibilities. Qualifications To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.

Education and/or Experience High school diploma and 5 years banking experience with 2 years commercial related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience

Language Skills Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions and procedure manuals; Ability to write routine reports and correspondence; Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organization

Mathematical Skills Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals; Ability to compute rate, ratio, and percent and to draw and interpret bar graphs

Reasoning Ability Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form; Ability to deal with problems involving several concrete variables in standardized situations

Computer Skills Ability to learn or knowledge of Microsoft Office products including Microsoft Word, Excel, Access and Powerpoint; Ability to learn or knowledge of Outlook email program

Other Skills and Abilities Ability to learn or knowledge of Microsoft Office products including Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Access; Ability to learn or knowledge of Outlook email program

Knowledge of or ability to learn internal bank reporting systems

Knowledge of or ability to learn Commercial Account Analysis, Commercial Loan Systems, and Commercial Loan Policy

Working knowledge of applicable software

Knowledge of internal bank reporting systems

Knowledge of Commercial Account Analysis, Commercial Loan Systems, and Commercial Loan Policy

A working knowledge of Commercial Loan Documentation

Certificates, Licenses, Registrations No specific certifications, licensure or registrations are needed to satisfactorily perform this job.

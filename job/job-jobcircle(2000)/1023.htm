Senior Network Engineer

Posted:  December 1, 2010

Senior Network Engineer

Location US- CA, Santa Barbara

Req #  9726 Functional Area Technical Operations

Preferred Experience Senior Level

Preferred Education Bachelors Degree

Position Summary Objective of Position The Citrix Online network consists of a large number of routers and switches spread across the globe in various datacenters and offices. These devices support tens of thousands of ports and a large amount of Internet traffic every day. Additionally, the network also supports the internal workforce and their communication needs. The purpose of the Senior Network Engineer is to design, extend, operate and maintain all of these components while ensuring an uptime of 99.99%. This position will be tasked with larger projects such as the expansions and capacity upgrades, as well as be involved deeply with the day-to-day operations of the network. A senior network engineer is expected to collaborate with the Network Architect to go over product modifications. A person in this position will be required to make tactical and strategic decisions on their own, may specialize in Routing and Switching, Voice technologies, or WAN infrastructure and may be responsible for specific subsystems from one of those classes.

Principal Responsibilities ? Translates group strategy into day-to-day tactical decisions ? Documents and applies guidance on industry standards to improve current environment and practices ? Maintains high network availability build for redundancy and resilience; periodically tests the resilience by active testing ? Calculates capacity requirements; understands and accounts for peak load; manages peak load events ? Defines Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for operational efficiency ? Continuously investigates and recommends alternatives to bring down operational costs ? Works with the Network Architect to define requirements around network performance such as QoS and traffic shaping ? Evaluates and oversees implementation of a carrier grade monitoring system ? Performs security audits of the subsystems to continuously improve the security posture; defines day-to-day procedures to achieve this end ? Ensures compliance with various audit requirements such as SOX and PCI ? Maintains operational relationships with service providers, and equipment vendors ? Is the highest level of support for escalated end-user issues ? Configures routers, switches, load balancers and other network devices in accordance with best practices; codifies these best practices as templates and publishes to the group ? Plans for disaster recovery both on a global and on a site-by-site basis ? Creates reports in system-wide performance metrics ? Ensures configuration management and reviews it is in place and being rigorously followed

Other Duties and Responsibilities ? Is available for on-call rotation 2 weeks a month ? Attends conferences and expos in the field ? Inculcates and imparts BCPs as part of every project ? Guides and instructs junior members of the NSG

Supervisory Responsibilities ? None at this time

Qualifications and Requirements Knowledge and Skills ? Bachelors degree in CS or ECE or equivalent experience; prefer candidates with Masters in the same field ? At least 5 years experience with Enterprise or service provider Networks ? Experience building and maintaining a 100person or 1000+ server network ? 3+ years of on-call technical operations experience a plus ? Must be fluent in multi-area OSPF and BGP, MLS and stateful firewalls ? CCNP or equivalent certification required ? Fluency with Cisco IOS (JunOS a plus) ? Experience with the Cisco 65xx, nexus, 72xxx platforms ? Experience with billing and CDR management systems ? Experience with Cisco Call Manager, UCCx, Avaya CM/IC, Nortel PBXes a plus ? Fluency with Basic Networking: Ethernet, TCP/IP, HTTP, SMTP, NTP, SNMP ? Advanced knowledge of VoIP: SIP, SCCP and H.323 ? Understanding of voice encoding methods and codecs (G.711, G.729, G.722, iLBC, iSAC) ? Basic programming experience a plus (Perl, SQL, JAVA) ? Ability to be very customer-oriented and willing to engage ? Able to solve complex problems by successive decomposition ? Experience juggling multiple responsibilities and prioritization of conflicting requirements

Competencies ? Teamwork: Collaborates with others toward shared goals, exhibits cooperativeness; participates in and supports effective conflict resolution; collaborates cross-functionally; leads teams effectively; puts needs of organization over personal needs ? Communication: Listens, asks for and uses input; shares information appropriately and openly; is accessible and receptive to positive feedback, constructive criticism and both good and bad news ? Excellence Orientation: Strives to be the best that one can be; exhibits resilience, persistence, tenacity and a continuous mindset for improvement; is goal-oriented with very high standards for quality, accuracy and timeliness of work; prioritizes multiple tasks; is strongly results-oriented; helps others keep things on track ? Accountability: Holds self and others accountable for keeping commitments and delivering the best possible performance, pays attention to detail; assumes responsibility and follows up personally ? Authenticity: Shows a genuine, accessible and caring personality that builds trust, mutual support and credibility; listens, exhibits humility and gauges approachability of others; maintains composure and is able to be respectful and straightforward with others; develops trusting relationships; is highly ethical with a commitment to deeply held values; takes full responsibility for self; respects the confidentiality of the information they acquire, maintain, and use in their job, and personally disengages from it ? Innovation: Participates in and supports an environment that is conducive to innovation and new ways of doing things; exhibits curiosity and open-mindedness; is willing to take risks ? Relays the Vision: Conveys a compelling vision of where the organization is going to others and models behavior consistent with the vision to show others how they can best help the organization achieve its goals ? Inspires Others: Motivates others by demonstrating pride, enthusiasm and passion; celebrates success and gives credit to others; exhibits optimism and vigor ? Builds Talent: Aacts as a mentor and empowers others ? Leads Change: Considers change impact prior to implementation; initiates action; removes barriers

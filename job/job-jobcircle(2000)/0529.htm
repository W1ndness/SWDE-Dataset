FEP Technical Staff Member II

Posted:  December 3, 2010

Responsibilities PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES:  reporting to an IT Director the incumbents duties would include, but are not limited to, the following: % Duties and Responsibilities 1. 20% Lead matrix team of subject matter experts who have responsibility for strategy and development, including systems and application development, vendor management, and legacy systems integration.  Leads collaboration with business customers in examining solution options and in planning and managing multiple IT centric projects. 2. 30% Initiate and manage a leading role with IT management and IT colleagues to translate corporate/functional business and information objectives into an IT strategic/tactical business plan and systems development.  Consults within the IT organization to develop appropriate support for IT centric projects from various technology and service departments; integrate activities with business units, corporate departments, and IT departments to ensure the successful implementation and support of project efforts. 3. 20% Provide overall financial recommendations, and develop controls and measurements to monitor progress.  Develop, analyze and report IT resource requirements to support objectives (e.g., staffing, costs) needed to meet objectives via resource modeling efforts.  Leads vendor management efforts pertaining to sourcing. 4. 15% Initiate and provide input to the development of corporate IT standards and policies to ensure compatibility and integration throughout the company.  Negotiates IT centric contracts.  Approve/Manage subcontracting and vendor evaluation studies pertaining to the IT organization.  Stay current in new IT technologies. 5. 15% Leads communication between all key IT groups and the customer community via participation in meetings and the creation of status reporting mechanisms (weekly, monthly, and quarterly).  Supports large-scale initiatives through resource modeling and providing complex analysis and reporting to Senior IT management. SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITY:  May lead a matrix team of 10-25associates and contractors Qualifications Required: Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Business, or Engineering or equivalent work experience required.  Five-8 years of IT and business experience in strategic planning, development, implementation, and maintenance of systems, preferably across multiple hardware and software platforms.  Previous technical management experience in a dynamic high-technology environment is a key to success in this position.  Out of the 5-8years required, at least 5 years in a combination of the following: - Progressive broad-based information systems experience (which could include consulting) with state-of-the-art hardware and software systems is desirable; knowledge and implementation experience in new computing architectures and networked computing structures. - Experience in project management and execution of multiple development projects, particularly as a business lead on systems development, with working knowledge of applications development lifecycle - Demonstrated leadership experience, during which two or more of the following occurred: o Successfully led, developed and implemented new technologies and work processes o Demonstrated high competency in project management and the execution of multiple or large projects o Demonstrated competency in strategic thinking and leadership with strong abilities in relationship management o Demonstrated leadership of multidiscipline, high-performance work teams/groups o Demonstrated competency in developing efficient and effective solutions to diverse and complex business problems o Successfully led, developed and implemented applications using new and emerging technologies o Established a successful track record of managing joint IT/business teams through life-cycle phases o Must have played a leading role in a minimum of 5 successfully completed, some simultaneous, corporate level projects in excess of $10M each

Abilities/Skills:  Ability to lead a matrixed, dynamic, energized, and highly creative multidiscipline high-performance work teams in learning and applying new skills/techniques to respond to business needs.  Ability to effectively adapt to rapidly changing technology and apply it to business needs, developing and delivering IT centric projects using (where appropriate) leading-edge technology.  Subject Matter Expert and understanding of the companys business practices and direction, business principles, and business processes, plus familiarity with the companys products and resources.

Demonstrated success as an internal consultant in both interpersonal and managerial skills.  Solid teamwork and an ability to communicate and persuade customers, employees, and management at all levels and thrive in a cross-functional environment.  Demonstrated excellent oral and written communications; an ability to present and discuss technical information in a way that establishes rapport, persuades others, and gains understanding.  Demonstrated leadership, business planning, and development skills.

Exceptional project management skills, effectively arranging resources and managing multiple mid-sized to  large projects in a cross-functional environment; ability to analyze project needs and determine resources needed to meet objectives.  Ability to create original concepts and take heuristic approaches to testing/refining existing theories and to develop new system approaches to solve problems and seize opportunities to sustain business success.  Exceptional analytical, problem-solving, and conceptual skills.

Preferred:  Masters degree in IT or Business preferred.  Prior Health insurance industry exposure.  Demonstrated experience in successful large-scale project implementations leading matrix team of 10 - 20 people.  Specific experience in initiating one or more IT enterprise/tactical strategy and development, experience in Vendor management, Client management, and Technology Management.

Note:  The incumbent is required to disclose immediately any debarment, exclusion, or other event that makes him/her ineligible to perform work directly or indirectly on Federal health care programs.

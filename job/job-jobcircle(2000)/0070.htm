Application Developer

Posted:  December 3, 2010

PURPOSE Designs, develops, implements and maintains software applications to meet business requirements. Leads a project-focused software development team responsible for providing quality solutions, support, and technical assistance to the business. Performs full range of systems programming responsibilities following best practice programming standards while utilizing "state of the art" concepts, techniques and languages, and analytical capabilities to solve business problems and carry out the definition, analysis and design phases of systems projects. ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS 1. Configures, customizes, maintains, and enhances purchased software applications as required by writing efficiently operating program code from high-level functional specifications. 2. Analyzes and designs new systems based on business requirements and technical specifications that have been reviewed and approved by the client and technical team. 3. Evaluates, configures and builds front-end and server-side applications and database integration solutions. 4. Installs, tests and assists with the implementation of vendor supplied applications, upgrades, and associates operation systems upgrades. 5. Manages vendor technical issue resolution using vendor and IT supplied tools. 6. Creates test plans, tests, and debugs developed programs for new and existing programs. 7. Transitions tested programs to quality assurance or business analyst for systems and siness acceptance testing. 8. Works with quality assurance and business analysts to resolve programming issues throughout project testing phases. 9. Prepares documentation of developed programs in accordance with software development methodology utilizing standard templates. 10. Prepares estimates of work effort and reports status, activities and issues in accordance with IT procedures. 11. Provides technical assistance on software and acts as backup systems administrator when necessary. 12. Monitors all technical aspects of software, recommends, tests and implements performance tuning solutions to assure the optimum configuration of the database, modules, and application. 13. Develops and maintains controls and procedures for administering the system. 14. Oversees the maintenance of system database and data structure in accordance with industry and vendor standards. 15. Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, and establishing personal networks. 16. Evaluates and recommends software development tools and newly announced development standards with the goal of improving development processes and deliverables. ADDITIONAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 1. Assumes additional responsibilities and performs special projects as needed or directed. Requirements Bachelors degree in computer science or related field and 5-7 years systems programming experience or equivalent combination of education and experience. Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Personal Characteristics. 1. Ability to handle confidential information responsibly and exhibit sound judgment with maintaining that confidentiality. 2. Demonstrates a reliable, dependable, and trustworthy work ethic. 3. Ability to make sound judgments after all available information has been gathered or communicated. 4. Demonstrates a mature attitude and insight into matters affecting department self and/or company welfare. 5. Display strong abilities to learn new technologies and acquire new skills through independent study, professional training, and from more senior team members. 7. Demonstrates experience implementing, integrating and maintaining packaged systems across an enterprise application portfolio. 8. Ability to assess business requirements and translate them into technical specifications for development team members. 9. Developed knowledge and understanding of relational databases and data structures. 10. PC computer skills including systems programming capabilities using development tools and compilers. 11. Customer service oriented; ability to interpret and explain complex instructions. 12. Ability to communicate with all levels. Effective interpersonal skills. 13. Able to prioritize multiple projects and lead project-focused development teams. 14. Strong attention to detail; ability to perform duties with a high level of accuracy. 15. Able to organize work, engage in a variety of tasks simultaneously and consistently meet deadlines. 16. Able to apply common sense understanding to carry out complex instruction furnished in written, oral, or diagram form. 17. Ability to work independently and in groups with minimal supervision.

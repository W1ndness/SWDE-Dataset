Managing Editor, Social Media

The Communications department provides strategy and support for business-critical thought leadership, reputation, issues management, public relations, crisis and other communications initiatives for the businesses and brands of Thomson Reuters, Legal, a global provider of information, software and service for legal professionals.

Core responsibilities:

* Counsel senior leadership on key brand, reputation and issues management initiatives, leveraging current and new social media channels to support corporate objectives; * Develop and execute social media programming as part of broader market-facing communications program to support product, brand, reputation and corporate responsibility objectives; * Create compelling content in a variety of media formats including written and audio and video podcasts, building upon established franchises such as LegalCurrent, the LegalCurrent podcast, Thomson Reuters, Legal YouTube and Twitter channels; * Manage and enhance presence and reach of online Facebook, blog and Twitter feeds; * Liaise with marketers and others who support dedicated social media channels at the product, business, division and corporate level; * Serve in leadership role on Legal social media taskforce, and oversee general SBU-level social media activities to ensure compliance with social media guidelines; * Monitor relevant online discussions, issues and opportunities, prepare quarterly reports plus additional reports as needed; * Support product and business level communications objectives, developing strategies and tactics that combine social media with other external communications disciplines.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate will have:

* Confidence and presence in counseling with senior management and executives on critical social media issues and opportunities; * A proven track record designing, executing and managing social media programming; * Strong knowledge of strategic public relations, media relations, reputation management, corporate responsibility and issues management planning and execution; * Proven planning and project management expertise; * Outstanding oral and written communication skills and the ability to work in a deadline-driven environment.

Qualifications:

* BA with 6 years experience in progressive communications related roles in an agency, media or corporate environment; * Strong oral and written communication skills; ability to counsel and creatively present information to all level of the organization; * Ability to work in highly matrixed environment comprising global locations and colleagues, with proven project management skills; * Ability to manage multiple projects with competing demands under tight deadlines; * Ardent ability to influence and counsel senior management; * A proven track record developing and executing public relations, social media, public affairs and corporate social responsibility programs; * Highly motivated, results-oriented * Strong knowledge of communications, media and editing hardware and software including shooting and editing video * Ability to travel as required by the needs of the business

Organization and flexibility are key; creativity, drive for results and high-energy, "roll-up-your-sleev. es" attitude and commitment to success are imperative.

Job Description:

Production Support Engineer will maintain, monitor and support Enterprise Data Technology production applications on a 24 x 7 x 365 schedule. Ops engineer will work with business, Development, Quality Assurance, DBA and other support teams to co-ordinate and facilitate the scheduling, incident triage and timely resolution of production issues. In addition, he/she will assist in the implementation of new applications in production and facilitate Data Corrections, DBMODs and other adhoc executions in production environment.
KEY JOB FUNCTIONS
Provide production support or technical support to users of customized or proprietary applications.
Provide support to investigate and resolve production problems for applications developed using UNIX, Abinitio, AutoSys and Oracle.
Actively participate in development design, code and support documents reviews.
Execute Data corrections/DBMOD and other adhoc production requests.
Analyze monthly production schedule, determines risks and provide recommendations on appropriate course of action.
Contribute to development of Operational standards, policies and procedures
Provide best practices and implement process improvements and automation as needed
Monitor & identify database and UNIX server performance issues to minimize the process delays and avoid impact on downstream applications.
Actively participate in pre deployments meetings and deploy changes to production without any incidents.
Plan, contribute to, or conduct projects for developing requirements for system enhancement, revision or development of application, or related projects.
Strong written and oral communication skills are essential. Strong analytical skills and ability to resolve problems are desired. Some knowledge of mortgage industry required. products and operations preferred
Review and certify CLAM (Central Log Analysis ) activities in production.
Lead and contribute to all phases of the software development life cycle for Enterprise Data applications including current production and any future applications
Involve in preparing the daily production status reports and attend the company wide daily production status call to provide the status of the applications as needed.
Play the role of Functional Coordinator to review and approve change tickets
EDUCATION
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent required
MINIMUM EXPERIENCE
5-7 years of related experience

SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS
Require experience with Large Scale Enterprise Financial Systems.
Require experience for providing production support for the application which were developed using the technologies/tools Abinitio, Autosys, Unix, shell scripting and sql
Job scheduling experience using AutoSys is required
Prior 24/7 Support Experience a plus.
Ability to work independently and multi-task to meet critical deadlines in a rapidly changing environment
Experience with (working knowledge of) one or more of the following: ClearCase, ClearQuest, Business objects, Remedy PCM Plus required.
Experience in 's SDLC standard is preferred
High energy, self-motivation, attention to detail, creativity, flexibility, and ability to work under pressure.
Flexibility to work additional or after hours on an as needed basis to meet deadlines.

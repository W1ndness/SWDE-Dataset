Reference Number:
SoftwareEngineer

Job Description:
Looking for a SR Systems/Software Engineer who will be responsible for technical/team leadership of design and implementation activities related to software/systems development in Healthcare IT products.

Duties
? Responsible for performing a variety of software development lifecycle activities on various projects and programs.
? Responsible for doing software development while complying with various product development processes and QSRs
? Responsible for implementing modules/components, performing peer code reviews, performing unit testing, subsystem testing and participating in various system integration activities.
? Leads the coordination of system integration activities. Supports software quality assurance, with planning and execution of their activities.
? Responsible for reviewing and supporting creation of defect free requirements/uses-cases.
? Responsible for creating design artifacts based on requirements/use-cases and software architecture. Responsible for reviewing design artifacts produced by peers.
? Responsible for rapid prototyping of highly innovative concepts for concept/feasibility testing.
? Responsible for working on assigned change requests/defects. For assigned defects, provides accurate root-cause analysis and provides impact analysis to QA.
? Responsible for providing design/technical specifications to external design partners.
? Constantly keeps up with software/system technology changes in the industry and provides ideas for product improvement/evolution/revolution.
? Responsible for working within a geographically distributed team environment and meeting individual and team objectives simultaneously.
? Follows development in software engineering discipline and recommends improvements in software processes based on industry trends.
? Identifies ways to assist product management in product planning through requirements development and analysis. Proposes new product features and updates.

Requirements
? 5+ years experience in software development industry.
? Medical device/Healthcare software industry experience is highly desired.
? Highly process oriented and strong background in various system/software development life cycles/methodologies.
? Excellent knowledge of Java/J2EE and .NET technologies, open source frameworks, imaging systems (preferably medical imaging), database systems, networking and hardware is highly desired.
? System level knowledge of Microsoft OS. Experience in writing multithreaded applications is required.
? Very strong background in object-oriented architectures and proven experience in using design patterns to architect software systems. Excellent knowledge of UML required.
? Experience with various configuration management systems and concurrent development.
? Ability to reduce an overwhelming amount of information into a few salient issues to focus on. The ability to prioritize the most relevant from all other issues.
? Must be a self starter with the initiative to follow through on programs/projects and work with all levels of users as well as have the ability to coordinate efforts both inside and outside of the division.
? Demonstrated ability to lead teams in successfully completing programs/projects while also serving as a key individual technical contributor
? Places great value on continuous learning and building knowledge.
? Must be highly driven to achieve results and aggressively identify and solve problems that can impact the scheduled delivery of products/releases. Must be accustomed to effectively dealing with unforeseen crises with quick analysis and decisive action.
? Coordinates activities with peers in Japan
? Believes in her/his ability to successfully take on new and difficult challenges, question the status quo, and take risks.
?Very effective oral and written communication skills.
? Bachelors degree in computer science or electrical engineering. Masters/Ph.D degree is highly preferred

Following experience is desired:
Application design tools/framework ? MVC, Spring, Hibernate, Eclipse, etc..
Build and Configuration Tools: SVN, Maven, Harvest, Ant etc
Web and application servers ? Tomcat and Apache.
XML technologies ? XML, Schemas, AJAX, SAX, DOM, XSLT, Xquery, XMLBeans, JDOM XHTML.
Java technologies ? JDK, J2EE (Servlet, JavaBeans, JSP, etc.).
Microsoft Technologies - .NET, MVVM, WPF
Experience with automated testing systems is desired.

Job Description:
Sykes Enterprises, Incorporated is looking for a candidate to provide technical leadership and serve as the technical subject matter expert for several mission critical applications supporting Fraud Operations. This is a permanent job at a customer site in Houston, TX.
Experience developing/supporting Fraud applications is required.

Position Overview:

Provide technical leadership and serve as the technical subject matter expert for several mission critical applications supporting Fraud Operations. These complex applications have high availability requirements and must be supported across the Development, Certification, and Production environments. The applications include the following Debit Protect Real Time, Debit Protect Near Real Time, Neural Model refresh process, PinPoint, & Rules Plus.

Responsibilities:

-Work with vendor to implement, test, and maintain application solutions and upgrades
-Interface and collaborate with business and other technical teams to gain alignment on critical issues and to proactively communicate topics of concern
-Work with vendor to develop streamlined processes solving complex technical challenges that are required to support recurring change activities that are critical to the business.
-Ensure that all technical requirements are met and represent the team as required in the annual assessment process
-Work collaboratively with internal audit groups as required to maintain highly secure systems that meet or surpass industry security requirements
-Identify opportunities within the infrastructure and application to monitor and alert when certain events have occurred or thresholds have been exceeded.
-Develop detailed system and UAT test plans in support of project and change related activities.
-Serve as a technical leader for the team
-Technical "Go to Person" for Debit Protect Applications
-Have hands-on knowledge of all products and solutions supported by the team
-Review and develop implementation plans for all highly critical system maintenance and upgrades
-Document requirements for requests to other departments as required to ensure alignment on technical deliverables.
-Identify, create and promote solutions to improve productivity of entire team; ie automation of tasks
-Identify and document opportunities for cost savings
-Identify and document opportunities for efficiency improvements
-Handle IM tickets when backlog becomes excessive
-Handle CM tickets when work load becomes heavy, including nights and weekends as necessary
-Follow up on Audit Action Items to ensure compliance
-Identify risks to the business, document and bring to the attention of management for prioritization
- Represent the team on meetings
- Attend / participate in vendor Executive Briefings
- Assist Director in setting priorities for annual BAU plan

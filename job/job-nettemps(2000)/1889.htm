SkillsThe Storage and Backup Engineer is competent to drive and deliver projects and demonstrates anin-depth understanding of the Backup Infrastructure, Storage Arrays (SAN/NAS), SAN fabric, server and database technologies, and industry standards common to big production environments. The successful candidate for this position is recognized as adeptand experienced in the field of System, Storage, and Backup Engineering and Administration, being able to implement, and operate system level components and environments to meet the requirements of the stakeholders and applications that run on them.



A Storage SME must be able to lead initiatives, make recommendations when consulted by other internal groups within the organization, and assist leading other team members within the Enterprise Storage Engineering & Operations team. The Storage SME is responsible for assisting in managing daily storage and backups/restore operations. This includes day-to-day monitoring, reporting, provisioning, troubleshooting, and cross-functional integration of backups/restores, storage allocation, and SAN provisioning in an environment consisting of HPUX, Linux, Windows, and Solaris servers. The Storage SME will also participate in an on-call rotation schedule, as the Operations team provides 24x7x365 support for the customer-facing aspects of its service.

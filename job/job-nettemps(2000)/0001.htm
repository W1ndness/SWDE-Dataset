Job Description:
We are looking for a charismatic, organized Office Manager to join our team. If you care about building a product that can improve the health of millions and a team that will appreciate all the little things you do to make their lives easier, we want you!

Responsibilities:

* Keep the office organized (facilities, bills, supplies, travel, maintain flow of diet coke and coffee)
* Cultivate a fun environment and improve team awesomeness (plan fun offsites, team lunches, office decorations)
* Improve team communication (lead team announcements, schedule group events)
* Management team support (scheduling and travel)
* Recruiting (create and post jobs, keep the interview process moving forward)

About You
Requirements

* Highly-organized, friendly, outgoing, dependable with a ?can do? attitude
* Strong written and verbal communication skills to effectively communicate and collaborate with a diverse range of people and job functions
* Web and office technology-savvy since we rely extensively on services like Skype Google docs, Dropbox, OS X apps, MS Office, Facebook, Linkedin and forums
* Minimum 1 year experience as a receptionist, administrative assistant or related experience
* Absolute discretion and confidentiality on all business matters
* Hobbies/interests that naturally draw you to being a user of our product

Ideally

* BA/BS degree preferred with a strong academic record
* Some accounting experience, e.g. recording A/R, A/P and other financial details into Quickbooks, handling invoicing and reimbursements
* Some retail or logistics experience, e.g. receiving PO?s from retailers, working with the warehouse team to make sure orders are properly shipped and recorded
* Chinese language experience (Mandarin and/or Cantonese)

Come join the fun! It?s seriously awesome working on a product that you can physically show your friends and family, and watch them get excited.

Job Title: IT Software Architect Profession: Computer Engineering and Information Technology -> Software Design/Development IT Software ArchitectBusiness Line:Automotive ServicesRequisition Type:RegularJob Reference Number: US01451Country:United StatesPrimary ResponsibilitiesSGS is the world's leading inspection, verification, testing and certification company. Recognized as the global benchmark for quality and integrity, we employ over 59,000 people and operate a network of more than 1,000 offices and laboratories around the world.In North America, SGS has over 6,000 employees in over 30 locations. We are a growing organization and we are looking for someone who wants to build a career with us.Our Automotive Services division is currently seeking an IT Software Architect in Malta, NY, Rutherford, NJ or Sacramento, CA. This key role is responsible for:-Leading and coordinating Company initiatives from a software technology perspective.-Driving our ideas from inception through to evaluation and prototype, communicating the results and strategy to the Teams.-Ensuring that the software architecture and related development activities are consistent with the Strategic direction set forth by both the IT Team and Senior Leadership, and are in line with generally accepted Industry best practices.Reporting toVice President, ITSpecific responsibilities-Own and communicate the software architecture strategy, plan and execution as it relates to the Company initiatives. Demonstrate a Software Architecture Development plan that meets the future needs of our commercial and government clients in light of the technical challenges of our industry demands.-Be able to take high level direction, formulate a plan to achieve the goals and execute the plan.-Work together with the PMO Office, IT Managers, and IT Staff to plan and execute projects, the Software Architect ensures that teams have appropriate product and technical specifications, direction, and resources to deliver products effectively by establishing realistic estimates for timelines while ensuring that projects remain on target to meet deadlines.-Lead and coordinate projects from a technology perspective, ensuring that software development life cycle activities are consistent with the direction set by Senior IT Leadership.-Provide software technical leadership and vision to the IT teams. Lead regular software technology and strategy reviews. Test ideas with prototype solutions and revise plans based upon success and failures. Evolve the existing deployed software solutions and their related architecture as needed.-Exhibit leadership abilities to our commercial and government clients with the deployment of software solutions and fixes that are coordinated based upon defined release schedules. Will be held accountable for ---delivering projects on time, every time---.-Serve as the technical lead from a Software perspective in all client meetings which have implemented SGS Automotive programs and/or products. Collaborate closely with other IT Managers, their Staff, and the Vice President of IT on all new and existing opportunities we face with our customers.-Perform such Job duties as research, tool evaluation, documenting patterns and standards, defining technology strategy, technology evaluation and recommendation, mentoring developers through hands-on assistance as well as formal and informal training and some technical development.-Perform other duties as assigned. Software architects will be expected to work on multiple tasks and initiatives concurrently.Profile-Bachelors or Masters Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or related Business or Engineering field from a nationally accredited 4 year college or university where the degree curriculum is based upon science, mathematics, critical thinking, reasoning or empirical knowledge and At least 5 years experience in IT software design, development, architecture or solution design in a paid full-time position is required.Skills-Proficient level ability using general office tools including but not limited to Microsoft Windows based workstations, Microsoft Office suite of applications, and general Web based software tools-Deep understanding of IT infrastructure including server, network, storage and data center architectures-Demonstrable experience with Structured or Object Oriented software development/architectures in either Java/J2EE or .Net with additional experience with C++ desired-Demonstrable experience as a software architect and providing technical and thought leadership, guidance, and mentorship (including direction setting, articulating decisions, consensus building, conflict remediation, diplomacy, etc.)-Demonstrated self-starter with the ability to set the direction when there is little or none-Experience solving complex and large-scale hardware/software problems-A record of successful delivery of IT solutions as an individual, Team Lead and or/ IT Manager-Strong, project management skills, time management, and problem solving skills. Willingness to support delivering projects ---on time, every time----Applied level of understanding with regards multi-tier system development-Experience in multiple software development lifecycle phases, including scope and proposal, analysis, design, and software construction-Experience planning and breaking down a task into sub-tasks and estimating effort to perform the work-Familiarity with: Various requirements gathering techniques; Configuration management concepts and tools; Software usability concepts-Experience facilitating meetings with multiple customers and technical staff, including building consensus and mediating compromises when necessary-Software prototyping experience-Demonstrated strong logical and technical problem-solving skills-Demonstrated ability to learn and quickly comprehend new concepts-Demonstrated strong judgment and decision-making ability-Ability to excel in idea generation, evaluation, and prioritization, customer and partner engagement, and cross-functional technical program management-A willingness to dive into software architecture challenges and technical issues as part of maintaining the skills necessary to manage and advocate products and projects-Ability to work with technical and non-technical resources to get things done-Excellent written and oral communicationAdditional informationWorking Conditions: The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of the IT Software Architect job role.

Job Description:
SAP Consultant: Quality Management Lead
Skillman, NJ
Term: 10+ months

RESPONSIBILITIES:

SAP QM Lead will be responsible for providing functional support and project work for the SAP Make/QM mega-process. Areas of focus include: QM (Quality Management) and MM (Materials Management). The QM lead will also be responsible for configuration and technical support of the aforementioned components in a production environment. This person will ensure adherence to J&J SAP global standards. The incumbent will serve as the liaison between business partners and technical IT in the development of new RICEF's (Reports, Interfaces, Conversions, Enhancements, and Forms) for the QM space.

The QM lead will document business requirements, prepare functional specifications, review technical specifications and coordinate the development activities. The incumbent will configure SAP system with the functionality defined in the business requirements and participate in integration and user acceptance testing of the RICEF's. Other responsibilities will include the following: Manage interfaces to the SAP system and make sure they are processed in a timely manner with full integrity of the incoming and outgoing data; Conduct impact analysis of changes to configuration both within the defined area of responsibility as well as across other integrated areas; Develop and update all validation documentation required to support requirements definition, configuration, testing and change management and facilitate business process definition, assess issues, collect business requirements and validate business process designs.

The QM Lead will provide proactive and timely response to all support requests and actively manage support and involvement of power users and key end users. This person will troubleshoot production problems and engage SAP OSS (Online Service System) as required to obtain resolution.

Experience Level:
Unspecified

Requirements:
? A minimum of a Bachelor's degree
? A minimum of 5 years of Information Technology experience
? A minimum of 3 years of hands-on SAP development and configuration experience with QM (Quality Management)
? Participation in two full cycle implementations within QM
? A minimum of 1 year of run support experience
? Knowledge of SAP MM (Materials Management)/ SAP PP
? Experience integrating SAP systems with external systems
? Experience collaborating in a cross-functional team environment
? Knowledge of Process Excellence/Six Sigma tools and methodology is preferred.

MUST BE A US CITIZEN OR PERMANENT RESIDENT ABLE TO WORK ON OUR W2 WITHOUT SPONSORSHIP TO QUALIFY - NO CORP TO CORP CANDIDATES

COMPENSATION/COMMENTS:
Up to $117/hr (DOE) - If an offer is made, a 7 year criminal background check, education verification, employment verification, and 2 professional references will be required. Candidate must pass all screenings to be hired.

Local candidates are encouraged to apply as there is no relocation reimbursement.

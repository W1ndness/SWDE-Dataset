Job Description:
Job Category: Sales Location: Detroit, MI, US Job ID: 751358-38246 Division: Sales Are you looking for the BEST job at Microsoft? Do you have a passion for technology? Do you love working with students and faculty? Are you innovative, outgoing and driven? Are you a developer, technologist, marketer, spokesperson, collaborator and innovator all rolled into one? We need you to represent Microsoft as an Academic Developer Evangelist in the Michigan, Ohio, Tennessee and Kentucky. The Academic Developer Evangelist (ADE) is an elite field member of the world wide team of Developer Platform Evangelists (DPE). The overall mission of DPE is to secure platform adoption and revenue growth through evangelism, community engagement, relationship marketing and a vibrant solutions ecosystem. As an ADE, you are Microsofts representative to academia helping to win the hearts and minds of the student developer and their educators. The ADE is a technical role with the mission of engaging with the broad community of academics and driving excitement around Microsoft technologies. This position is a great opportunity to help improve perception of Microsoft in academia and to increase student usage of Microsofts technologies through breadth marketing programs as well as depth engagement with key accounts. We are looking for a person who is enthusiastic about technology and engaging with the academic community. ADEs must be able to talk about a broad range of technology intelligently and enthusiastically to students, faculty and IT professionals and demonstrate how to build applications for the cloud and for rich clients. Passion, curiosity, technical depth and exceptional communication and presentation skills are required. Developer, technologist, marketer, spokesperson, collaborator, mentor Responsibilities: You will develop and implement a territory plan that will: Win the hearts and minds of students by giving them a compelling reason to develop applications using Microsoft technologies Drive Microsoft's image as a technology leader and compelling choice across academia in your territory Engage and build strong, mutually beneficial relationships with university students and faculty Deliver academically oriented workshops/talks/presentations on applications development technologies at university campuses and academic events to both small and large audiences Build a vibrant ecosystem between students, companies and community organizations by integrating your efforts into the local software economy Convey a compelling and convincing case for the integration and use of Microsoft technologies both in and out of the classroom Market Microsofts academic resources and programs (i.e. MSDN AA, Dynamics AA) to ensure awareness and participation of universities Create an online presence for yourself Participate in academic developer online and offline student and faculty communities Qualifications: Located in Michigan or Ohio Unabated passion for technology and Microsoft, a keen eye for unobvious opportunities 4 year degree in CS, Eng., IS or related discipline, familiarity with CS/CE/IS curriculums, solid knowledge of software dev tools and programming models 5+ years related experience Flexibility in regards to work schedule and travel Strong technology skills Current programming skills a strong advantage, academic teaching or research background, or previous evangelism experiences preferred Specific knowledge of Microsoft (.NET, Visual Studio, ) and competitive technologies (Java, academic languages, Linux) working knowledge of database (SQL) Sales: DPEG

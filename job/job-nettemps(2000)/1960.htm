Job Description:
SUMMARY Maintain high availability of AIX pSeries Servers. Analyze information systems requirements and plan AIX based systems that will provide system capabilities required for projected workloads. Will plan layout, install, and modify existing systems by performing the following duties. ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES Under general supervision, responsible for completing assigned complex or long-term projects within budgetary and scheduling guidelines. Must be able to perform extensive cause and effect reasoning. Experience in dealing with external customers. Experience working in a team oriented environment. Work closely with operations personnel, vendors, and internal support to maintain stated availability of production servers. Initiate, track, handle, and resolve trouble tickets using Remedy application. Ability to independently perform the following systems tasks/analysis: o Performance monitoring o Disk Storage space o Processor utilization o Paging space o Network statistics o Process listings (i.e. I/O statistics) o Review error logs o User ID maintenance (i.e. adding, deleting, resetting) o Printer maintenance (i.e. adding, deleting, updating, queue management) o Review logs (i.e. backup, crontab) o Monitor and change nightly cycle where necessary o Backups/Restores o Perform software administration as needed o Perform non-system software installation o Insure compliance with vendor software licensing requirements o Software media management o Monitor failed login attempts o su attempts o Password changes o Manage file and directory permissions o Insure proper execution of batch programs during on-call periods o Monitor roots mail o Development of shell scripts to perform daily administration task o Problem notification (i.e. sending pager and e-mail notifications) o Independently perform hardware replacement for failing components Perform complete documentation of systems design / administration tasks. Configure and Maintain Disk Storage Systems Engineer Storage Area Networks (SAN) and Network Attached Storages (NAS) Design and Implement Tape Storage Systems (TSM) Implement Networks for AIX Systems Lead systems design efforts and make recommendations to management. Adhere to established systems standards. Provide technical leadership in both the planning and installation of new systems. Create technical drawings and lead layout activities for new computer systems. Provide second level support for Production Control Team. Provide work direction to Engineers, Analysts, and Technicians. Assume a leadership role in directing and reporting team activities to management. Handling of day-to-day operational service requests. Monitor production equipment to ensure expected operational capabilities are maintained. Perform other duties as assigned. QUALIFICATIONS To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE Requires a Bachelors Degree in a technical discipline or equivalent experience. Candidate must possess seven years of applicable experience. Experience with AIX Server administration with pSeries Servers Experience in implementing AIX servers in an Oracle database environment a plus. Must possess strong customer service skills. LANGUAGE SKILLS Must be able to speak fluent English and possess an effective command of the English language in both written and oral communication. REASONING ABILITY Must be able to perform extensive cause and effect reasoning. PHYSICAL DEMANDS The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. Should be able to safely lift 50 pounds to perform hardware assembly. WORK ENVIRONMENT The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. Most functions will be performed within a predefined cubicle/work station environment. From time to time the job will require work to be performed in other areas. +IT+

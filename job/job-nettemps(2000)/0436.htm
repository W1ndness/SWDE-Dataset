Job Description:
Financial services company in New York, NY, is looking to hire a senior Network Engineer to join their team on a full time basis.

This person will be part of a team that focuses on LAN/WAN networking design, engineering, implementation and operational support, involving primarily Cisco routing and switching products.

In addition to Cisco*s networking products, other vendor products include F5 Load Balancers, Riverbed*s Steelhead WAN acceleration products and UNIX based BIND DNS systems.

This person will also be supporting Juniper*s NetScreen firewalls, Cisco PIX/ASA firewalls and some network security issues. This is a senior level role. This person will be expected to lead projects as see fit.

Candidates need to have at least 5-10 years of experience with Cisco network engineering. Candidates need to have solid working knowledge of data communication protocol suites such as TCP/IP and other general data networking knowledge such as cabling, telecom services such as T1/T3/OC3, etc and be able to lead projects. Any experience with VOIP is a plus.

Required Skills:

Cisco Routers, Cisco Switches, LAN/WAN Design and Engineering, VPN, Firewalls, IP Telephony, SIP, MPLS

Hands-on experience with Cisco IOS-based routers: 7600, 7200, 3800, 2800, etc.
Cisco IOS based switches within the 6500, 3750 and 3560 families; and Cisco ASA 55xx firewall appliances.

In-depth knowledge of layer 3 technologies, such as HSRP, OSPF and BGP.

In-depth knowledge of layer 2 technologies, such as Rapid Spanning-Tree and various LAN/WAN mediums (i.e. PPP, HDLC, etc.) Wireless IEEE 802.11.

High capacity data circuits, SONET, SONETc, OC-3, OC-12, OC-48

WAN Acceleration devices

Central Authentication, Authorization and Accounting via TACACS+

Logging aggregation and reporting via SNMP and SYSLOG

Ability to lead and document proof of concepts (POCs), assessments, detailed evaluations, and final selections.

Ability to lead infrastructure performance diagnosis and scalability assessment activities

Working knowledge of Juniper firewalls and firewall management platform or alike.

Solid understanding of network topologies / design: routing, switching and MPLS

Solid understanding of IP networking (TCP/IP and packet analysis)

Ability to write technical and operations documentation

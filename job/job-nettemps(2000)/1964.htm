Job Category: Customer Service & Support Location: Issaquah, WA, US Job ID: 741611-30767 Division: Services & Support Do you want to be in a position to influence big changes in customer support experiences? Do you want to help deliver cutting-edge support automation for applications running on current and future versions of Windows? If the answer is yes; you want to join the Consumer Windows Supportability Team! The Consumer Windows Supportability Team needs a talented, driven individual to evangelize the Windows Troubleshooting Platform. Come join a small, agile team committed to improving the support experience for Windows customers and contribute to a growing library of automated self-help and assisted support solutions which help millions of customers find and fix common computer problems every day. As a Technology Evangelist for the Windows Troubleshooting Platform youll work with OEMs, ISVs, and internal Microsoft teams to: Build a community of Windows Troubleshooting Platform developers Promote ecosystem usage of Microsoft-developed troubleshooters Increase adoption of the Windows Troubleshooting Platform Share best practices and lessons learned Gather feedback and contribute to future support automation initiatives Drive requirements for technical content, value proposition, and processes to implement Drive structured feedback to the product teams to improve products and adoption goals To be successful in this role, youll need the following: Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills A solid technical background in Windows and troubleshooting Excellent knowledge of technology trends, industry participants and leaders Solid planning skills and the ability to execute on ideas and plans Leadership skills and results orientation Enthusiasm for learning about new technologies and businesses Social media experience Qualifications/Requirements: 2 years technical marketing, evangelism, or PM experience required Undergraduate degree in business/marketing or technical discipline required. Masters preferred. Must be able to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously, and must be capable of managing work across virtual teams across many organizations 1 year Software technical support agent/management experience PowerShell or C# experience preferred Microsoft will consider for this opening only candidates who reside within 50 miles of the job.

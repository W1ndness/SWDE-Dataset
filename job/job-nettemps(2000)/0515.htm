Job Description:
Responsibilities: The Solution Architect Designer contributes to the growth, effectiveness, and profitability of the organization by providing senior technical leadership for the enterprise system/software architecture, integration and ongoing development of technologies/services that deliver highly available, scalable, high performance application functionality to a wide spectrum of users/stakeholders. This position is strategic and demands that the incumbent comprehend a wide range of technologies, services and best practice approaches in the face of a rapidly changing technical environment. He/she will participate in technical strategic planning activities as a member on the Technology Architecture Board for the organization in partnership with IT leadership, other organization units, and vendors.

Essential functions of the position include:

1. Designs and leads the implementation of enterprise system/software architectures that integrate commercial/custom applications, authentication services, multi-tier components, performance acceleration servers, web services, database services, search technologies, payment systems and high availability systems. [20%]
2. Provides technical oversight of/mentoring to the development staff utilizing best practice methodologies, principles and tools. Develops project estimates, conducts code reviews, and develops/adheres to enterprise system development processes, standards and guidelines. [20%]
3. Plans and conducts design efforts that ensure:
- elimination of duplicative efforts,
- conformance to the technology architecture guidelines,
- avoidance of re-work efforts, and
- achievement of business objectives. [10%]
4. As part of the continuous improvement effort: /
- provides quality designs for systems/applications that ensure optimal performance and future extensibility,
- develops/maintains system/application design processes,
- identifies existing applications where designs could be improved and further optimized, and
- identifies potential design problems before they occur and implements solutions. [10%]
5. Evaluates and develops enterprise strategic/operational plans that ensure enterprise system/software architecture objectives are published, known and followed so the organization's long-term business objectives can be met. [10%]
6. Identifies implementation risks, and analyzes potential impact to the enterprises objectives. Participates in the development/review of enterprise system/software projects schedules to ensure critical paths are identified and risk management approaches outlined/followed. [10%]
7. Plans/coordinates cross-departmental management needs to ensure timely fielding of new systems/software as well as triage of critical support instances. [10%]
8. Leads, motivates, and empowers development staff in the successful performance of their project tasks and responsibilities while encouraging innovation, continuous improvement, and timely execution. [10%]

Requirements: A BS or BA degree in computer science or a related field is recommended. A MS degree in computer science is preferred.

* Minimum of seven years experience--to include a development management role--with system/software development.
* Minimum of four years experience as a lead for developing/delivering technical designs/solutions.
* Must have led successful service-oriented architecture (SOA), object oriented, data warehouse/business intelligence and system integration design/implementation/support efforts during the past four years.
* Demonstrated history of successful project completion leveraging best practices, processes, techniques, standards and tools.
* Demonstrated leadership, interpersonal, organizational, verbal/written communications, analytical and problem solving skills.
* Demonstrated mentoring/training abilities as well as a passion for researching/learning technology (past, present, and emerging).
* Understanding/familiarity with enterprise architecture frameworks, such as Zachman, FEAF, and TEAF is highly desired.
* Understanding of/experience integrating with software as a service (SaaS) solutions is highly desired

* Java Certification highly desirable.
* Oracle Certification (e-Business Suite/SOA Development/etc.) high desirable.

* Expert-level knowledge of architecture principles and practices across technology, business, applications, and data architecture.
* Strong proficiency with SOA principles, BPEL concepts, OO design/ programming, design patterns, database development and n-tier architectures.
* Proficiency with Internet/WAN/LAN topology, web/infrastructure hardware/software/ protocols/concepts (such as routers, switches, firewalls, proxies, load balancing, failover, TCP/IP, HTTP, SSL, PKI, Cookies, Sessions, etc.), and Payment Card Industry Digital Security Standard requirements.
* Command of Java/J2EE, XML, XSLT, XSD, HTML, SQL, AJAX, JQuery, Javascript, and related technologies.

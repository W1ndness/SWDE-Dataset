Job Description:
Title: Consultant Cost & Technology
Location: US-Texas-Houston

IHS (NYSE: IHS) is a leading source of information and insight in pivotal areas that shape today's business landscape: energy, economics, geopolitical risk, sustainability and supply chain management. Businesses and governments around the globe rely on the comprehensive content, expert independent analysis and flexible delivery methods of IHS to make high-impact decisions and develop strategies with speed and confidence. IHS's customers range from governments and large multinational corporations to small companies and technical professionals, doing business in more than 180 countries. IHS has been in business since 1959 and became a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange in 2005. Headquartered in Englewood, Colorado, USA, IHS employs more than 4,400 people in more than 30 countries around the world, and had 2010 revenues of $1.075 billion. The IHS family of companies includes several industry renowned brands including IHS CERA (Cambridge Energy Research Associates), IHS Jane's, IHS Global Insight, IHS Herold, and IHS Fairplay.
Summary:

Working within the IHS CERA Cost and Technology Group you will have the opportunity to build, model and develop a market intelligence tool to support our clients' needs. This activity will support the direct needs of the costs forums and directors. Through detailed research and analysis efforts, you will be seen as the market expert and have an opportunity to contribute significantly to quarterly market reports, roundtable presentations and specialized research and consulting engagements. The research associate will be responsible for the ongoing research activities and communicating major insights and implications across all forums. This position requires data gathering, analysis, problem solving, communication skills as well as building and maintaining relationships (internally and externally).



Required Experience:

Build strong relationships with vendors, suppliers and service companies through daily phone calls to establish a data channel for costs research and utilizing a call log for historical tracking purposes (updated weekly)
Track and monitor prices and rates collected through service calls (weekly) in an organized structure that shows a "like with like" analysis
Develop a costs model (using excel) for each market that enables you to organize the rates and prices that you have collected from suppliers and service companies in a way that buckets it by market section.
Develop a cost model (using excel)for each market that will provide short- term forecasts (1-3 years) as well as long-term forecasts (3-7 years) and maintain over time
Monitor and develop reports based on service and supplier financials for your market concentrations
Perform due diligence and deep understanding of the competitive market and potential activity
Develop quantitative and qualitative analyses to identify trends
Communicate with and deliver presentations to internal and external teams as well as cross-functional teams to present your market research and findings clearly and concisely
Provide an adequately written report every quarter on each of your markets summarizing the key trends, latest activity and outlook based both on modeling techniques as well as conversations with service companies and suppliers.

Qualifications
The qualifications of the Market Research Associate include:
Bachelor's degree or MBA focusing on Economics, Finance, Statistics, Engineering or Mathematics fields preferred.
2-5 years of experience, preferably in the energy industry or in management consulting, private equity, investment banking with focus on the energy industry.
Consistent track record of demonstrating analytical and problem-solving skills as well as leadership abilities
Excellent analytical skills; the ability to translate and synthesize data and financial reports
Proficient in quantitative analysis and basic statistics
Superior critical thinking, writing and presentation skills
Proficient in Microsoft Excel, Word and PowerPoint
Experience working with databases
Self starter; ability to work independently and accomplish tasks
Ability to meet tight deadlines
Must be detail-oriented and very organized

Job Title: BI Developer - Central Area Profession: Analysis/Consulting/Project Management -> Business Analysis (Software Implementation) Job Ref 9730/249 Job Title BI Developer - Central Area Job Type Full-time Location Chicago-Detroit Description Position Description:Information Builders' Professional Services organization works with customers to understand their Business Intelligence (BI) needs and develop and deploy robust solutions. We are seeking BI experts to work closely with our customers to define and deploy new BI systems. While knowledge of our tools is not required for consideration, strong knowledge of competitive products (including architecture, design, and coding experience) is expected. IB will provide training in our WebFOCUS tools. A Senior BI Developer is involved in all facets of BI application design, development, testing, deployment and maintenance. They must be capable of working independently without close supervision.-- They will architect small applications as well as individual modules within larger applications.-- He/she formulates client requirements into a written form and transforms them into specifications for other developers to follow.-- The Senior--BI Developer also performs data analysis and modifies or recommends changes in the database to facilitate the reporting application.-- They will help customers understand best practices for working with WebFOCUS in their environment.-- They will also perform some project management tasks, and develop technical plans for their projects.-- They may participate in estimating work for client projects during the sales cycle. Description of position's duties: Provide analysis and design and leadership consulting services to clients using the IB core product line and specialty products based on business requirements. Specific tasks include, but are not limited to, application maintenance, application development, application deployment, application maintenance, and problem resolution Quickly develop a thorough knowledge of core IB products. Continue to expand and develop skills in core IB products and, when applicable to business needs, operating systems, database management systems and associated client tools. Display a working knowledge of Database Management Systems, including both legacy DBMS's and relational/SQL DBMS's. Acquire a working knowledge of HTML, JavaScript, and Cascading Style Sheets for creating and interfacing WebFOCUS reports in a self-service environment. Lead small to mid-size projects at customer sites, supervising and advising more junior developers. Recognize and help exploit business opportunities and pass the appropriate information to management. Produce Project Management reports, such as the weekly status report, on a timely basis. Must be willing and able to travel as necessary. Minimum education/training required: Bachelors Degree (B.S. or B.A.) in a technical or business field; or equivalent work-related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Exposure to GUI tools or other front-end technology. Exposure to multiple Database Management Systems, with exposure to SQL based DBMS's a plus. Demonstrated proficiency in multiple operating systems and multiple programming languages. Ability to write solid documentation that supports the coding and project management effort. Experience in applying project management principles via formal methodology, using the associated tools and techniques. Sound customer relations skills and the ability to work with demanding clients. Good communication and interpersonal skills Minimum experience required: Four--(4) years of Business Intelligence application development and maintenance. Four--(4) years of WebFOCUS or another high level Business Intelligence language. Two--(2) years of database architecture and database design. Two--(2) years of data analysis, construction and testing. Two (2) years of Consulting experience. One--(1) years of Business Analysis. Desired Attributes Profession: Analysis/Consulting/Project Management -> Business Analysis (Software Implementation) Software Implementation: Application development tools Business Analysis: Conduct risk assessments Business Analysis: Conduct business plan analysis/feasibility studies Business Analysis: Evaluate technical solutions Business Analysis: Create/build business solutions Business Analysis: Contribute to proposals Business Analysis: Document functional design Project Type: Software Implementation/IT Technical Design and Analysis: Configure products/Perform product configurations Technical Design and Analysis: Perform software product installations Software Implementation: ERP/Enterprise business applications Industry: IT/Technology/Electronics Industry: Computer Software

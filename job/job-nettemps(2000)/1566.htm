Job Title: Data Analyst Profession: Analysis/Consulting/Project Management -> Business Analysis (Software Implementation) Data AnalystRequisition ID 7041Location New York, NYDescription MANAGER, TECHNOLOGY - GLOBAL MARKETSAt Sapient Global Markets, we are quite literally on the front lines of the biggest issues facing the global markets leaders today - investment banks, asset management firms, wealth managers, oil and energy firms, and government and regulatory agencies. Driving transformation and leading change within these industries requires big thinkers who bring insight, knowledge, technical acumen and passion for innovation. We offer the opportunity to join a recognized leader in developing and delivering groundbreaking solutions for today's dynamic global marketplace and an award-winning culture that fosters growth and leadership.As Manager, Technology at Sapient Global Markets, you will help to realize high-end technology solutions for our clients in the financial and commodities trading industry. You will lead a multidisciplinary, international project team and manage the development, design, user interface, technology integration, and site architecture management. Moreover, in this hands-on managing consultant role you will engage in business development, as well as in building and maintaining client relationships.PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:. Work closely with clients to establish technology solutions at a business and technical level.. Leading the design, estimation, planning, and implementation of business solutions that require IT system(s).. Interact with business stakeholders to provide technical solutions delivering to business needs.. Consult clients' senior technical leaders on best practice point of view.. Establish high, mid and micro level plans working with a Project Manager.. Provide leadership to international, multidisciplinary team of up to 20 members.. Help to build client relationships and participate in business development and proposal work.. Deliver compelling presentations to internal and external stakeholders.REQUIREMENTS:. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.. Deep experience in architectural development, design patterns and implementation of custom developed solutions (and/or product integration projects).. Experience in a consulting or client-facing role in large and complex organizations.. Deep experience in the Financial Services (Investment Banking, Capital Markets, Asset Management) and /or Commodities (Energy) trading industry highly desired.. Experience in architecting trading applications for areas such as Capital markets, Fixed Income, Equities, Hedge Funds preferred.5+ years of strong experience in ETL development using Informatica PowerCenter version 8.5 or greater;. Strong experience in Oracle (10g, 11g) database development;. Strong SQL query-writing ability;. Knowledge of error handling techniques in PL/SQL. Knowledge of bulk operations in PL/SQL. Strong UNIX shell scripting ability;. Ability to deliver complex Informatica transformations and mappings;. Excellent verbal and written communication skills;. Experience in or interest in Agile Data Warehousing or Lean Integration; and. Opportunities for leading small teams.. Work with business requirements analyst and team lead to identify and understand source data systems. Map source system data to data warehouse models. Design, Develop , Document and test ETL processes. Adapt ETL processes to accommodate changes in source systems and new business user requirements. Experience maintaining models in a 'run the shop / change the shop' environment. Understanding of modeling techniques for OLTP, Hub-and-Spoke DW, Bus-DW. Can demonstrate normalization and denormalization (at least to 3NF). Proficiency in a data modeling tool. Preferably Embarcadero E/R Studio Data Architect. Understanding of OLAP/MOLAP/ROLAP. Experience capturing and maintaining metadata from all data warehousing components. Communicates physical database designs to database administrator. Knowledge of Materialized Views, Triggers, Functions, PL/SQL and other Oracle Objects.

Job Description:
Zensar is looking for Cisco UCM resource on Fulltime. Location - Seattle, WA Work Authorization - US Citizen, Green Card , EAD, H1B (we can transfer Visa) Position Purpose: Responsible for analysis, configuration, repair, and administration of a highly integrated and complex unified communications environment. Maintains operations of Clients' North America Cisco Unified Communications Manager (UCM) , Unity Connections, and all telephones in North America connected to this centralized node. Supports WAN provisioning, implementation, and monitoring of UC networking requirements. Provides Clients the ability to be self-sufficient in administering and maintaining its Cisco UCM/Unity node in North America - to meet the telephony & voicemail expectations of its customers. Principle Accountabilities: The following duties are intended to be representative of the work performed by incumbents in this position and are not all-inclusive. The omission of a specific duty or responsibility will not preclude it from the position if the work is similar, related or a logical extension of the position's purpose. 1. 60% - Operations: Provides user and technical support to both Tier I escalated incidents (repairs) and service requests (moves/adds/changes) 2. 10% - Operations: Evaluates alarms, faults, logs and all data necessary from UCM/Unity to identify root cause of operational issues. 3. 5% - Operations: Escalates Tier II issues to Tier III, when necessary, with adequate documentation 4. 10% - Project: Collaborates with project team on tasks related to Portfolio and Small projects, including data entry/provisioning, feature enablement & enhancements, user acceptance testing, etc. 5. 10% - Project: With project teams to review and ensure continuity in regional roadmaps and works with project teams in application integration efforts. 6. 5% - Project: Review end-user & ISA training needs and create/update/distribute material. Qualifications: 2-5 years experience in administration/support role of Cisco Unified Communications Manager and Cisco Unity Excellent interpersonal communication and analytical skills Latitude: This position expresses the first impression of Clients' Information & Technology Service's Telephony Operations - to all end users and especially the Tier 1 ISAs (Information Systems Analysts With expertise and service, this position responds and delivers operational support to incident & service requests; coordinates the delivery of minor changes to the user's desktop/telephony/voicemail environment; works through administration practices that affect individual users, while risking impact to a large number of users. This position also prioritizes projects for self, and collaborates on Clients I&TS initiatives & requirements with the Telephony/UC Infrastructure team. Decisions referred to a higher authority include: negotiations and signing of contracts; final purchasing decisions; project scope, timelines & implementation phases; system changes to production environment Decisions dictated by policy or procedure include directions generated by common business partner goals and scheduling system and network outages. Major Challenges: One of the major challenges facing this position is supporting a growing community of UC end-users (currently 150, scalable to 2000+) with service, expertise and fun. Another challenge is progressively maintaining best-practice-operations while delivering the full potential of Clients' UC experience. A third challenge is responding to escalated incidents and faults within SLA's. And one of the biggest challenges is sustaining collaboration with a team and user community abroad. Zensar Technologies is a globally focused software and services company spread across eighteen countries across the world. Zensar provides end-to-end services from IT development to Business Process Outsourcing, from consulting to implementation. With more than 6500 associates and sales and operations presence across US, UK, Germany, Sweden, Finland, Middle East, South Africa, Singapore, Australia, Japan and Poland, the Company delivers comprehensive services in mission-critical applications, enterprise applications, e-business, BPO Services.

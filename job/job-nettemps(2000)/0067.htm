Project Description:
Looking for an energetic and motivated technical planner for a new generation CDN infrastructure. Will include planning and coordinating new development, rollout and migration aspects. Responsibilities will include creating high level planning documents and technical project plans.
Technical experience is critical. Previous end to end system engineering/technical planning/project management skills are a MUST.

Technical Planner and Implementation Manager

- Energetic, enthusiastic, motivated and proactive individual needed to help plan and manage development, testing and deployment activities of network and application infrastructure
- Will manage projects and day to day activities of technical teams
- Need to have technical project management background
- Quick to learn new technologies, concepts and processes
- Will need to have technical Systems Engineering/Planning background to help track, communicate status/progress and facilitate resolving issues.
Needs to have technical background, especially in the area of IP/networking/DNS/CDN
- Experience with executing, managing or leading complex technology migrations a plus
- Testing experience a plus
- Great team player

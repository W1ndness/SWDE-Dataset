Managing and growing assets has brought new complexities and exciting opportunities for investors and wealth management service providers alike. The face of wealth management is changing, bringing with it a growing population of retirees and high-net worth individuals. Meanwhile a convergence in the industry is causing increased competition, a proliferation of service and product offerings.

As your firm faces an increase in competition for assets, the service you provide becomes the differentiator for your clients. Today, SunGard works with thousands of institutional and independent customers in the wealth management industry. These firms have realized that by utilizing an integrated technology platform to help link activities across the lifecycle, they are able to focus more time and energy on their clients' needs rather than on underlying business processes.

Approximately 2,500 customers use SunGard's wealth management platforms
SunGard processes 500,000 transactions per day on its accounting platforms
Some 600 customers, managing more than 650,000 accounts equaling more than $3.4 trillion in assets, use SunGard's accounting platforms
STN connects approximately 1,900 asset managers, 650 issuers, 270 broker-dealers representing 500 global trading destinations

Job Description:
Position: Search Engine Optimazition Manager
Location: Washington DC 20049
Hours: 9:00am to 5:00pm
Duration: 12 Month+
Rate: $40.00- 45.00 /hr

our client is seeking an experienced SEO Manager that will lead all organic search engine marketing efforts for Digital Strategy & Operations and other business units within client. This role is responsible for developing and managing all elements of SEO strategy from concept to execution. The SEO Manager must be results driven and technically savvy, with excellent communication skills to effectively train and work closely with key stakeholders across the organization including Product, Editorial, Tech, Development, Social Media, and senior executives. The SEO Manager will be accountable for developing SEO strategies and overseeing their execution to meet or exceed SEO traffic goals.

RESPONSIBILITIES

**Be an expert in search engine optimization best practices and techniques
-Work with teams to implement SEO audit findings and complete related project plan
-Develop SEO roadmap and identify and implement strategies and tactics for increasing organic traffic
-Deep understanding of key factors, on-page and off-page, that influence organic search rankings
-Create and maintain a development standards document/checklist to ensure efficient and accurate implementation of search optimization strategies as products are launched
-Research, test, manage link building campaings for high quality inbound links from paid/free directories, top industry sites, blogs and more
-Conduct keyword research using Wordtracker, Google and internal tools
-Recommend SEO opportunities based on keyword research and internal tools
-Make recommendations for content changes to new and existing content, including site side and link-building analysis and recommendations
-Proactively monitor search engine changes and document search engine algorithmic changes
-Work with development team to create landing pages as needed
-Train Editorial, Development and other stakeholders on SEO best practices
-Become an advocate of SEO within the brand

**Provide and create detailed analysis to trend campaign performance
-Interpret Web analytics data for performance improvement. Be comfortable with Omniture.
-Report by channel and AARP.org overall
-Create and update daily, weekly and monthly SEO reports

**Manage SEO Projects to timely execution
-Develop project plans, create action items for the team based on findings and manage deadlines
-Accurately quote time estimates for campaign initiatives and assist in the development of detailed project specifications

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE:
-BA or BS
-Minimum four years related SEO experience.
-Minimum 1-2 years management experience.


Experience Level:
Unspecified

Requirements:
REQUIREMENTS:
-Great communication skills, written and verbal
-Ability to juggle multiple short-term and long-term projects and be effective at task and time line management.
-Knowledge of HTML, JavaScript, FLASH, AJAX, and CSS as they relate to SEO.
-Proficiency in paid and free SEO and analytics tools, including Google Analytics, Google Webmaster Tools and Bing Webmaster Tools.
-Comfort working with content management systems
-Ability to work individually and with cross functional teams to drive results
-Excellent analytical skills to analyze large quantities of data
-A self starter with an ability to work in a fast paced, dynamic environment
-Proven project management skills
-Obsessive attention to detail
-Excellent written and verbal communication skills
COMPENSATION/COMMENTS:

Up to $45/hr (DOE) - If an offer is made, a 7 year criminal background check, education verification, employment verification, and 2 professional references will be required. Candidate must be pass all screenings to be hired.

Local candidates are encouraged to apply as there is no relocation reimbursement.

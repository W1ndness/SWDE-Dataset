Job Description:
TCML is currently seeking a Contracts Administrator for a contract to hire position with our fortune 500 client in White Plains, NY.

Duties
? Conduct regular reviews of Service Provider SLA performance to validate SLA measures are an accurate reflection of underlying data
? Conduct regular reviews of Service Provider reporting to ensure compliance to agreed to structure and standards
? Conduct regular reviews of Service Provider compliance to ticketing process (ticket priority, communication, content, ticket association)
? As operational problems occur, reviews Service Provider recovery and permanent fix plans
? Review post incident review reports
? Assignment and follow through of action items
? Attendance at any Outage Meetings as process owner.
? Attendance at any Root Cause Analysis Meetings as process owner.
? Identify and track implementation of permanent fix
? Document risk for any problem where a permanent fix has not be applied
? Track escalated performance issues and disputes with Service Providers
? Facilitate conflict resolution regarding service effectiveness
? Reviews and monitors Service Provider problems, change management processes, and escalation procedures
? A contact for day-to-day communications between Service Provider and the specific Tower/Domain
? Ensures an in-house process is maintained for handling of high severity and stagnant issues/events in both the supplier and the retained environments
? Educates Service Provider on program/product/operational details, including the development and implementation of Service Provider processing schedules and coordination of data flow ensuring timely transmittal to Service Provider
? Develops and distributes written communications as needed to the Service Provider
? Consults on the definition and execution of the Service Provider's Disaster Recovery Plan to ensure business continuity
? Monitor enhancements and changes to application portfolio
? Tracks Service Provider performance and ensures business viability of service levels
? Providing early warning to client management regarding degrading or missed service levels
? Review Standard Operating Procedures and other documentation
? Review shift turnover documentation
? Monitor Service Provider compliance with security and regulatory requirements
? Proactively monitor the key delivery problems or changes
? Manages SLA adds/changes/ decommissions
? Managing annual continuous improvement process
? Monitoring and tracking application solution delivery execution (quality, cost, milestones) for infrastructure programs in the region across all towers
? Monitoring compliance and utilization of project management process
? Produces standard reports and scorecards relating to Service Provider performance on a regular basis or ad hoc as required
? Monitoring Service Provider performance for compliance with organizational needs and requirements, including highlighting the need for follow-up checks for corrective action
? Monitoring and analyzing trends and providing recommendations based on analysis of trending information
? Managing Supplier performance audits (financial, regulatory, technical, security, risk, etc.)
? Review test cases and execution

Job Description:

SUMMARY: Provides SAP business analysis, support and training by performing the following duties. We are looking for an individual who will complement our effort in the full life cycle implementation of SAP. Duties will consist of providing configuration expertise, business process analysis, data migration requirements, and Conference Room Pilot training. The environment for the implementation, post implementation support, and future acquisition implementations will be one of a team oriented, multi-discipline, full life cycle support environment.
-- Responsible for SAP project support including analysis/requirements and project scope documents, business process design and configuration.
--Candidate will be responsible for project support including analysis/requirements and project scope documents, business process design, and SAP configuration.
--Define and implement work procedures, standards, and methods.
--Work with other Business Analysts to develop best practices around the integration and deployment of SAP applications within existing architectures in a multi-site environment.
--Report and interface functional specifications, training development and execution.
--Support business processes as they relate to the SAP FICO module. Additionally, the candidate will need to scale outside of traditional FICO disciplines as required.
--Develop training scripts and business process procedures. Work directly with users in training capacities.

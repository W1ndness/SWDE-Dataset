Job Title: Software Developer Profession: Computer Engineering and Information Technology -> Software Design/Development Job Title: Software DeveloperState: MassachusettsCity: LexingtonDescription: The Weather Sensing Group develops sensors, automated forecasting systems, and decision-support tools to reduce the impact of adverse weather on commercial aviation. To accomplish this, the group combines MIT Lincoln Laboratory expertise in innovative signal, image, and sensor data-fusion processing with physical insights furnished by staff meteorologists. Key accomplishments have included the development of the FAA's Terminal Doppler Weather Radar, ASR-9 Weather Systems Processor, Integrated Terminal Weather Systems, and Corridor Integrated Weather System.Software developer with strong mathematical and algorithm development skills needed to develop and implement algorithms for operational prototype systems providing decision support to air traffic managers. Candidate will support infrastructure and research applications software development on a Linux cluster for weather and aviation-oriented applications. Analysis of software development to support scientific improvements will also be needed. Software development environment includes software design and code reviews, use of software standards, and real-time testing of algorithm performance. Successful candidate must be able to understand, implement, validate and modify algorithms and communicate clearly with team members and collaborators from other organizations. Creativity, flexibility and ability to work independently as part of a small development team are critical.Requirements:MS in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Math or Geophysical Science. In lieu of an MS degree, a BS degree with 3 years of directly related experience will be considered. Strong C++ and MATLAB experience in development of scientific or mathematical software is required. Algorithm experience in AI techniques, pattern recognition, statistical optimization or other is strongly desired. Software experience in C, Fortran, and scripting (csh, perl, jython) is also desirable.

Job description
As a Consultant within the Professional Services team your primary responsibility will be to assist IBM's high profile customers to achieve successful implementations of the SPSS/IBM Data Collection and other Predictive Analytics Solutions.

The Consultant will be proficient in delivering consulting solutions based on SPSS products. These solutions will primarily be based on our Data Collection products and, potentially, Statistics and Predictive Analytics products and applications).

This individual will be responsible for establishing a deep expertise in our products as well as maintaining professional disciplinary expertise in survey industry advances and leading practices. In addition to delivering services, the role will facilitate the sales process by estimating effort, writing proposals and identifying opportunities for cross-sell and add-on sales.

The position requires a background in survey research services and software with a track record of successful implementations and a strong commercial acumen. In addition, the ability to define solutions and ensure their successful implementation is an advantage, as is IT architecture expertise.

Responsibilities:

. Deliver high-quality consulting services in line with the SPSS Implementation Methodology (pr3, CRISP-DM, or similar
. Help build ROI and identify cross sell and up sell opportunities
. Assist in the determination of customer needs
. Assist in the development of work effort estimates, project plans and statements of work, as and when required
. Perform consulting tasks to fulfill engagements at customer site or remote
. Assist and guide external consultants with the completion of consulting projects
. Maintain currency with product development
. Attend internal and customer meetings
. Help train and mentor more junior consultants

Knowledge and Skills Qualifications:

Required

. A strong background of relevant work experience in Survey Research technology or equivalent experience containing a significant development component
. Or, 3 plus years working in a similar consulting role and customer facing environment with either a survey research or related software firm's consulting organization, or an independent consulting services provider
. Experience with projects that involve integrating multiple technologies and datasets to solve a business problem
. Readiness to travel up to 50%

Preferred

. Master's or Bachelor's Degree
. Microsoft technology skills (Windows Server, IIS, SQL Server, etc)
. Software application development utilizing accepted coding conventions
. Languages such as VBScript, VB.Net, C#, .Net 2.0
. Database design and development utilizing SQL Server and SQL
. Web development including tools such as JavaScript, HTML, DHTML, VBScript, .ASP and ASP.NET, XML, Flash, AJAX
. Experience working with IBM SPSS Data Collection products, including scripting and system management, a major plus
. Skill with one or more of the following:
o A BI Reporting tool
o A multidimensional database
o Infrastructure, Networking, & Security
o Operating Systems and Frameworks (e.g. Unix, Linux, .NET, J2EE, Application Servers)
o Data and Business Intelligence (e.g. Data Architecture, BI and ETL tools)

. Good problem solving skills with strong business acumen
. Ability to work as part of a project team
. Good presentation, organizational, and planning skills; including comfort and experience using Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
. Strong sense of personal responsibility and willingness to take ownership for delivering expected results

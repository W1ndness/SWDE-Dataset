Reference Number:

Job Description:
CITADEL is looking for Sr Project Managers & Program Managers with following skills. These are long term contract to hire opportunities located in NY City.
Description:
Apply the principles, techniques and best practices in the planning, organizing and implementation of high-profile, complex projects. Prepare and manage project documentation, deliverables, milestones, resource management, and schedules.
Partner with IT to promote open lines of communication and adherence to SDLC standards and processes.
Recommend continuous improvement of the overall project management process.
Identify business objectives and participate in strategic planning to map long-term goals.
Manage multiple cross-functional projects with the goal of obtaining synergy between projects.

� Serve as Project Manager for the implementation of system projects due to new, enhanced or integrated functionality, as well as the introduction of new product offerings.
� Proven experience in managing IT and corporate projects.
� Initiate, plan, execute, control and close project activities:
� Define and verify scope.
� Develop project plans and timetables.
� Create roles and responsibilities matrix.
� Verify schedules with resources.
� Develop work plans for each subproject/department and prepare summary for management review.
� Execute and maintain project plans.
� Review, track, monitor and report on project status.
� Identify, track, and/or resolve issues by proposing viable solutions.
� Calculate, mitigate and monitor risk, creating contingency plan and advising management, as appropriate.
� Adhere to the change management processes and document all changes.
� Monitor quality assurance and user acceptance testing stages.
� Document post-mortem learned lessons.
� Communicate both formal and informal project updates with all stakeholders as appropriate:
� Communicate to all levels and all divisions of the organization.
� Identify audience and agenda for status presentations.
� Schedule recurring meetings, as necessary.
� Create and maintain project documentation.
� Lead routine forums and invite staff participation in process improvement.
� Provide satisfaction to customers and strive to continuously improve service.
� Establish and maintain working relationship with all departments, stakeholders and external customers involved with project.
� Participate in a procurement process, if business decision is made to utilize third-party resources.
� Participate in business requirement review sessions.
� Perform defect management:
� Verify priority ranking with users.
� Communicate with IT to mutually agree on timeline.
� Decide where defect resolution fits on release roadmap.
� Mentor a team of project managers, if necessary.
� Perform other responsibilities and duties deemed necessary (i.e. analysis)

5+ Years

� Demonstrated proficiency in Project Management methodologies, including SDLC
� Excellent written and oral communications skills
� Strong teamwork and interpersonal skills� Effective presentation skills
� Meeting facilitation experience� Leadership experience
� Proven organizational and time management skills
� Impeccable work ethic
� Interest in process improvement challenges.

� 3 years of experience in Managed Care or Healthcare environment
� Demonstrated strategic planning with abilities in relationship management
� Experience on a project team that successfully implemented new technologies, new work processes or process improvements
� Working toward PMP Certification (if not already attained)

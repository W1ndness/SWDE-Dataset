Job Description:
Our direct client located in Fort Lee, NJ has a long term (12 months+) contract position open for a Mobile Application Development Lead. If you are interested, please send a copy of your resume (preferably in Word format to mpotenza@techlink.com and/or call (201) 786-2413. Please be sure to include rate and contact info.

Client is looking for a Mobile Application Development Lead to help provide vision and leadership to fulfill their goal of dominating the financial presence in the mobile market space. Client is looking for someone who can work in a small, rapidly growing development group leveraging SoA and distributed technologies and able to adapt quickly to a rapidly changing mobile experience. The Mobile Application Development Lead, largely addressing the dominated smart phone space, must be familiar with different mobile technologies and development methodologies and be dynamic enough to address limited documentation and disparate problems in varying mobile technologies.

Client is looking to dominate the mobile marketplace and introduce a new level of financial functionality not seen before in the mobile arena.

We are looking for smart people who are:

? Problem solvers ? thinkers, learners, doers?those not afraid of problems or people, who can research and solve technology challenges and deliver exciting functionality under driven timelines.
? Full-cycle competent ? someone who knows their value in the product lifecycle and believes in adding value to all phases from product vision to deployment strategy.
? Agile proponents ? believe in short release, consumable release cycles and the vision and concept of the team.

Desired Experience: Obviously not all are required, but the more the better)

? Well versed in the history of mobile technologies from WML, XHTML to robust, dynamic thick clients
? More importantly the candidate must be comfortable communicating complex solutions and be able to speak to their audience
? The candidate must be able to set the standard for communications of release candidates, feature development and delivery as well as defect identify, resolution and delivery
? Must have experience decoupling the concerns of presentation from the technologies of the mobile arena (read MVC and CMS)
? 5-7 years of solid OOA/D development experience (C++, Java, C#/.NET, Perl, PHP, Go)
? 3-5 years of experience developing applications for mobile devices, operating systems, applications, security, mobile networks, including hands-on experience with either iPhone, Android, Blackberry SDK development including experience with the platform's App Store. BREW and Symbian OS an added benefits.
? Technical proficiency with Java, Objective-C, HTML5, JavaScript, CSS3, Windows Mobile 6+, web services and other web-based development tools and programs.
? Must understand how a user agent can dictate the mobile web experience.
? Must have experience with technologies such as WURFL and Windows Mobile Toolkit
? Must have experience developing towards application store fronts (Apple, Blackberry, Android, Nokia, BREW)
? Must have experience with push notifications to handsets for hot updates of applications either from storefronts or deployment sites.
? Must understand the nuances of mobile functionality (signal strength, retry, double handshake operations)
? Must understand the capabilities of the mobile experience across varying radio technologies (CDMA and TDMA) as well as the strengths of each
? Experience with Mobile Marketing Association and opt-in flows on various carries for PSMS campaigns.
? Experience with SMSC, MMSC aggregators and integrating with SMS aggregators
? Experience with platform directed SMS
? Experience working in an agile, test-driven development environment
? Must have a solid understanding of
? Experience working directly with product management teams at the earliest stages to participate in the development of ideas and concepts ? you must be able to generate ideas, not simply execute them.
? B.S. in computer science preferred

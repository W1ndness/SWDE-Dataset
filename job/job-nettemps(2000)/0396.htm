Job Description:
Looking for a Clinical Content Developer The primary responsibility to elicit, analyze and validate the real needs of the end users as it pertains to clinical content in the Healthcare IT products that Olympus offers in the marketplace.

PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
* Responsible for all facets related to defining requirements for the portion of the Medical IT application that allows clinicians to document the clinical procedure(s) they perform.
* Responsible for the design and architecture of the clinical content and ensures that design is done keeping in mind maintainability, scalability, extensibility, etc.
* Obtains clinical content from subject matter experts and mines the training database to determine which customizations should be made available to the installed base as an out-of-the-box offering.
* Uses the appropriate tool designs and implements clinical content for product releases. Requires creation of SQL queries and application of software specific maintenance tools.
* Performs peer reviews of the clinical content including content developed by others. Leads or supports clinical validation of newly created clinical content.
* Helps create necessary documentation that is necessary to release clinical content including requirements, release notes and/or user guides.
* Participates in demonstrations to internal and external customers
* Supports QA relative to verification and validation of clinical content inclusive of reviewing and providing recommendations on approaches to testing.
* Manages changes through effective application of change control processes and tools. Continuously monitors the defect tracking system for enhancement ARs. Disposes of them in a timely fashion.
* Assists with the development of the organization*s requirements engineering policies, procedures, and tools.
* Proposes new product features and updates. Implements ways to reuse requirements and clinical content across products.
* Works with product and program management in release planning.
* Performs other duties as may be assigned from time to time by management.


MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS
?X Bachelor*s degree in engineering, computer science, nursing or equivalent education highly preferred. Candidates without a Bachelor*s Degree must have at least seven (7) years of relevant experience.
?X 3+ years experience in Healthcare, Medical Device or Medical IT industry is required. Candidates with an equivalent amount of experience in computational linquistics or related disciplines may also be considered.
?X One or more of the following core skill sets is required:
o Excellent knowledge of medical procedures and knowledge of human anatomy. Strength in GI and/or Pulmonary domains is preferred.
o Excellent linguistic skills, including strength in Syntax. Ideally possesses a Computational Linguistics educational background with a Natural Language Generation subspecialty. Preferably, having previous exposure to natural language generation software.
o Excellent knowledge of logical and/or physical data modeling including skills in SQL. A background in health informatics is preferred.
?X Excellent interviewing skills, to talk with individuals and groups about their needs and ask the right questions to surface essential requirements information
?X Analytical skills, to critically evaluate the information gathered from multiple sources, reconcile conflicts, decompose high-level information into details, abstract up from low-level information to a more general understanding, distinguish presented user requests from the underlying true needs, and distinguish solution ideas from requirements.
?X Facilitation skills, to lead requirements elicitation workshops.
?X Observational skills, to validate data obtained via other techniques and expose new areas of customer unmet needs.
?X Writing skills, to communicate information effectively to customers, marketing, managers, and technical staff
?X Organizational skills, to work with the vast array of information gathered during elicitation and analysis and to cope with rapidly changing information.
?X Interpersonal skills, to help negotiate priorities and to resolve conflicts among project stakeholders (such as customers, product management, and engineering)
?X Places great value on continuous learning.

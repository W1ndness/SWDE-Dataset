Job Title: Verbal Content Curriculum Developer Profession: Education/Training/Information -> K12 Curriculum Coordination/Development The Company Revolution Prep (www.revolutionprep.com) was founded in 2002 with the vision of using innovative instruction and technology to transform education while remaining true to a social mission. By attracting and retaining a group of dedicated, talented and performance-minded individuals, Revolution Prep has become a national leader in educational services and software. This collaborative, fun and entrepreneurial company, is privately held and has achieved profitable, fast-paced growth. Over the next five years, Revolution Prep intends to continue on its path of growth by expanding into new markets and product lines while unlocking our students potential through smarter learning. The Opportunity The Verbal Content Curriculum Developer will be responsible for creation and review of English language arts content under the guidance of the K12 Curriculum Director. S/he will work with the Curriculum team to develop sound instructional tools including lessons and test items for use with our unique online platform. This position will be responsible for the accuracy of content in K12 materials and will work with in-house and freelancers to build materials as needed. The Candidate This ideal candidate is knowledgeable of academic content standards, aligning materials to standards, comparing content standards from different sources, and fundamentals of curriculum design and implementation. To be considered, candidates must meet the following requirements: Bachelor's degree required (formal degree in English or Literature not required)Facility with English-language arts content through high school levelPreferred experience in K12 that reflects a knowledge of age-appropriateness for presentation and level of materials Ability to work comfortably in word processing, spreadsheets, and on-line editing environmentsStrong written and verbal communication skillsGreat attention to detailAbility to balance multiple priorities, problem solve, plan and manage multiple projects on deadline, and work as part of a team Compensation: Salary with performance-based bonusBenefits: Medical, Dental, and Vision Insurance, employee options plan, 401k with company matching, generous paid time-off, life insurance, casual dress, yearly company retreat, vibrant company culture, employee discount for test prep and tutoring services, and strong social mission Desired Attributes Profession: Education/Training/Information -> K12 Curriculum Coordination/Development Work Environment/Setting: Corporation Curriculum Development Tasks: Conduct needs assessments Curriculum Development Tasks: Determine skill levels Curriculum Development Tasks: Liaise with publishers/vendors Curriculum Development Tasks: Create/update policies and standards addressing curricula content/guidelines Curriculum Development Tasks: Develop/implement methods to measure effectiveness of curricula Curriculum Development Tasks: Recruit/train/supervise curriculum consultants and staff Curriculum Development Tasks: Prepare grant proposals and budgets Subject Areas: English

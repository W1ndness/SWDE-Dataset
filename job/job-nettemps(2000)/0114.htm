Reference Number:
PRS1846-BSA

Job Description:
This is for the Bond BPC (Business Project Center). This person will gather requirements from both business and IT sides. Strong communication and presentation skills. The person will interact with internal customers.

Individual provides operational and business support for Bond & FP Management Liability Systems. With direct supervision, this individual participates primarily in information gathering and/or information analysis and documentation.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities: (Accountability)

-Analyze business functions on daily basis to ensure system functionality
-Interview subject matter experts to elicit business need and document results
-Develop, validate and maintain business processes
-Capture, organize, validate and maintain detailed descriptions of business processes, including roles,process steps, supporting systems, opportunities, decisions and risks according to established standards.
-Participate and at times lead facilitated sessions
-Serve as a lead for requirements on small to mid-size enhancement projects and initiatives

Experience Level:
2 Years

Requirements:
Business Domain Knowledge
-Good knowledge of insurance industry and workflow
-Ability to quickly gather information and learn enough background to participate on project team

Planning / Project Management
-Work with management to plan time; coordinate activities with others to accommodate deadlines
-Organize work to ensure that it is carried out efficiently and effectively

Mentoring / Leadership
-Ability to lead a small enhancement project

Communication
-Good written and oral communication skills
-Ability to interview subject matter experts, both individually and in groups
-Ability to present information to technical and non-technical audiences
-Ability to facilitate meetings both in person and via teleconference

Problem Solving / Analytical/Decision Making
-Developing the skill to recognize and analyze business need
-Extremely detail oriented and willing to re-work deliverables for accuracy and to meet standards
-Works under minimal supervision, though typically works as part of a team
-Escalates issues for discussion when appropriate
-Takes responsibility for decision and action

Technology
-Solid understanding of MS Office toolset (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio, Project)
-Familiarity with various Requirements management approaches and techniques

Education / Work Experience:
Minimum of 1-3 years Business Analyst experience
PMP/CBAP a Plus

Hartford CT

6 - 9 months

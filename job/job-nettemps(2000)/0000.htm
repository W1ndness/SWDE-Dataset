Job Description:
Department Overview
Client Facing Technology team in New York City intends to hire an exceptionally talented UI developer for the sales analytics BI/reporting team. This role requires a combination of Flex and Java development and can expect over time to work on a variety of technologies including sharePoint 2010, Javascript and AJAX, Office Add-Ins, SilverLight, WPF and potentially SQL Server-based database development including Analysis Services
Sales reporting portal is a web-based analytics tool delivering critical data and analytics to salespeople, traders, sales leadership, and supporting Chief Operating Officer teams. We support a large group of business users who care greatly about the application and user interface. Candidate should have a collaborative teamwork approach, strong work ethic and communication skills, and should expect to work directly with business users in sales, trading, and finance.
The current development team is split between London and New York with a 24x6 second line support team based in Singapore. The candidate should be prepare for global travel, primarily to London.



Main Duties

? Assist with the design and development of sales reporting portals and other web based sales analytics applications for Equities using Flex, Java and J2EE frameworks

? Handle the database development required for the sales portals
? Drive full project life cycle until delivery and provide 3rd level of support for the application there after
? Interface with other IT applications to collect and analyze requirements and deliver solutions
? Deliver projects in fast-paced environment and work with different technology and business groups to produce quality and timely solutions.
? Provide level 3 support for the sales applications
? Continually stay aware of market trends to identify and apply new innovations for commercial results

Person Requirements

Requirements
Must have:
? Excellent knowledge and experience in Flex and Java
? Java Script, DHTML, AJAX
? Three or more of the following skills : SharePoint, BI dashboards with any product (e.g. Cognos, Business Objects), Office Add-Ins, SilverLight, WPF, WinForms, ASP.NET, Flex, ExtJS
? The ability to be ?technology ambidextrous? ? quickly being productive in other similar languages/environments
? User interface design & implementation experience
? SQL experience
? Experience with agile development
? Collaborative team-work approach, good work ethics
? Good analytical skills and creative thinking skills, quick learner
? Energetic and motivated, willing to take on challenging tasks, work independently and take on time pressure
? Communication and organization skills

Nice to have:
? Spring and other framework knowledge
? Ant
? OLAP understanding
? Experience working in capital markets

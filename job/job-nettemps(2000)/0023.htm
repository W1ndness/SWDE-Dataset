Job Description:
SOFTWARE ARCHITECT


We are an exciting NYC based mobile start-up company looking for an ambitious, creative technologist with proven real-world experience to join our core team.

We'd like to meet you if:

- You are an exceptional problem solver.
- You keep the big picture in mind and use it to influence your decision making.
- You can communicate concepts and ideas clearly. (verbally and written)
- You know where using a particular design pattern makes sense before writing any code.
- You can manage a large continuously integrated codebase.
- You are someone who gets it done.
- You work well under pressure.
- You are excited about working in a start up environment.



Your Role:

- Work directly with our lead architect and management team.
- Work on the architecture and development of our web and mobile products.
- Provide insight, ideas, and direction on a regular basis to help the product and company grow.
- Collaborate with existing development teams.
- Enhance, maintain, and optimize existing code base.



Your Experience Includes / Requirements:

- 7+ years of software development
- PHP5/MYSQL/NOSQL
- Realtime systems
- High availability application design
- oAuth 2.0 implementation (consumer and provider)
- Scheduling / recurrence calculation
- Suggestion / reccomendation algorithms
- Cross-time zone conversions
- Geospatial searches (geohash proximity and radial)
- Relational database design and architecture
- Map / Reduce
- AGILE / SCRUM



Experience with the following technologies:

- Social API's (facebook, twitter, last.fm)
- Propel, or other ORM layer
- MemcacheDB, Hypertable or other NOSQL DB
- Sphinx or other fulltext search engine


Additional Skills/Experience:

- Music API's (echonest, rdio)
- Mobile content delivery (downloads, streaming, in-app purchasing, etc..)
- Mobile platforms (iOS, Android, Blackberry)
- APNS or other PUSH messaging protocol
- XML/XSLT
- XMPP or other realtime protocols
- Symfony or other established PHP Framework
- Multi-lingual (human or computer)
- Music lover
- Rock star


This is a challenging and exciting project in an interesting space.
If any (or all) of the aforementioned items have piqued your interest, please introduce yourself!

In your email, please include your CV and links to examples of your best work.

Job Title: Sr. Associate, IT - Global Security Profession: Computer Engineering and Information Technology -> IT Security Sr. Associate, IT - Global SecurityRequisition ID ---- --5925Location ---- --Boston, MADescription ---- --SENIOR ASSOCIATEGLOBAL SECURITY OFFICE, SECURITY ARCHITECTUREWe are Sapient. Since 1990, we have been partnering with some of the world's most recognized brands and helping them transform their businesses and operations to succeed and thrive in a fast-changing, increasingly digital world.We operate three divisions: SapientNitro, Sapient Global Markets, and Sapient Government Services, each with extensive capabilities and expertise to address the particular industries they serve.But what we all have in common, what each of our 7,000+ team members across the globe share, is an all encompassing drive to do whatever it takes. To overcome any obstacle, to exceed expectations, and create fanatical advocates - within our clients and ourselves. In fact, we're proud to claim that our unique culture and values are part of case studies being used by MBA programs at Harvard and Yale.Sapient is looking for individuals who want to make a difference. Our environment fosters growth and creativity, demands openness and client-focused delivery, and celebrates initiative and risk-taking.If this speaks to you, then we should talk.JOB SUMMARYThe Senior Associate will play a key role in ensuring that security, privacy and compliance concerns within both internal and external initiatives are properly addressed. The Senior Associate will regularly and upon demand meet with project sponsors and stakeholders, to assess security, privacy and compliance concerns, document findings, and provide implementation guidance and oversight to ensure the proffered mitigations are successfully implemented to reduce or eliminate risks.The Senior Associate must demonstrate solid security architecture skills within networking technologies, system / server technologies, domain level technologies and understand proper application security architecture.The Senior Associate will leverage his/her hands on experience with information security architecture to guide project teams in the deployment of secure solutions for our clients to ensure adherence to all related security, privacy and compliance requirements including regulatory and statutory requirements based upon the geography of the implementation, contractual requirements, and policy requirements.The Senior Associate will provide solid hands on experience and understanding of authentication and authorization within applications, logging, encryption, firewalls, VPN, SSL, PKI, OTP tokens, and related products and technologies.The Senior Associate will have excellent documentation skills, communications skills (both oral and written), time management, listening, and team skills.RESPONSIBILITIES- Understanding of EU Data Protection Directive, MA 201 CMR 17, PCI DSS requirements, information security best practices, ISO27001, and how to architect a project solution securely taking into account networking technologies, system/server/endpoints, and applications.- Liaise with business units, internal teams, and client teams to understand business requirements, and design reasonable security solutions around the project at hand in order to support business requirements and goals while at the same time achieving the needed security goals.- Work with fellow security staff to help coordinate responses to security incidents.- Must be familiar with security risk assessment methodology (ISO 27005 a plus) and have experience in performing security risk assessments.- Should be familiar with various Firewall, VPN, Intrusion Detection System technologies and PDA encryption solutions as well as security testing tools such as Nessus, and enterprise level information technology.- Strong oral communications and writing skills are a must.- Must be a self starter with strong organizational skills to enable navigation of the company to identify sponsors, stakeholders and interested parties.- Will be responsible for conducting compliance audits and tracking compliance over time per project initiative.- Should be familiar with business continuity planning and disaster recovery.- The ability to perform computer programming is not necessary, but the candidate should be able to read code such as BASH, ASP.NET, SQL, HTML, Java Script, etc--- and understand its basic functions in order to identify and respond to security risks / vulnerabilities.MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS- Bachelor's degree within a science or related discipline.- Solid understanding of IT security technologies, and particularly security technologies.- 2-4 years direct experience developing and implementing documented information security policies and procedures within a global organization.- 3+ years working in an IT related field.- 2+ years in an information security related field.- Excellent oral and written communications skills and listening skills.- A ---can do' attitude team player who works well under pressure and with dispersed groups, worldwide.- CISSP and best of breed product certifications (Cisco, Juniper, Microsoft, etc) are a plus.

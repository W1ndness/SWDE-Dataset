Job Description:
The First-level Phone Support with AVAYA Consultant will provide first level support to our client's users, ensuring excellent customer service and proper use of system tools for maximum benefit.

Responsibilities:

Work independently, performing all the duties of a Solutions Center IS/Operations Specialist (Combination of First Level Support and Computer Operator).
Deliver high quality customer service and technical support to our US and Global internal clients.
Monitoring systems, performing backups and restores of files and file systems on and Windows operating systems.
Assist with technical issues and answer queries surrounding computer hardware, software, network, and telecommunications systems that originate by telephone, voice mail, or e-mail.
Record, maintain and update records in the Incident Management system.
Performs analysis of problems and assist with corrective action to restore functionality.
Work with technical or development staff to resolve recurring problems and issues with applications and/or products.
Document and submit problem resolutions to the NOC knowledge database in order to assist other support team members with problem resolutions.
Strong written and verbal communication skills are required.
Ability to multi-task several mission critical tasks with little or no supervision.
Follow documented processes.
Escalate issues as appropriate.
Candidates must be willing to work at 7am to 4pm, 8am to 5pm and 9am to 6pm shift as the shifts will change over time.

5+ Years

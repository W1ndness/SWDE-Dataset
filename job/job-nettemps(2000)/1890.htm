The Senior Technical Analyst leads the Analysis, Design, Development and Support of the Agile PLM application. This role also acts as the primary interface between Functional Analysts, Developers and Testers to enable implementation and/or extension of existing applications. The incumbent will act as an advocate of Product Lifecycle Management best practices, methods, and processes, and successfully lead large, complex projects with little or no supervision.- Provide technical solutions to PLM business requirements primarily using Agile PLM.- Propose and lead appropriate business process improvements/changes that reflect a broad understanding of the supply chain and Product Data Management (PDM) context, objectives and constraints.- Define and execute PLM application portfolio roadmap in collaboration with business stakeholders to migrate legacy PLM applications to Agile or other go-forward applications.- Design and implement application configurations to meet business process and application requirements. Review and approve technical specifications and conduct code walkthroughs- Coordinate client resources during testing phase to assure that developed solution satisfies client requirements including but not limited to developing test scripts, setting up test data, and assisting with activities relating to unit, performance (load, stress), integration, conversion and cutover testing, particularly packaged application configurations.- Act as key liaison between vendor (Oracle) and platform teams related to third-party services. Participate in vendor user groups to comprehend vendor roadmaps.-- Influence vendor to address ON Semiconductor's business needs.- Provide technical (L2/L3) support for applications. Supports the users and functional analysts in troubleshooting both application and business process issues.- Act as lead for managing Post Go-Live Support issues. Assist in creating deployment plan (Pre Go Live, Go Live, and Post Go Live).--Position Requirements- Candidate must possess at least Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science/Computer Engineering/Information Technology/Industrial Engineering or related discipline. Advanced degree preferred.- Candidate must be authorized to work in the U.S. and meet ITAR certification requirements.- Strong analysis, design and project management skills and ability to plan and execute projects independently.- Candidate must have a minimum of 2 -5 years direct experience on Agile 9.X versions (9.3 preferred) implementing Agile PC (Item and BOM preferably for Hi-Tech industries) and preferably Agile PPM solutions through the entire lifecycle from requirement gathering to implementation and sustaining support.- Must be able to succeed in fast paced design and manufacturing high tech organization using influential leadership. Team player with strong English written and verbal communications skills and ability to work with a diverse client group.- The successful candidate should have strong analytical and troubleshooting skills, be positive with a can-do approach, independent (ability to take on and manage projects through implementation with little or no supervision) and an excellent communicator.-- Additionally the successful candidate should be a good team player and able to work with team members located in different countries/ geographies in a fast paced global environment.- Strong acumen in BOM data structuring, methodologies and validation with PDM/PLM (Agile), ERP (Oracle EBS), and APS (I2) IT solutions. Advanced level of competency with BOM generation & structure, part number generation and description nomenclature, etc.- Some familiarity with Java and Oracle is necessary to create/analyze/design application specific Procedures, API, Packages, Functions, Materialized Views, Triggers and other database objects, etc.- Experience with Mainframe environment is an added advantage.

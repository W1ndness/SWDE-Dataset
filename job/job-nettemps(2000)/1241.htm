Can You See Yourself in the New Kodak?

Every day you make a choice about where you work - which company deserves your talent, your best thinking, and your passion. Work within a small fast paced team to develop and maintain web based enterprise software. The software manages the creation and storage of commercial artwork from conception to production. Good communication skills and the ability to work as a member of a team are critical to this position.

* 2 years experience developing database driven applications with Oracle 9 or higher.

* 2 years experience with code repository management tools such as Subversion (SVN)

* 2 year experience developing Web 2.0 User Interfaces with AJAX, Javascript and JQuery

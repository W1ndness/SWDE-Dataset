Sykes Enterprises, Incorporated is looking for an AIX/Linux System Administrator to fill a long term contract opening we have. This is a remote position.

This position is remote supporting the Sykes Global Network. This position is the organization?s expert in complex technical matters pertaining to the implementation and daily operations of systems monitoring and management that enable the Sykes Network Operations Center to manage and maintain the Sykes Global Network and Enterprise Systems.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Adherence to SYKES policies on ethics and integrity
- Monitor, Configure and Test enterprise management systems and applications.
- Perform Unix System Archival Duties utilizing Omniback Software.
- Test and Implement new tools enabling centralize management and administration.
- Oversee technical activities associated enterprise-based management systems and applications implementations to include standing membership on the Sykes Change Implementation Management Committee.

- Provide input for standards, best practices, and training materials for the use of enterprise management systems.
- Provide Training; conduct inspections to ensure compliance with Sykes policies and procedures governing the use of enterprise management systems.
- Provide technical specifications for procurement of systems, equipment, and services for enterprise management.

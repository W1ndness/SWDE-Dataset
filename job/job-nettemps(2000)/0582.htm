Job Description:
Our client, a leading global financial services company, has approximately 200 million customer accounts and does business in more than 140 countries. They provide consumers, corporations, governments and institutions with financial products and services, including consumer banking and credit, corporate and investment banking, securities brokerage, transaction services, and wealth management.

Job Responsibilities - IT Project Manager

As a critical and central contributor to the management of AIS financial services projects, assume the following responsibilities:
- Manages complex IT projects through the full development lifecycle; POC, kickoff, requirements gathering and review, design, development, unit testing, QA, UAT and implementation.
- Interfaces with business users and technical teams to develop clear and realistic project plans and drives project to completion.
- Clearly understands the business and technical aspects of the project and acts as an integral part of the technical team while being a liaison to business partners.
- Drives communications between business unit leaders and IT. Provides timely updates via project dashboards.
- Tracks issues and risks closely and escalates as necessary.
- Be acutely aware of milestones and project?s critical paths.
- Understands and updates project finances, resource utilization and all related documents.
- May coach more junior staff
- Automates processes and assists in redesigning business processes.
- Should be able to manage projects using different methodologies; Iterative, Waterfall, Agile.
- Identifies systems problems through user acceptance testing and develops solutions
- Maintain deep understanding of application functionality and technical architecture, as well as knowledge of product positioning and Client market.
- Participate in design meetings with developers to scope out implementation of features.
- Update project plan based on business need and scope.
- Work with QA to determine high-level functional test cases; perform manual testing on an as-needed basis.
- Communicate new features to documentation representative; review all documentation.
- Prepare and deliver new features training to Support.
- Handle escalated support incidents on an ongoing basis.
- Maintain working knowledge of other products and teams to identify and define cross product integration opportunities and plans.

- Team-oriented; ability to work effectively with a wide range of people and roles.

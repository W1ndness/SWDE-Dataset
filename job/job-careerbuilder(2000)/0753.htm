Job Description 


GENERAL SUMMARY OF DUTIES:  Accountable for the design and implementation of solutions that meet software functional and non-functional requirements and adhere to established development standards. Conceive and design programs, translating specifications into computer language, testing results to ensure accurate logic, operation, and output, and designing and assisting in the design of file structures. Work with others across the business to understand customer needs and develop high value solutions.
 
SUPERVISION RECEIVED:  Director – Database and Development
 
SUPERVISION EXERCISED:   N/A 
 
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:  Designs, configures, develops, and implements business logic within the PeopleSoft enterprise, and all related applications, including the Imagining system. 
 
Serve as a functional and technical expert for PeopleSoft ERP, Payables (AP), Asset management (AM), General Ledger (GL), Travel and Expenses (EX), Image Now, and all related interfaces such as Ops Technology, Timberline, OBIEE, and Real Page One-Site. 
 
Responsible for production support for end-users and the batch transaction processes.  
 
Responsible for documentation and configuration of application administrative functions supporting security, workflow, pay-cycle, journal and voucher uploads, ftp, Mass Change, and batch processes. 
 
Responsible for documentation relevant to the knowledge base, functional application support, nightly audit, and issue reporting for all PeopleSoft, Image Now, and other related Financial System applications or integration points. 
 
Responsible for change management of maintenance activities such as server recycles, Period Maintenance Packs, upgrades, vendor management, etc. 
 
Responsible for development tasks associated with issue resolution, reports (queries, nVision, trees, crystal, xml publisher, dashboards, etc.), and projects, including coding, testing, validation of unit testing, user acceptance, and implementation.  
 
 


Job Requirements 

 
PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS:   Knowledge of organizational policies and procedures.    Ability to apply policies and procedures to solve everyday issues.  Excellent verbal and written communication skills.  Ability to work in conjunction with Company managers and employees.  Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from customers.  Ability to write routine reports and correspondence.  Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively with customers. 
 
Knowledge of computer systems, application development, communications and networks to include Java, .NET, XML, SQL Server, Oracle.  Skilled in the use of spreadsheet, relational database, and word processing software.  Ability to process computer data and to format and generate reports.  Ability to create, compose, and edit written materials.  Performs duties that require strong comprehension, analytical, research, composition, communication, and organizational skills.
 
Knowledge of the operational and procedural aspects of computer systems, software, and peripheral equipment.  Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.  Ability to exercise initiative, problem-solving and decision-making skills.  Ability to establish priorities and coordinate work activities.  Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.
 
Ability to proactively recognize and implement superior customer service to internal and external customers.  Performs duties that require considerable initiative, independent judgment, and strong communication skills. Ability to exercise initiative, problem-solving and decision-making skills.  Ability to establish priorities and coordinate work activities.  Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables. 
 
TYPICAL PHYSICAL DEMANDS:  Requires mobility sufficient to travel.  Some bending, stooping and stretching.  Occasionally lifting items weighing up to 30 lbs.  Requires eye-hand coordination and manual dexterity sufficient to operate a keyboard, photocopier, telephone, calculator, and other office equipment.  Requires good hearing, eyesight and color perception to evaluate the quality of Web sites and internet content delivery systems.  Extensive data input and required ability to sit for long periods of time.
 
TYPICAL WORKING CONDITIONS:  Normal office environment.  Frequent evening and weekend work.  Available for IT question and problem escalation 24 x 7.
 
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
Bachelor's Degree in computer science, business with IT/MIS major, engineering or equivalent field, or its equivalent experience, required.
 
Minimum of five years experience supporting and maintaining the PeopleSoft Financial modules, specifically, AP, GL, AM, and EX.
 
Minimum of three years’ experience with PeopleTools, PeopleCode, Application Engine, Integration Broker, and Component Interface.
 
Minimum of three years’ experience with nVision, Crystal, PS Query Reporting, and XML Publisher.

Job Description 


www.msbinfo.com
 

Marshall & Swift/Boeckh (MSB) is the leading provider of property valuation and related business information to government agencies, and the real estate, lending, investing, and insurance markets. Utilizing state-of-the-art databases, systems, and proprietary technologies, MSB enables enterprises to access and manage large volumes of mission-critical data.


An available portfolio of high-value analytical services provides clients with core business solutions that transform ordinary data into valuable and actionable business intelligence to provide for improved risk-based decisioning, marketing initiatives, and similar situational analysis.


Premised with the understanding that knowledge is power, MSB software and tools enable businesses to utilize their data, along with MSB provided analytics and automation to arrive at better, more consistent business decisions.


Purpose

The Data Manager - Appraiser Software is responsible for the management and data/product execution throughout the Data Asset life cycle from strategic planning, thru tactical activities to data delivery. Responsible for the ownership of the data quality product to include data values, logic, algorithms, tables, and processes. Responsible for data/product documentation, communication and working closely with business owners and sales to ensure the product supports the company's overall strategy and goals. The Data Manager - Appraiser Software is responsible for managing staff’s workload, quality of work, productivity, and process efficiency. Work in conjunction with both the Data Compilation Manager and Collection Manager to ensure data delivered to the products and end cost result meets acceptable thresholds identified in the Quality Control Plan (QCP). Manage data anomalies and produce variance reports.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities:



  • Ownership and Management of the Retail/Government Suite of Products.
  • Shadow in-house Domain Expert for knowledge transfer.
  • Define the data product strategy and roadmap.
  • Work with Business Unit to strategize for data augmentation and future data requirements.
  • Manage staff workload and data quality.
  • Produce weekly reports of work load, staff resources and efficiency benchmarking.
  • Create and set up processes that will generate reports for ongoing Quality Assurance of product data in alignment with the Data Asset Quality Control Plan (QCP).
  • These reports will show changes, variances, deviations in data and other anomalies.
  • Maintain ongoing validation process of Data Asset product estimates.
  • Manage and maintain all external/internal communications for Products (White Papers, position papers, product bulletins, etc.).
  • Work with Account Managers, Product Managers, and Sales for client support which may include client visits and presentations.
  • Attend Account Manager and Product meetings on a regular basis.
  • Work with Product Managers and Account Managers on data issues, RFIs, client inquiries, external communications as required.
  • Partner with in-house construction Subject Matter Experts (SME) on special research, building code changes.
  • Regular and predictable attendance and punctuality.
  • Act as a leader within the company. 
 


Job Requirements 

Education & Work Experience:


  • Bachelor's degree - Computer Science or Engineering degree preferred. Minimum of five years experience as a Product Manager.
  • Two years in supervisory capacity.
  • Prefer work experience as an Appraiser or in an Appraisal or construction related field.

Supervisory Responsibilities:

This position does have supervisory responsibilities.


Specialized Knowledge and Skills:



  • General: Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority. Demonstrated success defining and launching excellent products. Excellent teamwork skills
  •  Statistical: Good analytical skills with econometric forecasting skills. Ability to look at and review data from both high levels to detailed view.
  • Technical Skills: Proficient in database programs such as SQL, Access and Data warehousing concepts such as Data marts and OLAP cubes. Exceptional spreadsheet skills a must.
  • Office: Excellent written and verbal communication skills. Good clerical and organizational skills. Excellent presentation skills.

Work Environment and Physical Demands:


  • General office environment.
  • No special physical demands required.
  • 10% travel required. 

MSB is an MDA company. Located in Vancouver, British Columbia, with extensive operations in Asia, the United Kingdom and North America, MDA has been providing mission-critical data and knowledge to a variety of international markets for more than 30 in years. MDA subsidiaries in the UK and Canada collect data about property title, real estate transactions, and building information. Additional MDA businesses include space-based information collection systems such as RADARSAT 1, associated satellite ground reception systems, airborne radar surveillance systems, mapping, and other information services.


Other MDA companies include:


MDA Lending Solutions provides advanced information solutions that improve the decision making and operational performance of customers by driving time, cost and risk out of real estate lending transactions.


DataQuick is provider of comprehensive real estate asset information. The market-comparable valuation data and Automated Valuation Model (AVM) products it offers complements the cost approach valuation data and technology provided by Marshall & Swift to the same market of appraisers, assessors and mortgage lenders.

Company Overview 

Based in Wichita, Kan., Koch Industries, Inc. owns a diverse group of companies that have a presence in nearly 60 countries and employ about 70,000 people. Koch Industries has completed more than $32 billion in acquisitions and investments since 2003, growing more than 2,200-fold since 1960, and outperforming the S&P 500 nearly 32 times. It is one of the largest private companies in America according to Forbes magazine. Koch companies supply communities with energy to heat and cool buildings, fibers for superior carpets and garments, chemicals used in manufacturing, process and pollution control equipment, fertilizer, forest and consumer products, financial services and more. Familiar Koch companies' brands include STAINMASTER® carpet, LYCRA® fiber, Quilted Northern® tissue and Dixie® cups and tabletop products. Koch Industries is focused on creating value for its customers, its communities and its employees. Job Description 


Koch Business Solutions, LP is the centralized shared service company that provides services to all Koch companies. Our major product lines include Human Resources Services, Technology Services, and Accounting/Financial Services. Koch Business Solutions' employees share Koch Industries core values of humility, integrity, and respect for others. These values also include a discovery mentality, which is reflected in our employees' initiative and desire to learn. The result is Principled EntrepreneurshipTM, - doing well by doing good. Our customers view us as valued partners in their success.

Koch Business Solutions, LP Enterprise Technology Solutions provides specialized knowledge and expertise in application development and support, project management, infrastructure and customer service to the companies of Koch Industries, Inc. Our information technology professionals work in a challenging environment that is dedicated to improving processes and meeting customers’ needs. If you want to work for a fast paced organization that is extremely collaborative and has several avenues for career growth, the KBS Enterprise Technology Solutions group is for you!

Job Description:
  • Supervise the day-to-day server build activities for the Windows Operations Team
  • Lead/Assist with identifying, researching, and resolving, problems relating to server related issues and process.
  • Accountable for ensuring problems are documented and tracked
  • Accountable for insuring KBS processes, guidelines, and procedures are followed
  • Accountable for insuring the windows provisioning activities are meeting Service Level Agreement requirements
  • Manage and challenge vendors to ensure the best value for Koch and to understand product direction and problem escalation process
  • Provide leadership & be an effective mentor for Windows Analyst by understanding their strengths and position them to be successful in their role
  • Identify needs for training to ensure each team member has the tools they need to meet or exceed expectations
  • Leverage other IT groups and product owners to improve processes and to identify new opportunities
  • Participate in the process of hiring, coaching, mentoring, and separating employees
  • Demonstrate being a catalyst for change to increase value and improve efficiencies within the business
  • Acting as Liaison and Point of contact for our customers
  • Identifies opportunities for improvement and makes constructive suggestion for change
  • Oversight for projects and request for work ensuring projects are delivered on-time, on budget and with quality
  • Understand how to communicate difficult/sensitive information tactfully

Job Description 

2169
A Service Delivery Manager is the main interface between the Customer and the ICT Factory.

The Service Delivery Manager role will be the primary point of contact into the ICT Operations factory and manages the internal delivery network in order to fulfil the Customer contract across the TSS service line.

The Service Delivery Manager will focus on one or more specific business units within the Customer structure. He/She will assist the Senior Service Delivery Manager and/or Head of TSS Operations Management/Service Delivery Management to manage the account as a whole.

The Customer’s contract (SLA) is subdivided into internal contracts (OLA’s) for all internal and external delivery units. This includes the dynamic planning, scheduling, measurement of services and delivery against OLA’s to provide the agreed levels of service, taking account of changes in both supply and demand and seeking continuously and proactively to improve services for the benefit of the Customer. The Service Delivery Manager is responsible for ensuring that the SLA is matched accordingly with OLA’s.

Specific Role Responsibilities:
Working in partnership with our client and the Client Director, the SDM will be responsible for the smooth operational delivery of managed services across the Americas. The principle responsibilities of the role include:

 Owning the operational relationship between internal and client organisations, acting as the prime interface. The SDM will normally be assigned to accounts which match those of the Client Manager.
 Responsible for the quality of the service provided to our clients including management against SLA.
 Responsible for the management of the cost of the service provided to our clients by having a good understanding of the clients’ business and technical environment.
 Responsible for identifying service improvements to the service provided to our clients whilst also managing client commercial expectations and delivery to commitments.
 Driving additional project related revenue through the customer and engaging in up selling services to the customer.
 Production of monthly service report. Will have input and receive information from Service Line Managers.
 Billing data control, including chargeable activities. Managing client’s challenge of billing information.
 Management of Service Variation process and production of proposals.
 Tactical management of operations, including matrix-management of internal teams (Field and Helpdesk).
 Hold regular team meetings and ensure regular communications motivate and communicate with on-site staff via meetings and ad-hoc briefings.
 Ensure adherence to T-Systems procedures

Commercial and Contractual
  • Good understanding of the contract.
  • Responsible from a customer business unit perspective for the cost and quality of solutions delivered from the technical delivery teams including offers, implementation and operations
  • Drives up the quality of solutions (availability and performance) and drives down cost.
  • Understand and underpin the implementation of IT Service Strategies supporting Customer environments.
  • Recommend portfolio consolidation and standardisation, define KPI’s and obtain benchmarks

  • Participate in the gross profitability of the account.
  • Work to mitigate the impact of service failures and improve quality.
  • Responsible for the continued provision of delivered services, to agreed quality.
  • Manage the delivery of service from delivery units and other direct service suppliers.
  • Provide input into bids, due diligence and transition planning for new contracts.
  • Involvement, approval and co-signature in proposal and contract negotiation phase – at the latest from sales stage IV (e.g. technical feasibility, costs per service element).
  • Approval during drafting of contract for delivery structure and calculation of costs.
  • Responsible for checking the suitability of offerings from the Solutions Team. Ensure that solutions are built using T-Systems standards i.e. Standard Offering Portfolio compliant solutions. Ensure that solutions can demonstrate the level of availability required to meet the customer requirement.
  • Plan, forecast, optimize and control all production costs (internal cost allocation and OE costs) on order and customer level.
  • Responsible from a customer business unit perspective for managing the investigation of and checking the suitability of the discontinuation of services.
  • Ensure OLA’s, SLA’s and any third party supplier agreements are in alignment with and capable of delivering services within contracted timeframes.
  • Underpin the Operational Level Agreements within the delivery units where specifically needed (i.e. where the Customer requirements differ from the service level in standard service descriptions).
    Processes

  • Support the Process Owners and ensure process functionality and delivery.

  • Incident Management - Act as a hierarchical escalation point for Incident Managers to communicate updates to the customer.
  • Incident Management - Maintain a real time view of all Incidents affecting the Customer and escalate wherever necessary.
  • Problem Management – Responsible from a customer business unit perspective for the identification and tracking, within the delivery teams, of true problems to completion. Initiating any necessary escalations.
  • Change Management - Review all major changes and projects affecting areas of responsibility, checking the impacts of each change.
  • Configuration Management - Monitor of the CMDB and the quality of information held therein. Driving the Configuration Manager to ensure regular audits are held to keep the data current.
  • Configuration Management - Work with the Configuration Manager, manage the resolution of data inconsistencies within the CMDB.
  • Release Management – work with the ICTO delivery units to ensure that operational patching for the various delivery units are carried out in a timely fashion.
  • Service Level Management - Assist in the definition, preparation and timely distribution of all operational Service Reporting against SLA’s.
  • Service Level Management - Investigate and report all breaches of OLA’s and manage the proposal and implementation of solutions to prevent future occurrences
  • Capacity Management – Ensure that Capacity Management plans are delivered on time and to the correct level of quality. Risks articulated within the plan need to be shared with the customer and an agreed action plan documented.
  • Availability Management – work with the ICTO delivery teams to agree the monthly availability figures for applications/infrastructure within your business unit. Report availability figures with the customer. Identify any issues resulting from customer discussion and put an appropriate action plan in place to manage risk e.g. Service Improvement Plan (SIP).
  • Operational Acceptance – Authorise Operational Acceptance changes as part of project lifecycle– ensure that all underpinning service support & service delivery processes are in place to provide a smooth operational phase of service lifecycle.
    Relationships

  • Responsible for a positive result in the annual TRIM Customer satisfaction survey.
  • Form strong relationships with the Customer building an understanding of Customer requirements and business drivers.
  • Act as a single point of contact to Customer Centres and Senior Management for delivery and high priority issues and incidents.
  • Service Support
  • for information gathering and reporting to the Customer, following an incident escalation including distribution of MIR’s.
  • Support the country/Customer specific elements of international delivery.
  • Assume the Global Service Delivery Manager role when necessary and work with T-Systems International as appropriate
  • Identify, initiate and manage continuous improvement projects.
  • Represent T-Systems in Service Operations Meetings, Service Review Meetings and periodic Quality Reviews.
  • Actively participate in the Service Delivery team meetings.
  • Actively participate in the Change Advisory Board (CAB) meetings.
  • Prepare status reports for Customer.
  • Identify current status of Customer expectations and satisfaction levels.
  • Provide weekly report to Senior Service Delivery Manager (where applicable) on status of business unit.

    General
  • Support the Senior Service Delivery Manager (where applicable) in achieving his/her objectives.
  • Will be required for on-call rota.
  • To maintain a sound knowledge and understanding of current methodologies, technologies and services as employed by the Customer and as promoted by the Company whilst maintaining an awareness of future trends within the market place.
  • Adherence to ITIL Service Management processes.

    Work experience requirements:
  • Extensive and recent experience in a similar position, preferably within an outsourcing environment.

    Technical Requirements:
  • ITIL qualified
  • High level knowledge of a very broad range of network systems and how they inter-relate.

    Additional Skills:
  • Analytical and problem solving attributes.

  • Exhibiting tact and diplomacy.

  • Broad experience of post sales support.

  • Extensive experience of Customer relationships, confidence and presence to deal with Customers.

  • Strong interpersonal, communication and presentation skills.

  • German or Spanish speaking an advantage.

  • Cost control and budget planning.

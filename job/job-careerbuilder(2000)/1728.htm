Job Description 

PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION
Responsible for providing day-to-day support for call centers.  Will perform daily system maintenance and respond to customer requests as needed.  Person will be responsible for directing the design and programming for all routing requests from our business partners.  Will need to have 3-5 years experience working within a Call Center environment and on the job experience with CTI scripting, AT&T Route it and Aspect CCT routing. Will need to be able to program changes to the systems while limiting business risk.  Detailed plans/schedules will need to be developed for all assignments.

PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES
-Design and implement routing changes using AT&T Route it to provide necessary call routing

-Participate in implementation of routing script changes for the call center

-Provide troubleshooting for routing systems as necessary

-Provide day-to-day support for emergent requests

-Provide training and second level support for the team members

-Team with Architect to provide documentation of our current systems using Visio

 

 


Job Requirements 

Minimum:
-5 years experience programming routing changes in a call center

-5+ years on the job experience with call center technologies including Aspect ACD, AT&T route it, CTI scripting

-Ability to work with VP and above personnel

-Must have good interdepartmental communication skills (verbal and written)
-Solid leadership skills

Preferred:
-Engineering or Technical degree
-Team Lead Experience

POSITION SCOPE
Responsible for day-to-day call center operations particularly for designing and implementing routing changes within the environment.

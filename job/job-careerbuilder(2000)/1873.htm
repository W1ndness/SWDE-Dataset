Franchise Information

"Over 20 years of experience and one of the Top Ranked Franchises by Entrepreneur Magazine year after year!"

Paint Scratches, Dents & Dings, we do it all!

If you've always dreamed of owning your own business, Colors on Parade, the "Original Body Shop on Wheels," offers you incredibly affordable opportunities to be part of a successful and proven franchise system.

With more than 250 franchises operating in the U.S., Colors on Parade has long been recognized as the leader in providing onsite repairs to non-collision automotive damage like dents, dings, and scratches to auto dealers, fleet operations, body shops, and individuals. In fact, we have consistently been ranked one of the top franchisors by Entrepreneur magazine.

The Colors on Parade franchise system is unique in that we provide unparalleled training, certification, and support to our franchisees.

 


Qualifications

When you join us, you’ll also enjoy the following advantages:

  • Recession-resistant sector - The automotive repair industry withstands economic fluctuations.
  • Mobile, home-based business model - Which keeps your overhead and operating costs down. 
  • Innovative technology - You’ll provide the highest quality services in the automotive appearance technology field. Our systems keep you on the cutting-edge.
  • Strong growth potential - From individual consumers, to car dealers and body shops, there is a vast customer base and widespread demand for the services you’ll provide.

 

The Business Model that’s Right for You
Colors on Parade is a two-tiered system consisting of area developers and operator franchisees. Therefore, we offer two different ownership opportunities:



  • Operator Franchisee Business Model -
    The operator franchisee is a hands-on, technical business owner, specializing in providing the Colors on Parade services from a mobile unit.
  • Area Developer Business Model -
    An area developer owns a Designated Market Area (DMA) and is responsible for the onsite support of all operator franchisees working within their protected territory. Area developers provide operator franchisees with technical and business support and marketing support to attract new clients. They assist with the development of accounts and are responsible for providing quality assurance and customer service to all accounts within their DMA. Area developers also recruit new operator franchisees within their territory. Prospective area developers should possess strong management and leadership skills, along with an understanding of business planning, financial forecasting, marketing and sales.

 

Cost Structure:

  • Operator franchisees: Approximately $15,000 plus the cost of a new or used cargo van.
  • Area developer franchisees: $45,000-$60,000 which includes your franchise fee, mobile unit van down payment, laptop, software and training. Franchise Fee is $25,000, a Mobile Unit van with equipment is approximately $15,000-$30,000, based on whether an appropriate pre-owned vehicle is available. Plus the cost of your DMA, which is calculated at $5,000 per 100,000 of population.

 

Sought-After, Automotive Repair Services
Colors on Parade franchisees provide high quality repairs that are guaranteed under our lifetime warranty. The three primary services you’ll offer include:

  • Paint repair is our core business. Our system duplicates a car's original paint and finish, so repairs are seamless and invisible. Because we only paint the damaged area, we preserve more of the vehicle's original paint, which keeps costs down and takes less time.
  • PDR (Paint-less Dent Repair)- PDR reshapes metal back to its original condition, without affecting the original paint finish, so the finish remains unharmed and the vehicle keeps its original value.
  • Interior Repair- To restore vinyl, leather, plastic and other surfaces.

 

Superior Training and Support
When you join us you’ll receive:

  • Extensive training that includes a combination of in-field training with your area developer and final certification at our corporate headquarters.
  • Access to our innovative technology.
  • Support and guidance from your area developer and the corporate office.
  • Marketing and advertising support.
  • And more!

 

Honorably discharged military veterans can also take advantage of the special discounts we offer, including a $1,000 discount for unit operators and a $5,000 discount for area developers!

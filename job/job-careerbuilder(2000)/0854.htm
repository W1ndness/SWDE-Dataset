Company Overview 

Founded in 1976, SEKO is a global third party logistics provider with 47 offices in the United States and offices in more than 40 countries worldwide. It offers a full range of supply chain solutions, including transportation services such as domestic and global air and ocean freight forwarding, customs brokerage, ground transportation, trans‐border distribution, U.S. home delivery and DC bypass shipping, on an expedited and time‐definite basis, 24/7. International logistics solutions include warehouse management services (WMS), asset management and distribution, contract logistics and reverse logistics services. Its IT solutions offer a broad range of real‐time Web‐based shipment management and data exchange tools for a high degree of supply chain visibly. For more information, visit www.sekoworldwide.com.

Job Description 

Summary:

The Product Manager is responsible for product planning, development, implementation, support, related project processes and project management. This includes managing the IT software product(s) throughout the product lifecycle, gathering and prioritizing projects, outlining customer requirements, defining the product vision, and working closely with development, to deliver solid IT solutions. It also includes working with sales, marketing and support to recognize opportunities and meet customer requirements. Responsibilities also include ensuring that the product and marketing efforts support the company's overall strategy and goals.

Learning and understanding SEKO’s software products, functional capabilities and underlying technologies, including environmental and application software and integrated vendor products, used frequently in company implementations will be required. 

This position would report to the Director of Product Management.

 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:  This position encompasses a variety of job duties and responsibilities including but not limited to the following:

·        Define the product strategy and roadmap

·        Work with external third parties to assess partnerships and opportunities

·        Be an expert with respect to the competition

·        Develop the core positioning and messaging for the product

·        Perform product demos and training (sales, operations and customers)

·        Assist sales in assessing scope of work for projects, creating a detailed statement of work, and competitive pricing

·        Work with IT & Marketing to develop sales tools and customer/sales support documents

·        Propose an overall budget if necessary for future roadmap

·        Capable of briefing and training the sales force (attendees) at meetings

·        Implement project & customer deployments, which include:

1.       Manage project schedule and action items

2.       Define project requirements

3.       Coordinate IT and Development resources

4.       Ensure timely delivery and implementation of projects

5.       Meeting or exceeding deadlines and deliverables

6.       Communicating with key stakeholders  on project status

7.       Coordinating training of clients/end users, including both on-site and web training if necessary

8.       Coordinating QA testing efforts of new functionality and requirements

9.       Document workflow and standard operating procedures

10.    Participate in and lead client meetings to gather business requirements and to communicate project status and issues

·        Coordinate and maintain multiple environments for prospective customer and implementation testing

1.       Coordination with development resources to ensure timely delivery

2.       Coordination of  IT resources for successful production implementations

3.       Providing or assisting with customer training

·        Ability to exercise judgment and discretion and to set priorities and manage competing demands

·        Point of contact to field operations, internal users and customers for assigned projects

·        Work on multiple, complex IT projects independently

·        Write test scenarios based upon Product specification documents and business user interactions as well as create test scenarios based on understanding of systems in order to Integration testing, new functionality and performance conditions

·        Accountable for the delivery of outcomes, strive to meet and exceed expectations

·        Has a flexible approach to change, is able to work effectively in a variety of situations, constantly seeks improvements and is forward looking

·        Demonstrate a reliable, responsive and positive work ethic with the highest degree of integrity

·        Comply with SEKO’s C-TPAT Security Profile.

·        Expected to uphold SEKO’s Core Values.

Job Description 

JOB SUMMARY:


Manages Technical Support is responsible for the overall post implementation customer experience for all Hill-Rom IT Solutions (HITS) customers and solution sets.
This position is responsible for the development and improvement of people, processes and service offerings that continually reduce the cost to serve while improving both responsiveness and customer satisfaction.

This person is responsible for the team that provides first, second and third level system support for over 900 hospital customers utilizing Hill-Rom IT Solutions (HITS).
This support is provided 7 X 24 to remote end user customers from our Cary, NC solution center in both a queue environment and a periodic evening and weekend on call rotation.
The Manager is responsible for ensuring that the team identifies, troubleshoots, debugs and records customer problems in accordance with quality system requirements.


ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES Other duties may be assigned:



  • Provide leadership and guidance to the Customer Support call center, third parties, field service management, field service personnel, and sales personnel to drive business results and a customer focused culture.

  • Develop, communicate and execute the Technical
    Support strategy

  • Establish and monitor appropriate individual and team customer satisfaction and performance metrics for use in managing the customer support business.

  • Responsible for team providing superior customer service and technical support to our customers.

  • Establishes clear communication and escalation procedures for technical and customer issues with cross-functional teams.

  • Responsible for communicating with Product Development teams to include tools and processes for Technical Support in new product development efforts

  • Lead and partner with other functional areas such as Implementation services, Sales, R&D, Quality, Regulatory, Finance and Business Unit leaders as necessary to improve service quality and improve customer satisfaction.



  • Manage internal and third party staff to provide seamless service experiences to HITS customers.

  • Develop and strengthen on-going relationships of key clients.

  • Establish short-term and long-term plans, policies and standards for direct staff and for indirect staff that execute Customer Support Services tasks.

  • Engages in the new product development process to ensure that the Technical Support staff is properly prepared and ready to support new products and/or releases.

  • Work cross-functionally with a variety of groups to meet goals and objectives of the team as well as to communicate with customers, both internal and external, regarding service requirements, issues, needs and customer satisfaction and performance metrics.

  • Manages customer escalations and ensures proper handling of customer situations.

  • Responsible for proper tracking and accounting of
    Fee for Service customers

  • Continuously improve the operational efficiency, effectiveness and service quality of the Customer Support Services team to drive improved customer satisfaction

  • Manages departmental budget, approving expenses and controlling costs to goal.

  • Recruit, direct, guide, develop and support Customer Support Services technical staff.


  • Responsible for performing this job in accordance with all applicable regulatory and Quality System Standard Operation Procedures and Work Instructions, and the Hill-Rom Standards of Business Conduct.

  • Performs other similar or related duties as necessary.


    MANAGERIAL RESPONSIBILITIES:


    Directly supervises a team of approximately 10-15 technical customer support representatives who are responsible for providing customer satisfaction and expert problem resolution for HITS solution sets, including but not necessarily limited to the Nurse Call, Patient Safety, and Wireless products and service sets.
    This position conducts supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws and is responsible for the overall direction, coordination, and evaluation of the customer support representatives. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, training and developing employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; motivating and managing employee performance; addressing complaints and resolving issues.


     



    QUALIFICATIONS:


    To perform this job successfully an individual must be able to perform each “Essential Duty” satisfactorily.
    The requirements listed are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required.
    Management experience in a technical and regulated environment is required. Knowledge of call center standards, methodologies and industry “best practices” or equivalent is required.
    Business management knowledge is required.


    EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE:



  • Bachelor's degree in a business, management, or technical discipline required (or equivalent combination of education and experience).

  • 7 10 years related experience in customer service for healthcare IT, medical device, or other technology products/services business is required.

  • 2-3 years experience in a supervisor role in a technical support environment desired

  • Knowledge of customer service operations and technical call center best practices and processes.

  • Knowledge of business planning and execution best practices and processes, preferred.

  • Excellent skills in business management such as communication, negotiation, motivation and persuasion.

  • Demonstrated ability to communicate and interact with high-level customers, sales team, internal senior management, and various technical experts.

  • Experience in FDA, Medical Device or other similar environment desired.

  • Working knowledge of network hardware and software technology and ability to understand details of technical issues required.

  • Knowledge of the clinical communication and workflow of acute care environments as it relates to patient safety is a plus.

  • Ability to multitask in an ever changing environment with demonstrable record of handling multiple work assignments simultaneously

  • Superior customer service abilities and experience

  • Strong PC skills using Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Project, Visio, AutoCAD, and Lotus Notes.

  • Ability to travel is required (estimated 25% or less).

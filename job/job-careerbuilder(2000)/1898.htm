Job Description

Asurion is the global leader in technology protection services. From lost, stolen and damaged wireless handsets to malfunctioning computers or HDTVs, Asurion provides more than 90 million consumers worldwide with best-in-class, next day device replacement. Asurion also offers protection of user content and software. As the worlds largest provider of technology protection products, we make replacing wireless devices fast, easy, inexpensive, and hassle-free: Over 90 million wireless customers around the world just like you are protected by Asurion Asurion partners with North America’s top 5 nationwide wireless carriers, many regional providers as well as other worldwide wireless companies to help customers get a replacement phone quickly Asurion has over 5,000 employees dedicated to providing great service

The IT Operations Leader is accountable for the management and support of the production infrastructure environment.  The Operations Leader must be capable of making critical decisions regarding operational issues and is accountable for the satisfaction of Operating Level Agreements with Product/Service Owners and customer satisfaction with business end user communities.  The ideal candidate is a strong people leader with a proven track record of managing in a highly competitive market.  This role  is a manager of managers with several SR Manager roles reporting to it.  Provide management of all preventative and proactive Operational functions and real time responses within the Global Support Center including, but not limited to:

  • Database Administration
  • Backup and Restore Administration
  • Schedule Analysis/Workforce management
  • Workload Management - Perform workload scheduling
  • Security Administration - Execute operational security strategy
  • Storage Administration
  • Systems Administration
  • Application Administration
  • Technical Administration (Tools)

  • Responsible for setting, reviewing, and execution of the operational plan that compares well with industry best practices for comparable environments. 
  • Work closely with product and project managers on the implementation of new projects and infrastructures including operational readiness, change preparation, change execution, and post-change QA validation.
  • Manage senior level notifications during outages.
  • Coordinate with product management on roadmaps, automation, and operational improvement activities across the environment with accountability for continuously raising the bar on operating level agreement targets. 
  • Recruit and maintain strong Operational Team capable of addressing operations exceptions in line with Operating Level Agreements.
  • Set direction for the preparation and delivery of regular management reports on the daily/weekly/monthly operations status
  • Responsible for maintaining a functional and high performing offshore staff
  • Provides and supports a viable BCP strategy, including participation in regular contingency and failover testing.
.



Job Requirements

  •     
    • ITIL Foundations Certified a must
    • ITIL Practitioner Certified a plus
    • 4 year college degree or equivalent plus a minimum of 7-10 years professional experience
    • A minimum of 5 years experience managing IT operations for a large enterprise environment.
    • Excellent written and communication skills
    • Working knowledge of major distributed operating systems and development and management of global infrastructures (network, server, virtual machines)
    • Demonstrated ability to function in a high pressure production support environment and make informed real-time decisions based on business priorities and implications

    About Asurion:
    Asurion is the global leader in technology protection services. From lost, stolen and damaged wireless handsets to malfunctioning computers, gaming systems and more, Asurion, together with sister-company NEW, provides more than 200 million consumers worldwide with best-in-class service, repair, replacement and delivery. The company also provides products which can protect a consumer's wireless content and software. Asurion is the exclusive provider to many of the world's premier telecommunications and retail companies. The company is privately-held with more than 10,000 employees and operates in six countries across three continents. For more information, please visit www.asurion.com.

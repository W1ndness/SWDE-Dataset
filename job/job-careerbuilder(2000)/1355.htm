Printer Regional Specialist "Urbandale Area"
Report It

Job Description 

Pitney Bowes (NYSE: PBI) is the $5.6 billion global technology leader whose products, services and solutions deliver value for customers in the mailstream and beyond.  A member of the S&P 500 since 1957, we have consistently recorded year-over-year revenue growth.  We do business in more than 130 countries and employ over 35,000 mailstream professionals.  

 
The Printer Regional Specialist (PRS) position will be dedicated to High Speed Ink Jet Color Printers. The PRS is a lead technical resource responsible for supporting existing ink jet color printers and any new printers along with pre/post printing products. Product mastery is required and the PRS will be engaged supporting the field and customers. The PRS must be able to master and fluently communicate technical issues with the Service Engineers and the Technical Program Manager. As the PRS he/she will need to lead multiple concurrent efforts including a high degree of competency with Ink Jet Color Printing Hardware/Software/Electrical/Color Knowledge. In most cases the PRS will reside on a site that has the Intellijet Printer, providing problem resolution, training, knowledge transfer, escalations and customer support along with identifying business growth opportunities.
 
The PRS is responsible for providing timely and accurate status updates to Service Engineers and Technical Manager and project stakeholders. This role may require up to 25-50% travel along with working in a 24x7 environment. In most cases the PRS will be dedicated at a customer site but not excluding the PRS's ability to travel to other sites supporting a Printer or Pre/Post equipment/Software for installation, training, service and escalations.
 
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • This is a key exempt position reporting to the Technical Program Manager
  • The PRS position in most cases will be territorial but may also be limited to site
  • Team player, looked at by the field as the technical "go to person" Supports a 24x7 business and handles escalations 24x7 on a rotating business  
  • Provides training, installation, problem resolution and red alert support
  • Analyzes technical problems quickly and effectively
  • Customer/Service Focused, understands VOC, Service and the needs of the business
  • Part of driving total customer satisfaction and ability to resolve customer issues
  • Becomes the Technical Printer Service expert who can resolve problems onsite or remotely with phone support or remote diagnostics
  • Ability to simplify yet innovative, well organized and can document information in a way that addresses the issues
  • Action Orientated/Empowered and accountable
  • Drives problem resolution, uses analytical approaches to identify timely solutions to complex issues
  • Drives site standardization and efficiencies
  • Excellent communicator, able to communicate technical issues
  • Supports trade shows if needed
  • Is knowledgeable with color/ink and high volume color printers and is considered a resourceful go to person who can mentor and instruct others to drive field self sufficiency
  • Handles multidiscipline tasks and makes decisions based on data, facts and input from other members of the multi-functional team
  • Is responsible for corrective action feedback
  • Ensures systems, spare parts, publications and training are all in place and accurate
Pitney Bowes is an Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action Employer that values diversity in the workplace. Women and minorities are encouraged to apply. We maintain a drug-free workplace and perform pre-employment substance abuse testing.
 


Job Requirements 

  • Minimum 5 years experience with Software/Color/Ink and Printing Service experience
  • Candidates must have color/Ink/Printer experience
  • Production Mail experience is a plus
  • Software/Networking experience is a plus
  • Must be willing to travel up to 50% of the time. Also may require length of stay exceeding weeks at a time
  • Qualified candidate must have the analytical skills and technical background to understand software/hardware and complex systems within the entire Mail Stream business
  • BS in Engineering or equivalent experience

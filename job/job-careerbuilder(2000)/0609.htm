The CSI Companies, Inc. was established in 1994 and is one of the fastest growing private staffing organizations in the country. Driven by a leadership team with more than 50 years of combined HR experience, The CSI Companies delivers top talent, human resource efficiencies and deep industry expertise to serve the workforce needs of companies nationwide.

The company was named to the Inc Magazine's national list of 500 top performing private companies and Florida’s 100 fastest growing companies. Most recently, CSI was recognized as one of the top 25 fastest growing staffing firms in the nation by Staffing Industry Report.

CSI features four distinct operating units that specialize in the placement of office professionals (Custom Staffing), Executive Search and Finance & Accounting (CSI Search), Clinical and Non-Clinical Healthcare (CSI Health) and Information Technology (CSI Tech).

Channel Manager
Report It

Pay is $45-55k. 30% bonus. top 3 important factors:

1)attitude

2)intellect

3)Experience

High-growth software firm in South, FL, has an immediate opening for a talented and energetic Software Account Representative to add to their core team.  Qualified candidates will have a love for customers and a for passion technology.  The Software Account Representative is the primary point of contact with current clients and will represent the company to the marketplace and be the voice of the client internally.  This role will be accountable for providing world-class service and support to senior client decision-makers, managing existing client relationships, providing new account training, and identifying opportunities to advance the customer experience and business success.   Seeking individuals that posses great communication skills with a that can think outside the box.

 

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Develop and manage client relationships in order to boost satisfaction, usage, and retention
  • Provide efficient  on-board training, and ongoing training and support for client administrators
  • Help clients with technical questions and general support
  • Advocate best practices for computer-based test administration across customer base, with goal of increasing satisfaction and usage
  • Learn from client interactions to help company develop new products, services or support tools
  • Learn from client interactions to help innovate client experience improvements with better software and support tools
  • Identify early symptoms, patterns and solutions for upcoming technology challenges
  • Develop best practices, tips and training scripts for end-users on proper use of program

 

Strong working knowledge of Windows and/or Mac OS is required.  Excellent communication and presentation skills are a must. We prefer 1-5 years of previous account management/technical support experience.

Company Overview 

Founded in 1962 and a public company since 1983, Raymond James is a diversified financial services holding company with subsidiaries engaged primarily in investment and financial planning, in addition to investment banking and asset management. Our stock is traded on the New York Stock Exchange (RJF).

Through its three broker/dealer subsidiaries, Raymond James Financial has more than 5,300 financial advisors serving approximately 1.9 million accounts in 2,300 locations throughout the United States, Canada and overseas. In addition, total client assets are currently $233 billion, of which approximately $31 billion are managed by the firm’s asset management subsidiaries.

Raymond James has been recognized nationally for its community support and corporate philanthropy. The company has been ranked as one of the best in the country in customer service, as a great place to work and as a national leader in support of the arts.

Job Description 


Organization:  Marketing

Shift:  Day Job

 
Job Summary:
Under general supervison, uses knowledge and skills obtained through education and experience to perform data analysis utilizing data mining techniques, design and produce statistical models to address a varity of business opportunities.   Develops algorithms and use analytic tools to develop and evaluate a broad spectrum of analytics for large data sets. 
 
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Develops statistical models and methodologies to predict, quantify or forcast various operational and performance metics.

  • Performs data analysis develop algorithms and computational solutions as necessary to support the business.

  • Performs data management and reporting of product performance data including return rates, failure rates, failure models, and returns forcasting.

  • Provides support when implementing mode results and ongoing business processes.

  • Ensures data quality and reliability and provides feedback to business process owners and to the IT department.

  • Observe current systems and process, interacting with the appropriate teams.

  • Utilizes a varity of sources, collects and analyzes information to support the modeling and analysis.

  • Collaborates with leadership to identify, define, and solve a varity of problems and supports various corporate initiatives.

  • Assists in the preparation of  management reports defining the problem; documents the analysis and recommends courses of action in order to determine the best outcome.

  • Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.

 


Job Requirements 

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
Knowledge of:
  • Basic knowledge and demonstrated experience of statistical models and methods.

  • Basic knowledge of large relational databases, and SQL programming.

  • Basic working knowledge in SAS toolsets.

  • Basic knowledge and familiarity with data mining and visualization tools.


 
Skill in:
  • Superior skills with Excel, SQL, Access, Crystal Reports, PowerPoint, and stastical analysis packages.

  • Simplify and present time series and cross-sectional data.

  • Read, analyze, and interpret common scientific and technical journals and  financial reports.

  • Application of advanced mathematical concepts such as exponents, logarithms, quadratic equations, and permutations.

  • Application of mathematical operations to such tasks as frequency distribution, determination of test reliability and validity, analysis of variance, correlation techniques, sampling theorym and factor analysis.


 
Ability to:
  • Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from customers.

  • Define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions

  • Ability to interpret an extensive varity of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.

  • Organize, manage and track multiple detailed tasks and assignments with frequently changing priorities in a fast-paced work environment.

  • Communicate effectively, both orally and in writing with internal customers and other IT professionals.

  • Work independently as well as collaboratively within a team environment.

  • Provide a high level of customer service.

  • Establish and maintain effective working relationships with others. 


 
Educational/Previous Experience Requirements:
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, MIS,  Mathematics, Engineering, Statistics or related discipline.

  • A minimum of two (1) year relational business experience.


  ~or~

  • An equivalent combination of education, training or experience.


 
Licenses/Certifications:
  • None required.

User Reviews

11 October 2000 | by harris.fleming (Northern NJ) – See all my reviews

This movie was just plain bad. Leguizamo's stage show was good - funny, real, crazy but deep. The Pest is idiotic. Movies that aren't about anything can still be funny, but this one isn't. There are no jokes, no good sight gags...it's really nothing at all. Again, a few laughs would make it tolerable but I don't think I even smiled throughout the whole thing. I kept waiting for something good to happen, but it never came. Honestly, I probably would have laughed now and then when I was 12 or 13, when bodily functions and odors were something that made me think, "Now THAT'S funny!" and a bird pooping on somebody's head was something I hadn't seen 50 times. Once you're out of that stage though, watching The Pest is not going to be a pleasant experience.

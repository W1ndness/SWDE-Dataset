Storyline

An eccentric man aged about 40 lives alone in a decrepit house in Tokyo. He periodically transforms into a giant, about 30 meters tall, and defends Japan by battling similarly sized monsters that turn up and destroy buildings. The giant and the monsters are computer-generated. Written by Ed

User Reviews

 
Awesome! One of the funniest movies you will ever see.
3 April 2008 | by jrireland (United States) – See all my reviews

This is one of the funniest movies I've ever seen. The beginning was a little slow (i.e. the first 20 minutes or so) but after that it got absolutely hysterical. This isn't a movie with a lot of funny and quotable lines, it's more of a fun parody. The end makes virtually no sense, but it is still one of the funniest things I've ever seen.

The CGI isn't that great, but that's almost the point. Comedies are funnier when they don't have these huge CGI budgets. The monsters are funny, and their appearances are always accompanied by hysterical descriptions of their abilities and weaknesses.

As I already said, everyone in the theater was almost rolling in the aisles during the last half hour.

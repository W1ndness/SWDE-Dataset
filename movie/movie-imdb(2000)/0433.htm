Storyline

A mysterious killer video tape is circulating around. One look at this tape and you have seven days left to live. News Reporter Cindy Campbell (Faris) witnesses this video tape and tries to work out a way to prevent her death. But this is not the only mystery to appear. Crop circles have been appearing in the local farm of Tom (Sheen) and George (Rex). With help from Aunt Shaneequa (Latifah), Cindy suspects that the aliens may be linked with the killer tape and must now work out both mysteries before it's the end of the world. Written by Film_Fan

User Reviews

 
haven't laughed this much in a long time
7 February 2005 | by the_magician85 (Edoass) – See all my reviews

-After the bitter disappointment "with Scary Movie 2" I honestly didn't expect much but this movie did something that a comedy hasn't done in a long time which is actually make me laugh. And not just ha ha laughing, but soil your pants, choke on your drink, fall of the couch funny. the rap battle scene has to be one of the funniest scenes ever to be put on film and that KKK gag just kills me every time

-The great David Zucker proves why he's one of the best comedic director in the business by returning to the old "Naked Gun" days. The dialog is insanely hilarious, the actors do a great job of not being too over the top but still funny as hell, and the movie has moments that will make you drop your jaw in shock. The horse taking a massive crap being the #1 shocker

-This is movie is great fun, has a wicked sense of humor. It's perfect for when watching with friends and loved ones

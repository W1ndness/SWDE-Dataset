Movie Details:

  • Director: Louis Malle
  • Produced By: Selta Films, SDICC, Cine Neighbor, France 3 Cinema, Paramount, Planfilm
  • Year: 1980
  • Run Time: 104 minutes
  • Country: Canada, France
  • Language: English
  • MPAA Rating: R
  • Category: Feature
  • Genre/Type: Romance, Crime
  • Filmed In: Color

  • Alternate Titles: Atlantic City, U.S.A.
  • Key Cast: Burt Lancaster, Susan Sarandon, Michel Piccoli, Kate Reid, Robert Joy, Hollis McLaren, Al Waxman, Wallace Shawn, Full Credits

Burt Lancaster stars as Lou, an aging mob flunkey, barely making a living in Atlantic City. Susan Sarandon plays Sally, a casino croupier whose husband Dave (Robert Joy) steals a large supply of drugs from the mob. When he is killed, the narcotics pass to the unwilling Sally. Lou, in the midst of longtime affair with middle-aged gangster's widow Grace (Kate Reid), falls for the much younger Sally, becoming her savior by killing the mob thugs sent to shut her up. The killings serve a therapeutic value for Lou, proving that he hasn't lost his old panache. by Hal Erickson, Rovi

David O. Russell's documentary Soldiers Pay showcases some of the people who served as soldiers during the Iraq war as they relate tales of how their lives have changed because of their involvement in the war. The film takes a look at a variety of men and women, one of who became involved in a storyline that bares a certain resemblance to the tale told by Russell in his film Three Kings. by Perry Seibert, Rovi

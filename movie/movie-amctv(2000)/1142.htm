Movie Details:

  • Director: William Bindley
  • Year: 1997
  • Run Time: 88 minutes
  • Country: USA
  • Language: English
  • MPAA Rating: R (for horror violence and gore)
  • Category: Feature
  • Genre/Type: Horror
  • Filmed In: Color

  • Key Cast: Maximilian Schell, Stanley Tucci, Christopher McDonald, Wendy Crewson, Rachael Leigh Cook, Full Credits

This ominous apocalypse thriller sports a fairly ambitious but ultimately confusing plot involving a prophecy which proclaims that the return of Satan will be precipitated by the arrival of eighteen physically-perfect angelic beings. In order to fulfill the prophecy, a secret sect of Etruscan monks conspire with an obsessed geneticist to artificially manufacture the requisite number of perfect specimens, with the only setback being the lack of a suitably beautiful face for each of the eighteen clones. To complete the equation, the monks devise a plan by which they can obtain faces from unsuspecting "donors." The last of the potential candidates is lovely model Rachael Leigh Cook who, after a bizarre series of events beginning with her mother's death, finds herself in the monks' evil clutches. Decent production design gives some class to this direct-to-video production, together with good performances and a pervasive sense of doom. The film ultimately falls short of cult-classic status due to an excessive amount of irrelevant and frequently unresolved subplots -- particularly one involving the family cat. by Cavett Binion, Rovi

In this film based on a novel by Charles Williams, an inspector puzzling over a series of murders begins to realize that the victims are only a procession of aliases for a man involved in a multi-million dollar bond theft. The inspector (Alex Sheafe) must also deal with greedy mobsters. by John Bush, Rovi

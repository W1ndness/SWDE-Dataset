The Tamarack Junction has two restaurants, one is the coffee shop the other is Sully's Sports Bar and Grille, and a deli. Both restaurants are good, my favorite menu items are the chicken fried steak for breakfast, chicken salad for lunch and the salmon or shrimp for dinner.

“Old World Charm”
VelvetLady   1 contribution
Indianapolis, Indiana
Feb 24, 2010

We have been to this restaurant several times. You feel like you are in an authentic restaurant in Italy. It is small and cozy, with wonderful food. It has the charm of an old world bistro. Sitting by the fireplace while sipping your wine and enjoying fine wine is a lovely, romantic experience. more

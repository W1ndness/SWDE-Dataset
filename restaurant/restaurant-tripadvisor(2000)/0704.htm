“Great food. Great staff.”
shorewayguy   3 contributions

I've eaten at Caffe Roma twice. Each time was a great experience. The staff is so pleasant and welcoming. The food is really good. Great appetizers, entrees (pastas and meats), and desserts (get the gelato). They don't sell alcoholic beverages, but you may bring them with you. They have a nominal corkage fee.

This popular chain is growing so fast that there might literally be one on every corner soon. It’s easy to understand why the public likes it so much, as it’s very visually appealing, with huge racks of fresh-baked bread (the brioche loaf is divine), and display cases of muffins, cinnamon rolls and desserts. You order cafeteria-style, looking at each tasty possibility before deciding. Sandwiches are available in the traditional style, in mini versions (good for pairing with a cup of soup or a small salad), grilled, or as train sandwiches---good for people on-the-go. The best of them all is the chicken pesto baguette, with tomatoes, arugula, and pesto mayonnaise. The best of the salads is the you-pick-them salad trio, and the pizzas can be ordered half or whole. Corner Bakery also offers good breakfasts, and even has toasters on the condiment bar so you can toast your bread exactly how you like it. The filling egg strada will keep your motor running all day, as will the fruit-loaded mueslix.

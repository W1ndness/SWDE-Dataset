This environmentally friendly café is a literal paradox. You never know what to expect: Will it be packed? Will the servers be earthbound or practicing space cadets? Will there be an irreverent meat item on the menu? Two things are certain: you're sure to encounter a variety of vegetarian dishes at super-affordable prices, which is why this is the place to fill up on healthy comfort food when the month lasts longer than the paycheck. Paradox’s motto is: We will veganize anything we are able to cook. Check out the cornmeal pancakes, pesto rigatoni, or a tempeh Sloppy Joe. The secret to eating here is to chant a good-service affirmation in the car before you arrive. The proprietors simply ask that you “chill-lax.”

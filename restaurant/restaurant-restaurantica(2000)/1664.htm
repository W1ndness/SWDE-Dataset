  • We go to Marissa's every few months. My husband has been more adventurous on the menu...I fell in love with the blue corn offerings and rarely venture out from that. Good selection of local brews.

    Family owned and operated. I stopped in one Sunday afternoon after spending a night and morning in the ER at the local hospital with my son. I went in, placed my order and collapsed (literally) into a booth. The order was VERY fast, staff was solicitious and tossed in a bit of extra. Very highly recommended.

  • Order: Artichoke con Queso Dip was so good I ordered some extra to take home. I had a three item "build your own combo" meal with a chile relleno, a fajita steak, and a chicken enchilada.

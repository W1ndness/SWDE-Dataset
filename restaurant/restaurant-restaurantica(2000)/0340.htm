  • This is the closest restaurant to my house and thank God! It's also one of the best in the area, the best Mexican as well. They atmosphere is wonderful, usually very busy. They have great drinks modestly priced and the food is always great. The only downsides might is a little bit slow on service (10-20) minutes depending on the crowd (usually full) but it's worth the wait. A good deal of the waiters/waitresses speak great English a couple not as much but always understandable. Everything I have ordered their has been great and they do have vegetarian options. The food does has a slightly Americanized flavor to it but then again I'm not big on the more authentic Mexican as well. You'll love it, I promise.

  • Order: Pollo Campeche

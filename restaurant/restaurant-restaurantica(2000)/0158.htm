  • I've eaten at the Bright Star more than I can count-I really love it. It's very historical and many of the servers there have been there for a long time. The owner greets guests frequently and the Southern style cajun and homestyle food is delicious. Lunch is the best time to go because you can get everything you could get at dinner for the lunch price! Would definitely recommend to anyone visiting the Bessemer area!

  • This was our first and LAST visit to this restaurant. To begin this adventure, we were seated in an extremely hot area - closed in porch, maybe?? The service was very slow, we waited over 2 hours for our food. It arrived cold and completely tasteless. Well over half of the steak was fat and the grilled chicken was dry. This was the worst food and service we have ever experienced. When our complaint was heard by the "owner", she blew it off by offering a gift card for our next visit but still insisted that we pay for the garbage that was served. The gift card was for a whopping $15.00. How insulting. As the previous poster stated, "they need to get a few things ironed out" is a complete understatement! I understand everyone has a "bad night", but this was totally and completely unacceptable especially when the situation was not rectified by management.

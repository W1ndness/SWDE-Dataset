  • Amazing, amazing food. I cannot believe I did not discover this restaurant before. Some of my personal favorites are:

    -Butterfly roll: If you happen to be in the mood for crunchy tempura shrimp, cream cheese, masago, creamy avocado, topped with a hint of spiciness, this roll is heaven to the taste buds.
    -Sweet and Spicy crunch roll: not particularly spicy (although you can always ask for more spicy sauce), but this roll really great in the respect that you get what you pay for. The pieces are super big, and super tasting as well.
    -Monkey balls: I believe that this is one of their best selling dishes; it consists of hot, cooked spicy tuna wrapped in a large, juicy mushroom and deep fried in tempura batter. It is then topped with thin dried fish strips, sweet eel sauce and dynamite sauce. To be honest, I do not really like mushroom, but the monkey balls make them taste delicious.

    Those are just some of the dishes I enjoy there; there are many, many others that I advise people to venture to try.

    The restaurant itself is comfortable and not too big, comely decorated in a Japanese style, with cute wooden crabs hanging on the ways. The service is excellent; waitresses and waiters are exceptionally friendly and willing to cater to your needs. Definitely try out this restaurant! I know I visit GoGo at least 3 times a week!

  • Order: All their specialty rolls are incredibly delicious. Each respective roll is good in its own way, it just really boils down to what you are in the mood for eating that particular day.

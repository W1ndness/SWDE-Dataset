  • Texas Roadhouse is by far the best place to take your family or visiting guests for food. The service is fabulous (make sure you call ahead for seating) and the food excellent. Their buttered rolls are to die for, and they keep them coming. There is something for everyone from steak to chicken. Peanuts in the shell are offered at the door, and a bucket sits at your table.   • What a treat! This chain of restaurants prides itself on its quality beef, the preparation of its ribs, the freshness of all the items on the menu and on service. They lived up to their billing totally! We have never had beef so good - tender and tasty, and the ribs were just as tender and flavourful as they claimed to be on the menu. In addition, the vegetables were totally fresh and perfectly prepared, even the salad dressing (made in house from fresh ingredients) was terrific. The service was attentive without being intrusive, and it was prompt and pleasant. We were asked when we were being seated if we had enjoyed our last visit to this restaurant, and when we said this was our first visit, the manager visited our table to welcome us and describe the restaurants philosophy of service and quality, fresh, prepared on site, food.

    What a treat! We'll look for this chain again.

  • Order: Ribeye steaks and ribs

  • Very Cozy Tea Room
  • Reviewed by CaliBreezes
  • Overall
    5.0 stars
    • Food
      5.0 stars

      5.0 stars

      5.0 stars
    • Price Range (per person): $11-$25

  • I have been to the tea room several times for different occasions, and each time was absolutely lovely. The room itself is bright, airy, and delightful. My girls loved the peanut butter and marshmallow sandwiches and being able to choose their own pot of tea. The ladies who were serving us were gracious and attentive, without being intrusive. The food was tasty and its presentation was visually pleasing. My family, friends and I look forward to being able to spend another afternoon reminiscing and relaxing in the tea room!

  • Order: Afternoon Tea

  • I really liked the California Chicken Sandwich. It had avacado, tomato, chicken, on great bread.
    My wife had a pasta. It was spaghetti sauce over an unusual pasta. She liked it, but she said it was not the best she has ever had. She is of Italian heritage.
    Desserts looked great, but we were full and could not eat more.
    Atmosphere and was very pleasant.
    The price was very reasonable, especially compared to other restaurants in Monterey.

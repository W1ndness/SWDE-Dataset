  • This was the first time I have gone to The Auld Dubliner Irish Pub & Restaurant in Riverside, Ca. and it certainly will not be the last time. I had a $25 gift certificate that motivated me into first going, and now that I've gone, The Auld Dubliner Irish Pub & Restaurant has a customer for life. Their Shepherd's Pie was superb, as was their Stuffed Chicken and Turkey Burger. They are specialized in Irish cuisine and drinks, so their alcohol is a superb collection of not just Irish specialties, but domestic brands as well.

  • Order: Stuffed Chicken

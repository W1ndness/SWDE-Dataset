  • Best food I've EVER eaten in a restaurant!
  • Reviewed by Paul Householder
  • Overall

  • I travel a lot and consequently eat at many restaurants around the country and found the food, the service and the views just awesome. I've never had an elk, lamb or buffalo burger that came close to comparison and it's easy to see why that elk stew is such a hit. The manager is part native American and follows a family recipe for that and the trout was really the best I've ever had in my life. The waitresses are the pretties and most friendly in Estes Park and you can sit out on a deck and watch the hummingbirds all over the place. I'd easily recommend this place to anyone visiting Estes Park. You won't want to eat anywhere else. And the prices are much more in line than other local restaurants we visited.

  • Order: elk stew, elk burger, lamb burger, trout, buffalo burger

  • I had the Elk Stew and a Fat Tire Beer. I have long been a fan of elk - elk burgers, elk stew, etc - and without a doubt Trailhead Restaurant in Estes Park serves the best Elk Stew on either side of the Rockies. The value is outstanding and the service is second to none. Not to mention the views! My family had a wonderful time. A must stop prior to entering Rocky Mountain National Park!

  • Order: Elk Stew and a bottle of Fat Tire Amber Ale.

  • This was our first trip to Augies Place. We dined around 2:00 p.m., so there were not too many customers. The decor is modern and simple with wood panels and large booths. There are several framed pictures and art pieces on the wall and the fireplace was a nice size and gave us a warm, comfy feeling. Service was quick and our server made sure our Ice Tea and lemonade was constantly refilled. The food was excellent and the portions were almost too big. As much as we enjoyed the wedge fries, lightly seasoned, but not over-powering,neither of us could finish them. The menu offered a good variety of choices and prices were in line with most casual dining restaurants. Our bill was about $25 for the 2 of us. Standard dinner selections (e.g. fish or steak) ranged from $10.95 to about $25.00. Several sandwich offerings were slightly cheaper. I intend to try it again for dinner and will write another review when I do so.

  • Order: Shaved Prime Rib Sandwich (French Dip)with wedge fries and sauted mushrooms. Husband ordered cheeseburger with wedge fries. Ice tea and lemonade were our beverages.

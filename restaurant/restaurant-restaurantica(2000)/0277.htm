  • When we came in, there was no organization and we wondered if we might just need to seat ourselves... until a lady noticed us (all 8 of us) and sat us in the back at two tables pulled together. The problem was that there was another rather large party right behind us and the tables were so close that the backs of our chairs were touching the chairs of the other guests.

    We each waited in line at the buffett for clean plates on 2 seperate occassions. The food was mediocre, lacking taste for the most part.

    The women's restroom was the most disgusting bathroom I have ever encountered. Initially, we were told we would have to wait because the bathroom was being cleaned. We were told shortly that it was open again. There was nothing clean.... toilet paper all over the sticky floors! My sister asked because we thought it was being cleaned, and the clerk told us that they closed it to clean up vomit, that no one had time to worry about the other mess.

    When we got ready to pay, there was another crowd that had formed waiting to be seated, and no one was meeting their needs. Also, we each had to come up with cash because the credit/debit machine was not working properly at the time. We will never ever stop there again.

  • Order: Dr. Pepper & Buffett

  • Worse than the worst I have ever had. I have to agree with LaJuntaFoodie860 on most things. Two of our 4 plates were stove hot and the others were COLD! The burritos were ok, the enchiladas had no meat at all. The hot green chile was not hot at all and the enchilada sauce was just plain weird, what little there was!! The salsa tasted like Pace and the chips were cold and stale.

    It had been years since I had been back to La Junta and I wanted Mexican food. I should have listened when friends told me to skip Mexico City and go to El Azteca, I wished I had listened.. We went to El Azteca the next night and it was great. I remember when Mona had the place and it was excellent then, she must really be upset what the new owners have done. As far as the bartender goes, we had no dealings with her but once I heard her I remembered her from the Elks Club so that explains that. We must have all left at least half of our food there.

    All I can say is SKIP this place!!!

  • Order: burritos, enchiladas but did not eat it all.

  • WOW!! What a phenomenal restaurant! The service was spectacular and the food...Best in DURANGO!!! We really enjoyed the atmosphere with the large fish tank and aquarium. Our table was located next to the open kitchen - which gave us great pleasure to watch the chef(s) as they prepared our dinners. Our server was very busy although she was more attentive than I have ever experienced! Our meals were beyond belief -5 star qualities in taste and appearance! Of course I cannot forget dessert - literally to DIE for! One of the desserts we ordered was their ‘signature’ dessert…Death By Chocolate with espressos and Latte's. I have never seen a dessert so BIG and our server made our Latte's with hearts designed in the foam. We Loved the Red Snapper will be back soon!

  • Order: Civichie - Full Salad Bar- Steak & Shrimp - Steamers - Pasta - Lobster - Chicken - Prime Rib - Death by Chocolate - Keylime Pie - Creme Brule' - Espressos & Latte' Art

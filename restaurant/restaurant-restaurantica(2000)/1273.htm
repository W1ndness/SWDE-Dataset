  • I read the reviews on line and assumed this would be a good bet for a first date. The experience certainly made an impression. I had a beer at the bar because I was early. When the bartender could tear herself away from talking with other employees she brought my beverage, I was never offered a glass, profered a coaster, of even the perfunctory "how is everything?" When my dinner companion arrived we were seated in the last booth (We had reservations) where we got a to close for comfort view of the band setting up. We had no fewer than three servers. All at best disnterested,, or more often than not no where to be found. After ordering it took no less than 23 minutes to get my heaping portion of mushy noodles in ketchup. I'm no snob and spent over 12 years in the service industry. I lived on my tips, On a scale of 1 to 10, ten being the best, Fresco II rates a 1 for service, a 1 for food that would be better religated to the frozen food section at the local Walmart, and a 1 for the overall experience. When a single employee is less than first rate, that is understandable. Everyone has a bad day. When the entire staff presents themselves that way it's a culture. Poor service and horrible food do not a memorible evening make. If you find yourself near this establishment, do yourself a favor. RUN as quickly as you can. There is a Pizza Hut right around the corner. Same quality food, better, more friendly service, and a third of the price.

  • This is a great, quaint little spot that offers a...
  • Reviewed by Marilyn Richards
  • Overall
    5.0 stars
    • Food
      5.0 stars
    • Services
      4.0 stars
    • Atmosphere
      5.0 stars
    • Price Range (per person): $26-$60
    • Visit Again?: Yes
    • Party Size: 9 or more

  • This is a great, quaint little spot that offers a fine, striking menu of Mediterranean flavors. Although it may be diminutive in location and the service a little slow as well as a bit pricey, the atmosphere is quite welcoming and cordial. I highly recommend the Salmon Fresco which is, by far, the most splendid and flavorful dish I have ever eaten in this Valley. The salmon is well-seasoned, adorned with artichoke and sun-dried tomatoes with homemade mashed potatoes and a serving of fresh cooked vegetables on the side. Every savory bite is a delightful, flavor fest on your palate! Truth be told, there are not many restaurants in this Valley I favor but Fresco II is an exception. In addition, there are not many Mediterranean/Italian restaurants that serve cannoli which Fresco II offers. It is a classic Sicilian pastry which is a crispy, tube-shaped wafer filled with sweetened ricotta and aromatized with chocolate chips and powdered sugar. Overall, Fresco II is a superb and charming place to dine with a significant other or as a group.

  • Order: Salmon Fresco, cannoli

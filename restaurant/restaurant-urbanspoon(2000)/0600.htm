This restaurant is very well managed and always maintains a high level of service. The only thing they don't have is draught beer. The prices here have gone up some in the last few years, but I think the prices are very much in line with other restaurants serving food of this quality. Try a piece of Missy's pie.

What an awesome place!
by Ciara (1 review)
August 03, 2010 - Likes it
The Ramseys on Harrodsburg Rd is awesome! First of all, you are usually greeted right away by a friendly hostess...sometimes you might have to wait just a second because the hostess is so kindly helping the waitresses out, but trust me the food and service is worth the wait! Heck the management staff over there is so great that they may even be the one to greet you at the door....how many other places do you get that kind of family greeting? No where!
Everthing on the menu is popular because it is so good, so make sure you try something new every time you go! I highly recomend the Buffalo Chicken Strip Salad! You can't get anything like it anywhere else in town! Put that together with a drink made from one of the awesome bartenders, the awesome service from your waitress, and you are set for an awesome evening! Don't forget to save room for pie! It surely is the best in town! No questions asked!

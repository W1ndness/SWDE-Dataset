Sandwhichhaven
by Jeanie-chan (9 reviews)
April 29, 2010 - Likes it
I have been to this 4th Ave. stop-over many a time and each time I arrive hungry and leave happy and full (and usually with leftovers too). Reading some of the reviews that expressed confusion or disappointment over not "getting the hype" about this place has inspired me to share what I love about this place that keeps me coming back. 1) The food. There is nothing more satisfying than stepping inside to enjoy a large iced tea and huge sandwich after being outside on 4th Ave. during our 100 degree summer days. Forget Summerhaven, I want to go to Sandwhichhaven! 2) The bar. The entire place is situated around this horseshoe shaped shine to adult beverages. Even though i don't drink often, I like that fact that I can enjoy my intensely satisfying food and my escape from the heat, served with a side of - "And, would you like a Drink with that?" 3) The patrons are regular people!! While I do only go during the daylight hours, I can honestly say I have never been exposed to a messy/gross drunk person, nor a person/s who is incapable of NOT cursing, nor have I been worried about taking my 5 year old there to eat our during lunch (she said she loved her bread bowl)! In short, this place does not serve you "fancy", the servers do not have french accents, and the cooks probably don't even have those funny hats on when they are making your food. But that doesn't matter to me. What matters to me is that when I go in hungry, I always leave happy.

Recommend | Not helpful

Keep coming back for more
by erin (4 reviews)
December 31, 2008 - Really likes it
I have recently moved from Arizona, but everytime I return to the valley I make it a point to visit Bison Withces either in Tucson or Phoenix. All their food is amazing; it's tasty and hearty. Not to mention, it's inexpensive and they have a great selection of beers to accompany their soups, salads and sandwiches. The simple menu also makes it easy for you to narrow down what you want to eat since there's so much good stuff to choose from.
I recommend starting off with the CCQ (chili con queso) that is a spicy cheese and jalapeno dip served with tortilla chips. As a vegetarian, I think their veggie sandwhich is one of the best because it includes a garden of gourmet vegetables such as artichoke, sprouts, avacado, tomato, olives, onion and includes cream cheese and cheddar cheese melted on one of their delicious breads. I suggest the rye!

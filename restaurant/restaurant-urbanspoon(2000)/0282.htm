August 10, 2010 - Likes it
Love this restaurant. The gluten-free offerings are many plus the bring out gluten-free bread so I m able to enjoy the delious cheesy, herby butter. We've eaten there several times and always have great service and great food. Plus peace of mind. They seem to really understand GF needs. We ate off the summer menu last night and had the rice penne with chicken and artichokes. The sauce was incredible. Just the right amount of cheese but not gooey. The rice pasta was cooked to perfection. Ee started with the salads. The melon and prosciutto was fine but the corn and tomato salad was really special. Very simple but the ingredients were excellent so the salad became truly memorable. Very delicious meal. I highly recommend this place

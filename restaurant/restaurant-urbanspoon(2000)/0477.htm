Amazing
by slidsouth (25 reviews)
November 15, 2009 - Really likes it
Rudy and Paco's is a do not miss spot if you are in Galveston or even close by. This is your traditional steak house with a Latin flair. The decor is upscale with a nice bar in the front. Staff was welcoming and the owner was even present to provide a personal hello.
The wine list was impressive and offered good selections at all price points. Equally impressive was the menu containing steak and seafood along with other dishes, some of which were prepared with Latin flavors.
When you are seated you are immediately served crispy plantain with two dipping sauces (house salsa and chimichurri). They both pair well and were delicious as well as being an uncommon starter.
Our party also had a appetizer sampler consisting of empanadas, fried calamari, and tail on shrimp in a white sauce with capers. The empanadas were good and the calamari was average. The star of the dish was the shrimp. Large shrimp floating in white sauce which was absolutely outstanding.
I also enjoyed the crab bisque which was hands down the best I have ever had, and with the bread could have been a complete meal in itself it was so good.
Our table had several different entrees which I should add was served with perfection along with the rest of the meal. Rudy and Paco's employs "swarm service" with the entire staff appearing each with a single dinner and simultaneously giving each diner their dinner. Very impressive.
I enjoyed the filet which was cooked as requested and very flavorful. All of the steaks are advertised as aged. Several people had the steaks and one was impressed that it could be cut with just a fork. Other dinners included snapper and shrimp Diablo which included again the perfect shrimp in a spicy pasta.
I am looking forward to my next meal at Rudy and Paco's and believe it is one of the best Galveston and probably Texas has to offer.

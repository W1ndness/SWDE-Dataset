Flagship
by Jane Doe Eats - Seattle (179 reviews)
December 20, 2008 - Likes it
Mekong put Vietnamese on the map in Richmond but as they grew, service got slower. They still have the most amazing house noodles I've ever had anywhere and the brothers running the place were always super friendly and knew us well. They've kept the place up nicely. I introduced a dozen people to Vietnamese here and they still talk about the place. They handle large family dinners very well so this is the spot for us on Mother's day, chix night out and other special events.

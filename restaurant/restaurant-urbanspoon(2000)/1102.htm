Not so gluten-free friendly
by draba_verna (1 review)
October 06, 2008
As a person who used to be able to eat gluten and dairy, I loved Opa Opa. Since I found out I was gluten and dairy free, I have not loved Opa Opa. The one time I went there as a gluten/dairy free customer, I found very little that I could order, and the staff had no idea what I was talking about when I explained my diet. I ordered one of their burgers without the bun and requested rice as a substitute. As a note, I generally figure that if a restaurant isn't giving me something that costs, like a bun, they can make it up with something cheap, like rice. Opa Opa charged me extra for the rice and they didn't put any condiments or tomatoes or lettuce or onion on the burger. Clearly, they didn't understand gluten and dairy free.
As I mentioned before however, as a gluten and dairy eater, I loved going to this place. the food was interesting and tasty. I was really disappointed that they weren't more understanding and more accommodating to my different diet, and maybe I just talked to the wrong staff?

Good vibe,great pizza
by The food avenger (123 reviews)
December 28, 2009 - Likes it
This place always puts me in a better mood . I'm not sure why. The crowd is truly eclectic, something I don' t necessarily seek out. This may be the most truly cosmopolitan restaurant in the area.
We always have a delicious salad, they seem to vary seasonally. Ingredients always fresh,crisp and a little surprising.
Then the pizza. Nothing I can put a finger in, I mean on, but they are delicious ! Thin crust, a little charry sometimes, great cheeses, top notch toppings. They make a million of them and they are excellent.
Anyone can come here,feel welcome,get good service and terriffic pizza. If all of life were only this good........

Pizza, wine, Faygo grape - what else do you need?
by Chris Anderson (17 reviews)
February 01, 2010 - Likes it
I was thinking the other day, 'where can I get a tasty pizza and a bottle of Faygo grape soda?' and sure enough, La Grande Orange had already put that heavenly taste combo together for me under the same roof. Did I mention I was twelve and only liked cheese pizza?
Well, not really. But even the plain cheese is good here. Try the Rocket Man combo pie for a little garlic and spicy pepper flavor. Great desserts, too. Salads are fresh and seasonal. Nice selection of artisanal domestic and imported cheeses.
Another place to start working my way through the menu.

Great Neighborhood Place
by megsafoodie (4 reviews)
August 06, 2009 - Likes it
LGO just has it figured out. all of their restaurants are fantastic, with excellent food, great service, and the best ambiance--each place having its own perfect vibe.
Pizzeria is one of my favorite places. I LOVE the communal table closest to the line where they prepare the food for all to watch. It's fun to watch them toss the dough. The finished product is delicious, too! I love the Mushroom Party, and my bf always gets the Gladiator. Both so yummy!
But beyond their name, don't forget that they also have fantastic salads and an absolutely mouth-watering Green Chile Hamburger that rivals the best at Deluxe! I also recommend the white sangria. Definitely go for the pitcher, as they go down so easily. :)

Best Veggie Friendly Place in Town!
by skuchars (5 reviews)
November 21, 2009 - Likes it
Being a veggie for over 20 years, I really appreciate a great veggie friendly restaurant and this place is the best in town!!! There are so many great options...roasted corn pizza, veggie sushi, sonoma salad, veggie sandwich...and the list goes on and on. In addition, to great food the vibe is great any time of day. You can show up on your bike in workout clothes, or done up in a dress and heels. There is a great array of people and gifts to occupy your time while you wait for your food. As busy as the place can be, they still are incredibly quick and so friendly. Plus, they have added a great feature...curbside pickup. I recommend this place to anyone that's a veggie or not...I've never met anyone who didn't love LGO, the Pizzeria, Postinos, or Chelsea's! All offer simply delicious food and great service.

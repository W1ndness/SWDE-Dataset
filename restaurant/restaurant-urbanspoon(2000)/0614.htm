This has been one of my favorites since moving to LA. Tte space is just so inviting and sunny. Veggie lasagna, tomato rice soup, tortilla soup, curry chicken sandwich are outstanding. And my god the desserts! Pineapple cake to die for. Pumpkin pie is light and delicate. All the fruit tarts are good too. Bill can rack up quickly when you dine with another and each of you get a meal a dessert and a latte. But that always seems to be the case when I go cause I can't resist the temptation.

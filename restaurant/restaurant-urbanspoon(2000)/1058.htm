mobile review
by MuadDave (24 reviews)
March 31, 2010 - Likes it
They serve excellent food in a strange location. More on that later.
If you're there any time other than winter, you can see fresh ingredients growing around the building - that's always a good sign. Everything I've had there was great, but the duck curry was deep, complex, and just plain delicious. My wife, who isn't a Thai devotee, has liked everything she's tried there. As has been stated, this isn't the kind of place to judge by the inside or outside of the building. They let the food do the talking, and I like what I'm hearing.
About the location: the building sits seemingly inches from an active freight railway line. Trust me on this - you do _not_ want to be in the restroom when the RF&P thunders by.

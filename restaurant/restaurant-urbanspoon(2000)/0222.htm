Superb food and great service
by Elduder (114 reviews)
June 22, 2010 - Likes it
This place has it all great food and great service. My wife has been here a couple times and she was dying to take me here and I am so glad she did.
We started with the combo appetizer that was 5 appetizers each was perfectly prepared. For entrees she had the lamb kababs and the lamb was perfectly perpared so juicy and tender and an ample portion. I had the fish speical of the day a nice large full sea bass head and all. This was very well prepared too. The stuffed eggplant a friend of ours had was equally delicious. I would think anything you get will make you happy
The deserts were perhaps the highlight of the meal. We split 2 a custard which was very nice and I cannot remember the other but it was a pistatchio dish that was heavenly. The Turkish coffee was very nice too
They also make a strong drink so this place has it all. Our server was excellent had a nice sense of humor and one of the owners stopped by our table to fill water or do what needed to be done.
I do not see how this is not the best Mediterranean restaurant in the city. Worth the trip to go here if you do not live in Anderson.

mobile review
by Frankie G-String (1 review)
July 05, 2009 - Likes it
This area of town is not exactly known for fine dining, so you can imagine how surprised we were to find a restaurant of this quality just a 5 minute drive from home. Cafe Med. can hold it's own with some pretty fancy company in any major east or west coast cities in the US. Everything was beautifully prepared & tasty. The menu is varied and sizeable (I had the mouth-watering red lentil soup to start followed by perfectly prepared lamb chops.) Service was friendly & excellent. Just right for my taste. The server was around when you wanted him but didn't hover The prices are quite reasonable - actually they're a huge bargain, probably because of being located in this part of town. However even this has a silver lining. The location in the recently- refurbished Beechmont Mall means easy parking practically at their front door. There's even a pleasant place to take a stroll if you overdo it when the desert tray arrives. I discovered the place quite by accident & needless to say I expect to be returning!

My New Favorite Discovery!!!
by Colleen Soppelsa (1 review)
June 13, 2009 - Likes it
Believe me...I was a doubter. An authentic Mediterranean restaurant on Beechmont Ave in a strip mall setting??? A place that doesn't incorporate shredded cheddar cheese into every entree choice? I thought the positive reviews were bogus. I went, nonetheless, due to an extreme falafel craving, but with my expectation level set on "low". This place completely blew my socks off!!! My husband & I ordered falafel & borek for appetizers, I got the doner kebab and he ordered the combo meat kebab plus 2 beers = ~$60 total. Portions were just right, atmosphere very clean, warm & inviting, and superb service! Perfect for either a date night or larger group. I highly recommend this place and it is now my #1 choice for Mediterranean food in the Tristate. The other places in town have served falafel that could double as a hockey puck for the Cyclones. You won't be disappointed at Cafe Mediterranean!!! It's worth the trip.

Crown Jewel
by KC (2 reviews)
September 20, 2008 - Likes it
I absolutely LOVE the cuisine at Cafe Mediterranean! I have dined there 5 times this summer. The food is healthy and delicious. Atmosphere is comfortable: either in the dining room; white tablecloths or the bar which has a HDTV on the wall. There is an extensive wine list; appetizers are awesome. The chefs are from New Jersey and are very particular and selective about the ingredients they buy. The lamb is American raised and bought from a distributor in Chicago. The meat is Kosher and so delicious and tender it would be a disgrace to ruin it with mint jelly or ketchup! Don't forget to save room for Baklava for dessert! It is the best in Cincinnati! I really appreciate the owner's attention to detail, perfection and customer service. There is a pride exhibited which cannot be found at any chain. Cafe Mediterranean is the jewel of fine dining in the crown of Anderson Township!

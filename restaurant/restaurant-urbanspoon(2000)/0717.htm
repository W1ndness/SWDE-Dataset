We've been dining at Antonio's for over 14 years. The food is excellent...but the menu has never changed. If I hear "our special tonight is shrimp bisque in a bread bowl..." one more time! Seriously please add some new things to the menu.

Delicious and Outstanding
by EasyWriter (5 reviews)
February 18, 2009 - Likes it
This is a top-quality, well-established Italian restaurant. All meals freshly cooked. Delicious bread. Perfect desserts. Sophisticated atmosphere. Great destination for a date, an anniversary or another special occasion.
Prices are very reasonable for the food quality.
My wife and I live nearby and dine here several times a year. Never disappointed.

mobile review
by Winerep (3 reviews)
March 06, 2010 - Likes it
I must say that this was one of the best places I've eaten here in Nashville. Food was wonderful, service was spot on and the atmoshphere is very well designed. Only thing to be careful about is that a Caeser salad is an extra $5 because they consider it a "speciality" salad (hmmm at an Italian restaurant) and the cork fee is $20! That is the highest I've seen in a long time. But overall I am definetly going back. Just not drinking and having the house salad.

Nice Surprise
by Barney (485 reviews)
January 05, 2009 - Likes it
I will admit that I haven't enjoyed other M&S that I've tried over the years.  With that said, I enjoyed a meal at this location very much.
Started off with the regular sized oyster sampler (half dozen) that were very fresh and tasty.  I enjoyed the big plump James River oysters the most.  It had the usual accompaniment.. other than the horseradish, which I missed a little.
For a main, I had the scallop special that included saffron rice and some steamed vegetables, along with a nicely spicy asian-themed sauce on the plate.  Overall, 5 nice medium large scallops cooked just right (in other words, a little on the rare side... NOT overcooked).  A very nice tasty entree that was not too large.
For desert, had the chocolate creme brulee, which was very good and easily large enough for 2 to share.
Nice space, and good service.  The only odd thing that I ran into was the option of "small" or "large" glasses of wine (5oz or 8oz).  A little odd, but I guess it's better to get a large glass than have to do a second glass when you don't really want a full second glass.

After snatching a great outside table an hour before our assigned late reservation, we sat down to enjoy our dinner. Since we did manage an earlier spot we had to forgive the initial delay in service that seemed to be due to a mix-up in table assignments. Once service began though, everything went very well and the food was excellent. The fish dishes were all fresh, seasoned and cooked (or not) perfectly. The one steak that was ordered came out just as ordered. Overall, a very good dinner experience and evening.

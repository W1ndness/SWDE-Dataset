Exceptional
by enrique (1 review)
August 05, 2010 - Likes it
The best bread I've had in the US. If you like bread that is crusty outside, soft inside and doesn't weigh a ton, this is your place. The inside of their breads is airy and has the right texture, flavor and consistency. Most of their products have this character too, not just their breads: the croissants and macaroons are soft and airy, with a touch of crusty outside. You cannot get this at the supermarket.

outstanding!
by Mandy Zettell Miller (1 review)
February 11, 2010
I think I may be addicted to Blackbird. Everything I've tried has been absolutely delicious, but in my opinion, nothing beats a simple loaf of bread with a crusty exterior. A few favorites are: the blonde levain, the multigrain bread and the ciabatta rolls (I keep a few in the freezer at all times.) Their scones are pretty perfect, too. (I would love to see them offer a challah bread, even if just on Fridays--it makes outstanding French toast.) I've always found the service to be friendly and quite patient as I stand there trying to decide what to get.
I'm just grateful I don't live within walking distance.

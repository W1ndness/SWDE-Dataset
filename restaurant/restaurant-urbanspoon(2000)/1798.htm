Not my favorite
by DAVE (11 reviews)
July 15, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Im just not all that impressed with Newk's. The food has just been "ok" in my opinion and the prices are not all that family friendly either.
To be different I ordered a pimento cheese sandwich, it came to the table toasted. TOASTED????? This was the most greasy, nasty, messy sandwich I had ever tried to eat.
My wife loves their salads and because I love her we will go back, but I probably wont enjoy it.

December 23, 2009 - Doesn't like it
When Newk's first opened it was a fresh and quick alternative to same old work-day lunch. However, I have seen the quality of food and consistency in quality decline in the last few months. There is also a big difference in quality between the one downtown and the one on 280. If I HAD to go back again I would go downtown but I would only order pre-made items like soup.

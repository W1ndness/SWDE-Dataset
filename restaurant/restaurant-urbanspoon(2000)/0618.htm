Homerun Strikes Out
by Fred Moore (1 review)
January 15, 2010 - Doesn't like it
2 burgers, 2 fries, 2 soft drinks, = $15.33
For this price you can get a better burger at W.W, Cousins, Chilis, and most fast food drive throughs have better tasting burgers and fries.
Burgers, pretty small for the price. Burger: like someone forgot they were on the grill, burned on the outside, just right on the inside, meat high in the center, thin around the edges. The bun was the reason that I needed 8 napkins; it's the kind of roll with swirls in it, the swirls are an "I come apart here" feature which turns your bun into four pieces while everything inside starts to come out.
The fries are the kind that seem to be everywhere in New York; unpeeled, soggy (no crisp whatsoever), and dark.
Not that it means anything, but on Friday at noon, 85% of the customers were male.
If you want to try them, do it soon; they won'r be here long.

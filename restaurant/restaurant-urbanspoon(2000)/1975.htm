User reviews of Chili's Grill & Bar

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.
2-for-1 Margaritas (If you want to wait and hour)
by Amanda (36 reviews)
May 12, 2009 - Doesn't like it
My boyfriend and I were visiting his family in Niagara Falls a few weeks ago. Us and some of his friends decided to go out to Chili's on a Thursday night because of a 2-for-1 Margaritas and $1.99 Bottomless Chips and Salsa special. We all ordered a round of Margaritas, Mark and his friends ordered regular ones and his sister and I ordered strawberry and mango. Finally about 20 minutes later here they come. Im not sure since when it took 20 minutes to pour a frozen drink and serve it. Not only did it take forever to get our drinks, it took another forever to get our chips and salsa. I do admit this place was busy and it had only been open for a little over a week, but at most establishments, when they have a promotion like that, they are more prepared and have all this in stock and ready. Plus, it is Chili's, how long have they been around in general!? As soon as our chips and first round were gone, our waitress didnt come around until about 15 minutes later to check on us. While the food and margaritas were good, the service was horrible. It was a draw. Thank god I was only there once, Im not sure if I would go back.

Recommend | Not helpful

No complaints really
by Chris (3 reviews)
February 04, 2010
I've been to Chilis twice. The first time it was later, around 9:30pm, our waitress was great, very attentive, the only thing was that my friend and I stayed a while and every time the waitress went by we didn't really need anything, then after maybe an hour when we were ready to leave, she stopped checking on us and we wanted to pay and leave. Not a big deal, we found another server who politely checked is out and we left. It would have been nice to say goodbye to our server though because she was good. I wish I could remember her name. The second time I went it was for lunch and the food came fairly fast and it was pretty busy in there. If the managers of the place are reading this, please please please make sure your standards don't fall, hire people who know how to smile and Chili's will be the talk of the town in Niagara Falls. Applebee's is fine, but it's been the only place of it's kind really in the area. You can really compete with them if you do things right. Make sure your hostesses greet people as soon as they come in, there's nothing worse than going unnoticed!!

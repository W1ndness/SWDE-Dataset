Hard to say much about Woodstock's.
by Andrew Mansfield (58 reviews)
January 17, 2010 - Likes it
The pizza is pretty good. I've eaten a lot of pizza in my life. It is on my top three food list. My favorite place I've ever had pizza is a place call Zachary's in Oakland (with a second restaurant in Berkeley). I'm a fan of deep dish or stuffed pizza. As far as regular, thin pizza, this is perfectly fine. Eating there is not recommended. It is loud, crowded, and certainly not an enjoyable environment. If you are into sitting in a college crowd with both music and television on a flat screen in the corner, you might find it OK. Again, if you'd like to get draft beer and sit outside in Isla Vista on a beautiful day, it again might be just fine.

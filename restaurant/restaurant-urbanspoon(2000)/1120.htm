The Best Around
by 1Foodie2Another (1 review)
December 17, 2009
I love Cafe Rio!! The food is fresh and flavorful- the antithesis of your typical beans&rice Mexican place. Everything has its own flavor- like the cilantro lime rice, and the Tomatillo ranch dressing. The pork barbacoa is super addictive. And they make fresh hand-made tortillas right before your eyes. I no longer live in Utah, but every time I go back to visit I have to hit Cafe Rio.

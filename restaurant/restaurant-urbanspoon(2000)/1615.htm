Message from 360 Primo Coffee Bar

Spins the coffeeshop concept, adding in lunch and dinner entrees, happy hour drinks and a splash of vibrant attitude.

  • Locally Owned Coffee Shop
  • Outdoor Seating
  • Free Wi-Fi

360 Primo is not just another coffee shop. Locally owned & operated, we not only serve a great cup of java, but we do it quickly for those on the run & we offer free wi-fi for those who want to hang around. In addition to delicious pastries to go with your chai latte, we offer tasty breakfast items as well. But, don't just come for breakfast-we're open for lunch and happy hour too! Try a freshly made Panini, some soup or salad and finish it off with a smooth & sweet cup of gelato. Weekdays from 3pm-7pm you can enjoy $1.00 off during our Happy Hour! We have a great wine and beer selection to choose from. So, yes we make a killer espresso, and we'll even make your cappuccino with caramel and whipped cream because we are a superb local coffee shop with fabulous baristas, but 360 Primo is so much more. Come by today and discover for yourself! We look forward to meeting you!

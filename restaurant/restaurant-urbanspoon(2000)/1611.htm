Giant Portions, Low Prices
by Jeff Eberhard (36 reviews)
April 08, 2010 - Likes it
Went here last Friday for lunch for the first time. I've always been here for their breakfast skillets, which are awesome (if not a little too unhealthy).
One of my co-workers recommended the cuban po-boy and it was amazing! The sandwich itself was giant (see the picture I uploaded), the pork was perfectly tender, and it came with guacamole, a vey yummy cilantro dressing, and pico de gallo.
My fries didn't show with the sandwich, but the waitress brought them very quickly once I asked about them. There were lots of them, but they were disappointing. Limp, cold, and unsalted. I'd recommend going with the potato salad or fruit bowl as a side instead.
Another person in my group ordered the fried chicken sandwich, and it had the largest piece of chicken i've ever seen in my life. Cut in half, it was still too large for the bread.
For dessert I got 7 muffins, most of them were to take home :) The muffins were very moist and seemed to be filled with butter. Not healthy, but totally worth it!

mobile review
by Jenn O'Syde (59 reviews)
April 27, 2010 - Likes it
Very flavorful, and I was delighted with the variety of menu options. I was seeing soups, sandwiches, pastas and salads with a Latino flair, and even a few Thai dishes. I started with a delicious Caesar salad that had a refreshingly pronounced citric quality. They gave me flatbread in a paper sack, which was adorable. The bread itself was a tad salty, but very fresh and flaky and I loved it. I had a dish that included shrimp lighty fried and covered in crispy garlic, accompanied by pasta, tomatos, avocado, and grilled onions & chilis. Yum! Service was very sweet and friendly. Portions were huge, though, so bring a friend.

The Best!
by claire (2 reviews)
September 04, 2009 - Likes it
Pams has THE BEST food around. I love the freshness and don't mind waiting for a real grilled chicken breast or salmon on my salad than the typical frozen precooked variety! Lunch is counter service and goes FAST. Dinner is waitstaff with a larger menu. Confusing the first time I went for dinner but I love the shrimp tacos, and the grilled Shrimp Salad which has the freshest tomato basil olive oil dressing. Somehow this little place has a great wine selection and always recommends a really tasty glass . Wine prices seem a little cheaper than most places. Pams is a little place that makes you appreciate real food again! Funky local art too that changes often.

I'll have to come back and try something else off the menu.

Best Thai Food I've Had
by Tony Brown (1 review)
January 08, 2010
I have travelled extensively, and enjoyed Thai cuisine all over the US and Canada. I always long to go back to Scottsdale for Malee's. It is a little upscale, higher quality, and has more exquisite dishes than any other Thai restaurant I have visited. It is also one of the very few Thai food restaurants that server actual "Thai hot" level when requested. Most others Westernize to food to a low spice/heat level. It is the Best for Thai food junkies!

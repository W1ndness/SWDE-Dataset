Sad but true...
by Michael Maier (5 reviews)
April 18, 2009 - Likes it
... regarding the other reviewer. The service and the atmosphere are less than stellar here.
The food is simply amazing. Their use of mint and basil in various dishes is mind-blowing. The lemongrass drink is delightful and the usual soup that comes with your meal is very simple but light and delicious.
I've tried three different main courses and all have been wonderful. Even the one I didn't like was delicious (IIRC, it had thin noodles and tomatoes, don't remember what it was called). The Pad Thai is my favorite.
I will say that if you go and are not a regular, order much less hot than you might think you want. One visit they were BRUTAL with the spice and my "4" on the hot scale was inedible. That would be fine except I had eaten their "4" previously and it was a very fine and pleasant burn (I like heat that makes my nose run and sweat, so I'm no stranger to spices).
But their staff was unhappy about my companion sending hers back for excessive heat when she ordered the burning "2" at their recommendation. So be cautious.
Great flavours, tepid attitudes. If the food was only mediocre, I'd say skip it. But I LOVE the food.
So go, but let the food be the star and not the staff.

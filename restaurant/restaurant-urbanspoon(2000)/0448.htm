Visited 2 years ago
by Me (16 reviews)
September 25, 2008 - Doesn't like it
x2 people both with combo plates so that we could try a lot.
I was only impressed with the pork dishes (Kailua Pork and Lau Lau I think) and one of the dipping sauces (hot/spicy one). We got about a 10 second set of instructions on what sauces go with what foods, etc. I honestly don't remember any of the names of the other dishes and basically don't care. I don't remember the costs either.
I had heard this was a great place to eat, from many people but I've never been back. It was "fun" but they have a small dining area and you'll have to wait outside on a busy night.
Staff were friendly as I recall and the food was served promptly and hot. There is a bit of an atmosphere of controlled chaos, which perhaps exist in all restaurants but you notice it more in cramped spaces.
And the final remark, there are many other places to obtain Kailua Pork (which is probably the best thing you will eat during your stay here).

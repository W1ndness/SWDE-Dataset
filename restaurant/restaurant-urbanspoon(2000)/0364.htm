Landmark
by Lauren McAuley (20 reviews)
August 27, 2009 - Likes it
I've been coming to Lipuma's since I was a little kid - so, for about 20 years or so!
The food is great, fresh and fast. The best thing on the menu is the original coney with a chili cheese fry! The service is entertaining and courteous - don't forget to tip so they ring the bell!
Definitely worth the drive into Rochester for. Sit outside on the patio and enjoy the Clinton River and picturesque serene location.

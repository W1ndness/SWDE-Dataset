Another PIzza place in Ghent?
by Tsuru (41 reviews)
July 03, 2010 - Likes it
I was kind of saddened to hear they were coming to Ghent. Another pizza place? But to be honest, they do a good job. The food is much better than the other pizza hole down the street (and the trolls they hire to tow cars out of their lot) with a gellato bar and wide array of choices. The noise is much better than it was when it was Zio's but still not as quiet as when it was Szechuan in Ghent. Back in those days you could hear the crickets chirping (or was that the roaches?) In any case, this should appeal and suffice to anyone who likes Italian but is not looking for gourmet options.

Ghent location.

Eggplant rolitini is very good, lots of flavor, and didn't even mess with a diabetic relatives blood sugar! Everyone liked the pizza as well.

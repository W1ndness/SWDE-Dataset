I love this cool little cafe in the Pearl District across the street from a cute little park in Portland. The food is innovative and the menus change seasonally, we have enjoyed lunch and a dinner by the exhibition kitchen at this restaurant and both experience were very memorable. This is a great place to dine if you're looking for some interesting menu items prepared by one of the best chefs in Portland. Bon appetit! :)

One of Portland's finest.
by Anton Chigurh (9 reviews)
April 01, 2010 - Likes it
I've been here twice and both times my wife and I have ordered the chef's choice dinner. Both times the food was different and not a single dish out of the 10 each night was anything short of perfect.
Look for odd meats and different foods. I've tried thing I've never heard of here and enjoyed it.
If you are trusting and adventurous I suggest you let the chef decide you food choices for you and just sit back and enjoy.

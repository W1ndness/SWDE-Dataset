Great Salsa, pretty good food, super service
by S M (49 reviews)
February 26, 2010
These people know how to hustle. Salsa is really tasty. The margaritas are cheap, and you'll wonder what you're getting when the bright green drink comes out. It's not bad, but not a fresh margarita, and it'll creep up on you if you get a refill or two. I've liked everything I've had, and food quality is always consistent and dependable. Again, it's fairly Tex-Mex in nature. It's not a place for a 1st or 2nd date, but it's good for a 20th or 30th, when you're comfortable with each other and don't mind the louder, functional environment.

Recommend | Not helpful

by Lucavex Ayanami (6 reviews)
November 04, 2009 - Really likes it
Hands down, the complimentary salsa here is the best I've had in Tulsa so far. It has the perfect combination of flavor and spice. Their food is reasonably priced, and delicious. I recommend the Tacos El Carbon. Give this place a try, it's well worth a visit, and won't break your wallet.

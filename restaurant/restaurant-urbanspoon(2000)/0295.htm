Greatness
by Matt R. (21 reviews)
March 21, 2010 - Really likes it
I eat at the original Roanoke location and the Carrollton locations most... what can I say... what Babes does they do better than anyone.
First, to explain the business model. They serve family style (meaning shared) portions of green beans, creamed corn, mashed potatoes, gravy, biscuits, and salad.
Then you pick a meat (fried chicken is always at every location, other locations have chicken fried steak and other options). They bring you a lot of that too.
Then you eat until you can't walk anymore. If you run out of veggies, you order more.
I *hate* creamed corn... I *love* Babe's Creamed Corn, I don't know how they did it, but I love it. The mashed potatoes are better than average (dunno what they put in it but it's good).
The gravy, green beans, biscuits are average. The salad is bland enough that it's just filler... pass on it and fill up on the chicken!
Their fried batter is pretty tasty. The chicken and chicken fried steak is the best they've got, though I've had their pot roast and catfish too and they were just fine.
ps - If it's the holidays and they've got their pecan pie, get it. You won't be sorry.

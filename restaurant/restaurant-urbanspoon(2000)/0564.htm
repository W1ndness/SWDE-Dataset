Good service, ok food, expensive
by Rich (133 reviews)
September 19, 2009 - Doesn't like it
As far as the service goes we have no complaints. Even though it was Sat night we were seated promptly and our waitress was great...very friendly & kept our glasses full, which is a metric I use to rate service. Got the avocado wraps for a appetizer as they were recommended; they were ok/good. I ordered a SW blackened chicken salad w/dressing on the side; it came soaked in dressing and was fairly bland for something supposed to have some kick to it, and it was on the small side for the price. 1 kid ordered fettucini alfredo, which came as a generous helping but we thought it was odd no bread came with it. 1 kid ordered a mini pizza, which was ok but nothing to write home about. Wife ordered a breaded chicken dish that came with 2 huge pieces of chicken, a glob of bland mashed potatoes, and about a tablespoon's worth of broccoli...it was ok. We then split a dessert between the 4 of us, what was supposed to be a thick cookie drenched in choc sauce w/ice cream came out as a flattened mush (the cookie "fell" while baking, according to the waitress). For drinks we had 2 waters and 2 iced teas. The bill was $70+ bucks before tip!!! Might try again for a lunch special but nothing was good enough to justify the high dinner prices.

Happy Hour is always a plus!
by Amanda (36 reviews)
June 08, 2009 - Likes it
Although we weren't there for happy hour, they has some great specials. $3.00 of any appetizer, $1.00 draft beers, and I am pretty sure even $1.00 cocktails. The happy hour was Monday-Friday from 3 pm til I am guessing close and Sat-Sun 10pm close. You might want to call to make sure. We also didnt eat, we just had a few drinks and dessert. I had the Hard pear cider, which was great! Very light and crisp. We also had a chocolate chip pizookie. It was AMAZING. Everyone with us ordered dinner. It did seem a bit pricey, but definitely worth it for the portions.

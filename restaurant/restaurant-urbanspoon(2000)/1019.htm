Good Sushii, Horrible Service
by Mike S (33 reviews)
April 27, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Unfortunately for this restaurant their own service ruins their otherwise good sushii. I have never been to a restaurant that attempts to "hurry" you through your meal and out the door like this one does. Its a true shame that they don't understand how obtrusive they are, and how much they are ruining their own reputation. On the positive side, it is B.Y.O.B., and as long as you leave at least one piece of sushii on your plate, they can't kick you out until you are ready to leave. That is, if you can deal with your server asking you every 5 minutes if you are all set, if you want your check, and if your meal is complete.

Great Veg' Sushi
by Lizbeth (1 review)
April 28, 2010 - Likes it
I go to Sakura a few times a month, and do take out from there as well. It has a lot of vegetarian sushi options as well as entrees, and the yam roll (aka idaho or sweet potato tempura roll) is excellent. This is also one of the only sushi restaurants I've been to that have special veggie rolls. Those are tempura rolls, but it is still great to be able to have something fancy once in awhile. At lunch, they have a veg special that includes vegetable sashimi which is YUM!!!! If your a fan of seaweed salad or plain miso soup, these are also veg' and decent. Not the best I've ever had, but good. Plus the BYOB means you can save a bundle on drinks, especially with a large group of people. And the prices themselves are good, especially if you choose one of their complete dinner options which includes Soup, Salad, Entree, and Desert.

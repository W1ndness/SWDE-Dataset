Decent pizza
by Cassandra the Mad Critic (35 reviews)
May 21, 2009 - Likes it
The service is usually mediocre which is funny because I swear the staff outnumbers the diners usually by 2/1. It isn't fancy but the pizza is pretty good...to their credit--they use better quality ingredients--lots of cheese but they are chinzy on any other toppings. I always order salads for my family to share and those are always very good--not cheaply made at all and they are generous with everything in the salads. The prices are reasonable. Expect to wait to get a table on weekends--unfortunately there is no waiting area--you just have to stand there staring at diners waiting for a table to open up since it is "seat yourself" always.

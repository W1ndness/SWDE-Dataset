Probably my best dining experience in New Orleans
by Tom B (67 reviews)
April 30, 2010
We wandered in to the Palace with 10 people on a whim. They were very accommodating to us "out-of-towners" with a table in the middle of the main dining room. The restaurant is a spacious, two level affair with mosaic tile floors and a sweeping open staircase to the mezzanine level above.
I had my first taste of alligator at the Palace. It was also my last. I was not impressed. I found it tough and chewy and did not taste anything like chicken. The shrimp remoulade and the duck cassoulet, on the other hand, were superb. Not being a fish enthusiast, I had a lot of beef in New Orleans. The strip steak with shrimp were made to order for my tastes.
The Palace is a little pricey, but the meal, service and ambience were well worth it.

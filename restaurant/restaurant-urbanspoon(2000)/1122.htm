by becklaughs (11 reviews)

Coming from NJ where we have a gazillion Indian restaurants, SLC is always iffy when it comes to the cuisine. With "Bombay House" passed as "the best", I find Tandoor Indian Grill inspiring. For those of you who know your food & are looking for southern Indian cuisine- this place is for you. Service is a bit slow, but the bottom line is the food is great, portions are generous, and the price is perfect. Try one of their Dosas. You'll be happy you did.

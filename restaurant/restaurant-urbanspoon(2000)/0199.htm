Portillo's has ruined me!
by tonhe (12 reviews)
May 04, 2010 - Really likes it
Ok, I admit it.. I can't eat at other hot dog places now. Not after trying Portillo's.. and the worst of it is, I don't know why. I've been to plenty of other places that serve Vienna Beef hot dogs, but something about their 'process' has me hooked. Best Chicago dog, and a truly amazing chili cheese dog. Not to mention their wonderful dipped Italian beef sandwich - delicious!

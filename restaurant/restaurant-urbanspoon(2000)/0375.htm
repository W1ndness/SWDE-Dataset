Ann Arbor Mexican hot spot-literally I was sweating bullets
by dreric1kansas (177 reviews)
January 01, 2009 - Likes it
I used to live in Ann Arbor and went back to visit friends. They took me to this place that they said has some of the best Mexican food in A2. They also have a lot of hot sauces. I was enjoying the chimichanga with its very good chicken (I love those fried burritos). However I wanted some then decided to ask for some hot sauce. They brought out a plastic crate with about 20! I took out few bottle, looked at some and then started trying several. All was going well until a few minutes into eating, one of the sauces, I did not know which one, started "hitting me hard". I know that you should try fruit syrup, milk or something else to "cut the heat" not water. I was on fire (nothing usually hits me this hard!). All I could think was I had to run to the bathroom instead. I just sat on the top of toilet with sweat dripping down my face like a gushing stream. But I was fine in about a minute. An experience that I would not forget. The food is tasty and fresh here. Nothing out of ordinary but everything is excellent. But watch out for those hot sauces, even for you die hard fans. You never know when one will bite you! But maybe that is part of the fun and the part of the experience you might remember as I did.

Tio's -- Yessssssssssssss!
by Life and Food (1 review)
September 11, 2008 - Likes it
I make a point to visit Tio's while doing business in Ann Arbor.   The food is always fresh and wonderful.  The salsa is grand, though you have to pay for any extras.  It is the best Mexican food in the area.  My favorite is the Huevos Rancheros, have ordered it at least 20 times and have never been disappointed.

Tios is Awesome
by Brian Williams (1 review)
October 07, 2009 - Likes it
My wife and I ate at Tios last Tuesday and absolutely loved it. The food was great and the service was excellent. Their new store is a huge step up from the old place. I really enjoyed the Chile Rellenos and my wife liked her taco platter. If you're in Ann Arbor and you want some great Mexican you should definitely check this spot out.

Message from William's Gourmet Kitchen

  • Catering, Meetings, Wi Fi
  • Grilled half pound Hambergers
  • Business meetings, Box Lunches

The owner of Williams Gourmet Kitchen Will Roumanis, welcomes everyone to one of the finest gourmet restaurants in Durham, NC. They are now open for breakfast! Stop by and enjoy our gourmet coffee, fresh made breakfast sandwiches, waffles and omelets. Early morning meeting? Treat your employees to one of our coffee carts to start the day off right.

Need a lunch break? Feast on one of our gourmet sandwiches, fresh grilled half-pound burgers, cool wraps or fresh salads. E-mail out of control? Take advantage of our free Wi Fi service while you eat. Need to feed a crowd? Try our gourmet lunch boxes, with options for any appetite. Order ahead and pick them up at our convenient window the day of your meeting or event

Fantastic
by Gary (1 review)
September 13, 2009 - Likes it
Taste, service, price and quality; What more can be asked in a restaurant?
The homemade food is phenomenal. Regardless of what one chooses, flavor will be delivered. The breads are soft and fresh, the chicken is always hot and juicy and the burgers are just out of this world. One can only describe them as heaven between a bun.
I have visited WGK several times while at full capacity. Even so, I received my food extremely fast, specially when one considers the fact that everything is made fresh in front of your eyes. On top of that, the staff is always friendly and more than willing to give you a sincere greeting. Plus, the place oozes with character. From the menu to the art on the wall, everything has a cute characteristic touch. WGK truly has a Mom and Pop dinner feel with the speed of a full corporate franchise.
I can go on and on about WGK but no words will give it justice. I just recommend going to WGK and seeing for yourself how incredible lunch can be.

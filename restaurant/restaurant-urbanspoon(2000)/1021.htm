Luckily they are the only cuban place in town
by Mario (3 reviews)
February 05, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I've been to the cuban revolution twice. After the first time I only went back for drinks the second time. First the pros. The bar is huge and the decor is excellent. They have a wide variety of mixed drinks. The atmosphere is quite nice. Now to the cons. If you've ever gone to another Cuban restaurant, then you know this one does not have good food. The food is bland beyond belief. The rice and beans certainly came from a bag of uncle bens minute rice and a can of goya beans. The espresso was the worst I've ever had.
Its nice to have Cuban option in RI, I just wish it was good...

step it up a notch!!
by Ms. Fryer (21 reviews)
November 29, 2009 - Doesn't like it
For a place that claims to have the "best" cuban sandwich and posts pictures of che every 3 inches in the restaurant..you'd expect a little more authenticity. I am pretty sure that I could get a better cuban somewhere like modern diner. Eating their version only makes me want to venture to other lesser visited corners of the city like the south end to find something with true authenticity. The beer selection also leaves little to be desired. For somewhere so commonly talked about, you'd definitely expect a whole lot more. The one thing I can say that I've had here that's worth mentioning is the goat cheese stuffed peppadews..it is now a recipe I use quite often for entertaining. They should try being a little more creative!!

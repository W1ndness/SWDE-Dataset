Not bad, not great, okay...
by Edward (16 reviews)
November 02, 2009 - Likes it
I swear I wanted to fall in love with Tara Thai the first time I saw her years ago. It just didn't happen. The cool interior became the girl with all the looks but with little substance. She wasn't bad, she just wasn't, well... But she was sweet and always has a few tricks up her sleeve that keep me coming back. But I never get the urge to see her more than once every few months instead of developing that serious relationship. And I tried with her. Really. I have been up and down her menu. So that is where we are. Now when I go I settle on the tried and true dish that brings a smile to my face. Nicely spiced and tasty. Sorry, but I won't say what it is because if everyone else starts ordering it they are bound to screw it up. So, don't get me wrong, Tara Thai is a nice girl, she is not bad. She's just for lack of a better word, okay.
June 2010 update -- went last week for lunch and sampled some of the spicy eggplant with basil sauce. The basil was fresh and popped the flavor. Very very nice dish. My "usual" dish had noodles that were overcooked but the flavor was still quite nice.

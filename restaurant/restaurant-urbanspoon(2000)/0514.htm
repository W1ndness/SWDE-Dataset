WOW....
by Angel (1 review)

WOW... I am a patron of the Secret Garden on a regular basis since February 2010... My husband took me to their Valentines dinner to celebrate the holiday. I found the ambiance, service and food to be exceptional. It is rare that you can enter a restaurant and see smiling faces on the servers. The food was hot, fresh and very well seasoned. Nothing I ate that evening or anytime since could be classified as "store bought" except for the products used to create these wonderful concoctions of flavor. I was even more surprised when I needed to use the restroom and was able to see the kitchen in my passing, how clean the entire place was.
The prior posts of negativity is sad because the cost of a meal is right on target for any place with high standards. I would prefer to pay an addition penny to ensure quality over quantity as many that I have spoken to about the Secret Garden have agreed with me. Walking through the grocery store and seeing the cost of produce these days only shows that the Secret Garden is concerned for their customers because the meals are priced equally with area restaurants that place quality in the foreground.
I many times have spoken with the employees of the Secret Garden; servers, the older lady at the front counter, the young man that clears the tables, and the chefs and at no time have any of them ever expressed a disappointment in how they are treated by the bald men (I assume owners?) I have heard stories from the younger serves about the time they are taken out by the owners for celebrating moments to departures of employees needing to move out of town. I do not know of any establishment that would honor you for leaving them?
I am sorry this is very long winded, but the people that post reviews out of anger or jealousy should think twice on how it makes them and what they represent look. The person that owns their own establishment and bashing another only makes them look as if they are jealous. The other person that posted also only sounds like they complained for something free? Please note all of the rave reviews before these and keep these in mind. I am one that will always be in the waiting line for the Secret Garden, and I have had to wait on holidays because everyone loves it.

I will add the ambiance in not only romantic with the dim lighting but it also makes you feel like you are lost in a garden with the portraits on the walls, the flowers, and the wonderful service. I honestly have to say on such a busy night I did not find one server without a smile on their faces. That is a rarity in this day, someone happy to be serving.

Word From The Secret Garden
by Secret Garden Cafe (2 reviews)
May 13, 2010 - Likes it
Greetings diners. Typically we as a business NEVER interfere with editorial reviews from our patrons. We do however feel the last two posts to Urban Spoon deserve a fair response. First we would like to acknowledge BOTH of the entries (Jessica and Casey) and thank them for at least taking the time to express their concerns in a written format. While we usually would have an unhappy customer speak with us on premise we will accept that the two individuals here preferred anonymity. First let us say that our number one priority is split 50/50. We consider our work force just as large a part of our success as we do our customers. If one were to take the time to speak with the staff we feel confident that they would find we have a VERY close relationship with each and every one of them both at work and personally. This NEVER gives them an excuse to deliver poor customer service, but speaks only about their relationship with us as an employer. To hear that someone has experienced "so-so" service is not acceptable as we want each and every visit for anyone dining with us to be exceptional.
In regards to the pricing structure, we are proud to say that even during the worst of economic times (with pricing skyrocketing for us all during 2009 and through 2010) we have had NO adjustments in our menu pricing. This was our way of helping our community. Furthermore, we do take offense to the comment made that we are a "greedy" company. We are a small independent business that has a proven track record of giving back to the community. We have a discount program for Duval County School teachers, we contribute to numerous Churches and Schools, we contribute to local Animal Support groups and Shelters, we support and raise money for Breast Cancer groups, we have hosted several benefits for individuals who have been affected by sickness, fire and other natural disasters, and we most recently hosted a night for Aids awareness, assistance and research. We have done all of this without any wish of anything in return, and will continue to help these causes because it is who we are.
I can say confidently that we have a staff that is proud to represent us just as much as we are proud to have them on our team. To our customers, we thank each of you for over 10 years of continued patronage, and thank you for this opportunity to set this record straight. It is hard to believe that ANOTHER restaurant owner who considers our establishment run so poorly would have continued to dine with us for "a few years". As for the food, well, we can let it speak for itself...those of you who know the Secret Garden definitely know how we run shop. In ending, the Grilled Cheese mentioned (which isn't offered on our regular menu) can be wrangled up at $6.00 with side item of choice. Thanks for the opportunity to share our thoughts with you!

Eh, it was Ok...
by Christopher (12 reviews)
June 01, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I tried Port of Call on a recent Friday night in New Orleans with a friend. I'd read how great their burgers were, and I knew in advance that they didn't offer fries, so that was not a surprise to me. We waited about 45 minutes or so out in the late-May heat (not a lot of standing room inside, or sitting room either for that matter). We were seated in the far dining room, and packed rather tightly in with the other diners. We both had cheeseburgers and baked potatoes. The burgers were good, don't get me wrong, but they weren't the best I've ever eaten. I've had homemade burgers that were as good or better, grilled on my own grill. Also, the shredded cheese was just piled on top. It had not melted any, so it just sort of fell off as you ate. The potato was great, granted, but it was just a baked potato. The "bacon bits" were the typical soy-based product, and there were so many of them, it was sort of like eating gravel. I'm glad I tried it so I can say I've been there, but I don't think I'll go back. The wait staff was great, by the way. No complaints there.

Message from Cunetto House of Pasta

Head to The Hill for a great selection of Italian pastas and desserts.

  • Best of Citysearch winner 2005
  • Lunch specials
  • Freshly prepared food

Since 1972, the Cunetto family has consistently provided great food at great prices. With a spacious, casual atmosphere for diners and a prime location right on the Hill and with plenty of parking, Cunetto?s has become a place for both locals and tourists looking for the quintessential Italian meal.

So come in and find out why Cunetto House of Pasta tops the Best Restaurants polls year after year. Anyone visiting St. Louis must try Cunetto?s and make it as much of a destination as the Gateway Arch or the Zoo. It may be the highlight of the trip.

To sum this place up I would say this, I am not, I repeat not a chain restaurant guy, but, my wife and i ended up going to Maggianos for dessert.

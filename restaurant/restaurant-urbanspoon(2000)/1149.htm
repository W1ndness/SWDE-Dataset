by Dan S (2 reviews)
May 31, 2009 - Really likes it
Good sushi for a great price. There is almost always a wait at this small restaurant, though they do takeout as well. If you don't mind standing outside for a while or sitting close to other people it is well worth the wait. If that isn't for you but you still want good and inexpensive sushi, call it in and hope you can get through.

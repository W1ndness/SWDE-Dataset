Sushi Staple
by NAK (10 reviews)
November 15, 2008 - Likes it
I cannot tell you how deep my love it for Nippon Tei.  I consider myself a regular customer and my week is never complete w/o at least one roll from there.  Plain and simple, my favorite rolls are the spicy tuna or spicy crab which are carefully made with the freshest fish and seaweed.  The house salad is as beautiful as it tastes and I swear they have the best miso soup ever.  Not sure exactly how this simple magic comes about but I do know this is the ONLY place to eat sushi on this side of town.
Take friends for dinner and drinks; they opened up a new lounge side with couch seating, deep house playing in the background, and top shelf liquor.  I love the Lychee Martini--- not too sweet with a smooth finish.  
Sushi-panicked friends should not fret.  They serve a full menu of cooked asian fusion so even the unadventurous palate will be satisfied.  You MUST order the crab ragoon-- no, it's not like all the other's you've had.  Chicken curry is fantastic as  is are the scallops with eggplant.

Best Sushi in West County
by Gabe Usry (9 reviews)
December 22, 2008 - Likes it
Nippon Tei is our fallback place to eat whenever we go out. If nothing else sounds good or inspires us, we get a spicy tuna roll, yellowtail roll, and one of the specialty rolls.  Nobody in St Louis has a better spicy tuna roll. Nobody.  The seafood and squid salad is very good as well.
Bryan and his staff are always friendly, fast, and helpful. Even on busy nights.

Great Food/Wonderful Atmosphere
by Jilly (1 review)
July 29, 2008 - Likes it
My husband and I have been going to Nippon Tei for 4 - 5 years now, and we absolutely love it. It's our favorite sushi place. The food and the drinks are awesome. Our favorite drink is the Opium. It's really great!
The service is always good, and we love Bryan, Whitney, Nina and Angela. They are the best! Whitney is the best Sushi Chef in St. Louis hands down!!! We go almost every week unless we are out of town, and we will continue to go for years to come.
They are about to open a new side to the restaurant, and I can't wait! It's going to be a lounge w/bar area. I'm sure that it will be great! The atmosphere is the best too. We love the nice romantic feel, and the decor is really nice too. We celebrated many special occasions here, and we would highly recommend Nippon Tei for any occasion.

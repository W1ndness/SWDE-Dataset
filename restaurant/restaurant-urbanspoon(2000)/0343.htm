French-MidWest Flair
by Mike S (33 reviews)
April 27, 2009 - Likes it
Django is a restaurant that brings classic French-bistro flair to the mid-west. A popular spot amongst the "hip" locals, they take pride in serving what they creatively title "all French with no attitude". The food is excellent and service is friendly, although occasionally too friendly with local friends, thus slowing service. Despite this minor set-back the entree-sized "Duck Confit & Portobello Salad" made up for this. The Portobello mushrooms were spectacular and mixed with the balsamic-cranberry dressing were delicious.
A boisterous bistro, great food, and enjoyable atmosphere. Just be sure to bring good company, otherwise you may be sitting and watching your server equally enjoy the social scene.

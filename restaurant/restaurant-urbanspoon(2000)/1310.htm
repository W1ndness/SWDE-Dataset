Nice little spot
by Michael (90 reviews)
May 10, 2010 - Likes it
Dropped in for some lunch on a Saturday with my family. Overall I like the atmosphere and the feel of the place, with those big windows and tall ceilings, it feels very open and free. Also, points for having decent beer on tap. Any place serving Dogfish head gets a gold star in my book. Mix in some Rogue and you're on my love list.
I ended up ordering the Bacon Cheeseburger which was well portioned and as solid as a burger can be. I particularly liked the bacon they used, which was thick and cooked just right. Those with me also ordered sandwiches and they all looked good. Another plus, everything was very reasonably priced. If I lived here I could see this being a popular hangout for myself and my friends. Hard to find anything wrong with a place serving good burgers and beer.

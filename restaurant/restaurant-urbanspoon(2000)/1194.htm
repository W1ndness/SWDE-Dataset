Terrible Layout
by Gordon'ish (85 reviews)
January 26, 2010 - Doesn't like it
The pizza is okay... I'd go back if there were open tables.. but it's not worth standing in line.
In most restaurants, you request a table, wait to be seated, order, eat, pay, leave... NOT at Il Vicino... Here, you stand in line waiting, inch your way to the cash register, wait some more, stand at the cash register, wait, then finally order AND pay... AFTER a table opens up, they seat you...
This is all well and good IF it were a ridiculously popular and delicious place... but the "busy" factor is a facade... the only reason people are waiting in line is BECAUSE of the foolish layout of the restaurant.
So I'd recommend you go here if they're slow... but if there are people at the door waiting, go somewhere else. It's just not worse the hassle.

Over priced...
by iEatAtTheBar (374 reviews)
November 08, 2008 - Doesn't like it
I was there when they opened. I listened to the owners lament on all the in's and out of how they could open a wine bar in Georgetown and serve a little food to get by the private club.
That attitude has filtered into their operation. The sandwiches are over priced and small. The only saving grace is the nice patio space out back (in the alley).

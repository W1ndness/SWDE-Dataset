Marks Bistro
by topcat (71 reviews)
April 16, 2010 - Likes it
Unfortunately the weather wasn't working for a night on the patio, but dining inside was fine. The interior is fairly small, but tables overlooking the Dundee neighborhood were nice.
I tried Mark's signature mac and cheese with "chef's embellishments of ham and asparagus. It was quite hardy and delicious--certainly not the mac and cheese I grew up with. A decent wine/bar selection also satisfied. A featured/temporary app of some sort of duck/slaw/wraps with an Asian dipping sauce was quite flavorful, but not my cup of tea.
Service was somewhat slow/inattentive given the light crowd, but not to the point of putting a damper on the evening.

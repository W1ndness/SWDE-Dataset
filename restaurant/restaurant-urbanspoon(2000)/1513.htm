Overcooked food. Terrible service!
by Amy (4 reviews)
March 05, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Just came home from lunch at Mosaic. I will never return to this restaurant, nor should you. I went with a friend. No one greeted us at the hostess stand for 9 minutes. My friend ordered the mini-slider plate and I ordered the sliced steak sandwich. It took 50 minutes before we received our food, and the server did not check on us even once during those 50 minutes. My steak was completely overcooked and tough, chewy. After flagging down the server, I politely asked for it to be replaced. The server told me she would bring me another one in "just a minute." It took another 40 minutes before she returned with the new sandwich. I wanted to cancel my order after 10 more minutes of waiting, but she was nowhere to be found during those 40 minutes. There were only 3 other small tables of diners in the entire restaurant. The replacement sandwich was just as overcooked as the first one -- I had requested Medium, but the meat was brown all the way through. Terrible. I paid for the sandwich and left a meager tip. Very disappointing since I had read good reviews for this place, but found it horrendous. Do not waste your time or money here.

Recommend | Not helpful

Great eats, comfy atmosphere!
by M.B. (1 review)
October 07, 2009
We made Tuesday evening dinner reservations coming from out of town, but arrived 45 minutes early and they took us no problem. Since we were so early in the evening (4:30), we were the only couple in the restaurant. Our server was wonderful...heard we were going to a concert of someone we'd never heard the music of, and he pulled out his phone and shared a clip of their work!
They had a drink special going for $5 cosmos...it was a delightful blueberry mix and such a light drink, perfect for early dinner. We both ordered caesar salads with light croutons. Hubby ordered a filet mignon that was probably the most tender meat he's had in his life! I ordered tilapia that was on a bed of wonderful slightly sweet rice, which offset the strong spicy crust of the fish. Plate presentations were wonderful and filling.
We shared an apple turnover type dessert.
Definately try this restaurant, it is a hidden gem! Restaurant.com has coupons available for them all the time, take advantage of the savings but count on quality food.

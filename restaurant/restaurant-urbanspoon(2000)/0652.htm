Best Restaurant in Memphis
by Seth (1 review)
November 23, 2008 - Really likes it
Huey's is a Memphis original that is consistently voted best burger in town by various magazines. It is a bar/sit down restaurant with a sporty feel to it. If you happen to stop by, try the Chicken Philly with a side of fries and ranch dressing. You won't be disappointed.

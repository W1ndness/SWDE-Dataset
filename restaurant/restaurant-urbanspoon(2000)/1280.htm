Rivals the best

Owned by the same group as the one here in OKC, this is maybe better. The steaks are to die for. The first time I went was with a large group and we ordered the appetizer sampler platter. Unfortunately we had already placed our meal orders, or we could have been satisfied with the dozen or so treats - each of which was delectable. This place is killer with seafood! I had the steak and lobster, which rivaled any I've had on the east coast - succulent, sweet, flaky - perfect. Magnificient!

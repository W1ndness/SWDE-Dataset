Not worth the time!
by pmrigney (1 review)
October 16, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Maybe Vernon's Grill hasn't gotten the opening blues out of the way yet. The food and service were terrible.
One of my pleasures in life is good barbecue. I have had the opportunity to enjoy BBQ in NC, VA, KY and TN. Vernon's was possibly the WORST I have ever tried. I have never been to a BBQ restaurant that serves the sauce in pre-pack commercial containers or does not offer slaw on the sandwich.
They had no cutlery available. My wife requested a knife and fork and she was told they didn't have any, but they would bring a knife from the kitchen to cut her sandwich. She had a burger, requested medium. It arrived as a hockey puck, that would have survived an Ice Bears game.
The iced tea was not consumable.
If you like good BBQ, look somewhere else.

My Kind'a Place
by UncleG (1 review)
January 29, 2010 - Likes it
Many restraunts struggle and fail to find their niche. Vernon's Bar and Grill has found it's niche. A relaxed, comfortable neighborhood pub in the Karns area. Chris Brown and his crew always make you feel welcome and a part of the 'local crowd' whether you are or not. Food is very good, fresh, high quality pub grub with some twists. BBQ is as good as you'll find anywhere in K-town..It's cooked on the smoker just out front. Being located just next door to the Coastal Connection Seafood Shop, allows for being able to offer fresh, just almost off the boat peel and eat shrimp. There generally is a daily special aside from the menu. Most Friday and Saturday evenings will find live music. Like I said, finally I've found my kind of place. I'll keep coming back.

I go there almost once a week to have the Chicken Fried Chicken with mashed potatoes and, on sundays, green beans. I love it. But what I love most is going after 5pm because then the waitstaff becomes about 20 or so years younger and they interact with the customers like they're long time friends. Over all the service is great and the prices are low.

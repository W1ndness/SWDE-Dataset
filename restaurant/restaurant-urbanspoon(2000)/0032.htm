The Best Curry! I can't get enough
by Meigan Black (1 review)
April 20, 2010
I have been going to Thai Town for about 2 years now. My company used to be located right down the street and recently moved. I have no problem what-so-ever taking the extra time to go there.
1) The customer service is welcoming and continues to ask if there is anything else we need. We have never went in without a smile and left without one from the staff. I am not sure if she is the owner, but she will come and visit me if she sees I am there. She has even given my family and I new dish or two just to try it. Atno charge of course :)
2) The quality of the food is awesome. To be honest I have been ordering the same thing for quite some time *Kang Curry* It always arrives streaming hot and fully cooked. I have also tried some of the seafood dishes. Sometimes when you go out to eat, the seafood is either over-cooked or under-cooked. It is very close to perfect. The lunch I order is only $8.50 and comes with soup, thai salad and a main course. By the time your done, your full as ever! And all for so little.
I would %100 recommend it!
DID I MENTION IT HAS NO MSG IN THE FOOD???
I love it! :)

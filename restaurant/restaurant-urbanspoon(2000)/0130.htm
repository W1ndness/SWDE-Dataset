Small "at Home" Feeling
by Mike S (33 reviews)
April 27, 2009 - Likes it
This small restaurant with its excellent food and clever wine list will surprise even the tough critic. The cuisine is carefully and creatively presented without any pretentiousness. On my visit I thoroughly enjoyed the raw salmon starter accompanied with a radish salad. The main entree was a scrumptious stuffed pork tenderloin with roasted asparagus.
Our service for the evening was terrific, extremely knowledgeable of the menu, and made for an even better dining experience. Excellent food, impressive wines, and service that was a true delight.

Recommend | Not helpful

take your $$ elsewhere
by Jenn T. (18 reviews)
May 19, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Came here for the new years eve prefix menu and was VERY disappointed. the menu looked so good on paper but the food was really just average. the steak tartare was probably the best out of the 4 entrees. the salad was...a very average salad. i was very excited about my lamb but it really was just okay. i was more excited about eating the barley in the sauce than the lamb.
i would have left the place fairly happy if the waiter didn't just put our bill on the table without us asking for it. seriously, i understand that Hamersley's is a bistro but at its price range, i expect fine dining service. i expect a waiter to give me the bill without asking for it in chinatown so they can clear the table for the next party but at 11:30pm on new years eve with the restaurant half empty and no one in line, dont give me the bill without me asking for it. i wasnt ready to leave but now i won't be going back.

Recommend | Not helpful

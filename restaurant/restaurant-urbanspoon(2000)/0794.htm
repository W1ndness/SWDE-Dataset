Thin Crust Pizza Not to be Missed!
by Dennis Chu (10 reviews)
June 20, 2010 - Likes it
I took a trip down to Pete & Elda's to take on the Whole Pie Eater's Challenge. The thin crust pizza was very delicious, with the cheeses melted perfectly and the toppings (I got mushrooms and meatballs) having a nice distribution and adding even more depth to the flavor. The crust, however, was so crisp it was almost like eating a cracker. I would have preferred a slightly more doughy crust, but overall the pizza was excellent.
Do expect long lines for tables at the restaurant, as they don't take reservations. The service was friendly, the atmosphere was great. If you come early and hungry, you'll most certainly leave happy.

HOLY CRAB!
by Ryan Arthur (20 reviews)
July 22, 2010 - Likes it
It not just an expression it is a soup on the menu. I had one of many great lunches there today and the lunch special price make it affordable. I had the Grilled Chicken Rustica Panini and the soup. Have I mentioned the soup? I normally get the Mad Italian Panini because that's my favorite but I broke out and tried the Chicken it was good but I still prefer the Mad Italian. Ok one more time for the Holy Crab and Crawfish chowder. Crab and Crawfish tails simmered with sweet corn onion red bell pepper and a little spice. Man O Man... If you or into salads you need to try the Walnut Grove anything with roasted red pepper and walnuts, red onion, blue cheese crumbles and a wine vinaigrette on it has to be good right,.. even Lettuce. There are just so many things on the full menu you can't get board and I am not going to mention the soup again.. ok.

by russ7020 (2 reviews)
June 18, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The service was terrible- the servers were far too busy talking to pay attention to the guests and the food was inedible. The pizza was too greasy to eat, the pasta was over cooked & bland & the salad was wilted & warm. My friends & I will never eat here again & urge you to do the same!

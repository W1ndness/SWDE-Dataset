Best steak house in Tucson Decor-Cowboy\Western decor all the way. A place non Tucsonans think all of Tucson is decorated with. I do love the wood paneled walls with all the signed dollar bills on the wall. Service - Good, you can tell the servers have been here a long time and they know what they're doing. Food - The steaks are what this place is known for. Awesome mesquite grilled steaks cook outside on an open pit.

by FUSAI (7 reviews)
June 24, 2010 - Likes it
Daisy Mae's is a charming place that some who say a "dive". Just west of dowtown Tucson, it is an institution. If you go, get a steak. I had the ribeye, which was correctly prepared and seasoned well. The meal came with a potato, beans and best of all - Texas toast. Food is fast, staff is nice and the price is right.

Not what I would call a 5-star restaurant by looks, but definately by the food. All five of us wondered how we missed this place in all of our trips to Tucson! Don`t miss the 2 pound porterhouse, as good a piece of meat as I have ever had. We definately will be making this our number one priority in fture Tucsn trips!!!!

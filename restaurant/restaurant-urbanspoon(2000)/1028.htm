delish & divine!
by Dahlia (1 review)
December 09, 2008 - Likes it
Broadway  Bistro is a charming restaurant with a modest sized menu, a very romantic atmosphere, and an extensive wine list.  
The food menu consists of one page, split between appetizers and entrees.  Think quality, not quantity here.  Every bite is full of masterfully balanced flavor and texture, almost sinfully perfect.  The menu changes from time to time, so while the selection is not as large as in most restaurants, the menu size clearly gives the chef time to absolutely perfect every bite that you will enjoy.  
The bistro has very low lighting with candles to enhance the mood, yet the effect is not a forced effort at romance, but rather almost an accident.  The server was quite knowledgeable about the menu and the specials, and completely non-intrusive to our dining experience.
Just note that while there is a wine and beer list that is two pages (food menu is only one page), there is no hard alcohol served.  Soft drinks are available.
I highly recommend Broadway Bistro, and I am looking forward to visiting again soon.

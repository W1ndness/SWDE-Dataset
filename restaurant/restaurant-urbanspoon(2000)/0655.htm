Good, but not Great
by MustLoveFood (58 reviews)
March 02, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Samurai is good, but far from great. While the teppanyaki is decent, most of their chefs lack that flair, personality and talent that really this type of meal a special experience. Samurai has one chef who is definitely a stand out, Phat. The rest, not so much.
I've been to Samurai a few times - It's a gamble. When you get a talented teppanyaki chef, the experience is worth every penny. When you don't, you're better off getting take-out Chinese.

Decent Teppanyaki Restaurant
by AshleyM (42 reviews)
August 27, 2009 - Likes it
My husband brought me here for a surprise dinner one night, as my favourite restaurant is Kobe's Japanese Steakhouse in Orlando, FL.
While this teppanyaki doesn't serve nearly the quantity or quality of Kobe's in Orlando (Not to be confused with the Kobe's in Dolphin Mall, as that one is HORRIBLE), Samurai is decent, but expensive.
I would reccommend this for a special occasion where you have the money to order the steak and scallops, any other combination would not have made the meal so enjoyable.

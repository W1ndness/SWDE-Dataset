BEST PIZZA IN San Antonio!!
by Lisette Tackitt (5 reviews)
May 17, 2010 - Really likes it
Florio's has the VERY BEST Pizza in all of San Antonio! Being originally from the East Coast, time and time again, I am sent to different Pizza places boasting about their "NY Pizza". Disappointment after disappointment - and then I'm always back to Florio's!!! Gorgeous Brooklyn style foldable slice, cheesy, saucy, and dripping with some oil. Makes my mouth water thinking about it. AND to add to that the family that owns and operates Florio's are wonderful - don't let some of the other reviews scare you into thinking they're mean and rude. It's just not the case. I wish they had a couple of more locations throughout the city. So if you want the very BEST pizza - and want it New York style - and Brooklyn style - then this is the absolute place for you!

Good Pizza, Never had to wait in Line
by GuyWhoLikesFood (13 reviews)
July 17, 2010
I'm from Philly, where the pizza is pretty much the same as Brooklyn style. It is pretty authentic here. It would be nice if they could get real silverware for the salads and such, instead of plastic forks and styrofoam bowls, but if all you care about is good East Coast style pizza, this is your place. I notice several reviews talk about long lines and having to wait. I've been there a dozen times and never had more than one or two people in front of me. We're going tonight and I may try one of the sub sandwiches--I expect to be disappointed because they don't make good bread in Texas like you get in NY and Philly, but I'll update the review to let you know.

Message from Ruggles Cafe Bakery

A laid-back bistro serving comforting soups, creative entrees and museum-quality pastries to students and soccer moms alike.

  • Delicious New Menu Items
  • Take Out w/Curbside Pickup
  • BYOW -NO Corking Fee

Much more than a dessert bakery, Ruggles Cafe Bakery in Rice Village has been serving flame grilled burgers, hearty soups and pasta, fresh salads, sandwiches and wraps as well as award winning desserts for over 9 years. We pride ourselves in using the hightest quality ingredients all prepared fresh daily in our own kitchen. Crowd pleasers include the Grilled Beef Tenderloin Salad, Pasta Primavera with Grilled Chicken, the lean Buffalo Burgers & the Beer-Battered Fish Tacos.

Our lunch and dinner specials are selected by the chef and change daily. Don't forget to save room for dessert. We invite you to take a moment and browse our three bakery cases filled with an impressive selection of cakes, cookies and other treats. BYOW NO Corking Fee.

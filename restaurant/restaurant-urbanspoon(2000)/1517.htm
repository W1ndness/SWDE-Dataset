Like walking into another part of the world
by DavidInGa (11 reviews)
July 17, 2008 - Likes it
Cafe Efendi is a very interesting experience for lunch. You're warmly greeted at the door, where you order from the lunch menu. There's also a buffet if you're interested. They seem to favor sandwiches and wraps, and their spicy sauce really adds some flavor. The fries are homemade and delicious, and the service is excellent.
During soccer season, the restaurant transforms. People from outside the US all seem to know that this is the place to go to watch the World Cup and other soccer matches. It's thorougly entertaining to people-watch during the matches even if soccer isn't your sport.
A friendly atmosphere, good food, and reasonable prices make this a great place for lunch.

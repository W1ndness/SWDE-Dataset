Jaxson's
by David Aldridge (124 reviews)
April 06, 2010 - Likes it
What a great ice cream place! The staff was very friendly and it's a really cool atmosphere. They bring popcorn to your table while you're waiting for your order!
We had a hot fudge sundae with a cookie on the bottom and it was very good. The ice cream was awesome and it was covered with about 6 inches of whipped cream and the hot fudge was great too. The cookie was a little hard (probably because it had to support all the ice cream) but I'd definitely love to go back and try some more different things.

Not Chinese
by Brea (8 reviews)
May 16, 2010 - Likes it
I've read a lot of complaints on here about the food not being authentic and the only thing I can say is, "What did you expect from a chain Restaurant?" Is the food good, for the most part yeah it is. Is it Chinese? That's the one that's debatable. Nothing on the menu is something that you are going to find of the table of any Chinese family, but with in the context of Americanized Chinese it pretty much is. As for the plasticity of the food that I've heard some commenting on, I've never gotten a meal that at least didn't taste like it was somehow influenced by Eastern cooking. Are some dishes better then others? Of course they are, that's the way any mainstream restaurant is going to be. Setting aside the authenticity of the food it is a great trendy little eatery for special occasions and fun nights out. Other then that, I would recommend any of the other less hyped restaurants in Lexington for a quiet night of Chinese food.

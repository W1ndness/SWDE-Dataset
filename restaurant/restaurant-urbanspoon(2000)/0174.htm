mobile review
by Peyton (2 reviews)
April 02, 2009 - Likes it
Ive lived in Charleston for 6 years and I've never noticed this great little Italian joint. There wine list has an impressive collection of Italian wines and the menu is classicly Italian. Not classic American-Italian, but real Italian, the sort of dishes that remind me of my Sicilian grandfather. The Penna alla Maremmana was awsome. Perfectly rich and the spicy sausage really set the dish off. The atmosphere was unique and extremely enjoyable. I felt as if I had walked into a quaint little Italian resturant right of the side streets of Roam. The service was quick and hospitable, and the food was prepared and served with lightening precision. I would recommend this place to anyone.

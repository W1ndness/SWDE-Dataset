Hands down one of our favorites
by dreric1kansas (177 reviews)
October 05, 2008 - Really likes it
We have been coming to the Metcalf place since it first opened. It is consistently good with good sides, condiments, entrees and much more. The best time is to hit one of the buffets at lunch or the weekends. There you can sample all different types of food. What is nice is that during the buffet they also have free chai tea, mango lasse beverage (excellent) and great Indian bread which makes it even better deal. The samosa patries , curries, goat , chicken and other dishes are excellent and very tasty. Once in a while they have lamb or chicken saag (spinach) which is one of our favorites dishes. The waiters are most gracious, You can also order off the menu during lunches and of course night too. We like Indian food it was one of the best ones we have ever eaten at (Wash DC, SF etc). If you get drinks, an extra desert, and the regular menu items at night , things can set you back some $$$. Just be sure to set you limits !! But it is still worth it :) But try the buffet.

An Indian Cheers
by Shel (5 reviews)
November 23, 2008 - Likes it
My husband and I love the Metcalf location. The staff are always warm and welcoming and honestly seem happy to be there...probably the reason the place is always packed when we go. My husband's favorite is the Tandoori chicken while I'm a Lamb Korma fanatic.  Great naan and samosas. They mix up a sause (mango chutney, hot carrot chutney, mint sause and tamarind sause combined) at your table to go with the lentil wafers that comes free with your meals and it's great stuff. They also bring you free samples of pakoras, mango ice cream and chai...but you really must try the Gulab Jamun (taste and texture of a donut soaked in honey and cardamon) for dessert.

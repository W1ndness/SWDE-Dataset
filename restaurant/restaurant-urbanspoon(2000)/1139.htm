La Tuna has a great location and is a good place for a few beers at the picnic table in an open atmosphere. I had the ribeye steak sandwich with a side of nopalito (cactus) and buffalo wings. The presentation was attractive, but the sandwich didn't amount to much more than tasting like Steak-umms. The chicken wings, while large, had a catchatorie sauce on them which I did not dig. It was my first go with nopalito so I don't know if it was bad or good, but it personally did not suit my taste. The burgers that I saw looked good, but I doubt I return anytime soon to try them.

Outstanding
by Bigtwistee (23 reviews)
August 23, 2009 - Really likes it
Ultra casual drink and eat joint consisting of two old buildings and picnic tables under huge liveoaks. Order food at one building, beer and wine at another, hang out in between and listen to live music. The 'Cheesy Burger' may very well be the best burger in S.A. Flame-broiled with homemade bun, served with steak fries and fixings on the side. Weekends offer specials including shrimp and fish creations that rival much fancier and more expensive restaurants in both flavor and presentation. First-timers are often surprised by this quality given the general appearance and super casual atmosphere. A true Southtown gem, so far unknown to touristas.

Recommend | Not helpful

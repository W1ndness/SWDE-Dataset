Decent Ribs & Chicken,, GREAT signature Lemonade
by daisyferret1 (130 reviews)
June 14, 2010 - Likes it
My husband and I have eaten here a few times. The food has always been good and the atmosphere is very comfortable. The ribs were a little dry for my taste, but they were decent. I like mine juicy and dripping in BBQ sauce. So this just wasnt a match for my taste. I prefer the ribs at Smoky Mountain Brewery. However, the chicken was much better in my opinion. I also loved their signature lemonade! The service here was good, the prices good, and the food good. I wouldn't say it was spectacular, but I would say that everthing has always been good. IF anything, stop in and try the lemonade--it alone is worth the visit.

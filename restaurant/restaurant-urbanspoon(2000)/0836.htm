mobile review
by NMherron (72 reviews)
April 19, 2009 - Really likes it
4.5 Stars -
Taylor's location in Central Napa is located off First Street immediately next to Oxbow Public Market and COPIA. This one of the most fun and cool burger joints around. Probably in the Top 5 burgers I've ever eaten. It's all high quality fast food grub sporting sustainable and organic items. They have wonderful milkshakes...the white pistachio is fantastic. They even serve their shakes in cups made from corn that are biodegradable. They have a large outdoor patio to enjoy the Napa sunshine. A little pricey for this type of food, but it feels worth it. Highly recommend.

WORTH THE PRICE AND A WAIT IN LINE
by ScottySauce (235 reviews)
August 04, 2009 - Likes it
the original taylor's is in st. helena, on the side of the road. it is quite possibly the nicest hamburger stand in the around. they've got a gorgeous lawn with huge picnic tables in back, and the friendly staff will even bring your order out to you.
and man, is the food good! besides the expected burgers, taylor's has corn dogs, chicken sandwiches, garlic and sweet potato fries and super thick shakes made with double rainbow ice cream. sure it's always hard to resist the great burgers but they have a very scrumptious ahi tuna burger and some of the best fish tacos and fish and chips i have ever had. could it be the mahi mahi or all of the quality ingredients that make it all so addictive.
make sure you check for daily specials because they do have them. the meatloaf sandwich and in the fall a pumpkin milkshake make any drive out that way oh so worth it.
if there is a wait or not it's always well worth the experience. it's actually kind of nice to wait in a line and get out of your car to stretch and take in the nice fresh air at this one of a kind old fashioned upscale fast food joint.

Recommend | Not helpful

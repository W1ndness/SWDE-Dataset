Not so much
by HungryInVB (30 reviews)
August 08, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Our group had very high hopes for this place. It always smells so good when I am going to the grocery store int he same strip mall.
Anyway, we finally made it there for dinner last Saturday night. Between all of us we ordered quite different items from the menu.
First let me say that I was disappointed that I had to ask for bread. Whats that all about?
Second when you order a special that costs almost $20 I would think it should come with a side salad but alas no...you have to pay $3 EXTRA for a side salad that consisted of 99% iceberg lettuce, 2 (single) rings of red onion & 1 cherry tomato. Plus the dressing did not taste home made. Tasted like some funky dressing out of a bottle.
As for the entrees the were bland. so bland that the leftovers that were boxed up were thrown out when we reached our final destination after dinner.
As for the service, it was lackluster. Our server kept disappearing. I'm not sure where he kept going but he was no where to be spotted in the small dining area.
OK now for some good, the pizza items are tasty but over priced. $14 for medium pizza & $9 for a calzone is a bit high. they do taste good, but nothing that is so mind blowing that you need to rush over there.
In the end we all had a conversation about the place and we don't think we would go back.

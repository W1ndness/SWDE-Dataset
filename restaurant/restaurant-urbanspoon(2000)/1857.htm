The New Spot!
by Holly Crow (33 reviews)
July 27, 2010 - Likes it
So excited JP has landed a new spot that provides good brews and food. Went here for the first time last night and was surprised at the steady stream of customers for a Monday night! The host (could be the owner?) was very friendly and gave helpful tips on the menu, the atmosphere was brighter than zons and had playful decorations.
Came here for dinner and got a delicious barley risotto but could also see myself sitting at the bar and ordering some appetizers. The prices were completely reasonable and I'm super excited to try out their brunch. Overall I think this could be my new neighborhood watering hole.

+ 1 more photos from Perkalicious Very Impressed
by Perkalicious (59 reviews)
October 28, 2009 - Likes it
We ate here last night, and all were very pleased by the time we left. Our waiters were very polite and knowledgeable, with great senses of humor.
Dinner took a while to prepare, so they brought us an edamame appetizer while we waited. It was delicious! Very flavorful. We ordered an assortment of rolls and a sushi platter. The presentation was beautiful, and our waiter took the time to specify which roll was which for us. I wish I would have gotten a photo of the spread, but we were all too excited to dig in!
My favorite was the Mango Fiesta roll. It is described on the menu as "???????????" but was a California roll (made with real crab meat, yum!) with spicy tuna on top. In close second was the Melissa roll; I recommend trying them both. ;)

December 06, 2009 - Likes it
This is by far the best Japanese/sushi place in the Milwaukee area. The prices are realistic and the food is fresh and presented well. As others have stated, it does take a bit for the food to come out, but the server's do warn the patrons of this ahead of time. The servers are focused on customer service and attentive, which seems to be lacking in some other restaurants in the area.

If you want a REAL NJ/NY DELI this is IT!
by Jake (3 reviews)
June 07, 2010 - Likes it
You won't find a better hot pastrami on Rye south of Jersey. Seriously. The owner is a nice Jewish boy from NJ. His food is great, I hate to use the word authentic because he doesn't need to be authentic, he is the real deal. I grew up on corned beef and pastrami, kugel and knishes and kreplach, I would eat at his place if it opened up in NJ or NYC.
Oh have to mention, I ordered a platter of bagels and lox and schmere for my son's Bris. Price was good, he piled a MOUNTAIN of lox on there, I mean crazy unbelievable AND then he had his guy drive it out to us and we live a good 45 -50 minutes away.
GO THERE NOW.

Call in order to go
by sottileva1 (1 review)
August 07, 2009
Extremely disappointed. When I called in and placed my order, they told me it would be ready in 10 min. When I got there to pick up the food,within the timeframe they gave me, I waited 10 min and still no food. Needless to say, I had to cancel the order because I was on my lunch break and couldn't afford to wait any more. The cook came over and gave me a "couldn't care less " apology. Considering their over-priced menu, I would think that one could expect better treatment and service. They obviously think that they don't have the need to care about their customers because, at this point, their business is booming. I wonder how they will feel when the economy catches up with them and the newness wears off....

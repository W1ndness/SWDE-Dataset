A lot of food...
by Rufus (166 reviews)
April 08, 2009 - Likes it
They are generous with the portions at Ted's. It's pretty good food too. Queso/Salsa is bottomless... but the queso is sorta humdrum. The fajitas... they're good but a little shy on meat and heavy on everything else. Also the meat is cooked in a skillet as opposed to over open flame like some other places if that matters to you. The bottom line here is Ted's is likely a bit overrated but still offers a fine meal with a lot of food.

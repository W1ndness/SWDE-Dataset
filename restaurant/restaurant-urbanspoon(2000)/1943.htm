Food is OK, arcade needs help.
by Eating out in WNY (63 reviews)
September 28, 2009
This place is a great for kids or the sports fan. Hundreds of games to choose from keep the kids busy while Dad watches the game on one of the many flat screen TV's that circle the bar area. The selection of food is pretty good, but nothing really stands out. Last time I was there most every game I tried to play was broken or froze up half way though. ugh! Best for just drinking and watching sports.

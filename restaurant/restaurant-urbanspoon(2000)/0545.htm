Unfortunate...
by Ftn Cty Foodie (27 reviews)
November 17, 2009 - Likes it
Friends took me to Lakeside Tavern for lunch on my birthday and it was a disappointment. We sat on the lovely patio over looking the lake and I was looking forward to an equally lovely lunch. It was not meant to be. The menu had recently changed and I ordered the Cobb Salad. It was too large and not prepared very well. The dressing was gelatinous and syrupy sweet. Somtimes they get things right and then sometimes they don't. It's a shame that such a beautiful restaurant can't make consistently good food...

The delivery was a disaster, but not as bad as the food!
by ThomasDekker (1 review)
September 26, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I was looking to try something new, so I decided to try Fu-Do. I called at around 6PM on a Saturday night. The woman who took my order made it seem that I was a telemarketer wasting her time until I ordered nearly $75 worth of food, and then she was quite cheerful. I got a bad feeling about the whole deal, but was willing to give it a chance. She told me that my order would be ready in and hour. Well, one hour and forty-five minutes later, our order finally arrived. We opened the bag, and it did not seem like we had everything, but it was just that the portions were rather small compared with other Chinese delivery we have had in the past, even though the price was the same! However, I was glad there was so little food because it was easily the worst Chinese I have had in Alaska, if not in my life.
First off, each order came in a paper container, and each one leaked profusely. The restaurant obviously knew this would happen because each order was then placed in a plastic bag and tied in a knot. However, when we tried to separate the orders, it was a huge mess. All of the food was just about cold. We ordered the Mongolian Beef, and it was too salty and slimy-yes, slimy! The vegetable fried rice had green beans in it, and that would have not been a problem, but it tasted "off" as if it had been mixed with fish (and it was not fish sauce, either). I had ordered the spicy squid, and I can tell you that it was little more than pre-cut frozen squid bits you can get at Asian markets. It smelled rotten, was very overcooked. It was so nauseating that I actually could not eat more than a few bites (which is odd for me). All of the other food was just about as bad, except for the sesame chicken, which was about average, but much better than the other dishes.
The worst part of the entire experience was waking up in the middle of the night with a migraine from all the MSG in the food. A little MSG does not bother me, but there must have been a ton in what little I ate to cause me to have a headache. I am glad I tried a new place, but I will not be ordering from Fu-Do again.

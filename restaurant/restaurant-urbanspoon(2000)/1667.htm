Spice is right!
by Kim A. Mason (1 review)
June 06, 2010 - Really likes it
We were greeted as we stood in line. We were asked if this was our first time to the restaurant. Since it was, they asked us to sit a try a tasting menu and then decide what we would like. The tasting menu consisted of 4 entrees, 3 side dishes, and their signature drink. The entrees were curried goat in a pleasantly spiced gravy, brown stew chicken, and curried chicken, and reggae pasta. The brown stew chicken was spiced mildly with a sweet finish. The curried chicken was standard but flavorful. The reggae pasta was a mixture of chicken, shrimp, penne pasta, and pepper in a savory cream sauce. It was alfredo with cajun kick. The sides were plain rice, potato salad, and rice and peas. The rice and rice and peas were boring alone but go great with the entrees. The potato salad would make it to anyone's bbq. Their signature drink is a punch consisting of orange, guava, and pineapple juices. The blend was refreshing and delicious as we purchased extra to take home. I chose the reggae pasta and my partner chose the tropical stir-fry. The portions were huge. We had enough to take home for another full meal. We will definitely visit this place again.

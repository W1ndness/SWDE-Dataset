Message from Uncle Maddio's Pizza Joint

Pizza your way!! Trend setting in fresh, fast, healthy & authentic.

  • Committed to quality & service
  • Best new cheap eats in Atlanta
  • Family friendly-patio dining

Maddio's is a next generation pizza joint where 'gimme the usual' meets the unusual, where nostalgia meets whim. The history of pizza is a history of innovation, a history of creativity. What started as peasant food was later adopted by royalty. But whatever the case, it was always made to order - the ultimate in personalized. Most of the early inventiveness of pizza - using fresh, local ingredients; the world's first 'blue-collar lunch box' - has been lost to larger appetites. We make every pie right in front of you. A little more of this, a little less of that. Traditional pizza-making philosophy meets a modern pizza-making kitchen. Fresh. Fast. Personalized. Come see us at Maddio's, for lunch or dinner. Maybe you'll discover something new. Attitude, ambience, appeal. Come see for yourself!

+ 2 more photos from Barney First Week Open...
by Barney (485 reviews)
January 09, 2009
Okay, is it really fair to comment after only one week...
The Good: Nice thin crust, sauce (tomato basil) had nice taste and wasn't too sweet. Abundant toppings, nice casual space (pretty much the same as the previous noodle shop), really nice new covered and "heated" patio.
The "needs more work": As the first ones in the door for lunch, a little disappointed that they had to remake one of the 2 pizza's at our table (one of their signature combinations) leading to a one person starting to eat after others had finished. I would have liked to see a little char on the crust (though maybe I'm alone in liking this feature of a traditional NY pizza). Some work on some of the toppings... the "caramelized onions" were disappointing because they were closer to raw than caramelized
All in all, probably one of the better quick and cheap lunch pizza options around and fairly minor issues that are probably related to the recent opening. I suspect I'll go back because I want to try the toasted sandwiches.

mobile review
by MB (1 review)
August 06, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I really want to love Carmine's but I just feel like a sucker every time! Since I live a few blocks away and love Italian food, I keep giving it "one more chance" and keep coming away disappointed. The atmosphere is great, no question. The food is hit or miss. I've had some good and some very bland food. But the value for money is very poor. I never feel bad paying $100+ for 2 at Luca D'Italia but at Carmine's I feel cheated. Yesterday I ordered some takeout, and the $30 "family style" portion of Smoked Chicken Ravioli was barely covering the bottom of a 10 inch round foil pan! In my opinion Carmines is simply riding on their reputation and gouging it's customers.

Disappointing
by buffiejm (1 review)
August 01, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Since pizza is my favorite food and I've heard how good the pizza is at Dolce Vita, I went there for dinner. All I can say is that is was very disappointing. The crust tasted like cardboard and the pizza didn't have much flavor either. Domino's crispy thin pizza is much better and a lot cheaper. The appetizers were tiny and not cheap. A tiny cube of cheese (like 1" X 1") that was supposed to be an appetizer cost $6. The artichoke croquettes were OK, but just three tiny bites. There are numerous places in Houston that have much better pizza - Grotto's, Fuzzy's, Star Pizza, Biba's on Memorial and Tiny Boxwood's to name just a few. And they are all cheaper, too.

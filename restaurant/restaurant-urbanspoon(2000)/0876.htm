Authentic New Orleans food
by Captain Napkin (284 reviews)
January 02, 2010 - Really likes it
Jazz offers as authentic a slice of Cajun and Creole food and atmosphere you are apt to find this side of Lake Pontchartrain. The atmosphere is spot on for the French Quarter or downtown N.O. Live music on weekends on the second floor balcony adds to the festival atmosphere. We love the food. Many menu items are offered in half and full sizes, and there is a separate lunch menu. Service is always friendly, and usually prompt. But this is a place to come and sit and chat and be in no rush to dine and dash.

Mint Julep? Boudain? Really?
by Allen Huffman (14 reviews)
January 11, 2010 - Really likes it
I was very impressed with the decorations, food quality and service in this restaurant. I grew up in Texas, and learned of Boudain at an early age. I have never found it since I moved to Iowa in 1995. This place has it, and even had mint julep on the drink menu (which I learned via non-alcoholic versions served at Disneyland's New Orleans Square in California).
Our server was great, service was good, and they answered tons of questions about menu items (and even brought a sample of something for us to try). The food quality was very good, and prices are about what you would expect for a themed restaurant like this. My girlfriend and I look forward to coming back again soon even if it's just to try more appetizers while having a drink at the bar.

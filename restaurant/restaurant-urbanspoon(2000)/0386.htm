ROLEX
by action1 (11 reviews)
March 08, 2009 - Likes it
I eat out quite a lot. From the very first visit at Yosemite Ranch the dinning experience has been very enjoyable. From the moment you enter you are greeted by smiling, usually very attractive staff who reflect a sincere attitude of happyness at your chosing to spend a delightful hour or two or three with them. I/we (2 up to party of 10) have never felt rushed. This cannot be said flateringly about some other "nice" restaurants in town. From the general manager to the wait staff Jeremy is so capable and attentive you'd think he's on your personal payroll. I have said from the very beggining that no staff of any restaurant in fresno is trained better and with such a desire to make your dinning experience memorable and returning on a regular basis. Also, Dave Fansler, the owner is always stopping by customers table to make sure they are happy and well cared for. He and Sharon his general manager are sincerely hands on for their customers happiness. They also have a great bar. It has a warm, relaxing atmosphere with attentive, sincere service, where you can also try any of the tasty appetizers or a full course meal while watching your favorite sporting event, or just discussing events of the day with the very friendly patrons who are drawn to quality. The soft, but listenable background music all make this place a wonderful place to enjoy. The do need a good spinich dip however ... maybe soon.

Absolutely Fabulous
by Duke (1 review)
April 21, 2010 - Likes it
Yosemite Ranch is a true gem in Fresno. You must try their baby back ribs and Kobe Beef ribs. They are to die for and you get them for a steal here! If you're looking for a great side choice, get the golden mashers. They use a gravy that is so tasty and the mashers are perfect and creamy. I also prefer the apple blue salad. The combination of raisins, toasted almonds, dry blue cheese and thinly sliced apples creates a delicious introduction to a fine meal.

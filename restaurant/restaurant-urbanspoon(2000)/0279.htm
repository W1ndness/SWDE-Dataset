the chef has left the building :(
by swamp kat (1 review)
December 12, 2009
I've been here 3 times. the first time was magical with a jazz band playing, the food and service was great and the owner dropped by to check on us. The 2nd visit i got the prime rib which was the worst i've EVER had. The 3rd time was the charm. The salmon, potatoes, vegetables, and calamari were all over cooked, the potatoes had already been cooked and were beyond their last leg when they reached the table much like the asparagus which too thick and tough to eat....we got the tiramisu to go and i can't describe how disgusting it was...imagine 2 weeks old left in the fridge uncovered..yuck...all this for a 90 some dollar tab...no thanks-save yourself the trouble.

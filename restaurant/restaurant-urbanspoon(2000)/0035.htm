Heaven on a bun
by BigBear (18 reviews)
July 03, 2010 - Really likes it
with an onion slice. Unbelievable burgers. So good they'll make you want to hit your momma. Fresh buns, fluffy with a nice crust and pillowy interior. Burger is huge, hefty, delicious, perfectly cooked, moist, meaty....I've had different variations, bacon is always manly portions, jalapeneos are nice an warm, cheese is always just enough and close to too much - in other words perfect. If you get shrooms you get a lot of em. And the fries: Hand cut, fresh fried, perfectly seasoned, best fries around. Onion rings are just as good. Lots of cold beer. This joint is worth the drive from fairbanks. The workingman's feel means it's not overpriced and the prices are perfect. If you want to show someone you really know this town, take them here. You will not regret it.

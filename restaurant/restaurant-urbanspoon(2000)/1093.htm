by sf (5 reviews)
June 17, 2009 - Really likes it
You must order the nutty taco; it's truly unique and my absolute favorite. An Old Fair Oaks Village gem. And you don't have to be a vegetarian to love this place. I've been dining here for years and other then the prices going up with inflation and having to pay for your chips and salsa (which started years ago), their food has stayed consistent and great! Love the homemade salsa and their shakes are thick, just the way they should be. Interesting patrons and roosters for entertainment.

Chill out in Fair Oaks
by JesseO (3 reviews)
August 12, 2008 - Likes it
This is a great vegetarian restaruant, on par with Delta of Venus in Davis.  Sort of tucked away in a small lot, the ambiance is nothing to get excited about.  You can eat in a cool, tree-filled environment with roosters crowing all around you.  Thier nut burger and nutty taco are amazingly good, and the variety of smoothies offered are exqusite and refreshing.  My son, an avid carnivore, was impressed by the taste of the food.  Take yourself, date, or family here and enjoy!

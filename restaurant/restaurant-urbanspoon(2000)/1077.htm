Horrible Management
by Kevin (1 review)
February 18, 2010 - Doesn't like it
This place used to be awesome, they used to really focus on customer satisfaction. Lately they have begun to care less and less. The hostesses at Salena's are the absolute worst. They have not seated us on a number of occasions, clearly just skipping our names over, while when friends of theirs just walk right in without a wait. When trying to speak with them it is difficult to tear them from their cell phones or the co-workers they are socializing with. We waited one day for 2 hours and without being seated, even though we were given a pager and a 45 minute timeframe. The hostess told us, after we told her we were waiting for 2 hours, and our name had clearly been skipped over, that we would be seated in 15 minutes. When this was brought up the the General Manager Jason we were promptly ignored. I will never give them my business again. Their are too many Mexican places in Rochester to watse my time here. DINERS BEWARE!

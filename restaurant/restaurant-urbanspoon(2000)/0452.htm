There's a reason it's frequently reviewed on the Food Network!
by Kristen Spangler (5 reviews)
June 26, 2010
Reached the Grass Skirt Grill just after it'd closed the first time I was in Hawai'i, so I was really excited to take my parents there for lunch when I travelled back to O'ahu with them last week. Though the Ahi was on the menu, I opted for the Grilled Ono with Sesame Sauce and the Garlic Fries. Dad got the pulled pork and mum got a burger and onion rings. We all ate with gusto. My dad is a huge fan of pulled pork sandwiches, but he's also a picky eater: things have to be just right for him to like his food. It must've been just right here, because he savoured every single bite of that sandwich, being the last one done of our party. The ono was perfect and perfectly complimented by the sesame. The fries order was so big that it took three of us to finish, but had we not eaten breakfast, we'd have been fighting for the chance to be the only one. They were golden and crispy on the outside, light and fluffy inside; the garlic sauce was light and didn't mar the flavour of the potatoes. The service was super fast even though there wasn't a seat left outside or inside, and we enjoyed listening to the owner and the other workers banter with locals as we ate. Will DEFINITELY go back there the next time I'm in Hawai'i, which can't come soon enough.

Great new restaurant
by Ted Shanteuagh (1 review)
May 22, 2010 - Likes it
A group of friends and I dined at Roux on a friday evening and we were all very impressed. I live in Roswell and have been looking for a good place to get good oysters for a while (Hooters doesn't count as either) and was pleasantly surprised. The prices were fair especially on the oyster specials (Chesapeake oysters for 14$ a dozen!). Service was good and the food was great including a cobia special that was one of the best pieces of fish I've ever had at any inland restaurant. Around 1030 or so Roux shifted from a very warm restaurant to a very casual and inviting bar atmosphere just as anywhere on Canton street does. Our party decided to stay for a few after dinner drinks and dessert and found both the wine and beer selection very unique and very good. Their selection of craft bottled beers was varied and unique and you wont find their draughts anywhere else in Roswell (Try the Andy Gator). Over all 4.5 out of five stars, the only reason it's not a five is it's decoration leaves a little bit wanting (but it's a restaurant, don't worry about it).

The food is unbelievable. This is exactly what canton street needed. The oysters are the freshest I have ever had this far from the coast. And to top it off they are shucked at the bar. The red fish was possible the best I've ever had. The chef went to every table to make sure they were happy, you don't ever see that. My wife had the shrimp and grits and they were defenetly not microwaved!! The shrimp was blackened. The best Cajun experience i've ever had.

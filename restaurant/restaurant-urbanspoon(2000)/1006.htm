Best bar!
by delia (35 reviews)
November 25, 2008 - Really likes it
This is my favorite bar in Portland. Their menu is all vegan with maybe the most delicious "meatball" sandwich ever made among the options. They also some delicious black-eyed peas and an assortment of other southern-inspired foods at super cheap prices. The drinks too are wonderful. I usually get the hot toddy (at an affordable $5). The atmosphere is dark and adorable all at the same time.

Boring? But I'm not Vegan...
by Emily (66 reviews)
June 23, 2010
I thought my plate of food was fine-- it just did not excite me. Tofu stir-fried in a barbeque sauce and brussel sprouts with salt and pepper. I'm not vegan but I have eaten and enjoyed plenty of vegan fare, and this did not strike me as anything special. I felt like I could whip that up at home in ten minutes. Drinks were good, environment is neat. Overall I liked it, but wasn't blown away.

Always a positive dining experience!
by Jeni (56 reviews)
August 11, 2009 - Likes it
I've eaten at Bonefish a dozen times in Exton but I've also been to locations in Raliegh, NC, Myrtle Beach, SC, Boca Raton, FL and Willow Grove, PA... I have never been disappointed at this chain... food is always great, service is excellent, prices are as expected for the quality of the food. They even do a special order for me witout complaint - I get the parmesan pecan crust on Chilean seabass.. we've had every appetizer, every single one is amazing.. it is hard to pick a favorite.. bang-bang shrimp, mussels josephine, seared ahi, calamari... the house salad also has a great dressing. Seriously I am not a fan of chains but you wouldn't know this place is one!

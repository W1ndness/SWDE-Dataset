What, It's Vegan?
by jbhudspeth (1 review)
February 10, 2009 - Likes it
I absolutely loved this restaurant.  Upon entering its doors, veggie lovers are instantly at home with the menu.  All the food is guilt-free and far from bland.  I even got my carnivorous father to partake in dinner there and he enjoyed it!  The Bali Bliss is a fantastic take on a tempay patty and is especially good with fresh avocado.  The Sweetheart fries are a must along with the fresh (yes fresh) lemonade.  To top it off, the chocolate pudding or oatmeal chocolate cookies are scrumptious.  This is an up and coming restaurant headed in the right direction :)

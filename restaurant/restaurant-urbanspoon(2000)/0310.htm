The Best Restaurant.
by Liz (10 reviews)
June 02, 2010 - Likes it
The best restaurant. Note the period, Vesta's is the most fantastic restaurant I have eaten at. Anywhere. Their drinks are inventive and delicious; do yourself a favor and get one made with Stranahan's Colorado Whisky to start the evening off. The cheese plate is ever-changing, but always features a nice balance of flavors and textures. The scallops with fingerling potatoes in a creamy, bleu-cheese dressing (when they were on the menu) were one of the top dishes I have ever had, but it's hard to go wrong with any of their offerings. Ask the waiter for a wine recommendation to go with dinner, or just order the Vesta red or white. Somehow after the cheese plate and an entree, I can always manage to find room for the Boca Negra: the semi-flourless chocolate cake that has ruined me for all other chocolate cakes. If you have something to celebrate (and the fact that you are dining at Vesta's is reason enough to celebrate), get the Century of Port to pair with your cake. Aside from the phenomenal food the atmosphere is fantastic; well decorated, ultra-lounge music, with knowledgeable and friendly staff.

Too expensive, Food was bad, poor service
by Mark (1 review)
March 01, 2010 - Doesn't like it
This place used to be called Kyoto Japanese Restaurant, and it USED to be amazing. My wife and I would go there at least 1-2 times per week. The portions were huge, the food was on par to that of a 5 star restaurant, and the prices were reasonable.
We went into the restaurant one day to see a completely different staff (the owner wasn't even there). However they still had the same decorations and menus. I though nothing of it and decided to order the usual.
I ordered beef tataki and a chef's special roll (tuna I believe). The portions were soo tiny it was ridiculous, and the prices stayed the same. It should be way cheaper considering the quality and amount. Honestly, they were nothing near Kyoto's portions. Not to mention, the chef/ person preparing the food was un-experienced, and the food had little to no flavor.
The seafood in my sushi tasted rotten, and since that day I have vowed to NEVER step foot in Blue Ocean restaurant ever again. Not to mention the terrible service. The waitress took a good 30 minutes before she even came to our table to ask what we wanted to eat, and after that, she did not even bother to refill my sprite.
Overall, it was a terrible experience and I will never ever go into this restaurant again. I want Kyoto back!

Neighborhood bar with world class micro-brew.
by Kenitoquerque (173 reviews)
May 03, 2009 - Really likes it
I can't say enough about this place. It is solace for those new to the city who hail from smaller towns. Such a down-to-earth place; unpretentious. Emphasis is on the beer, which is how it should be. Nothing fancy, unless you are a beer-o-phile who appreciates a new cask of liquid delights every week, loves a good range of beers-on-tap that are all top notch, or wants to bring home a growler or six-pack of their masterworks. I love how non-uptown this place is. Real people of all stripes drink beer here. Make yourself one of them. The grub is good too!

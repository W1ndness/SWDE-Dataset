Best Pizza in Tampa
by Lauren (1 review)
March 05, 2010 - Likes it
My husband and I jokingly call ourselves "pizza aficionados" and we've tried a good number of the pizza shops in Tampa. This is by far the best pizza we've ever had!! They make the perfect not too thick, not too crispy crust, their sauce to cheese to dough ratio is spot on and speaking of their sauce, it's the perfect consistency...not too sweet, not too tart and just the right amount of garlic.
Their prices a superb for the quality of pizza you're getting. When they say "your pizza will be ready for pick up in 20 minutes", they mean it! No extra wait!
I can't say enough good things about this local shop.

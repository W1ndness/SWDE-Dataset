Great casual seafood
by Patrick (22 reviews)
August 10, 2010 - Likes it
There are three things you need to know about Waterfront:
1) The sushi is some of the best in town (second only to Miyabi 9). It's very good, and I wouldn't hesitate to take a sushi-lover there.
2) The seafood is consistently very good. Whether you want scallops, sea bass, or raw oysters, you can order with confidence that it will be fresh and prepared properly.
3) The prices are good. Compared to other fresh seafood places, the menu here is very reasonable.
That's about it. It's not a gourmet place. It doesn't have a seasonal menu. It doesn't serve a lot of really unique stuff. But if you want a piece of swordfish prepared simply and properly, or some delicious sushi rolls, you can't go wrong with Waterfront.

Not Worth The Hype
by Eezarf (9 reviews)
May 26, 2010
We are visiting Des Moines for the V A Golden Age Games and I had researched restaurants for the area. This one kept coming up, and since I love seafood, I decided we would try it. We went for lunch on Wednesday, May 26. I got 1/2 dozen oysters on the half-shell, a cup of seafood gumbo and an oyster po-boy. 3 of the 6 raw oysters were still attached to the shell. The crackers they gave to me to eat with them were all broken up. The oysters did not seem fresh. The seafood gumbo was fantastic. It was just amazing. The oyster po-boy had 4 oysters on it. Really? I paid $11.00 for a po-boy and got 4 oysters? The coleslaw that came with it was good though. There was way too much sauce on the po-boy which made it very messy. We will not be visiting this establishment again.

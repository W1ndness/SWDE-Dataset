Tasty and Filling
by Nicholas Rovisa (15 reviews)
May 27, 2010 - Likes it
I ate at Cibon last week. It was sweltering out, but we still opted to eat outside. The place was packed as I'm sure it always is and the people were nice-I wouldn't go so far as to say they were friendly though.
The cheese plate (Appetizer) was delicious, featuring a good mix of quality cheeses, and my shrimp panini (meal) was outstanding featuring just the right amount of goat cheese, not to mention their signature, superb dipping sauce.
Prices for food are fair for the area/quality, but the drink prices are a bit high IMO ($8 for a small mojito). Overall, I found Cafe Cibon to be an enjoyable setting with good food and I wouldn't hesitate to eat there again.

User reviews of Kairos Kafe

Really great food & people
by priesttotheking (28 reviews)
February 23, 2009 - Really likes it
Kairos is amazing. I've only been once and got the Marsala chicken which was RIDICULOUSLY good. Great bread served with their signature butter. Sides are incredible, company tomatoes and fried green tomatoes and mac and cheese are phenomenal. Very cool Christian atmosphere with Bible verses and stuff everywhere. The people are the nicest people ever. Kairos means "God's special moment," which I thought was cool. The building and ministry has a lot of history in this city and you can tell has had a lot of impact. Grab a lunch at Kairos and you will not be disappointed!

YUMMY
by MoonBaby (21 reviews)
March 16, 2010 - Really likes it
This is stick-to-your-ribs southern feel-good fare. Muffins with homemade strawberry butter get you started. Then look out for FRESH veggies and those fantastic fried green tomatoes and succulent crispy fried chicken fingers. Thank god I don't count calories because I want to pig out when I go to Kairos!

mobile review
by D.B. (23 reviews)
July 25, 2010 - Really likes it
Chaotic queuing at this location...they really need to come up with a more orderly and efficient process for seating customers. There was no set line per se. The experience can be rather off-putting. Had my usual grilled turkey burger and as usual it was perfect! Extremely tender and seasoned just right. My friend ordered the chicken & shrimp combo plate. I tried a bit of it and it was pretty good...had a rather bold smokey flavor to it. The chicken was nice and tender and it even had bits of nice smoked sausage included in the dish. Also sampled a piece of their key lime pie...it also was really good. At most places key lime is usually sickenly sweet...but this one had just enough sugar that it was just sweet enough. As with the Miami location I reviewed service was okay due to the sheer number of customers. Overall, the dining experience was the consistent Cheesecake Factory quality I've come to expect.

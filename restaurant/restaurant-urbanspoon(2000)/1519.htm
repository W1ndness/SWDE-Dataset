Name Change !
by Barney (486 reviews)
January 31, 2010
This weekend "The Grange Public House" officially became "The Marlay House". Same folks, same food, same everything. This was the result of somebody getting legal on them for use of the word "Grange" (hah! I said it... I'll say it again... "Grange"). Nice to see they had a sense of humor about this kind of foolishness and had a renaming contest. Nice folks whatever it's called.

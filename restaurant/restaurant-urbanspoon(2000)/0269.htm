Cajun Fries...yay
by NK (1 review)
March 30, 2009 - Likes it
So, I already knew what I was getting into when I got to Five guys I have been going to them for quite a while in DC and Virginia. It's a brilliant chain. It serves a really good burger with just about anything you ever wanted on it... I get the "little burger" with cheese, bacon, grilled onions, mushrooms, pickles and mustard. it's not the burger from your backyard grill and if you are a "the more meat and the thicker the meat patty the better...person" this place aint your thang. if you just love a good cooked burger with tons of stuff to put on it and the most amazing fries in the city...this is your place. A regular fry is enough for two... or one big eater... the cajun fries are absolutely spicy and Yum! i love it... and if you get bored with the burgers head over to mellow mushroom at the other end of the shopping mall...it's good too.

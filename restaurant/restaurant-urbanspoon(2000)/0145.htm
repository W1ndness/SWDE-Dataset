Excellent!
by Mark (8 reviews)
May 16, 2010 - Likes it
Me and my gf went here last night and it was excellent. We sat in the Tapas/Bar area which had a nice decor. The Bartender was our server and he was incredibly attentive.
Now on to the food. We ordered the spicy chorizo foccacia lamb lollipops chipotle chicken skewers crab cakes and the scallops.
The chorizo foccacia was quite good. Smoky sausage balanced well against sweet peppers and sun dried tomatoes. I wish the bottom was a bit crispier but it was very good. The lamb lollipops were the star of the show. Very tender and grilled to perfection. Although the bartender informed me that the lamb was grilled over a gas grill the lamb had a pronounced smoky flavor that was excellent. The chicken was so-so. It was grilled very well and had that same smoky flavor. But it was too much cumin and not enough chipotle in my opinion. Definitely needs to be hotter! The crab cakes were also excellent. Full of sweet crab and a nice crust they were delicious. Finally the scallops were superb. They had a nice sear on them and were served with delicious smoky bacon.
All told it was a great experience. I give it a 4 out of 5.

Recommend | Not helpful

A slice of Queen West
by paul mezhir (7 reviews)
March 01, 2010 - Likes it
I live in Toronto during the weekend and dine out often when in the city. Wine on Third and it's neighbor restaurant, Lola, make for a little slice of the big city right here in Niagara Falls. I didn't think I would ever see the day.
The ambiance is comfortable but not coddling....the stunning artwork on the wall by local artist Thomas Paul Asklar, is simply mesmerizing. The best part is that the food is excellent, and the staff makes for a truly warm evening.
I had a flatiron steak that was prepared a perfect medium-rare. The meat could have rested a couple more minutes as the plate was full of red juice before it made it to the table although the flavor could not have been better. Besides, I had the artwork and the crowd to look at while enjoying a delightful meal with my family and friends.
A delightful big-city experience right here in Niagara Falls. Yes, it's pricey, but the experience alone is worth the cost.
I highly recommend Wine on Third!

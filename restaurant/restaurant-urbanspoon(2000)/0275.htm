Message from Cap City Fine Diner and Bar...

American Comfort Food

  • Diner Food with Flair
  • Lunch and Dinner Daily
  • Happy Hour and Specials

As with any diner, at Cap City Fine Diner and Bar you feel immediately comfortable and at ease. Like no other diner, it?s artsy and cool. The Cap City menu follows suit, with recognizable favorites like meatloaf receiving unexpected and delightful upscale twists, alongside trendier chef-driven dishes with big flavors. Desserts at this Cameron Mitchell Restaurant are retro-cool, taste better than you remember and are showstopper-sized.

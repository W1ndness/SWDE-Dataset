So flavorful!
by Riverrunner (217 reviews)
February 14, 2010 - Really likes it
UPDATE: Every dish I've had here is just excellent. Too bad their portion sizes are somewhat lacking. But their pleasant ambiance alone is enough to make up for that. Their menu is massive, with both American Chinese style food and more authentic noodle dishes as well. The hovering servers can get very annoying at times and even downright rude trying to rush you through dinner to free up your table. It gets crazy busy here on the weekends, since people come from all over the Wasatch front. But it is well worth the wait. I recommend their Honey Walnut Shrimp and asking for Five Taste Chicken.

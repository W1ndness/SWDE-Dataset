True Gelato in the Old Pueblo
by The Owlclub (5 reviews)
May 02, 2010 - Likes it
The rows of colorful and flavorful gelatos reminded me of my vacation in Rome. Of course the flavor, while quite good is not up to the gelato you can find in Rome, or in San Diego, for that matter. But, for Tucson, Arizona, it is pretty darn amazing: Tart citrus, slightly bitter dark chocolate and smooth/crunchy stracciatella.
The team working the counter can either be super attentive or super oblivious depending on the day, so be prepared to roll that part of it. And the prices are high enough to reduce the temptation to come in daily basis.

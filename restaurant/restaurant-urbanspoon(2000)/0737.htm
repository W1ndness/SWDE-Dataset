Get. The. Crabcake.
by beanwave (14 reviews)
January 12, 2010 - Really likes it
I eat here almost every Sunday because their brunch is delicious. Get the crabcake. Get the crabcake. Get the crabcake. I'm a Maryland crab snob, and this is almost as good as a Smith Island crabcake. The potatoes crackle when you bite them, only to be pillowy soft and delicious on the inside. I love seeing what the weekly pancake will be; past seasonal options have included gingerbread and pumpkin. My boyfriend loves the eggs cochon. The omelettes are pretty great, too.
Plus, the service is just as good as the food. I like coming here because the staff always remembers who I am and what I order. To me, that makes the occasional wait well worth it.

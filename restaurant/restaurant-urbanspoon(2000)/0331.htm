I was pleased to discover a Pho restaurant in this neighborhood. The Pho broth tasted great and I will definitely be back. One area for improvement is the noodles. They use thinner noodles than the type used in other Vietnamese paces; the noodles seemed a little soggy and did not have the great chew that I really like. For some reason they pre-cut the noodles - I prefer the longer noodles that requires a little slurping and chopstick dexterity.

Not too bad. . .
by Franklin (2 reviews)
August 28, 2009 - Likes it
So I love Pho and had to try this place as soon as I heard it was open. My first experience was pretty good, the staff was friendly, the food was good and the restaurant is pretty nice. I ordered a Pho with meatballs and brisket and rare beef and the Viet Egg Rolls to go . . . both were delicious and pretty good sized portions (especially for take out). FYI - If you order take out they give you one to go box with all the veggies, meet etc and then a giant container with the broth . .
A few cons - I went a second time for lunch around 1130a and was disappointed that they were closed even though the store hours said open at 11. Also, they Pho selection is kinda limited compared to other places and only comes in one size.
Overall - I'd say 4 outta 5 stars.

Pho so so
by charlie c (3 reviews)
May 23, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The pho selection is very limited and the meat was not fresh...the steak had a brown tint around the edges...not a good sign. The soup stock was made with generous ladles of msg. I'm pretty sensitive to this food enhancer and noticed the side effects immediately. I tried three orders of dim sum...shrimp dumpling, pork dumpling and the bbq buns. At 5 bucks a pop that's 15...a bit pricey. Why would I come back here for dim sum when I can get fresher, friendlier service at Star Kitchen. For Pho I prefer Pho 79...the portions are always generous, their meat is always fresh and service is quick. Besides this place is so NOT kid friendly. I would not recommend coming, it is closer to home but a good meal and friendly service is worth an extra 10 minutes drive.

I had the Tamarind Chicken as recommended by the waitress, and it was fantastic! Got it spicy, and it got me a little hot. Quite enjoyable. The flavor was a little sweet. The dish included plenty of veggies and chicken.

The better older brother to Pop!
by RFGuy55 (42 reviews)
June 03, 2009 - Likes it
The newer Pop! in DT St. Paul gets all the press, but this Pop! has far better, more consistent food. The salads are excellent, and the daily specials are usually very good as well. It's hardly ever crowded at lunchtime, but the service and food all always top-notch.

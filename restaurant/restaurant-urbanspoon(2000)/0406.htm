Big Apple in the Bay
by sheepswool (6 reviews)
August 07, 2010
Something about the people working there with a hearty Long Island accent is reassuring. Sat at the bar, had a couple cold beers some garlic knots and a meat lovers. Mon-Fri 2 for 1 domestics/draft/rails for $3. Garlic knots were tasty while waiting, good service. Had a medium meat lovers and finished that and garlic knots between myself and one other, we were stuffed. We will be back for sure to check out some other pies.

Delicious!
by Sarah (2 reviews)
February 18, 2010 - Really likes it
The pizzas might look a bit expensive at about $16.00 for a large, but they are HUGE (enough to feed three or four people with quite the appetites, especially if you get garlic knots, too) and extremely delicious. Everything about the pizza is good- the crust, the sauce, the toppings. Yum! I've been here twice, but only had the meat lover's. All of the others sound great, too. The non-specialty ones are also cheaper. I've also had the garlic knots, which are scrumptious.
The pasta entrees looked delicious, and are not badly priced. I just love pizza!

Can't Argue With #1 Ranking
by Don Incognito (14 reviews)
March 17, 2010 - Likes it
Pretty solid choice for #1 restaurant in Fresno. Nothing fancy here, just some good comfort food at a relatively cheap price. It's not a traditional sit-down restaraunt. Dog House Grill caters to the plebian--it being located near the Fresno State campus. You order your food, serve your on soda, and after your number is called, grab your own food. The inside is cavernous, like a giant cafeteria--adding to its communal, for-the-masses appeal. There are projectors and TVs going wall-to-wall surrounding the entire restaurant with different sporting events.
My only knock would be that it's sometimes so busy that finding a table can be a hassle (you aren't seated, it's basically a free-for-all).

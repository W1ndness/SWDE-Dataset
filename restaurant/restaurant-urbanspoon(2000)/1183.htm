Message from La Rustica

Cozy, Romantic Italian Dining Overlooking the Puget Sound

  • West Seattle's Hidden Treasure
  • Northern Italian Cuisine
  • Catering for Weddings/Parties

If you venture a bit further into West Seattle, drive out the scenic Harbor Avenue beyond Alki Point and to Beach Drive, you'll be handsomely rewarded with a dining experience like none other at La Rustica. A hidden treasure with an amazing view of the Puget Sound, La Rustica has been serving Seattle authentic Northern Italian cuisine for more than 12 years. Reminiscent of the small cafes in Italy, our dining room is cozy.

Come stay warm inside our cozy, romantic restaurant and try favorites such as filet mignon al gorgonzola and marinated prawns wrapped in pancetta. Pair your dinner with our favorite dry Italian Lambrusco wine. Reservations are accepted for parties of 6 to 10. La Rustica also specializes in catering for weddings and small parties. Call for reservations at our quaint Italian restaurant, La Rustica.

On the Whole, Better than Italy
by Bob's Stomach (173 reviews)
January 27, 2010 - Likes it
When my brother visits from NY I never take him to any of our local Italian restaurants because he is one spoiled Italian-food-maiven--but I would take him to La Rustica. The cozy and tasteful ambiance makes you feel immediately at home, while at the same time transporting you far away. An excellent, romantic little spot where a guy could even propose marriage (as one of our family members did). As it's night time in the dead of winter, we didn't get a view but in the summer it must be spectacular around sunset.
The four of us each had a different dish including two house specials (one cod another lemon sole) , saltimbocca, and vitello scampi. My scampi was delicious, with the prawns wrapped in thin slices of veal, as was the accompanying pasta and sauteed vegetables. The presentation was colorful and appealing and the food measured up to its looks. Franscesco was an attentive and amiable server and did a great job of taking care of our needs. All in all a delightful experience.

Come for the Oysters, Stay for the Burger
by Jacqueline Church (13 reviews)
March 14, 2009 - Likes it
That's right: the burger. This is one of the unsung burger heroes in Boston. Gorgeously prepared, served with two fried oysters on top.
The Chowder - light not thick and gloppy like the pedestrian variety served everywhere - this has the perfect balance of oyster, cream and potatoes that make you wish the bowl would go on forever.
The lobster roll comes in two varieties the more common cold version is anything but common here. Still, the one that will make you come back is the hot buttered lobster roll.
Fried clams to make Ipswich jealous and Bellinis and Prosecco.
Of course, sparkling fresh and expertly shucked raw bar is the real draw.

hispanic influenced cuisine
by kd (75 reviews)
September 18, 2009 - Likes it
In addition to Spanish dishes like tortilla, Ibiza features Latin American influences as well. A must is the jalapeno margarita. Thinly sliced jalapenos settle to the bottom of the glass and add just a hint of "bite" to the cocktail.

great service, satisfactory food
by PickledPinkish (21 reviews)
May 16, 2010 - Likes it
I went for a friend's birthday and when we walked in, the waitpeople were wonderful and accommodating. They even gave us free appetizers since business was slow at that time. Our waiter reminded me of a grandfather.
I had one of the specials-- scallops with a potato soup/sauce. The flavor of the scallops blended too much with the sauce, making the whole dish uninteresting. I also had a bite of my other friends pasta dish, which was pretty good, but tasted like something I could do at home. My friends steak was very good however. Like other high end New Haven restaurants, Ibiza was pretty good but not worth the price. I would still rather go into New York for the really awesome restaurants.

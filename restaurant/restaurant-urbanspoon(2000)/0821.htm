Worth going to once at least!
by Meredi (111 reviews)
April 24, 2010 - Likes it
The vegetarian samusa soup was really yummy - I would definitely have it again. The tea salad had a very unique flavour, which really grew on me. Definitely worth trying.
Oh, and the coconut mango drink was like mango lassi, but coconut-y! Awesomeness.
The maitre d' guy was really nice, as were all the servers. I'm not sure I'd have waited an hour (especially when their sister restaurant, B-Star, is just down the street and serves all the most popular dishes), but we waited 15-20 minutes and it was definitely worth it.

Fresh and Homestyle Italian
by Robin (18 reviews)
March 28, 2010 - Likes it
I'd recommend asking the chef to make something for you, if you're not interested in anything on the menu. There are vegetarian and vegan options, but the chef will also prepare something special for you. If you want variety, request an appitizer size portion of more than one item. Homestyle Italian - the chef uses his grandmother's recipe. They grow herbs and vegetables behind the restaurant in the yard. The only drawback, in my opinion, was the noise level. Because it's in a house with wood floors, the sound carries in the small space and it can get quite loud, depending on the crowd, even though it's a bit upscale. Overall, it was incredibly delicious, and the service was terrific!

the good ole days
by Drexler McStyles (206 reviews)
August 13, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Monte Vista has seen better days. The bartenders that were entertaining and made for an interesting visit (Angelo, Drew and Nicole) are gone now, leaving us with spaced out drones who seem to be getting paid to text message and watch baseball while you are looking at your empty glass and wondering what happened.
The quality of the food has been on the decline for quite a while and what was once quality pub food is now cheap garbage. I guess thats what happens when the guys who wash the dishes also make your food because the owner is too cheap to hire a real cook.
Oh well, at least we have the memories.

Why it's the best bar in Nob Hill
by Stoney (4 reviews)
June 30, 2010 - Really likes it
If you are in the mood to drink and eat in a relaxed atmosphere (read: No attitude about dressing up.), the Monte Vist Fire Station (MVFS) will serve you a very good drink for a very good price, considering where it's located.
I'd actually have to admit that the MVFS and Gecko's are tied for the top two bars in Nob Hill if you're in the mood for this type of atmosphere. I'm just upset there's no free Wi-Fi and limited sports channels at MVFS, even though there's a ton of TV's. There are the "standard" dish channels available, but no packages or special sports channels. Something Two Fools has on MVFS, but that's about it. The food is very good for a bar and the food specials during the week are a steal. 22 cent wings, 75 cent tacos, $4 ruben meal is hard to beat.

Can't Wait to go Back....
by amylayton (1 review)
December 07, 2008 - Likes it
This restaurant has a pleasant and casual atmosphere.  The patio is PERFECT!!  We eat here at least once a week.  Our experience here is always exceptional.  Severs are very friendly and efficient.  Food is delicious......best chicken tortilla soup, chicken florentine, salmon, pork tenderloin (the list goes on), and the best variety of desserts.  Make sure you save room for dessert, or do what we do and take it home with you for later.  The chocolate cake and carrot cake are the best!!  
I've used their catering services too.  You can even buy a whole chocolate or carrot cake for special occasions.

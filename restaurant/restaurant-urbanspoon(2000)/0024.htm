best chinese in AK
by prime8phd (15 reviews)
June 01, 2009 - Likes it
Alaska doesn't do chinese well. Usually, it's all deep-fried, fast-food quality pseudo-Cantonese crap. Charlie's is the only place I have found that makes reasonably authentic chinese food, and it's good, cheap, and fresh. I love their wonton soup, and they make a sticky rice wrapped in banana leaf that is really spectacular. Avoid the short ribs, though. I wish they had a real restaurant instead of just a deli, but it's still the best thing we've got for Chinese in AK.

Recommend | Not helpful

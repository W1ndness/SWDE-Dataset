outstanding
by lizbomze (1 review)
August 10, 2009 - Likes it
An absolute gem of a place. The from-scratch, local food, charming indoor and outdoor seating (and serving platters), and service is the best anywhere, especially for the money. Get the raspberry scones with curd and creme fraiche, sweet potato pecan and key lime pie, house-cured salmon on Rolings bagels, Lancaster ham and brie...oh, the list goes on...

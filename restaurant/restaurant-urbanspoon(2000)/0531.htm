Downsized
by m watson (2 reviews)
May 15, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I've downsized my fondness for Paulo & Bill's in proportion to their downsizing of entree portions. It's such a great location, comfortable, a favorite neighborhood place to gather, and the menu has always pleased. But the last several visits (Feb-March 2009) were a huge disappointment. Increased prices for greatly reduced portions. I'm not a "bigger is better" fan, but the shrinking plate was really noticeable, by all members of our party. A spoonful of vegetables for a side? A fish fillet that could have gotten lost on the bread plate? Bottom line: too $$$ for too little. Most restaurants are trying to lure customers in creative ways due to the economy. I guess they don't need to. I hope they re-think this, I'd love to put P & B back on my go-to list.

Burritos Way Too Big, In My Opinion
by Allison Miranda (63 reviews)
October 01, 2009 - Likes it
The ONLY reason I said 'Likes It' is because they have really good guacamole.
My chicken burrito (if you could call it a CHICKEN burrito, since there was such a small amount of chicken on it) was HUGE. All it did was fall apart while I was eating it (with both hands). After I finished eating it, there was enough filling left in the foil to make another burrito! I don't know who came up with this "big burrito" idea, but it's not cool. I prefer quality and sufficient ingredients over hugeness. Other than for the guacamole and chips, I don't think I'll go back.

Recommend | Not helpful

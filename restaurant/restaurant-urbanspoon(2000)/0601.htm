Solid Citizen
by jim schnobrich (5 reviews)
December 29, 2009 - Likes it
Houston's is a solid choice, it does not disappoint. Houston's is a small chain and can be found in several cities. However, it never feels like a chain. It feels like a good quality restaurant.
The Houston's in Pasadena is a mid century, one story low structure. It is a little bit dark (bring your reading glasses). The fare is American - burgers, steaks, fresh fish with some sushi choices.
The kitchen is open, allowing one to see how well run this establishment is. The service is friendly and the wait staff knows the menu and wine list and are always helpful with a suggestion.
Houston's a good solid Pasadena citizen.

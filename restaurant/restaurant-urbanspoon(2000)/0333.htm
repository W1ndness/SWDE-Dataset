Great Sushi, Great Staff
by Pizza Lover (2 reviews)
December 07, 2007 - Likes it
I am a huge supporter of the Independents.  Wasabi Sushi Bar is a welcome edition to the Lakewood Belmar area.   This round beautiful building is right next to the ice rink.  The owners started in St Louis where they have 2 locations which have won "Best Sushi Bar In St Louis 2006, 07.  No need to go downtown anymore for fantastic sushi.  I mean this is really the best sushi I have ever had.  The Sashimi fillets are so fresh and the fillets cuts are very generous.  The soups and salads are great.  
So we all know how expensive sushi can get.  If your tight on funds there lunch menu has a great selection and very reasonable prices.  Better yet there happy hour 4:30 to 6:00 and 10-12 on Fri and Sat. 2 for 1 sushi unbelievable prices on rolls, appetizers and drinks specials. $1 hot sake, $2 Wasabi Bomb(sake and Coors), $2 drafts,$3.50 Jack, Stoli, Beefeater, wine.  Squid salad $3, Dynamite $5. The happy hour menu is quite extensive and to long to list here.
The bathrooms are something to see.

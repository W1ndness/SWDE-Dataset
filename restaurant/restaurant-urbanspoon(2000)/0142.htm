Neighborhood Gem!
by Anne Marie (1 review)
August 06, 2009
When telling people you are heading to Bethlehem Park for dinner some look at you like you have 2 heads…and then when you take them there they thank you over and over for the best meal ever!
This hidden gem offers some of the best Italian food in the area in a very comfortable and welcoming atmosphere…like being in someone's home.
From the homemade salad dressings (the Chianti is my fav), to the meatballs (which are bigger than your fist and cooked to perfection) to the homemade desserts (which you should eat first if you have a sweet tooth because the baker outdoes herself daily), OH, I can't forget to mention the entrees…each time I have been for dinner I suffer through the decision making process! The lasagna (usually a special) is 3 layers of gigantic goodness and is something you should try for sure. The gnocchi (my favorite) is made fresh and is rich and delish! Eat with the meatball or with sausage and hot peppers if you like some heat. You'll be glad you did. I've never heard anyone say they didn't like their meal! One more great, and for me the most important reason for dining at Mulberry's…the service! Very attentive servers are there for you throughout your meal and are not intrusive at all and yet, they manage to not miss a beat. I really can't say enough great things about this place…go try it! You'll be glad you did!

Shhh, it's great.
by Steven Raines (24 reviews)
July 17, 2010 - Really likes it
Tucked away in Lackawana, you probably wouldn't run into it without someone telling you about it. Mulberry's is the kind of place you hate to tell people about for fear that it will get too crowded for you to get a table. But the food is just too good to pass of the opportunity to share with someone you like.
Everything is wonderful and homemade and the atmosphere is fantastic. It's definitely one of Buffalo's best "real" Italian places.
This place was featured on the Diner's, Drive-ins, and Dives on Food Network. You can see this segment here: http://budurl.com/nu6f.

Superb
by Giovanni (1 review)
March 21, 2010 - Likes it
Everything I have heard about this place is true. It's a very busy place and for very good reason. The food is among the best I have had anyplace. We got treated to a visit to our table by the chef and it just made our already enjoyable experience that much better. Our server Sue was very friendly and very knowledgeable about the menu. The sauce is very reminiscent of the type of sauce I had in Naples. Very good and very fresh, not overseasoned or overpowering. I would recommend this restaurant to anyone.

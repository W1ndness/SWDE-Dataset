Can you say éclair?
by Sparrow (2 reviews)
December 11, 2008 - Likes it
Don't let the name fool you-- it truly is a working Dairy Farm and Bakery. The cream pastries here are AMAZING. My favorite is the mini éclair, but the strawberry boats and cream puffs are equally incredible. I've never been fond of cake, but the fresh cakes made here will make a fan out of anyone. And did I mention the hermits? There's always fresh milk in the refrigerator cases and you can even see the cows being milked if you show up at the right time. Did I mention that the pastry is to die for?

Great Place
by BobW (1 review)
January 29, 2010 - Likes it
CrystalCakes, you are reviewing the wrong Wrights. Wright's Dairy Farm doesn't have chicken. That's Wright's Farm. Both are in northern RI, but are completely different. The prices at Wright's Dairy Farm are very reasonable, the selection (especially of items with real cream) is excellent and the taste is very good. I wouldn't say that it's the best I've ever tasted, but it is VERY good. Even the pizza is excellent. Presently this is our favorite bakery and we travel from Newport to go there.

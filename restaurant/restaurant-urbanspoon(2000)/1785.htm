My Favorite Sushi Place
by Thogar (1 review)
December 19, 2009 - Likes it
I've eaten at most sushi restaurants in the area and you can't find better sushi anywhere. Only a few others are as good, but the prices can't be beat at Famous Sushi. They even have an "All You Can Eat" option now and have doubled the size of the restaurant. Service is prompt, professional and friendly. The gentlemen behind the sushi bar are very helpful if you have any questions and the restaurant is immaculately clean.

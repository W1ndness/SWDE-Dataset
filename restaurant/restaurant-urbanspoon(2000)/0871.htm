Best salads in town
by Hawks (6 reviews)
February 01, 2009 - Likes it
I seize every opportunity to eat here, but I still get excited every time I go. Known for their salads (at least in my mind), the food is somehow just a notch above what I get at most restaurants. The coconut chicken salad is probably the most popular dish. I've had the salmon, asian, and grilled chicked and they were all great. I've only been here for lunch - it tends to be louder and more crowded than usual, but wait times were rarely > 15 minutes.

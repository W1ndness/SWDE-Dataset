THE ONLY HOTDOG RESTAURANT IN CINCINNATI
by Andrew (3 reviews)
November 13, 2008 - Likes it
Okay, here's the scoop...
Everyone who has ever ventured outside of Cincinnati knows that there are cities, towns, and states that actually sell HOTDOGS not "Coneys".  A coney is a chicken lip, raccoon butt, and pork ear hot dog.  Usually a coney is covered in some god-awful "cincinnati chilli".  -Its called cincinnati chilli because it is nothing like real chilli.  -You should see the reaction of people who come to cincinnati and order a chilli dog that are from other states - they are horrified at the taste.
ANYWAY - back to the review of Hot Doggin' It...
This restaurant is the only, ONLY, hotdog restaurant in all of Cincinnati - and yes they have real hotdogs.  -Not just all beef hotdogs but vienna beef hotdogs.  This is a special type of all beef hot dog that has a natural casing that gives it a sort of "snap" when you bite off a piece.  They are incredible.  Hot Doggin' It is also the only place in Cincinnati to get "almost" a genuine Chicago Dog.  
What is a Chicago Dog you ask?  Its a hotdog on a poppy seed bun with two tomato slices, a dill pickle spear, mustard, raw onions, relish, two sport peppers, celery salt and two small cut cucumber slices.  -I mention that Hot Doggin' It "almost" has a genuine chicago dog because, for some reason, they chose not to put the cucumber slices on.
Anyway - this is the best hotdog out there and they make it incredibly well.  Check them out!  You will be happy to taste a hot dog that doesnt have 17 different animals in it (like you would get at skiline, goldstar, or sonic).  Bon Appetit!

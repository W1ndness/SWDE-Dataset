Message from Graziano's Pizzeria

Graziano's Pizzeria has been your families Pizzeria and Italian restaurant since 1987. Casual, simple and friendly. Great pizza and an atmosphere that makes you feel right at home. Come on in and let our staff treat you with the best pizza you'll ever have.

With 180 seats, Graziano's pizzeria is the ideal setting for your casual party.  Call ahead for reservations.

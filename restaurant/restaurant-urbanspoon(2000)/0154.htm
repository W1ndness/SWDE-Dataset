Eileens is Amazing!
by Joel Malley (1 review)
April 09, 2010
I've been going to Eileen's for decades. It is extraordinary. The almond danish is awesome, the jelly and peanut donuts are delicious. I bring in donuts to my students and they rave about them.
Above all, the staff is so nice. My son always walks away with a free cookie.

Disappointing
by Jennifer Kowtalo (1 review)
January 22, 2010 - Doesn't like it
My son and I just moved in up the road. We were excited to see the bakery and were not familiar with them before. I am very disappointed. The prices are high and the quality poor. We've tried several products so far: butter rolls, homestyle white bread, eclairs, pastry hearts and a dutch apple coffee cake. We were very unhappy. The flavors were dull, the pastry very dry and unappetizing. Our purchases went in the trash. I'll go back to Wegmans. They're cheaper and the quality is better.

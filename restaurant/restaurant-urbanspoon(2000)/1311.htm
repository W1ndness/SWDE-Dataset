Falafel!!
by Michael Ahlberg (2 reviews)
July 28, 2009 - Likes it
I lived in New York for five years before moving back to DC. While in NY, I tried falafel on a street cart for the first time. After that, it was at shope, street fairs, corner of 46th and 5th...you get the point.
This finally brings it all home for me.
The first time I went in here the guy behind the counter was really really helpfuly explaining all the sauces and discussed their origins and when they were made (that morning). Not waht I'd expected at all.
The owner later came out and said I was gonna burn my "f*&^ing" mouth off using all the hot sauce I did.
A great experience overall--the sandwiches were to die for!
Is it expensive? Yeah, kinda. But that's what you get when they have to pay rent in Adam's Morgan and they're the only decent place for falafel in DC.

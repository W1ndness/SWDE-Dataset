Excellent stuff!
by Hobbes (198 reviews)
June 04, 2010 - Likes it
Took a couple coworkers [we're all out of Indiana; I was born/raised here though] here recently - hot and humid St Louis afternoon - after a lunch at Pappy's BBQ... what a treat, frozen custard, multiple varied flavors after a very good lunch. Surprisingly was not much of a line, got our dessert, and tasted the scrumptious frozen icon of the Gateway City. The looks on those not expecting to see their custard handed to 'em upside down is cool too ;) Is it the best frozen custard... I don't know... but it is however durn good.

Every trip I amke to St. Louis includes a stop at Ted Drewes to get a concrete or a hot fudge sundae. I love it when they turn the concrete upside down to prove how thick the concrete is.

Uncaring Staff
by Kathy Estes (3 reviews)
March 28, 2010 - Doesn't like it
In February several ladies that I work with and I met there for lunch. There was still snow in the parking lot and an elderly lady had fallen as she was coming in to eat. Thankfully one of the ladies I work with was able to help her up and come in with her. Upon telling the hostess that the elderly lady had fallen in the parking lot the hostess quickly told us that "that isn't our fault..it's up to the property owner to clear the snow"....not a I'm so sorry that happened, how are you, nothing!! Made me think this was a snooty place to eat and I won't be back!!

Delightful!
by sassy (1 review)
May 26, 2010
I've been taking my daughters to the Tea Room since 1994. Whenever they had a day off school or good report card, we went. During college breaks, we went. Now, years later, we always find time to eat here when visiting our old hometown. We always order the Tea Room Medley & Spice Tea & get more muffins to take home for breakfast. Lots of wonderful memories for us. You won't be disappointed! Love the crepe dessert too.

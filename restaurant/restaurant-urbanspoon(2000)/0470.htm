Best in Houston - Seriously!
by Jeff Zimmerman (8 reviews)
January 28, 2009 - Really likes it
Frenchie's is Zagat rated and is honestly some of the best Italian food in Houston! All the astronauts eat here and there is memorabilia all over the walls. Go for lunch and order at the counter. Frenchie will call your number over the intercom when your food is ready for pickup. Come for dinner and it's full table service. The food is authentic and awesome! Try the Chicken Parmesan, Chicken Alfredo, and the Lasagna.

Good food. Crap service.
by savoy99 (19 reviews)
September 19, 2009 - Doesn't like it
This restaurant likes to boast about it's tradition with astronauts in the Clear Lake area. You think after 20+ years they would know how to provide service. People - their service is BAD. You will wait an inordinate and illogical amount of time.
You will wait if you dine-in for someone to recognize your existence. You will wait inside for someone to put your order together if you phone-in for take out.
YOU. WILL. WAIT.
Do not go here if you are in any sort of time crunch. Do not go here if you had a bad day and are feeling a bit impatient. Do not go here if you have to be anywhere at a certain time.
You will be ignored. You will stand/sit with your thumb up your butt as you wait to give them your hard earned money.
There are far too many restaurants in the Clear Lake area for this sort of customer service to be acceptable.
I'm never going back.

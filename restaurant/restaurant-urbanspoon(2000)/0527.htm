A hidden happy hour gem!
by SparkleAggie (45 reviews)
September 08, 2009 - Really likes it
I could definitely write a nice, long review about what an incredible experience we had for dinner at Bluestem. From food, to service, everything was impeccable and impressive. We had expected that, with all of the glowing recommendations we had gotten and reviews we had read.
What is unexpected, is their fantastic happy hour deals! Tuesday through Friday from 5-7, they have 1/2 off all food on their lounge menu and drink specials. For example, Wagyu beef tartare for $5?! Really? A large plate of P.E.I mussels steamed in boulevard wheat for $7?! Really? Calling all foodies on a budget!

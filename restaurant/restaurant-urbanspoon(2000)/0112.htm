User reviews of Taziki's Colonnade

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.
Always good
by priesttotheking (28 reviews)
February 26, 2009 - Likes it
Taziki's in the Colonnade is a sure bet for great food all day, any day. Lamb is good, friday special, chicken, rice, polenta, and tabouli are all amazing, pretty much anything on the menu is great. One of my favorite places to eat in the entire city. Please go here if you haven't, you will not be disappointed!

by Charlie (3 reviews)
July 20, 2008 - Likes it
Nothing is better than going to Taziki's on a Saturday to get the Friday special.  Penne pasta rich with chicken, tomato, basi, feta, and mixed with greens! YUM!

mobile review
by Kathryn (1 review)
February 14, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I was dissapointed. The gyro was not from shaved lamb. It was more of a stir fry variety. The dolmades had an abundance of curry. They were not very good. The taziki is missing something. Garlic maybe. The prices are moderate but you definitely do not get what you pay for. I would rate this place a 4 on a scale of 10. If you're looking for authentic Greek, look elsewhere.

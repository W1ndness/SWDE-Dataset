October 10, 2009
by Valerie Reiss (1 review)
October 11, 2009
It was unfortunate for me, that I took out of town guests to Della Rose's on 10-10-09. The bar was poorly staffed, making it extremely hard to get service. At the lower end of the bar, the barmaid spent more time talking to a gentleman, then in doing her job. Myself and approx. 4 others stood at the bar, while others came up behind us, and got immedialte service. Apparently they were friends or regulars, therefore, the new customers where shunned. Once we did get service, it was as though we were bothing them. Being employed in that area, I have had several business lunches at Della's, with no problem. This unfortunate event, has made a bad impression with me, along with being embarrased in front of friends.
Hopefully it was an isolated case, however, I will have second thoughts about returning.
Thanks and best regards

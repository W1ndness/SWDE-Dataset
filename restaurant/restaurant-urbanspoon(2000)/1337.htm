It was okay. Great service, average food.
by Spunky (42 reviews)
February 15, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Went there for Valentines Day and had an average meal with excellent service. But it's nice to see a non-chain restaurant open up in my neighborhood. We ordered a couple glasses of wine, Ribeye Steak with crabmeat/asparagus sauce and a traditional Prime Rib. Our meats were cooked to order, but I was let down by the lack of quality in the meat. We're used to having nice thick meats from Costco and this was no where near as good. The whipped potatoes were a little cold and dry too. I think I enjoyed the side vegetable the most on my plate!
We also ordered carrot cake (delicious!) to round out the meal with a coffee. Our bill came out to nearly $75 for 2 people. For that price, I'll just buy some steaks at Costco and fire up the grill. Plus my mashed potatoes will be fluffy, hot and creamy too.

This place is okay at best. Food is decent but not great and not worth the price your asked to pay. For the price you should get a lot more. No salad or even bread given with meal. Wine is way over priced as well. Service was good but seemed quite stuffy. Staff should be aware of wine origin and not pass along incorrect information, such as where a wine is made. Not critical but telling a customer wrong information leads one to believe staff is not well informed and knowledgeable. I do not recommend Black Olive.

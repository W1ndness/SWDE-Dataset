Papa Joe's Italian Eatery is an Italian Restaurant in Rochester, NY. Papa Joe's Italian Eatery is an eat-in restaurant or a take-out restaurant. Papa Joe's Italian Eatery offers drive-thru service. Papa Joe's Italian Eatery caters Italian food. Papa Joe's Italian Eatery offers pasta specialities and speciality sandwiches. Papa Joe's Italian Eatery offer chicken entrees, veal entrees and seafood entrees. Papa Joe's Italian Eatery offers homemade pasta.

This is the real thing!
by BoodlesTonic (18 reviews)
December 29, 2009 - Likes it
Yes, it's a small place but that's OK. Maybe it will discourage people from stopping in and it's crowded enough now!
The calamari is to die for....lightly breaded and fried and served with banana peppers and marinara on the side. Both the red and marinara sauces are wonderful. The scampi sauce is heavenly!
Drive up take out window is great when the place is too packed.

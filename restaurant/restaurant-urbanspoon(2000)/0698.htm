Sushi is not so bad
by BizzNessMPLS (127 reviews)
August 19, 2009 - Likes it
I have never been the biggest fan of sushi, typically limiting myself to veggie sushi rolls, which is not sushi. I decided to join a friend for happy hour and let him do the picking since he is more versed. We ordered the happy hour special which consisted of your standard sushi rolls with tuna, some sea bass sashimi, and yellowtail nigiri. We also ordered some edamame as a snack. All was really good. I had a little trouble with the sashimi due to the texture, but hopefully as time goes by, this will become delicious.

Unique, but not worth the trip
by Jonathan Wantuch (11 reviews)
July 01, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Fat Cat can definitely be considered unique, but I find no reason to go back for a second visit. Their drink menu, while containing some unique and unfamiliar elements including cane sugar cola, a few of craft beers, and their widely talked about wine menu, contained nothing common or familiar. (A friend of mine was disappointed when he ordered a Jack and Coke.) I got the impression that the owners were trying to push a snobbish agenda rather than fill customer requests. In addition, the food was disappointing. The pizza crust was thin and burnt and lacked any flavor along with their sauce. To top it off, the atmosphere was to loud to cary on any conversation with my friends. Ultimately, I'd have to recommend staying clear of Fat Cat unless you have good reason to go in.

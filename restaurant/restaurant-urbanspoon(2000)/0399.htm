User reviews of Krolls West

Don't tell my wife.
by Barney (485 reviews)
August 29, 2008 - Likes it
I don't get through Green Bay as much these days as I used to, but as far as I'm concerned Kroll's West is a required stop.
Not at all flashy, but great food for the dollar.  I may not admit it in polite company, but I always go for the Butter Burger with a side of chili/cheese fries.  
This is a place for solid Diner food with really friendly folks working the tables.

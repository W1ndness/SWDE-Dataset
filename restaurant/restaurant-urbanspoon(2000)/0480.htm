moving soon..slightly
by J (91 reviews)
May 20, 2010 -
Mass Ave. Bazbeaux moving - just a little
By Dana Hunsinger
Posted: May 19, 2010Comments (15) Recommend E-mail Print A A
Bazbeaux Pizza, a Massachusetts Avenue mainstay, is moving - but just down the street to a fancier address.
Bazbeaux has signed a long-term lease to fill 4,732 square feet of retail space on the first floor of Three Mass Condos.
Just last month, a condo there sold for more than $1 million.
The restaurant's move will allow for more seating, not only inside, but in an outdoor café as well. The current location is about 3,500 square feet.
Even more critical for the business, however, is space for a bigger kitchen," said Deb Borchelt, co-owner.
"We are having to turn away business, which we do frequently," she said. "All four ovens are full of pizza and we have nowhere else to put more. This will really help our business."
Bazbeaux, which has two other locations in Broad Ripple and Carmel, will immediately begin building out the space and plans to open by the end of the year.
The Ball & Biscuit, a new upscale bar and lounge will open this summer and be a neighbor to the new Bazbeaux location.

average at best
by DC (1 review)
March 25, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Bazbeaux is an ok place for pizza. Perhaps the hype surrounding the place skewed my expectations, but every visit to Bazbeaux has been met with indifference. The wheat crust is average, and generally indistinguashable from the other crust. There is nothing special about the toppings, and the pizza itself is middle of the road. The service is average to subpar, and the pizza lags behind other local pizza in Indianapolis. It is not that I don't like the pizza, it is just very average pizza; and falls well below of the hype.

Mmmm :)
by Sierra (13 reviews)
July 30, 2010 - Really likes it
I can't say I've ever been inside. My husband always brings home pizza from Pizza Cottage. We've gotten carry-out about 5 times now. It has been wonderful every time. They have a wonderful selection of fresh, large ingredients, fair prices, and tasty creations on their menu that you can't get at nearby pizza places. The quality is awesome and my pizzas are hardly greasy (which I think american yellow cheese and pepperonis are usually the culprit of grease puddles).
I hope they keep this up and we will keep using their service. :)

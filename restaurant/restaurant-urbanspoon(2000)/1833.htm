Killer tacos
by Charlie (183 reviews)
June 24, 2010 - Likes it
I've passed this place a hundred times and chuckled, "Camp Taco, really?" Yesterday I saw the Urbanspoon reviews, however, and decided to give it a shot. Yet another Birmingham favorite served well by sage advice: don't judge a book by its cover. Camp Taco is awesome.
I had one beef brisket taco w/ grilled pablano peppers and onions, and one grilled fish taco. Both were freakishly good. I've been a die-hard Rojo mahi taco fan for a couple of years now, but I'd be lying if I said that Camp Taco's fish taco isn't the best in town. It is. The beef brisket taco was incredible, too. The food here seems anchored by freshness, quality, and a bunch around the grill who know what they're doing.
Also great was the queso dip w/ corn and cilantro.
The people here are very nice, and they make the meanest tacos in town. I'm going to help spread the word... this place is too good to die by the wayside.

If Camp Taco were an actual camp, I'd go
by Steph (8 reviews)
June 02, 2010 - Likes it
I really enjoyed this restaurant! We got so much food for our money: probably 5 tacos, a burrito, and some beans for around $15. The vegetarian and vegan options are quite excellent and interesting to boot--one taco comes with pumpkin seeds! The staff is friendly, the food is cooked magically in front of your eyes, and it's a really laid-back atmosphere. Love it, will totally visit again!

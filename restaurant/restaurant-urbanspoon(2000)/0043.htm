Start your day off right with a hearty breakfast at Rise-N-Dine, Atlanta's finest family-friendly restaurant. From our famous sweet potato pancakes to traditional French toast, our Atlanta restaurant truly has something for everyone. Stop by our family-friendly Southern-style restaurant today for the best breakfast menu in town. Rise-N-Dine serves mouthwatering breakfast dishes all day. Our generous portions, combined with our incredible prices, make us a family favorite. Drop by today to experience our light, shabby chic atmosphere, personalized services, and of course, extraordinary home-cooked meals. Be sure to try our pancake special, featuring two buttermilk and two sweet potato pancakes, two eggs, and a side item! All pancakes and waffles are available a la carte. We look forward to seeing you at Rise-N-Dine in Atlanta today.

Even though I have been to this little treasure what seems like thousands of times, I still love it. Great Breakfast in unique setting
by Keith (2 reviews)
March 17, 2010
My wife and I were in Atlanta for a concert (we live in Charlotte) and we always enjoy finding unique local places to try. This place is great. The food was good, the service was snappy (we only had to wait about a half hour to be seated on a Sunday morning) and the staff was friendly. It was a typical young college crowd mixed with older folks like us ; )
We ordered the sausage tacos and sweet potato waffles, both were very good. The tacos were spiced nicely, not too spicy and with a delicious pico de gallo which included a nice amount of cilantro. I love cilantro!
The waffles were good too but kinda spongy. We still enjoyed them but probably could have been cooked just a bit longer. They have waffles and pancakes made from sweet potatoes and the hostess said the waffles were better.
If you live in Atlanta, especially near Emory, you have to know this place. If you are visiting the city, seek it out. It's not too hard to find and well worth it.

So good!
by Wes (152 reviews)
June 12, 2010 - Really likes it
Oh my goodness...never had ice cream this fresh and delicious before. Everything is apparently made in house with fresh local and/or organic ingredients. Had a small cone with Milk Chocolate Hazelnut and NJ Honey. The latter is memorable because I could taste the actual honey mixed in with the ice cream. The place is small and there is a line out the door usually from what I heard. Cupcakes looked great, but it was so busy I didn't stick around to check them out. Don't miss this place if you are in Princeton and want an amazing ice cream.

Peerless Ice Cream.
by Daniel (13 reviews)
August 04, 2010 - Really likes it
Absolutely the best ice cream I've ever had, bar none. All of their ice cream has a creamy, silky-smooth texture and is clean and natural-tasting. Their varieties range from subtle twists on the old stand-bys to sublime original varieties such as Jersey Corn. I seriously doubt that ice cream that could even dream of being as good can be found anywhere within 100 miles of the place. Their cookies are amazing, too. Also, the people behind the counter couldn't be more pleasant and patient.

Recommend | Not helpful

July 18, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I haven't been really impressed with Bent Spoon's offerings. The ice cream is hit or miss---the raspberry is full of seeds but the chocolate chip was good if unspectacular---but the tiny portions aren't what I was expecting for the price. The cupcakes are good enough, albeit the cake is pretty airy and the selection is limited to only yellow or chocolate.

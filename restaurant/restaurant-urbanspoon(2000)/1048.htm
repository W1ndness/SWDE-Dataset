Impressed
by Mokushii (6 reviews)
April 28, 2010 - Really likes it
I was skeptical going to Carolina Sushi & Roll for the first time, but after walking inside, my thoughts changed. The atmosphere is very calm and relaxing with their fountain and service. The food is also excellent. Keep and eye out for the specials of the week, which should be listed on every table. I ordered 4 / 5 of them, an app, soup and two drinks. For everything it was $35 + tip, and my date and I were stuffed. Will definitely be going back.

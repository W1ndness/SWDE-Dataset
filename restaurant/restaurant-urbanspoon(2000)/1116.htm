LOVE this place
by KeKe Rocks (11 reviews)
May 20, 2010 - Likes it
Park Cafe may be a little "hole in the wall" joint, but it's more than the location or size. This place has great ambience and always has such a fun crowd of cool people. And the people watching at Liberty Park can't be beat.
I always get the French Toast Foolishness. It's two thick, huge slabs of french toast, perfectly cooked. With it comes a side of Park Potatoes and the most crisp, succulent bacon you have ever tasted. Perfect for Sunday brunch.
The bad - the wait is always pretty long on the weekend, but you can grab a mug from the hostess and filler up with fresh coffee while you wait outside.

Best Breakfast
by Meesha Ray (3 reviews)
November 15, 2009 - Likes it
There are a few reasons I think the Park Cafe has the best breakfast. First, it's fast. My order usually comes out within 10 minutes of ordering. Second, it's inexpensive. I usually spend no more than $6 before tip, and the portions are huge! Third, it is delicious, I especially love the strawberry jam. Everytime I eat there, the place is packed. The servers are so friendly, and are really on top of it. And you get a view of the park while you eat!

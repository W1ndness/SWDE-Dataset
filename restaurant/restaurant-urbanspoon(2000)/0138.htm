Please, stop with the music
by giantnegro (41 reviews)
March 01, 2009 - Likes it
I hate folk music. It's a personal problem, I'm not blaming anyone else. But it does put me in an awkward position; statistically speaking, vegans like folk music. I'm not vegan, just vegetarian, but I love vegan restaurants because I love being able to order anything on the menu.
That's why veggie planet drives me nuts! I love their food, but I hate folk music. Frankly, the only thing I hate more than folk music is live folk music, and they've got live folk music. But I can't complain too much; I'm from Texas so I'm used to suffering for being a vegetarian. Besides, I've always got an ipod.
Cash only, buddy!

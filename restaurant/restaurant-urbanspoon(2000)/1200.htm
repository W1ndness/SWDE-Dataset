MMMMMM!!!!
by Kathy (3 reviews)
August 15, 2010
A friend introduced me to this place, claiming that they had the best steak sandwich around. The place has a typical Irish pub atmosphere and very friendly staff. Sometimes food comes quickly and other times i have waited a while ( they were short staffed and very very busy).
The steak sandwich was good, but made of ribeye that has some fat throughout. If youre the type that can eat steak with fat in it, then you will love this sandwich ( dont forget to add cheese!). If not the burger is AMAZING! Their blue cheese dressing seems home made. Creamy, nothing tastes artificial and there are blue cheese chunks in it( yum).

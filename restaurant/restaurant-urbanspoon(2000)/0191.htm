Tokyo is simply the best Japanese restaurant in the Ballantyne area. I've been at least five times now, and never had a bad experience. The staff is always friendly, inviting, and on hand to answer any questions. The sushi is delicious, and every item I've ordered from the menu has been a pleasure.
If you've never been to Tokyo, you're due for a treat. A fair warning, though. It will ruin all other Japanese places for you.

Not really worth the time
by Randal (28 reviews)
February 21, 2010 - Doesn't like it
My wife and I tried this restaurant tonight and were thoroughly unimpressed. The shrimp tempura roll and spider roll were exactly the same, the only difference was one had shrimp and one had softshell crab as the main ingredient. Both had watermelon, rice, and imitation crab making up the supporting ingredients. We actually had a tough time telling which was which. The tuna roll, was made from a couple of pieces of thinly sliced tuna, that kinda looked like leftovers that he just slapped in there.
We also tried the hibachi chicken and fried rice and I do not know what they were thinking because these were absolutely horrible. The fried rice tasted like plain white rice with corn mixed in and the hibachi chicken tasted like it was stir-fried in a wok rather than on a hibachi grill and it was soaked in some weird sauce. It really didn't taste like any hibachi I have ever had.
Overall, I have to say, I don't think we will be returning.

I go every time I'm in town.

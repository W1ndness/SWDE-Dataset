Not bad. Not great. Middle of the road food
by dfwdean (253 reviews)
June 14, 2009 - Doesn't like it
There really is nothing unique or special about this place: there are lots of comparable average tex-mex places in the hood that serve the same items and quality.
Very Gringo friendly menu, dumpy restaurant (dives at least have charm, and newer places look like the owners at least give a damn).
Come here once to experience the hype, then find something better and go there instead for the rest of your life.

mobile review
by Newberry (3 reviews)
January 31, 2010 - Really likes it
Get the brisket tacos! You will not be disappointed. The salsa was delicious as well and the chips were fresh. I am certain they make their chips and tortillas fresh everyday. The tortilla soup was fantastic. The service was quick and the prices were great. This is not a fancy place. Nothing to say about the decor but if you like food and could care less about the decor like myself then this is the place for you. Please do not confuse a lackluster decor for being dirty. The restaurant was clean as were the bathrooms. The only thing I would change about my visit was if I would have been able to sit on the patio but it was too cold.

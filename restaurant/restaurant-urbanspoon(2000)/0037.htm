Sensational
by razza (1 review)
March 20, 2010 - Likes it
My mom has been raving about this place for the last year and finally brought me and my girlfriend in last night after dinner. First, let me say that my mom's a former pastry chef, and I am a sous chef in Atlanta, so when I hear her recommendation about something, I take it seriously. The first thing I noticed is that their presentation is outstanding. Every single truffle is hand painted in brilliant shining colors. I was most impressed with the variety of flavors and selections, though. Many of the combinations were creative twists on desserts, like creme brulee, red velvet cake, carrot cake, and tiramisu. I lost count of the fruit flavors, but found the "pineapple upside down" truffle just crazy good. They had just unveiled a new creation called the "rain forest" truffle that had raw chocolate nibs with toasted amaranth (which I love to use in organic breads) in dark chocolate couverture. Sensational! I've eaten chocolate from coast to coast and have never seen or tasted anything that even comes close to what I had here. I don't always agree with my mom, but in this case she was totally right.

Style with Substance
by WandaRoar (2 reviews)
August 08, 2009 - Likes it
I think this place may be completely redefining what it means to be a chocolate store. I don't believe I've ever seen or even heard of a chocolate store that carries all kinds of unique chocolates and brands under one roof. And on top of that, they make their own truffles in the store that can only be described as works of art. That's the style.
But the substance comes when you taste everything. From the Amedei bars (that I can't find anywhere else in Atlanta) to the chocolate truffles that have flavor combinations that are as amazingly tasty as they are unusual. I haven't found one I can call my "favorite" yet, because they have so many and they seem to have new ones every week or two. I don't how in the world they come up with some of the flavors, like juniper berry and cabernet or pancakes and bacon. One even has cheese in it. And the combinations work. They really do!
Every visit has been a pleasant memory. I can't recommend this store any more highly. It is fabulous!

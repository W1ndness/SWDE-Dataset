Dinkers
by topcat (71 reviews)
April 03, 2010 - Likes it
Plain old bar atmosphere, but with a good array of tasty burgers. There are several flat screens if you want to catch a game and the smoking ban has cleared the air. If you really don't want to sit in a bar, there are a couple of outside tables too (the view of 480 can't be beat!). I can't say if they're the best in town just yet, but they were very good.

Awesome neighborhood bar
by Maggie V (12 reviews)
May 17, 2010 - Likes it
Dinkers' wings and onion rings are amazing, especially with their home made ranch dressing. Their burgers are very good (especially the Husker burger), and so are the $1.50 tacos. You order your drinks at the bar and your food at the grill counter, but they will bring it to your table. Great place to watch the game, or for a quick bite of yummy, greasy bar food. They post their specials daily on Facebook, which is really handy!

Sophisticated Dining
by mjkane (3 reviews)
November 01, 2009 - Likes it
Eve is the perfect place to go for a sophisticated, grown-up dinner in Ann Arbor. Set in a quiet off-street corner plaza in the leafy Kerrytown area, the cuisine is delicious, fresh, inventive chef-American. The crowd is lively, and everyone seems to be having fun dining out. A lovely way to treat your student-children with a nice dinner!

Best Mexican Food
by JW (30 reviews)
October 05, 2009 - Likes it
I have gone to this place for years (as well as the Sunset Ridge and Oak Hills locations). La Fonda remains my all time favorite Mexican Food restrauant. The chips are fresh and warm. The hot sauce/salsa fresh and tastey. The best chips and hot sauce I have found in Texas. The same goes for the cheese enchiladas (authentic red tortillas with a flavorful red sauce). Fresh corn tortillas (try these over flour!). I have never had a bad meal. La Fonda Special #1 please!

Recommend | Not helpful


My Favorite
by JFB in SA (14 reviews)
September 21, 2008 - Really likes it
This has been my favorite mexican food restaurant in San Antonio for years.  If it is a nice day, you can't beat sitting outside on the back patio.  My favorite dish is cheese enchiladas with tomatillo sauce.  Only the chicken version is on the menu now, but if you ask, they'll make it with cheese instead.  Oh, and if you like "sour' margaritas, "Steve's" margarita is unbeatable (make sure you've got a designated driver though).

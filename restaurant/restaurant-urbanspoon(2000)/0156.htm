A great addition to Hamburg!
by Katie (1 review)
October 11, 2009
Isabella's is terrific. The food is great, always made with the freshest ingredients and plenty of TLC. I also love that the owners are so accommodating. As a vegetarian, its great to have place where they genuinely care about meeting special dietary needs. Many of the dishes (including soups!!) are made vegetarian, and they always accommodate my requests for special sandwiches :) It's also great to be able to purchase fresh bread in whatever quantity I need, so none has to go to waste! The folks at Isabella's truly care about meeting the needs of the Hamburg community, and Hamburg is lucky to have them!

YUM and they're in Adelanto too!
by x (1 review)
February 23, 2010
Bravo Burgers is FANTASTIC! The guy who said it was dirty might have been at Carl's Jr. on the other side of the 5 freeway. :-(
Bravo Burgers has a large offering and there is definitely something for everyone. The owners and their family work at the place, so you know they are keeping an eye on things--this is not a Coco's with some random manager in charge. The quality is good. Ask to meet them when you go. They are very nice.
If you're unsure of the spiciness of something, ask for a taste first! Spiciness is kind of relative!
Casual, nice furnishings, much much nicer than a typical burger joint.
BONUS! If you drive up the 395 (like to go to Mammoth) there is another Bravo's JUST OFF THE ROAD near the baseball stadium in Adelanto. As it turns out, another family member owns/manages that one. A little more plastic and vinyl in the furnishings, which is understandable given the location (it is Adelanto, after all!).
Our whole extended family loves this restaurant. We come after the beach in the summer a lot. It's on the way home. Nobody has to start making dinner plans or stop at the grocery store on the way back.
I hear there is a Bravo Burgers in Apple Valley too, but have never been out that far.

Message from Oregano's Pizza Bistro (Tempe)

Local pizza joint serves up good times, garlic breath and Chicago-style pizza.

  • Pizza----Pasta
  • Salads----Sandwiches
  • Original Pizza Cookie Dessert

Bring the whole family to this original Chicago-style pizza joint that serves award-winning thin crust, stuffed and pan pizzas to the sounds of Sinatra, Martin and Big-Band music. Oregano's unique menu includes delicious and original tastes, wings, pastas, salads, sandwiches and The Original Pizza Cookie dessert.

Lunch with Pizzaz
by Bob (116 reviews)
July 23, 2010 - Really likes it
Had lunch here with a client, it is always busy here, and you must arrive early to take advantage of a parking space and getting a table. Everyone says hello when you walk in which is welcoming. We were a party of four, and since it had rained, we elected to sit inside. The booth seating has shown its wear, as you sink into the seat, and have to sit up to reach the table, its time for the booths to be refreshed/reworked. I ordered the Italian sandwich Jr. size, which was the prefect size, good flavors and not over powering.

Standard Oregano's fare and service
by Jason Gerepka (21 reviews)
February 22, 2010 - Doesn't like it
But damn this place is ALWAYS packed, and they really do want to close at 930 not 10 on weekdays.
There are no happy hours, and the bar area is to cramped to really try to snag some drinks while waiting.
It gets LOUD and can be crazy, because it is so popular.
The parking lot can and has caused serious car accidents off of University, so you need to really take care when pulling in - it's small just like the building.
I would recommend going to the one in Mesa on Dobson, more choices around to eat and a larger patio and bar - better parking too.

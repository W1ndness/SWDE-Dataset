Frequent guest at Santo Suosso's
by KD (1 review)
June 12, 2010 - Likes it
Each Friday for the past 20 years, 10 friends get together and go out to dinner ... somewhere in Medina since most of us live in the county.
There is no doubt that Santo Suosso's is our favorite and most frequented restaurant. EVERYTHING about it is significantly better than most places we go... the atmosphere, the staff, the owner, the decor,... but the food is the best. You will never be disapointed.
I actually get cravings for their Vodka sauce (on anything), and my husband says their pizza is one of the few around who truly use fresh and high quality ingredients. You will not be disappointed.
We were just there last night - love their eggplant "parm"... and of course my husband got the pizza. The rest of the Friday crowd ALL ordered something different and it was all great.
This lovely place is a diamond in Medina. Check it out. (Anyone who gives this restaurant a poor review is just trying to keep the crowds down so they can always get a fast table;-)
ENJOY

mobile review
by Shripal Vora (1 review)
February 14, 2009 - Likes it
Amazing service. The atmosphere was quite romantic, so a good place for a date, but also a place for a family. A bit pricey but nothing outragous. The bruscetta(spelling check?) was amazing. The stuffed shells were a major let down. They looked amazing, the pasta was amazing and home made but the cheese was dry and grainy and ruined the dish. I wouldn't recommend it. My girlfriend had the tuna steak and said that it was good. For desert we had the chocolate chip canoli(spell check), and the Oreo cake cheesecake both of which were splendid. I am vegetarian so for all of the other vegetarians out there, the selection was slim there, I would love it if their lasagna was vegetarian but it's not. Over all it's a 4/5 experience, definitely worth a shot. Maybe your stuffed shells will be better than mine. Very nice setting though.

CARE and HEART found in a locally owned Itialian Market -- PERFECT!
by Mark Cleveland (1 review)
January 27, 2009 - Likes it
I love Italian food.  I also love a well run business.  I love to see it all come together -- to see the great care given to little details by an owner who invests everything they can give a deliver a product they care about... and it shows.  Restaurants are hard to run and every meal is a report card on the chef, the owner and the tam.  To see it all done right is inspiring!  The Italian Cafe really is the newest, MUST TRY RESTAURANT for everyone in the greater Nashville area.  Atmosphere is light, easy and welcoming -- nothing stuffy about it!. The kitchen is newly remodeled, the building is unassuming and overhead low... it's a perfect combination for an outstanding meal at a great price.  I have eaten there with friends and on business at lest a dozen times since they opened their doors.  I just love the place.  A smart and mouth watering variety shows up in their menu.  Hart and Care is evident with every bite and it's exactly what you are looking for!  The Italian Market -- it's PERFECT!

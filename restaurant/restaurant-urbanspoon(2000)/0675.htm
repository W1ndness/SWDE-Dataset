Darn Good and Reasonably Priced, too
by John Paul (3 reviews)
February 21, 2010 - Likes it
I really recommend this place for lunch or dinner. They have very decent sandwiches, nice music, and good beer on tap. The service is spotty and the music is sometimes bar-loud way too early (but you can try the lunch counter side of the restaurant to avoid that). I haven't always had great food, but it's never bad - just not always well though out. For example, the philly steak is hard to eat without spilling everything all over and gnawing up the whole pile of beef or hacking your sammy up with a knife, and the chicken pitas are also hard to eat without the pita crumbling and chicken coming out in a hunk. Even if its sometimes messy, though, it's always tasty. The quality isn't an issue. There are neat options, too. I especially like that you can get 'Asian slaw' instead of french fries with sandwiches - its really tasty and far more filling than fries. The Croque Monsieur is excellent, as are the fresh pies!

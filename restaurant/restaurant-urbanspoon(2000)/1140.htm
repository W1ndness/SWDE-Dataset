The pickled onions were very odd on the salad, too.
The man I assume is the manager (as we overheard him referring to "HIS chef" and "HIS hostess" ) came to our table but our mouths were full and we just didn't feel like giving him feedback at that time.
Waitress came back a few times to check on us which was good and then asked about dessert but didn't offer any suggestions (we never saw a dessert menu).
Overall the food was ok although the menu was very impressive. The service was dismal (except for water/bread guy who was adorable). And what's up with the blue jeans? One guy's were so big they were nearly at his ankles and another female server's were so tight we had to look away. I know this may be offensive but it was very unprofessional.
I hope the feedback is received in the spirit in which it is intended. We would love to make this a regular spot but it will require some work...

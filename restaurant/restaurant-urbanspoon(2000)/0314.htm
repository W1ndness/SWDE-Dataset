Highlight of our trip to Denver!
by amara-nickerson (1 review)
June 14, 2010 - Likes it
Everything about this restaurant is great: the food, the atmosphere, the service. My two friends and I were here on a Monday morning. There was a 30 minute wait, but free coffee and water were put out for us to sip on until our table was ready. Our waitress was super friendly and the atmosphere was fun and relaxed. We couldn't decide between sweet or savory, but our waitress made the decision easy by bringing us a complementary chocolate-chip pancake with white chocolate espresso sauce (amazing!). We all settled on egg breakfasts and were blown away by the generous servings and the delicious, fresh ingredients. Nothing about our dining experience could have been better. If you haven't been here yet, you're missing out!

Absolutely loved it
by Alyssa (25 reviews)
December 02, 2009 - Really likes it
I went there for brunch with some friends, was put on the wait list for 30-45 mins, but it was definitely well worth it. It has a very trendy atmosphere for a breakfast place, with giant windows outlooking to the street. I loved the salmon benedict and the food was suprisingly filling for all of us. Service was great, our waiter took the effort to ensure our food came out quickly since we were on a time crunch. Loved it and will definitely be going back soon

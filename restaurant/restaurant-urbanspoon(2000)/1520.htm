Great coffee & artwork!
by Patrick Dennis (3 reviews)
December 17, 2009
As owner of the Decatur Market & Gallery, it's a great pleasure to introduce an installation of new works by artist Jill Bates at the Dancing Goats Coffee Bar.
Coffee at DG is the best in metro Atlanta (and I'm picky!), and the atmosphere is terrific and friendly. Manager Lorrie keeps it all in constant motion.
The excellent artwork on display by Jill Bates is pastel on sanded paper, framed perfectly. Prices are VERY affordable. If you see anything you like, just contact us at the Gallery: 404-377-0755. We're right up the street at 153 Ponce de Leon Pl.
Enjoy that hot cuppa this winter. I do!

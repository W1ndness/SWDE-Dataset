Great burgers....for a price
by OffTheBeatenPath (512 reviews)
October 19, 2008 - Likes it
The Tavern has been around for years, recently a local radio personality took it over and brought it back to life.
The burgers are outstanding. Homemade buns that are probably the best in town. Trouble is a burger at this place is north of $10. Might be me, but I just can't spend $10 on a hamburger.
Other daily blueplate specials are always good. Fried chicken, chicken fried chicken, chicken fried steak, burgers, meatloaf, pot roast, it's all good.
Given the historic nature of this place, the haunted room upstairs, and the location, they probably have to price their menu items this high to keep the doors open. Food quality is top notch, and you can't get more olde Austin.
Give them a shot, I promise you'll like it.

Way to ruin a fun place
by Austin Blues (55 reviews)
April 09, 2009 - Doesn't like it
You know, I will never understand why folks want to mess with something that seemed to be working. I certainly don't know if they were making any money, and perhaps this is why the change occurred, but I went in today for lunch, and low and behold the menu was completely different. Gone were the great wraps and somewhat healthy fair, and back to burgers and stuff. I got the grilled chicken sandwich. Came with a loaf of bread, soggy fries, two well preserved tomato's and very fresh cut up lettuce.. WTF... it was completely meh. I want my wraps back, I want to sit by my little TV at the bar and watch footie.. which is now broken.... Hali, the barkeep was the bright spark in today's munching.. she was very attentive and took care of me well. I believe this is the beginning of the end.... bummer used to be a great place...

Decent
by Devan (5 reviews)
January 13, 2010 - Likes it
Ate there for lunch yesterday. Got there pretty early to beat the lunch crowd, and there was only one other guest downstairs with me.
The service was a little slow, but I didn't really mind. The staff was friendly, but not over friendly where it felt they were bugging you.
Ordered a burger that was bit messy, and tasted a bit "factory", but it wasn't bad. The lettuce was a bit odd as it was long thin strips that weren't very crispy. I'd give the burger a 3 out of 5 stars.
The onion rings were pretty good. Battery and not real crispy, which I like. 4 out of 5.
Most of the tv's were turned down pretty low so I couldn't hear what was being said on Sports Center, but it's not like there was a game on or anything. Nice place. I'd go back.

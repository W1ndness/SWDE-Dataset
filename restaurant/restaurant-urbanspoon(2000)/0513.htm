Another Great Meal
by FoodMaven (31 reviews)
June 16, 2010 - Likes it
Stopped by today for lunch with my wife and daughter. We had only eaten at the CCC for breakfast, so we were excited to see if lunch was just as good as our previous morning experiences.
We ordered the Turkey Burger Sandwich, Grilled Chicken Sandwich and Grilled Chicken Salad.
The sandwiches were served on fresh bread with lettuce, tomato and mayonaise. You can choose from fries, chips, slaw, or a side salad. We both opted for the fries which were the nice thick steak fries.
The salad was loaded with spring mix, tomatoes, carrots, cucumbers, and a large portion of grilled chicken. Allthe salad dressings are made fresh daily.
Once again, another great meal.

A Jewel in Tinsel Town
by FoodMaven (31 reviews)
May 23, 2010 - Likes it
Went here for breakfast this morning. We walked in and almost half the tables were taken.
The ambiance is interesting with a fireplace on one side and a custom designed water feature in the middle.
We ordered a veggie omelete with wheat toast and the "All-in-one-slop" This interesting concoction incorporates 2 eggs, bacon, onions, green bell peppers, home fried potatoes, grits and cheddar cheese on top. These ingredients are layered on the plate like a mountain!
When we sat down after ordering, within 15 minutes the remaining tables were filled and people began waiting.
Our food was served quickly and pipping hot, exactly as ordered. The eggs were fluffy and well scrambled, toast lightly buttered and the coffee selection was great. I chose the Kona coffee for the first cup and then the Columbian for the second cup.
This is our new weekend breakfast spot.

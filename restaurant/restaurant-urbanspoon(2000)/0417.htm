July 03, 2010 - Doesn't like it
This is still just a diner, folks... Stop worshipping it. I mean, if you're the kind of guy that drinks mad dog 20/20 and thinks fart jokes are cool, ok you may enjoy it, but I have a thing against old fish and cold potatoes. I think it's different on the weekends, but I went during the week and sat where I could watch the cook, who needs a steen lecture on food safety if you ask me. The unusual looking menu is a sham. The waitstaff are unbelievably annoying. Slow, lazy, rude high school kids who clearly don't appreciate employment. Seriously disappointed after seeing such a delicious looking spot on tv.

The Best is Back :)
by Lola (4 reviews)
March 07, 2010 - Really likes it
I have been going to O'Rourke's since I was six years old. I would get the same sandwich every single time, sausage, egg and cheese on a grilled hardroll. They have pieces of pound cake that they make on the table while you wait. you see the history through the pictures and sometimes people who have been going longer than I have been alive! If you want great service, great atomsphere and GREAT food, than O'Rourkes is the best place for that!!

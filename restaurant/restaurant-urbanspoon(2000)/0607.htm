Good Sweet Treat
by Uke-Mochi (177 reviews)
January 07, 2009 - Likes it
If you are looking for an old-fashioned ice-cream parlor, Fosselman is your place. It is not any trendy franchise chain, but it gives you feelings of a small midwest town. They have many variety of ice-cream, from traditional flavors chocolate, vanilla, strawberry to cappuccino, green-tea and Ube. Their ice-creams are very good quality, smooth and creamy. They also sell 1/2 gallon buckets for take home. Their menu consists of shakes, malts, sundaes, banana splits, brownie sundaes, smoothies, floats and ice-cream sodas.
It is truly a landmark in SGV.

San Gabriel Valley icon
by Michael Szeto (51 reviews)
January 08, 2009 - Likes it
There are few food icons of the San Gabriel Valley. Fosselman's Ice Cream is one of the few. It's been around since 1919 and the ice cream is just as good. All of it is made on site from very good ingredients. You can taste the real chocolate or real strawberries as you have a cone of the ice cream. it's a comfortable place to go after dinner or lunch for dessert.
Fosselman's is sent across the country, if not the world. They also make sorbets and sherberts which are just as good as their classic ice cream.

Root Beer Floats
by David from Culver City (61 reviews)
March 13, 2010 - Likes it
Not only does this place have the best ice cream in Los Angeles county, but they have a big selection, the atmosphere is nice because it's very old-fashioned, and it costs about the same as Baskin Robbins. The ice cream isn't particularly rich, but the texture is nice and fluffy and has a homemade charm.
If you go there when it's not rush hour, it's you buy a half gallon or two and drive them to your freezer at home (assuming you live in Culver City like me) before they melt. Fosselman's also make their sodas the old fashioned way (mixing seltzer and syrup)--their root beer floats are amazing, because the root beer flavor is much more intense than what you get out of a can. My favorite ice cream flavor is cafe vanilla, but they seem to have discontinued it. Still, they have cappucino and other sorts of coffee flavors that will do in a pinch. Being in the San Gabriel Valley, they also have a decent selection of asian-influenced ice creams, such as green tea, ube, and lychee, that are also very good.

Nostalgic
by Picky Japxican (8 reviews)
August 15, 2010 - Likes it
Delicious old fashioned ice cream, not filled with corn syrup or artificial sweeteners like a lot of ice cream is nowadays. High cream (read: fat) content make this ice cream buttery and smooth - the way ice cream is supposed to be!
They sell cakes, pints, quarts, and all the usual sundaes, cones, etc. Their quart containers, however, serve as a great dinner party gift when you're tired of showing up with a bottle of wine over and over. They're the perfect excuse to indulge in some of the more refined flavors like "taro", "espresso" and "green tea"!

Crust
by Roseann Scrivens (2 reviews)
March 14, 2010
What I liked most about this place is their unique crusts. They have different crusts in bagel like flavors. They have poppy seed crust, sesame crust, etc. They claim to be "gourmet" so most of their pizzas have many toppings. I often don't like all of the toppings so it ruins the slice. I prefer to order a custom pie the way I like it.

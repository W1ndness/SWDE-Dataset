My Favorite Greek Restaurant in Tucson
by Dean (7 reviews)
March 31, 2009 - Likes it
I stumbled upon this cafe one day and was blown away by the good Greek food and low cost. I also really like the interior: clean, roomy, just an overall good feel. I went by a few days ago and see they moved down a couple of doors so I haven't been in since the move and need to see if they kept the same interior (hope so). I really enjoy their olives, their pitas, chicken, lots of choices. They don't make their hummus quite like I want it, but only one place in town does. However, their hummus is still good.

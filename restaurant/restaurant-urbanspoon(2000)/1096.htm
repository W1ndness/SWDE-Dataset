One of My Fav 5
by JoAnna (2 reviews)
March 03, 2009 - Likes it
Mikini's has been around for a long time (Hazel Ave) and I have watched the restaurant evolve. Although it has grown from a small Japanese Restaurant to a chain of sushi bar/sportsbar/restaurants it hasn't lost it's appeal. The focus is on creating delicious dishes that display nicely for a very reasonable price. They still greet every customers with smiles and as "hi hasmimoto' or 'hi ajinamoto' something that sounds very Japanese, but probably means nothing or something like 'yes, we do NOT use MSG' or something like that. The food is great the decor is impeccable and the service is great. The sushi chefs are trained. They offer fun happy hours and you must get on the mailing list and join the Koki Club. They also offer sushi making classes, very fun and a great way to meet people.

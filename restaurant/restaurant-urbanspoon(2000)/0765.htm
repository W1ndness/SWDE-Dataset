I guess...
by adrienne (22 reviews)
January 12, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I guess I shouldn't fault them for being true to their name. An out-of-town friend and I went there, and we're both faux foodies. We looked at the menu for a while, but nothing caught our eye. Just the same old salads, sandwiches, and plates. Even the "new" items included a pierogi appetizer (dressed up like potato skins) and a Philly cheesesteak. Please - a little imagination, people! Even the specials were blah... steak or a fish burrito. We wound up sharing the chicken nachos (with plans to eat a real meal elsewhere) but then the plate was so HUGE we just ate part of that and went home. Nothing terribly wrong with this place, there's the attentive service and attractive decor, but the menu is downright boring. I hear the brunch is similar.

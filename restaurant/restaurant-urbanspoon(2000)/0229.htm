Some good stuffand great pie!
by Local Foodie (60 reviews)
June 03, 2010 - Likes it
Some of the stuff is really great here, like the snowball (a HUGE ball of vanilla ice cream covered in toasted coconut and then drizzled with a homemade chocolate sauce - large enough to share), but some things are just so-so, like the cheeseburger (They didn't put a metal pan over it to allow the cheese to steam-melt, so I got a burger with shredded cheese falling out all over the place). But the real price here are the pies. They are all homemade and all fantastic, but they sell out fast. So get there early and grab a slice and some coffee and you'll be set for the day. Pie is the best breakfast, and Echo is doing it right.

Consistently Good
by David (7 reviews)
November 23, 2009 - Likes it
We eat breakfast here a couple time per month on Saturday. There is always a crowd but the lines tends to move quickly. I like their oatmeal, their eggs benedict, and the protien platter if I'm feeling like avoiding carbs. My companion loves their traditional pancakes. I recommend skipping the home fries and trying the potato cakes. They are unique and tasty. On the last visit the bacon was fatty and undercooked... watch out for that. Otherwise, this is a good, fun, old fashioned place to eat.

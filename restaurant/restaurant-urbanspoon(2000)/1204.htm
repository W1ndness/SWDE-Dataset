not really all that greek...but tasty
by Food Queen (11 reviews)
August 27, 2009
I have been here about 3 or times for various celebrations and have had a nice meal each time. After I got over the fact that this was not "authentic" Greek food I was much better.
The food is good and flavorful but it was more Greek "inspired" than actually Greek. The last time we were there we had to wait pretty long to place our orders. The food came out right as the belly dancer started her show. She was fun and a really good dancer. She got my nieces up to dance with her and they all had a good laugh. This is usually a highlight of the night.
We like the chocolate baklava dessert. A good mix of sweet, chocolatey and with a bit of saltiness in the phylo.
Overall, a good but less than authentic Greek cuisine experience.

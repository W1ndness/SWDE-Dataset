+ 4 more photos from Mew Great sushi lunch specials
by Mew (5 reviews)
January 16, 2010 - Likes it
I really enjoy the sushi at Bayridge. My favorite items are the Tuna Kobachi, Spicy Tuna and Spicy Yellowtail. I love that they mix tempura crunchies into the spicy tuna and spicy yellowtail mixture. as of right now the lunch special we seem to get often is: 7.25 for two rolls, miso soup, and salad. i usually say "no soup" because the miso here is not my favorite.
(fans of arrested developement might understand when i described it as smelling like Hot Ham Water even tho I know fully well what is in Miso)
The bento lunch special is a pretty good deal as well. 7.25 i think. (tempura chicken, veggies, california roll, rangoon, white rice) recently i've been given dumplings instead of rangoon. i'm not certain what is to be the norm.
A few weeks ago (when this location was closed after a fire) we learned of a second location on 436. Their lunch special is cheaper (5.95 for two rolls, soup, salad) but I warn you the rolls are smaller! One good thing is they have a Tuna Carpaccio app which is so good. I have taken many photos at Bayridge so keep an eye out for those in the album.
I would recommend Bayridge. Check out their specials as well and if you like it, take advantage of their frequent flyer type card for discounts on future meals.

Uh Ohhhhh
by Local Gourmet (1 review)
November 03, 2008 - Doesn't like it
I live in Newport, RI, and so going to Providence for dinner, I was Witjh great anticipation I entered a relaxed, yet upscale atmosphere. The prices on the alacarte menu were slightly high, however they had a 3-course menu most nights that was reasonable. I don't eat pasta, so I ordered ala carte. I had the cesear salad, which was good, filled the plate nicely and had a good balance of garlic and cheese. Then came my main course. I ordered the filet mignon. On the menu it comes with Italian mashed potatoes and green bean salad. Since I'm alergic to some of the ingredients, I thought I could substitute something else like a steamed veg or ? No dice. I had to order a side of asparagus at $6, in addition to my $9 salad. The piece of meat all alone was $37. OK, I figured my $50 dinner was going to be really great - WRONG! I ordered my steak med rare - medium. When it arrived it was barely cooked on the outside and not only raw, BUT REFRIGERATOR COLD on the inside! I sent it back. It came back about 5 minutes later with the outside dried up and stringy, and once again waaay too rare inside. I did not want to wait another 20 minutes to have them now over cook it, so I asked them to remove it from my check and I would pay for the other items. No way. The chef said the meat wasn't defective. I said the cook was not skilled in the preparation of the meat. The manager/owner was not in the restaurant to see this. They forced me to pay for the meat and my meal was ruined. I was out $67, and will never go back. Save yourself the aggravation. Go elsewhere.

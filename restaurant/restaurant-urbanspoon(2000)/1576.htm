There is no other
by Wilkins (3 reviews)
February 26, 2008 - Likes it
Hands down the best BBQ within the city limits, and some of the best I have ever had. Black's  and Kreuz's in Lockhart, Louie Mueller's in Taylor, the Salt Lick, Sonny Bryan's in Dallas, they are all good in their own way, but Ruby's is The Best. Free-range, all-natural B3R beef brisket is slow-smoked over oak fire the way God meant BBQ to be done. Enjoy it with the tangy-sweet sauce and a side of the spicy-hot pintos, and you can forget for a moment how californicated Austin has become and how perilously close we are to the collapse of civilization  as we know it. Yeah- I need a BBQ fix now!

Not sure it was worth the wait...
by el Brando (79 reviews)
February 01, 2009 - Likes it
Cuppies and Joe is a worthy addition to OKC fun, eclectic dining scene. Open late (midnight) so there's a good place to go after hours that's not a bar.
I had a chocolate/chocolate frosting (Chocolate Rain) that was so-so. The cake was a tad dry but the terrific frosting helped out a lot. Overall, this one was a 3.5/5.
The other cupcake was a thing of beauty. It was a peanut butter/chocolate filled (the Traveler). Warm it up just a touch and let the rich, moist deliciousness wash over your palate.
The coffee was pretty good and my dining partner said the latte was excellent. Only problem was that there were about 10 people in line when we got there and it took 30 minutes from the time we arrived to the time we had our cupcakes. The staff seemed inexperienced and out of sorts. If they can get there stuff together in this department, this place is gonna be quite excellent.

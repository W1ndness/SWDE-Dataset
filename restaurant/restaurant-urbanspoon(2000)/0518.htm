Hola Mexicana!!
by Clark H Smith (53 reviews)
May 05, 2009 - Really likes it
Finally, a real local Mexican food joint that doesn't make you wish you'd just eaten at Taco Bell. Mi Ranchito has blown up in the JoCo area in the last couple of years because they dish up GREAT Mexican food. Everything is great - Carne Asada, Chili Verde, and the folks I hang with like the fish tacos (which I can't explain).
The great thing about Mi Ranchito - that distinguishes it from Jose Peppers and Jalapenos, for example - is the authenticity of flavor and ingredients. Other restaurants taste like they were making food for Kansas Citians. Mi Ranchito tastes like it was made by Mexicans for Mexicans and we just get to party along with them. I won't eat at any other place but Mi Ranchito.
And yes, get there early!!!!!

Hand Down, The BEST!!
by Twister (11 reviews)
May 29, 2010 - Really likes it
Not quite 100% pure authentic Mexican fare. But, wayyyy better than the run of the mill Tex-Mex type restaurant that seems to permeate the city. Have been eating at the various Mi Ranchito locations for years, since the first one opened in the old Pizza Hut building in Olathe, Kansas.
The owner is a tough SOB, but boy do you get exceptional service and food over 905 of the time. My personal favorite locale is the 119th street locations in Olathe Kansas. Call in ur reservation and still plan to wait. It gets that busy 7 days a week.
The service is usually top notch with the occasional hiccup every now and then. The food will ALWAYS come out blazin hot to the point of danger. U never, and I mean ever touch the plates unless you want to suffer a third degree burn after the waiter/waitress places the plate on ur table. Don't worry, they for-warn you and Id advise listening.
Food quality is top notch and reasonably priced. Keep a look out for the free espanaco dip coupons that are frequently mailed out around the city. Plenty of weekly specials as well. Can't really recommend one particular item on the menu. Its a vast and sprawling selection so take ur time, and return many more times to get a true feel for the place.
I swear I've eaten at the various locations over 150 times. I really don't know where to begin or end this review. More to come..............

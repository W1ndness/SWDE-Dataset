Lamb tacos, go!
by Michael (90 reviews)
December 23, 2009 - Likes it
Yucatan is what I wish Fuzzy's still was. Maybe not in the sense of the upscale decor or a few of the avant garde dishes, but just a simple taco. They know how to do it quite well. The fish taco is how a fish taco should be, nice flaky pieces of white fish. That it is tempura fried is a little different, but still good. The star of the show for me however is the roasted lamb taco. It's fantastic, loaded with tender pieces of lamb and some nice garnishes. I've also tried the tenderloin tamale, the spicy fish cakes, and their version of clam chowder. All are very tasty and worthy of praise. My friend raved about the torta which she has yet to find an equivalent to anywhere in town.
In short, you can't go wrong with anything on the menu. Try it out.

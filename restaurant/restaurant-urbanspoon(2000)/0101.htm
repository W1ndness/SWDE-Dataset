A Jewel
by Gerri Hobdy (1 review)
December 15, 2009 - Likes it
Elegant, and warm but not too formal. This family owned business is a jewel. When you enter you are part of the Strands Cafe family. Hugs and easy conversation abound. The hand dipped chocolates are made at night and so allow time to ask what is the special ingrediants are. Just like your mother's kitchen every day there is something new. The staples like the fresh lemon curd made daily to sit will with their perfect scones. The coffee and teas are there but the show stoppers are the pastries that are made fresh every night by the same loving hands that serve them. If you haven't been to Strand's you haven't really experienced the spirit of Baton Rouge.

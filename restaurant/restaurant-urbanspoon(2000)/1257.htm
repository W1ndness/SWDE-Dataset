I like this place very much!
by David (53 reviews)
October 16, 2009 - Likes it
If you are hungry, this is the place; or, two people can share a sandwich because they are big. Various breads makes it difficult to choose because they are all good; such as, Cracked Wheat, Marble Rye, Pumpernickel, Paremssan & Herb, Jalepeno & Cheddar, Garlic Italian, Focaccia, and more! Then, there are the three dozen or so types of sandwiches you can pick from - hot or cold. There are soups and salads to choose from; and, some very nice deserts and special baked yummies.
Great value for great food and worth the visit.

Recommend | Not helpful

Great sandwiches!
by Zedora (12 reviews)
May 16, 2010 - Likes it
Love the sandwiches at BB. Especially the Colette's Cordon Bleu... chicken breast (thick slices), ham, swiss cheese, and honey mustard on some fresh sourdough. So good! And bread bowl soups? I'm so there!
I've yet to go in and try their sweet offerings...because I'm afraid I won't leave for several hours. The display cases filled with sweet treats look SO good!

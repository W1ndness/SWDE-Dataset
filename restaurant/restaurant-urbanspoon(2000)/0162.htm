Authentic Irish Pub
by carnellm (46 reviews)
September 15, 2008 - Likes it
Walking in Madra Rua reminds me of going into the pubs I visited in Dublin years ago. It should though, one of the owners is fairly recently from Ireland!  A nice dark but friendly atmosphere and lots of soccer on the televisions. It used to be hard to get a table, but they have recently doubled in size and that isn't a problem any more.  The lunch specials are great. My favorite lunches are the fish & chips, hamburgers, and Irish stew with soda bread.

Kudu sucks
by alcole92 (1 review)
February 05, 2010
The old Kudu was awesome. Now that it has switched ownership, Kudu feels like just like an other coffee shop. There's just no point in going there any more. I mean, you can still get their old coffees, but they charge you extra for it. And they do not have a selection of teas available, only the "tea of the day", so unless you just want an average mocha, and not something more elaborate, don't go there.

KUDU
by jsbell33 (1 review)
May 15, 2010 - Likes it
I am one of the new managers at Kudu. Since the change in ownership, we have never had a "tea of the day". However, we do provide teas from the Charleston Tea Plantation and from Harney & Sons. We have never charged more money for coffee that came from Kudu's previous roaster. We now carry Krankies Coffee. They are a small batch roaster located in Winston Salem, NC. They are excellent to work with and we've had many compliments on their product. We also carry sandwiches and quiche from Normandy Farms Artisan Bakery and baked goods from Saffron Bakery. Thanks.

don't be scurried!!!
by sarah (24 reviews)
May 03, 2009 - Really likes it
I know you may have heard negative things about the neighborhood....but no need to fear. Just b/c you see people of color don't mean you need to be alarmed...we don't bite.
BUT the food here definitely does!
yes, the food here is a Philly's kept secret...at least when it comes to Latin Flare.
By far the best Paella I have had in Philly.
Large generous portions...def a guy place on that.
Well cooked Latin seafood dishes...and the steak enciebollado is pretty good too.
The decor and atmosphere will make you forget you in North Philly.
Good mojitos...and a dance club is upstairs, so if you feeling bold after your meal...go upstairs and get your salsa on.
note- call them to find out what night they have salsa lesson nite.

Recommend | Not helpful

It's OK
by Andrew (8 reviews)
May 16, 2009 - Likes it
For Philadelphia Tierra Colombiana is acceptable. I don't think in New York or New Jersey anyone would get overly excited over this restaurant as there are so many. This place is just basic Colombian cuisine, and that's OK.
Also, I took a friend here who is a native Colombian. He wasn't thrilled with the neighborhood. Yes, there are people from outside the US, even from poor countries, who are amazed by our American urban decay.

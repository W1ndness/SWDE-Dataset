Wonderful, charming, delicious
by Andrew Mansfield (58 reviews)
January 17, 2010 - Likes it
Via Maestra's food is so good it is hard to add enough superlatives to represent it. I unconditionally recommend it. My only comment other than to broadcast the incredible food is to warn those thinking of going there how small it is. I believe there a half dozen tables and a few bar stools. At lunch, one orders from the counter. It is informal. Given the cost of the food (very expensive for counter service, especially), I was a bit surprised. Of course, I should have read more reviews here on Urbanspoon.
I would not let the size deter me in the least. Eat at Via Maestra 42. You will be impressed.

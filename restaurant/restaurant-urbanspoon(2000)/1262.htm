Yummy French Toast
by David from Culver City (61 reviews)
June 13, 2010 - Likes it
I try to make it to Bobo's every time I'm around Tucson, and sometimes when I'm only as close as Phoenix. They have the best French toast anywhere, because they put brown sugar on top of it and somehow make it totally crunchy and delectable. Part of the attraction is the ambiance-- it's the perfect type of place for a grad student at the U of A to take a visiting out-of-town guest for breakfast, and I guess it brings back some nostalgia.

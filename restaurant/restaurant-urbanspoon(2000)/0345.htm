Great food and authentic British seediness
by JC (23 reviews)
August 08, 2010 - Likes it
The food is good and they have a wide selection of beers including some that you wouldn't find at place like Old Chicago. I tried the shepards pie and a friend had the guinness steak pie both were excellent. The waitresses all also very good looking, which I have to attribute to the flight of stairs they climb up and down to bring you food.
The crowd really depends on what day and time you're there but expect some punky and alternative kids on the weekends starting at dinner time. Overall it's definitely a place to try if you're in downtown DM and want to try something different and good.

British Pub Moved to Midwest US
by Mike S (33 reviews)
April 26, 2009 - Likes it
Walking through the doors of this mid-western pub you'd swear you were just transported to London or Ireland. This terrific pub serves a vast array of fine scotches ranging from $4-$14 and a beer menu that is mind-boggling. The food is authentic Irish-pub cuisine, such as the Guinness Stew or the house special "Earl of Sandwich" (triple-decker rye, turkey, ham, corned beef, bacon, and Swiss w/ sweet aioli.
Renowned amongst locals for the great atmosphere, cheap drinks, and hearty pub food, this spot is a great place to spend an afternoon with good company.

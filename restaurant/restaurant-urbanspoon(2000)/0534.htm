by The Food Carpenter (157 reviews)
February 08, 2010 - Likes it
To echo the two reviews below, I have always returned to the Tokyo Japanese Steak House for their great and friendly service, good food, and much more appealing price point. Granted, you won't find too much to do after dinner in this part of the neighborhood, but if dinner is all you're after, it's really hard to beat this.

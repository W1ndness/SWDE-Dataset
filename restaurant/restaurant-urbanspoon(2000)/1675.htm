Horrible
by Nick (19 reviews)
March 09, 2010
Placed a call for pick up and was quoted 45 minutes. That was fine, we took our time and ventured to pick it up. Upon arriving we were told it would be up soon. After nearly another hour of waiting we finally got our meal and we were met with the owner informing us of the great favor bestowed on us. They had run out of the environmentally friendly fish my girlfriend had ordered and they had taken it upon themselves to substitute Chilean sea bass. We didn't make a fuss, but it was a serious downer for the rest of the meal.
By the time they finished cooking the massive endangered fish the rest of our food was cold and probably not as good as it could have been. I don't think we will be returning. I understand the reasoning, but adding on another hour to our wait for a fish that we make a conscious decision not to eat rined it for us.

Great food, but don't be in a hurry
by Derrick Hale (1 review)
October 12, 2009 - Likes it
This place is nothing to look at aesthetically, but the food is great. Don't plan to zip in and out of here unless you call way ahead. Go here when your wallet is a little heavy and you don't have much else to do. if you go on a day when your schedule is tight, you will be disappointed. Everything is prepared fresh. You will even here the chef grinding his own spices. I had the seafood platter and took half of it with me. They give you big portions. it is worth the trip. Don't rush the meal, you will wait a while and don't expect to be cheap that day. I love the place.

Excellent Restaurant
by Keith Barbera (1 review)
May 21, 2009 - Likes it
Although I normally get the Firecracker Fish 'n Chips, all the dishes at Fresh Fresh Seafood are home-made and delicious. (See the reviews at the restaurant's www.freshfreshseafood.com.) Owner and Chef Ricky Parker has been putting his heart and soul into cooking for more than twenty-five years, and he is ably assisted by his wife Darlene, who is co-owner and an excellent host. Patrons are made to feel at home, and the expertly prepared fresh food is always worth the wait. I'll be heading back to New England soon to go to law school. But this restaurant is one big reason that I'll return to Maryland for visits.

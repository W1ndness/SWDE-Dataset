Good fish and chips
by purleygirl (10 reviews)
April 30, 2010 - Likes it
A short walk from Faneuil Hall and Quincy Market, the Bell in Hand Tavern is close enough for tired travellers to lurch over for a refreshing pint (or two) yet far enough away from the cacaphony of buskers. Cosy, good service, and a reasonable prices. The front is especially good to watch an international parade of tourists pass by on the Freedom Trail.
I highly recommend the fish 'n' chips, which was lightly breaded and came with crispy fries and fresh made cole slaw. We washed it all down with a pint of their house brand, the Bell in Hand (an amber) and a half-pint of Harpoon, a local cider. Delicious!

Great Experience

October 18, 2009 - Likes it
Went there for a late lunch. Were seated quickly, ordered and served quickly. The burger, grilled cheese, New England Clam Chowder, and shrimp rolls were delicious. We got juice for the kids and thought that $3.25 per glass seemed high but other than that it fits the $$ category.

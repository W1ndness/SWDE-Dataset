Worth a weekly visit!
by Doc (6 reviews)
May 09, 2010 - Likes it
This teppan-yaki style Japanese Steakhouse is top among its competitors in the quality of its food, friendly staff and "fun factor" for kids and adults. One added bonus is that the sushi (often overshadowed by the "flames") is also fantastic... usually you either get one or the other.
For those with small children and limited time... you can be in and out in 1 hour without cutting any part of the experience short!

okay at best
by dondk (32 reviews)
April 02, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I've been here a few times, tried the sushi once, not WOW but good. Can you find better? sure.
Now to the Hibachi side... You will end up with a lot of food, more than most other Hibachi houses will serve. Now to the quality, having an idea what a good steak looks like, this place does not serve it. I'd be surprised if the meat was choice grade, including the Filet. I generally always gets the emperor's (filet, lobster, scallop, shrimp) where ever I go. The lobster every time is the smallest lobster tail I have ever seen, at least 1/2 of what most Hibachi houses serve. They load you up on rice, vegetables and noodles and skimp out on lesser quality and quantity meats. Not just this location, but other locations as well.
Overall, the hibachi experience is standard, is it all worth the price? I am sure you will not walk away hungry, is that enough to justify the price? That is something each will have to decide.

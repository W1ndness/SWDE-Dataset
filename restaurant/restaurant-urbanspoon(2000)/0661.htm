Best in town!
by Jackie (1 review)
November 23, 2009 - Likes it
I stumbled upon Green Street when I moved into the area 4 years ago. Since then I have become a "regular". Whether it's breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, or a late night with friends for drinks, this is the place. The staff and I know each other by name, the owner has become a friend, and even other "regulars" and I have established friendships. It's the neighborhood Cheers, just a little bit more refined. My favorite mojitos (of course with Bacardi Superior on request) are made by Meghan. Sad to say, I often prefer to come on days she works because her mojitos are by far the best! Jorge, the bartender, also has a great personality. And Aziz and Eric (the managers) are spectacular! Silvano, I commend you on the hidden jewel in the Grove you have founded.

The furnishings and decorations bring an air of mystery and distinction which makes Green Street Café stand far above the rest and their Mediterranean menu will delight even the pickiest of palates. With flair of extravaganza and a dash of VIP seating, Greenstreet Café is simply the place to be. Situated in the heart of Coconut Grove, there is never a dull moment as Greenstreets pleasant and friendly staff make sure you receive the most impeccable of services and the best meal in town.

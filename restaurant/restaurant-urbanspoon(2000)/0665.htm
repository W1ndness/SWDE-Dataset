The Boss loves the RG
by Tom Roland (37 reviews)
March 21, 2010 - Really likes it
The Boss and I must pass fifteen or twenty Chinese/Japanese/Asian restaurants to go to the Red Ginger! Why? Because of the food, service and ambiance of this outstanding restaurant.
Located in a little strip mall on the north side of Sample Road, the Red Ginger has a large dining room and a nice little bar.
Last night, after being seated with our regular server (Thanks, Joel), we had the Lettuce Wraps as an appetizer. Fried noodles, chicken, corn and a tasty plum sauce wrapped in big crunchy pieces of lettuce.
The Sunset special comes with soup or an egg /spring roll but if you go after Sunset, make sure to try the best rolls around.
I usually go with the General's Chicken or the Garlic Shrimp. Last night, it was the General and the Boss had her Green Curry Chicken. Big portions, fresh vegetables and presented beautifully.
We are not big Sushi people but there is a full Sushi bar with a wide range of selections.
Red Ginger is a great place . . . much more than Chang's or the local Chinese take out. Give it a try. And try and sit with Joel, one of the best waiters around. Friendly, professional and attentive without being intrusive.

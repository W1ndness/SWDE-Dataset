Get the Cheesecake....
by OffTheBeatenPath (512 reviews)
October 19, 2008 - Likes it
So everything at this place is good, the steaks are always good, sides are large with a good selection, but I always go for the cheesecake.  I've never found any cheesecake that even comes close to thiers.  It's light, not heavy and tastes wonderful.  Save room for dessert and order the cheesecake, you won't be disappointed.

Horrible -- Not for a Party of 8
by Teri (1 review)
December 15, 2008 - Doesn't like it
"NOT FOR A SMALL GROUP---8 PEOPLE PARTY TOO MUCH FOR THE IDIOTS TO HANDLE.
We had a small party of 8 at the SaltGrass......NEVER AGAIN...... When you have an 8 person party the waiters know the gratuity is an 18% given no matter how bad the service is!!!!  First the young people there couldn't understand the party was for 8.... they tried to seat us with 5, next try was for 6 then finally a half hour later we finally found someone who could count to 8.  Once seated and the menus handed out....another interminable long wait for a person to serve us drinks.  There were about 3 empty tables around us at the time we sat down....people were seated at those tables, received their drinks AND their food before the waiter even took our order!!!!  Then it took another life time to get the food.....evidentially my shrimp and chicken had gotten cold waiting for the rest of the meals to be made because they saw fit to nuke the meal back to warm....nuking shrimp and chicken turns the meat into rubber!!!!!  Disgusting horrible place to pop over $200 and 3 hours.  If you have a holiday crowd....find someplace else to eat.....these people in Round Rock, Texas are pathetic!!!!!"

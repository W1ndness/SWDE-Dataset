Great Ribs!
by William Ingalsbe (125 reviews)
July 17, 2009 - Really likes it
This place is all about the ribs. They are the best I have ever had. They are pricey, however. With a drink and tip, lunch was over $30. The ribs were well worth it. The meat just fell off the bones, and I ate the whole meal with a knife and fork. Beside the price, the only other downside was the noise level in the restaurant. It was very loud. The waitress was friendly and the service was good. If you like baby back rids, this is the place to go.

Which to order???
by Stacey Boudier (5 reviews)
August 06, 2008 - Likes it
Remember how the smell of someone barbecuing in the neighborhood could make your mouth water?  Well a drive by Houston's will  do the same.  Their tender sweet-marinated rib-eye steaks are in competition with their incredibly tender, fall-from-the-bone ribs covered in tantalizingly tangy red sauce.  Both are complemented with those delicately crunchy shoestring fries.
But a tasty lunch that's not to be overlooked is the light, tempura-like crunchy chicken nestled among the avocado, greems and crispy bits of bacon in the fabulous Cobb salad.  (Lace it with the garlic buttermilk dressing!)
Save yourself the agony of deciding, and just come back again later in the week.

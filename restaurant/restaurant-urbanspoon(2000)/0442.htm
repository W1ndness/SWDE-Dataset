Great experience
by Pat Peters (2 reviews)
December 04, 2009
We went to Town on our last night in Oahu. We especially enjoyed the fish and chips appetizer, salads, gnochhi, risotto, and chicken. The steak was expensive (it's listed on the menu as $2/ounce and the portion served was 27 ounces or $54) and a little chewy for my taste, but otherwise everyone loved the food, atmosphere, and service. Great drinks, too!

mobile review
by Waiter D (5 reviews)
February 25, 2009 - Really likes it
Finding a good neighborhood restaurant on O'ahu can be difficult. Unless your neighborhood is Kaimuki. Town is a comfortable oasis where the food takes center stage. The freshest local ingredients find their way onto the menu that is changed so frequently that the old menus are recycled into coasters for your drinks. Expect your server to great you by saying "tonight we are featuring." Wonderful.

Real Burgers - Real Calories
by D T (20 reviews)

This is definitely not the place to eat if you're watching your waist line. The burgers & fries are cooked the way they were meant to be cooked, before fast food chains took over and replaced real meat with breaded fillers and other 'lite' foods.

Although I recently saw that the double cheese burger was listed as one of the most unhealthy burgers on the planet, I'll still return for my share of "moo"-burger and carbs. Sometimes, taste beats healthy.

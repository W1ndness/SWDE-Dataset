Try the Etouffee!
by Tastes of Tulsa (1 review)
February 26, 2010 - Likes it
I highly recommend the Shrimp Etouffee, which reason enough to frequent the Chickory & Chives. The blonde roux gravy in the etouffee is just the right mix of savory and spice. It has a smooth butter and onion base with a spicy cajun note at the end. The dish comes with really cheesy cheese & chive toast that compliments the dish perfectly. Gumbo was a good substitute for the salad but if salad is your thing then the dinner salads are larger then at most diners and are a garden mix with tomatoes and cheese, not just iceberg and tomatoes.
Also, thier Onion Rings are far different then any onion ring I've ever had. They use a flour batter instead of the usual cornmeal, so if your looking for a different spin on an old standard try the onion rings.
My wife had the Chicken Fried Steak which was large, hand breaded, and good, but nothing out of the ordinary. If you go here, go Cajun.

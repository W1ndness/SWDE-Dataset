We consider ourselves epicures; fine dining is our passion. La Viola is impressive visually as well as to the tastebuds. The meal began with iced tea that queched our thirst and produced a wonderfully pleasant mouthfeel. Ceasar salads accompanied by warm fresh bread delighted our mouths. Our delightment grew when we are presented with the house special, a spinach pasta with shrimp and salmon, and a penne to die for.

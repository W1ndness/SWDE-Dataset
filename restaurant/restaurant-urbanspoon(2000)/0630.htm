Wow! On a lot of levels....
by Lisa Woods (76 reviews)
May 24, 2010 - Really likes it
Okay, let me start w/ the bad - because there was definitely some bad here. The service sucked tremendously (what service?) unless you are sitting @ the bar & then you get to listen to surly wait staff complain. When we finally went inside to place our order because no one showed up outside like they said they would, we were told it would be an hour! An hour for BBQ? We went ahead & begrudgingly ordered anyway. After an hour, just as we were beginning to get really pissed off (& a little tipsy) the meals finally showed up & delighted our tastebuds. Suddenly I was not so upset. All of the sides we tried were exceptional! We had cole slaw (think we detected blue cheese in it), green beans, broccoli w/ cheese & baked beans. The ribs were also killer - falling off the bone tender! Would love to eat their food again. Maybe we'll do carryout this time......
To summarize, the food was the best overall BBQ I've had to date in the Louisville area. The service, some of the lousiest ever. That being said, I'm still adding it as a favorite.

A Pleasant Surprise
by Will Brake 4 Steak (4 reviews)
May 23, 2010 - Likes it
I have driven by this place many times and thought it was just some redneck bar. A friend of mine and I were in a desperate search for real barbecue one night (anything but mainstream). We walked in and sat at the bar (due to the fact that the place was packed) We ordered the wings just to try the place out...they were out of this world!!! We then ordered the ribs and pulled pork...absolutely awesome!!! The sides were also out of this world! The employees were very friendly and attentive and the atmosphere was great! It was so good, I ordered carry out there the very next day and got the half chicken...fantastic!!

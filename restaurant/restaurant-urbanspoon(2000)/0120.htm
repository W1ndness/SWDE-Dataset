Worth the drive
by Danny Nicholas (1 review)
June 27, 2010
I go past at least 3 other Chinese places to get here - The General Tso, Mongolian Beef and Shrimp with Lobster Sauce are all top notch. I also recommend the egg rolls and Hot and Sour soup with crunchy noodles. Others say this is "Americanized" Chinese, but it is superior to any other Chinese in Birmingham (and most anywhere else).

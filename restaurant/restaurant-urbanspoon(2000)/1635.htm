A PHENOMENAL sushi bar within one's budget
by David E (1 review)
November 23, 2009 - Really likes it
Maki Toki is an ABSOLUTE TREASURE in central north Austin!
It all comes down to taste and they nail it consistently.
Maki Toki doesn't have budget sushi; they have fresh, quality sushi within one's budget. Their prices are so good one actually has the luxury of eating until they're full without busting their wallet.
They're located in the relatively new plaza right behind the Walgreens at the intersection of Braker Ln and Metric Blvd.
They have great lunch specials, but perhaps my favorite time to go is during their Sushi Angry Hour from 4:30-6:00PM seven days a week.
Writing this review has made me think about them and made my mouth water. I'm going there right now -- no joking!

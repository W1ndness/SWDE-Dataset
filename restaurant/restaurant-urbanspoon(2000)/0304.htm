Go, just don't go hungry.
by Robert Tate (1 review)
December 14, 2009 - Likes it
First the good: The atmosphere is outstanding. The staff is very friendly with quick service, short waits, and a drink refill on your table before you know you need one. During Christmas they over decorate to the point of being ridiculous. They have two model trains that circle the restaurant entertaining children every 3 minutes. A very fun place to be.
Now the bad: To say the food is subpar is doing Campo Verde a kindness. It is perhaps the most bland TexMex I have ever experienced. Their queso is a suspicious orange color that resembles Tang. They do have a few exotic meats on their menu but since they are woefully unsuccessful in making the basics, it would only stand to reason that the exotic dishes would be the same.
In short, don't ignore Campo Verde just because the food is bad. It's still worth going, just don't go hungry.

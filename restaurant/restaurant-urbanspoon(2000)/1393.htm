Best Thai Food In Albuq For Years
by Galloping Gourmand (18 reviews)
July 30, 2010 - Really likes it
Bangkok Cafe (as it was called for many years) was either the first or second Thai restaurant in Albuquerque, the other being the Thai House on Buena Vista down by the University.
They have been serving up the best Thai Food in Albuquerque for more than 20 years. Superb is the word I would use for their curries. For a long time, the green curry was my favorite. It went downhill during a management change a couple of years ago, but is now almost back to where it once was. Their Massaman curry is excellent. They also have a great Thai fried rice, very different from the Chinese or Vietnamese varieties. (Don't worry, carnivores will find plenty there to please them as well.)
They have undergone a lot of management changes in the past few years, but their food and menu have essentially remained the same. When they became Asian Fusion, they had a hammer dulcimer player on Friday and Saturday nights who echoed loudly around the gymnasium acoustics of the place and made conversation difficult. Luckily they got rid of him.
They have recently changed management again (and wait staff, unfortunately). They are no longer Asian Fusion, just Bangkok Thai. The food is better than before, if anything. They have recently divided up the warehouselike dining room into smaller, more intimate areas, which is much better than it's ever been, but it might make seating for large groups difficult.
They have a lot of competition, with Thai House (formerly Thai Ginger) and Mom Thai a mile south, and Thai Cuisine II and Orchid Thai a mile west. I would rate Bangkok the best of the five restaurants. In fact, I'd rate it the best Thai food in Albuquerque.

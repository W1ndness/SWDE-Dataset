Delicious noodle dishes
by queenbeethatsme (30 reviews)
September 25, 2009 - Likes it
I've only eaten here a few times but that only is because at least half of my family does not relish oriental cuisine and unfortunately, that half is usually with me when I'm in Westport.
I found both the food and the atmosphere to be delightful. It got a little pricey because we each ordered two entrees to provide a sort of tasting menu for the group and I ordered several appetizers as well. The best way to sample a new restaurant is to order ala carte, not to do the buffet (at first) and to order a mix of seafood, meats, noodles, rice and spicy and bland dishes--just to get an overview.
All restaurants should be reconnoitered. by going in first and sampling as much as possible, this allows informed choices the next go round. This works best in a large group where everyone agrees before hand to a tasting scheme. Beware that someone will get an absolutely divine dish and will not want to share and another person will get a meal that is the equivalent of culinary old maid, with them tricking you into giving them some of your great dish for their meal from hades. But that is part of the fun.
I found most dishes here edible, but cannot recall the name of any--I will make a purposeful trip back, order a few and return with a review. :)

Takeaway Gone Bad
by Finius (1 review)
May 15, 2009 - Likes it
I recently ordered takeaway from the Thai Place in Westport. Considering the impeccable accuracy from previous experiences, I didn't check the order. I ordered the Golden Fried Rice (crab meat), Gapow Pork and Crab Rangoon. Instead of the Golden Fried Rice, I received Shrimp Fried Rice. At first, I was fine with it. Then I noticed that the shrimp had not been deveined. Begrudgingly, I called Thai Place to explain the error. Unfortunately, the lady that answered didn't' believe me. I asked that she just give me credit back for the incorrect dish and we could be done with it. She declined. So I had to make a 20 minute trip back to the restaurant. When I arrived, she witnessed the "poo-packed" prawns. I asked for credit back for the Golden Fried Rice as well as re-firing my entire order since I had to bring it all back. She quickly rejected my request. She countered that I could keep the shrimp dish, receive the Golden Fried Rice, and get credit back for the Golden Fried Rice. I decided to accept the offer but I didn't want the shrimp dish. I then asked why they didn't devein the shrimp and she replied, "We don't devein any of the shrimp in our dishes." Are you kidding me?? As she witnessed my disgust, she then offered up a fresh order of Crab Rangoon. Of course, I accepted. But as for the shrimp, I've had plenty of shrimp dishes there before and they never had the "crap-canal" in them! I'm obviously going to give the Thai Place my business in the future, but with a much more critical approach.

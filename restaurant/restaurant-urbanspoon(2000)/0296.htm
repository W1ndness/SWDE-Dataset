Message from Tex's Star Grill

Family-friendly grill provides fresh, made to order burgers, salads, gyros and more in North Richland Hills

  • Burgers, Gyros, Chicken, Shrimp
  • Catfish, Salads, Sandwiches, Kids Menu
  • Dine-In, Drive Thru, Carryout

Tex's Star Grill in North Richland Hills truly has something to please everyone.

From classics like chicken fried steak, burgers and cheeseburgers to kid favorites like hot dogs, corn dogs, chicken nuggets and grilled cheese sandwiches, Tex's Star Grill prepares all menu items made to order.
Enjoy the chicken kabob dinner, sirloin beef kabob dinner, shrimp and catfish dinner, or one of many other tasty dinner options. Dine on sandwich favorites like gyros on pita bread, golden turkey on garlic toast, pastrami or tuna. Salad lovers will enjoy the Greek salad, with or without chicken or gyro meat and other salad options. Complete your meal with a freshly made dessert item.
Tex's Star Grill offers mouthwatering burgers, homemade chicken fried steak, authentic Philly CheeseSteak, salads, tender chicken sandwiches, kabob dinners, and the famous Chicago-style gyro sandwiches.

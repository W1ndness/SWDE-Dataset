Middle Eastern food on a budget without cutting corners
by Will York (5 reviews)
February 19, 2010 - Likes it
If only the federal government could give us all that we want for next to no cost. Aladdin's, a local chain, accomplishes this magic. The food is just what you expect; there are really no "specialties" per se, but on the other hand, there are no bad surprises. The portions are huge, the service is gracious, and the desserts (as un-Middle Eastern as they are) are not to be missed.

Best Pizza in Rio Rancho....
by Z's Choice (2 reviews)
May 26, 2010 - Really likes it
...quite possibly the best Pizza in Albuquerque Metro Area. Get the New York Pie you will not be disappointed, plus if you order the 18" New York Pie you can get a 12" pie for a buck. Since the restaurant is in Rio Rancho you can guess that this is New York Style Pizza. The only drawback to this place is occasionally they leave the pizza in the oven a few minutes to long and the crust gets a little to crisp. I think they are right up there with Saggio's.

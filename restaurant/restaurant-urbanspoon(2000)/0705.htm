A Fan No More
by BizzNessMPLS (127 reviews)
October 21, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I originally liked this place, but after the last pizza we had, I have no desire to go back. It was terribly greasy, which I know people will say, "of course it is, you are eating pizza." There is a difference between good greasy and bad greasy. Black Sheep hot salami pizza = good greasy, Cossettas = good greasy, Red's Savoy = good greasy but Fat Lorenzos = bad greasy. The sauce at Fat Lorenzos was good, but the crust was borderline terrible, really soft and pillowy and without flavor. I have also had the steak and cheese hot hoagie, also unimpressive. The gelato on the other hand, was pretty good.

by Ruby (33 reviews)
March 19, 2010 - Likes it
Take this place for what it is. Do not hold it to your pretentious "authentic Italian" standards. The pizza is pretty much unrivalled if it is the type of pizza you like -- fully loaded and of gargantuan proportions. It is hard not to have a nice time here unless you are a snob. The service is impeccable, so even if you are a snob you might be tempted to concede you had a good experience.

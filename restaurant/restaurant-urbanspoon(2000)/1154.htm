Best in SD!
by CityGal (81 reviews)
June 24, 2009 - Really likes it
Really good fresh sushi should melt in your mouth and taste like the ocean. Sushi Ota delivers and then some. Real sushi the way Japanese and Asians adore so be adventurous and so don't go looking for the "Dragon Roll" try something new. Don't let the strip mall location fool you, this place is top notch so no flip flops and swim wear and reservations are a must. Limited English spoken by the staff (so point and smile) and interior is small and cramped. A seat at the sushi bar requires reservations far in advance as Japanese tourist book the seat before their flights. Worth every penny! Ota-san opened a 2nd shop named Hane - same high caliber quality.

Oh dear oh dear oh dear
by Mike Song (16 reviews)
June 25, 2010 - Likes it
it sounds like the people who dislike this place are butthurt about some experience where they did not get treated like kings.
Grow up. This place is about the food and the sushi quality is excellent. It's some of the best sushi you'll ever eat. It is deliciously cut. I watch my friends smile as they experience their first bite of sushi ota goodness.
The service here is ok. They are a bit in a rush but hey, I'll take that sacrifice for food that is consistent and delivers at a high quality level every time. Easily my favorite sushi spot in san diego.
Make reservations before you go, you'll have a hard enough time finding a parking spot.

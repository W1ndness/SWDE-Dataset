Message from Boardwalk Fresh Burgers...

Burgers and Fries Made Fresh Daily!!!

  • Since 1975!!
  • Never frozen grass-fed beef
  • 100s of possible combinations!

Boardwalk Fresh Burgers & Fries brings the boardwalk experience to life providing customers with made to order burgers and fries in a fun, beach themed atmosphere. As the sister company of Boardwalk Fries - the only national french fry brand in the United States - Boardwalk Fresh Burgers & Fries has nearly 30 years of corporate brand recognition. Brothers, Dave and Fran DiFerdinando created the quick casual concept Boardwalk Fresh Burgers & Fries in 2007. Inspired by the boardwalk in Ocean City, Maryland, the concept provides customers with a fresh, made to order burger and fries experience in a family-friendly, beach-themed environment. Boardwalk Fresh Burgers & Fries is on the forefront of a new wave of fast foods that are quick and convenient, but still natural and unadulterated.

Good food, great service
by Gilbert Nichols (2 reviews)
April 08, 2010 - Really likes it
Before you roll your eyes and say, "what? another burger joint," you gotta check this place out! I had the Bleu Burger with a "small" order of fries and a drink for a fair price and followed it with their signature dessert, Funnel Cake sticks that are shaped like french fries but are like their cousins in the festivals - topped with powdered sugar. Like the picture, the Bleu burger was large and tasty and enough for most people. I, unlike most people, am seven feet tall and had a little more room for all the food on my order yesterday. The small order of fries was more than I could handle and appeared more like the super-sized fry orders from the typical fast-food chain stores. Boardwalk belongs in Atlanta and I highly recommend everyone stopping by to see what I'm talking about!

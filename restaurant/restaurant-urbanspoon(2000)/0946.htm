Maybe a seasonal thing. The only problem with this place is that they have priced themselves out of my budget. Sadly, I have to recommend it with reservations due to the prices. i.e. $12 a bowl for the aforementioned soup.

Absolutely delicious
by BEandB (7 reviews)
December 29, 2009 - Really likes it
My family and I must have ordered at least 20 different tapas on the menu here and every last one of them was PHENOMENAL. Our server was outstanding, and paced the meal for us so that we did not have to worry about ordering the correct tapas in the appropriate order. Given that they serve Basque-style Spanish tapas, we ordered a nice Rioja to accompany the meal. I would definitely recommend to everyone!

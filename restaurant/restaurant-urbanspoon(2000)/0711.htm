Positively vocal at the Local
by Kris (17 reviews)
July 21, 2010 - Likes it
Located on the corner of 10th St. and Nicollet Mall, the Local was an enjoyable dining experience. Spacious inside and outside seating. Opted for outside on this first visit.
The Big Ginger drinks were quite tasty and I believe is their signature drink... though I'm not so sure Jameson and Ginger Ale is a newly found thing, but they served their purpose. ;-) I ordered up a plate of grilled chicken and greens, both of which were done right and delicious.
In all, the drinks and eats were good, the staff pleasant, facilities clean, and atmosphere upbeat.
Would happily revisit.

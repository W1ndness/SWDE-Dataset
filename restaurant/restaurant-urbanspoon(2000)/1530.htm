cut above the rest
by JK (7 reviews)
June 08, 2010 - Likes it
This is truly a great place to eat and drink. The menu is a major surprise and offers much more than just the normal Italian fare. The staff is awesome and the place is usually managed by multiple family owners who truly make every effort to make sure your experience is a good one. They have a very stable wait staff so you have consistent service and the Chef will alter items for you and raises a high bar for standards of food preperation and presentation. The bar is a true Trader Vic style local with incredible original drinks and specialty martinis and other concocsions. The bartenders here are not just a shot and beer level players, they know how to mix a damn good drink. Recommend this place highly and it is a great atmospere and clean as can be. Great value.

Excellent taste of Italy, right in Acworth.
by Ray (10 reviews)
May 24, 2010 - Likes it
A friend recommended Fusco's. I didn't know what to expect, having never been to Main Street Acworth before. I was pleasantly surprised by the decor and ambiance; this would be an excellent place for date. The wedding soup was amazingl I'm not Italian but I assume this is authentic. I had the lasagna, which is the ruler I measure Italian places against. It is served as a generous portion with a ginormous side salad. to this day I don't know how I finished it all, plus 2 beers. The bartender was very friendly and obviously knew several of the other patrons, so this is a place where you can get to know the staff and be known. I highly recommend this place. The only caveat is that it is a tad pricey. But it is worth the cost.

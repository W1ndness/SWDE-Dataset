Message from Wazee Supper Club

The Grandaddy of LoDo, Over 35 Years Strong!

  • Great Pizza
  • Happy Hour
  • Lively Bar

Surrounded by brick and stone reminders of Denver's colorful past, The Wazee Supper Club has long been an inevitable destination for social gatherers. The Wazee Supper Club, with its classic urban atmosphere, has earned favor with artists, politicians, architects, theater-goers, entertainers, businessmen, movers, shakers and generally people of good character from around the globe. The Wazee also quickly became known as a source of the best pizza in the civilized world.

Late Night Haven
by Tommy (5 reviews)
May 10, 2010 - Likes it
Wazee is one of my favorite late night watering holes. The staff is great, welcoming and friendly and while it may take one or two times to be considered worthy of the rest of the regulars who flock to the bar, once you are in, you're in for life.
Good beers on tap and great late night food...specifically the pizzas. It gets packed so if you are lucky enough to grab a seat at the bar, guard it with your life.
Bottom line at Wazee, it pays to be considered a regular and it may just be one of my favorite bars in the city!

Wazee strombolin'
by JacquelineH (3 reviews)
November 13, 2008
This is a classic Lodo joint, a place right out of the movie Diner, but instead of the boxcar look, you get great food in a typical old industrial Lodo building.  Very cool.  I was back recently for a group lunch and found it hadn't changed.  But then I'm not sure if it's changed too much in 30 years.
The pizza is well known and very good, but my favorite has always been the strombolis.  You can't go wrong with any of the variations and there's enough toppings to make two pizzas.  But a late night pie is a popular choice.
Management and staff are well trained but if it's jammed, and it usually is, you might have to flag someone down.  Order a stromboli or pizza, then get a huge basket of fries or rings, and kick back to watch a typical diner scene unfold.  This is about as mixed a demographic as you'll find downtown, and occasionally the mayor stops in for one of his favorite meals.

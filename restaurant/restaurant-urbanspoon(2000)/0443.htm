Cinnamons, just thinking about it makes me want to jump off bed and take a 20 min drive for breakfast here. I have friends that recently moved and the 2nd thing they miss is going to Cinnamons-their 1st is Hawaii itself, I'm just glad I live here so I can enjoy more often. I tried their guava chiffon pancakes....imagine a spongy buttery pancake smothered in a sweet delicious light guava syrup with cloudy whipped cream in your mouth. For me eating this pancake was an amazing experience. Their Customer service was good too, although we had to wait (obviously cause it's so good locals & tourists come here) their service was pretty fast. The wait, may I say, gave us time to have a happy conversation (one of the side effects of smelling the fresh air at their restaurant). In basic terms: good ambiance!

LOVE IT!!!!!!!!!!
by Lilly Kehau (11 reviews)
March 02, 2010 - Likes it
Best hollandaise ever. One of the only places that still makes their own hollandaise from scratch, by the way... their crab cakes are to die for, their daily breakfast specials are creative and amazing.
Every Cinnamon's host, waiter and waitress I've ever talked to is friendly, helpful, and GREAT at what they do. They definitely know how to keep people coming back, and I've been eating there without a complaint for over 20 years.
I suggest the crabcake benedict, the guava chiffon pancakes, the carrot cake pancakes, or the S.A.N.D. (start of a nice day, pancakes, eggs, the works). Oh, and the corn cakes with blueberries. For lunch they have an out-of-this-world curry chicken salad in a half papaya, which may sound strange, but trust me...........

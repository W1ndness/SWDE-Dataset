Message from Flying Biscuit Cafe

Come share some Biscuit Love!

  • Breakfast All Day! Lunch & Dinner too!
  • We serve Beer & Wine
  • Let us cater your next event!

At the Flying Biscuit Cafe we want to provide you with a taste of heaven! Located in the Stonecrest Shopping center off of 485 and Rea road we are a down-home, but world-wise restaurant that has earned accolades not only form locals, but also from the likes of Bon Appetit, Rachel Ray, Food Network, Zagats, Good Morning America and many more! Stop by today and see what all the fuss is about. You can also contact us for all of your catering needs for your special event.

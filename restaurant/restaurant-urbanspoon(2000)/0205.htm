Blown Away
by JB (10 reviews)
June 25, 2009 - Really likes it
Truly one of the best all around meals i have had in my life. I work in the industry and dine out frequently and this was a rare experience. Everything from the menu and food execution down to the designer server uniforms is top-notch. I was with a large party on a weekend night with a younger group and quite frankly expected to be the restaurant's lowest priority as we were clearly not their average clientele BUT it was exactly the opposite. Our server made us feel very comfortable and patiently explained the menu to people in our party less familiar with fine dinning. He made excellent by the glass wine pairings for each course, including desert. Since i was with a large group i was able to taste many of the menu offerings and i was in love with all of it. i think the mark of a great chef is the ability to take an ingredient that may not be someones favorite and create a knock out dish anyway and that was certainly the case at Blackbird. when it came to dessert time we just had to order everything on the menu and although some were little out there all were fabulous. Truly amazing, i will not soon forget that meal. Sadly i no longer live in Chicago but i will definitely return next time i am in the windy city.

Amazing food - more than $$
by kelly (1 review)
April 22, 2009 - Likes it
Easily the best meal we've had in years. The duck with bacon was delish. The flatiron steak was fabulous. The service was great and the atmosphere was quite nice.
Be warned: this was way more than a $$ restaurant. More like $$$+ or $$$. We each had one entree and one glass of wine, no appetizers or desert and the bill topped $100. Most entrees are $30-$35, although there are few in the $20s.
Reservations recommended, although we managed to squeeze in on a Monday night at the bar.

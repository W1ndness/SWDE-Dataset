A Little Slice of France in MWC
by socalbeautyjd (1 review)
October 07, 2008 - Likes it
This place is just fantastic.  If you're looking for authentic French home cooking with mouthwatering French styled sub sandwiches, then this is the place for you! From the quiche to the onion soup to the Normandy (#9) sandwich, I've never been disappointed. The owners come from France, whose accents are just as charming as they are. The food is authentic, fresh and fast. I bring all my out-of-town visitors to Napoleon's. As a mom and pop establishment they don't take credit or debit cards, so be prepared to pay with cash or personal check. This place is worth the drive to Midwest City. Napoleon's is truly a slice of France in Oklahoma!

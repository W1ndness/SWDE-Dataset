Cleanliness is Godliness...and the last couple of times I've been there it has not been nearly as clean as it used to.  Frankly, it's been a bit dirty and unclean/unsanitary if you look closely at the floors, corners, decorations, and preparation areas.  Food is decent...but it's like most other high-end Hibachi's now-a-days, just dirtier.

Lovely Lunch
by carnellm (46 reviews)
May 08, 2009 - Likes it
My wife and I hit The Early Bird Diner for lunch today and loved it. We were a bit worried, since it is the former location of a not-so-great place, but on entering we knew things had changed. The atmosphere is funky-diner and the staff were all helpful and friendly. I had a patty melt with fries and my wife got breakfast fare - the scrambler. This is a mixture of scrambled eggs, hashbrowns, sausage, and cheese served with a biscuit and a side. Wife chose fried green tomatoes for her side. The food came out quick and the quality was great. Basically, hot and yummy! For these two meals, two drinks, and tip the bill came to about $24, which is right in line with what I would expect.
So, don't be put off by the exterior. The place has a great menu, nice people, and is open late on the weekends!

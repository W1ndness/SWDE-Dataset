I hate this place
by Terri (1 review)
February 25, 2010 - Doesn't like it
My husband grew up in AA and raved about this place. He warned me about the "quirkiness" of the ordering process. I had also seen them featured on Food Networks "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives" (this would fall into the "DIVE" category), and knew about the burger Nazi. When we visited during the AA Art Fair, I thought I was ready. I knew what I wanted. I waited patiently. I ran through the order in my head. HEAVEN FORBID that you mess up! I did not want to be the center of attention. If you don't like being the center of attention, DO NOT EAT HERE. I mean don't even think about it. I was so keyed up about getting my order straight that I screwed it up. Within one word escaping my lips, the burger Nazi flipped out, making a big example of me, whereupon every single person in front of me in line turned and stared at me with these silly grins on their faces. I get it. It's supposed to be funny. Whatever. If you like that sort of thing, and enjoy greasy burgers, go for it. I choose to patronize establishments with more taste and less gimmick. It's not about "lightening up". It's just stupid, in my opinion. If you don't think so, great. Have fun.

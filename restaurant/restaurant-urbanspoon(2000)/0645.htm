The Flying Fish
by Mike Walker (30 reviews)
January 12, 2009 - Likes it
Absolutely outstanding!  As always, I have no vested interest in this place.
What can I say about a place where the fried catfish comes out and it is still making noise?  I don't know how you rate "fresh and hot" but in Air Traffic Mike's book here, THAT'S fresh and hot.  Excellent sized portions, I can barely finish the three piece catfish dinner.  Somehow, I always find room.  My Lenten resolution this year is to eat there for all 40 days.  
Don't limit yourself to the fried catfish though.  The entire menu is a wonderful experience.  Hey, who doesn't like oysters?  Fish tacos anyone?  Trust me, you can't go wrong with this place when it comes to getting your seafood fix.
If it sucked, I'd tell you right away.
This place is a spot on pick if you're in the mood for fish, especially fried catfish.

Gulf Coast in Memphis
by J.R. Ewing (27 reviews)

I highly recommend the Flying Fish.  If you like the "seafood shacks" of Destin, Orange Beach, and other Gulf Coast eateries, you will love the Flying Fish.

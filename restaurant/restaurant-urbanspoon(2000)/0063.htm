So understand first this is not hill country rustic barbecue like the Salt Lick, or traditional German meat market barbecue like Louis Mueller. Be warned this place is also priced higher than your usual joint. So for starters, the deviled eggs are wonderful with fancy pickles and caviar. The rock shrimp are very good, but the best appetizer is the wild boar ribs, with a take on buffalo wings with s spicy hot vinegary sauce and creamy cabrales blue cheese. Great! For salad I like the fried green tomatoes with crabmeat. Fantastic the brisket, pulled pork, and jalapeño hot links are all very good. Besides the frito pie, the other winning dishes are the ribeye with brown sugar mustard crust and the pork chop. I like his Ft worth place better.

Great food
by Michael H (9 reviews)
August 24, 2009 - Likes it
In the new second street district, Lambert's has a nice downstairs area with some nicer seating, and slightly less nicer seating upstairs but with a stage for musicians (don't get me wrong - it's not "bad" upstairs... it's just not AS nice as downstairs).
As for the food, I ordered the "Mustard and Brown Sugar Crusted Niman Ranch Ribeye" and it was DELICIOUS. They cooked it a perfect medium rare, and they use a torch to quickly 'crust' the outside and it gives it a great flavor and texture. I haven't had a steak taste like that before, but I thoroughly enjoyed it. I also sampled a baby back rib that someone else ordered, and it was also very good.
Great place, has a little bit of everything - bar, nice/romantic seating, another bar upstairs, a stage for live music, and best of all - great food!
---
I also want to address the reviews below about it being overrated, overpriced, etc. You have to know what kind of place you're going to... this is a higher end bbq place and not Rudy's. Not that Rudy's isn't great - I love it - but this place is meant for nicer meals with some creativity. Yes they use sauces, glazes, etc and it's not just meat with some seasoning, but that's also the point. You go to a nicer restaurant to spend more and get a creative, unique tasting meal. If you want some good, basic ribs go to Rudy's.

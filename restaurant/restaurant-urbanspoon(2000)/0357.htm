Uncle Wendell's Barbque is the best
by Ken Root (1 review)
January 15, 2010 - Likes it
I am a big fan of barbque so I take it seriously. Wendell's restaurant on Ingersol has some great meat that is well prepared and served right. I particularly like the barbque beans. Wow! The are as much meat as beans and the texture is as good as it gets. He also bakes his own bread and that could stand alone but when you add the meat (particularly the pulled pork) and the finely done bread you get a texture that is something you won't find in many places. If I could get him to make one change it would be to bring out his own sauce. He has several that are good but a great place, like Arthur Bryant's in Kansas City or Gates in the same town, both have their signature barbque sauce th seal the deal. I like a rich and spicy sauce and you can sort of make it from a couple of the gallon jugs in his restaurant but a real killer sauce would set me free!
Price is good, place is spare, servers are friendly, barbque is the best in town.

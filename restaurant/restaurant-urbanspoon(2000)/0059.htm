Good American Bistro
by Lolita Hugo (10 reviews)
April 18, 2010 - Really likes it
I really love this little restaurant. Pros: very fresh, great-tasting food; quaint setting; professional waitstaff; prices reasonable to a little high; good dish presentation; and great flavor profiles/combination of dishes. The only cons I can think of: the crowd and the ambiance are a little poseur; the prices can be a tad high; and there's nothing extremely unusual for the taste buds. That being said, this really is a great place for bistro American cuisine. I continue to go back for more because everything is consistently tasty and extremely fresh. And really, when you consider how much chain restaurants charge these days, the prices aren't all that bad for the great quality. The panko-encrusted fish sticks (special), baked brie with apple chutney, panko and pecan encrusted chicken breast, vegetable enchiladas, and grilled sirloin meatloaf are all very good. Definitely worth your time to check this place out.

Delicious food
by Mom of teens (96 reviews)
June 01, 2010 - Likes it
We recently tried Dazzo's for the first time. We had a group of 20 and showed up not knowing what to expect for dinner on a Saturday evening. We, too, were a bit put off by the owner's caustic manner. The only waitress in the place just shrugged her shoulders, shook her head and even smiled a little at his reprimand, so we took her cue and decided not to be too outraged.
Three of us had to be somewhere before the rest of the group and asked if we could have our food prepared first. A calzone, a meatball sub and a chicken caesar salad arrived before the rest, as promised.
Shortly thereafter the rest of the food, including kids' meals and pizzas, arrived.
Because the families we were traveling with frequently dine out together with our teens, we will often help the wait staff. We did so on this occasion because there was just the one waitress and there were other diners besides us. We distributed the beverages for her and I took our orders on a napkin to save her some time. Perhaps this improved our standing with the owner.
The food was excellent and piping hot, the salad fresh and enough for 2 people.
As a group we have already declared this a necessary stop on our next visit to Knoxville.

Great service!
by TSW (4 reviews)

Called to order a to-go pizza right AFTER closing. Mr. Loyer answered the phone and offered to stay open to accommodate us (suggesting that we could even stay in the dining room and eat). We placed a to-go order for his largest pizza with hamburger and mushrooms. The pizza was ready by the time we arrived (we called on 275-S, less than 10 minutes from downtown).

Dazzo's is the best...
by Cyal8ta (1 review)
May 20, 2009
Italian food in Knoxville... Hands down. Being an Italian with Italian grandparents and living most of my life in New York I can say that Dazzo's is very VERY good. It would beat most places in NY so as far as Knoxville is concerned it is the BEST.

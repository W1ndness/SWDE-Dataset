So so
by Sarah (19 reviews)
April 27, 2009 - Doesn't like it
My husband and I really enjoyed our experience at Fox's other restaurant, Zinburger, and decided to give this one a try. it had some good qualities...kind service, relaxed feel, and short wait time but the food was just so-so. My husband and I both got margaritas that were too sweet but it could have been a bad bartender. We had a cheese crisp for an appetizer and it was on par with the ones we like at El Charro but my salad & my husband's burrito were nothing special.

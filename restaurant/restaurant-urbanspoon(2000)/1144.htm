mobile review
by Alex E (10 reviews)
June 13, 2009 - Likes it
I'm a huge fan of Bronx Pizza, Pauly's, and Pizzeria Luigi. I thought I hated deep-dish or Chicago style pizza, so I avoided Lefty's like the plague. As it turns out, I'd just never had a good one (I've had DeMille's, and years ago tried Uno). A friend surprised me with a slice and it was heaven on a golden, buttery crust. The ingredients are high quality, and served generously. The tomato sauce was the freshest I have ever tasted, and though I'm not usually into the meaty pizzas, the pepperoni and sausage were delicious. I can't wait to go there myself and try it again.

Great place for a date!
by CoopMan (3 reviews)
November 24, 2007 - Really likes it
The atmosphere is wonderful and the food is light. They have a terrific wine selection, however, their website hasn't been updated recently and the wine I went for was no longer available. :(
But the bartender was very knowledgeable and recommended two wines throughout the course of the evening and meal. Both were fantastic and I ended up taking a bottle home.
I loved the patio but ended up sitting inside as it was too cold and they do NOT have heaters... so bring a coat if you go in the winter time; even if you sit inside because they keep the large garage doors open all the time.
The food was amazing, especially the Bruschetta plate and the seasonal cheese selection.
For a nice evening out and some great wine this is the best place to go. You might even see a celebrity or two when you are there. I will be back many times!

My favorite place in Phoenix
by Sean Boudreau (6 reviews)
December 07, 2009 - Really likes it
Let me start off by saying, I do not understand the reviews saying Postino's is expensive, uptight, etc. Everyday from 11AM-5PM you can get ANY glass of wine or pitcher of beer for $5.00! That's a damn good deal, furthermore the concept of this Winecafe is to go with friends, share a few bites of food so split your food and enjoy the delightful savings!
As an out of towner, I love coming to Postino's and bringing family/friends/whoever I can find with me. It's has great atmosphere, very casual, affordable yet comfortable. Just all in all a great place. As everyone else mentioned the food is very good and fairly priced, sit on the patio year-round and sip some good wine or a great craft-brew and enjoy some olives and bruschetta or a pannini or a great bowl of soup.
I highly recommend this place, I really love the whole premise/concept/model that Postino's uses, Winecafes are not huge here in the Mid-West. It would be awesome to see something like this here.
Once again, it's a great place, go during happy hour and give it a try, you're sure to like it too.

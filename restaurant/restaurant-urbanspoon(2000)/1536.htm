Interesting place
by z84976 (1 review)
February 04, 2009 - Likes it
This was my first time to Mi Barrio, my wife's second.  She recommended it to me.  As you pull up, it kind of looks like a converted laundromat or something, but painted red and situated right by a check-cashing place.  Maybe it could have been a bait shop in a previous life.  Walk in, and it's quite informal, not by any means "fancy" or anything.  Very friendly, and most clientele seems to know the people working there.  Wifey had the Chile Relleno, I had the Fried Tilapia (they have the Mexican name on the menu, but I forget it).  The relleno was vegetarian, and had a massive lump of cheese in it.  Overall, for 9ish dollars, very good food.  When I ordered the 12ish dollar fish, I figured I'd get some breaded, fried fish, and that was ok by me.  But what I got was a whole tilapia fried.  WOW it was tasty.  Very pleasantly surprised.
Don't go here for some fancy atmosphere (Dog the Bounty Hunter was on the TV), but go for some good food and really friendly service.  Oh, and while we were eating our standard chips-n-salsa while awaiting our food, they brought us some sort of pickled cactus stuff for the chips.  Really nice.

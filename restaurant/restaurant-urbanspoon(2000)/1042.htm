mobile review
by Jordan Smellie (1 review)
May 22, 2010 - Likes it
Absolutely fantastic. I'm a summer intern at UNC, and some coworkers brought me here. The amount of condiments/toppings available is unbelievable, and none of them are an extra cost. I combined the two daily specials- Cajun burger topped with andouille sausage (on a burger! amazing!) but got goat cheese instead if pepperjack; they were hapy to accomodate the odd request. The best burgers in town are here and at PT's Grille a few blocks east. Be warned, they give you a TON of fries. I'll be going here very frequently this summer...

by David M. Bruce (1 review)
November 06, 2009 - Likes it
This place has made me a fan of the burger again! 18 no-charge toppings make for a gourmet burger for under $5. I had the combo with fresh cut sweet potato fries and drink for $8. Service was super friendly and quick. They have a great selection of bottled drinks and beer as well. The inside is nicely designed and clean. Next time I visit I will have to try either the turkey burger, the tuna steak, or salmon sandwich. A must visit in Chapel Hill

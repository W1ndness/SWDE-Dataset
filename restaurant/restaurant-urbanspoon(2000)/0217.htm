Great food, good service and great atmosphere
by Candace (1 review)
October 21, 2007 - Likes it
I have been to Nuevo Leon twice and both times were a real treat. The food is excellent and the prices are very reasonable. We got so much good food for our money. My roommate had the mole and loved it. I tried standard enchiladas and loved them. My friends were more adventurous in their choices and were not disappointed. Every meal also comes with a free appetizer, this really delicious pork dish served with warm corn tortillas. The place was packed each time, but we never had to wait long. It is a great place for families and gatherings with friends. It isn't romantic due to the crowds. The decor is also very nice. I plan to go back often.

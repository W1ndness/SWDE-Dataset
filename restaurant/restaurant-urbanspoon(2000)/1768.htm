Lunch Specials Are A Bargain
by SouthernBelleCritic (30 reviews)
June 30, 2010 - Likes it
Galatoire's Bistro has daily lunch specials that are a bargain. Many times my husband and I split a lunch special. There is just enough for two adults to have a lunch that doesn't stuff you into lethargy. Everything is delicious and high quality just like Galatoires in N.O.
Highly, highly recommend for lunch, dinner or brunch.

Food is as good as Galatoire's in New Orleans....
by Mark A (18 reviews)
January 27, 2009 - Really likes it
Galatoire's is my all-time favorite in New Orleans, and Baton Rouge is lucky to have such a great restaurant. The aptmosphere is not the same, but food measures up to the reputation. The trout almondine and Oyster's Rockefeller are great choices.

mobile review
by Gina (14 reviews)
July 02, 2009 - Likes it
Food as good as the "mothership"! Service is excellent... Waiters remember your preferences. Favorite appetizer is Escargot. Poisson Crabmeat Yvonne is great when you're trying to eat healthfully, the lamb chops with bernaise are wonderful indulgence when you want to throw caution to the wind. I'm usually not tempted by desserts, preferring savory fare, but 50% of the time I order the apple almond pastry something... OMG!

January 06, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Good drinks, reasonably good service, ok food. The fare is mostly Tex-mex (choice mostly burritos, nachos, salads, and sandwiches) and is served in the same orange, mesh plastic baskets you might find at any fast-food joint. The Crispy Chicken Burrito was not much better than I would fix up hastily for myself. Overall, maybe not a bad spot for a snack and drinks with friends, but not a place to bring a date. The Mexican wrestling theme is a little weird, too, given the generally upscale look of the place.

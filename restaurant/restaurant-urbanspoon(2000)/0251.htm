A personal favorite
by The Grumpy Gastronome (4 reviews)
December 23, 2009 - Likes it
India Garden's buffet is one of the best deals in town. While the selection is somewhat limited, the food is good and fresh, and there is always plenty of naan and condiments to round off the feast. There are typically at least two dessert offerings as well.
The interior of the restaurant is clean, neat and always properly climate controlled, and the wait staff is very friendly and attentive. There is plenty of parking in both the restaurant's parking lot as well as on Detroit Avenue and the adjoining side streets.
The dinners are rather more expensive than the lunch buffet but, once again, the quality of the food, atmosphere, and dining experience during evening hours is good and I would have to say that the overall value of the dinner menu is better than that of Café Tandoor (which, unbelievably, charges separately for rice!).
No major negatives here. The one minor negative: they use what appear to be 6 oz. glasses for their beverages, which means that frequent refills are needed--at least by this patron. The formal stemmed goblets are in any case entirely unnecessary for an informal lunch buffet. Cheap, hard-plastic 20-ounce tumblers would do--and would make things easier on both diners and wait staff.
One of my favorite places to eat in the city. Kudos to an excellent operation!

Love Bosco's Yogurt, Great Flavors
by SouthernBelleCritic (30 reviews)
June 21, 2010 - Really likes it
I have been to Bosco's many times and love the flavors of their yogurt. Currently the Lemon Meringue Pie flavor is my favorite. The vanilla, cake batter, peach and root beer float are very good too. I like the self-serve counter where you can add an incredible selection of toppings to your yogurt. At 41 cents an ounce, it is a pretty good buy too compared to other yogurt shops. The staff and owners are very friendly and the store is really clean. Overall, a great place to cool off with your favorite flavors. (they have homemade cupcakes and cookies too but we haven't eaten any of them yet)

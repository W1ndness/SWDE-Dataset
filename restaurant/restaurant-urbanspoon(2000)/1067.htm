Yeah, But....
by Michael. C (11 reviews)
January 29, 2010 - Likes it
The best part about CAH, are the specials:
Dollar Burger - Granted only plain burgers are a dollar, but the crafts are reduced too. Best when you are a little short on cash...
Steal the Glass - Get a brew from the featured brewery, keep the glass featuring the logo. Best special ever.
American Microbrew - Discounted microbrews. Expand your horizons.
There are other special nights, but these are my favorites. The rest of the food is very good, but lacks value. So if you are out with a group, go for dollar burger night and use the money you save to buy the DD a steak for their willing self-deprivation.

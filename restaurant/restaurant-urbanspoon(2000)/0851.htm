Very Good Mexican
by CASEY (225 reviews)
November 13, 2009 - Likes it
NOT to be confused with the Moore location -
Very busy mid week, but the service was excellent. I totally enjoyed the catfish dish that they call Whale on a Platter. A good 10 inches long and served whole, this was prepared by slicing into the width of the fish then sauteeing in a garlic tomatoe sauce that rocked! (Not the typical, but also delicious method of frying.) My dining partner enjoyed every selection on his combo platter. I really like it when a place gets that lime on the rim of the chilled beer glass just right too.

Great Way to Start the Day
by Ant (222 reviews)
August 15, 2009 - Really likes it
Just a few miles away from the strip on Flamingo....Egg Works delivers way more than your hotel's breakfast buffet. They have a HUGE menu. Take a peek at what other's are eating once you walk in, everything is so appealing, whether it be your standard egg & bacon breakfast, to crepes and pancakes.
We shared a bacon, ham, and sausage skillet, with a side of fruit. The skillet included their crunchy home potatoes, perfectly seasoned, mixed in with all the breakfast meats, topped with a layer of melted cheese, and 4 eggs cooked your way. Mmmmmm. We will definitely be back.

I (heart) their breakfast!
by wahmei (15 reviews)
March 04, 2010 - Likes it
Usually breakfast joints aren't very crowded earlier in the morning, but that was not the case here! About 8:30am they were crowded - folks waiting inside & out. Wait times would've been longer for a larger party. If you don't wanna wait, there's usually instant seating at the counter.
The menu is great! All kinds of b-fast/brunch dishes, but also lunch dishes for the non-breakfast folk. The variety was pleasing to me - I'm looking forward to going back to try another dish! I'm just going to plan a day during the week rather than the weekend to (hopefully) avoid the wait... Also, did I mention that they have free Wi-Fi?
I had their waffle with bacon & eggs - and of course, coffee. I love that they served up a pot of coffee rather than waiting for a busy server to get back around to filling up my mug.
I give 2 thumbs up - for sure! The only bummer was the awkward waiting area (or lack of)... Highly recommended.

Message from Cafe Riviera

"Eat Healthy, Mediterranean specialties- Italian, French and Middle Eastern cuisine, a large variety of paninis, salads and vegetarian delicacies.

Our freshly baked bagels, croissants and pastries are the talk of the town.
Daily specials with a variety of soups and oven baked meals are served in house or ready to go.
Breakfast and lunch available from 7am to 6pm Mon-Fri and 7am to 4pm on Saturday- Closed on Sunday.
Special catering is provided for your business meetings with breakfast and lunch boxes to go.
You can order full meals, cocktail snacks and pastries for your very special occasion or party.
A variety of brewed coffees are served alongside fresh squeezed lemon and orange juices and other cold drinks."

Tasty and pretty healthy, its quickly becoming a favorite of mine!
by Adric Menning (19 reviews)
June 29, 2010 - Really likes it
Tried the Falafel Wrap, and the Tabouli salad. Both were wonderful, very tasty, the salad was a little heavy on the olive oil, but really the perfect blend of tangy and savory he must use really good olive oil. the falafel were very good too!
Even gave me a very cute little pie peach as a 4th of July/Returning customer thing.
As always been very impressed by the food!

Tried them out yesterday
by Adric Menning (19 reviews)
May 26, 2010 - Really likes it
Dropped in about 1:45pm yesterday, was the only person there, was served by the new owner.
I ordered the poboy roast beef sandwich, and while he quizzed me about what i wanted on it, i told him hes the sandwich maker make it as he thinks it should be made and i will give it a try.
the end result was fantastic! plenty big, perfectly toasted, nicely spicy and full of flavor.
He still operates the genuine water boiled bagel bakery too, and has various soups and breakfast options.
I give the place two thumbs up!

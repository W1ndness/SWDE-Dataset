Best Pizza in Fairfax
by Chris A. in Fairfax (7 reviews)
September 08, 2008 - Really likes it
As a native New Yorker, I can tell you this is hands-down the best pizza in Fairfax county. My family makes the 45 minute drive to Tony's at least once a week. That's how much we like it. (Ninety minutes round trip!) Good pizza.
A true New York pizzeria, the best test is a slice of plain. The sauce is flavorful, the crust has a slight crunch when you fold it, and the cheese is just right. Of course, all the traditional toppings are available, as well as Sicilian pizza. You can go by the slice, or order complete pies.
They also have good salads, some pasta dishes, and hero sandwiches. But come for the Pizza. You will never settle for Pizza Hut again.

mobile review
by Martin Draper (1 review)
November 22, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Very noisy and chaotic. The pizza was so-so, similar to what I have had in NY City, but not among the better efforts. The service is cafeteria-like and very cold. Everything is rushed. If you don't know the routine, no one is going to be helpful. The is a patio area out front between the parking and the storefront. It is ridiculously noisy there. Patrons feel quite free to honk their horns five feet from your table. I would have thought it was an anolmoly, but it happened three times as people locked their cars. I might go back, but only for take-out.

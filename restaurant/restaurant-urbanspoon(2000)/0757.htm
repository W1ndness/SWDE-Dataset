Best Thai Place on the Upper East Side
by Kristina (4 reviews)
April 06, 2007 - Really likes it
This is by far the "best" (as in, most gourmet) thai restaurant on the UES. The food is great, the presentation of the dishes is superb, and the staff is very friendly. The only drawback is that the space is small and tables are tight, so it gets loud. But I can highly recommend this place for food & service.

Good food and presentation
by Jay (8 reviews)
June 29, 2010 - Likes it
My wife and I went here for the first time. It's a small restaurant with a great menu. As the waitress was brining out food to the tables around us, everything was very pleasing to the eye. Very beautiful presentation. I had the pad Thai which was good but I should have ordered something else. My wife had a squid dish that was cooked in a spicy pepper sauce that was amazing. I shouldn't have cheaped out here. A couple more dollars and I could have had something amazing. We'll definitely come back here.

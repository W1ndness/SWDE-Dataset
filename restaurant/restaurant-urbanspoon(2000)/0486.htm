My Favorite Neighborhood Place
by Lori Schlabach (4 reviews)
July 23, 2009
Great pizza right down the street in my neighborhood! They have been here almost as long as we have (20 plus years). Definitely a place where everyone knows your name - at least during the week. We are partial to "the Stan" and "Greek" pizzas. Nice selection of beers - they had Bells before everyone was drinking it. The kids go for cheese and peperoni pizza with orange soda. I don't care for the salads (too much cheese and iceberg for me) but some neighbors rave about them. These days we carry out more often than not but the atmosphere is great. Thanks for believing in our neighborhood Some Guys!!!

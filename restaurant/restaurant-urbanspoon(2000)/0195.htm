Message from Coast Sushi

Romance is in the air at this sexy Bucktown Japanese joint.

  • Sushi
  • Traditional Atmosphere
  • Online Ordering

Since Coast Sushi Bar opened in March of 2003 in it's hip neighborhood location, regulars and newcomers alike have enjoyed top quality sushi in a relaxed but lively, BYOB atmosphere.

Our chefs have created a menu that will satisfy sushi connoisseurs and novices alike. Through years of experience, they have mastered the pairing of simple but complex flavors to create some of the most tasteful and innovative New-Japanese cuisine, using only the freshest ingredients and top quality fish from around the world. Coast offers something for everyone.
The unique experience of dining at Coast is enhanced by the handcrafted furniture and tableware. With minimal decor and a dimly lit dining room, Coast presents the guest with an atmosphere for romance or just dining out with friends.

I think my biggest issue with Coast is it tries too hard to straddle the line between being a traditional sushi house like Itto Sushi and a fusion restaurant like Sushi Wabi or Japonais. The sushi is ok but not fantastic and the prices are a bit high. If somebody wanted to take me there, I'd be open to going again but wouldn't choose to go there.

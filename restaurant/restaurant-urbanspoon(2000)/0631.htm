mobile review
by Gloria (2 reviews)
June 28, 2009 - Doesn't like it
We had the 'mediterranean' salad and the 'traditional' gyros. If you are offering a Mediterranean salad, then don't use cheap sliced back olives out of a can, you use kalamata olives; i have had a better Greek salad at Skyline Chili. And as far as the gyros went, the bread was excellent and the meat was good, but they skimped on the amount of meat and concealed it with the salad they plopped on top when I told them I wanted everything on it which in my decades of experience eating gyros meant sliced tomatoes, onions, tztziki sauce and at times, feta cheese. Instead i got lettuce, diced tomatoes, pizza olives, and the only thing they got right were the olives. I won't be surprised if this place ceases to exist 6 months from now. I hope they read this review and take it's contents into consideration.

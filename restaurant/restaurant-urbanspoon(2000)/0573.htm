Good Bagels
by Diane (4 reviews)
August 30, 2009 - Likes it
I go here for the bagels and the baked goods. I like the soup, but it is hit or miss. I am not a big fan of chicken skin, and while I like knowing it is homemade, a little more care in shredding the meat from the carcass would qwell the "ick" factor of me finding skin in the soup. The sandwiches are big and I love getting them on occasion. BUT the main reason I go is the chewy, crunchy blood pressure sky-rocketing salt bagels. hard to find these anywhere in town but here, and I love them! I also love the customer service, the staff really does get to know the clientele and it is nice to not be just another "sale", but a person.

Big time Buffalo BBQ
by Eating out in WNY (63 reviews)
September 28, 2009 - Really likes it
Want to take a break from Chicken wings, pizza and beef on weck? Well then I would have to suggest Kentucky Greg's Hickory Pit. Hands down the best BBQ in WNY. Wait did I say BBQ in WNY? I sure did. It really is as authentic a BBQ you can get considering were a long way from Texas. Greg 's smokes all his meat right next door to the main restaurant in an old ice cream shop. All the food is of high quality. The Chicken and Sausage Gumbo is very tasty and just a bit spicy. All the sandwiches are served on toasted Wonder Bread and served with two side of your choice. Sides like Corn Bread and Black Eyed Peas lets you know this is real deal BBQ. Platters of Ribs, Pulled Pork, Chicken, and Breaded Catfish are really good and priced right.($9-$18)The only thing I would like to see on the menu is Mac&Cheese.

Best BBQ in the area
by David Bishop (23 reviews)
May 23, 2009 - Likes it
Much better than Fat Bob's (see my review). I started with the fried banana peppers, which are just as good as buffalochow.com reported them to be. Watch out, as there is much more than one person can eat, and I've never had good luck reheating deep fried food. My main dish was a pulled pork sandwich on texas toast and fries. The fries were seasoned well but not really remarkable otherwise. The sandwich was just right, with the toast nice and thick, but not too crunchy. The pulled pork was really good; though there wasn't enough sauce on the sandwich straight from the kitchen, there are handy bottles of the stuff at each table. The waitress actually got ahead of me on drinks, with a full glass at my table before I finished the previous one each time. Overall, I had a great experience and will definitely be back.

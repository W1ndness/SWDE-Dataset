Almost had me.
by Mike (1 review)
February 15, 2010
I recently moved to Albuquerque, NM and I haven't really been out to see what this city has to offer. My wife and I decided to go out for a bite to eat. We see Nick and Jimmy's Restaraunt & Bar and thought why not.
It was a fairly busy night it seemed and it took us no time at all to get a table. The service was excellent and the food was outstanding. My wife and I are in the middle of eating our meals when we hear this loud talking, almost shouting. This old disgruntled man, who I can only assume is the owner, is berating some of his staff, some waitresses and what I believe to be a manager.
This was so unprofessional and upsetting to see someone that runs a restaraunt acting this way to his employees. If we hadn't already eaten I would have left without ordering anything. I thought, I dont even want to give this guy my money.
This restaraunt almost had me, the linens, the atmosphere, the food and service was top notch, but the old man ruined it for me. I will not give my money to an old grumpy man that can't treat people with common decency. Will I go back? No, well, not while he is there. Will I recommend this place to anyone? Absolutely not, unless they enjoy watching a decrepit old man treating his employees like trash.

Excellent Food, Great Value
by karmathecat664 (4 reviews)
March 31, 2010 - Likes it
There seems to be something about diners in Albuquerque and moderately priced upscale restaurants that mix like oil and water.
I felt that our dinners ( $25.95 for the 16 oz ribeye special and $18.95 for the lamb shank ) were more than fairly priced for the quality and taste of the food. My husband's ribeye ( this is not a cut known to be very lean ) had the standard very small strip of fat running along one side of the steak that he simply cut off and my lamb shank was of a generous size, well seasoned with a tomato based sauce. Our sides of rice pilaf were well cooked and well flavored. Even the mixed vegetables tasted fresh, were not overdone and clearly did not come out of some prepackaged bag.
The waitstaff and other employees were very attentive and professional, we did not feel rushed, nor did we wait long for our greeting, order to be taken, salad and soup to be delivered and food to be prepared.
Dessert was great...coconut pie and creme brulee were delicious.
This is going to go on our regular rotation for date night!

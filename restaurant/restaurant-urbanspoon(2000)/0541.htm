Farragut landmark
by Justin (49 reviews)
November 19, 2009 - Likes it
Aubrey's has been around for a long time in Farragut. The food is always quite good and has a variety of traditional American-style entrees. I like the selection of side dishes (i.e. squash casserole, spinach, etc.). Also, the bread is excellent.
Recommended.

Outstanding!
by DP (13 reviews)
December 04, 2009 - Likes it
Locally-owned, high-quality food, well-trained staff - it all comes together in the form of a very good restaurant that has never failed to please our appetites in the 3+ years we've been eating there.
Baked scrod is a favorite of mine, as is the Thunder Road burger.
The owner is a fantastic corporate citizen, too. Aubrey's name always showing up in fund raisers and community involvement. I like supporting this place.
A very high recommendation.

Hidden Southside Gem
by Doug (40 reviews)
August 04, 2010 - Likes it
Someone recommended this place to my wife and I for date night. We had never heard of it much less knew where it was. It was fantastic. Not sure what to call the cuisine but I would say it is Miami Spice. Loved the interior. Don't be scared off by the shoddy retail center it is housed in. Food is great, long and cheap wine list and just an interesting menu. You won't find many dishes like this elsewhere in Richmond. Short trip off Rt 288 for those of you north of the River.

Good if you like seafood, not so much if you don't
by Mandalay (19 reviews)
November 02, 2009
Pescados is Spanish for fishes, which gives you an idea of what's available. I don't really eat seafood but I do like crab so I got the crab nachos--interesting and tasty. But this place isn't cheap and on weekends you'll be shouting over the loud background music and the louder conversations. I used to live fairly close to the restaurant but for me it's not worth the trip. Seafood devotees though will love it.

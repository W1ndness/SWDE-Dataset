Message from Parisi

From the heart of Tuscany to the center of Denver

  • Fine Wine and Beer
  • Imported Gelato and Espresso
  • Trattoria Pizzeria

Parisi is a casual, Italian-dining experience with the kind of huge menu selection you would see in Italy. Offerings of dessert, espresso, fine wines and gelato will tempt your senses of smell and taste. Come shop in the market and deli for special imported goods. Join this truly authentic Italian experience.

Parisi's is the "treat" pizza for us, when we're willing to spend a little more and have a craving that only Parisi's will satisfy. The dining room, however, is uncomfortably tight and loud and NOT kid friendly and many times I just don't want to deal with the cafeteria and having to navigate through the crowd to get a napkin. Maybe that's why their dishes are so light on the sauce, so you don't need extra napkins. I'd prefer more sauce.

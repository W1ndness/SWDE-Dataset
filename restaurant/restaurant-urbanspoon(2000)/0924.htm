Not a tourist stop!
by Bruce (16 reviews)
June 22, 2010 - Likes it
Without a doubt, the best cheese-steak sandwiches in Philadelphia. If you are from out-of-town, people may try to steer you to Pat's, Geno's or Jim's, but these places have basically become tourist stops. D' Alessandro's has the real thing that Philadelphia is famous for. Worth the trip into the Roxborough neighborhood.
btw - try to eat it as soon as it comes off the grill. There are tables outside now. They are not as good if you wrap-it-up and take it home... the roll gets soggy and the sauce tends to soak into the bread too.

Great Steaks!
by Jay Ashby (10 reviews)
July 17, 2010
Dalessandro's easily has one of the best cheesesteaks in all of Philly. Everything in the steak meshes together on the roll perfectly creating a delicious sandwich. Pricing is very reasonable, especially considering the size of the sandwich your receive.
The staff at Dalessandro's sets it apart from other cheesesteak places in Philly. They are always very friendly and attentive, when I went there for the first time they treated me like I had been coming there for a couple years, which makes the experience all the better.

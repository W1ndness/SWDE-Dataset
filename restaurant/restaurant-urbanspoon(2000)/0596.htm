Excellent Toppings and Locally Owned
by Mary Margaret (83 reviews)
May 24, 2010 - Likes it
This is not your average pizza joint. There are over 50 toppings: artichokes, broccoli, chorizo sausage, clams, onion marmalade! Lots of vegan options. Locally-owned "chain". Friendly service and Kentucky Ale by the pitcher. Lots of intriguing combinations, calzones, and salads. OK, I'm getting ready to order a "Shrimp Santori" right now!

Recommend | Not helpful

Good food! You need to go!
by Angela (11 reviews)
August 05, 2010 - Likes it
Had lunch there today with a friend, I ordered Scamorza $4.95, which is an appitizer, and a house salad $2.85. She ordered the Slice Salad & Drink $6.65, which is a slice of pizza a house salad. Food was great, salads were plenty and not too large. Scamorza was delicious...I had it as my meal but it is great for sharing...very delicious. Waiter was friendly and accomidating, however, he must have taken a break during our stay because he was MIA for a little while when we needed refills but all in all I recommend this as a great place to eat! PS..you know I got dessert...I got Choc cake and she got cheesecake...very good but HUGE so share a dessert!

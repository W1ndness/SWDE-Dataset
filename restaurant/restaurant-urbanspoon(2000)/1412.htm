Not the "Best"
by Terry (1 review)
April 22, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I went there sunday and was not impressed. I ordered Hamachi, Maguro, and Scallops. The Scallops were good but small. The Maguro was soft, tastless, and mushy (old). And the Hamachi (Inada) was small and had a little tangy bite to it. In addition to being from a very small YellowTail (Inada) the Hamachi was sliced really thin. I could see through the fish when I held it up. I asked if he had any White Tuna. He cut a couple thin slices from some white fish. It didn't taste to bad, When I asked him what kind of fish it was he said, Wahoo. As I said I wasn't to impressed with this visit. I think the fish could be fresher. Maybe they are not busy enough yet and don't get fresh fish more than once a week or every two weeks. But why should I make excuses for them.
If you get the chance (but not at Ichiban) try Buri or Buri Toro. These are from a little bigger YellowTail but is much richer. The toro is so rich it will almost melt in your mouth.

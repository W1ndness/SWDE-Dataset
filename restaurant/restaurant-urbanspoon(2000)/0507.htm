Great food, prices, service, atmosphere
by JF JaxBch (2 reviews)
April 15, 2010
My wife and I frequent the Mellow Mushroom at the beach at least once per week...usually more. Everything on the menu is great...and we have tried nearly everything. The prices are very affordable. 2 people can share a 10" pizza and be reasonably satisfied. We particulalrly like the Build Your Own Salad, though. With drinks, this is only about a $15 bill. The atmosphere is unique and colorful. My 5 month old barely blinks, when she is there. It is also a great place to watch a game.
The service is excellent...we often give 30-40% tips. We have never had a bad experience. Whoever does the hiring and managing of the operation could teach his local competitors a lot.

sadly disappointing.
by Ncloverbrown (15 reviews)
January 13, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I so wanted to enjoy this place. Their food and drink menus looked amazing from their website, and for on average $30 I was expecting something pretty great. I was looking forward to either the lamb chops, the bacon wrapped jerk pork or the rum painted salmon. all sounded fantastic.
Sadly the whole experience was disappointing - We were seated at about 7:00pm, and didn't get our order taken until about a 1/4 hour later. I appreciate that the general theme of the food and experience is slow and relaxed, but there is a limit. We ordered our food and drinks: 2 mojitos, the lamb chops and the herb coconut salmon. We then waited another 1/4 of an hour just to get our drinks (it can't really take 15 minutes to make a couple of smallish mojitos). By this time, another couple that were seated a few moments after us were halfway through their meal.
There is nothing more infuriating in a dining experience, than seeing people who arrived after you getting their food before you.
The food finally arrived.
here's the description of the "Mojito lamb chops" on the menu:
"Grilled, cilantro marinated, lime infused sauce,
served with roasted tomatoes and West Indian polenta"
here's what it was:
4 very small lamb chops, chewy, lukewarm. average tasting sauce that definitely wasn't "lime infused". No hint of cilantro anywhere. I completely forgot about the "roasted tomatoes" and if I had remembered I would have definitely called them on the 2 carrots and pieces of broccoli that were undercooked.
For $28 + $10 for the mojito, and then TIP INCLUDED (which for a party of two is completely ridiculous) the grand total per person was about $45.
This place gives you horrible service, horrible food and then makes you pay way too much for it.
I couldn't recommend this to anyone.

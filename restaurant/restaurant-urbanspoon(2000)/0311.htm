Mmm... beignets...
by Pegleg (10 reviews)
November 11, 2008 - Likes it
Best place for brunch in Boulder if you're not concerned about calories or carbs, have time for a wait, and it's not too hot out (they don't have AC).  Everything is rich and delicious, and the portions are ample - even a half-order will fill you up.  Eggs sardou (gulf shrimp and creamed spinach with hollandaise over poached eggs, plus grits or potatoes) is probably my favorite.  Best biscuits in town, to boot.

Recommend | Not helpful

Best Beignets outside of New Orleans
by jkdurden (3 reviews)
November 23, 2009 - Likes it
Lucile's is a wonderful place with the best beignets outside of New Orleans. If you've been to New Orleans, I can tell you these beignets are closer to "Morning Call" than to "Cafe Du Monde'" but either way, you're going to love them!
The staff are attentive and really work hard to provide you with a level of service I haven't had since I left New Orleans. The table size is small and I understand this place is pretty warm in the summer due to a lack of air conditioning. Those things aside, I can highly recommend this restaurant to you.

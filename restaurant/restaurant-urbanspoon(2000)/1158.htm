A - List. Highly Recommended.
by David J (49 reviews)
January 31, 2010 - Likes it
A - List. Recommended. I was walking through Old Town one night and spied a shopkeeper who I thought would give me a genuine opinion of the food in and around Old Town. Immediately, she said by-pass Old Town and head to this restaurant one block off the Old Town strip - Casa Guadalajara. She was right. The food is excellent (never had a bad meal), the quality of the ingredients are better as compared to many of the Old Town strip restuarants, many of the portions are larger (you will likely take half of it home) than that found in the Old Town tourist area, and the prices are, in some cases, less than Old Town strip. I guess they are doing something right. I went back for 4 yummy visits on my 2 week holiday to beautiful San Diego. The locals come here. And the Mariachi band is an excellent addition to the evening. I think they do the traditional mexican food best. I enjoyed the vegetarian fajitas the most with an extra side dish of guacamole. The corn tortillas are fresh and delicious. I'd stay away from the fish/seafood - they do it well but they really do the Mexican side better than most. Try sitting outside near the fountain and a heat lamp. I agree with Emily J. - Casa G is fun, festive and delicious!!! This one is a winner.

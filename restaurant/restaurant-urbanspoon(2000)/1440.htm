User reviews of McGinley's Pub

Good place for lunch
by Jim C. (34 reviews)
August 28, 2009 - Likes it
I've had lunch here a few times. A group of seven stopped for lunch recently. Most had salads of some sort, but a few ventured to other offerings. I had the steak salad and wasn't disappointed.
The food arrived timely, especially since we only had an hour for lunch. We arrived a bit after noon, and expected a big delay, but it wasn't any problem.
I don't think anyone around the table was disappointed. My salad was fresh and tasty. I swear the tomatos must have been local; they actually had flavor. The steak was done 'just right', with a hint of seasoning I couldn't identify; but it was good.
I've had their shepard pie, fish and chips, and other traditional dishes on other occasions. Have yet to not be satisfied. Prices are not out of line and service was good.

See what the Irish know about eating & drinking.
by Ruth Quinlan (5 reviews)
July 06, 2010
Good food, good service, a filling sandwich. All my needs were met at McGinley's Irish Pub.
I haven't been to McGinley's in a while. I ended up there for a late lunch last week.
The menu offers a variety of hearty foods and tasty salads. I was in the mood for hearty and I was not disappointed.
One friend got the corned beef and cabbage and split it with a friend and still had plenty to eat. Another friend got the Shepherds pie and we were impressed with the taste and the portion.
I got a carved turkey sandwich with bacon on toast. No flimsy sandwich meat here. This was real turkey and real bacon and lots of it. I ate half the sandwich for lunch and the other half for dinner and was stuffed both times.
Visit McGinley's Irish Pub at 7th & G and see what the Irish know about eating and drinking!

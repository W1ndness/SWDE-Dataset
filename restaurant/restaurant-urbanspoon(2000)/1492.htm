Great food, great value
by surmaylo (9 reviews)
July 25, 2010 - Really likes it
An overlooked gem. This quiet place brings a bit of Asian elegance to Decatur. Every person there catered to our needs with a smile & made good recommendations. You may want to make the ravioli appetizer your entree- it deliciously melts in your mouth. I also especially liked the eggplant side with sticky rice. another person in our party ordered the prix fixe dinner & loved getting to try 3 different appetizers- so good she didn't share. Others in our party raved about the lamb & the duck confit. Add all this to a very nice wine list & great coconut ice cream & you might easily pay double in Buckhead. The only downside is a lack of offerings for non meat/fish eaters, but like another reviewer, our group decided to dine here once a month! I look forward to it.

My default restaurant
by food-dood (5 reviews)
April 20, 2009 - Really likes it
This place is within walking distance from my house, and it's got a special place in my heart. I love that the menu runs from expensive specialty entrees to cheaper (but well-made) comfort food. Also the beer and wine selection is outstanding. Don't miss the hand-made steak-cut french fries or the homemade salad dressings. (Ask for the dressing on the side, as the salads are frequently kind of watery.) I have been eating here for so long that I can attest to a couple sub-par food experiences (a wilted salad comes to mind), but these were always addressed when I brought up the issue. I have never had a rude waiter or waitress.

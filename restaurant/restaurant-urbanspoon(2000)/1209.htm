Changed my mind
by FoodPirate (51 reviews)
July 01, 2009 - Likes it
The first time I visited, the staff was great and we had a great time, sat outside and had a few drinks. We went this week and I was quite dissapointed this time around. We wanted to sit outside in the beer garden again but beware....bring your appetite otherwise you can't go out there until 10. So, since we weren't eating, we sat inside at a table, but NO ONE would wait on us. So, we moved up to the bar and FINALLY got a drink. Once it was 10, we went out into the beer garden and if you have a large group, MAKE SURE you don't move the tables, it almost caused the manager to have a heart attack! So basically if you want to go there make sure you bring your appetite otherwise you'll receive some crappy service. Not going back.

by Tod Kimbro (4 reviews)
July 11, 2009 - Likes it
What a great place! Tasty food, laid back atmosphere, and terrific microbrews. I had a fantastic spinach salad with pumpkin vinaigrette, a "grown-up grilled cheese" with Gouda, pecans, roasted apples, and greens on country bread (awesome), and a beer sampler. Didn't care for the Dubbel, but the Double Red, Agave Wheat, and Scotch Ale were all wonderful. Nice ambiance, great service, and a menu that made me want to try everything. Comfort food with a nice, sophisticated twist.

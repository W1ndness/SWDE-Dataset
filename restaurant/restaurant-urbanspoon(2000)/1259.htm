Apple Pie (the drink, not the food)
by erin (4 reviews)
December 31, 2008 - Really likes it
This place has a great patio for sipping drinks and people-watching around U of A.  The best drink on a hot summer day (which there are many in Arizona) is the Applie Pie, a drink comprised of hard cider and gin.  
Their pizza is great after a long day of drinking on their patio because it's thick, cheesey and greasy.  But, it's not for those seeking a light snack!  The rest of their menu is similar - good food but very filling.

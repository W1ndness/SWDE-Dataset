P.F. Chang's Jr.
by c (46 reviews)
November 07, 2009 - Likes it
Pei Wei is a fun place to go when you don't have the desire to go to Chang's but still want some of their super food. Their food is really close to Chang's, though portion sizes are smaller and you don't have the family style option...at least not as easily. There are no servers but the restaurant still retains an atmosphere of a decent "sit-down" restaurant.
On the negative side, prices are a little higher than I think they should be...granted it is good food but I feel it's just a little pricy; what you save in tip you make up in up-front cost. Also, if you go here during a prime meal time (weekend dinners in particular), this particular Pei Wei seems to always be crammed full with people, the line is almost out the door and almost all the tables are full. The front of house manager appears to do his best to clear things out quickly but it's just way too small, especially when the winter limits the outdoor dining.
Overall, Pei Wei is a great place to eat a quick version of Chang's, and it seems that we all would enjoy the possibility of having Chang's more often.

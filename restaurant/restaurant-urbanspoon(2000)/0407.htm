Horrible brunch.
by mr m (1 review)
February 15, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Took the family here for a Sunday brunch on valentines day. Reservations for 12:30. They got around to seating us at about 1:15-1:30. Hostess seated us, and we waited for the waiter. After 10 mins, we said screw it, and went up to the buffet. Food was not up to par from what others have said. The "eggs" looked like yellow cottage cheese. Stuffing had the consistency and taste of paste. Biscuits were good. Chicken was good. Desert table, well, depends on when you went. We saw some people with good looking cheese cake, but when we all went up, nothing but cream puffs (were hard as a rock) and lemon cake. Waiter finally shows up, says "would you like anything else besides water?". 1/2 way through, we see another table, and their waitress asks which type of juice they would like, because it was included. Our waiter never offered that. We never got a chance to ask about it, because the next time we saw him, he brought the bill. He brings the bill and says "I'll take that when ever you are ready" No "how was your meal?" "Can I get you anything else?" God forbid we wanted coffee. To top it off, when we were walking out the door, overheard a manager type telling a waiter "give it another 5 mins before we start asking people to leave. Understandable you quit serving at 2pm, but asking people to leave at the stroke of 2? Give me a break. If you are overbooked, get more staff, or decline reservations. We will never return.

hmmmmmm....
by Mary Anne (1 review)
September 26, 2009
I just returned from 1951 West, after celebrating my husband's 50th birthday. It was the first time we had eaten here, feeling confident that we would have a great fine dining experience......1951 came very highly recommended.
I was very disappointed. Although we had a reservation and were seated immediately, we waited a full hour before our entrees arrived. When they did arrive, my salmon was a little dry (disappointing!), my baby red potato was undercooked, and my coleslaw was laying under the plank that held my salmon - not very pleasing presentation.
I will not be returning, and would be hard pressed to recommend 1951 West.

Great!
by MGosslin (1 review)
September 30, 2009 - Likes it
Went to dinner at 1951 west and had a wonderful time. The service was phenomenal! Our waitress was at our table within a minute of us being sat and immediately took our drink order. She brought our drinks and helped us decide what we should get for dinner. She suggested there prime rib explaining that it's the best in Green Bay (bias?) but she was right! The prime rib was sooo tender and juicy! I recommend everyone go here to have prime rib. The food was consistently good throughout our entire dinner. Bread, Salad, Entree. A sweet young manager stopped by to ask us how every thing was, which was nice and appreciated. Our service was wonderful and the knowledge of our adorable big dimpled waitress was great! Food and drinks couldn't have been better. We will be returning there on Wednesday or Saturday for there prime rib special!! Clean, friendly, relaxing atmosphere!

GREAT!!!
by venture (1 review)
June 21, 2009 - Likes it
Everything that I had at 1951 West was fantastic. We stayed at the Comfort Suites Hotel adjoining and did not know what to expect with a restaurant connected. This was NOT your typical hotel restaurant. The decor was downtown Chicago with an atmosphere of Green Bay casual. The food was great and service superb. There is clearly some pride taken in this restaurant by everyone we ran into. I give 1951 West an A+. A great place to go to get away from the stuffy supper club atmosphere we found in other Green Bay restaurants.

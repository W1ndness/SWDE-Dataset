After-Airplane Food
by Stinky (196 reviews)
October 08, 2009 - Really likes it
When I go to New Orleans, I typically stay in the Metairie area, and this is usually the 1st place I go when I get off the plane if I fly in at night (about 1/2 the time) It is almost identical in layout and decor to the one in Dallas, and is one of my favorites places to relax, eat a good meal, and enjoy a glass of wine while I talk to some of the friendliest locals ever and the bartenders (I always eat at the bar - even if I'm with someone) I believe the bartenders at this Houston's are some of the best in the country, definitely 5 Star. The menu is the same everywhere (I believe - only been to 5 different ones) so I just eat whatever I'm in the mood for, and its ALWAYS good. This location has the Grilled Artichokes more than most, and if they have them, I get them. They have a wine called Smith & Hook (Cab Sauv) that i just LOVE! Goes with almost anything. I realize you shouldn't eat at a chain in New Orleans and I try to stick to that rule, but I make this exception because it is such a pleasant restaurant, especially after a grueling day of traveling.

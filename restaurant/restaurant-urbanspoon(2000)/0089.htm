Bad dishes or par for this place??
by Stephen (1 review)
January 02, 2009 - Doesn't like it
My $0.02...
Me & wifey are SoTex natives and have experienced TexMex, CalMex, and many other true Mex and SoAmerica styles.  We were hoping to find a decent local Mex lunch regardless of regionality.
Regular chicken enchiladas (served 3!) had sooo much cheese (monterrey jack?) and rice with no beans on plate.  Mole enchiladas (2 and much less cheese) had beans and rice.  There was enough spicy kick to the mole, but a little off on the sweetness.
Good service, portions fair, but afraid of revisiting for fear of being disappointed.

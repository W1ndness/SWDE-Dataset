I liked it
by darren (14 reviews)
July 10, 2010 - Likes it
I went here once for dinner and beers. Actually I tried to go to the nearby Cajun place because it's so popular, but the wait was an hour, so my group decided to try this place instead. And after having tried the Cajun place at a later date, and being able to compare the two, I believe the Irish Bred Pub is a superior restaurant.
Fish and chips were great. Bangers and mash were great. beer selection was pretty good, though could be better. (I don't think there are many if any places in Douglasville with a really killer beer selection). If I remember correctly I believe I had a pretzel there that was pretty good, too. And I believe some homemade potato chips, unless I'm imagining it.

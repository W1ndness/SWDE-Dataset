Good food, nice people, but limited menu.
by Anthony (18 reviews)
November 17, 2009
We tried this place because we were looking for something different and there aren't many "German" options in the area. Upon entering one finds it's a humble but homey place. Its best described as very casual, clean and neat. The people who run the place instantly greet you and are very friendly as well.
The menu doesn't offer much diversity; it focuses mainly on Wiener Schnitzel, the exceptions being a few chicken and brat dishes. I ordered the Wiener Schnitzel and my wife had the brat with kraut. Overall it was very good. A particular standout was the superb potato side dish they have. Like all food of this type it was very heavy.
It's a nice place. The only reason I don't rate it higher is the limited menu and my personal limited tolerance for fried heavy foods. As far as Wiener Schnitzel goes it was very good. If that is one of your favorites this place is a must try.

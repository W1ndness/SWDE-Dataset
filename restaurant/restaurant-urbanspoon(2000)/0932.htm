Popular, Touristy, But I Like it...
by hinser (17 reviews)
June 18, 2008 - Likes it
OK, ok...this place is a Steven Starr restaurant.  So it isn't popular with the "foodies".  But - you can and will have a good time here!!  The menu is eclectic.  The bar is VERY good - they are first and foremost a Martini Bar.  It can be crowded but they also have outdoor seating.  The house music sets a good back drop for food that is very tasty!  I love the Wasabi Mashed Potatos!  So lose the snobby vibe and try a popular, yet tasty spot in Old City OR Center City!

Message from Nuestra Cocina

  • Outdoor Dining
  • Authentic Regional food
  • Tapas

Nuestra Cocina, translated simply as 'Our Kitchen,' invites patrons to experience fresh, authentic flavors of Mexico with recipes that have been passed down through generations.

Featuring exquisitely simple dishes from Central Mexico, with flavors from Oaxaca, Yucatan and Vera Cruz, this delightful and unique cuisine can be enjoyed in the restaurant, which features beautiful mosaic tile, or outdoors on the patio.

Best food and service in Portland!
by Lane Musgrave (2 reviews)
January 06, 2009 - Really likes it
I could go on and on about this gem on the eastside on Division but I'll keep it short. The feel of the place is great with an open kitchen and bar where you can eat and watch your food being made by the chef and owner. Their margaritas and chile infused tequila are to die for. The daily ceviches and other entrees never fail. Great all around restaurant! I forgot to mention the wonderful staff. The host is great and always seats as soon as possible. The waiter(tress) have all been fantastic as well as the benevolent chefs who are always fun to talk to.

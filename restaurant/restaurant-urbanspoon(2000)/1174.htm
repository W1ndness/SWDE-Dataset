Blueberry Cornbread Muffins
by rishi-k-patel (1 review)
April 01, 2010 - Likes it
We discovered this place just driving by and have made it a near-weekly destination. The cornbread muffins are worth writing home about as are the huevos rancheros. The white bean nachos are amazing - just ask for sour cream on the side (it makes them soggy). The pancakes are light, fluffy, and great. The iced teas have always hit the spot (green tea-pineapple was great - sounds weird, tastes great). We haven't made it there for dinner yet, but the breakfasts and lunches are wonderful.

mobile review
by Gregg curtis (5 reviews)
December 28, 2009 - Doesn't like it
We really wanted to like this spot, however the food was mediocre, service was not great and they only had white sugar on the table. No soymilk. I will say the pancakes were light and fluffy and the vanilla caramel sauce was nice. My burritto verde had dried out chicken chunks and my wife got a pathetic excuse for a fruit bowl (mushy). The french cafe ambience was interesting, although confusing with the staff in vibrant tye-dye t's. Time for the cafe nouveau to make their way into the 21st century and focus on quality food.

Doesn't live up to the hype
by B (3 reviews)
January 02, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Key's Cafe gets a lot of hype in the Twin Cities, but from my experience, they don't live up to it. I've been to about half of their locations many times over the years. Food is OK but really overpriced. Their radio ads brag about the brands of food that they use, but it's nothing better than what you can get at the grocery store. Also according to their commercials, they use soup base (not uncommon of course), but it really annoys me that they still call their soups "home made" (also not uncommon, but it's a pet peeve of mine). They usually have good service though and it's probably a good place for families. There are many other (and better) places to get breakfast in the Twin Cities. Go to Key's a few times, but don't make it the only place you go.

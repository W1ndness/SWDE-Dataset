Wow, I really want to lie..
by tadreed (5 reviews)
June 15, 2010 - Really likes it
...and say that nobody should go here. I want to keep this one all to myself. Since Stagecoach BBQ in Ashville burned down a few years ago, I have been resigned to the fact that the only good BBQ I can get in central ohio (or anywhere near it) would have to come from my own smoker.
Thankfully, a co-worker revealed the secret of Route 62 BBQ to me. I was skeptical having been burned by many bad BBQ recommendations. For example, "You should try XXXXXXX BBQ, they are awesome, almost as good as Hoggy's." That sound you heard, was my taste buds committing suicide. Quick, someone get them some Route 62!
There is literally nothing on their menu that is not incredible. I hate cooked greens with a violent wretch-inducing passion...except for here. I was convinced chicken wings should never be smoked, they end up with dried out meat and rubbery skin, but no smoke flavor...except for here. How good can $10 ribs possibly be...incredible here. Anyone can make a decent pecan pie...until I had it here, now everybody else sucks.
I could go on and on, but instead I'll just go get some Route 62 BBQ before my taste buds stage a coup. You should stay away.

Best BBQ around!!
by Richard (1 review)
February 21, 2009 - Likes it
I live in Dublin and travel about 45 minutes round trip to get RT 62 BBQ at least twice a month. In doing so I pass at least 20 other so called BBQ's on the way. I am a huge BBQ fan as I am originally from the South and after having been exposed to RT 62 no other BBQ in Columbus cuts it. The brisket is always tender and juicy and the sides from the cheesy potatoes to the brocolli salad are always amazing. The staff is always friendly and accomodating and the amount of food you get for your dollar is unreal, I feel like I should always pay them about $3 more than I do because after a meal there I'm full for the next 2 days! Amazing stuff, hopefully they'll open up a location in Dublin or if not maybe I'll just have to move to Johnstown!!!! Can't beat it, if you are a fan of BBQ you owe it to yourself to make the trip out to Johnstown for some of the best grub you'll ever have!

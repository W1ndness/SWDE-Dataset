Sandwichy Deliciousness
by Arwen (2 reviews)
July 25, 2009 - Likes it
There are so many reasons to return to this place that it's difficult to know where to start. Their regular menu is outstanding. The Beef on Weck is a fancified "Roast Beef Au Jus" and is incredibly addictive, with its tender, melt-in-your-mouth thin sliced roast beef and crusty roll with coarse salt and rye seeds sprinkled on top. The Cuban sandwich is to die for. The Fries from Hell are a treat for the spicy food lover. And then there's the rotating menu of daily specials, which always includes a cheese sandwich, a veggie selection, a turkey sandwich, a "funky", an "extra funky", and several choices of burgers and sides. As a sandwich lover, I can't say enough good things about this place. It just hits the spot.

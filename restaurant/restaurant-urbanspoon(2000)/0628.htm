Great Family Pizza Place
by Lauren E (2 reviews)
June 25, 2010 - Likes it
Bonnie & Clyde's is a wonderful family restaurant. The food is great and it's one of the only pizza places where you get a hot, fresh, brick-oven baked pizza. The owner is very nice and is always there. Pizza prices are a little higher than Pizza Hut or Papa John's but the quality is well worth the extra few dollars. Definitely a place to go and recommend to others!

Great Pizza!
by Becky Dorton Byers (1 review)
March 24, 2010
This is our very favorite pizza place (other than Piece O Pizza in southern California, but we don't get there often). We have been going there since it opened many years ago. The ingredients are fresh and good. Then they cook the pizza in the old brick ovens that have been there for 40 years. Even the salads taste better at Bonnie & Clydes! The decor is unique; not fancy and I don't know the day when it was updated, but all this together makes for the most delicious pizza I know about in this area!

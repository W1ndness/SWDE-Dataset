We've been going to Amici's for 10 years. Love it! The family special is great: awesome pizza, garlic rolls and soda. I also love the chicken parmesan sandwich - delicious, the gnocchi, the eggplant mozzarella. Their servings are generous, food is fresh, simple and well made... and prices are fair. Love it!

Not your ordinary burger joint
by Candy (4 reviews)
March 14, 2009
Intriguing from the outside. Classy diner on the inside. We had the wine country burgers. Very yummy and different -- must have been that sauce.
Those sweet potato straws are addictive!. The lychee smoothie was disappointing for my husband and he is a fan of the fruit. I thought it was alright. Great consistency but the mix of lychee and ice cream was a little lacking in flavor. Although the burgers were only 1/3 lb, we were full. The desserts looked scrumptious so next we'll save room!!
Lisa E.

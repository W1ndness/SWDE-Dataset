Never Again
by awesomedude (1 review)
September 22, 2009 - Doesn't like it
*WARNING* , this place is very small and dirty. DO NOT USE THE BATHROOM! You will not want to stay for your cracker-like burnt pizza. I have no idea why people seem to like this place, the pizza was OK at best, and I would never go back again! The way they pack people in to such a small area has got to be a fire code violation. Also, this restaurant is very close to the ghetto. If you miss the 72C exit, do not take the 73A exit, just keep going to green bay and turn around! It's not worth it, and neither is Zaffiro's! Maybe I'm spoiled with Chicago pizza, but this was lame as F.

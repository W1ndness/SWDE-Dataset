East Side Version of Cool Basil
by Tara (26 reviews)
March 14, 2009 - Likes it
Strip the nice seating, draperies, paintings and other pieces that give Cool Basil it's ambiance and you have the East side's Thai Flavors. I belive I have heard that they are owned by the same people - which I totally believe after trying Thai Flavors this weekend.
But ... I must say, the food was actually better. Cool Basil can be hit or miss, Thai Flavors really delivered on the taste. And, we had no wait getting in. I believe we have found our new favorite Thai resturant.

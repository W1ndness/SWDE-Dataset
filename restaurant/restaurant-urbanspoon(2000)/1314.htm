+ 1 more photos from Jake Zigler Awesome
by Jake Zigler (155 reviews)
May 18, 2010 - Likes it
This place was suggested to me girlfriend and I while on a trip to Annapolis, MD. We wasted no time eating here; helping ourselves the first night of our trip.
For an appetizer we ordered the fried green beans that had some kind of sauce on them. They were absolutely addictive.
I got the "Waterfall" which is a beef entree on top of salad. It was SUPER spicy but delicious. It was seriously one of the spiciest things I've ever eaten. The presentation and the flavor were superb.
My girlfriend thoroughly enjoyed her "Ka Pow" fried rice. The dessert was outstanding and was a great way to cool my mouth off.
The service, presentation and atmosphere were all top notch at Lemon Grass. Next time my girlfriend goes to Annapolis on business I will be sure to come along and will be stopping at Lemon Grass!

Make sure you try the pizza
by undorgre (34 reviews)
October 19, 2009 - Likes it
I visited Joey B's over the weekend as they were celebrating their 1st anniversary. The decor at Joey B's is very plain, but is fine for a solid neighborhood corner bar. Beers are reasonably priced at $3/beer (at least for domestic) and the one item I ordered off the menu, fried pickles with spicy ranch, was tasty. The bartenders were very friendly and very engaging. I'd recommend Joey B's as a place to start your evening before you head out for a late night.
I recently had a chance to try their pizza and I give it a high rating. If you tell yourself to only eat 4 slices, you'll end up eating 6... very addicting.

Joey B's will make your tastebuds dance!
by LJ (7 reviews)
July 18, 2010 - Likes it
In my book Joey B's can't do no wrong. The portions here are HUGE and the food taste great! Usually when I go to restaurants that serve huge portions the quality and flavor of their food isn't huge and bold compared to the portions. However, at Joey B's their huge flavor is equal to their huge portions. The onion rings are massive, perfectly seasoned and fried to perfection. The Joey's deluxe pizza was very tasty and so was their house salad. Since I was with a lot of my family members I was able to taste a number of their dishes such as the Chicken Modiga, spaghetti, hot wings and their house made potato chips. Throughout the meal my tastebuds were having a party and I guarantee your's will have the same experience when you go to Joey B's!!

Good Food We Will Keep Returning
by Jana McG (1 review)
November 26, 2008 - Likes it
My coworker and I had Joey B.'s "to go" and overall I we were very impressed. I was told that the salad was huge,and it was. I got the Blue Cheese Buffalo Chicken Salad and it was delicious! They served it "upside down" in its container which was good thinking, as I wa able to scoop out  small amts at a time. I filled two plates and still have over half left over. It was also served with yummy bread from a Hill vendor I am sure. I will definatley get this salad again! My coworker had the Thousand Island Burger with fries which he said was very good also. He did feel it  was just slightly over priced. He was given a large amt of fries and they were cooked perfectly. Also, a nice "to go" touch was putting his garnishes under the paper so the bun did not get soggy.
Also, last week when I was "under the weather" my boss brought me back a cup of their clam chowder and it was really delicous. I can't wait to try their other soups.

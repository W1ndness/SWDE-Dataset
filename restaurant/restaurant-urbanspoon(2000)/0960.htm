Good food despite false advertising
by voluptuous vegetarian (39 reviews)
December 13, 2009 - Likes it
I saw an ad in the Saturday paper for this restaurant and decided to check it out. The ad said it had happy hour and reverse happy hour everyday, so we went for dinner during reverse happy hour to score some cheap drinks. However when we got there and asked the hostess about happy hour she said it was only Monday through Friday. I asked her if she is sure because the ad said everyday and she rolled her eyes at us and said it was only Monday through Friday. After the charismatic hostess sat us we were greeted by a much nicer waiter, thank God. I ordered a premium house margarita which tasted average and was weak on the tequila. Luckily the food was pretty good. I had a green corn tamale, cheese enchilada, and tostada combo which was very tasty. My boyfriend had a chicken dish which he also enjoyed. At the end of the meal we pointed out the false advertising to our waiter who acted much more apologetic than the hostess. Overall a good experience and we will definitely be back.

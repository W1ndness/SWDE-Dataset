Chain atmosphere, chain food.
by Alicia (49 reviews)
July 03, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Jimmy Buffett's Margaritaville restaurant on the Strip looks super inviting and fun, especially in the evenings when karaoke from inside the restaurant can be heard out on the sidewalk, for better or worse. However, we were not impressed with the food or the prices. Eleven bucks for a hamburger and fries? It was comparable to standard fare at Chili's and the like. And the margarita I had was so weak I wondered if there was even alcohol in it! For eight bucks I expected a lot more from the signature drink of his restaurant and song lyrics. You're better off hitting up the casinos down the street like Gamblin' Bill's for multiple 99 cent margaritas.

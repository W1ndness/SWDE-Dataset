Ab-fab NY pizza... In Vegas!
by Andrew D (249 reviews)
November 08, 2009 - Really likes it
Again, we're SO blessed to have so many great pizza houses in the Henderson area! This is one of them.
The pizza packs plenty of New York attitude and flavor into something Las Vegans can really love. The coal oven gives the crust some char, but inside it's still soft enough to chew and love. Add some quality cheese and toppings like mushrooms, pesto (!!!!), and/or sun-dried tomatoes, and you have quite the amazing pizza!
Oh, and don't forget dessert. The cannoli was rich, creamy, sweet (but not too sweet), and FRESH! I'd go back just for that!
Again, I just feel blessed to live so close to Patsy Grimaldi's. I can't wait to go back and try more of my new favorite New York style pizza.

Claims to be NY Style Pizza but isn't
by Adam (1 review)
July 27, 2010 - Doesn't like it
We dined at Grimaldi's last night, wooed there by very positive reviews on this site. Once there, the hostess and the waitress explained that their pizza is NY style and excellent. We started with the house salad which was a very generous portion and pretty tasty. Then came the pizza. We chose the plain cheese. Well, my dinner companion and I grew up in Westchester County NY and we KNOW our pizza. All we can say is that the pizza at Grimaldi's can't hold a candle to authentic NY pizza. First, REAL NY pizza has a crust that's thin, but not wafer-thin as at Grimaldi's. Second, REAL NY pizza actually has flavor. We found the tomato sauce extremely bland and the portions of cheese on top stingy at best. Grimaldi's pizza, to us, tasted like Passover pizza, which for those of you who aren't Jewish is NOT a good thing! We did end up eating our pizza as we were hungry. We expressed our disappointment to the waitress who relayed it to the manager. While the manager him/her self never came to our table to solicit our opinion (BAD MOVE GRIMALDI'S...makes it look like you don't care about what you serve your customers!), (s)he did have our server bring us a piece of "NY Style" cheesecake on the house. We both enjoyed the cheesecake, which was a LOT more authentic than the pizza.
In all, we were VERY disappointed with our dining experience here last night. We did appreciate the attentiveness of our waitress, though. REAL New Yorkers visiting or transplanted to Vegas...Don't bother dining here if you want REAL NY style pizza!

Fresh vegetables but not much else great here
by HelloSmile (1 review)
July 13, 2010 - Doesn't like it
A few years ago we visited Aladdin's because "everybody loves Aladdin's". I remember thinking it was just okay and we didn't need to go back- and we hadn't- until a week or so ago and it was so hot that we didn't feel like cooking and decided to stop in. First, it is not air conditioned- so why we stayed (and what other people were doing there) I have no idea. As far as the food- I don't get why (supposedly) people love this place. I hope the other location is better because the Pittsford location is not craveable in the least.
The vegetables in the pitas were fresh, so that is a plus, but the sauces were disgusting (even their "greek" dressing just tastes like peppered oil... ew) and the falafel was blech. I could hardly eat it and I normally love falafel in pretty much any way shape or form. My husband said his gyro was "okay". Nothing special. We were miffed when the "grilled chicken tenders" we ordered for our 17 month old son came out looking like a pink pile of innards - so we asked for them to be cooked more and the waitress informed us that because it was dark meat they would look pink. Dark meat?! She said they could remake them with white meat and did but we were miffed that they default to using dark meat for children's menu items and fail to mention this (and also fail to mention that white meat is an option unless you complain about the dark meat). It looked horrible. I didn't even know it was possible to mold dark meat into chicken tenders but somehow they did it. I just can't believe they would serve dark meat as the default on the children's menu without at least giving a heads up in the description. Anyhoo. Fresh vegetables are a big plus for me. "Meh" food is a big minus. Service was not great... hard to get the attention of our waitress- we asked for several sauces (one never came with the food and one to replace a sauce we did not like) and they took incredibly long to come out... for being just spooned into cups... other small annoyances but overall... "meh." Sadly we will not be back.

by Michelle (4 reviews)
April 11, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I have been to this place three separate times over the span of five years and each time is more awful than the last.

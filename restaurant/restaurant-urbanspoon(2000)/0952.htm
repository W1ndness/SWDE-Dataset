Top 10 Hot Dogs in the World
by Pam Jackson (1 review)
October 11, 2008 - Likes it
Ted's Hot Dogs is routinely rated as one of the TOP TEN HOT DOGS IN THE WORLD.
What's not to like...good old fashioned German hot dogs charcoal grilled to perfection and a ton of tasty traditional condiments! And don't forget to try Ted's Famous Hot Sauce.
At lunch and dinnertime the lines are long, but this talented crew has perfected the art of having your food ready when, and only when, you reach the pick up windo.
People rave about the Onion Rings too! They are as sweet as onions can be and made fresh daily. Their looks may surprise you - but don't be fooled, these onion rings are delicious.
Want to try your hand at grilling your own dogs, pick up a package of Sahlen's hot dogs and a jar of Weber's horseradish mustard and you be the talk of the town next time you fire up the grill.
If you're from Buffalo, NY Ted's Hot Dogs is a little bit of home, after all, Buffalo is famous for more than wings. Know matter how you slice it Buffalo offers some of the finest foods around.

Really Close....
by jeff lowy (3 reviews)
May 12, 2009 - Likes it
Ted's is really close to being one of the best hot dog places ever. I've been in recently and can't decide if I've forgotten that they had "jumbo dogs" or if it's a figment of my imagination....cuz all that they have now are regulars and footlongs. If they DID have jumbos, they need to bring them back. The service line is confusing to some...and some of the people behind the counter don't pay enough attention to what you want on your dog. If you're persistent....you can get a great East Coast style dog, any way you want it....NY style (Buffalo). Chicago Style is definitely missing...but Ted's is not going to change. Great dogs, great value, some other interesting menu items. If they could get the jumbo dogs happening (the regulars are too small and the footlongs are just silly) in addition to the Polish, Brats and White hots, it would be back to perfect....well, almost. The cheese fries are always fresh, but the cheese could be done better. Homemade and imported ingredients, always clean....not many equals in the Valley. Ted has got it going on!

Best Dog West of New York!
by TT (4 reviews)
January 12, 2010
My husband and I went to Ted's Hot Dogs for a quick snack about 4:00 PM on January 9, 2010. There were about five persons in line ahead of us. The line moved quickly. This was a stroke of luck given the popularity of the place! We sat inside. Hubby had a foot-long with mustard, cheese, onions and chili. I had a foot-long with mustard, catsup, onions, relish, tomatoes and dill pickle. We split an order of onion rings. The beef & pork dogs were light and tasty. The fresh made onion rings were really good. Hubby probably would have another dog, maybe me too, however we had just gotten into town and already had dinner plans. Sure wish there was a Ted's in San Diego. This was our first dining experience on our DDD & Roadfood extended weekend in the Phoenix area. The food is tasty, fresh, hot and cooked to order at Ted's. We would come back again and again to have a dog at Ted's (whenever we're in the Phoenix area). I know next time we'll probably have to wait in a long line for a dog at Ted's, however Ted's is worth waiting for!

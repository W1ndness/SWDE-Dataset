We prefer another sushi joint in Eagle River, but this is my sons favorite so we still come here sometimes. Fun place to sit at the sushi bar and watch the chefs go to work. Good sushi rolls and tempura. Mediocre atmosphere that could use some nicer finishings/furniture to revamp its image to match its prices. Overall, aother nice option for Japanese food in Eagle River.

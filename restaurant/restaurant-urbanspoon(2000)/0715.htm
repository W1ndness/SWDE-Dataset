so I'm not a bubble tea aficionado, but the Chill Spot has the most creative and diverse bubble tea menu I've seen in Nashville, and in my opinion, serves the best bubble tea in Nashville.
my first visit was tonight and the guy that served us was great. he was incredibly helpful and you could tell he really liked what he was doing. the only down side to that was he wasn't satisfied with what we ordered and kept suggesting that we spice it up and make a more elaborate drink. but it was harmless because he seemed excited for us to try the menu.

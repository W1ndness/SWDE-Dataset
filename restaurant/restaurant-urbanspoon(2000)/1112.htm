If You're a Fan of the Pub...
by Charlie (24 reviews)
January 24, 2009 - Likes it
If you are a fan of Deseret Edge (The Pub)/Red Butte Cafe/Martine, then check out Stella. There are a few items from the Pub and Red Butte menus with some new additions of their own. If you want an old favorite, order the French Onion soup and pasta of the day. For something new, try the goat cheese appetizer. The cheese is drizzled with honey and stuffed with jalapenos and red peppers. It is accompanied with a side of greens and toasted bread. Fabulous with a glass of Ford Coppola!

Varied menu, great service.
by bigPixel (5 reviews)
March 14, 2010 - Likes it
Saturday night, 7:30pm, 4pax.
Some friends of ours recommended Stella to us so we went as a group. The menu is one of those that you look at and wonder if there's any way they could bring it all out to you. They have a varied selection of Americana from philly cheese steak sandwiches and burgers to salads, soups, some fish dishes and a sprinkling of Indian-inspired food. Stella was busy when we were there but the staff were friendly and prompt and spent time to talk to us on and off during the meal. The food was well prepared, well presented and excellent quality although their French onion soup was a little weak if I'm honest.
Thoroughly recommended but it's wise to go a little early as it seems to fill up fast.

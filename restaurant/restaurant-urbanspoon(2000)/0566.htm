No need for soy sauce
by Chase Acosta (54 reviews)
May 18, 2009 - Really likes it
I was actually on my way to a Thai place and made a wrong turn. I was very hungry so when I saw the "I love sushi" sign I thought...hey, I love sushi so I will give it a try. Amazing! I had the Kanpai special roll, honey roll and escolar. If my stomach would allow it I could have eaten for hours. The flavor of the fish and chef's special sauces were so good that I didn't even touch my soy sauce. For me that is a sign of a great sushi place, when the sushi can stand on it's own.

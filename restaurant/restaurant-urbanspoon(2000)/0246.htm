France ... in your mouth
by Will York (5 reviews)
February 19, 2010 - Likes it
The selection isn't huge, and their hours are weird, but the baguettes and other breads remind me more of Paris than any boulangerie I've visited in Northeast Ohio, or maybe anywhere in the United States. The sweets are pretty great too, but definitely don't miss the traditional French bread.

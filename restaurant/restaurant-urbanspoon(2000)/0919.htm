Oh how I miss thee...
by Chris (76 reviews)
January 16, 2010 - Likes it
I love Bangkok Square. It still holds a very special place in my heart.
I spent many lunches and dinners there while attending UCF with roommates, friends, and classmates.
I've never found such a great place that offers Thai AND sushi! Both of which are phenomenal!
My basic order, for sushi, was always the mexican roll. Delicious. I do not remember the other rolls I liked, but I believe it was one with either grouper or red fish or something -- incredible.
Their pad thai is the best I've ever had -- I have tried my hardest to find it better elsewhere (I'm hoping I do in my trip to Thailand this April!).
Their tom yum kha is great, as well.
The staff is great, and many of the watiresses are very pretty.
Eventually, from going so much, they'd know exactly what I wanted to order and would just ask me sushi or thai. They knew my drink order (easy, water with lime) and that if I had sushi, I'd like a side of spicy mayo.
The restaurant is great... you have to check it out

Tempura - To Die For
by I Love Food (1 review)
March 17, 2010 - Likes it
I can't tell you what their other food tastes like, because every time I go here or order takeout (from a delivery service) I can't help but order the tempura. It's always absolutely perfect - fresh and perfectly cooked vegetables, in the most amazingly crispy thin tempura. The shrimp tempura are huge, and the chicken tempura is so moist and flavorful. The teriyaki sauce that accompanies is is the most perfectly sweet thin soy sauce.

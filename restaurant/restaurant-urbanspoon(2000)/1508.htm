great spot for chicken wings
by Woody (1 review)
October 24, 2009 - Likes it
This place is great. It's right in the middle of everything but a little hard to find. It's right off the freeway near North Avenue. The chicken wings are awesome! They're not very big but they are so tasty. They have Buffalo style and lemony pepper. When I'm hungry, I usually order the burger. They're huge! They have a decent selection of beers, including Yuengling. Oh yeah, the tiny parking lot is hard to find also. It's behind the restaurant at the end of the skinny alley the runs beside the building

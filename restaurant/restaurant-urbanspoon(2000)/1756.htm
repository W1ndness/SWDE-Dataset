really great lunch
by Sean Cornish (56 reviews)
February 18, 2010 - Likes it
I had lunch here with my client and had a great lunch. The spinach artichoke was looser than I would normally prefer, but the hot pita chips were perfect. The lunch portions were gigantic and the tabouleh was spot on. Really enjoyed this lunch.

Delicious!
by Abby (13 reviews)
June 04, 2010 - Likes it
I eat at Romans at least once a week (to go that is). Me and my boyfriend enjoy sharing their generious sized entrees. Aside that, the food is spectatular! Between the homemake pita bread and the freshly made hummous, every time I am impressed. A MUST eat if you are shwarma loving person!

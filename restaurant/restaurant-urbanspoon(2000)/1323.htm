Fabulous!!
by sharon (1 review)
May 17, 2009 - Likes it
I held my 60th birthday party at Ray's, in their private dining room. It was superb! The staff was very attentive and aimed to please at every opportunity. But the food...ohmygod. It was perfect! The steaks were excellent, the sauces delicious. The crab imperial dish was unbelievable - it had about 2 lbs. of lump crabmeat in each dish. The salmon was also delicious. Don't let me forget the h'or douvres - absolutely delicious grilled shrimp and scallops. My guests were so impressed, as I was. I can go on and on...the desserts were wonderful, the wine was great. This is the place for a great dinner and I am truly grateful for Ray's being in Silver Spring.

Excellent steaks
by kahn7 (1 review)
February 15, 2010 - Likes it
We were there for Valentine's Day dinner February 14, 2010. We were greeted warmly, seated promptly, and served with excellence by our waiter Dara and his support staff.
The atmosphere was pleasant.
It took 75 minutes from arrival to departure.
The appetizers, potatoes, and dessert were really tasty. Best of all were the steaks. They were prepared exactly the way we wanted and were absolutely superb!
We did not care for the creamed spinach, the bread, nuts, or coffee.
We absolutely will return.

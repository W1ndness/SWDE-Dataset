We celebrated our anniversary at Lucca last night and we were not disappointed...the staff was wondeful, timing of the courses perfect due to the kitchen being open and in the middle...the food was delicious. it is hard to recommend a specific dish as the menu is constantly changing, but I can tell you I had a wonderful skirt steak served over perfectly whipped mashed potatoes and spinich. the lemon tart was to die for!

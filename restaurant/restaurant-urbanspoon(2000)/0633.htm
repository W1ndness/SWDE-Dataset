Louisville's Sushi Gem
by Norm McGlone (30 reviews)
August 29, 2009 - Really likes it
I've always found the service here to be above average. There can be a wait with some of the specialty rolls, but order strategically and don't go here in a hurry. Their beer, sake, and wine selection will leave you with a hand that's never empty.
The food itself is out of this world. Reminds me of my days of west coast living. I've eaten at every place in town with so much as seared tuna on their menu. You want real, genuine, bonafide sushi in Louisville? Go here.

Went there with another couple for my birthday. None of us have ever been there, but went on a recommendation from another individual who raved that it was the best sushi in Louisville. They have a fantastic drink menu and we really savored the entire evening out on their nice patio. Highly recommend Maido.

mobile review
by Andrew R. (3 reviews)
February 22, 2009 - Likes it
This is a great restaurant! There is not only a tremendous sushi menu, they have an impeccable libations menu with great wine selections, hot and cold sake (of course), and some amazing beers! There is a strong presence of Belgian beer in the list, including craft beers both local and international. No Bud or Miller to be found! Can you say HopHead? I highly recomend being brave and branching out when ordering. There are some amazing flavors hidden in their large, well thought out menu. Also remeber to hit their happy hour after work, including the pocketbook friendly Appreciation Menu offering rolls for as little as a dollar! See maidosakebar.com for details.

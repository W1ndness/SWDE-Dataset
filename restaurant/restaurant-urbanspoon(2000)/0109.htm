#10 Says It All
by Noel Bagwell (20 reviews)
June 25, 2010 - Likes it
As I write this, P.F. Chang's China Bistro (Summit Blvd.) is #10 on the Urbanspoon list of Top 10 Restaurants in Birmingham. That kind-of says it all, doesn't it? I mean, it's Birmingham... and a chain restaurant is #10 on the Top 10 list. Compared to a lot of other restaurants in Birmingham, P.F. Chang's is better. Compared to other P.F. Chang's locations, this one is better than, say, the one on West End in Nashville, TN or the one in Knoxville, TN, but maybe not as good as the one in Cool Springs in Nashville, TN. Birmingham just isn't a foodie paradise, even though it does a have a few local gems.
If you want good, authentic Chinese or Taiwanese food in Birmingham, do yourself a favor and go to Mr. Chen's in Hoover. It blows P.F. Chang's out of the water, even if it is only #51 on the Urbanspoon list (at the moment).
If you're dead-set on eating at P.F. Chang's, you'll probably get only a little less than you pay for. It's really not bad. It's just not as good as Mr. Chen's, and it costs a lot more.

Fantastico!
by Nick Brooks (23 reviews)
July 11, 2009 - Likes it
I had to try out Johns because of it's legendary status among pizza eaters in New York, and I was not disappointed. Visitor Beware however, this is not a slice place, you need to order an entire pie. I comfortably split a small pie with 2 other people. Excellent crispy thin crust, with very fresh and flavorful healthy ingredients. The brick oven gives the crust a great home cooked taste, an art that is lost at the vast majority of pizza places today. It is obviously a very popular restaurant, however we had not problem sitting down for lunch without reservations. I would rank this among my top 3 pizza spots in NYC, which is saying a lot. Give it a try.

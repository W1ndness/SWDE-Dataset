PF Chang's
by Bill (8 reviews)
March 06, 2009
I lived in Southern California for longer than I care to admit. With a large asian population I'm used to eating excellent asian food. For an ethnic restaurant run by people not of that ethnicity, PF Chang's is good. I especially like the Cantonese Chow Fun with chicken. Chow Fun is noodles made from rice flour, while Chow Mein is noodles made from wheat flour. Most Chinese restaurants do not have Chow Fun, so I take advantage of this at PF Chang's.
The service is good and the prices reasonable. The only negative thing about the place is the noise level. It is almost impossible to have a conversation without shouting.

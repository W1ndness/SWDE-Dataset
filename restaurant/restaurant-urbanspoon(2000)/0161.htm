Second try...3rd strike and you are out!
by Howie is Hungry (128 reviews)
May 07, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I was determined to give High Cotton another chance after my first trial. I went for dinner a few nights ago and left, again slightly disappointed. For starters I order a soup and they brought out the wrong one and instead of politely changing my soup, they insisted I "changed my mind" even though my entire table heard my order the correct soup. Even if it was my mistake, I am the guest and this is supposed to be an upscale restaurant. You don't treat your customers that way.
My food came and I had lobster mac n cheese and tenderloin. The mac and cheese was really delicious. The steak was chewy and fatty...i didn't eat any more of it.
What do you have in store for me next time High Cotton?

Great Dinner
by carnellm (46 reviews)
February 15, 2009 - Likes it
My wife and I went to High Cotton this evening for their Valentine's dinner special. The meal and service were excellent. She had an appetizer of shrimp and grits followed by filet mignon and lobster with a dessert of banana split.  I had veal sweet breads as an appetizer followed by lamb chops with potato casserole and a desert of crème brûlée.  All of the food, and there was an over abundance of it, was perfectly cooked and presented. The meal was not cheap by any means, but it was a perfect Valentine's celebration.

Could Have Been Better
by jonesgirl (1 review)
September 27, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I expected my dining experience at High Cotton to be significantly better than it was.
Buttermilk Fried Oysters with Arugula and Green Goddess Dressing left MUCH to be desired. The oysters were flavorless and the breading was pointless. The Green Goddess' flavor was off in some way and more like a mayonnaise than Green Goddess.
Ordered the Livin' Large Burger medium rare. Every component complimented one another and the beef-- except for the bread. Albeit I don't really understand the point of burger buns. Be prepared for a fatty burger.

Won't disappoint
by undorgre (34 reviews)
January 06, 2010 - Likes it
I recently attended a wedding reception that was hosted in Maggiano's private dining room (highly recommended, can accommodate 100+ people). I can't say enough good things about this experience. Rather than choosing a dish the bride/groom chose a menu that was delivered to each table family-style (two thumbs-up on this approach), so it allowed everyone to try a little (or a lot) of everything.
All-in-all I really enjoyed everything I tried. For starters they had four cheese ravioli (huge) with marinara and fried zucchini served with a lemon cream sauce. I missed out on the zucchini but the ravioli were filled with a plenty of cheese. Both salads served were tasty (although I was saving room for the main dishes).
On to the entrees. My favorite was the chicken & spinach manicotti. It was very rich and creamy with a fair balance of chicken and cheeses. I could have eaten it all evening. After that also offered was the chicken saltimbocca (awesome, had never had this dish before) as well as sliced pork accompanied with mashed potatoes and crispy fried onions (again, very well done).
Rather than a traditional wedding cake the couple decided to go with New York style cheesecake for dessert and it did not disappoint... pretty rich though, one slice is a lot.
Note that there were a lot of leftovers taken home from this even and the food was excellent after being frozen and warmed up in the oven later. I'll definitely be back to Maggiano's sometime soon.

won't be back
by alix00 (28 reviews)
January 09, 2008 - Doesn't like it
I didn't enjoy the food at Maggiano's. It was too Americanized for my taste.
For instance, I had bruschetta, and it had some sort of butter flavored oil on it, instead of olive oil, which would have tasted better, been more authentic, and would have been healthier. I was equally unimpressed with all other items I had that day.
If you don't mind the food being Americanized, then you might like it.

suprisingly good
by Corinn45 (48 reviews)
February 21, 2010 - Likes it
Went here last night with a big group b/c some were on a budget, so my expectations were not high. However, the meal was quite good! We ordered everything "family style," which seems to be the best way to go, as ordering any other way means everything a la carte. Unlimited refills of appetizers, salads, entree, AND pastas this way. Not the best Italian I've had, but still quite good. Many different options for entrees and pasta. I would definitely go back.

Possibly Best Wings on Planet Earth...
by klingercrazy (114 reviews)
June 27, 2009 - Likes it
The Wild Wing cafe has a supurb and well rounded menu. As for the wing menu if you cant find it here then they probably dont make it! I Love the Red Hot & Blue wings! They are a special recipe that somehow cooks a blue cheese flavor right into the wing! No breading to do it eather these are the real Mccoy -- Buffalo style hot wing with Blue Cheese. My second choice is the Garlic wings -- Yummy too! The menu isn't just for the wing lover -- they offer a well rounded menu to please even the pickyest eaters. Oh - they don't skimp on the celery or blue cheese either!

Wing Heaven
by Alan W Rollins (60 reviews)
November 08, 2009 - Likes it
Welcome to Wing Heaven. If you like wings as I do, you will love Wild Wings outside of Knoxville. I am a traditionalist so by that, I mean I stick to the HOT, tried and true. However, Wild Wings allows you to order half one way and half the other so, i have ventured beyond my traditionalistic boundaries.
And, EVERY single one that I have tried, I have liked.
It is taste-budically impossible to be disappointed here.
...and as a side note, my wife does not eat wings and I have NO problem getting her to eat here. Everything is GREAT.

Boneless wings
by RTT_2003 (2 reviews)
March 17, 2010
I like the sauces, but am not a fan of Wild Wing Cafe's boneless wings - they are way to breaded and just not good. Their regular wings are good, but I'm not a fan of bones so am fairly disappointed they don't do their boneless wings better. Although I must say the atmosphere here is much better than the atmosphere of Buffalo Wild Wings just down the road.

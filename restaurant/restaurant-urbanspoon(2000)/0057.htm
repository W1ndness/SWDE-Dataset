Message from Hut's Hamburgers

Thick, juicy burgers topped with just about anything in a fun, laid-back space.

  • Dine In or Take Out
  • 2 For 1 Specials!
  • Milk Shakes & Malts

Welcome to Hut's Hamburgers! We are conveniently located on West 6th Street and West Avenue in Downtown Austin. Our menu has over 20 different mouthwatering hamburger choices. In addition we offer Texas natural beef, buffalo and veggie burgers! Not hungry for burgers? Choose from our sandwich menu, or try a chicken fried steak, a salad, buffalo wings, appetizers or desserts from our menu. We are casual, kid friendly and fun! Come and enjoy the atmosphere with family, friends or for lunch with the office. Most of all, enjoy the food. We look forward to your visit!

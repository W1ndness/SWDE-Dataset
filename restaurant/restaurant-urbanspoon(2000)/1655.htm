We cordially invite you to join us at The Capital Grille. Come and experience everything from our famous dry aged steaks to our exceptional seafood. Enjoy the sights and sounds of one of America's most historic port towns; choose from an award-winning selection of 400 wines while soaking up Baltimore's cool sea breezes.

Great night out downtown
by Angie (5 reviews)
June 17, 2010 - Likes it
The Capital Grille is a great restaurant because you can go for a special occasion like a birthday, a casual lunch with friends, or a business meal. I was there for a birthday dinner, and the restaurant really made the night special.
The food was delicious, and the menu was so big, it was almost overwhelming. My steak was cooked just as I requested, a perfect medium rare, and it was so good.
For dessert, we ordered a selection from the menu, and our waitress put a candle in the cake. Thanks Capital Grille!

HERE'S THE BEEF!!!
by Larry A. (16 reviews)
June 21, 2009 - Really likes it
This was our first time dining at "The Capital Grille," and our lunch experience was terrific! The decor was old-style steakhouse class, with warm rich walnut accents accompanying various portraits of famous Baltimoreans, including Johnny Unitas. Both my wife & I ordered filet mignon w/cipollini onions & mushrooms, complete with mashed potatoes. Her appetizer was a flavorful spinach salad, my starter was a savory lobster bisque. The wine list was detailed and offered great variety, and the dessert menu was rich and decadent.
Our server, Manny, was tremendous! Timely in his bringing servings to the table, observant to keep our beverage glasses full, and a tremendous sense of humor.
We will be back!

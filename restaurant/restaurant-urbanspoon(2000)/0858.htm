Excellent Italian
by A Tevington (707 reviews)
May 08, 2009 - Likes it
The food here is delicious. The cheese ravioli is the best I've had in Oklahoma. The penne pasta that comes as a side order with non-pasta meals has a tasty but reserved tomato sauce. The veal Marsala's cream sauce is exquisite, and the meat is properly cooked. Veal can be very boring, but this Marsala dish is full of flavor and a great texture. The chicken Marsala is similarly prepared. The calamari appetizer could use a little seasoning, but the squid itself is perfectly cooked--nothing rubbery here. And the vegetarian vegetable soup is a real treat, with a full-flavored broth and a great choice of vegetable ingredients. The décor is a bit over the top--even a little cheesy--but the wait staff is extremely friendly and among the best in Oklahoma City. Despite the comment of an earlier reviewer, the prices are entirely appropriate. An Andy Williamsesque lounge singer entertains with standards from the 1930s through 1950s most nights. Vito's is open for supper only.

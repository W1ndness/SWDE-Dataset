Spice heaven
by Icewolf (150 reviews)
June 14, 2009 - Really likes it
There is not a meal yet that has disappointed me at Chino and there have been many, many meals. My favorite of all time is the Emerald Chicken, it's ginger and green onion mastery will leave you thinking about it's flavor long after you've eaten it. Of course just about everything on the menu is delicious. While I love the jerk chicken, I find that the Hengrenade is superior with it's more tempered amount of jerk you can still taste the chicken yet enjoy the jerk. The snickerdoodles are always generous in size and quantity and always satisfy. If you get there on the weekend, the tostadas are delicious but it is 2 small shells inside one container. You could order 2 of them or a burrito to accompany and it'd satisfy. Love the Chino, it's my favorite takee-outee.

Pollo Diablo, Chicken of the Devil
by Doug Bruhnke (151 reviews)
December 26, 2009 - Really likes it
But oh so good. #13 Pollo Diablo delights with garlic and hot peppers. Chino Bandido is a definitely a funky place as everyone says. It's in a not so fancy strip mall with not so fancy decor inside. But look past it and you'll have some fun. Besides the Pollo Diablo the #10 Emerald Chicken has a wonderful flavoring including ginger that is well teamed with the jerk fried rice. Yes it is an odd mix of Mexican, Chinese and Caribbean flavors, and it works. Very inexpensive.

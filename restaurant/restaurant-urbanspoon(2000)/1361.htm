Cowboy BBQ ...
by Larry McGoldrick (128 reviews)
September 14, 2009 - Likes it
When you need a BBQ fix and Mad Max's is closed, try this place. It is *really* good. Real people eat here.And they are all smiling. I also rate it a small cut above Rudy's. And on Friday evening, you are likely to hear some good, live, boot kickin' music in the patio room.
Try the smoked burger and the potato salad.
Gil eats here a lot.

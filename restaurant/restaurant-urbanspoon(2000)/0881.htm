Yum!
by Michal L (15 reviews)
November 06, 2009 - Likes it
Pepperjax is a great place to get a Philly cheese steak in Omaha. I love the straight up plain steak Philly with cheese and a side of fries. The seasoning they put on their steak is amazing and the fries are thick and tasty. If you want, you can also get peppers and onions on your sandwich and they do also have a chicken version of the Philly. My wife loves to get the rice bowls which are basically a Philly without the bun and mixed up with rice, beans and other vegetables. If you plan to go to Pepperjax over the lunch hour it is usually packed so plan on waiting in line. Pepperjax is definitely my favorite Philly in Omaha. If you like Philly sandwiches, don't miss out on Pepperjax!

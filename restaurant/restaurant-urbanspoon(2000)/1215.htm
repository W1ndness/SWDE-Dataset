Great Pizza
by Miller Lite Brian (28 reviews)
February 09, 2009 - Likes it
The pizza at Remo's is always a treat. The thin crust pies baked in a brick oven are awesome. The sandwiches and salads are also exceptional. The classic pizza parlor atmosphere, combined with a great view of Bedford street make it a great choice for lunch or dinner.

Second Best Pizza in Town.
by Liam M (10 reviews)
January 27, 2010 - Likes it
Short of Colony grill, this place has, quite possibly, the best pizza in town. What sets it apart from Colony is that, with its prime location in downtown Stamford, its close to many shops, theaters and a middle-sized mall. Also, their thin crust pizza is complimented by a diverse menu, also unlike Colony. In all, its perfect for a family dinner, especially if you want to see a movie afterward.

Yum!
by Gratefulgrrl (6 reviews)
January 01, 2010 - Likes it
The fresh mozzarella pie is so good as is the service. Remo's is right in the middle of downtown Stamford, so you might have to hunt for parking. Try the lot in the back. Great food all-around and it's a nice atmosphere for a date or a family.

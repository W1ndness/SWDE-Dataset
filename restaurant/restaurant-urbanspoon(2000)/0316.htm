Overpriced Food, So-So Beer
by gethoht (7 reviews)
June 02, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Colorado is the microbrewery capital of the US(by capita at least), which is a good thing because if the Pumphouse was the only microbrew in town we'd be in trouble. Red Alert, and 4 Alarm are good as these recipes were crafted by their old brewmaster. All the new recipes, especially the seasonal's, are barely drinkable, with some being so bad I can't even drink it at all. The food isn't too bad, but they just raised menu prices in the middle of a recession... that's tasteless. Save your money and go to Oskar Blues, which has much better food, a better atmosphere and MUCH better beer.

Burgers, Beer, and Surprises...
by Vanselus (5 reviews)
August 18, 2009 - Really likes it
We've been faithfully patronizing the Pumphouse for over a year now - especially on "Free Beer w/ a Burger" night. The burgers are so big, juicy, and varied that for quite a while I never strayed from charred ground beef into the other menu options. My mouth is extremely happy to say that I've seen the err of my ways! The Chili Rellenos are not exactly authentic Mexican cuisine, but they are incredibly good, full of cheese & fried with reckless abandon. My wife raves about the shrimp tacos, and the grilled, beer broiled, bratwurst with house-made mustard and grilled onions was write-home good. Great atmosphere, very kid friendly - nice prices & oreos included. Parking can be a bit rough, but go figure - this is one of the best spots in Longmont.

Bad Experience
by sheree_4 (1 review)
November 15, 2009
My husband and I were so excited a place like this opened in our community. We visited Sip Saturday night. We were seated and it was 15 mins before anyone greeted our table. We then ordered wine tastings, a cheese plate, and shrimp. After several mins the waiter returned with only water. He explained they were out of 2 of the 6 wines we had ordered. Finally we had our drink pairings to the tapas but no food. It took FOREVER to get our food. The waiter informed us that they were out of bread which went to the cheese plate and the complimentary tapas he had promised us. When the cheese plate arrived it had no bread, which we had told that is why it took so long, but crackers in its place. We never received the complimentary tapas. Also, the atmosphere was very loud for a wine bar. Terrible service and experience!!

Great dinner experience tonight!
by Hank (16 reviews)
April 09, 2010 - Likes it
I went tonight for the first time to SIP. Was seated immediately at the bar even though it was packed. Waitress was very pleasant and knowledgeable. Suggested a wonderful wine. I had three tapas items and all were excellent and cooked exactly as I ordered them. There is lots of energy in the place and it was loud, but not loud enough to keep me from having a conversation - a very positive energy there. The owner was very approachable and friendly; but who wouldn't be with such good food and such a great chef there? I felt warmly welcomed, like visiting friends. The menu has lots of items I want to try on my next visits. Sitting at the bar, I was able to watch the chef in action. Everything he plated looked and smelled delicious. I did not eat dessert but the ones I saw looked delicious. They are unique and different from what you'll find in other restaurants. Can't wait to go back!

Wonderful place!
by Sheryl (1 review)
November 17, 2009 - Likes it
I heard about this place when some friends of mine went for dinner, and they raved about it! They are the picky type, so I knew I had to check it out. We went in and were greeted by the hostess who took us on a small tour of the retail area and restaurant. She showed us the cool machines that dispense wine.. (how fun is that?!) And she gave us a background on the restaurant. I thought that was a nice touch because I didn't know anything upon walking in.. it made me feel like I was at someone's home. The kitchen is open and the buzz around the place was so positive. Our server was great! Explained to us the menu, the wine... it was unpretentious and knowledgable at the same time. The food was undeniably some of the best food I have ever had! The crawfish cheesecake, the pistachio scallops.. I wish I could write it all down, but hands down... the food was prepared perfectly. I'm just so happy to find a place that has such a great, all around atmosphere. My friends were right and I am so excited to have found a place that has such a great feel (not to mention excellent food!) Will definitely be back!

What's there to say...
by Noel M (111 reviews)
November 10, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The Cheescake Factory is a great addition to the city! The choices are absolutely fantastic. The food is consistently good. Not the best in the city, however, good in it's category as a mid-priced restaurant. As a whole, the restaurant is decent.
I have weighed this out in that there are a few problems that I can't overlook.
My first problem is that the tables are a little to close to close to each other. Whenever we eat there, we hope to get an empty table next to us. The problem is that we are only 12 inches from the next table. It is way to cramped in.
My Second Problem, that really ties in the with the first one is that the noise level is deafening. I have it attributed to the fact that they have jammed people into the restaurant. Space the tables out!
I have dined at other Cheesecake Factories, and the ones I have been to had large seating areas and overall looked better.
One good note is that sometimes they space everyone out, which is good. But don't go there on a Friday and Saturday night, it will be busy and you will be sitting on top of someone!

Consistently Good
by Frederick (217 reviews)
April 04, 2009 - Likes it
Say what you want about corporate mediocrity, at least these mega-chains offer consistently excellent service - decent food, convivial atmosphere, and expertly trained service where you are ALWAYS greeted at the door within five seconds of arrival.
This outpost of Cheesecake Factory's empire in Town Center is a fine place to take a large party - their menu is the same as all other C.Factories which means it is incredibly comprehensive offering something for every conceivable taste and food preference. It can get quite busy on weekends and wait times of 2 hours or more are not uncommon and they DON'T take reservations.
I am partial to their Thai Lettuce Wraps (far far better than P.F Changs!) and their Shrimp Scampi. The portions are huge so expect a doggy bag at the end of the meal.

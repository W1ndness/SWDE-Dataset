Perfectly delicious
by Knappst3r (44 reviews)
January 24, 2010 - Really likes it
There's nothing about Meditrina that I didn't love.
From the Scrabble name tags for the wait staff, to the warm and low lit dining room, my experience here was something that I wish to repeat, often and soon.
The small plates dining experience is simple, and great for tasting things you might not have otherwise tried. Select a few to start, and then add more as you need.
Just save room for dessert, you'll thank me.
Dishes that impressed included the antipasto plate, patats bravas, and lamb lollipops. Dessert was the panna cotta special and the signature drunken oreos.
I highly recommend you take a step from the hustle and bustle, and be whisked away by Meditrina's charm.

Great food, relaxing atmosphere
by utemike (3 reviews)
August 23, 2009 - Likes it
We were looking for a place with outside dining on a summer evening. We didn't even realize tht meant we wanted to eat slowly and relax, but that's what happened at Meditrina. There was no beach nearby, but there should have been with the feeling this restaurant creates. The server appears promptly, goes into the level of detail you want about the food, the wine or even quirky liquor laws and is attentive the rest of the night.
The food was varied and excellent, wine could be ordered by the half glass to pair with the variety of food and we had a lingering, pleasant evening ordering a couple plates at a time, then a couple more.
Don't let the Oreo port-infused desert with ice cream put you off. I was really suspicious but we tried it and will order it again.
In fact, we will return to this restaurant soon, it is one of the best in Salt Lake. Go there before it becomes the latest rage and you have to wait to be seated.

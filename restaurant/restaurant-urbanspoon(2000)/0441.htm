Circus on the beach!
by D. Vower (30 reviews)
April 06, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I suppose it's worth going here at least once for the carnival-like atmosphere, but if you don't enjoy dining in a circus, why bother. We went here five years ago and decided to give it another try. Guess what, the menu hadn't changed in five years! And there were no specials! SO, if you don't mind eating the same food day in and day out and like having waiters and waitresses bussing past you at the speed of light and ladies asking if you would like to pay them to take your sunset picture, go for it! The food is passable but I find the circus drama atmosphere very undesirable and unpalatable. For a couple on a honeymoon, make an early reservation (weeks in advance and ask for a window seat), have your picture taken as the sun sets and tell everyone you've been to Kauai. For us, once was enough and twice was a mistake.

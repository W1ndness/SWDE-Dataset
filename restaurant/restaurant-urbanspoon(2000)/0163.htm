Great food, warm people!
by pigangel (8 reviews)
April 28, 2009
I really liked the overall feel of this restaurant when we came in.
Of course, as a dog owner, one would almost have to. Pictures of dogs of Folly adorn every square inch of free space on the walls and we found ourselves trying to rec ognize our local dog friends!
After perusing the menu carefully, I quickly decided on the lox and bagel, my friend had the biscuits and gravy with bacon and we bothe shared a delicious cinnamon roll.
One thing I particularly liked was the way they assembled the lox and bagel...they actually mixed the cream cheese with the capers and chives , which made it far easier to eat and consistent bite!
I would have liked to see more in the egg department as far of choices.....
Prices reasonable and look forward to going back!

Affordable?!
by Drexler McStyles (205 reviews)
October 08, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I had to laugh at a couple of the comments about this place. First, it is totally overpriced. There is nothing cheap about it. This is a rowdy, loud college place (located across from UNM) and I guess if theres 10 of you splitting the bill it might seem cheap or affordable. Much like Frontier, the high rating comes not from the food, but from the sheer bulk of people that eat there. Spending $30 for a Pizza and a couple of Cokes seems a bit steep. College kids paying with daddys credit card seem to fit the bill here...otherwise, forget it.

Not Just A Pizza Joint!
by Chef Dare (167 reviews)
June 13, 2009 - Likes it
Saggio's is NOT just a pizzeria. It is a full scale, inexpensive Italian restaurant with a ton of delicious menu items. No matter what you order, you can't go wrong, but here are some of my favorites: Thai Chicken Pizza, Meatball Calzone, Tuscano Pasta Salad, Mediterranean Pizza, Neo Classico Panini, Baked Ziti, Citrus Salmon Salad, Chicken Alfredo Pizza...okay! You get the point! Saggio's is a winner for an inexpensive and delicious Italian meal.

A little slice of heaven!
by His Joeness (24 reviews)
November 15, 2009 - Likes it
If you have not been to Saggio's you will! Featuring Giant Slice's of Pizza that could satisfy Michaelangelo (the Turtle!), pasta salads, pastas, cannoli and, of course monstrously sized pies that can feed a famished family of five, Saggio's is THE Pizza place in the 505.
Ask any starving college student from the University and they wil likely tll you that they frequent Saggio's at least once a month (often more regularly).
The beer selection is limited, but the sports offerings on the multiple T.V.s throughout more than compensate. The offerings boil down to one game when the Lobos play. Totally understandable!

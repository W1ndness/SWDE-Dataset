Over Rated!
by Justin (10 reviews)
June 10, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Being a resident of Oakville I have come to the conclusion that Oakville residents are satisfied with mediocrity. Oakville residents do not have a lot of selections for eateries and it is almost like they just make do with what they have. That is such a disappoitment. Garvey's was a great place, in the early to mid 90's. They had a good rep for the wings back in the day, now they have the same wings that everyone else has in America and the size of the wings are smaller then they were and the sauce lost it's zip that it had in the past. I can't go to a bar/grill and drop $9.50 on a quesadilla that doesn't contain meat on it. Pizzas are good however but they need to adjust prices on food items and get back to what they were known for back in the day WINGS.

Recommend | Not helpful

Garvey's Grill
by e-courtois (1 review)
May 29, 2010 - Doesn't like it
A neighborhood place with decent food. Deluxe pizza was good & a little unique. No beverage prices on menu. Domestic beers were $3.25 per bottle. Price very high to the nearby competition such as Billy's Bistro or Fitzgeralds. Place was a smaller version of Mattingly's in Florissant., with much higher beer prices. Beer also needed to be colder. I will spend my money at the other places.

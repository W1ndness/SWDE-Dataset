Great cajun food in the cold WNY!
by treedogg24 (9 reviews)
June 06, 2010 - Likes it
Went to New Orleans last year, and have ever since been constantly craving some crawfish. Saw the menu and went the next day! For a thursday night they were pretty busy, but the wait staff couldn't handle the crowd. They did apologize and tried to sit us relatively quickly. Started off with a cup of the gumbo and a crock of the jambalaya. Boy was it ever good! Brought me right back to Louisiana! Such complex flavors, and quite filling! Next we ordered 2 orders of the crawfish. Had to ask the waitress to refresh us on how to eat them, and she confessed she didn't know????!!!??? Cajun restaurant, really? Little disappointed in how the head had little to no real appetizing taste(which it should). Although the tail meat was good. All in all, I still would go back here. The soups were a hit, and there were a few other items on the menu that we wanted to try!

Message from El Charro Cafe

This Old Pueblo institution has served authentic, homemade Mexican favorites since forever.

  • Family-Friendly
  • Gift Cards
  • Two Locations

Established in 1922, El Charro Cafe of Tucson, Arizona is the nation's oldest Mexican restaurant in continuous operation by the same family, featuring traditional Sonoran cultural style and innovative Tucson-style Mexican Food. El Charro is truly, as Gourmet Magazine writes, 'a taste explosion'. With a large array of products which feature a synergy of Sonoran ingredients, El Charro is Tucson's culinary history. People come from all across the country to experience El Charro and we invite you to do the same. The El Charro family is also proud to feature our signature line of retail products, including salsas, tamales, calendars and gift cards, our Latin entertainment and tequila bars and our full featured catering services.

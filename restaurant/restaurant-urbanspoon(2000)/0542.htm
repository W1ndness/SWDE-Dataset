Excellent ... really good Seafood!!!
by Darren (1 review)
April 24, 2010
We tried Captain Ernie's Fish House yesterday and it was simply excellent. The food was very good quality, well-cooked and the staff was very friendly. There are a wide variety of dishes and prices ranging from about $10 to $25, so you can spend what you want to ... which is not the same as being expensive or over-priced! It is not fast food, so the meal takes a few minutes to prepare because each meal is prepared fresh when ordered. Captain Ernie's does remind me of the good blue-collar seafood restaurants along the coast I miss very much. I highly recommend this restaurant and will certainly go back ... probably many times because I love seafood.
(And to the pinhead who made the derogatory comment about "Mexican's cooking seagood" .... I suspect; from the menu, the decor and the big map of Cuba on the wall that the folks are Cuban ... and since Cuba is an island I suspect they know a great bit about preparing seafood. By the way, I also like good Mexican food!)

All wrong
by aspring2 (6 reviews)
February 09, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Initial impression: I was not greated upon entering, but the cooks did look at me...awkward. The tables and chairs are wobbley and look like they belong at the local VFW hall.
Service: 5-10 minutes before even being acknowledged by the server who dropped off some menus (paper, not even laminated) and ran off. He appeared to be the only server in the whole place, and could not handle it.
Atmosphere: I think they are going for the local beach type seafood place and although the wall are decorated appropriately, the rest of the place is lacking. There was not any music playing and only one small TV turned up too loud.
Food: I have to recommend the yellow-tale snapper *baked* not pan-fried as recommended by the server. The salad was small and nothing to write home about, the mixed vegetable seem to be frozen rather than fresh. Prices: Way too high for the quality. I understand that they are 'flying the fish in daily from Key West' but they are not living up to their prices.
Overall: I would not go back. I am willing to pay the price for a great meal, but this was subpar.

A Former Employee's Opinion
by CyberReviewer (1 review)
April 05, 2010 - Likes it
First, let me say that I'm a former employee so I know what I'm talking about. I will not comment on my reasons for leaving. I will comment only on the restaurant.
The food is excellent! The coleslaw, all the sauces, the key lime pie, and much more... is all homemade. Ernie has some great recipes, seasoned out of this world.
I see some comments about the price but if you go to the beach, you're going to pay pretty much the same, and fresh fish doesn't have to be trucked or flown in at the beach.
I also saw comments about the service. Unfortunately, I can't give an educated statement about this because it has been quite a while since I worked there. The one thing I will say is that the food here is made to order. Nothing is pre-cooked so yes, it might take a little longer to get your food. But would you rather have something that's been sitting there for who knows how long or would you rather have it freshly cooked?
Overall, the food is excellent and most people love the beachy atmosphere. If done right, this place is a goldmine and I hope they do very well with it.
Kim T.

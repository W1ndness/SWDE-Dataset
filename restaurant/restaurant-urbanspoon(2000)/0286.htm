Message from Buca di Beppo

Buca di Beppo is the perfect place for great Italian food and fun! Buca serves family-style dishes in an eclectic, vintage sett

  • Big Portions. Bigger Value
  • Great for celebratory dining.
  • Hooray for giant meatballs!

Buca di Beppo gets to the heart of fresh Italian cooking with favorites like Prosciutto Stuffed Chicken, Baked Rigatoni or Spaghetti with their famous half-pound meatballs! Buca serves family-style food in two portion sizes. Buca Small feeds up to three and Buca Large feeds an average-sized country. Well, actually it feeds up to six. Buca is the perfect place for great Italian food and fun! Whether you?re celebrating a special occasion, planning a business dinner or having a night out with family and friends, you?ll enjoy Buca?s modern Italian cuisine in an eclectic, vintage setting. Ask about Buca?s specialty tables - a ?Pope?s Table? that seats 12-18 and a ?Kitchen Table? that seats 6-10.

No longer a fan
by klahdane (44 reviews)
October 20, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Years ago when Buca first came to Columbus, I was in love with the quality of the food. We went with a large group and had a wonderful time. Fast forward seven years or so and insert utter blah. The chicken marsala is incredibly sweet--apparently that is the authentic way to prepare the dish. We were sorely disappointed when it tasted like sweet and sour chicken at roughly $20 for the entree. Dessert was ok--too sweet. The bruschetta was the best part of the meal.

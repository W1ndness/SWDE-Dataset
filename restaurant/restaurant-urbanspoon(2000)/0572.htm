Amazing food at a cheap price. I've been coming to this Faustos for years and I've never been able to find another Mexican food restaurant that is even half as good. Faustos is as authentic as you can get, and the service is awesome. All of the cashiers know my order when I walk in, and all I need to do is pay them. They're ALWAYS willing to remake any food item that isn't up to par (rarely ever). Ive came in every week at least twice for years, and I've always had a great experience.

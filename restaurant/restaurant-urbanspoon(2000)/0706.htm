Message from Tum Rup Thai

  • Dine-in and takeout
  • Daily drink specials
  • Authentic Thai cuisine

Tum Rup Thai is located on Uptown Row on the corner of Fremont Avenue and West Lake Street in Minneapolis. Just two blocks east of Calhoun Square. Come join us for lunch from 11pm to 3pm daily. Try Thai cuisine classics such as spring rolls, curry and pad thai. Take advantage of our daily drink specials from 4pm to 6pm and again from 9pm to 11pm.

A gem, but in uptown
by TJ (19 reviews)
November 17, 2009 - Likes it
Being from the burbs, it is a haul to go to Uptown, but this place is well worth it. Watch out - mild spice is VERY spicy. No kidding, they have MN's best Pad Thai. Been there at least 20 times in the past couple of years and only 1 was sub-par. When that happened, the manager apologized and credited our bill instantly. Get there before 6:30 on weekends or expect to stand for a while. Entree prices are so reasonable (10-14) that it makes the drive worth it.

Best Thai in the cities
by thaifreak (1 review)
November 18, 2008 - Likes it
I know Thai food.  I love it.  I lived in Australia for a while, where Asian cuisine rules.  Tum Rup Thai is the best Thai food I've found north outside of Australia and Thailand.  I've eaten at most of the Thai restaurants in the twin cities, hoping to find another place to rival Tum Rup.  It hasn't happened yet.  The prices are decent--more than I'd usually spend on dinner and a glass of wine, but they're totally worth it. They do a great job of catering to vegetarians.  I recommend the Pad See Ew for noodle lovers.

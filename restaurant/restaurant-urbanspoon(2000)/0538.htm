Great Southern Meals
by daisyferret1 (130 reviews)
June 09, 2010 - Really likes it
We love the Old Mill. It is one of our MUSTs each & every time we visit the Smokies. The breakfasts are simple homestyle dishes and are cooked perfectly. The prices are not super cheap, but with each breakfast you get a small orange juice each, basket of mini banana nut muffins, a bowl of sawmill style gravy and grits to share. So you almost get another whole breakfast with what you order--making this a great deal. It is usually chilly in there in the morning--so bring a sweatshirt.
The Southern Style Lunches/Dinners are great too. We have tried the fried chicken, hot prime rib sandwich and hot turkey sandwiches so far. Each is served with mashed potatoes and seasoned green beans. Their seasoned green beans are the best beans I have ever had. And the turkeys is amazing!! Plus with your meal you get a basket of corn fritters and a bowl of corn chowder (or substitue vegetable soup). Note-the corn chowder is good but contains CLAM juice. We have never gotten a bad meal at the Old Mill. PLUS the portion sizes are HUGE. We usually have enough left over for a 2nd meal.
I highly recommend the Old Mill for breakfast, lunch or an early dinner (at lunch prices). Dinner is a bit more expensive (but includes dessert). If you do visit, be prepared to become a repeat customer--even IF it means visiting multiple times on the same vacation! And don't forget to visit with the ducks along ther river bank in front of the restaurant.

Cute place, gets crowded and loud when the band plays. The irish dancer is way over rated and only comes on for a dance once every two hours or so. We've met lots of new friends here and look forward to returning when we're back in December!

Tourist Trap
by UCF Knights Fan (69 reviews)
December 30, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The positive reviews on here confuse me. This place is a tourist trap and nothing more. The food is bland and uninteresting. The portion sizes are laughable, and the prices are outrageous. Similarly, I find nothing authentic about this place. Our male server was gracious, knowledgeable, friendly, and hospitable, the outside patio is nice, and the inside decor is rather eye catching.
However, because of the taste of the food, portion size, and absurd prices I cannot see myself going back here. For a more quality irish cuisine dining experience, try Liam Fitzpatrick's in Lake Mary or Fiddler's Green in Winter Park.

mobile review
by Jason H. (24 reviews)
January 30, 2010 - Really likes it
First, consider this review in context. After all, this restaurant is at Donwtown Dinsey, which means that the prices are going to be higher than similar Irish pubs in Lake Mary or Winter Park. For a Disney restaurant, this is one of my favorites. Disney service is good, as expected, but the food is far better than average. In the land of the Mouse, a $15 hamburger is not unusal. So, if you're going to spend that money anyway, why not spend it on some great sheperd's pie, fish and chips, or bangers and mash accompanied by a well drawn Guiness? If you're going to eat at Downtown Disney, you could certainly do a lot worse than Raglan Road.

Loved my salad
by Cameron (126 reviews)
July 15, 2010 - Likes it
I have been wanting to check out this place forever and I finally went!
It' is definitely a waspy lady hang out. I thought I was going to throw up if I saw another Tory Burch logo.. but I digress.
It reminded me a lot of Hungry's. In a wealthy neighborhood, nice modern decor but still very natural. Unlike Hungry's - the food was great! I ordered the salad with grilled chicken, pecans and blue cheese. I devoured it. The ice tea was fabulous as well. They were also had fresh baked cookies that smelled delicious, but I am trying to stay away from sweets, so I passed.
I will definitely go here again to try out the burgers and sandwiches, which looked wonderful.

I eat breakfast out, and this was the worst. The location is pretty, but that is all. I will never return.

A palate cleanser amidst a restauraunt-ridden city
by Andy Shelton (2 reviews)

Tiny Boxwood's has a unique atmosphere. Set within a garden nursery, Tiny's huge windows allow you to view the romantic landscaping while enjoying some delightfully different entrees. The French picnic will give you something light yet exciting, while the buffalo burger will make you question why you'd ever eat anything else! The menu changes from lunch to dinner, so try both and see what you think!

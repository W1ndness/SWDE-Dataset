Many vegetarian options!
by Elle (13 reviews)
March 30, 2010 - Likes it
I have a hard time finding a good vegetarian sandwich with more than the basics: cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickle. At Cheba Hut, however, they have several wonderful vegetarian options, portobello mushroom sandwich, hummus, guacamole, etc. They have plenty of options with meat of course, which I hear are delicious as well. They are very friendly, and have some pretty delicious brownies. It's also nice that there is a bar inside.

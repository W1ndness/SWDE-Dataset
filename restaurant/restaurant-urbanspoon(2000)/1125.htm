all about ambiance
by KS (11 reviews)
May 02, 2010
we go to the gristmill 2 or 3 times per year, not because we crave anything on the menu, but because the atmosphere and location are so special.
we don't mind driving 30 minutes+ to gruene, scrambling to find a parking spot, and arriving to hear the hostess inform us that there will be an hour wait to be seated. we fully expect to grab a beer and a mug of sangria, squeeze onto a bench and enjoy the fellow gristmillers who just want to relax and have fun.
nothing beats hanging out in that garden with a group of friends, getting sauced and then when you finally get seated, you're so hungry and happy to be there that it really doesnt matter that the food is nothing special, nothing to write home about. you will, however, want to write home about what a fun and unique time you had with your friends out on the deck above the river in gruene.

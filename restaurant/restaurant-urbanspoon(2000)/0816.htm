Awesome Thai Restaurant and Bar in Bayonne
by johnoed (2 reviews)
October 22, 2008 - Really likes it
This is the best Thai food in Bayonne and Jersey City. I've been there twice in the first two weeks since they've been open and I can't wait to go back. The service is friendly and helpful, the place cozy and well decorated and the food is absolutely fantastic!!! Let me be more specific...I've gone twice for lunch. The first time I went to the restaurant I had the shrimp with tofu and my friend had the sweet and sour chicken lunch specials. The portion was perfect for lunch and it was delicious. We also ordered the scallion pancakes as an appetizer, which were great. The lunch specials come with a choice of soup and salad. I had the salad, which was again...delicious and my friend had the soup, which I had a taste of and was also delicious!!! I wish I was a food critic so I could use words other than delicious to describe the meal. How about Amazingly Delicious!!! I brought another friend to lunch there today and we both had the Pad Thai. Because what's a Thai place without an amazing Pad Thai dish...and yes again...Amazingly Delicious. In addition to the lunch specials we had the Fried Tofu as an appetizer...Awesome!!! I'm really hoping that the night life for this restaurant takes off cause they have a cool looking bar in the back with Karaoke and a happy hour from 4 to 7.

mobile review
by Stinky Chunky (7 reviews)
February 14, 2010 - Likes it
Went to Thai O'Brien after a movie for Valentine's Day. The place is nice an cozy inside. Dimly lit, candles on the table, but comfortable on the eyes. Maybe 7 to 8 tables. We had the Mai Tai and the Zombie for drinks, both were ok nothing out of this world. Also I tried the Chang beer from Thailand. Pretty good. The Pad Thai with Shrimp was ok. However the noodles were gummy. Not an expert in perfect Pad Thai. But it didn't blow me away. The Thai fried rice was ok. Seemed a bit oily. The calamari the same just ok. Nothing to make me excited about it. The fried ice cream was good. Not much more to say. Overall it was an ok meal. Service was great. I guess I expected more. Might go back and give their beef in basil and chili a try.

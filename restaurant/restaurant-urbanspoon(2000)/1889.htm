An much needed addition to Center st.
by Steve P. (2 reviews)
November 10, 2007 - Likes it
I went to Auntie B's Place this morning after hearing great things about it from a few co-workers.  They had told me about their amazing breakfasts and homemade potatoe and pasta salads.  After hearing this, I was eager to see what all the fuss was all about.  Immediatly after walking in I was seated at the one available booth near the window.  As I sat down I noticed the great ambience of sports memoirabeila, vintage comic books and the traditional diner feel.  I ordered the "Italian Job", one the the really unique specials and a side of potatoe salad.  The service was prompt and my meal was cooked exactly as I had ordered.  I ate it and could really taste the greatness of the homemade potaoe salad.  The sandwich was great, and it was on a special bread made fresh from a local bakery.  Overall, it was a really great expierence and I would definatly go back soon.

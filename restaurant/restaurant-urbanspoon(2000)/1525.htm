A nifty little place with Awesome Food!
by Matt (1 review)
November 15, 2009 - Likes it
I have been eating there for nearly 3 years. I love the atmosphere best. The walls are covered in old baseball memorabilia and vintage Gangsta photos from back in the day. The counter/Bar top is pure marble threw and threw, its always cool to the touch. The seats at the bar are 50's style diner stools with chrome trim and spinning tops. The booths have what I can best say "an interesting look" with a marble table top.
This place is operated by Mark, like all of us he had a regular 40 hour a week job for most of his life but he, like all of us, wanted to do something on his own, something fun. So he, along with his wife opened this place with big hopes and dreams.
The food is great! It is always made to order and his menu is full of different choices. You can get a classic Vienna all beef hot dog, or my favorite, the JUMBO Vienna beef dog! Then you have the chicken salad or tuna melt sandwich or even a gyro or a good ole hamburger! All very delicious! For side items you can get chips, fries, onion rings or even home made chips!
Overall I can say this, the food is good, the atmosphere is fun and lighthearted. Mark loves to chat when he has a few minutes free. Please stop by and check this place out. You will be pleased.
-Matthew

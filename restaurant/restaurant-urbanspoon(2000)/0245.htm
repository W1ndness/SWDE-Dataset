Moosehead Saloon/Bay Village, Ohio--Bar food at its best
by seagullrock (5 reviews)
October 17, 2009 - Likes it
This is a great place just to be. Decor is lodge-like, and they have a great belt-driven series of ceiling fans. Very attractive, very comfortable. I'm not a "bar person" but, if I were, this is the one I'd frequent. It's great all year long, but especially inviting on a winter evening. Service is always good. Burgers are delicious. Sandwiches are generous, but not ridiculously over-sized. Homemade potato chips with ranch dressing are very special. Salads are nice. My only complaint is that their cola is watery, so I drink lemonade or water with a twist instead. Not a big deal. It is a small establishment, and you see fewer families here than you see at the chain restaurants. I'd miss it if they packed up and disappeared.

Recommend | Not helpful

Good service, good sandwiches
by Hungry in CLE (11 reviews)
February 08, 2010 - Likes it
This is a great little place. There aren't many tables, so expect a wait on the weekends.
I ordered the French Onion Steak Sandwich, mostly because it comes with French onion soup for dipping. I just loved it. It's real steak cooked to order. A friend commented that it looks like something they'd have at Melt. But it was better than anything I've had at Melt.
I don't like chips that much, and I even loved the chips. Once in a while, you'll get one that isn't completely crispy. Those are the best ones.

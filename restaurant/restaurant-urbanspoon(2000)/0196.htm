Overrated
by Ryan (43 reviews)
July 15, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Granted that the dogs are cheap and fast, there are many other places in Chicago to get a better product. Firstly, they don't dress their hot dogs completely with all the toppings to make it a Chicago dog. Second, I've had some really bad meals there due to spoiling onions and old buns.
I will admit that the fries can be tasty IF the oil has been changed recently. But all in all, with all the other options out there, this one doesn't even crack my top 10.

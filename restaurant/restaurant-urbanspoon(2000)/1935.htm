by Menudo Terremoto Williams (3 reviews)
March 19, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I am Mexican-American from San Antonio, Texas. Where do I start? The salsa tasted/looked like Italian tomato sauce, ketchup with pickled jalapenos thrown in. The tortillas were not fresh. Disgusting. It tasted like nothing I have ever had. What part of Mexico was this type of food supposed to be from? Seriously, this restaurant is for people who have lost their sense of taste or never had real Mexican food before.

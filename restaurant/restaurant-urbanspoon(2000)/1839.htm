Fantastic!
by 2006acd (2 reviews)
July 16, 2007 - Likes it
My boyfriend and I come here very often for dinner. He always gets the veal and I always try something new, however I'm insanely partial to the linguini with white clam sauce. Delicious escargot and antipasto appitizer as well- and the service is a real authentic Italian treat. The main floor does become slightly congested so I strongly suggest asking for a seat down stairs which has a beautiful mural of the almafi coast. The owner is is always present- he's usualy hanging out the front door, and the servers have been there for years and are excellent at their skill. Desert on the other hand we have not had the blessing to try, we are obssesed with Mikes Pastries, cannolies and tirramisu- which is for the most part right next to Saraceno. All in all we love this place, it's our favorite restaurant, and not too mention walking around the North End is really nice too.

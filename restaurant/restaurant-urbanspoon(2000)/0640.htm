Don't be deceived by the 78% score. They forgot to say that the other 22% just don't like Bar-BQ. DON'T miss this Memphis gem and enjoy real GREAT Bar-BQ.

Consistent

That's what I like about Corky's. It isn't my all time favorite here in town, but by far the most consistent.
The ribs are always smoky and tender. The pulled pork is always moist. I really like their dry seasoning. My biggest disappointment is their sauce. It isn't bad mind you, but there are many I'd take over theirs.
Service? Excellent. Atmosphere? Racous fun and second only to Rendezvous. Value? You bet. Recommend? Absolutely.
I've taken many family members, friends and sent visitors over there. When a place is this consistent, you have to.
As always, I have no vested interest in this or any other restaurant.

Great BBQ!
by Dave P (1 review)
October 17, 2008 - Likes it
Went to Corkys on a Sun night, decent crowd about 5pm.  Went for the pulled pork shoulder and ribs combo.  I had my food within 10 minutes of ordering.  The pork was tender and very tasty, the barbaque sauce complimented it very well.  I got the ribs dry, their recommendation, and the first time I've had dry ribs.  Have to tell you I was VERY surprised!  The ribs had great taste and flavor, done just right, tender and easy to eat.  
Had to try the Key Lime pie, and it was great.  The only problem was it over stuffed me!  
By the time I was leaving, about 6:30, there were at least 20 people waiting outside and the drive-in line was ON-THE-ROAD by 2 cars.
Overall, very friendly and super fast service.  The food was very tasty, definatly one of the top BBQ I've had.

delicious
by nellieallen (2 reviews)
May 02, 2010
After a disappointing Juicy Lucy experience at the 5-8, my girlfriend and I made our way to Matt's Bar with high hopes. We sat down at the bar with no wait, the gentleman behind the bar was prompt and helpful, explaining that we can order the Jucy Lucy with or without onion and pickles (which we did). We watched the tail end of the Twins game while waiting around 15 to 20 minutes for the burger. One bite into the Matt's Jucy Lucy and we both knew this burger was far better than 5-8 based off of taste alone. The meat was tastier and the cheese was delicious and hot. $2.00 beers and a $5.15 Jucy Lucy beat the 5-8 less than satisfactory $5.50 Juicy Lucy, long wait and neglectful wait service.

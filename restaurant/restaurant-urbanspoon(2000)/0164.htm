Great authentic Mexican Fare. Great Value
by Chef T (30 reviews)
August 25, 2009 - Likes it
Was pleasantly surprised, Actually amazed that there was this much energy created in an old Waffle House in not the "best" part of town. Truly Authentic Food, Nice Atmosphere, Great Energy, Attentive Service, Ton's of Value, Huge portions... Really can't say anything more. Most everything on the menu is less than 12.00 with the majority resting right between 9 and 11... That is a good value anywhere, but for Charleston it is Amazing....
This is not just affordable food it is really high quality authentic fare. Great Chips and Salsa, High quality meats, Excellent variety and "Dead On, Complex and Authentic" seasoning blends. It is not just the "hot" or "cumin plus chili" seasoning choices that you find at so many local south of the border restaurants.
I'm hard to impress, but I left feeling very "satisfied" on many different levels... Highly recommend the pork pastor...the steak encebollado, or the chicken mole with a really complex ancho undertone...."Nice, Nice" Just left there an hour ago and I'm already planning my next trip....
That's a good sign!

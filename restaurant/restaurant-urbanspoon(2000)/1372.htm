User reviews of Little Anitas New Mexican Cafe

Good and Cheap
by atheling (16 reviews)
August 05, 2010 - Likes it
We were tired and hot from wandering around Old Town and decided to stop here for lunch.
We both ordered the stuffed sopapillas. I had the carne adovada and my friend ordered the chicken. The adovada was very good: spicy, tender and flavorful. My friend enjoyed the chicken. It was the first time we had stuffed sopapillas and I will definitely order them again. Beans and rice were standard, nothing to complain about and yet nothing to rave about either. But for the price, you can't go wrong, especially when you get dessert in the form of sopapillas and honey.
One thing I don't get is what is with the servers wearing blue eyeshadow up to her eyebrows? Oh well, it was a pleasant place and no complaints whatsoever.

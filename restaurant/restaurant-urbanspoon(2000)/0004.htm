A Buffet of Goodness
by Drexler McStyles (205 reviews)
September 17, 2009 - Really likes it
This is the first Indian restaurant we've been to in Albuquerque (after having just spend 6 months in India) and we werent disappointed. There was a good selection of both meat and vegetarian dishes at the buffet and the servers were on top of things. $7.99 is more than reasonable for the food we had. Parking was a bit of a nightmare at high noon, but outside that, no complaints. I would definitely like to return to try something from their Dinner menu.
UPDATE 9/15/09: 2 years after writing this review (and a few Indian restaurants later) I can say now that India Palace (along with India Kitchen) is easily the Best Indian restaurant in ABQ. Period

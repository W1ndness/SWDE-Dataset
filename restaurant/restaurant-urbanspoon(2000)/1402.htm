Bad service, good food
by Soleil (34 reviews)
May 05, 2010 - Likes it
This place is kind of a half and half.
It has good food and drinks, but HORRENDOUS service.
This is the place you'd go to listen to music and casually eat whenever your food decides to arrive.
Nobody usually comes to my table to even ASK if I'd like something, I always have to make my way to the front and order something - and wait at least an hour before it arrives. When it does arrive, it's generally cold - or on the verge of being cold, but it's still surprisingly good.
If you come during the times they have bands the music is always way too loud; I could literally scream at the top of my lungs and not even hear myself. Although sometimes that is fun, I guess.
Try this place out during OFF hours and it may be better
-
Or if you like a place that the servers don't check up on you, your food takes forever to get to you, your ears are ringing by the time you leave, or you don't want to eat at all and just take up space - then this is the place for you.
But honestly the burgers are delicious, the fries are amazing, and the drinks are wonderful. They have vegetarian options which is always nice, and they have a good menu. If they worked on service a little more, or could separate the people who want to eat from the people who want to take up space this place would probably be better off.

Stay Classy Tulsa-eigo
by c (46 reviews)
November 21, 2009 - Likes it
Cheddars has been a classic Tulsa choice for quite some time now...just about every time I've gone, the parking lot has been full. Of course they have a prime location, so that probably doesn't hurt. The food goes a long way too, by the way. I do agree with Tom, I don't think I've ever seen the same server twice, and I don't remember ever having a notable service experience here, despite countless times of coming, but I disagree in the food section. I think at Cheddars you get good bang for your buck (dishes are enormous) and normally they're quite flavorful. My favorite are the chicken tenders (though those are not tenders from any chicken I've ever seen, these are really like sliced chunks of also huge chicken breasts), normally well seasoned, never burned or dry and come with some good mashed potatoes. Normally not enough gravy though, but only a small complaint. Overall, the service could be better and there could be a hint more parking but Cheddars will give you a monster of a meal that actually tastes good too.

Some of the Best Pizza in North America
by ddrucker (5 reviews)
July 27, 2008 - Likes it
Now that I've moved away to Canada, and on the other side of North America, I miss Emma's more than just about any other restaurant in Boston. The pizza can be exquisite, and add some beer from the brewery across the street and you have a classic combo, worthy of any gourmet. The smokey rosemary sauce, the fabulous homemade bacon, the crispy crust...I think I'm going to have to book a ticket back to Cambridge for a visit to get some of this fabulous pizza again.

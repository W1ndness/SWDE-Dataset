Loved it!
by Wendy (2 reviews)
June 24, 2010 - Likes it
A friend asked me to meet him at Taste about a month ago, and I'd never heard of it. I was thrilled with my food (I got the "Wedgie" which was a black bean quesadilla and he got a huge Belgian waffle topped w/strawberries, which he let me taste!) and I also got a fruit smoothie which was awesome. It was very busy and it was a little hard to hear the names being called when the orders were ready, but overall I thought it was great and if I lived closer I'd be back there all the time, but I live about 30-35 minutes away so it will be a "once in a while" type thing.

service bad
by stonegirl (1 review)
January 31, 2010
We moved back to East Aurora around 1 year ago and go to taste fairly often for breakfast due to the fact that my 3 year old loves it there. The service has not been good since we started going there, and from what I hear around town, it never has been good Maybe it is the fact that its usually a weekend morning, but they always seem irritated that they have to take our order. If they had better service, we would be eating there all the time. I know that it is hard to find good employees but I've even heard stories of the owner not giving good service. Raise the bar on the service, and the place will be packed

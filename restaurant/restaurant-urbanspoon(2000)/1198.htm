Superb menu, superb wines
by Andrew Michael (6 reviews)
August 15, 2010
I visited Robust for the first time last night with my girlfriend. I was pleased to find that the wine menu was diverse and offered some unique features, all of which can be bought in store. All of the wine/glass prices are standard to what you'd find at other wine bars.
The food was excellent as well. Each item on the menu is essentially 'paired' with a style of wine from the previous pages. We had the charcutterie sampler which included some of the most excellent pate I've ever had, along with equally as good dried pork shoulder and prosciutto. If you decide to try Robust, you must get the dried duck flatbread. It was, by far, the highlight of the meal and was pillowy, melted in your mouth, and had the most perfect meld of salty dried duck, grapes, roasted garlic puree, cheese and arugula.
I would also recommend sticking around for dessert and if you enjoy port, try the dessert flight. The 'flight' comes with a chocolate and chocolate raspberry truffle and a glass of a Missouri port which is magnificent.
If you haven't tried it, this should be the next restaurant on your checklist.

Great for Sampling and Sharing
by erin (3 reviews)
August 12, 2008 - Likes it
I went last night with a group of 8 for dinner and it was fabulous. It was a good number of people for sharing and sampling, we got to try a large part of the menu. Our group loved the baked camebert plate and the sampler meat plate. The steak and scallops were the big favorites in our group from the tasting menu, but we liked everything we tried.
It has a great atmosphere and decor, attentive and friendly service; even the owner checked on us. The wine and food was great, I really enjoyed the experience and my meal. I would recommend it over and over again.
Its great for a quick glass of wine and a snack or a leisurely evening of conversation and tasting. It was a the perfect way to try a number of things.
We let the urbanspoon application for the iphone choose the restaurant and we were all pleasantly surprised with this restaurant. None of us had a bad thing to say about it and we can't stop talking about it. Thanks Robust and Thanks urbanspoon. I will certainly be back.

Priced Appropriately, Outstanding Wine Selection
by Wiley (2 reviews)
December 09, 2008 - Really likes it
Robust has amazing wine flights catered to all palates. Whether you're looking for something light and bubbly or stunning and racy, you'll find it at Robust. Each flight is a window that gives you a glimpse into the past, the region, and mind of the winemaker. Off the top of my head, I can't recall one place in St. Louis besides Robust that offers a flight containing a powerhouse like the italian Brunello. It will probably be a little pricey for the new world over oaked palates of the novice wine drinker but I can assure you it will make the wine enthusiast or those looking to expand their knowledge extremely pleased with their decision.

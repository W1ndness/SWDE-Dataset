by Dezracer (43 reviews)
February 20, 2009 - Likes it
Decor - Very modern and contemporary
Service - Good...seems like its a family owned and operated place. On of last visit, I got the impression that the mom ran the front of house, the dad did the cooking and daughters were the severs.
Food - Been there 3 times and each time had the lunch buffet. The food was good for a buffet...but only average for Indian food. It just seemed to lack some of strong spices and flavor of Indian food. With that said...it was still very good for a lunch buffet

Chiken in Vegetable Biryani!
by Vaibhav Bora (1 review)
February 12, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Hi,
While this might sound great to some people, I am a vegetarian and when I order a vegetarian dish in an Indian restaurant, I expect to vegetarian, and not with a dish with meat removed!
The food at Saffron is pretty average...
For those who are familiar with Indian food, Biyani is the a very fragrant and flavorful rice preparation.
The Biryani I ordered lacked any kind of taste. The raw carrots on the side were more tasty than the biryani and I found a piece of Chiken in it!

Excellent road food
by dsmgirl (6 reviews)
August 19, 2009 - Likes it
Passing through Omaha the other day I wanted to find somewhere quick to stop that wasn't a chain restaurant. I found that California Taco was featured on "Diners Drive-Ins and Dives" on the Food Network. I decided to stop and give it a try.
It's located not far off the Interstate in the Boy's Town area. It's kind of on the edge of what looks like a semi-bad neighborhood and a good neighborhood. I wasn't afraid or anything, it just looked like the area was a little run down.
The restaurant had a lot of character. It was grimy and worn-in but in a good way, in the way that you love putting on your favorite pair of jeans that are falling apart. It was comfortable.
The service was excellent, fast and friendly.
The food was great! The guacamole was very fresh tasting and the flour chips were yummy! My husband and I shared a steak, chicken, and beef taco. We liked all three, but thought the beef taco had the most flavor.
If we're ever passing through Omaha again we'll definitely stop here again.

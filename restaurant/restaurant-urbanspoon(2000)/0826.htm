Great Fresh Urban Rustic Food with Great Mixology in the Glass
by Yuka EIC (16 reviews)
December 10, 2008 - Likes it
NOPA does it right.  When you eat and drink here, your body takes is everything with ease. That's a solid proof that everything on the plate and in the glass is fresh. I have never had a bad meal here.
Food: 4.5 stars
The menu changes frequently, but the format of the menu somewhat stays the same. You can find all the crowd favorite most of the time on the menu: the little fried fish, baked beans, the best burger in the world (on brioche and with gem lettuce!) and the heavenly pork chop. Unfortunately, the rotisserie half chicken (another standard on the menu) can be a hit or miss. Occasionally  it can come out quite dry.
Beverage Program: 5
Emphasis on "new" classic cocktails: classic combination of liquor with the fresh ingredients of Bay area, the cocktails here are amazing. The wine list is quite extensive and the bottles are at a great price. Wines by the glass menu changes often and it is a great way to try what they have in under the sleeve.
Service: 5
The servers here are always very knowledgeable and professional. Surprisingly, they are very personable as well. Always always ask these professionals any questions you have on the menu. The chances are you will learn something new about good food and drink.
Atmosphere: 5
Great space. Open kitchen, beautiful bar with large community table, counter seats in front of the kitchen with the view of all the action and upstairs terrace seat with the view of the whole restaurant. Always busy and vibrant. The only complaint is that they are always busy, and if you are going with a group in a middle of the dinner time, you will have to wait.

Well....
by bkhage01 (3 reviews)
June 19, 2009
I know that everyone LOVES this place, and I admit, it's a very fun environment to spend your entire evening. However, my dining experience was less than excellent. First off, let me say that this is probably the coolest patio bar in Lexington. The beer is no more expensive than any other bar, but they don't put prices on the beer menu, which I find quite annoying. It makes you seem like a tightwad when you ask how much the beers are, but seriously - who wants to find out at the end of the night that they were paying $5.50 for Rogue Dead Guy Ale on draft, when equally premium beers are $3-4 in the bottle? That being said, the servers and bartenders were nice and attentive. It took us a long time to get a table, and we basically had to hover around a table when people looked like they were going to be leaving soon because it is cut-throat to get one. The food was expensive, and most of it was good, but the grilled chicken breast was quite over-cooked and chewy. At one point, I left the table to use the restroom, and when I came back to sit down a manager was attempting to clear our table -- with my girlfriend still sitting at it!! He grabbed my beer, which was still about 1/2 full, by the rim and started to take it away. We had to ask him to leave our food and drinks there because we were not finished, and I had to get a new beer after he put his hands all over the rim of my current glass. The band was excellent, the ambience was enjoyable, and the scenery is one-of-a-kind. But get there early if you're going to go, and beware of overzealous customers and employees...

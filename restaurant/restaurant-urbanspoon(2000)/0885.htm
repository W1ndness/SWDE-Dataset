Restaurant of the Year

This is from the VegNews Magazine "Best of 2008" issue:
Restaurant of the Year: The Veggie Grill
Somewhere in the eating-out ether, nestled
between fine dining and fast-food, lies an
intriguing, semi-new category that, until recently,
wasn't on most vegetarians' radar: fast-casual.
With the expanding Southern California
Veggie Grill empire, that's about to change.
Kevin Boylan and T.K. Pillan, the masterminds
behind the brand, possess fancy academic creds
and impressive business backgrounds, and
with the help of chef Ray White, the triumvirate
conceived a brilliant plan based on delicious,
wholesome food served in a casual, modern
environment. "The lines are out the door," says
Boylan, a self-described "numbers-oriented"
guy who, together with his crackerjack team, is
launching a third SoCal Veggie Grill this November,
and a fourth just one year from now. New locations
will open quarterly thereafter, which means lucky
Californians will have more access to delectable
menu items such as the best-selling Santa
Fe Crispy Chickin' sandwich, the Carne Asada
sandwich, and Sweetheart Fries with chipotle
dipping sauce.
Scoop! New menu items debuting
this fall include The Stack - grilled veggie-steak,
onion rings, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and Thousand
Island dressing, and the All Hail Kale salad of
marinated organic kale, red cabbage, roasted corn
salsa, and agave-roasted walnuts with a gingerpapaya
vinaigrette. You heard it here first!"

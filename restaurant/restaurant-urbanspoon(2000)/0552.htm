Knoxville's Best Pizza
by Ftn Cty Foodie (27 reviews)
February 25, 2009 - Really likes it
When I want good classic pizza the only pizzeria that comes to mind is Pizza Palace. Everything is fresh, homemade, uncomplicated and VERY good. If you are wanting gourmet varieties of pizza, this is not your place, but if you want the best classic varieties definitely make sure you come here. The Peroulas family opened Pizza Palace in 1961. It's been an East Knoxville fixture ever since. My parents used to come here on dates and I remember loving the curb service as a child. One of the original servers still works here part time...! Truly a classic...!

A Taste of Heaven
by LilPiggy (23 reviews)
August 01, 2010 - Really likes it
My parents started taking me here when I was a kid and my Mom's parents took her here when she was a kid. My favorite thing to get is the veal parmesan. I've eaten more baby cows then probably anyone else and I've had veal at many, many more expensive restaurants and theirs is the best. The pizza, huge salads, and spaghetti are always excellent. The consistently great comfort food is a welcome change to the cookie cutter chain restaurants that clutter Knoxville.

you just gotta go!
by sprice5105 (13 reviews)
February 18, 2010 - Likes it
I was skeptical at first but everyone was right! Great place. The wait staff are super nice here and the pizza is wonderful. Not the gourmet, granola cruncher type pizza you get at a place like the Tomatoe Head but real, good old fashioned pizza. Be prepared. It will make your car smell like pizza for days! And the onion rings are outstanding. I thought it was very strange that my friends told me to order onion rings with my pizza but once you have the onion rings you will understand why. They are so good, you just can't pass them up! Oh, and my husband likes that he can order beer at the drive in but he normally just brings his own in a cooler. BRING CASH because they do not takes credit cards or checks.

Message from Salvation Pizza

Pizzeria specializes in East Coast Napoletana-style pies made with all fresh ingredients. Also serve salads, wine and beer.

  • Hyde Park/Rosedale area
  • Napoletana-style pies
  • Beer & wine

Located in a cozy bungalow in West Campus, Salvation serves Neapolitan-style thin crust pizza with both traditional and gourmet ingredients. A newly expanded menu includes salad, appetizers and desserts. Come grab a seat on the covered patio from 4-6 p.m. daily for half price Live Oak beer and enjoy some of Austins best pizza.

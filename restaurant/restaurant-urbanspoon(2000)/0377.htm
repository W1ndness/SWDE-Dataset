New BBQ Spot
by Brian (4 reviews)
December 18, 2008 - Likes it
The BBQ food is great here. I've had the pulled pork and the ribs. I favored the pulled pork. The craft beers could be a lot better. This place is owned by the same people who own Grizzly Peak, so I expected a lot from the beer. I was a little disappointed, but it certainly didn't affect my opinion of the menu. The desserts are also pretty good. I had the Bourbon Milkshake (I might order an additional shot next time), but it was really good. I also really liked the atmosphere of the place. It was very trendy, but rustic.

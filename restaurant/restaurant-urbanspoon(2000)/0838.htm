Good admixture of styles
by A Tevington (707 reviews)
July 01, 2009 - Likes it
The folks at Cafe 501 do things their own way. Breakfast is very casual and heavy on pastries. Lunch is jam-packed and strives to be fast. Dinner is romantic, slow and almost formal. Dinner service is very good. Italian cuisine definitely influences the fare, but this is not and does not pretend to be an Italian restaurant. The food is a modern American take-off from Italian with plenty of Mexican tastes thrown in. Virtually everything on the menu is very good. I say "virtually" because pork dishes can be overcooked and hence dry. Everything else is great, muy bien, magnifico--whatever fits.

terrible Experience
by southernheiter (1 review)
March 22, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Recently I met a friend and his daughter and friend from out of town. By the time I arrived, the father had ordered a pizza and a pitcher of beer and I added a small mufaletta and coke to the order requesting a separate ticket. The service was bizzare to say the least and went downhill from there. The waitress asked me if I wanted a glass for the beer and I told her no, that I would not be drinking. She then went on to say that she could not sell a pitcher of beer unless 2 people were drinking! I have never heard of that and thought it odd but said, ok bring me a glass and I might decide to have some later. She then asked me if really was going to share some of the pitcher??? Is this strange or what????
She brought the pitcher, my coke, refilled water for the younger girls. We sat talking etc but time went by and we discovered that we had been waiting for 45 minutes for our order during wich time the waitress had brough refills of water, my friend had consumed one glass of beer and I had finally finished my coke,and read two local papers. Being 4 PM no other patrons were in the establisment eating though some did have a glass of beer etc and left. The waitress though attentive was often intrusive to our conversation if not hovering. Finally my friend decided that we should see what was happening to the food and offer to pay for the beer and pepsi if it would not be ready in a short time or pay for it all and ask it to be put in to go containers. The man in the kitchen (perhaps the owner?) that has been there since its opening gestured to the kitchen and said "the pizza is there-its hand made and takes time". My friend could not see if it was cooked or not so we decided to wait a few more minutes. Still the pizza did not arrive so he went in to see if he could settle the bill and leave without the food. At that moment, the waitress came with the pizza and the man came out from behind the counter and started a loud rant yelling "You have been nothing but trouble since you have been here!" The waitress then said. "I knew I shouldn't have brought that pitcher of beer as if to accuse my friend of being drunk. All this in front of his teenage daughter and her friend. Then we were told that we had to pay the bill like we hadn't tried to just a minute before.
I will NEVER go back to that restaurant-actually have only been there about 5 times in 21 years. I will never reccommend it to any guests from out of town or any one with younger children. It created a lasting horrible impression of Birmingham on the 2 girls visiting from Ohio Uninversity.

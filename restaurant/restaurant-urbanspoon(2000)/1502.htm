Message from Smokey Bones BBQ & Grill

Good food, good drinks and good times at Smokey Bones Bar & Fire Grill

  • Fire-grilled, big flavor food
  • Ice-cold beer and drinks
  • Lively bar and atmosphere

Smokey Bones Bar & Fire Grill delivers good food, good drinks and good times at lunch and dinner, late at night and when sports are on. Our grill masters do not take marinating lightly, especially when grilling steak, cooking up BBQ chicken and smoking pork and ribs to perfection. At Smokey Bones Bar & Fire Grill, we serve the fun all day and smoke pork and ribs by night. Our fun bartenders deliver ice cold beer, wine and specialty liquor drinks at the bar, and our servers bring to the table much more than just BBQ chicken, ribs and other favorites. Visit Smokey Bones Bar & Fire Grill to fire up good times with sports, music and fun. While you're there, order a beer and try a Create Your Own Combo with two favorites, like pulled pork and BBQ chicken with two sides for just $9.99. It's the place to go for an ice cold beer or drink, your favorite sports or a night out with friends.

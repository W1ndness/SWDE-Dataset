The littlest bistro that could!
by johnnjill (100 reviews)
December 10, 2008 - Likes it
I really loved La Laiterie from top to bottom. It's in a quaint little shop co-located on one side of its Farmstead cheese shop in Wayland Square.  My wife and I were impressed with the small plate offerings, the attentive staff, and the obvious enthusiasm of the patrons at the dozen or so tables. Stand-outs from our meal included a tasting plate of local charturtrie, salty & sweet sardines and perfectly prepared grilled shrimp.  I'm sure we'll go back again.

Maybe the best in RI?
by Mario (3 reviews)
February 05, 2010 - Likes it
Tonight was my third time at Farmstead. While we were waiting for a table I decided to come up with my five favorite restaurants in RI. Farmstead was in my initial list.
After tonight's meal, I think it may be the best place. The cheeseboard we ordered to start the night literally blew my mind. The two soups I've had there have been second to none. One wouldn't think about ordering a burger at a place like Farmstead, but it easily beats any burger in RI. The bread, meat and of course the cheese are so fresh and delicious that you actually think Farmstead is undercharging you/
This is a must visit. The beer selection is also very good.

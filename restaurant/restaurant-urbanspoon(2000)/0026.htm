Message from La Hacienda Mexican

The ambiance is cool and calming with large windows, aqua blue walls and booths, pastel colored chairs, hanging plants and hanging lights. Hacienda Mexican Restaurant serves authentic Mexican cuisine with chicken, beef, steak and seafood entrees. A wide selection of appetizers, soups, salads, sandwiches, burgers, fajitas, combo platters and desserts complete the menu. They have a full bar with domestic and imported liquors, wines and beers. They also offer a children's menu and take out. <BR> <BR> <BR>Accomodates Private Parties <BR> <BR>

Tacos, Appetizers 1/2 Off, Burritos, Chile Rellenos, Chimichangas, Combination Platters, Enchiladas, Flautas, Grilled Steak, House Margaritas $7.25, Jumbo Pawns, Nachos, Quesadillas

Last experience was really weird..
by Paxson (16 reviews)
June 28, 2010
I remember a few good meals here, so my last visit was definitely a surprise. The salsa had this wretched, soapy flavor, and the hot plate for the "fajitas" was little more than a shallow bowl of shimmering oil with a few burned-to-death green bell peppers floating in it. This was a while ago, so, considering my past good experiences, I'll give them the benefit of the doubt and hope they've made some staff chagnes and gotten back on track. If I go here again, I'll re-review.

Yogurt junkies will no doubt love the tastes in store for them at Yogurt Mountain. My Name Is Noel And I'm A Yogurt-Mountain-a-holic
by Noel Bagwell (20 reviews)
June 25, 2010 - Really likes it
Where to even begin... this place... this place is genius! I mean, okay, listen. I'm just gonna walk you through the experience, okay? Here we go (hold on to your hat)!
You come in, usually pressing through a crowd that is there for a very, very good reason. There's rarely any seating inside, and there are usually people standing outside or sitting on the curb.
You grab a cup (16 oz. and I think there's a larger size, too; I've never been that brave), and head for the soft serve wall. That's right: soft serve wall. It's entire wall of soft serve yogurt machines of various flavors. Fat Free yogurt flavors include Acai Energy, Banana Pudding, Birthday Cake, Bubblegum, Cheesecake, Chocolate Mint, Chocolate, Honey Dew, Coconut, Mountain Blackberry, Peach, Pistachio, Pomegranate Energy, Rootbeer Float, Swiss Chocolate, Vanilla, Vanilla Bean and Wild Strawberry). There are also Fat Free tarts: Blueberry Tart, Green Apple Tart, Original Tart and Raspberry Tart. Low fat flavors include Cookies N Cream, Egg Nog, Espresso, French Vanilla, Old Fashioned Peanut Butter, Pumpkin Pie, Spiced Apple Pie, Strawberry and Triple Chocolate. They have No Sugar Added yogurts, too; flavors include Chocolate, Vanilla and Wild Strawberry. If that's not enough, there are Mango, Pink Lemonade, Orange and Watermelon sorbets. That's the Great Wall of Soft Serve. I don't know if every single flavor is available all the time; flavors like Pumpkin Pie and Egg Nog strike me as the sort of flavors that would only be offered seasonally.
And then there are the toppings! Oh, the toppings... another whole wall unto themselves: Almonds, Animal Crackers, Butterfinger, Chocolate , Sprinkles, Cinnamon Toast Crunch, Cocoa Pebbles, Coconut Flakes, Crushed Graham Cracker, Dark Chocolate Chips, Fat Free Granola, Fruity Pebbles, Golden Grahams, Heath Bar, Honey Almond Granola, Kit Kat, Lucky Charms, Mini Carmel Cups, Milk Chocolate , Chips, Mini Gummy Bears, Mini M&M's, Mini Reese's Pieces, Mini Swedish Fish, Oreo Cookies, Peanuts, Pecans, Pretzels, Rainbow Sprinkles, Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, Sour Patch Kids, Trail Mix, Twix, Vanilla Wafers and Walnuts. And on the cold bar: Bananas, Blackberries, Blueberries, Cantaloupe, Cheesecake Bits, Chocolate Raisins, Cookie Dough Bits, Fudge Brownies, Honey Dew, Kiwi, Mango, Maraschino Cherries, Mini Cinnamon Buns, Mochi, Peaches, Pineapple, Raspberries, Snickers and Strawberries. Oh, and there are a variety of sauces: Hot Fudge, Butterscotch, Fat Free Sugar Free Chocolate, Honey, Hot Carmel, Marshmallow, Pineapple and Raspberry.
It's unbelievable... and the pricing strategy for this place is the most genius of all. You pay by weight. So, once you've created your own, custom dessert, you take it to the counter, place it on the scale and pay accordingly. I think it's, like, $0.45/oz. or something like that. I won't tell you how much my last little adventure to Yogurt Mountain cost, but let's just say that I had to promise my wife we'd only go there once a month. If your wife is pregnant, and she knows about this place, God help you.

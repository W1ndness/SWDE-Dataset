Great food worth the time
by BB (4 reviews)
September 22, 2008 - Likes it
The food at Papa Mojo's never disappoints. The cajan specialities are great including such classics as Crawfish Po-Boys, Crawfish Etouffe, Jambalaya, and Gumbo. They have a nice selection of seafood, and grilled cajan dishes as well, and to top it off, they have Abita Amber on tap,
I've take friends who lived in the Crescent City and they all love the food, saying it is very authentic.
The only downside is the service, which is very hit or miss. They don't mess up orders, but sometimes it is inexplicably slow. I can't currently recommend them if you've got a time constraint.

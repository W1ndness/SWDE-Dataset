by Roux (145 reviews)

I came in for a quick bite and had the bruschetta. The bread was crispy good but the vinaigrette dip's acidity was strong. The dining area felt cozier than usual as it was busy and the tables are very close together. In any case, the waitress was polite. She was as pleasant as the previous time I was here. Having ordered coffee, she returned a little later indicating that a fresh pot is brewing. I like being informed.
If you didn't think much of da Vinci before this, you certainly will after seeing the inspirations on the walls.

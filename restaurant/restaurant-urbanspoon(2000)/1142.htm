Excellent restaurant
by mark (10 reviews)
March 27, 2010 - Likes it
The atmosphere, food, and service were all outstanding. Although it was a busy night, we were greeted and seated very quickly. As soon as we sat down we received warm bread next to a dipping dish with olive oil and herbs. We enjoyed sitting outside a few feet away from the river. The house salad was fresh and delicious; many different flavors: 3 varieties of lettuce, fresh olives, feta cheese, diced tomatoes, and vinaigrette. The signature shrimp paesano did not disappoint. The lemon butter sauce was savory and the shrimp were huge. My wife especially enjoyed the sauce. I had the baked zitti with italian sausage. The sauce was very flavorful, the meat was tender and hearty, but the best part was the toasted mozarella cheese that blanketed the entire plate. It looked so delicious that even the guy sitting next to us commented on its visual appeal (I thought he was gonna try and steal my plate:)). We have eaten at several Riverwalk restaurants (including Boudros) but this was by far the best.

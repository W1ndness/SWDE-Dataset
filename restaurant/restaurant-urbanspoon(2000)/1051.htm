Classic American Pizzeria
by jason s (23 reviews)
July 08, 2010 - Likes it
The GOOD: Turns out a good, classic american pizza (medium crust, medium sauce, heavy on the cheese). Definitely among the top 5 pizzerias in Richmond. Their salads, pastas, sides, and desserts aren't half bad either. Carryout. Delivers to about half of the city. There is plenty of street parking in the residential neighborhoods behind the restaurant.
The BAD: Busy!!! At times unbearably so... with shoulder-to-shoulder crowds packed into the tiny lobby area. Pizza can be a little greasy for those watching their diet. Not enough tables to handle the demand. Picking up carryout takes too long (always a line).
VERDICT: A winner! They just need to move to a larger location! Hopefully the recent addition of Stuzzi will take off some of the crowd...

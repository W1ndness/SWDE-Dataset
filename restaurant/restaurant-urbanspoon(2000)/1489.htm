User reviews of Goldberg's Bagel Company...

Dependable Breakfast
by Barney (486 reviews)
July 21, 2008 - Likes it
Grabbed breakfast here this morning... just wish I could make it here more often.  Nice neighborhood feel to the place.  Had the usual, eggs and corned beef hash, a bagel and fruit.  The fruit is inevitably "blah", but everything else is always good with the hash being very good (not sure if they make it themselves, but it sure doesn't seem like something out of a can, much sharper corned beef flavor) and the bagels are always very fresh.  Coffee is always good and they never let it get down any lower than half full.  Good bunch of folks working here.
Weekends can be crazy unless you go early, but weekdays are always pretty relaxed.

Recommend | Not helpful

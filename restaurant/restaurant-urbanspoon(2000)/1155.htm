Great but small portions
by peter McConnell (1 review)
November 20, 2008 - Likes it
Cafe Chloe is one of my favorite cafes downtown. Great, maddison ave style bistro is gourmet without being stuffy. My only issue is the proportion. I get the French aversion to gluttony, but this glutton wants to be well fed without going broke. Just a little larger portion would suffice. All the same, my complaint for more is only a compliment to their awesome dishes. I also disagree with the cost stars. I'd give this three stars on price level.

Local dive
by CJ (3 reviews)
March 31, 2009 - Likes it
Love the cheap beers, chili dogs, chili mac and french fries. You are expected to get up and get your drinks yourself and get your condiments, but that is part of the charm of the place. The kids get to color their placemats and hang them on the wall. Great place to catch a game and meet up with friends.

Chili Dogs and Football
by Seth Kaplan (2 reviews)
October 14, 2008 - Likes it
It's like a college bar on the inside - crowded, crumbling floor, wooden booths falling apart, cheap beer and the most amazing chili dogs you've ever had. This is the epitome of "local dive" and will probably be around longer than the cockroaches after the nuclear winter.
So don't expect fine dining, but expect a decent blue plate special and to stand for a while during Monday night football until grandma gets up from the corner because she's finally out of chili fries. Highly recommended.

Interesting
by Lewandow (34 reviews)
June 06, 2009 - Likes it
Had breakfast there yesterday. You come in, sit yourself, get your own water, soda, coffee, whatever and eventually someone comes to give you a menu and maybe show up to take your order. Though very odd paradigm the menu was REAL cheap and food quality was OK. Would not bring out-of-town guest there but what I had was avaerage for this area for breakfast, which is not all that great. Appears a lot of the same people come in daily for the same thing day over day so I guess who am I to complain.

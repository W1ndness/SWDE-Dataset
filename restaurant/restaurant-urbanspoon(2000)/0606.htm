by Happi Devil (10 reviews)
February 19, 2009 - Likes it
Why is Yogurtland awesome? Because they do everything by weight! That means if you just want a little bit, you only need to buy a little bit... if you want a lot... well then load it on up! If the choice is between Yogurtland and Pink Berry, go Yogurtland all the way! Sure they don't have "Green Tea" yogurt... but they do have Cheesecake, not to mention about a dozen other flavors, and a nice bar of toppings that includes fresh berries, or cereal...

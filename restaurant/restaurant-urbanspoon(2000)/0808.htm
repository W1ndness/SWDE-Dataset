Bring your wallet
by Simon (137 reviews)
July 20, 2009 - Likes it
This place has class, an extensive menu (family style in most cases), and a big pricetag.
I do enjoy the finer things in life, and this place delivers on making you feel special, as long as you have the cash.
I love Italian food, and sometimes you need to splurge on someplace like this, just cause you can. The food was great, and the service we received was focused and friendly.
A great treat.

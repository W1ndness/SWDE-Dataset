Maki Fresh - so great!
by jenniferbama (2 reviews)
December 14, 2008 - Likes it
On my first visit to Maki Fresh, the bright green and soft blue colors immediately caught my attention. This place looks like a chain with a very cool mod-Japanese design, but it is a Birmingham original. The staff greated me at the door and offered me a menu. There is a self-serve kiosk by the front door so I could grab sushi and swipe my credit card if I was short on time. All rolls in the cooler are only $5! Very convenient for a young professional like myself, who is ALWAYS in a hurry! Today I dined in. The food was amazing - very fresh, clean flavors with a little Southern twist. My friend, who eats nothing raw, was completely satisfied with a sake poached chicken rice bowl. The food came out very quickly and with a smile. I realize this place is the perfect fit for me - much better quality than grocery store sushi but with quick service and low prices, unlike fine dining. I am definitely going to be a regular!

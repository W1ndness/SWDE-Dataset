Dinner is great
by Bluefield (20 reviews)
July 12, 2010 - Likes it
We love dinner at Café Muse. They just started their dinner menu about a month ago. We have dined there four times since, tried something different each time, and have never been disappointed. From apps to dessert, everything was cooked perfectly and layering of flavors was expertly done on several dishes. The chef's insistence on using local ingredients adds a freshness to the dishes, especially vegetables. The only drawback is the rather limited bar, however we were assured that they were in the process of expansion. The wine list is small but interesting and ever-changing. We will be back soon.

Inspired.
by asheblogs (21 reviews)
November 11, 2009 - Really likes it
Everything here is amazing, often with an creative twist of flavor. Definitely deserves its name.
The most fun thing about going to Muse is the fact that the daily specials list is nearly as long as the menu! There are usually several options under sweet breakfast, savory breakfast, and lunch. Doesn't get much better if you're a nut for variety like me. Just the other week there was an amazing lobster scramble. You just never know what you'll find.
If undecided, try the hanger steak sandwich or the famous three-cheese grilled cheese sandwich (with basil and honey), voted the best in the country. I'm also a huge fan of the chicken-apple-gouda sausage. The soups (served with a small piece of sweet muffiny bread on the side) are also always excellent, ranging from cold fruit soups to rich bisques.

Varied menu, delicious food
by vgonzalez2034 (526 reviews)
June 18, 2010 - Likes it
I love the beet and goat cheese salad. Nice mixed drinks here too. Prices are on par with others in the area, not bad, a tad high for some. Hubby was pleasantly surprised at the menu variation as it's everything from chick food (as he calls it) to manly food for carnivores.

Pedestrian and Touristy - but good bread and BEST Cheesecake
by Cookiedessert (132 reviews)
May 05, 2010 - Likes it
The portions are huge, the menu is ridiculously large and there is definitely a place and time for a CF hit. Nothing is really good (the bread is delicious), but nothing is really bad either. I usually get a salad and cheesecake and can never finish either.

Pretty much agree
by Krystal (49 reviews)
May 06, 2010 - Likes it
... with the other reviews. I mean, it's Cheesecake Factory-- they have that HUGE menu with a billion things to choose from. The portions are profanely humongous and always remind me of how gluttonous American eaters are! But everything is perfectly tasty and they have a fun drink menu too. I've actually had 2 bad experiences at this location, although both situations were taken care of swiftly and in a way that exceeded my expectations, so props to them for that. Despite those experiences, I wouldn't hesitate to go back. It's a fun place to celebrate birthdays, etc.

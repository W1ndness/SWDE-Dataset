Great Idea, Tasty Food
by Meme (170 reviews)
July 13, 2010 - Likes it
I had the Buffalo Quinoa Hash and it was fantastic, I will be going back for this sometime soon. I also tried their hummus and cheesecake all were good.
Note: they don't have indoor seating so the idea for this location it take and go.

Recommend | Not helpful

Friendly and convenient
by Carijane (2 reviews)
July 14, 2010 - Likes it
It is nice to have healthier options for grab and go food. The staff was friendly and offered their suggestions. I had the veggie nori rolls and although they were labeled as a snack, it was filling enough to be lunch. They were a tad on the sweet side for me, but the spice of the sriracha worked to counterbalance that. This is all personal preference, of course. I still enjoyed them.
The tabbloueh was awesome, and I am very picky when it comes to tabbouleh.

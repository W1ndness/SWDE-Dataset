Message from Amaryn's Thai Bowl & Sushi

Fast, Authentic Thai Cuisine & Sushi - 2 Convenient Intown Locations

  • Large Thai & Sushi Menu
  • Fast Service & Great Prices
  • Beer, Wine & Cocktails

With 2 great locations (Decatur and Buckhead), Amaryn's Thai Bowl & Sushi delivers high quality food in a fast, casual environment. Whether you are looking for a quick & healthy lunch spot, a place to go for fresh sushi & drinks after work or you just want to grab some yummy Thai food to go, Amaryn's has it all! We even offer delivery in a limited area through Zifty.com. We are a family owned business and are completely committed to your satisfaction. Both locations have ample patio seating to enjoy the beautiful Atlanta spring weather, a great selection of beer, wine and spirits and Special menu items every week. We invite you to come in and give us a try. Please see below for a SPECIAL OFFER for Citysearch users.

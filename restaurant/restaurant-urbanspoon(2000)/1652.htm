Message from Thundercloud Subs

  • Fresh, Fast & Healthy
  • Part of Austin for 30 years
  • Lunch Specials

Our Story

Welcome to ThunderCloud Subs, a neighborhood sub shop with a rich tradition of good natured people serving FRESH, FAST & HEALTHY food in a great atmosphere.
IT'S ALL IN THE INGREDIENTS...We slice all our meats, cheeses and produce daily. We prepare our spreads from scratch every day. We have our bread made from scratch and delivered every morning and we choose only the finest ingredients.
Our People
The ThunderCloud sandwich artists are notoriously good natured and famously talented. We take pride in a job well done. We believe in team work, doing a little bit more than our fair share and having fun both on and off the job. Though we have plenty of company policies to help keep the 'machine' running smoothly, we think we allow enough liberties to foster individualism, self-esteem and self-pride

Awesome Sandwichs...
by OffTheBeatenPath (512 reviews)
July 22, 2009 - Likes it
Thundercloud is an Austin institution. They're been around since jesus...anyway, they've been around a long time.
The other reviewer that commented about not wearing gloves, I don't worry about that. They have a system where one person handles the money, everyone else stays "clean" and handles the food. Works for me and I've never had an issue.
Mostly I get the American Classic all the way. Never disappointed with Thundercloud.

by desoto6 (16 reviews)
June 09, 2010 - Likes it
My husband and I used to order subs to be delivered to our dorm when we were students at UT. While raising our kids, we would get subs and picnic by the turtle pond on campus. Now our kids are grown, a couple have gone to UT, and we still make sure we stop at Thunderclouds when we are in Austin. They are just the best subs.

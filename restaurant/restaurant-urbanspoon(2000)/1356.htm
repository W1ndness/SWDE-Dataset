Fantastic!
by Stephen (1 review)
February 05, 2010 - Likes it
We've been going to All Out for several weeks now, after being continually let-down by several other sushi restaurants in town. All Out Sushi is fantastic! The fish is fresh, the staff friendly and helpful, and the shop is run by a local family who also own several other small businesses in ABQ. Stop by and support the locals, and find out how Sushi is supposed to taste!

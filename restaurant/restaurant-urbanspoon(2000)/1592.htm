A great local bar on Austin's South side.
by Michael Hill (1 review)
May 15, 2010 - Likes it
What a great local bar. Can get busy weekend nights which is a good thing, but afternoons and lunch rule there if you appreciate a slightly slower pace and scene.
Tinsey and Jen behind the bar always make sure your drink never goes empty and Mike...a REAL chef, puts out bar grub that is superior to most sports bars, and more important, his specials are awesome as weekly he takes you on a tour of some different international cuisine.
Who ever heard of lamb and hummus being served at a sports bar! It was great. Thai wings are an awesome change from traditional buffalo wings (which are also very good with a nice bite and a little vinegar finish) with an incredibly crisp tempura coating that would put half the Asian restaurants to shame in Austin.
Other favorite specials are the "Greek plate", Vietnamese sandwich, Peruvian chicken sandwich and their burgers are also some of the best in South Austin, and possibly beyond. They have a good variety of beers on tap and in bottle and their beer and drink prices are very good with regular specials.
The regulars (and there are many) are an eclectic, diverse group of friendly folks. Nice people, fair prices and great food. What more could you want out of of a bar, particularly a sports bar.

Don't let the name fool ya, this is a good American Diner
by iEatAtTheBar (374 reviews)
February 27, 2008 - Likes it
Went here for breakfast at 11:30am on a Saturday. AWESOME!
Do not let the name fool ya, this is a Diner style place that serves some gourmet coffee, not the other way around. Now mind you I did not have coffee, and I AM NOT saying that their coffee is gourmet, but the name is just does not give a true representation of the restaurant.
I had the Java Special, 2 eggs, 2 bacon or sausage, 2 toasts, with hash browns and a coffee or tea - $5.69
A bargain and the food came fast. Short order fast. Then at noon the place got REAL busy, but they still turned the tables.
Go here, disregard the name. It is a full kitchen place, not just a coffee shop.

Fresh Food
by lauragabs (1 review)
July 08, 2010
I went to Mezza for the first time yesterday. The decor was great and the restaurant was cool compared to the 90 degree weather outside. I ordered a falafel wrap and my cousin ordered the margarite pizza. Both dishes were great. The portions weren't huge but we both ate half and had half to take home. The bill was $16 for two dishes and a drink..really reasonable and good!

Pricey Hookah
by Michelle (4 reviews)
August 01, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I just went here for hookah and drinks, but they didn't have a liquor license and the hookah was expensive. Also, they came off stingy because they charge a ~$12 "corkage fee" and were bugging us after an hour about paying more for the hookah ("Your hour is up, you need to pay more for another hour.") It was a disappointing experience, especially since there are not too many hookah places around Buffalo.

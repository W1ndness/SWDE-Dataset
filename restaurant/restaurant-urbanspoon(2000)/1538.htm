Message from Tara Humata Mexican Grill

A Fusion of Mexican Flavors, Art, and Live Music

  • Over 153 Tequilas
  • Authentic Mexican Cuisine
  • Beautiful Seductive Atmosphere

Tara Humata brings a sence of intimate dining in a eccletic artsy atmosphere while providing authentic Mexican cuisine for a great value. Stop and taste the delicious and refreshing Margaritas, Caipirinias, and Mojitos that grace Tara Humata's bar. Remember you will find over 100 different brands of Tequila at any given visit or time. Come and enjoy the live music on the weekends and ask about our drink and lunch specials. The menu features Carne Asada, Ceviche, Fish Tacos, of course rich and creamy quacomole dip and so many more yummy delights. So much fun to be had at Tara Humata.

Stopped in for lunch with a group of folks and enjoyed a nice lunch outside on the patio.
Nothing magic here but at the same time nothing bad. I went with the Taco Al Carbon. 3 steak tacos with various salsa's on the side and a little bowl of beans. Not bad. One comment I DO have is that if you take the time to ask me how you like the steak in my steak tacos cooked (I asked for medium), then it makes you look less than efficient when you deliver tacos with well-well-done steak. Not a big thing for tacos, but simply not impressive.
All in all, seems like a fairly good place to drink Margaritas and eat cheap TexMex if you live or work close by. Not sure it's a place I'd drive too far for.

mobile review
by Animal (11 reviews)
December 27, 2009 - Likes it
This is one of my favorite places on the north side. The menu has the "typical" Mex stuff that gringos like to order, and if that's what you ask for, well, that's what you are going to get. Nothing over-the-top about it, and reviewers who order the usual fare will not be overly impressed. There are some really outstanding choices on the menu, though, and that's what makes Tara Humata special. The Camarones El Delirio are wonderful and a unique treat. The guacamole is some of the best you will find, chunky, fresh and perfect! The shrimp or lobster enchiladas are also to die for. Margaritas are expensive but very good also. If you want the same old tacos and burritos go to Taco Bell, but for something really special, AND reasonably priced, this place rocks!

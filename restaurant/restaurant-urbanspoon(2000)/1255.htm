Loud, Rock N Roll, and Eye Candy
by Fred G. (7 reviews)
March 08, 2010 - Doesn't like it
A friend dragged me there for a Kobe burger which is some sort of Japanese process whereby they make the cow into a big fat pig. The extra fatty beef is supposed to make it delicious. I think the principle of more marbling best applies to steaks. There's no difference, in my opinion between Kobe ground beef and that low-grade 20% fat beef from the store.
They have a few wines, but the atmosphere doesn't make it worth it. I spent 30 bucks eating a really fatty burger and two glasses of wine while wishing I had ear plugs (its not just the loud music, its the acoustics in there). They figure we will pay that because the place is crawling with pretty young waitresses in mini skirts. Next time I'll swing by the wine store, then pick up some beef at the store, take it home and grill me up a burger. I'll sip it with the bottle of wine that cost less than two glasses at Zinburger and if I want the eye candy, I'll subscribe to the Playboy channel or something.

Tasty Tucson Gourmet Burger!
by Priti K (2 reviews)
January 25, 2010
Tastiest Gourmet Burgers! Every time I go to Zin, my burger is cooked to perfection. I recommend the Plain & Simple burger or the Kobe Burger. Each burger delight comes with it's own gourmet toppings but you can add custom toppings to fit your tastes. They also have an excellent chicken sandwich and turkey burger. There is something for everyone here - even a delicious veggie burger that melts in your mouth! The food is never greasy but very juicy and satisfying. Appetizers like the sweet potato fries and zucchini fries are excellent. The milk shakes are heavenly--for chocolate lovers the Bars of Zin shake is my favorite - There is a Date & Honey milkshake which I noticed recently was taken of the menu; however, we asked for it and they were able to make it for us right away! The service is fast and friendly. The atmosphere is upbeat - a great place to go with friends for happy hour and great for families. Good wine and beer and mixed drink selections, outdoor seating is available. Great prices, trendy and the best burger joint in Tucson!

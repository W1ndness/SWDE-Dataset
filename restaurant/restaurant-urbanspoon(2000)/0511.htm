Prix-Fixe Marvel
by Frederick (217 reviews)
December 17, 2009 - Really likes it
We had the ever-changing prix-fixe dinner here last night, 3 courses for $27, although the final bill was a bit higher because we ordered a fantastic Pinot Noir that the server had recommended. I was surprised at the generous portion sizes on the prix-fixe -- my starter was an Oyster Vol au Vent which was just right for an appetizer, and my main course was a flawless flank steak. I chose the green apple crisp with hazelnut streusel and ice cream for my dessert, and it was probably one of the best I've ever had. This is definitely sophisticated food for a city like Jacksonville, on par with what you'd find in Miami or Atlanta. Our server was knowledgeable and friendly, and patient with all our questions.

Nice place when it's not hot outside...
by Christopher W. Wickersham (23 reviews)
October 19, 2008 - Likes it
Pastiche is a great all-around place. The service is good, the wine list excellent, and the food is creative and well executed.
You're just kind of limited from going there in the summer, because it gets so absurdly hot under the awning on that patio, and because of the way the place is set up the patio accounts for 90% of their seating. So if you can get inside seating during the summer.
Outside is great the rest of the year though. I definitely recommend this place highly. The sweet potato fries are awesome.

Delightful Intimate New Years Eve Dinner
by Darcee (8 reviews)
January 02, 2009 - Really likes it
We have always gone for lunch (usually on "Saling" days=Estate Sales). Pastiche always offers a comment card of your experience/impression & you can indicate birthday/anniversary dates with your address & will be mailed a discount card for $10 off a $40 tab during the month of the event. I'd never used them as I could never imagine spending $40 at lunch for 2 or 3 -the usual # in our group.  
We received a card for our December anniversary, which we didn't think we'd get to use. I thought of it New Years Eve day at work around 1-2PM.  I phoned to inquire about any openings at such a late notice & wanted later rather than early to allow spouse to get home & unwind.  Was able to confirm 2 for dinner at 8:30. We arrived about 15 min early and were invited to sit at the bar and enjoy a complimentary glass of champagne while waiting for our table.  
We were seated promptly, service was not hurried, rather a casual pace, which we loved, not feeling rushed to be IN & OUT. We ordered a bottle of the same champagne (You can BYOB for a corkage fee of $15 for up to  3 bottles) we'd been served at the bar and shared a hot appetizer of "Donnas World Famous Sweet Pea Falafels (4) with Mint, curried crab and green apple salad", which was wonderful, different and almost too much for the two of us, anticipating the rest of the meal. We managed to finish them-they would be great for 3-4 to share.
We decided on the braised lamb shank and the Mixed Grill-a tasting of 3 individual  Meat and Fish Preparations, which that night was Tandoori salmon, beef tenderloin Bordelaise and seared sea scallop. We were delighted with all and not disappointed with any of the preparations or sides. We made quick work of the mix grill & opted to take home the remainder of the lamb shank as we felt it would "travel" better.  We didn't have room for dessert but were brought a plate of  wonderfully rich homeade chocolate truffles!
In the past, I have let some of my birthday/anniversary discount coupons to expire. Our New Years Eve Dinner after the discount, came to $83.+ $20 tip. We thoroughly enjoyed it & will try very hard not to let them expire again.  BTW when I called, he indicated they don't hold hard & fast on the expiration dates on the discount coupon/cards.
Most of the seating is outside within a canvas awning covered walled garden. During the summer days, they have numerous fans that abate the heat. In the winter there are outdoor space heaters, and they drop the canvas sides on the awning. I can't imagine it being uncomfortable except in the most extreme heat or cold.
I LOVE supporting local restaurants!! These folks aim to please!!
PS - One more thing: not offered on New Year's Eve, but Weds & Thurs they usually offer a Prix Fixe dinner that looks like a GREAT value!!
Check them out: http://www.mypastiche.com/index.html

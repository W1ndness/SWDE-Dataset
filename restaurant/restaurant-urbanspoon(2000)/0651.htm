Mama Mia's is a small place in a small strip mall in Cordova. You can guess that the atmosphere is lacking and the ovens make it warm in there, too. We tried the breadsticks and the Meaty Mama pizza. The breadsticks cannot compare to those from our home state of Indiana where they are garlicky and come with nacho cheese. I can't understand why that has not caught on in TN? I guess not many places here even have breadsticks. The Meaty Mama was good, but my visit to Russo's last week made a tough comparison. Overall, good, but not great.

Fantastic all around
by Meggle (2 reviews)
April 16, 2010 - Likes it
You absolutely can not go wrong at Mama Mia's. Their pizza is some of the best I've ever had. The crust is almost perfect - not too thick, not too thin, has a nice crunch on the outside but it soft on the inside, and is seasoned appropriately. The toppings are FRESH, and you can tell. The specialty pizzas are very well done; the Big Mama is a personal favorite of mine, the Cheesy Mama has a wonderful flavor, and the Flamin' Rooster is another winner.
Don't skip the starters, though. The asiago pesto breadsticks are TO DIE FOR. You absolutely must try them.
The staff is friendly, professional, and helpful. The owner is very customer and community-oriented, and knows how to run a business.
This is definitely one local business that deserves plenty of local support.

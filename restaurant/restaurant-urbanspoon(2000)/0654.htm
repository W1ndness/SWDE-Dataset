Very Good All Around
by BurgerEater (4 reviews)
January 05, 2010 - Likes it
I love the fries here. Fresh cut and very tasty. These are boardwalk style fries, so they are thicker and tend to be a little more oily than, say, thin-cut or shoestring fries. They are not to everyone's liking, but these are GREAT and more GREAT.
Burgers go without saying are excellent, I enjoy the high quality ingredients. I don't mind paying over $8 for great food that fills me up. I really recommend this place.

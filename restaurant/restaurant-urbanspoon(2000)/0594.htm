One of the best...
by Michael Alsip (18 reviews)
July 24, 2010 - Likes it
Asuka is easily one of the best Japanese restaurants in the area. I was pleased on my first visit to find that Rudy, the former head chef at Hananoki, had found his way to Asuka. After the meal, I was very impressed. The big thing that I really enjoyed was the fact that I was able to watch a UK game while sitting there and eating. The addition of TVs to my favorite type of food atmosphere only adds to the great package that Asuka has to offer.

Wonderful!!!!!! A slice of heaven in a big bag of fun!
by Jordan Young (7 reviews)
March 23, 2010
My experience with this place may be different than most, but a friend of mine was having his birthday at Asuka and his parents rented the place out so we were the only ones in the restaurant. The waiter was absolutely amazing his name was Ringo. The food was amazingly impressive, and the sushi was the best I've ever had (I have had a LOT of sushi) Also the staff walked around with squeze bottles of Saki spraying it into everyones mouths. When you see Hibachi grills in movies, and the wonderful experiences people have that's what Asuka gives you. I will add that it isn't the cheapest place to go, but if you're experience ends up being as fantastic as mine was it is well worth the money!

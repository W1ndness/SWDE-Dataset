The food is consistently good - I'd say this is the best seafood in Lexington by a fair leap. However, I have to recommend two things:
1. Bang Bang Shrimp - by far one of my favorite appetizers in the city.
2. A Martini - Try Ketel One, up and dirty with three blue-cheese stuffed olives. FANTASTIC.

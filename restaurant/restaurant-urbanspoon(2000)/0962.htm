+ 2 more photos from Stacy Lynn Always Scrumptious!
by Stacy Lynn (61 reviews)
February 16, 2009 - Really likes it
Nello's is one of my favorites!  Whenever we can't decide what we want to eat, we can always go to Nello's and know it's going to be good!  My favorites are the Eggplant Bruschetta appetizer, the Old World Classic salad (always with HOUSE DRESSING), and the Classic European pizza on thin crust.  Thin crust is only available in 12", but it is crispy, crackery thin, just like I love it.  Last time, we went with some friends, so we decided to try something new.  We got the Mozzarella Platter for an appetizer, the Antipasto Salad and a thin crust pizza.  Everything was just as delicious as always.  The one thing that didn't seem to fit was the carrots and celery on the appetizer, other than that, perfection as usual!  Oh, and how can I forget to mention the Four Peaks Hop Knot on tap!!!?

good food - bad managerial decision
by Scott (1 review)
June 01, 2010
Yes the food is great - in fact i've eaten there on average once every week or 2 weeks for 12 years. But tonight's dumb decision trumps the food, at least for now. Tonight, is Tuesday, and their hours on their takeout menu, website and on their door is posted as 10pm. I called to order a pizza at 9:09pm (not that i need to justify it, but I was busy and couldn't order before then and was really looking forward to the food). The person on the phone says 'oh, we were slow so we decided to close at 9'. When i said i thought that was kind of lame since their menu says 10, she says 'oh sorry, but it wasn't my decision'.
If you, Nello's managers, want to close at 9pm, then fine. change your hours. But if you expect regular customers to be regulars, then don't make inane decisions like this. Being a regular is based on expectation of regularity.
How much money did you save closing 1 hour early? How much money is 3 pizzas a month for 12 years worth?

mobile review
by Allen Henderson (18 reviews)
October 30, 2009 - Really likes it
Here's an example of customer service at Nello's Ahwatukee. I ordered several pizzas for take out. The young lady at the counter will open the box to show you your pizza. When she opened one of my pizza boxes she commented that the pizza looked over cooked to her. She asked if I could wait 10 minutes because she was going to have another pizza made up and baked to their standards. I agreed to wait. I got a replacement for the slightly burned pizza and the other pizzas were placed in the oven to be warmed up. My salads had been placed back in the fridge to keep cold. The young lady not only replaced the less than perfect pizza, warmed up the other pizzas, kept my salads cold but also gave me the less than perfect pizza as well. I was impressed to say the least. This is the attention to detail and customer service that will keep me coming back to this Nellos location. (10/30/09 - 0700pm)

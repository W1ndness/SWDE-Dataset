A comfortable evening
by jjwms (1 review)
January 10, 2009 - Likes it
Friday after Christmas, we took our grown sons to Juniper61. Unlike Cafe Lulu's, their other restaurant, the bar is nicely set apart from the dining area. We enjoyed the ambiance, warm and mellow. We had wine, appetizers of ribs which were tasty and the Meditteranean plate that was just enough to share. Entres were a comfort, lamb shredded over noodles with some veggies, (thing osso buco flavor), the pork loin looked good but was on someone else's plate! We finished off with apple pie, nearly homemade, a la mode and coffee. We got there early at 5:30 and the place was not empty. By the time we left, standing room only at the bar. Plan ahead for a comfortable evening. Dinner for 4 adults, under $100. Thanks!

mobile review
by Jon S (45 reviews)
August 08, 2010 - Doesn't like it
We looked forward to eating here; it is a really cool and comfortable place for cocktails and dinner. Upon review of the menu we were both a bit puzzled by what they were trying to accomplish with the odd flavor combinations and numerous amount of ingredients. IMHO they are trying too hard to be a cutting edge gastropub. After flipping the menu over several times we settled. Looking around it seemed others were a bit overwhelmed with the menu - although the dining room was full. Once we received our food it was bland. They maybe should work on mastering the basics before trying to cook such with complex flavor pairings and combos. We will be back to the bar, maybe to give the dining room a 2nd shot...

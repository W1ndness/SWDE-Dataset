Not my thing, but has a following
by fluorophore (15 reviews)
January 20, 2009 - Doesn't like it
This place aptly has "dough" in its name, because that's a whole lot of what you'll get.  The crust is thick and doughy making it more of an endeavor to eat than a traditional NY style or thin crust.  I'll be honest:  the thick crust really isn't my favorite style in a pizza, but some people truly love it... and I can respect that.  The rest of the pizza seems to be unremarkable to my taste.  Still, for Ames it might be one of the better pizza options if you're a fan of crust that thinks it's an emerging loaf of bread.

Not my thing...
by Paul Cowley (10 reviews)
February 07, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I've often heard friends rave about the pizza here, and was excited to try it for the first time myself. I'll admit I'm a thin-crust pizza kind-of-guy, but I also don't mind a pizza with a thicker crust. This crust, unfortunately, was a massively thick chunk of undercooked dough with less-than-average toppings and cheese. They did offer packets of butter or something like it to top the edge of the crust when you were done with the toppings, so if you're looking for a loaf of bread to eat with your pizza, this is your place.
Again, not my thing... I won't be back.

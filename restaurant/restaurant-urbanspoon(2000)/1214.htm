Best Pizza- staff needs to be nicer
by Suburban Girl (3 reviews)
March 13, 2010 - Likes it
Love Pizza? Then make the trip because it is THAT good. Worth waiting in the line that is nearly always out the door. BUT be ready for poor service. I have 4 kids and my husband and I can't understand why each time we go the staff seems unable to get correct pitcher of soda or cups or plates... it's never just one problem! I still go but not that often because there are lots of great pizza places willing to pay attention to customers since they want them to come back. This place seems to think since their pizza is delicious they don't need to attend closely to service issues. Too bad.

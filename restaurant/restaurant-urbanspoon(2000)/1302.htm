Sandwiches
by Kitchen Geeking (133 reviews)
January 31, 2010 - Really likes it
All the reviews about the pizza and the market are great, but I think the true wonder of the Italian store is the sandwich menu. Cappicola, Mortadella, Prosciutto, salami of all types with provolone, mozzarella, and a host of other cheeses STUFFED onto tender rolls with pepperoncini, oil/vinegar, olives, lettuce and other trimmings. I would drive an hour (which is what it would take) on a random Tuesday just to get a sandwich here.

Italian Convenience
by goeschei (75 reviews)
March 26, 2009 - Likes it
In a million years I wouldn't normally buy a slice or pizza or a sub at a specialty store. Sure, sure...homemade raviolis and pasta sauces, tortellinis, canalones, tiramisu, and panne cotte...all delicious. But to walk in and order a sub, I just wouldn't ever do it. That is until I stumbled upon the Italian Store. I would go out of my way to get a sub from the Italian Store. I would wait for the Italian Store to open in the morning and be late for work to get a sub from the Italian Store!
Fresh ingredients, home baked bread...can't be beat. Highly Recommended.

Excellent Pizza and Subs
by ScottS (21 reviews)
April 18, 2009 - Likes it
Like some of the other reviews here, I have made the 45 -50 trip from my home to order a sub or pick up a pizza from the Italian Store and I've never been disappointed.
I really love that they make subs correctly. By pulling some of the bread out of the roll and folding the meat over the veggies the sub stays together. Heaven!
The ambience inside the store is one of organized chaos. I usually expect to wait about 15 minutes for my sub, so I just look around at their wine selections, pick up some cookies and look at their handmade pastas in the cold case.
Highly recommended.

The Italian Store, nestled in a strip mall on Lee Highway, hides a traditional Italian food store.

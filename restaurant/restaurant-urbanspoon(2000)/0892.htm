I'm a Bonehead and I like it - ah ha, ah ha, ...
by Chuck Davis (1 review)
February 10, 2009 - Likes it
I took my family to Disneyland last month, got off the freeway at lake forest to refuel my car and family of 4.
As Parents we are always telling our daughters to make a "Healthy Choice" so when we pulled into "The Orchard at Saddleback" we were confronted with choices.  We were tired of all the same old stuff and checked out the BoneHeads menu - Grilled Fish, Vegies and Chicken vs the others, my daughters made a healthy choice to try Boneheads.  I remembered reading the restaurant was Top Pick in 2008 by the OC Register.
Honestly, I went with the flow to support the healthy choice but did not expect to receive FANTASTIC tasting food and a reasonable rate.  The ordering was easy for a first timer, the food was served quickly, and it tasted awesome.  Fish or Chicken - get the Vegies - Zuccini grilled just like I do at home - only not burned.  And SAMPLE ALL the sauces.  Piri-Piri sauce was my personal favorite.   It was the best part of dining tasting them all and having a discussion about what was in them that was healthy.
I believe in a higher power than myself and in this case we were guided by someone to do something different.  Now we are calling each other Boneheads for making a healthy choice.  Not sure that's a good thing or not, but it sure is fun.  It was a great start to a Disney weekend.
I wish there were more locations but I'm sure the owners are working on this one.

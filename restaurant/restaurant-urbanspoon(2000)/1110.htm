Bad waffles...for a hefty price!
by Robin (1 review)
January 24, 2010 - Doesn't like it
If you don't read any more than this first sentence - my advice: DON"T EAT HERE. I'm a foodie. I come from California. I've been fortunate to expect and receive great food in the Bay Area. I was in Park City for the Sundance film festival and on my way to the airport wanted to get a great breakfast. We found this place on Urban Spoon and the website description looked great. Sadly, it wasn't. FIrst of all, shouldn't a restaurant with the word "waffle" in it's name make great waffles?? Here's the thing - they don't!! Their waffle was soggy, under cooked, not crispy at all and had zero flavor. Forget the bitter canned peaches and lack of syrup that topped it. My friend got the "Happy Hour" waffle and it was floating in a sea of buttery and too sweet syrup and topped with iime sugar. It's supposed to be like a margarita. It was grotesque. I got the "Peach Melba" which is supposed to be the best seller. How could people like the canned peaches, soggy waffle and squirts of cream chese? Maybe this is the standard for Utah? I don't know. What I do know is I walked out of there spending $14.55 on a soggy waffle, bacon and coffee. A word about the service - super slow. If you are in a hurry, this is not the place to go. For that matter - skip this place altogether and go to iHop accross the street.

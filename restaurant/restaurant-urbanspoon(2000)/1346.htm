Good Food - Good Service
by Chris Iller (48 reviews)
April 03, 2010 - Likes it
I've dined at this location about 10 times now and never had the service or problems that were mentioned in the other review. The pizza has been great. I usually get the Pizza Margherita (#1) and my wife gets the Bianca Pizza (#13). I haven't tried one of their sandwiches or Calzones yet.
The atmosphere is one that might make you think this is a snooty upscale place. But it's not. The prices are very reasonable too. When you can take a party of 6 (4 adults and 2 kids) and pay a bill of $27 and everybody walks away will a full tummy and a smile on their face...I would have to say, try this place out.
It's family friendly too. When we asked if they had the traditional crayons and coloring page for the kids, they said "no" but they would bring a piece of pizza dough out that they could play with (like play dough, but actual dough). My daughter loved it.
Usually the pizza is out within 10 minutes. Drink refills are supplied without asking.
I would have to say that's good service.

Where's the management?
by Spunky (42 reviews)
December 07, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I liked the decent quality associated with their Nob Hill location, so we decided to check out the new NW location near my house.
Yikes, where do I start?
When we sat down at the table, a server came by and literally threw our silverware at us! Why? There was only two of us at a small table. Good thing I caught it before it flew off the table. And the server could have cared less! Then a totally different server brought us our nasty beers and iced teas. We tried the root beer too. It was warm and flat, just like the regular beer. How do they serve this crap to people?
We ordered pizza and a panini sandwich. The pizza was okay, but the panini was nothing but an untoasted, soggy, microwaved, olive-oil drenched focaccia sandwich. We took one bite of the sandwich and left it on our plate. The other customers look confused that we didn't eat the sandwich, if only they knew why! I didn't even bother bringing it home to my dog, it was that bad.
We ended up having to stop somewhere else because we were still hungry!
I don't think we'll be back. I was really embarassed to bring my mother here, she was craving a good panini sandwich. The management really needs to train their employees on providing better customer service and better food.

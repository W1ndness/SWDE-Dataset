mobile review
by Maxine Tomaschoff (2 reviews)
February 01, 2010 - Likes it
I've been going here for over 34 years, and it's my favorite place to go if I'm hungry, bored, lonely, or whatever. FABULOUS food(breakfast burrito supreme, anyone!). Lots of entertainment, from the diverse crowd to the amazing waitstaff. Sometimes I go there just to watch the cooks when it's super busy. Kind of like an urban ballet. And the teeny tiny young waitresses balancing all those plates in that nutty crowd? Cirque du Pete's! An example of how great their waitstaff is- a couple of years ago I had a motorcycle accident right outside the restaurant, after a big ol breakfast. My waitress, who I had never met before that morning, took me to the hospital. Talk about above and beyond..and don't forget- FAB FOOD, BIG PORTIONS !!!

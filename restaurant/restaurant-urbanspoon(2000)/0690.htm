Lucia's The Legend
by VegetarianPrincess (174 reviews)
June 11, 2010 - Really likes it
"Always my 1st Fling of Spring"
I've been going to Lucia's since they opened (20 years?) , it's the 1st place I think of when spring comes.A Very Euro Style experience in Dining.Sit outside, enjoy the Colorful Array of People from Every Walk & Influence that make Uptown a Town onto itself & like no other. Sit in the Sunshine, sip great Vino, nibble on Salad, & have the most memorable soups in MN.I still can taste an EXCEPTIONAL Watercress Soup from 10 years ago, that, of course, they never had again!The menu is always a Unique Surprise,Changes weekly, Fresh Local & Imaginative Ingredients not found elsewhere. Never disappoints, & I fall in Love with it again & again.
No where else do the Dogs, Walking their People, look so much like their owners.As though they went to the Pet Store & specifically requested a Pup with their own Look & Mannerisms--it is a Hoot!!! Couldn't be any better People Watching, anywhere.
One hot Summer Eve there was a Hit & Run Accident right in front of all us Patio Patrons.Everyone had Celly in hand, reporting it, doing a little Civic Duty to Feel Good about with their Feel Good Meal.Unexpected entertainment with our meal.
Lucia's is Nationally Renowned, Winner of James Beard Awards, & is a well kept secret of every Foodie I know.I have heard Rumors that Lucia has been made "Dame Lucia" by the Queen for her contributions in Organic Agriculture.So what is the Scoop on that?
All I know, this here, Self Appointed Princess, has always thought of our Lucia as "Quite a Dame",but more like the Fairy God Mother of Great Simple Local Cuisine.
Lucia's is what Uptown is all about, great food, great people, an experience that proves MPLS., Mn is a Proud, & Cultural place to Live, Eat, & Rejoice.
I have only one complaint: I bought your Heartland Cookbook a few years back, Luc, & none of my favorite Soups or other Recipes were in there. PSYCH. "Can We Talk ?"
Abundant Tiara's to you Lucia,You Legend of Great Eats, Treats, & Vino, you put us on the International Map, GIRL.
There are not enough Tiara's to do "Dame Lucia" Justice, Infininty & Beyond

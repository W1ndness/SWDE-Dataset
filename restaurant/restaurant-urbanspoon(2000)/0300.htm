mobile review
by KY Connection (186 reviews)
May 13, 2010 - Likes it
Stopped by here for lunch, good BBQ. Tried to sample several items. They had a rib special today, 4-6 ribs, two side or fries and drink for $7.95. I opted for 1/4lb of brisket, 1/4lb of sausage and shared some turkey. The sausage was really good, great flavor. The turkey was also very flavorfull, nice smoke really moist. Brisket was OK, I had a few pieces that were hard and very dry. Ribs were good, lacked a good rub flavor for me and needed a little more smoke. Lots of meat and they pulled away from the bone nicely, a definate plus. Sides were OK, similar to most other BBQ places.

Solid ribs
by Michael (90 reviews)
October 16, 2009 - Likes it
I've eaten at Railhead once and had the rib dinner. Their ribs aren't the best I've ever had (that honor goes to Smitty's in Lockhart), but they are easily the best I have had in Fort Worth. Extremely tender, meaty, and smoky. They really do fall off the bone, which you have to exercise caution with as they can create a mess!
Still, good food. I think the rib dinner was about $11 and it was worth every bit. Oh, and the best part; $2.50 beer schooners all day every day! I'll definitely be coming back in the future.

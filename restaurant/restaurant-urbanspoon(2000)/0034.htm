Great Option in Midtown
by Marcus Biastock (22 reviews)
May 13, 2009 - Likes it
What more could you want for lunch? Reasonable prices, quality food from fresh ingredients, quick service, and a comfortable and clean area to eat it in. These guys know what they're doing and they do it well. Simple fast quality.
Bonus: Great delivery service and dependable office catering.
Also.... free wi-fi while you eat. Good for students and a "business lunch".

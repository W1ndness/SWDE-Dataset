The wings ain't the only thing hot in here
by Psych Eater (33 reviews)
September 06, 2008 - Likes it
The Wings are thick, juicy, and flavorful as the hundreds of people ordering wings will tell you. But what really sets this wing joint above the rest is the owner's spicy disposition that is sure to entertain while you wait for your wings. So if you have time, pop in and order some wings, and enjoy the local hospitality and spicy company while you wait.

What a waste
by rhianelmo (2 reviews)
July 27, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Ok, first off when I went (yesterday) there wasn't a friendly owner to greet me as stated in previous reviews. There was a not so friendly lady who couldn't speak much english. Not sure if she was not so nice b/c she couldn't understand me or she just had a not so sunny disposition. Secondly, there was only one other person in there - not hundreds.
The wings were ok - nothing spectacular. The sauce was - well, it was more like sweet and sour sauce with a tiny hint of somthing spicy in it. I did notice there was a sweet ans spicy flavor but that is not what I ordered - maybe they got my order wrong. Plus, they covered the wings with so much sauce I had to wipe some of it off. I was just disappointed after reading the spectacular reviews it had gotten. I should have gone into Publix and gotten their wings - PUBLIX HAS AWESOME WINGS!

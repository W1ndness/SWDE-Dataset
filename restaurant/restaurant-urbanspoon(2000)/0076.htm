Possibly! Finding La Tolteca was a wonderful thing for me as I would visit usually on the weekend only after arriving back in the Baltimore area after travel out of the country each week. I have visited many authentic Mexican restaurants in North America and La Tolteca is easily my favorite. How fortunate am I to live close enough to visit fairly regularly? Mostly dine alone here due to being single and while I normally don't like to dine alone in favorite eating places, this place has the right feel and sitting down solo is not a problem.

Great brew pub in Tempe
by arizona-beer.com (2 reviews)
May 01, 2009 - Likes it
Four Peaks one of the few independent brew pubs left in the valley. They're located just outside of the main Tempe university district in an old and only slightly renovated warehouse. There is ample seating inside & on the patio as well as at the large bar. Although sitting at the bar has more of a sports bar feel than the comfortable "Cheer's" feeling. The bartenders are typically too busy trying to keep up pouring beer to engage in conversation and a glass can go dry all too easily. But, the atmosphere is fun, open & relaxed. While the place is almost always busy, and particularly crowded on weekend nights (starting Thursdays), it never becomes unpleasantly loud. The food is great by bar standards and there are always very appetizing lunch and dinner specials as well as special holiday menus (very pleasant Valentine's Day & Father's Day meals for example).
Four Peaks is not only a Tempe favorite but certainly worth a drive across town for true brew pub fans.
…now in eager anticipation of going back today for a glass of the Hopsquatch barley wine which is only offered the 1st of every month.

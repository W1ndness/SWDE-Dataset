in all, it looked like a plate of yellowish brown slop. additionally, the pre-meal salsa is a can of tomatoe sauce with some tomato chunks. dont fall for coyote cafe just because its a popular local spot!

Ummm - Mexican??????
by Melissa (5 reviews)
September 03, 2009 - Doesn't like it
If you call Mexican food a giant plate of greased potatoes dredged in Lowry's seasoned salt and loaded with "salsa" (unseasoned canned tomatoes with a few pepper flakes), cheese and really bad chorizo, well, ole'! This place actually made me angry it was so bad. The only remedy was for me and my family to make fun of it for days. If you want something that even slightly represents good Mexican (although we don't have anything truly Mexican in these parts), try LaGalera in Niagara Falls.

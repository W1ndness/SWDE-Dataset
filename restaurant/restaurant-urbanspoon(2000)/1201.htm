Excellent Mexican in a Fun Environment
by Underground Wires (8 reviews)
April 11, 2009 - Likes it
I love everything about Chimichanga, my favorite Mexican place in St. Louis so far.
A large selection of the usual American Mexican restaurant fare, but it has always been fast, fresh and great-tasting. My favorite is the eponymous chimichanga, delicious and filling. Oh, and cheap, an especially important distinction these days. The complimentary chips are warm and fresh, and the salsa tastes great.
Perhaps my favorite thing about Chimichanga, however, is the atmosphere. The palette is bright- festive defines this place perfectly. But at the same time, it doesn't go overboard. The staff have always been friendly as well.
And though I've personally yet to indulge, as I'm not a big drinker during meal time, I have it under good authority that the margaritas are as cheap and delicious as the food.
Definitely among my favorite places, especially when it comes to Mexican.

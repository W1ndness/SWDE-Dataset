House Special
by Sherrel Harris (72 reviews)
June 19, 2010 - Likes it
My family and I decided to have pizza last night, and decided on Mellow Mushroom because we had not been there in a while. We started with the Hummus appetizer. The hummus was surprisingly flavorful. If you're a hummus fan, this one is definitely worth a shot.
Next we had the House Special, which is a pizza topped with pepperoni, sausage, ground beef, onions, green peppers, mushrooms, black olives, tomatoes, ham, bacon, and extra cheese. The pizza was above-average, but nothing really made it sing, either. For this meal, I give the following ratings:
Food: 2.75
Service: 2.25
Atmosphere: 2.5
PS: It is also worth noting that they have several good beers available, including Bell's two-hearted and Oberon.
****************
Explanation of rating scale:
1: Does Not Meet Expectations
2: Meets Expectations
3: Exceeds Expectations
4: Far Exceeds Expectations
So, on this scale, a 2 is not a bad score. It's a solid, middle-range score. 4s will be very rare. (And hopefully so will 1s).

Great pizza with a really great price
by Jake (8 reviews)
June 01, 2010 - Likes it
Mellow Mushroom offers gourmet pizzas that pack flavor, PLENTY of variety, and offer one of the best pizza crusts I've ever tasted. What's best is the price of the food...so cheap considering the quality. At first take, it appears to be a tasty local eatery, yet it is a national chain. However, this location is the only one in Indy, and if you like flavorful pizza, I highly recommend this place. Even if you don't like it (which I doubt will be the case) at least you won't spend an arm and a leg to try it.

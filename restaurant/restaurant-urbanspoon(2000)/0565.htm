mobile review
by Pete (30 reviews)
November 29, 2009 - Likes it
First visit here, and definitely liked it. The place was packed, and the tables pretty close together where we sat. Service was just ok, but we sat next to the waiter station. Salad bar was good, but a few more hot side dishes would have been nice for the price. All cuts of meat I tried were great, just the guy with the filet mignons was absent for extended periods of time, and we had our waitress chase him down. Something other than meat on the spits would have been nice, too. Other places like this have corn or pineapple, with goes nicely with the meat. I signed up to their club online and received a 25% discount, so for three hungry guys the tab came to $150 with drinks, not too bad. The filet mignon alone was worth the visit, so I'll be back. Make sure you're hungry !

You Gotta Know Jack
by Captain Napkin (284 reviews)
January 03, 2010 - Really likes it
I'm from Omaha, and when I get away for a weekend to KC I always try to have at least one BBQ experience to tide me over until my next visit (BBQ in Omaha is definitely more miss than hit). I have been dining at Jack's place since it was known as Smokestack BBQ and only had the original place down in Martin City. I remember hour long waits outside in the courtyard before our name would be called.
Well, times change, but the food has remained a constant at this local chain. The meats are awesome, and the BBQ beans can't be missed! The other sides are pretty good as well (not sold on the onion rings. They look better than they taste). There are a ton of other great BBQ restaurants in KC, and I like eating at them as well, but for great food and snazzy atmosphere, you gotta know Jack!

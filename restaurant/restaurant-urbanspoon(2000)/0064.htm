sub-par food and bad service
by christina_l_huey (4 reviews)
April 30, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I think that this restaurant is banking on the fact that they are advertised on every tourists map and hotel info sheet. We went here April 25, 2010. I had the pulled pork sandwich plate with mac and cheese, and potato salad. The mac and cheese was good, potato salad was excellent, however, the pulled pork was dry. It lacked flavor, and basically tasted like saw dust. I couldn't even finish it. Also, the service was horrible. We had to wait for our waitress to take our order for more than 15mins. The place wasn't even that busy on a Sunday evening. If I go back to Austin, I would not return to this place.

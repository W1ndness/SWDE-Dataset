Best Restaurant in Twin Cities
by wallymw (3 reviews)
May 02, 2010 - Likes it
We discovered this little gem after some of favorite other Cuban restaurants were closing (Cafe Havana, Bobalu). This place beats both of those places hands down!! The site is small and very modest, but the food and service is un-believable. The wait staff is always fun, inviting and charming. The empanadas are the best I've ever had. All three flavors (chicken, beef & cheese) are all good, so we usually do a mix of all three. The pork with lime is to die for and the sides of rice are always good. The prices are very reasonable, I just with the restaurant was closer to our house! Run, do not walk to Victors for the best Cuban food you've ever had!!

Mmmm, mango waffles
by Jed Anderson (53 reviews)
January 04, 2010 - Likes it
Victor serves up great Cuban breakfast. Expect the coffee to have chicory in it and you'll enjoy yourself. The shack is a little sticky, but the regulars like it that way.

June 17, 2010 - Really likes it
I really like Moonlight's pizza- but I prefer to get a calzone here. They are delish. I've had some other items, but always go back to a black olive and banana pepper calzone. Any they are large enough that my husband and I share one w/ a salad for a very nice- and inexepensive meal. One note: I would recommend sitting outdoors because it's an awesome patio and because the inside of the restuarant smells funny.

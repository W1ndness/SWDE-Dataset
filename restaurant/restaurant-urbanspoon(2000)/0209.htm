¡Muy Bueno!
by WisdomSeed (2 reviews)
February 08, 2009 - Really likes it
It was crowded as all get out, but the table wait was not very long (well there may have been an adjustment in expectations). I have to preface this by saying, I like Mexican food, but I am not that adventurous when it comes to dining, so I go with what I know the first time I eat anywhere.
Well the first unexpected pleasure are the chips and salsa. They were both warm, nice and warm. It was a very different experience than what usually happens in a restaurant, and they were refreshed with even warmer chips and salsa (which is a really nice chipolte salsa), which only made it better.
So I ordered the 2 taco dinner (yeah, 'Only two?', I was thinking) and for a mere dollar more you could get the supreme which was steak instead of regular beef or chicken. Well my friends, that was a dollar well spent as it turns out. The steak tacos were the bomb, and two is all you need. The steak was grilled (it tasted wood grilled too)  and just as tender as you would expect a steak to be if you were ordering a steak, not a taco. I man it was actually melt in your mouth, kind of steak.
So I will return to explore some more of the menu, because they took something so basic as chips and salsa and two steak tacos and made me chew with my eyes closed.

by Teresa MacDonald (5 reviews)
July 26, 2010 - Likes it
Enjoyed our dinner although I could not get over the fact that after taking time reading the menu, eating an appetizer and almost completing my entree, the server never refilled my water. I finally asked another server working on the patio (who had looked at my empty water glass previously). Also, our entrees came out while we were mid-appetizer and had no room on the table. Otherwise the employees were pleasant and the food tasted well. The flavors were quite yummy! Also, our martinis were very good. My compliments to the chef and the bartender!

good times, great beer
by kevin diRt (11 reviews)
April 16, 2010 - Likes it
this is by far the best pub in the area, and i've spent many hours of my life here. the restaurant and pub are located in the old willimantic post office, a grand early 20th century building. the owners have done a fantastic job putting it together.
dining room acoustics are dreadful, so head straight for the pub. you may have to fight for your seat on a busy night, but it's well worth it. you'll get the same prompt and friendly service either way.
the beer lineup changes constantly, and you'll often find quirky or experimental brews on tap. if you don't know what to drink, order the beer sampler and get a taste of everything.
the nachos and burgers are the best around, but the dinner menu leaves a lot to be desired. entrees are mediocre... but good enough to make this the finest restaurant in town.

Miss this Place
by M S Bishop (24 reviews)
November 19, 2009 - Likes it
Another of our favorite places that we miss a lot since retiring and moving out of the area.
We visited here on almost a weekly basis.
Great Friday night spot after a week of work. Would even meet friends after work here for the largest selection of microbrews around and very good food. They have a fun electronic trivia game that you play against people in the bar and other places across the country.
Located in the old granite Willimantic Post Office, the atmosphere is so unique and accommodating to the large number of visitors.

Hidden Gem
by Nifer (10 reviews)
May 20, 2010 - Likes it
Do not come here if you are in a hurry. This is definitely a place to bring a few people who's conversation you enjoy to savor the relaxed atmosphere, and savory food.
The service was great - our waitress was attentive, as well as knowledgeable of the menu. She was able to answer questions, and make recommendations to the group of us who were unfamiliar with most of the traditional Serbian foods.
I was with a large-ish group, so we were able to all order different things and sample each other's appetizers and entrees. Not a single thing was bad. The flavors were very unique and savory is definitely the word of the night.
The burek, pastry wrapped deliciousness large enough to easily feed 3-4 people, was the highlight of my dinner. The dough was flaky and crisp, and the filling - a mixture of cheese and spinach - was creamy and delicious. The meat stuffed burek was also delicious. They take about 45 minutes to prepare, as they are made fresh when you order it, so be prepared to sit back, relax, have some good conversation, a Serbian beer, and enjoy the old tavern-turned-dining room.
This is definitely a place I will plan a return visit to.

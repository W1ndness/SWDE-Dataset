Definitely worth a stop.
by Kenitoquerque (173 reviews)
May 03, 2009 - Really likes it
Whether you plan on having your own wine locker with a Kopke '77 for everyone to gawk at, or want some uptown fine dining, this is the place. Also an excellent lunch stop. I like eating at the bar, ordering the light chicken lunch special which has a tasty salad, asparagus, and well prepared chicken breast. Atmosphere is sophisticated but subdued enough for a flip-flops and t-shirt lunch. Great bar and wine list.

A definite must!
by JohnO (66 reviews)
January 23, 2010 - Likes it
I've been here nearly a dozen times in the last 2 years and it is fast becoming a favorite. The Lobster Bisque is an absolute must. The best I have found in the U.S. so far...
Pretty much everything on the menu is good and the atmosphere is excellent if you want to impress...
The Wine List is much better than anything close by and if you are a Port lover - ask for the 1977 tawny!!! you will not be disappointed at less than $1 per year...
When you are there, look for me by the window, if I am in town...

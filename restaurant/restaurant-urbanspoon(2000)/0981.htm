This place is great
by AlexW (56 reviews)
April 14, 2010 - Likes it
We went on Monday night around 6 and had no problem getting a table on their awesome front patio. Our server was great, he actually asked if he could play a prank on our friend who had kept us waiting. (When our friend finally arrived, our server pretended like he didn't see him for two stops at our table. Needless to say he effectively pissed my friend off. He made it up on the third trip by bringing him a nice cold beer.) He was also very very helpful when it came to suggesting beer to go with our dinner.
During happy hour they have half priced drafts and half priced wings. We ordered the honey pepper wings and they were awesome. It was one of the best wing sauces I've tried. One friend and I orded there bacon cheese burger, and my other friend ordered the delmonico sandwich. The food was pretty standard, and the portions were good.
If you're looking for a quick place to grab something to eat, this may not be the place for you. I feel like the service is a little slower paced because they want you to sit back, relax, talk, and enjoy a beer or 10. This is a great place to enjoy a warm evening.

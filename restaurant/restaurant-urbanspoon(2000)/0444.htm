I'd go every day if I could.
by Rebecca (5 reviews)
March 12, 2010
My husband and I go here every few weeks. We haven't found anything to complain about. Green curry- awesome. Pad Thai- friggin' awesome. Some weird crispy banana dessert that I ate even though I hate bananas-awesome. Singha-bitter and hoppy, as it should be. My husband spent some time in Thailand and says this is as close to authentic as he's found.
There's a good vibe here, too. Not crowded, kinda romantic. Good service.
Parking tip: when you go in the driveway, go around back and look for a spot first. If you go through the front lot and there's nothing, there's no backing up or turning around. You have to go back to the street and do the whole mess again.

Consistent... Good...
by belchmelch (3 reviews)
July 26, 2009 - Likes it
I have talked to a few Thai people/persons who say that Souvaly is not totally authentic... but guess what they still say... "It's still good..."
Look i have never been to Thailand nor been fed by someone who regularly cooks authentic Thai dishes. All I know is that every dish I basically have ordered (so far... which includes 5 times) have been good.
I can vouch for their house specials regarding curry. Also good: the beef salad and a really nice appetizer... 'angel wings'.
This restaurant's decour makes you think you are dining in town or Waikiki... if that was their intention then good job. Reasonably price.
I have been to other Thai restaurants and Souvaly has a few items that none these do not offer.
Give it a try and leave a review...

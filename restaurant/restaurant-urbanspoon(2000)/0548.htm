Kobe in Halls

This is a great little restaurant. There's not a lot on the menu, but everything they have is great. They have their own special salad dressing that can also be used on your rice and meat that is just great.

Meh
by J D (23 reviews)
December 20, 2009 - Doesn't like it
If Burger King had a "Japanese" eatery, the food would taste like it does here. It's not that great, but I've had worse. I find it hilarious that it's so highly rated compared to a few of the other Japanese restaurants in Knoxville. It just goes to show there are too many people with access to computers that don't have any cultural experience.

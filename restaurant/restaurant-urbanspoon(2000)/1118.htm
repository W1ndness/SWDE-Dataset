+ 4 more photos from Hungrycanadian Enjoyed the Burger
by Hungrycanadian (231 reviews)
October 21, 2009 - Likes it
Well stop #5 on the Diner's Drive in's and Dive's stop for Utah didn't take too long to drive here. Was it worth the trip from Salt Lake...I think so prices were good enough. Did you have to wait like the previous review stated..Yes but not that long as they make everything fresh so makes sense. I ordered the Big Cheese Ben that comes with fries and Utahs noted fry sauce and a rasberry shake. If your counting calories I suggest don't when you come here as the bun it self is larger than your hand, I do however reccomend you get 2 or more patties as the patty is very thin although large but thin. You need at least 2 to make it worth it. The fries were very good and the Rasberry shake wow I thought typical shake comes with a straw...Nope soo thick you can only eat it with a spoon really a spoon! For the value its a hit and the food quality is good I can see why it does so well as its very close to the Air base, friendly service and great food stop by I give it a thumb and a half up.

Way Below Average!
by Chuck Lee (46 reviews)
January 02, 2010 - Doesn't like it
We stopped here because we had seen it on "Diners Drive-Ins & Dives" (Food Network) and could not have been more disappointed. The burgers were thin, flat, flavorless, and dripping with way too much "fry sauce". The chocolate shake was not even thoroughly mixed -- when I got to the bottom there were big lumps of soft serve and chocolate syrup. The frys were good, not great, just good. The place is over-rated and over-hyped.
Now that In 'N Out has made its way to the SLC area, these places will need to up their game if they want to maintain business.

Mega Yummo! Pizza!
by Pearleen Do (21 reviews)
January 30, 2009 - Likes it
For starters, it is pretty laid back and their pizza is …. ENORMOUS! I repeat, E-N-O-R-M-O-U-S! You can put anything you want on your pizza. There is no “standard pizza.”
Secondly, for a small pizza place, they sure carry a lot of cool beers on tap, i.e. Brooklyn Lager, Pabst Blue Ribbon, Terrapin India Brown Ale, Orange Blossom Pilsner, New Castle Brown Ale, Woodchuck Amber Draft Cider, Lagunitas PILS, and few more others. In addition they have a fridge with several different types of bottled beers like the Hebrew Jewbelation 12, Yeti Imperial Stout, and Left Hand Milk Stout.
To read more go to megayummo.com for more info and pictures! :o)

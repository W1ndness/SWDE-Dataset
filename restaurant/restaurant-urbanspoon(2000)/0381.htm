Believe it or not...
by Andrew D (249 reviews)
July 19, 2009 - Likes it
One can find decent Italian food in Fresno. I went out here with some friends when I was in town, and I was surprised to find OK eggplant parmigiana. OK, so it wasn't quite what I'd consider top honors in SF or Vegas. But for Fresno, it was amazing.
If I ever find myself in this corner of Cali again, I'm pretty sure I'll stop here for dinner.

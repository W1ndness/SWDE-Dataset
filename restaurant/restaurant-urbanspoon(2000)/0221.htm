Good pizza with unique toppings
by Local Foodie (60 reviews)
June 02, 2010 - Likes it
I like the place. It's not in my top 3 but it's in my top 5 around the tri-state. Their margherita pizza is very good, and their hoagies are good, but some of the special pizzas are where it is at. Some are better than others. The lasagna pizza was a bit disappointing for what we were expecting, but it was still good. I love that they have so many available toppings, such as goetta (the only place other than Trotta's that I know serves goetta on pizza), and potatoes. I was hoping they would have some kind of specialty pizza to showcase the potato topping, but unfortunately they didn't have one. I'm hoping someday they will. Service is always good and the atmosphere is comfortable. It's a good place to go with friends and enjoy the company before heading off to your next destination.

The beer selection is quite good -- heavy on the microbrews.
The dessert we tried (chocolate chip cookies run through the pizza oven) were good and chewy but were a little heavy on the flour.

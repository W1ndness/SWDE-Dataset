Home is...
by Joseph (2 reviews)
August 20, 2008 - Likes it
This place does it right every time. Walk in for lunch, and you'll see just how may people agree. This is a "Mom-n-Pop" kind of restaurant that pushes out some seriously high-end Italian eats! Blink and you could miss it, as driving down San Vicente can be a bit of a swirl, but when you find it, you'll be happy you did! The food and the service is excellent! Fabrizio the chef is a warm and friendly man with some serious talent, and the overall cozy appeal makes this place a top spot for very reasonably priced food. Go now, you'll be glad you did!

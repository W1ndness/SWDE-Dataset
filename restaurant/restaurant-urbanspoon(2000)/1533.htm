We love this place
by cfelisa (1 review)
March 11, 2010 - Likes it
We really like The Library. Its a laid back place, cozy and comfortable with great food. We love that the food is really fresh. Its not greasy and you can really tell that its made fresh, nothing from frozen. Every-time we go there we say: its so good to know that a restaurant owner cares about the quality of the food they serve. Portions are good and very reasonable priced. Its our favorite place to eat in Ashford Park.
The wine list is very good too and we always received great service. Draught beers are $3.
Thumbs up! We really hope that more restaurants open in the area!

Hit and miss
by miakittycatmeow (1 review)
July 14, 2009
I went for the first time last Sunday (lap top in tow) and had a pleasant morning with an iced caramel latte and breakfast casserole. The latte was better than the casserole. The dish was the right proportion, but the eggs seemed to be pre-made or something. It didn't taste bad, just looked like it came from the microwave.
Second experience was on a Tuesday morning before work. The ladies seemed rushed and confused working the drive thru and counter at the same time. At one point the barista was asking the two others if she was making drinks that no one ordered. When mine finally arrived, she asked me if it was mine and just handed it to me. Maybe I caught them on an off morning...was not the happy coffee experience I so dream of (or have become accustomed to at other neighborhood coffee haunts).
I will try them for drinks and tapas...gotta round out the entire experience of this place. I am going to chalk it up to still-trying-to-get-their-bearings...

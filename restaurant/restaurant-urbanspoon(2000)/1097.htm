Great artichoke and crab dip
by Lisa Guinn (80 reviews)
May 11, 2009 - Likes it
This was my second attempt to get into Paesanos -- this time we went for a late lunch on Saturday and got in! The warm artichoke and crab dip was fabulous. The pizza was good, but I wouldn't make a special trip for it. The Caesar salad was fine, but not outstanding. The Italian cheese steak sandwich was killer. Service was attentive.
I would definitely like to eat here again!

2nd trip.... Well, I would like to sat that I really like paesanos the second time around so much better than the first time! They have a great bartender, Erika, who is wonderful and their staff really rocks it at night. I have tried their house vegetable soup, and it is a hidden gem behind a simple name. Really flavorful, and perfect for fall with crisp garlic breadthat is warm and soft in the center. Try this place out on a weeknight.... Or any night!

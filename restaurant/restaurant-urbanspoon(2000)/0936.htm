I had a Great Evening !
by mkelly28 (1 review)
September 10, 2009 - Likes it
I have been a frequent patron to this cafe (about ten times now) and I can say that the Witch does not disappoint. The other day, I went there for dinner and I started with the Roasted Beet Salad which had the goat cheese, and I absolutely devoured it. For my main course, I selected the Pocono Trout with the Citrus Cous Cous and that was absolutely perfect (I had a hard time between choosing this dish or the Striped Bass…..maybe on my next visit). Finally for dessert, I chose the Monkey's Delight and that simply capped off a perfect evening. I would definitely recommend this place to anyone and I plan to use the Witch for a party at a future date. The parking is convenient, and the service is outstanding (they really make you feel welcome).

Everyone should try this place!
by Janet (1 review)
April 25, 2009
My husband and I are so happy to have a great new restuarant in our neighborhood. We've eaten their twice and had a great experience both times. The squash soup and mussel appetizer were both inventive and very tasty. Last night our group had the the mahi mahi, crab cake special, and 2 stuffed pork chops and thought it all was delicous. Both times the service was great and on our first visit the chef even came out to meet us after we complimented the food. Oh yeah - almost forgot - the chocolate fondue dessert came with marshamallows bananas, graham crackers, and fruit to dunk, which was really fun to share. I think everyone to try this place!

Is my pint half full or half empty?
by Soup Fork (69 reviews)
February 24, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Sorta a mixed bag on my first visit. The inside of this sports bar looks like a million bucks: there are flat screen tv's everywhere(including above the urinals in the mens room). My 2 servers(one in training) were fast, focused, smiling and knowledgeable about the days food and drink specials. The food was a bit of a let down because while the Kitchen Sink sampler was piled high with fried foods(chicken strips, onion rings, loaded fries and mini corndogs) it all seemed to have come straight out of a plastic bag in the freezer and went straight in the fryer. The portobella "burger" was better and the grilled onions added to the overall depth, but I've had better and bigger for the price elsewhere. I will give it another try because the menu is very, very, large and I will try some more of the "homemade" options.

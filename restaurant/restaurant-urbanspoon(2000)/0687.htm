Great food, Better people
by Chicago Steve (11 reviews)
February 23, 2010
We drive out to Cudahy about once a month to get a dago beef, juicy, fries and a coke. My pal always gets the Chicago dog, with all the trimmings. You are always met with an enthusiastic hello from the employees. Really good food, great people and don't forget to say Hello to Steve who cleans the tables. He's as nice of a guy as there is. One more thing, Home Made Rice Krispie treats, Nuff said.

YAY for sausage
by Rachel (46 reviews)
April 22, 2010 - Likes it
Went here for today for lunch after discovering that Diners, Drive-In and Dives had featured Amato's on the show.... I ordered the Sausage and Pasta (which comes with a salad and garlic bread). The sausage was AWESOME- flavorful, a little spicy, crispy on the outside and moist on the inside. The marinara sauce was also good- a simple sauce, but you could tell it was made with high quality ingredients. The sauce complimented the sausage extremely well. Noodles were cooked to a perfect al dente. Also, the plain bread and garlic bread were both very good- tasted very fresh.
Overall, I was impressed by the quality of food and would be more than happy to eat there again. My only complaints are 1) I wish I could have eaten more off the menu (the Italian omlett sounded really good and the Italian Beef Sandwich was tempting) and 2) I wish I would have tried the Lasagna- looked awesome!

Recommend | Not helpful

Eat here
by The Secret Fork (32 reviews)
October 17, 2009 - Likes it
Like almost everyone else who doesn't live in Omaha, I found out about Amato's by watching Guy Fieri's DIners Drive-Ins and Dives. Finally, after two previous road trips to Omaha, the place was open. Third time's the charm.
Unfortunately, it's breakfast only on the weekends, but well worth the stop, even if what I was really after was a big Italian sausage sandwich. Have an omelet or some eggs, but whatever you do, get the sausage. It's the best. Period. The pancakes also sounded great- next time.
Best part of the stop was listening to Sam Amato visit with diners and talk about his experience with Guy and Triple-D. Sammy Tomato is the real deal and the food is as authentic Italian American as you are going to find anywhere. Get here and eat.

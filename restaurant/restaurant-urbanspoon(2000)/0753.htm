chocolate pizza! it exists!!!
by Nick Brooks (23 reviews)
July 02, 2009 - Likes it
Well, what's not to love about a chocolate wonderland in NYC? I was tempted to pull an Agustas Gloop from the start and jump into the large chocolate tubs, connected to a series of overhead pipes. It was very busy as I expect it always is, so be prepared for a small wait, but its worth going to at least once for the experience. Not cheap at all, but the desserts are so rich that they can easily be split between a much larger group than expected. I split a large chocolate pizza, which was a soft sweet crust topped with melted milk chocolate and marshmallows, between 3 people. It was way to much for us to handle, we had about half of it left when we couldn't fit anymore down. Intense, but delicious.

enjoyable trip
by Julie A (5 reviews)
October 26, 2007 - Really likes it
The restaurant is very nicely decorated.  However, I went with a friend on a Saturday afternoon and it was extremely crowded. We were told the wait would be 20 minutes, and it ended up being around 40.  But the waiter was very nice and made a note for the chef that I had a peanut allergy so that the food would be safe.  The gifts in the store are also nice. But be careful because I was told by one of the workers that if you have a nut allergy, you really should not get any of the snacks in the gift shop as products containing peanuts are processed on the same machinery as products without peanuts. They do have very cute mugs and drinking glasses if allergies are a concern or you just want something that will last for years to come.

Attn: Chocolate Lovers!
by Missy (2 reviews)
January 08, 2009 - Likes it
If you are like me and could eat chocolate for breakfast, lunch and dinner...you will absolutely fall in love with this place.
Walking in is like stepping into a Willy-Wonka movie set. Pipes of chocolate run through the restaurant to supply the dessert bar with all of the necessary goodies: White Chocolate, Milk Chocolate and Dark Chocolate.
The menu is mildly bland, but just order a sandwich choke it down and get ready for the best chocolate desserts in town!
You can also stop in and take a chocolately treat to-go, grab a crepe and a frozen chocolate drink and you'll have a true buzz all day-
Enjoy!!!

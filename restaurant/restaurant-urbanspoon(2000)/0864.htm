Wonderful all around
by Captain Napkin (284 reviews)
August 03, 2010 - Really likes it
Friends invited us to dinner at Charleston's, and it was a wonderful choice. The restaurant is very attractively laid out, with dark woods, lots of booths, and brick columns accented with (faux?) gas lamps. It adds up to a great dining room atmosphere.
The menu is streamlined, but features a nice selection of salads, soups, fish, fowl, beef and comfort food. Prices are very reasonable, especially since most dinners include two sides.
The food we all had was excellent, and the service was fast, friendly and unobtrusive. Water glasses were refilled without our needing to ask, and plates were cleared barely without us noticing.
I highly recommend Charleston's for a slightly upscale lunch or dinner on the west side of town.

Chain Food, Chain Service
by Andrew Gilbert (15 reviews)
March 16, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Despite the look, this is a chain. The wait staff and the front of house staff will make you think of a place where they have to wear funny uniforms and sing songs. Only they don't. They should. It might make you forget you are paying more for the food then you would if you were at one of those places.
The food is fine(ish). Order a hamburger - 50/50 it will come done the way you ordered it. Salads oddly enough come with a sweet donut like creation. the rest of the food is overpriced for what you get. There are better places to eat in Omaha where the staff has less attitude.

Consistent. Consistently downhill.
by JeffB (6 reviews)
August 12, 2010 - Doesn't like it
When this place first opened years ago, it was great. Sure it is a chain, but solid and good food. This was a "going with my parents" special occasion type place that we would hit up probably once every three months over the course of years and I felt the place just kept slipping.
Soups, especially the broccoli cheese, was always hot a fresh, now? Boring and bland. The delicious salads with the fresh croissants with their little glaze on them, amazing. Now? Reeked of yesterday.
While it isn't for for me to be too harsh, it has been probably 6 -9 months since I've given them a chance, seeing them listed so high, I felt I needed to at least give my review of why I don't go there anymore.

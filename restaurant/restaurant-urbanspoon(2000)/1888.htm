Healthy Tourist Trap
by gthekidd (3 reviews)
August 06, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Do yourself a favor, and skip past this place. I'm sorry to say, but this is the type of place you take a vegetarian you want to start eating meat again.
You'd be better off (and save A LOT of money) just going to Whole Foods and buying this food - and the cafes there have better ambiance anyway. Protein shakes go as high as $11 and the food tastes like flavored cardboard.
I'd rather donate my money to a charity... Go to the Buddhist place in Central/Harvard Sq - you will thank me!

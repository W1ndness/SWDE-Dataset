Tasty desserts
by Mike Saxvik (5 reviews)
June 21, 2009 - Likes it
I know there is a lot of debate as to which is a better cupcake place in St. Louis. For my money, it doesn't get any better than The Cupcakery.
All of their cupcakes are simple and straight forward: cake and frosting. Thankfully for them, they have mastered both. Something about a moist and delicious cake with a buttercream frosting that just makes me smile.
Their regular flavors are good, but I really like the seasonal flavors more. Honestly, I think I could move into their pumpkin cupcakes in the fall/winter. Also, if you don't feel like a cupcake, get the oatmeal cookie sandwich...mmmmm.
Such a good treat and still not too expensive. Go there, you'll be happy you did.

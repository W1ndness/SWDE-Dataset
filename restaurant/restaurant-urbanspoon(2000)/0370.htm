One of the BEST Pizza Places in S.E.Michigan
by Charles Senseman (7 reviews)
May 29, 2009 - Likes it
Loui's Pizza has historical roots stemming from Buddy's as the original founder learned the ropes at Buddy's and the went out on his own many many years ago. Loui has created one of the best pizzas I've ever encountered anywhere, with distinctive light flaky dough and taste that is without rival even at Buddy's. My favorite is everything with anchovies and green olives. The salads are also amazing, very generous portions for sure and the dressing is quite incredible. I'm still trying out the rest of the menu. The ambiance is nostalgic of late 20th century decor, and it's a lot of fun. This restaurant is definitely a destination even from 40 miles away. Don't miss it.

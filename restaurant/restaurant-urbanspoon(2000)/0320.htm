Best Chinese food I've found in Colorado
by Linds (1 review)
June 01, 2010 - Likes it
My husband and I found this place while looking for good Chinese online. The reviews don't lie. Everything I've had here was spectacular. The staff is great and even the owner is great. I love their pan fried noodles but I had ordered them a few times and they were served a bit over crispy for me. I finally ordered them without being fried and Mr. Tang, the owner, came out to personally ask me if there was a reason I ordered them that way. When I told him they had been bit too crisp lately he said he'd fix it and I've never had that issue since. Yes, there are a lot of political figured on the wall but they are neither predominantly left or right. In fact Mr. Tang has several framed photos that he rotates. In light of recent legal events my husband and I still eat there once a week at least. If Mr. Tang is there he always comes over to greet us himself and ask how the family is doing. I'm a lifetime customer and I recommend Heaven Dragon to everyone I know.

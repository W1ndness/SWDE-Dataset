How Lucky Are We?
by Beverly (6 reviews)
July 11, 2010 - Likes it
To live only four minutes from this wonderful place. The food, the wine, the interestingly unique items in the grocery aisles, and the patio. Okay, WDM Gateway has the patio from heaven overlooking a lake, but for the center of the city, I'm happy with 'our' patio. I try to dine al fresco whenever possible because we are housebound for so long in this climate and Gateway helps me do just that.

Wonderful food...
by Tara (26 reviews)
March 14, 2009 - Likes it
All of the food I've tried at Gateway Market cafe is top notch. If the chef and recipes were to re-locate it would be one of the top spots in DSM for dinner... really.
The lasagna is wonderful, the mediterranean platter (feta, olives, roasted red pepper hummas and cucumbers) is not only delicious - it could feed an army! I felt terrible ordering it as an appetizer and then couldn't come anywhere close to finishing it. Nice to know that you can also pick those items up in the market and bring it home for your own party or indulgence! YUM!
oooh, I almost forgot - breakfast is wonderful there as well - try the french toast is is amazing, made on challah bread (I believe) with pecans and strawberries it is really good.
While it is hard to forget you are dining in a market, it is nice to sip a little wine next to the fireplace.

Best in Town
by Mike Mann (11 reviews)
October 14, 2008 - Likes it
If I had to pick my last meal on Earth I would choose their Thai Red Curry Lo Mien.  It has better flavor than any Asian dish I have ever tried, and that is saying something because it's not even their specialty!  They were also rated one of the top 10 sandwich stops in the USA by Bon Apetit Magazine.
The Market side is a huge bonus.  Wine, Cheese, and plenty of Gourmet foods to try.

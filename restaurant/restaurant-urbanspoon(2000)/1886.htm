Never Again
by Rosane W (15 reviews)
August 12, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The owner was rude and acted like we were inconveniencing him by being there. We were a little late for our reservation but we called and let them know. There were plenty of empty tables at the restaurant when we got there. The food was pricey and I couldn't even enjoy it after the way we were treated. I would have left but it was my cousin's celebration and he wanted to eat there. I don't care how good the food is, if I'm not treated with respect I'll never go back.

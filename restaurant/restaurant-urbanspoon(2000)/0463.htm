Yogurtland
by Aiko (2 reviews)
August 03, 2008 - Really likes it
This is the place to get Frozen yogurt. Lately this place has been getting packed with customers especially at night time. They are open from 10am-10pm. It is self serve yogurt, so just grab a cup and get what you want.
There are lots of great flavors to choose from:
Vanilla
Chocolate
Strawberry
Cheesecake
Kahlua
Chocolate mint
Cookies n' Cream
Taro
Green Tea
Tart (Plain, Strawberry, Mango, Blueberry, etc.)
Peanut Butter
etc...
(NOTE: The flavors vary everyday and even throughout the day. Depending on how fast they run out of certain flavors. So it's pretty random. And you can choose from about 10 different flavors.)
and toppings as well:
Fresh Fruits: (Strawberries, bananas, blueberry, kiwi, pineapple, etc.)
Mochi
Azuki
Cereals
sprinkles
Candies
Nuts (walnuts, peanuts)
etc....
Then just take your creation to the register to be weighed and paid for. It is 39 cents an ounce, so the cost varies on how much yogurt you get.

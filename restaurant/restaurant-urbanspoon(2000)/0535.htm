Kokoro - Cheap, quality, and great atmosphere!
by lww764 (17 reviews)
February 22, 2010 - Really likes it
Kokoro Maki House is my favorite sushi place in KC. First of all, it is cheap. An 8-piece sushi roll is anywhere from $5-7.50 and a $1-2 for a nigiri piece. The sushi is great, my favorites are the Spicy Salmon and the New York Roll, which has oddly has apple in it, but is so good. While a little rough on the outside, from the moment you step inside the restaurant, the environment is amazing! The staff are very courteous even though they do not speak English very clearly.

Recommend | Not helpful

Great resonably priced Sushi!
by jastereo (1 review)
December 16, 2009 - Likes it
We've been eating at Kokoro for the last few years and have never had a bad experience. Everything has always been fresh and very tasty!. Highly recommend the Sunday Morning roll (would no doubt be a few $$ more expensive somewhere else), New York roll (like a Philly but with apple) and the Yum Yum roll. Nigiri has always been very good as well and VERY reasonably priced. Only drawback at all is dealing w/ parking around there for takeout, and an occasional wait (generally only 1 person making most of the rolls) but we, as well as everyone we've introduced it to, find it's well worth the wait. We have been to pretty much every other sushi place in town and this is the one we choose for take-out 95% of the time.

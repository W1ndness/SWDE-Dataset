Good neighborhood restaurant
by Phartina (9 reviews)
July 11, 2010 - Likes it
This restaurant is fast becoming the number one Mexican restaurant in Memphis. We visited three times and have not been disappointed. The Guacamole appetizer is the best and freshest I have ever eaten and I am hooked! The food is flavorful, the service is attentive and the prices are absolutely low, which makes this affordable for customers of all walks of life. If you want a fancy sit down Mexican restaurant, this is not it. This is simply a good, authentic Mexican restaurant that fits our neighborhood. It's way better than El Porton, El Mezcal, Casa Mexicana and others.

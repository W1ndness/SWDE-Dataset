Incredible Los Agaves
by Jill M. Cullen (37 reviews)
May 15, 2010 - Likes it
We wanted Mexican so we consulted Urban Spoon for Mexican food in Santa Barbara. There were many positive reviews for Los Agaves. It is an adoreable restaurant where you self order. I had the chicken quesadillas with black beans and rice. It was very good. My friend had the chicken chimichangas which he thoroughly enjoyed. They serve warm tortilla chips as an appetizer and they are so delicious with their guacamole. The prices and the food are well worth it and incredible.

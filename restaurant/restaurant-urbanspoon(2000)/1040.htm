Great Fries, Poor Service
by HappyFoodie (1 review)
February 15, 2008 - Doesn't like it
If you're looking for a place with awesome fries, this is the place to go. However, if you're in a hurry (or even if you aren't in a hurry but would like good service), don't even think about coming here and expect to leave happy. We had to ask for water re-fills and the server walked by several times without a pitcher and then when she finally picked up the pitcher, she didn't even come to our table. I would probably return for the fries (the house salad is just a pile of mixed greens doused in slightly acidic oil) but definitely not for the service.

mobile review
by LeftofLA (6 reviews)
December 23, 2009 - Likes it
Very good service; attentive without being overly solicitous. Eclectic menu presents slightly unusual and interesting choices. The swordfish was quite good with a light but tasty sauce, perfectly cooked, but I personally prefer a somwhat thicker cut. The salad was fresh and the flavors well balanced. Overall a very pleasant dining experience with service mindful of our plans to make showtime across the street. It was noisier than I prefer but not the worst in the area - and they do not make it worse by playing background music, so normal conversation is possible. I will go back and feel confident recommending Jane to others.

Excellent and different
by Mary Margaret (83 reviews)
January 22, 2009 - Likes it
Specializing in Persian cuisine, Saffron's combines classic Continental preparation with Middle Eastern flavors. The service is professional, with good recommendations for wines. The prcies are not cheap, but the menu is so interesting that it is well worth it. This is a great place if you are in the donwtown convention area. If you like to try new cuisines, Saffron's may be for you.

Best Persian cuisine!
by Laura R (15 reviews)
July 18, 2010 - Really likes it
All I can say about Saffron's is that it is truly amazing. Of all the times I have eaten at this restaurant, I have never had anything I didn't like. First of all, the appetizer that they serve with all the meals (the cheese, herbs and pita) is a perfect way to start any meal. My first experience with pistachio soup was at Saffron's. The soup was as amazing as everything else I have tried. My favorite dish is the salmon, but it's hard to pick just one "best" at this place. I absolutely recommend this restaurant to anyone who likes Persian cuisine or is interested in broadening their horizons by trying it!

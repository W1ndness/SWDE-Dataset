Tonight was our first visit...definitely won't be our last!!! We had an absolutely superb meal, with great service at a very reasonable price. This place is DEFINITELY on our list! We give it 4 thumbs up!

Great Food! Ghastly Service!
by amurogay (1 review)
May 04, 2009
I was greatly impressed by the food and the atmosphere when I went tonight. The complimentary valet parking was also nice. Everything was great, in fact, until we got to the hostess stand. I have to assume that whoever the woman was that sat us (conveniently in the mostly empty restaurants least desirable table, right by the door), had to have been a relative of the owner or something, because I have trouble believing that someone working this job for a living could afford to be so snippy, condescending, and otherwise unpleasant. Perhaps I could be unfairly judging the server, who may very well have had many other tables to attend to despite the appearance of business being rather slow, but the few times we saw her she seemed eager to push things along, up until we needed the check, of course. If you are looking for some great (if pricey) Greek food (try the amazing tzatziki!) this would be the place, unless of course, you were hoping to be treated with the slightest bit of dignity or respect.

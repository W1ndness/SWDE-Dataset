Better that Average
by DSattler (53 reviews)
September 03, 2008 - Likes it
This is our favorite sit-down Chinese restaurant in the Catonsville-Elkridge-Ellicott City area. The food, mostly traditional Chinese-American, is a cut above average, and generally not the over sauced glop that passes for neighborhood Chinese food around here. Service is efficient, but without much personality. Not my first choice for carryout.

Really like this place
by MDfoodlover (3 reviews)
May 09, 2010 - Likes it
The previous reviewer can't possibly be talking about the same restaurant, because I've been going here for years and Little Phoenix is great. I have NEVER experienced either bad service or bad food at either the carry-out window or inside the restaurant. This is one of the better Chinese restaurants in the area. If you eat in, the service is great, food is ready quickly and is always fresh and tasty. Their steamed dumplings are some of the best I've found in the area (and I eat Chinese food a lot), and the Orange Chicken & General Tso's Chicken are delicious. The Moo Shu Pork is another one of my favorites, and when you order this dish, you get enough food for at least 2 (or 3) meals. If you like Chinese food and haven't been to Little Phoenix, it's definitely worth checking out. You won't be disappointed.

Good prices, awful service, awful food
by Scott Rothman (1 review)
February 14, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I went to this restaurant with a friend of mine for Valentine's day. After walking in, you're not quite sure where to go. There is an obvious dining area and an obvious window for cary out, but no one is standing there to seat you. My friend and I walked over to the cary out window and asked where we go to be seated. She just hastily responded "15 minutes!"
My friend and I sat down and looked over the menu. The prices are very very reasonable, which is good because the food was awful. We split a combo meal of General Tso's chicken, an egg roll, fried rice, and egg drop soup for about 11 dollars. The soup was delicious, the chicken was nearly inedible, the egg roll was 99% cabbage, and the rice was dry and hard.
We were finished eating in about 10 minutes, because neither of us could stand the food. We ended up having to sit for another 30 minutes before anyone came to check on us to see how we were doing. When someone finally came by, I asked for a box and the check. The waiter grabbed a box and a bag that were sitting on the ground leftover from a recently departed party.
I will not be returning.

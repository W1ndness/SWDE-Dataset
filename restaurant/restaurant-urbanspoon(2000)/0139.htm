Message from Upper Crust

Charles Street's finest pizza shop churns out fresh specialty pies and light salads.

  • Catering

Each and every one of our ingredients is the most important piece of our delicious puzzle. That means from our freshly rolled dough to each one of our savory toppings and cheeses, only the best ingredients are used to create an authentic, thin crust Neapolitan-style pizza that is sure to make your mouth water with anticipation.

Loved It!
by Anamaria Bermeo (4 reviews)
April 27, 2010 - Likes it
I'm a big pizza fan, and true pizza fans like different types of pizza. This pizza is very thin but a bit on the gooey side. Hardly greasy at all! (Which being a health food nut is an almost must in my book). Everything is fresh, and you can watch them make it right in front of you. The place is small, so be prepared to walk away with your slice of pie, or you'll sit next to a very nice stranger a large dinner-like table. Check it out!

Well I thought this would be a great place to start with Pizza here in Boston. Walking by the place on Charles street it smells heavenly and crowded for the most part. I order in to have it delivered to my home which is right around the corner.

I know customers reviews should focus on the experience .. but the food and the service were BOTH terrible ... I have tried Upper Crust a couple more times as maybe it was a fluke .. but same time the pizza quality is TERRIBLE.

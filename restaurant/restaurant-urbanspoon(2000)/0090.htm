A Superior Dining Experience
by Sampson (36 reviews)
May 05, 2009 - Really likes it
Roy's Hawaiian Restaurant is awesome! Expensive, but a great place to go. The food fuses Asian (Chinese, Philippine, Japanese) with Hawaiian and American culinary influences.
Each Roy's restaurant has a local master chef who adds his own flare to certain menu items. Thereby giving the restaurant the ability to have positive features of a chain restaurant and positive features of an independent restaurant.
The service is also fantastic.
There just isn't another restaurant like it.

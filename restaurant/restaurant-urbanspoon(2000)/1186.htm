keep it simple
by johnnjill (100 reviews)
June 10, 2009 - Likes it
Fast food doesn't have to be bad. The second location of Paseo along Seaview Ave proves that point. In what used to be a hotdog stand, Paseo has opened a fast food joint that serves cuban favorites quickly and with pitch perfect consistency. My recommendation is simple: Go and get the pork Sandwich (half ought to be enough for one person with a big appetite) with onions and peppers on a baguette. Oh yes, don't forget to get an orange soda to wash it down with. All together, less than $10 and mouthwatering good.

Meaty run & fun
by johnnjill (100 reviews)
December 02, 2008 - Likes it
Paseo's second location is an old hot dog shack along Shilshoe Ave. It's ideally situated for a bite after going to the beach, running along the waterside or hiking up and down the stairs to 32nd Ave NW (that last is quite a workout!). But Paseo doesn't fail to please. Anything meaty will do: Roasted, pulled, fried, etc. But the pork is tastiest. There's really no place to sit down but the picnic tables outside, so plan to swing by and take a sandwich or a plate with you to the beach.

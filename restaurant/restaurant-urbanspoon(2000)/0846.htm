Good Ole Ted's
by CASEY (225 reviews)

You can always count on Ted's for good basic Tex Mex. Nothing to really blow you south of the border here, but consistently fresh, well prepared dishes. The service is always good, the prices very reasonable. The flour tortillas are about as good as any you'd find anywhere in Mexico. I just wish they'd once and for all invest in some decent cutlery. What they have is one step above plastic.

Unique, Creative Dishes
by Pyper Roe (1 review)
February 14, 2009 - Really likes it
Ted's has a wide selection of unique, creative dishes that cannot be found at other Tex-Mex eateries. A particularly scrumptious selection is the Pollo Ranchero, which combines a rich, creamy sauce with grilled chicken strips and sides of corn creama and pappas. But every dish is carefully prepared with attention to detail and taste combinations. The tortillas are absolutely superb; they are made in house and are very thick, soft, and tasty.

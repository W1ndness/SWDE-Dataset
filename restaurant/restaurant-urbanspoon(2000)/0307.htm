I Would Stick To The Sandwiches
by Bobby G. (36 reviews)
June 16, 2010
I enjoyed many take-out sandwiches from Angelo's while working in Fort Worth, but ate few meals in the restaurant itself. After an absence of several years, we recently stopped by for lunch and were mildly disappointed. The casual atmosphere and friendly employees are still there and the brisket was excellent. But the sides were quite bland and seemed to take on the identity of the disposable serving containers and plastic utensils. Even the barbecue sauce seemed overly mild. I still recommend the place but next time I will stick to the sandwiches.

Blah bbq
by MPT (6 reviews)
September 14, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I finally went to Angelo's this weekend and I can't understand why it gets such good reviews. It was one of the worst places I've ever been to and my boyfriend ended up sick afterwards. I got the brisket and the pork ribs; both lacked any smoky flavor and tasted stale - like they had been sitting on the steam table under plastic wrap for too long. I couldn't taste any rub on the meat despite their marketing of a dry rub. I had to pour all of their thin, watery sauce on the meat just to give it some semblance of flavor. Sides were just standard. Yet further confirmation for me that there is no good BBQ to be had in the DFW area. What is up with that? Looks like I'll have to make the drive south if I want any good 'cue.

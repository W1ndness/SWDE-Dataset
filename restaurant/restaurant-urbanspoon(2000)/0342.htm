Great hangout place
by fluorophore (15 reviews)
January 20, 2009 - Likes it
Stomping grounds is neatly situated on the south end of campus and is a popular place to have a bite and relax.  One of the more appealing aspects is the patio area that is perfect for a warm day.  There are some big trees that provide some nice shade and the vibe is very relaxed.  The staff is very friendly and quick with service.  For the most part this is a coffee place that has surprisingly good sandwiches, smoothies, salads, soups, and desserts.  Lots of people chilling with laptops or notebooks.  It's a favorite of campus staff and students alike.  At night you can sometimes find live music here which can be equally enjoyable to the relaxed daytime atmosphere.  Overall, a very good spot to meet friends and enjoy a drink or have a meal.  Probably one of the top five best spots in Ames.

Recommend | Not helpful

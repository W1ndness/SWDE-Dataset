Needs Improvement
by Erin K. (2 reviews)
June 27, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Petite Chou's food is good, if overpriced ($3 coffee? Really?). What has been a consistent let down is the service. There's always a lack of smiles at Petite Chou ... which could be from the loud music that is always playing. (Not the best mood setter on a Sunday morning, you know?) I've also had problems with the food's execution. I've ordered the broken yolk sandwich twice and each time its been over cooked. If you're going to advertise a "runny yolk" sandwich, better make sure the yolk is runny; that's kinda why people order it.

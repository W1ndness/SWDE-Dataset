Always understaffed
by Emily (8 reviews)
September 28, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I Love the fresh, always rotating beer selection. The food is decent for a bar, if you have several hours to kill. But while the Southside and Glen Allen locations have great service, this is not so for Downtown. I frequently see angry lunch crowds requesting their food to go, after waiting at a table for their food for over an hour. Since I work nearby, I have visited many times, both at peak and non-peak times. Consistently servers are understaffed and kitchen chaotic. When the crowd is light, the hosts are conveniently missing, and potential diners leave frustrated. I have attended ticketed shows in the lounge, with equally poor service. Consider making the drive to Southside, you will get a beer faster than if you had waited in the Downtown location for a server.

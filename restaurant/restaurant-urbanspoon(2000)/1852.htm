Message from Similans- Fine Thai Cuisine

The acclaimed Brown Sugar Cafe's Cambridge outpost serves fresh Thai fare to Kendall Square workers.

  • Thai Cuisine
  • Relaxed Atmosphere
  • Take-Out and Delivery

Having built a loyal following of repeat customers, Brown Sugar Cafe added a second location at 1033 Commonwealth Avenue in 1999 and are pleased to announce the opening of The Similans on 1st Street in Cambridge. Despite the expansion, Brown Sugar Cafe maintains the attention to detail and commitment to quality that made them popular.

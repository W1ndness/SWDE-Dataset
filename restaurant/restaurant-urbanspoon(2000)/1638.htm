2 Scoops Of Yum!
by Mitchell Knutzen (1 review)
May 08, 2010 - Likes it
There's nothing not to love about Fresh Cup. Best toppings bar that I've seen (and that goes for quality AND quantity). This place has everything you can possibly think of to toss on top of what's already the tastiest swirl of yogurt in town. The candies, fresh fruits and baked treats are ALWAYS fresh, cut in store, and in plentiful supply.
Can't forget the yogurt! Every time i've stopped by there's something (or a few somethings) new on their 'yogurt wall', and most of it is shockingly low on calories, though i'd personally eat a stick of butter if I thought nobody was looking, so calories aren't on my radar.
The store itself is very clean and comfortable and the staff is really friendly. The free Wi-Fi isn't the worst deal either.

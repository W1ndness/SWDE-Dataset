Great food at a reasonable price just like the other locations.
The gyro plate is probably the best deal they have going, LOTS of food for a reasonable price.  Hidden away on the south side of the shopping center, it's hard to find and easy to miss, but keep looking, the hunt is worth the effort.

Great value, flavorful food, fun change of pace
by BW (59 reviews)
March 26, 2009 - Likes it
There's nothing fancy about Tino's. Slide your tray down the cafeteria-style food bar and call out what you want. They have a very good variety of tasty entrees and sides, and the portions are quite generous. The soups in particular are very flavorful--try the lemon chicken! The food here also holds up well as a to-go order, so this is something to keep in mind if you're in a hurry.
I'll be back many more times in the future.

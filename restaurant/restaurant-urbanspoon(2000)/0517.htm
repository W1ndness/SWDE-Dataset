Delicious Tranquility
by The Food Carpenter (157 reviews)
February 02, 2010 - Likes it
Talk about a warm and inviting experience from the moment you arrive till the moment you leave. The beautiful and soft ambiance would make it easy for anyone to justify this setting for any occasion.
Around the table we ordered salad wedges and crab bisque for starts, followed by the baked tilapia with lobster risotto, fillet with crab cake and the braised short ribs for entrees. The fish was perfectly prepared as were the steaks (not to mention the small wood-fire notes that were seared in). The short ribs with baby carrots and pearl onions finished with a marsala sauce were incredible. Service was very attentive the entire evening which helped earn my future repeat attendance.

A Gem in the Chain Restaurant Jungle
by Jimmy Hollinshead (3 reviews)
November 29, 2008 - Likes it
If you have had your fill of chain restaurants in overland park, take a walk on the wild side and visit J. Gilberts. This restaurant is part of a boutique chain and offers a level of food, service and ambience you won't find elsewhere in Overland Park.
Excellent steaks. Nice Seafood Presentations. Killer potatoes in several interesting and tasty versions and very private and quiet.
Not cheap but very, very nice.

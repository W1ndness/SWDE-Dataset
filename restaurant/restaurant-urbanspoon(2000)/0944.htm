Art Museum Mainstay!!
by hinser (17 reviews)
June 18, 2008 - Really likes it
This place is great!!  If you'd like a good beer in a GREAT area of town - then this is the place!!  The Collar is a mainstay of the Art Museum area and it should be!!  The bartenders (whether it is Big John, Weezy, or Pat) are very compentent, cordial, and know their way around a bar!!  The Collar is your typical Irish bar - It can be crowded - because this is a very attractice haunt.  The food is average - but if you want a nice night out in the A.M. Area - you'll have a good time here!

Awesome Food!
by Kehartle (2 reviews)
June 09, 2010 - Likes it
The Bishops Collar has great atmosphere, great beer and great food. I was pleasantly surprised when I ordered. The food was delivered quickly and hot. The food is always fresh and homemade. The Bishops Collar is not only a great bar, but also a great place to grab a bite to eat. I tend to go here sometimes just for the food. I would highly recommend this to anyone who enjoys homemade sandwiches and beer.

November 07, 2008 - Doesn't like it
The meatloaf sandwich was beyond dreadful (dry, chilly, unadorned, and tasteless), the turkey burger overcooked and forgettable, and the fries were doused in Old Bay seasoning. The service was harried, and the music stunk. Maybe we hit a bad night, but we'll never go back when there are other reliable choices nearby.

mobile review
by Peder (1 review)
November 11, 2009
I would rate this as just below standard pub fare. Stuff you wouldn't mind eating with a buzz on or if your last meal came on a plastic tray. Menu was nondescriptive. "Burger with your choice of topping" but no list of toppings. I picked and choose through the other sandwich and chicken wing toppings to come to a decent combo. Service was good and I sat outside so the atmosphere was great. I voted I like it only because I didn't dislike it. Sometimes I'll put up with mediocre food for good beer, service and atmosphere. This plase however has plenty of room for improvement.

Consistent and Good
by sk8oar (3 reviews)
August 09, 2009
I've been going to the Collar for ten years. Its remained consistent, which, in my opinion is one of the most important qualities a restaurant can have - whatever the level. I love the chicken quesedillas. They come with a fruit garnish of berries and sour cream with guacamole. There is always a beer on special and the prices are very fair. Sitting outside in the nice weather is also bonus. Staff are always attentive.

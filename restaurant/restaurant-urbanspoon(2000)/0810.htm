BAD Pizza!
by SaLouLa (8 reviews)
January 27, 2010 - Doesn't like it
My husband, sister-in-law and I went to Grimaldi's only a week or so ago, and were very disappointed.
The pizza base and crust was good, but the sauce was disgusting. I ordered a plain cheese pie (which comes out as a Mozzarella and Basil) and not only was the sauce cold, it was really runny and honestly tasted like cheap canned tomatoes.
My sister-in-law's Spaghetti and Meatballs had a similar sauce also, and just flavourless.
My husband ordered a Pepperoni pie which again was bland and the pepperoni's looked just like cheap Sausage.
We came here after reading a New Jersey Magazine article, which rated Grimaldi's as one of the Top 25 Pizza Places in NJ. Whether we are spoiled from eating anywhere else after getting fantasic pizza everytime from Pizza Suprema or we came here on a bad night - we won't be coming back.

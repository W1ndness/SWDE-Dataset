Strike One!, but will try it again
by Kozmo (23 reviews)
October 20, 2008 - Doesn't like it
We went here for the first time Saturday evening. While we have been wanting to try Edina Grill it was a spontaneous decision to go here, so we forgot to re-read the reviews therefore we forgot to try the turkey burger and the deep friend green beans.
I had the fish & chips, my wife had the Garlic Shrimp Linguini.
The Fish & Chips were ok. and for the price they were not great. The fish was decent and while I like flavor and spice, they were very peppery. I think if you took Van de Kamps frozen fish and added a lot of pepper and deep fried it, it would be close to Edina grill fish. Anohter words I've have far better Fish & Chips at many an Irish Pub for a lot less $. The chips are not the fries you expect of traditional Fish & Chips, but frou frou shoe string fries. Certainly not the fries that what you expect with fish & chips. They were served in a "fake" London newspaper which was kinda neat , I'll give them points there. However, for $12.49 you get some fish and shoe string fries. no Cole slaw, nor dinner roll or anything else. not a good value for an ok meal. this is something I'd expect to pay about $7.99 for at most restaurants and you'd probably get slaw with it as well.  
The Garlic Shrimp Linguini was very good, but again the value was not. You get 6 shrimp, the tails still on the shrimp was a bit of a pain. but again for $15 I'd expect at least a little salad with it. this would be worth having again if the price was about $9.99 rather than $14.99.
Overall it seems the prices are high and you do not get much for you $$$. We will try it for breakfast and lunch, but never go there for dinner again. also a pint of tap beer is $5. ouch!  They say Edina does not allow us to have any drink specials. - huh?  Want to cut the price in 1/2, try TJ's of Edina. while not as trendy as Edina Grill, the food is as good and prices far cheaper and the atmosphere is more of a standard family owned diner/grill than the more frou frou Edina Grill.

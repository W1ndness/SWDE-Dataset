The best Vietnamese on South Grand, hands down!
by stadtmillerb (3 reviews)
February 27, 2010 - Likes it
Pho Grand, just writing this review is making me salivate.
My fiance and I either go to or get carry out from Pho Grand at least 5 times a month, and we have never been disapointed.
My fiance is pretty picky about food, and to get him to try anything that isn't steak and potatos is quite a task. i took him to Pho Grand for a date night about 5 years ago, he looked over the menu a few times and finally came to the decision to order #1508 which is curry beef. Our food arrived at our table, he looked at me, took a bite, and I didn't hear a peep from him until the plate was cleaned. He loved it, he is not an adventurous eater so for the past 5 years he has ordered the exact same thing; #1508 which he drenches in rooster sauce (Sriracha) and a glass of soy milk.
The food, the service, and the ambience of Pho Grand is second to none. And I aboslutely love how the owner or manager, not exactly sure who he is, comes to the table and shows you how to make your soup even better by utilizing the chili sauce and plum sauce on the table.
i recommend Pho Grand to many people and not a one has ever come back to me to tell me something negative, I always get a thanks for the recommendation and positive feedback about their experience.
Don't go anywhere Pho Grand. But, if you ever decided to build a second location in the St. Louis Hills area, that would definitly NOT be a bad idea :)

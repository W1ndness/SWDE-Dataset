Best Thai Downtown
by Jen (14 reviews)
May 25, 2009 - Really likes it
Don't let the small, unassuming store front fool you, this is one of the gems of downtown dining.
This is the kind of Thai food that you wish your grandmother could make. The portions are large, spiced perfectly, and they have that stick-to-your-ribs quality that not only leaves you full, but happy.
All of their curries are fantastic, but the Red Curry is simply amazing. Some of the other standout dishes are the Tom Yum Gai soup, Chicken Wing appetizer, and anything with the peanut sauce.
The wait staff are friendly and helpful. They are happy to describe or recommend a dish, and will help you find the right spice level for both your taste and to bring out the best of the dish. The prices are reasonable and a very good value for the satisfaction.

Good but the atmosphere is on the decline.
by Adam W. (1 review)
December 28, 2009
I lived in St. Petersburg for 2 years until June '09 and this was my absolute favorite restaurant downtown for lunch and dinner.
The food is downright fantastic and the portions are good. Prices are very reasonable for the quality and service is decent.
I recently took a quick trip to St. Petersburg and decided to stop in. In the past 6 months they have installed flat screen TV's in the restaurant and had football games playing. TACKY! If I wanted to watch TV while I ate I would stay at home, in a restaurant it is obnoxious and distracting. They have also ditched the lounge music that did wonders for creating a cool ambiance to enjoy your meal and time with friends.
Food is still good, unfortunately the ambiance is no longer desirable.

mobile review
by Acsims (1 review)
March 24, 2010
We chose this restaurant fr a friends birthday because of a word of mouth recommendation. All of us were highly satisfied with the experience. A dimly lit bar with an open floor plan left us feeling comfortable and classy, and the food was delectable to say the least. The menu has dishes organized by the type of alcohol that inspired the dish, and they have happy hour every weekday until 9pm. Happy hour consists of the obvious drink specials but also includes half price appetizers (and the list of choices is quite extensive). The only let down was the blonde brownie I had sent to the table for the birthday girl. It was so HARD that a fork couldn't even stab through it...brownies are not meant to be crunchy and they shouldn't make a thud when you throw them. Next time I will order a free birthday shot instead of a dessert but, regardless of this small mishap the rest of the experience was purely delightful! I will absolutely go again.

A Pleasant Surprise
by Esquire (1 review)
March 13, 2010 - Likes it
Although I do not normally submit reviews, after reading the comments posted by Foodie Buddha on this website, I realize that failing to respond to Foodie Buddha's negative review would constitute something I try to eliminate on a daily basis: injustice.
After reading several of Foodie Buddha's reviews, I find myself wondering why someone, using a pseudonym that represents peace and tranquility, would take the time to write so many negative comments. My best advice for Foodie Buddha: "Eat at home."
In regards to Capt. Shrimp, this restaurant is a reminder of what the United States has always represented: a country where people have the opportunity to form small businesses and actively participate in the economy. Unlike other restaurants in the area, Capt. Shrimp provides healthy alternatives to burgers and pizza by also preparing steamed seafood, pasta and veggies. I have and will continue to recommend this restaurant to friends and colleagues in the future.

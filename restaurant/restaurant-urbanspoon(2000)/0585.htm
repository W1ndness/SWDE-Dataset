Boring Menu, Overcooked food
by Mary Margaret (83 reviews)
December 19, 2008 - Doesn't like it
I know the "Meat and 3" is a big local favorite. But the menu is so boring. It is plain diner food from the 50's. Maybe some people find this "comfort food" but to me it's just ho-hum.
They never have any specials. (The only excepetion is when they have local veggies. Even then, it's just a side dish, no menu planning or thought involved.)
On the plus side, drinks are cheap during happy hour and you will get plenty of food here.
Now, if they could just put a little thought into updating their menu and offer some interesting specials!

Ramsey's is Unfriendly to Handicapped Guests
by Elvin Harkins (2 reviews)
August 07, 2010
We were looking forward to a delicious lunch at Ramsey's, but were shocked to find out that they would not open the only handicap accessable door for us to enter. I, and many of my friends, have entered through that door many times, but they would not permit a 91 year old woman in a wheelchair to enter through that door. There were several open tables visible through the window - we would have been thrilled to have sat in any of them.
The young man at the front door offered no suggestions on how to get inside. He didn't refer us to another Ramsey's. He just smiled and told us there was no way we could get the wheelchair inside.
I won't be back, and I'll make sure all of my friends know why I won't be back. How can you refuse service to a handicapped person in 2010 in Lexington, Kentucky?

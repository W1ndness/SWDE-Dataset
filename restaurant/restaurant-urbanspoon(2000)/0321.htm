GREAT FOOD-GREAT SERVICE
by KPOP (2 reviews)
January 24, 2010 - Likes it
A group of 6 of us tried Houston's last night. We were met immediately at the door by a pleasant hostess and shown our table. Our waitress was fantastic from drinks through dessert. I had the ribs (which fell off the bone and were delicious), the best coleslaw I've ever had, and delicious spinach. Others had steak, fried chicken and salmon. I almost forgot to mention the really, really good crab cakes. I lived on the east coast and good crab cakes are hard to find in Denver. These were great. Everyone thoroughly enjoyed their meal. We all said we woud go back again. I was surprised to see sushi on the menu as well. I definitely recommend this restaurant!

It was a pleasant experience
by Cheryl Llewellyn (1 review)
August 24, 2009 - Likes it
My husand and I, along with another couple, had dinner there on Saturday night. My husband had eaten at the location in Boca Raton, FL and loved it so we thought we would give it try when we found out they opened in Denver. We made reservations and were seated after only a very short wait upon arriving.
The service was some of the best I have had at a Denver restaurant and the decor was very nice. The music was a tad too loud and we were seated at a booth near the end of the bar close to the live jazz band that was playing that night. Once the band started to play it was very hard to hear the waitress and the other couple that was sitting across from us.
We all started with the traditional salad. Which was very tasty. The gentleman I was with commented that he could have drank a big cup of the bacon vingaerette without the salad - he liked it so much.
My husband ordered the Crab cake dinner and he wasn't very impressed. I ordered the Filet Mignon and foudn that it was a nice cut of meat but it lacked a bit of flavor and found myself wishing I would have ordered the Prime Rib. The wilted spinach side dish was amazing and I wish I had more of that. The same gentleman that wanted to drink the salad dressing said that his Prime Rib was the best he had in any restaurant in a very long time. The other person with us ordered the Hawaiian Rib Eye and really liked it.
Overall the food was good, the service was fabulous, and the prices are all right ( atad on the high side) but it would be a great date spot with a nice atmosphere. But it is a first date and you want to hear yourselves talk ask for a table away from the bar and live music.

Service and food to match
by Jim C. (34 reviews)
August 28, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I was in here about a year ago for lunch. It was barely ok.
Before that, it was quite a while, because I said I would never be back after that occasion.
We stopped in; a late night. Didn't have to wait too long. It was certainly busy that night.
We were seated near the restrooms. Ok, at least we got a booth. I ordered the fish and chips, because I thought that would be safe. My friend ordered a steak of some sort.
It took forever. The entire restaurant marched back and forth to the RR. Finally the food arrived.
My fish and chips was more like still, somewhat frozen Mrs. Paul's. Although, I could only have hoped for Mrs. Paul's. That would have been so much better.
So, how was the steak? Well, to start, well overcooked. The sole of my Red Wing boot would have been more palatable.
But... the kicker. Maybe it was because we were crammed against the bathroom... But, I swear... The steak tasted like those deodorizers they place in the urinals, smell. After my friend said something, I tried a second bite. Yep, that was it.
I couldn't eat my "fish" (if that's what they call it.) The steak was equally inedible.
The service was non-existent.
I paid the bill, and then we got out of there. Totally unsatisified. That was more than a few years ago (and soon after they opened) and I haven't really had a desire to go back. It would be hard to compare, but this is one of the worse places I have eaten.

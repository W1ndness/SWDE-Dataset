Clearing up some confusion
by OliviaR (1 review)
July 21, 2010
Grovers is closed on Sundays
The hours are Mon-Thurs 11-10 and Fri and Sat 11-11
It does not have an ATM inside and only takes cash
They do take out as long as the restaraunt isn't on a long wait (because it would interfere with the people who are dining in's service)
There is cheeseburger soup almost everyday (however, the later you come in, more of a chance they will run out because of its popularity)
Friday nights are very busy because of an amazing fish fry (which by the way, is only on Fridays) so expect a long wait
Sign in on the chalkboard toward the front when you first come in and the waitresses or hostess will find you a table when your name is up
There are only two large tables in the place so if you have a larger party, just expect a slightly longer wait (they will still try and get you in asap)
Otherwise, great food, great service!

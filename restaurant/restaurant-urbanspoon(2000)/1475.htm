Message from Jake's Ice Creams & Sorbets

Homemade ice cream shop constantly rotates serving more than 70 unique flavors.

  • Ice Cream & Sorbets
  • Sandwiches, Soups & Salads
  • Over 40 Flavors

Jake's Ice Cream believes that ice cream is not just a dessert, but should be an experience. This ice cream is the best in Atlanta and the sorbet is made from the freshest and juicy berries. There's an unmistakable home away from home feeling in every one of our stores. We will be happy to take you on a personal taste tour, allowing you to sample any flavor that tempts you. There's bound to be at least one that will make your heart race. Our menu also includes good old-fashioned BLT, chicken salad and grilled cheese sandwiches. Our award-winning Fried Green Tomato BLT is served on tradional wheat toast, but with our own homemade pimento cheese and pepper pear relish. Our soups are homemade and hearty and our salads. Jake's Ice Cream has been voted 'Best Ice Cream in Atlanta' by Citysearch, Creative Loafing & Atlanta Magazine!

Transitory
by UpscaleATL.com (2 reviews)
July 25, 2010 - Likes it
Jake's Ice Cream places notoriously close and relocate every 6 months or so. Always call each location you plan to go to to see if they're still open. If you can track an open one down, it's EXTREMELY worth it. If I ever move out of state, I'm going to arrange for them to ship me order with dry ice... I can't live without this stuff, and it doesn't come ANYWHERE close to comparing to anyone elses.

Really liked it, really. But...
by Nantucket Guy (11 reviews)
June 04, 2010 - Likes it
3 adults, 1 sixteen yr. old, 2 < 10 kids...
I had the Gordo's burger - cooked a perfect medium as ordered, with a bun that held up extremely well. The bacon was crisp, sauteed onions and mushrooms flavorful, and the Goat cheese was warm but not runny. Loved it!!! I give it an 8.5 out of 10...only because I want to allow other burgers a fighting chance if they so deserve it. 9.5 is as high as I go, only Nadia gets a perfect 10...
My wife had the Jean-Robert burger. Same rating...she loved it! The grape compote was an unexpected favorite.
Everyone else in our party was a serious regular, and their entrees were of the high quality they rightfully expected. OK, one of them was one of the kids, but her Mac-n-Cheese was huge and awesome - love the broccoli florets !
However...
The side dishes left a bit too be desired. I opted for polenta instead of fries as my side, and I was disappointed. Crisp on the outside is good, but dry throughout is not. It was more like really dry cornbread...
Two others got the green beans as their side, knowing it has always been terrific, but somehow it was different tonight, and didn't like it. Consistency is important.
Food gets a A- / B+ It was very good.
Service was well below par however. Our server was very nice, but it was apparent that he was more than simply a server. My guess is that he was the Manager on Duty, and was waiting on us because no one else could. He was busting his butt overseeing the whole place, which I totally respect, but that doesn't cut it when basic table service suffers. I had to flag him down 4 times during our meal:
1 - My wife ordered a beer once we were seated and after 10 minutes I had to get some one's attention to actually bring it to the table.
2 - We have 2 under 10yr. old kids that would like to order off the children's menu. Can we get one ?
3 - Can we replace this side dish for another ? 15, yes 15 minutes later she got it. That's too long...
4 - After I stacked up all of our empty plates and put them at the far end of our table (sorry, but I'm all about pre-bussing tables) and the unrequested check showed up on the table, I got his attention to order 3 desserts, 3 coffee's and 2 after dinner drinks (a $34 addition to the check). That's well worth the extra 10-15 minutes of table time.
Gonna give service a 7 out of 10 because I think he was really trying hard, and his table side manner was great - hey, 5 out of 10 is truly average, and it could've been a 9.0 (except for the East German judge), if he just had a bit of support.
Mr. Gordo - please shell out another $3.65/hr for another server on the dinner shift - it's worth it . Perhaps a part time server XU student that would be happy with $50-$75 a shift...
Look, I really like this place, and I love that RG stuck his neck out here. Just a bit more attention to the service side and this place will be over the top! We will certainly be back very soon.
8.0 out of 10 rating. For what that's worth... :)

HOT, BUT NOT IN A GOOD WAY!
by versailles151 (1 review)
May 31, 2010
My first visit was Friday evening, 5/28/10. Our server was fantastic, but everyone should have been told that the air conditioning was not working at all. It was extremely hot & humid outside, about 89 degrees, and it was hotter than blue blazes inside the restaurant. I had checked the internet for info on Gordo's before we went. They only had four entrees listed, which I thought was a little strange. My husband and I decided to go with the short ribs. When we ordered, we were told they didn't have the short ribs, however, they were still on their web site and still listed on the menu. That left three items: a chicken dish, a salmon dish and a Basa fish dish. We weren't thrilled with any of these, however, we chose the Basa fish. The fish itself was okay, but it was on a bed of red cabbage, which I normally like, but this red cabbage was extremely sour tasting. It was covered with a Poblano cream sauce, which was good. Anything else was ala carte. No bread or butter served, etc. They do have an extensive list of gourmet burgers, but we are not burger lovers. To conclude, I would give this restaurant, out of 10, a 2. More entrees are needed, some with beef, pork, etc. And, for God sakes, put a sign on the door if the air isn't working. Persons with breathing problems should not go in. The heat and humidity on Friday was horrible.

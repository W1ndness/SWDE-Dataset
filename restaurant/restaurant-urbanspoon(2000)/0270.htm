Vietnamese Chicken Salad
by klahdane (44 reviews)
September 06, 2009 - Likes it
I am a huge fan of Columbus's newest treasure. Don't let the old Taco Bell building fool you, This place is wonderful. Vietnamese options in Columbus are few and far between, and this is a treat that I can exercise the drive-thru option on my way home from work. The Vietnames chicken salad so far is my favorite--although the Singapore noodles are great, too. The salad, though, is fresh and light complete with mint, shredded carrots, cucumbers, pieces of chicken, sliced Vietnamese egg roll on top, chopped peanuts, onions, bean sprouts, and light rice noodles. For the summer and hot evenings it is refreshing and more than enough for one to eat. We love you Pho House!!

nothin goin on but the rent
by Drexler McStyles (206 reviews)
May 31, 2010 - Doesn't like it
We had a very average meal here. First off, the food came out VERY quickly, like in 3 or 4 minutes. That doesnt exactly conjure imagery of food being freshly prepared in my mind. Anyhow, my Red Curry was spicy, as I asked for it to be, but lacked any other real flavor, while the Eggplant in it was pure mush. My wife had the Ginger Tofu and she said that it was greasy, that her Tofu could best be described as deflated and that there was barely a hint of Ginger in the dish....
The prices were very reasonable and the service was fine but with so many other Thai places in town to choose from I see no reason to come back...
Grade: C (average across the board)

Reliably Delicious and Affordable
by Tommy (3 reviews)
March 01, 2009 - Likes it
My wife and I LOVE Chaba. Not only because its close to our house, but because we can spend < $25 and get some amazing food that's fresh, made with quality ingredients and always take some home for leftovers. Chaba is THE deal for NE Portland. We go there every week, it's our most reliable and delicious restaurant. The only dings on Chaba are the location, hidden in a less than attractive storefront on Sandy, but trust me, the food is awesome. Some menu favorites: fresh rolls, pad thai with shrimp, pineapple fried rice, massumum curry with chicken, tom yom soup and volcano beef.

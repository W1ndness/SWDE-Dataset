On the fence
by designermillion (41 reviews)
November 20, 2009
We've been regular customers of Millhouse since it opened, but only stick with one dish (Millhouse Gorgonzola), because they can't seem to cook their steaks correctly. Case in point, last night my husband ordered the Ribeye cooked medium. It came out medium-rare (on the verge of rare). This has happened no less than 5 times (we counted on the way home), but usually when he orders the filet. Their steaks also have NO seasoning, which means no flavor. Honestly, I'm not sure if we would go there if the Millhouse Gorgonzola wasn't so good, because nothing else we've ordered there has been that great. Their menu is also way too big, and they're tying to cover too many food types. They should stick with steaks, and make the best steaks in town. Not Thai, Italian, etc. We already have Green Papaya and Olive Garden for those dishes.

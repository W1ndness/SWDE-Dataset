Yummy noodles. So So staff.
by Meghamium (53 reviews)
February 13, 2010
This is a good Chinese place with delicious house made noodles and good dumplings. The black bean sauce is delicious too. However, every time I go, the servers seem annoyed by my presence as a diner. They are always busy, and I'm sure this has something to do with their attitude. They used to have a manager who made up for the rude servers, but he has since left. He turned me onto the almond bubble tea with Jim Beam. Oh sweet delicious! Overall, I prefer Po's Dumplings which is right up the street, because they have a larger menu, friendlier service, and (in my opinion) better dumplings.

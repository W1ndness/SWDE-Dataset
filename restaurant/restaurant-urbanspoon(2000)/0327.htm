by jaczarni (36 reviews)
March 24, 2010
These guys cater to those that want their Sushi "fun" (see Americanized). And that's totally fine, because the two rolls we had, one covered in some kind of baked sauce, were delicious. Not exactly what I think of when I think "sushi," but who am I to turn down gooey rolls of crab? The problems arise in the service. Our waitress was clearly annoyed we didn't use the pencil and paper, which we didn't notice amongst the sea of over-sized menus. Her retribution was ignoring us for most the service. Our food was delivered by the (I'm assuming) GM, who was completely silent. Not a good impression, but I'm open to trying it again.

Wednesday night happy hour
by klahdane (44 reviews)
July 06, 2009
You absolutely can not beat Wednesday night $3.50 burger night. You can't do carryout on the burgers or else I'm sure the place would sell out and lose a bunch of money. You have to dine in, face the crowds and wait patiently for a table. Sip on happy hour priced cocktails while you wait, and prepare to enjoy. Every burger on the list is a real treat--especially the blackened burger for all you Saracha hot sauce lovers.

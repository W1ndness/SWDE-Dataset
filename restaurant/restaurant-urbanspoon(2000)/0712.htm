Fantastic. I can't tell you what a pleasure it was to have my huge party (20 people - and it is not a big place) at the Strip Club. I was nervous - trying to plan an evening out for so many women from a distance (planning from Boston), but the management (the owner was fielding all my MANY calls) made it an absolute pleasure. great name and better food
by Arah (12 reviews)
May 08, 2009 - Really likes it
Husband and I brought a relative from out of town here, as it's one of our faves AND he'd be able to brag to his aviation buddies that he'd been to a place called the Strip Club with his daughter and her husband.
The strip steaks here are fabulous, especially if you love (or need) steak but don't have it often. Be sure to get the beans & toast as an appetizer to nosh on, while sipping one of their great cocktails. The cassoulet they do is intoxicatingly savory, and husband loves the sturgeon so much that he has ordered it twice.
The service there is great and convivial as well! The only detractor from a meal here was the teeny tiny red wine glasses. The wine portion seemed appropriate, but the glass seemed out of place in a venue offering such strength in flavor and visual character. I felt like a giant!

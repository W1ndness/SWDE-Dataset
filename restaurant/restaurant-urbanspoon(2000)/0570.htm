Mr. Attitude below must have been having a bad day
by aarontlowe (2 reviews)
June 03, 2009
The reviewer who gave this place a scathing review must be a real gas to hang out with. No wonder he/she was eating alone. It's like he/she was just looking for things to irritate him/her. Anyway, I have no connection with the company other than that I love their burgers, custard, and the friendliness of the staff every time I go there. So many fresh ingredients to choose from, and sauces to spice it up. The meat is very high quality and I've never had a bad experience there (and I've been there many times). Also, what the negative reviewer failed to mention is that the fries are included in the price of a burger, so you don't have to order those extra. I can't figure out why he would order three mini-burgers rather than just a good regular sized one. To each his own, I guess. But that certainly would have cost him less. For a combo with a drink, it's probably about 2-3 dollars more than a "value meal" at fast food chains, and it's entirely worth it. Their frozen custard is phenomenal. It's probably my favorite in town, which in this town isn't saying a whole lot, but trust me. It's scrumptious. I'm getting hungry just writing this review. I think I'll go there for lunch now.

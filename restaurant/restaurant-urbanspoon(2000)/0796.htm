There is a reason there is mainly a 2 hour wait
by johnnyeats (9 reviews)
May 05, 2009 - Likes it
This restaurant is simply a place you want to be. The atmosphere, the food, the service. Those are the 3 keys to a successful restaurant. I dare you to find an item on the menu that isn't of the highest quality. Their burgers are ground daily to ensure the best freshness, their steaks are so tender they melt in your mouth. The 2 famous apps (peel and eat shrimp, and the artichoke and spinach dip) are must haves when you come with a group of people, but do be prepared to wait, I one time waited 2 hours for a table at 5pm on a thursday.

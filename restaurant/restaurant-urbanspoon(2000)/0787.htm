Unique 1st visit. . .
by R K Puma (190 reviews)
July 26, 2009 - Likes it
. . .Max was next door gaming with his team mates, so I thought I'd bring him a snack. Point is, I was alone. The waitress brought my iced water with lemon but accidentally dropped it in my lap. Not only profuse apologies from her (on her first day working there)-- but the manager saw the unfortunate event and comped an iced tea (sugarless, which I am certain wasn't on the menu) but insisted on helping the new girl. Hey, it could happen to anyone, even a veteran in Food Service. I can't recall such civility: a throwback to those days when people actually cared and showed they appreciate your patronage. So rare these days. Restauranteurs could learn from this "new" waitress and her manager. It brings people back.
The ribs and sides and oh, the complimentary cornbread were outstanding, like the County Grill & Smokehouse in Hampton. What a rarity these days, free bread! When you work your way through college in Food & Beverage Service you learn you make more $ with 3 F's: fast, fresh (from the kitchen) and friendly. It matters. --RK

2nd Best Tulsa Restaurant? Seriously???
by S M (49 reviews)
March 04, 2010
It's good pub food, but not GREAT pub food. I like the wide range of beer selections. No way this rates #2 restaurant in Tulsa. Good selection of foods, and it's all decent, but nothing is outstanding. You people must not have very refined taste buds, or haven't traveled much. :-) Weds burger night is a good time to go. Sweet potato fries are good. Like all of Elliot Nelson's restaurants, it (they) seem to have pizazz and atmosphere, but the food seems to lack "something," and does not stand up to the image the place portrays. Go in, grab a beer, order a meal, and you're left thinking "OK I figured it would have a little more taste, or more originality..." (this is a problem I feel is worse at El Guapo, admittedly, than McNellies). Again, good food, but a notch below the Brook and the former Blue Rose in flavor.
I saw some comments about bad service. Sometimes it's a bit slow, but I've been OK with that because we weren't in a rush...but when we needed a refill or check they were always responsive if we asked. If you're in a hurry to go see a show at the PAC, you might get a little miffed trying to speed them up, but if you're there for a beer and a meal (such as happy hour) service has always been fine for that, and I've been numerous times.
I think people tend to "like" or rate McNellies higher, because they think the place is "cool," or a hip hippy place to be. Sortof like how In the Raw on Brookside gets rated really high, probably because it's a hip wannabe/prep place to be, but the service is gawd-awful there and I cannot see how anyone can rate it highly. My belief is people are rating really heavily on non-food and non-service items at McNellies as well.
Oh I forgot to add...if you're in town for a business trip during the week, there's not a lot to do in Tulsa. McNellie's is a good choice to spend evening hours after work before heading to your hotel.

Simply put...Awesome!
by Casey (6 reviews)

They have the biggest selection of beer! With Over 200 different to choose from, they have something for everyone. When you walk in and see the wall of beer taps, you know this place is special. The food is very good too. Their burgers are amazing. There is not thing fancy about these burgers, just a really good hamburger where you can taste the in all its beefy goodness. The sweet potato fries are amazing!! They really are as good as everyone says, you should order them with your meal or get them by themselves. My sis ordered Chick Caesar Wrap, it was pretty good too. If you like to try different styles of beer and haven't been there, you are missing out. The price is right too!! 2 can eat for about $25. Beer isn't too expensive, they have great beer specials, like their pint nights, and monthly beer specials. It is one of my top picks for Tulsa restaurants.

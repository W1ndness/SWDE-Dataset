Delicious!!
by Phillip (7 reviews)
August 19, 2009 - Likes it
Ok I love pizza. Eaten it all over the world and I gotta say Evo has pretty much topped my list of exceptional pizza joints. Everyone knows there is different kinds of pizza so lets just go with gourmet pizza. They are great here! I first started eating their pizzas back when they would show up on the sidewalk across from their current restaurant with their wood fired cart to dish out fresh tasty pizzas for people. Quickly this turned into one of those things were you had to leave for lunch early otherwise there would be a huge crowd and it would take a while to get your food during lunch event. Months later they established the restaurant where it is now and it has a clean, beautiful feel to it. The waiting staff is attentive and on the spot. Again, you usually need to arrive early to get a seat here.

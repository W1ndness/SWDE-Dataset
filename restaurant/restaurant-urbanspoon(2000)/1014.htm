Third time was not the charm
by Wafkins (1 review)
July 22, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Lincoln is lacking in good choices for dining out and Asia Grille is no exception. The only thing this place has going for it is cheap eats and fast service. But if you care about the quality and taste of your food, spend a few extra bucks and go elsewhere for Chinese. The food is tasteless and mushy and definitely made from frozen rather than fresh ingredients. We gave it one last chance tonight but found ourselves disappointed again. We ordered steamed dumplings and the filling looked like canned dog food! Our other dish was swimming in thick brown sauce with wilted veggies with no flavor even though is was supposed to be spicy. The duck sauce had the consistency and flavor of apple sauce. The booths and walls are stained and in need of a good cleaning and service is mediocre at best. Our plates were taken away while we were still eating! Sorry, Asia Grille - we're going to Galaxie in North Providence or Cumberland instead next time we crave lo mein.

December 03, 2009 - Doesn't like it

good burger, good toppings
by StephRanieR (61 reviews)
March 04, 2010
you can't get bell peppers and fresh jalapenos at most burger places. here, though, fresh veggies and other tasty toppings abound. they also make tasty and plentiful servings of french fries. and they have big buckets of free peanuts. but the main attraction, the burgers, are what i'm here to write about, and they're what gives the five guys their repeat business. not too thick, not too thin, well seasoned, and always cooked through, these burgers are a champion of pittsburgh-area food products. skip the soggy primanti's sandwich and come here instead.

Best "Quick, not fast food" in town
by Matt (9 reviews)
October 02, 2009 - Likes it
They offer either a Hamburger, Cheesburger, Double Hamburger or Double Cheeseburger. I had the the double (which is called a Cheeseburger and the Single is called "Little Cheeseburger") and it might have been one of the best burgers I have ever had. If you like fresh and not processed burgers, then this is your place to go. The peanut oil fries are above average. I love the fresh cut aspect, but not a huge fan of peanut oil, but it is a different change to the regular oils.

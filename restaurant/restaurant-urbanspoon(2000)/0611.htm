enjoyed the food and the ambiance
by trina (19 reviews)
July 06, 2010 - Likes it
we had the red curry with beef (excellent) and the pad thai w chicken (very good). had the peanut dumplings as an appetizer - didn't like those so much. the crushed peanuts tasted a bit tinny and fishy - was expecting a taste more peanut-buttery like satay. the thai black iced tea was fantastic and made me wish they had refills for it.
we were seated on the back patio which is outdoor (canopied) and very pleasant in the evening. festive atmosphere. would return.

Luv it...great for us vegheads
by pasveghead (2 reviews)
June 02, 2009
I am so happy I live near this gem...I am a vegetarian and am sometimes bummed at the lack of veggie choices in Pasadena without resorting to pizza and pasta. Of course if I want to wander afield there are some good Chinese places in Rosemead/San Gabriel but why when I can eat at Daisy Mint almost just down the street? With plenty of meat options for you carnivores, Daisy Mint serves up very fresh, tasty fare. Love the summer rolls, peanut dumplings, tofu and spinach soup (a fave with my kids), green curry with tofu, tofu with mixed veggies. Great tea selection too.

Recommend | Not helpful

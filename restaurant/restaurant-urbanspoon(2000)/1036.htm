Fantastic!!!
by thatchers (1 review)
September 07, 2009 - Likes it
Way too much food for $7.00 (lunch price). My wife and I have been here 3 times since it's opened, twice for lunch one once for dinner, dinner price is still a great deal. Best sushi I have had at a buffet and the hibachi grill is a nice addition. Everything you would see at a china buffet and a lot more. Like Hong Kong chicken, pot roast and a carve your own ham station.
I have no real complaints but some comments would be that they could use a dedicated chilled seafood area, the oysters are next to the jello. The hibachi grill could use a nice sauce station, all of the condiments/hot sauce are at the end of one of the salad bars. And they have more items than they have room for, so something you like might not be there and the names are often wrong. The flip side of that is there are nice surprises. Highly recommended if you like this kind of thing!

Wonderful Buffet Restaurant!
by Diane (2 reviews)
January 28, 2010 - Likes it
I just went to the Hibachi Grill & Supreme Buffet for the first time today (for lunch), and have to agree with everything the previous posters have stated - this place is wonderful. In addition to the large variety of items to choose from, the fresh sushi (a large variety of that, as well!), and reasonable prices, the place is really CLEAN (which sadly, I can not say the same of most restaurants I frequent). That's the first thing I noticed when I walked into the place ... how beautiful, and new, and clean everything was, LOL. (Even the bathroom was beautiful!)
If you haven't had a chance to check out this new buffet yet, what are you waiting for? I believe they have different (additional?) items for dinner - I know that the person making the sushi mentioned that certain types of sushi were just for their dinner hours (but no worries, they had a large variety out for lunch!)

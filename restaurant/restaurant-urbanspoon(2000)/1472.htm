Message from Antica Posta

Pastas and hearty meat dishes bestow Atlantans with a taste of the Tuscan good life.

  • Extensive Wine Menu
  • Italian Happy Hour
  • Private Parties

Antica Posta is an authentic Tuscan style restaurant located in the heart of the Buckhead entertainment district. Antica Posta combines fresh ingredients with traditional recipes for a dining experience that until now, was only available in our first restaurant in San Casciano, Italy. You will enjoy the delicate blend of meats, fish, vegetables, spices and condiments that has pleased the palate of the most demanding Florentines for centuries. Antica Posta's cozy dining rooms and elegant bar and lounge beckon diners to experience a touch of Tuscany nestled in the archetype Buckhead.

Open seven days a week, we offer a full-service bar and private banquet facilities are available.

Over-rated and Over-priced
by kaypage7898 (1 review)
February 28, 2010
I'm perplexed by good ratings because our dinner was very disappointing. The four of us spent more than $400 and would rather have gone out for pizza -- honestly. Two appetizers and two meals were cold. Steak ordered medium-well was rare, but it took so long to get the meal we didn't send it back. Ravioli was just plain boring. Our main waiter was friendly but inattentive. What really made us annoyed was being charged $36 for one appetizer. My friend asked if she could get a smaller portion of risotto (listed as a main course) for her appetizer -- which they said yes. (It IS nice that you can switch appetizers for main courses.) What they failed to tell her? She would pay the same dinner price for her appetizer. Kinda sleezy if you ask me.

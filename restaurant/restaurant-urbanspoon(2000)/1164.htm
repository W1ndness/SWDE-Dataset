Yummy, authentic, affordable
by voluptuous vegetarian (39 reviews)
July 31, 2010 - Likes it
We were looking for an inexpensive place to dine on our last day of vacationing in San Diego. Whenever I am visiting cities along the west coast, I HAVE to eat at a taco shop. I liked how they had a detailed list of vegetarian options. So helpful, especially in Mexican cooking when you don't know if something is cooked in fat or broth or bacon. My favorite was their potato taco. It was HUGE and delicious. My boyfriend really enjoyed the carnitas taco. We enjoyed talking to the owner when we placed our order. He was friendly and seemed to LOVE his job.

Perfect Burgers Mews!
by Jill M. Cullen (29 reviews)
July 21, 2010 - Likes it
The Mews is known for their burgers but there are a wide variety of menu choices. We sat in a beautifully hand carved booth in the Celtic Lounge. It was wonderful and totally private! We both ordered the 69 Prime Burger which is black angus ground sirloin topped with your choice of cheese, fries and cole slaw. I had the cheddar with sweet potato fries. My honey had bleu cheese with onion rings. Perfect Burgers Mews, great service and prices.

Mews
by rick atkinson (13 reviews)
March 25, 2009 - Likes it
The options are outstanding 69 drafts, 200 single malt scotches and 18 burgers.We discovered Mews tavern in Wakefield when we moved to RI 5 years ago. It has great atmosphere for lunches. It gets quite crowded in the evenings especially on the weekends. Food is always fresh, great service with good luncheon special prices. We have always been pleased dining here.

Good for drinks only
by Dave (12 reviews)
September 03, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The Mews is a great place for beer and scotch. The selections of both is literally second to none in RI. However - the food is mediocre at best, the wait is too long for such average food, and the restaurant itself is shabby, unclean, and in desperate need of a renovation. There is an unpleasant odor in the dining rooms, probably from dirty, beer soaked carpeting. Go for the booze and eat elsewhere.

GREAT ATMOSPHERE
by Jim (4 reviews)
April 29, 2010 - Likes it
I love the Mews Tavern, it has always been one of my favorite bars for years! They have the best selection of microbrews in RI and I love their wings and nachos. They have 3 different bars, all with a different atmosphere, 2 dining rooms - one with a huge tree, and now they have live entertainment in their Celtic Pub. The place is so unique with so many rooms, you can actually get lost. Go in for a beer, and you might leave with a new "favorite"!

by Mary (24 reviews)
November 07, 2009 - Doesn't like it
We went on a Friday night - the place wasn't completely full, but there was no reason for the worst service I've ever experienced. Our waitress was completely clueless about waiting tables & had no knowledge of the beers available. My margarita was very watered down and tasted like a pathetic lemonade. I highly doubt there was any tequila in it. We were never offered a menu and after an hour and ONE drink per person, the waitress was no where to be found. My friend had to hunt down the waitress to get her to bring our check, and then we were afraid we'd never get our card back either. I am very reluctant to even give this place a second chance....

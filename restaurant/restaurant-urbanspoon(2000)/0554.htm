Amazing Lasagna
by daisyferret1 (130 reviews)
June 11, 2010 - Really likes it
Best Italian is just that---THE BEST place to get Italian in Gatlinburg. We had tried another place on the parkway a few years back & it left us disappointed. So, we wondered IF we would ever find GREAT Italian in the Smokies. AND I am happy to report that we did!
I ordered the Lasagna and my husband ordered Chef Paul's Seafood Medley. We also got the mozzarella sticks. Our meals were served with a salad. Everything was delicious. My husband enjoyed the garlic rolls...said they remind him of garlic knots he used to get in NY. I really enjoyed the marinara sauce with the mozzarella sticks and the lasagna is the BEST I have ever had. My husband thoroughly enjoyed his seafood medley that consisted of scallops, shrimp, baby hard shell clams and linguini noodles. The Medley had a thin red sauce that made it appear like a big bowl of seafood soup. He REALLY loved it.
From start to finish our meals were unbelievble. I can't wait to go back! Sometimes were have seen LONG lines, but I can tell you that it is worth the wait. The food is amazing, the portion sizes HUGE and the prices decent. This place is a MUST!

Pricey but fantastic!
by Tony Sapp (44 reviews)
July 29, 2009 - Likes it
We sat outside on the patio, the weather was great.
Our order was 2 lasagnas ( 16.99 each) 1 large 2 topping pizza (24.00 ) a dozen garlic rolls (6.00) and 3 soft drinks. The tab came to $80.00 before tip.
The food and service was excellent in all aspects!
The portions are huge the large pizza was enormous. The lasagna could easily serve 2 .
We were still eating the pizza lasagna and rolls the next day.
Three nights later we got a medium pizza w/ 2 toppings to go for $19. the medium is bigger than most chains extra large. We did not want to send an extra $10 of soft drinks plus tip.
Can't wait to go back for more pizza!!

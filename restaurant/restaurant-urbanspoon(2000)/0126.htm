HORRIBLE service
by Jenn T. (18 reviews)
May 21, 2009 - Doesn't like it
This is one of the worst restaurants I've ever been to, in terms of service. Food is okay. First time was too salty; second time we never got seated (with a reservation).
The first time I went with my boyfriend. It was crowded but I don't remember waiting too long before being seated. Our waiter seemed disinterested in serving us, acted as if it was a chore. The food wasn't bad but VERY salty and we thought that perhaps it was due to the fact that the place was busy so we decided to give it a try some other day.
The second time we went was a Friday, and as we had more people, I called and made a reservation for 5 at 8pm. Three of us got to the restaurant first, and I went in to check in with the hostess. I let her know that two more are coming but they're very close. Just walking over from a few blocks away. She told me that it'll only be a couple more minutes and will seat us when it's ready. Great, thanks! I told her and that we'd wait outside as there's really no waiting room inside. The other two friends arrived but they stood to the side for a cigarette (out of the view of the restaurant). We waited. we saw that our table was ready; then we saw a party of four walk in, and we saw the hostess giving our table away. I went in and asked when our table would be ready (this is about 20 minutes of waiting). she said that she had to give our table away since not everyone was here for our party. i asked her how she could have known that we weren't here when she never bothered coming out to check. she didn't care so we left.
funny thing was, the first time i went in there to check in with her, there was a table of two who had just paid and were ready to leave. i overheard the woman saying to the guy whether it really was okay to not leave a tip, and the guy was SO mad that he just said, let's get out of here...
We went to Chinatown and got awesome service, and awesome food

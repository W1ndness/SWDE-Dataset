Best Pub - Japanese Style...
by Ken O. (29 reviews)
May 20, 2009 - Really likes it
Be patient, the grub is worth waiting for. I like the many choices, the style allows you to sample a little bit of this and a little bit of that. The hamachi handrolls and dynamite tuna are fantastic. The yakitori stuff is salty, but goes great with your favorite ice cold Japanese Beer (mine is Sapporo). After indulging on the varieties of appetizers, try the tempura shrimp udon, it is the best soup base for udon in the OC. Oh and did I mention they are open to 1am! Not too many of those places in the Irvine and Tustin area.

It give you lots of odd/different, well prepared, well seasoned dishes to try. I suggest that you look over at the next table and take a risk and say I want one of those.

One of My Favorites
by Ryebread (6 reviews)
April 24, 2010 - Likes it
I'm not huge on Asian cuisine but for my money this is one of the best and as far as I can tell one of the more authentic Japanese restaurants in Orange County. Honda-Ya opens at 5 and stays open well past midnight so it's a perfect after drinking spot. Located in a quaint little area known as Old Town Tustin, Honda-Ya is my go-to spot to get my sushi and miso fix.

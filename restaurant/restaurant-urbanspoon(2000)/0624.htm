Good and inexpensive
by Academic Foodie (3 reviews)
March 20, 2010
I call most Mexican food, outside of what I can find in Latino groceries or in the Southwest, "Mexican Mess": little of this, little of that, put cheese on it all.
This place rises above the crowd, however, with fresh ingredients and some distinct flavors. I got a combo: the chile relleno was nicely prepared, and the sauces distinct from the other two items. The tamal actually tasted of corn, too...a nice touch in a usually bland item that can be drop-dead great.
It's inexpensive and worth a bite. As great as Los Dos Molinos in Phoenix? No way. But better than most places that serve up Mexican Mess.
I'm going back again for another meal...it's also nice that you can sit at the bar if you are by yourself and now feel too blue about it :)

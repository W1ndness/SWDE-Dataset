Average
by Ricardo... (45 reviews)
October 16, 2009 - Doesn't like it
While Hanford is among my favorite valley communities, I'm still questioning the wide spread good reputation of this place. When I have been there, the service has been very slow, even when there were more employees on duty than customers present. Incredibly ordinary food, not really very good at all. Even the ice cream is no better than just ok; we can do better at the supermarket within walking distance of our home in Fresno. I've been there multiple times over many years due to convenience, when needing a (not so) quick lunch and may still go again. Mostly just when alone, working in the area.

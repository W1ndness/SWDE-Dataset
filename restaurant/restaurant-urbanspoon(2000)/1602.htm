Yummm!
by Steve Elmore (4 reviews)
April 21, 2010 - Really likes it
Always good! This is quintessential Austin. When not creating salads for customers, the counter staff are getting more tattoos. The place is always packed at lunch, but there are tables on the sidewalk. Love the Capitol Cobb salad especially, but have never had a bad one. Lettuce is always fresh, and the portion size is perfect.

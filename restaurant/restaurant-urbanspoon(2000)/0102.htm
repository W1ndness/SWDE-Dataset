Try the Pizookie!
by pugmama (44 reviews)
October 07, 2009 - Likes it
The service at BJ's can be hit or miss but has improved over time. I love the Berry Burst Cider and my husband and I keep coming back for the Pizookie (for the uninitiated this is a warm cookie with ice cream on top that comes in various flavors: chocolate chunk, peanut butter, cookies and cream, etc.) However, I have not found an entree there that's really good and believe me I've tried. Most of the time I try something it's edible and all but I don't think "yum!" A notable exception would be their pizzas which are great I just don't want to eat pizza EVERY TIME. We'll often split the huge nachos and leave it at that. If you want to have room for dessert, there's no way to fit in an entree in addition anyway.

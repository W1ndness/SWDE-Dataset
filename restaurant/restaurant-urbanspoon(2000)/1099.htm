Decent Sushi, GREAT Price
by Doug (12 reviews)
January 05, 2009 - Likes it
I have only been here for lunch. Not sure about prices and offerings for dinner. For lunch they have an all you can eat sushi buffet. When a friend drug me here I can VERY begrudgingly, after all, I've eaten at Asian buffets and the results were not good! And the thought of an old, dingy, dim Smorgi-Bob's style buffet serving sushi made me cringe. But I was very pleasantly surprised!! The place is new, clean, nicely decorated, very open and airy, and the sushi was surprisingly fresh. No this isn't a Mikuni by a long shot. But it's really good food for really cheap and it tastes - well - pretty damn good.
If you want elegant, top-rate sushi, don't bother. If you and your friends are hungry and want good sushi that won't break the bank - put Mizu on your list.

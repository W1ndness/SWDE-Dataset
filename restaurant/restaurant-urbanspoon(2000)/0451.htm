Stay away from the Hash
by Me (16 reviews)
September 25, 2008
I've heard nothing but good things about this place so it was with great joy that I decided to give them a try. It was breakfast time, so I got the egg/hashbrown/rice plate.
Service was quick and an understanding of english by the staff seemed strained - but friendly.
It was basically sticky/dry white rice, some strange egg square that was supposed to be scrambled eggs, and OMG the hash was awful. (BTW, my friend who I gave the food to thought it was "good"). I think it was $6.
I have never tried corned beef hash from a can (only "homestyle" - real meat, real potatoes, etc.) and I probably will never again. I like my eggs hot, made with cream/milk/butter, fluffy etc. I like my rice non sticky and full of flavor (prefer spanish rice, my version of course) and I like my corned beef hash homestyle (real meat, fresh potates, etc.).
I'll probably go back but only if someone else REALLY wants to go and they're going to buy my lunch/dinner.

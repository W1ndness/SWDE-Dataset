Wish there was a "neutral" voting option. Burger and fries with a shake were great. Nice tastes in their special sauce in combo with a thick vanilla shake. Great burgers right on the beach
by Britt (13 reviews)
August 13, 2010 - Really likes it
Teddys on kapahulu st. has a great nostalgic feel with surboard racks on the wall, and a giant mural on the walls depicting all the popular surf spots in waikiki. The best part is you can get a burger, fries, and drink for about $10. Try the original if you like traditional style burgers, or try the paniolo which has cheese, bacon, bbq sauce, onion ring, grilled pineapple, and teri sauce. (its a cross between the western and hawaiian burgers and en employee favorite)

ONION RINGS!!!!
by Me (16 reviews)
September 25, 2008
The majority of my experiences with Teddy's have been very good to excellent.
The hot tip here is to get the onion rings (upgrade or side) and the orange dream shake.
Just order the monster burger combo (small size) and ugrade to onion rings/shake.  It should be around $10-12 dollars.
Atmosphere is 50's fun and mostly clean.  It's nice to people watch from the booths by the window.  
Just an FYI - I believe that Duke's has the best burgers in town, so far that is.  
Sorry Teddy's.

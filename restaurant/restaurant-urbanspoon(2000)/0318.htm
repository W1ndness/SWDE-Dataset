Denver's Best Comfort Food
by suburbanhome (3 reviews)
July 15, 2008 - Likes it
Fried Catfish, Mashed Potatoes, Peach Cobbler, Jalepeno Corn Bread, Sweet Tea, Cheese Potatoes, Broccoli Rice, Chicken Fried Steak...these are all things that you can choose when you go to Tom's Home Cooking. Everything is delicious and the guys who run the place are super friendly. The best of the deep south in 5 points.

it's ok
by Devon B (12 reviews)
April 30, 2010 - Doesn't like it
The atmosphere is great, but the food is just ok. The fried chicken was a bit overcooked and over-salted. The fried cat fish was average - again, a bit overcooked. Mac n cheese was great. I'd accept a lunch invitation to join a friend, but wouldn't plan another visit on my own.

Thanksgiving called...they want their meal back
by Nate (4 reviews)
August 14, 2009 - Likes it
Holy Catfish! This tucked away gem is truly worth the effort to find in the middle of five points! Yes, white folk, its okay to venture out of the CCN district for some good home cookin.
Tom's offers meals you just cant find around these parts. The crazy thing is that, once they're out of food...they close!
Try the collard greens, cabbage, mac n cheese, fried chicken, it doesnt matter...its all fantastic. Dont forget to wash it down with a Strawberry soda and coca-cola pie. Bring cash though.

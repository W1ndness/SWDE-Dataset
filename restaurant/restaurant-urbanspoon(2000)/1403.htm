Not up to par...
by Jim C. (34 reviews)
August 15, 2009 - Doesn't like it
We've eaten here a few times, but after the last, it truly will be the last. The service wasn't up to par. I've had a difficult time ordering, since I really don't like the food or selections. Even my friend didn't care for it this last time.
The beers are good, especially the stouts and porters. The music is usually good on the weekends, and if you get a table by the window, the view is nice.
For beers in the evening, it isn't bad. Otherwise, I'd tend to stay away from lunch and dinner.

consistently disappointing
by thegoodstuff (3 reviews)
May 25, 2010 - Doesn't like it
When thinking of a place to eat with outdoor seating, Snow Goose never comes to mind- we were reminded of why after dining there again tonight. The service was sub-par, but more importantly the food was terribly disappointing. They are skimpy on featured ingredients (a problem our neighboring table also encountered) and the wait staff show no remorse. They do manage to fry their frozen fries well, but aside from the great view and good beer, there's nothing to keep this restaurant in mind.

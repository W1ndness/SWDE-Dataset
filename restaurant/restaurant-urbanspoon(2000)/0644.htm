Brownies
by GT (31 reviews)
December 09, 2009 - Likes it
I confess my favorite thing here is hard to come by. Its a brownie with some sort of marzipan topping. To die for just about sums it up in a somewhat cliched fashion. It is in-cred-i-ble. The thing I like least about this place is the styrofoam plates and plastic junk. The food deserves some nice white diner plates with a blue rim. It is on the first floor of a real nice deco apartment building. You can buy Italian groceries there too. But get the brownie. If you get a chance.

Pleasent compulsion
by Jeanie-chan (9 reviews)
April 29, 2010 - Likes it
Every time... EVERY TIME, I go down University off of 4th Ave. I have to stop into Time Market. Since I do a lot of wandering during our summer season, it is a refreshing rest point that provides AC. But as I am browsing/cooling off, I can't help but realize that I "really need" an off color labeled bottle of wine... or a pint of home-made potato salad... or some pizza... or a latte. I have never been disappointed by what this store provides. They also have some of the chilllest people working there, which allows the atmosphere to stay much cooler than the outside.

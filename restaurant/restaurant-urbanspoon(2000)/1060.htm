Well Built Food in Charming Setting
by jason s (23 reviews)
July 17, 2010 - Likes it
The GOOD: Fun variety of tasty dishes... something for everyone. Great atmosphere with legendary "bathtub salad bar". Historic Fan neighborhood. Lively bar. High "charm" factor. Famous breakfast. Little or no wait to be seated at dinnertime.
The BAD: Mobbed on weekend mornings for brunch (30-45 minute waits). Tiny waiting area. Street parking is limited -- you may have to park 3-4 blocks away.
VERDICT: Definitely worth the trouble for breakfast if you have guests from out of town... it's an experience. Otherwise wait till dinner and avoid the wait. Recommended!

Bathtub Salad!
by Krystal (41 reviews)
November 29, 2009 - Likes it
I've been to Strawberry Street both for their Sunday brunch and for a quick dinner. I must say that I'm a big fan of the buffet. Not only is it fun because their salad bar is a bathtub, but the quality of food is good, too. I'm a salad lover and as a result, I'm pretty picky about my salad bars. The one at Strawberry Street has great variety including fresh vegetables, fresh fruits, protein options, and more. In my many visits here, I never once experienced less-than-fresh options.
The one time I ordered a "meal" instead of opting for the salad bar, I had a chicken wrap. It was good, but nothing terribly special.
I recommend that you go and try out the salad bar at the very least!

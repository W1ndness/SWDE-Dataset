Upscale cafeteria.

Upscale cafeteria. You get in line point out what you want in the case or up on the board the help gives it to you you put it on your tray just like in school, you choose a drink you pay you try to find a table unless you already got it saved - smart. I usually get the three salad combo plate. Soup and sandwiches coffee are good too. They give you bread to go along with it all. The food is so healthy looking smelling and tasting. Desserts are a specialty and really do a good job negating the benefits of the healthy chow.

Cheesecake devine
by Joy Petersen (8 reviews)
May 12, 2010 - Likes it
Here's what you do.
1. Go with a friend.
2. Purchase 2 or 3 kinds of cheesecake.
3. Put the plates in the middle.
4. Dig in.
I split Key Lime Pie cheesecake and plain with berry preserves. Definitely rich and tasty.
I enjoyed the plain with berry preserves the best mostly because it wasn't overdone. Exactly what it is. Cheesecake. Preserves. Done. Sweet and rich, yet not overstated.
The Key Lime had a chocolate shell, which wasn't necessary. Lime and cheesecake are enough flavor to punch you in the jaw. The lime is smooth at first but gets you in the taste buds toward the end of your bite. Word to the wise: don't eat the decorative limes on the Key Lime Pie. It's unnecessary and the rinds will disturb you later.

Recommend | Not helpful

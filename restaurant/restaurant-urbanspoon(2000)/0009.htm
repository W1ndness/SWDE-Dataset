Overrated Breakfast
by Chef Dare (167 reviews)
April 26, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Hurricane's is a favorite of many here in Albuquerque. However, I find the breakfast's here to be HIGHLY overrated. The dining room is so compact that you feel as if you are sitting in your fellow diner's lap! The hideous red vinyl booths are set WAY to low for the table and your chin may as well be resting on your plate! Service was friendly, fast and efficient, but the food was...disappointing! Breakfast was boring, greasy and terribly overrated. The list of better restaurants for breakfast is endless: Mannie's, Weck's, Owl Cafe, Gold Street Cafe...

New Mexico Diner Food
by Merrie (2 reviews)
August 13, 2010 - Likes it
I love Hurricanes. Their food is consistently good and their prices are reasonable. It's not haute cuisine, but they don't claim to be an upscale restaurant, they're a New Mexico diner. Their green chile sauce is the best in town, hands down. I usually get the chicken burrito with extra green chile sauce. Mmmmmm! Their home fries are also very good. Their biscuits and sausage gravy are okay, but I'm used to Southern-style biscuits and sausage in Missouri, and Hurricanes' just don't stand up in comparison.

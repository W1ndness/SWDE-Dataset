Good South-Side Thai
by Charlie (183 reviews)
October 13, 2009 - Likes it
The Thai house is a more authentic and tasty alternative to the more popular Surin West in South Side. It's also especially nice as an alternative to Surin since there is hardly ever a wait. In general, the flavors are more pronounced here (as they should be), though perhaps the quality of the meat is not quite as good as Surin. Not the very best in Birmingham (that award goes to Taste of Thailand, easily), but certainly a pretty good establishment.

Very disappointing
by Dorkas (1 review)
November 09, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I signed up for an account on UrbanSpoon just to review this place. After all the great reviews, I had high hopes as I LOVE Thai food and Surin is just gross. However, the pad thai I got was nothing more than white rice noodles with fish sauce, in a small, dry portion, with a slice or two of scallion, no other vegetables or seasonings.
The fish flavor was so strong I ended up not eating most of it. If you have a craving for Thai food, I recommend going to Whole Foods and getting something frozen for half the price.

Great Value!!!
by Miller (5 reviews)
February 01, 2009 - Likes it
Thai House is a great value that is close to UAB / Southside. The dishes are not greasy, like other restaurants and the shrimp doesn't have a frozen taste.  The service at Thai House is really friendly with someone always making sure you water glass is full. Love the coconut and shrimp soup. The chicken noodle bowl is great too. Close enough to walk to from the hospital. Defiantly one of Birmingham's hidden gems!

by Spam (43 reviews)
March 09, 2010 - Likes it
Elmo's was great! The had excellent service, and the food was of exceptional quality. I had-and recommend-the Cranberry Granola pancakes. I was quite hungry, but the 3 thick pancakes cooked with granola & cranberries and topped off with cranberry sauce & a mountain of butter was more than I could eat. Excellent place to take the out-of-town friends for a late breakfast.

Out with a friend & took a shot...
by Jenni Gahr Schmidt (2 reviews)
May 09, 2010 - Likes it
I was in Philly metro for just a few nights and had the chance to catch up with an old friend and we both thought sushi sounded like a great idea for dinner.
We found Ooka via google maps based on what was close to my hotel... and it had good reviews, so off we went. We certainly weren't disappointed. The menu had tons of tempting choices, some appearing very traditional and some fusion.
I chose a simple chirash sashimi and my friend had (blanking) fish cooked in an amazing sauce. Both of our meals were served with miso soup & salad and a side of rice.
We tasted each others' dishes and were both happily met with delicate and amazing flavors.
The miso was most excellent with a good balance of flavors, plenty of seaweed and tofu to satisfy us both. It used a white miso base.
The salad was pretty much the standard tossed iceberg with slightly sweet ginger dressing, but it was just the way I like it.
My sashimi was all of very good quality, all the different fish pieces just melted in my mouth. I especially enjoyed the tuna that was very lightly seared on the outside with the inside still raw and tender.
Her cooked dish of fish and mushrooms was wonderful.
The rice that came with both of our dishes was lightly seasoned sushi rice, a nice compliment to the simplicity of our entrees. The presentation of our dishes was also beautiful. I wish I'd taken pictures.
I highly recommend Ooka in Willow Grove... made this midwesterner remember why sushi on the coast is so good. Though we have really good sushi in Columbia, Missouri, Ooka really is a cut above.

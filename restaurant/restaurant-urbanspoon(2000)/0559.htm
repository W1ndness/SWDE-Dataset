Message from Kanpai of Tokyo

Kanpai of Tokyo is the perfect place to take a group of friends or family for a great food and show. Kanpai of Tokyo is a Japanese restaurant that prepares all its food on Hibachi Tables that seat eight to ten people. Diners are treated to an extraordinary demonstration of food preparation by highly trained chefs. After watching the chef slice, chop, flip, and separate meat, seafoods, chicken, and vegetables, one wonders that they still have their figures!

Japanese Steak House, Sushi Bar & Lounge, Children's Menu, Filet Mignon, Hibachi Chicken, Hibachi Salmon, Hibachi Scallops, Hibachi Shrimp, Hibachi Steak, Hibachi Vegetables, Reservations Recommended, Yakiniku

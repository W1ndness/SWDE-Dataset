high quality all u can eat
by Bort (33 reviews)
November 23, 2008 - Likes it
I go here every year while I'm out in Vegas on my Birthday with co-workers. It's about 20 min from the strip, but if you have a car and want friggin' awesome and reasonably priced sushi,..it's worth the trip. If they had hot edameme vs cold, it would be a perfect 10.
There is a big U shaped central sushi bar that seats about 15 or more people. the chefs are highly trained and they have fairly good service.
The clientele here are people that know good food. The eel rolls, and of course the rainbow rolls are great,..i am a big avacado fan. Not sure you will get a better value in Vegas for sushi. The girl at the sushi shop in the Venetian recommended this place to me a few years back.

Sushi-Mon
by RDS (16 reviews)
December 10, 2009 - Really likes it
Las Vegas/Henderson? Check.
All you can eat? Check.
Sushi? Check.
Everything on the menu is game? Check!
Sushi-Mon was a surprise for me, when I first visited the Las Vegas location (I've been to the Henderson one, too). All I have to say is - WOW. All you can eat sushi and it's pretty dang good sushi to boot. My only little complaint is that by the time we ordered our next round of sushi and rolls, we had to wait. Like I said, little complaint.
Overall, the sushi was great. The smoked salmon, smoked albacore, and yellowtail were awesome. The 911 Spicy tuna wasn't as hot as I expected, but that's okay. And to finish off the meal with green tea ice cream was excellent. Love this place, and would go there again!

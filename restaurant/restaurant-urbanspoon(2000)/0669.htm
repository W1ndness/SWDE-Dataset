Thank You My Friends:)
by Dan I. Cook (98 reviews)
September 03, 2009 - Likes it
I want to thank my friends Talani and Birdman for recommending this cafe. What a place!
This restaurant is very good with EXCELLENT service. Everytime I put my water glass down someone was filling it up. They brought that dipping sauce (see pic) out with some bread and I went crazy. It's very heavy on the garlic and has a spicy kick to it. Man this stuff is good.
My buddy Birdman suggested that I then try the Tamarilo Juice. Never hear of this stuff, but it was way to sweet fo me. It was a brown colored fruit drink that I guess is popular. I didn't like it...sorry Birdman.
My meal came with two sides and for one side I picked a salad (see pic). The dressing was a housemade vingerette. It had a sweet flavor to it which made it taste unique. It was pretty good.
For my main meal I had the Chicken Breast Platter (see pic) . It was smothered in grill onions and I got a side of tostones. I squeezed a little lime on it and dug in for the kill. I think the favors were awesome and every other word out of my mouth was mmmmm lol.
Overall, this is a "Must See TV" type of place, if you catch my drift. Remember to tell them Dan sent you and Stay Hungry!

One of my favorites
by Ellen (8 reviews)
July 22, 2010 - Likes it
I work nearby so I go for lunch fairly often. Every Friday they have a Crab & Shrimp soup that is wonderful - it omes in 2 sizes, large & small. But today is Thursday so I ordered off the menu - Mariscada - soup - a tomato-based broth with everything from sea - 1/2 a crab, a huge prawn, 1/2 a filet of tilapia, shrimp, calamari, scallops. Served with a scoop of rice which i put into the soup,. It was amazing. I didn't think I'd be able to finish it but I couldn't stop and I cleaned out the bowl. My friend had the skirt steak with shrimp and said it was wonderful. The ambiance is lovely and the service is great. Definitely one of the best restaurants in Ft. Lauderdale.

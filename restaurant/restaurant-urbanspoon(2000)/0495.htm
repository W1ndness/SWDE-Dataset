Home away from home
by Lindsey (1 review)
February 25, 2010 - Likes it
Kickbacks is by far one of Jacksonville's most eclectic and yummy restaurant. I my self eat lunch here almost every day and have sampled just about everything on the menu. Everything from your standard fare bar food to gourmet dishes. But, even the bar food is top notch. The Thai chicken wings come in a amazing sauce and never greasy. Another treat is the sandwich the Fat Julian. A interesting mix of chicken fingers, french fries, fried cheese stick covered with marinara on a huge hoagie bun, sounds crazy but it is yummy. There are foods that are lighter fare and all just as nice. Kickbacks also has Jacksonville's largest selection of beers. With 60 or so on tap and another hundred by the bottle. But, best of all is the staff. They make everyone feel like family. Remembering you name and your favorite beverage. Good service is a rarity these days so I hold them in high regard. I recommend this restaurant to everyone. From a family looking to grab a quick bite to the foodies of America. Please do not pass up the opportunity to experience this amazing restaurant. And be will to stay a while. Live music, online trivia, ton's of flat screens and a weekly poker night makes it hard to leave on you sit down. Oh, and they are open like 22 hours a day and serve breakfast all day long.

Great food, crappy service.. depends what you are looking for!
by Shandi Lynn (6 reviews)
November 01, 2009 - Likes it
I have been coming here since it opened and have seen it go through so many changes. The menu is extensive and almost everything is fantastic. I love the Monte Cristo, the Roast Beef Panini and the Southwest wrap. The Breakfast burrito is delicious too and sweet potato fries are the best side dish. Unfortunately, do not come here if you are actually hungry, because you will probably have eaten your table before you get served. The service is by far the worst I have ever seen- the servers and staff themselves are really nice, they just never ever take your order and if you do get them to take your order, it will be at least another 30 minutes before you get your food.... and that is for a sandwich. I am afraid to order a steak or anything that requires a longer cooking time. The weird thing is, we have been coming here forever, the servers all know us by name/face, they should know by now that we ALWAYS tip at least 20%... wouldn't you want to actually give service to someone who is going to be nice to you and leave you a big tip??? I just don't get it... So, its up to you and what you are looking for- if you are looking for a great place to hang out and drink some beer and possibly even get the food you ordered, this is the place for you. If you are hungry and want to eat in the next hour and you are just coming here for lunch/dinner, do not bother- go somewhere else.

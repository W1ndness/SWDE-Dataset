I give it 3 stars...
by Jeff Eberhard (36 reviews)
April 13, 2010
Lunch today consisted of 1/4 lb of lean brisket and one link of sausage. With a drink and my Dell (10%) discount, the total was just under $7.
The brisket was dry, but edible once I put some of the Smokey Mo's Hot BBQ sauce on it. The sausage was very juicy and smokey tasting - a definite win.
There is a "salad bar" of jalepenos, onions, pickles, etc off to the side so you can get as much as you want of the toppings.
3 stars for dry brisket and a not-very-inviting atmosphere.

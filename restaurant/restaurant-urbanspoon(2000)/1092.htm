+ 3 more photos from Doug Crepeville
by Doug (505 reviews)
October 22, 2009 - Likes it
oooo Crepes! woohooo... Surprisingly not as good as I expected, but still real tasty. Cheap and delicious.
The crepe itself wasn't as perfect as a crepe really needs to be, however the basic elements, were all there and didn't fall short by all that much.
I would have liked more choices, not that their menu is small, however, I did feel limited for some reason. I would very much like to have had some sort of honey mustard chicken or something; for some reason... anything is possible with crepes, and I think they should tap into that just a tiny bit more.
I had them add chicken to the pesto, and chicken to the Greek crepe, both were outstanding and quite filling.
They have some good choices, I just wouldn't say they "have it all" as someone put it.
Tea served in a pot good for two, or three.
Decent atmosphere; your basic sit n go, order at the register kind of place.
--Bring cash, no credit--
-side note: REMEMBER TO PAY for parking, I was sick and it was a rainy day, so I failed to look at the signs when rushing into the creperie, the result was a cheap $25 ticket.
Really great, down to earth place with yummy crepes!

Best breakfast I've ever had.
by JeremyK (1 review)
September 26, 2009
I eat here at least twice a week and most of the time I make it in about three or four times and if they served breakfast all day, I would go there more often than that!
Chiloso blows Chipotle out of the water and anyone that says otherwise must work for a competitor or have gotten fired from Chiloso. The proof is in the pudding...read on.
This restaurant has been featured in D Magazine and was listed as one of the best values in the Dallas area for under $5 (breakfast).
I highly recommend that you make it in for breakfast and get the three pack of breakfast tacos. I'd suggest the potatoes (cooked with onions and super yum), bacon, cheese and jalapenos (if that's your thing).
They have about a billion different toppings to choose from: onions, jalapenos, tomatoes, potatoes, beans, eggs, roasted corn...you get the picture. They have bacon, chorizo, sausage or barbacoa as meat options and top it off with one of five different salsas/sauces.
Chiloso has been in Rockwall since 2006 and it's won Taste of Rockwall every year since it's been in business and there's a good reason why.
mmmm...I can almost taste it. Thankfully, I WILL taste it tomorrow because I go there every Sunday. If you don't try this place it won't bother me any. Maybe it'll keep the line of regular customers down to a manageable level :)

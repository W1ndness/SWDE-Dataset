A new twist on an old favorite!
by cosmopolite (2 reviews)
September 18, 2008 - Likes it
I heard of S'Mac about a year ago in a discussion stemming from my weekly ritual of going to Whole Foods and buying Macaroni & Cheese for my office's lunch meetings. The discussion was on the increasing tendency for a restaurant to specialize in one area and make new twists. A new restaurant called S'Mac was introduced and I knew I had to immediately try it. S'Mac offers a wide selection of different macaroni & cheese recipes, ranging from the classic mac 'n' cheese to some exotic variations. You can choose to eat in the restaurant, have it delivered, or take it on the go. Although the ambiance in the restaurant is great, it is a little cramped. So don't take more than a date to experience a true culinary delight!

Good comfort food!
by Krystal (41 reviews)
November 28, 2009 - Really likes it
Favorite establishment for comfort food in NYC. I always order the Garden Lite and have never had a bad experience with it. I do recommend that you tread carefully when making your own creations. My boyfriend tried to build his own mac and cheese and it turned out to be a terrible combination. You might want to leave the creativity to the chefs.
The prices might seem a bit steep for macaroni and cheese, but that's NYC for you. Trust me, you aren't likely to need anything bigger than the "nosh" size. It might look small at first, but that thick cheese will hit you like a brick sooner than you think.
It's a bit out of the way to get to if you don't live in the immediate area, but it is certainly worth the trip if you like good comfort foods.

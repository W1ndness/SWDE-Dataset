Message from The Glenwood Gastro Pub and...

Atlanta's 'Best Gastro Pub', Wine Bar & Live Jazz

  • Live Jazz on Wednesdays
  • Beautiful Outdoor Patio
  • Wine Bar

The accolades keep coming for this chef-driven Gastro Pub in the East Atlanta Village.

Named 'Best Gastro Pub' in 2007 by Atlanta Magazine, The Glenwood has also been featured as one of the 'Twenty Great Meals Under $25.' Most recently, The Glenwood was named one of the 'Fifteen Best New Restaurants' by Atlanta Magazine. Chef Derek Evans presents an eclectic, seasonally-driven menu focusing on local and organic products. This neighborhood pub with a garden deck also offers a thoughtfully-crafted wine list in their recently expanded non-smoking, wine bar with over 50 wines-by-the-glass and over 150 beers. The new wine bar celebrates both old world and new world wines in a decidely hip and low-key environment. The artisan meats and cheeses are a perfect pair for any of the wine selections. Live Jazz every Wednesday with no cover.

Great Sunday Brunch
by LoveVera (9 reviews)
December 22, 2009 - Really likes it
My friend and I discovered The Glenwood for brunch this past Sunday because we were in the area and noticed their "Brunch from 11am - 3pm" chalkboard sign. I had the Logger's Skillet, which is bacon, potatoes and eggs all drizzled in syrup. Absolutely scrumptious!
The only downside is that it is a pub, so there was smoking at the bar. We were able to find a table in the corner to escape the smoke fumes.

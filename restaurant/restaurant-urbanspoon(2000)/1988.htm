Best Melijano Salata EVER.
by Abbycakes (2 reviews)
August 30, 2009 - Really likes it
Melijano Salata (eggplant spread) - I've been eating this since I was 3 (I'm 28 now) best one ever. I do wish they would add a bit more pita with the order. I've tried similar ones in other places, in other cities - NOTHING compares. Warning: do not eat this if you plan on talking, kissing, getting intimate with anyone afterwards. Very garlicky but tastes soooo good. I go here solely for this. But they do have some other great items, I like their soluvaki, veggie gyro, and the greek pizzas are great! The pasta dishes - sometimes they are great, sometimes needs work but I'll always love this place as long as they have my eggplant spread!

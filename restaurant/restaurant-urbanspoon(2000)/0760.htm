folksy at home
by Trudy Studebaker (6 reviews)
February 03, 2010 - Likes it
I live in Ottawa Canada. Make a point of attending this place when in NY. The serving a very large. Meant for 3 or more. The provide side plates with the main platters so that one can take smaller portions and share with companions. Home cookings style. Sweet sausages are fabulous. The mini-canole for desert are winners if one still has room for desert. This place is large but fills quickly during peak hous. Go during off hours or make a reservation.

If you want to talk, go at lunch
by Bill B (3 reviews)
January 08, 2008
It's a different restaurant at lunch.  I NEVER go in the evening anymore because it's an impossibly long wait for a table, and the acoustics of the place combined with the crowds make it very challenging to hear each other.  
But at lunch, not only are the portions a more manageable size, it becomes gracious, refined dining.

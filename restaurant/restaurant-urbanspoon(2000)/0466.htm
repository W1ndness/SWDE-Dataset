Fancy recipe but lack of good ingredients
by SeriousFood (1 review)
February 22, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Visited on Sunday. Lots of ppl. Menu seemed to be interesting w/ more than 30 different kinds of rolls.
After my sushi and sashimi combination arrived, I immediately found out the fish being used on the plate are all HEB/Kroger standard including frozen tuna, crab imitation meat, salmon, and pre-frozen white tuna.
Fresh fish should melt in your mouth in no time. But I had to chew through the fish to get it swallow down my throat.
Totally not Japanese. I would say it's more like a Chinese/Japanese restaurant .

Awesome!
by Matt Neighbors (23 reviews)
April 18, 2010 - Likes it
My wife and I tried this place out for a Sunday lunch, based mostly on Urbanspoon reviews. This place is awesome! The decor is subtle but modern, the dining room is cozy and the people are friendly and fun, and the lunch special prices are quite good.
I had the sushi/roll combo, and the eel roll was very good, as was the seared tuna sushi and eel sushi.
The only thing I didn't like was the smoked salmon. The flavor was very pungent and overbearing. Not authentic at all.
Definitely check this place out!

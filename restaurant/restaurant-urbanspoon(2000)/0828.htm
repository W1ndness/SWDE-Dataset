Excellent Food
by Lisa Guinn (80 reviews)
November 22, 2009 - Likes it
The food here was not just good; it was expertly prepared. I had the Sambusak Dajaj, which is filo dough stuffed with chicken, mushrooms, onions, etc. The texture and flavor were remarkable. My SO had the Bamieh -- lamb shank cooked with tomatoes and okra. It was also wonderful, with the meat still on the bone and the sauce thick and flavorful. Why haven't we eaten here before? I don't know, but we will definitely be back.

Have it YOUR way!
by KungPow (1 review)
February 19, 2010 - Likes it
Every time I go into Baja Jack's, there is always a line of people waiting to order. That is a good thing, because there is a reason people are always waiting to order! Baja Jack's is definitely different with the Southern California Style atmosphere and taste. The most popular item is the build your own burritos. While your waiting to get in, you fill out a form, choosing from all the selections of your own custom burrito. Then there is the famous cold salsa bar where you can mix up your own custom concoction from the variety of salsas and spices. I take my friends to Baja Jack's and they always come back!

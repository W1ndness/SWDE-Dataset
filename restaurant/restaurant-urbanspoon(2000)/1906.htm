Wonderful.
by Katie (10 reviews)
May 28, 2009 - Likes it
I went to Bertucci's over the weekend, and was very impressed. My family loved the sausage pizza they ordered. It was flat crust with tons of gooey cheese. The sausage was flavorful and fat. For myself, I ordered a sausage dish with consisted of penne, green and red peppers and this special kind of sauce. The sauce worked perfectly with the sausage, which was juicy and delicious, unlike regular sausage. I found the restaurant clean and welcoming.

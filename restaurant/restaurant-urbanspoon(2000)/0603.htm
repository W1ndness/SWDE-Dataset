Mediocre at best
by 2deirdre (12 reviews)
May 26, 2008 - Doesn't like it
Okay, the place has a nice patio.  The service is fine, and my waitress was very helpful.  I began with a Bloody Maria, but it was a far cry from anything good.  In fact, the bartenders are not bartenders; haven't a clue how to pour a drink.  The Bloody Maria was watery, practically without tomato juice, and so many spices you couldn't taste anythig else.  I believe most people happen to like this drink because they like tomato juice, but I could be wrong.
I ordered the imitation Cobb Salad.  I believe they have some cutsey name for it: Billy Bob Cobb Salad.  Bob Cobb is rolling over in his grave.  Served with that MDF turkey or chicken - unknown ingredients with little taste, and worst texture imaginable.  I hate the stuff.  However, that was my fault; I should have asked the waitress if they served REAL turkey or chicken.  The vinegrette dressing was not bad, however.  No crackers, rolls or bread served with it.
After dinner, a Mexican Coffee.  Again, the worst.  My server took it back, handed it to a different bartender, and I got the same exact drink: tequila, coffee and phoney whipped cream.  Ou est la Kahlua? J'ne sais pas.  I had even told the waitress, just have them add a little more Kahlua, or whatever coffee liqueur you're serving.  No dice.
This restaurant is on a par with most of the eating establishments in the Los Feliz area, and it's a shame.  I'd like to see someone open a place that has a rreally good chef and a management that knows that there are a few of us who refuse to eat at MacD's, and really appreciate relaxing after working all day, enjoy a cocktail, and be served a truly tasty and satisfactory meal.

ALl COmers haVE not been sorry! (cheesy, I know)
by Kennedy (3 reviews)
December 08, 2008 - Likes it
Alcove would be perfect if it wasn't so damn popular! There's ALWAYS a line (I guess that's a good thing) and finding a table can be a bit of a chore during peak hours but if you're willing to brave all of that, your stomach will thank you. The salads, burgers and many desserts are all I've ever eaten there but they are, one for one, some of the best I've found in all of Los Feliz. There's also a cheese shop attached to the main restaurant that makes sandwiches known to knock diner's socks off. All in all, Alcove is a wonderful addition to the many delicious eateries in the area. Try it now before the lines get any longer!

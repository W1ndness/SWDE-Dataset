Horrible
by chickenguy40 (1 review)
June 27, 2010
Drinks were weak, bar tab was wrong and food was very disappointing. Mac and Cheese tasted like Stouffers and the quality of the Mahi and Steaks have gone down hill. I can get better quality at Publix. Don't waste your time on the fondue dessert. The chocolate is not properly melted and very waxy. The poundcake tasted like freezer burn.
Service was decent at first until the app's came and then we had to chase some help down. Returned a dish to the kitchen and our server didn't even know. Management tried to correct issue and was compliant but not caring.
This restaurant has obviously fallen on hard times as there wasn't even a wait on a Saturday night and there were empty tables throughout the evening. What was once part of our regular repertoire has seen us for the last time as it progressively becomes more disappointing.

Not worth the wait
by Joe Bitson (4 reviews)
December 27, 2008 - Doesn't like it
I have visited Mikuni’s many times in the last several years and have rarely been disappointed in the food. I have noticed that they have a lot of rules and regulations posted on their menu and have a real problem with reservations and large groups. Today I called both the Fair Oaks and Roseville locations to see if I could get reservations for a group on 10. I was told at the Roseville location that they didn’t take reservations and the Fair Oak location said they could not accommodate me. Wow!  With restaurants folding up left and right, here is a place that tells me they don’t want my business. When a restaurant tell me they have more business than they can handle it is time to find a place that appreciates my business. I called Samuari Sushi in Rancho Cordova and they said they could accommodate me with no problem. They have the opposite attitude. They recognize that that every customer is important not matter how good they think their food is! The experience was way better at Samuari and the food was just as good.  Why stand in line at Mikuni when there are many great places out there that need and appreciate your business.

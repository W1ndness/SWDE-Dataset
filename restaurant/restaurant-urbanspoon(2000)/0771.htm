The "pitts" is right
by Chris (1 review)
October 28, 2009
Worst ribs I've ever eaten. If you are a barbecue fanatic, you expect your ribs to be smoked, or at least grilled. They claim theirs are baked, which would be sin enough, but my half rack was gray, and coated with red sauce, with not a hint of bark or grill marks or anything. The meat had been boiled in vinegar water. Taste was flat, texture mealy. When I showed the woman at the counter my half-eaten rack and asked if this was how they were supposed to be, she replied yep. And the heck of it is, the pork sandwich I ordered was fine - definitely smoked. So at the very least, avoid the ribs. Better yet, avoid any barbecue joint where the burger is the best thing on the menu.

Local Favorite / Casual Wonder
by rguess (2 reviews)
December 02, 2009 - Likes it
Outdoor dining under towering trees... it does not get much more relaxing than this local favorite. The Leaping Lizard Cafe serves fresh, local, healthy fare including daily local seafood specials, crisp salads, sandwiches made on fresh, homemade bread (baked daily), and delicious grilled flatbread. The food is kept so fresh that they do not even have a freezer!

Beer good, food not so much.
by subterr (42 reviews)
January 17, 2010 - Doesn't like it
This was my first visit here and it seems after reading reviews that my experience is a pretty common one. I sampled a few different beers and they were all good. They also have a good selection of hard to find bottled "guest" beers that change every month. The food on the other hand was not so great. I had a burger with onion rings. The burger was overcooked and the onion rings (all three of them) were undercooked. The $11.50 for this burger is about twice what it should have been for what I got. The fact that they source many ingredients locally is awesome but they need to get someone in the kitchen that knows how to cook. It's not that hard to make great food when your menu is so small.

Average brew pub
by Leo Bloom (10 reviews)
November 25, 2008 - Doesn't like it
I am not sure why the popular allure of this standard Brew Pub -- the food is average to poor; the beers are good, but not as good as those at Grizzley Peak just down the road, and the atmosphere is loud and (often) highly intoxicated. But the place is always packed, and it is friendly. Many of the local groups and clubs that hold meetings downtown often use this as their after-meeting drink place, often making it even more crowded and more noisy. Not a place I would normally frequent for either food or for drinks.

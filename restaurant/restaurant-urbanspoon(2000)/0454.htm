Message from Hy's Steak House

The Premier Honolulu Steak House Dedicated to Exceptional Quality

A Perennial winner of Honolulu Magazine's Hale 'Aina Award (the people's choice). Hy's Steak House, one of the best known steakhouses in Hawaii, has been a tradition in Waikiki for a quarter century and continues to be a favorite with visitors and locals alike.

Kiawe broiled steaks & chops, prime rib, pepper corn steak, veal chop forestiere, roast rack of lamb, fresh hawaiian fish, scampi Sicilian, succulent lobster & scallops, USDA prime steaks, famous caesar salads, flaming desserts, award winning wine list, live entertainment

Five Star Five Guys
by Haley Stephan (1 review)
November 17, 2009
On a budget, yet craving a delicious burger with whatever you want on top? Cooked in front of your eyes you can hear the sizzling of your burger with all of the free topping you can imagine. Your nostrils flare, mouth salivates and stomach screams for food while you wait in line. College and high school students crowd the open tables, as well as young families with children. Everyone loves the burgers at Five Guys. Many fans visit frequently this well known burger joint, even President Obama in May stopped in for a burger and fries (and the President of the United States can dine anywhere he wants and he chose Five Guys).
If you are lucky enough to know where a Five Guys restaurant is located, this should be a prime destination if anyone ever hungers for a hamburger, cheeseburger, bacon burger, or even the infamous bacon cheeseburger. Opening its first restaurant in 1986, there are now more than 400 Five Guys locations including Pennsylvania, Colorado, California, and Georgia. You can go to www.fiveguys.com to find a restaurant near you. The menu includes hamburgers, cheeseburgers, bacon burgers, bacon cheeseburgers, little hamburgers, little cheeseburgers, little bacon burgers, little bacon cheeseburgers, Kosher hot dogs, cheese dogs, bacon dogs, bacon cheese dogs, veggie cheeses, grilled cheeses, regular fries, large fries, plus regular and large drinks. If you order any type of burger or hot dog there are a variety of toppings that you can choose, such as A-1 sauce, mayo, pickles, green peppers, relish, lettuce, fried onions, sautéed mushrooms, ketchup, tomato, mustard, jalapenos, barbecue sauce, and onions. No order is complete without fresh, hand-cut french fries. While you wait for your meal to be cooked, you can snack on complimentary peanuts.
The prices of these burgers range from the simple one patty hamburger for $3.29 to the double patties of a bacon cheeseburger for $5.79. Also with the sizes as they go up so does the caloric intake per meal, the little hamburger scoring 480 calories while the bacon cheeseburger has a total of 920 calories. Regular fries cost $2.59, while large fries cost $4.19. The fountain drinks or lemonade can be placed in a regular cup costing $1.59 or large cup costs $1.79. While the burgers, fries, and a drink prices may seem higher than what you could get at McDonald's there is no close comparison to the quality of these burgers.
The ambiance of this restaurant is noisy, so a close intimate date may be better suited with a takeout to a romantic location. Five Guys is all about the community, drawing people in and having them talk and socialize with each other is their aim. The music plays and inspires conversation along with their open non-booth environment which encourages talking between tables. The kitchen is seen by everyone. The ordering is placed before the kitchen and is open so the cashier can tell the makers of the food what the person requested. You can actually watch the preparation of your food so as to guarantee your satisfaction and can witness its cleanliness.
This "show all" style reveals the importance that Five Guys places on cleanliness. Cleanliness is important for many, no one wants to eat contaminated food or see things that should not be part of the food making process. In addition, their products do include peanuts and using peanut oil for their cooking which is why the restaurant cautions patrons about removing peanuts from the restaurant as to not contaminate those who are allergic outside the store. They are conscious of the community and it shows through the many hazards that are posted before entering the restaurant and before leaving.
The amazing taste of the patties is unlike any other competitive burger sellers and the bacon is actually crispy. Before consuming these amazing morsels make sure to have a handful of napkins. Because these amazing burgers are greasy enough to be a juicy and moist burger yet not too much that it oozes out everywhere. Make sure to claim the extra half cup of french fries that were placed in the bottom of the bag complimentary of your purchase.
If you are looking for an upscale burger area good luck finding quality burgers at a fancy restaurant that will be as good as Five Guys. A meal at Five Guys is low in price, high in flavor with a great atmosphere of a burger joint anyone would want to feast inside. At Five Guys cravings for perfection are cured.

Not great...
by Mariposa (59 reviews)
April 27, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The food is fine if you've never eaten really good middle eastern food. Otherwise, you'll be disappointed. The chicken kabob I ordered was bland and uninspiring, the rice was mediocre, and the odd medley of vegetables was out of place and probably from a frozen bag mix. The best food I tried was the hummus, and the warm pita was pretty good, too. I won't be returning.

Best Hummus in Indy.
by Corey Dalton (28 reviews)
April 14, 2009 - Really likes it
Sorry, Santorinini.... Your baba ganoush is my favorite, but Bosphorus serves up the best hummus I've had outside of Israel. On top of the hummus (sometimes literally), Bosphorus features a number of other delicious dishes such as adana kebabs, gyros (although don't expect a Greek gyro), and stuffed eggplant. As for the prices, the dinner menu is reasonable, but their two-course lunch menu is a bargain. Oh, and the owner is extremely personable and friendly as well.

My Turkish Delight
by Ellen John (2 reviews)
October 12, 2008 - Likes it
From the first moment I entered the door, I was in love.  The short hallway set the mood.  A handsome waiter seated us near the window.  Tables were set with white tablecloths.  The different dishes were explained in the printed menus.  The restaurant is in what was an older home with a beautiful ceiling, painted floor design, and walls rich in subtle colors.  The music finished the scene.  The bonus was watching Turkish tv.  Their news proves we are more alike than different.
     What a perfect setting for a marriage proposal.  Guys, she never forget it.

A love affair!
by Ellen John (2 reviews)
December 14, 2008 - Likes it
The romance began as soon as we stepped through the door.  The front dining room is my favorite with wooden floors touched by a loved painted design.  The walls are a luxorious green and the ceiling is to die for.  
The crisp white talblecloths brought a smile to my face.  It beats those paper dingies that so often are use elsewhere.
Since I love lamb, where else would I find it done so well?  The lentil soup is a lure for me.  Best of all, the owner and all of his employees are sincere in wanting the diner's experience to be top-notched.
My one wish is that someday lamb shanks will be offered.  When that happens, bet on seeing me there two or more times a week!    Ellen H.

Fabulous fish!
by Maureen (11 reviews)
February 18, 2010 - Likes it
We were seated at exactly the time that the hostess estimated, and our cocktail waitress then server promptly appeared. Our table on the deck was lovely in the balmy night air. Three of the four of us ordered the monchong, two of them cooked "Kimo-style" and mine was parmesean crusted. We all agreed it was a delicious, light, flaky fish, but the parmesean crusted style was the favorite of the table. Initially we thought it was a bit pricey, but in comparison to other Lahaina restaurants for a similar fish, it was competitive. All entrees come with a ceasar salad, which was forgettable, but the fish was the highlight. We highly recommend this stop for dinner.

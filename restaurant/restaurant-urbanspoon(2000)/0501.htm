I have to say the food is of good quality. I am not a sushi fan, so going to a sushi restaurant for the food is rare for me. I usually will only go to a sushi place when I am with someone who has a craving. This place; however, is an exception.
Opened in the new area called oakleaf, this place offers great food, and from what I can judge from my sushi lover friends, good sushi.
The management/owners take great pride is customer satisfaction. The environment is fantastic.
Overall, I would HIGHLY recommend this restaurant

Was driving out in the middle of brandonfield and I found this place waaaaaaaay back in there. I thought I'd give it a try since I was hoping to find a hidden sushi gem.
First the good...
Place was clean, waitstaff was very friendly, the atmosphere was on par with other sushi places.
Now the not-so-good...
First off, the shrimp sauce is not good!
The standard rolls (JB, Cali, Philly, etc.) were very small and not rolled nice and tight. The $15.00 roll I ordered was ok, but we just spent way too much here for food that was just...ehhh????
I'd only go here if I was getting some kind of inncentive...like if I had a coupon/ gift certificare. Otherwise I'll stick to my favorite sushi place in all of JAX!!!

Good eats
by Tom E (1 review)
January 21, 2009 - Likes it
I took my son, who is not a big sushi eater. In addition to sushi (good size of fish on the salmon sushi I ordered), they also have bento boxes. We had teriyaki chicken and beef, and both were great. The bento boxes include your meat on white or fried rice, 2 pieces of California Roll sushi, a bed of veggies / noodles, and some pan-fried dumpling things. Large portion size, decent prices, and you can order ala carte sushi pieces.
Good for both sushi and japanese food lovers! Two chopsticks way up!

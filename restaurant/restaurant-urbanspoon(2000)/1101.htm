And breakfast, too!
by Barbara B (6 reviews)
June 28, 2009 - Likes it
We were following our noses last Saturday while downtown, which led us to Zocalo. I had no idea they were open for breakfast. It was great - great food, comfortable atmosphere, lots to watch on the street.
We both had carnitas with eggs and the chorizo potatoes. The carnitas were excellent, but the potatoes were the hit of our breakfast. Light and fluffy inside, crunchy outside, and the flavor of chorizo permeated throughout. With the cilantro rice, a total hit.
We'll be back!

Great food! Careless service!
by Michael G. Morgan (2 reviews)
August 04, 2010 - Doesn't like it
Let me begin by saying that the food here is most always terrific. That said, the dining area is extremely small and you will have to enjoy sitting VERY close to other tables. Forget private conversation! The staff also seems to have a rule about seating. There are 'four tops' and 'two tops' and, while all of the tiny two-seat tables are movable, NO effort will be made to seat a party of two, if only 'four tops' configurations are open! They make it clear that you can wait or leave! This exact scenario happened to my wife and I last night. We left. On a previous occasion, we had such an arrogant bad waiter - also the bartender that evening - that we asked for a different waiter. Even though we requested it, no manager or owner got involved. After that experience, I called the owners three times to share this experience, but never received a call back. As noted, this place has great food, but be willing to put up with a compromised dining experience. We eat here once it while, but usually make another choice.

Really enjoyed!
by adrienne (22 reviews)

My first time at Luna Maya, but have been wanting to go for awhile. It was pretty full tonight, but only had to wait a few minutes for a table. Service was attentive and quick.

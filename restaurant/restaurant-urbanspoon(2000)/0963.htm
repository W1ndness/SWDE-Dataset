NICE
by rupert (15 reviews)
December 17, 2009
This is one of the only thai restaurants in Maricopa county that ive return to over and over again. The food quality is consistent. and their not skimmpy on the vegetables. i usally order red or green curry with veg. a nice thick curry sauce great coconut flavor and lots of veg. they have brown rice too which is a plus as some thai restaurants dont offer it. the food here is not spicy at all so if your looking for a more authuentic this may not be the place. But give it a try any way the food is very tasty. Portion sizes are a little small. but over all this place has great thai food.

Fair to say... Thai Basic
by Mr. Creosote (5 reviews)
February 22, 2010
Good some would say... I'm not really feelin' that. This place is fair, just fair. And definitely a lot of effin' hype. Sure the food is not bad (no sparkley magically taste-bud juice inspirings going on though) and the lunch prices seem fair but if you try to lunch there it's really obnoxious and definitely not wheelchair accessible. It isnot set up for relaxed dining. Where with some places' the smallness adds to its charm Thai Basil seems more of a 'here's your gruel now gimme yo' money and GTFO! please' *bow with no eye contact* 'thank you' *bow with no eye contact* kinda place.
For Thai in Tempe, Lemon Grass is so much more tempting and tantalizing and in every terrific kind of way. It's almost like a happy ending tabletop side but you know... you have clothes on and are eating food and drinking tea and clean up with warn wash cloth... isn't as messy.

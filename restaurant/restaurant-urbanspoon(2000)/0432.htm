A Connecticut Staple
by Adam (1 review)
August 26, 2009 - Likes it
For locals and folks "in the know", Capitol Lunch is a must visit meal destination. Capitol Lunch's claim to fame is their hot dogs with the famous sauce, which they have been preparing for customers since 1929.
The sauce is a family secret. It's not hot, and there are no beans - just meat and spices. If you want to order like an old pro, get a hot dog with "everything" - that's the dog, sauce, mustard, and fresh chopped onion.
At under $5 (including tax) for 2 hot dogs with everything and a large drink, it makes for a great lunchtime pit stop.

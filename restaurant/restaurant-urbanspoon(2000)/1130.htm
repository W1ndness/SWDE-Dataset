File under "Family Fun"
by Super Ego Id (3 reviews)
November 21, 2009 - Really likes it
After more than a handful of visits to Willies, I can comment on the average experience. On the whole, Willies consistently delivers tasty food prepared well (never had anything undercooked, oversalted, or just plain bad). It's not amazing gourmet cuisine, but it is great grillin' and chillin' food. Nice proportions, enough extras, and a broad selection of things to chow down on (in other words- there should be something here for everyone, even finicky little mouths). A big old vat of beer on ice greets you at the door, which jives well with the toned down casual look & feel of the place. You can bring the family in their shorts and sandals and messy hair. And while the grown-ups are relaxing in the comfortable air of the patio, the kids can practice their social skills in the large central sandpit. It's easy to keep a watchful eye on the youngsters (though some parents are apparently less observant than others!). Yep, it's surely a great place for the family to go for food and fun. Personally, I love the hot dogs with ice cold beer. The grilled fish selections are pretty good too!

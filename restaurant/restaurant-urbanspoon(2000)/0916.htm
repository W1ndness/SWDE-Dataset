Very bad service....
by adventureDiner (3 reviews)
January 28, 2008
The food was 6 out of 10.. (10 being the best)  But the service was horrendous!  It was a Tuesday night and we were seated in the outside patio.  It wasn't incredibly busy or anything... The waiter took about 10 minutes to get us water.  Another 15 minutes to get our appetizers, which .. I guess was ok.. But it took an hour for us to get our sushi.  ( I think they had to go catch the fish themselves.)  An hour.. ??!! C'mon!  We actually got so tired of waiting , that we had it packed to go.  I live very close to the restaurant, but I have only been to this restaurant 2 times.  I'd much rather go to AMura's which is further away from me.  
The second time I went there, I sat inside.. the service was a bit better.  But the sushi still took a while to come.  But it wasn't  an hour.
Its a hip atmosphere, where the pretty people go I guess, but I was just looking for good food. =)

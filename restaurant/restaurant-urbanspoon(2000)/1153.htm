This restaurant is one that is a must visit for anyone that truly enjoys Spanish delicacy. We will eat here again, and I suggest everyone else should try it as well.

AMAZING!
by jlopez86 (1 review)
April 06, 2009 - Likes it
I do not live in Sand Diego, however I do visit quite frequently. Whenever I find myself in the city I make a point to visit Costa Brava. I was so very suprised by the number of locals that have never eaten here and make sure I introduce it to everyone I meet. The last time I was there was with my family and they were so accomodating to my 7 year old nephew. The staff treated us like we truly were family. The owner recognized me from my past visits and made sure to come and say hello. I was amazed by this since it was at least a year in between visits. Everytime I have been to COasta Brava the staff has been amazing. I will usually order a few Tapas (yum) and a pitcher of Sangria to start the night off. Then on to the Paella. This was truly my reason for coming to this restaurant in the first place. I had been craving Paella for months and since we are lacking a suitable Spanish restaurant where I live, I made the trek to Costa Brava. I was blown away by the quality of the Paella. I have not had Paella this good since I visited the Valencia regian a few years ago. It truly took be back. I would highly recommend this resataurant to anyone who has not been here before. Believe me, you will adore the restaurant. Can't wait for a business meeting in a month when I get to go back and enjoy the Paella again!

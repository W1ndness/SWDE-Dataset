Rioja taught me fine food.
by ghost (76 reviews)
October 21, 2009 - Likes it
I love this place. I think my boyfriend and I first went to this restaurant on my birthday in 2004. It is always such a treat to eat here. Chef Jasinski really knows her stuff, and every dish I have tried in her restaurant I have enjoyed. The service is well-trained and professional - there when you need them, absent when you don't.
I recommend this restaurant to any person seeking a great dinner, good wine, excellent service, and an enjoyable evening. I plan to return here again and again and again.
PS: The bar is a beautiful copper slab, and you can have dinner there too. Perfect for solo evenings in Denver.

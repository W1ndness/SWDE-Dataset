by Ash (7 reviews)

I have been a Edgar's fan for a long time and even though some of my family like the "other" bakery in town this is my fav! The red velvet is to die for and they even made my wedding cake (red velvet of course for a Christmas wedding). I have never had a bad experience here and have even walked in and got a personalized b'day cake that was yummie! This is the best bakery in B'ham and will always be my go to shop.

mobile review
by John Dahle (2 reviews)
July 31, 2009 - Likes it
I eat here regularly as it is in walking distance from where I work. The food here is well made, and the environment is conducive to conversation or having a quiet meal alone. I especially like that they have wifi and you can get a serviceable cup of coffee while you work. Outdoor seating is available in a garden-like setting. The quiche here is really good as are the soups. You can actually get in and out in under an hour and have a relaxing lunch.

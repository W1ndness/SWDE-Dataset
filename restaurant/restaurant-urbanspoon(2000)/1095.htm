by Foodie (36 reviews)
October 27, 2009 - Likes it
A reliable midtown salad haunt. I find that I like this place most when it is busy...weird I know...but the sides are fresher and even if there's a wait it's not too long. Love the cheese bread...however, the mac and cheese is eternally bland. Like salads over the sandwiches and I adore the veggie sides (asparagus, brussel sprouts, cheesy broccoli!!! Oh my)

Downhill
by Nik (5 reviews)
August 04, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I've loved Jack's for years...until now. Each time I go for a salad I get more salad dressing than the visit before. The last two times I have been I have ordered 1/2 of a spinach salad and specifically asked the staff to go "easy on the dressing" and I asked nicely! I don't know if they just hate me or they have a seriously skewed idea of how to dress a salad! Both times I have gotten home with a salad that is soaked in dressing - so much dressing I can pour part of it out. I'm pretty bummed and had to throw that out there

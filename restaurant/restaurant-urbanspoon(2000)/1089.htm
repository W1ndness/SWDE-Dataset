Come hungry!
by LKD (35 reviews)
January 21, 2009 - Likes it
Agatina's is a lovely yet basic Italian place. The staff was helpful, attentive and very pleasant. Everything was well paced. We were very surprised by the amount of food you get for the money! I got the sample platter of Italian favorites that consisted of gnocchi, meat lasagna, ravioli and a meatball. This came with a cup of soup AND a salad, all for $12.50.
So if your looking for comforting Italian food that won't disappoint, then this is the place. Although I have to admit it is not our typical choice of cuisine or location, we will be back sooner or later for sure.

Recommend | Not helpful

Ok meal at Agatinas.
by Greg (13 reviews)
April 10, 2010 - Likes it
We went to eat here on a Friday night and it was about a 40 minute wait.. If you are in a hurry to go there on Friday which we werent but would probably not go back unless we went earlier. We sat in a back room which was nice and quiet. I ordered the ziti with ricotta and mozzarella in it. The nice thing about this was that both soup and salad are included. The portion is very good however for my taste the sauce was kind of bland and needed more seasoning. It was ok tasting however needed just a little more of a boost to it. My friend had the steak which was excellent. We would probably go there again however not on Friday but I would order something different to give the sauce a second chance maybe.

Recommend | Not helpful

Hefty, tasty and reasonable...
by Pepsival (4 reviews)
June 06, 2009 - Likes it
Spent my birthday here last year, with hubby and 3 adult kids. Service was great, even though they were having Christmas parties in the other room and seemed quite full. The menu is expansive, reasonably priced and you get great portions. The bread is a hefty italian bread, probably from a local bakery, and served warm, with oil or butter.
Usually a bit crowded and hard to get into - but well worth the wait. I dont think they are open Sundays, but not sure right now...

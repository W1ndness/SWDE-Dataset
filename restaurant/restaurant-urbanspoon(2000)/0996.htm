Love the staff, food, atmosphere
by Tom Cantwell (1 review)
November 03, 2009 - Likes it
I've been eating at the By-Ways once a week for over a year as it's just down the street from where I work. I have not had a bad meal here. The burgers are so big they have to be cut in half. The buns are toasted and smothered in sesame seeds, salads generous and I've gotten to know the staff by name. The malts are a throw back to more innocent times as are the cow pie sized chocolate chip cookies...ummmm! The Turkey burger my fav. Keep up the good work guys.

Recommend | Not helpful

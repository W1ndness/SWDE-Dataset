Opa is Fantastic
by John M. (3 reviews)
March 13, 2010 - Likes it
My girlfriend and I went here to introduce her Greek food after reading the recommendations here. We were quickly seated after ordering drinks were soon brought a plate with fresh warm pita bread and some really excellent tzatziki and hummus. We ordered a starter of stuffed grape leaves and roasted red peppers which were both delicious. I had the gyro platter and it was very good, not too spicy or dried out like you get at some places, and the salad and rice were very tasty. My girlfriend had the lamb souvlaki which was very tender and cooked to perfection. The portions were more than enough to fill us and unfortunately we did not have room to sample any of the desserts. The wait staff was very attentive without being a nuisance and we did not notice the noise issue that someone had said was a problem in another review. Overall we had a wonderful experience, in fact it was so good that we came back again two days later and again the service and food was again fantastic.

I Loved Opa!
by Kia Jeen (1 review)
February 13, 2010
I went to Opa with my mother and father for her birthday on the recommendation of a friend who had raved about the pita and hummus and the chicken gyro. We went just after the lunch rush.
I am now the one raving! She was right! The pita bread was moist and fresh, the hummus and tzatziki sauce were delicous. My mother ordered the gyro platter, my dad had the salmon, and I of course had the spicey grilled chicken gyro. The portions were generous, the food well seasoned, and the service prompt. The waitstaff and the owner were all charming and welcoming. I failed to mention the delicious baklava, a must have! I would definitely recommend Opa!

Oh tasty Joy!
by Deeeno (1 review)
April 12, 2010 - Likes it
What can I say, this place is just great. When we arrived the place was a bit busy but we were determined to eat there since we heard so much about it. After the friendly hostess took my name down she mentioned that she would call us on our cell so we have time to walk around NODA. We decided to stop by smelly cats to get an espresso (great service and environment by the way). Half hour later I received the awaited phone call from the crepe cellar.
There were plenty of yummy selections to choose from, we decided with the buffalo oysters which were superb! They were an explosion of flavor on your mouth, especially when dipped in the blue cheese dip it came with. We also tried the mushroom crepe, the ham and Gruyere crepe and the steak with biscuit meal. Wow! everything was just great. The fries that came with the meal were very tasty and the macaroni and cheese was out of this world.
The waitress accidentally brought us an extra macaroni and cheese, when we told her we didn't order it she was nice enough to leave it for us anyway.
Overall, it was a great experience. Friendly staff, good food, nice environment. Can't complain!. Highly recommended

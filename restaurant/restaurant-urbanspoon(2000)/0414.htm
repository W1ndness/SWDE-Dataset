Delicious Food...Amazing Service!!!
by colleen-nordin (2 reviews)
February 05, 2010 - Likes it
I only have great things to say about this restaurant. It's definitely a diamond in the rough. Who knew Green Bay had such a great place to dine!
Loved ambiance at the bar, very classy, but also very comfortable. We felt right at home. We asked the bartender for wine suggestions, and he was spot-on.
We had a tough time deciding what to order, the menu was full of great selections. We started with an appetizer which was amazing. Our entrees were also perfect. The steak was wonderfully seasoned and cooked to exactly the right temperature. There was nothing left on our plates!
I really cannot say enough about the Wellington. The staff was wonderful, and the food even better. We definitely recommend it to anyone looking for a nice evening out - whether just to grab a drink or for dinner - you can't go wrong. It's our new favorite spot in Green Bay!!!

Recommend | Not helpful

Great place for drinks and dinner
by Daniel Burke (1 review)
February 02, 2010 - Likes it
The atmosphere at this place is superb. The bar is great for drinks before you eat, it has really nice wood work throughout. The dining room is very elegant and romantic. My girlfriend and I ate here recently and we had the grilled lobster tail appetizer which was very good. I had the Australian Rack of Lamb which was fantastic and my girlfriend really enjoyed her stuffed tenderloin. The service was terrific and we will definitely be coming back soon as there were a lot of things on the menu we would like to try.

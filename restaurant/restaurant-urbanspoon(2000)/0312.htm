Authentic, Delicious Thai
by ghost (76 reviews)
October 11, 2009 - Likes it
I love this restaurant. The wait is never terribly long even though this restaurant seems always busy. The service is welcoming and efficient.
Most importantly, the food is consistently fresh and delicious. You get to choose your level of spice, and the kitchen is happy to accommodate your requests. Even the most spicy of dishes are very flavorful, the heat not obliterating the taste of the dish itself.
I would recommend this restaurant to any person who seeks a true taste of Thai in the suburbs. I have never been let down by this great restaurant and plan to return again and again.

mobile review
by Lance F. (50 reviews)
April 16, 2010 - Likes it
I took colleagues here for a team dinner and everyone left raving about the food. We sat in a large round table in the corner that seats 10. We shared som tam, tom yam goong, and tom kha gai. Staff from DC, SF-South Bay, Central Florida, and Denver all greatly enjoyed. Among the entrees, it was clear that no nam pla (fish sauce) was used, making this less than "authentic". Dried red chilis were also used in place of fresh sliced ones. The rice was more Chinese style than Thai jasmine rice. For American tastes, this restaurant strikes just the right balance of authenticity and American palatability.

Not the best food in Alaska...but close.
by James Vest (7 reviews)
May 04, 2009 - Likes it
The Haute Quarter is my idea of fine dining. It's small, dark and comfortable. The food is always great, the menu changes (which is nice) and the atmosphere is good. The only complaint I have is you always wait a long time for your food...however that's what fine dining is all about. The food is cooked to order, fresh and perfect. I am always willing to wait for something like that. Expect your dinner to last at least 2 hours. If you are in a hurry this is probably not the place for you.

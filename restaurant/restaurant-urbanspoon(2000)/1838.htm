nice addition to Southside
by CRog (176 reviews)
October 14, 2009 - Likes it
Courtyard Bar and Grill Southside is the new sister establishment of the Courtyard 280 OB&G--and is pretty much like the other eatery. Good pub food (alittle pricey)--and fresh oysters (25 cents apiece during happy hour). Friendly barmaids and pretty fair pub-grub. I hope it does well on Southside--we need more "mom-n-pop" places in Southside.

Depends on what you want...
by Jason (6 reviews)
March 12, 2010 - Doesn't like it
If your looking for somewhere to get drunk and not be concerned about how your treated by the staff, then this will probably do. If your looking for somewhere with good atmosphere, courteous and attentive staff then your going to be disappointed. Another poster on here mentioned the staff and managers being rude...I would certainly agree. Complete "A' holes to just about everyone who came in . Overheard the bar manager complaining that the band was doing a sound check. I mean ....really?? I would assume you'd want the band to be at their best, but I guess not. Plus the later it gets the more "F" ed up the crowd becomes....very muchlike the 280 bar...nothing but drug addicts and hippie wannabes after about 2AM.

Terrible....
by jarafree (1 review)
November 16, 2009 - Doesn't like it
Needless to say, I won't be going back. The place has sketchy clientele, and the food is terrible! I love hot wings; I ordered 12, tried to eat 3, but had to give the rest of them away they were so raunchy. The hot sauce is pretty much tabasco sauce, and the other seasonings are not sauces--they are seasonings. The managers are rude, and altogether, it was NOT a good experience. I would rather go back to J Clyde to get crappy service than go back to Courtyard Southside. The only decent thing I noticed was the bartender giving okay service. They were going to charge me at the door to eat there too. $5 to get in, to then pay to eat! I would not recommend this place to anyone.

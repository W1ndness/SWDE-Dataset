+ 2 more photos from Karen Juicy Burger but Outrageous Prices
by Karen (18 reviews)
July 27, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I was seriously on the fence about this one. But the thing that pushed me over was the fact that I'd just paid $13.75 for what would have been equal to a Wendy's Junior Cheeseburger, small Frosty and cheese fries. The service was outstanding. But still.
I must admit. The burger was melt in your mouth juicy. The cheese sauce on the fries was very yummy. And the Caramel shake was quite nice. Would I come on a nice sunny day and wait in a tremendous line for it? Ahhhh....nope.
I think the bun was potato bread. I've never like potato buns because of their sweetness. And that's precisely what spoiled what would have been an above average burger.
My fries weren't hot enough. Either that or the sauce wasn't and cooled the entire thing off.
The Caramel shake was the best part of the whole deal.
Overall, if you like potato buns and happen to stop by in the middle of a raging thunderstorm like I did, so you won't have to wait in line, try it. Otherwise hit the nearest Wendy's.

First Time Dinner, had a horrible time!
by Kingston Cash (1 review)
June 27, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I went to P.F Chang's for the first time ever and i was super excited because i love chinese food and all my friends said great things about this chain of restuarants, so finally on 6/26/2010 i had dinner reservations for a table of 5 at 7;30pm checked in at 7:20pm and the host stand said it would only be 5 to 10 mins after res time to get seated. my party explained that we had a show at 8:30 to catch, and asked if we shud eat somewhere else and the host stand guarunteed us that we would be fine. we didnt get seated until 8:00pm and the waitress had a super bad attitude and said i cant get the food out that fast and pretty much argued with us about us telling her we needed the food rushed. finally after argueing enough we got a manager to the table and it took the manager coming to the table to get a sense of urgency. i the food finally came and the whole time the server was like chuck norris in a mia film , missing in action, so not only did i feel rushed i also felt abandoned. the food was no better than panda express at 5 times the cost. if i wanted to feel rushed and taste garbage i could've gone to the foodcourt and ate at a panda express or chicken connection for the same taste and half the price. There desserts looked amazing as i was exiting the restuarant, i dont know what they tasted like because i didnt have the chance to order a dessert due to the genius host stand. also use the bathroom before you go because the restaurant does not have any, you have to leave the restaurant and go search in the casino for a restroom. but i will give this restaurant credit and the benifit of the doubt once it took argueing for 10mins and the manager having to get involved, the manager was able to get the food out quickly. this was the worst expierence ive ever had eating out in las vegas. this restaurant has a long way to go before ill ever return or recomend it to any one. i work at a much finer restaurant on the strip, im not going to say which one, but i will say that because i work in a restaurant i try my best not to be a pest or get upset because ive been there, but the attitude from the servers and the lies from the host stand did not help.

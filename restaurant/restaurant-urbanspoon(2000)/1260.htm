not too bad
by Guillermo Astorga (119 reviews)
February 26, 2009 - Likes it
Zona 78, in my opinion, is greatly overpriced for the quality of food you're getting. don't get me wrong - it's good food - but i'm pretty wary of paying so much for a pasta dish that is in no way any more impressive than what i make myself at home. the anti-pasti plate is very disappointing as well. granted, there's usually some great scenery and talent there, i choose to not spend 65 bucks on a meal for two, when we're both getting pasta.

Fantastic
by Metal Mark (12 reviews)
February 17, 2009 - Likes it
I'm with the other reviewers...this place is addicting. For real, I have actually dreamt about the Roaring 40s pizza with its caramelized onions and stinky blue cheese. Ahh...
Great salads, love the cilantro hummas dip and the wines are amazing. Plus you get anything to go. Even that amazing Australian stinky cheese.
Love Zona 78!

Best Happy Hour in Town!
by Jenna (9 reviews)
December 27, 2008 - Really likes it
I absolutely love Zona 78! Not only is the atmosphere fun for any occasion but the food and service is outstanding. Tucson Weekly voted Zona 78 for the best happy hour in town and they were right! $2 draft beers including Guinness and Peroni, you can't beat that price. If you haven't ever been here you need to check it out!

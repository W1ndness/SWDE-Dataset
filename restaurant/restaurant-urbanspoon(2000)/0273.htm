Great value, nice ambiance
by SammyR (3 reviews)
December 06, 2009 - Likes it
Just by splitting the $9.99 sampler platter, my husband and I got more than we could possibly eat in one night, which made for delicious leftovers for lunch. We started with lentil soup, which came with homemade pita chips, and a side of kibbeh. Everything was fresh and flavorful. Our hostess/owner (and cook?) was gracious, welcoming, and sweet, and obviously proud of her good product. This is a cozy new addition to the Grandview hub, and a very welcome non-chain restaurant. I liked it better than Aladdin's, for sure.

Slowest service on earth
by maryarose (1 review)
January 20, 2010 - Doesn't like it
The food at Mazah is wonderful. I've been there three times - once with a group of 7, and twice with just one other person. With a big group, it took us 2.5 hours to order, eat, pay, even though we were all having the same thing (the wonderful sampler platter). Picking up food to go the third time I went took 25 minutes even though all three dishes required minimal preparation!
The owner seems to be the only person who knows what items go on the plate with certain dishes, and there's no coordination of who's waiting on which tables. There is clearly no planned workflow for the service in this place.
In sum, I won't go here again on a workday, as the time commitment is too great.

Great Addition to Grandview Ave
by cbusfood (1 review)
December 17, 2009
I went to Mazah last night and was very impressed! It is a family owned restaurant and the owner seems to put a lot of heart into each dish. By the end of dinner, I felt like I was part of the family! I would highly recommend the vegan rice pudding and falafel (although all of their small dishes were good). A Mediterranean Must
by Wolfgang Rumpf (5 reviews)
February 01, 2010 - Likes it
We just had dinner here and got the Couples' Sampler platter - everything was absolutely superb, from the chicken mushroom soup (my girlfriend had the lentil soup) to the dips and falafel. Vegetarian grape leaves were to-die for; everything was fresh, homemade, and tasty! The staff was amazingly friendly and courteous. Turkish coffee at the end was an excellent way to wrap up this experience. We'll be back!

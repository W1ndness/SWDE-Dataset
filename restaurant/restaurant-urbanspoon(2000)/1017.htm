nice lunch spot
by johnnjill (100 reviews)
January 15, 2010 - Likes it
the atmosphere is a little wanting, but the Singapore (rice vermicelle) noodle curry was perfect. If you've never tried Singapore noodles, you're in for a treat. It's something of a kitchen sink dish -- everything's in it -- it can combine all sorts of meat and vegetable depending on what the kitchen has at hand. Typically, there is pork and chicken or seafood mixed with vegetables.and yellow curry and stir fried in a wok. The service was a slow, but the menu selection includes just about every Chinese favorite. Only wish there were more vegetarian options that included "fresh" steamed (rather than fried) tofu.

Wonderful
by rick atkinson (13 reviews)
June 21, 2009 - Likes it
Went to Asian Palace with wife and daughter and was delighted with everything, especially with the excellent service from our waitress Sabrina. Great atmosphere and especially the presentation and quality of sashimi, sushi and tempura. We all had the Luncheon Bento Boxes and I had the luncheon sashimi which was fantastic. Couldn't have been any fresher if it was still flipping on the boat. Miso soup was great. Prices are very reasonable. Asian palace recently opened and is immaculate. It is not a very large place and was pretty full at lunch. I highly recommend vision here.

Fresh, Friendly, Fine.
by Lyle Telefunken (1 review)
June 17, 2009
The ASIAN PALACE is a new destination for Pan-Asian dining in Providence. It is obvious from across North Main Street the time, effort, and expense that went into preparing the location for it's intended purpose. Entering the establishment and making your first contact with the staff reassures you that your experience will be the best that the people there can provide. You should be aware that the Pan-Asian concept is not yet mature and that the Asian Palace's concept of a particular dish may or may not exactly match your notion of what a particular dish should be. Rest assured though that your experience will reflect the fresh, honest food and friendly atmosphere provided.

Excellent lunch...
by RI Traveler (4 reviews)
June 19, 2009 - Likes it
My family and I stopped here for lunch this afternoon. We opted for the Bento Box lunch. The chicken and shrimp tempura was delightful, served with a wonderful assortment of tempura-style vegetables. It was a surprise for all of us to see tempura-style onion, but on first bite, we were hooked.
My father, who enjoys the uncooked sushi / sashimi ordered a sashimi plate. The presentation was elegant and graceful, extremely fresh, and arranged perfectly on the dish. It must have tasted as good as it looked because it did not stay on the plate very long.
Their California rolls were mouthwatering, as well were the little dumplings that come with the bento box.
The tea (which is served in mugs) is fresh and light. Does it really matter that you don't pour it yourself if it is hot, fresh, and tasty?
We ended our lunch with splitting a green tea ice cream, and a lovely surprise of a slice of chocolate cake was sent over from the staff.
The service was excellent, our waitress was attentive and complimentary. The menu is extensive, and we can't wait to try more. Having this level of sushi and Japanese/Chinese variety within walking distance will prove to be a challenge to my diet.

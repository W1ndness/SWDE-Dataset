i absoulutly love everything about this resturaunt !!!!
food was ready fast but not too fast!
decor is beautiful and the view of the river is magical!
the pieces of shrimp and oyster and such were huge, well portioned entrees. delicious and mouth watering and the smell was amazing.
hot wing appetizers were sooo good, we had citrus because it was too early for anything too hot and they hit the spot.
to top it all off what really made me love this place was the waitress, she was awesome. most places the girls will stand around talking if they see a friend. ours left a convo she was having just to come over to check on us and of course we were doing great. she checked on us and kept the drinks coming and was sooo polite. the nicest waitress ever. we went on the fourth of july and assumed anyone would be having a bad day just to be at work away from feastivities but she acted like she was genuinly pleased to be there. she really made the time here perfect!
i am going this weekend for the second time!!!!!

Wonderful food and atmosphere
by Kimmy813 (2 reviews)
June 09, 2009 - Likes it
There is almost always a wait so it is worth it to call ahead. The rolls are delicious and ask for honey to dip them in! The onion blossoms are a great starter. I really haven't tried anything that I haven't liked here. I prefer to order the filet but the sirloin is pretty good as well. All the sides are excellent especially the mashed potatoes loaded like a baked potato. The chicken fried chicken and steak are really good as well. Kids love that you can throw peanuts on the floor and there are line dances as well. I would recommend this as a nice place for family dining or casual dining.

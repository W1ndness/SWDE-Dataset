#1 reuben in the galaxy
by SparkleAggie (45 reviews)
July 08, 2009 - Likes it
This place has, hands down, THE best reuben I have ever had. The quality of the corned beef is awesome, and they slice it really nice and thick. I'm not sure whether it's proximity to the old Blackstone Hotel, where the reuben was originally created, has anything to do with the caliber of this sandwich, but what I do know is that when I'm craving a reuben, this is my first and only thought! Order it.

Definitely #1 for Reuben fans
by FireFly (34 reviews)
June 12, 2010 - Likes it
Just got some carryout from here for the first time today. The reviews below praising their Reuben are correct - I live with a huge Reuben fan, and he ordered one today. He says it is probably the best he has ever had, and he has tried many Reubens from many different places over the years.
I had the sourdough burger, which was also very good, and we split some baked potato soup, which we liked.
Not sure on the fries...they were quite greasy/soggy, but not sure if that's because they were badly cooked or that they just didn't survive the trip home.
At any rate, given the status of the Reuben with my Reuben-lover, I'm sure we will be back again sometime for me to try other items and maybe eat in next time.

Great beer selection
by JJHardy (4 reviews)
August 01, 2010 - Likes it
So they have roughly 34 beers on draft in the main room, with 2 additional rooms offering even more selections, and they have a store that has even more selections to take home. Food is what you would expect from a bar (average). I don't know who decided it on the name of the wing sauce, Inferno, it's flavorful but not especially spicy. You don't go here to eat, you go here to drink.

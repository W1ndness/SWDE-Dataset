(Almost) always a good meal
by Sweetsville (3 reviews)
August 23, 2009 - Likes it
I have eaten there several times and the service has always been good, the atmosphere is great (upscale and stylish but not stuffy). A few weeks ago, a group of us (8 of us to be exact) went to see a matinee movie and the plan was to eat afterward. I suggested The Wine Exchange and everyone was coming up to me afterward telling me they were very happy with the choice of restaurant. Now, having said all this, there have been a couple of entrees that have been disappointing. A friend and I ate there today and she said her Eggplant Parm Sandwich was greasy and "kinda gross". Another time I ordered the Spinach Ravioli which had way too much garlic in it (and I like garlic) but this overwhelmed the dish; you could barely taste anything OTHER than garlic. So I have decided the best thing to do is find something you like and stick with it. In my case, it is the Vineyard Pizza which has been fantastic every single time I have gotten it. Who would have thought that grapes on pizza would be good? But it is. I have also heard wonderful things about the Baked Brie appetizer. They also have a nice outdoor patio area.

An experience worth repeating
by Harrison (5 reviews)
January 01, 2009 - Likes it
The interior greets you with it's funky-chic atmosphere, and you know you're in for an experience. It being a cool evening, we opted for the patio.
The staff was friendly and attentive without being overbearing. Our server was not as well versed on the wine as she was food (not a problem, the teamwork from the staff was wonderful), and the food and wine pairings they came up with went beautifully. Just give them an idea what you enjoy and let them take care of you. It wasn't the most inexpensive night I've had out, but it was most definitely worth it.

Bama' Style BBQ Goodness
by Rob.M (114 reviews)
July 28, 2010 - Likes it
I've been a patron of Alex's BBQ since it opened, back in the 90's. At the time, Alex had ventured out from Rev, Campbell's BBQ in Northdale (long since gone) where I clearly remember as a kid, seeing him chop pork to a beat. But I digress, Alex established his own BBQ joint and it's been a Carrollwood staple. The chopped (hard to find anymore) pork is awesome as is the chicken and even the ribs to some extent. All BBQ has a distinctive smokey taste and a visibile smoke ring, giving credibility to it's down home goodness. Prices are a little high, but in line with what you would expect to pay at anywhere else in Tampa. Highly reccomended if you are a fan of Alabama style BBQ and sauce.

Kansas City in Mobile in Tampa...unsure
by Matthew David (12 reviews)
March 23, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I usually vote that I "don't like" a place because whether or not the food was okay, I would not choose to return.
Alex's is...okay. I had Ribs, pork, sausage, fried okra, and what I think were fresh cut fries. Nothing was bad, in fact the fries were quite good and while the ribs were a bit too fatty for me they were definitely tender to the point of falling off the bone. The sausage and pork were on the juicy side, not bad in small amounts.
And the owner, Alex, is a really nice guy who is probably doing his best to serve up real 'Bama BBQ. 'Bama BBQ cannot be captured with one style but if you are going to do just a tomato based sauce then you should IMHO do at least a regular and a spicy sauce. Alex has only one sauce, and it is not spicy or standard; it is sweet, more like Kansas City than Mobile or Tuscaloosa.
To their credit they have been around for over 15 years and are very welcoming. I want very badly to want to go back, but it would only be to try the beef, which Alex admits is not as good as the pork.
If you happen to be near there, you may want to give it a try. I would love to be proven wrong here.

Best BBQ in a Long Time
by michaeleschmidt (2 reviews)
February 06, 2010 - Likes it
We were out looking for something different and stumbled upon Alex's. First time. Never seen it before. What a find! It's smokey inside. We met a lady walking out and asked her how it was. Big smile. "You will love it!" She was right.
I let the lady at the front know it was our first time and we straight away got a small sample plate. Couple of sausage pieces, small ribs, small chicken, pulled pork and beef. Fall of the bone good. I really am not a BBQ fan but I really liked this meat. I ordered the ribs and my girlfriend ordered the chicken and ribs. Both were as tender and juicy as a meat can be. We ordered fried okra, greens and baked beans. Came with a large piece of white bread. We got takeout. Our portions were large ( I could not eat it all) and the sides were tasty stuff.
Go ahead and forget all other BBQ joints in Tampa. You will be back to this one over and over again.

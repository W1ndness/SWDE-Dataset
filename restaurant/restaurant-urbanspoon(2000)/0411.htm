Great food, great service
by Sue (1 review)
May 23, 2009
I went in on a Saturday morning. I'd been in a week-long funk, between work and kids' activities. My college student came home and had to be in Neenah by 9 on Saturday morning, so that her youth group could head off on their trip. I saw the sign and decided to give it a try. I'd had a craving for good pancakes.
I went inside and was greeted by the hostess, who asked me to wait a minute while she tended to some checks. Not a problem. I grabbed a take-out menu and looked for pancakes. I asked if there were real blueberries in the blueberry pancakes. The hostess said yes, would I be ordering for here or to go? When I said here, the hostess said, "Oh, Hon, go find a seat and let *me* take care of you."
This was sooooooo much what I needed to hear at that moment. She "hon-ed" and "sweetie-ed" me, and made sure that I got served. My pancakes did indeed contain real blueberries. Those pancakes were HUGE. I took half of one home, just to show my son and husband the size. These were light and fluffy and included a good number of real blueberries. The side of bacon included at least 5 slices, nicely done. Tasted good, not like the bland bacon you can get at OCB.
Service was excellent. Only the hostess used terms of endearment, but the other waitresses were quick, polite, and made sure that I had everything I needed.
I will be back!

Don't Fogeta La Fogata
by Stinky (196 reviews)
October 05, 2009 - Likes it
We go here almost every time we visit San Antonio. Its a little north of downtown, so all the locals are very familiar with it. Its edges one of SA's older neighborhoods, and is surrounded by vines and ivy - you'll miss it if you're not paying attention, but all the cab drivers know it. One of the coolest Mexican Patios I've ever sat at, complete with roving Mariachi's (no-its not cheesy - its actually very cool). Start with one of the best Margaritas in Texas. I don't know how its made, but its just awesome. Smooth & Tangy at the same time. Start with the Shrimp Cocktail, which is fresh and flavorful, bursting with lime and shrimp. Look the menu over carefully, cause theres lots of offerings. I usually get the La Fogata Special Combo, which has one of everything. Its just great, and I can never finish it (Tastes just as good at midnight in the hotel!) Service is deep San Antonio friendly and fast. A must visit for great Mexican food and Atmosphere. One of San Antonio's best.

BEST Seafood!
by Krystal (41 reviews)
November 28, 2009 - Really likes it
When I lived in NYC, this restaurant was right down the street from my apartment. It is definitely my favorite NYC restaurant, hands down.
The restaurant is always packed for dinner so be prepared for a wait. However, the staff are super friendly and will bring you water and take drink orders while you're waiting to be seated.
The restaurant is small, so be prepared to sit in close proximity with other diners. It's not uncomfortable by any means, however. The restaurant is very family-oriented and you'll feel at home.
Everything on the menu is delicious; you really can't go wrong. We always started our meal with either the peasant salad or Greek salad. They're both traditionally made and taste like it. As for entrees, The seafood is out of this world fresh and delicious. I usually opted for the salmon or the whiting. All of the sides are great (my favorite being the steamed dandelion. My roommate was partial to the lemon potatoes). One thing I did pass on however, was the free dessert. It's some sort of bread pudding/custard and I am not a fan of the texture.
If you happen to be in Astoria, you must try this place!

Very good.
by Sarah Louise (17 reviews)
January 13, 2010 - Likes it
This is a really good restaurant with a sleek, modern vibe. To be honest, it doesn't fit in its location, but the food is definitely worthwhile. While the prices may not be as enticing as Akashi and Shiki, the food is excellent, and the service is top notch.
For starters, I absolutely recommend getting either the Chicken Lettuce Wraps or the Fried Tofu. Both are delicious. All of their rolls are good. I also really like the Albacore, Salmon and White Tuna.

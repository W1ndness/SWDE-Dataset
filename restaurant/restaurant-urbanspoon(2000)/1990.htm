Their food is outstanding and the atmosphere is great. Its the perfect local pub complete with darts and shuffleboard. The patrons are friendly and the jukebox offers a wide range of music. I make it a point to bring out of town guests here for the wings and consider it my "neighborhood pub" even though I live in Kenmore and pass many others to get here. Best Fish Fry!!!
by buffjunkie (2 reviews)
August 24, 2009 - Likes it
My husband and I are Buffalo fish fry enthusiasts. Believe it or not, we probably eat a Friday fish fry every two weeks or so. We love trying new restaurants, and the snyder bar and grille was recommended to us by my girlfriend. All I can say is that it was one of the absolute best fish fries that we have ever eaten!! I don't know if they get their fish somewhere special, or if it is a special batter, but what an unbelievable treat!! We would HIGHLY recommend it to any fellow fish fry lovers!! They give you generous portions and the prices are very reasonable. I have used urbanspoon for a long time and this was the first time I felt compelled to comment! Definitely will be returning soon!!!

Very Disappointed
by fabricoh330 (1 review)
July 21, 2009 - Doesn't like it
I was very excited to try Michael's Pasta in the Pan and finally did the other night. It was a major let down. The service was terrible, they couldn't even get our salad dressing right. I ordered the Shrimp and Scallop ala vodka, and when it came there was no scallops. After I asked they said they ran out and so they game me extra shrimp. There was a total of 4 shimp in the dish. Now I'm not a huge shrimp fan so to me that was not a big deal, however, if 4 shrimp is extra shrimp what would you normally get? Then they said in addition to the "extra" shrimp we will take something off the bill, that turned out to be 2 dollars. The money is also not the issue but dont make a big deal out of a discount and "extra" shrimp when really what you did amounts to nothing.
Yet another poorly run/managed restaurant to which I will not be returning.

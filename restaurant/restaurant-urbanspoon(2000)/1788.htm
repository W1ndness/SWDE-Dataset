No to Take Out at Ralph & Kacoo's
by Jeanie Champagne (2 reviews)
January 16, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I ordered Stuffed Shrimp by phone because we were getting ready to watch the Saint's Game at home. The both and food werer thrown together - in the box were 5 stuffed shrimp - mostly a hunk of stuffing around a piece of shrimp tail, the fried quickly and softly - small tiny cup of red seafood sauce, very small cup of old vegetables - said to be steamed vegetables - they were definitely left overs. There was no bread, no napkin, no plastic wear. The food and the box looked as if it was thrown out to us! and the cost -- $17.95. Popeye's would have been a better choice - 1/3 the cost, more food and would have had a much better taste !! Don't order take Out from Ralph and Kacoo's -- they simply do not care about the Customer Service.

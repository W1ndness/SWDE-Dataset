Very Disappointed
by Sachiko F. (8 reviews)
August 14, 2010 - Doesn't like it
My husband and I were anticipating a great dining experience on our anniversary but was very disappointed. The level of service definately did not match their prices.
I guess it was my fault for ordering crab legs at a steak house. Server and manager seem more interested in serving a group of tourists. I had to ask for clarified butter to dip my crabmeat in but that was not the worst part of our experience. After waiting 10 minutes for the butter (and my crab was getting cold), the butter brought to us was solid and cold! My hubby did not want to make a scene and used the warming candle to try to melt the butter. While he was holding the candle under the butter the server walked by, looked at us and didn't say a word to check for satisfaction. The manager stopped by the table, looked at what my hubby was doing and asked if everything was fine. We were shocked and disappointed . Save your money. There are other great places to eat steak. We will never eat here again.

Recommend | Not helpful

However, the steak they brought me was barely even cooked..."blue rare". It looked the same as a piece of seared ahi, not a rare steak. Then they tried to convice me it was rare, not just seared. I have been to many Ruth's Chris...and loved them all...except this one. Go to Waikiki instead.

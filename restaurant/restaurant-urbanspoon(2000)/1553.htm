Fresh sushi & friendly service
by Amber (24 reviews)
May 12, 2009 - Likes it
I was in Atlanta for a conference with a few coworkers, and my friend who lives in Alpharetta took us to Harry & Sons when we asked for great sushi. The restaurant certainly delivered! I was impressed with the sushi/Thai mix. I had the hot & sour soup with shrimp, and was impressed by the taste, and the fresh mushrooms were a huge bonus! We ordered 7 different rolls, and loved them all. My favorites were the Wasabi Maki and Shiro Maki. The waitress was friendly and patient, even gently stating that we might be ordering too much sushi. If you're in the area (or anywhere in a 20 mile radius) stop on by.

Good Enough
by Baily Broomell (9 reviews)
March 25, 2009 - Doesn't like it
The restaurant itself is nice, good atmosphere, very attentive waitress and decent prices. I ordered the filet and frankly wasn't impressed. It didn't taste like much... same thing with the stuffed mushrooms that I heard great things about - they had a lot of potential but didn't really have a lot of flavor. The glass of Pinot Noir was very tiny for $9, but good none the less. Overall I'd recommend going here but not to bother with the filet.

Great casual dining
by Dollars (1 review)
October 13, 2009 - Really likes it
Was stumbling around Fullerton looking for a bite to eat and found Stubriks. Nice casual environment with a very attentive staff and good steaks. Was drinking a martini and asked if they had blue cheese stuffed olives. The waitress said they didn't but she could bring me olives and blue cheese. She brought me a small bowl of each. Enough for 3 martini's and then some. The steak was really good as well. I would recommend this place to anybody.

Lucky Find!
by Zack (1 review)
October 12, 2009 - Likes it
I use to live in Fullerton back in my college days and unfortunately didn't stumble upon Stubrik's Steakhouse until today. Don't know why. Probably because it's a bit nondescript, but that's part of the charm. It's not overdone. It's simply just right. The pork chops I had were absolutely amazing. I also tried my wife's filet which was better than the filet I recently had at The Palm. Great atmosphere, nice bar and even pool tables upstairs. Not too loud, attentive service and amazing food (the bananas foster was outstanding). Nice to find a place that truly cares about their customer's experience. I will be back many times over and I'll be sure to tell others.

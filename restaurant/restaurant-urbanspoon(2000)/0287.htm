User reviews of Pei Wei Asian Diner

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.
Not bad Asian
by JED (47 reviews)
May 09, 2010 - Likes it
Pei Wei is my idea of Asian fast food, but with better quality ingredients than you would fine at a fast food joint. If you are looking for a less expensive version of P.F. Chang with many of the same menu items, then this is your place. The lettuce wraps taste almost exactly like the ones at P.F. and they are cheaper. I don't dine here on a regular basis, as I like to keep my money going towards non-chain establishments, but it is nice change of pace.

Recommend | Not helpful

Fast & Yummy Asian Bistro
by Stacy (11 reviews)

My hubby is not much of a fan, but then again he is not a fan of PF Chang's either! If you like Asian Bistro style food you will love this place. The price's are really cheap as well.

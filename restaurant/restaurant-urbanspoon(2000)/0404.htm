Best Fine Dining in Appleton
by LemondropPinup (3 reviews)
February 15, 2010 - Really likes it
The atmosphere, food, and service at the Apollon are some of the best I've ever had. From the appetizers to the desserts, every dish is prepared perfectly and in large portions. Each entree comes with a soup and a salad, a great deal that you won't get at most restaurants. The setting is very intimate, it's a great place to bring a date. The Apollon gets my vote for best fine dining, best mediterranean food, and best value.

Apollon
by Cassie (1 review)
January 17, 2010
Atmosphere was wonderful, great wine selection (by the bottle only), and our server was very professional.
I ordered the chicken cacciatore. The soup and salad were delicious, however, my entree was WAY too salty. I like salty foods, but this literally tasted as if I was eating spoonfuls of salt. I could hardly eat--only ended up taking a few bites of the chicken. Disappointed with the entree, but would go back again to try something else.

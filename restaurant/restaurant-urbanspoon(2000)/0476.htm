mobile review
by Laur (3 reviews)
March 15, 2010 - Really likes it
After living on Long Island my entire life, I moved to Houston 2 years ago and started my search for a bagel place. When I stepped into New York Bagel and saw about a dozen jewish grandmothers having brunch I knew this was going to be the place. So happy. This is it. Its the only place in houston (possible the only place in texas) to get a real bagel. I love these bagels - it is amazing. My only gripe is that they don't have egg bagels. But whatever - I will gladly make the 40 minute round trip for these delicious bagels.

Okay Italian - Afforadable
by cmartin (96 reviews)
May 24, 2009 - Likes it
If you find yourself in Merrick Park with no dinner plans and want to catch a quick bite, then this place will do just fine. But that is the extent of my involvement with Villagio for good reason. This place is maybe, I stress *maybe* half a step up from Olive Garden in food quality. That's no knock on Villagio, I've eaten at OG before too and it isn't bad in a bind. But like OG, Villagio isn't in my top ten (or top 20) Italian places I'd like to go.

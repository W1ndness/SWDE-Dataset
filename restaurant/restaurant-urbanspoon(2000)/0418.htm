Favorite sushi in Connecticut
by Kevin Costner (32 reviews)
June 09, 2010 - Likes it
I lived about 10 steps away from this place and it quickly became my favorite sushi restaurant in Connecticut. With names like "Saucy Little Geisha Boy Roll" and "Drunk Pig Roll" this place has it all. Great atmosphere, great food and they do a really good homemade saki drink called "The Firecracker". I highly recommend Miya's.

mobile review
by Alex Redcay (1 review)
November 04, 2009
Excellent sushi and I agree Bun (the manager/owner) is great. Miya's used to be my favorite place. However it's is very expensive and the service is about the worst I've ever experienced. As the novelty of the sushi wears off, the terrible service and expense become more apparent. I lost the motivation to endure the bad for the good. Last note: if bun is actually working that night the service and quality of sushi improves drastically. Bun makes Miya's worth it.

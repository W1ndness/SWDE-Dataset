I am not sure, but maybe its the natural carnal urge to devour flesh. Sullivans is good, avoid the soufflet, try the blue cheese nachos and the iceberg wedge.

I like most of what I remember.
by Chris James (59 reviews)
October 12, 2009 - Likes it
I have been to the one in Charlotte and Raleigh. Both are fantastic but somehow someone keeps giving me these pineapple vodka things that get me too drunk to remember the food. I do remember the crab claws with the white romulade sauce were great as was the Kansas bone-in. This place seems more of a group setting vs a date night location.
The Raleigh restaurant is kat daddy in location, atmosphere and wait staff. The Charlotte location needs work in all three areas (waitress was rushing us, atmosphere is confusing and I didn't feel safe waiting outside for my car). But again I was drinking ;)

It's busy and I like it that way. The food and service is great. Having an after dinner cigar outside during summer was relaxing. The foyer section is too intense, however. There's more than one hostess yet it seems to be a problem coordinating things.
But once you're past that ... everything else is yummy, tasty and a great experience. Looking forward to a brunch there.

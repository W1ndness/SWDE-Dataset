Why?
by KB (7 reviews)
June 08, 2010 - Doesn't like it
I don't get why this place is to highly ranked! The chips are just ok, and the bizarre white sauce that you tend to find in Richmond Mexican places was sweet. The enchilada sauce was overly cinnamon-y. My spinach burrito consisted of just spinach, nothing else in the filling, with a sauce that looked and tasted like an Italian marinara sauce. I defintely won't be back.

Last Monday my girlfriend and I came here for dinner to try this place out. We heard from some of our friends that we have to try some of the ethnic food and that the Ground Beef Burek was amazing, also that the Sarma (Cabbage Rolls stuffed with ground beef and some spices) are to die for. mobile review
by Daniel a (3 reviews)
May 09, 2009 - Doesn't like it
From time to time my girlfriend and I have enjoyed pizza at intermezzo. This past Friday we tried some of their other entrees, and we were very disappointed. My piroska was little more than a calzone sliced on the top. The crust was burned and the flavors were bland and boring. My girlfriend's sarma was boring and a little too salty. The cobbler we ordered was undercooked, and the berries were bitter in a most unpleasant way. The food overall was quite terrible, and I probably won't be going back again. Pity that this once good restaurant has become so sub par, and the food barely edible.

Best Pad Thai in Baton Rouge
by SouthernBelleCritic (30 reviews)
June 23, 2010 - Likes it
I am a Pad Thai fan. Thai Kitchen has the BEST Pad Thai that we have found in Baton Rouge. A perfect blend of cilnatro, lime, peanut, none of them overwhelming the other and the perfect mix of egg, noodle and shrimp. We have had the Pad Thai probably 15 or more times. We had the lunch buffet at Thai Kitchen once and will go back again. There weren't as many food selections as a regular asian restaurant but that was ok with us. Everything they had on the buffet was high quality. I would rather have fewer selections and higher quality for my buffet. The service is always good. We are always seated promptly and served quickly. Highly recommend.

Good food, great karaoke!
by Racheal (8 reviews)
June 12, 2010 - Likes it
I enjoy eating at Thai Kitchen, though the real draw for me is the karaoke on Friday nights. The food is good, but the lunch buffet is nothing to boast about. I'm vegetarian and they rarely have anything I can eat. For dinner they have great options, like fried tofu, pad thai, and green beans in lime sauce. I recommend for dinner. If you're looking for a good Thai buffet for lunch, I recommend Thai Pepper on Florida.

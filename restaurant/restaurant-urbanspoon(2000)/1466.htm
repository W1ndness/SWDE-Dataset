Message from The Capital Grille

Enjoy Fine Dining at The Capital Grille in Atlanta. Set a Reservation.

  • Private dining rooms
  • Award winning wine list
  • Satellite broadcasting

We cordially invite you to join us at The Capital Grille. Take in the breathtaking Atlanta skyline right from your table. Your penthouse view will look even better after savoring a dry aged steak paired with a wine selection from our award-winning list. Even the bar will earn high marks from you and your guests. Complimentary valet parking makes it easy to satisfy your cravings and enjoy a relaxing night out in the metropolitan center of the South.

strange menu strange taste
by Megan Calas (15 reviews)
June 16, 2010
How do people think this place is so great? Okay, the bar scene is decent, but its a little on the meat market side, Dunwoody + Meat Market = ummm, kinda on the ugly side. I mean its not too bad, but sometimes there are just too many people wanting to hook up being WAY too obvious about it.
I have been here multiple times meeting up with people or late at night. At first I was excited by the exotic menu(I will call it exotic, because at first glance, that is what it is). Now if everything tasted good, I would continue to call it exotic. Now when I describe this place, I just say, "The menu is weird." Thats what it is! I have tried many, many items... I am not picky, and I was intrigued by their interesting combinations and tapas plates. How dissapointed when I when the food that sounded odd actually tasted odd?
Here is the breakdown of things I remember having:
Fried Goat cheese (Okay, but I prefer good old mozerella sticks)
Hummus(nothing special, tastes bland)
Quesos Espanoles(um, okay, not memorable)
Costillas Espanoles(not impressive)
Hamburguesas(I definitely should have passed on this)
Calamari Fritos(um, I guess this tastes like any old Calamari dish)
Carne Asada(definite dissapointment-especially after reading the reviews)
Empenada(GROSS- GREASY AND NASTY- STAY AWAY)
Two things that are actually alright:
Accitunas Y Almendras(Olives and Almonds)
Chuletas De Borrego(Lamb Chops, DELICIOUS)
So anyway, I only tried the Lamb Chops recently after reading reviews. They were the first thing here that I have actually liked. The next visit I decided to maybe try something else. I keep thinking some of the stuff has to be good, but my experimentation has not ever paid off. I tried an Empenada on my last visit and it was not good at all, just greasy and deep fried. So I guess I would recommend sticking to just the Lamb Chops, but hey, others seem to like the weird menu so hey, whatever man!
Anyway, I give this place 3 stars because the bar(even though its ugly) can actually be fun. If you can avoid all the old single vultures or get too drunk to care, this place is a good time. It is always crowded, and when they have a live band, it is totally fun to get up on that dance floor.
And, the grapefruit martinis are SO GOOD, just don't drink too many of them, they can be dangerous.

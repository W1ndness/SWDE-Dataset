I was recommended to Indochinois from a friend and I must say, this is some amazing food. I've been there four times now and have tried a different dish each visit. I've been searching for a good Asian restaurant for almost four years now in Tampa, and I am just so glad to have finally found a place to routinely eat.
The portions are comfortably satisfying to where you don't have to waste food nor feel too full. (And as I recall, I did not have to pay extra side rice with my meal).
The service was good, in which they helped me choose what to order. But my favorite is that it is a family owned restaurant and they really remember you when you come back. Great place to go, I definitely recommend everyone to at least try it.

Best Vietnamese in town!
by Big Poppa Pump (1 review)
April 10, 2009 - Likes it
Lets see where to begin...
So I was out last Saturday night with my girlfriend; doing the usual hike down Dale Mabry trying to decide what we wanted for dinner. Well we happened to notice Indochinois and decided to try it.
The place has a nice ambiance as soon as you walk in. Was greeted promptly at the door and was seated immediately.
A few moments later our waiter came by i believe his name was Stephen. HE pleasantly asked us to look over the drink menu while he grabbed us waters.
Drink menu consisted of a few Domestic beers and a large selection of asian beers. (Also had a nice wine lost too)
Settled on two #33 beers.
Our waiter suggested a few appetizers and entrees for dinner.
We ended up ordering as apps: Crispy eggrolls and a papaya salad. Both which where excellent.
For dinner I had The BBQ pork over rice noodles. (can't remeber what it is called in Vietnamese) It was one of the best dishes I have had in a long time. Very flavorful and light. My girlfriend had the Pad Thai which was also REALLY good.
We didnt end up ordering any desert but they do have coconut ice cream.
I really enjoyed our dinner that evening and would recommend this place to anyone.
Food and service was top notch!

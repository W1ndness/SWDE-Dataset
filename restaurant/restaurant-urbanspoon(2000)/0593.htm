A goood, not great, Mexican restaurant
by R. E. P. (279 reviews)
July 20, 2009 - Likes it
I took a party of five to Casa Fiesta Sunday evening. Overall it was a pretty good experience. They have a nice brightly painted tidy building with a large patio. The food was average to slightly above average Mexican, which in my book qualifies as pretty good. Service was friendly without being overly attentive, and I think prices are about average for Mexican restaurants in Central Kentucky, perhaps a bit higher for other Mexican restaurants just in Richmond. My biggest complaint was with the salsa. It was about as thick as tomato juice.

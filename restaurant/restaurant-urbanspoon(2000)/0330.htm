Yes, the place is tiny, the servers often surly, and the bathrooms awkward as all get out, but it's the Je-ru! Denver's Best
by Bruce J Bobitt (25 reviews)
February 16, 2010 - Likes it
Jerusalem Middle Eastern restaurant next to DU has been serving up the best traditional Mediterranean grub in Denver for years. Always consistent, I have a hard time ordering anything but the dinner combo so I can enjoy the humus, tabbouleh, gyros, sheesh kabob,falafel, rice and grape leaves. This food is never a let down.
Jerusalem is more of a walk up than a restaurant because of the very limited seating but treat yourself. As a late night stop or lunch during your workweek, you can't go wrong.

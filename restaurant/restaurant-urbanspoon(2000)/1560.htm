Message from Imperial Fez

Authentic Moroccan Cusine in an Exotic Dining Environment

  • Nightly Belly Dancers
  • Private Dining Rooms
  • Catering Available

The Imperial Fez offers authentic Moroccan cuisine in an exotic dining environment. Guests dine on comfortable pillows and enjoy savory food eaten by hand. Every plate served is a reflection of Moroccan cuisine at its finest, handcrafted with the freshest produce, finest meats, and the best herbs and spices available. Shoes are left at the door and guests are magically transported to Morocco. It is a truly unique dining experience with nightly belly dancing performances and live music starting at 7:30p.m. Imperial Fez offers private dining rooms for groups up to 150 people, which are available Sundays through Thursdays. The Imperial Fez has over ten years experience in catering events of all sizes, from intimate social gatherings to large corporate luncheons or dinners. For your convenience, The Imperial Fez can prepare a customized menu for any special occasion.

Rude servers and mediocre food, but nice atmosphere.
by Mary E. (13 reviews)
December 01, 2008 - Doesn't like it
We made a reservation for Fez Fest, the second Tuesday of every month, when meals were two for the price of one. My boyfriend and I celebrated our anniversary there for September Fez Fest and brought a group to introduce them to the whole experience for the October special. The salads were in the same amount for 2 people as for 8, which was not a big deal. However, service was slow and we were given a table in an area where we could hardly see the belly dancers- one of the main reasons to pay $65 for a meal for two, not including drinks. When the bill came it was for full price, despite the fact we told them when we sat down "We're here for Fez Fest!" because they had not taken the discount the our anniversary either. The waiter told us that promotion was over and then when I pointed out we had come to the previous month's he said he had just returned from vacation and walked away to "check" on things. Another gentleman returned 15 minutes later to let us know that sorry, we had to pay full price. I asked to speak to the manager and the original waiter came back 5 minutes after that to tell me the bill was correct. I asked to speak to the manager again and she finally agreed to honor the special that had been in place when we made our reservation 3 weeks before. However, she charged gratuity and tax on the original price and treated us like we were being unreasonable. The food was mediocre at best and half of the restaurant was empty so our poor table position was that much more aggravating. The house cocktail was watered down and overly sweet and the lamb was in a sauce so bad that all three of the people at our table who ordered it left over 80% of it on their plates. I won't be back- Cafe Istanbul has better prices on food and drinks and much better service for the same experience- dinner on the floor and belly dancers- plus they will let you smoke the hookah at your table!

Fantastic Experience
by Dave Vigliotti (1 review)
May 21, 2010 - Really likes it
I chose to take my wife to Imperial Fez for her birthday. I did not alert the staff so as to just enjoy a quiet evening. The atmosphere was authentic, the service was wonderful and helpful and the food was truly an experience. The 5 course meal is $45 per person, well worth the flavorful faire and atmosphere. I would recommend anyone choosing to sample the Fez should go with an open mind. The Chef/Owners have created an experience that is authentic and will give you a chance to experience a full dining experience -- the sights, sounds, feel and tastes. You will sit on pillows and low chairs, eat with your fingers and enjoy entertaining bellydancers. This evening we were treated to both a sword dance and a fire dance. The dancers were beautiful and quite engaging even with a young boy's birthday party. Family fun for all... Don't miss any of this!!

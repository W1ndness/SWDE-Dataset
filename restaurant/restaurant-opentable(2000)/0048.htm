  • Whether you’re craving a romantic ocean front dinner for two or a quick lunch with your family, EAST restaurant will be just what you’re looking for. Enjoy our famous complimentary breakfast (pictured below) each morning of your stay. Savor your lunch in the warm glow of the sun on our oceanfront dining deck. Delight your taste buds with local seafood specials while watching the moonrise over the tides. With award-winning cuisine and the breath taking view of the beach and our ocean front gardens, EAST is sure to please at any time and for any occasion

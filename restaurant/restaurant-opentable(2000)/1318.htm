Cantina Lounge

writes...
  • Cantina Lounge is Orange County's premiere modern Mexican cuisine restaurant featuring creative fresh Mexican dishes including ceviche, ahi stack, carne asada and lobster tacos. Cantina Lounge will become your favorite Mexican restaurant in Orange County. Request a seat at the 20 person communal table for a fun evening. In addition to the great food, the venue has 48 - 50" Plasma TV's, large bar with great margaritas and martini's, two patios with fire pits and a water feature. After dinner, stay for the live DJ and dancing to complete a great memorable night.

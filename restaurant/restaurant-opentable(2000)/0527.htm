Bella Monica

writes...
  • Bella Monica is a family-run trattoria with recipes handed down from our grandparents. Our menu features Neapolitan dishes like gourmet thin-crust pizzas called, Flatbreads, pasta, seafood, chicken and pork dishes. Our tomato sauce is made from imported San Marzano tomatoes and is so good that we sometimes eat it like soup! Without a doubt, we serve the best cannoli you will ever taste! Our wine list features all Italian, hard-to-find selections that are a perfect match for our pastas and Flatbreads. We showcase local art on the walls and play a great upbeat music mix. You can dine al fresco on our patio and enjoy live acoustic music on Wednesday nights. Voted "Best Italian” by AOL cityguide & CitySearch 2005.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Raleigh
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.bellamonica.com

    AMEX, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Corbett Monica
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Large, shopping center lot.
  • Catering: Sundays at our Nana’s house were special days. It began when we entered Nana’s house and the smell of the ‘gravy’ simmering on the stove would greet us at the door. Nana would fly out of the kitchen to pinch our cheeks, give us hugs, and lead us to the table full of food saying “mangia, mangia”. Sundays with Nana were days where everything seemed to stop for family and friends; everything except for pasta. Aunts, uncles and cousins would trickle into Nana’s house throughout the day to take their places at the table. The day never seemed to end as we would sit for hours sharing the food.
  • Entertainment: Acoustic music Wednesday nights on the patio 8-11pm.

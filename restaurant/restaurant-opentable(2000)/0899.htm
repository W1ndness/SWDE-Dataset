Duck Club at Stanford Park Hotel

writes...
  • Conveniently located across from the Stanford Shopping Center, the Duck Club Restaurant affords an opportunity to dine in style in an approachable environment.
    The Duck Club Restaurant is attached to the landmark Stanford Park Hotel, a convenient gathering place for both locals and out of town guests alike. The restaurant offers breakfast, lunch and dinner with plenty of free parking. Service is attentive, unobtrusive and caring. The thoughtful offerings afford a wide variety of choices and showcases fresh regional ingredients. The Lounge at the Park offers a casual yet sophisticated atmosphere, cozy fireplace and your favorite libations making it a perfect place for a before or after meal rendezvous for drinks, appetizers or desserts. Offering over thirty wines by the glass, as well as a complete cellar of full bottles.

  • Opened in 1996, Xi'an has become the local favorite Chinese restaurant in Beverly Hills, offering healthy and tasty traditional Chinese cuisine with a contemporary twist. The atmosphere is vibrant and energetic with our private Shoji Room for large parties, a beautiful patio for outdoor dining, and a full service bar.

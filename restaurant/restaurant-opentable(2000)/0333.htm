Carmen's at the Top of the Bridge

writes...
  • Carmen's Restaurant, a Boca Raton Tradition for fine Dining and Entertaining is located at the Top of the Bridge. Renowned for creative Cuisine, Impeccable service and fabulous entertainment, Carmen's is the perfect destination for dining, dancing and special celebrations. Carmen's Award Winning Sunday Champagne Brunch is unparalleled on the Gold Coast. Carmen's is also the picture perfect location for elegant wedding receptions and Social Events, seating up to 175 guests

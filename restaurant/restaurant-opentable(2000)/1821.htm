  • Mambo and …The return of the Italians

    After two decades spent feeding and educating New Yorker’s to the art of eating and drinking well, the ownership and management of many restaurants around the city have finally moved to Little Italy, NYC, with the simple purpose of trying to revitalize “il quartiere Italiano” (the ancient Italian neighborhood) by utilizing our passion, professionalism and expertise in order to contribute to make Little Italy (one of the most visited locations in the world) a greater place with a stronger Italian identity, based on gastronomy, traditions, music, arts and costume : An incomparable destination for travelers from every corner of the planet and for locals who want to experience the real thing: The Italian joy of life in the only and unique Piece of Italy outside Italy!
    Mambo‘s people, his kitchen and floor staff welcome all visitors to a memorable culinary and entertainment experience and invite You to spent some serene and great time!

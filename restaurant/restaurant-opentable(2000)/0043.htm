Bianca's An Italian Eatery

writes...
  • Bianca's An Italian Eatery is the perfect place to fall back in love with Italian food. Bianca's has been delighting it's patrons for over 17 years by offering delicious homemade Italian food. The quaint and romantic atmosphere is the perfect place to casually enjoy friends and family over great food and wine, with excellent service, the way it used to be at Grandma’s house. Bianca's is unique by including an appetizer, house salad, garlic bread and dessert with every entrée. Each week the inclusions change with seasonal availability and the vision of the chefs. Your experience is never the same twice. Located on the corner of Spring Garden and South Chapman streets.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Greensboro
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Phone: (336) 273-8114

Riccabona's

writes...
  • Nestled in the heart of Ketchum/Sun Valley, Riccabona's Restaurant is renowned for its fabulously flavored, Mediterranean-influenced dishes. A historic log cabin was completely remodeled in 2000 into a romantic and comfortable fine dining restaurant. Candlelit interior, fresh flowers, leather chairs and a melodious ebony black baby grand piano combine to create a truly memorable dining experience. Riccabona's Restaurant is famous for its European favorites including Wienerschnitzel served with lingonberries and Spatzle, herb-crusted Rack of Lamb, Veal Scaloppini, and Tournedos of Red Venison Tenderloin. We also serve American Kobe Beef Tenderloin, which far exceeds the quality of all other U.S. beef brands. Our fresh fish includes Grilled Wild Alaskan Filet of Salmon and Seared Atlantic Sea Scallops. We also feature an extensive list of award winning premium wines. Join us for dinner. Riccabona's Restaurant ... where dining is a work of art!
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Mediterranean, Italian
  • Neighborhood: Ketchum
  • Cross Street: 4th Street
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Email: sriccabona@cox.net
  • Phone: (208) 726-1166
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Winter Season - Wednesday - Saturday 11:30am - 2pmDinner: Sunday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Dave Pederson
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Beer, Entertainment, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: The Sun Valley transportation buses stop at the intersection of Main and 4th Street. Walk down the hill on 4th Street for two blocks and the Hillside Restaurant is located on the left side of the intersection of 4th Street and 1st Ave.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Located at the corner of 1st Ave and 4th Street, Riccabona's Restaurant has extensive curb-side parking. We are in the heart of the Art Galleries which are located on 1st Ave, and 1st Ave is one of the widest streets allowing for both curb-side and center-street parking.
  • Catering: For special occasions, Riccabona's Restaurant coordinates events with our neighbor, the Gail Severn Gallery. The ability to roam the spacious rooms of the Gail Severn Gallery, viewing exquisite art and enjoying sumptuous delights is a rare opportunity and a memorable experience. Riccabona's Restaurant also provides catering services for special events or private dining.
  • Private Party Facilities: Riccabona's Restaurant has the flexibility to accommodate up to 70 people for air-conditioned inside dining. During the summer, we can accommodate approximately 150 people on our beautiful patios which have extensive views of the surrounding mountains. The restaurant has hosted a number of wedding receptions, rehearsal dinners and private birthday celebrations.
  • Private Party Contact: Steve Riccabona (208) 726-1166
  • Entertainment: Riccabona's Restaurant features nights of piano entertainment to enhance the dining experience.

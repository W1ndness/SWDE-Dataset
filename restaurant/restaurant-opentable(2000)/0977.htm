Presidio Social Club

writes...
  • The Presidio Social Club is open daily for all your social gatherings. Our menu features Classic American Cuisine with occasional twists guaranteed to liven up even the foggiest San Franciscan days. Classic favorites include our famous Bunker Buster Burger, Meatloaf with sour cream smashed red potatoes, and seasonal menu offerings that appeal to both simple and sophisticated palates. Our bar serves classic and contemporary cocktails, imported and domestic beers and ales, affordable wines by the bottle or the glass, and no attitude! Whether it is a casual brunch, an early evening cocktail, or a special social occasion, PSC will be there to provide great food and great service.

Seaglass Restaurant and Lounge

writes...
  • Panoramic ocean views. The feel of waves breaking gently beneath your feet. A contemporary yet comfortable atmosphere. Fresh food served in an environmentally sustainable manner. This is Seaglass Restaurant, a new 175-seat upscale casual destination dining experience now open at the edge of the Atlantic Ocean on Salisbury Beach. Ideal for intimate get-togethers as well as gatherings with friends or family, Seaglass Restaurant offers fresh fish, steak, seafood and seasonal, farm-to-table vegetable entrees prepared with regional ingredients and “green” practices. Committed to being environmentally conscious in its mission and actions, Seaglass Restaurant is seeking recognition as one of the state’s few certified green restaurants by the national Green Restaurant Association. PLEASE NOTE THAT SEAGLASS TRIES TO HONOR ALL RESERVATION REQUESTS, HOWEVER THERE ARE A LIMTED NUMBER OF WINDOW SEATS THAT RESERVE WEEKS IN ADVANCE. PLEASE CONTACT THE RESTAURANT FOR AVAILABILITY.

La Merenda

writes...
  • La Merenda is a casual tapas bar & restaurant located in Milwaukee's historic Walker's Point. From antipasti to meze, local brews to South American wines, we will satisfy your craving for regional and international flavor. At La Merenda we are proud members of Braise RSA, a group of restaurants concerned with local and sustainable food. We use as many local ingredients as possible.

Vince and Eddie's

writes...
  • Since 1989, Vince and Eddie's has established a reputation for serving quality New American comfort cuisine to a well-heeled Lincoln Center audience. Specializing in wonderful hearty meat oriented dishes and creative fresh seafood entrees, the restaurant offers a cozy New England - style ambience in a beautiful space appointed with three fireplaces.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Comfort Food
  • Neighborhood: Lincoln Center
  • Cross Street: Columbus & Central Park West
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.vinceandeddiesnewyork.com
  • Phone: (212) 721-0068
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Wednesday: 4:30pm - 11:00pm,
    Thursday - Saturday: 4:30pm - 12:00am, Sunday: 4:30pm - 11:00pm
    Brunch: Saturday - Sunday: 11:00am - 3:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Delivery, Fireplace, Full Bar, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wine
  • Public Transit: B, C Subway to 72nd Street and the 1 Subway to 66th Street.
  • Parking: No
  • Private Party Facilities: Private parties are available in our enclosed garden atrium.
  • Private Party Contact: Peter Dee or John Daniels (212) 721-0068

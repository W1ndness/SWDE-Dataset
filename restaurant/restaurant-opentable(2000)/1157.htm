Triumph Grill

writes...
  • With the wind at your back on the open road, life often offers you a fork. Do you choose the familiar or are you feeling adventurous? What happens when those two paths meet? With American favorites such as our delicious All American Burger and Southwestern Smoked Shrimp Enchiladas to more BOLD tastes like our Grilled Vegetable & Three Cheese Lasagna or our Cowboy Reuben; Triumph Grill is that intersection! Triumph Grill is located in Grand Center adjacent to the Moto Museum and offers a décor inspired by vintage motorcycles and culture. We serve lunch and dinner, offer outdoor and indoor seating, a fine wine & cocktail selection and plenty of things to admire. We look forward to serving you.

  • Cuisine: American

  • Public Transit: Metropolitan bus routes offer stops near Triumph Grill on Lindell.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: There is ample parking immediately behind Triumph Grill. Street parking is also available.
  • Catering: If you’re looking to have an event catered, and want to stray away from the mundane, let us provide you with BOLD AMERICAN tastes that are sure to go over well. Triumph Grill can provide anything on our menu for your catering needs. We will also customize a menu based on your needs. Contact us for more information
  • Private Party Facilities: Triumph Grill welcomes Private Parties to our Agusta Room. This is the inner sanctum for the sportsman motorcyclist. We have designed this room without restraint for the motorcycle enthusiast. Dominated by the two motorcycles racing each other on the ceiling (a 1956 British BSA and a 1952 Czechoslovakian CZ), the Agusta Room also features “The History of the St. Louis Motorcycling Community”, a photographic essay that continually changes. In fact, we invite our guests to bring photographs of themselves with their motorcycles, no matter what age, and we will display it with other members of the St. Louis motorcycling community. This room has a maximum capacity of 40 occupants and offers a large projector screen for movies or presentations, A/V hookups and wireless Internet for power point presentations. The Agusta Room is perfect for a party or business meeting.
  • Private Party Contact: Todd Wyatt (314) 446-1801

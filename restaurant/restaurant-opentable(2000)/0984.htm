The Rotunda at Neiman Marcus - San Francisco

writes...
  • We invite you to take part in a time honored San Francisco tradition and lunch at the Rotunda at Neiman Marcus. This spectacular restaurant sits underneath a stunning glass dome constructed of more than 2500 pieces of colored glass. The room curves around a central atrium four stories above the entrance. Be sure to request a cozy private booth or a window seat overlooking historic Union Square. Indulge in a glass of bubbles while noshing on our signature Lobster Club or savor delicate sweets and tea sandwiches with our Afternoon Tea.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, American
  • Neighborhood: Downtown / Union Square
  • Cross Street: Geary Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.neimanmarcus.com/store/info/restaurant.jhtml?rest=200010
  • Email: annemarie_domizio@neimanmarcus.com
  • Phone: (415) 249-2720
  • Hours of Operation: Sunday 12:00pm - 4:00pm Monday - Thursday 11:00am - 4:00pm Friday - Saturday 11:00am - 5:00pm Afternoon Tea 2:30pm - 4:00pm Sunday - Thursday Afternoon Tea 2:30pm - 5:00pm Friday - Saturday
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Barney Brown
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, High Tea, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: - Powell Street BART station. - #30 Stockton Bus (Stockton/Geary stop going south, Kearney/Geary stop going north). - #38 Geary Bus (Stockton/O'Farrell stop going east, Geary/Stockton stop going west).
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Choose from 3 conveniently located garages. - Union Square Garage - Ellis-O'Farrell Garage - Sutter-Stockton Garage.
  • Catering: The Rotunda specializes in evening events including wedding receptions, corporate and convention dinners, cocktail parties, rehearsal dinners and holiday parties. Holiday parties are especially beautiful with the four story Neiman Marcus Holiday Tree rising up through the restaurant. The floor to ceiling windows offer guests an intimate bird's eye view of Union Square.

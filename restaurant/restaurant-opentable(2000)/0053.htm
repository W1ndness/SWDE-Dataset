Aquariva

writes...
  • IMPORTANT NOTE: when using GPS, please use address 4650 SW Macadam Ave, Portland, Oregon 97239. Easygoing elegance. Relaxed sophistication. Perfect riverside location. Aquariva Italian Kitchen offers a seasonal Italian-inspired menu in its dramatic space on the banks of the Willamette River just minutes from downtown. Aquariva is open for dinner seven days a week. Serving Lunch Mon-Fri, Sunday Brunch 10:00am - 3:00pm, Dinner 7 nights a week, featuring happy hour every day 3:00 - 6:30! Outdoor riverside patio dining. Complimentary valet parking and wireless internet access.

Perry's Italian Grille

writes...
  • Perry's Tradition…. For more than two decades, Perry's has been creating memorable dining experiences by combining superior food with outstanding service. A Houston-based, family-owned business, Perry's Italian Grille offers Grandmother Attanasio's homemade recipes, an exclusive wine list and private dining rooms. We take pride in our exceptional quality, creativity and world-class attention to detail, all of which ensures Perry's Italian Grille is for any occasion. Experience the tradition today.

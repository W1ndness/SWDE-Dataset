Restaurant Sabor

writes...
  • Step into Baltimore’s exciting new culinary treasure. Beautiful architecture, rich wood detail, lush fabric, and handsome artisan lighting surround you with luxury and comfort. Peer into our open kitchen and watch in awe as Chef Domacassé and his crew create unique and delicious cuisine. Restaurant Sabor features an old-world community table, where you can drop in and join others, come with friends, or just enjoy time alone while you dine. No reservations are necessary for seating at the community table. We invite you to join us for your next meal.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Continental, Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Lutherville
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

  • Private Party Facilities: We can accommodate private parties for brunch on weekends, and during week for lunch or dinner.

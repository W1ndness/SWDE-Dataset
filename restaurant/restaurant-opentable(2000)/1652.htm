Amber Gramercy

writes...
  • Amber serves up delicious Asian-fusion inspired dishes to New Yorkers and tourists alike. This Asian-inspired restaurant and lounge is located in North Gramercy in Manhattan. With great music and lively décor, this Asian restaurant has a relaxed, downtown feel. Amber offers a delicious menu with fresh Asian cuisine in a great ambiance. They have three floors and high ceilings for an overall unique experience. On the first floor, there is a large bar with two large flat screen TVs and open windows that allow for fun people-watching. In the main dining area on the second level there is a giant Buddha on the top of the Koi pond.

Our Private Dining Facilities

    Total Capacity 300

At Astaria, guests are met with a blend of classic architecture and reassuring comfort. The bar and lounge run the entire length of the east wall and includes an area that entertains parties of up to 35 seated. The entire restaurant is available for buyout and can accommodate up to 150 guests. Astaria's history is events; prior to opening the restaurant in 2003, we owned a catering business for 8 years and searched for a restaurant that included private dining space. The Peninsula Room hosts an intimate dinner for 20 as successfully as a corporate cocktail party for 200. Our attentive and knowledgeable staff is committed to helping you create an event that is not only unforgettable but reflects your personal style and budget. Private Dining: Peninsula Room - Reception 200, seated 150. Fireside Room - Reception 150; seated 120, Astaria Restaurant – reception 150, seated 120, Bar and Lounge – reception 40, seated 35. Astaria has WIFI throughout all dining areas.

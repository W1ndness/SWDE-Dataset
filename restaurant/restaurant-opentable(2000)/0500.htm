Preston's

writes...
  • Located in Preston Center at the corner of NW Highway and Preston, this upscale casual Park Cities restaurant offers superior USDA Prime steaks, mouth watering seafood and creative specials that are sure to impress. The new American with a texas "twist" menu features locally grown produce and offers daily game and seafood specials options.

    Preston's offers live piano music four nights a week, perfect for relaxing at the end of a long day.

    Be it business or pleasure, Preston's is your restaurant for special gatherings. The private dining areas seat 6 to 80 guests and a personalized menu can be designed specifically for your event by the restaurant's award winning chef. Call today and we will be happy to help.

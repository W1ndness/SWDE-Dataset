Delmonico’s Steakhouse at the Hilton Philadelphia City Avenue

writes...
  • Located on City Avenue inside the beautiful Hilton Philadelphia Hotel, Delmonico's Steakhouse boasts an atmosphere of relaxed elegance & style. Delmonico's Steakhouse serves classic steak house offerings such as chops, fresh seafood & only serves prime, dry aged meat.
    The restaurant features a large wine list, professional, gracious service & a glass enclosed private dining room for those special occasions. Should you wish to secure private dining accommodations, please call the restaurant directly.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, American
  • Neighborhood: Center City
  • Cross Street: Belmont

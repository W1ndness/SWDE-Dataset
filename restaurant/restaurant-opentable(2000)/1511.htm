  • Cafe Noir, a brasserie and bar offering a casual twist on classic French cooking. Our comfortable design invites guests to relax and unwind as they savor simple yet elegant food that features ingredients prepared in classic French style, perfect for a special occasion or a quiet night out. Lively bar scene on weekend nights.

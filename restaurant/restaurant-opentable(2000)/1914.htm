  • Welcome:

    Thank you for visiting Shalezeh, where fine Persian cuisine mingles with creativity for a truly unforgettable experience. Shalezeh and Persepolis. Each offers a unique, culturally enriching experience that celebrates the enjoyment of food, wine, music, art and architecture. Shalezeh's ambition is to offer each client a memorable dining experience; memorable for the indisputable quality of the ingredients, expert preparation and beautiful presentation. First time clients discover a Persian cuisine celebrating the most glorious ingredients and revealing their delicious flavors. Returning customers enjoy the depth of this “the worlds oldest” imagination, constantly savoring dishes from the Persian-inspired specials as well as tasting the delicious Kebobs interpretation of classic recipes.

    Recently Shalezeh was Awarded with 1-STAR in 2010 MICHELIN GUIDE.
    Bon Appétit.

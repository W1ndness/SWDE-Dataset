Thalia Spice

writes...
  • Thalia Spice, inspired by land and sea, is an eclectic collection of various pan Asian cuisines served in tapas size portion for your tasting enjoyment in a collection of intimate, modern loft dining rooms.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Asian, Pan-Asian, Sushi
  • Neighborhood: River West
  • Cross Street: Green St
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://thaliaspice.com
  • Email: info@thaliaspice.com
  • Phone: (312) 226-6020
  • Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 2:29pm
    Monday - Thursday: 2:30pm - 10:00pm, Friday: 2:30pm - 11:00pm, Saturday: 3:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 12:00am - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Noy (Mom) Sengsourichanh
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Cafe, Counter Seating, Delivery, Fireplace, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Destination "Chicago & Halsted". Take the BLUE, BROWN or RED Line to the Chicago Stop. Then head towards 833 W Chicago Ave. by walking or taking the CTA Bus #66. Eastbound from BLUE (.25 mile). Westbound from RED (1 mile) or BROWN (.5 mile).
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Thalia is surrounded by ample street parking and attached to a “self-park” parking lot made available by the Chicago Center of Performing Arts. Coin operated meter parking is available on Chicago Ave too (8 quarters for two hours with the City's new parking meter rates).
  • Catering: Thalia offers off-site catering enabling you to bring their most popular dishes to your home or office for a lunch or dinner your guests are sure to remember. We regularly diliver lunch orders feeding 175 people. You can think big, and think of Thalia.
  • Private Party Facilities: Thalia can "wow" your guests with any combination of their spectacular dining rooms. From an intimate dinner for two or a hundred, Thalia will make your romantic evening plans something special.
  • Private Party Contact: Anna (312) 226-6020

  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Free parking available in Roseville Galleria Mall parking lot.
  • Private Party Facilities: Our large, high ceiling Main Dining Room is filled with rich woods, Tuscan style hand-painted frescoes and huge picture windows. Il Fornaio welcomes groups in this main dining area. With groups of 10 or more, Il Fornaio will assist in creating a special menu that is suited for the needs of your group. Our Loggia contains decorative archways and is adorned with beautifully quilted Italian drapes that can create a semi-private dining area to host receptions up to 100 people. The drapes can be closed to section off certain rooms in the Loggia to accommodate parties of 10 or more. The Mosaic tile floors, high ceilings and distinctive furniture give the Loggia an al fresco feeling with indoor comfort. Our beautiful decorated Italian style Piazza or patio is partially enclosed and is accentuated by two Italian fireplaces in each corner. This area can accommodate parties of 10 to 100 people weather permitting.

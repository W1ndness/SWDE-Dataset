Amanda's Restaurant

writes...
  • Amanda's has been delighting customers for over 15 years. Guests enter our elegantly restored brownstones and are warmly welcomed by an accommodating staff. Although geographically close to Manhattan and certainly as culinary sophisticated, we're not slow to smile and truly aim to please each guest. Highly rated by the Zagat Guide for food, service and decor, we are proud to have been named #5 in the state for Most Popular Restaurant. Our popularity stems from the talents of our CIA-trained chef, Rodney Petersen, our dedication to warm, gracious service and the comfortable elegance of our dining rooms. Amanda's is the natural choice for so many occasions: the romantic couple on a "date to impress," a family brunch on Sunday or the place to enjoy pre-theater Early Dinner at a price that is really too good to believe. Sophisticated wine lovers enjoy our monthly five-course wine dinners.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Hoboken
  • Cross Street: between 9th & 10th Streets
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.amandasrestaurant.com
  • Email: manager@amandasrestaurant.com
  • Phone: (201) 798-0101
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm,
    Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm,
    Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm

    Brunch: Saturday - Sunday: 11:00am - 3:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Rodney Petersen
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: While parking in Hoboken can be a challenge, we offer validation for the Independence Garage on 12th & Hudson Streets. Simply tell the attendants you are dining at Amanda's & they will provide you a ticket which we will validate at the restaurant. (Please note that we are unable to provide validation for our early dinner guests.)
  • Catering: Amanda's can provide off-site catering for groups of most sizes within the area. To view our catering menu, please visit our website www.amandasrestaurant.com.
  • Private Party Facilities: Amanda's provides the perfect venue for a personalized party with private dining rooms that accommodate from 10-90 guests. Saturday and Sunday Brunch parties seem favored for baby and bridal showers as well as baptismal celebrations. We also host dinner parties for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings and corporate dinners. For parties of twelve guests or more, we ask that the party host/hostess pre-select a menu so that their guests will choose from a personalized menu. We are happy to accommodate special menu preferences. Please call us at your convenience with any questions.
  • Private Party Contact: Dana (201) 798-0101

Rughetta Ristorante

writes...
  • Rughetta, located on a quaint street on the Upper East Side is everything an Italian Restaurant should be. Friendly attentive service along with quality ingredients. Moderately priced tabs make this restaurant a winner. Signature dishes include the "Carciofi alla Giudia" and the unusual "Calamari Votapiatto," as well the "Tortelloni di Carne". The exclusive and extensive wine list offer an array of solid choices at fair value. Finish off your meal with one of our tempting, delicious, and home-made desserts.

The Bridge

writes...
  • The Bridge offers a menu that change weekly, featuring California fresh ingredients and seasonal Farmer’s Market finds. Expect appetizer highlights such as Wild Mushrooms on Toast with Veal Jus, Grilled Radicchio with warm Pesto Goat Cheese, and Poached asparagus with Toasted Hazelnuts. Entrées might include the Pan Seared Striped Bass with Fennel Puree and Ribeye with Bone Marrow Butter, while the dessert menu will come complete with Rhubarb strawberry Tarte and lavender ice cream. The hotel’s famed mixologists are stirring up self-serve sangria specials to match the weekly menus. White and red wine sangria will feature seasonal ingredients such as black mission figs, white peaches, and apricots, mixed with fresh herbs and spices. Additionally, a weekly wine menu will feature $30 bottles and glasses between $7-$13. With main plates averaging around $20, appetizers falling under $10, and bottomless Sangria at $7, The Bridge is an affordable and simple alternative to summer dining.

Yemanja Brazil

writes...
  • Yemanja Brasil offers tropical warmth , exposed brick walls adorned with macarame hammocks plus plants and a colorful patio. Enjoy a "caipirinha" Brazil"s national drink with many of the delicious dishes prepared to order such as the "Moqueca Bahiana" a seafood stew in a spicy coconut broth. Yemanja Brasil will take you away to heat of South America without the airfare.

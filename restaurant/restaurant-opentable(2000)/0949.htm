Madrona Manor

writes...
  • At the nexus of three appellations just a mile from downtown Healdsburg, Madrona Manor’s quintessential Victorian estate is at the heart of Sonoma’s wine country. With an invitingly sumptuous atmosphere, connoisseurs of fine cuisine will find much to enjoy at the Manor. Superb ala carte, Chef’s five or seven course tasting menus, an extensive cellar, the mastery of professional staff, and the elegance of the dining rooms integrate seamlessly. Zagat Survey 2007 rates Madrona Manor as tied for third among all restaurants in Napa and Sonoma counties. Chef Jesse Mallgren takes full advantage of a wealth of local, seasonal produce from the on-site organic garden and from local purveyors to create a spectrum of flavors sure to delight your palate. His menus draw inspiration from both classical French and Asian cuisines, refined to reflect California. Ever creative, his palette is often inspired by the garden with subtle nuance and wine-friendly flavors. We look forward to your company!

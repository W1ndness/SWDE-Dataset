Joya Restaurant & Lounge

writes...
  • From tapas to entradas, Joya Restaurant and Lounge provides a modern interpretation of the rich culinary lineage of Spain and Latin America. Joya is proud to have teamed with Shopworks (designers of W-Hotels in New York, Chicago, San Francisco, Seattle and San Diego) to create a natural-hued, contemporary restaurant and lounge that imparts guests with a feeling of comfortable exclusivity. So if you are looking to enjoy a refined Latin-influenced dining experience, new age cocktails and festive live music, we invite you to join us at Joya Restaurant & Lounge.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Latin / Spanish, Tapas / Small Plates
  • Neighborhood: Palo Alto
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.joyarestaurant.com
  • Email: info@joyarestaurant.com
  • Phone: (650) 853-9800
  • Hours of Operation: Open Daily from 11:30 am - 10:00 pm Open until Midnight on Thursday - Saturday
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Fabrice Roux
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Dancing, Entertainment, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: Palo Alto Caltrain stop is just a few blocks away.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Abundant free parking adjacent to Joya in the multi-level Florence Street parking garage.
  • Private Party Facilities: From initial design inception, Joya was envisioned to beautifully accomodate private parties. As such, Joya is a collection of unique, yet flowing separate spaces capable of hosting private parties from 8 to over 200 people in comfortable, modern style.
  • Private Party Contact: Sara Mork (650) 853-9800 x3
  • Entertainment: Every Sunday night, from 6 - 9 PM, Joya features live music performances on its open-air patio. Joya hosts live music and dance events on other evenings as well. The lastest event information is announced on our website: www.joyarestaurant.com

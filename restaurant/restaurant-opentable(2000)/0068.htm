Ten 01

writes...
  • Ten 01’s Executive Chef Michael Hanaghan takes a modern approach to classic American flavors, producing precise dishes focused on balanced, pure, seasonal ingredients. The award winning wine list is the perfect partner to Chef Hanaghan’s refined cuisine. Ten 01 is a restaurant that combines contemporary design with environmentally friendly principles. Sustainable sourcing in the kitchen is mirrored in the make up of the restaurant itself. Downstairs at Ten 01 combines the energy of the bar with the action of an open kitchen, while the second floor provides diners with views of Portland’s bustling Pearl District while enjoying an exceptional fine dining experience.

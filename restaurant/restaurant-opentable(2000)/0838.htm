5A5 Steak Lounge

writes...
  • 5A5 Steak Lounge is San Francisco's first 'Steak Lounge' - a sleek, contemporary version of the traditional steakhouse with an upbeat ambiance of a cocktail lounge. Executive Chef Allen Chen (Alexander's Steakhouse) has developed an outstanding menu which both complements and explores the boundaries of the traditional 'steak & potatoes' fare. Steaks: we serve US Certified Angus Beef, American and Australian 'Kobe', and Japanese A5 Wagyu imported from Japan. A5 stands for the highest grade of beef in the world 5A5: 5 senses/the A5 experience. 5A5 Steak Lounge is conveniently located in the northern FiDi, perfect for happy hour, dining, and late night cocktails. Please view our website for happy hour specials and other events.

Noted as one of the top 5 restaurants in SF (Malou Review, KTVU Fox channel 2), 5A5 combines amazing food with excellent service in elegant but fun surroundings. 5A5 is proud to offer several unique spaces for private events of up to 400 guests, ranging from passed hors d'oeuvres and cocktails to formal, seated events. 7 days a week – any time of the day. Our event planner and chef tailor each event to your specific needs. Our wine director can recommend wines from our extensive wine list including over 150 selections primarily from California and France. 5A5 is an ideal spot for social events, corporate events, cocktail parties, special occasions, rehearsal dinners and weddings. Audio/Visual Capabilities exist on site – with the ability to project presentations and corporate logos onto a large 24ft wide by 6ft high panel upstairs in the main room, and a 10ft wide by 6ft high wall in the private dining room.

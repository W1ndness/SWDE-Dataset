  • Three venues in one location!
    Circa Restaurant, Orlando Magazine voted "Best New American Restaurant" '09, Circa Pub, a casual "come as you are" British Pub and Circa Lounge, the place to dance after dinner until 2am.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Winter Park
  • Cross Street: Canton
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

  • Email: info@circa1926.com

  • Hours of Operation: Lunch Daily: 11:00am - 3:00pm

Bistro One LR - Ritz-Carlton - South Beach

writes...
  • With its brightly polished retro appearance, imaginative cuisine, lively atmosphere and a young, talented chef from Barcelona, Bistro One LR is one of the top, new restaurants on South Beach, and happens to be in the city’s newest Five Star-caliber hotel - The Ritz-Carlton, South Beach. Its New Age take on classic American cuisine makes the restaurant a unique addition to the SoBe scene and combines stylish elegance with an innate sense of fun. Executive Chef Thomas Connell orchestrates a symphony of flavors with each dish, using fresh, natural products and a passionate belief in capturing the essence of the basics. Those who dine inside are privy to the hotel’s superb art collection, stainless steel accents, attentive service and action from within the adjacent Lapidus Lounge ?the city’s newest gathering place for evening cocktails against the backdrop of the latest European and world lounge music spun by international DJs.

Bourbon Steak - MGM Grand Detroit

writes...
  • Bourbon Steak is a modern American steakhouse, the restaurant offers modern American fare with a focus on all-natural Certified Angus Beef and American Kobe cuts of beef cooked over Mesquite wood. Other specialties include Japanese A5 Kobe, American Kobe Beef Short Ribs with horseradish-celeriac puree and red wine jus. 21+ only.

Sonoma Wine Garden - Santa Monica

  • Cutting-edge Californian cuisine with Mediterranean, Italian, and Austrian influences served tapas-style. Located at the brim of Santa Monica Place, Sonoma Wine Garden offers breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean beach. Featuring a wood-burning pizza oven, outdoor garden patio, and upscale lounge area that offers countless wines from all over the world. Sonoma Wine Garden is a sophisticated new addition to the bustling atmosphere of Santa Monica Place.

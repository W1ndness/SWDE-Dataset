310 Lakeside

writes...
  • A new addition to the 310 Family, 310 Lakeside makes you feel right at home with great food, extensive wine list and full bar. We are committed to serving only the highest quality and freshest foods, always prepared to your desired taste. Relax and enjoy our award winning “New American” cuisine and our professional service with a personal touch. Daily lunch and dinner specials accompany our menu along with a late night menu and our signature desserts to round out your experience. 310 Lakeside has a beautiful view of Lake Eola in downtown Orlando and offers outdoor dining as well as a casual and relaxed dining room. We can now be your choice for special occasions as well as wedding receptions, business functions and any other catering needs. Sunday Brunch is served along with our full menu and reservations are accepted either online or by phone. We know you will enjoy our great outdoor atmosphere or the indoor ambience of our restaurants.

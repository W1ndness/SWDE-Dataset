Frida Restaurant

writes...
  • At Frida you will find plenty of spice on the menu and a fresh attitude throughout the restaurant. With a unique approach to healthy, authentic Mexican cuisine, you will experience the real traditional flavors of the owner's ancestors in these Mexican recipes. Exceptional appetizers and specialty entrees not to be missed include Mole Poblano and Pescado a la Talla. Among the dessert favorites are Pastel de Tres Leches and Crepas con Cajeta. Gorgeous bar offers great margaritas in a classy Beverly Hills setting.

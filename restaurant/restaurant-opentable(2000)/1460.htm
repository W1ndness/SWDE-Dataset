Whisper Restaurant & Lounge

writes...
  • The Whisper Restaurant and Lounge is located at the end of a cobblestone street at the world famous, The Grove, tucked away from the crowds and fast pace of shoppers and passersby. Join us in our speakeasy inspired dining room and bar as we serve a California eclectic menu offered by Los Angeles' own, Anthony Jacquet.

    Settle into an oversized booth, or allow us to sit you on our year round heated patio for an experience unrivaled anywhere in Los Angeles. Handmade specialty cocktails range from our 'Blueberry Lemondrop' to the famous 'Alley Whisper'. An international line up makes up our wines by the glass, with carefully selected labels invoking the best of each region selected.

    The highlight of The Whisper Restaurant and Lounge is the various selections offered by Chef Jacquet. Locals favorites include our Spinach and Roasted Mushroom Salad, Chili-Garlic Calamari, Grilled Salmon with Housemade Gnocchi, and our selection of flatbreads.

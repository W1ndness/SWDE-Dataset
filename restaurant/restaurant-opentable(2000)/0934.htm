La Briciola

writes...
  • Authentic Italian restaurant located in the heart of SOMA. Enjoy Northern Italian food in a cozy & intimate environment. We specialize in Tuscan and Piedmontese cuisine, serving a variety of home-made pastas in addition to traditional dishes Dalla Terra & Dal Mare.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian, Seafood, European
  • Neighborhood: SOMA
  • Cross Street: Bryant
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.labriciola-sf.com
  • Email: labriciola.sf@gmail.com
  • Phone: (415) 512-0300
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Mon-Fri 11:30am - 2:30pm
    Dinner: Sunday-Monday 5:30pm - 10:00pm
    Friday-Saturday 5-10:30
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Gian Luca Toschi
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Counter Seating, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: Located within walking distance from both Bart and Muni systems.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Large & affordable parking lot right around the corner on Stillman Street. There is also plenty of street parking (meters run until 6 pm).
  • Private Party Facilities: We have a semi-private room that can accommodate up to 25 guests. In addition, we can accommodate larger parties in our main dining room. Please contact us to plan your event here!

La Pastaia

writes...
  • The four star rated, family owned La Pastaia Restaurant continually delights Bay Area diners with it's Contemporary Italian cuisine and metropolitan ambience.

    Located in the breathtaking, art deco, boutiuqe Hotel De Anza, La Pastaia is just minutes away from the San Jose International Airport, and walking distance to the Arena, Center for Performing Arts, the Tech Museum and the Convention Center.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: San Jose
  • Cross Street: Notre Dame
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.lapastaia.com
  • Email: morgan@lapastaia.com
  • Phone: (408) 286-8686
  • Hours of Operation: Hours: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 3:00pm, Saturday - Sunday: 12:00pm - 2:00pm Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
    Enoteca open daily.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Forrest Gingold
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Santa Clara St. Light Rail
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: There is ample street parking, hotel valet service and a private parking lot.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private rooms range from the intimate and self-contained to the beautiful Mediteranean style Palm Court Terrace. Private dining can be arranged for parties of 2 to 50 in the restaurant. The outdoor terrace is also available for private parties during lunch.
  • Private Party Contact: Cara Martin (408) 286-8686
  • Entertainment: Live music is featured in the lounge every Friday and Saturday night.
    La Pastaia's Enoteca is open daily to enjoy flights of Italian wines, espresso and an array of small plates. The Enoteca is also available for private tastings.

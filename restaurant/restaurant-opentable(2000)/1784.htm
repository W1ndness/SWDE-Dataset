Hollywood Brown Derby

writes...
  • The Golden Age of Hollywood was defined by glamour, romance and sophistication. Since 1926, nothing embodied those qualities more than the Brown Derby Restaurants. Defined by their picturesque interiors, notably the caricatures of Hollywood actors, producers and directors who personified the glamour years of Hollywood. Soon you will be able to create your own Brown Derby memories as the Mallozzi family brings this renowned restaurant to the Capital Region of New York. Celebrate in style, dining with your "someone special" in an atmosphere showcasing the grace and elegance of this legendary Hollywood haunt.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Albany
  • Cross Street: Chapel Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.thehollywoodbrownderby.com
  • Email: greg@thehollywoodbrownderby.com
  • Phone: (518) 463-1945
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:30pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Jon Simmons
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: The Brown Derby offers complimentary shuttle service for our guests that are working in the Albany area. CDTA also stops right outside our front doors on Clinton Avenue.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: The Brown Derby offers each guest valet service. For our guests that may be attending an event at The Palace or Capital Rep, after dinner leave your car with us and enjoy the show and then return to the derby where your car will be waiting for you.
  • Catering: The Hollywood Brown offers a full service menu for off premise events. Whether it be an office party for 10 or a large corporate dinner for hundreds, the Brown Derby is ready and willing to cater your event.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Derby Loft offers an intimate setting with beautiful views of downtown Albany. The loft will accommodate served dinners up to 40 and cocktail parties up to 125 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Greg Mardigian (518) 463-1945
  • Entertainment: The Brown Derby offers live entertainment every Friday evening from 7:30pm to 11:00pm.

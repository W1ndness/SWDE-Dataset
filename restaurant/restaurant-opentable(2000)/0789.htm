Scott's Seafood Grill & Bar - Folsom

writes...
  • Voted the Sacramento area's best seafood restaurant for the past 16 years, Scott's is always on the hunt for the freshest and best sustainable seafood available, served by a friendly knowledgeable staff in a comfortable elegant setting. Whether your tastes run to steak, pasta, chicken or vegetarian, Scott's has something for everyone.

  • Cuisine: Seafood, American
  • Neighborhood: Folsom
  • Cross Street: Madison
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

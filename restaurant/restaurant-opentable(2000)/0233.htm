The Marine Room

writes...
  • Opened in 1941, the multi-awarding winning Marine Room Restaurant continues a tradition of excellence along the shores of La Jolla. Join Chef Bernard Guillas and Chef de Cuisine, Ron Oliver on a culinary journey as you indulge in global cuisine rooted in the French classics. With the ocean tide just inches from your window it is more than great dining it is an experience not to be missed.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Global, International
  • Neighborhood: La Jolla / Golden Triangle
  • Cross Street: La Jolla Shores Drive
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.MarineRoom.com
  • Email: MR@MarineRoom.com
  • Phone: (858) 459-7222
  • Hours of Operation: Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday to Thursday: 5:30 p.m. - 9:30 p.m. Friday and Saturday: 5:30 p.m. - 10 p.m. Lounge: Daily: 4 p.m. to Close Happy Hour Sunday to Friday: 4 p.m. to 6 p.m. Featuring "two-for-one" lounge food items on Thursday and Friday during happy hour!
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Bernard Guillas
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet parking is available for a nominal fee. Street parking is also available.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Marine Room offers a unique location for your next special event. Celebrate your special occasion in our new private dining area, which seats up to 50 people, or host an elaborate daytime affair for up to 200 people in our spectacular oceanfront restaurant setting. Enjoy enchanting views of the sun and surf while the Marine Room is exclusively yours for your event.
  • Private Party Contact: Lauren Van Zile (858) 964-1977
  • Special Events & Promotions:

Big Papi's Grille

writes...
  • Big Papi's Grille is an upscale casual restaurant designed to appeal to a wide audience, with featured menu items including natural steaks, gourmet burgers, entree salads, and much more. BPG has several dining options, including table 34; a semi-private 20 seat dining area preferred by Big Papi and his family and friends. We also have a private dining room for 60 people and patio dining with "loungy-comfy" furniture. Of course, don't miss all the Red Sox/Patriots/Bruins/Celtics action on our many HD TV's.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Comfort Food, Latin / Spanish
  • Neighborhood: Framingham
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.bigpapisgrille.com
  • Email: bigpapisgrille@classicrestaurants.com
  • Phone: (508) 620-9990
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch 11:30am-4pm Dinner 4pm to Close
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Executive Chef Scott Olsen
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Pull into one of our many spaces in our well lit parking lot.
  • Private Party Facilities: Big Papi's Grille features Table 34 and the Fenway Room for private or semi-private dining. The rooms are perfect for business functions, retirement parties, anniversaries, birthday parties, rehearsal dinners, showers or any other special event.
  • Private Party Contact: Kate Benz (508) 620-9990
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Our private rooms have large HD flatscreens with both internet and laptop connectivity for presentations (ideal for Business Power Point or for sharing family photos i.e. birthdays, anniversary's, etc).

Hefner Grill

writes...
  • Hefner Grill is a culinary collection of well executed favorites from the classic urban tavern to the corner bar & grill. The atmosphere is relaxed with a friendly, well informed service style. Our view of Lake Hefner will make you feel miles away. Hefner Grill is the only restaurant on the lake that takes reservations, so come as you are, bring an appetite and let us take care of the rest.

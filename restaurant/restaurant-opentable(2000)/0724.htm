Flyte World Dining & Wine

writes...
  • Flyte World Dining & Wine is a unique culinary experience. The kitchen staff incorporates organic produce, free-range meats and beautiful presentations into a seasonal, multicultural menu. Our eclectic, world-wide, detailed wine list and knowledgeable staff take the guesswork out of your wine or beer pairings. Flyte features 100+ interesting wines by the glass, a connoisseur draft beer list, and "flytes" of wine, beer and even special dinner menu items. There's also a 20 seat "kitchen dining" room, where you can dine in the midst of the action. Flyte's service and hospitality focus provide personal touches from the moment you make a reservation, to the second you leave the restaurant. The restaurant interior is lively, colorful and beautifully lit to provide the perfect setting to your dining & wine adventure. $35 3-course "Flyte Fix" available any time (selections vary day to day). Happy Hour from 4:30 to 7:30 in the lounge everyday. See website for details!

Our Private Dining Facilities

  • Average Price $$$
  • |
    Total Capacity 225

Flyte has a 24-seat Kitchen Dining Room that is suitable for presentations. Or, cut loose and let your group watch and interact with our chefs as they create your culinary experience. For larger cocktail parties that require lighter food options (or for those who like a bargain), our 60 seat lounge is available for your enjoyment. Buyouts start at just $1,000 (pre tax and tip). There is a 42" LCD TV for your pictures or slideshow. For even bigger groups, we can make our whole space available to you. From a sit down dinner, to food stations, to passed apps, Flyte can do it all. In addition to the LCD, we also have a wireless mic available for you to use free of charge. We have hosted many large corporate events and wedding receptions. Since we are owner-operated, when you have a question, you get an answer right away! Special discounts available for Sunday and Monday bookings. Flyte has a 60 space lot, but valet is available. References available on request.

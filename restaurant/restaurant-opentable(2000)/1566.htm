Mission Oak Grill

writes...
  • Set in the beautiful seaside town of historic Newburyport, Massachusetts, Mission Oak Grill is an inviting, warm restaurant filled with wood tones and classic leather. Guests can dine in our bustling mission style dining room or at our spacious and lively bar. Serving a full menu seven days a week, the food is simply prepared using fresh local ingredients of the highest quality. Mission Oak Grill welcomes you to join us for dinner, or to sample our extensive international wine list. We are sure you will find the service to be exemplary and your experience to be wonderful time and time again.

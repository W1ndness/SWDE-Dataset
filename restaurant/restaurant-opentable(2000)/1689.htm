  • Bistro de la Gare is a neighborhood restaurant serving fresh Mediterranean cooking with strong French and Italian overtones. Maryann Terillo and Elisa Sarno are your hosts. Stop by soon for a table in our newly opened garden area! We now offer a selection of French, Italian and state-side wines and beers.

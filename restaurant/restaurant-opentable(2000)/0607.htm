The Vineyard Grill

writes...
  • A Steakhouse...Naturally. Located on the ground floor of the elegant Ritz-Carlton Orlando, Grande Lakes. Unwind in the relaxed surroundings of The Vineyard Grill, a steakhouse featuring an excellent selection of prime meats, mouth-watering side dishes and fresh seafood. Chef Mark Kaba prepares delightful plates using the freshest regional ingredients, which are complemented by our diverse selection of wines. The Vineyard Grill features indoor and outdoor seating, wine dinner events seasonally, festive Holiday Brunches and knowledgeable, professional service. Every Sunday you can enjoy Chef Kaba's culinary creations during our gourmet Sunday Brunch featuring live entertainment with interactive cooking stations and unlimited Sparkling Wine. Sunday Brunch is served from 12:00pm to 3:00pm, $69 for adults and $29 for children.

Ram Restaurant & Brewery - Rosemont

writes...
  • The Ram Restaurant & Brewery is the place for fun! Whether the occasion is meeting co-workers for freshly brewed beer after work, a cozy family dinner, or meeting friends for the big game; The Ram will make your event a memorable one! The smartly decorated interior houses a casual, though sophisticated environment; & there are no dress code requirements. Conveniently located 3 miles from O'Hare Airport & nestled between dozens of hotels, The Allstate Arena, The Rosemont Theater, & The Donald E. Stephens Convention Center.

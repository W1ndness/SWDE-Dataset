Aquavit Dining Room

writes...
  • Aquavit Dining Room offers Executive Chef Marcus Jernmark’s modern takes on contemporary Scandinavian Cuisine complemented by an extensive winelist and an Aquavit infusion and cocktail program. For lunch, we offer an a la carte menu and a 4-course $42pp tasting menu and for dinner a 3-course $78pp prix fixe and a $105 8-course tasting menu... On Sunday, we serve a traditional Smörgåsbord Brunch for $48pp including a complimentary Carlsberg beer or Danish Mary.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Scandinavian, Seafood, Wild Game
  • Neighborhood: Midtown East
  • Cross Street: between Park and Madison Avenues
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Email: aquavitreservation@aquavit.org
  • Phone: (212) 307-7311
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday 12:00pm -2:30pm Dinner: Monday - Sunday: 5:30pm - 10:30pm Brunch: Sunday: 12:00pm - 2.30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Marcus Jernmark
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Full Bar, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Weekend Brunch, Wine
  • Public Transit: E/V on 53rd and 5th Ave. 6 to 51 street. F on 57th and 6th Ave.
  • Parking: No
  • Parking Details: Parking garage on East 55 street (bet Park Ave & Lexington Ave)
  • Private Party Facilities: Our private dining room accommodates up to 50 guests for luncheons and dinners. The largest single table seats 22 guests. Superb quality audiovisual equipment and high-speed internet connection offer conference room capabilities. The luminous frosted glass wall and subtle bas-relief sculpture create a charming environment for every occasion.
  • Private Party Contact: Peter Klein (212) 957-9045

Bistecca

writes...
  • Bistecca Steakhouse and Wine Bar, located on the terrace level at the Meadows Racetrack and Casino, is the fine dining destination in the greater Pittsburgh/Washington area. Chef d' Cuisine Adam Johnston has brought together classic steakhouse favorites, fresh seafood and Italian cuisine. Bistecca also boasts an eclectic wine list featuring Italian and American wines that is diverse enough to please any palate. Enjoy the panoramic view of the racetrack or step outside Bistecca to overlook the Casino below. Bistecca also has two premiere private dining rooms equipped with audio visual capabilities and five luxury boxes for race events or casual parties. On-line reservations are valid for the main dining room, please call directly to reserve our private dining room areas.

Bistecca offers a range of private rooms for your next event, whether you are looking for the perfect private setting for family and friends or a corporate event with colleagues. Our chef has created specialized menus for the sky boxes or we can assist in creating a special package for your event.

BOA Steakhouse

writes...
  • With it's award winning cuisine and spectacular views of the Las Vegas Strip, BOA Steakhouse is a "must do" for the Las Vegas diner. BOA's menu offers prime steaks, seafood, poultry and chops. With it's expanded dining hours, BOA Steakhouse is a favorite for those catching a show or looking for a late night dining experience. Reservations are recommended.

  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Californian

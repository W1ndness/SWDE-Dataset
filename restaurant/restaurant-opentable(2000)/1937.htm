Tony's Di Napoli

writes...
  • Tony's has served up some of the best old NY Italian favorites. Our cooking comes from Southern Neapolitan traditions and served up in huge family style platters that serve 2 to 3 people.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, Steak, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Upper East Side
  • Cross Street: Between 83rd and 84th Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.tonysnyc.com/
  • Email: reservations@tonysnyc.com
  • Phone: (212) 861-8686
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Saturday: 2:00pm - 5:00pm, Sunday: 2:00pm - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 12:00am, Sunday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Chef Andreas
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Beer, Delivery, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: 4,5,6 Train to 86th and Lexington.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Meter parking and 2 public lots within 1 block.
  • Catering: We offer our special catering plan prices for the groups who want us to bring the party to you. Perfect for office parties, Birthdays, Holidays or any other special occasions.
  • Private Party Facilities: Tony's has beautiful private party rooms available at both of our locations.  We offer a party package planner designed to fit any budget.
  • Private Party Contact: Renee Skubish (212) 861-8686

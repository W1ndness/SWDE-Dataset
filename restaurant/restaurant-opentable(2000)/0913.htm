Gordon Biersch Brewery Restaurant - SF

writes...
  • Enjoy views of the San Francisco Bay from our contemporary atmosphere located in the historic Hill Bros. Coffee building just blocks from the Pac Bell Ball Park at the edge of the waterfront. Gordon Biersch is an established microbrewery and restaurant that brews lager beer in the traditional German style. Our brewery uses the state of the art equipment that was fabricrated in Germany exclusively for Gordon Biersch. Fermenting tanks are visible from both the bar and dining room. We also offer a full bar. Our menu specializes in Pacific-American: contemporary dishes drawing from local and regional products. Located walking distance from the Financial District and Moscone Convention Center we offer some of the most beautiful bay and bridge views in San Francisco.

We also offer custom menus, brew master dinners, off-site catering and tailgate parties.

  • Another Milestone for the Papakonstantinou family! Charlie Papakonstantinou, who has co-owned The Maugus Restaurant since 1980 with his family in Wellesley, MA, has just opened up his new restaurant with his wife Camille, called Milestone. Charlie and his Milestone staff are looking to bring the same tradition of great quality food and service with a southern european flare. Come and experience these exquisite dishes that chef Gabriele Stipo has created.

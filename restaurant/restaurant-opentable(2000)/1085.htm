Axel’s offers casual fine dining at its best. Reminiscent of the classic American supper club experience, Axel’s boasts a winning combination of high quality cuisine and professional, attentive service in a charming, yet modern environment. Focusing on quality and freshness, Axel’s prefers to purchase ingredients locally and all food is Trans fat free and prepared from scratch, including sauces and dressings. The menu features charcoal-grilled steaks, chops, seafood and the freshest and broadest selection of walleye in town. The full-service bar offers a vast and discriminating selection of fine wines and an unequaled breadth of scotch and whiskey.

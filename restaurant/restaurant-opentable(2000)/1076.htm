Jesse's Restaurant

writes...
  • Jesse's is a unique and eclectic dining experience. Located in the quaint village of Magnolia Springs on the massive live oak lined street of Oak Street. Our main dining room is located where the Post office was housed in the late 1800's. The original post slot stands as the wait station. Our glassed in patio area and The Cold Hole bar offer up the same divine dining experience in a casual setting. Live lobster tank and the freshest of seafood and produce will light up your taste buds. Chef Gannon Leary and staff offer a plethora of daily creations. We have been described as "Casual Dining at it's Best," Southern Creole Cuisine, Best service, most romantic dining and best sweet tea. Jesse's has been listed By several periodicals: Southern Living, Coastal Living, Mobile Bay Monthly, James Beard Foundation and Alabama Bureau of Tourism named Jesse's Whiskey Steak 100 Places to Eat In Alabama Die. Step back in time and forward in flavor with award winning steaks and wine lists.

  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: We have a large gravel lot with a great deal of parking for the restaurant and grocery store. There is additional parking across the street at the chapel.
  • Catering: Chef Gannon Leary oversees all catering along with the General Manager and Owner. We do catering on site and off and can cater to any need. Our restaurant shares the building with a meat market and specialty grocery store where we have 2 huge smokers. We can offer a casual barbecue all the way to the most formal sit down rehearsal dinner to exquisite wedding reception and everything in between. At your service.
  • Private Party Facilities: The dining room may be used for private dining and it sits up to 60. The glassed in "Patio" area is often used for bridal showers, baby showers, luncheons and dinner and hold up to 35. The Cold Hole Bar has a side room that holds up to 25.

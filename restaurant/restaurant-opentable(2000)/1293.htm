Antonello Ristorante

writes...
  • At Antonello Ristorante, we transport you to not just another place, but to another time. As you walk into Antonello's Main Dining Room, a typical scene from the streets of Bistango, Italy comes alive.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Santa Ana
  • Cross Street: Sunflower & Plaza
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.antonello.com
  • Email: info@antonello.com
  • Phone: (714) 751-7153
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Friday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm, Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Franco Barone
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Going to the theater or Opera at Orange County Performing Arts why not have dinner at Antonello, leave your car in our parking lot and take our complimentary shuttle to and from the theater. When making reservation please inquire about the theater shuttle program
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary Valet & Self parking
  • Private Party Facilities: Antonello offers many options to meet your event needs. Our kitchen staff can accommodate cocktail events, intimate dining parties, business dinner and luncheons. We have seven private rooms that can accommodate parties from 2 to 95 guests. Each event is personalized to meet your needs from a customized menu with the guests of honor's name, to the company logo. We also offer audio visual equipment, musicians, flowers and balloons. Please call and ask for Vicenta Canton the banquet manager or visit www.antonello.com to see pictures and videos of all private rooms.
  • Private Party Contact: Vicenta Canton (714) 755-1195

Gilly's at The B.O.B.

writes...
  • Gilly's brings the art of the bar to a whole new level with an outstanding menu highlighted by fresh seafood features, fish tacos, classic bar food and the essential highballs and cocktails. The atmosphere is classically low key and you can even find a jazz master or two doing their thing on a Thursday night. Add in GR's favorite traditional raw bar and you have the best casual bar and seafood joint in town.

The Mad Greek

writes...
  • Experience Greek & Indian Cuisine in a classic casual setting. Enjoy the best Hoomis & Baba Ghanouj in town. A Cleveland favorite for over 30 years, we are known for our classic Greek food, and home style Indian curries from special family recipes. Large & spacious dining room, outdoor patio with waterfall, street side dining, full bar, happy hour daily, catering, carryout & delivery. 5 minutes from Cleveland Clinic, University Hospitals, CWRU & University Circle.

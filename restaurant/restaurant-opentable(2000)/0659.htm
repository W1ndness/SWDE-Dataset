Houlihan's Restaurant - Park Ridge

writes...
  • Our restaurant is built entirely around what the progressive consumer seeks in a restaurant + bar experience. Forget 'casual dining.' That's an industry term so out of touch with how consumers eat and drink today. We completely re-imagined ourselves, and got back in touch with the consumer. New menus. New plates. New building design, new soundtrack. Stylish uniforms, and a modern, warm restaurant design with an open kitchen and prominent bar. For premium quality and style; fare that's at times familiar and other times adventurous and a laid-back modern setting, Houlihan's is a true original. And we plan to keep it that way!

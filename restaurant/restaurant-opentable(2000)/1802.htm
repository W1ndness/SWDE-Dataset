Lahiere's

writes...
  • Lahiere's restaurant in historic downtown Princeton, New Jersey is a family-run business that has been in operation since 1919. We have provided area residents and businesses, University faculty and students, politicians, dignitaries and celebrities with great imaginative food, an extensive wine list, and warm, gracious service and look forward to having you as our guest.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Princeton
  • Cross Street: Nassau Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://Lahieres.com
  • Email: lahieres@comcast.net
  • Phone: (609) 921-2798
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:30pm, Saturday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Friday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm, Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Paul Robinson
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: NJ Transit stops include Princeton Junction Train Station, about a 10 minute cab ride, and Princeton University station, about a 10 minute walk or 3 minute cab ride. Suburban Bus Lines as well as NJ Transit Bus Lines both have stops within minutes of Lahiere's.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: There are three parking garages within 2 blocks of the restaurant, and 2 hour parking meters on most streets. These spaces are metered until 7 pm.
  • Private Party Contact: David Wagner (609) 921-2798
  • Entertainment: Area favorite Pete Lauffer plays piano in our bar Friday and Saturday evenings from 8-11pm. With a booming voice and a vast repertoire, he has become a fixture at Lahiere's. It's not unusual for a friend of his to show up unannounced and sit in on a couple of numbers. Dining tables in the bar are often booked well in advance due to his popularity.

Dick's Last Resort on The Chicago River is the hottest spot for a party in Chicago! With an open, lofty floor plan, Dick's can accommodate any size party. The panoramic views never change, and either does the always open, lush patio. The variety of Southern-inspired American food comes served in giant portions and booze flows freely. For your next special event, Dick's Last Resort is the place to be! Located conveniently by all public transportation, guests say it is a great place to hold your corporate and private events, business meetings, lunches, dinners, cocktail parties and just about anything else you can think of! Dick's Seats 325 and can accommodate up to 500 for a cocktail party with passed appetizers or a mingling dinner. Dick's Last Resort is the definition of partying in the big city! With a view overlooking the Chicago River and the entire downtown skyline, a night out at Dick's is a roaring good time with live music seven days a week and never a cover!

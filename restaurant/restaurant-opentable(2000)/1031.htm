Amavi Restaurant

writes...
  • Amavi Restaurant’s Chef Megan Tucker, offers regional French, Spanish and Italian cuisine featuring locally grown ingredients and an outstanding wine selection to compliment your meal. Acclaimed as the “hot as can be” new restaurant by the Santa Fean Magazine. Newly renovated bar and lounge also serving the full menu. Open for dinner seven nights a week, 5:30pm - 9:30pm Sunday - Thursday, 5:30 - 10:00 Friday & Saturday. Bar opens at 5:00.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Mediterranean, Contemporary European
  • Neighborhood: Santa Fe
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.amavirestaurant.com/
  • Phone: (505) 988-2355
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday 5:30pm - 9:30pm, Friday & Saturday 5:30 - 10:00. Bar opens at 5:00
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Megan Tucker
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Farm to Table, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Takeout, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: There is street parking available in front of the restaurant and also public city parking within 100 yards available.
  • Private Party Facilities: Amavi offers a semi private room for parties of up to 50 people. We also feature a wide variety of tasting menus with wine pairings for private parties or we can custom tailor a party to any guests wishes. We also encourage large parties of up to 100 people for the whole restaurant.
  • Private Party Contact: Mark Johnson or Megan Tucker (505) 988-2355

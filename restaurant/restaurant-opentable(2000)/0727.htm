Rumba - Nashville

writes...
  • Rumba, a satay bar & grill, serves up a delicious combination of Latin American, Asian, and Caribbean style cuisine, all priced affordably.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Latin / Spanish, Asian
  • Neighborhood: Nashville
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Email: rumba@jctrestaurantgroup.com
  • Phone: (615) 321-1350
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 12:00am, Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 1:00am
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ben Kirk
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies)
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: rumba offers complimentary valet parking every night along with limited street parking on 30th Avenue South adjacent to the restaurant. Our parking lots behind the restaurant are available for self-parking by late-night customers.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private parties are welcomed and accommodated in a variety of ways, from set menus to private bartenders and servers to dedicated party areas indoors, on the main side patio or the smaller front patio.
  • Private Party Contact: Chris Yokley (615) 321-1350

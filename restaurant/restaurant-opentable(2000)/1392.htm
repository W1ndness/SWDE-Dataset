Maria’s Italian Kitchen is your neighborhood Italian restaurant and the destination for upscale casual dining in Downtown Los Angeles. We are located in the heart of the Financial District, across the street from Pegasus Apartments and catty corner from The Standard Hotel. We have several private and semi private rooms to host your event. All rooms are equipped with flat screen monitors and videoconference capabilities. Power Point Projector, microphone, and screens available for rental. We have amazingly affordable pre set menus ranging from $23pp to $70pp as well as buffet style appetizers to accommodate your celebration, seminar, mixer, or breakfast meeting! We host intimate gatherings to entire restaurant functions. Our space is incredibly versatile, come on down and take a look!

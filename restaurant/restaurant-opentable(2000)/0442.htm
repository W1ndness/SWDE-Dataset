Chez Shea

writes...
  • Join us for dinner at one of 12 cozy tables for French inspired Northwest regional cuisine. We offer a full a la carte menu, 4 cs and 7 course Chef Tasting Menu, that change with the season. We are happy to accept reservations for parties of six or more Sunday-Thursday. We can not accommodate parties larger than 6 on Friday and Saturdays. The dining room may not be suitable for some small children. We do not have a children's menu or booster seats. Join us in the Lounge for a casual repast! Our intimate 7 table Lounge dining room is the perfect place to drop in for a quick bite and glass of wine, for guests 21 and over. Reservations are suggested in the Lounge on weekends. Please call 206-467-9990 for Lounge reservations. We do NOT accept reservations for the lounge via OpenTable.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Northwest, French
  • Neighborhood: Belltown / Pike Place Market
  • Cross Street: 1st Avenue & Pike St
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.chezshea.com
  • Email: Lotta@chezshea.com
  • Phone: (206) 467-9990
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Tuesday - Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm Our last reservation - 9:45pm. Please note, we do not accept reservations for parties larger than six guests on Friday and Saturdays. Shea's Lounge is open for casual dining 5:00-10pm Closed-Mondays, New Year's Day, Christmas Day and Thanksgiving.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Koichiro Ikawa
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, View, Wine
  • Public Transit: 1st Avenue bus stops
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Valet Parking available Thursday, Friday & Saturday $10 with validation. Free Validated Parking after 5pm, at the Public Market Parking Garage at 1531 Western Avenue. For additional detailed directions to the Public Market Parking Garage please contact the restaurant. We do not validate for parking at the Market Place Garage under Steinbrueck park. Street Parking is limited in the Market area
  • Private Party Facilities: Large parties are accepted for weeknights only, maximum of 32 people. We are available for private dining, for up to 32 guests in the dining room. For a more intimate private event, we can accommodate 8-20 guests in our Lounge dining room. There are seasonal inclusive minimums for private events, in both rooms. Our usual format is a sit down multi course dinner. Arrangements can be made for an informal reception and cocktails preceding dinner, depending on the number of guests. A seasonal, customized menu is served. We are not available for private events in the dining room on Friday, Saturdays and holidays. We are available for semi private events at Shea's Lounge Sunday and Tuesday - Saturday.
  • Private Party Contact: Lotta (206) 467-9990

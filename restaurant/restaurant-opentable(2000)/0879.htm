Brix

writes...
  • Located in the heart of Napa Valley! 1 Mile North of Yountville on Highway 29. Take a stroll around the grounds or through the vineyard after dessert. You’re sure to fall in love with Brix. We look forward to seeing you at Brix! If you are celebrating a Birthday, Anniversary, or other special occasion, please note that restaurant policy is to provide custom handwriting on the plate of the person celebrating when you order dessert.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Californian
  • Neighborhood: Yountville
  • Cross Street: 1 mile north of Madison St
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Email: info@brix.com
  • Phone: (707) 944-2749
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:30am - 3:00pm Buffet Brunch: Sunday 10:00 - 2:00pm Dinner: Monday - Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm (The bar menu is available from 3:00PM – 9:00pm at the bar and in the lounge.)
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Anne Gingrass-Paik
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Farm to Table, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Two parking lots, located on the north and south sides of the restaurant.

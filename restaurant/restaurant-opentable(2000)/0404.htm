Kentmorr Restaurant

writes...
  • Chesapeake Bay waterfront restaurant. We feature fresh seafood such as crab, oysters and striped bass. We have a full selection of land lubber delights such as ribs, chicken and filet mignon. We feature a special kids menu. Private waterfront, sandy beach with adjoining crabdeck and swinging hammocks for you to relax in after your meal. Live entertainment on summer weekends. We have plenty of free parking and free slip dockage while dining. The Kentmorr Community has an airstrip just yards away from the restaurant and marina. Please call to reserve your table and the largest crabs for your family. Reservations for parties of 8 or more, we ask that you call the restaurant.

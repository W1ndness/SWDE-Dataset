Brass Monkey Tapas Bar

writes...
  • Welcome to the Brass Monkey Tapas Grille, a family owned and operated restaurant founded in Jacksonville Florida located in the heart of Tinsel town. Chef Jozef Gjergji's has chosen his center of influence for his exceptional, award winning, cuisine around the Mediterranean. His global tapas and entree menu includes a little something for everyone even the kids. Tapas is a interactive style of dining with a wide variety of small plates in Spanish cuisines that are served either cold or warm. We offer fresh dishes from hand crafted burgers like our "funky monkey" to flat breads and our delicious humus. Our succulent lamb chops, mojo marinated tenderloin skewers and bake brie are some of our guest's favorites. Pasta and seafood lovers with return for our fettuccini and diver scallop duo. Relax and start your evening off with a glass of our famous sangria and enjoy our wide variety of live entertainment. Come in late night and show your salsa moves off on the dance floor.

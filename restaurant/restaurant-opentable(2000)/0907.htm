FIVE

writes...
  • Executive Chef Banks White and Chef Scott Howard bring to life a Modern American Bistro that couples knowledgeable and attentive service with an accessible, yet innovative menu. The farm-to-table approach supporting local, sustainable, and organic providers ensures the diners not only a richly flavorful dining experience, but the additional windfall of connecting with the community and helping the planet. To complement dinner is a wine list over 100 local wineries from California, Washington, and Oregon with a focus on those that use sustainable and biodynamic farming and production techniques.

Chef Warren's

writes...
  • Chef Warren’s serves fusion cuisine - a mélange of ethnic styles infused with Warren's own playful twists. The ambiance is fusion as well - bistro style that is at once classic and comfortable. Warren and his wife Marianne have spent the last twelve years delighting diners with their worldly cuisine and Southern hospitality. Think of the restaurant as a hip Mom and Pop's. Marianne's organic garden supplies Warren's kitchen with his beloved fresh produce and both are actively involved in the local food movement.

Grill du Chateau

writes...
  • Welcome to Grill Du Chateau, we are located in the heart of the Palos Verdes Peninsula, behind Bristol Farms. We have built an excellent reputation of providing a dining experience that please all the senses. Our strict commitment to quality translates in to the highest standards for Food,Service, Ambiance & Value.We use the best Meats, Seafood,Produce & ingredients in today's market. Come & join us for a Romantic Dinner or night out with friends or family. We Recommend our famous Lobster Raviolis, French Onion Soup,Lobster Bisque or Escargot to start, for entrees we recommend our famous Duck with Orange Grand Marnier Sauce,USDA Certified premium Angus Beef Steaks, Like Rib Eye with 4 peppercorn Cognac Cream Sauce or our most tender Filet Mignon w/ Roquefort Sauce, Wild King Salmon w/Crab Meat & Lobster Sauce, Rack of Lamb w/Rosemary Sauce & don't forget Creme Brule, Godiva Chocolate Cheesecake & Fine Wines, Drink Dine & Love, Bon Apetit your Host Al.

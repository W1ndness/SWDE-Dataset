Cosmo Cafe - Sacramento

writes...
  • Cosmo Café brings a completely new and exciting dining experience to downtown Sacramento. Located at 1000 K Street in The Cosmopolitan building, the cafe is part of an entertainment and social hub that includes The Cosmopolitan Cabaret Theatre and Social Nightclub. The diverse menu features modern small plates, steaks, chops, fresh seafood, poultry, house made pastas and New York-style sandwiches. Full to-go service, including delivery and catering, are available to surrounding businesses and select menu items will be available to patrons of the Cabaret during the show.

The Hall at Palihouse Holloway

writes...
  • The Hall courtyard brasserie serves traditional brasserie fare al fresco on a leafy, brick-lined patio and in an adjoining dining room. The heart of Palihouse Holloway, a boutique urban lodge offering the atmosphere of a stylish metropolitan hotel with timeless traditions and personal service, The Hall is a neighborhood restaurant that welcomes locals and out-of-town visitors alike for intimate gatherings and spirited fêtes. Open daily for breakfast, lunch, dinner and weekend brunch, The Hall’s menu features Provençal classics prepared with fresh ingredients and served in a fun, festive atmosphere.

    The Courtyard is the heart of the hotel, featuring an open air leafy, brick-lined patio alongside an enclosed dining room offset by floor-to-ceiling sliding glass doors. This venue is ideal for banquets and larger scale parties. Smaller dinner parties of 8 to 20 are welcome as well with a special prix fixe menu available.

  • Lobby Lounge

    The Lobby Lounge at Palihouse is a collection of living room settings complemented by a Moroccan tile floor, distressed leathers, dark walnut staircase and industrial loft accents. A vintage vanity table has been re-imagined as a DJ booth for music entertainment. Table side bar service offers classic cocktails made-to-order using fresh ingredients and boutique spirits. This space is ideal for parties ranging from 30-150 guests.

Watershed

writes...
  • Watershed restaurant, owned by Indigo Girls’ Emily Saliers, has received awards and rave reviews on both local and national levels for twelve years. Watershed Chef, Joe Truex, cooks with the freshest, organic, and locally grown ingredients. We specialize in seasonal southern cooking with dishes like Creamy Stone Ground Shrimp Grits, Salmon Croquettes, and Fried Chicken. We prepare old fashioned, made from scratch desserts like our famous Very Good Chocolate Cake, and Georgia Pecan Tart with Shortbread Crust. Housed in a converted gas station, Watershed offers some of Atlanta’s finest dining and an extensive wine list in a casual, relaxed atmosphere and neighborhood setting.

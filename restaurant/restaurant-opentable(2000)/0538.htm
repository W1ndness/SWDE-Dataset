Saint Jacques French Cuisine

writes...
  • A French heaven in this hospitable south, Saint Jacques is run and operated by French native Lil Lacassagne. We serve authentic and traditional French cuisine, discreet and polished service, and elegant atmosphere. Fine dining at its best. "Voted one of the top 50 in service in the U.S. by Open Table Diners - 2010", "Voted one of the top 50 restaurants in the U.S. by Open Table Diners - 2009", Voted "Best overall restaurant in Triangle by Open Table Diners - 2008" "Voted best French Restaurant in the triangle - Citysearch 2006". "Voted best French Restaurant 2007 & 2008- Metro Magazine".
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: French
  • Neighborhood: Raleigh
  • Cross Street: Spring Forest
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50

    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Lil Lacassagne
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes

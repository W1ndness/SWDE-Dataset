Greenwich Tavern's private dining room can accommodate up to 40 guests. Larger parties may be accommodated in the main dining room. Whether for business or pleasure, a large gathering or an intimate group, Greenwich Tavern offers a variety of menu options that will delight both you and your guests. You may choose one of the sample menus, order a la carte off of our regular menu, or work with our event coordinator to create a personalized menu suitable to your event. Each dining room also has its own music system which can be personalized for your event.

Miro District Food and Drink

writes...
  • Miro District Food & Drink is a very comforting neighborhood bistro. Specializing in classic bistro fare with influeces from Southern regions. Happy hour is Monday-Friday from 4:00 to 7:00 and includes half price draft beer, house wines and wells. Monday night enjoy $5 martinis, $1 oysters and half price bar food!

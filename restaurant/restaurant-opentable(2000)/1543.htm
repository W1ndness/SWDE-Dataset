John Harvard's Brew House

writes...
  • John Harvard’s is located on the third floor of the Country Inn at beautiful Jiminy Peak Resort in the Berkshires of Massachusetts. We feature hand crafted beer, a terrific wine list, hot specialty beverages, unique martinis and full bar service. Our classically trained chef and staff prepare scrumptious meals ranging from our signature pub classics: Chicken pot pie, our famous grilled meatloaf and New England fish and chips to steaks, ocean fresh seafood, bountiful salads, premium burgers and intriguing daily specials. Meals are complimented by rich desserts including our rustic apple crisp and brownie sundaes. John Harvard’s overlooks the beauty and grandeur of Jiminy Peak Resort, including views of the ski slopes, fall foliage and summer activities. A true New England dining experience awaits!

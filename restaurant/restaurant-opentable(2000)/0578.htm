101 Downtown

  • 101 Downtown is an International Fusion Restaurant and Lounge, located in the heart of historic Downtown Gainesville, Florida. International Fusion is a new age style cuisine prepared by combining widely differing ethnic ingredients, styles, flavors, and techniques from diverse cultures and countries and bringing them to each of our signature dishes. We also have a full service bar offering the finest martinis Gainesville has to offer. On behalf of all the staff at 101 Downtown, we invite you to come EAT, DRINK, and LOUNGE.

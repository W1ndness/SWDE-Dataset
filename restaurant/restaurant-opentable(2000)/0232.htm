Marble Room

writes...
  • Madame Cora's Marble Room offers a unique historic view into the colorful and risqué past of the Gaslamp Quarter. The decor pays om age to Madame Cora's historic brothel and saloon that was once a popular attraction in the red light district of The Gaslamp Quarter. Executive Chef Bill Sauer has created an all American menu of hand cut steaks, fresh seafood, gourmet burgers and more. Our world-class wine list and adventurous signature cocktail menu are a perfect fit for this fun, casual, sexy, restaurant and lounge. We also feature live music ranging from rock-n-roll to blues to a sing a long piano player.

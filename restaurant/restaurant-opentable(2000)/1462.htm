Woo Lae Oak - Beverly Hills

writes...
  • Located in the prestigious Restaurant Row in Beverly Hills. Woo Lae Oak has been the pinnacle of modern Korean cuisine for 25 years. While remaining true to the traditional Korean flavors, patrons will delight in an array of exciting new culinary creations along with our innovative approach to wine, sake, and spirits created by our award winning sommelier Caitlin Stansbury.
    Come enjoy the elegant, yet relaxed, atmosphere that is the cornerstone of the restaurant's experience. Woo Lae Oak continues to inspire devotees and new guests alike.

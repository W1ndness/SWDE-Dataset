Giorgio's Grill

writes...
  • Giorgio’s Grill a taste of the Mediterranean in South Florida, Centrally located On Beautiful Hollywood Beach, 15 minutes from Ft. Lauderdale International Airport. Overlooking the intercostals, Giorgio’s fabulous and relaxing Waterfront dinning arguably boasts the best Sunset in all of Florida. Wood and Brass are the fixtures giving you that elegant classy feel with surprisingly affordable prices. Giorgio’s grill restaurant is a Hollywood Icon, formerly known as Joe Sonka's Gold Coast the famous Rat pack hang out of Frank Sinatra and his buddies, possesses all the characteristics of America's Golden Age with a modern twist.

Zach's Cafe

  • Come join us for a relaxed meal in our patio or in one of our cozy indoor rooms. Our menu features a wide variety of classic Italian dishes made our own way. We also feature many original dishes such as our Mushroom and goat cheese lasagna, and Adam's baby green salad. Vegetarian dishes, some which can be made Vegan, salads ,pizzas, and subs are also available.

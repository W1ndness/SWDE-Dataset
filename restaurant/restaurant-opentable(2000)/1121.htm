Roman Anthony’s is named after Chef Justin Grecco's first son and was inspired by his trip to Tuscany, Italy in his early 20’s. Pasta, Sauces & Bread are made from scratch and meat is butchered by hand, each day in the Roman Anthony kitchen. Caesar salads are made tableside and cocktails are handcrafted ”The Old Way”. The restaurant also features a 100 bottle wine list and many rare spirits. Roman Anthony’s has a classic Italian design with black and red tablecloths, granite bar and granite bathrooms. With a 35-seat bar and 180-seat restaurant, Roman Anthony's is the second largest restaurant in White Bear Lake. Specializing in catering we will cater your event in our restaurant or any location of your choosing. We have cooked private dinners in the homes of our guests and catered wedding receptions for up to 400 guests. We have an exceptional catering menu and love special requests.

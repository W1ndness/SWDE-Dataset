The Republic

writes...
  • Classic meets contemporary at our spirited neighborhood gathering place, designed to embody the comforts of a time-honored bar and grill, with a modern twist. The Republic offers a contemporary menu featuring creative takes on traditional bar snacks, grill fare and comfort food made from local, seasonal ingredients. We also feature classic cocktails - and our modern spin on them, a wine list focused on artisan producers, as well as the largest all-American craft beer list in the city.

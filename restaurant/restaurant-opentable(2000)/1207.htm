Pepi's Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • Located in the heart of Vail Village, Pepi's Restaurant serves an Austrian continental cuisine in the main dining room and wild game gourmet in our Antlers Room in the winter. During the summer, enjoy our outside deck dining with an international flair. We also have year-round live entertainment.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: German, International
  • Neighborhood: Vail
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.pepis.com
  • Email: info@pepis.com
  • Phone: (970) 476-5626
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Daily, 11:30am - 3:00pm. Dinner: Daily, 5:30pm - 10:00pm.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, View, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Vail Transportation Center, which is located 100 meters from the restaurant.
  • Private Party Facilities: In the summer, the Antler Room is available for private events.
  • Private Party Contact: Kira G. Cameron (970) 476-4671

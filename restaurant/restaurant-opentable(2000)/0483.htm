Isabella's Italian Restaurant

writes...
  • Today's Italy is a blend of old and new, traditional and modern. Isabella's offers an authentically designed room to book parties of 18 or less for private events. Isabella's is a unique dining concept, featuring Italian Cuisine from four regions in Italy. Lombardia, Piemonte, Toscana & Veneto. Isabella's delivers cuisine that is unique in preparation, modern in style, yet classic by foundation. Isa Bar is a beautiful Wine Bar & Cocktail Lounge featuring Wine Flights, Specialty Cocktails and an extensive $25 Wine List Isabella's is located on the Main Piazza next to the spectacular water fountain, just behind the Silver Fox Restaurant.

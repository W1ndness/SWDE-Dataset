  • We serve comfort American food in a warm, relaxing atmosphere. Our menu features classics such as Tavern Onion Soup with Melted Gruyere Cheese and our Tavern Burger and exciting offerings such as Spaghetti with Crabmeat and Kansas Sirloin Steak. The dinning room is spacious and light with plenty of seating for large parties or for smaller groups. Our bar is perfect for meeting friends for drinks or dinner and to watch a game.

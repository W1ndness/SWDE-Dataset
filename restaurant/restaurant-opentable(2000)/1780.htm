Haru Sushi - Wall Street

writes...
  • Elegant, fun Japanese restaurant, sushi bar, and bar offering the latest in cuisine, cocktails, sake and decor. Located in the heart of the financial center at One Wall Street Court (just a few steps from Water Street).
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Japanese
  • Neighborhood: Financial District
  • Cross Street: Stone and Pearl Streets
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.harusushi.com
  • Email: wallstreet@harusushi.com
  • Phone: (212) 785-6850
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 4:30pm
    Dinner: Daily: 4:30pm - 10:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Nori Aratake
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Delivery, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, Wine
  • Public Transit: 2, 3, 4 & 5 trains to the Wall Street Stop.
  • Parking: No
  • Catering: Corporate or residential, we offer full service catering at all levels. We can provide services up to an on-site sushi chef with servers and bartenders, and everything in between.
  • Private Party Facilities: We offer two private dining rooms, for up to 10 guests. Each room has flat panel LCD TVS and full multi-media capabilities.
  • Private Party Contact: Vivian Fong (212) 744-0818

Balans - Lincoln Road

writes...
  • Since 1997, Balans has been a favorite hang out amongst the local residents of Miami Beach. We offer a great dining experience in a relaxed environment with indoor and outdoor seating right on the famous Lincoln Road pedestrian mall. Open from 8am everyday for breakfast, Balans serves a modern brasserie - cafe style menu with many dinner favorites such as the Chilean Sea Bass, Jambalaya, Thai red Curry, Double baked cheese soufflé, Chili rubbed Skirt Steak to name but a few. Come and join us for consistently good quality food at affordable prices and served by our team of friendly staff. We want you to return so don't forget to join our royalty program for additional rewards

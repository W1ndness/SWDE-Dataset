Olenjack's Grille

writes...
  • Olenjack's Grille is the namesake restaurant of Executive Chief Brian Olenjack, offering contemporary American cuisine with a Texas flair. Olenjack's was recently named one of the Top Ten Best new restaurants in Dallas/Ft. Worth by the Dallas Morning News. The food is simple yet creative and only the freshest ingredients are used in daily preparation. Entrees range from steaks to seafood to pasta. Some of the amenities include outdoor seating, banquet room, complimentary wi-fi, and separate bar area. Large party reservations available. Olenjack's is open for both lunch and dinner; as well as for Brunch on Sundays.

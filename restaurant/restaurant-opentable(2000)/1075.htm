The Chophouse - Huntsville

writes...
  • The Chophouse is the perfect location for entertaining, a special occasion or just to enjoy an incredible meal unlike any other. An elegant and upscale atmosphere treats you to a luxurious setting of fine dining. With entertainment and valet parking, you are sure to enjoy an evening in one of Huntsville's premier restaurants. The Chophouse is known for its award winning service. From a romantic dinner for two to entertaining the out-of-town guests and business clients, The Chophouse will provide the finest dining experience. If its a large party or wedding you are entertaining, in house catering is the perfect choice. *Important* The Chophouse allows smoking in the bar area only. By Huntsville Ordinance, we are unable to accommodate guests under the age of 19. Please note that smoking in the bar area should not impact guests in the dining room.

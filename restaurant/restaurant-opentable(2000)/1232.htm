Bice Bistro - Glendale

writes...
  • Bice Bistro (pronounced Bee-Chay) located in the Westgate City Center is a fashionable restaurant perfect for casual dining, private events, drinks at the bar and outdoor dining. Bice Bistro's world famous menu consists of food that is prepared using the finest and freshest ingredients possible. Bice's entrees from land and sea are complimented by an extensive wine list. Behind closed doors, Bice Bistro's private dining room seats 45 comfortably and is perfect for meetings or special events. The Bice tradition started over 80 years ago in Milan and has proceeded to obtain high standards of service and dining. Open seven days a week, the restaurant is perfect for an evening out on the town or just drinks in the lounge. Bice Bistro is open Sunday through Thursday 11:30am to 10:00pm and Friday/Saturday from 11:30am to 11:00pm. In Glendale and around the world people simply say "Let's go to Bice".

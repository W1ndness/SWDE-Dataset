Hanuri Korean B.B.Q. Restaurant

writes...
  • Hanuri is reflective of the elegant restaurant,specializing in Korean B.B.Q and other traditional specialties. Hanuri is famous for providing authentic,tasty,and healthy Korean cuisine. All the barbecuing is on the grill of the table or is done in the kitchen. The atmosphere is cozy and ambient with very modern and captivating decor. Whether the occasion is a family gathering, a business outing function,or simply a casual meal among friends, Hanuri is the place to be for a truly satisfying and delicious Korean meal.

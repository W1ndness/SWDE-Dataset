Sushi Ran

writes...
  • We invite you to enjoy our vibrant fusion of traditional Japanese and Pacific cuisine. Outstanding chefs, great people on the floor and in the kitchen, plus a wine and sake list unparalleled in California. We believe these are the ingredients that have made Sushi Ran one of the top-rated restaurants in the Bay Area. We are passionate about what we do, and take special delight in introducing our guests to new wines, great cuisine, and surprising pairings of food with wine and sake.

Firenze a Tavola

writes...
  • Tucked in the lower level of Parisi’s in North Denver, is “Firenze a Tavola”, meaning “Florence at your table”. In Firenze a Tavola, we recreate hearty, authentic Tuscan and Florentine dishes with an ever-changing menu. Firenze a Tavola offers a specially selected wine list from our cellar featuring some of Italy’s finest. On select Wednesday evenings we feature our signature event, the “Community Table”, where neighbors come to dine together family-style. Community table diners will enjoy a multi-course, prix-fixe menu and "divertimento" (fun). Reservations are available Wednesday through Saturday evenings for traditional dining beginning at 5:30. The lower level is also available for private functions. The private dining room on the lower level seats up to 26 people and can be personalized to your event. The entire dining space is also available for up to 70 people. We hope you can join us soon for North Denver’s most authentic Tuscan experience! Ci vediamo presto. … CIAO!

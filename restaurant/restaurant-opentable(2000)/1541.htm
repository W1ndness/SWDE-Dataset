Joe Tecce's Ristorante and Cafe

writes...
  • Joe Tecce's opened in 1948 and has continued to be a family owned business. We now have outside seating which allows you to enjoy the view of the Zakim Bridge and the Bunker Hill Monument from the outside Sidewalk Cafe, from May thru October. We offer Traditional Neapolitan Cuisine, a great wine list and a children's menu is available. Each dining room takes on a different ambiance. We are located minutes from Haymarket and No. Stations and the TD Garden. Our full menu is available for take out.

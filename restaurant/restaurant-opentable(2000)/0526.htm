Simon & Seafort's

writes...
  • We could tell you that your favorite part about dining with us will be our panoramic views of Mt. Susitna and the Alaska range, or the freshest wild Alaskan seafood in town, or our uncompromising quality of service; but we'll let you decide. Whether you visit us for a business lunch, a dinner full of friends and family, or that oh-so-deserved date night; you will find that our dedication to excellence is apparent at every step. Executive Chef James Shepherd combines wild-Alaskan seafood with traditional American cuisine to create dishes that make memories. For the best Happy hour in town visit us between the hours of 4pm and 6pm and from 9pm to closing daily, half price appetizers and featured drink specials are served in the Saloon. Also, the dining room at Simon & Seafort's offers a $25 three-course meal for the first hour we are open every day. So join us, Simon & Seafort's is your everytime restaurant for any occasion. View our menu options at www.simonandseaforts.com

Bol Restaurant

writes...
  • Located in Vail’s newest village center, Solaris, Bol offers an experience unlike any other in the world. This is not your Grandma's bowling alley! Bol is a full service restaurant offering fresh, contemporary takes on comfort foods as well as artisan pizzas. Bol has a custom cocktail menu and over 100 bottles of wine. Guests may dine in our chic, modern dining room, at our sixty foot bar, or in our bowling lounge while they bowl. Bol brings a metropolitan vibe to the mountains and has a high standard of service to reflect that.

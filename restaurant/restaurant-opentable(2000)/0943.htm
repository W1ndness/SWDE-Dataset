Le Zinc

writes...
  • Step into Le Zinc and you will feel transported to a French Bistro in Paris with its beautiful antique zinc bar, cream tiled floors, dark wood bistro tables, wine cellar and warm lighting. Chef-owner Max Braud offers an original menu of traditional favorites such as 'escargots and 'steak frites' as well as innovative tasting plates with influences from North Africa and Asia which are strongly prevalent in Parisian cuisine today. Indulge in our 4 course Tasting Menu for $40 or our Classic 3 course Prix Fixe for $36. From the bar select a delicious, great value bistro wine or splurge on a special grand cru. Over 20 wines, representing the main wine regions of France, are available by the glass and carafe. Sip a glass of wine and watch the world go by from our sidewalk terrace or enjoy our sunny garden patio in the rear. French-style Tapas dishes, as well as Cheese and Charcuterie platters, are served daily between lunch and dinner. THURSDAYS - 30% off your bottle of wine!

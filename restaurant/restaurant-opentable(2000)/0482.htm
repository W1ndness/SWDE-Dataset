Iron Cactus - Dallas

writes...
  • Iron Cactus Dallas | Dallas Casual Dining, Restaurant and Bar Iron Cactus Dallas is located in the heart of downtown Dallas. This breathtaking 14,000 sq ft restaurant stands 3 stories over the famous Pegasus Plaza. With a Rooftop Patio that boasts a fantastic view of downtown Dallas the Iron Cactus is an oasis amid the hustle of the big city. As you enter the first floor the smell of homemade tortillas give the restaurant a truly traditional Mexican feel.

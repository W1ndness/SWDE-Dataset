Ten Tables Cambridge

writes...
  • Krista Kranyak and David Punch are proud to announce the birth of a second Ten Tables....Ten Tables Cambridge! Krista Kranyak was so happy with the success of her first small restaurant, Ten Tables JP, that she made Chef David Punch a partner and they now own Ten Tables Cambridge together. We will bring you the same menu concept and design as Ten Tables JP. We have decided to stay with a very similar concept just on the other side of the river! We will continue to use local, organic meat and produce. Ten Tables Cambridge believes in providing you with affordable food, consistent service in a relaxed, bistro setting. We hope you visit us soon so we can make you happy!
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Cambridge
  • Cross Street: Concord Ave
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.tentables.net
  • Email: ijrose78@gmail.com
  • Phone: (617) 576-5444
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:30pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: David Punch
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Farm to Table, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wine
  • Public Transit: Take the Red Line to Harvard Sq and exit via the Church street exit. Take a right and proceed up Garden St towards the Sheraton Commander Hotel. Continue on Garden St towards the lights after the hotel. Bare left at the lights and continue on to Concord Ave. Take a left at the next set of lights on to Craigie St, we are on the righthand side.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: There is almost always ample parking in front of the Armenian Church on Brattle St. Continue to the end of Craigie Street, where it runs into Brattle Street and park on Brattle Street in front of the white Armenian Church. There is unrestricted parking here for about 15-20 cars. Also, you can drive past the Church on Brattle Street going toward Harvard Square. You will see additional parking spots on the right.
  • Private Party Facilities: We have semi-private dining for up to 26 people in our back dining room. We can accommodate larger parties though require the restaurant be rented for the evening. We have several menu options for both styles and are equipped to handle casual cocktail events as well as more formal seated dinners. Please contact us for more details.
  • Private Party Contact: Ian Rose (617) 576-5444

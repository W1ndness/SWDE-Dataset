Passionné

writes...
  • Quickly becoming a local favorite and destination hot spot, Restaurant Passionne has won accolaides from such publications as the New York Times, Zagat dining guide and much more. Join Chef Mike Carrino, a recent winner on Food Networks "Chopped", for an evening of divine cuisine in a warm invinting atmosphere. Chef Carrino offers French inspired dishes with world influences. Join us for a celebration, a special event or just for a night of great food. Viva la cuisine!!!

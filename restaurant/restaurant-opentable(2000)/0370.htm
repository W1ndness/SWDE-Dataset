Racks Italian Kitchen

writes...
  • Italy Just Got A Little Closer! Gary Rack, founder of Boca Raton's most established family brand Coal Mine Pizza, has launched Racks Italian Bistro and Market directly on the Intracoastal Waterway. Racks offers patrons an unparalleled experience of authentic Italy--with its Italian style outdoor patio with panoramic water views, intimately romantic Old World Italian bar and New Age villa-style restaurant that encapsulates the opulence of an urban chic Italian pizzeria and marketplace. Guests enter through the market and "Salumeria" stocked with a full range of Italian groceries. Passing the "pizzeria" and Chef's Table-- a communal interactive dining experience, the restaurant opens fully to the Intracoastal Waterway. Rustic regional specialties using locally sourced produce and seafood will be the menu's highlights. Hand crafted pastas and pizzas round out the menu. Creative Late Night Bar Menu will satisfy late dinners. Open 7 days a week. Lunch, Dinner and Saturday and Sunday Brunch

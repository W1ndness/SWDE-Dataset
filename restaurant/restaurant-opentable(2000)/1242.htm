Different Pointe of View

writes...
  • ENJOY THE SUMPTUOS 3 COURSE PRIX FIXE SUMMER MENU FOR ONLY $39 Poised 1,800 feet above the Valley of the Sun, Different Pointe of View offers guests breathtaking views second only to the outstanding regional American cuisine that features fresh local and organic ingredients. The restaurant's mountaintop location and floor to ceiling windows provide stunning views of the desert landscape, the city's twinkling lights in the distance and Arizona's brilliant sunsets. No restaurant in the state has a longer streak of consecutive AAA four-diamond awards and Sunset Magazine named it one of the top 10 mountaintop restaurants in the nation. Valley residents have come for years to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, romantic evenings or just treat themselves to one of the most unique Arizona experiences. Recently, the Terrace Room Lounge, next to the restaurant, has undergone a beautiful remodeling and now features one of the largest patios and a nine foot fire pit on the cliff side
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Phoenix
  • Cross Street: 7th Street, 3/4 mile south of Thunderbird Rd.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.differentpointeofview.com/
  • Phone: (602) 866-6350
  • Hours of Operation: Dining Room: Tuesday - Thursday 6 - 9:30pm Friday - Saturday 6pm - 10pm. Closed Sunday and Monday Terrace Room: Tuesday - Saturday 5pm - Close
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Anthony DeMuro
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Dancing, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary valet and self parking available.
  • Catering: Known as the premier destination wedding location in the state, Different Pointe of View's cliff-side ceremony site and stunning terraces are unmatched. Also very popular for corporate events, Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, and celebrations of all kinds, you have to see this venue to believe it. Contact Traci Fox at 602-866-6397 for a site tour today!
  • Private Party Facilities: Adjacent to the restaurant, the recently remodeled Terrace Room was the sight of over 30 premier weddings in 2008. Additionally, this venue is often booked for corporate parties, charity events, retirement dinners and other occasions when the event simply cannot be compromised.
  • Private Party Contact: Traci Fox (602) 866-6397
  • Special Events & Promotions:

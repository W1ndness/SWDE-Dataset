Tastes Wine Bar and Bistro - Uptown

writes...
  • TASTES Wine Bar & Bistro is a neighborhood, small-plate tapas restaurant serving international wines, beers and spirits, classic cocktails, cheese, tapas and dessert in a metro, organic, art gallery setting. TASTES is a place made for conversation - no TV"s and no loud music. It's the perfect spot to spend time with old friends or make new ones, dates, girl's or guy's night out and parties or events. TASTES has two locations each with its own unique ambiance.

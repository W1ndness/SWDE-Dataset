Pismo's Coastal Grill

writes...
  • Pismo's Coastal Grill is the newest restaurant in Fresno developed by local restaurateur, David Fansler and the Fansler Group. Our food offerings and atmoshpere will conjure up images of wine country, seafood, the beach and coastal BBQ. We are open 7 days a week, serving lunch and dinner. Fresh is king at Pismo's and enormous effort and planning go into securing the very freshest and most pristine ingredients. We have developed a fabulous menu with the quality and flavor of food that surpasses all expectations. Out coastal theme is represented by an open are feel with unique roll up windows, Adirondack style booths, Santa Barbara rock columns. Come see where the Wine Country meets the Sea in Fresno!!!

Paris Bistro

writes...
  • Located in the heart of Winter Park, our quaint little Bistro offers authentic French cuisine in a charming and elegant atmosphere. Paris Bistro resides within the interior of the Shops on Park. This unforgettable experience begins as you walk past giant koi fish in pools towards the glass wall front of our elegant bistro. Come enjoy French bistro-style cooking while surrounded by classic 18th century cranberry and tan toile wallpaper, large tiled mirrors and a cambered wood ceiling. Recently featured in Orlando Magazine, Joseph Hayes writes "If I had to suggest a perfect restaurant for Park Avenue, it would be this one."

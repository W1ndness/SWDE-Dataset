Doc's Place

writes...
  • Doc's Place is a restaurant featuring American Cuisine with a Cajun flare, coupled with live entertainment ranging from music to comedy. At Doc's, anyone can come enjoy good food and great music with a fun atmosphere. Our place is your place!

    The Lounge (150 people) is the setting for our happy hour & dinner crowd. The lounge is also great for networking, private parties and more.

    The Dining Room (300 people) has been host to Doc & Nayo's live Jazz show on Fridays & Saturdays and other special events. The dining room is great for corporate events, comedy shows, speaking engagements & more.

    The large patio is adorned with plenty of comfortable seating making it conducive to mingling with great people.

    Good food, good people, great music.

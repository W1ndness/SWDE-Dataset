Trademark

writes...
  • TRADEMARK, sister restaurant to THE ELITE CAFE, is located on the Belden alley in the heart of San Francisco's financial district. It is the newest addition to the most vibrant dining street in the city, Belden Place. Surrounded by French, Spanish, and Italian restaurants, TRADEMARK features exemplary contemporary American cooking, with everything from one of the best burgers in San Francisco to the freshest seafood to Kobe beef to a variety of entree salads. Executive Chef Jerry Mendoza, in charge of The Elite Cafe kitchen as well, brings his American cooking sensibilities from his years at The Meetinghouse and Moose's Restaurant. We will be open throughout the entire day offering sustenance to the Fi-Di crowds between lunch and dinner. TRADEMARK will be featuring "CLOSING BELL," one of the most exciting happy hours in the downtown area. We will offer Dollar Oysters, draft beer specials, terrific wine features, and a spectacular house cocktail list as well.

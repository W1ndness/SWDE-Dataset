Bacana Brazil

writes...
  • Bacana serves only the choicest cuts of meat grilled to perfection over an open fire and presented on skewers, carved directly to your plate. From the moment you walk through the doors, you will recognize that Bacana takes pride in providing an authentic Brazilian dining experience in an attractive setting focused on our customers' complete satisfaction. BACANA welcomes you to enjoy their selections to your heart's content; customers choose from a wide selection of grilled meats, shrimp and salmon, or well-appointed salad island and hot line and stop only when they decide they have had enough. Bacana's desire is to present this unique and enticing cuisine in such a way that they live up to their name: BACANA: (slang), a term meaning... Amazing! or... Great! or... Cool!

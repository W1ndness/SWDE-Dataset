Bogota Latin Bistro

writes...
  • On par with some of Manhattan's best Latin restaurants but located in Park Slope, Brooklyn, Bogota Latin Bistro is a neighborhood restaurant that offers Latin-inspired /Colombian dishes and drinks including mojitos, caipirinhas, aguardiente and imported Latin American wines and beers in a fun, funky and casual environment. We have a great happy hour and serve brunch Saturday and Sunday with reduced prices on our most popular drinks. Our menu features an assortment of small plates, large plates, side dishes, and desserts that allow you to mix, match and share dishes to create a customized dining experience on every visit. House specialties include Bandeja Paisa, Arroz con Pollo, Empanadas, Arepas and other Colombian classics. Friday and Saturday, our kitchen is open until 1AM and we have a late night happy hour from midnight until closing. We also have an enclosed patio which is available for private parties all year around.

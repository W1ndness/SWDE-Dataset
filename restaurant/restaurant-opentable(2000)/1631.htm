PlumpJack Squaw Valley Inn’s acclaimed three star restaurant, PlumpJack Cafe, is widely regarded at Lake Tahoe’s premier dining destination and was voted “Readers Choice for Dining” by Condé Nast Traveler in 2005. Attentive service and extensive wine list selections are available in both our restaurant and our fire lit bar, the perfect place for gathering with friends or business associates. Allow Executive Chef Ben Dufresne to create your menu off his love for sustainable, organic, and seasonal ingredients as our helpful staff seamlessly assists you in creating an occasion to remember! Our split-level facility features natural wood beams beneath a high vaulted ceiling with views of the surrounding peaks, rich tapestry wall panels and subtle color palettes that accommodate a variety of decorating possibilities.

Left Bank at Stonehedge Inn and Spa

writes...
  • Experience the grace of an intimate English Country Inn nestled in New England's woodlands while being pampered with our European service, renowned cuisine and a world of wine while dining at Left Bank. Internationally recognized for its cuisine and wine list, Left Bank boasts its own Wine Cave, with the second largest wine collection in the country! A repeat winner of the coveted Wine Spectator's Grand Award for Outstanding Wine Lists, plus the AAA 4 Diamond Dining Award, Left Bank is a dining destination unlike any other. Enhance your evening with an overnight stay in one of our luxurious suites. **Join them for Celebration of Wine 2010, their Wine Dinner program. Call or email for more information**

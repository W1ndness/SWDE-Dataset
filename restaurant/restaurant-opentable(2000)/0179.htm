Starfish Brasserie

writes...
  • Starfish Brasserie offers a seasonally changing menu with an emphasis on fresh seafood & local produce. Our dining room has a casually elegant setting with a nautical theme & offers full bar service & a Wine Spectator award winning wine list. The entire restaurant & bar are non-smoking to enhance your dining enjoyment. Stop by for a full meal or a light snack. Our full menu is also available in the bar. We can accommodate groups for special events.

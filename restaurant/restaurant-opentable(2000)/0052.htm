23Hoyt

writes...
  • 23Hoyt is your New American Tavern. Chef Chad Church's menu is built with the comforting, fresh flavors of local, seasonal product brought to you in the smartly designed space that is at once sophisticated and contemporary and deeply comfortable. At 23Hoyt you'll be treated to Bruce Carey's signature impeccable service. So whether you're finishing up a day of shopping on Portland's premier shopping row, or going out for an evening, a visit to 23Hoyt, sister restaurant to Bluehour, Saucebox, and ClarkLewis makes for an unparalleled dining experience on Portland's Upper Westside.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Northwest, European
  • Neighborhood: Downtown Portland - NW
  • Cross Street: Hoyt Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.23hoyt.com
  • Email: info@23hoyt.com

  • Hours of Operation: Happy Hour every night from 3:00 pm Dinner every night from 5:00 pm

    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Chad Church
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual

  • Public Transit: Street Car stop at NW Lovejoy and NW 23rd Avenue.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: A Paid Parking lot is available behind the restaurant in addition to street parking.
  • Private Party Facilities: With our two-level room your event can be either intimate of convivial. Whether you host a dinner or cocktail reception in our tree topping mezzanine overlooking the breathtaking dining room, your guests will feel lightened, happy and at home at 23Hoyt. Intimate or large, our staff will treat your guests like no other in the city. For group events we offer a semi-private wine table in the mezzanine can accommodate from 8-16 guests. If your group is larger, the entire mezzanine can host from 30 to 60 guests. The restaurant is also available for complete buyouts where you have exclusivity of the space. The beautiful space is also available for daytime events as well. All private events require a food and beverage minimum depending on the date and space requested. Whether it be a luncheon, dinner or cocktail reception, 23Hoyt is the perfect venue.

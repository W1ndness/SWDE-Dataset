Skosh Monahan's Steakhouse & Irish Pub

writes...
  • Celebrating our 10th year, Skosh Monahan's is known for our tantalizing steak, lamb & seafood dinners, excellent beer, wine & cocktail selections and fun, relaxed atmosphere. We serve Happy Hour until 6:30 pm daily, with sports on 12 HD flat screen TV's, Trivia night Tuesdays, and live entertainment on Friday & Saturday. We also offer Kid's menus, a Gluten Free menu and daily dinner specials. So come in and enjoy yourself, let your hair down and engage in the old Irish custom of "Blarney". "The taller the tale, the merrier the laughter". The Spirit of St. Patrick's day lives every day at Skosh Monahan's.

Afternoon Tea at the Briarwood Inn

writes...
  • Experience the elegance of afternoon tea at the award wining Briarwood Inn. For over 30 years one of Colorado's finest dining traditions. Nestled in the foothills of Golden, The Briarwood Inn offers classic dining amidst the grandeur of old world charm and European ambiance. Our tea menu offers a selection of teas from around the globe, freshly baked scones, soup du jour, warm flakey tartletts, an array of savories and tea sandwiches. Your afternoon is completed with homemade sweet,specialty cake, and a refreshing fruit sorbet.

Hornblower Cruises & Events

  • Hornblower Cruises and Events offers champagne brunch cruises, dinner dance cruises, supper club cruises and lunch cruises for you to celebrate your special occasion. Enjoy the dazzling skyline of the "City by the Bay" including the landmark TransAmerica Pyramid, Embarcadero Center's four "fingers", and Coit Tower on Telegraph Hill. Cruise around Alcatraz Island, sail under the magnificent Golden Gate Bridge and enjoy the festive lights adorning the Bay Bridge at night.

  • Get ready to fall in love... Zula Restaurant offers a luxurious dining destination that captures the soul of Houston. With the most dramatic, versatile event facility available and a picturesque location in Houston's Theater district, it's the perfect backdrop for a variety of banquets and events: corporate parties, wedding rehearsals, birthdays, anniversaries, and more. Your special event and Zula Restaurant -- it's a match made in Heaven. Zula is all about people, food and creating an atmosphere of excitement. Zula's goal is to mix the comfort of fine dining with the thrill of Las Vegas.

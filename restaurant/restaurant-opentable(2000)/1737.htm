Deco 1600

writes...
  • Art Deco style thrives at Deco 1600–Long Island’s chic new family-style Italian-American restaurant. At Deco 1600, we offer your favorite traditional delights elegantly prepared and served in a setting rich in décor and ambiance 7 days a week. Once you pass through the elegant lobby adorned with characteristic details of NYC"s Chrysler Building and into one of our ultra-comfortable lounges, you’ll understand why Deco 1600 elevates the dining experience on Long Island. From unique cocktails and aperitifs to impressive wines and soothing after-dinner drinks, there’s something for everyone. Whether you’d prefer to dine on the main level, amid the cozy warmth of our fireplace, or atop all the action upstairs, our multi-million dollar facility is as diverse in experience as its depth of character. Ultimately, the delectable cuisine, exemplary service and relaxed atmosphere of Deco 1600 will keep you coming back for more.

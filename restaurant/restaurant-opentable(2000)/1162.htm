Allred's Restaurant

writes...
  • Telluride's most unique dining experience is Allred's, where culinary excellence, exquisite views and impressive ambiance mesh to create a unique dining experience at 10,551 feet above sea level. Allred's Contemporary American cuisine is available daily for dinner throughout the summer season. The Highlights: Fabulous views of the surrounding mountains and the Town of Telluride 1800 feet below. Creative American Dishes with International Overtones. A Private Dining Room for Gatherings of up to 30 Guests. An award winning wine list. Bar with great views and fireplace.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Steakhouse, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Telluride
  • Cross Street: Top of the Gondola overlooking Telluride
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.allredsrestaurant.com
  • Email: allreds@tellurideskiresort.com
  • Phone: (970) 728-7474
  • Hours of Operation: Bar: Daily: 5:00pm - 10:30pm Dining Room @ 5:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Mike Weist
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Take the free Gondola to Station Saint Sophia, the midway point between Telluride and Mountain Village. Enjoy the spectacular views at 10,551 feet above sea level.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: MOUNTAIN VILLAGE: Free parking is available in the Mountain Village parking structure. Take the short gondola ride to the Mountain Village core and then take the main Gondola line up to Allred's, the midway point between Telluride and Mountain Village. TELLURIDE: Free parking is available in Telluride's public lot located a short distance west of the Gondola on Pacific Street and metered parking is available throughout the town. (free after 6pm and all day Sundays)
  • Private Party Facilities: Private dining room for up to 30 guests overlooking the Town of Telluride 1800 feet below. The private room has exceptional views and a fireplace to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Please visit www. allredsrestaurant.com for more information.
  • Private Party Contact: Carrie Smith (970) 728-7455

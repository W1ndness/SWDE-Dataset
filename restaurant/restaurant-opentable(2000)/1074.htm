Bellini's Ristorante & Bar

writes...
  • Bellini’s Ristorante & Bar features organic black angus steaks, seafood, pizzas and classic Italian dishes. Bellinis maintains an extensive wine selection, weekly entree and dessert specials. Our bar opens daily at 4pm for drinks and dinner service begins at 5pm. Come join us and see why "Life is Sweet" at our place. Bellini's is open for Lunch and Dinner.

    We have two private dining areas available for large parties. Our private dining room accommodates 20 to 25 patrons and features a 48" TV with audio visual capabilities. Our smaller private dining room accomodates 10 to 15 patrons and is located off of our bar area.

    Reservations are suggested. If you cannot find an available time please be sure to call Bellinis to see if we can accommodate your request.

    Bon Appetito!

The Mantel Wine Bar and Bistro

writes...
  • The Mantel Wine Bar and Bistro is considered a Fine Dining Restaurant. Our cuisine consists of Prime Steaks, Seafood and Pasta's. We offer chef's specials daily both land and sea. Our executive chef creates all of our dishes to order, therefore special dietary requests can be satisfied. We also offer an extensive wine list, with 36 wines available by the glass and over 100 available by the bottle. Our staff is well trained in wine knowledge and cuisine, therefore we are happy to answer or suggest selections for our guests. We offer wonderful desserts, with our Chocolate Gateau, Carnegy Deli New York Cheesecake, and tableside Bananas Foster. We look forward to seeing you at the Mantel Wine Bar and Bistro.

  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Contemporary French

  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ryan Murphy
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Smoking Area, Takeout, Wheelchair Access
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: The Mantel Wine Bar and Bistro is located in Oklahoma City's historic Bricktown. We have on-site parking at no charge and a valet service nightly.
  • Catering: Our off-site catering is available, you may choose from our menu or our chef would be happy to create a menu for you.
  • Private Party Facilities: We have a small intimate restaurant which is available for private functions. We seat 75-85 comfortable and are happy to rent out our entire facility if desired.
  • Private Party Contact: Lorin D. Doan (405) 236-8040
  • Entertainment: We offer entertainment on our outdoor patio every Friday and Saturday. Bands range from Jazz, Blues and Contemporary. The full menu is available for our guests on the patio.

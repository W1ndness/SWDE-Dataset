The French Gourmet

writes...
  • Our Zagat-rated restaurant serves California-influenced French cuisine in a quaint countryside environment. We are a long-time favorite of locals who appreciate the value of the menu and our extensive wine list. After three decades of serving our clients, it is as much an honor and a privilege, as it is a reassurance to know, that while culinary trends come and go, appreciation and dedication to quality endure. Breakfast and Lunch served daily; Dinner Tues-Sat.

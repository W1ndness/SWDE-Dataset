Café Routier

writes...
  • Cafe Routier, Bistro and Lounge, serving a three paneled menu format; Regional (a different region of the world every 8 weeks), Seasonal (locally sourced seasonal ingredients) and a specialty menu of classic Bistro fare. A 40 seat lounge serving 'small plates' and tapas adjoins the bar area. Ethos is important to us.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, French, Tapas / Small Plates
  • Neighborhood: Westbrook
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.caferoutier.com
  • Email: info@caferoutier.com
  • Phone: (860) 399-8700
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday 5:00pm -9:00pm,
    Friday - Saturday: 5:00 - 10:00pm
    Lounge menu after 10:00pm on Friday and Saturday
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Bryan Wright, Jeremy Scott
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: A well lighted parking lot offers 50 spaces.
  • Catering: Off Property catering available for your special events from 40 to 250 people.
  • Private Party Facilities: Parties from 50 to 120 people on site with menus designed to meet every and all respective needs.
  • Private Party Contact: Randy Hodge (860) 399-8700
  • Entertainment: A diverse global approach to background music, programmed every night to meet the 'tempo' of the respective evening. Downtempo, lounge, acid jazz, bossanova, french fusion, easy house, classic jazz, etc . . . every night you can expect a unique tour to match our mood.
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Lounge parties. Wine tastings. Private luncheons. Please let us know how we can make your private function memorable.

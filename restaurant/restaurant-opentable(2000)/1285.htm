Sergio's Saravá

writes...
  • Saravá evokes the rhythm, sophistication, and energy of Brazilian street life. The term Saravá captures the essence of Sergio's warm hospitality and the multi-cultural flavors of Brazil. Saravá is located in the heart of the vibrant Shaker Square area, and offers multiple dining and lounge options to guests. An extensive 24-seat bar adjoins an intimate dining area. There is a cozy fireplace lounge, with additional booth seating, & a private dining room. The restaurant features a Brazilian & cross-cultural menu, extensive raw bar, & an array of pizzas & small bites. Perfect for a late night stop!

  • Cuisine: Brazilian, Global, International, Seafood

  • Cross Street: Shaker Blvd.

The patio seats 120 guests. Our beautiful covered veranda overlooks historic Shaker Square. All function areas may be booked for dinner or luncheon events.

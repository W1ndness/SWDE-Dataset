Area 31 - Epic Hotel

writes...
  • Area 31 delivers a dramatic menu of fresh, local seafood prepared with Mediterranean flavors. The name comes from the United Nations 'Fishing Area 31,' which encompasses the local waters surrounding Miami and the Florida coast. The UN is dedicated to supporting sustainable development in this region, a vision that our restaurant has embraced as well. The menu by Executive Chef John Critchley, combined with the chic ambiance created by famed designer Cheryl Rowley welcomes locals and visitors to its stunning 16th floor location for business or pleasure. Guests may dine indoors or on the expansive outdoor terrace overlooking the Miami skyline. The restaurant features an extensive list of high-end and unique Mediterranean wines as well as champagne thoughtfully assembled by Master Sommelier Emily Wines (of the Michelin-starred Fifth Floor Restaurant). Our Bar also showcases an innovative cocktail list from Master Mixologist Jacques Bezuidenhout.

Funky Monkey Wine Company

writes...
  • Restaurant Description: The Funky Monkey Wine Company is a relaxed neighborhood wine bar and eatery near downtown Orlando in the Virginia-Mills District. This restaurant and wine bar is beautifully decorated with hard wood floors, tablecloths, and warm candlelight where earth tones, a stylish red couch and beautiful unique red bar set the mood. This "stylish yet unconventional" wine bar serves 130-wines by the bottle; 40-wines by the glass all served in beautiful Diva Glassware and offers diners an Asian-American influence. An extensive menu offering delectable salads, unique appetizers, entrees ranging from Ostrich to Sushi and wine and cheese pairings are just a sample of what this new hot spot offers locals and tourists alike. Monthly Wine Dinners featuring guest speakers from around the world, Monthly Dinner Theatre and nightly specials keep the Monkey exciting and new; everything about this place is fun and pleasantly Funky Monkey. Visit us online at www.funkymonkeywine.com.

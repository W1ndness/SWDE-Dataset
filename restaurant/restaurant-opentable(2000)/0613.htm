Biaggi's - Champaign

writes...
  • Biaggi’s is a casual, Italian restaurant offering large portions of affordably-priced pastas, soups & salads, pizza, seafood, steaks and desserts in a comfortable, relaxing environment. Interested in placing an order for carry-out? Ask about our curbside service.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Champaign / Monticello
  • Cross Street: Windsor Road
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.biaggis.com
  • Phone: (217) 356-4300
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:00am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 10:30pm, Sunday: 11:00am - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Nathaniel Lane\Managing Partner Jamie Trainis
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Ample parking is available onsite.
  • Catering: Whether it’s a rehearsal dinner, business meeting, family reunion, or other important occasion, let Biaggi’s cater to you! We offer the perfect combination of authentic Italian food, distinctive ambiance and affordable pricing to make any gathering an event to remember.
  • Private Party Facilities: Biaggi's has private room options for a variety of occasions to accommodate up to 100 guests. Whether you prefer a reception format, buffet or any number of table configurations, our event coordinator will be happy to help you determine what best fits your needs.
  • Private Party Contact: Hannah Kramer, Event Coordinator (217) 597-9640

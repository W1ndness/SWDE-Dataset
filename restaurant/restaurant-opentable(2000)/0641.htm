Feast - Bucktown

writes...
  • Feast is a neighborhood restaurant in Chicago's Bucktown/Wicker Park area offering upscale dining in a casual atmosphere. The cuisine is globally influenced by owner Debra Sharpe's travels with famous rock stars including Paul McCartney and The Rolling Stones. In winter, enjoy hearty, seasonal favorites, while summertime offers dining in a beautiful outdoor setting. Monday and Tuesday: 1/2 price bottles of wine including Reserve List Wednesday: 2 courses plus a glass of wine for $25.00 plus tax and tip. Online delivery: feastrestaurant.com Every day: Half price bottles of wine to go

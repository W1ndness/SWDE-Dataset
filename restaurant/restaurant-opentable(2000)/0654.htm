Graziano's - Chicago

writes...
  • Graziano's is a family style Italian restaurant serving handcrafted foods made from fresh natural ingredients. We bake our pizzas in an authentic wood-fired brick oven. The combination of apple, cherry and oak hardwoods at temperatures of 700 to 900 degrees make for a distinctive flavor and crisp crust.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, American
  • Neighborhood: Niles
  • Cross Street: Lehigh
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://grazianosrestaurant.com
  • Email: steve@grazianosrestaurant.com
  • Phone: (847) 647-4096
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Everyday: 11:30am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Javier Franco
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Delivery, Full Bar, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: The Local Pace bus and the Niles Free Bus stop in front of the restaurant. The Metra stations at Edgebrook and Morton Grove provide convenient rail service.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: We maintain a large lighted parking lot wit over 200 parking spaces. Parking is never a problem at Graziano's
  • Catering: Graziano’s features a variety of banquet and catering packages to suit your tastes and budget. We can accommodate parties from 10 to 100 people in our dining room with our family-style menu. Or, we can deliver items from our extensive catering menu direct to your home or office.
  • Private Party Facilities: We can accommodate parties from 10 to 100 people in our dining room with our family-style menu.
  • Private Party Contact: Steven Demet (847) 647-4096

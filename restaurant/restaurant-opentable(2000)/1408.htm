The Palm is a classic American steakhouse legendary for prime aged steaks, jumbo Nova Scotia lobsters, premium fish and heritage Italian entrees, prepared to perfection, using only the highest quality ingredients and served in generous portions. What makes us truly unique is our genuine old world hospitality and unique atmosphere. The dining rooms are a panorama of the classic cartoons and caricatures of local regulars, politicians, and celebrities which make up our impressive customer base. Each room is filled with warms smiles from our gregarious staff and familiar guests, and always a buzz with a sea of conversation and laughter celebrating the best of everything. At The Palm we offer several customized menus for your private events. Each menu gives your guests a wide variety of options to choose from. We'll carefully handle every custom detail, the Private Dining Room fee is always waived, and there is never a food and beverage minimum--a significant reason for choosing The Palm!

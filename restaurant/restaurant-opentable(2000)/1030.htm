Ziryab

writes...
  • Come in to Ziryab, the Mediterranean Grill, where the exquisite menus range from Middle Eastern to Moroccan cuisine. Using a charcoal grill is an addition to the flavorful meals. In our extensive crisp appetizer menu, rests hand-made humus, falafel, home-made cucumber yogurt, tabula, baba-ganush, and more. From the mouth-watering all natural meat chicken and lamb shawarma, to the lamb rack platter. Have your favorite dinner with your best companion in the outside heated patio. Enjoy our special dessert, knaffea, with fresh hand-made Arabic coffee. Our full bar will offer a great variety of cocktails and high end spirits. The serene music will guide you to the beautiful parts of Arabian lands. Our amazing aromas blend in with the modish scenery to make eating our foods inspiring. Now open for new Middle Eastern breakfast at 9:00am until 2:30pm.

  • Sel de la Terre offers the rustic country fare of Provence & surrounding Southern France.

  • Neighborhood: Back Bay

  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website

From the rustic simplicity of Sel de la Terre’s cuisine to the lavish opulence of L’Espalier’s modern New England-French cuisine, Au Soleil presents a variety of options to suit every style of event. Call Heather Braccialarghe at 617-442-4200 or visit www.ausoleilcatering.com for more information.

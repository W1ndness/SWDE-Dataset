Hector's on Henderson

writes...
  • Hector's on Henderson features American cuisine with a Texas twist. This vibrant restaurant, only one of six recommended in Dallas by Gourmet Magazine (Nov. 2005), was named one of 10 Best Places to Eat in Texas by Texas Monthly (Jan. 2005) and one of the Top 10 Best New Restaurants in the DFW area by the Dallas Morning News (Dec. 2004). D Magazine (Feb. 2006) named it their #1 restaurant choice. D Magazine (Feb. 2008) named it one of the Best Neighborhood Restaurants in Dallas. Nightly jazz music, a professional clientele, and a modern interior in a former iron works studio round out the experience. The attentive, knowledgeable staff anticipate needs and execute a terrific dining experience. Located in the heart of the energetic Knox-Henderson Area, Hector's on Henderson is the perfect restaurant to enjoy your dinner in Dallas. The air conditioned and heated patio allows great year-round outdoor dining. Fun. Fine. Food.

  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet parking is available in front of the entrance to Hector's on Henderson. It is complimentary until 10 PM and afterwards costs $5. The Knox-Henderson Area is a lively urban area of restaurants and clubs. Valet parking is encouraged for your convenience.
  • Catering: Hector's on Henderson will cater events from the intimate dinner party in your home, up to the large events in any venue in North Texas. We can provide a personalized menu, full bar, all personnel, and rental coordination for your event. Let us take care of your party. All you provide is the guest list.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private Events are available on Sundays or Mondays anytime or during the day, Tuesday through Saturday. Our restaurant can host a seated dinner for about 100 or a stand-up event for about 185. A personalized prearranged menu is required, as well as a signed contract and deposit. Please contact our office for complete information.

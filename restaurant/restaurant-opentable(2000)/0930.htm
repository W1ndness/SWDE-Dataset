The Kitchen Table

writes...
  • Boasting a relaxed atmosphere with elegance and charm, The Kitchen Table is destined to become nationally acclaimed for its California Contemporary Kosher cuisine, artfully crafted by executive chef, Steven Long. The Kitchen Table is dairy-free and peanut-free, and uses only the freshest ingredients. The pastrami and corned beef are cured and smoked in-house. Please join us for a memorable and wonderful experience. If you are unable to reserve a table for a particular size and time please contact the restaurant for assistance.

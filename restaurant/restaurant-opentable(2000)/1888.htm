Prime at Saratoga National

writes...
  • Prime at Saratoga National is Saratoga's favorite restaurant in the venerable tradition of the Great American Steakhouse. Prime at Saratoga National’s dynamic menu will showcase signature entrees as well as classic favorites.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Saratoga Springs
  • Cross Street: 12866
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.PrimeatSNGC.com
  • Email: info@primeatsngc.com
  • Phone: (518) 583-4653
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Saturday: 11:30am - 5:30pm, Dinner: Monday - Saturday:5:30pm - 9:00pm
    Sunday: Closed Brunch: Sunday: 10:00am - 2:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Jason Saunders
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Catering: Mansion Catering can bring its reputation of exceptional food and service to any location. We are committed to helping you design the event of your dreams. Whether you are planning a wedding, fundraiser, corporate or social event, Mansion Catering can help you transform your ideas and visions into reality. Meticulous care goes into each and every detail to ensure superb cuisine and responsible service.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Crystal Room is a warmly elegant private room serves as a multi-function space to be used for meetings up to 35 guests as well as a formal dinner setting for up to 50 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Stuart deVoe (518) 583-4653 x603
  • Entertainment: Live music is offered in our lounge Thursday - Saturday 6:30pm - 9:30pm. Happy Hour daily from 5pm - 7pm. We also have our popular Sunday Jazz Brunch with great jazz music to serenade you while you enjoy our classic brunch buffet.

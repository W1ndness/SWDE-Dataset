Darioush

writes...
  • Darioush Danesh is owner and chef of Darioush Restaurant. His cozy and intimate restaurant provides an elegant and classic setting on restaurant row in Beverly Hills. Chef Darioush offers local foodies an exciting menu to tantalize the palate, using local and seasonal, mainly organic and free-range produce blending the wonderful Persian flavors with French cooking techniques. His signature dishes include oven-roasted duck breast with a date sauce and more traditional Persian dishes and a selection of mesquite char-grilled kebabs. The global wine list includes many of the fine Darioush wines as well as signature champagne cocktails and sangrias.

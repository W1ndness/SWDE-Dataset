Pearl's Saltwater Grille

writes...
  • Fresh Seafood and Choice Steaks ….enjoyed overlooking the Herb River Nestled in the heart of Sandfly lies one of the most closely guarded secrets when it comes to seafood restaurants. Pearl's Saltwater Grille has been a local favorite for more than twenty years. Chef David Weikert's menu is a Seafood Lover's Paradise. From Parmesan Crusted Grouper to Crab Stuffed Flounder to Jumbo Grilled Caribbean Shrimp there is something for everyone at Pearl's Saltwater Grille. Landlovers may select from choice steaks, chicken or pasta specialties. Enjoy the Spectacular view of the Georgia coastline and the wild life that inhabits the marshlands while you dine. For reservations over 6 people please call Pearl's Saltwater Grille Directly at 912-352-8221.

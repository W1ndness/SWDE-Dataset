The BLVD - Beverly Wilshire Hotel

writes...
  • The place to see and be seen, this sophisticated all-day dining spot features lofty ceilings and large windows overlooking the fashionable intersection of Wilshire Boulevard and Rodeo Drive. The Blvd encompasses a world-class restaurant and a distinctive lounge area, focused on an 18-foot (5.5-metre) illuminated onyx bar and a large backlit wine display containing over 1,000 bottles. The outdoor sidewalk patio is perfect for people-watching. Thursdays BLVD presents BLVD CLASSICS! Our tribute to classic martini's and live jazz artists. Join us for live music on Wednesday nights from 7PM to 10PM and on Friday and Saturday nights from 8PM to 12AM.

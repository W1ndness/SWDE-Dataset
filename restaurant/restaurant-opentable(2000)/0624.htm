Carmine's

writes...
  • Carmine’s invites you to experience an unforgettable meal made with authentic Italian recipes passed down from generation to generation. A true cosmopolitan hangout located in Chicago's trendy Gold Coast neighborhood, Carmine’s offers live entertainment, an extensive bar, and an outdoor café that is the place to see and be seen from sun up to sun down. Whether dining al fresco or inside the elegant two level dining room, even the most demanding taste buds will be overjoyed by Carmine’s impressive menu selections. Signature dishes include clams and oysters on the half shell, steaks and chops grilled to perfection, melt-in-your-mouth veal and chicken dishes, and of course generous portions of award-winning pastas. From the moment you are seated and served, you’ll understand why Carmine’s is packed with food aficionados every night of the week. Unfortunately, reservation requests for the outdoor patio cannot be taken online.

Relax at a cozy table downstairs or grab a seat at the spacious bar for a perfect view of the performers who begin at 7:30 Thursday through Saturday evenings.

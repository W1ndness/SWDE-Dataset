Mezza Mediterranean Grill

writes...
  • Mezza is new Mediterranean restaurant in the heart of Culver City. We specialize in shawarma, fresh salads, grilled meats and "mezza" or appetizers including grape leaves, hummus, kibbi and much much more! Our food is made from the freshest ingredients scrutinized for perfection every day by our chef Najwa. The menu is varied and can accommodate most any diet. We offer many vegan dishes as well as a wide array of meats fresh off the grill or off the vertical broiler.

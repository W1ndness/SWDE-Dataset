EightK at Viceroy Snowmass

writes...
  • Eight K is a vibrant new addition to the Snowmass and Aspen restaurant scene. A quick breakfast before the slopes, lunch with the family or an evening out will all be treated with the stylish sophistication that defines the Viceroy. The sleek mountain design provides a consistent backdrop for casually elegant dining. The room draws you in from the lobby with a long bar perfect for groups to gather and graze from a menu of small plates. Inside the dining room are two communal tables, two private dining rooms and patio seating offering an abundance of different options be it for dining, cocktailing or socializing. The menu is distinctly American with strong emphasis on local, sustainable and organic ingredients.

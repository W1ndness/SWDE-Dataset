White Heron Grill

writes...
  • White Heron Grill and its plush, softly lit dining room boasts clean contemporary lines of crisp white paneling, rich brown fabrics, and modern art that blend timelessly with Stone Harbor's prestigious beach elegance. White Heron Grill sets the standard for exceptional dining in South Jersey. Please join us in our elegant, friendly and relaxed atmosphere. Our chef uses the freshest ingredients to prepare classic American shore fare and comfort foods. Our philosophy of elegant yet relaxed dining mirrors the truly unique atmosphere of Stone Harbor; perfect for a romantic dinner or a large gathering of family and friends. Tantalize your taste buds with delicious dishes such as smoked salmon and crab salad, spice crusted scallops with blood orange reduction, and our famous jump lump crab cakes. Gift certificates are available and reservations are recommended. We look forward to seeing you.

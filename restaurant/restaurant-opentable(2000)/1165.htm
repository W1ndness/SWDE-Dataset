Avenue Grill - Denver

writes...
  • The Avenue Grill is renowned for its award-winning martinis, wine list, friendly bartenders and a welcoming, inimitable style. The Avenue is a favored lunch and dinner destination among the uptown Denver business crowd, as well as a popular neighborhood bar and grill. What attracts them is the unpretentious, superior service, comfortable ambience and delicious food. The menu is reminiscent of a San Francisco-style bar and grill with Asian and Italian influences.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Uptown
  • Cross Street: Washington Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.avenuegrill.com
  • Email: eat@avenuegrill.com
  • Phone: (303) 861-2820
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 4:00pm, Saturday Brunch - Sunday Brunch: 10:00am - 4:00pm Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 12:00am, Sunday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Andrew Lubatty
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: The Avenue Grill offers free parking. Our private lot is located directly across the street from The Avenue Grill on the northwest corner of 17th and Washington. Within a two block radius the Uptown area offers free two hour parking until 6pm. After 6pm the parking is unlimited.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Eldorado Room is the private dining room at the Avenue Grill. The Eldorado Room combines all the elegance of the Avenue Grill’s fine dining with the comfortable atmosphere of a neighborhood gathering place. The room is perfect size for an intimate meeting space to go over your weekly budget with a staff of 8, or hold a holiday cocktail party for an office of 65. The Avenue Grill specializes in personalizing any event with creative touches that will enhance any function you choose to have in the Eldorado Room. Let the Avenue Grill help you plan your next wedding reception dinner, holiday cocktail party or business luncheon.
  • Private Party Contact: Adrienne Watson (303) 861-2820
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Events: Join us on Monday nights for a Colorado traditional favorite: succulent prime rib. The Avenue Grill offers a 10oz hearty cut for $16.00. It's the perfect early evening meal to enjoy right after work or while you're watching your favorite game.

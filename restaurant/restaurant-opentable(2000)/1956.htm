Whispers

writes...
  • Located inside the historic Hewitt-Wellington Hotel, Whispers is a Victorian setting, crystal chandeliers, silver service, white linen table coverings, and elegant fine dining. Whispers serves modern American food with an creative touch. Please feel free to Bring Your Own Beer, Wine or Champagne. We have Riedel Glassware to serve you at no extra charge.

  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Spring Lake
  • Cross Street: 2nd Ave.

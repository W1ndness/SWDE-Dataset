Vetri

writes...
  • A rustic thirteen table dining room located in Center City Philadelphia, Vetri specializes in authentic Northern Italian dining featuring homemade pasta. A la carte as well as a tasting menu are available.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Center City
  • Cross Street: 13th Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.vetriristorante.com/
  • Email: info@vetriristorante.com
  • Phone: (215) 732-3478
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday (A la Carte and Tasting menus available) 6:00pm - 9:30pm Friday and Saturday (Grand Tasting Menu Only): 6:00pm - 9:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Marc Vetri
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Non-Smoking Area, Wine
  • Public Transit: SEPTA routes C, 27, and 32 stop at Broad and Spruce Streets. SEPTA route 23 stops at 12th and Spruce Streets. PATCO High Speed Line stops at Broad and Locust Streets as well as SEPTA's Broad Street Line. Regional Rail stations are located at Market East (11th and Market) and Suburban Station (16th and JFK).
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking Lots available at Juniper and Spruce, Juniper and Locust, Broad and Locust, and 15th and Spruce.
  • Private Party Facilities: Vetri is available for private parties of up to 34 guests. Please contact the restaurant for availability
  • Private Party Contact: Steve Wildy (215) 732-3478

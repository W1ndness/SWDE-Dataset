Chadaka Thai

writes...
  • Hip, inventive Thai eatery brings an Eastside vibe to downtown Burbank. Nestled in the heart of the Burbank Village you will find the first Valley outpost from the family that brought you Chao Krung, tuk-tuk & Rambutan. Deep browns contrast with creamy whites in the dining area; the half-moon frosted glass bar and lounge stick to the darker hue. Kimono-style silk patchwork lines the cathedral ceilings and adds a hint of color. The menu includes staples like noodles and curries, as well as specialties like rib-eye steak with hot pepper sauce and green pear salad with roasted cashews.

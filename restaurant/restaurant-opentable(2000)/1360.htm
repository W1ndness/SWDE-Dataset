Gus's BBQ

writes...
  • A South Pasadena landmark since 1946, Gus’s BBQ was recently purchased and remodeled by brothers Chris & John Bicos (owners of The Original Tops in Pasadena). The new Gus’s BBQ specializes in Traditional BBQ - California Style. Dive into an enormous plate of St. Louis spare ribs or Memphis style baby back ribs. You’ll definitely be back for their juicy Carolina style pulled pork sandwich, delicious cast iron cornbread or one of their many dry-aged burgers. In the mood for classic comfort food? Try their Jack’s Mac and Cheese smothered in 4 cheeses, applewood smoked bacon, crimini mushrooms & drizzled with white truffle oil. You’ll leave knowing exactly why Gus’s food has been hailed, “….so good that we found ourselves eating more than we’d imagined” by S. Irene Virbila, Los Angeles Times.

    Recently named best newcomer by Zagat & one of the top 7 BBQ restaurants in Southern California by ABC 7, Gus’s boasts a super hip bar that serves an impressive list of bourbons.

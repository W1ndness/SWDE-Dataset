Solera's Spanish-inspired and decor create a lively and unique setting for your most memorable event. Tapas, or "small plates" is a dining tradition that has long been a part of Spain's culture. Its popularity has spread throughout Europe and the Mediterranean - and now Minneapolis. Tapas dining encourages tasting and sharing a variety of unique foods to create a lively and entertaining atmosphere. Of course, if you prefer, a traditional entree course is also available. From the exotic main floor Alhambra Room and two additional floors of reception rooms to private patio tables with umbrellas, Solera offers an ideal setting for your event. Solera can easily accommodate events with groups from 10 people to 1,200 (using all reception and dining rooms). Your guests will enjoy a vibrant Spanish-influenced atmosphere, exceptional service and outstanding food and Spanish wines. Please call or email for a personalized proposal, or to schedule a tour of our distinctive event space

Trader Vic's - Atlanta

writes...
  • Trader Vic's is a Polynesian theme restaurant with exotic drink flavors served from the Mai Tai Lounge. The cuisine features fantastic fare from Singapore, Samoa, Shanghai & points west. Reflecting cuisines from around the world, Trader Vic's offers continental dishes, smoked-oven-roasted specialty meats & fish in the "Roti" tradition (never exposed to open flame) from Chinese wood-fired ovens plus classic wok presentations.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Pan-Asian
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.tradervicsatlanta.com

  • Public Transit: MARTA - Peachtree Center Station
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Parking Details: Complimentary self parking is available in the hotel parking garage with the purchase of an entree.
  • Private Party Facilities: Trader Vic's will easily accomodate groups 20 - 200 in Private Dining areas. The Puka Room seats up to 80 guests or a reception for 150. The Garden Room overlooking the Oriental Gardens, is perfect for a party of 120 or a reception of 200.
  • Private Party Contact: Vanessa Souza (404) 221-6339

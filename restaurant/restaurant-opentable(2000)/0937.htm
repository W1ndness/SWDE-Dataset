La Strada (Palo Alto)

writes...
  • The Giovannotto family and Chef Osvaldo Tomatis, a native of Turin, Italy, welcome you to La Strada Ristorante Italiano in the heart of downtown Palo Alto. Chef Tomatis’ seasonal menus meld the finest of Italian ingredients with the freshest local produce. In addition, a daily "La Cenetta," a 3-course dinner, offers the savvy palate with items a bit above and beyond the seasonal menu. Each and every dish from the traditional homemade pastas and pizzas to the most contemporary dishes exhibit the expertise and precision of Tomatis' training and passion.

  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Palo Alto
  • Cross Street: Florence Street

  • Public Transit: The Palo Alto Caltrain stop is just a few blocks away.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Abundant free parking adjacent to La Strada in the multi-level Florence Street parking garage.
  • Catering: Bring a taste of Italy to your family reunion or holiday celebration with catering from La Strada. Chef Osvaldo Tomatis will bring his talents to your table. With his menu personally tailored to your desire, your guests will leave raving about the culinary creations.

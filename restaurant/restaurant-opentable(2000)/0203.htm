M bistro by Chef Matt Murphy

writes...
  • M bistro is named after Executive Chef, Matt Murphy of The Ritz-Carlton, New Orleans. Growing up just outside Dublin, Ireland, Murphy never envisioned having his own restaurant but he has paid his dues in Michelin-starred restaurants as far away as Asia and under the tutelage of master chefs Daniel Boulud, Dennis Lenihan, Marco Pierre White, Patrick Guilbaud, Paul Flynn and Michael Martin. His recipes have been published in numerous publications internationally and he has appeared on The Today Show, PBS, E!, & Travel Channel. The menu is an indigenous approach to the preparation of the finest meats, seafood and produce from farmers in Louisiana, Texas, Mississippi and Alabama. Fresh, organic, seasonal, local and farm to table, that is M Bistro. Emery Van Hook, Director of Markets for Market Umbrella says that "Chef Murphy's long-standing support of the farmers markets is invaluable to us, and even more so to the local farmers and fishermen whose names you'll see on his menu."

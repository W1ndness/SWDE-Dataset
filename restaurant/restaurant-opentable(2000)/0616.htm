writes...
  • Named one of Food & Wine's Best New Chefs in 2008, Executive Chef Giuseppe Tentori features contemporary, seasonal American food. Recently added to the a la carte menu are 2 degustation menu options (6-courses for $85, 9-courses for $115). Chicago Tribune rated it 3 stars and called it "one of the city's best." Chicago Magazine also awarded it 3 stars and has called it "the hottest ticket in town." Chef Tentori was named a rising star by both Star Chefs and Restaurant Hospitality magazine. The restaurant was designed by Bogdanow Partners and features sweeping architectural sails and a cell phone booth. The outdoor patio is stunning with 90 seats.

Rio Del Sol

writes...
  • Rio Del Sol (formally Bistro Blue)brings the exotic flavors of Mexico to savor and heat up the palate. Relax and enjoy your dining experience in this colorful restaurant located in the heart of Evergreen. Rio Del Sol offers dishes that satisfy any taste. Menus reflect traditional and exotic appetizers and entrees along with in house specialty desserts. Rio Cantina offers Live live music on the patio. Treat yourselves to the best of Evergreen.

Bricco - Harrisburg

writes...
  • We at Bricco believe that great cuisine features the freshest & best quality ingredients on the market. As such, we emphasize the use of Pennsylvania agricultural products & local producers through the PA Preferred Program. Pennsylvania is a world leader in the production of high quality agricultural products. Bricco’s Mediterranean cuisine serves as the gastronomic canvas upon which Pennsylvania products are used to create a culinary masterpiece. Our students gain invaluable experience learning how to source local producers & utilize them as partners in the creation of world class cuisine. Bricco is a collaboration between the Olewine School of Culinary Arts at Harrisburg Area Community College & Harrisburg Hotel Corporation HACC’s chefs & restaurateurs of the future perfect their skills alongside some of the brightest stars in the industry today. Don’t forget to visit our two retail shops on Chestnut Street, Olewine’s Meat & Cheese House, & Ciao!, a bakery.

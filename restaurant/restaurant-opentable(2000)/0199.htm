Ernest's Orleans Restaurant

writes...
  • Ernest's Orleans Restaurant overlooks downtown Shreveport. Continuing 60 years of tradition, Chef Ernest Palmisano Jr. builds on the legacy of his mother Marjorie Palmisano of the historical Elmwood Plantation Restaurant and his father,Chef Ernest Sr.,from the Ernest's Supper club on the riverfront. Serving the finest is a motto that still holds true today.Fresh gulf seafood,prime steaks,and finest Italian cuisine are the primary dishes found on the menu.Featuring "off menu" items verbally communicated is another fine touch to Ernest's.Our club room has live entertainment and dancing nightly.If a quite romantic evening is the plan, our spacious and elegant dining room filled with detail oriented staff will make the evening memorable.The "Captain's" room with a personal bar near is the ideal choice and the Veranda has a beautiful view.We have an in house florist to make that special evening perfect.Have fresh flowers delivered tableside by our in house florist,the lovely Tina Marie!

We are conveniently located at the downtown area and near all major hotels and casinos.We have limo service that can pick your clients up at the airport or hotel.Please feel free to call us to discuss your needs.(Min.30-Max.100)
  • Private Party Contact: Tina Marie Palmisano (318) 226-1325

Local favorites sing the night away as your every need is attended to by our most attentive wait staff. Our full bar accommodates any diner, paired with a wonderful wine list and our fresh Louisiana seafood, prime steaks, or traditional Italian dishes this will be a night to remember.

Vintage Bar & Restaurant

writes...
  • A destination spot for many years to everyone from Hollywood & Broadway Stars to Hell's Kitchen regulars, all mingling, equally enjoying over 200 martinis, 100 domestic & international beers and a large selection of international wines by the glass. In addition to the numerous beverages choices, we have a varied food menu from our world famous nachos to apricut filos, burgers to chicken in a white wine sauce, ice cream cookie bites to decadent chocolate truffle cake with strawberries. Seating is available at the bar, low & high top tables and a private lounge for large groups. Seasonal garden seating is also available. Our music caters to our patrons wishes with 80's, 90's, classic vinyl, alternative & pop.

The Raymond Restaurant

writes...
  • Pasadena’s most beloved restaurant is experiencing a renaissance that celebrates its considerable charm. We’re preparing inventive seasonal menus - each with a deliciously eclectic bent. Classic Raymond recipes are newly inspired with the freshest, locally grown ingredients. Even the Raymond wine list is more full-bodied, offering superb selections from top West Coast and international wineries. Renowned Interior Designer, Matthew White, has revitalized the look of The Raymond, complimenting its historic cottage personality with a stylish new level of comfort. Come rediscover The Raymond - where the fine dining is the finest it’s ever been. The ambiance is warm and welcoming. And the impeccable service is a long-standing tradition. The Raymond Restaurant represents a slice of Pasadena’s colorful past and carries on Rose City’s great traditions of hospitality and architecture.

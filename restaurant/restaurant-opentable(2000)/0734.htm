Dressler's - Charlotte

writes...
  • Dressler's is an upscale contemporary restaurant with patio dinning, featuring a menu of steak, imaginative seafood, and creative appetizers paired with an extensive wine list and full service bar specializing in martinis, ports, and single malt scotches. Dressler's has a close knit goroup of managers who have worked together many years. Chef Scott Hollingsworth, Manager Stefan Stanton, Bar Manager Brian Lorusso, and Marketing Manager Dinah Grills have worked with owner Jon Dressler for 28 years combined. The camaraderie and warmth are obvious from the moment you walk through the doors. An accommodating staff will ensure that your experience is memorable. The 160 - seat, 6200 sqr ft, Dressler's Metro has unparalleled views of downtown Charlotte. Kim Dressler, wife and co-owner, did the interior design.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Steakhouse
  • Neighborhood: Charlotte
  • Cross Street: 277/Kings/Kenilworth
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.Dresslersmetro.com
  • Email: jon@dresslersmetro.com
  • Phone: (704) 909-6295
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Sunday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday ,Saturday 5.00pm - 1100-pm Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Scott Hollingsworth
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary valet parking is available at the front door for dinner service. Otherwise, there is a six story parking garage located across from the restaurant. We suggest to park on Level 2 and stairs or elevator down to the main level. Level 2 and above are much easier to park than Level 1.
  • Catering: We have a box lunch program that be delivered to your place of work. The box lunch menu costs $11. Please inquire with dinah@dresslersmetro.com
  • Entertainment: There is a stage that is directly behind our patio. The landlord will utilize it for a concert series. We are able to use it on nights that the landlord does not use it.
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Dressler's offers two private rooms that accommodate from 10 to 75 people.

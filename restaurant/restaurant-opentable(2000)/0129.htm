Divan Turkish Kitchen

writes...
  • Cash Only. Divan Turkish Kitchen brings a taste of traditional Turkish cuisine to Philadelphia's up-and-coming Graduate Hospital area at the corner of Carpenter & 22nd Streets. This family-owned, Turkish Restaurant is beautiful, warm & inviting. On the menu are soups, salads, hot & cold appetizers, sandwiches, charbroiled kebabs, lamb, chicken, seafood dishes, whole grilled fish, desserts & more. Divan serves lunch & dinner 7 days a week from 12pm - 10pm & is even open on holidays for your dining pleasure. Come experience why Turkish food is not one of the best foods in the world, it is the best food in the world! We have Turkish Vine selections and Turkish Beer, along with traditional Turkish Raki. We are the only restaurant in Philadelphia that serves alcohol and also BYOB. $10 cork fee on Fridays and Saturdays. 30% off from the entire menu 7 days a week 12noon-5pm

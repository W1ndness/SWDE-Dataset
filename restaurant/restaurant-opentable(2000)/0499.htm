The Place at Perry's

writes...
  • The Place at Perry’s is an award winning, locally owned restaurant and uniquely Dallas treasure. The Dallas Morning News dining critic Leslie Brenner gives The Place four stars, writing “...everything I tasted in two visits was terrific...” The food and service are nationally recognized in guides like Zagat’s. The Place is Dallas’ dining destination for the local business community and a comfortable neighborhood retreat - the perfect setting for intimate dinners or large parties, with a private dining room available for special events. Guests can enjoy a leisurely lunch or dinner or grab a quick bite. The updated menu features sustainably raised beef from Niman Ranch and fresh seafood delivered daily. Top-notch side dishes, salads, appetizers and desserts complete the fine dining experience. The menu is complimented by a signature wine list featuring private reserves. It’s The Place to be for happy hour with highly rated wines at half price and complimentary hors d’ oeuvres.

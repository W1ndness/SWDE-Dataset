F. Scott's Restaurant & Jazz Bar

writes...
  • F. Scott’s features Nashville’s finest contemporary cuisine with French influences using local ingredients as often as possible in a setting that combines the comfort of a neighborhood restaurant with flair and sophistication. With more than 300 wine selections and a menu that boasts the finest fare of the season, F. Scott’s is a restaurant for all occasions, all the time. Located in the heart of Green Hills, F. Scott’s presents live jazz nightly, and is a smoke-free restaurant.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, European
  • Neighborhood: Nashville
  • Cross Street: Off Hillsboro Road
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.fscotts.com
  • Email: fscottsjazz@comcast.net
  • Phone: (615) 269-5861
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm -11:00pm Bar opens 4:30 Monday - Friday Happy Hour: Monday - Wednesday 4:30 - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Will Uhlhorn
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Delivery, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary Valet is offered on premise or self parking is available in surrounding lots
  • Catering: Full service catering available, including wine and spirits. Our catering reflects seasonal ingredients and tailored menus. We can accommodate small private dinners or groups up to 500. Wedding receptions, charity events, business functions. For more information contact Wendy Burch at 615-269-5861.
  • Private Party Facilities: Our private dining area seats up to 75 persons. In addition, the entire restaurant can be booked in advance for special occasions & events. Daytime meeting space is also available.
  • Private Party Contact: Wendy Burch or Cynthia Nelson (615) 269-5861
  • Entertainment: Our lounge features live Jazz piano on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday from 7pm until 10pm and full live Jazz bands on Thursday from 7pm to 10pm, Friday from 7pm to 11pm and Saturday from 7:30pm to 11:30pm. Some of the top musicians in the country perform at F. Scott's. We do not take reservations in the lounge. Reservations are for the dining room only.

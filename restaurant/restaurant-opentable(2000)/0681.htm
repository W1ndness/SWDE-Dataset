Myron and Phil Steakhouse

writes...
  • Celebrating our 39th year in business, Myron and Phil is a second generation family owned and operated restaurant. We have kept all of the tradition of our signature steaks and seafood, while continually updating our menu selections to reflect the dining desires of our guest. Begin your dining experience in our cozy "clubby" atmosphere with generous cocktails from our full service bar. Enjoy complimentary assorted relishes and our famous homemade Chopped Liver served with a delicious assortment of freshly baked bread. All dinners include salad and a potato or vegetable. Make sure to save room for our wonderful desserts. Dine in, carry out or delivery available

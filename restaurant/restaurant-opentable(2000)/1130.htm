Victor's 1959 Cafe

writes...
  • Victor's 1959 Cafe is a little piece of Havana in South Minneapolis offering traditional Cuban fare such as Picadillo, Ropa Vieja, Cuban Sandwiches, Yuca frita, Sweet Plantains, Tostones, and other tasty recipes from Cuban cuisine. Independently and locally owned, you'll find friendly service and a festive environment with lots of writing on the walls! (Ask for a sharpie so you can add your comments to our walls filled with grafitti). Visited in 2008 by Food Network's Guy Fieri, Diner's Drive-ins and Dives, who cooked up "off the hook" Cuban food with owner Niki Stavrou. Check our website to watch the episode! We're a tiny restaurant with only 11 tables inside and 10 more tables on our tropical summer patio. Also popular for our mango pancakes and unique breakfast menu, served every day, reservations for breakfast are taken for parties of 6 or more. Enjoy our Wines, Beers and Tropical Mimosas! 5 mins from Downtown/Uptown 15 minutes from Mpls/St Paul Int'l airport

Dawson's - Hyatt Regency Sacramento

writes...
  • Classic foods prepared in a comfortable and contemporary atmosphere. Step into Dawson’s with its rich mahogany walls, stylish atmosphere and big comfortable chairs and enjoy the creations of Chef Ian Libberton and his culinary team. Dawson's is a lively restaurant and bar featuring prime rib, hand cut steaks, hearty side dishes and a creative wine list.

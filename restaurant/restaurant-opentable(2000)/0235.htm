Market Restaurant and Bar

writes...
  • Just one-mile from Del Mar beaches, MARKET Restaurant + Bar offers award-winning chef Carl Schroeder's Contemporary California cooking in a sexy, modern dining room. Clean lines, warm colors and gorgeous fabrics set the stage for this new, perpetually packed eatery. In our effort to bring you the freshest of ingredients our menus changes daily based on the seasonal availability of seafood, produce and meat our local purveyors have to offer. Our menus are designed to offer a variety of fresh seasonal foods for each price range.

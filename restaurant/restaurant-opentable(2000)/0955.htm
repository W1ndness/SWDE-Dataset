Mezes, A Taste of Greece

writes...
  • Located in San Francisco's beautiful Marina district, Mezes is a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Mezes is Greek for "small plates meant to share". Enjoy everything from delicious dips and zucchini cakes to prawn plates or platters of grilled meats. With it's charming ambiance and it's great variety of flavors, Mezes will make you feel as if you've been magically transported to Greece. A knowledgeable staff is available to guide you :-) Mezes has been recognized by the San Francisco community as one the most authentic Greek restaurants in the Bay Area (SFgate, SF Weekly).

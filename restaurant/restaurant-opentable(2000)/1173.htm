Brook's Steak House & Cellar

writes...
  • Brook's Steak House serves the finest USDA Prime Steaks, Colorado Lamb, and South Australian Lobster available. They have been recognized as one of the Top Ten Steak Houses in America by MSN CitySearch for 2003 and 2004. Brook's award-winning wine list of over 1200 wines from around the world will surely please the most discriminating palate. From the moment you enter the restaurant, you will feel the difference only an independently owned restaurant can offer.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Steak, Steakhouse
  • Neighborhood: Tech Center / Greenwood Village
  • Cross Street: Arapahoe
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.brookssteakhouse.com
  • Email: primesteak@brookssteakhouse.com
  • Phone: (303) 770-1177
  • Hours of Operation: Daily: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Abel Gomez
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Self parking also available.
  • Private Party Facilities: Brook's offers 3 private dining rooms: the Bordeaux Room seating up to 80, the Oak Room seating up to 60 & the Cellar seating up to 25.
  • Private Party Contact: Angela Beaty (720) 529-2921

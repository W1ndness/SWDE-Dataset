i. Cugini

writes...
  • Perched at the edge of the Pacific Ocean, i. Cugini offers a daily menu of outstanding fresh seafood and authentic Italian cuisine. Whole fish roasted in rock salt serves up a traditional Mediterranean experience and is offered right alongside the more modern wild New Zealand Salmon with grapefruit citronette. Our wood burning oven bakes everything from fish filets to the thinnest, crispiest pizza. Pair with a wine from our Wine Spectator Award winning wine list for the perfect complement to your meal. Enjoy an unparalleled ocean view from our covered and heated patio or the high energy of our Grand Cafe style dining room. DEALS: Free Corkage all day, every day for 2010.

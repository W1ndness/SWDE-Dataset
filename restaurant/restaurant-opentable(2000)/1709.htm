Café on the Green

writes...
  • An easy to reach hidden treasure. The upscale decor and table settings along with attentive and gracious service are enhanced by the sweeping views provided by huge active windows that encompass three sides of the restaurant. All of this is surpassed by Northern Italian Cuisine delicately prepared with the freshest ingredients. Zagat Rated Excellent 2005, Wine Spectators Award of Excellence 2005. We promise dining out will be unforgettable.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian, European, Mediterranean
  • Neighborhood: Danbury
  • Cross Street: Mill Plain Road
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://cafeonthegreenrestaurant.com
  • Email: cafeonthegreen@comcast.net
  • Phone: (203) 791-0369
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch Daily: 11:00am - 5:00pm
    Dinner Nightly: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Tracey Kydes
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: 250 space parking lot, lighted.
  • Catering: Please contact Tracey Kydes for details from a private dinner for two to a grand reception of 200 all privately catered to your specific tailored menu.
  • Private Party Facilities: Accommodates to 12 to 120 guests with veranda accessibility for cocktails and hor d'ourves, we create custom designed menus for all events of all sizes, make each event very special and unique.
  • Private Party Contact: Tracey Kydes (203) 791-0369
  • Entertainment: Every Friday and Saturday night we have live singing and piano playing for the restaurant.

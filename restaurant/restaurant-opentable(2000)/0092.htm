Strip House in Houston

writes...
  • Strip House is located in downtown Houston within walking distance to the Convention Center, Toyota Center and Minute Maid Park. Strip House's innovative menu includes prime cuts of meat charred to perfection, signature side dishes such as truffle creamed spinach, goose fat potatoes, and the famed 24 Layer Chocolate Cake. A well balanced and expansive wine list is available. The striking red interior decorated with black and white seductive images of women in various forms was designed by David Rockwell. Strip House has been named One Of The Top Ten Best Steakhouses in America by Maxim Magazine and one of America’s Greatest Steakhouses by GQ Magazine.

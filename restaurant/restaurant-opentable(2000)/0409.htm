McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Baltimore

writes...
  • McCormick & Schmick's prepares fresh seafood in a traditional manner with a contemporary twist. Entering our traditional-style restaurant is like being transported to an earlier age. The hand-crafted walnut wood, beveled and stained glass create a warm, sophisticated atmosphere of a private club. "Inviting dining rooms with their superb waterfront views will immediately put you in the mood for treasures of the sea....The deep selection will have you agonizing over choices" - Baltimore Magazine, April 2000 McCormick & Schmicks guarantees reservations for indoor dining only. Specific seating requests will be honored based upon availability. Outdoor seating is done on a first come, first serve basis.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak, American
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Cross Street: Eastern Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: William McKinley
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Water taxi
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet parking after 5:00 pm.
  • Private Party Facilities: McCormick & Schmick’s of Baltimore has been voted best view by Baltimore Magazine and located is 711 Eastern Avenue. Our elegant decor features traditional wood-paneled decor accented with hand-crafted chandeliers, mosaic tile floors, stained glass ceilings and a mahogany bar. This warm, classic ambiance is further enhanced by the breathtaking, panoramic view of Baltimore’s Inner Harbor Skyline.McCormick & Schmick’s provides Private Dining Services and Banquet facilities for groups of 12 to 2000 and anywhere in-between. Please give us a call to assist you in planning your next event and we will make it everything you want it to be.

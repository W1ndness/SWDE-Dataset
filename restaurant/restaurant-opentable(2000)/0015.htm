Garduno's

writes...
  • The Best of the Southwest! Garduno’s was recently voted 2010's "Best Mexican Restaurant in Las Vegas" by the readers of the Las Vegas Review-Journal. Garduno’s has won this award every year since it opened in 2001! In 2010, Garduno's also won "Best Brunch in Las Vegas" by the staff of the Las Vegas Review-Journal. Garduno’s serves authentic New Mexican specialties that are famous for distinct flavor. If you are reserving a party of 12 or more please look at our special large party menu ($30 per person) at http://www.palms.com/pdfs/Gardunos_Large_Menu.pdf. Monday-Thursday, we are proud to offer "A Taste of Garduno's" Prix Fixe Menu. Includes appetizer - Guacamole Fresca, Chile Con Queso, or Rolled Tacos. Also includes fajitas - steak, chicken, shrimp, or vegetable. Also includes house margarita and flan - a complete meal for only $22.99. Must be 21, not valid with any other offer or coupon. Not valid on holidays or special events. Management reserves all rights.

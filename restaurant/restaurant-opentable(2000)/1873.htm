Pazzo Pazzo

writes...
  • Located just off the "green" in Historic Morristown, New Jersey, Pazzo Pazzo is a four star Italian Restaurant serving regional Italian cuisine. Guilano Hazan, Author of "The Pasta Cookbook and Every Night Italian" has created our menu. Although pasta is Pazzo's specialty, serving dry to fresh pastas made directly on the premises, we also offer daily specials which complement our extensive menu which includes a variety of salads, appetizers, meat and seafood items. The inside and outside bars & lounges offer a great escape. With over 50 beverage choices on our specialty drink list plus a wide array of single malt scotches, whiskeys, grappas and cognacs, guests are guaranteed something to please them. The wine list features many Italian favorites and other bottles from around the world. On a nice evening enjoy the magical setting of the multi tiered outdoor patio and lounge which serves as an urban getaway for many of the tri-state's elite. Come check out our vibe...

Facilities 1 of 4
  • Atrium

    This private space is great for intimate gatherings or corporate dinners.    The space lends itself to a cocktail style setting, captains table, or rounds of up to 8.    The private dining room is bright and airy with french doors separating the space from a beautiful outdoor terrace.    This room can be configured in a multitude of ways and is great for any type of gathering.

    6560
  • Main Dining Room

    The main dining room is situated off the bar & lounge area.    Although this room is considered semi-private due to the proximity, your guests will enjoy the energy and ambience of Pazzo Pazzo while dining or cocktailing in a grand open space.

    Capacity
    10090
  • Grand Luxe

    This option is available as a restaurant Buy-out only.    It is a combination of the Main dining room and Atrium opened up for a grand feeling event.    The entire space is surrounded by french doors which open to a Multi-tiered private terrace with full bar, fountain, oversized planters and built in banquets with plush pillows.    This space is perfect for weddings, corporate buy-outs, bar/bat mitzvahs, and any excuse for a party.

    Capacity
    250140
  • Outdoor Terrace

    The multi-tiered outdoor patio lends itself to a variety of configurations including intimate dinner parties or large corporate & private events.    With the oversized banquets complimented with plush pillows and the built in cement bar, this venue is guaranteed to produce the wow factor.    The large stone planters overflowing with bright colorful flora make this space truly unique during the warmer months.    For peace of mind, all events are booked with contingency for rain in our indoor space.

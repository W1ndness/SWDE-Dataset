  • Nestled away in the Penrod’s Beach Club Complex, Nikki Beach Restaurant and Beach Club is a unique dining experience. The innovative menu, décor, and atmosphere tantalize all your senses for an evening you won’t soon forget.

  • Cuisine: Global, International, Fusion / Eclectic

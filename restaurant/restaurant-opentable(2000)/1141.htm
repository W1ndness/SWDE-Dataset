Eau Bistro

writes...
  • Eau Bistro is located in the heart of the Central West End within the historic Chase Park Plaza at Lindell and Kingshighway. Enjoy fine cuisine while you relax in one of the cozy oversized banquettes or private dining alcoves. Executive Chef Brian Hale and his talented crew tantalize you with their famous four-course tasting menu prepared specifically for your tastes. Or enjoy Eau Bistro’s seasonally fresh ala carte menu. The delightful wine list boasts over 200 labels and showcases your favorite California, French and Australian producers. The most amazing service staff is ever-attentive, knowledgeable and promises to please. Eau Bistro offers St. Louis’s favorite Sunday Champagne Brunch (10A – 2P) with made-to-order omelets, carving stations, fresh shellfish display, and delectable desserts, always with complimentary mimosas and Bloody Marys. Parking will be validated at The Chase.

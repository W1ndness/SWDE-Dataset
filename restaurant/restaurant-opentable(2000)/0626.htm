Chataigne at Le Cordon Bleu - Chicago

writes...
  • Chataigne is the full service, student run restaurant at Le Cordon Bleu College. Enjoy French cooking with an inventive modern flair in this casual fine dining establishment in Chicago's trendy River North District. Reservations are recommended. Châtaigne is occasionally closed for large parties or school breaks. A reservation will confirm that we are open and have availability. After you dine at Châtaigne, we would welcome and appreciate your feedback to help us further educate our students.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Continental, American
  • Neighborhood: River North
  • Cross Street: Orleans
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.chefs.edu/Chicago/Restaurant
  • Email: chataigne@chicago.chefs.edu

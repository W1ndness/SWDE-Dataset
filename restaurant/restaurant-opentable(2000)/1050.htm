  • BLUE PLATE AWARD OF EXCELLENCE- LANE REPORT 2010
    FOUR STARS- LEXINGTON HERALD LEADER 2009

    The otherwise bland stretch of Old Todd’s Road belies the elegance and sophistication of the restaurant’s cool interior, and of the food. School occupies a cozy, cheerful space with colorful wooden geometric patterned walls and minimalistic furniture. The ingredients range far and wide and straddle the familiar to foreign. Sushi transported on a conveyor system which can be selected at the patron’s convenience takes center stage. If sushi does not strike your fancy I especially like what School does with French selections. Don’t be fooled, this is authentic French. School also boasts a Culinary Cocktail Lounge (one of a kind in Lexington), 40+ different bottles of wine & shochu, and a private karaoke room that can be reserved by the hour. While located in heart of the Bluegrass you will definitely feel you have been transported to a much larger city for the evening.

Lucille's Smokehouse Bar-B-Que

writes...
  • Lucille's Smokehouse Barbecue is an exciting, upscale restaurant serving the finest Southern Barbecue in the area. We take pride in serving only Certified Angus beef products and smoking them slowly on premise daily. In addition to preparing mouthwatering ribs, chicken and tri tip we offer southern favorites such as-Blackened Catfish, Jambalaya and Gumbo. All this is served to you in a fun and friendly environment surrounded by authentic southern art while you listen to the best blues music in town, with live blues playing in the Flying Pig lounge every Friday and Saturday. Lucille's is a great place for lunch or dinner and especially fun to host your next birthday or graduation party. We offer our entire menu to go and can help you cater you next party or business function, as we have special designed platters that serve large groups.

Lucille's professional catering staff can bring with the southern BBQ flavor and our Finest Southern Hospitality to your office or home. With a range of menu and service options, we are able to create events to meet your needs. We cater out of all of our locations, and will do our best to accommodate any special requests.
  • Entertainment: Start your weekends at Lucille's with a Mint Julep or an ice cold draft beer and enjoy live blues in our Flying Pig Lounge, every Friday & Saturday night from 6:00pm until 10:00pm. They Flying Pig Lounge opens daily at 11:00am, offering a full menu of food, beer, wine, spirits and Southern libations.

Left Bank Santana Row

writes...
  • Fun. Festive. French. Luminary chefs Roland Passot & Joel Guillon present classic French dishes such as Escargots & Coq au Vin, as well as house-made sausages & pates, natural meats, sustainably-fished seafood & all sorts of French goodness. You can also look forward to craft beers, an unpretentious winelist & hand-crafted cocktails such as the Femme Fatale, Fleurs du Mal & Kiwi Nuage. The closest thing to France in the South Bay.

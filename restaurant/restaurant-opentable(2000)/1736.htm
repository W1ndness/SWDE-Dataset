Day Boat Cafe

writes...
  • A New England-style seafood restaurant specializing in chowders and fresh fish delivered daily, with an extensive selection from our raw bar. Our menu is a mix of comfort food, and updated takes on classics offered between Pawtucket and Bangor. We offer seven microbrews on tap, and a small but funky and affordable wine list.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Comfort Food
  • Neighborhood: Irvington
  • Cross Street: Main Street
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.dayboatcafe.com
  • Email: info@dayboatcafe.com
  • Phone: (914) 231-7854
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 12:00pm - 3:00pm, Sunday: 1:00pm - 8:00pm

  • Payment Options:

  • Executive Chef: John Durkin
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress

  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: The restaurant is located directly across from the Irvington Metro-North train stop.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: The Bridge Street Properties parking lot is open to all restaurant guests.

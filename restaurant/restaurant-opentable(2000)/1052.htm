Ambrosia Ristorante - Broad Ripple

writes...
  • Founded in 1979 by Italian-born restaurateur Gino Pizzi, Ambrosia has quietly flourished as an environment where food, drink and conversation can be enjoyed in equal measure. Whether you come to Ambrosia for quiet one-on-one conversation, relaxed dinner with friends, a wedding reception or other large-scale events, Pizzi, and the rest of Ambrosia"s knowledgeable staff are dedicated to ensuring that your experience is characterized by the unhurried charm and authentic cooking that are the two inseparable components of true Italian dining.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Indianapolis
  • Cross Street: Westfield Blvd & Guilford
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.ambrosiaitalian.com
  • Email: info@ambrosiaitalian.com
  • Phone: (317) 255-3096
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Gino Pizzi
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Catering: Our two Ambrosia restaurants offer special services for your events and catering needs. Both locations have private rooms that are perfect for your next event. Ambrosia can also cater anything from a small, intimate gathering to a gala event. For more information please contact us at 317.635.3096 (Downtown) and 317.255.3096 (Broad Ripple).
  • Private Party Facilities: Ambrosia offers a comfortable private room for up to 36 guests. Semi private arrangements are also available for larger functions.
  • Private Party Contact: Sherri Elston (317) 255-3096

Himmarshee Bar & Grille

writes...
  • This is the most dynamic restaurant in the city - you'll love eating here. Himmarshee Bar & Grille provides a culinary experience that is smooth and sophisticated, elegant without being fussy, casual and relaxing. Himmarshee is a great place to share with your friends or indulge on your own. Rated excellent by the 2006 Zagat Guide.

Clarke's

writes...
  • A neighborhood bar and grill with Irish pub flair, buzzing with conversation and laughter, Clarke's is a most welcome vacation from the typical South Beach scene. You don't have to be famous to get the star treatment, since Clarke's considers each and every customer important in his or her own right. Pair the enticing selections, prepared daily from scratch and ranging from traditional pub fare to contemporary dishes, with a pint of Guinness or a bottle of wine from the comprehensive and well-priced wine list. The exceptional food and drink quality is well met by the relaxed and friendly yet highly efficient service. With dark woods and brick walls, Clarke's delivers a collective vibe of New York, Dublin and Miami. For local diners looking for a comfortable place to gather, it is the gold underneath the typical South Beach glitz.

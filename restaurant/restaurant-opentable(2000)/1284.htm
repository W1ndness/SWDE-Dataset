SASA

writes...
  • SASA..... a Japanese tapas style restaurant serving contemporary Asian cuisine. Located right next door to Shaker Square Cinema, SASA is the perfect place for a small meal, dinner or late night snacks. SASA offers an array of tastes from Japan and an Asian spin on tasty classics. Known as Cleveland's premier sake bar, SASA also offers Japanese beer on tap, exotic drinks such as an Asian pear martini & delicious favorites. Welcome to SASA.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Sushi, Asian
  • Neighborhood: Shaker Heights
  • Cross Street: Shaker Blvd
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.sasamatsu.com
  • Email: sasamatsu@sasamatsu.com
  • Phone: (216) 767-1111
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Friday and Saturday: 5:00pm - Midnight, Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm

    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Scott Kim

  • Public Transit: SASA is on the Rapid Transit Line--Shaker Square stop.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Valet available. Self parking available in the large, secure lot behind SASA (Dave's Market). On street parking also available.

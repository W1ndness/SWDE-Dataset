Province

writes...
  • Province is an American café influenced by the modern, evolving cuisines of Spain and South America. Housed in a gold-level LEED-certified green building, Province incorporates green building materials, recycled design materials and Energy Star-rated equipment. Sustainable, organic, and biodynamic sources are the foundation for food, wine, and beverages of Province. In the warmer months enjoy Province's spacious and comfortable patio. With seating for up to 50 guests, the dramatic space is one of the West Loop's most desirable outdoor dining destinations.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Latin / Spanish
  • Neighborhood: West Loop
  • Cross Street: Between Randolph & Lake
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

The Private Dining Rooms reflect the modern approach to farmhouse aesthetic that is the signature design of Province. Clean lines with modern edges make this a perfect choice for any business or social event. Let Province create the perfect menu for your party with every attention to detail. The entire restaurant may also be used for larger private events. Contact the restaurant for more details on your next event!

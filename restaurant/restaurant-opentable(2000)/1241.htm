Core Kitchen and Wine Bar at The Ritz-Carlton Dove Mountain

writes...
  • True American cuisine that is approachable, innovative and inspired by Chef Joel Harrington. The menu features Sonoran and regional influences complemented by an extensive wine list in an upscale yet casual atmosphere. The open kitchen highlights the culinary team creating á la carte offerings with plenty of interaction. The private dining room provides an option to entertain larger parties and family gatherings with a personalized chef menu. The chef’s table sets the tone for a world-class interactive, personalized gourmet dining experience. CORE serves breakfast and dinner.

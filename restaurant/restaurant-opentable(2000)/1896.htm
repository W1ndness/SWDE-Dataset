Rio Rodizio - Newark (Formerly Seabra Rodizio)

writes...
  • Rio Rodizio is an all-you-can-eat dining experience like no other. Sit back, relax, and enjoy this popular Brazilian, Rodizio-style barbecue, where perfectly seasoned cuts of beef, pork, filet mignon, chicken, ham, turkey wrapped in bacon, and Brazilian sausage are slow-roasted on large skewers. The sizzling cuts of meat and poultry are brought to you and carved tableside by authentic Gaucho chefs. You may choose from as many different seasonings and cuts of meat as you like. Featured meats include beef rib, top sirloin, rump steak, rib-eye steak, flank steak, skirt steak, shell steak, lamb, and more. Every meal is accompanied by homemade, traditional Brazilian side dishes and an extravagant all-you-can-eat 30-item salad bar fit for a king or queen. Rio Rodizio also offers an ala carte menu with traditional Portuguese entrees and a sushi bar where the amazing fresh sushi is prepared to please your taste buds. Also a variety of delicious desserts like freshly made cakes and tarts.

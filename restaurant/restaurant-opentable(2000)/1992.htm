Finz Restaurant at Sandestin Golf and Beach Resort

writes...
  • Finz Beachside Grille offers beachside dining where only the cuisine outshines the view. Under the direction of Finz Restaurant Chef Justin Stark, Finz Beachside Grille offers a combination of flavors that will have guests wanting to return for more. Entrees include a memorable collection of seafood, fish, steaks, lamb and chicken. Finz Beachside Grille’s unique flavors are inspired from the Gulf Rim including the Caribbean Islands, Jamaica, Haiti as well as hints from New Orleans. For families and junior diners, Chef Justin offers children’s favorites served with fruit salad and a cookie. Table and banquette seating are available to provide a private eating experience perfect for any size dining group, from a table of two to a party of 60.

Furlongs - A Louisiana Restaurant

writes...
  • Furlongs - A Louisiana Restaurant offers cuisine with authentic Southwest Louisiana flavor. The menu features the following appetizers: Stuffed Mushrooms, Fried Banana Peppers, Grilled Andouille, Fried Crawfish Tails, Crab and Crawfish Cakes, Shrimp on Andouille Grits, Crawfish and Andouille Wantons, and finally, Blackened Chicken or Shrimp Quesadillas. The entrees cover Poultry, Salads, Steaks, Ribs, Seafood and Pasta. The Poultry entrees include Blackened Chicken over Jambalaya; Grilled or Fried Quail. The Salad selections include a Zydeco Cobb, a salad with grilled andouille, chicken, and shrimp with fresh tomatoes, cucumber, onions and boiled eggs. Our 12 oz. Ribeye is served over roasted garlic mashed potatoes and topped with bourbon caramelized onions and a bleu cheese compound butter. Seafood entrees are highlighted by Oysters Bordelaise and also Bouillabaisse. Our Specialties include Stuffed Catfish, BBQ Shrimp, Crawfish Extravaganza, Crawfish Etoufee and Baked Duck.

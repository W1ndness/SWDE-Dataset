Zarela

writes...
  • Zarela serves home-style regional Mexican food in a fun and festive atmosphere. We offer a full bar with a large selection of tequilas and the best margaritas in New York City.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Mexican / Southwestern
  • Neighborhood: Midtown East
  • Cross Street: Between 50th and 51st Streets
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.zarela.com
  • Email: tacoz@mindspring.com
  • Phone: (212) 644-6740
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 12:00pm - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:30pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Zarela Martinez

    Chef de Cuisine: Hermene Fernandez
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Delivery, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: E & V trains to 3rd Avenue/53rd Street and the 6 train to 51st Street and Lexington Avenue.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: There are two private parking lots close by. One is located on 51st Street between 2nd and 3rd Avenues and the other is on 49th Street between 2nd and 3rd Avenues.
  • Catering: Zagat's says: Zarela "does the best job" around at supplying the fixings "for a fun Mexican event" with awesome regional cuisine set up by the crew for a corporate or private "authentic" fiesta . Contact catering director Winston Cole for assistance at 212-663-7004.

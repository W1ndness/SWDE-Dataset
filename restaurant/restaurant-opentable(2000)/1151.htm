Mosaic - St. Louis

writes...
  • Located in central loft district on Washington Avenue, this stunning modern fusion restaurant is an eye-pleasing mix of granite tables, high ceilings, tall windows and a beautifully designed curvaceous freestanding bar with exquisite black galaxy granite top. Beneath that striking display of wall art, a row of Bunsen burner-style candelabra anchors a small lounge area with seating pillows along a bamboo bench and a pair of lounge chairs: a little pit of love and fire, the spot where you just know the throngs will cluster once things get under way. Executive Chef Ben Lester, with owner/Chef Claus Schmitz, a German native and veteran of European, American and especially Pacific Rim cuisine offers up such delights as Mosaic Pot Stickers filled with tender suckling pork, Manchego stuffed Quail, Korean BBQ Pork Apple ,Mango Caprese, Flight of Soups & many more. Exceptional food meets exceptional Libations, Fresh fruit Mojitos, Chilling Martini's, and a wealth of experience.

Mosaic offers two exclusive dining experiences. The PRIVATE DINING ROOM offers the ultimate in privacy, accommodating from ten to forty people for a sit-down lunch or dinner and up to sixty people standing for cocktail parties. Mosaic Lounge offers stunning cityscape views from our floor to ceiling 24ft wall of windows, accommodating up to 90 guests for formal seating and 250 guests for exclusive cocktail events. Each private area features its own unique touches and ambience, but common to all are 47” plasma televisions, microphones and state-of-the-art electronics. From relaxed business dinners to formal board meetings, unparalleled presentations to elegant rehearsal dinners, from lighthearted birthday parties to commemorative graduation celebrations, allow Mosaic to create a memorable experience for you and your guests.

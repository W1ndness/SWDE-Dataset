Peachwood’s Steakhouse at the Inn at Pasatiempo

writes...

  • A Santa Cruz favorite, Peachwood’s is located at the beautiful Inn at Pasatiempo where every meal is a 6-course culinary experience. We specialize in choosing the finest grades of beef and premium selections of poultry, seafood and vegetarian cuisine. Let’s not forget our vast array of appetizers and tempting desserts!

    Peachwood’s is open daily for lunch, dinner and Sunday champagne brunch. Stop by and enjoy one of our specialty cocktails in the lounge while watching your favorite team on the big screen. We offer a full bar, banquet facilities and all inclusive wedding packages.

Euno Ristorante

writes...
  • A hidden gem in the heart of Boston's historic North End, Euno serves only the finest in Italian and Mediterranean cuisine and wine. Savory sea bass, exquisite veal osso bucco, tender pollo parmigian are just a few of the delectable dishes our culinary artists will create to delight each and every palate. And if it's a taste of Americana you desire, please enjoy our classic filet mignon or world famous Boston lobster (served in-season only). We are open nightly for dinner at 5pm. Buon Appetito. Enjoy!
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, Mediterranean
  • Neighborhood: North End / Waterfront
  • Cross Street: Cooper Street
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.eunoboston.com
  • Phone: (617) 573-9406
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday thru Thursday 5:00pm - 10:00pm , Friday & Saturday 5:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: George "C"
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Fireplace, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Haymarket, Aquarium
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Public Lot on adjacent Cooper Street (intersecting)
  • Private Party Facilities: Ristorante Euno is the perfect location for any and all functions from corporate dinner parties to bridal showers to graduations. With two distinct levels of dining we offer privacy for 25 people or more. Enjoy our Mediterranean ambience complete with open fireplace and Tuscan wine cellar. Reservations in advance.
  • Private Party Contact: Mr. Ameziane/Manager (617) 573-9406
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Parties are always welcome. We seat two private dining rooms. For reservations please phone: 617.573.9406

Michael Jordan's the Steakhouse N.Y.C.

writes...
  • Situated on the North and West balconies of New York's Grand Central Terminal, this restaurant was designed to reflect Michaels sense of taste and style. Michael Jordan's the Steakhouse N.Y.C. ranks among the finest steakhouses in Manhattan, offering a world class menu and an extensive, eclectic selection of wines and spirits.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse
  • Neighborhood: Midtown East
  • Cross Street: 43nd Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.theglaziergroup.com/restaurants/michaeljordan/index.html
  • Phone: (212) 655-2300
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 3:00pm, Dinner: Monday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:45pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:45pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Frank Dyer
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: 4,5,6,7 & S Train to Grand Central.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Convenient Garage
  • Private Party Facilities: Private party space for parties from 8 to 200 people.
  • Private Party Contact: Alexandra Sulu (212) 655-2300
  • Entertainment: The winter beer program at Michael Jordan’s The Steak House N.Y.C. will offer a series of complimentary Wednesday evening tastings showing guests how to pair cuts of steak with different styles of beer. Michael Jordan’s Chef Michael Vignola noticed that the different cuts of steak vary so much that they go with styles of beers in unique ways. For this pairing series, the chef is highlighting his favorites. All tastings will be held in the lounge at Michael Jordan’s The Steak House N.Y.C. from 6:00-7:00 PM.

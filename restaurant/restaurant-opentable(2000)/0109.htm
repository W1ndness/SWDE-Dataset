Black Bass Hotel

writes...
  • The Black Bass Hotel offers a variety of delicious dining options. The Restaurant at The Black Bass Hotel, with a breathtaking view of the Delaware River, is open Monday through Sunday. Guests can dine al fresco, weather permitting, on The River Deck, situated along the scenic Delaware River. Come enjoy everything from Breakfast to Lunch to Dinner & of course we continue to offer a fabulous Sunday Brunch! Whether you are dining in The Restaurant, outside on The River Deck, enjoying coffee or a cocktail in the comfortable Lantern Lounge, or having a relaxing meal in The Tavern, we’re confident you will find the food & the setting to be to your liking. The Black Bass is the perfect Bucks County setting to enjoy a fine dining experience in our riverside Restaurant at The Black Bass Hotel, more casual fare in our Tavern at The Black Bass Hotel, or a luxurious weekend stay in one of our amazing Suites. Please call for dinner parties of 5 or larger.

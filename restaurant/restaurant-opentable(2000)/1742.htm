Diva

writes...
  • Diva has been on the SoHo scene for fifteen years. This cool, ultra-hip locale is a favorite of celebrities, locals and tourists, who want to eat, drink and relax in a laid-back, comfortable environment. The décor consists of beautiful, autumnal hues and elaborate ironwork made by the artist Arturo DiModica, known for his landmark statue of a charging bull on Wall Street. It is sexy and romantic, perfect for making a lasting impression on a first date. Diva's candlelit ambience, dramatic crimson-stained walls, floor to ceiling windows and long-sleek bar give Diva a warm and inviting, yet stylish and elegant look. The service is attentive and polite in a caring way, yet not intrusive. The food is described as Italian-Mediterranean but there is also a good sampling of American fare to keep everyone happy. What makes Diva's food so unique is that it is simple, but yet sophisticated. We look forward to welcoming you at Diva.

Hola! Mexican Restaurant & Cantina - Burlingame

writes...
  • Hola! Mexican Restaurant & Cantina in Burlingame invites you to come and experience delicious Mexican food of both the traditional and regional kind along with more than 100 specialty tequilas. Fresh flavors, great drinks and knowledgeable staff are just a few of the things you will find here. The two level restaurant located on Burlingame's main drag is complimented by an outdoor patio and is festively decorated with saltillo tile floors, handcrafted tile tables, hand carved wood and leather chairs. Other works by talented artisans of Mexico, such as punched tin, glassware, iron work and ceramic ware are also on display. Your visit here will be a truly sensory experience. Hola! Burlingame is also your place for all your banquets, parties and wedding receptions. We are also available for catering and take out. So come join us at Hola! Burlingame to sizzle your palate Mexican style with fresh traditional favorites and fine regional and coastal specialties.

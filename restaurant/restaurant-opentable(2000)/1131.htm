Vincent - A Restaurant

writes...
  • Welcome to Vincent - A Restaurant! Vincent offers contemporary French cuisine in an elegant setting with beautiful views of downtown Minneapolis. The ever-evoloving seasonal menu reflects chef-owner Vincet Francoual's eclectic style. Private dining and catering services are available.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, French
  • Neighborhood: Downtown Minneapolis
  • Cross Street: 11th & Nicollet
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.vincentarestaurant.com
  • Email: reservations@vincentarestaurant.com
  • Phone: (612) 630-1189
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Vincent Francoual
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: 11th or 12th and Nicollet
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Street, valet, and public parking lots available. Vincent guests receive discounted valet parking. Valet is located immediately outside the restaurant on 11th Street and is available for dinner service only. Vincent also validates for "Do The Town" parking facilities. For details visit the "About Vincent" section of the restaurant's website @ wwww.vincentarestaurant.com.
  • Catering: Catering services are available for groups, large or small. Chef Vincent Francoual will work with customers to design a menu to suit your needs.
  • Private Party Facilities: The semi-private mezzanine offers beautiful views of downtown while comfortably accomadating up to 48 guests for seated luncheons and dinners, and up to 80 for cocktail receptions.
  • Private Party Contact: Ann Mortenson (612) 332-1442 x11

  • Wolfgang Puck Grille is a contemporary version of the classic 'bar & grill' and features modern American cuisine accentuating Wolfgang Puck's trademark standards for creating incomparable tastes using fresh, seasonal, all-natural and organic ingredients. The restaurant features casual as well as sophisticated fine dining in a spectacular setting. Wolfgang Puck Grille offers breakfast, lunch and dinner every day of the week.

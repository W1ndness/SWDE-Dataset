Judges’ Hill is a wonderfully unexpected setting for private dinner parties, meetings and events. Regardless of size, formality or budget we can weave a tapestry of simple elegance into your function. We offer over 8,600 square feet of private dining, meeting and pre-function space which our highly-trained, professional staff can transform to create the ambiance you desire whether you are a party of 2 or one of 250. Our managers and catering professionals are here to guide you through the planning and menu selection process. Together with our awarding-winning chefs, they will provide a customized proposal based on your specific needs and requests. Our meeting and event space offers a feeling of warmth and luxury that isn’t easily found elsewhere. Your guests will enjoy a unique setting far from the ordinary Austin hotel ballroom. A serene oasis in the heart of downtown Austin, the Mansion’s location makes it the ideal choice for your next event.

BonCaldo

writes...
  • We at BonCaldo feel our menu is truly unique among Italian Restaurants. This is because we are dedicated to featuring authentic offerings from all the regions of Italy. The rich aroma and flavors of authentic open wood fired grilling, fresh made pasta, and homemade desserts will make you feel as if you've not just dined out for the evening, but embarked on a voyage to Italy.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Norwood
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.boncaldo.com
  • Email: boncaldo@beld.net

  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:30am - 3:30pm

  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa

  • Dress Code: Business Casual

  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: up to 150 cars
  • Catering: BonCaldo Catering offers our guest the opportunity to have the same hand created Italian specialties from our main menu, at the location of your choice. Our Catering Menu offers many selections which are available for both delivery & pick-up. If you are planning any affair from 2 to 300 guests, BonCaldo can accommodate your every need including set-up, servers, bartenders, rentals and more. Please call for more information.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Special Event Program at BonCaldo can accommodate groups from 10 to 85 guests. BonCaldo offers both private & semi-private space for any type of event. We specialize in large group dining, providing an extensive selection of menu choices, an award winning wine list; and exceptional service and professionalism. Please contact Special Event Manager, Adam Laliberte for booking availability.
  • Private Party Contact: Joe Boncaldo (781) 255-5800

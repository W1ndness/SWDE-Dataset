Fresco Italian Cafe

writes...
  • Fresco Italian Café offers dine dining Italian cuisine, adding fresh herbs & seasonal ingredients lightly accenting their sauces & dressings, all complimented by exceptional attentive service. Fresco Italian Café features a daily selection of risotto not to be missed! In warmer months, the candlelit patio is a lovely setting for the delicious food.

Portabella's Sports Bar & Grille

writes...
  • Portabella’s Sports Bar & Grille blends great food, fine spirits & good times in what can only be described as a modern & edgy but conservative restaurant. Portabella’s boast a sophisticated, yet unassuming crowd, a spacious bar & a private dining area for special occasions. The versatility of our location is ideal for a power business breakfast or lunch, a casual after work get together or a late night gathering with friends. As our name states sports fans, don’t worry, we have you covered as large plasma screens broadcast all the big games. Outdoor patio seating is available seasonally. We are located right outside of Middletown in Londonderry Township, conveniently off the TollHouse Road exit on Route 283 & only minutes from Harrisburg International Airport.

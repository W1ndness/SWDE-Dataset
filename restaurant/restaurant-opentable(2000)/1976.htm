Piatti - Carmel

writes...
  • Piatti is a casually elegant restaurant featuring moderately priced regional Italian cuisine. Entrees include wood-fired pizzas, spit-roasted rotisserie chicken, fresh local fish and fine grilled meats. Enjoy dining by the cozy fireplace or on the rooftop, heated patio.

  • Cuisine: Italian

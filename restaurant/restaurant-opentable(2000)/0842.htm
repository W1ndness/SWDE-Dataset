    The Waterfront Restaurant's Main Dining Room is available for private dining and business receptions and evening events. Spectacular views provide a breathtaking experience for up to 100 guests, with a variety of seating options. Window walls surround guests on three sides, offering what is perhaps one of the most picturesque, if not unrivaled, views of the San Francisco skyline and the Bay.

Our Private Dining Facilities

  • Average Price $$$

    Total Capacity 450

Waterfront Restaurant is the perfect setting for your next private function. With options that range from a small dinner party to a stand-up reception for 450, The Waterfront provides their client a true dining experience featuring our Local sustainable food from Executive Chef Erik Hopfinger. Private party menus are located on our website. Our Main Dining Room is ideal for seated dining up to 100 and can be completely private. North Room seats up to 32 for private dining and 40 for stand-up reception. The Main Dining Room and the North Room features a view into the Bay Bridge and Treasure Island. Don't forget, our tented dining area is also available for sit-down or stand-up functions with view into the Bay Bridge and Treasure Island. The Tented Patio comfortably accommodate up to 100 and 150 people respectively. If you're looking for a great experience, Waterfront Restaurant can accommodate your every need.

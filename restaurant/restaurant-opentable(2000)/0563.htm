Bistro 333 - Hyatt Regency Milwaukee

writes...
  • Bistro 333 is truly a restaurant to call home, situated conveniently in the newly renovated Hyatt Regency in the heart of downtown Milwaukee. Bistro 333 serves breakfast, lunch and dinner with each menu specially hand crafted by our chefs to offer the finest cuisine of the state and region while combining the best of urban chic and casual comfort. Bistro 333’s warm decor invites you to experience top quality and unassuming fare that you might find in the home kitchens of Wisconsinites everywhere. Whether it is cozying up to Bar 333 and enjoying one of our many freshly prepared cocktails, sitting in the spacious lobby area and enjoying a bottle of wine from our hand-picked selection, or enjoying a nice quiet dinner in our private dining area, the staff at Bistro/Bar 333 is eager to accommodate every need. Come in today for the hottest dining experience in Milwaukee!

L'Amore

writes...
  • L'Amore Restaurant is a family owned and operated neighborhood Italian restaurant located in the heart of Phoenix. We offer fine dining with a family atmosphere. We personally operate the restaurant being there to meet and greet each guest as if they were entering our own home. Here's a secret - we are told we have the best Cioppino in town.

    Enjoy the formal dining room, the friendly bar with great, live, soft music from our musician Jimmy who plays on Thursday - Saturday night, or sit in our beautiful tree-lined patio over-looking the Phoenix Mountain Preserves. We're happy to serve your every need. If you're on your way home, call us for a take-out dinner, or if you're thinking of a holiday party, set up an appointment to speak to us about our catering.

  • Public Transit: City buses are available.

  • Catering: We can tailor any occasion. Our specialty is designing an event to meet your needs. From a small house party, to a large holiday event or rehearsal dinner, we are experts at helping you make your catering event one that will be talked about for years to come.

We will custom design an event that you will never forget. Ask us for an appointment to sit down and design your private party! Lets start having some FUN!

Pasquale's

writes...
  • Pasquale’s Ristorante Italiano is one of Edison’s favorite family restaurants. We offer our guests classic Italian cuisine in an elegant yet casual ambience. Chef Pasquale uses only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to make his signature dishes. Whether you come to enjoy a Panini or our Risotto of the day, our knowledgeable staff will assist you in finding the perfect wine to accompany your meal. We are sure to have something to please every member of your party in our bar, lounge or dining room. Please see our website for our Happy Hour “Wine Down” specials and Family Style Sunday dinners; www.pasqualeristoranteitaliano.com. We look forward to having you as our guest!

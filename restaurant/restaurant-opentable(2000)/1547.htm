La Galleria 33

writes...
  • Delectable creations, prepared in an open kitchen, deliver Italy's best flavors in a stylish dining room that faces Salem Street. The friendly staff and romantic atmosphere will keep you coming back time and time again.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, European
  • Neighborhood: North End / Waterfront
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://LaGalleria33.com
  • Email: LaGalleria33@aol.com

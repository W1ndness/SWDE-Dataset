Ponzu

writes...
  • From small plates to large, Ponzu's food offers scrumptious dishes that bridge the gap between the exotic East and West. It's Asia without a map. Chinese, Japanese, Thai and Vietnamese ingredients blend with contemporary presentations to create dishes that are perfect to share. Dishes that typify the menu have bright, bold flavors with brilliant accents that will tantalize your taste buds from appetizers to desserts. The boldness of our menu pairs perfectly with our restaurant's décor. From small details to large, Ponzu's design captivates guests as much as the menu. Blue terrazzo floors with white polka dots. Low, undulating walls divide the dining room into cozy spaces. So let your senses be your guide. Try a little of this, sample a little of that. We encourage you to share so you all can experience as many different flavors as possible.

Our Private Dining Facilities

  • Average Price $$
  • |
    Total Capacity 150

Choose Ponzu for your next private party or meeting event in downtown San Francisco's Theater District. Located on the mezzanine level of the adjacent Serrano Hotel, Ponzu offers 3,000 square feet of flexible meeting and private reception space for all types of private parties and events, accommodating from 10 to 120 guests in style. Five flexible meeting rooms with 8-foot ceilings and their own lobby/pre-function area echo the sophisticated Spanish Colonial style of the hotel's lobby. All feature state-of-the-art telecommunications capabilities: they are wired for complimentary high-speed Internet access, real-time video conferencing, and multi-media presentations with the ability to run multiple computers on a single connection. And, of course, all our meeting rooms offer private dining menus from Ponzu.

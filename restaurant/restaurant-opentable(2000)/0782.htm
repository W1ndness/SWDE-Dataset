Enotria Restaurant

writes...
  • Enotria means 'Land of Wine'. The cuisine offered at Enotria Restaurant and Wine Bar is perhaps best described as California cuisine with focus on seasonality, local ingredients and influences from the Mediterranean. As one of Sacramento's youngest new chefs, Chef Anthony displays his unique talent with creative flavor profiles and well balanced dishes that pair deliciously with Enotria's award winning wine list.

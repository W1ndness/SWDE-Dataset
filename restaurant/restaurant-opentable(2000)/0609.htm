Ai Japanese Restaurant and Lounge

writes...
  • Ai Japanese Restaurant, the hottest sushi bar in River North, features Chicago’s only sake bar, offering sakes from various prefectures of Japan. A unique variety of sushi and sake flights are signatures to Ai. The softly lit lounge with a warm fireplace provides an intimate setting for a relaxing cocktail. Open for lunch Monday to Friday, and late night on Friday and Saturday. Carryout and delivery are welcome.

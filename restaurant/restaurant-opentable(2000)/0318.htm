The Vines European Cuisine at The Edelweiss

writes...
  • Located at the Edelweiss German Inn, The Vines is a casual fine dining restaurant. The menu highlights North Georgia's Wine Country inspired by recipes of the German, Italian and French wine regions as well as other parts of Europe. We feature locally grown produce and our chefs can be seen picking herbs from the garden to make your meal complete.

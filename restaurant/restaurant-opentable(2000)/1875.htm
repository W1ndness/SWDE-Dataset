The Pearl Room

writes...
  • Welcome to the Pearl Room a restaurant that specializes in seafood. We are open seven days a week for lunch and dinner. Garden dining is available for the spring and summer and our fireplace provides warmth in the fall and winter.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Continental
  • Neighborhood: Bay Ridge
  • Cross Street: 82nd and 3rd Avenue
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.thepearlroom.com/
  • Phone: (718) 833-6666
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Sunday - Saturday: 12:00pm -4:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday - Wednesday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Thursday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Fireplace, Full Bar, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: R to 86th Street
  • Parking: Valet
  • Catering: We provide a wide selection of appetizers, salads, pastas, entrees and desserts available by the half tray and full tray. Breads, napkins and utensils are included.
  • Private Party Facilities: We have several rooms available for use for private parties. Four different menus are available to choose from in addition to the fact that we can custom create a menu for you.
  • Private Party Contact: Kate Merlino (718) 833-6666

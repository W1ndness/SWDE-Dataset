Sargasso of Morgantown Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • Sargasso is North Central West Virginia's premier dining destination. Sargasso is a unique, upscale restaurant located in Morgantown, West Virginia. Sargasso presents Morgantown’s only “world fusion” restaurant, which incorporates influences from diverse cultures, Caribbean, French, Italian, Asian, and Continental cuisine. The menu changes frequently to keep up with culinary trends and the freshest ingredients. Our highly trained chefs come from diverse backgrounds bringing with them experiences from different cuisines, techniques, styles, and regions of the world. Working together, they represent a winning team with a creative perspective on the concept of world fusion cuisine.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Global, International, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Morgantown
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.dinesargasso.com
  • Email: steves@dinesargasso.com
  • Phone: (304) 554-0100
  • Hours of Operation: Brunch: Sunday: 10:30am - 2:00pm
    Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Wednesday: 5:30pm - 9:00pm, Thursday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Simon Poulin
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, View, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Catering: Sargasso will cater parties at the restaurant or at a location of your choosing. Within the restaurant, Sargasso has the ability to host parties for up to 200 people. Outside of the restaurant there is no limit on what Sargasso catering is capable of.
  • Private Party Facilities: Sargasso has three private rooms. The rooms can be reserved on any night and can accommodate up to 40 people. The rooms are equipped with flat screen televisions, which make them perfect for professional presentations or meetings. We also offer the option of renting out the entire restaurant for receptions, showers, birthdays, anniversaries, or any other special celebration.
  • Private Party Contact: Stephen Stoll (304) 554-0100
  • Entertainment: Sargasso occasionally hosts live musicians for weekend dining during the summer.

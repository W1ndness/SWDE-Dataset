The Tillerman

writes...
  • Welcome to the Tillerman Restaurant, a Las Vegas Landmark for over 20 years. We offer the freshest seafood available, sourced from all over the world. Our signature 1.5lb. select portion Alaskan King Crab legs, cracked tableside, will just melt in your mouth. We also have the finest Prime Filet Mignon, New York Strip, and our famous 24oz Blackened Bone in Ribeye to name just a few of our signature cuts. Our wine list is award winning and features many well known wines as well as some from smaller boutique wineries. The food and service are equaled only by the restaurant's unique interior and comfortable atmosphere. Join us at the Tillerman for an evening to remember

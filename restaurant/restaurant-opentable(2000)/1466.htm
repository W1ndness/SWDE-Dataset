  • Yujean Kang's Gourmet Chinese Cuisine offer the true essence of Chinese cuisine with finest ingredient from chef Yujean's own taste and experience. Since 1990, Yujean Kang's always been the top S. California Chinese by Zagat rating, also from many other highly respectable sources such as Los Angeles times, New York Times, Gourmet Magazine, and Wine Spectator magazine. A restaurant where we offer the most authentic Chinese preparation with the most updated food combination in a gourmet way. Pairing with personal selected wine selection from Chef Yujean's palate and served in a comfortable casual setting. We welcome you to Yujean Kang's Gourmet Chinese Cuisine.

Harborside Grill and Patio-Hyatt Harborside

writes...
  • Enjoy elegant surroundings, spectacular food and stunning city skyline views through floor-to-ceiling windows when you dine at our Boston waterfront restaurant. Featuring sumptuous bistro and New England seafood specialties. Harborside Grill and Patio is open for breakfast, lunch, dinner and cocktails. Bask in Boston’s warm weather pleasures on the expansive outdoor patio. As you enjoy breezy al fresco dining, festive tiki torches, plush couches surrounding intimate fire pits – ideal for cozying up during a romantic evening out. During the summer months we shake things up with live entertainment on Thursday and Friday evenings. You"ll be sure to find tempting options for every occasion, including holiday dinners on Thanksgiving, New Year's Eve, Valentine's Day, Easter and Mother's Day. After dining, you can take the water taxi to almost anywhere across Boston Harbor from the restaurant.

Francesca's Bellezza

writes...
  • Nestled in the quaint downtown shopping district of the Village of La Grange, Francesca's Bellezza opened its doors to guests in March of 2009. As the 17th addition to the Chicago-based Francesca’s Restaurants group, Francesca’s Bellezza offers guests “simple rustic cooking, friendly service and fair prices”; a winning recipe for Francesca’s since 1992. Their menu features the zesty, earthy cuisine of Rome and the surrounding areas of Tuscany, Umbria and Lazio. A carefully chosen collective of wines and several signature martini’s make the perfect accompaniment to an outstanding dining experience. Italian for beauty, Francesca's Bellezza lives up to its name featuring an intricately designed bar, lofty ceilings and a quaint trattoria feel. Striking photographs of rural Italian life by Paul Elledge line the walls to create a comfortable, urban setting throughout.

Our dining room can accommodate up to 80 guests in a semi-private setting and is the perfect spot for social events and company outings where complete privacy is not required. For group events of 14 guests or more, we encourage you to dine in the traditional Italian style of “Tavoli di Amici” or “Table of Friends”. This concept creates a lively and interactive meal, offering an eclectic selection of flavors spanning four to five courses. An extensive selection of wines and hourly beverage packages are available for group events. Our event planners are able to assist you in selecting a menu and beverages to compliment your budget. Group events are available 7 days a week and both lunch and dinner, including Sunday lunches!

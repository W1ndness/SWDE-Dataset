Strip

writes...
  • Strip Restaurant is Chef Tom Catherall's newest dining experience in Atlanta's new Atlantic Station. Strip offer a menu of Steaks, Sushi and Teasers. Strip also offers multi-levels of dining and lounge area. Strip has several outdoor patio areas.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Steak, Sushi
  • Neighborhood: Midtown
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.heretoserverestaurants.com
  • Phone: (404) 385-2005
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 4:00pm, Saturday & Sunday: 12:00pm - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:00pm - 12:00am, Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: David Silverman
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Dancing, Entertainment, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wine
  • Public Transit: Public transits stop about 2 blocks away
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: We also offer valet parking from 11:30a.m-2pm Monday-Friday and dinner valet Mon-Thurs 5pm-11pm. Atlantic Station offers some metered street parking. Most of the parking is located below.
  • Catering: We have a catering company. It is called Here to Cater.(404) 364-9360
  • Private Party Facilities: Yes, we have a private dining room that seats 40 people.

Sambuca

writes...
  • At Sambuca, we serve Southern Italian Food the old fashioned way -“Family Style”. Our generous portions are served on platters so everyone can share in the delicious fun. The wonderful aromas and mouth watering flavors will remind you of what Italian food is all about. So when you are thinking of a place go for dinner with 1,2 or 200 of your friends, family or business associates, think of Sambuca! Come and join us at Sambuca and let "Our family serve your family"!

  • Cuisine: Italian, Steak, Seafood

  • Cross Street: between Central Park West and Columbus Ave

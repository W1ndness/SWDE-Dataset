Genoa Restaurant

writes...
  • Genoa combines its legendary past with a fresh, updated style. Long-time fans of Genoa will be happy to see that the restaurant maintains its remarkable tradition of world-class food served in a cozy and unpretentious atmosphere. Newcomers will find traditional Italian food expertly prepared with the finest ingredients, and served with warmth and grace.

    We invite you to experience an updated, five-course prix fixe dinner in our beautifully remodeled dining room. Chef David Anderson brings his own fine sensibility to interpreting the classic Italian fare that made Genoa famous.

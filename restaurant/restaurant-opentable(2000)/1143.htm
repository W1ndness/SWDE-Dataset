Ginger Bistro

writes...
  • The Ginger Asian Bistro (Ginger) is a full-service restaurant located at the Delmar Loop in St. Louis, Missouri. The restaurant is recognized for diverse menus, distinctive architectural design, and exceptional value that create a unique dining experience for our guests.

    We provide full menu of healthy gourmet food infused with Pan-Asian, French, and North American cooking techniques. Our chefs are trained to produce distinctive entrees such as the prime Australian beef tenderloin with Gorgonzola almond crust, wasabi-smashed potatoes, and lamb T-bone with sweet potato Dauphin Oise that our guests come to enjoy nowhere else.

    We also offer elegant private and executive rooms for our guests to accommodate many social and business occasions.

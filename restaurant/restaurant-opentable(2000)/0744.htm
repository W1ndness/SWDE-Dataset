Sullivan's Steakhouse - Charlotte

writes...
  • Sullivan's is a contemporary American steakhouse offering the finest high-end choice beef, select seafood, pork, lamb and chicken entrees, appetizers, soups, salads and side dishes. Desserts are made in house daily. Sullivan's has an extensive wine list and a complete inventory of bourbon, single malt scotch and cognac. Sullivan's features the finest steaks and seafood, unparalleled martinis, and great live jazz.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Seafood, American
  • Neighborhood: Southend
  • Cross Street: Tremont Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://sullivansteakhouse.com
  • Email: sullivans.charlotte@steakco.com
  • Phone: (704) 335-8228
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Friday: 5:30pm - 11:00pm, Saturday: 5:30pm- 11:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Jonathan Leary
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cigar Room, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area, Smoking Area (bar only), Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: The Lynx Line runs directly behind our restaurant. There is a stop at East/West Blvd and we are a 1/2 block south on the left.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary Valet, and a parking deck
  • Catering: Sullivan's provides many options for parties ranging from 10 to 250 people. We offer pre-set lunch menus from $14-$27 per person, featuring sandwiches, soups, salads, entrees and desserts. Our dinner menus offer many choices including Filet Mignon, Bone-In Kansas City Strip, and Chilean Sea Bass and range from $34-$75 per person. We also offer a full appetizer menu for cocktail parties and happy hours.
  • Private Party Facilities: Please inquire for details.
  • Private Party Contact: Mandi Bane (704) 335-8228
  • Entertainment: Live Jazz Six Nights A Week!
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Swingin' at Sully's every Thursday night. Featuring live jazz, $5 bar entrees, specialty martini list is $5, including the Knockout, and $5 wine features.

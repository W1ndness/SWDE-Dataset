The Heritage Restaurant - The Inn at Gig Harbor

writes...
  • The award winning Heritage Restaurant at the INN at Gig Harbor serves American fare with a Northwest flair 7 days a week between 7am and 9pm. Decorated in a wood rich classic craftsman style, The Heritage offers a refined, yet not stuffy, family friendly environment. The Heritage Restaurant offers both traditional Pacific Northwest favorites as well as seasonal specials which are sure to please everyone's palate and pocketbook. In addition to The Heritage Restaurant, The INN at Gig Harbor also offers 4,000 square feet of flexible banquet space which offers the same great food and beverage selections for your next event. Whether you have need for an intimate dinner for two, a family meal for twelve or an event for upwards of 250, The Heritage Restaurant and The INN at Gig Harbor would be honored to be at your service.

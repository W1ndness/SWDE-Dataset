Acorn Restaurant

writes...
  • Opened in the Winter of 2002, The Acorn Restaurant burst onto the Nashville dining scene & was voted "The Best New Restaurant in Nashville" by a Nashville Reader's Poll. Since then, The Acorn has been consistently voted as one of Nashville's Best Restaurants & Bars for food, service and atmosphere. Independently owned & operated by a Vanderbilt Alumnus, The Acorn is conveniently located in the heart of the West End District near the university & many fine hotels. Through our atmosphere, service & food, we strive to be a purveyor of welcome, warmth & hospitality for all who enter our doors. We look forward to seeing you soon!

  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Private lot across the street and on street parking available
  • Private Party Facilities: Conveniently located in Nashville's West End, near Vanderbilt, downtown & many of Nashville's finest hotels, The Acorn provides two private dining rooms for private reservation. The Parthenon Room, with views of Centennial Park, can comfortably seat approximately 40 people & can be reserved at any time for your next private function or gathering. The Fountain Room, our climate controlled outdoor patio with views of 28th Avenue North & our fountain, can comfortably seat as many as 80 people. Larger parties can be accommodated. We are sure you'll find The Acorn to be the perfect setting for your next function or party.

Since 1997, BOTTINO has been at the center of New York's West Chelsea art scene, offering contemporary and classic Tuscan cuisine in a cool mid-century modern interior. With three private spaces available--- including a beautiful urban garden --- BOTTINO is a perfect setting for showers, rehearsal dinners, gallery opening dinners, small weddings, or any special event, with private spaces available for 18 - 80 guests, or a total capacity of 150 +. Situated on Tenth Avenue between 24th and 25th Sts, BOTTINO is easily accessible to your guests coming from outside of Manhattan. BOTTINO offers several prix fixe menu options as well as cocktail party menus, but we are always happy to tailor-make a menu to suit your taste and to accommodate your budget.

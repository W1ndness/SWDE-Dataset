Ebbitt Room

writes...
  • The award winning Ebbitt Room restaurant is nestled within the stylish Virginia Hotel on Jackson Street in the heart of Cape May's historic district. One of Cape May's most popular & famous restaurants, The Ebbitt Room serves inventive contemporary American cuisine from Chef Lucas Manteca in a rich elegant setting designed by Colleen Bashaw Interior Designs of New York. In addition to its regular a la carte menu, The Ebbitt Room offers nightly pre fixe menu's showcasing the best of what each season has to offer. Their wine list, the recipient of numerous Wine Spectator awards features selections from all the major wine regions of the world with an emphasis on boutique & cult producers.

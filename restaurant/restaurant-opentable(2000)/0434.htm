35th Street Bistro

writes...
  • The 35th Street Bistro is a delightful country European restaurant located in the heart of Seattle's Fremont neighborhood, featuring tastefully prepared food from fresh and sustainably grown ingredients, an extensive list of hand-picked wines, and a full bar. "...a great neighborhood bistro." — June 2006 Seattle Times

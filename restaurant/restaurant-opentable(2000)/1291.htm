AKASHA

writes...
  • AKASHA restaurant/bar/bakery features New American cuisine offering comfort food with big flavors and sustainable ingredients, for carnivores and herbivores alike. Chef/Caterer Akasha Richmond brings visionary cuisine to her own neighborhood eatery located in the historic Hull Building in downtown Culver City. Keeping the principles of sustainability in mind, the building's original steel, wood, concrete and brick arches were restored while the design materials pair with a seventies modernity to exude an inviting and lively atmosphere. The artisan bakery has both sweet and savory selections, as well as locally roasted fair trade coffee. The 20-seat bar highlights fresh juice cocktails, organic and premium spirits, beer and wine. The entire menu is available for catering or to go.

Our Private Dining Facilities

  • Average Price $$$
  • |
    Total Capacity 300

AKASHA offers private dining in our Cafe Room; a warm inviting semi-private (screened off) space that can seat up to 30 guests for a sit down dinner or up to 45 guests for a cocktail party. Dining options include a 3 or 4 course prix-fixe menu that starts at $45 per person, plus tax and gratuity. The Café Room is the perfect space for social business mixers, book signings, product launches, and holiday parties. Our cozy street side heated patio can also seat up 30 guests for a sit down dinner and up 45 for a cocktail party. We are happy to design custom themed menus and cocktails, and beer or wine tasting dinners. The entire restaurant is available for buyouts (capacity 300). AKASHA is also a catering company, and we can handle all aspects of event planning, including live music, rentals, flowers, décor, valet parking and more.

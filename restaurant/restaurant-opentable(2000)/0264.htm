CRU Urban Lounge

writes...
  • Come visit Atlanta's best kept secret: a modern loft transformed into a hip neighborhood bar. Inspired by the Manhattan lounge scene, CRÜ offers an elegant setting where you can indulge in an array of eclectic wines, ultra-premium liquors, and international beers. Enter CRÜ and your senses are stimulated by the glow of candles, the warmth of fabric and leather, and the lush sounds of trip hop music. Travel the world without leaving the city. Experience a glass of chilled sake for the first time, or try the best Mojito north of Cuba. Sample our gourmet appetizers and desserts while unwinding with a glass of wine, or get the night started with one of our original martinis. Have your own unique drink in mind? No problem…just ask for it. We’re here to make your experience special. Finally, a lounge as diverse and interesting as you are...CRÜ.

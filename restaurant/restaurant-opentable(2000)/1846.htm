Montenapo

writes...
  • Montenapo is a new restaurant in the New York Theater District located in the New York Times Building, overlooking Renzo Piano's breathtaking birch tree garden. Named after Milan's most fashionable Avenue, Via Montenapoleone, Montenapo brings the glamour of Milan and authentic cuisine from all regions of Italy to New York City. Brilliantly situated at the intersection of Fashion Avenue, Times Square and the Broadway Theatre district, Montenapo provides an exquisite culinary experience for important business lunches, romantic dinners, and extraordinary celebrations. We are passionately committed to creating each meal thoughtfully from the finest products, including grass fed beef, free range poultry and wild caught fish as well as a full range of seasonal grains, fruits and vegetables, homemade pastas, breads and desserts. Montenapo also features a spacious, stately bar area

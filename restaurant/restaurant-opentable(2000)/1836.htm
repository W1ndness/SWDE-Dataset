McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - New York

writes...
  • Bringing the freshest seafood to New York City, McCormick & Schmick's Seafood Restaurant, in Midtown Manhattan, is located close to Radio City Music Hall, the Theater District and famous 5th Avenue Shopping. It is a great place for pre-theater or after shopping dining. We serve more than 30 varieties of fresh seafood flown in daily from around the globe, available from menus that change DAILY and is THE place to socialize in the best of traditions. Prepared using fresh ingredients, your food is served in a timeless and traditional presentation. Enjoy the spirited and lively atmosphere of our bar after a show and enjoy watching our bartenders practice traditional mixology, making hand-crafted cocktails using fresh-squeezed juices, never using blenders or mixes.

McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Irvine

writes...
  • Now celebrating its 17th year, McCormick & Schmick's brings the freshest seafood to Irvine. Featuring up to 30 species daily, with emphasis on new and often exotic species from Hawaii, New Zealand and the Northwest. Taken from the purest waters and prepared in traditional fashion, the kitchen showcases the superior natural flavors of all the seafood served at the restaurant.

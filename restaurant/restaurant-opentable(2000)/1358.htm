The Greek at the Harbor

writes...
  • On the water's edge in beautiful Ventura Harbor, The Greek at the Harbor offers a "dining experience" rather than just a meal. Open daily for lunch and dinner, Saturday and Sunday for a full breakfast and Sunday Champagne Brunch, The Greek is a great location for family and business gatherings. Menu selections vary from traditional Greek moussaka and specialty lamb dishes, to "made to order pastas", fresh seafood, Saganaki flaming cheese, delicious entree salads and many charbroiled items including New York steak. The Greek is also family friendly with a kid's menu and coloring activity sheet. The great food and atmosphere is only part of The Greek at the Harbor dining experience. Belly Dancers perform evenings and by arrangement as do the Greek Dancers. The Greek specializes in parties from 20-50 and a variety of group menu selections are available. Inside seating capacity is 150 and patio capacity is 125. Full Bar

  • Catering: We offer a catering menu for selections. Party platters to full dining service available.
  • Private Party Facilities: Inside dining seats up to 150 guests, and is available for all or part. Minimums are required for Friday and Saturday exclusive use of inside or patio areas. Patio area is divided by a drop down curtain and seats up to 65 on each side. Most groups of 20-50 are seated in a section, rather than completely private, due to the entertainment. More flexibility is available on week nights to accommodate guests requests. We do a lot of business and private groups as well as rehearsal dinners. We also can handle wedding parties and offer several packages for beach ceremonies and receptions.

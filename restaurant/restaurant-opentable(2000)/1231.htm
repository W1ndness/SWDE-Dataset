Avanti

writes...
  • Avanti, a Valley tradition since 1974. . . Dramatic, crisp black and white interiors, candlelight and a quiet atmosphere. Italian favorites with a Mediterranean influence. Vegetarians and Seafood lovers will be awed by the selections of homemade pasta from deliciously light Gnocchi Alfredo, Eggplant and Meat Lasagna, to seasonal hand crafted Ravioli. Fresh Fish and seafood dishes vary- from a Jumbo Shrimp Cocktail to an Italian crafted Cioppino. Avanti has also garnered numerous awards and accolades for our Ossobucco, said to be the best in town as well as robust steaks and chops (lamb and pork). Afterwards,enjoy dessert and Cappuccino in the piano bar sweets that are carefully guarded family recipes include, Tiramisu, Cannoli, Creme Brulee with a crunchy burnt sugar crust and Spanish inspired Flan that has a creamy custard caramelized top but if your passionate about chocolate the chocolate mousse is infused with rum and whipped so fluffy and light you'll swear it's heaven sent!

Facilities 1 of 4
  • Main Dining Room

    The ambiance is quiet, candle lit, romantic & nostalgic, yet upbeat; and refined without being pretentious. The dramatic black & white interior is accented with a zebra motif throughout the restaurant.

    Capacity
    175125
  • Patio Room

    The Patio Room is located at the front of the restaurant and is separated from the Main Dining Room with a wall of windows flanked by flowing draperies and privacy shades. Stunning wall colors and modern art create a festive ambiance.    This room is perfect for audio/visual presentations and has a remote control screen, as well as internet connection and phone line. It is completely private with a door which facilitates business meetings/presentations.

    Capacity
    7550
  • Piccolo Room

    An intimate room with subdued colored walls & eclectic artwork, is situated in the back of the restaurant. The Piccolo Room has access to the internet and a dedicated phone line.    We can provide a complimentary screen for any audio/visual presentations.

    Capacity
    5030
  • Piano Lounge

    For a more casual setting, The Piano Lounge offers a variety of seating options and dining options. There are tufted booths around the entire perimeter and a dance floor, if needed. The zebra motif is carried throughout this room, with small cocktail tables scattered throughout the seating area.    This room is great for buffet-type functions and casual hors d'oeuvres. It is often used by groups for their appetizers & drinks prior to their formal dinner.

    Capacity

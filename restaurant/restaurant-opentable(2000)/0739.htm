Omaha Steakhouse

writes...
  • Omaha Steaks’ award winning tradition of excellence spans almost 80 years. The company’s reputation as a premium purveyor and marketer of steaks, meats and other gourmet foods is built on the commitment to superior products as well as service to our customers. In 1952, the Simons began to sell their products by mail, and thus was born a new name for the company...Omaha Steaks International. Omaha Steaks selects only the finest quality corn-fed beef from the Midwest. Since 1996, Omaha Steaks (through a licensing agreement) has teamed with Culinaire Intl. to provide an unparalleled dining experience...The Omaha Steakhouse! We offer a unique comfortable atmosphere, where the dining experience communicates quality, authenticity and most of all…a memorable experience. Of course, all the beef we serve is tender, Midwest corn-fed beef from Omaha Steaks.

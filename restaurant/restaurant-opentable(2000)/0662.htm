Kamehachi of Tokyo

writes...
  • Newest upscale location of Chicago's oldest Japanese restaurant and sushi bar. Serene and distinctive setting featuring wide variety of sushi and kitchen dishes. Beautiful bamboo-birch panels, bamboo waterfalls, and private shoji dining rooms. Other locations in Old Town and Northwest Suburbs.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Sushi, Japanese, Asian
  • Neighborhood: Gold Coast / Streeterville
  • Cross Street: Fairbanks
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.kamehachi.com

  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm

Nokomis Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • Nokomis Restaurant and Bar is a stones throw away from Lake Superior on the Scenic North Shore Drive between Duluth and Two Harbors. The progressive cuisine changes daily and with the seasons as owner and chef Sean Lewis shares his passion for flavor and the possibilities of food. Nokomis Restaurant and Bar is a fine dining experience that can be shared and enjoyed under Lake Superior moonlight.

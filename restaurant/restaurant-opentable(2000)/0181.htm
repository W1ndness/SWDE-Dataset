writes...
  • There is nothing which has yet been contrived by man, by which so much happiness is produced as by a good tavern." -Samuel Johnson Throughout time, taverns have been synonymous with Philadelphia. They were the social hub of the neighborhood; a place for men and women from all walks of life to meet, eat, drink, toast, sing & argue. The concept is timeless, the story inspiring, the room inviting, the people irresistible.... Welcome to Tavern 17. This stunning restaurant and bar in the Rittenhouse Square district of the city offers an innovative menu described as Artisan Flights and Bites.From 5pm - 7pm Monday through Friday is Happy "17:00" Hour featuring $2 Cheeseburger Sliders, $3 Drafts, $4 Cocktail, $5 Wine & $6 Snow Crabs (8-10oz). There is a wine centric beverage menu featuring over 45 glasses of white or red wines. Two private dining rooms, outside dining and a great brew menu complement the restaurant well. No reservations for outside seating accept.

Our Private Dining Facilities

    Total Capacity 120

Tavern 17 provides a fresh alternative venue for that special event or celebration you are planning. It is the perfect atmosphere for rehearsal dinners, engagement parties, birthday and anniversary dinners and networking events. Tavern 17's private dining rooms can also be utilized for small meetings and business luncheons. Two Private Dining Rooms await you and your guests at Tavern 17! Accommodations: Rooms can be arranged for stand up receptions, sit down dinners at one table or in small groupings for 6 - 8 at a table. Design your own Menu: Pre-Select your favorites from the Tavern 17 Menu – up to 4 salads or appetizers and 4 entrée plates. Your tables will be set with pre-printed menus from which your guests may select the evening of the event. Or let the Tavern 17 team suggest a reception menu and their servers will offer menu samplings during the course of your event.

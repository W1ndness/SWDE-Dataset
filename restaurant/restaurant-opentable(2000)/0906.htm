Finnegan's Marin

writes...
  • Offering lunch and dinner nightly, along with a sumptuous Saturday and Sunday brunch, “Finnegan’s Marin” will welcome all seeking a reprieve from the day. Catch a game on one of our TVs, schedule a business lunch, bringing the family down for an early dinner, enjoying a drink at a bar with an old friend (or a new one!), or perhaps enjoying the atmosphere at our 35 foot remodeled bar later in the evening, Finnegan’s will harken to the day.. a place.. where and when life was easier, slower, and friends and family spent more of their time enjoying each other’s company than planning what to do or where to go next. Sit back and get comfortable.

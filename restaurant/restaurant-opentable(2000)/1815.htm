Lua

  • Lua Restaurant, located on historic Sinatra Drive, overlooks the breathtaking skyline of Manhattan. Our Latin inspired menu is the perfect compliment to our prime, waterfront location. Lua Restaurant offers a 150 seat dining room, a spacious 360 degree bar area with a private VIP lounge. Weather permitting, join us in our 80 seat outdoor café.

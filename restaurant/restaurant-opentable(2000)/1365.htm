Hamakaze Sushi & Izakaya

writes...
  • We are serving fine sushi and Izakaya cuisine. Fresh sushi is done by Chef Nick Nishi well known local chef in the area. Izakaya is like French bistro or Spanish tapas. All comes in small portions so our guest can enjoy variety of items. A lot of vegetarian dishes are available. We also have Bento Happy hour 5pm ~7pm daily. Our bento has 1 main item and a lots of bite sized Izakaya items to let you experience our offerings. Bento box also come with miso soup and bowl of rice. We make our own infused sushi dipping soy sauce and salt to satisfy gourmet sushi fins. Sake selection is great and we make infused fruit sake here. We have SAKE TASTING on Mon~Thur after 9pm to close. You get to choose 3-2 oz shot out of 17 different sake for $ 10. Sake tasting is not only good deal but a fun and good learning event for you.

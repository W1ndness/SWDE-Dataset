Leatherby's Cafe Rouge

writes...
  • Located in the newly constructed Renee and Henry Segerstrom Concert Hall, Leatherby's Cafe Rouge is an exciting new dining destination in Orange County. The intimate, high-backed banquettes surrounded by a sea of earth tones provide a serene setting, perfect for lunch gatherings, lively dinners, pre- and post-performance dining, or simply for a great dining experience (WE ARE ALWAYS OPEN - SHOW or NO SHOW!). Feast your senses on the luxurious flavors and artful presentations that are the hallmark of Chef Joachim Splichal at his newest restaurant.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Costa Mesa
  • Cross Street: Avenue of the Arts
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.patinagroup.com/cafeRouge/
  • Email: lcrouge@patinagroup.com
  • Phone: (714) 429-7640
  • Hours of Operation: Tuesday - Saturday from 5 PM - 11 PM and Sunday from 4 PM - 10 PM. Stop by before the show for Executive Chef Ross Pangilinan’s pre-theater menu, after the show for amazing desserts, or any evening for dinner, bar bites, or a glass of wine.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ross Pangilinan
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Chef's Table, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Valet parking is available on Town Center Drive. For self-parking, the Plaza Tower Parking Structure at Anton and Park Center Drive is most convenient to the restaurant.
  • Catering: Joachim Splichal’s Patina Catering offers unrivaled cuisine and impeccable service for events held throughout Orange County and Southern California. Our experienced staff can help you create the perfect event at one of our landmark locations, including the Orange County Performing Arts Center, or at your home, office, or favorite destination. Contact Ruth Davies at 714-540-0500
  • Private Party Facilities: The restaurant e offers an array of exquisite locations for private events. Within the restaurant, private dining rooms can seat groups from 10 to 40, while larger events can be held in the main dining room. Additional locations are located throughout the Orange County Performing Arts Center, or at your home, office, or favorite destination.
  • Private Party Contact: Stacey Brady (714) 429-7657

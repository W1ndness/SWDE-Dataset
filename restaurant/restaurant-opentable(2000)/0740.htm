Pewter Rose Bistro

writes...
  • Thank you for your interest in our restaurant and nightclub. We have been serving friends, family and guests for over 20 years. Our location is less than one mile from the uptown business district; in the heart of historic South End and the Dilworth neighborhood. Plus we have a stop on The Light Rail! The restaurant and nightclub occupy the second floor of a turn-of-the-century textile warehouse. Its original massive wood beams and soaring 30-foot ceilings, flanked front and back by walls of French paned glass provide a unique home for two of Charlotte's most popular establishments. Chef Bragg and staff offer menus that are "Internationally- American," drawing on the diverse, ethnic and cultural heritage of the United States - relating it to the palate of the region. Local produce, fresh seafood, poultry, beef, pork, game meats, and vegetarian items are all interpreted in unique yet "comfortable" ways.

We pride ourselves on custom crafting menus to your guests' specific tastes, needs and budget. Our goal is to provide you a worry-free event; and your guests' a memorable time with great food and refreshing beverages. We typically suggest large groups (over 75) be served butler-style as a heavy hors d'oeurves and cocktail party. In general you could expect the final, "out-the-door" price, including alcohol, taxes and gratuities to be between $50 and $70 per person. Lesser priced menus can also be arranged. Either way we have found ways to "stretch" your budget dollar to offer you and your guests an event you will all fondly remember. Should you have a smaller party, fewer than 75 guests, we would suggest our Tutto Mondo night club and martini bar located next door as an alternative venue.

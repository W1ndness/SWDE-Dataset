Cork Tree Restaurant

writes...
  • Cork Tree Restaurant is built around California products. Local ingredients are used whenever possible and wines of the West Coast, particularly California, are featured. Chef Herve Glin and his staff bring creative and exciting items to his menu.

    The wine list is always a work in progress, with a balance of well known wineries, as well as boutique wineries.

    There is a wonderful patio for dining under the stars in the beautiful Coachella Valley. Additionally, depending on the size of your celebration, two private dining options await.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Californian, Contemporary French
  • Neighborhood: Palm Desert
  • Cross Street: Cook Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.thecorktree.com/
  • Email: info@thecorktree.com
  • Phone: (760) 779-0123
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Herve Glin
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Chef's Table, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: There is ample public parking for the restaurant.
  • Private Party Facilities: We have two private dining options at the Cork Tree. There is the intimate Chef’s Table which seats 6-8 people comfortably, as well as the Meritage Room, a private room overlooking our beautiful patio that seats up to 30.
  • Private Party Contact: Kathy Hernandez (760) 779-0123
  • Entertainment: Currently, there is entertainment on Friday, Saturday and Monday nights. Please call for details.

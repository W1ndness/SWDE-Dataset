Bistro LQ

writes...
  • “Best of LA 2009 -Los Angeles Magazine number 2 Best New LA Restaurant 2009" Join us with a menu of complex recipes that exploit and enhance the natural tastes and textures of dishes such as sea urchin tapioca pudding with yuzu kocho. What would Bistro LQ be without main courses of lamb, sweet bread and Cote De Boeuf, to name just a few? Our wine list reflects a passion for the vine and the hope for discovery - the world of wine has never been more exciting! This is just a hint of the eclectic cuisine that awaits you at Bistro LQ.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: French, Californian, Fusion / Eclectic
  • Neighborhood: Beverly / Fairfax / La Brea / Third St.
  • Cross Street: Fairfax
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.bistrolq.com
  • Email: bistrolq@gmail.com
  • Phone: (323) 951-1088
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Tuesday - Thursday: 6:00pm - 9:30pm, Friday - Saturday: 6:00pm - 10:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Laurent Quenioux
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Chef's Table, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Smoking Area (patio only), Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet Parking and ample street parking nearby.
  • Catering: Let Bistro LQ Catering help you celebrate the big milestones in your life! We can accommodate parties in-house of 50-70 people, and off-site for up to 300-400 guests. With advanced notice, the restaurant can be available for your private party on Sundays and Mondays, when we are usually closed. Please contact our Catering Director at (323) 951-1088 for more information.
  • Private Party Facilities: Contact our Director of Catering for details about Bistro LQ's private party facilities.
  • Private Party Contact: Eric Bouty (323) 951-1088
  • Entertainment: Bistro LQ wishes to tantalize all the senses of our guests, and the presentation of the meal is only one component of the perfect dining experience. Every three months, one of the walls at Bistro LQ will be dedicated to featuring the work of an artist we love. Please come in and enjoy the work of our current guest artist, Johnny Taylor.

SR24

writes...
  • Located in the Temescal Triangle, SR24 is a classic American eatery, with a consciousness for sustainability and the community from chef Josh Woodall and friends. Our menu features comfort food with a modern twist and our wine list is adventurous and affordable. We offer Happy Hour every day from 3-6.30pm with a selection of bar bites and discounted drinks.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Comfort Food
  • Neighborhood: Oakland
  • Cross Street: 51st Street
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.sr24food.com
  • Email: info@sr24food.com
  • Phone: (510) 655-9300
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:00am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 6:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 6:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Josh Woodall
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Counter Seating, Farm to Table, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: The closest BART stop is MacArthur station. SR24 is a ten minute walk up Telegraph Ave.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: There are 25 spaces in the Temescal Triangle parking lot which is located in front of SR24. You may park here free of charge for up to 2 hours. We also have an abundance of both metered and non-metered street parking surrounding our location.
  • Entertainment: We feature an assortment of soundless films; anything from vintage silent films and American classics, to visually stunning modern favorites.

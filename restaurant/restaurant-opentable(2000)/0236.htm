The Melting Pot - La Jolla

writes...
  • The Melting Pot Restaurant offers the perfect setting for your dining experience. The Melting Pot's fondue style of dining features the finest four-course meals, fabulous wines, great cheeses, decadent chocolate and a relaxed atmosphere. Our convenient location, unique menu offerings, romantic decor and dedicated staff make dining at the Melting Pot a truly unforgettable experience... Located in the Aventine, across from the Hyatt-La Jolla, and very easy access from the 5 freeway! Check out what the media is saying about us (http://www.sandiegometro.com/2006/may/dining.php)
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Fondue, French
  • Neighborhood: La Jolla / Golden Triangle
  • Cross Street: La Jolla Village Dr.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.meltingpot.com/sandiego
  • Email: fbecker@meltingpotnw.com
  • Phone: (858) 638-1700
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm ***Special Mother's Day Weekend Hours Saturday and Sunday doors open at 11:30am***
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Armando Lopez
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet parking available for $7. Hyatt Parking Garage discounted with validation. Street parking available.
  • Private Party Facilities: Semi Private party room accommodates up to 28 guests. Reservation fees apply. Entire restaurant available for daytime functions. We will staff and serve with complete privacy. Speak with a Manager for details.

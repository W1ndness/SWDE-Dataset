Flat Iron Tapas Bar and Lounge-Bulfinch Hotel

writes...
  • Located in Downtown Boston's Bulfinch Hotel is the critically acclaimed Flat Iron Tapas Bar & Lounge. Flat Iron offers "a menu of deliciously creative and affordable small plates" (Boston Herald) in a "hip black-and-paprika haunt" in Boston's West End's Bulfinch Triangle. Join the Downtown North area restaurant crowd for drinks and small plates - an assortment of tapas that combine ingredients from around the globe in what is becoming Boston's favorite Mass General and Banknorth area's in spot. The world background music - Portuguese, Spanish and French has been called "tres chic" by the Boston Globe. We offer our own spin of the classic cocktails you are familiar with - the Classic Cuban Martini with Spanish-infused olive juice, the Iron Margarite with lemongrass mint-infused vodka and tequila, and our very own Flat Iron, with fresh citrus juices and hibiscus-infused vodka.

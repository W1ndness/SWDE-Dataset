  • Enrico's "Eponymous" sidewalk cafe was founded in 1958.It was immortalized in Bullitt with Steve McQueen's "lets go to Enrico's".Over the decades, Enrico's has been at the heart of San Francisco's entertainment, social scene, culture and spirit.

  • Cuisine: Italian, American
  • Neighborhood: North Beach
  • Cross Street: Kearny

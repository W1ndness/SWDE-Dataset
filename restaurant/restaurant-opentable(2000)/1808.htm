le Magnifique

writes...
  • Upon entering LE MAGNIFIQUE one Immediately feels transported into the Parisian`sRive gauche associated with “La Nouvelle Vague” *(Group of french filmakers influenced by Italian Neorealism and classical Hollywood cinema)* through unique black and white prints. Our guest are warmly greeted and made welcome by friendly familiar faces always making one feel part of the family and ready to have a bit of carefree fun! You can also count on being served the freshest traditional Bistro food, made with lots of love *(which we all know is the most important ingredient)* all paired with a variety of great affordable wines and Champagne... LE MAGNIFIQUE is about great moments...moments of spontaneity, culinary satisfactions and laughter with friends! These are all part of the LE MAGNIFIQUE experience THAT'S JUST THE WAY WE LIKE IT!!!

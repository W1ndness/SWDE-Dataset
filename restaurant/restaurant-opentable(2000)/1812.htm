The Libertine

writes...
  • Classic meets contemporary at this culinary hotspot that reinterprets pub fare to bring you the ultimate modern English tavern. The menu features traditional favorites with sly twists, creative small plates, and a full raw bar. While the lively bar showcases imaginative house cocktails, an impeccably curated artisan beer selection and an impressive wine list. The upstairs Library Bar offers full dining, as well as an intimate setting for private events.

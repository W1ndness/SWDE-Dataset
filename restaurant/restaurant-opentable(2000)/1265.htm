Roka Akor

writes...
  • Roka Akor is Scottsdale's Sushi Restaurant and Japanese Robata Grill that was recently awarded "Top 10" sushi restaurants in USA by Bon Appetit Magazine. The adjacent Shochu Lounge features shochu infusions and signature cocktails in contemporary yet relaxed surroundings. Additionally, the restaurant offers two private rooms and an outdoor patio.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Japanese, Sushi, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Scottsdale
  • Cross Street: North of Indian Bend
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.rokaakor.com
  • Email: info@rokaakor.com
  • Phone: (480) 306-8800
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday from 11:30am - 2:30 pm.Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm **** Shochu Lounge Happy Hour / Izakaya is offered seven days a week from: 5:00 pm - end of dinner service
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ce Bian
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Counter Seating, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Roka Akor offers self parking as well as valet service.
  • Catering: Please contact Joy Schneiderman at 480-306-8800 or via email at Joy@RokaAkor.com
  • Private Party Facilities: Adjacent to the main dining room, our two private dining rooms are available for seating parties up to 20 guests.

Michael's on Main

writes...
  • We at Michael's on Main in Soquel offer cutting-edge comfort cuisine, with a comfortable fine dining experience. We are a green restaurant using local farmers for our produce, cheeses, & meats. We have a wonderful bar along with an extensive wine list from many local wineries. The ambiance at Michael's is warm with indoor & outdoor setting. We have a gazebo that overlooks the Soquel Creek, which is nice for weddings & parties. Fireplaces in the bar/lounge and another in one dining room. Our walls are decorated with new local artist work each month. Thursday through Sunday we have live music for your listening & dancing pleasure. So come and join us for some Pot Roast & Mashed Potatoes, or a Rack of Lamb with Sweet Potato Fries. It’s a wonderful dining experience you won’t want to miss.

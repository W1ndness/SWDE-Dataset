T1 Tapas

writes...
  • Come see the most technologically advanced restaurant in the Queen City at Birkdale Village in Huntersville. At T1 Tapas every Connection Booth has a touch sensitive tabletop where up to six users can interact on one screen to order food, play games, and watch their favorite videos. You can also connect your MP3 player, camera, flash drive, or laptop to enjoy your personal media on super high definition monitors built into the Connection Booth. Our menu is so much more than tapas. We’ve adapted some of the best dishes from around the world to suite all tastes and appetites. We have a new kids menu designed to please our youngest customers. Bring your portable technology and enjoy great food and drinks—T1 offers more than your traditional dining experience. Check the T1 Tapas website for special events happening every month. You have to experience it to believe it! Be sure to request a Connection Booth--first come, first serve!

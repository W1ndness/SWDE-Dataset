Le Caprice

writes...
  • Le Caprice welcomes friends and guests to its New York home at 795 Fifth Avenue. A hit when it first opened its doors in London’s Green Park in 1947, Le Caprice has over the years established itself as a social institution, with its effortless style and coveted cuisine. The menu showcases modern European and international favorites, presented with flair but without fuss, and includes classic dishes such as Salmon Fishcake with Buttered Spinach & Sorrel Sauce, Steak Tartar and Crispy Duck & Watercress Salad. An extensive wine list and charming service completes the picture for a truly unforgettable experience.

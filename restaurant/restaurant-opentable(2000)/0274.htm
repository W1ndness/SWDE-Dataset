The Magnolia Thomas Restaurant

writes...
  • The Magnolia Thomas Restaurant features the best in new southern cuisine, a fabulous wine and beer selection, all in a comfortable setting. Join us for a business dinner, romantic night out, dining with friends or that special family occasion. A unique dining experience, reasonably priced and close by all the north and northwest Atlanta suburbs... come experience "Southern Dining with a Silver Spoon Treatment"!

  • Cuisine: Southern, American

Nell's

writes...
  • Nell's is an intimate, chef-owned restaurant located in the vibrant Green Lake neighborhood. Seven nights a week Nell's offers European-inspired New American cuisine featuring the finest Northwest seasonal ingredients. Chef Philip Mihalski's cooking unites his classic training in some of the most celebrated kitchens in New York City and France with his passion for freshness and simplicity.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Green Lake
  • Cross Street: Latona Ave NE
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.nellsrestaurant.com
  • Email: info@nellsrestaurant.com
  • Phone: (206) 524-4044
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner Nightly: 5:30pm-10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Philip Mihalski
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Private Lot available for guests as well as neighborhood street parking
  • Catering: Nell's Catering is a perfect choice for a variety of events from formal, multi-course dinners to large receptions to platters available for pick up or delivery. The same exquisite cuisine and service we provide in our restaurant can be tailored to suit the individual needs of your event - please contact us for more information, menu ideas, and quotes. We look forward to working with you!
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Chef Philip participates in a variety of benefits in the Seattle area every year as well as hosting exciting wine dinners like the Chef Tribute Dinner Series at Nell's. For more information see our Events page at: www.nellsrestaurant.com

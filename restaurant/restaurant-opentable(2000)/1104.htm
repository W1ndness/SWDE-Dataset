Harry's Food & Cocktails

writes...
  • All new and exciting, Harry's provides a casual neighborhood atmosphere with an upscale flair. There are comfortable booths, large bar and dining areas, tree-lined patio with fire pit, and an amazing chandelier reconstructed of Grain Belt Beer bottles. Harry's is the ideal place for an important business meeting, or a great place to just meet friends. Our Chef promises original renditions of familiar dishes at moderate prices. Prices range from $10-$20 on average. Located in the Mill District. Adjacent to the McPhail Center for Performing Arts, 2 blocks to the Metrodome and 2 blocks to the Guthrie Theatre. Private dining available for up to 50 guests.

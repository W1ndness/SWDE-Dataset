Thali Regional Cuisine of India - New Canaan

writes...
  • The newest sister restaurant Thali in New Haven has been rated EXCELLENT by The New York Times - September 17, 2006. After successfully opening the Thali in New Haven in June of 2006, Executive chef and owner Prasad Chirnomula was dubbed "...the most exciting Indian Chef in United States," by David Rosengarten in the Rosengarten Report.
    It's difficult to believe Thali resides in the suburbs --it is so chic and urban in its interior. Dramatic with an overhead waterfall, a glamorous copper bar with high tables and chairs and a sleek private room in the back for parties. The New Canaan location is housed in an historic 1913 bank building. Thali's owner and chef, the incomparable Prasad Chirnomula has been a fixture of the restaurant scene over the last decade and has utterly changed the way people in this area think of Indian cuisine.

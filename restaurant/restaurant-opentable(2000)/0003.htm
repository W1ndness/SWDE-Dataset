Binion's Ranch Steakhouse

writes...
  • The most spectacular view downtown Las Vegas has to offer. Binion's Ranch Steakhouse is located high above Las Vegas on the 24th floor. USDA prime aged steaks prepared to your liking while listening to our baby grand player piano sets the mood for a romantic candlelit dinner when celebrating special occasions. We proudly serve the best Lobster Bisque in Las Vegas along with traditional entrees such as Filet Mignon with an Australian Lobster Tail, Prime Rib of Beef, twenty one (21) ounce Porterhouse Steak, Halibut, Salmon, beer battered Prawns, and our signature dish "Chicken Fried Lobster". Great dining atmosphere for those times when you just want a great steak and a great view...Las Vegas' best kept secret.

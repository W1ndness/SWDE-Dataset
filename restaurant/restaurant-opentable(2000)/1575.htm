Papa Razzi - Wellesley

writes...
  • At Papa Razzi, guests will enjoy a taste of Italy without the airfare! Our modern Italian trattoria features made-to-order signature pasta dishes, ultra thin-crust pizzas, seafood specials along with our mouth-watering desserts. Guests will also enjoy a Tuscan-inspired Sunday brunch like no other. Whether it be a casual business lunch, cozy dinner for two, family gathering...Papa Razzi is the perfect choice.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian, American
  • Neighborhood: Wellesley
  • Cross Street: River Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.paparazzitrattoria.com
  • Email: papa-wellesley@bbrginc.com
  • Phone: (781) 235-4747
  • Hours of Operation: Brunch: Saturday: 11:30am - 2:00pm, Sunday: 11:30am - 3:00pm
    Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:30am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 11:30pm, Friday & Saturday: 4:00pm - 12:00am
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: James Martin
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: There is parking available in the Papa Razzi's parking lot and in the lot directly across the street.
  • Private Party Facilities: Papa Razzi in historic Wellesley features a private dining room that can accommodate up to 100 guests for a "sit-down" dinner and 175 guests for a cocktail reception. In addition to our private rooms, we have 2 semi-private rooms available that can accommodate up to 50 at lunch and 25 at dinner.
  • Private Party Contact: Christine Mangerian (781) 235-4747

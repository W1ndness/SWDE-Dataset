Raphael

writes...
  • Raphael is a family owned casual fine dining restaurant focused on “World Cuisine.” Raphael aims to provide Studio City with a fine dining experience unlike any other in the Studio City area, as can be seen from its casually luxurious and elegant décor. The menu reflects the family’s continued commitment to a global perspective, featuring dishes inspired by regional cuisines from around the world. Chef Steven Barkulis, formerly of Water Grill, has interpreted this notion beautifully by focusing on traditional rather than fusion dishes. In keeping with the theme of “world cuisine,” Raphael offers a range of wine selections from a vast number of appellations, drawn from diverse regions. The wine bar specializes in by the glass offerings and is currently serving over fifty wines by the glass, many rare or rarely offered." Please join us for a dining experience, we hope to see you soon. -The Raphael Family

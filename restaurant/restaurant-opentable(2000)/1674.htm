Bar Henry

writes...
  • Welcome to Bar Henry Bistro, we're fond of wine here. We're partial to the balance and restraint of old world wines, and we also enjoy the ripe robustness of new world wines.
    We offer wines that are ready to drink. This means older wines that are made to age and younger wines made to be drunk young. You'll find a mix of classic and unusual wines that are ready to drink young in our "Market" section. There's also a collection of classic, mature wines from France, Italy, Germany and the United States in our "Reserve" section.
    We would like our guests to try many wines. Bar Henry guests may order half of any bottle in the "Market" section. That goes for half and full bottles, adding about one hundred choices to the wines offered by the glass.
    Enjoy our wine list and our delicious New York Bistro fare, and have a great time.

Katie's on Shore Road

writes...
  • Katie’s is a casually elegant restaurant and lounge. Although we are in the vacation destination of Ogunquit, Katie's is anything but "tourist-y". Situated in a century-old house midway between downtown Ogunquit and Perkins Cove, Katie's is a perfect respite from the hustle and bustle. Chef Jason Grant changes the eclectic menu every day, adding what is finest and freshest from the local farms and purveyors. The secret of Katie's is in the simple preparation of the freshest ingredients. Known for its delightful appetizers, salads and soups, Katie's divides it entrees into small plates and large plates. Each is an entree-sized portion. The small plates are more in the European tradition where the entree follows an appetizer, soup, and a salad; while the large plates are more in the American tradition where the entree can dominate the meal. At Katie's, each of you can select the tradition that appeals to you at that time.

  • Executive Chef: Jason Grant
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Wine
  • Public Transit: During the season, Katie’s is on the Ogunquit Trolley line on Shore Road which stops at the corner of our property.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Katie’s has parking for 14 cars in its private lot. During the height of the season, nearby patrons are encouraged to walk to Katie's from nearby public parking lots as well.
  • Catering: Chef Jason Grant can cater anything from a dinner for six to a wine or beer dinner where the dishes are specially selected to complement the wines or beers to be sampled to a wedding reception for 100. The key to all such catering events is the intelligent selection of the finest ingredients. Catered events require thoughtful preparation and planning and as much notice as possible should be given Chef Jason.
  • Private Party Facilities: Because Katie"s is located in a century-old home, we have the option of closing off a room or a porch for the exclusive use by private parties ranging from 10 to 25 guests. Very occasionally, Katie's will close the entire restaurant to accommodate a group as large as 80 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Chef Jason Grant (207) 423-2307
  • Entertainment: Occasionally, Katie’s will have a local musician play an acoustic guitar or have similar entertainment

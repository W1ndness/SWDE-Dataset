Equus and Jack's Lounge

writes...
  • Equus and Jack's Lounge is a fine dining restaurant offering progressive American regional cuisine. Serving Louisville for 20 years, 4 Star rated Equus and Jack's Lounge specializes in prime meat, game, fresh seafood, fabulous soups & salads & wonderful desserts & features an award winning wine list. Adjacent to Equus is Jack's Lounge, a New York style lounge like no other in Louisville.

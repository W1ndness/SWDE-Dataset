  • A new chapter has been opened in Seattle — Library Bistro and Bookstore Bar. Reminiscent of a 1940s and 1950s style restaurant with high back booths, charcoal blue bookcases that display hundreds of books by famous and infamous authors, the Library Bistro is a perfect place to enjoy a truly memorable meal or skip a few chapters and have dessert first.

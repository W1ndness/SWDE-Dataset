Hudson Grille

writes...
  • A new addition to White Plains, the Hudson Grille brings contemporary American dining to a new level. With seating capacity of over 270, the Hudson Grille provides casual yet elegant dining, attentive service and delicious cuisine that will satisfy the most discerning palate. From the "Fennel & Beet Salad" and "Sautéed Pei Mussels" to the "HG Burger" and "Duck Confit" - fresh local seasonal ingredients cooked to perfection!

    The beautifully appointed dining room, with plush red drapes and private bar can play host to a romantic evening or a private gathering of family and friends. Signature cocktails and our extensive wine list can be enjoyed indoors or our beer garden with its granite bar is always a fun alternative.

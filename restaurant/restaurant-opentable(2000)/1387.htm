Luna Park strives to bring its guests delicious, creative food and a one-of-a kind cocktail experience amidst a unique, trendy atmosphere. Our kitschy, flea-market chic, ambiance lends itself as the perfect environment for any occasion. Whether you are having a business lunch, celebrating with loved ones, on a first date, out with the girls, or looking to meet someone, from our lively bar scene to our cozy dining areas, Luna Park is always the perfect place to go, and with our reasonable prices, you won't feel put out picking up the tab. Large groups are accommodated at the many large tables throughout the dining room and the private, heated, enclosed patio.

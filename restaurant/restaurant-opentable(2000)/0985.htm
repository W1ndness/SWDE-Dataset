Rue Saint Jacques

writes...
  • We are located in the Nob Hill Area right around the corner from the Grace Cathedral the Masonic center, a very pleasant short and flat walk from the Mark Hopkins, the Fairmont and the Huntington . We like sharing our years of experience in an elegant setting yet very unpretentious as is our chef’s cuisine. He is surely in touch with the local richness that Bay area farmers and producers bring to the table. Still a no nonsense approach to the cooking experience. Our wine list is very engaged with the times reaching every continent and regions with emphasis on France and California.

  • Bistro 921 offers creative Pacific Northwest cuisine in a cozy atmosphere. Open for Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner, we feature Local Product, Traditional Sandwiches, Hand Tossed Pizzas & More! We are the perfect place for Pre-Theater Dinner & After Theater Dessert.... If you are on the Go downtown and need a place for Business Breakfast or Power Lunch, Beat the Rush hour Cocktails & Appetizers or just a special dinner ~ Bistro 921 can do it all with fast & friendly Bistro style! We invite you to come experience our newly updated Menu!

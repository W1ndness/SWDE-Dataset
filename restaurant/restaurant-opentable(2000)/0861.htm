Billy Berk's

writes...
  • Variety is the spice of life. With Prosciutto and Manchego Flatbread, Hot and Spicy Chicken, Guava Glazed Sticky Ribs, Crab and Artichoke Hot Pot, Paella, Mojito Skirt Steak, and Cornbread Cheddar Skillet just to name a few, Billy Berk's has flavors for all palettes. Set in a historic, downtown San Jose brick and timber building, Billy Berk's features a large island bar with 4 50 inch TV's and an open-air patio, perfect for after work, pre-game festivities, and dates; let us know other good excuses.

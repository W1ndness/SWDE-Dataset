Prime: A Steakhouse

  • Welcome to Prime: A Steakhouse; we have a goal to offer a unique experience for dining. Everything from our U.S.D.A. Prime and Choice cuts of Beef to our décor has been carefully chosen to make each visit an experience like no other, anywhere. Our passion for great food, excellent wine, and exciting people are the driving factor in everything we do. We serve the finest beef, the freshest seafood, and premium spirits and wine to give our guests the best possible meal in Oxford’s only Steakhouse. Located just north of the square in the Downtown Oxford Inn & Suites, we strive to offer the best service to make your meal the perfect dining experience, not just “eating out”. Come have a drink and be seen at our bar that offers Oxford’s finest wines and cocktails or have dinner in our elegant, relaxed dining room and have a night to remember.

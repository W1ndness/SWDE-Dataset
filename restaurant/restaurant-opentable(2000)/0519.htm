Martine Cafe

writes...
  • Martine Cafe offers the unique flavors of North Africa, Spain & Provence. A Euro style dining experience with new world tapas & international wines & spirits nestled in an historic brownstone in downtown Salt Lake City. Zagat Survey listed us as "best, most unique and innovative cooking in town,' plus there's an 'imaginative wine list' to boot.”

  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: We have several parking options. Some are more accommodating than others. Valet is available directly in front of our restaurant. If you prefer to park yourself, Regent Street parking is available around the corner on Regent st. and is $1.00 an hour & credit card only. Parking is free on the streets after 6 PM.
  • Catering: Martine Cafe has partnered with Lortsher Vlasic Catering & Events for 17 years. Fresh imaginative cuisine is the centerpiece of our company. Our seasonal menus offer a wide array of choices that are uniquely flavorful, visually exciting and memorable. We’re constantly searching out new flavors and cooking methods in our travels. The results of these "gourmet adventures" invariably influence our menus and our approach to presentation. Visit their website @ www.lortshervlasic.com

Martine social menus give you the option of a stand up cocktail scene with tray past hors d'oeuvre. It's sure to impress your associates. If you are looking for a formal setting our Chef, Tom Grant will work with you to create a specific coursed out prix fixe to fit into your budget. Some featured entrees may be almond crusted wild salmon with fennel-caper pan sauce, grape leaf wrapped halibut with saffron veloute, serrano ham wrapped quail with romesco or local braise lamb shank with french lentils. The creativity goes on and on. Whatever your culinary and service accommodation needs are, Martine will be sure to please.

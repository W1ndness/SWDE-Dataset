Bar Americain at Mohegan Sun

writes...
  • Bar Americain at Mohegan Sun is an American Brasserie featuring regional American food created by Chef Bobby Flay. Located in the Casino of the Sky, Bar Americain celebrates America’s diverse culinary heritage, inspired by Bobby’s extensive travels throughout the country. His decidedly American menu is mirrored by the energetic fast pace of the time-honored European brasserie.

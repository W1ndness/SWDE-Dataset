Mitchell's Fish Market - Carmel

writes...
  • Welcome to Mitchell’s Fish Market, located at Clay Terrace in Carmel, just 15 miles north of downtown Indianapolis. At Mitchell’s Fish Market, the fish is off the boat fresh, the drinks are made with fresh-squeezed juice, and the atmosphere is as warm and inviting as a day on the water. Mitchell’s Fish Market is obsessed with freshness because we know that’s how you like your seafood. Whether you crave a classic like Chesapeake Bay Crab Cakes, or you want to indulge in delicacies from a distant shore with dishes like our Asian-inspired Hoisin Glazed Yellowfin Tuna, fish any fresher would still be in the ocean! Come experience what an obsession with freshness tastes like at Mitchell’s Fish Market. Please visit our menus online at www.mitchellsfishmarket.com!
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Indianapolis
  • Cross Street: Meridian Street (State Route 31)

  • Price: $30 and under

  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Paul Miranda
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress

  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: There are public lots and street parking available for our guests. Mitchell's Fish Market also offers Valet Parking on Friday and Saturday evenings during dinner service for $3.00.
  • Private Party Facilities: A special occasion warrants a special meal. That’s why we remind you to remember Mitchell’s Fish Market when you are planning a private celebration or corporate event. Our Captain's Quarters offers private dining for up to 12 guests. For larger parties, our Angler's Cove accommodates 35 guests and our Map Room accommodates 44 guests.

  • Buona Sera Ristorante is located in the Riviera Valley in South Redondo Beach between Catalina Avenue and Palos Verdes Boulevard. Exquisite fine Italian cuisine and great service make a perfect combination at this new Italian restaurant. Our Chef offers quality and sophisticated dining with emphasis on traditional regional Italian dishes. Our staff offers perfection in the service and dining experience. There is a full bar, private room, indoor patio and an extensive wine cellar. Zagat rated 'Excellent' 2004, 05, 07, 09, 10 in the South Bay area.

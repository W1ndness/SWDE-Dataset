Vareli

writes...
  • Vareli will be closed for renovations from August 8th through the 16th of 2010. Thank you for your patience while we continue to improve your dining experience and we look forward to serving you again!

    From the owners of Westside Markets comes this new and exciting restaurant and wine bar on the Upper West Side of Manhattan. Vareli strives to bring a unique blend of old world Mediterranean charm and modern, elegant yet bold flavors in its beautiful, warm and comfortable setting. Two floors of dining with bars on both floors replete with a fireplace and outdoor waterfall are complemented by an inspired menu and world class wines.

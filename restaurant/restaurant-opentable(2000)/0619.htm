Braxton Seafood Grill

writes...
  • Tucked between Macy's & Pottery Barn at Oak Brook Mall, Braxton serves fresh seafood flown in from around the globe & offers signature items including award-winning Maryland crab cakes, lobster bisque & the "always on your plate" sweet biscuits. In addition to great seafood, Braxton offers 21-day aged steaks including center cut filets & prime strip. Executive chef Rex Horrell takes the art of food very seriously, & the Braxton staff is just as concerned about the service. Guests enjoy their meals in a sophisticated yet lively atmosphere; there are alcove tables for privacy, tables for groups, private rooms & the signature "dome room" that features an impressive stained glass domed ceiling.

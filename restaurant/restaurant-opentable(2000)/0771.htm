Pulehu, an Italian Grill

writes...
  • Pulehu an Italian Grill, located in Maui, Hawaii, sets the standards for Italian dining. We offer Italian cuisine with a local flair, designed for families and couples seeking a romantic dinner. The menu is designed to capture Italian favorites such as Veal Parmesan and Chef's Lasagana while adding incorporating locally farmed produce such as the Olowalu Tomato Caprese Salad. This intimate dining experience offers the freshest island catches and prime beef with impressive wine selections and pairings. Whether your palate desires seafood, pasta, beef or poultry or you are looking for a celebratory destination Pulehu an Italian Grill will certainly satisfy.

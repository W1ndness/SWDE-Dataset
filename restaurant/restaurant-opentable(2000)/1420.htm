The Restaurant @ L'Ermitage

writes...
  • The Restaurant is one of only two AAA four diamond rated restaurants in Beverly Hills - located in the L’Ermitage Beverly Hills. Whether you choose to dine in the main dining area with two story windows looking out onto the private garden or al fresco on the more casual Patio, you’ll discover an elegant dining experience and superior service reminiscent of a fine private club – for a select in-the-know few.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Californian
  • Neighborhood: Beverly Hills
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.lermitagebh.com
  • Email: drew.berry@viceroyhotelgroup.com
  • Phone: (310) 385-5302
  • Hours of Operation: Daily Breakfast: 6:30am - 11:30am
    Lunch: 11:30am - 2:30pm
    Dinner: 6:00pm -10:00pm (Tuesday-Saturday)
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Fireplace, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: To enhance your evening, our local courtesy car is available to transport you to the restaurant. Arrangements must be made in advance and are subject to availability. Complimentary transportation is available within three miles of the restaurant.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet 2 1/2 hours free validation
  • Catering: The Pavilion, a private dining room for up to 20 guests, is located adjacent to The Restaurant. Two Garden Suites — These private open floor plan suites (1,400 square feet) are located on the hotel’s main floor down a private path. The L'Ermitage Suite — Conduct formal or informal business in the privacy of a club-like atmosphere. 1,000 square feet of space with kitchen area, built in bar, 60” screen, and fireplace affords executives something truly different form the standard meeting room.
  • Private Party Facilities: Pavillion Garden Suite L'Ermitage Suite Roof Top
  • Private Party Contact: Timothy Rutland (310) 385-5028
  • Entertainment: The Lobby Bar — a club-like atmosphere, which features comfortable seating, paneled walls, and signed scripts from major motion pictures on the walls surrounding the room’s fireplace. This room and the adjoining outdoor fireplace patio provide a relaxed setting for informal meetings and conversation over cocktails.

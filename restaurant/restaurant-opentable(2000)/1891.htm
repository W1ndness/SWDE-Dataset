Rayuela

writes...
  • Inspired by the children's game of hopscotch, Rayuela introduces Estilo Libre Latino (Freestyle Latino) cuisine, which respects yet redefines, traditional Latin American and Spanish dishes. Located in the Lower East Side at 165 Allen Street, it is owned by Chef Maxino Tejada, Ray Fernandez, and up-and-coming restaurateur Hector Sanz. Rayuela is open seven days a week for dinner, with Lunch\Dinner served on Sunday 2:00pm-10:00pm.

  • Cuisine: Latin American, Tapas / Small Plates
  • Neighborhood: Lower East Side
  • Cross Street: Between Rivington and Stanton Streets

  • Public Transit: The F & V Subway to 2nd Avenue and the F, J, M & Z to Essex St./Delancey St.

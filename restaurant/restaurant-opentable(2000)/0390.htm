Aida Bistro & Wine Bar

writes...
  • Howard County's Award Winning Progressive Italian Bistro is located in Columbia, MD near Route 175 & 95. Recognized as the Best Italian Restaurant in Baltimore & in Howard County for the past 6 years, our family owned & operated restaurant is designed to offer clean, lively & comfortable surroundings with a warm and friendly atmosphere. AIDA Bistro & Wine Bar specializes in classic Italian favorites as well as an eclectic mix of non-traditional entrees & small plates from a variety of culinary & cultural backgrounds. Our menu focuses on fresh, local, nutritious & fairly priced Italian style Bistro food. Although it has an Italian core of entrees and freshly made pasta, the Chef adds Weekly Specials to the menus which are both contemporary and non-traditional Italian. We offer a three course prix fixe menu nightly, available with hand chosen wines & beers to compliment each course. Our comfortable Wine Bar features a frequently updated wine list.

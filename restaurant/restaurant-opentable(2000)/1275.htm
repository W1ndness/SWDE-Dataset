  • 100th Bomb Group Restaurant & Banquet Facility Unique location where the view never ends. Featuring steaks, fresh seafood, pasta and chicken entrees. Children’s menu available. Wonderful Sunday Brunch - Sunday 9:30- 2:30 featuring over 100 items. Dinner daily at 4:30. Lounge – full service, outdoor patio and observation deck. (weather permitting) Reservations suggested.

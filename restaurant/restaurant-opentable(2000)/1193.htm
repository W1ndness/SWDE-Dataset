Kevin Taylor's At The Opera

writes...
  • Kevin Taylor's at the Opera House will provide the high caliber dining experience that Taylor patrons have come to expect, with menus designed for the pre-theatre diner, highlighting contemporary American cuisine. The restaurant operates throughout the Denver Performing Arts Complex season, including performance seasons for Colorado Ballet and Denver Center Attractions.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Downtown / LoDo
  • Cross Street: Champa

  • Payment Options:

  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes

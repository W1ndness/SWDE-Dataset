General Warren Inne

writes...
  • Historic 1745 Country Inn featuring American-Continental Cuisine with a contemporary flair, table-side prepared specialties at dinner, attentive service amid charming candlelit dining rooms, all with working fireplaces. Home of "The Warren Tavern", featuring a lite fare menu or the full menu, all available without reservations. Full bar and extensive International wine list. Outdoor dining at "The Springhouse Terrace", available all year-round for cocktail parties, receptions and intimate weddings and receptions. Three private party rooms for 15 to 55 guests, eight elegant guests suites for overnight stays, including a served continental breakfast.

The General Warren Inne specializes in custom functions including cocktail parties with food stations, carving stations and butler hors d'oeuvre service, in addition to our award winning white cloth table service. Your event can be tailored to be formal or casual, based on your needs. Please contact a function planner at 610 296.3637 or info@generalwarren.com

Heated four season brick terrace for cocktail & hors d'oeuvres reception. Beautifully landscaped property perfect for formal or candid photography. Eight elegant guests suites available for out-of-town guests.

Also, the exciting new General's Supper Club: a monthly special dinner served with a nationally acclaimed Jazz Combo. See calendar for more details!

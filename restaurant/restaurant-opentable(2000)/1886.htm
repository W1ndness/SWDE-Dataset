Porto Vivo

writes...
  • Long Island’s newest hot spot promises to bring a sophisticated experience to diners with quality service, exceptional cuisine and a stylish atmosphere. Owned by LI famed inventor and entrepreneur extraordinaire Joy Mangano of Home Shopping Network and classically trained, European restaurateur Philipp Seipelt, Porto Vivo is set to impress Long Island dining scene. Consulting chef John Doherty, born and raised on LI, and who for 20 years was the executive chef at the Waldorf =Astoria Hotel, will head the kitchen. The design of the space emphasizes the history and the character of the building, while offering a contemporary rustic ambiance. The main floor dining room offers a grand entrance with floor to ceiling glass windows and marble tile.

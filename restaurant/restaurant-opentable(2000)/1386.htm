Locanda Positano

writes...
  • Welcome to Locanda Positano, a cozy small gem in the heart of Marina Del Rey. Our Chef, Massimo Denaro, from Florence, created an authentic Italian menu focusing on the seasons. Using local, fresh, and organic produces and only quality ingredients imported directly from Italy, Locanda creates a refined and sophisticated feel in a romantic setting. Our wine list offers a wide and selected variety of Italian wines and, as well of beers.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Marina del Rey
  • Cross Street: Washington
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://locandapositano.com
  • Email: locandapositano@gmail.com
  • Phone: (310) 526-3887
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Tuesday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Massimo Denaro
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Delivery, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: We offer valet parking service or we have plenty of street parking after 6pm.
  • Private Party Facilities: Locanda Positano is available for buy-outs at certain times. Please call and we will see if we can accommodate your business meeting or special event with our traditional and innovative Italian dishes and pasta specialties. Locanda Positano offers an extensive selection of Italian and California wines. Menu highlights include traditional chicken and veal specialties, fresh seafood, pasta creations, homemade soups, fresh and innovative salads, and vegetarian dishes.

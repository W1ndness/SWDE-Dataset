Choptank

writes...
  • Choptank is a full-service seafood restaurant inspired by the flavors of the Chesapeake Bay. It offers first class service, creative twists on authentic seafood, and a warm, inviting ambiance. Choptank serves approachable cuisine with value, delivering a menu of casual sophistication constructed with the neighborhood diner in mind. It is a place to come frequently, either for an intimate sit down dinner or to enjoy nibbles and a craft beer in the bar area. Native Marylanders will be proud of authentic Chesapeake dishes like jumbo lump crab cakes and peel and eat shrimp, while generalists will be able to stretch their palate with mid Atlantic-inspired fish dishes. Balance and diversity are achieved by introducing selections of terrestrial fare such as wood-grilled steaks and chops, fried chicken, and other rotating specials. At Choptank, all menu items are prepared from scratch, using only the highest quality ingredients, humanely raised meats, and fish filleted in-house that day.

Choptank has several unique areas perfect for your next event or large party dining experience including an outdoor garden, enclosed patio, and a semi-private table within Choptank's bar area. The restaurant's layout allows us to customize your experience in addition to serving you with our signature brand of Mid-Atlantic cuisine, micro-brewed beers, small production wines, handcrafted cocktails and warm hospitality. Private parties may reserve Choptank's signature steamed Blue Crabs (when available April through September) or discuss with our events manager to select a menu. Beer, wine and cocktail menus are also subject to your preference. There are few requests that we cannot accommodate.

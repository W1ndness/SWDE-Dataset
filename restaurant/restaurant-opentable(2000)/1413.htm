Pete's Cafe & Bar

writes...
  • Pete's Cafe & Bar is a friendly neighborhood eatery in the heart of Downtown LA's Old Bank District. We offer a casual atmosphere, very much in the tradition of a New York style bistro.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Cross Street: 4th
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.petescafe.com
  • Email: office@petescafe.com
  • Phone: (213) 617-1000
  • Hours of Operation: Serving Food and Drink: Monday - Friday 11:30 AM - 2:00 AM Saturday - Sunday 11:00 AM - 2:00 AM Late Night Dinner menu served 11:00 PM - 2:00 AM daily
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Nick Shipp
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Counter Seating, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Smoking Area, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: $5 parking available at the Athena parking garage located at 425 S. Main Street.
  • Catering: From private dinners up to large celebrations. We are here for all of your catering needs.
  • Private Party Facilities: Pete's Cafe & Bar can accommodate private parties of all sizes. Please contact the restaurant directly if your party is more than 8 people.
  • Entertainment: Live Ambient, up-beat background music, but NOT loud

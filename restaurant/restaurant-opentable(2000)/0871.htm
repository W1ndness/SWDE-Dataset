Bocanova

writes...
  • Bocanova, located in Jack London Square, features Pan-American food in a lively and colorful setting. Available for all your dining needs, we offer intimate tables in our dining room, a number of communal dining tables for larger parties or if you're in the mood to meet new people, and counter seating up by our exhibition kitchen, when you're in the mood for a show.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Latin American, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Oakland
  • Cross Street: Embarcadero W
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.bocanova.com
  • Email: info@bocanova.com
  • Phone: (510) 444-1233
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Sunday: 11:30am - 3:pm

  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot

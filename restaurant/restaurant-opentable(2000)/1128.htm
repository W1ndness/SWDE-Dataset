Sushi Tango

writes...
  • Sushi Tango has moved! Don't fret, we are just across the hall in our beautiful new location. Still in the heart of Uptown Minneapolis, this hot-spot destination features award-winning sushi and newly added teppanyaki. Now featuring an extensive list of artisan sakes, ever-changing tap beers, hip specialty drinks, house-made infusions, and an impressive wine list; you may just "forget" how much fun you had! The legendary Tengo and his team will keep you laughing while they serve up amazing, one-of-a-kind creations you will fantasize about. Nice day out? Come and enjoy it on our new full-service patio, or groove to the sounds of local dj's that play late nights Thursday through Sunday. Please note: reservations made online are for restaurant dining only and not teppanyaki or hibachi. Hours for teppanyaki are: Mon-Thu 5-9:30 Fri 5-10 Sat noon-10 Sun noon-9:30 Shows start every half hour. Please call for reservations.

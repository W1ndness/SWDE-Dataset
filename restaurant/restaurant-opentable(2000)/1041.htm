Avalon - Louisville

writes...
  • A Louisville Original, Avalon is located in the heart of Louisville's trendy historic Highlands neighborhood. One of the city's hottest dining spots, Avalon features casual dining in a bistro lounge atmosphere, award-winning wine, and a menu that offers a variety of flavorful selections including a fun selection of lounge plates. In addition to being known for creating contemporary American cuisine with a twist, Avalon is known for its hip, contemporary interior and elaborate outdoor patio and bar. Avalon is happy to accommodate your special personal or business dining needs. The restaurant is available by reservation for private parties, business lunches or dinners, and other special events. (Reservations not taken for outside seating. First come, first served on patio.) Avalon's daily Early and Late Happy Hours feature select 2-for-1 cocktails and $5 appetizers. For more information please call (502) 454-5336.

Ana Beall's Tea Room

writes...
  • Ana Beall"s Tea Room is a full-service restaurant, offering morning sweets and lunch during the week, brunch on the weekend, a Southern-inspired supper on Friday and Saturday nights, along with a great-for-sharing Small Plates menu. And, true to our name, we offer traditional British-style tea service every day. Our onsite pastry chef bakes up imaginative and delicious new treats every morning, and our savory chef prepares your brunch, lunch, and dinner selections to order. We are pleased to cater your private parties at our lovely site in Westfield, or at your location, as you wish. Our highly trained kitchen team, and our attentive staff hope to make your visit to Ana Beall’s an elegant and serene experience. Visit us at 415 Westfield Avenue in Westfield or at www.anabeallstearoom.com. Contact us at info@anabeallstearoom.com or at (908) 264-4221.

Ana Beall's is a full-service restaurant with specialties that include elegant daytime and evening options, including Brunch, Luncheon, Traditional Afternoon Tea Service, Appetizer and Dessert Parties, and Seating Dining. Our menus are eclectic, American fare, and can be customized to suit the palates and special requests of our clients. Our site features a private dining room for small to medium sized groups (15-20), or our entire second floor (two rooms) for larger groups. We also are happy to provide run-of-the-house, which includes our first floor cafe area which can be set up to include a BYOB bar. Ideal for parties of 50-60 people, with room to mix and mingle over appetizers and desserts!

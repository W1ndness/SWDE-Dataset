Volare Ristorante

  • Volare Ristorante is Michigan's finest Italian Steakhouse, serving prime dry-aged steaks & chops, regional Italian cuisine & a 300 bottle wine list. We are pleased to offer a delicious & distinctive menu featuring innovative adaptations of traditional dishes. Volare's wine bar offers an array of choice selection with a strong emphasis on bottles from Italy. For lunch Volare offers an "Express" 3 course menu for $20.

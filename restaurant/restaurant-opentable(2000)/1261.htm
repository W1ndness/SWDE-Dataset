Modern Steak

writes...
  • Modern Steak, Fox Restaurant Concepts newest culinary project, will open mid-October 2009 at Scottsdale Fashion Square. While it is no secret that Scottsdale offers many quality steakhouse options, expect to discover an entirely new experience at Modern Steak. Modern Steak is all about glamour. From the striking white metal sculpture that artfully shades the outside climate controlled patio and all throughout the beautifully appointed dining room, bar and three private dining rooms, Modern Steak exudes hospitality and comfortable, yet affordable elegance. The menu features tantalizing appetizers, mouthwatering prime-aged beef and seafood dishes, exquisite sides and salads, irresistible desserts, creative cocktails and an outstanding wine selection.

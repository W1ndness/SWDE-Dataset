The Marathon Restaurant at North Pond

writes...
  • The Marathon Restaurant at North Pond is an upscale American Bistro featuring a modern twist on classic American favorites. The "Phantom Gourmet" recently picked us a "Hidden Jewel" and we also received a "Gold Plate" from TV Diner. We are conveniently located off of Route 495. The restaurant boasts an extensive wine and beer selection, and comfortable seating by our 2 fireplaces. The bar area features 10 HD TV"s!

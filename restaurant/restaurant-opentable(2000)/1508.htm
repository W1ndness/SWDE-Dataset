Our Private Dining Facilities

  • Average Price $$$
  • |
    Total Capacity 200

Salon Privé, the private dining room in Brasserie JO, is an elegant choice for your next social gathering or business dinner. Appointed in the same rich cherry wood and marble as the main dining room, the walls are decorated with vintage French posters, giving it an unusually exuberant air without the stuffiness of an ordinary banquet space. Salon Privé is perfect for small or large occasions; the room can easily be configured to suit your particular needs - accommodating a meeting for up to sixty, to a sit-down dinner for eighty or a reception for one hundred. Audio Visual and sound equipment are readily available to meet your needs with the latest technological services. The cuisine and wines mirror the traditional, French menu of the main dining room. However, we would be happy to customize a special menu to suit your particular needs or tastes. The service is of the same high standard that can be experienced in the main restaurant.

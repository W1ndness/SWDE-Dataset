  • A FRESH PERSPECTIVE ON INSPIRED FARE

    Located in Warren, NJ, Uproot will present Modern American cuisine, with a focus on bold flavors inspired by nature and prepared with expert craftsmanship. The experience will feature frequent changes based on the proper sourcing of exceptional ingredients. The signature of Uproot is its constant evolution, and its ability to provide a spectacular dining experience to each of its guests. The space, which offers several dynamic atmospheres and styles of dining, has been thoughtfully designed using basic, natural elements in unconventional ways, conveying the twisted sophistication of the concept.

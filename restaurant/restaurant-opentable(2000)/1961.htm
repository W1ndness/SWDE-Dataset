Zucca Trattoria

writes...
  • After 35 years of restaurant experience, Gino and his son Frank have created an "at home" Italian feeling for your dining experience. Located in the heart of the West Village, this truly exquisite experience gives you the decor and warm Italian atmosphere that invites you to try the regional rustic dishes of Italy. Escape the busy streets of New York City and immerse yourself into an atmosphere that could be easily mistaken for an authentic ristorante in Italia. Be guided by the knowledgeable wait staff through a traditional Italian menu, rotating list of creative daily specials and an extensive wine list. Specializing in on and off premise catering for all occasions, the professionals at Zucca Trattoria promise to provide delicious, authentic Italian cuisine with a smile.

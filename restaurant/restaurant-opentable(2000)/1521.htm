Cottonwood Cafe

writes...
  • Cottonwood Restaurant and Cafe is located directly across from the Old Hancock Building on the corner of Berkeley and St. James, just outside historic Copley Square in Boston's Back Bay. In 1992, Cottonwood first introduced Boston to a fresh and singular approach to Southwest cuisine and award winning margaritas. Ever since, Cottonwood's enthusiastic staff continues to welcome loyal patrons and new customers alike, earning Cottonwood's reputation as a perennial favorite among Boston's ever expanding and vibrant restaurant scene.

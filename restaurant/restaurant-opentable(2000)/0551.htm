Easy Bistro

writes...
  • Chattanooga meets Old World comfort food. Easy offers the best of French Bistro dining: delicious yet affordable, stylish yet friendly. No fussy meals here, just perfectly prepared steaks, fresh seafood, roast chicken, authentic Raw Bar oysters and crab legs, and more. All served in the dramatic urban interior of a century-old building that was the world's first Coca-Cola bottling plant. Liquors infused with unique flavors by our bar staff are used to create one of a kind cocktails. The wine list highlights reds and whites that showcase both traditional quality and new taste trends. The private dining room is perfect for business or social events. One block from the Tennessee Aquarium, Easy Bistro & Bar is Chattanooga's perfect night out downtown. Want a meal to enjoy and remember with loved ones? The choice is Easy. Open for dinner every day, brunch Saturday and Sunday.

  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Seafood

  • Public Transit: Easy Bistro & Bar is located just outside the Chattanooga Downtown Electric Shuttle Terminal. If you ride the shuttle simply walk out of the tunnel to Broad Street and take a left.
  • Parking: No
  • Parking Details: Ample On-Street and Public Parking lot spaces are available. We recommend any of the Republic Parking lots that are in view of the restaurant on Broad Street.
  • Catering: Easy Bistro & Bar offers custom designed off-premise catering menus for all sized events ranging from small take-away luncheons to large wedding and corporate parties. We also offer custom designed cocktail and wine parties with our creative and talented Bar staff.
  • Private Party Facilities: The private dining room at Easy Bistro & Bar is visually and acoustically separated from the main dining room. It provides a relaxed and private setting for up to 34 seated or 50 standing guests. It may also include audio-visual equipment. Private Party Packages and custom designed menus for events.

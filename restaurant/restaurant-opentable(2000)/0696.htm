Ria

writes...
  • Ria is reinventing traditional fine dining on the third floor of the Elysian hotel. Awash in glittering colors, fabrics and aesthetics, the restaurant's comfortable dining room exudes a subtle energy perfect for any sociable meal among friends or a peaceful dinner for a hungry guest. Ria offers a taste of inspired elegance with a menu that express the best of land-meets-sea. Executive Chef Jason McLeod casts a wide net of inspired ingredients, from the Norwegian seas and the Alaskan coast to the lush Midwestern farmlands found in Chicago's backyard. The menu is an ever-changing progression of the freshest possible fish and sustainably sourced meats, poultry and produce. The utmost care for ingredients is practiced in the kitchen to ensure the sustainably sourced menu is given the respect and attention to detail it merits. From the welcoming flavors at the beginning of the meal to the eye-opening mignardise at the finale, dinner at Ria is an inspired culinary experience.

The Grand Station Restaurant & Cocktails

writes...
  • The cuisine at the Grand Station is a great collection of American favorites and American classics. The menu consists of selections that reflect classic American comfort foods, some of which have been given twists to create new ways for our guests to enjoy the classics. The philosophy of The Grand Station, as it is with all FWHG concepts, is to provide guests with the highest quality ingredients that can be found. Executive Chef Juan Gonzales believes that there is absolutely no substitute for finding and using quality ingredients in everything from his own dishes, to the drinks behind the bar, and that is reflected in each of the Grand’s menus. The Grand works every day to uphold the mission of providing…“Five star quality at a five star location with five star service and a five star atmosphere, in an effort to always deliver the “FIVE STAR EXPERIENCE” to its guests at a three star price!”

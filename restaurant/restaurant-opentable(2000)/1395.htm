McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Burbank

  • McCormick & Schmick's Seafood Restaurant brings the freshest seafood to Southern California. Featuring a menu that is changed and printed daily which includes fresh, quality seafood served in a classic & traditional presentation flown in from both U.S. & international waters. Stained glass ceilings, richly paneled walls set the tone for comfortable dining.

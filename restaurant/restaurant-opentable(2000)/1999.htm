Buddha's Belly

writes...
  • Buddha's Belly Royal Thai Cuisine is the first restaurant of its kind in JAX beach An upscale casual destination from eminent restaurateur Thai-raised, Los Angeles-trained Guy (Star of Siam, mangosteen and Wild Thai), Buddha's Belly showcases the cuisines of Thailand, Vietnam, Japan, Korea India and China. Drawing upon memories of family meals his mother made, culinary experience to bring together the exhilarating tastes of a half-dozen Southeast Asian nations. Dishes are served Asian family-style to create an atmosphere that encourages sharing & conversation. Buddha's Belly, features a full bar with an extensive wine list and exotic cocktails plus a creative fresh Asian-influence desserts. Please Note: Online reservations are available for parties up to 6 people.

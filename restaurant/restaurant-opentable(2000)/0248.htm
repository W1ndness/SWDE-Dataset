Urban Solace

writes...
  • Join us at Urban Solace Restaurant, featuring New American Comfort Food and a fantastic selection of West Coast wines, handcrafted beer and a full selection of liquor. We are located in the heart of North Park’s “Restaurant Row”, near bustling neighborhoods of Hillcrest, South Park and University Heights, and just north of Downtown San Diego and Balboa Park. We are open seven days a week and feature our famous “Bluegrass Brunch” every Sunday. Reservations are generally only for the interior dining room. The patio is reserved for walk-in guests. On special occasions such as New Years Eve and Valentines Day, the patio is used for reservations as well. If you do not want to sit outside, please call the restaurant once you have made your reservation online.

    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa

  • Public Transit: We are located at the transit hub of 30th and University Ave.
  • Parking: Street Parking

  • Private Party Facilities: We do have limited availability for larger groups. Please contact the restaurant for more details.

  • Entertainment: We feature live jazz on our patio from 6 pm to sunset on Tuesday's and Wednesday's and live bluegrass every Sunday for our famous "Blue Grass Brunch", from 10:00 am to 2:45 pm.

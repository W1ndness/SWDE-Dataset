Butler's Restaurant

writes...
  • Butler's Restaurant & Tavern at The Essex Resort proudly features farm-to-table options with a focus on local ingredients. The menu is a bold reinterpretation of American contemporary cuisine, coupled with the best of tavern fare. We offer a wine list recently recognized with a “Best of” Award of Excellence by Wine Spectator magazine, and we also feature an extensive list of Vermont microbrews and specialty cocktails. Live entertainment Thursday, Saturday, Sunday evenings. Children’s menu available.

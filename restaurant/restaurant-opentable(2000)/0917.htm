Heaven's Dog

writes...
  • Charles Phan's newest addition brings San Francisco a combination of Noodle Shop and Restaurant/Lounge in the SoMa grand. We welcome reservations for our dining room and accommodate walk-ins in our Noodle Shop and Lounge on a first come first served basis. Feel free to stop by without a reservation and we will do our best to seat you!
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Chinese, Comfort Food, Organic
  • Neighborhood: SOMA
  • Cross Street: Between 7th and 8th Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.heavensdog.com
  • Phone: (415) 863-6008
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday-Sautrday 5:30pm - 1:00am Sunday 4:30pm - 9:00pm Lunch, Monday-Friday 11am-3pm (Please note, guests may be sat in our private dining room at our community table. Thank you.)
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Charles Phan/Andy Wai
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Civic Center BART, and Muni. 14 Mission Street and 26 Valencia Street busses directly.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: The Soma Grand Parking Garage offers parking,$1 for the first hour and $4 for up to 4 hours.
  • Private Party Facilities: Our Private Dining Room can accommodate from 8-14 guests, and our Lounge can be booked for cocktail parties of up to 30 guests. Please contact the restaurant for further details and to make a reservation.
  • Private Party Contact: Erik Adkins (415) 863-6008
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Contact us directly or visit our website: http://www.heavensdog.com for information on our events.

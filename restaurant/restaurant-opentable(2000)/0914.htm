Grill'Em

writes...

  • Located less than a mile south of Campbell's Pruneyard on Bascom Avenue, Grill 'Em Steakhouse is fast becoming a local favorite amongst San Jose Steakhouses. Customers are able to grill their own steaks on a six foot by two-and-a-half foot communal grill. Great for birthdays, first dates, or any kind of special celebration. Break the norm and come to Grill 'Em and experience the best Campbell Steakhouse. You do the grilling and we do the cleaning!

    And for those who are not so adventurous, Grill 'Em offers burgers, sandwiches, and salads prepared by our highly trained staff.

Beacon Street Tavern

writes...
  • Located at Brookline’s border to Boston, this relaxed kitchen and bar offers American cuisine, helpful service and welcoming atmosphere. This woody, spacious beautifully-lit bar and grill emits aura of cozy neighborliness (with a lively outdoor patio to boot). Its menu where the emphasis is on quality and value, is one that ably walks the line between comfort food and contemporary creations. The Nitecap Lounge, a separate room with its own bar is the perfect venue for a private functions or a just a relaxing drink on the weekends.

Root Down

writes...
  • Root Down is a restaurant offering globally inspired seasonal cuisine with high-level service and a funky casual atmosphere. The menu incorporates fresh ingredients from local growers as well as sustainable meats and fish. The midcentury modern décor is infused with recycled and reclaimed items creating its hip progressive spirit. Root Down is a one of a kind restaurant with a story-rich and sophisticated dining experience. Root Down is happy to accommodate guests with allergies or special dietary needs such as Gluten Intolerances and Veganism.

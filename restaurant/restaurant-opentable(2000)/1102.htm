Forum

writes...
  • Forum is a celebration of American history, culture and culinary diversity. Located in City Center next to the Marriott on 7th Street between Hennepin and Nicollet. The stunning Art Deco interior dates back to the 1920"s and fondly remembered as The Forum Cafeteria. Forum features salads, steaks, fresh seafood and regional American comfort food. The scratch bar follows suit offering fresh squeezed juices, a sophisticated cocktail menu that compliments not only the grand decor but also the cuisine and a full wine list.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Downtown Minneapolis
  • Cross Street: Hennepin Avenue
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://forumrestaurantmn.com/
  • Email: christian@ringorestaurants.com
  • Phone: (612) 354-2017
  • Hours of Operation: Monday-Friday 11am to 10pm Saturday 5pm to 10pm Sunday 5pm to 9pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Christian Ticarro
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Delivery, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Forum is located just 2 blocks from the Metro Transit Light Rail as well as on several local bus lines.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Forum offers curb-side valet service to all our guests beginning at 6pm each evening. It is located at the front door on 7th Street. Forum is also very close to some of the best hotels in Minneapolis. The Marriott, Radisson, Grand Hotel, Graves 601, Marquette, Hyatt, and Hilton to name just a few.
  • Catering: We are available for take out or take out with delivery, as well as other events that fit our menu and style.

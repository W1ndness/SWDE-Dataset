Shaw's Crab House - Schaumburg

writes...
  • Big & Bustling, Shaw's Crab house stands as a monument to fish & seafood. Besides the usual popular dishes, Shaw's management team is passionate about presenting treats such as Pacific salmon, fresh king crab, & smelt during their short seasons. The Oyster Bar is open all day long & for lunch on weekends. Reservations made through Open Table will be for Shaw's Main Dining Room. If you would like to dine in the Oyster Bar, please choose "Shaw's Oyster Bar" when making a reservation.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak, American
  • Neighborhood: Schaumburg
  • Cross Street: 53 & Higgins
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.shawscrabhouse.com
  • Phone: (847) 517-2722
  • Hours of Operation: Main Dining Room: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 9:00pm Friday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm Saturday: 5:00pm - 9:30pm Sunday Brunch: 10:30am - 1:30pm Oyster Bar: Monday - 11:30 - 9:00pm, Tuesday - Thursday: 11:30am - 10:00pm, Friday: 11:30am - 10:30pm, Saturday: 12:00pm - 10:30pm, Sunday: 12:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Juvenal Reyes
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Parking is complimentary on Monday - Friday lunch between 11:30-2:00pm. Available Mon - Sun evenings for $5.00.
  • Catering: Pre-set packages are available with a menu consisting of a combination of the main dining room and the Oyster Bar.
  • Private Party Facilities: There are both private & semi-private rooms available that can accommodate 8-150 guests. There is also a pre-set menu.
  • Private Party Contact: Lura Reyes (847) 517-2722
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Join us this Thanksgiving for a special holiday meal. Shaw’s will offer a special family style meal featuring traditional Thanksgiving dishes, along with some Shaw’s signature specialties. So gather the family and make your reservations today!

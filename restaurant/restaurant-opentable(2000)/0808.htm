Rococo Restaurant and Fine Wine

writes...
  • Rococo Restaurant and Fine Wine is located at 2824 N. Pennsylvania Ave, in the location that was Tony’s Italian Specialties for many years. The menu is diverse along with carefully selected wines, spirits and beer. An international menu where everything is hand crafted with so many appealing dishes you will feel compelled to come again and try the dishes you missed! Chef owned and operated, we are obsessive about quality and service. The atmosphere, comfortable and stylish, whether at the bar, by the cozy fireplace or in one of our “gangster” booths. There is always something happening at ROCOCO!

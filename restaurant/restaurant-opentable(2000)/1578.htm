Pazzo

writes...
  • Just in time for al fresco dining, Pazzo (Italian for “crazy”) Restaurant and Bar is now open on historic Newbury Street in Boston, MA. This Meditteranean inspired restaurant offers two floors of indoor dining and bars in addition to prime patio seating. Patrick Buben and Vina DePaolo, who in addition to these two restaurants, own and operate North End favorite Bacco, have been in the business for well over 20 years and have every reason to anticipate yet another success.

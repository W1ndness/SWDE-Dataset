Alden Park Bar and Grill

writes...
  • EAT: Alden Park is a casual upscale restaurant featuring creative American cuisine. Stemming from Chef Armando"s expansive palate, our menu features an array of classic dishes with a current, fresh twist.

    SEE: The modern and inviting interior, designed by the acclaimed Steve Todisco, blends a mix of straight lines with flowing curves. Your eyes will be drawn up to the vaulted ceilings by the large archways, highlighted by metallic blue, sea green, and warm brown tones. In the middle of it all stands the eye catching bar area, with its white bar top contrasting a dark wood facade, and topped off with a cool blue glow that surrounds the bar like a halo. RELAX: Whether out for late night drinks or an intimate dinner for 2, Alden Park offers the perfect atmosphere for any occasion.

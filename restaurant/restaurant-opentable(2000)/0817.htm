Cava - Bay Harbor

writes...
  • Cava sits on the waterfront next to Bay Harbor Marina in The Village of Bay Harbor. Cava seats 60 in the main dining room with additional bar & lounge seating as well as a newly redone “marina side” outdoor patio. Hungry? Cava features hearth fired pizzas, appetizers, salads, steaks, fresh seafood & more. Thirsty? We have a huge wine list, draft beer, bottled beer, full bar or try one of our signature drinks such as the “cavatini” . Cava offers patio dining for both lunch and dinner. We open at 11 am every day and we close just before the cow’s come home. Cava features a private dining space upstairs for 60 seated guests or 100 standing guests. We also have a separate lounge with a pool table that is open to the public or can be rented for private functions.

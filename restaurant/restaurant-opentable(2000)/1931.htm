Tao

writes...
  • A 16-foot Buddha, which floats above a reflecting pool filled with Japanese carp, presides over the scene at Tao. A favorite destination for celebrities and athletes, this Pan Asian restaurant specializes in authentic Hong Kong Chinese, Japanese and Thai cuisines. Tao has three levels of dining including the prized "Skybox" which offers views of this former movie theater unparalleled in New York. In addition to its 300 seats, Tao includes a sushi bar, lounge and two bars at which to enjoy the food and the show. Appetizers range from $8 - $18; Entrees: $16 -$39. Offering $24.07 Prix Fixe Lunch Menu year round.

    Our prized Skybox offers a superb private dining experience, overlooking the entire restaurant from its two-story, 35-foot high perch.    This exclusive nest, with its own private restroom, is available seven days a week for dinner, and Monday through Friday for lunch.

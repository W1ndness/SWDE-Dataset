Spezia Omaha

writes...
  • Spezia Restaurant ~ Serving Omaha for 6 years with an upscale-casual restaurant located in Omaha's Midtown area just north of I-80. Featuring Italian, Steaks, & Seafood in a warm and comfortable environment. Happy Hour M-Sat 4-6pm Half Off Beer, Wine, and Cocktails. Spezia originated in Sioux Falls, SD 13 years ago and has since opened another concept called Bracco. By the summer 2008, the company will have 2 Spezia locations and 2 Bracco locations: Sioux Falls,SD; Omaha,NE; and Okoboji, IA.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Midtown
  • Cross Street: Spring
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.speziarestaurant.com
  • Email: spezia.omaha@gmail.com
  • Phone: (402) 391-2950
  • Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 2:30pm, Saturday: 11:00am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Brian Reilly
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking available in restaurant's main lot and also in Ronald J. Palagi's Law Office lot next door.
  • Catering: Large Group To-Go menu featuring family style pastas and salads. Perfect for Business meetings, pharmaceutical lunches, or private events. Delivery Available. Please call for more details 402-391-2950.
  • Private Party Facilities: Spezia dining rooms are designed to have a warm and comforting feel. Complete with a wood mini bar, original artwork, wood table tops, carpet, and a Bose sound system. For groups under 20 guests full menu or limited menu is available. For groups over 20, an appointment is requested so that a menu can be discussed. Room “R” has a big window facing the main patio, and a private patio for guests. This room can be used for groups between 10-30 guests. The room has a $100 (M-TH) or $200 (F-Sat) charge that is waived if $1500 or more is spent. Room “W” has original art work, a wooden bar, and an impressive wine display. This room can be used by 25-50 guests, with a $150 (M-TH) or $300 (F-Sat) room charge or $2500 spent. When both rooms are opened up, they can accommodate groups up to 100 with a $250 (M-TH) or $500 (F-Sat) room charge or $3000 in food and beverage. It’s important to reserve the rooms as soon as possible, they do book fast.
  • Private Party Contact: Felicity Turco (402) 391-2950

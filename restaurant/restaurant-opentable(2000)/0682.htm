  • NAHA offers a seasonal contemporary American menu that comes alive with the passions and flavors of the Mediterranean and effortlessly blends the culinary, design and management talents of the cousins Nahabedian.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Mediterranean, Fusion / Eclectic
  • Neighborhood: River North
  • Cross Street: Illinois
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.naha-chicago.com
  • Phone: (312) 321-6242
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch, Monday - Friday 11:30 - 2:00p.m. Dinner, Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm Sunday: Closed (some Sundays open for major conventions)
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa

  • Dress Code: Smart Casual

  • Public Transit: Red Line - Grand stop Clark Bus

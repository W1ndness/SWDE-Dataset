Mara's Homemade

writes...
  • A family owned and operated restaurant serving Southern cuisine featuring Cajun and Arkansas barbecue. We fly live crawfish, oysters, crabs and shrimp into NYC 2 to 3 times a week, serving a traditional Louisiana Crawfish Boil daily. Arkansas style BBQ is also available. Hurricanes, Mint Juleps and Abita beer are featured.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Barbecue, Seafood, Cajun
  • Neighborhood: East Village
  • Cross Street: 1st and 2nd Avenues
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.marashomemade.com
  • Email: david@marashomemade.com
  • Phone: (212) 598-1110
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday, Wednesday and Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:30pm, Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm Open FAT TUESDAY, February 20, 2007. Closed on Tuesdays.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa

  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine

  • Parking: Street Parking

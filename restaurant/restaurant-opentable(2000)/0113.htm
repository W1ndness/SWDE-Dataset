Buckeye Tavern

writes...
  • GREAT FOOD & DRINK GREAT ATMOSPHERE & FUN FOR THE WHOLE FAMILY Since 1735, this location in Macungie has been a local landmark, serving the finest in tavern food & libations to several generations of regular patrons & visiting guests. Opened as an inn & hotel in 1735, operating through the late 1800s as the East Macungie Hotel & later as The Load of Mischief, the property has long been in the business of serving customers with their own unique local style – a blend of true hospitality & fine food. A visit to the Buckeye today offers a glimpse into the past of this area of Pennsylvania & life at the “local roadhouse.” Pay attention to the pieces of the past that are on display throughout the restaurant that honor the history of the building. Join us as we offer the finest food, perfectly prepared & served with style. We look forward to having you as our guests. It's where you go!

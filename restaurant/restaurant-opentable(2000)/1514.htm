Ceres Bistro at Beechwood Hotel

writes...
  • Ceres Bistro at The Beechwood Hotel is a farm to table bistro dedicated to offering the freshest and best quality products available. Our seasonal menu will change to highlight our sources. Whenever possible we will feature locally grown and raised products. Our Chef"s use classic culinary technique to highlight these amazing ingredients and the atmosphere at Ceres is modern and serene that sets the tone for an elegant dining experience. Our service and hospitality is unmatched as we take you on an affair to remember.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Worcester
  • Cross Street: Belmont Street
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.ceresbistro.com
  • Email: manager@ceresbistro.com
  • Phone: (508) 754-2000
  • Hours of Operation: Breakfast: Monday - Friday: 6:30am - 10:30am, Saturday & Sunday: 7:30am - 10:30am
    Lunch: Daily: 11:30am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 4:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Volker Wagner
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Parking Details: Parking is very extensive at Ceres Bistro with a large lot for self parking as well as complimentary valet parking at the entrance to the restaurant.
  • Private Party Facilities: We offer several options for private dining at Ceres Bistro from our Executive Board Room with Private Lounge to our Club Room with Private Outdoor Patio Space and Outdoor Bar to our Wine Room with views of our extensive wine collection.
  • Private Party Contact: Lynn Cheney (508) 453-1192

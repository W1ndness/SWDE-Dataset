Il Mulino - Long Island

writes...
  • Buoníssimo! Born in Greenwich Village, Il Mulino New York was created with a gourmand’s love for food and a divine calling to bring native dishes from Abruzzo, Italy to the United States. Rated Zagat's Best Italian for over twenty years, Il Mulino's cuisine speaks for itself in intense, lavishly delicious tones. With its Long Island outpost, the restaurant proudly serves that famous food with an unrivalled elegance. Comely waiters in black tie unobtrusively satisfy your every whim moments before you think it. Even the ultra-cognoscenti are amazed by the exquisite perfection of this dining experience. The dish par excellence is the Osso Buco, which strikes a balance of surprising lightness between its highest quality veal and delectable sauce. With a fabulously overwhelming selection of pastas, meats, and delicate desserts, Il Mulino New York showcases impressive, distinctive tastes that are unforgettable. Sunday Supper: 4:00pm 4 courses; $39 per person. Kids under 13; $20

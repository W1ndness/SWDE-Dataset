The Grill at Hacienda del Sol

writes...
  • The Grill at Hacienda del Sol is one of Tucson's outstanding restaurant's located in a beautiful setting at the base of Tucson's Catalina Foothill's. Breathtaking mountain and city views are captured from The Grill's dining room where you will be treated to "Spirited Southwestern Hospitality" and a remarkable menu created by Executive Chef Ramiro Scavo. Ramiro recently won the title of "Iron Chef Tucson" and is the city's reigning champion Chef. The Grill's wine list was recently bestowed "The Award of Ultimate Distinction" by Wine Enthusiast Magazine and has been awarded the "Best Of Award of Excellence" from Wine Spectator for ten years in a row. A great place to dine for a special occasion or a relaxing night away from home. There is a charming open air bar patio with entertainment on the weekends for those who prefer a casual setting featuring our Terraza Menu.

Venice Ristorante and Wine Bar is the perfect place for your next function, be it business, holiday parties, family functions, weddings or birthdays, Venice has much to offer to make sure your event is a success. With innovative menu options offering classic Italian cuisine combined with modern technique as well as one of Wine Spectator's top wine lists in the country. Our wait staff is experienced and professional and will cater to your needs so that your experience is worry free. Our restaurant is located at the base of Cherry Hills in Greenwood Village on the corner of Orchard and Holly Street, in The Orchards shopping center. We have a main dining area that is available for rental for up to 120 people and two seperate dining rooms that can accomodate 16 to 30 people. Private dining menus range from $49 per person to $75 dollars per person. You are sure to fall in love with the vivid ambiance of Italy paired with authentic Italian cuisine.

red/bar brasserie

writes...
  • red/bar has the look of a sophisticated Hamptons house: creamy white walls, hardwood floors, potted palms, and wainscoting. Newsday reviewer Peter Gianotti commented, “The place has a glow to it.” New York Magazine wrote, “With its bentwood chairs, large windows, and candlelight, it manages to be both social and romantic.” Red bar’s outstanding service – “Super” according to the New York Times – ensures that whatever the occasion, red bar brasserie delivers a wonderful dining experience.

  • Cuisine: American, Contemporary American

  • Cross Street: Lewis Street

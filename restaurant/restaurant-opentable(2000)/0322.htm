  • Acqua Restaurant Located off the main lobby on the seventh floor, Acqua offers innovative contemporary Seafood with a Miami flair. Pair your meal with a bottle from our award-winning list of more than 200 labels, including our “green wines”. For lunch, enjoy our Botero Lunch Break offering a two-course prix fixe menu for $22 or three-courses for $28. International flavors and traditional favorites make our Sunday Brunch one you won't want to miss. Enjoy a selection of action stations, a Champagne cocktail bar, unlimited Mojitos and an expansive dessert buffet. Acqua features outdoor seating overlooking the Hotel's lush pool terrace. 14 Thirty-Five Located across from Acqua restaurant, 14 Thirty-Five offers a tantalizing array of appetizers to accompany signature mojitos, specialty martinis or a glass of wine. 14 Thirty-Five is currently serving dinner, every day of the week, where Acqua's innovative menu can be enjoyed in a more intimate setting.

Loon River Cafe

writes...
  • The Loon River Cafe is critically acclaimed throughout the metro Detroit area. Award winning Chef Ray Hollinsworth has put together a menu that features "the best Michigan has to offer" in this North Woods lodge-like setting, Loon River Cafe is a genuine treat for those seeking a new affordable adventure in dining. Open daily for breakfast, lunch, dinner and Sunday brunch.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Sterling Heights
  • Cross Street: 15 mile
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.sterlinginn.com/Loon%20River/Loon.htm
  • Email: roberth@sterlinginn.com
  • Phone: (586) 979-1420
  • Hours of Operation: Breakfast: Monday - Friday: 6:00am - 11:00am, Saturday & Sunday: 7:00am - 11:00am
    Lunch: Daily: 11:00am - 4:30pm
    Dinner: Monday - Friday: 4:30pm - 11:00pm, Saturday: 4:30pm - 12:00am, Sunday: 4:30pm - 10:00pm Please contact us for your holiday reservations.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ray Hollingsworth, C.E.C.
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Chef's Table, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: M-Dot stop right in front of our hotel facility.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Large private parking area under 24 hour security surveillance.
  • Catering: Off-site catering available through the banquet facility.
  • Private Party Facilities: Chef Ray's Private Dining Room seats up to 12. The Atrium can hold up to 30 and has a view of the fireplace and a large collection of Pewabic pottery tiles.

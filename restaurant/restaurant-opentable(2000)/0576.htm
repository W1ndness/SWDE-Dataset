Republic Chophouse

writes...
  • Republic Chophouse is the ultimate in steakhouse dining with steak, seafood, chops, casual bar food, and decadent desserts. The moment you walk into our restaurant you know you're in for something different. We are redefining the urban steakhouse with a departure from the "stuffy boy's club" and a swan dive into the energized world of stimulating cuisine in a relaxed atmosphere. Our "no problem" philosophy promises the very best in food, service, and atmosphere. Join the Republic.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, American
  • Neighborhood: Green Bay
  • Cross Street: Cherry Street, Pine Street, Walnut Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50

  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking is a breeze at the Republic Chophouse with on street parking and a public lot nearby, both are free after 6:00 pm. Republic Chophouse is also proud to be offering valet parking.
  • Private Party Facilities: Be it a corporate meeting, birthday party, or just a get together, Republic Chophouse has a room with your name on it. Choose from their three private rooms, or if you have enough friends book the entire restaurant. GLASS ROOM Dinner seating for 10 in an intimate and posh setting for your exclusive private party. PRIVATE ROOM Dinner seating for 12 to 30. This room offers the ultimate in privacy with solid sliding doors and a secluded location. It is perfect for business events with wiring for A/V presentations and a built-in screen and projector. PLANK & COPPER ROOM Our largest section of the main dining room with plenty of flexible space for groups of 25 to 40.

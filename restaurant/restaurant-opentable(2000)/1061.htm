Matteo's Ristorante Italiano

writes...
  • Matteo's is THE authentic Italian restaurant in Indianapolis. Voted one of the top 25 restaurants in Indianapolis for the 4th year in a row. Matteo's is a wonderful dining experience, perfect for business or pleasure, and famous for stuffed meats, homemade bread & delicious pasta.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Indianapolis
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.matteosindy.com
  • Email: damatteo@sbcglobal.net
  • Phone: (317) 774-9771
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:30pm
    Sunday: 11:00am - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Matteo DiRossa
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Parking on street or in Lots 1 block in every direction from restaurant. Lot at the corner of 10th & Conner St. is available after 5:00pm.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private upstairs room seats up to 65 people. Bar available upstairs. Perfect for parties, meetings & small weddings.
  • Private Party Contact: Emily DiRosa (317) 774-9771

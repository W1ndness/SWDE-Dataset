Barclay Bar and Grill

writes...
  • The Barclay Bar & Grill is a popular meeting spot for both hotel guests and New Yorkers alike. Located off the hotel's elegant Lobby, the Barclay Grill is renowned for its outstanding International cuisine, offering guests a fresh and innovative dining experience. The cuisine combines a simple, elegant presentation with the season's freshest ingredients to create an exciting new approach to light and healthy dining. Additionally, experience Executive Chef Carmine Marletta’s Antipasto Bar as well as our “Natural Power” 100% organic breakfast buffet. The buzz of New York can be felt each night in the Barclay Bar, which is the perfect place to relax and unwind with one of our specialty cocktails, prepared by our mixologist, after a busy day in the city.

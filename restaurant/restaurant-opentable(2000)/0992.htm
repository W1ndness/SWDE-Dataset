Servino Restaurant

writes...
  • Located on the waterfront in the beautiful town of Tiburon, Servino Restaurant enjoys views of the San Francisco skyline and Angel Island. Inspired by the warm hospitably and culinary delights of Southern Italy, Servino specializes in homemade pastas, fresh seafood, and locally influenced interpretations of classic Italian favorites. Dine on the outdoor patio, listen to live music or have a drink in the bar and cocktail lounge, or host your next special event in the upstairs private banquet room. Whatever the occasion, Servino is the perfect place to celebrate "la dolce vita".

Servino hosts breath-taking views of the San Francisco skyline, Angel Island and the Belvedere Marina. Our menu reflects chef-owner Angelo Servino’s love for his homeland’s southern Italian cooking. We specialize in housemade pastas, fresh seafood, and locally inspired traditional Italian favorites. The second floor Bellavista Room is a beautiful and charming private event space with its own bar and lounge area, as well as a waterfront deck overlooking the bay. The room can accommodate groups of 25 to 135 for sit-down or buffet meals, and up to 200 for a cocktail reception. We have hosted intimate ceremonies on our private patio and many celebratory receptions in our main banquet room. No event is too small, too formal, too simple or too complicated for our staff to coordinate. Whether you are looking for the perfect place for a birthday party or business function, rehearsal dinner or wedding reception, we will be more than happy to help make each event an unforgettable occasion.

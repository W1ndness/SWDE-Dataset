Caffe Phoenix

writes...
  • Part fiery mythical entity reborn out of ashes, part Italian coffee, Caffe Phoenix has served fresh, innovative cuisine in a comfortable bistro style atmosphere since 1989. Our open, well-lit space with large windows overlooking bustling Front Street serves the dual purpose of perfect people-watching restaurant and commission-free gallery dedicated to supporting the arts with a new exhibition opening monthly.

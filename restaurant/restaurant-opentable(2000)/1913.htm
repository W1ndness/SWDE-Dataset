SerRa Mediterranean BisTro

writes...
  • Serra Mediterranean Bistro brings an innovative perspective on adding a healthy twist to authentic Mediterranean specialties from regions such as Spain, France, Italy, Greece, Turkey and Northern Africa. The Mediterranean Bistro stemmed from co-owners Cesare and Tio's passion in serving fresh, local, "0" mile healthy dishes as they spent years in the Mediterranean basin for the love of the local dishes by learning how to create the art of handcrafting local Mediterranean dishes utilizing generations old techniques. Both were inspired by how the menus of family owned local restaurant menus rotated frequently, creating dishes that highlighted the freshest local ingredients of each season at the top of their flavors.

    Serra's menu is created with this motto, providing not only classical innovated dishes filled with fresh ingredients, but also a wide variety of regional specialties rotating frequently.

Vivas

writes...
  • Vivas Classic Latin Cuisine, located in Belmar Plaza on 8th Avenue, is the premiere Latin restaurant along the Jersey Shore. Customers dine on the freshest seafood selections and eclectic Latin fare while surrounded by Vivas’ charming Latin décor. Vivas is a BYOB restaurant with indoor and outdoor seating and a newly renovated coffee house, featuring various gourmet coffees and desserts. Vivas Classic Latin Cuisine is Zagat rated and has been featured in the Artful Diner and The New York Times. Vivas has won numerous awards, including being voted “Best Tapas” in 2010 by the readers of Monmouth Health and Life Magazine.

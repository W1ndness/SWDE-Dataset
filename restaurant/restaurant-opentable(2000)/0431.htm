Sazo's Latin Grill

writes...
  • A unique experience is emerging in San Antonio. One that captures the mosaic of flavors and entertainment you can only find in Spanish and Latin American cultures. With grand spaces crossing the realm of rich flamenco reds, the subtle yellows of an Argentine sunrise, and ornate iron arrives Sazo's Latin Grill. A fusion of flavors and a passion for daring. Grand picture windows overlook San Antonio's River Walk, as guests catch glimpses of the riverboats and the bustling of the crowds. As afternoon turn to evening the bluecast sky gives way to glistening lights sparkling over the river. Sazo's Latin Grill... Feel the Flavor, Taste the Heat..

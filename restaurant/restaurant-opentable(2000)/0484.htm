  • Fresh seafood, tender steaks and a variety of salads and pasta expertly prepared for your enjoyment. JAXX Steakhouse welcomes you to taste the renowned JAXX Pepper Steak and our Chilean Sea Bass -a symphony of flavors you won't find anywhere else. With a decor of dark woods, cut glass and brass accents you may feel that you're in a classic New York or San Francisco restaurant. A tradition of value in Addison since 1992; this independent restaurant with a full bar offers daily specials, fine wines, and JAXX signature dishes in a casual, fine dining experience. We also have a private meeting room.

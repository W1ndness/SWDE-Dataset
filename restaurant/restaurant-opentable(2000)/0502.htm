Ristorante Nicola

writes...
  • Ristorante Nicola serves authentic, hand-crafted Italian cuisine using recipes and cooking methods developed over centuries. Its sleek interior is a dreamy combination of contemporary design and timeless elements. An open kitchen lends a theatrical component to your dining experience, and two private dining rooms allow groups of 14 to 50 to entertain in the abundant manner of La Bella Italia. For great people watching and cocktails, visit the gorgeous loggia for the most glorious outdoor seating area in the Park Cities.

  • Cuisine: Italian

  • Parking: Valet

Inglenook Restaurant

writes...
  • The Inglenook Restaurant is-as the name implies-cozy, warm, and comfortable. The Inglenook greets customers with an ambiance of warm colors, a distinctive art collection and soft soothing jazz music that sets the tone for a very enjoyable and relaxed dining experience. The Inglenook Restaurant is somewhat unobtrusive for first time visitors. It is located at 530 North Cleveland Ave. in downtown Loveland Co. at the corner of 6th and Cleveland Ave. There is plenty of parking on both sides of the street and parking lot behind the restaurant. Whatever the occasion- special or not- the Inglenook is definitely a dining choice to be added to anyone's list, and once visited, will remain a top choice for many years to come.

Cu29 Wine and Bistro

writes...
  • A delightful Sayville restaurant, with Bar, lounge, and welcoming dining rooms. Creative and exciting food which includes Oysters, Seafood sampler, rich soups, various flat breads and a refreshing roster of salads. Entrees priced so that some should be shared and some should be savored. Mustard crusted Salmon, Coco Dusted Short Ribs, Seared Day Boat Scallops with Wild Mushroom Risotto, Roast Lamb with Mint Demi-Glace, Lobster & Gnocchi with Sweet English Peas, Cajun Grilled Mahi and Prime NY Strip Au Poivre. Desserts, Espresso and an assortment of fine Ports and Cordials help to finish your evening. A full Bar with Specialty Martinis, extensive wine list and an assortment of interesting beer in bottles. Our seamless service will help to make for a relaxing and fun evening.

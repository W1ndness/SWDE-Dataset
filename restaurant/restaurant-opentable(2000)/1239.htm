Chad's Steakhouse & Saloon

writes...
  • Chad's Steakhouse has been locally owned and operated for over 13 years. A Tucson favorite, Chad's offers some of the best Prime Rib and Steaks you can find in a casual, relaxed atmosphere. Our distinctive salads and famous baked cheese bread set the tone for a wonderful dining experience. We have just introduced a fantastic lunch menu to go along with our ever-popular dinner menu. Filled with western memorabilia, this great Northeast location offers a little bit of the Old West.

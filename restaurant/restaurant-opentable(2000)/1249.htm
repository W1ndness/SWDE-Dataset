The Flying V

writes...
  • Your host, Chef d'Cuisine, Josh Willett welcomes you to his lovely Southwestern style grill with views to die for and food and wine pairings by Manager, H.Rock Saul that will thrill your culinary senses. Chef Willett has a strong local following in the Southwest area and Arizona. His mastery of flavors and exciting presentations will excite anyone.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Southwest
  • Neighborhood: Tucson
  • Cross Street: North Kolb/Sunrise
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.flyingvbarandgrill.com
  • Email: rsaul@loewshotels.com
  • Phone: (520) 615-5495
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday 5:30 - 9:00 Friday & Saturday 5:30 - 10:00
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Chef Alexis Martines
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cigar Room, Counter Seating, Delivery, Fireplace, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Smoking Area, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet and self parking are available.
  • Catering: Please contact General Manager,H.Rock Saul, for any of your catering needs. (520) 615-5495 or rsaul@loewshotels.com.
  • Private Party Facilities: Call Rock Saul to book your group onto the lovely Flying V patio which overlooks the 18th hole of one of two signature Tom Fazio courses, complimented by Chef Willett's flavorful Southwestern cuisine.

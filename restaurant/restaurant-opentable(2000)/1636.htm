Conveniently located in midtown, '21' offers 10 beautifully appointed private rooms in an elegant townhouse setting. Parties of 10 to 200 for lunch or dinner and up to 400 for a reception will enjoy our Grand Award-winning wine list, sumptuous American cuisine, and discreetly attentive service. Inclusive packages from $125. Plan an event in our legendary Prohibition-era wine cellar for a truly incomparable dining experience. The famous Bar Room is also available for dine-arounds. Offering convenience, incomparable service, and value, '21' is equipped to meet all of your private event needs. Our event specialists and in-house audio/visual team can help you with all your planning needs. Whether you are hosting a corporate function or family celebration, our goal is to ensure that everything goes without a hitch. We want you to be able to sit back and enjoy your event as much as your guests!

Bar Harbor Club

writes...
  • Bar Harbor Club sets the standard for waterfront dining in Bar Harbor. Breathtaking Oceanfront elegance awaits you each time you dine at the historic Bar Harbor Club. Originally built in the 1800's, the Bar Harbor Club has enjoyed a long, storied history of entertaining the luminaries of its time. Today, it has been restored to its former grandeur offering a classic New England feel with old world club charm. Spacious windows circling the room offer panoramic ocean views, polished antique woods, fine linens and experienced staff take you back to another time in our Club Dining Room. A talented culinary crew prepares a sumptuous array of signature entrees featuring innovative and seasonally inspired interpretations of classic New England cuisine. Dining at the club epitomizes the New England spirit - very much in the present but grounded in the time-honored pleasure of the past.

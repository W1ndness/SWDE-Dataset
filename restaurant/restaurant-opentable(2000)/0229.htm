  • La Costa Grill is a classic American Grill & Bar, offering fresh seafood, steaks, pastas, sandwiches and salads in our dining room, bar and covered courtyard. We offer an extensive wine list, full bar, and one of San Diego’s best selections of tequilas and premium liquors. Be sure to come and watch your favorite sporting events in our lounge, and enjoy fresh oysters, seafood and appetizers from our Oyster Bar.

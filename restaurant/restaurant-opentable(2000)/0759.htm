TRIO - Four Seasons Austin

writes...
  • Described as "the best steakhouse in the city" by Austin restaurant critic Dale Rice, TRIO offers USDA prime steaks, fresh seafood and an extensive wine list, along with a convenient downtown setting overlooking Lady Bird Lake. A vibrant addition to the downtown restaurant scene, TRIO's creative menus and stylish décor encourage lively conversations and a memorable dining experience, whether sitting on the outdoor patio, in the main dining area or at the bustling Wine Bar. Executive Chef Elmar Prambs' emphasis on local and seasonal ingredients ensures only the freshest, most flavorful dishes, from expertly prepared entrées to inventive side dishes, appetizers and desserts. Our wine list offers more than 300 bottles and 30 wines by the glass. Sunday Brunch -- widely considered Austin's best -- features an expansive buffet, including eggs Benedict; carving, griddle and omelet stations; fresh seafood and salads; traditional breakfast selections; and a decadent dessert bar

Mint Leaf - Berkeley

  • Mint Leaf is family owned and operated. We are passionately committed to making the best tasting food while keeping it healthy at the same time. The menu features authentic Indian food prepared with innovative, creative flair. Our renowned chefs are devoted to serving our guests dishes made with freshness and goodness. We rely on seasonal organic produce and free range meat from local farmers. All of our Indian spices are fresh and of artisan quality, and handpicked by our own family members in India. Our passion for food and thirty years of experience drives us to bring our guests the best tasting and highest quality cuisine.

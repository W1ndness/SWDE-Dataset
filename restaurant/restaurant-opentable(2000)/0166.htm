Octo Waterfront Grille

writes...
  • OCTO Waterfront Grille is a seasonal outdoor restaurant featuring lobsters, seafood, steaks & pastas. At night OCTO turns into the areas top nightlife venue under the stars. OCTO host live bands & DJ Thursdays thru Sundays. Located in a marina on the Delaware River, OCTO brings the seashore atmosphere to an urban setting. Join us for lunch & dinner daily. For more information please check out www.octophilly.com or call 215.923.OCTO.

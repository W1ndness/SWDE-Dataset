Harvest at The Hotel Hershey

writes...
  • Located at The Hotel Hershey, Harvest offers genuine American cuisine made from the freshest local and regional ingredients. The menu features an innovative selection of both classic & traditional American fare served during lunch and dinner, with daily specials. For guests of all ages, Harvest offers a friendly, casual, & inviting ambiance where everyone can relax and enjoy the breathtaking views. Harvest features a bar and adjoining adult game room with two pool tables and shuffleboard, and offers a variety of beverages including micro-brews, hand crafted cocktails, and an American wine list. Outdoor dining available seasonally.

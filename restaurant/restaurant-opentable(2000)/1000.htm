Sundance Kitchen

  • Sundance Kitchen is a casual elegant restaurant located next door to the Sundance Kabuki Cinemas, across the street from Dosa. Our Chef focuses on locally grown and organic ingredients and dishes with a Pacific Islander flair. Centrally located near The Fillmore concert hall, Yoshi's Night Club, and the heart of the Fillmore Street shopping district..

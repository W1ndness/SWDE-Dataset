Blackfish - Conshohocken

writes...
  • Chef/owner Charles "Chip" Roman describes Blackfish as seafood centered, progressive American cuisine with French influences. Blackfish was voted Top 10 Restaurant in Philadelphia two years in a row and Best Restaurant Suburbs by Philadelphia Magazine. As well Best New BYOB by Mainline Life. Blackfish also received 3 Bells by acclaimed Philadelphia Inquirer restaurant critic, Craig Laban.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Contemporary American, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Conshohocken
  • Cross Street: 1st Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.blackfishrestaurant.com
  • Email: blackfishrestaurant@comcast.net
  • Phone: (610) 397-0888
  • Hours of Operation: We are closed for lunch until September 1st 2010 Dinner: Tuesday - Thursday: 5:30PM - 10:00PM Friday: 5:30 - 11:00PM Saturday: 5:00PM - 11:00PM Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm CLOSED MONDAY
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Charles J. Roman Jr.
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, BYO Liquor, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Public parking lot located at the corner of 1st Avenue and Fayette St. Street parking is available everywhere in Conshohocken
  • Catering: Charles Roman Catering. (Off site Catering) Please check our website: www.charlesromancatering.com
  • Private Party Facilities: Blackfish is available for buyouts.
  • Private Party Contact: Mickael Decoker (610) 397-0888
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Blackfish has special chef tasting every Tuesday evening. Each week chef/owner Chip Roman will feature a special seasonal ingredient and create a 4 course tasting menu for $45.00 per person. Please call 610-397-0888 for more information.

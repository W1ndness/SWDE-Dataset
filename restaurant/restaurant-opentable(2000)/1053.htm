BARcelona Tapas - Indianapolis

writes...
  • BARcelona Tapas prides itself on creating a traditional authentic Tapas Bar atmosphere. This includes a warm decor and the traditional hanging of Serrano hams and dried bacalaos. The service reflects a traditional tapas experience as you might find in Madrid, Barcelona or Sevilla. Small plates of food are prepared to order and delivered one or two at a time, as to encourage sharing of the plates and conversation together with great Sangria. Barcelona Tapas Indianapolis is an open air restaurant when the weather is nice! Come and enjoy one of our two large patios or sit at the bar and enjoy a breeze through the restaurant on warm summer days. We are also proud to support FC Barcelona! When at all possible we show the games with volume on. GO BARCA! Barcelona Tapas is proud to present our new 2 for $30 deal! Come in on Tuesday's for dinner and receive a six tapas meal for two for just $30!

  • Public Transit: There are numerous IndyGo bus stops on all streets surrounding us. Please see www.indygo.net/routes.htm for bus schedules.

There is a parking garage on the Northwest corner of Ohio and Delaware, a parking garage on the south side of Ohio street between Delaware and Alabama, and a $3 honor lot just north of our building on Delaware which is open after 5pm weekdays and all weekends.
  • Catering: Tapas Party at your home or office? We can do it! We accommodate everything from box lunches to full on-site catering and everything in-between! Please look at our website for more details or call 317-638-TAPA and ask for Scott to set up a delivery or full fledged Tapas Party with or without our Tapas Crew to serve it all for you!
  • Private Party Facilities: We have the space for you! Up to 30 of you - that is...... A seperate private dining room with large Barndoors that close, a flatscreen TV ready for your presentation, your own music and, of course, our "Party Platters" or simply choose from the Tapas Menu. Call us to help you make this a successful "private party" to remember. In the summer we also have a patio that can be reserved for parties which seats up to 25.
  • Private Party Contact: Scott Hauser (317) 638-8272

McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Dallas

writes...
  • Renowned as The Fresh Seafood Authority, McCormick & Schmick’s Seafood Restaurant is located in the upscale NorthPark Shopping Center and joins prestigious retailers such as Neiman Marcus, Nordstrom and Tiffany’s, to serve Dallas residents and tourists alike. The menu at McCormick & Schmick’s is printed twice daily and features more than 30 varieties of fresh seafood flown in from around the world, served in a timeless and traditional presentation. Enjoy your meal in the dining room, bar, or on the spacious outdoor patio, perfect for savoring the sun and warm breezes. Whether dining with friends while shopping, having dinner with family or enjoying drinks in the bar, McCormick & Schmick’s Seafood Restaurant is the perfect location for great food in a relaxed and sophisticated atmosphere.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak, American
  • Neighborhood: Park Cities
  • Cross Street: Northwest Hwy and Central Expressway
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa

  • Public Transit: We are accessible to and from downtown Dallas, the Dallas Convention Center, North Dallas, Richardson and Plano by the Dallas Area Rapid Transit (DART) Light Rail System. A complimentary NorthPark Center/DART Shuttle operates to and from the nearby Park Lane DART station and the west entrance to NorthPark Center near Nordstrom Court.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary for our diners.
  • Private Party Facilities: Our front dining room can accommodate groups up to 50 people seated in one area. However, the total room can accommodate up to 76 in an open table setup. With luxurious decor, our facilities are designed to accommodate your personal or business needs. Relax in the privacy of beautiful mahogany woodwork, beveled glass and stained lighting, all while enjoying McCormick & Schmick's signature fresh seafood. We also offer a selection of small private snugs that can seat up to 6 people. For groups of 18 or more, we do require a limited menu. Our special menu provides several choices of seafood and steak entrees at varying prices to fit your needs. We also offer an extensive list of wines from around the world. And a full bar featuring several vodkas and over 35 different selections of single malt scotches.
  • Private Party Contact: Peggy Frey (214) 891-0100
  • Entertainment: Be sure to visit us for our famous happy hour, featuring select menu items starting at $1.95. This famous Happy Hour menu is available both in the restaurant bar and on the bar patio. Drink specials are also available during happy hour. Our extensive happy hour menu is served weekdays, both early and late evenings. Don't forget to get your $5.00 movie tickets with every entree purchased. Then go upstairs to the AMC Northpark to enjoy your show. This promotion valid from July 24th until September 30th.

Fahrenheit Restaurant & Lounge

writes...
  • Experience the scene and cuisine at this cutting-edge lounge and restaurant. Fahrenheit is the South Bay’s premiere venue for dining, entertainment and special events. With innovative design concepts by award winning designer Jones Baker, Fahrenheit boasts a décor not yet seen in the South Bay. Deep hues of red, captivating lighting effects and chic architectural features radiate a modern vibe throughout. Incorporating various cooking techniques from around the world, a menu of Modern Global Cuisine offers items such as Pan-Seared Wild Alaskan Salmon, Asparagus Rib Eye Steak Rolls and Ahi Tuna Tartare Tostadas. Conveniently located in the heart of downtown San Jose, Fahrenheit is within walking distance from many local hotels, the San Jose Convention Center and a variety of performing arts venues that populate the area. All this, and more, in a sexy, sophisticated and stylish environment. This is the destination for those looking to experience the best that San Jose has to offer.

Fahrenheit Restaurant & Lounge’s menu and friendly service are available for private events in five unique spaces in our building. We can accommodate any type of event from 20 to 350 guests. Our event coordinator, Eric Haraga will work closely with you to create the perfect menu and to select the perfect wines. We are also happy to coordinate all the details: fresh flowers, decorations, AV equipment, and entertainment. Our unique and varied spaces, along with Fahrenheit’s highly personalized service, combine to create the perfect event for you.

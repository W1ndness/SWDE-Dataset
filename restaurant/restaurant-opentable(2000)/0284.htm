Old Vinings Inn

writes...
  • In the quaint town of Vinings, steeped in local history, visit one of Atlanta's oldest historic sites. Our charming building dates back to the early 1800's and has been lovingly preserved to provide you with a comfortable and gracious dining experience. The Vinings Inn offers Southern Cuisine and our award-winning wine list has been carefully selected to complement and enhance your dining experience.

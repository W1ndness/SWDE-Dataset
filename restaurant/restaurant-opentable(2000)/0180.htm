Sushi Planet - Philadelphia

  • Located in Philadelphia’s bustling Queen Village, Sushi Planet delivers and serves the best sushi this side of the moon. With a unique dining experience, friendly atmosphere, great sushi and music - this Philadelphia sushi restaurant is sure to please. Philly's Sushi Planet takes pride in preparing tasty sushi. With Sushi Planet's BYOB policy and scrumptious unique choices like the popular Apple Roll, Spicy Tuna Tempura or Emperor Roll, delicious sushi and sashimi, your craving for fresh Japanese food in Philly is sure to be met. This Philly Sushi restaurant also caters to the vegetarian in your party with healthy options. Reserve a table now or call Sushi Planet to inquire about catering or private party options.

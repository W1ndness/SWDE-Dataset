Redline Food and Drink

writes...
  • Located in the heart of Harvard Square, Redline caters to a hip cross-section of the city with its casually refined menu, buzzing bar scene and late night entertainment. Redline's approachably sophisticated cuisine is as diverse as the clientele it draws, boasting entrees such as Grilled Salmon and White Truffle Mac & Cheese, and appetizers like Roasted Vegetable with Mozzarella Empanadas, Calamari and Carnitas Sliders. Redline also offers an extensive selection of salads and sandwiches, making it a perfect choice for either a casual bite to eat or a nice dinner out experience.

  • Cuisine: Contemporary American

  • Cross Street: Between Winthrop & Eliot

  • Price: $30 and under

  • Public Transit: Harvard Square Redline T stop within walking distance as well as several bus routes that empty into Harvard Square
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Ample on street parking, Harvard Square Parking Garage directly above and valet available. Free Parking after 5PM in the Harvard Square Parking Garage Monday through Wednesday with the purchase of 2 dinner entrees.
  • Private Party Facilities: Redline's private room is the ideal setting to gather with friends, co-workers or family, for parties, meetings, celebrations and more. With special dinner and cocktail menus available, Redline's management will work with its kitchen and bar staff to build a memorable event to suit both your needs and your budget. For those wanting to extend their evenings in true VIP-style, Redline is now accepting reservations Wednesday through Saturday after 10pm, allowing guests to comfortably unwind at private tables.
  • Private Party Contact: Tyler Titherington (617) 491-9851
  • Entertainment: Since opening its doors in 2002, Redline, with its bustling bar scene, stylish surroundings and prime location, has caught the attention of area students, hip locals and movement-minded masses looking for a memorable night out on the town. Fast becoming one of the Boston area’s most talked-about evening destinations, Redline provides its cool clientele with sleekly-decorated digs in which to unwind and enjoy a full selection of beer, wine, and specialty cocktails. The after-dark vibe is driven by an impressive variety of DJs appearing Thursday through Saturday evening

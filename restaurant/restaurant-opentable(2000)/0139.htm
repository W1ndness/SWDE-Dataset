Our Private Dining Facilities

    Total Capacity 100

The Ballroom at Generations has been hosting memorable events for our guests for over 30 years and now with its renovation in 2008, its appeal has grown larger still. Generations' Ballroom plays host to civil service groups, political figures, philanthropic organizations, weddings, rehearsal dinners, showers, religious affairs, graduations, anniversaries and birthdays to name a few. The Ballroom is a flexible space too; it's floor-to-ceiling accoustic grade partitions allow for groups to reserve only a portion of the room for events for 50 guests or less. The following amenities for any function are also available; including private bar, Wi-Fi access, dance floor, podium, microphone, projector, projection screen, easels, private restrooms, and much more. Also, Generations' Ballroom is situated on ground level so it is easily accessible to those with disabilities. Call now to learn which of Generations' plush private areas will be perfect for your next group event.

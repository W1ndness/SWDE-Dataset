Hal's - Buckhead

writes...
  • A restaurant that triumphs in taste over trend, Hal's is not the typical highbrow restaurant so often equated with food of this quality. It is a place of white table cloths, flowers, warm brick, & candlelight.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Steak, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Buckhead
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.hals.net
  • Phone: (404) 261-0025
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
    Bar opens at 4:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Daniel Garcia

Rum Bar

writes...
  • Located just off Philadelphia’s famed Rittenhouse Square Park, Rum Bar offers a unique escape & strikes the perfect balance between upbeat & intimate, lively, but laid back. Music ranges from contemporary Rock ‘n’ Roll to Reggae to late-night lounge.
    A tempting menu pivots on Caribbean influences, while staying grounded by fusing island flavors & American favorites.
    While boasting well over 150 rums, Rum Bar has defined itself as Philadelphia’s premiere place for sampling this complex & spellbinding spirit. A rum-flight program is the perfect opportunity for sampling & expanding one’s knowledge of this age-old elixir. A unique wine program features select vintages hailing only from South America.
    Owner/mixologist Adam Kanter’s carefully designed cocktail menu stretches the palate. Not a rum enthusiast? Rum Bar’s highly trained bartenders will introduce you to the spirit in a way catered only to you.

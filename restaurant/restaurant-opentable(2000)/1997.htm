Bistro Aix

writes...
  • A city-hip restaurant located in Jacksonville’s historic San Marco district, Bistro Aix brings an intriguing mix of vibrant sights, sounds, and flavors to Jacksonville. Chef Tom Gray’s seasonal Mediterranean and French bistro-inspired menu features wood-fired oven and grilled specialties including pizzas, pastas, risottos, steaks and seafood, complemented by professional, unpretentious service. A Wine Spectator Restaurant Award recipient, Bistro Aix offers over 250 wines by the bottle and 50 by the glass. A wide variety of hand-crafted cocktails and specialty martinis are available from the full bar. The bustling main dining room is highlighted by original 1940s brickwork, a curved marble bar and an open kitchen. A second dining room includes a glowing backlit onyx bar and open-view wine cellar that bring the feel of the big city to the cozy neighborhood of San Marco. Outside, an intimate dining patio is accented with oversized flowerpots and climbing vines

Bistro AIX's on-site private dining rooms are the perfect venue for corporate meetings, rehearsal dinners, wedding receptions, birthday parties, or any special occasion. Beautifully appointed with glowing candelabras, original artwork and French antiques, as well as integrated audio visual/media presentation screens, the private dining rooms can easily accommodate groups of all sizes up to 100 guests. Our professional catering team will work with you to create an event that is special and distinctly yours. Selecting items from our seasonal menu and pairing cocktails or wines to enhance your guests' enjoyment, we can help take the guesswork out of event planning so you can focus on enjoying your party. As a distinctly different, locally-owned and operated restaurant, we enjoy sharing our unique ambiance, Chef Tom Gray's award-winning seasonal wine and food menus, and warm service with you.

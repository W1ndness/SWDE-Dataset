Wild Vine Bistro

writes...
  • Wild Vine Bistro serves up Mill Creek & Bothell’s finest foods! At Wild Vine Bistro, every dish is created using only the freshest, finest ingredients. We serve only the choicest meats & seafood, the freshest vegetables, & breads & pastries that are baked fresh daily. We have an eclectic wine menu that ranges from local Washington wines to California & all over Italy. We offer two happy hours daily in our full bar. Live Music five nights a week.

Ocean Prime - Miami

writes...
  • Introducing Ocean Prime. The Modern American Supper Club. Ocean Prime showcases a large selection of prime seafood & steaks, as well as handcrafted cocktails & world-class wines. This Cameron Mitchell Restaurant encompasses retro elements of a supper club, including warm finishes & rich woods, all combined with contemporary styled sheer drapery, state of the art lighting & granite bar tops. The atmosphere at Ocean Prime is electric, sexy & sophisticated and the service is unsurpassed. Ocean Prime also features a beautiful outdoor terrace, a lively cocktail lounge & two private dining rooms with seating for up to 35 guests.

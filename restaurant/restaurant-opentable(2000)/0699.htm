Rosebud Trattoria (fka Ballo)

writes...
  • The upbeat atmosphere at Rosebud Trattoria transforms an ordinary dining experience into a feast for all senses. The open kitchen breaks the barrier between chefs hard at work & guests enjoying their culinary creations including authentic Italian meatballs, house made pastas, seafood and veal specialties. Begin your dining experience with a crispy thin crust pizza or an antipasti platter with cured meats, artisanal cheeses and house marinated vegetables. Red leather banquettes and cozy tables encourage a feeling of camaraderie within the restaurant & entice customers to let worries drift away while feasting on stuffed pasta shells filled with ricotta cheese or Rosebud’s famed, brick chicken. Enjoy an after dinner drink in the comfort of the lounge or a glass of wine from the eclectic & extensive wine list as the late night energy intensifies. Rosebud Trattoria is the perfect destination for a celebration or enjoyable evening in the city combining the best in dining and nightlife.

In Italy mealtime is considered a true festive event. Whether sharing a table with your extended family or a large group of friends, join us at Rosebud Trattoria to host your next social gathering and we’ll make it one to remember! Conveniently located in bustling River North, Rosebud Trattoria is located steps from the heart of the loop and Chicago’s famed theater district. The stylish and intimate wine room offers privacy for groups of up to thirty people and provides an ideal backdrop for any celebration. Enjoy authentic Italian classics like fresh fish, chicken and veal dishes cooked to perfection. Top off your meal with an after dinner drink like our homemade limoncello or indulge in a creamy slice of tiramisu or a made-to order specialty cake. Customized menus, an extensive wine list, valet parking and catering services make each event handled by Rosebud a success!

Chicago Prime Steakhouse

writes...
  • Chicago Prime Steakhouse is an independently owned and operated establishment that is sure to win you heart and your palate. Schaumburg's newest Zagat rated restaurant is proud to serve USDA prime aged steaks, fresh seafood, and daily specials prepared to perfection. Chicago Prime features an award winning wine list, live entertainment in the relaxing piano lounge, private dining rooms, an outdoor patio, and complimentary valet parking and coat check. Experience the truly satisfying cuisine, exemplary service and elegant ambiance that makes Chicago Prime Steakhouse the most legendary steakhouse in Chicagoland.

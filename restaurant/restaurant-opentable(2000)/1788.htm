Il Bastardo

  • Il Bastardo is an innovative restaurant in Chelsea featuring steak and Italian food. This Italian steakhouse offers diners the finest, freshly prepared food as well as a full bar and outdoor cafe. Il Bastardo also boasts a private dining room which is the perfect spot for parties or special events.

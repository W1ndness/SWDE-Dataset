First Cabin at Balboa Bay Club & Resort

writes...
  • The First Cabin Restaurant is luxurious, yet simple and serene, resembling the ambiance that all guests experience at The Balboa Bay Club & Resort. Serving breakfast, lunch and dinner, guests can enjoy innovative and seasonal California Cuisine featuring fresh Pacific seafood, top-end prime beef and local ingredients paired to an extensive selection of great wines ranging from the top Californian wines to fine French Champagne. The First Cabin Restaurant offers waterfront dining with intimate banquettes and romantic tables overlooking the bay as well as alfresco seating on the heated patio.

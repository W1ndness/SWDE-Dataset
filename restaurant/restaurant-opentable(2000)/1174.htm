California Cafe - Denver

  • Located at Park Meadows Mall, California Cafe offers an escape from the outside world. We feed the senses - California style - through our chef-driven creations, engaging service, and relaxing ambiance.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Californian, Contemporary American, Organic
  • Neighborhood: Highlands Ranch/Lone Tree/Parker
  • Cross Street: Between Nordstrom and Crate & Barrel
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://californiacafe.com/littleton/index.php
  • Email: parkmeadowsevents@californiacafe.com
  • Phone: (303) 649-1111

  • Public Transit: Located directly across from County Line Light Rail Station.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Ample parking adjacent to restaurant in mall parking lot.

  • Private Party Facilities: The Sonoma & Napa Rooms are private areas that seats up to 40 guests. Creative options are available for semi private dining with groups of any size. Please consider us for any corporate or social event such as a rehearsal dinner, bridal shower or family gathering. We will customize menus to meet your vision and the needs of the group. Wine tastings can be arranged as part of your menu to make for a unique and memorable event. A complete buyout of the restaurant is available for approximately 200 people.
  • Private Party Contact: Linda Kuhns (303) 649-1111

    Wine Dinners are offered on a monthly basis. Simple seasonal fare is created to enhance the selected California wines characteristics. Presentations by the winemaker are a highlight of the evening.

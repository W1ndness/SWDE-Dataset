Mustang Harry's

writes...
  • Mustang Harry's is the perfect stop for dinner or drinks with your friends, just 2 blocks from Madison Square Garden and Penn Station it is THE venue before a concert, Knicks game, Rangers game or just before you hop on the train home. we boast one of the best happy hours in the city, and we are very proud of our menu, with excellent daily specials. If you are thinking of planning a party, be sure to speak to our manager and she will be happy to go through all our party options. we look forward to serving you soon.

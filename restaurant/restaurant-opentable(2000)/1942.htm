Tuscan Oven Trattoria

writes...
  • Step into this casual yet sophisticated regional Italian restaurant and you just skipped the airfare to Italy. Our authentic Italian cuisine is served by knowledgeable and friendly staff, located conveniently in Norwalk CT, right off 95 and the Merritt Parkway. Ask to sit near the fireplace in the winter or enjoy the patio in the summer. The garden room is a great place for your next family, social or corporate event. Be sure to sample our pasta dishes, wood fired brick oven pizza, fantastic fish, meat and poultry dishes and daily changing specials. If you aren’t coming for lunch or dinner, just relax at our full bar with one of the many wines by the glass and share an appetizer or two. If you enjoy fine wine, make sure to peruse our all-Italian award-winning wine list. Wine Spectator Award of achievment and Wine Enthusiast Award of Recognition, we are also a proud member of the Gruppo Ristoratori Italiani (GRI).

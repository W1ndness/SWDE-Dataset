Cameron's Steakhouse - Birmingham

writes...
  • There are no compromises are this sophisticated 21st century update of the upscale American steakhouse. Cameron's Steakhouse is Birmingham's premiere, classic steakhouse. From the rich walnut woods & jazz influenced art to the dark leather upholstery, Cameron's creates a sophisticated, inviting atmosphere. The menu & the service are extraordinary, featuring the finest choices of prime steaks, flown-in-fresh daily seafood and house made side dishes, complemented by a progressive 200 bottle Wine Spectator award-winning wine list. Cameron's Steakhouse is open for dinner only, seven nights a week.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Detroit
  • Cross Street: Old Woodward
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.camerons-steakhouse.com

  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Christopher Hines
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Private Room, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet parking is available during dinner service for $5.00. There are also public parking lots and street parking available for our guests.
  • Private Party Facilities: Cameron’s Steakhouse features one private dining room that can accommodate 16 guests at 1 table or up to 20 guests at 2 tables with space for AV equipment. Two semi-private areas are also available, one accommodating 40 people at 5 tables and the other 50 people at 6 tables. For both semi-private areas curtains are used to separate the space from the main dining room. Three distinct banquet menus are featured to choose from for parties of 16 or more.
  • Private Party Contact: Sally Gravat, General Manager (248) 723-1700

Gracie's Station Restaurant

writes...
  • Modeled after a late 1800’s western railroad station, the ambiance and décor cater to not only the southwest region, but also reflect the restaurant’s proximity to the decommissioned San Pedro Rail Line that ran adjacent to the property. The motif, though primarily railroad specific, does reflect the characteristics of an entire old west railroad town. The Saloon offers a place to enjoy a frosty beverage before or after a meal. The three dining rooms, named The Hotel, The Round House, and The Brothel, each take on their own atmosphere and offer an entertaining dining experience The dinning environment itself will leave you feeling visually inspired. However, the food will keep you coming back for more! Gracie’s Station menu boasts a variety of dishes that cater to both the area and restaurant concept. Gracie’s puts a whole new definition on “meat and potatoes” that will remind you of home cooking and won’t let you leave hungry.And don’t forget to stop by Makena Kate’s General Store!

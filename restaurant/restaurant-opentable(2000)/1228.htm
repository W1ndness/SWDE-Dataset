Aiello's

writes...
  • East Coast Italian means hearty Southern Italian Fare at its best! Earthy colors embrace the warm sleek feeling of this modern Italian Bistro, offering the specialties of acclaimed Chef Joe Aiello. “Aiello’s has got to be the Best Italian restaurant anywhere!& my favorite…” Rick D'Amico, Fox News. "Every neighborhood should be so lucky as to have a great little Italian place like this. With luscious food, accessible prices & an inviting atmosphere… The Big Apple's loss is Phoenix’s gain…" Michele Laudig, Phoenix New Times. The menu features fabulous authentic pastas using only the finest in imported Italian products, or try one of the many fresh and healthy grilled or sauteed dishes. A business dinner, romantic interlude or family feast, This spot says it all. Full bar and moderately priced Italian Wine list. You’ll find the Aiello's anytime either behind the open kitchen or on the floor entertaining their diners with Tombola, a game of Italian Bingo and perhaps a free dinner.!

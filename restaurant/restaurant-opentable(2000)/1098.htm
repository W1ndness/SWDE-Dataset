Dover Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • Dover Restaurant and Bar is located in the lower Atrium level of the Double Tree Park Place Hotel. We offer breakfast, lunch and dinner every day as well as a breakfast buffet. Also located in Dover is the Hall of Fame private dining room which can seat up to 12 guests and is dedicated to the Minnesota State High School league. Our menus are American fare offering a wide range of choices from tasty salads, sandwiches and burgers, Walleye and Salmon and great steaks. We also offer a great bar menu as well. Menu prices range from $8-$30. Dover also offers a comprehensive selection of spirits, regional beers and full wine menu with many choices by the glass or bottle. Décor is contemporary with flexible table seating and booths. The centerpiece of the restaurant is the large stainless steel high top table that seats 12 people as you walk into the room. The bar area offers 6 HDTV’s with Direct TV programming.

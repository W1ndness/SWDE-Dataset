The Duck and Dumpling

writes...
  • Come taste the flavors of Asia! Chef James Yott Boyle offers many of the same great favorites of retired Chef David Mao, plus his own Thai inspired recipes. The Duck and Dumpling is casual Asian cuisine at its best with carefully chosen wine, beer and sake selections to pair with our affordable menu. Whether you want a comfortable dinner for two, or to share small plates with a group of friends, the Duck and Dumpling can accommodate you.

Union Jack's - Columbia

writes...
  • Not quite a bar and not quite a restaurant, Union Jack’s is a British hybrid of sorts, offering a casual- meets-refined atmosphere that welcomes beer drinkers and wine aficionados, non-fussy eaters and foodies alike. We are a casual pub that serves great food! The reputation of dining out in Great Britain used to be on par with its reputation for fine dentistry. But at Union Jack’s we have decided to change that perception. We have incorporated fresh ingredients in a menu that focuses not only on signature British dishes but also features American standards and seafood items. Our atmosphere offers a "comfy" lounge and fireplace as well as an upscale-dining room. There is a large pub-style room that is great for "game-watching" or kicking back with an after-dinner drink while shooting a game of billiards or shuffleboard. Union Jack’s is a comfortable place where the food is just as important as the drink!

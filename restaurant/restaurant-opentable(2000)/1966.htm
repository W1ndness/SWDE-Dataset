Corner View Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • Located on a great corner along the entry to charming San Luis Obispo is Corner View Restaurant & Bar. One of SLO's downtown restaurants it has already garnered the approval of the locals as one of the “Best of SLO” in the annual New Times readers poll. The beautiful restaurant is both spacious and charming. An elegant mission style interior is accented with vintage photographs of old time SLO and scenic photos of the vineyards of Edna Valley and Paso Robles. A relaxed atmosphere and friendly service make Corner View “the spot” for a great dining experience. Our U-shaped bar is perfect for meeting friends. Let us craft an icy cold martini or suggest the perfect local wine to pair with your appetizer or dinner. We pour over 25 wines by the glass, featuring many from Edna Valley, Paso Robles and Santa Ynez Valley. Don’t be surprised if a local winemaker is behind the bar as a guest bartender for the evening.

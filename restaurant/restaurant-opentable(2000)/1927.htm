One of New York's favorite hideaways, Sueños offers an escape to authentic Mexico with its innovative cuisine, exceptional service, creative ambiance, and festive decor. Chef Sue Torres delivers passionate, culinary art through her tasty dishes and flavorful recipes. Since its opening in 2003, Sueños continues to transport its guests to another world, fulfilling the ultimate experience in Mexican dining. Chef Sue Torres is an expert on combining flavor, color, and texture through her culinary art. Sueños, Spanish for "dreams", features an explorative private dining menu sure to aimed to enhance your party experience through its variety of fresh Mexican dishes. Sueños is also the home of stellar margaritas and cocktails.

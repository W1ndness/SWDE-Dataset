McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - St. Louis

writes...
  • Reminiscent of traditionally-styled Pacific Northwest seafood eateries. McCormick & Schmick's Seafood Restaurant offers a traditional dining experience in an unpretentious atmosphere. Our menus are printed twice daily and offer over 30 varieties of fresh seafood flown in each day from U.S. & international waters along with aged beef, chicken and pasta. Bartenders create hand-shaken cocktails made with fresh-squeezed juices in our bar where we also offer an extensive list of premium wines & beers. Join us for our famous Happy Hours (4-6 & 9-10 pm) featuring a $1.95 food menu Monday through Thursday. Hospitality is the key at McCormick & Schmick's, whether for private functions, catering, business or family dining we can take care of your special needs

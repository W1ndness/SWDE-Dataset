Apollinare Italian Restaurant

writes...
  • Apollinare Italian Restaurant boasts over 50 wines by the glass & a displayed brick oven. Apollinare celebrates the delight of Italian food & wine in a comfortable, yet sexy, urban atmosphere located in the newly acclaimed Piazza at Schmidt's in the Northern Liberties section of Philadelphia.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Center City
  • Cross Street: Germantown
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.apollinarerestaurant.com
  • Email: info@apollinarerestaurant.com
  • Phone: (215) 923-2014
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 12:00am - 5:00pm Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 12:00am Sunday Brunch: 11:00am - 3:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: By the Market Frankford Blue Line that stops at Girard. By bus lines 5, 15, and 57 run up & down Girard Avenue.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Free street parking is available on most streets surrounding the restaurant as well as a free parking lot on the corner of 2nd Street & Germantown Avenue.
  • Private Party Facilities: Semi-private dining is available in the Lower Lounge for 15-25 guests & in the Alcove for 30-40 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Fabio (215) 923-2014

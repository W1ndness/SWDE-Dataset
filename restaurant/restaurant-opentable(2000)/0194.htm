  • Capital City Grill offers an casual New Orleans Style atmosphere. We offer the freshest seafood and Choice Steaks. Our Covered Courtyard patio provides guest a relaxing dining experience. Full bar is available with an extensive wine list. The bar rich in wood features Flat screen TVs for any sporting event. We can accommodate most large parties, including rehearsal dinners, graduation parties, as well as Christmas parties during the Holidays.

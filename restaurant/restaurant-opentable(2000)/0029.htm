The Las Vegas Palm has been open for 15 years and continues to become a favorite to newcomers, repeat out of towners as well as locals. The fine quality of our delicious prime aged steaks and outrageously oversized lobsters as well as our professional but casual service keeps guests coming back for more. One of our best-kept secrets however is our “Private Dining” facility. We can accommodate anywhere from 10 to 100 people in our “Private Dining Rooms”. Not only do we offer many choices of group menus and a wonderful wine list, we also offer our A la Carte menu for smaller groups of 20 people or less. We go out of our way to make sure that our guests enjoy the traditional “Palm Experience”. We offer group luncheons from 11:30AM to 2:30PM and dinner from 3:00PM to 9:30PM.

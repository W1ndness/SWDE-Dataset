The Stinking Rose

writes...
  • Located on Restaurant Row in Beverly Hills, The Stinking Rose is as unique as its name--romantic tents for dining and garlic-themed rooms for special occasions or just a bite to eat.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Beverly Hills
  • Cross Street: Wilshire Blvd.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.thestinkingrose.com
  • Email: comments@thestinkingrose.com
  • Phone: (310) 652-7673
  • Hours of Operation: Daily 11:30 am - 10:00 pm
  • Payment Options:
    Carte Blanche, Diners Club, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Andrea Froncillo
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Free parking during the day in our large parking lot and valet parking in the evening beginning at 4:00pm.
  • Private Party Facilities: Four distinct rooms offer venues for events and parties of varying sizes--Michelangelo Room seats 85 under a canopy with twinkling lights and plush banquettes, all in a private setting: the Chianti Room seats 45 in an Italian Cafe setting with hanging Chianti bottles and candlelight; Dracula's Grotto, a secluded den for 42 people and the Cork Table, a long banquet table for 14 in a secluded alcove.
  • Private Party Contact: Massimo Marmorino (310) 652-7673 x2

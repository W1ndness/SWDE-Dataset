BJ's Steak & Rib House - Selinsgrove

writes...
  • Located in charming downtown Selinsgrove, just blocks from Susquehanna University. BJ's Steak & Rib House is housed in a historic old hotel dating to 1874. Established in 1982, BJ's is known throughout the valley as the quality dining destination. The restaurant features thick-cut USDA Choice Black Angus Steaks, authentic smoked-in-house barbecue babyback ribs, fresh seafood selections, burgers, sandwiches, & salads - all served until closing! Our Bar & Cocktail Lounge features 12 fresh brewed beers on tap & a value priced quality Wine List. At BJ's you are assured quality food with personal quality service.

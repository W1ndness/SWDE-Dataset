Table 6

writes...
  • Table 6 is a classic American bistro, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere. Chef Scott Parker prepares succulent dishes, delivered with elegant style and complimented with a dynamic wine list, created by General Manager/Sommelier Aaron Foreman, which represents most of the flourishing wine districts from all over the world. We accept reservations for parties of 4 or less before 6pm and after 9pm. For parties of 5 or more or if you don't find the reservation you are looking please call us for further assistance.

  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Cherry Creek
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

  • Executive Chef: Scott Parker

  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Beer, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Weekend Brunch, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: A lot directly behind the restaurant is available at no charge. Guests may enter the restaurant from the parking lot through the rear entrance.
  • Private Party Facilities: Reservations are accepted for our private dining room. It is a quieter, more intimate room, which is good for business meetings or for people who just enjoy more privacy with their dining. The room provides seating for up to 18 people.

Legal Sea Foods - Long Wharf

writes...
  • The signature dish at Legal Sea Foods – really fresh fish. Enjoy ocean-side dining just steps from the New England Aquarium. Our restaurant couldn’t be more convenient to the Financial District, Freedom Trail, Faneuil Hall and waterfront hotels. We think a visit to Boston must include a meal at Legal Sea Foods. Legal Sea Foods has long proven that a consistent, high quality dining experience never goes out of style. With over 40 varieties of fresh fish and shellfish available throughout the year as well as an award-winning wine list, there’s no doubt why legions of fans have fallen hook, line and sinker. We’ve received a boatload of accolades including “Most Popular Boston Restaurant” (Zagat, 2010/2011), “Best Seafood Restaurant” (Improper Bostonian, 2010), “Best Chowder” (Boston Magazine, 2010), “Best Seafood Restaurant” (Boston Phoenix, 2009) and the honor of serving our legendary New England Clam Chowder at every presidential inauguration since 1981.

  • Neighborhood: Faneuil Hall

  • Price: $31 to $50

  • Public Transit: Aquarium Station (Blueline)
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Our validation will net you savings, daily after 4:30pm. Park at discounted rates for up to 4 hours in the Boston Harbor Garage, located at the intersection of Atlantic Avenue and Milk Street.
  • Private Party Facilities: What a way to say “Welcome!” An invitation to Legal Sea Foods at Long Wharf is an invitation to be impressed. Guests will eagerly anticipate the thrill of waterfront dining in the heart of Boston’s Faneuil Hall and Quincy Marketplace area.

    But tucked inside the busy Long Wharf location is an oasis of calm. This private dining space can accommodate more than 50 and is perfect for entertaining your business or social guests. The carpeted room features glass walls, warm wood trim, and sophisticated décor with flexible seating arrangements to suit any occasion. The staff can coordinate table linens, audiovisual support, flowers and other party details to make your special event shine. And our culinary crew will be pleased to custom-design menus to suit your budget and creative style.
  • Private Party Contact: Shion Hara (617) 828-4191
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Celebrate a special occasion or host a corporate event at Legal Sea Foods. We customize every menu and finishing detail. Here at Long Wharf, our private dining room accommodates up to 55 guests.

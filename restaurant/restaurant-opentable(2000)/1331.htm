Cigale Cafe

writes...
  • Located in beautiful Oak Park, Cigale Cafe is a French bistro reminiscent of France’s neighborhood restaurants. It is the perfect setting for casual meals, a business meeting, or to celebrate happy occasions with loved ones. Owner and Chef Denis Rion wants you to enjoy his authentic take on traditional French cuisine. The majority of the dishes are prepared to order using fresh, quality, and often locally grown ingredients.

SARDIS HISTORY FACTS * Sardi’s is located in the heart of the Theatre District since 1921. * Alex Gard was an unemployed caricaturist who Mr. Sardi hired in exchange for one meal a day (lunch or dinner) for him and a guest. *Alex Gard drew the first 721 caricatures. * Sardis and the Broadway Theatre go hand in hand. - Plays have been written at Sardi’s - Opening show celebrations have been happen at Sardis - This is Broadway’s unofficial Hall of Fame - Sardis is known as “The Toast of Broadway”

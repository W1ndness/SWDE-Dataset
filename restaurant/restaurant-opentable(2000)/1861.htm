North Square is one of the finest choices for private events in lower Manhattan. Located on the northwest corner of Washington Square Park, North Square offers a beautifully designed Art Deco room tailored for intimate dinner events and cocktail parties. In addition to the private room, our lounge and dining room are also available for events. Whether the party is a grand celebration or a small get together, North Square offers a variety of possibilities to meet every events needs.

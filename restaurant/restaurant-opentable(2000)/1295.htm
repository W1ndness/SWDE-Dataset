Back Bay Bistro

writes...
  • Enjoy waterfront dining at its finest at the NEW Back Bay Bistro, located at the entrance of the Back Bay in Newport Beach California. Enjoy breakfast, lunch, or dinner on this elegantly designed patio in a casual, friendly atmosphere. A state of the art retractable roof allows guests to dine year-round, with a great view of the bay & marina. The Back Bay Bistro offers free valet services on Sunday mornings for our Sunday brunch -two hours of free self parking with validation and dog friendly dining.

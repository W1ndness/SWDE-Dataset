South Coast Winery Restaurant

writes...
  • South Coast Winery Restaurant is the Orange County extension of South Coast Winery Resort and Spa in Temecula. South Coast Winery is 2008 winner of the "Golden Bear Award" as the top award winning Winery in California. The restaurant offers great food in a Wine Country environment. Wine Bar is available for tastings and wine purchases during all hours of operation.

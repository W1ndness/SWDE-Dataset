Terra Nova

writes...
  • We are excited to bring you Terra Nova, Restaurant/Crudo/Wine Bar. A new, casual yet sophisticated restaurant conveniently located in the heart of South Jersey, featuring Contemporary American cuisine with a Napa Valley influence. Our chef’s creative cuisine with a New World style offers guests an exciting & diverse dining experience. The introduction of a sushi & raw bar takes the menu’s modern flair to new heights. Our adventurous wine list presents over 200 wines by the bottle with 50 available by the glass or our unique wine flights. Just as distinctive as the cuisine, the décor at Terra Nova is characterized by its clean design and Northern California architectural elements that are elegant yet warm. Please continue to visit our website for updates and postings. We hope you join us for lunch, dinner or a drink at our bar.

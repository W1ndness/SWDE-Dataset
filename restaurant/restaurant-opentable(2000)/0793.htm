  • Vines Cafe features traditional breakfast and lunch menus and a bountiful buffet that change daily to emphasize the rich diversity of the Northern California region. Locally-grown produce and farmed fresh meats and poultry are specialties. On Sundays, Vines is home to our award-winning champagne brunch. Fountainside patio dining is also available, weather permitting.

Brinkley's

writes...
  • Brinkley’s Pub & Kitchen is a SoHo neighborhood favorite. A super lively bar crowd, great music, delicious food, cocktails, local wine and beer make Brinkley’s a downtown staple. Our seasonal, fresh menu includes pub classics like the Stilton Blue cheese Burger, Monkish Fritters, Duck Liver Pate, Pear & Goat Cheese Salad and the best Meatballs around. All of the Beer and Wine are from New York state. Adding to the atmosphere are two large flat screen TV’s, behind the bar, showing European/American sports. Come early… come late… we look forward to having you!

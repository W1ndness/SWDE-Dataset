2 Bird Cafe

writes...
  • The Two Bird Cafe is nestled in the tiny Hamlet of San Geronimo, CA. A scenic drive will take you to a beautiful, rustic cafe where there's always a special to tempt your taste buds. Whether you dine inside, by the fireplace, or outside on the deck, you'll enjoy meals that will tantalize your palate, and ease you into the laid back pace of beautiful Marin County.

  • Cuisine: Californian, American, Burgers

Originally opened in 1993 by founder Bob Sambol the name “Bob’s” has been synonymous with a great steak house experience. Bob’s is unique because it is simple, big pieces of red meat in a comfortable and masculine setting. All of our steaks are USDA Prime and all entrees include a choice of potato and the signature glazed carrot. Consistently ranked #1 in Dallas, Bob’s will personalize the traditional steak house menu for any size group.

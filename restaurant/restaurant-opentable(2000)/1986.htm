Lakeview at Desert Springs JW Marriott

writes...
  • Great Food, Great Views! At Lakeview Restaurant you'll find Classic American Fare where our guests are spoiled with hearty choices from house made soups, great salads, popular sandwiches and great steaks. Our menu includes a variety of sustainable and organic selections as well as our fabulous pastry chef selection of delectable desserts. In addition to our popular menu, our buffets are favorites of resort guests and locals alike. We serve an extensive breakfast buffet featuring; omelets made to order, Eggs Benedict, salmon, fresh baked pastries and much more. Friday and Saturday nights offer our "All You Can Eat Dinner Buffet" featuring; crab legs, shrimp, prime rib carved to order, pasta made to order, a tempting dessert buffet and much more. Lakeview is the perfect place for food, fun, friends and family!

Chautauqua Dining Hall

writes...
  • A National Historic Landmark, Chautauqua Dining Hall has been a Boulder institution since 1898. You can savor spectacular views from the enchanting wrap around porch while enjoying classic American cooking at breakfast, lunch and dinner. The Dining Hall offers a full bar with local beers and great wines to complement your meal. And, there is no finer venue in Boulder for weddings, private parties, or any special celebration. After a hike on the nearby trails, before a concert in Chautauqua Park's storied Auditorium, or anytime you'd like to meet friends and family in Boulder's most scenic setting, the Dining Hall is the perfect place to go.

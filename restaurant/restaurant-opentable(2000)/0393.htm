Belmont

writes...
  • The Dine and Discover speaker series at historic Belmont offers an exceptional dining experience that satisfies both intellect and palate. Starting with Cocktails and Conversation, a regional expert will share an exclusive insider's guide to contemporary topics in music, art, theatre, travel and more. Then, be seated as Chef James Jennings prepares and delights you with his culinary creations. The setting is the beautiful 18th century Belmont Manor located on an 82 acre historic estate in Howard County. Dine and Discover events typically are held Friday and Saturday evenings. Each evening offers a different topic. A new menu is introduced approximately every two weeks. Book your reservation now for an evening you will long remember.

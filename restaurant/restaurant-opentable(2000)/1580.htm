Petit Robert Bistro Columbus Avenue

writes...
  • With a casual atmosphere and a classic menu, Petit Robert Bistro feel like an authentic French bistro. Francophiles with a hankering for onion soup gratinee, beef bourguignon and apple tarte tatin need not break the bank, as the bistro keeps its prices modest (all entrees are under $20). Those with a sweet tooth enjoy made-to-order souffle or a rich chocolate gateau with a chocolate Eiffel Tower. Bon Appetit!

SUSHISAMBA strip

writes...
  • Celebrate the culture and cuisine of Japan, Brazil and Peru at SUSHISAMBA strip! From kobe sliders to the sizzling robata grill to the freshest sushi, sashimi and ceviche on the strip, SUSHISAMBA has something for everyone. Eclectic menus for lunch or dinner blend Brazilian, Peruvian and Japanese flavors in a carnaval-spiked atmosphere that’s synonymous with fun. An award-winning wine and sake list, extensive beer menu, signature cocktails and creative desserts round out the experience.

  • Cuisine: Sushi, Brazilian

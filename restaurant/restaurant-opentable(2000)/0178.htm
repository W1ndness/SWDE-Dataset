Sola - Bryn Mawr

writes...
  • At Sola, our mission is to be regarded as an outstanding fine dining destination & a valued treasure of the Main Line & greater community.
    We strive to serve contemporary cuisine in an intimate & pleasing room with the proper level of service (not too much, not too little). We want our guests to enjoy the window of time that they spend with us, savoring their food & wine & enjoying the company of their friends & family.
    Our intent is to always remain a BYOB, knowing how important that is to many of our guests. Sola will provide a dynamic menu, driven by what is available fresh for the season. The expectation is that everyone will find a number of choices that appeal to their palate.
    Our wish is that most of our guests will become regulars & will view us a spot they can enthusiastically recommend to their friends & can rely on when hosting visitors.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Contemporary French
  • Neighborhood: Bryn Mawr
  • Cross Street: Penn
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50

  • Payment Options:
    Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Dave Clouser
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: BYO Liquor, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wheelchair Access
  • Public Transit: Bryn Mawr R5 stop is about 1/4 mile away from the restaurant.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Catty-corner to the restaurant is a church & school with a large parking lot. The church is named "St. Thomas of Good Council". Guests are permitted to park there while dining at Sola.

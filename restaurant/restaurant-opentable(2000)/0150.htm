JAMES

writes...
  • In just three years,JAMES has received many impressive local and national accolades including: a glowing three bell review from Philadelphia Inquirer’s Craig Laban, Food & Wine magazine’s 2008 ‘Best New Chef’ award, Semi-finalist for James Beard Award - 2008, 2009, 2010 - ‘Best Chef Mid-Atlantic’, and most recently a finalist for Bocuse d’Or USA 2010. These credentials do little to speak of the passion that Jim and Kristina inject into every service at JAMES; one only needs to ask a frequent diner of the restaurant to get a sense of that. JAMES is a fine dining establishment serving modern American cuisine with impeccable service in an elegant & friendly environment. Our menu focuses on the importance of seasonality & it changes frequently.
    JAMES has a modern bar & lounge area complete with love seats, cocktail tables, & a glowing fireplace. We offer a thoughtful “boutique” wine list concentrating on small producers dedicated to natural methods & sustainable viticulture.

JAMES is a fine dining establishment in Philadelphia’s Bella Vista neighborhood serving modern American cuisine. Our service is impeccable; the dining environment, elegant yet friendly. Our menu focuses on local, seasonal produce, humanely raised meats, wild seafood, & artisan cheeses. Because we select only fresh locally-grown ingredients, our menu changes with the seasons. JAMES has a modern bar & lounge area complete with love seats, cocktail tables & a glowing fireplace. Our thoughtful, “boutique” wine list offers amazing selections of small producers dedicated to natural methods & sustainable viticulture. Our cocktails inspire with smart, top-shelf creations mixed with house-made syrups, fresh juices and seasonal ingredients.JAMES offers intimate dining in a stunning private room. Our Chef’s Table seats up to 12 comfortably.Tasting menus & wine pairings are thoughtfully prepared & highly encouraged. This room is a perfect backdrop for special occasions or business functions.

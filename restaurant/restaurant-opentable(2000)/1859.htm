Nolita House Restaurant

writes...
  • Nolita House's friendly setting conveys a neighborhood charm where guests can experience great comfort food, libations, artisan cheese and entertainment in a relaxed environment. Our culinary offering is a thoughtful balance of globally focused comfort food with an upscale twist. As a compliment to the cuisine, our wine list encourages guests to sample lesser-known varietals that have been selected from boutique producers around the world. The special cocktails, draught and bottled beer selections are inventive and affordable. Nolita House is open seven days a week serving lunch, dinner and weekend brunch and offers free neighborhood delivery. On Thursday, Friday & Saturday evenings, the kitchen stays open until 2am with a late night menu. Additionally, we offer full service corporate & special event catering at our place or yours. We are Located in the Nolita (North of Little Italy) area of Manhattan. We look forward to seeing you at our House!

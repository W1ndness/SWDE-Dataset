Seven Sushi Ultralounge and Skybar

writes...
  • Up one flight from The Steakhouse is Seven Sushi Ultralounge. This unique space offers its guests the finest design in Japanese dining in a trendy urban chic atmosphere that is a welcoming compliment to the overall experience. The sushi bar is designed with a hand painted mural created by a local artist using a traditional Asian technique involving Gold Leafs and acrylics. Whether the desire is for an assortment of Nigiri or the popular White Ninja roll, the sushi chefs are prepared to deliver a presentation of perfection and a creation that is a master of design. The restaurant offers an extensive selection of Sakes’ to tantalize even the most educated of palates. With a variety of custom-made seating options, oak floors, backlit onyx bars, and a multitude of LED variety lighting, each diner is given the ability to dictate their own experience. Above the Ultralounge is the famous Seven Sky Bar. Our rooftop patio contains 2 full bars, couches, cabanas, and high top table seating!

Bistro Rollin

writes...
  • Bistro Rollin is a warm, welcoming, lovely wood paneled French bistro, with exquisite food, prepared by Executive Chef Manny Lozano, who honed his skills as Sous-Chef at L"Absinthe in NYC, as well as other fine Manhattan restaurants. The proprietors, Barbara and Arthur Bratone will greet you when you arrive, and see that your total dining experience is a pleasure. Only open since June of 2009, Bistro Rollin has already garnered great reviews from the New York Times and Westchester Magazine. Classic French bistro food prepared with skill and passion along with exciting specials served by a skilled and enthusiastic staff with your dining enjoyment as their goal.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: French
  • Neighborhood: Pelham
  • Cross Street: Third Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.bistrorollin.com/
  • Email: owners@bistrorollin.com
  • Phone: (914) 633-0780
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Tuesday - Saturday: 12:00pm - 2:30pm
    Dinner: Sunday, Tuesday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:30pm
    Brunch: Sunday: 11:00am - 3:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Manny Lozano
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wine
  • Public Transit: The Pelham station on the Metro-North line is located about a 10 minute walk south of the restaurant.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: A municipal parking lot is located 1/2 block north of the restaurant that offers free parking every day after 6pm, and metered parking during other hours. Extensive on street parking is also available
  • Catering: Bistro Rollin provides limited off-site catering for special events. Mainly to organizations who have their own serving staff and prep kitchens.
  • Private Party Facilities: Bistro Rollin has a lovely private dining room that can seat up to 25 people for completely private events. Larger groups of up to 50 can be accommodated utilizing part of the main dining room. It is possible to book the entire facility under certain circumstances.
  • Private Party Contact: Barbara Bratone (914) 633-0780
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    We have a beautiful private dining room for small groups (up to 25) and can accommodate groups up to 100 in Main Dining rooms. We will arrange flowers, special linens, and menus.

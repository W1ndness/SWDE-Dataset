Havana Grill

writes...
  • The next best thing to visiting Cuba’s famous capital, Havana! Send your senses on vacation, as you taste the exotic flavors of the authentic dishes. Indulge in the delicious, fancy cocktails; and enjoy the exciting music and beautiful culture of everything that is Cuba. Chef Sergio Perez, a native of Havana, has more than 15 years experience creating traditional dishes of his homeland. He brought his mother’s recipes with him when he immigrated to the United States more than two decades ago.

    Happy Hour 7 Days a week 4:00-9:00pm.
    2 for 1 drinks (full bar). 1/2 off full appetizer menu.

    Please visit Havana’s sister restaurant, Florida Café, on the Las Vegas Strip. A showcase for local Cuban artists, the eatery is a favorite among locals.

Slocum House

writes...
  • Situated atop a gently rolling hilltop, Slocum House was built in 1925 and still conjures memories of Fair Oaks' colony days. An outdoor courtyard surrounded by 100 Oak trees, along with maples and a lush gardens provide pleasant year round dining (weather permitting). Requests for patio dining are subject to availability.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Global, International, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Fair Oaks
  • Cross Street: California & Sunrise
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.slocum-house.com
  • Email: slocum1@sbcglobal.net
  • Phone: (916) 961-7211
  • Hours of Operation: Sunday Brunch: 10:30am - 1:30pm
    Dinner: Tuesday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 9:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Kelly McMullen
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: There is 1 handicap spot in back, limited private lot parking, and public parking.
  • Private Party Facilities: The restaurant has several different options in regards to hosting an event on our property. There are options to renting out the entire restaurant along with renting our different areas of the restaurant as well.
  • Private Party Contact: Tanya Noujaim (916) 961-7211
  • Entertainment: On Friday and Saturday nights, May thru October, The Nicole Hodson Quartet jazz band will entertain you in the courtyard. During Sunday Brunch, harpist, Pamela Pamperin plays in the courtyard. Weather permitting.
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Host your special event at the Slocum House. From baby showers to rehearsal dinners, the Slocum House provides a perfect ambiance for your day. Please Contact Tanya Noujaim for information regarding weddings and special events.

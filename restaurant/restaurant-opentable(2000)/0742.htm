Providence Cafe

writes...
  • One of Charlotte's most established favorite neighborhood restaurants, Providence Café is located in the heart of Myers Park and serves fresh American cuisine complemented by an extensive, award-winning wine list. Our fresh house-baked breads and desserts are well known. Providence Café offers a warm, comfortable ambiance including spacious booths, inviting bar area, patio dining, and two large semi-private rooms for large groups. We have a full bar. Open 7 days a week. Sunday Brunch. Convenient to Uptown and SouthPark Areas. Come see why Charlotte has loved Providence Cafe since 1992!

Burbank Bar & Grille

writes...
  • Burbank Bar & Grille offers the best in sports entertainment on our 21 HDTV flat screens than any other Burbank Sports bar, offering you viewing of every major sporting event. Come in and enjoy our California-cuisine menu daily for happy hour specials, dinner or drinks and enjoy a great atmosphere indoors or on the heated rooftop patio with wood-burning fireplaces. Burbank Bar & Grille is also the best place in Burbank to celebrate your birthday, office party or customized special event. Also enjoy our live entertainment nightly which includes an acoustic set on the rooftop patio and 70’s and 80’s band on Fridays and Saturdays on the main stage. You can also come and sing your heart out to your favorite song on karaoke Thursdays! We also host college football Saturdays and NFL Ticket Sunday football with a great brunch menu!

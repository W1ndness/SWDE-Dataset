Riverview

writes...
  • Located directly on the shore of the East River at the Avalon in Long Island City, Riverview Restaurant commands the most spectacular views of New York City's skyline. Directly facing the United Nation with Citicorp Center, Trumps International Hotel and the Queensboro Bridge to the north and the Chrysler and Empire State Building to the south, Riverview is unmistakably, " A Room with a View".
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Contemporary American, Mediterranean
  • Neighborhood: Long Island City
  • Cross Street: 49th Ave & East River
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.riverviewny.com
  • Email: info@riverviewny.com
  • Phone: (718) 392-5000
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday thru Saturday: 12:00pm - 4:00pm Dinner: Monday -Thursday: 4:00pm -10:00pm /Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm Sunday Brunch: 11:00am-3:00pm /Dinner: 3:00pm - 10:00pm Sunday Brunch: 10:00am to 3:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Cisse
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Chef's Table, Dancing, Delivery, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: 7 Train to Vernon/Jackson Blvd stop. Just walk 2 blocks towards the east river. New York Water Taxi - 1 block /LIRR Hunter's Point Station
  • Parking: Valet
  • Catering: All occasions, Weddings, Private Parties, Baby Showers, Birthdays and more.
  • Private Party Facilities: Riverview can accommodate 275 people for sit-down and 500 people for cocktails.

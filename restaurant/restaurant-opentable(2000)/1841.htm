Meson Sevilla

writes...
  • For over 25 years meson Sevilla has established itself as a staple in Midtown Manhattan. Serving classic Spanish cuisine, trendy tapas and Italian specialties, the menu can satisfy everyone's appetite. The restaurant is family owned and operated, and treats all of their guests as family.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Spanish, Tapas / Small Plates
  • Neighborhood: Theater District / Times Square
  • Cross Street: Between 8th and 9th Avenues
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.mesonsevilla.com
  • Phone: (212) 262-5890
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Daily: 12:00pm - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 3:00pm - 11:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 3:00pm - 12:00am
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Joaquin Lucero
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Private Room, Takeout, Wine
  • Public Transit: A, C & E trains to 42nd Street/Port Authority.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking is available in public lots between 8th and 9th Avenues.
  • Catering: Catering is available with our large trays that can serve 12 - 15 guests. Delivery service is available within Midtown. Please consult our website for further details.
  • Private Party Facilities: The private room at Meson Sevilla can accommodate up to 45 guests. Menu packages are available ranging from $40 to $65 per person. Buffet parties are available for $35 per person.
  • Private Party Contact: Benny or Leo (212) 262-5890

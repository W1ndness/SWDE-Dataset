Yankee Pier - Larkspur

writes...
  • Yankee Pier Larkspur is harbored in a quaint historic landmark house built in the late 1800’s. The main dining room seats 49. Outdoor seating is available on our heated patios, serving 14 on the rear patio, and 24 out front with a sandbox for the youngsters. Styled after New England seaside clam shacks, our upscale casual atmosphere lends itself to families and friends, young and seasoned. Our menu includes Fried Whole Belly Ipswich Clams, Lobster Rolls, Live Maine Lobster, and Clam Chowder. Fare from our local waters abound such as oysters and manila clams from Tomales Bay as well as local fish and crab caught and delivered daily by local our purveyors. For those landlubbers among you, Yankee Pier also has great burgers, naturally raised steak and free range chicken. Our all American wine list boasts some of the best from California, Washington and Oregon.

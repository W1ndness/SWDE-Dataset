Cafe 14

writes...
  • A small quaint American French bistro in agoura hills. Husband and wife owners are also the chefs. The menu changes with the seasons, and the chefs love to support local farms by shopping at the farmers market. We have a spectacular wine list. Michelin guide and Zagat rated.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Californian, French
  • Neighborhood: Agoura Hills / Westlake Village
  • Cross Street: Reyes Adobe
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://Cafe-14.com
  • Phone: (818) 991-9560
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 9:30pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm -10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Claudine Bernard and Neil Kramer
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Full Bar, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: We have a large lot with ample parking spaces.
  • Catering: From small intimate dinner parties to weddings. Neil Kramer has a extensive background in the L.A. catering scene.
  • Private Party Facilities: Cafe 14 has been known to plan everything from elaborate wine tasting menus for their guests to small afternoon tea bridal showers.
  • Private Party Contact: Neil (818) 991-9560

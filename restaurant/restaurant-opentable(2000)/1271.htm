Talavera

writes...
  • Talavera features contemporary American cuisine with an emphasis on prime cuts of meat, poultry and game from boutique purveyors, as well as fresh seafood and other specialty dishes. Named for a type of brightly colored, hand painted Mexican pottery, the central focal point of Talavera is a dramatic fire element framed by glass – positioned for both indoor and outdoor diners to enjoy - and showcasing dramatic city and desert views.

Captivate your guests with a special evening at Talavera at Four Seasons Resort Scottsdale. A variety of private indoor and outdoor spaces offer perfect options for celebrating milestone occasions such as birthdays, anniversaries and retirement parties for groups of four to more than 100. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Valley from the main dining room with an exclusive buy-out of the restaurant. Or, reserve the private Wine Room for a special evening among the restaurant's premier wine collection. Host an interactive night in the Central Table room, with the ambiance of the dining room, but privacy of a private affair. For an outdoor option, the Talavera Patio is the ideal locale. Start the evening with cocktails and appetizers in a reserved area of Onyx Bar & Lounge. The options are endless for making your evening magical with a private celebration at Four Seasons Resort Scottsdale.

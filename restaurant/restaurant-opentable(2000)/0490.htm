Maximo Cocina Mexicana

writes...
  • We have been voted Dallas’ Best New Restaurant of 2009 in D Magazine! The executive Chef, Amador Mora invites you to explore the roots of regional Mexican cuisine, from the Aztecas of the south to the Tarahumaras of the north. Our Margarita Lounge is warmly appointed with leather seating, velvet walls and woodwork that take you away to another time. Maximo Cocina Mexicana & Margarita Lounge is the perfect place to relax and enjoy our Maximo Heat, voted "The Best Margarita in America outside of Rick Bayless Topolobampo". As home of the best Happy Hour in North Dallas, come in for one of our Mexico City inspired specialty cocktails like the refreshing La Paloma or the "Margarita of Mexico City", our Tamarindo Maximo. Our Private Dining Room is perfect for groups from 12 to 36. Let Chef Amador create a customized tasting menu for your group and pair it with the perfect wines for a palate memory that will last a lifetime! Great patio and full-service catering is also available.

We offer full-service catering and a wide variety of menus; from authentic Modern Mexican dishes like Salmon al Vapor, steamed in aromatic herbs and served with sweet corn mash and jicama-jalapeno slaw, to Tex-Mex, barbacoa or the best steaks in town. Our professional service staff will do all the work for you to ensure a seamlessly easy and memorable event!

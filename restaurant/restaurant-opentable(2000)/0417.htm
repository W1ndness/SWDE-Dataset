Tsunami - Annapolis

writes...
  • From fresh sushi to chef prepared steaks and seafood, Tsunami caters to all appetites. Surrounded in a modern, intimate, and lively atmosphere Tsunami welcomes all to enjoy anything from a creative drink, to a quick snack to a night of dinner and dancing.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Sushi, Pan-Asian
  • Neighborhood: Annapolis
  • Cross Street: Calvert/Cathedral
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.kapowgroup.com/tsunami
  • Email: info@tsunamiannapolis.com
  • Phone: (410) 990-9868
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Nightly: 5:30pm - 11:00pm, sushi bar until 1:00am (If interested in requesting BOOTH dinner seating, please note that booths are generally only available at 6 / 6:30pm or 8 / 8:30pm. Otherwise we will do our best to accomodate your request.)
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Bobby Massa and Jonathan Hsu
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Dancing, Entertainment, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Best spot is Gott's Public Parking Garage located on Calvert St. between Northwest St. and West St. Once parked, exit garage near ticket dispenser and pass through small walkway and you will be right across the street from Tsunami.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private party room available for up to 22 people. Special, customized menus available. Room Fees or Guest Minimums apply for private events held Thursday through Saturday.
  • Private Party Contact: Any Manager (410) 990-9868
  • Entertainment: DJ (alt rock) many nights after 10pm. Individually scheduled theme parties, art shows, release parties and fundraising events.

Flatwater

writes...
  • Flatwater, situated just below the Clark Street Bridge, offers a casual contemporary American fare by Executive Chef Alex Negrete, as well as imaginative cocktails in an easy, urban setting. Surrounded by exquistite views of the Loop and the Chicago River, Flatwater is Chicago's newest hot spot. Open for breakfast, lunch, dinner and weekend brunch; Flatwater provides every diner a truly unique and exciting dining experience.

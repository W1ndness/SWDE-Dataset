Chop House Miami

writes...
  • Chop House Miami (formerly Manny's) has changed the Name but continues to serve the same Custom Steaks with a professional flare; Chop House Miami evokes the great steakhouses of New York & Chicago - & eclipses them in critical & popular acclaim. Rated Top Five in the U.S. by the Zagat Guide, named one of America's top Steakhouses by both the Knife & Fork Club & the Independent Cattleman's Association, & a perennial winner of newspaper & magazine reader polls, Chop House Miami commands a fanatical local following & a national reputation. Wrote the New York Times: "There's good news for those who still share the secret pleasures of red meat, well-cut & seriously served." In addition to USDA-certified, dry- aged, center-cut beef, Chop House Miami offers chops, chicken, fresh seafood & a Wine Spectator Award-winning wine list.

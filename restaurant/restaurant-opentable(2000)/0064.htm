MetroVino

writes...
  • Metrovino is a restaurant and bar located in Portland's Pearl District, just a few walking blocks north of Jamison Square Park on the Portland Street Car line. We offer an inspired menu featuring refined yet rustic cuisine. Executive Chef Gregory Denton uses time-honored techniques and flavor combinations to prepare fresh, local ingredients simply and in season. Using an Enomatic wine system, the bar features Portland's largest wine by the glass selection, plus a strong list of locally distilled and internationally renowned spirits.

Cafe Cabernet

writes...
  • Café Cabernet has a casual yet sophisticated atmosphere. A place where a guest of any age can select from over 30 wines by the glass or 1000 bottles in our temperature controlled cellar. Café offers the area’s largest liquor selection so no matter what your mood; Cafe will have something for it. Chef Joe has put together an eclectic menu using only the freshest local ingredients; from prime steak to fresh local seafood we offer something to please the most discriminating palette. Whether you’re in the mood to sit out on the patio and enjoy a fine cigar and port or sit in our dining room for that special occasion, Café is sure to please. When you are planning a larger event Café has several rooms to choose from that can accommodate groups of many sizes. Please come and see what is new and exciting at Café Cabernet.

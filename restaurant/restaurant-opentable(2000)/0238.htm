NINE-TEN

writes...
  • An ongoing commitment to innovative cuisine using only the freshest local produce makes NINE-TEN a must visit for culinary aficionados. This intimate restaurant, led by chef Jason Knibb and pastry chef Jack Fisher, offers the perfect combination of sophistication and casual elegance and an ideal location along La Jolla’s Prospect Street. Enjoy the best of the harvest from local artisan farmers, where produce is selected daily for the restaurant’s Evolving California Cuisine. The bounty of the sea is also featured with local fish selections and items arriving daily from Hawaii and the North Pacific and Atlantic regions. Special prix fixe menus are available and feature matching wines from the extensive wine cellar. Enjoy dining indoors or on the sidewalk patio or ocean-view terrace. A separate bar offers limited menu and happy hour specials daily. Open daily for breakfast and lunch and dinner Tuesday through Saturday.

Large windows provide natural sunlight during the day and intimacy at night. This private room features its own sound system, chandeliers & draperies with blackout capabilities.

    Named for the skylight and natural light that floods the space, the Sun Room overlooks the pool and ocean. Natural sunlight streams in through the windows creating an inviting atmosphere, enhanced with fresh flowers and a fireplace, for brunches, lunches, dinners, receptions and cocktail parties.

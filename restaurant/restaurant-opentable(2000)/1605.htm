Specializing in fresh seafood and New England-style dishes the Summer Shack is perfect for locals and visitors. World-renowned Chef and Cookbook Author Jasper White's restaurant design offers the pure, raw classic look of coastal clam shacks with delicious food and friendly service. Enjoy a lobster clambake within walking distance from Hynes Convention Center, Fenway Park, and many of Boston’s hotels and attractions. Boasting one of Boston’s largest raw bars and a huge lobster tank, the Summer Shack guarantees freshness. For non-seafood eaters the fun corn dogs and famous fried chicken do not disappoint. Brown-paper tablecloths double as drawing paper. The philosophy behind Jasper White's Summer Shack is 'Food is Love' and proof is in the dishes. Enjoy a tradition New England clambake or choose from a great selections of hors d'oeuvres, plated menus,food station menus, buffet menus, etc. We will be happy to work with you to customize a menu for your particular party.

Metropolis Cafe

writes...
  • A cozy and casual cafe overlooking bustling Tremont Street, in Boston's Historic South End. Offering our guests fine dining, hospitality and casual dining prices. We serve dinner nightly including a popular neighborhood brunch on both Saturday and Sunday.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Mediterranean
  • Neighborhood: South End
  • Cross Street: Between Clarendon St.and Dartmouth St.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.metropolisboston.com
  • Email: manager@metropolisboston.com
  • Phone: (617) 247-2931
  • Hours of Operation: Brunch: Saturday - Sunday: 9:00am -3:00pm Dinner: Sunday - Wednesday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm Thursday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Seth Woods
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Cafe, Counter Seating, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wine

  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet service is available.

Creation Cafe

writes...
  • Creation Cafe is a fun and funky bistro located at the North end of Indy"s downtown canal. Enjoy a fresh cup of coffee in the morning, our signature Mother Clucker sandwich for lunch, or a drink and steak for dinner. Looking for lighter fare? We have great salads too. We do it all! We focus on home-made and locally sourced food as much as possible, which sets us apart from many other choices in Indianapolis. We also have a great outdoor patio with fabulous views of Indy's skyline and the canal.

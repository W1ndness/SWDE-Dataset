Maya - San Francisco

writes...
  • Chef/owner Richard Sandoval brought something unique to the City that has seen it all when it comes to restaurants. Looking to his native Acapulco for inspiration, Sandoval created an authentic upscale Mexican restaurant to showcase a side of Mexican cuisine that is rarely seen north of the border.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Mexican
  • Neighborhood: SOMA
  • Cross Street: Harrison St. & Folsom St.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.mayasf.com
  • Email: carlos@mayasf.com
  • Phone: (415) 543-2928
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Tuesday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Sunday & Monday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Richard Sandoval
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking Lot across the street
  • Private Party Facilities: Private room for parties from 10 guests to 220 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Carlos Delgado (415) 543-2928

Compadres Rio Grill

writes...
  • Located on the West bank of the Napa River, just North of a revitalized downtown, Compadres Rio Grille is the most unique dining and party venue in the Napa Valley.
    Compadres is a contemporary casual restaurant featuring a style of food that has been called Western Cooking with a Mexican Accent, highlighting products and presentations from Valle and Jalisco in Central and Northern Mexico as well as the American Southwest and the SF Bay Area.
    Compadres offers one of the largest selections of Tequilas North of the Border, a wide variety of Margaritas and other specialty bebidas, a small but carefully selected wine list, regional Micro Brews on draft, and a large selection of the best Mexican bottled beers.
    Compadres' young, energetic staff create a festive dining experience and make Compadres a great place to party...

  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Compadres features a large paved and landscaped parking lot that accommodates 140 cars.
    Street side parking is also available.
    Compadres can accommodate buses.
  • Catering: Compadres offers full service catering throughout the Bay Area, specializing in corporate picnics, rehearsal dinners, split station cocktail parties, bar/bat mitzvahs, garden parties, and multi-item buffets.
    Compadres is a featured caterer at the University of California, Berkeley providing services to CAL Football, The Bear Backers, The Alumni Association, The Haas School of Business, CAL Baseball, The Big C Society, and CAL Rec Sports. Compadres provides varied services to many of the finest wineries in The Valley.
  • Private Party Facilities: Compadres features two adjoining private party rooms, accommodating 30 and 70 guests for sit-down dining. One room is adjacent to a semi-private outside deck providing cocktail party space for up to 350.
    A wide variety of buffets and customizable menus are available to suit client needs.
    Compadres is a very popular location for rehearsal dinners, and the facilities are used by many corporate accounts for meetings and seminars.
  • Private Party Contact: Rick Enos (707) 253-1111
  • Entertainment: Local guitarist and Vocalist, Doug Howser, plays in the bar on Thursday evenings, 8-11 pm.

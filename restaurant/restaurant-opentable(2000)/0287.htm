Pasta Vino

writes...
  • Pasta Vino has been a favorite Italian Restaurant in Atlanta for many years. It’s a great place to sop up the slow-simmered red sauce with homemade bread and relax with a glass of wine. The food is made from scratch and the sauces start early in the morning to simmer all day. The grated cheese is imported parmesiana regianna cut from a giant wheel and grated in the kitchen. Pasta Vino has a variety of menu items including Eggplant Parmesan, NY Style Pizza by the slice, heaping plates of Spaghetti and small plates of favorite pasta rustica - as well as Veal and Fish. House specialties with the emphasis on flavorful authentic Italian meals and personal attention make dining with us a pleasure. Pasta Vino is "in-town" without an attitude. There is a friendliness that is many times missing in Buckhead as couples linger over wine, families mingle and children play. We invite you to relax, unwind and reconnect with friends and enjoy. Indoor and patio seating is available.

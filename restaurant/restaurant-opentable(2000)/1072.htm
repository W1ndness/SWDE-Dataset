Villaggio Ristorante & Bar

writes...
  • Welcome to Villaggio Ristorante & Bar where Elevated, Innovative, Italian lives. Established in 2003, we have an enduring reputation for providing comfortable elegant dining. Though we provide a finer dining atmosphere, we pride ourselves on accommodating any style of dress and personality.

    Villaggio Ristorante and Bar is a comfortable and casual Italian dining spot. Guests can feel right at home in shorts and a t-shirt and still be able to have a fine dining experience. Owner and Executive Chef Emilio Cento has been creating taste sensations in the Indianapolis area for over 15 years and brings his skill and art to visitors of this Zionsville spot for over four years. Located in the heart of Zionsville, 15 minutes from downtown, 10 minutes from Brownsburg and only 7 minutes from Carmel, we are sure dining with Villaggio will be a weekly occurrence.

Gia - Italian Kitchen and Wine Bar at the Eden Roc

writes...
  • Located lobby level in the Historic and hip Eden Roc Renaissance, Gia Italian Kitchen & Wine Bar embraces the simplicity of Tuscan cuisine while striving to support local farmers through the use of the freshest ingredients available.Enjoy dining alfresco while overlooking the infinity pools and the atlantic ocean or chose a more intimate experience in one of the private dining rooms.Gia's dining room will surround you in the casual elegance of slate, hand blown chandeliers and Sunflowers. A visit will not be complete without tasing a offering from Gia's wine room and chef crafted desserts. We invite you to come experience, taste and savor what we call Gia.

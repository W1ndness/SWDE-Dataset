Mill Race Inn

writes...
  • The Mill Race Inn is legendary for years of hospitality, good food and gracious service. Its river setting, rich history and many cozy rooms and spaces offer different atmospheres and seasonal features that can only be enjoyed fully after repeat visits. Join them for an old fashioned country breakfast, served from their Sunday menu instead of buffet style.

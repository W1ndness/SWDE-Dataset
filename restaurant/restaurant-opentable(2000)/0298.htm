Ruth's Chris Steak House - Savannah

writes...
  • Visit Ruth's Chris Steak House in Historic Savannah, Georgia. Whether it's a romantic steak dinner for two, an important business dinner, or a private party, Ruth's Chris Steak House can accommodate your needs. Our Savannah steakhouse provides guests with a warm, comfortable atmosphere and impeccable service.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, American
  • Neighborhood: Savannah
  • Cross Street: Whitaker
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://ruthschris.com
  • Email: RuthsChrisSA@rccharlotte.com
  • Phone: (912) 721-4800
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Cody Buford
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet is available directly behind the restaurant at a $5.00 rate Whitaker Street Parking Garage is available directly behind the restaurant at a cost of $2.00 per hour
  • Private Party Facilities: 2 elegant dining rooms with complete built in audio visual services including flat screen TVs for computer and DVD presentations.
  • Private Party Contact: Jamie Durrence (912) 721-4800
  • Entertainment: Live jazz entertainment on Friday evenings
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Ruth's Chris Steak House will be closed for Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, New Years Day and Super Bowl Sunday (February 7th). We will be open from 5:00 to 11:00pm New Years Eve.

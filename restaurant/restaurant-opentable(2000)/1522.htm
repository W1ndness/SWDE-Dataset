The Daily Planet

writes...
  • The Daily Planet — a hush eatery, funky bar and chic solarium. We give you the choice: let orb lamps and celestial surroundings illuminate your fresh-tap brew, go for stargazing au natural at outdoor sidewalk tables or from the indoor solarium, retreat into the dining room for a more private evening. Either way, you’ll be sharing space with tourists and locals, artists and after-work laborers, the retro and the nouveau. Be comfortable, whether alone or with friends. Our restaurant’s ever-evolving seasonal menu sends you spinning the globe. The food at our colorful alleyway eatery is, well, out of this world.

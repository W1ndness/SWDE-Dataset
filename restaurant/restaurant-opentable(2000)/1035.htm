La Boca

writes...
  • La Boca is a bustling downtown Santa Fe tapas house inspired by the casual tapas restaurants throughout southern Spain. Owner/Chef James Campbell Caruso tantalizes palettes with flavors originating in Southern Spain and Morocco. Please join us and allow Chef James( three time James Beard Best Chef of the Southwest nominee) and our pleasant and professional staff take you an a unforgettable culinary adventure.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Spanish, Moroccan
  • Neighborhood: Santa Fe
  • Cross Street: Lincoln
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.labocasf.com
  • Email: info@labocasf.com
  • Phone: (505) 982-3433
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Sunday: 11:30am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: James Campbell Caruso
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Entertainment, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Downtown Santa Fe meters are enforced Monday through Saturday from 8am - 6pm. Residents and visitors may also park at the Santa Fe Convention Center. The entrance is on Federal Plaza across from the Federal Post Office.
  • Catering: La Boca can bring the culinary adventure to your Santa Fe location! We specialize in delicious finger food tapas and paella parties for homes, offices and galleries. Request information at our office 505.983.2425
  • Entertainment: Every Wednesday night Nacha Mendez entertains the guests of La Boca with Spanish and Latin Jazz. Nacha begins between 6:30pm and 7:00pm.

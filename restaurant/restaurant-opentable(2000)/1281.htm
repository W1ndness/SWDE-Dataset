Naya Bistro and Lounge

writes...
  • NAYA combines a creative mix of internationally-inspired cuisine, an electric and stylish atmosphere, and a specialized wine list and cosmopolitan bar, the bistro offers innovative, multi-ethnic menu laden with dishes created from a fusion of Lebanese, Contemporary American and Italian fare. NAYA emphasizes a dedication to healthful eating and sustainable development. The restaurant"s chef's hand-select only the freshest organic ingredients, fruits, and vegetables from local growers whenever available. From free range chicken to hormone-free beef to organic wines and liquors, NAYA prepares exotic appetizers, entrees, desserts, and cocktails made with natural ingredients all with tastes unrivaled by eateries in the greater Cleveland area.

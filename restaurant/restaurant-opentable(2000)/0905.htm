Faz Sunnyvale

writes...
  • Faz Sunnyvale is a full service restaurant and bar featuring the distinctive Mediterranean-style cuisine of Faz Poursohi. the restaurant features exhibition cooking with wood burning ovens, a rotisserie, and a smoker.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Mediterranean, Italian
  • Neighborhood: Sunnyvale
  • Cross Street: Highway 237
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.fazrestaurants.com
  • Phone: (408) 752-8000
  • Hours of Operation: Monday - Sunday: 6:00am - 10:00pm
    Brunch: Sunday: 10:30am - 3:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Faz Poursohi
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Full Bar, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Weekend Brunch
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Free Lot in front of restaurant
  • Catering: Call for details
  • Private Party Facilities: Faz can accommodate up to 25 guests at one table in its main dining room. For all parties over 15, the chef will design a special menu. Credit Card guarantees are required. For more information please contact the restaurant .
  • Private Party Contact: Niloo Salehi (408) 752-8006
  • Entertainment: Piano nightly

Foxfire Restaurant

writes...
  • Foxfire Restaurant is renowned as an elegant, romantic and sophisticated fine dining establishment. An inland oasis where creative cuisine goes hand-in-hand with stylish ambiance. At Foxfire, meat, game, poultry and seafood dishes (including prime angus beef entrees aged to perfection) are delicious and affordable. One of the specialty of the house is the Italian veal Osso Buco, dubbed "awesome buco" by satisfied diners. Also available is the three course Foxfire Classics Dinner for $19.95 per person. The Special Classic Menu items include Prime Rib, Braised Short Rib, Seafood and Chicken specials. Private dining rooms with themed decor can accommodate a variety of social occasions. Enjoy a leisurely lunch or dinner on the enchanting Garden Patio. An amazing Sunday Champagne brunch, a lively lounge scene featuring karaoke and live bands, and superb cuisine all help make Foxfire one of the hottest restaurants in town.

Bhatti Indian Grill

writes...
  • Bhatti indian Grill is an ultra-authentic North Indian restaurant that’s refined yet affordable, upscale and still comfortable. However, the food is truly a gourmand’s treasure trove—after training with master chefs in New Delhi and traveling rural North India to gain access to the region’s most secret, almost ritualistic recipes, chef/owner Anand is able to deliver Indian dishes unlike any other currently being served in the city. The name of the restaurant refers to a special open fire grill used in Punjab. It was there that, generations ago, hunters would strive to achieve the freshest meals by carrying a grill that could be used in the same forests where they found their catch. The recipes and methods they used are recreated here, and in their tradition, Anand finishes each dish over lava rocks which impart a signature flavor.

Mitchell's Fish Market - Cleveland

writes...
  • We invite you to discover our fresh seafood obsession at Mitchell's Fish Market, where the fish is off the boat fresh, the drinks are made with fresh-squeezed juice & the atmosphere is as warm & inviting as a day on the water. At Mitchell's Fish Market we are obsessed with fresh seafood. Our mantra is a commitment & passion for freshness as a way of life - a pledge that our fish is 100% fresh or it isn't good enough. With more than 80 items, the chef-driven menu includes items such as Cedar Roasted Atlantic Salmon & Hoisin Glazed Yellowfin Tuna. Come experience what an obsession with freshness tastes like at Mitchell's Fish Market...fish any fresher would still be in the ocean! View our menus online at www.mitchellsfishmarket.com!
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Beachwood
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.mitchellsfishmarket.com
  • Email: mfmcleveland@mitchellsfishmarket.com
  • Phone: (216) 765-3474
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:30am - 4:00pm, Sunday: 11:30am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 3:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Blake Harper
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Complimentary parking is available in Eton's parking lot during lunch and dinner. Valet parking is also offered during dinner, Wednesday through Saturday for $4.00.
  • Private Party Facilities: Four private dining rooms are available at Mitchell’s Fish Market, Eton Collection. The Captain’s Quarters accommodates between 10 & 14 guests. For slightly larger parties two semi-private Stern Rooms are available, both accommodating 10 to 15 guests. Lastly, a completely private Stern Room is available, which can accommodate up to 36 guests with media capabilities offered for presentations. Whether planning a gathering with family & friends or a business meeting, make Mitchell’s Fish Market your first choice for private dining. Several banquet menu options are offered to help plan a meal that is sure to please your guests.

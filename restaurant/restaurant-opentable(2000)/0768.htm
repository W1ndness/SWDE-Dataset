Molokini Bar & Grille - Makena Beach & Golf Resort

writes...
  • Molokini Bar & Grille (Open nightly) - NEW Lounge with Pacific Rim Inspired pupu! Molokini Bar & Grille is the heart and center of the resort. This casual elegant bar is the perfect place to gather with friends and family. Sip on a signature cocktail and enjoy the 180 degree panoramic ocean view and brilliant sunsets, overlooking Molokini, Kaho'olawe and Lanai. Relax and enjoy light pupu (appetizers) in this intimate and thoroughly enjoyable lounge and ask the bartender to tell you the Tale of Mo'o and Molokinii."
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Tapas / Small Plates
  • Neighborhood: Kihei
  • Cross Street: Makena Alanui
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.makenaresortmaui.com/
  • Email: gmorton@benchmarkmanagement.com
  • Phone: (808) 874-1111
  • Hours of Operation: Lounge: Monday - Sunday: 6:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Greg Gaspar
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Entertainment: The “Makena Sunset with Bradda Francis” dinner show will feature entertainment from award-winning musician Bradda Francis, who is well known for his performances and recordings of Hawaiian, paniolo, slack key, pop and country music. As he describes it: “Singing from the heart, Hawaiian style.” The show will be held every Thursday and Friday, 2010, along with a buffet featuring Hawaii favorites such as ahi poke, char sui pork-fried rice and lomi-lomi salmon.
  • Special Events & Promotions:

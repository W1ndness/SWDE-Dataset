Maison

writes...
  • Maison is a casual French Brasserie located in the heart of the theatre district of Midtown Manhattan. Open 24/7, the space is lined by a picturesque garden and features indoor/outdoor dining areas, a smoking section and the largest (climate-controlled) umbrella in the U.S., ensuring enjoyable all-weather events. Seated under the umbrella one has a clear view of Time Square to the right, and Central Park to the left.

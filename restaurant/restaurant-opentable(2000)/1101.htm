    Total Capacity 45

Minneapolis’s favorite heartland restaurant, FireLake grill house & cocktail bar, is the perfect venue for celebrations with friends, families and co-workers. The restaurant offers two cozy private dining areas that are ideal for hosting a festive party while enjoying Chef Paul’s seasonal offerings. FireLake offers several private dining options to cater to groups of all sizes for their holiday gatherings. The Private Dining Room, separated by glass doors from the main dining room, seats up to 42 guests, easily accommodating office celebrations. Smaller groups can dine in the intimate Board Room, which is also separated from the main dining room. Featuring an elegant long table that seats up to 16 guests, the Board Room is also an ideal choice for a holiday family seeking to relinquish their cooking duties in favor of an enjoyable and stress-free dining experience with a gathering of friends or loved ones.

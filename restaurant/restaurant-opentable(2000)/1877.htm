Perry St

writes...
  • Jean-Georges Vongerichten delivers casual elegance to the West Side with the opening of Perry St, his latest culinary foray located in the Richard Meier Towers. Retreat to a relaxed & lovely setting for food that reflects his culinary strengths and sensibility more tidily than any of his restaurants since his flagship, Jean Georges, which opened in 1997. The dishes beautifully capture his instinct for flavor and texture combinations, his usually keen sense of equilibrium and, of course, his recruitment of traditions and ingredients from Asia.

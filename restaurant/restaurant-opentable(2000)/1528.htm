Evangeline

writes...
  • Evangeline is a modern style, french influenced bistro located in the heart of Portland's West End. We serve modern French cuisine along with an incredible selection of French wine and a cutting edge cocktail list. On Mondays we offer a 3 course Prix Fix Menu for $30. Closed on Sundays. (Except for special occasions)

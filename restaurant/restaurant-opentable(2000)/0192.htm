Facilities 1 of 5
  • Courtyard Room

    Overlooking the lush courtyard of the W Hotel, this room is bright and tropical during the day and decidedly romantic at night. The room is magnificently adorned with a hand painted mural of a trompe l'oeil representation of a stone castle on one wall and hand blown Venetian glass sconces on the other.The room will seat up to 30 guests and up to 35 guests for a reception.

    Capacity
    3530
  • Chartres Street Room

    This room features floor to ceiling windows that overlook historic Chartres Street, hand blown Venetian glass wall sconces, and a hand painted mural of Bacchus (the god of wine and BACCO's namesake) proffering a cup to a nymph on the ceiling.

    Capacity
    5040
  • Wine Room

    The Wine Room, home to a portion of BACCO's vast wine collection, is bathed in soft buttery light from three beautiful, hand painted silk chandeliers imported from Venice, Italy. This room is framed by a large window overlooking Chartres Street and is divided from the Skylight Room by an antique French iron gate.

    Capacity
    5550
  • Skylight Room

    Separated from BACCO's Wine Room by an antique French gate, this room is twice the size of the other dining rooms and features a beautiful vaulted ceiling with skylights. An authentic 18th century French compass point, elaborate in detail and grand in size, graces the west wall. Golden cherubs hang from the wall sconces assuring a good time for all.

    Capacity
    10080
  • The Hermann-Grima House and Courtyard

    BACCO is pleased the be the exclusive caterer for this beautiful French Quarter Courtyard. Built in 1831, the Hermann-Grima House is one of the most significant residences in New Orleans. This handsome Federal Mansion with its courtyard garden boasts the only horse stable and functional 1830's outdoor kitchen in the French Quarter. Through today, this richly planted courtyard provides a spectacular setting for unforgettable special events for up to 250 guests.

    Capacity

BACCO, located in the heart of the French Quarter, is the perfect location for all of your social and business entertaining needs. Noted for ambitious Creole Italian cuisine, BACCO offers a casually elegant dining experience amidst stunning contemporary decor. Specializing in housemade pastas and fresh regional seafood, BACCO's award winning cuisine has earned accolades from Esquire, Southern Living, and Bon Appetit magazines. Locally owned and operated by Ralph Brennan, he and the entire BACCO team understand personal touches make events special. Our dedicated sales team and BACCO Executive Chef Chris Montero will work with you to ensure that every detail - from the food and beverage to the service, ambiance, and timing - are exactly right.

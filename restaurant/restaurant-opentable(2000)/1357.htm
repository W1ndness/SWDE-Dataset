Gordon Ramsay - West Hollywood

writes...
  • Chef Gordon Ramsay’s first Los Angeles restaurant offers fine-dining inspired by California’s fresh produces. Under the direction of Executive Chef Anthony Keene - the Michelin-starred restaurant serves Western European cuisine with regional influences, and menus change regularly based on seasonal offerings. Enjoy a variety of market specials, several a la carte dishes and pre-set tasting menus. The ambiance is glamorous and energetic, with posh dining rooms that overlook dramatic views of the city skyline. Servers are knowledgeable and approachable, while an onsite Sommelier can recommend from a list of more than 500 bottles. Open daily for dinner.

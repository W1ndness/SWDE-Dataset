Seven Gables Restaurant

writes...
  • Chef Alain has founded, owned and operated Seven Gables Eclectic Restaurant since November 1988. It soon became a local dining treasure. Serving Continental Classics & Specialties which include Dover Sole Meuniere, Filet Mignon Bérnaise, Roast Rack of Lamb Romarin, Ribeye Steak Madagascar, Chicken Wellington, Roast Duck Breast, Pheasant and Quail…Lobster Tails, King Crab, Crabmeat Raviolis and Garden Linguini as well as Moroccan and Mediterranean Succulent Specialties are also available with 24h notice. We prepare from scratch in-house our breads, pasta, dressings, soups (Clam Chowder and Bisques), sauces and our renowned Italian Sausage. Seven Gables’ own pastry chef also prepares scrumptious desserts daily, Crème Brulee, Chocolate Ganach Cheese Cake, Carrot Cake…. We offer a full Bar, extensive Wine list, Espresso, Cappuccino and so much more.

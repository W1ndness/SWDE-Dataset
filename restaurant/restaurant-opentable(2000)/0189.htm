Zoubi

writes...
  • Zoubi opened in the Spring of 2001, & became an instant hit with the locals as well as visitors to the New Hope,PA & Lambertville, NJ areas. Zoubi"s unique setting combined with it's eclectic menu, makes every visit a memorable one. A fusion of French, Asian & Latin American Cuisine awaits guests, in addition to an exotic decor created by owner Louis Zanias along with friend Keneth Harvey. Veteran Restaurateur & Co-Owner Andre Lenoble encourages a mood of intimate & relaxed good spirits by taking a personable interest in each guest, intention all enjoying their dining experience.

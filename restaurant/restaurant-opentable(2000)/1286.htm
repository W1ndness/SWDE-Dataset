Stages at the Cleveland Play House

writes...
  • Stages at the Cleveland Play House is located on the Carnegie Avenue side of the Cleveland Play House. Newly renovated in the former Play House Club space, this exciting new restaurant features a creative menu, piano entertainment prior to the Cleveland Play House productions and musical events in the restaurant beginning at 9:00 p.m. on Fridays and Saturdays.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Cleveland
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.StagesRestaurant.com
  • Email: Stages@StagesRestaurant.com
  • Phone: (216) 795-1111
  • Hours of Operation: Tuesday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Chef Robert Buehner
  • Dress Code: Business Casual

  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: 100 car parking lot directly adjacent to the restaurant entrance, valet available for dinner hours.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private party room available for up to 100 guests, separate from the main dining room.
  • Private Party Contact: Tom Hlepas (216) 795-7000 x280
  • Entertainment: Piano entertainment during pre-theater dining. Musical events including jazz, classical, big band and Latin on Fridays and Saturdays beginning at 9 p.m.

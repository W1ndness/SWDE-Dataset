D'Amato's Restaurant & Goodnite Gracie Martini Bar

writes...
  • D'Amato's Neighborhood Restaurant & Wine Bar is a fun and inviting casual fine-dining concept offering a blend of classic and contemporary Italian cuisine. Goodnite Gracie, our companion night spot right next door, offers award winning martinis, music, an inviting atmosphere and a full premium bar.

    An Italian bistro at heart, D'Amato's allows its' chefs the creativity to branch out beyond traditional Italian techniques. The atmosphere D'Amato's is casual and inviting.

    The scene is blend of young professionals and established fun-loving adults. The restaurant's extensive wine list is carefully selected to offer a broad range of the finest domestic and imported selections. D'Amato's philosophy is simple: by providing a first-rate dining experience at a great value, customers will return time and again.

  • Private Party Facilities: D'Amato' Restaurant & Goodnite Gracie Jazz & Martini Bar offer several options for private dining. Our Florentine Room is perfect for up to 35 guests and can be reserved for formal sit down dinners or more casual cocktail parties. Goodnite Gracie is available for all types of get-togethers including, work functions, birthdays, bachelor, bachelorette parties, and more. Reserve a few tables or the whole place. The patio can be reserved as well. This is our space with and with an urban appeal and lovely outdoor setting. It is available for all types of events and can seat app. 35, but will hold 50+ for cocktail parties. The conference room is another unique space that is available for use. We offer conference space for 35-50. Dinner seating for 35 indoor and in addition, what makes this space unique is a beautiful attached outdoor patio that combined will hold 60-70. Our ATRIUM space boasting a fountain and curved staircase are perfect for dinners up to to 100.
  • Private Party Contact: Lisa Jackman (248) 584-7400

Anything from the original songs of Liz Larin and Teddy Richards, to the Gypsy Jazz of the Red Hot Sugar Daddies. Weekends might you might see a smooth Jazz combo or DJ Urban Kris groove on the turntable.

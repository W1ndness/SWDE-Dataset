Persepolis

writes...
  • Persepolis Is a Persian/ Mediterranean restaurant with a dynamic atmosphere and 20 years of experience! We are located in the Upper East Side of Manhattan on 2nd Avenue & 73rd Street. Our chefs goal is to serve the finest Persian Feast and the best ingredients available from local market & Persian import. we prepare a simple yet creative menu that highlights those ingredients. The restaurant is Long Famous for verity of Kebab’s And REFRESHING SALADS & GENTLY SPICED GRILLED MEATS. THE YOGURT & CUCUMBER, BLENDED WITH A LITTLE MINT, IS MADE EXCEEDINGLY WELL & IDEAL COOL COMPLEMENT TO PERSEPOLIS’S ROUNDS OF PITA BREAD AND BARBARI BREAD. KEBABAS, INCLUDING MOIST AND DELICIOUS SALMON or SAFFRON CHICKEN, ARE SERVED WITHOUT HEAVY SAUCES. THEY ARE SERVED WITH A CHOICE OF FOUR RICES, INCLUDING AN INTRIGUING BLEND OF BASMATI RICE AND MARINATED SOUR CHERRIES. Our personalized wine list truly complements the cuisine and we offer a classic full bar! Enjoy Your Visit At Persepolis.

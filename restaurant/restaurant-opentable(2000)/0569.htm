Hinterland Erie Street Gastropub

writes...
  • When it comes to great dining experiences in Milwaukee, Hinterland Erie Street Gastropub is among the short the list. Set in a hip, Third Ward warehouse, the dining room is set aglow by candlelight and oak wine stave chandeliers. Our lounge is a great place to retreat for a handcrafted beer, cocktail or an exceptional glass of wine. Hinterland also offers sidewalk dining. While Hinterland’s reputation for great atmosphere has made it a favorite among locals for romantic dinners, business meetings and wedding parties, its amazing food and service keeps people coming back again and again. Its menu continues to evolve and impress. Highlights are: Oven roasted, andouille encrusted monchong, Fennel dusted Japanese mero seabass, Wood fire grilled Certified Angus Beef® ribeye steak or Wood fire grilled Navarino elk tenderloin. In the lounge, Hinterland offers a more bar friendly menu featuring Kobe beef sliders with garlic French fries, chicken pad thai, Baja fish tacos and elk meatloaf.

  • Private Party Facilities: At Hinterland, the private dining options are endless. Whether your group’s size is 20 people or 125, we can provide the pinnacle experience and value for your guests. If your looking for an intimate space for up to 20 people, our private dining room is the perfect setting, or if a smaller business meeting is the agenda let us facilitate a private room with an LCD projector and screen. For groups of 20 to 40 guests, Hinterland’s lounge is the perfect space with its own private bar for reception and plenty of seating for a “sit-down” dinner or passed hors d’ oeuvre party. Also, the entire restaurant is available for private parties of 40 or more people for more formal wedding receptions, holiday parties, business meetings or for any occasion.

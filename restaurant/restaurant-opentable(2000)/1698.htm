Boulevard Five72

writes...
  • Boulevard Five72 is a New American Restaurant and Banquet Facility conveniently located in the center of Kenilworth. We are proud to announce that since our opening at the end of August 2008 , Boulevard Five72 has gained a reputation as one of the finest restaurants and caterers in our area. The Star Ledger named us runner up for the "Best New Restaurant in New Jersey for 2008" and the New Jersey Monthly just listed us as one of the top 22 Restaurants that you must try! Lastly, we received a review of 3 and 1/2 stars from the Star Ledger in October of 2008. Boulevard Five72 offers a Contemporary American Menu with Global influences under the direction of Executive Chef and owner Scott Snyder. We have a beautiful lounge, offering a full Bar as well as an extensive Wine List. Our Main Dining Room seats up to 60 guests, our Private Wine Room seats up to 25 guests and our Banquet Room can accommodate up to 150 guests.

We offer complete tasting menus to help you choose your menu and if you choose we can take care of every detail of your event including all entertainment, floral arrangements, equipment rental including but not limited to Tents, Tables, Chairs, Linen, China.
  • Private Party Facilities: Boulevard’s professionally trained staff is available for all of your private party needs and we have extensive experience in both corporate meetings and events. Our beautiful facility is newly renovated to include Wi-Fi access, Sirius Satellite Radio, and Verizon FiOS. The Wine Room and Lounge have HD TV's that can be accessed directly from your lap top and our Banquet Room also has complete audio and visual capabilities. We are committed to exceeding all of your expectations and will gladly customize your event to meet all of your needs regarding menu and pricing. Our Wine Room is the ideal setting for a Board Room Style meeting or wine dinner. The Wine Room accommodates up to 24 Guests Conference Style and the room has HD TV readily accessible to your laptop. A separate Dining Room is also available should you choose to meet in the Wine Room and enjoy your meal in our Dining Room.

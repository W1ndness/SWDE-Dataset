BORRACHO CANTINA

  • Located on the world famous Sunset Strip, the party never stops at Borracho Cantina. We have excellent home cooking, incredible prices and our cantina is the party place to watch every major sporting event with lot's of big screens for your viewing pleasure. Come try our signature cocktail "The "Horny Margarita" or have one of our tequila girls pour you that very special shot. Borracho offers a hip, fun ambience and two happy hours daily from 3 PM to 6 PM and 10 PM to 1 AM. Great food and drink specials everyday! Borracho has oversized horseshoe booths that creates the perfect party atmosphere. we have a great covered, heated outdoor cantina patio along the sunset strip to see and be seen!

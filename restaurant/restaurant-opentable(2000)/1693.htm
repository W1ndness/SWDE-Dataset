Blu- New York

writes...
  • Located within the heart of New York’s flower district in Chelsea, Blu Restaurant and Bar is a 70 Seat serves contemporary American food with Italian influence. Simple food coked in our unique style, created with great ingredients executed in a consistent manner. We feature a 70 bottle wine list consisting of wines from the US, Spain, Italy and France. We concentrate on smaller production winemakers that create more consistent wines year in and year out.

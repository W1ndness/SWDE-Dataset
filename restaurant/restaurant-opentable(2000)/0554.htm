Chez Philippe - Peabody Hotel Memphis

  • The Peabody's Hotel signature restaurant is the epitome of exquisite dining; French cuisine with an Asian flair. With impeccable service, distinguished wines and an atmosphere beyond compare, Chez Philippe remains the pinnacle as Memphis' only Mobil Four - Star Restaurant. Southern Afternoon Tea is served by reservations only; Tuesday through Saturday from 2:00 to 3:00pm; 24 hours advanced notice required.

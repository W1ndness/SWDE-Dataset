Posta Tuscan Grille

writes...
  • Tuscan Posta Grille is an authentic Tuscan style restaurant located in the Raleigh Marriott City Center. Posta Tuscan Grill combines fresh ingredients with traditional recipes for a dining experience that until now, was only available in our first restaurant in San Casciano, Italy. Marco and Gianni Betti, proudly invites you to taste the simple, flavorful dishes that have made Italian and Tuscan cooking the most appreciated cuisine in the world. Posta Tuscan Grille brings to our community the same genuine, sincere cuisine of Tuscany. Enjoy the delicate blend of meats, fish, vegetables, spices, and condiments that for centuries have pleased the palate of the most demanding Florentines.

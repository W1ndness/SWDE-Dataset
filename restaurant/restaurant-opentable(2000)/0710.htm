Sushi by Chef Soon

writes...
  • A contemporary sushi spot operated by Chef Soon with specialization in Japanese cuisine with over 17 years of experience in various upscale restaurants. Remember the unique and delicious sushi platters that you once enjoyed at Blu Coral Contemporary Sushi which was located at the Seven Bridges Mall? Well, the Head Chef of that place has now opened up his own venture close by. Head Chef Soon Park has enjoyed serving all his loyal customers that came to enjoy his unique but yet exquisite sushi that he prepared. Please come and visit him at the sushi bar located inside SeoCho Korean BBQ Restaurant in Maple Plaza. As an innovator of a new style Japanese food with unique blend of creative flair and passion for food, he will introduce you to a new level of sushi with great flavors and innovative presentation. On the extensive menu, Chef Soon features both contemporary and traditional sushi. Don't be fooled by the exterior strip mall. Come inside and enjoy some of the freshest sushi ...

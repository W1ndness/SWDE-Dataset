Lattanzi Reviews
 

Facilities 1 of 5
  • Sala Aurelia

    The Sala Aurelia is a wonderfully spacious exposed brick-walled room with Alinari photographs of Rome in the 1800's adorning its walls, an antique coal-burning stove in the center of the room and a very large, art-deco-inspired chandelier as its focus. Perfect for larger parties, especially since it is connected with both the Sala Claudia and the Terrazza del Pincio, the Sala Aurelia is a great choice if you wish to spread out a little!

    Capacity
    4535
  • Sala Claudia

    The Sala Claudia is a cozy, 1930's-inspired red room with a beautiful,    art-deco stylized bar at its head, an elegantly-crafted marble fireplace in its center, an old upright radio at its entrance and a large, antique mirror taking up much of a wall; this room would best suit small, intimate parties as it can be closed off from the Sala Aurelia.

    Capacity
    2520
  • Terrazza del Pincio

    Named after the Pincio park terrace in Rome, the world-famous garden-terrace looks out over the eternal city; breezy, airy and spacious, verdant with flowers and plants including our very own grape vine, abundantly shaded and furnished with large, mediterranean-style umbrellas and comfortable wooden patio furniture, it's easy to forget that you're in the middle of the city-until you look up! It's the perfect place to host a medium-sized party on either a hot summer day or a cool, spring evening.

    Capacity
    5035
  • Giardino Trastevere

    The Giardino Trastevere takes its name from the oldest district in Rome, located in its heart and running along the Tiber; it is an enchanting labyrinth of fountains, piazzas, lights and traditional trattorias and osterias packed with people from around the world. With a skylight, decorative water fountain, hand-carved scale ship replica, and antique photographs of the Eternal City and its people, this room is a relaxing and colloquial environment, perfect for small and medium sized groups.

    Capacity
    5040
  • Sala del Corso and Sala Pompei    

    Sala del Corso and Sala Pompei rooms are located on our first floor.    Sala del Corso has a seating capacity of 45 and a standing capacity of 60.    Sala Pompei is slightly larger with a seating room capacity of 70 and a standing capacity of 90. These rooms can be combined and may be booked for a single party for a total seated capacity of 155 and a standing capacity of 200

Lattanzi Ristorante is an elegant-rustic, family-owned Italian restaurant in the heart of the Theater District on Restaurant Row; established in 1984, this classic local landmark has specialized in serving traditional Roman cuisine to upscale theater-goers for over a quarter of a century. With four dining rooms, a cozy indoor garden, beautiful rooftop terrace and two independent bars spread out across both floors, we host private functions and parties for virtually any occasion and of almost every size. We have several different party menus to choose from or we can help you design one according to your style and taste. Also, the extensive drinks and wine lists we maintain are sure to provide you with the necessary ingredients for a cheerful evening! We will always try and accommodate any and all needs you or your guests may have, and rest assured that our courteous, professional staff will work with you to make sure your event is exactly as you want it.

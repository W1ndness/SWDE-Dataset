  • Front Street Grill on beautiful Whidbey Island, Washington overlooks Penn Cove and offers a classic American Grill experience along with a full bar and a great wine list. Located just three doors down from the historic Coupeville Wharf, our Dining Room and Bar offer amazing views of Penn Cove and Mt. Baker. Bring the whole family for lunch or dinner or just join us for Happy Hour. Either way, we will make you feel very welcome!

  • Jimmy Love"s is a classic American restaurant, bar, and nightclub featuring live music 7 nights a week. We feature live jazz and blues from 6:30-11:00pm Sunday-Thursday, on Friday and Saturday we offer top 40 dance music from 10pm-2am. Our menu consists of contemporary cuisine featuring steak, seafood, pasta, pizza, soups and salads, as well as a variety of appetizers. We are open 7 days a week at 5pm, we do offer happy hour from 5-7pm daily.

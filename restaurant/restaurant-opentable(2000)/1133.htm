WA Frost & Company

writes...
  • **PATIO RESERVATIONS** are not available ONLINE ========================================================= WA Frost has been a favorite Saint Paul dining destination since 1975. The restaurant is housed in the turn-of-the-century Dacotah Building which lies in the heart of the historic Cathedral Hill District. WA Frost has incomparable ambiance & rare old world character. It boasts several grand wood-burning fireplaces, a charming old bar & the European-style garden patio & garden bar. The menus change seasonally & are inspired by seasonal ingredients from both the local market & the Mediterranean. The states best wine list, best outdoor dining in the Twin Cities & the most romantic restaurant are just a few awards received.

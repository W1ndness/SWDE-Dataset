Bar Agricole

writes...
  • Bar Agricole is a contemporary tavern situated on an industrial block of San Francisco’s SOMA district. The tavern features traditional mixed drinks from spirits that celebrate the rich tradition of farmhouse distilling, natural wines, and Northern California Cuisine made with ingredients sourced from local organic and biodynamic farms.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Californian
  • Neighborhood: SOMA
  • Cross Street: Folsom and Harrison
  • Price: $30 and under

Players Steakhouse - Fantasy Springs Casino & Resort

writes...
  • Discover fine dining like you"ve never seen it in the Palm Springs area. Players Steakhouse serves the choicest USDA steaks, fresh seafood catches of the day, delectable side dishes, plus an extensive wine list with selections from California, the Pacific Northwest, Europe and other exciting wine-making locales. Executive Chef, Freddy Rieger oversees all dishes served at Players Steakhouse, and often greets patrons at their tables. The warm and inviting ambience replete with rich mahogany wood and classic furnishings make Players Steakhouse the perfect spot for a special occasion or just a night out with those who enjoy the finer things in life.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Seafood, American
  • Neighborhood: Indio
  • Cross Street: Golf Center Parkway
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.fantasyspringsresort.com/prod/dining/dining.php#players
  • Phone: (760) 342-5000
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Freddy Rieger
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Fantasy Springs and Players Steakhouse offers two complimentary valet areas. The most convenient to Players Steakhouse is the Casino Valet entrance. Patrons may also park in the self-parking lot behind the hotel, or in the self-park area in front of the casino.
  • Private Party Facilities: Players Steakhouse offer two distinct private dining rooms. The Wine Room seats parties up to 14 people. There is also a small private room with a table seating 6. This table is located at the front of the restaurant and looks out on the casino action. Exclusive staff services each private dining room. Diners may also ask for special request assistance for engagements, anniversaries, reunions and more.
  • Private Party Contact: Ajit Acharya (800) 827-2946

Takashi

writes...
  • Takashi pushes the envelope of contemporary Japanese cuisine, presenting unrivaled sushi and sashimi along with innovative small plates and hot entrees in a memorable downtown setting. Whether you prefer a bar side seat with a view of the action at the cutting boards, or a cozy table for two, our friendly and knowledgeable servers will guide you through the menus, the specials board, and our excellent collection of premium sake, beer, wine and fabulous specialty cocktails. While most seating is on a walk-in basis, we do offer a few early reservations through Open Table. If you have questions regarding reservations, please call the restaurant at 801-519-9595.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Sushi, Japanese, Fusion / Eclectic
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Cross Street: Approx. 350 South between Main and West Temple.
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.takashisushi.com/
  • Email: phindus@comcast.net
  • Phone: (801) 519-9595
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Takashi Gibo
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Conveniently located between both "Gallivan" and "Courthouse" TRAX (light rail) stops.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Valet parking is available on the corner of Market Street and Main. There are two public pay lots adjacent to the restaurant, as well as metered street parking.
  • Catering: Please contact Takashi's Director of Catering for any off-premise function questions. 801-519-9111.
  • Private Party Facilities: Chef Takashi can prepare and host your next occasion for client events, dinner parties, luncheons, gatherings and catering. Whether you want the entire restaurant to yourself or just the top floor, we have a package to suit your needs. Please visit our website or call for more information.

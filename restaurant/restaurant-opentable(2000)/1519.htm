The Cottage Restaurant

writes...
  • The Cottage Restaurant, opened in 2007 by a Wellesley native, is a neighborhood favorite. Located in Wellesley's newly redeveloped Linden Square, The Cottage, which is sophisticated yet family friendly, offers an affordable American menu and one of Wellesley's only bars. The restaurant has an open, airy Nantucket feel with white wainscotting, dark wood floors, high ceilings and green and blue hues throughout. There is an inviting fireplace in the main dining room and a private room with french glass doors for larger groups.

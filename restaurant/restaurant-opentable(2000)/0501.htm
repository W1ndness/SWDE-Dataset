Randy's Steakhouse

writes...
  • Randy Burks and his staff would like to invite you to dine at Randy's Steakhouse. Dine in our elegant, historic Victorian home built in 1869. Enjoy hand-cut prime steaks, fresh seafood, and our extensive international wine list. There is live entertainment in Randie's (named after Randy's daughter) Piano Lounge on Friday and Saturday evenings. Happy Hour is from 5-7:00 daily with $1 off all drinks served in our lounge. Randy's Steakhouse is also open for Sunday brunch from 10am to 2pm.

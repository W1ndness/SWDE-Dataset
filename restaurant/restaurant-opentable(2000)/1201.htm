McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Denver

writes...
  • Bringing the freshest seafood to the Mile High City, McCormick & Schmick's Seafood Restaurant is located in the Denver Tech Center at the Penterra Plaza. Our menus change twice each day and feature more than 30 varieties of fresh seafood, which is flown in daily. In addition to delicious seafood, menu highlights include tempting pasta, poultry and beef specialties, prepared using fresh ingredients and served in a timeless and traditional presentation. Embracing the time-honored tradition of hand-crafted cocktails with fresh-squeezed juices, the bar at McCormick & Schmick's Seafood Restaurant offers "traditional mixology" at its best. Don't forget to visit us for our famous bar menu, featuring select menu items for $1.95 every day from 3pm-6pm. Enjoy your meal in our comfortable dining room, lively bar, or under an umbrella-shaded table on our patio. Plan a special event, corporate or social, in one of our private dining rooms.

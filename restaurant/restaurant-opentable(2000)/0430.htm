Roaring Fork San Antonio

  • Eddie V's Restaurants brings bold wood fired cooking to San Antonio. Join us at The Roaring Fork serving lunch and dinner daily with happy hour beginning at 4 pm. At The Roaring Fork you will capture the spirit of the Old West, where the best food was prepared on a simple wood fire. This same spirit, freshness and flavor are at the heart of every Roaring Fork dish. Select from lamb, chicken, beef, pork and fresh fish entrees all perfectly prepared by wood fire rotisserie, wood burning grill or oven roasting. Whether it's cocktails in the saloon, a leisurely dinner on the patio or a pleasant dining experience in our comfortable dining rooms, Roaring Fork is the discerning diner's choice. Stop in tonight and experience the spirit of bold wood fired cooking where unique bold American flavors crackle with that rugged edge. Including: Wood-Fired Steaks, Rotisserie Chicken, Roasted Pork Carnitas, Pan Sautéed Trout, Campfire Salmon, Green-Chile Pork Stew, Slow Roasted Long Bone Beef Ribs...

Reds Steakhouse at the Wigwam Resort

writes...
  • Red’s Steakhouse at The Wigwam Golf Resort & Spa features a contemporary steakhouse atmosphere with a menu that includes prime steaks, chops, chicken, seafood and signature desserts from Chef de Cuisine Michael Scott. This hotspot includes a 100-seat dining room, two private dining areas, an extensive wine cellar, a fashionable bar & lounge with flat screen televisions, and a comfortable outdoor patio with a fire place and an relaxing water feature. The décor reflects the resort’s Authentic Arizona theme with accents of copper, steel, leather and distressed wood.

  • Cuisine: Steakhouse, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Phoenix
  • Cross Street: Litchfield Road
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over

    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Urs Balmer
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Parking Details: Valet Parking and Hotel Parking Available
  • Private Party Facilities: We have two private dining rooms that can accommodate parties for up to 20 people. Personalized menu selections are available.
  • Private Party Contact: Mark Hutchins (623) 535-4923
  • Entertainment: With his vibrant, emotive and high-energy style, Thaddeus Rose brings a new level of entertainment to The Bar at Red’s. Performing Friday nights from 8:30pm to 11:30pm, Thaddeus spans musical genres with a mix of popular songs, as well as his own heart-felt creations. Enjoy the musical talent of Bob Sheade on piano at Red's on Saturday evenings from 6pm to 10:00pm.
  • Special Events & Promotions:

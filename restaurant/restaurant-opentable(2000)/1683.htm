Belleville Bistro

writes...
  • Michelin Guide's Critic's Pic for 2010! This neighborhood favorite is a Park Slope landmark, known for friendly staff, amazing dishes and truly authentic Parisian atmosphere.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary French, French, Tapas / Small Plates
  • Neighborhood: Park Slope
  • Cross Street: (Corner of 5th St and 5th Ave)
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.bellevillebistro.com
  • Email: bienvenue@bellevillebistro.com
  • Phone: (718) 832-9777
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:30pm
    Brunch: Saturday - Sunday: 10:30am - 3:45pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Fabian Pauta
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Counter Seating, Delivery, Entertainment, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: F,R,M,G to 4th Ave/9th St
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Private Party Facilities: Belleville Lounge is Brooklyn's newest lounge space with an unparalleled atmosphere. The room can be reserved for groups of 10 to 70, sit-down or cocktail style. Both the restaurant and the lounge combined will allow groups up to 150.
  • Private Party Contact: Paul Kennedy (718) 832-9777
  • Entertainment: Belleville Lounge offers live entertainment with no cover! Check our website for the weekly schedule.

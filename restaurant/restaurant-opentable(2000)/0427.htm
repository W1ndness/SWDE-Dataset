Las Ramblas at Hotel Contessa

writes...
  • Perfectly situated along a tranquil stretch of unspoiled riverfront at the Hotel Contessa, Las Ramblas sets a new standard in Modern Spanish Cuisine. The menu features seasonally inspired entrees and tapas includes Zarzuela La Catalana, “Spanish shellfish stew”, traditional Spanish Paella and tapas such as Brussel Spouts with house made pancetta & sage. Las Ramblas & the Cork Bar offers over 30 wines by the Glass & Splash. Guests can enjoy both indoor and alfresco dining.

King's Wharf

writes...
  • Landlubbers who walk up the gangplank to the King's Wharf, set within the Marriott's Lincolnshire Resort, might easily get the impression that ships are expected to dock there soon. Boats are dry-locked on the ceiling, masts made of weather timbers flank the dining area and the walls are rigged with rough ropes. To double your pleasure, buy a ticket to the Marriott's Lincolnshire Theatre for a Wednesday or Thursday evening performance and get a complimentary dinner at the King's Wharf before the show.

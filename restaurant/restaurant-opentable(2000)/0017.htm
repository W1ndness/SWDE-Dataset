Hank's Fine Steaks & Martinis

  • Luxury dining with impressive menu selections, including steaks and seafood, to satisfy any palate. HANK’S Fine Steak’s and Martinis features tender, over sized dry-aged prime steak, signature bone-in meats and fresh fish flown in daily. A meat lover’s Mecca with masterful preparation and a 7,500 bottle wine loft. HANK’S never fails to attempt and delight. Enjoy a cocktail in the Piano Bar. Private Dining Room hosts up to 40 people.

    Happy Hour available 7 days a week, featuring 1/2 price Martinis!

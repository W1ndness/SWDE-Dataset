Darya Fine Persian Cuisine- Santa Monica

writes...
  • Darya Restaurant in Santa Monica, opened in 1996, is the sister location to the Darya Restaurant in Orange, CA. They offer marinated filet mignon, chicken, lamb, and fish Kabobs cooked over an open flame. They serve a variety of mixed rices and salads, as well as an array of appetizers, from Borani (sauteed eggplants with caramelized onions and garlic) to Dolmeh (stuffed Grape leaves). Darya Restaurant also prepares traditional stews from the freshest ingredients, slowly cooked to perfection. Darya where the D stand for Delicious.

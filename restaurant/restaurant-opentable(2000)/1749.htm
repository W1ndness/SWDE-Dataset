Edward's Steakhouse

writes...
  • Located in a newly renovated 1800’s brownstone in Historic Downtown Jersey City, Edward’s Steakhouse offers the finest in New York style steaks, chops, and seafood in a comfortable, upscale setting. The main dining room offers congenial elegance. Our backstairs dining room is perfect for private events. You may use either our standard menu or our Pre-Fixe menu; for a truly memorable experience, ask our award-winning executive chef to create a menu specific to your event.

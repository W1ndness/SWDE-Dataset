Bibou

writes...
  • Bibou is a small French Byob located in Bella Vista. Exclusively using fresh products, Chef Pierre Calmels offers dishes such as "Pieds de Porc" (pigs' feet), escargots, as well as many other traditional French dishes.
    Our 2 seats are the bar can only be reserved for our Menu Degustation. Bibou is cash only.

  • Cuisine: French
  • Neighborhood: South Philly

  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website

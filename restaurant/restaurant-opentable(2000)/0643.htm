  • The Flying Horse Restaurant

    Experience our seasonal specialties as well as seasoned favorites. Our diverse menu offers something for everyone: fresh seafood, flame-grilled steaks along with a wide selection of house favorites. When paired with our extensive wine list and exceptional service, you can be assured of a memorable meal. The Flying Horse is sure to be your favorite Galena restaurant. Make your reservation now and experience casual fine dining at its best.

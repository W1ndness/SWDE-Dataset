Muse

writes...
  • Located in the new Anchorage Museum, Muse offers contemporary cuisine featuring Alaskan seafood and local produce. The atmosphere is very modern with space for large groups. During the summer months patio dining overlooking an "urban" birch forest is available. The bar features creative cocktails, a selection of microbrews and fine wines. We offer a full lunch menu from 11am-3pm. From 3 till closing we feature an "Amuse" menu featuring small plates, pairings with creative cocktails, wine flights and microbrews.

Timpano Chophouse and Martini Bar - Ft. Lauderdale

writes...
  • Timpano Chophouse is reminiscent of the late 50’s early 60’s in Chicago & New York.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, Steakhouse, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Fort Lauderdale
  • Cross Street: 3rd Avenue & Las Olas
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.timpanochophouse.net/
  • Email: TimpanoFtLauderdale@e-brands.net
  • Phone: (954) 462-9119
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Wednesday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Thursday - Friday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: John Davis
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cigar Room, Dancing, Delivery, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Smoking Area, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: 450 East Las Olas Blvd.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet parking is available for $5.00 per vehicle. Self parking is available in the attached garage with validation in restaurant for lunch only.
  • Catering: Available in a limited delivery area.
  • Private Party Facilities: 4 Private rooms and semi private areas for groups of 10 to 300. Ask about our new Legends Room that can accommodate 100 seated or 150 reception style. This award winning themed restaurant offers a Sinatra style environment with excellent cuisine and service. The private rooms are perfect for business meetings, company luncheons/dinner or that special occasion social event.
  • Private Party Contact: Alyssa Lovitt (954) 462-3698
  • Entertainment: Live entertainment Wednesday thru Saturday nights in the Starlight Lounge. Call for our schedule of entertainers.

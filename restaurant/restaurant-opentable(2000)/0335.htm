Chima Brazilian Steakhouse - Fort Lauderdale

writes...
  • Located on the trendy Las Olas Boulevard just minutes from the beach, Chima offers a serene dining experience with a touch of exclusivity. The Fort Lauderdale location provides a chic dining experience from the minute you step foot into the captivating courtyard. The outside bar and courtyard provide an escape from the traditional happy-hour scene with our exceptional drink specials.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Brazilian Steakhouse, Steakhouse
  • Neighborhood: Fort Lauderdale
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.chimasteakhouse.com
  • Phone: (954) 712-0580
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 9:30pm, Friday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm, Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:30pm, Sunday: 4:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Maradona Lima
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Beer, Fireplace, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Smoking Area (patio only), Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet Parking $3 per car
  • Private Party Facilities: Chima has a beautiful private room equipped with A/V. The room holds about 80 guests.
  • Entertainment: Live Music on Wednesdays,Fridays and Saturdays.

Cha Cha's Tacos & Tequila

writes...
  • Fresh California-Latin Kitchen featuring cuisine steeped in tradition in a contemporary and festive setting. Dining room menu features cuisine prepared using a variety of cooking techniques from our large rotisserie to our wood-fired brick oven. A beautiful lounge area awaits you at cha Cha's featuring an extensive list of Mexico's finest tequilas. Hand-made, fresh-squeezed specialty margaritas are our specialty. Contemproary dining room with whimsical giant movie screen showing vintage black and white Mexican movies for your enjoyment. Heated patio dining.

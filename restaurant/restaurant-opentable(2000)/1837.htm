McDonagh's Pub

writes...
  • Located in the Heart of Downtown Historic Keyport in a turn-of-the-century brick building near the bay, McDonagh's pub combines Old-World charm with modern hip cuisine. We offer an amazing menu of Irish Fare, bistro-style salads, home-made awesome soups, signature sandwiches, wraps, sautés, thin-crust pizza, fabulous fish and chips, daily specials and over 70 beers and lagers!
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Irish, Continental
  • Neighborhood: Keyport
  • Cross Street: Broad Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.mcdonaghs.com
  • Email: mcdonaghspub@yahoo.com
  • Phone: (732) 264-0999
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch and Dinner: Daily: 11:30am - 10:00pm Late Night: 10:00pm - Midnight
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Hazlet and Matawan Train stations are located within a few miles from the Pub. Also, there is a bus stop in front of the Pub on West Front Street.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Our private parking lot is located next to the pub for your convenience. Please drive to the back of the municipal parking lot to enter the lot.
  • Catering: McDonagh's Pub catering offers a wide range of menus that can be custom-made to suit your needs. Please call us with any questions you may have.
  • Private Party Facilities: McDonagh's Pub is a fun, hip and friendly place that offers...GREAT FOOD, GREAT DRINK AND GREAT CUSTOMER SERVICE! We do a custom menu for each party to be sure to suit the requests of our customers. We host Holiday Parties, Christenings, Birthdays, Victory Parties, Engagements, Anniversaries, etc. If you would like more information in regards to letting McDonagh's Pub host your next function, please call us so we can answer any questions you may have.
  • Private Party Contact: Ken Lawlor (732) 264-0999 x5
  • Entertainment: We offer amazing live music from Irish Folk to Rock and Roll on the weekends and cool acoustic tunes in the bar on the weeknights. On Wednesdays come in for Karaoke!

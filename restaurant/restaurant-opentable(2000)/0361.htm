MR CHOW- Miami

writes...
  • Most recently awarded the 5 Star Diamond Award by the American Academy of Hospitality Sciences for its excellence, service, and cuisine this is the newest outpost of the legendary restaurateur MR CHOW located in the chic new W Hotel on Miami Beach. The restaurant features the original cuisine of the NYC, Beverly Hills and London locations as well as many new and exciting dishes.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Chinese
  • Neighborhood: Miami Beach / South Beach
  • Cross Street: 22nd and Collins
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.mrchow.com
  • Phone: (305) 695-1695
  • Hours of Operation: April to September Dinner Sunday-Thursday 6:00PM-12:00AM Friday and Saturday 6:00PM-1:00AM October to March Dinner Sunday-Thursday 6:00PM-12:00AM Friday and Saturday 6:00PM-1:00AM
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa

  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Entertainment, Full Bar, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Wheelchair Access, Wine

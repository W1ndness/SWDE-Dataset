Crust offers cutting edge flatbreads and more in a contemporary, green, yet casually approachable setting. We focus on the use of locally grown and organic products, from our boutique beer and wine list to our produce and meats. We also offer house-infused organic vodkas and a variety of specialty cocktails. Located in the heart of Wicker Park, Crust is the perfect blend of delicious and healthy food and a modern but comfortable atmosphere. Whether you are hosting a reception, rehearsal dinner, wine tasting, or birthday party, we would love to see to your every need. Thanks and we hope to hear from you soon!

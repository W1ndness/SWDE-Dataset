Crimson

writes...
  • Crimson offers an intimate and friendly dining experience. A 3 1/2 star restaurant, the food has been described as American comfort with global liberties, 'It's like going to dinner at a friend's home and your friend happens to be a great cook!' - San Jose Mercury News.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Fondue, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Los Gatos
  • Cross Street: Lark St.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.crimsonrestaurant.com
  • Email: chefdianerose@yahoo.com

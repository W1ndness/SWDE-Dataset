Wapango

writes...
  • Discover a unique dining experience in St. Louis. Featuring Latin-American cuisine, Wapango will take you away to a world filled with bold flavors, exotic beverages, and a lively atmosphere. The dining room is stunning in it's colors, ample seating arrangements for intimate dining and large groups alike. Our menu encompasses the flavors of South American countries such as Brazil, Chile, Argentina, Peru and Colombia. There are many dishes from Mexico, Cuba and the Carribean as well. USDA grade Steaks, fish and seafood items such as our signature Chilean Sea Bass, our wonderful Brazilian seafood stew Moqueca- and starters like our popular Colombian Corn Cakes with Shredded Pork Carnitas and Three-Cheese Empanadas. We have a Certified Pastry Chef on staff who makes our desserts in house and bakes our bread fresh daily. We offer an all South American winelist with the popular wines of Chile and Argentina. Great American food with a Latin twist!

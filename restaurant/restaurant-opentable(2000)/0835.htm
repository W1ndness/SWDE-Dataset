The Whitney

writes...
  • The Whitney is Detroit's most celebrated restaurant. Housed in a historic 52 room 1890's mansion on Woodward Avenue in the heart of Detroit's cultural district. The Whitney features award-winning cuisine prepared by Chef Dan Maurer.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Detroit
  • Cross Street: Canfield Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.thewhitney.com
  • Phone: (313) 832-5700
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Tuesday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Tuesday,Wednesday & Thursday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Sunday Brunch: 11:00am - 2:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Dan Maurer
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, High Tea, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: While the valet service is complementary, a gratuity is appreciated.
  • Catering: Catering Options Available
  • Private Party Facilities: The Whitney sustains an extensive and impressive résumé for private dining events which includes hosting classically themed weddings, corporate occasions and any number of other, miscellaneous, intimate parties.
  • Private Party Contact: Private Dining Manager (313) 832-5700
  • Entertainment: The Whitney offers live entertainment on the 3rd floor every Fri. and Sat. nights.

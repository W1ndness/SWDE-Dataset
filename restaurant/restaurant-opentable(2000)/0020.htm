Lagasse's Stadium - Palazzo Resort Hotel Casino

writes...
  • Lagasse's Stadium is located in the Palazzo Resort Hotel Casino in Las Vegas. The restaurant features Chef Emeril Lagasse's play on game-day favorites, a few of his New Orleans-style dishes and some twists on American classics.

    Lagasse's Stadium is a 21 and over due to gaming located inside the venue.

    Limited Seating for Major Sporting Events. Please call the restaurant for reservations.

  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Comfort Food
  • Neighborhood: The Venetian and Palazzo

  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website

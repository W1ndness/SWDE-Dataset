Al Vento

writes...
  • Al Vento is a romantic Southern Italian bistro with fine dining at affordable prices. The relaxed decor and elegance by candle light provide the perfect setting for friendly banter, family outings, or simply staring into the eyes of your lover with a fine Italian wine in hand. With notably some of the most knowledgeable and friendly staff in the Twin Cities, Al Vento is the perfect dining experience for you this evening.

  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: South Minneapolis
  • Cross Street: 50th Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

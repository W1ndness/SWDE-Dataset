Poggio Reviews
 

Facilities 1 of 5
  • Brescia

    The Salone Brescia room can accommodate groups up to 40 guests comfortably for a seated meal and is Poggio’s premier private dining space. The rooms are elegantly appointed and have several windows overlooking the Sausalito harbor. Custom a la cart and family style menus can be created. Separated by wooden doors, the Salone Topolino room is adjacent to Salone Brescia and can be used for a private reception prior to dinner.

    Capacity
    5040
  • Salon Grande

    Casa Madrona Hotel & Spa’s premier meeting space. The rooms are sub-divided into three sections (Salone Roma, Salone Artimino & Salone Firenze) and can be rented individually or as a whole. Ideal for break-out sessions! Each of the rooms has built in audiovisual screens, partial bay view, state of the art lighting system, high speed internet connection and flex back meeting chairs.

    Capacity
    150150
  • Villa Madrona

    The Villa Madrona Room provides a unique location and is a truly memorable site for a gathering. French doors open to private verandas overlooking a beautiful view of the Sausalito Harbor. This room also has two private bathrooms. Separated by doors, the Villa Madrona is adjacent to the Villa Poquita, which could be used as a breakout room or as a separate lunch space. This room can accommodate up to 30 people for a board meeting or up to 50 people for a seated meal.

    Capacity
    7050
  • Mikayla

    Mikayla is an unforgettable romantic and elegant location with sweeping bayside views and a spacious area for all types of events. The Mikayla room can accommodate groups up to 120 guests and has three-sided glass windows and a partially retractable ceiling with panoramic views that look across Sausalito Harbor, Angel Island and San Francisco.

    Capacity
    150120
  • Terrace Deck

    Located directly below the Victorian House is a 1,300 square foot deck overlooking the Sausalito Bay. It is a spectacular setting for wedding ceremonies or any outdoor reception.

Our Private Dining Facilities

Poggio can accommodate large groups in our private rooms. Our rooms are perfect for a variety of events including birthdays, rehearsal dinners, weddings, and showers as well as business conferences. Poggio serves healthy upscale Italian cuisine. Our kitchen creates vibrant dishes using organic produce fresh from the farmers market and our own organic garden, local fish and Marin-raised meats. Our Poggio staff pays great attention to detail for every dish they create including our homemade pastas and cured meats.

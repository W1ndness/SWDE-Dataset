Gardens of Taxco

writes...
  • Since 1971, The Gardens of Taxco has built its reputation on consistently providing the best Mexico-City Style Cuisine in Los Angeles. Your unique five course dining experience begins when your menu is presented verbally; combining details about the entrees with colorful signature sayings such as ‘born in the sauce’ and ‘Not hot, spicy’. The understated decor and festive atmosphere will transport you to a fine home in Mexico City.

  • Cuisine: Mexican
  • Neighborhood: West Hollywood
  • Cross Street: Santa Monica Blvd

We take pride in our ability to provide you and your guests with an experience that will be memorable. Every aspect of your event can be customized. Executive Chef, Jeremy Duclut has created a varied menu which will change seasonally to incorporate the freshest ingredients available. The menu consists of a Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Reception (Buffet) Stations, and a Dessert Menu. Our Special Events Staff will be happy to assist you in all aspects of staging your event, from specialty linens, dance floor and audio-visual equipment rentals, to arranging on-site ceremonies and ordering wedding or specialty cakes. For corporate meetings, showers, rehearsal dinners, social gatherings and so much more, georges’ is the perfect place to hold your Special Event. Because we listen to our guests, we are able to measure our success in customer satisfaction. We look forward to bringing your ideas to life, and making your event memorable.

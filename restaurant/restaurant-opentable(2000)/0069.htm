Tucci Ristorante

writes...
  • Located just 15 minutes from downtown Portland along scenic Highway 43, Tucci Ristorante in downtown Lake Oswego offers all the sophistication of Portland’s Pearl District, all the warmth of a cozy Italian hideaway, and all the best flavors of Italian style foods made with fresh Pacific Northwest ingredients. All this complimented by a selection of artisanal Italian wines. Pasta favorites, organic quality meats, and fresh seafood prepared in our open kitchen await you at Tucci Ristorante. Salute!!!
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian, Organic
  • Neighborhood: Lake Oswego
  • Cross Street: Between 2nd & 3rd on A Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.Tucci.biz
  • Email: TucciPosta@Tucci.biz
  • Phone: (503) 697-3383
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Tuesday - Friday: 11:30am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday, Monday: 4:00pm - 9:00pm, Tuesday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Roberto Alarcon
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Farm to Table, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: Only two blocks from the Lake Oswego Transit Center.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Parking is available on the street or in a shared parking lot in rear of building.
  • Catering: All private events are planned individually with the client. Please contact Tucci Ristorante for more information.

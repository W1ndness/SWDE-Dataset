Sonoma

writes...
  • The inspiration for Sonoma comes from California's rolling vistas, fine restaurants, wonderful wines and casual settings. Owner Pierre Bader and Executive Chef Bill Schutz have developed relationships with local farmers in order to serve the freshest (often organic) ingredients possible. A casual, upscale setting offering mouthwatering American cuisine with seasonal ingredients and warm hospitality. Located adjacent to the NC Performing Arts Center and center stage to all of Charlotte's uptown events.

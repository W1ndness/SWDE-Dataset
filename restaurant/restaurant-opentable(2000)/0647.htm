Francesca's Famiglia

writes...
  • Francesca’s Restaurants is happy to announce the 16th addition to the Francesca’s family! Francesca’s Famiglia, nestled in downtown Barrington’s quaint shopping district, features the zesty, earthy cuisine of Rome and the surrounding areas of Tuscany, Umbria and Lazio. In his latest rendition, Chef Scott Harris joins forces with fellow restauranteur, Michael Noone, to bring you a restaurant that encourages the lively art of dining with a contemporary, casually sophisticated trattoria ambiance. Simple rustic cooking, friendly service and fair prices make Francesca’s an experience to remember! This non-smoking establishment features three intricately designed dining rooms, an impressive oak bar and personal wine cabinets for its guests. Striking photographs of rural Italian life by Paul Elledge line the walls to create a comfortable, urban setting throughout.

Francesca’s philosophy of simple, rustic cooking - served in a casually sophisticated dining atmosphere - is one that we proudly carry over to event planning. We have everything you need to make your party a success. So, whether you're in charge of throwing this year's office party, putting together the umpteenth annual family get-together, or you're the one who found yourself "nominated" party planner for your club's holiday gathering, Francesca’s Famiglia can help you plan the perfect event. Our family style menu, “Tavoli di Amici” (Table of Friends), encourages an interactive dining experience for groups of 15 to 200 guests. Family-style dining offers you and your guests an eclectic selection of flavors spanning three to five courses. Our extensive wine list is also available for private functions. Francesca’s Famiglia is available for private events seven days a week during both lunch and dinner hours.

Stefan's at L.A. Farm

writes...
  • Cosmopolitan dining. Drinks with friends. Intimate private parties... All these seduce the discerning foodie and socialista to visit the new Stefan’s at L.A. Farm. A Santa Monica institution reimagined, Stefan’s at L.A. Farm is a casual bistro where celebrities and Hollywood power brokers mingle with Angelenos of all stripes. Welcome to an oasis of casual sophistication and attentive service. Sleek and modern, the dining room is festooned with American made Italian furniture, marble-topped tables and abstract artworks. Equal style and comfort is found on the patio, where guests can dine or just relax around a fire pit on leather banquettes. Its spacious bar area is equipped with two flat-screen TVs, making Stefan’s at L.A. Farm a great place to enjoy with friends and colleagues. A private wine cellar for 20 allows guests to enjoy wine tasting or host private parties. Yup, the ol’ Farm’s got a new owner. Pull up a chair and stay awhile...

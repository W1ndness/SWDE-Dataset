The Corner

writes...
  • Most of what you eat is nasty. But it’s not your fault. One of the main reasons behind the food and health crisis in the U.S. is a scarcity of wholesome, nutritious edibles available to the average consumer. We all figured out years ago that fast food makes your body cry. But what about what you find at the grocery store? Pure, processed garbage. Finding wholesome meat and produce isn’t easy. After all, not all foods-even foods that are the same from all outward appearances-are created equal. Our goal should be to eat foods as close to their original form as possible from local, carefully tended sources. Even with the prevalence of farmer’s markets, you would have to be certifiably insane to go through the trouble on a daily basis. Luckily, our Executive Chef is exactly that crazy. Organic Produce. Sustainable Seafood. Naturally Raised Steaks. We didn’t want to make a big deal out of all of this, we're too cool for that. No one's too cool to eat well. Welcome.

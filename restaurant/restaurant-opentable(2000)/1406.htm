Osteria Mozza

writes...
  • Osteria Mozza is the Michelin Starred upscale sibling to the adjacent Pizzeria Mozza, both the brainchild of partners Nancy Silverton, Mario Batali and Joe Bastianich. Anchored by Nancy's massive Mozzarella bar, the soaring Osteria provides a sophisticated but lively environment to enjoy a menu inspired by her dazzling work with Mozzarella and other fresh cheeses and Batali's exploits throughout Italy. The all-Italian wine list continues the tradition by offering bottlings from the country's finest producers with a focus on Piedmont, Friuli and Tuscany.

Surrounded by dramatic wood shelving stocked with some of Italy's finest wines, the Primo Ministro Room is a warm and elegant venue ideal for a variety of private functions. Up to 30 guests can be seated to enjoy the renowned cuisine of Mario Batali and Nancy Silverton served by a dedicated staff. Start the party in grand style with sparkling Prosecco and tray passed "Assagi". Move through Antipasti from our Mozzarella Bar, exquisite hand-made pasta, stunning Secondi and finally Silverton's legendary Dolci. Wines can be expertly paired with each course to round out the perfect Italian experience!

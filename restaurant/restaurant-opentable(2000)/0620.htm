Brazzaz - The Brazilian Steakhouse

writes...
  • Brazzaz A visually stunning steakhouse serving a dazzling variety of meats in a continuous table side service called RODÍZIO. Rodízio is a classic Brazilian style of serving barbecue; a variety of items are slowly roasted over coals to preserve their natural flavors and textures. These are brought to your table directly from the grill on the skewers and expertly served by our Gaúchos (Brazilian cowboy). You control the pace of your meal by using especially designed chips that stop or allow the service to continue. Before embarking on the high protein journey, you are invited to visit our spectacular Salad Bar that includes over 40 items, from seafood to pastas, such as Sushi, Oysters and Wild Mushroom Risotto. Vegetarians or those wanting a lighter dining experience can opt for the Salad Bar only for a reduced price. Join us for a culinary adventure like no other! Brazzaz, Brazilian Steakhouse is in the heart of Chicago. Brazzaz presents the experience, where tradition and classic methods meet modern flare and culinary innovation. Our Grand Salad Bar features over 50 items where you will indulge by sampling many prepared dishes or creating one of your own. Here you will dine on the finest selection of beef, lamb, poultry, seafood and much more! We offer a variety of group packages that are specially designed to meet the needs of your group. Whether celebrating a corporate meeting, holiday party, birthday, anniversary, or special occasions, Brazzaz can create the ideal and unique experience for your own environment! Assortments of room arrangements are available including seated dinners, buffet dinners and cocktail receptions featuring a personal bartender. We can accommodate a flexible event space of 20-300 guests for lunch and/or dinner. Audio Visual equipment is also available for your event.

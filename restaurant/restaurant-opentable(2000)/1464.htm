Yamashiro

writes...
  • YAMASHIRO is a “Mountain Palace”, located high in the Hollywood Hills and surrounded by lush landscaped gardens overlooking the best view of Los Angeles. Built in 1911, the grounds are an exact replica of a palace located in the Yamashiro mountains near Kyoto, Japan. The pagoda, koi pond, polished teak and million-dollar views are intoxicating. The menu is Asian influenced with items like Wagyu Steak, Korean style Baby Back Ribs, Seared Ahi, and Chipotle-Citrus Lamb Chops. We have an extensive Sushi list with a variety of specialty Maki Rolls. Our Lounge is open late serving fresh Muddled Cocktails, with a full liquor, wine, sake, and beer lists. You can also enjoy a beverage or appetizer in our newly renovated Pagoda Bar, nestled in our exterior Garden with a view of the entire city. For more information please log on to www.YamashiroRestaurant.com OR look us up on Facebook & Twitter. For a truly ROMANTIC and unforgettable experience, we hope you will visit us at YAMASHIRO!

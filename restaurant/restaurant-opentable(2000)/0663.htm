Karma Asian Food and Fine Wine

  • Located in the Crowne Plaza Hotel, the experience of dining at Karma transcends mere "going out to eat." With a 35 foot glistening glass pond, a hand blown bamboo grove, and glowing glass front bar, everything related to this restaurant is perfectly balanced and beautifully presented.

  • Cuisine: Asian, Pan-Asian, Fusion / Eclectic

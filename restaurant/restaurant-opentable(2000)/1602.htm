Sportello

writes...
  • Located in Boston's Fort Point neighborhood, Sportello (Italian for counter service) offers a menu of Chef Barbara Lynch's trattoria-inspired Italian dishes for both lunch and dinner. Grab a seat at the counter and enjoy a bowl of handmade pasta and a glass of wine from wine director Cat Silirie's approachable, artisanal wine list. Sportello also offers freshly baked breads, pastries, sandwiches and coffee from the bakery counter, along with a selection of prepared dishes and pantry items.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Fort Point Channel
  • Cross Street: A Street
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.Sportelloboston.com
  • Email: info@sportelloboston.com
  • Phone: (617) 737-1234
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:30am - 4:30pm Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 4:30pm- 10:00pm Friday & Saturday: 4:30pm - 11:00pm Brunch: Saturday & Sunday: 10:30am - 4:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Chef Chris Stephens
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Counter Seating, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: For those taking public transportation, we are a short walk from South Station.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: In addition to on-street parking, there are several open air lots and garages within two blocks of Sportello. Please call for more information regarding parking.

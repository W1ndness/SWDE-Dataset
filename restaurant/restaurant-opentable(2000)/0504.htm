Sevy's Grill

writes...
  • Sevy's Grill has been a Dallas favorite since 1997 serving the finest ingredients with a creative American flair. The menu features steaks, seafood, pastas and daily specials, that utilizes a wood burning grill for added flavor. We offer a range of libations in our bar, including over 60 varieties of scotch and over 40 wines by the glass. Designed by Paul Draper, the Arts and Crafts influenced interior creates a comfortable, warm environment highlighted by the amber glow of the back-lit bar. Weather permitting, the French doors along the front of the restaurant open to one of the patios. With a private dining room with a gas burning fireplace, two covered patios, and an outdoor putting green, we have something for everyone. Complimentary valet, off-site catering and unique menus for special events are additional services we are pleased to offer our guests. Come on in and meet Sevy, Jimmy, Stefaan and Buzzy - they guarantee your enjoyment.

For al fresco parties, we have two covered patios to satisfy your needs. You can invent your own "dream menu" with one of our chefs, or choose from one of our created menus that will allow your guests a choice while staying within your budget. No matter if your party is big or small, we guarantee your enjoyment!

The ambiance of Sotto Sopra's 19th century building in Baltimore's cultural district is the perfect partner with our contemporary Italian cuisine in creating special memories. Whether a rehearsal dinner,small wedding reception, family celebration or corporate events Sotto Sopra makes you the star. We are able to accommodate from 75 to 100 guests whether your needs are for lunch or dinner. We are only moments north of the inner harbor at the entrance to the Mt Vernon cultural center of the city.

Enigma Restaurant

writes...
  • South Dakotas top fine dining destination. Enigma Restaurant located downtown, provides the perfect location, atmosphere, cuisine for your first meeting of the day over breakfast, your high powered lunch meeting or your night on the town. The Enigma Restaurant, a AAA three diamond rated restaurant, is located at Radisson Hotel Rapid City/ Mt. Rushmore. Known for its appealing menu and stylish service, Enigma is one of the finest of Rapid City restaurants and features fine dining, specialty drinks and fine wines. Guests can enjoy our signature Mystery Dinners and specials. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served daily, and room service is available 6:30am-10pm daily. Part of the Enigma Restaurant is our, 445 Lounge, a non-smoking, upscale lounge, offering 65 delicious martinis with premium vodkas, great music and a relaxing atmosphere.

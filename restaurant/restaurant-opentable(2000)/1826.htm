Mariner's Harbor

writes...
  • Kingston"s historic Rondout waterfront is home to Mariner's Harbor - the finest seafood house in the Hudson Valley. Visitors can arrive by car or boat. Our restaurant is located in the former Daily Freeman building at the foot of Broadway directly in front of the Kingston City Marina. Here at Mariner's Harbor you'll enjoy the finest quality dining and the friendliest service.

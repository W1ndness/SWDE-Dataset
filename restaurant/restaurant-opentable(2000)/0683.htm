  • NIA MEDITERRANEAN TAPAS-

    Featuring contemporary elegant small plates in the heart of "The Randolph Street Market District"

    Nia's warm rich reds and soft yellows spin out of the drapes of her main room onto her garden patio (40+ seats)... alfresco that holds the true touch of the Mediterranean.

    Chef Tara Pietroniro prepares simple luscious fare, including shrimp brochette, a variety of skewered meats with delicious dipping sauces, crab stuffed mushrooms, lobster risotto, sauteed wild mushrooms and so much more, including extensive vegetarian and seafood options. Plus daily specials with a focus on local seasonal ingredients.

    Accentuate your dining experience with our famous sangria or enjoy a select bottle from our exclusive wine list.

    Late night kitchen and trendy cocktails, open Friday & Saturday to Midnight.

    Lunch and Dinner - 7 days a week

Our Private Dining Facilities

    Total Capacity 75

Nia is an intimate Zagat rated restaurant in the heart of the West Loop. Our restaurant is available for groups from 25-75 for lunch and dinner or we can bring the event to you in the location of your choice. In addition to packages Nia also spotlights a great variety of imported charcuterie, cheese and olives as an option for your cocktail party or a simple family style luncheon. An antipasti selection may also be added on to your menu to give a greater variety. Select wines from our diverse Mediterranean wine list or offer pitchers of our house made red or white sangria to guests to enhance the neighborhood experience that Nia offers.

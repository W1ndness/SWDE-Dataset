VILLAGE California Bistro & Wine Bar

writes...
  • At VILLAGE California Bistro and Wine Bar we feature California inspired cuisine focusing on the bounty of artisan quality seasonal ingredients from the Wine Country to the Central Valley and Pacific waters. Our chef creates ever changing seasonal menus with items that pair with our Wine Spectator award winning )5 years) wine list including a selection of small plates for creating your own tasting menu or for simply sharing. Our wine list contains over 400 wines mostly from boutique wineries producing quality small lot wines not easily found. We have one of the largest half bottle selections in the Bay Area and some of the best wines from the wine producing regions around the world. At VILLAGE, locally owned and family operated, we believe in selecting the best ingredients our local California producers have to offer bringing them to you in a casual and sophisticated dining environment at reasonable prices. Enjoy Bistro style dining inside or outside overlooking Santana Park

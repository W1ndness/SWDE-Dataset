Hamburger Hamlet - Sherman Oaks

  • Hamburger Hamlet is an upscale casual dining restaurant established in 1950 by Harry and Marilyn Lewis. The restaurants were packed every night with celebrities and a city-wide reputation for “Simply marvelous food and drink” was soon created. Today, the menu remains true to Marilyn's original commitment to quality and great tasting food. While famous for thick and juicy gourmet burgers, we also offer a wide variety of appetizers, lunch, dinner and brunch entrees, signature soups, salads, sandwiches, fresh fish and steaks, decadent desserts and a full bar!

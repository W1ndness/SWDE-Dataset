  • Pesce Italian Kitchen + Bar (Formaly Pesce Blue) Pesce Italian Kitchen + Bar is a casual restaurant specializing in simple rustic Italian food at a moderate price. The restaurant is based on simplicity, quality and passion for food, wine, and service. Our Italian-inspired menu features an array of small plates, antipasti, dried and handmade pasta, and simply prepared meat and fish. Serving Lunch: Dinner : Sunday Brunch

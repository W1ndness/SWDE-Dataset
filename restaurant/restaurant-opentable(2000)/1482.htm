Azalea Restaurant & Lounge

writes...
  • Azalea is located in the historic Hotel Kirkwood on the corner of 4th and Walnut street in downtown Des Moines. We feature a modern American cuisine by Chef Sean Wilson with Mediterranean, "southern" and other regional influences that utilizes fresh, local, and artisan ingredients to create a regionally inspired cuisine with bold flavors that are artistically presented in a relaxed, friendly, and beautiful atmosphere. Azalea offers guests the ultimate dinning experience without the stiffness of other restaurants. Sean’s vision of small and large plates are there to help kick off your evening, where it’s a special occasion, drinks with friends, or a casual meal, Azalea is where to go.

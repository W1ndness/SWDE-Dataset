Six One Six - JW Marriott Hotel Grand Rapids

writes...
  • As the JW Marriott Grand Rapids’ signature restaurant, six.one.six makes as unforgettable a statement as the hotel itself. Market inspired menus, sweeping views, and progressive rhythms combine to create a dining experience second-to-none in Grand Rapids. With dishes that tempt taste buds and seduce the senses, the restaurant is a haven for self-proclaimed food aficionados and lovers of all things yummy. Serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner seven days a week, six.one.six personifies the passionate cooking style of its Executive Chef, Andrew Voss, and satisfies the most discerning palate. Those wishing a truly indulgent experience can reserve our semi-private dining space where you and eleven of your friends can gather in absolute luxury. Wonderfully fresh, undeniably inviting, and utterly stylish, six.one.six is a restaurant you’ll savor in more ways than one. six.one.six is situated off the lobby of the JW Marriott in downtown Grand Rapids, Michigan.

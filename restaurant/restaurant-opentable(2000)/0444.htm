Dulces Latin Bistro

writes...
  • : Dulces Latin Bistro (pronounced dool-says) has been sharing their European fare with Latin, Mediterranean, and Spanish influences to a loyal clientele since 1992.
    Their 1,000 bottle international wine list is noted as one of Seattle's best priced and is the winner of ten Outstanding Awards from Wine Spectator, Wine Enthusiast Magazine and the "OUTSTANDING WASHINGTON WINE LIST" award from Wine Press Northwest.
    Dulces Latin Bistro is located in the Seattle neighborhood of Madrona at 1430 34th Ave.
    Private Dining is available for parties up to 60 and "free wine tastings" every Wednesday from 4 to 7 p.m.

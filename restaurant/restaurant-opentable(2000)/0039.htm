Todd English P.U.B.

writes...
  • Three-time James Beard Award winner Todd English debuts his first pub concept at Crystals. Todd English P.U.B. is everything you love about your favorite East Coast traditional hang-out translated Vegas style!  A full menu offers a mix of traditional American comfort foods and creative delicacies.  The beautifully decadent warm wood-curved bar features a succulent assortment of prime meats, a full raw bar showcasing the best of the sea and a selection of 30 premium beers.  A multitude of plasma TV screens make Todd English P.U.B. ideal big game viewing while patio dining allows patrons to enjoy the beauty of City Center’s stunning art and architecture.

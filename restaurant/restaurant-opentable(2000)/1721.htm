Ciaobella

writes...
  • Enrico Proietti has outdone himself with the newest star to the Proietti Group of fine Italian dining restaurants! He has combined all of the elements-beautiful atmosphere, great service, outstanding food, and a top notch wine list hand-picked by renowned sommelier Giorgio Manzio and worth talking about to create this magical eatery! From the moment you walk through the velvet curtain you will be ensconced in the luxury of exotic skins, rare woods and Austrian crystal while savoring some of the finest Italian cuisine like the sensational Cappellacci Di Zucca that will make your mouth water all while the friendly staff caters to your every whim!

Barolo Ristorante

writes...
  • Barolo sits just outside the doorstep of the bouncy boulevard, quietly observing the scene across 5th street. Barolo borrowed its name from the famous Italian wine produced in Barolo, Italy. The fact that Barolo is considered to be Italy's finest wine is no coincidence. The food at Barolo derives from a very basic interpretation of the art of cooking: simple ingredients are used to form amazing dishes. Our Chef thrives on the flavors and products of Italy: fresh herbs, organic vegetables, olive oil, local meats and seafood. The menu presents no reinventions, but is rather a celebration of elegant comfort foods. Her brilliance is in the simplicity of her dishes. With its mix of casual elegance and authentic flavors, our cuisine is considered a real gem among the locals on South Beach. So don't rethink Italian food: rethink Ocean Drive.

Andiamo Italia - Warren

writes...
  • The Crown Jewel of the Andiamo family, Andiamo Italia offers outstanding, award-winning cuisine in an intimate setting. Besides our extensive menu items, we offer non-traditional Milanese dishes like Osso Bucco & Risotto. Reservations accepted, extensive wine list, bar is cigar friendly, handicap accessible.

  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Detroit
  • Cross Street: Van Dyke

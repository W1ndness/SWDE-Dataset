Melange @ Haddonfield

writes...
  • Melange @ Haddonfield’s decor will be warm and inviting with original artwork by local artist Paul Gordon. The 90-seat dining room offers private dining as well as outdoor cafe seating for 40.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Creole / Cajun / Southern, Italian
  • Neighborhood: Haddonfield
  • Cross Street: Between Kings Highway & Haddon Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.melangerestaurants.com
  • Email: ChefJoeBrown@msn.com
  • Phone: (856) 354-1333
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Saturday: 11:00am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 4:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 3:00pm - 10:00pm Coming Soon: out side dining live jazz and blues on the patio
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Chef Joe Brown
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, BYO Liquor, Chef's Table, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Private Room, Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access
  • Public Transit: The high speed train drops you off right at Melange @ Haddonfield.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking is available behind the restaurant and metered parking is free after 6:00pm and during the weekends.
  • Private Party Contact: Joe Brown (856) 354-1333

The Mansion Inn

writes...
  • The Mansion Inn is a beautiful 1865 manor home & is a magnificent example of Second Empire French Victorian architecture. Our restaurant features an eclectic menu, which can be found on our website. We are located in the heart of downtown New Hope.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Continental, Fusion / Eclectic
  • Neighborhood: New Hope / Lambertville
  • Cross Street: Bridge Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.themansioninn.com
  • Email: themansioninn@gmail.com
  • Phone: (215) 862-1231
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday -Saturday: 12:00am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Sunday Brunch: 11:30am – 4:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Kris Greene
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Wine
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Parking Details: Our small parking lot accommodates all inn guests & can serve a limited number of restaurant guests during weekdays. A metered parking lot is available across Bridge Street.

Sushi Lounge - Morristown

writes...
  • Sleek and modern, Sushi Lounge provides a relaxed atmosphere where you and your friends can sip a martini, enjoy the finest sushi in the area and watch the game on TV, all while a DJ spins creative beats. The sushi has maintained the standards that let Citysearch to rate it among the best in New York City, and Hoboken.com to give its “best sushi” award four years in a row. Additionally, the kitchen menu has been expanded, delivering a broad range of American dishes fused with Asian flair, alongside an award-winning sake and martini menu. The cool lounge atmosphere is enhanced by a DJ five days a week and live jazz every Sunday. With all it has going for it, Sushi Lounge is bound to become your favorite Morristown nightspot. ******* Please note: turn time on parties of two is maximum of 90 minutes, all other parties may seat for maximum of 1:45 hours.

Park Grill Chicago

writes...
  • Park Grill is Chicago's newest destination restaurant at the gateway to Millennium Park and along Chicago's world-class Michigan Avenue. Park Grill's cuisine and seasonal year-round entertainment make it an experience unlike any other in Chicago. Executive Chef Bernard Laskowski brings a chef's interpretation to classic American cuisine, infusing it with unique Chicago flavor. When you sit down at the restaurant, the outdoor area, or the café, you can start a conversation, start a meal, start a business deal, or start a celebration. We hope your experience with Millennium Park starts here. Reservations are not accepted for our outdoor patio space. Preferences and requests for outdoor seating will be accommodated whenever possible, but are not guaranteed.

Located in the heart of the world famous Millennium Park and on Michigan Avenue, Park Grill is the city's ultimate room with a view. Led by Executive Chef Bernie Laskowski, Park Grill features Classic American cuisine with an emphasis on local and seasonal ingredients. Park Grill also offers an extensive children’s menu, perfect for family meals and celebrations. Park Grill is the perfect destination for private gatherings and celebrations. Accommodating 40-400 people, guests can choose from a private event in the Founders Room to a semi-private affair in the 275-seat main dining room. Park Grill features a comfortable and classic atmosphere with floor-to-ceiling views of the Park. The full restaurant can also be reserved. In the warm months, The Plaza at Park Grill can be utilized for outdoor gatherings for 650-1,800 people.

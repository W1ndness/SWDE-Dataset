  • In 1955, the Beverly Hilton opened it's doors to some of the world's greatest entertainers and parties. There's no time like the present to appreciate the past . . . Experience now, the newest retro-chic restaurant like no other in the heart of Beverly Hills, CIRCA 55 at the Beverly Hilton where the legacy meets the laid-back sophistication with a timeless taste and a modern twist.

Nobu Dallas

writes...
  • Nobu Dallas, located in the Crescent Court Hotel, serves innovative new style Japanese cuisine by Executive Chef and owner Nobu Matsuhisa.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Japanese, Sushi
  • Neighborhood: Uptown
  • Cross Street: Maple Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.noburestaurants.com
  • Email: dallas@noburestaurants.com
  • Phone: (214) 252-7000
  • Hours of Operation: Dining Hours: Sunday 6:00p – 10:00p Monday thru Friday 6:00p - 11:00p Saturday 5:30p - 11:00p Lounge Hours: Sunday 4:30p – 10:00a Monday thru Thursday 4:30p – 12:00a Friday & Saturday 4:30p – 2:00a
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Nobu Matsuhisa
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Discount Valet at the Rosewood Crescent Hotel. Public underground parking garage available.
  • Catering: Feature Nobu's cuisine at your next event. Personal sushi chef available upon request.
  • Private Party Facilities: Our private Skylight room can accommodate 20 - 100 people. The wine room downstairs can accommodate up 30 people. We also offer an outdoor courtyard for parties. Please contact us for more information.
  • Private Party Contact: Derek Smith (214) 252-7000
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Join us for Happy Hour daily from 5pm -7pm. Drink Specials at our bar and lounge.

Hudson's Ribs & Fish

writes...
  • One of Hudson Valley's more popular restaurants. Established in 1989, Hudson's has become a premier dining destination. Specializing in very fresh seafood - delivered daily and USDA hand cut wet aged steaks. Select a bottle of wine from and outstanding Wine Spectator Award winning wine list. Hudson's is famous for delicious homemade popovers with strawberry butter, a house specialty.

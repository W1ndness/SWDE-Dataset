Catch 10 Seafood Bistro

writes...
  • If you want South Beach ambiance combined with unbeatable home cooking, Catch 10 is where you need to be. Located in the Coral House, a historic landmark built in 1922 by one of Miami Beach’s founding fathers, Mr. Henri Levy, this quaint seafood bistro is brilliantly vibrant and perfect for enjoying the finest fresh fish while catching up with friends. Boasting the best Fish and Chips on the beach, served with homemade hand cut fries, Catch 10 also recommends their signature house beverage, a refreshing glass of Lemonmint. There are also hearty salads and seafood options rich in Omega 3 oils. Featuring a bright and lively interior, Catch 10 also offers outdoor seating, take-out, delivery, and reasonably priced portions served to you by a professional and friendly staff. This locally inspired historical landmark is certainly something that should be worked into every trip to the beach.

The Sky Room

writes...
  • Enjoy an unforgettable evening of dining, dancing, and romancing at The Sky Room, one of Long Beach's most romantic restaurants.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Steakhouse, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Long Beach
  • Cross Street: Ocean Blvd. (Inside the Historic Breakers building)
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.theskyroom.com
  • Email: info@theskyroom.com
  • Phone: (562) 983-2703
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 11:00pm. Sundays 4:30-8:30
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Juan Carlos Olvera
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cigar Room, Counter Seating, Dancing, Entertainment, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area, Smoking Area (bar only), Smoking Area (patio only), View, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: Taxis are available. Metro Rail is within a couple blocks from the Skyroom. The bus stop is located at the corner of the Breakers building.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Pull up to The Skyroom's entrance and the valet will assist you with your auto. Valet is $7.00. Near by self parking is also available.
  • Catering: The Sky Room hosts private parties, receptions and dinners for any occasion. Choose from an extensive menu to guarantee everyone's satisfaction for any party or event.
  • Private Party Facilities: Restaurant has 2 large dining rooms. Seating capacity for private functions to 120 in Deja View Room. City Lights room seats to 120. Private dining room seats 2 to 20 guests. Roof top bar holds approximately 60-70 people combined seating and standing room. Ballroom seats up to 250. Dance floor for special events can be arranged.
  • Private Party Contact: catering@theskyroom.com (562) 983-2724
  • Entertainment: Friday: Cornelius Herring, Pianist and Singer. Saturday: Tino Productions, an eclectic mix of Swing, Salsa, Classic Rock, Jazz, and Modern Hits.

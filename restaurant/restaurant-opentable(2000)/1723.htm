Cipollini Trattoria and Bar

writes...
  • Located in Americana Manhasset, Long Island's most impressive shopping destination, Cipollini Trattoria & Bar is a casual, Manhattan-Style Restaurant. A Metropolitan dining experience that offers freshly prepared, satisfying meals at moderate prices in a comfortable inviting atmosphere. Cipollini has gained a reputation for its array of contemporary Italian specialties as well as their Greek and American influenced dishes. Whether your palette prefers savory salads, enticing entrees or freshly made brick-oven pizzas, Cipollini offers variety and distinctive quality that will suit everyone’s taste.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: Manhasset
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.cipollinirestaurant.com
  • Phone: (516) 627-7172

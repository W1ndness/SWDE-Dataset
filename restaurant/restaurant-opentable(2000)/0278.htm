  • Our Promise... When you visit Mykonos Taverna you will experience what it means to be part of our family. You will experience exceptional service and authentic Greek cuisine in an environment that is an extension of your home. Serving only the freshest authentic Greek food... is what distinguishes Mykonos Taverna from any other restaurants. The Mykonos family hand picks our fresh produce and meats daily. Our seafood is flown in every day, delivering unparalleled taste and quality. We begin our day at 5:00am where we set out to find the freshest ingredients to create our exotic dishes. Each day we only have one thing in mind, "To deliver the most authentic dishes using only the freshest ingredients we buy daily."

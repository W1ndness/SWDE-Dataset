Thornapple Daily Grill

writes...
  • Located in the heart of Ada & less than 10 minutes from downtown Grand Rapids, Thornapple prides themselves in being the neighborhood's favorite restaurant. The diverse menu will astound you with casual fare suitable for any occasion, to more inspired dishes for the most discriminating of tastes. A vast wine cellar & specialty martini list are offered, for the more adventurous. Don't forget the "made from scratch" dessert menu. A wonderful backyard is available for lawn parties, rehearsal dinners or receptions. There is also an intimate wine cellar for private dinners or meetings & the classic, non smoking dining room with views of the backyard, which are especially breathtaking in the winter. Enjoy the large deck in the summer; it's shaded enough to make you comfortable on any summer afternoon. The taproom is comfortable & cozy with great happy hour features Monday-Friday. Please come down & see why Thornapple has been Ada's favorite restaurant for over 25 years.

Technique at Le Cordon Bleu - Dallas

writes...
  • Le Technique Restaurant offers a glimpse into Le Cordong Bleu College of Culinary Arts, one of the world"s premier culinary education programs. Dine with our students as they learn to operate in a restaurant environment where they will prepare and serve your meal from start to finish under the guidance of our chef and hospitality instructors. Enjoy a casual yet elegant menu highlighting the freshest ingredients prepared using classical and modern techniques. Join us as we celebrate the successes of our students. And as always, we appreciate your continued interest and support. Cheers! Reservations are recommended.

Junior's Supper Club

writes...
  • Junior’s Supper Club . We are a fine dining restaurant, noted for the finest steaks in Oklahoma City. We are also noted for lobster tail, imported from the cold waters of Australia. It is out of this world. The other item we are famous for is Caesar salad, which originated at Junior’s in 1973, and is made at the diners table. A nice touch, believe me.

Solu Restaurant - The Palm Beach Marriott Beach Resort & Spa on Singer Island

writes...
  • From the Latin word Sol, meaning sun. Solu is Singer Island’s premier oceanfront destination restaurant. We serve seasonal seafood dishes, Angus steaks and chops, and decadent desserts. It is a unique gem on the Singer Island dining scene. Solu features upscale casual dining with dramatic vistas of the cool blue waters of the Atlantic Ocean. With a casual yet refined elegance, the restaurant offers a modern contemporary flair with bold nuances. Culinary masterpieces take shape from the exhibition kitchen complimented by an extensive wine selection from the wine cellar and attentive service. Alfresco dining is also available under a covered loggia where calming breezes and the soft sounds of the gentle surf provide an inviting relaxed atmosphere.

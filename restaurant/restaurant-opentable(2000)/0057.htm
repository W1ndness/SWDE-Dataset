clarklewis

writes...
  • A bustling eatery in the central eastside industrial district serving Italian inspired local, organic, seasonal, sustainable fare. clarklewis has received many honors since opening, including restaurant of the year (the Oregonian), 10 most notable new restaurants of 2004 (Wall Street Journal) and 10 hottest new restaurants of the year (msn.com) Dinner reservations are highly recommended, however most evenings we have walk-in availability on a first come first served basis. Bar counter space is first come first served as well. Online booking available up to a party of 6.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Northwest, Organic
  • Neighborhood: Downtown Portland - NE
  • Cross Street: Taylor
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.clarklewispdx.com
  • Email: info@clarklewispdx.com
  • Phone: (503) 235-2294
  • Hours of Operation: Serving lunch: monday thru friday 11.30- 2.00, dinner: monday thru saturday 5.30- late, happy hour: mon thru sat 5.00- 6.30
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Dolan Lane
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: clarklewis has 6 spaces available all day, directly in front of the restaurant. The Eastbank Commerce Center (where clarklewis is located) has 72 spots open from 5:30pm to close; these spaces are reserved during the day.
  • Private Party Facilities: We can accommodate private luncheon or dinner events in our bar dining room: up to 32 seated, 36 seated cozily, 50 with extra notice. Up to 70 ppl for cocktail events. Buyout of the entire restaurant available on special request only.
  • Private Party Contact: Kurt (503) 226-3394 x1

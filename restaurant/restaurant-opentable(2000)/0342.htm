Emeril's Miami Beach

writes...
  • Chef Emeril Lagasse’s newest restaurant, Emeril’s Miami Beach introduces “New New Orleans” cuisine to Miami's South Beach. Located at the Loews Miami Beach Hotel, the newly renovated restaurant includes signature touches developed by the Rockwell Group and Emeril Lagasse, while paying homage to the architectural grandeur and significance of the unique historical art deco location. The expo kitchen and the food bar, located in the main dining room, offer guests a special dining experience. Although walk-ins can be accommodated, reservations are recommended. Our dress policy is business casual for dinner and casual dress for lunch with no tank tops for men. Sebastian Tribout, General Manager, and Brandon Benack, Chef de Cuisine, are dedicated to providing you with that memorable dining experience.

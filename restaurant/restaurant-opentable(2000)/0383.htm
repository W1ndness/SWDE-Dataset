SUSHISAMBA dromo

writes...
  • Celebrate the culture and cuisine of Japan, Brazil and Peru at SUSHISAMBA dromo! From kobe sliders to the freshest sushi, sashimi and ceviche, SUSHISAMBA has something for everyone. Eclectic menus for lunch or dinner blend Brazilian, Peruvian and Japanese flavors in a carnaval-spiked atmosphere that’s synonymous with fun. An award-winning wine and sake list, extensive beer menu, signature cocktails and creative desserts round out the experience.

  • Cuisine: Japanese, Peruvian, Brazilian
  • Neighborhood: Miami Beach / South Beach

The weekly Tuesday night extravaganza begins at midnight. SUSHI & SAKE 101 classes begin on Wednesday, August 11th! Roll up your sleeves and roll some sushi. Learn all about the history of sake and sushi, while tasting a wide variety of flavors that will have you leaving class an expert! Call for reservations today.

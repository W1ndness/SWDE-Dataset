Papa Joe's/Iacomini's

writes...
  • The Iacomini Family first opened their doors in 1932, The heart & soul has been "Papa Joe" Iacomini who passed away in 1998. Those who have gazed into his steel blue eyes, or have watched him tour his restaurants, always searching for ways to improve, know why the Iacomini Family has always been considered the first family of fine & traditional dining in the Akron area.
    Joe's legacy lives on with the fourth generation of restaurateurs. Judy, his daughter, & her son Jeff, & daughter Kim, continue to try & live up to his standards of excellence. This trio of new culinarians continues to bring you "The Best of Iacomini's"!
    2002 marks 70 years of serving the Akron area. From the early days of the Florentine Grill, to present day at Papa Joe's, we say thank you, & to your grandparents, for dining at our restaurants.

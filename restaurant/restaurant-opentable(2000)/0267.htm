Gilbert's Cafe & Bar

writes...
  • Be it an intimate dinner out or a colorful urban experience, Gilbert's offers a splendid bridge for an global taste in flavor, music, personality and design. Join us and experience our multiple award winning menu, atmosphere and special events. See why we have so many repeat customers who join us daily for dinner and (since 2000) for our famous weekend brunch. We invite you to be our guest and discover a very unique and tasteful cross cultural experience in dining. Relax, take in the warmth, and enjoy our intimate and comfortable approach to a great evening out.

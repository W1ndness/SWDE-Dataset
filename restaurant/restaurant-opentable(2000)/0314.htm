Tramici

writes...
  • What does Tramici mean? It means "among friends" and Tramici is the place where you want to be with friends and family to enjoy delicious American-Italian fare including Housemade Mozzarella with Basil, a crispy 4 Cheese Pizza with Arugula, and your favorite standby's like Spaghetti and Meatballs or Oven Baked Lasagna. This new restaurant is the second venture of Dave Snyder, owner of Halyards and The Halyard Restaurant Group. General Manager Bo Doss and Chef de Cuisine Brandon Stevens are on hand to ensure warm hospitality and a great experience. The decor is casual and highlighted by a 18 seat bar, 6 tables in our courtyard, and an open kitchen to watch the delicious food come out of the WoodStone Oven. The adventurous wine list and cold tap beers will pair well with the menu that is changed with the seasons. Tramici is family friendly with a full Kid's Menu and open everyday where tables for walk-in's are available all the time.

  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Neighborhood: St. Simons Island
  • Cross Street: Sea Island Causeway
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

    Thursday through Saturday from 5-10pm. Closed New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Labor Day, Fourth of July, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day.

    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Brandon Stevens
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress

  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Available in front of and to the side of Tramici is a secure, large, well lit parking lot. If you would like to drop off your guests at the front door, pull up under our porte-cochere to keep them warm and dry.
  • Catering: Tramici has an experienced catering crew to meet your needs and surpass your expectations. Event Coordinator Jessica Smith will organize your event to include delicious food, tasty beverages, rental equipment, tents, locations, service staff, and entertainment if you wish. Whether it be a low country boil on the beach, an intimate dinner for twelve, or a wedding reception with your 250 closest friends, Tramici will create an extraordinary event to remember.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private luncheons are available everyday at Tramici for business meetings, social events, bridesmaids' luncheons, or for whatever your needs may be. If you need a private function at night, please call to discuss a buy out of the restaurant for the entire evening or just a part of the night.
  • Private Party Contact: Jessica Smith/Dave Snyder (912) 634-2202
  • Entertainment: Tramici is located across the parking lot from Island Cinemas Movie Theater. Park once to have dinner and a movie. We have the movie times available.
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Happy hour is everyday from 5-6:30 with special food and drink prices. Tramici is involved with numerous local and national charities including the St. Simon's Land Trust, Cystic Fibrosis, and the Boys and Girls Club.

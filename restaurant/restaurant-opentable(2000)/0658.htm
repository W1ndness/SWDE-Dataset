Harvest at Pheasant Run Resort

writes...
  • You are invited to Harvest, Pheasant Run’s newest restaurant. Located in the unique & fitting setting of a stylishly renovated barn, Harvest offers traditional regional cuisine with a twist, outstanding steaks & seafood & original seasonal fare. Harvest features only the finest aged beef, exceptional seafood & seasonal dishes inspired by fresh ingredients available in local markets. The extensive bar not only features a wine list including outstanding vintages from Napa & Sonoma & unique selections from regional wineries but also wonderful fruit infused martinis. Finish the evening with one of our homemade desserts. Come & join Harvest tonight, your table is waiting! Gift cards are available for purchase.

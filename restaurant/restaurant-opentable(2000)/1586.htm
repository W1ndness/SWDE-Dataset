Regatta of Cotuit

writes...
  • The warmest of welcomes from all of us at The Regatta restaurant—serving delicious, award-winning New American cuisine. You'll be delighted with our personal attention and caring service in our 218- year-old mansion. You'll feel special in our cozy, candle lit dining rooms. Enjoy a seasonally created cocktail at our expanded bar, or dinner under the stars on our Terrace. Travel the world, without leaving your seat, with our Food and Flight menu, available every Wednesday. Too tired to cook- we offer take-out. At the Regatta, our world revolves around you.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Cotuit
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.regattarestaurant.com
  • Phone: (508) 428-5715
  • Hours of Operation: Dining in the Summer Season(Late June - Late September), open seven nights a week, serving your evening meal from 5:00 PM
    Dining in the Off-Season (fall and spring)we are serving your evening meal from 5:00pm, five nights a week (closed Sunday and Monday)
    Dining in the Winter Season (January- End of March), we are open four nights a week (closed Sunday-Tuesday), serving your evening meal from 5:00 PM
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Weldon Fizell
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking is plentiful, available in our own lots on either side of our building and also across the street.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Regatta offers 5 intimate, candle-lit dining rooms accommodating from 8 to 34 guests. This enables us to seat private parties as small as 8 in short notice. In addition to our al la carte menu and tap menu the Regatta offers 3 pre-selected menus for larger size groups. If a private dining room is of interest, please contact the restaurant.
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    The Regatta welcomes private parties, rehearsal dinners, and small intimate weddings. Please contact us for any information at 508 428 5715. We will be more than happy to accommodate you in any way possible.

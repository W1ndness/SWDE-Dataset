Sitti

writes...
  • Located in heart of downtown Raleigh, Sitti offers authentic Lebanese cuisine made fresh in house daily. We have all ABC permits and specialize in hand crafted beers as well as Lebanese and French wines. Our restaurant is capable of accommodating everyone from a single diner to large parties of up to 150. We also have an outdoor courtyard that is perfect for a sit down meal or just to enjoy a cocktail or two on a nice afternoon. Sitti is the perfect place to host company events, bridal receptions, or just a get together with friends and family. Please come join us for a great meal served with a smile, in an urban and exciting downtown area, at casual dining prices.

  • Cuisine: Mediterranean, Middle Eastern
  • Neighborhood: Raleigh
  • Cross Street: Hargett

  • Public Transit: R-Line stop # R-19 (free bus line)
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Street parking is available as well as two parking decks located within a block of the restaurant.
  • Catering: Catering is available. Please e mail paige@sitti-raleigh.com for details.
  • Private Party Facilities: Sitti offers 2 unique private dining spaces for any occasion. Our first is our downstairs dining area, which accommodates 70 seated and 80 standing. This venue is perfect for company presentations, business meetings, or simply provides a quiet, private place for any function. Our second space, our outdoor courtyard, accommodates 100 guests seated and up to 200 for a standing reception. This area provides an opportunity to dine in the sun or under the stars in a comfortable and secluded setting, unlike any other in Raleigh.
  • Private Party Contact: Paige Gunther (919) 239-4070
  • Entertainment: Live music in our courtyard- check our website or call for details

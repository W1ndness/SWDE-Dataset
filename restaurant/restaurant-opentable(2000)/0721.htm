B.B. King's Blues Club

writes...
  • BB King’s Blues Club Nashville: Where southern cookin’ and blues are good for the soul. Enjoy fried pickles, catfish, salmon, Cajun chicken pasta, succulent steaks, dry rubbed ribs and an array of wines that compliment every entree and satisfy any pallet. BB King’s also offers an entertaining club atmosphere where the music and dancing never stop, lively staff that keeps the party up, and a hot retail shop to help make your mark. To locals and travelers alike, BB King’s Blues Club is the spice of the night!

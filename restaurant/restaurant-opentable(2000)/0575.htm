Ocean Grill - Madison

writes...
  • The festive, inviting atmosphere of Ocean Grill combines the best of urban chic & casual comfort. Locally sourced and purchased market fresh items used in weekly and daily Chef's specials, as well as happy hour specials and meal discounts make the Ocean Grill a fantastic place to have a drink and some appetizers with some friends or a great spot to come for that special dinner. Take advantage of the beautiful weather of spring, summer & fall, & ask to be seated on our patio, which boasts views of the Capitol & the Monona Terrace.

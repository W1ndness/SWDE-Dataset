Vinology Ann Arbor

writes...
  • Vinology was created to be your everyday wine bar. Over the years the Jonna family has enjoyed sharing wine, food, and spirits through our love of experimentation and education. Whether you are enjoying a glass of wine at our extensive bar, an intimate dinner with friends, or gathering for a corporate or social event in one of private dining rooms, we maintain a passion and commitment towards exceptional and innovative food and wine. When you dine at Vinology, you have our commitment that every detail from the food we select, to the wine we serve, to the service we provide will be executed with careful attention to detail. At Vinology, "wine" down, relax, experiment, and have fun while you educate your palate and savor the offerings. “Great food. Award winning wine list. Intimately well-paced events”

  • Cuisine: Continental, Tapas / Small Plates

  • Cross Street: Main Street between Washington and Huron

  • Private Party Facilities: The Bubble Room, our largest private dining room, seats up to 50 guests and can accommodate 80 guests for a reception style party. The casually elegant Bubble Room features hundreds of hand blown glass bubbles suspended from the ceiling, original exposed brick, and a private bar. The Cellar seats up to 14 guests for private dining and tasting events. Wine bottles line the walls of this glass enclosed private dining room offering guests an immersive, intimate food and wine experience. To reserve a private room, please contact Joey at 734-222-9841 or e-mail vinology@vinowinebars.net.

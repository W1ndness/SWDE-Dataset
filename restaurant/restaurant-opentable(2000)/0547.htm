Bald Headed Bistro

writes...
  • The Bald Headed Bistro, where the West meets the South, specializes in USDA Prime Aged Beef, fresh fish, lobster, and assorted exotic dishes such as ostrich, and elk. The Bald Headed Bistro also offers wood grilled pizzas, rotisserie chicken, and an award winning wine list that complements the varied food selections. For your convenience, the restaurant also offers wireless internet access.

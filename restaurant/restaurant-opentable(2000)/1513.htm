Capt's Waterfront Grill

writes...
  • Waterfront dining at its best! Choose from outstanding charcoal grilled fresh seafood, steaks & burgers to traditional and contemporary New England fare. Rarely will you find a better combination of great food, service and ambiance anywhere. Capt’s Pub is located on the 1st Floor, Topsides Dining Room & Deck on the 2nd Floor.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Salem, MA
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.capts.com
  • Phone: (978) 741-0555
  • Hours of Operation: Brunch: Sunday:11:30am - 3:00pm
    Lunch: Daily: 11:30am - 5:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 9:30pm, Friday & Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: David Silviera
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: One can take the commuter train to and from Boston, The "T", about a 25 minute ride. The station is a 10 to 15 minute walk from Capt's. Taxi service is available by phone. There is ferry service available from Boston - near the New England Aquarium to Blaney St. in Salem - a 10 minute walk. There is a sight-seeing trolley service that stops here.
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: There is on street parking in front of Capt's, a private parking lot adjacent to Pickering Wharf and a multi-storey parking garage, also adjacent to Pickering Wharf.
  • Catering: We are pleased to cater on The Friendship of Salem, adjacent to Capt's, a large square rigged sailing ship managed by the Salem Maritime National Historic Park.

1906 at Longwood Gardens Reviews
 

Facilities 1 of 5
  • Ballroom

    Longwood Gardens' magnificent ballroom is located within the ever-changing    conservatory. The Ballroom, which re-opened in October 2005 after extensive renovations, was originally constructed in 1929 to house Longwood’s pipe organ and serve as a venue for concerts, lectures and dinners. The room is unique for its elaborate finishes including a parquet floor, fabric-paneled walls, and ceiling comprising 1,104 panes of rose-colored etched glass.

    Capacity
    350230
  • Fern Floor

    Also located within the serene conservatory, the Fern Floor, or Exhibition Hall,    has the original, sunken, marble floor which is usually flooded with a few inches of water for reflection. Occasionally the floor is drained and used for special displays and events. Dining tables may also be placed in the alcoves on either side of the main floor for additional seating.    This space also has a stage to accommodate live music.

    Capacity
    350230
  • Courtyard Tent

    Located in the lawn area near the Terrace Restaurant, this venue is available from early spring through most of the fall. The sides of the tent open to maintain a garden atmosphere while guests are dining and relaxing.

    Capacity
    300150
  • Founder's Room

    Located within the Terrace Restaurant, this newly renovated space is fully equipped with state-of-the-art audio visual capabilities, and incredible views of the gardens.    It is perfect for a working business meeting with presentations, and may be transformed into an intimate dining experience for a rehearsal dinner or anniversary party.

    Capacity
    10075
  • Terra Cotta Room

    The Terra Cotta Room has recently been equipped with the most current audio-visual tools necessary for a successful business meeting or working lunch.    This space accommodates theatre-style seating, classroom seating, or an early morning work session over continental breakfast.    When draped with linen and flower centerpieces, this room becomes a lovely setting for family brunch or a birthday party before heading out to explore the gardens.

    Capacity

Longwood Gardens is an ideal destination for a corporate event, business meeting, bridal brunch, anniversary party, family barbeque, holiday celebration, or birthday party. The lily pond and patio of oranges are perfect spots for a cocktail reception preceding a seated dinner on the fern floor or in the ballroom. After a long business meeting in the Founder's Room or the Terra Cotta Room, a breezy reception in the Courtyard Tent would be a fabulous way of connecting with colleagues. We offer package menus that change frequently to ensure that we are serving only the freshest local ingredients. We are also able to customize menus to better accommodate client preferences. Longwood Gardens and Restaurant Associates are committed to providing every guest with an unforgettable experience focused on hospitality excellence.

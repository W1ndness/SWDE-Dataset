333 Belrose

writes...
  • 333 Belrose is a contemporary restaurant featuring Chef Carlo's creative American cuisine. We offer both indoor and patio seating as well as a banquet space. 333 Belrose has a full liquor license with an eclectic American wine list.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Fusion / Eclectic, Californian
  • Neighborhood: Radnor
  • Cross Street: King of Prussia Road
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.333Belrose.com
  • Email: info@333belrose.com
  • Phone: (610) 293-1000
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday – Friday: 11:30am – 3:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Tuesday: 5:00pm – 9:00pm, Wednesday – Saturday: 5:00pm – 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Carlo deMarco
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: SEPTA Regional Rail line "R5" Radnor Stop. For taxi service, it is best to pre-arrange a taxicab pick-up, as some local cab services close earlier than the restaurant.
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: We have our own lot, and are across a small residential street from additional free parking. Two handicap-accessible parking spots are available in front of the building.
  • Catering: We would be happy to help you put together a nice meal for any occasion. 333 Belrose can organize anything from salad and sandwich platters to private dinner parties served in your home. Office lunches are a particular specialty, and we would love to talk with you about your upcoming needs.
  • Private Party Facilities: Our lovely Garden room, which is the oldest part of our colonial-era building, can accommodate private parties up to 30 guests. It is perfect for business dinners, cocktail parties and special family gatherings.
  • Private Party Contact: Kelly Finch (610) 293-1000
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    We can help you put together any type of special event. Chef Carlo deMarco has worked with clients to host specialty wine dinners, tasting menus, beer dinners, etc... Call us to speak with someone about hosting your special event.

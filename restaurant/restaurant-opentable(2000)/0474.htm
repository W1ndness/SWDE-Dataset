Ferrari's Italian Villa

writes...
  • One of Dallas’ most revered Italian restaurants for more than 25 years. Francesco & Jane Secchi, who hail from Italy and England respectively, serve Northern & Southern Italian cuisine with emphasis on fresh seafood, steaks and pasta from Francesco's native Sardegna.

    The adjoining wine bar serves flights of wines and martinis with a more casual menu consisting of hand- tossed pizzas baked in a mesquite wood-fired oven.

The private dining rooms at Ferrari's Italian Villa Grapevine have been tailored to accommodate both intimate or large corporate events and family gatherings. Our adjoining Ferrari's Pizza Patio has an open kitchen and outdoor professional bocce court for when the competitive juices start flowing. You may also decide to sip cognac and smoke cigars in our Padrino Room for a nightcap. We take pride in our 7 customizable menus and would love to work with you and your guests to create a memorable evening. Thank you and warm regards from Francesco and Jane Secchi.

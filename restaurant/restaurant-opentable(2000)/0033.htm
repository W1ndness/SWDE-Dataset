Red Square - Las Vegas

writes...
  • With a nod to the glory of Imperial Russia, Red Square transports guests to a world of romance and intrigue. Mingle in the Vodka Vault and hobnob at the famous frozen bar, which features more than 150 frozen and infusions, martinis and Russian-inspired cocktails. Along with an impressive caviar selection, diners enjoy such perestroika-inspired Russian and internationally influenced favorites as Siberian Nachos - smoked salmon, wasabi cream & tobiko atop crispy wonton chip and our classic Roquefort Crusted Filet Mignon with Potato Puree, Roasted Garlic and Red Wine Sauce.

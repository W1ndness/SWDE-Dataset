Lola Restaurant

writes...
  • LOLA (Love Often, Laugh Alot) is the brainchild restaurant of local restaurateur Wendy Rosano (Cucina Mio & Bistro Mio). This modern restaurant-ultra lounge located in West Delray, awakens Jog Road (South of Linton) with its glowing lights and bustling energy. Chef Bruno Silva and his culinary team utilize seasonal ingredients to create an eclectic range of whimsical Mediterranean bistro dishes. Coupled with Certified Sommelier Mike Lee’s stellar wine list and signature cocktails LOLA is the place to be. LOLA has dining choices to appeal to all preferences. Two relaxing bars, inside or outside, are complemented by a stylish dining room inside and a relaxing fire pit and full patio outside. Nightly entertainment and lots of exciting specials complete the scene. So come join the party! LOLA Restaurant & Ultra Lounge is located at 16950 Jog Road, Delray Beach, Florida. For reservations and more information call 561-496-LOLA (5652) or visit loladelray.com

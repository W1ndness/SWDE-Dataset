The Butcher Shop Steakhouse

writes...
  • The Butcher Shop is a fine dining yet moderately priced steakhouse with a business casual feel. When first arriving, guests have the opportunity to see first hand our selection of steaks and chops through our meat cooler. Keep walking and the aromas from our all charcoal grill which is open to the restaurant let you know that you are in the right place. We pride ourselves in serving only the freshest of ingredients. Our menu ranges from sushi grade ahi tuna and fresh water salmon to prime aged steaks, pork chops, chicken and the highly praised Kobe beef of Japan. Banquet and private dining available for parties of all sizes. Our friendly and professional wait staff and kitchen look forward to having you dine with us.

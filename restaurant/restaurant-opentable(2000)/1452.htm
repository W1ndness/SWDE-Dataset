Terra Malibu

writes...
  • Terra Restaurant, located in the heart of Malibu, serves Seasonal American cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. We grow many of our own vegetables and herbs in our garden and our bread and pizza dough are made daily in house. Terra was voted by OpenTable users as Malibu’s most romantic restaurant and The Los Angeles Times called us “a perfect fit”.

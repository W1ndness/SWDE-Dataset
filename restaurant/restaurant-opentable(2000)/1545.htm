Kashmir

writes...
  • Indian restaurant, located on fashionable Newbury Street, has 3 elegant dining halls with authentic antique & modern artifacts from Northern India. Weather permitting, service is available on the outdoor patios open to Newbury Street. Kashmir has been voted "Best of Boston" several times.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Indian
  • Neighborhood: Back Bay
  • Cross Street: Gloucester Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.kashmirrestaurant.com/
  • Email: webmaster@kasmirspices.com
  • Phone: (617) 536-1695
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Daily: 11:30am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Daily: 3:00pm - 11:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Harjeet Singh
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Counter Seating, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wine
  • Public Transit: Green Line: Hynes Convention Center or Copley T Stop
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Street parking also available.
  • Catering: Catering is available for all occations. Includes delivery to full scale. Including tables, chairs, tablecloths, China and waitstaff.

One Pico

writes...
  • WOW views of Santa Monica beach, the pier and colorful Ferris Wheel , the ocean, and the most spectacular sunsets! A relaxed and comfortable southern California beach style, warmed by the burning fireplace on cool ocean evenings. Foods from the local market and ocean, grilled and simply prepared. Enjoy prix-fix menus lunch and dinner. Enjoy just the right wines - by bottle or glass, plus fun cocktails.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Californian
  • Neighborhood: Santa Monica
  • Cross Street: Appian Way
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.shuttersonthebeach.com
  • Phone: (310) 587-1717
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch, Monday - Saturday: 11:30 am – 2:30 pm
    Sunday Brunch: 11:00 am – 3:00 pm
    Dinner, Nightly: 6:00 pm - 10:00 pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Michael Reardon
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, BYO Liquor, Entertainment, Fireplace, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: Ocean Ave./Pico Blvd.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Catering: From an intimate dinner for 5 to a corporate dinner for 55 to a catered wedding for 205, Shutters on the Beach offers relaxed elegance and friendly service to complement its delicious food. Overlooking the Pacific Ocean, there is no better place to plan your next special occasion. Let us handle every detail. We specialize in engagement parties (as well as engagements), bridal and wedding showers, rehearsal dinners, and of course, the big day too! Our private rooms are perfect for corporate dinners as well. Please call our catering offices at (310) 458-0030.
  • Private Party Contact: Toby B. Lent (310) 587-1717

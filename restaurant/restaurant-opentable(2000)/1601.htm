The Spiced Pear

writes...
  • Welcome to The Spiced Pear Restaurant offering the very best in Newport fine dining. We use only the freshest seasonal ingredients from New England and abroad. Enjoy fine dining in a beautiful oceanfront setting or enjoy relaxed elegant fare with a cool ocean breeze on our beautiful outdoor terrace beginning May 1st and continuing through Columbus Day weekend. The Spiced Pear Restaurant located in The Chanler at Cliffwalk is a Newport treasure waiting to be discovered! The best of Newport awaits you with endless cliff-side Atlantic views, romantic atmosphere and the highest quality service.

The Lodge

writes...
  • The Lodge Restaurant is an intimate venue with two cozy dining rooms and a festive Pub. In our main dining Room, you'll find a roaring fire during the winter with wooden rafters and beautiful pine accents, floor to ceiling windows, overlooking endless pines with a spectacular ridgeline back drop. During the summer, you can dine and relax on our deck with stunning views of the golf course and the lush Sierra environment. Year round there is always excitement and camaraderie in our lively Pub. We have a spectacular wine list as well as a Manager's wine list with incredible deals. There are both weekly and monthy promotions and unique events taking place in the restaurant so check out our events page. Our award-winnig chef will create a meal you won't soon forget (Specials change daily). Last but not least our freindly and welcoming staff will make you feel like an old freind and show you the true spirit of the people and the area, that is TAHOE.

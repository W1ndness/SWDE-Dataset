Tuscany Mediterranee Grill

writes...
  • Tuscany Mediterranee Grill is a fine dining Italian and Mediterranean restaurant located in the award-winning Irvine Concourse business complex and less than 1/2 mile away from John Wayne Airport. Since 2003, we have been preparing decadent dishes using only the finest ingredients. To us, quality food and a friendly, attentive staff are paramount in providing a dining experience to remember. We specialize in gourmet Italian cuisine, including succulen appetizersrs, house made pasta dishes, fresh seafood, black Angus beef, and fresh chicken breast. Our superb dishes are created with organic ingredients (precisely measured) and topped with delicious sauces made from imported hot red Italian tomatoes. We also serve original Mediterranean kebabs to give our returning clients variety. Our layout is great for large parties and groups, as well as for private engagements. We are a perfect place for a romantic dinner for couples or to meet clients for business lunch meetings.

Jer-Ne Restaurant and Bar

writes...
  • Jer-Ne Restaurant and Bar (pronounced Journey) on the street level of the hotel draws on the contemporary artistic energy surrounding it. Since opening, it has been a Boston hot-spot. With its double-height wall-of-windows looking onto Avery Street with theater-goers passing by, it is designed to be cosmopolitan and welcoming. Jer-Ne offers dining throughout the day and evening. The menu includes familiar American favorites, seafood and steaks all prepared with artistic flair. A perfect blend of fashion and tradition is what will make your visit worth it.

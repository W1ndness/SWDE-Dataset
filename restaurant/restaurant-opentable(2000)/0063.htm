The Lodge Restaurant at Black Butte Ranch

writes...
  • Come and enjoy this very unique Oregon dining experience. Nestled in the Cascades with panoramic views of the Three Sisters and Mt Washington, experience a central Oregon premiere dining destination. Our focus is on what's fresh in the region, featuring NW meats and seafood. Our service is warm and personable. Our outside dining and decks provide a wonderful way to enjoy a tasty beverage while soaking in the incredible view. We give new meaning to “alfresco!”

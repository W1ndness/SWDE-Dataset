Interstate Kitchen & Bar

writes...
  • Interstate Kitchen & Bar is an inviting, affordable environment where you can fill up on great comfort food and libations. The menu boasts many familiar sandwiches, salads and dishes, which are prepared with the greatest attention to every detail. Enjoy some of your favorite flavors, scratch-made, like Mom used to make. The bar, which focuses on American whiskey and beer, has over 50 of each to choose from. Craft cocktails, and the signature Torpedo Slushy Shot, ensure a smiley good time for everyone. Interstate has a separate dining room that can accommodate up to 65 people for private events, parties and celebrations. Don’t forget, the kitchen is open late-night, serving the full menu until midnight and happy hour until 1:30am. Always a great time and always a great value, Interstate is an essential stop on your itinerary.

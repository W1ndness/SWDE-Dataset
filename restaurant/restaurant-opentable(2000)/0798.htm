Cheevers Cafe

writes...
  • Cheevers Cafe, located just 2 miles from Downtown Oklahoma City, specializes in contemporary comfort food with Southwest influences. Cheevers serves a variety of prime steaks, fresh seafood & wonderful salads, as well as daily & nightly specials. The Cheevers family looks forward to serving you.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: American, Southwest
  • Neighborhood: Oklahoma City
  • Cross Street: NW 23rd
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.cheeverscafe.com
  • Email: rkpaul@coxinet.net
  • Phone: (405) 525-7007
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 4:00pm - 9:30pm, Friday: 4:00pm - 10:30pm, Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:30pm, Sunday: 10:30am - 3:00pm.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ryan Lawson
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Cafe, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area, Smoking Area (bar only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: We have 2 private lots adjacent to the building for parking
  • Catering: Cheevers provides catering for groups or private parties & will coordinate any type of event from weddings, rehearsal dinners, business events or private in-home dinners.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private rooms are available for dinner parties; one will accommodate up to 15 people & the other (which is the entire bar-area) will accommodate up to 50 people.
  • Private Party Contact: Henri Bailey (405) 525-7007

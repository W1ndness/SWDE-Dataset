Ten Prime Steak and Sushi

writes...
  • Guests can enjoy the ultimate dining experience among the backdrop of a cobalt blue romantic, sexy setting visually landscaped with art that stimulates the senses. 10’s atmosphere becomes fun and lively with sound effects in the bathrooms, bubbles showering over our busy bar crowd, and a Latin sound. Our signature prime sirloin steaks are hand cut from the finest corn-fed Midwestern beef. We age them 5 – 6 weeks, giving us the most flavorful, juicy, delicious steaks. Join us at our newly redesigned sushi bar or order off of our new designer steak menu! The menu features Corn-Fed Western Beef, USDA Prime, & All Natural Designer Steaks. Our All Natural Steaks our free from antibiotics, growth hormones, and fed a strict vegetarian diet.

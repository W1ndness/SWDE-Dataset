Zinsvalley

writes...
  • Located in the heart of downtown Napa. A short walk across the street from the Napa Valley Opera House shines a beacon for scrumptious food and a lively yet intimate lounge. Floor to ceiling windows in the main dining room offer excellent views of all that is happening downtown. In the evening the mirror wrapped walls come alive with the glow of the numerous votive candles. Cozy up to the beautiful fireplace in the lounge during the winter season and enjoy the relaxing outdoor courtyard in the warm weather months. The "game" is always on discreetly in the lounge.

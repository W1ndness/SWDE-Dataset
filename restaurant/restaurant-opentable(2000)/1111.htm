McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Edina

writes...
  • McCormick & Schmick's is a fresh seafood restaurant with 35 fresh species on our twice-daily printed menus. We also have a great aged steak menu along with chicken, salads & pasta for the non-seafood diner. Our location has two large patios with heaters & two spacious dining rooms. We cater all large parties in the meeting & banquet rooms in the Westin Hotel.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Edina
  • Cross Street: York Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.mccormickandschmicks.com
  • Email: ms110@msmg.com
  • Phone: (952) 920-1142
  • Hours of Operation: Daily: 11:00am - 4:00pm Dinner: Monday-Thursday 4:00pm - 11:00pm (bar food until 12:00am) Friday-Saturday 4:00pm - 12:00am Sunday 4:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Scott Thole
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: The #6 bus has stops along France Ave. and York Ave.
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Parking Details: There are several parking spaces directly in front of the restaurant, and a four-level parking ramp located behind the restaurant. The adjoining Westin Hotel offers valet parking at their front entrance, at a discounted rate for McCormick & Schmick's guests.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private parties for 20 to 400 guests are available.
  • Private Party Contact: Julie Broders (952) 567-5000
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Please alert the restaurant to any special occasion you might be celebrating. We would be happy to print customized menus and, for birthdays and anniversaries, we will offer a complimentary signature chocolate dessert.

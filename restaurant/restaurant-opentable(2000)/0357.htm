Martorano's

writes...
  • Martorano's brings the finest Italian-American food and atmosphere to its newest location in the Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino in Hollywood, Florida serving great Italian-American food from the World famous meatball as voted by Gourmet in 2007, to in house homemade fresh Mozzarella & Ricotta Gnocchi and our famous 16oz Veal Chop. Keeping with the Italian - American tradition, you will be listening to Sinatra, Bennett, Martin & Darin while watching classic Italian - American movies on our 36 flat screens. At 9:00pm the disco ball drops and the music pumps R&B classics. You're encouraged to get up and dance anywhere you like! Come and enjoy a night out with great food, movies and music! We are the newest location building our name on the great Martorano's quality you know. We are proud of our open kitchen and staff. Come and see what we are all about. You never know who you might be eating next to - Shaq, Ludacris, Stevie Van Zandt. Don't forget to visit Martoranoshollywood.com

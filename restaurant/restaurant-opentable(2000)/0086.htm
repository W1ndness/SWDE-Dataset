Pesce - Houston

writes...
  • Award - Winning Pesce Restaurant welcomes guests to an incredible dining experience. From the tasteful design & elegantly appointed dining room to the unparalleled cuisine created by renowned Chef Mark Holley, Pesce is a feast for the eyes as well as the palate. Decoratively, Pesce has incorporated several clever ocean design elements. There’s an aquarium built to tasteful dimensions & a wave-like ceiling, an elegant dining room, with a marble oyster bar & ornate mirrors.
    The carefully crafted seafood menu offers a melting pot of flavors. The menu dictates fish & shellfish dishes touched, though refreshingly not co-mingled, with a taste of the Mediterranean, Spain, Vietnam, China, France & Louisiana Creole.
    Pesce’s expert, attentive staff meticulously caters to every guest, setting the stage for an unparalleled evening of fine dining. Whether formal or casual, our knowledgeable & caring staff will help you plan your event that will be truly unforgettable.

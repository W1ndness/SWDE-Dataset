Olivéa

writes...
  • A new look at the tastes of the Mediterranean inspired by the cuisines of Spain, Italy & Southern France. Boldness of taste, purity of ingredients, simplicity of presentation and uncomplicated describes the food. Made by hand each dish showcases the flavors of this area. Olivéa's menu changes seasonally to make the most of the current harvests and reflect the values of ourselves as well as the countries we emulate. Olivéa also celebrates the Mediterranean shared sense of exuberant hospitality and extroverted love of food.

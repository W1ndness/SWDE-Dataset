Brown's Beach House - The Fairmont Orchid - Kohala Coast

writes...
  • Diner popularity and Big Island-inspired cutting edge cuisine takes center stage at Brown's Beach House known for its gorgeous ocean views, incomparable cuisine and service that is sophisticated Aloha. Innovative island-inspired cuisine is drawn from simple, pure flavors of locally grown produce using the diverse variety of fresh seafood from our island waters and the finest mainland meats, redefining the continuing evolution of Pacific Rim and most recently Hawaiian Regional Cuisine. Named after the last ali´i Francis Brown, we carry on the legacy to treat royalties and give you the opportunity to experience our value of ho´okipa (hospitality) here at The Fairmont Orchid.

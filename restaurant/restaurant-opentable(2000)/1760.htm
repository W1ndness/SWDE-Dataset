Flex Mussels

writes...
  • Flex Mussels is a mussel and oyster shack from Prince Edward Island transplanted in New York City featuring the freshest and highest quality product available in New York, specially selected and imported for us. Our menu features mussels in 23 different preparations, as well as fresh fish entrees, salads, lobster roll, and fried oysters.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Upper East Side
  • Cross Street: Between 3rd and Lexington Avenues
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.flexmusselsny.com
  • Email: info@flexmusselsny.com
  • Phone: (212) 717-7772
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 5:30pm - 11:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 11:30pm

  • Dress Code: Casual Dress

  • Parking: Street Parking

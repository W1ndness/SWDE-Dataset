Located in the heart of midtown, just 2 blocks from Grand Central, Domenico's offers Classic Italian Cuisine with attentive service in a warm & welcoming ambience. Group packages are available at multiple price levels in semi-private spaces and private rooms for groups of 12-150, designed to meet all budget goals. All menus can be modified to meet your particular needs. We cater to all social events...rehearsal dinners, weddings, bachelor/bachelorette parties, showers & birthday celebrations. For business meetings, our meeting rooms provide all of your audio/visual needs in a convenient and cost effective manner. Cocktail Parties, Fundraisers and Holiday Parties are also specialties of Domenico's, which has been serving a loyal clientele for over 37 years.

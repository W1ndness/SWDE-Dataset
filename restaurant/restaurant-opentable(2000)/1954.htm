  • Newly renovated our entire back facade is comprised of glass. We offer spectacular panoramic views of New York City, world-class decor and renowned cuisine. We also provide complimentary transportation from 23rd Street & The East River (Skyport Marina). Please call for Ferry Schedule.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Long Island City
  • Cross Street: 1 block West of Vernon Blvd.

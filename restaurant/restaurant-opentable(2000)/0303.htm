Chef Lance Gummere's constantly evolving Market Fresh menu offers New American Seasonal Fare with a variety of large and small plates to suit your fancy.With a Full bar and comprehensive, global wine list, you are sure to find something new and satisfying. Turn your everyday into a special occasion. We look forward to seeing you. Directions From 400s to 75/85s to 20 east. Get off at exit 59b. Turn right off exit. We are up on the right. From 20 west take exit 59a Follow sign to glenwood/memorial. It will loop you around the exit and you will take a right off the exit. We are located 1/4 mile up on the right. From the Airport: Take exit 75n to 20east. Take exit 59b, turn right off the exit and we are located up on the right.

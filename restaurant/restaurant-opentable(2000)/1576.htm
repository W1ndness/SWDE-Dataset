The Omni Parker House offers unique and historically charming function space and Parker's Bar for private parties and dining. Parker's Bar features its own bar, cocktail seating with fireplace and windows over looking King's Chapel and Old City Hall. Dining in Parker's Bar and our function space includes the same classic New England cuisine, beverage and service as Parker's Restaurant but in a more intimate atmosphere.

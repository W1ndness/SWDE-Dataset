Sugo - Clocktower Place

writes...
  • At Sugo we strive to create an exceptional dining experience at a great value with a sincere dedication to hospitality. We focus on Mediterranean cuisine that includes Italian and Greek influences. To accentuate the flavors of this unique fare we use estate bottled extra-virgin olive oils, aged balsamic vinegars, and imported cheeses. For Wine Lover's, we offer Wine Flights as well as fine stemware, decanting, and aerating for all of our wines. Recently awarded "Top 50 Italian Restaurants in America" by OpenTable.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Italian, Greek, Mediterranean
  • Neighborhood: Roswell
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.sugorestaurant.com
  • Email: nancy@sugorestaurant.com
  • Phone: (770) 641-9131
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday-Thursday: 5:00pm -10:00pm, Friday-Saturday 5:00pm -11:00pm, closed Sunday
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Federico Castellucci
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Private parking lot with valet parking available on the weekends.
  • Catering: We offer on-site as well as off-site catering. Please consult our events manager, Nancy Castellucci for more details. We can also email you our Special Events Menu. Simply send an email to nancy@sugorestaurant.com with your contact information and we will promptly send you an electronic version of our menu.
  • Private Party Facilities: We are happy to walk you through the process of booking your next special event. We have 4 private rooms available for booking.
  • Private Party Contact: Nancy Castellucci (770) 641-9131
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Monday Nights: Bring in any wine of your choice to enjoy with our signature dishes and we won't charge you a corking fee.

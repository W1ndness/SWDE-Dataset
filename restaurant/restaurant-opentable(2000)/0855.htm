Bardessono

writes...
  • We make food that is delicious and satisfying - prepared using local ingredients at their peak of ripeness. We partner with local farmers, ranchers, fish mongers, breadmakers, cheesemakers, and other artisan producers to prepare food that reflects our region and history. Our menus will change with the seasons and be inspired by our own travels and eating experiences. Effective November 30, 2009 until March 31, 2010, our breakfast service will change to a limited menu. Also, lunch service on Monday through Friday will be provided exclusively in the bar. Dinner and weekend brunch service continue to be served in the restaurant.

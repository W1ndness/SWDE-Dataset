Chantanee Thai Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • The Chantanee Thai Restaurant and Bar is a family owned and operated, critically acclaimed Thai restaurant. Described as North America's best Thai restaurant it combines the best in Thai cuisine with amazing drinks from its Naga bar, the only craft cocktail lounge in Bellevue. The Naga bar stays open later after the restaurant.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Thai
  • Neighborhood: Bellevue
  • Cross Street: NE 6TH ST
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.chantanee.com/
  • Email: info@chantanee.com
  • Phone: (425) 455-3226
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Sunday: 11:00am - 3:00pm
    Dinner: Sunday - Wednesday: 3:00pm - 10:00pm, Thursday - Saturday: 3:00pm - 11:00pm Happy Hour Daily 3pm - 6pm.

  • Public Transit: Bellevue Transit Center
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Parking is under the Key Center Building. Normal Key Center rates apply M-F until 5PM. Free after 5PM and all day on Sat. and Sun.
  • Private Party Facilities: The Naga Bar is available for buy outs. Naga is the only craft cocktail bar on the Eastside and has one numerous awards and press mentions for craft and obscure cocktails.

K'ya Bistro - Laguna Beach

writes...
  • Located inside the legendary Hotel La Casa del Camino, this local favorite offers light, contemporary European-Style cuisine, reminiscent of simple Bistro and mediterranean cafe dining. Small pour wine by the glass make for any evening of variety and fun with food and friends. Steaks, Seafood, Chicken, Pastas, Burgers, Desserts all small portions with every individual menu item under $10 or less. Come visit us during Happy Hour from 4:30-5:30pm Half off All Food. K'ya Bistro's cozy and casual ambience, make this the “…ultimate Laguna dining experience!” For amazing appetizers, cocktails and the best sunsets in Laguna Beach, with spellbinding ocean and coastal views, visit the Rooftop Lounge located at La Casa del Camino.

Deleece Grill Pub (on Clark Street)

writes...
  • Deleece Grill Pub is a neighborhood restaurant with the same casual sophistication as Deleece on Southport, but here the focus will be on grilled steaks and burgers along with a wide selection of mouth watering sides. The menu boasts eight different steaks including a 10 oz. peppercorn crusted hanger steak with Meadow Creek Grayson, a 14 oz. New York strip covered with grilled onions and Grafton Village 4 yr. white cheddar, and an 18 oz. ribeye with a garlic porcini ragout. The menu has plenty of choices for those not interested in red meat, including shrimp, fish, chicken, and mac and cheese five different ways in addition to a separate artesan cheese menu. The bar features a large beer list of primarily American craft brews in addition to a large American whisky list. In fact, the focus of this restaurant is “American made”.

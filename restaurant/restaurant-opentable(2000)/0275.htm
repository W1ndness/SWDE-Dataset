Max Lager's Wood-Fired Grill & Brewery

writes...
  • Max Lager's was one of the first restaurants to bring the fresh and natural wood-fired menu concept to Atlanta. The art of cooking with wood enhances the Max Lager's experience with bold flavors simply not found with alternative cooking methods. We feel that the investment made in the wood-fired grill is an investment that pays great dividends to our guests. All Max Lager's menu items are prepared daily from scratch. Wood-fired steaks, burgers, seafood, ribs, and pizza are Max Lager's signature items. An inspired selection of entrée salads and pastas add variety to the American menu.

Centrally located in downtown Atlanta, Max Lager's is within easy walking distance to major hotels, the Georgia World Congress Center, the Georgia Dome, Philips Arena, and the Civic Center. We can accommodate seated events up to 250 people, and receptions of 700+ people on an flow basis. We have a number of different spaces offering both private and semi-private options, including two outside patios and two full bars. Complete restaurant buy outs are available, and specific areas may be reserved. If you would like something special for your event, we can do it for you - From a Brews & Blues Casino Night to a Retirement Farewell. Just let us know what you would like, and we will take care of all the details with professionalism and panache.

Celadon - Napa

writes...
  • Enjoy Celadon's award-winning 'Global Comfort Food' in our beautiful dining room or on the lovely courtyard. The seasonally influenced menu features flavors from the Mediterranean, Asia, and the Americas. We are located in the heart of downtown Napa in the Historic Napa Mill.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: International, Comfort Food
  • Neighborhood: Napa
  • Cross Street: Fifth St.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.celadonnapa.com
  • Phone: (707) 254-9690
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch served Monday - Friday 11:30am until 2:30pm Afternoon menu served Monday - Friday 2:30-5:00pm Dinner served nightly from 5:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Marcos Uribe
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Fireplace, Full Bar, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Free parking in a private lot shared with Historic Napa Mill businesses along with a free public parking garage across the street.
  • Private Party Facilities: Accommodations available for up to 30 guests in the heated outdoor patio. Patio buyouts available for groups of 30-60 guests - call for pricing. Special Menus for more than 14 people; see our website for group dining menus. Room charge may apply.
  • Private Party Contact: Angela Eddleman (707) 254-9690

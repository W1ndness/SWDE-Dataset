Savory Restaurant

writes...
  • Piquant. Pleasing to the taste and smell. These words define Savory, Chef Paul Shoemaker’s new restaurant at Malibu’s beautiful Point Dume Village Shopping Center. Savory is a unique new experience for the beach community, with a seasonally-driven, locally-sourced menu of sustainable fare that brings new meaning to the term “organic.” Fresh vegetables in local farmers’ markets are served daily from farm to table. Savory features Shoemaker’s playful cooking style – like an artist with his canvas and paints, his cuisine captures the rich, natural flavor of an entrée and unites it with unique textures and colors to create a dining experience that touches on many senses.

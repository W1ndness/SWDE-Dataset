Marché Moderne

writes...
  • Driven by a passion for market-inspired ingredients and classic French cooking techniques, Florent and Amelia Marneau have created a truly modern French bistro in the heart of Orange County. For decades, the couple worked separately in some of the best kitchens in France and Orange County. With Marché Moderne, they have followed this natural evolution by combining their culinary careers as independent restaurateurs. The contemporary French Bistro which has been critically acclaimed since its opening in spring of 2007, is located at The Penthouse at South Coast Plaza, level three, adjacent to Nordstrom.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: French
  • Neighborhood: Costa Mesa
  • Cross Street: Bristol and Town Center
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.marchemoderne.net
  • Email: marchemoderne@sbcglobal.net
  • Phone: (714) 434-7900
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch-Everyday 11:30am-2:30pm Dinner-Monday-Saturday 5:30pm-9:30pm Sunday 5:30pm-8:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Florent Marneau
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, Wine
  • Parking: Public Lot
  • Parking Details: Valet is available on first level Parking structure.
  • Private Party Facilities: The restauarant happily hosts a large variety of events from business dinners to pharmaceutical programs to those important personal celebrations such as bridal and baby showers, birthdays, rehearsal dinners and wedding receptions. Most private parties are held in the restaurant's enclosed patio area, which has an outside entrance, retractable awnings and can accommodate up to 75 guests seated and 85 guests standing. The restaurant also has access to a marbled terrace. This public space can be transformed into a lively reception space for cocktails and hand-passed hors d'oeuvres before your guests step into the patio or the main dining room. The terrace, patio and main dining room are also available on a "restaurant buyout" basis for a maximum of 130 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Alyssa McDiarmid (714) 434-7900

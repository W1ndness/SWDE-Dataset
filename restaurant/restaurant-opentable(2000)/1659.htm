Antonia's By The Park

writes...
  • Owner/Chef Antonia Vene brings her international talents to this local, favorite restaurant in North Bergen on Palisade Avenue. She offers fine Italian cuisine in an elegant surrounding. Look at our weekly specials and challenge your taste buds with such items as venison, rabbit, duck or quail. Antonia's By The Park specializes in parties for any occasion. Contact the special events manager to make your event a successful one. Open seven days a week, Monday through Saturday for lunch and dinner, and Sunday for brunch and dinner. Come in and enjoy live music every Friday and Saturday. Explore all that this lovely restaurant has to offer. Take a few minutes to browse our website and contact us for any comment or suggestion. Also inquire for information about our special events calendar that includes salsa, oldies, jazz, etc.

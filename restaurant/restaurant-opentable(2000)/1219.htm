Toscanini

writes...
  • Located in Beaver Creek Village next to the ice rink, Toscanini offers Italian cuisine with a casual upscale dining experience. Watch Chef Michael Izbicki prepare individual pizzas, house-made pastas, and fresh seafood in the open kitchen. Savor the aroma and flavor of Italian wines specifically chosen for their hearty, robust qualities. A perfect stop for cocktails or dinner before heading down the escalators to the Vilar Center for the Arts.

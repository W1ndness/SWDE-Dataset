Bluefin

writes...
  • In Peabody Place, Bluefin is especially fun late night. The hip lounge setting plus a billiards room create excitement, while patrons sit and gaze at the sleek sushi bar. An open kitchen and large booths and sofas in the dining room add to the energy. Bluefin's fusion menu of American, European, and Asian cuisines offers everything from sushi to pizza. Weekends, hear some of Memphis' best deejays.

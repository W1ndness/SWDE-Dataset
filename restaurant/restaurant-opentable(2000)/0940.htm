Latitude

writes...
  • Located in Sonoma County's most central and beautiful waterfront location, Latitude features New-American cuisine inspired by the agricultural bounty of Sonoma County with Island flair. The wine list also reflects regional specialties as well as old gems. Latitude boasts specious, light and airy dining rooms as well as the areas largest patios and decks for dining al fresco. The glowing water panel bar in the Cafe features a full selection of signature cocktails, local beers and comprehensive wine by the glass selection with a focus on Sonoma and Napa counties. Ports and dessert wines are also available. Take a break and enjoy Latitude in the Wine Country.

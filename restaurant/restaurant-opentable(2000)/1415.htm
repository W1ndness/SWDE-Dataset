Plum Tree Inn

writes...
  • Plum Tree Inn is the culmination of over 30 consecutive years of culinary experience. Guests enjoy a blend of elegant and modern dining with classic Chinese dishes. Plum Tree Inn uses only the freshest, highest quality local ingredients combined with traditional Chinese seasonings creating the most memorable and delicious Mandarin and Szechwan dishes. The Peking Duck (which requires at least an hour of pre-ordering) and Honey Walnut Shrimp are both award winning dishes and top favorites with customers.

Cannons Seafood Grill

writes...
  • Cannons Seafood Grill offers magnificent views of the Pacific from our hillside terrace overlooking Dana Point Harbor, Cannons Seafood Grill has been delighting our customers for over thirty years. We are open for dinner seven days a week, Saturday and Sunday for lunch, and a spectacular Sunday champagne brunch, and is the perfect setting for romantic weddings and elegant banquets. Cannons unique and excellent food, friendly staff, and unmatched setting will make your visit a memorable one... Come for the food, stay for the view.

Guests will find plenty of delicious creative takes on traditional dishes from Chef de Cuisine David Connolly. Serving breakfast, lunch and dinner, Chef Connolly utilizes as many local and regional ingredients as possible. For those on the run, STATE AND LAKE’s breakfast menu offers quick dishes with delicious coffee, including an assortment of seasonal fruit parfaits, house made muffins, croissants and more, as well as indulgent breakfast dishes. Lunch and dinner menus range from light, small plates – ideal for those seeking dishes to share while seated at the 17–seat bar – to heartier entrees. Beverage Manager Myron Markewycz, one of Chicago’ leading beverage experts, offers an impressive selection of domestic, artisanal beers, many from the Midwest, including 16 on tap, to pair with Chef Connolly fare. An all–American wine list and a succinct menu of classic cocktails with a twist are also available.

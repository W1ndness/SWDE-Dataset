LB Steak

writes...
  • LB Steak, a modern American Steakhouse with a French twist, is led by two culinary titans: James Beard nominee and owner of San Francisco’s renowned La Folie, Roland Passot, and fellow French Master Chef, Joel Guillon. In addition to LB Steak, Passot and Guillon oversee the three Left Bank Brasseries throughout the Bay Area. Chef de Cuisine Chris Joslyn executes LB Steak signature dishes such as the 20-oz. Porterhouse with Traditional Béarnaise Sauce and the Rossini Foie Gras Burger, as well as a wide selection of fresh seafood, chops, salads and side dishes. The wine list features an array of domestic and imported wines, as well as an extensive selection of wines by the glass, offering diners options to fit any taste or budget. We look forward to serving you soon!

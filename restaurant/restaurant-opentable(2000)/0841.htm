A Divine Affair

writes...
  • A Divine Affair offers a casual, warm and inviting setting for lunch and dinner in the heart of downtown Healdsburg. Enjoy their seasonally focused menu and a glass of wine on their patio or dine at the bar. A Divine Affair also uses sustainable business practices supporting local growers and artists. With an Artisan wine selection and space for private events, A Divine Affair's passion in the kitchen is here to sustain you.

Twelve Restaurant

writes...
  • Twelve Restaurant offers Modern American food in a relaxed, and inviting setting. As the name implies, the menu changes each month, or "twelve" times a year. Please join us every month for a new experience.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: American
  • Neighborhood: Downtown / LoDo
  • Cross Street: Park Avenue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.twelverestaurant.com
  • Email: info@twelverestaurant.com
  • Phone: (303) 293-0287
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Tuesday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Jeff Osaka
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Farm to Table, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: There is a parking lot just north of the building for $3.00 on weekdays and $5.00 on weekends, with special rates during Colorado Rockies home games. There is also street parking for $.25 an hour.

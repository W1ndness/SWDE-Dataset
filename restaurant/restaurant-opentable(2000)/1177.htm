Craftwood Inn

writes...
  • The Craftwood Inn, located in historic Manitou Springs, stands at the base of our remarkable Pikes Peak. The location offers breath taking views of the Peak, beautifully landscaped gardens, and intimate fireplace and patio dining. The menu consists of a wide variety of Colorado Wild Game including Elk, Antelope, Venison, Wild Boar, Ostrich, Pheasant, Steaks, Seafood, and Vegetarian dishes. Whether you are looking for a close intimate dinner, a warm friendly atmosphere or trying to decide on a location for your special occasion, give us a call - you won't be disappointed.

Where better to take that important person for their special day? whether celebrating a birthday, anniversary, graduation, promotion or just an intimate night out, the Craftwood Inn's staff will make the evening one to remember. Entertaining clients has never been more enjoyable. Give your guests the taste of Colorado. They will marvel at the views of Pike's Peak and surrounding mountains from our hill top vantage point. Private dining rooms are available for groups of ten or more.

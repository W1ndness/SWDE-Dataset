SOHO - Atlanta

writes...
  • An American Bistro located in historic Vinings, SOHO serves contemporary fare inspired by many cuisines. Swift, attentive waiters, an award-winning wine bar and open kitchen all contribute to the high-energy buzz of SOHO. An enclosed cozy back porch overlooks The Vinings Jubilee Courtyard and a quaint patio provides guests a charming place to dine al fresco. Private dining areas are available for private and corporate events.

Soho’s catering department has collectively over 40 years of experience and can handle a variety of social events such as weddings, rehearsal dinners, corporate functions and group gatherings of groups of 10 to 150. With our eclectic menu offerings, we can offer many different types of cuisines for your next function. Please contact us about your next party for availability, menus, pricing and references. If you would like to rent the entire restaurant, please call for availability. For your review, our menu options for seated lunch or dinner functions over 20 guests are available for your review. Soho offers over 100 wine selections by the glass, and all are available for your event. Our bar manager has hand selected wines priced in three tiers for your convenience. If you would like to serve a buffet menu, we have menus available for lunch, dinner, and cocktail parties. Buffet menus will be priced according to selections and number of guests.

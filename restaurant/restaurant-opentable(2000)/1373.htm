Il Tiramisu Ristorante & Bar

writes...
  • Il Tiramisù offers the finest in Northern Italian Continental Cuisine, and is conveniently located in Sherman Oaks. We prepare a wide selection of fresh fish, and our breads, pastas, and desserts are all homemade, including our own special rendition of the classic Tiramisu dessert. Specialties include: wonderful Osso Buco with homemade gnocchi; Cappallaci – a pasta crepe filled with ricotta cheese and spinach in a light pink sauce; fresh salmon with orange, mango, and cognac; Chilean Sea Bass with lemon zest, basil, and a splash of vodka; New York Steak Sinatra; and many, many more.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Italian, Seafood
  • Neighborhood: Sherman Oaks
  • Cross Street: Woodman Ave.
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.iltiramisu.com
  • Email: comments@il-tiramisu.com
  • Phone: (818) 986-2640
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Tuesday - Saturday: 11:30am - 2:30pm
    Dinner: Tuesday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Full Bar, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout
  • Parking: Valet
  • Private Party Facilities: Il Tiramisù has 2 private rooms available for bridal showers, baby showers, rehearsal dinners, wedding receptions, office parties, & surprise birthday parties.
  • Private Party Contact: Peter (818) 986-2640

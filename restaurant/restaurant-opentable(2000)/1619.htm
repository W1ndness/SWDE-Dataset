White Barn Inn

writes...
  • Fine American cuisine served in a fabulously converted barn. The White Barn Inn Restaurant is a AAA Five Diamond, Mobile 5 Star and Relais Gourmand property with four course Prix Fix Menu at $98 per person, and subject to availability, a nine course Prix Fixe menu at $140 per person, both featuring the finest fresh local ingredients.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: American, European
  • Neighborhood: Kennebunkport
  • Cross Street: Route 9
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.Whitebarninn.com
  • Email: restaurant@whitebarninn.com
  • Phone: (207) 967-2321
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 6:00pm -9:30pm, Friday - Sunday: 5:30pm -9:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Jonathan Cartwright
  • Dress Code: Jacket Required
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Private Room, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complementary Valet and self parking in private lot.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private parties are offered in the White Barn Inn Wine Cellar. The Cellar seats 10-15 guests. Offered year round, seating at 7:30pm.
  • Private Party Contact: Kristen Caouette (207) 967-2321
  • Entertainment: Talented Jazz Pianist entertains evenings.

Bacchus

writes...
  • We specialize in classic and contemporary French bistro fare such as escargots, onion soup, steak frites, moules frites, Cajun-spiced tuna, duck spring rolls, braised lamb shank, braised short ribs, and salads. Known for our comprehensive French wine list, you can now join the Bacchus Wine Club and sign up for tasting events, or simply organize your own wine tasting event. Owner and wine expert Bruno Laclide will conduct and share with you his knowledge of the various French wine regions.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: French
  • Neighborhood: Boerum Hill
  • Cross Street: Between Bond & Nevins Streets
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://bacchusbistro.com
  • Email: info@bacchusbistro.com
  • Phone: (718) 852-1572

    Brunch: Saturday - Sunday: 10:30am - 4:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Jean-Marc Hardy
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Counter Seating, Dancing, Entertainment, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wine, Wine Bar
  • Public Transit: A, C & G trains to Hoyt-Schermerhorn, the B, Q, 2, 3, 4 & 5 trains to Atlantic Avenue, and F to Bergen at Smith.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Catering: Catering is available.
  • Private Party Facilities: Your private event can also be held at Bacchus. Our garden is a perfect setting for weddings. We'll close the entire restaurant if you so desire. Bacchus is a great place to celebrate your anniversary, birthday, Bar Mitzvah, Christening, or any number of special occasions.

Chicago Curry House (Indian and Nepalese Restaurant)

  • Fine dining Indian and Nepalese restaurant with very good wine menu. Full service bar featuring wine from California, Chile, Italy, Germany, Australia and India. Indian beers and others premium beers are also available. Very large Indian menu with chicken, lamb, vegetables, seafood, biryani, naan paratha and roti. Very healthy tandoori chicken, tandoori lamb, tandoori shrimp, and tandoori vegetables are available. The restaurant has a very quiet dining atmosphere, perfect for small groups or for meeting up with friends! The restaurant also has a large selection for vegetarians. Chicago Curry House uses Halal meat.

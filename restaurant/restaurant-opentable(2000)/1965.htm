Coast Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • Canary Hotel in Downtown Santa Barbara is home to Coast Restaurant and Bar, a fun and fashionable addition to the Santa Barbara dining scene. The expansive restaurant located just off the lobby offers a new vision for dining in Santa Barbara, coupling a warm and relaxed interior that can be best described as earthy, organic modernism with an accessible menu that goes for simple preparations to highlight the quality of superior ingredients. Coast Santa Barbara is the second venue dubbed Coast, after the original in Santa Monica at sister hotel Shutters on the Beach. The menu’s focus on local market fresh ingredients is evident from the first read. Chef Brian Parks' reasonably-priced menu is rich with comfort fare updated with clean, uncomplicated preparations, and geared toward the way people prefer to dine.
    Come visit the bar for our daily Happy Hour!

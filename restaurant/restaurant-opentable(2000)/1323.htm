Catalina Kitchen at Terranea Resort

writes...
  • Experience a culinary adventure throughout the day at Catalina Kitchen where it’s all about good food, at any time with kids, friends or colleagues. Dine indoors or out on the patio with its wide open space and views of the ocean and the adjacent Resort pool. Enjoy a full-service breakfast, occasionally in conjunction with hot and cold buffet offerings. An afternoon menu features flatbreads, panini sandwiches and Mediterranean salads. Throughout the afternoon, enjoy light meals, cocktails and appetizers. In the evening the menu broadens into California market/ Mediterranean cuisine with elements of Spanish, French Riviera, Greek and Southern Italian flavors. A wood-burning oven offers a number of specialty pizzas at dinner. Catalina Kitchen features an ala carte Brunch menu from 12pm to 3pm on Saturday and Sunday.

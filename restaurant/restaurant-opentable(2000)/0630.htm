The Claim Company

writes...
  • Home of nationally famous Mother-lode gourmet hamburger. "The best burger on the North Shore." The mother-lode is a build you own gourmet 10oz Prime burger and We have one of the largest fresh salad bars in the North Shore. The Salad Saloon has over 70 items to choose from. These two items and more in a southwestern decor for families and friends to enjoy in this casual and comfortable atmosphere. Please note: The Claim Company accepts priority seating, not reservations. Booking through OpenTable will ensure you are seated before other walk-in guests. Even with Dinner reservations, there may be a wait of 15-20 minutes for parties of up to 4, or up to 30 minutes for parties of 7 and larger. Lunch Proirity seating is no more than 5 to 10 minutes.

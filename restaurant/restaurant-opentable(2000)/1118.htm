Porterhouse Steak and Seafood

writes...
  • Porterhouse Steak and Seafood offers casual-fine dining with a Supper Club atmosphere. The menu consists of premium steaks aged a minimum of twenty eight days, fresh fish & seafood. All entrees are served with a choice of House, Caesar or Spinach salad, bread and choice of Potato or Vegetable.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Steak, Seafood, American
  • Neighborhood: Little Canada
  • Cross Street: Highway 35E
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.porterhousesteakandseafood.com
  • Email: manager@porterhousesteakandseafood.com
  • Phone: (651) 483-9248
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Sunday: 5:00pm - 9:00pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Ward Wollerman
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Private Lot
  • Parking Details: Well lit private lot surrounds building.
  • Catering: Full service catering for any event. Please contact Edward Wollerman (General Manager).

Petrossian Paris Boutique & Restaurant

writes...
  • We are a casually elegant restaurant and boutique just steps from the shops on Robertson Blvd and within walking distance to the Beverly Center and the Sofitel. We serve a wide selection of French inspired American dishes as well as showcasing our world renowned selection of caviars, smoked salmon and other culinary delicacies. We offer a casual menu with salads and sandwiches for lunch and more refined dishes for dinner. Our weekend brunch is quite popular, especially on our sidewalk patio. Our boutique is always open with our full menu available for take out, or shop for French culinary treats from baked goods and smoked salmon to foie gras and caviar!

Jonathan's Restaurant

writes...
  • The oldest seafood restaurant in downtown Salem, Oregon. A Salem icon since 1979, Jonathan's has continually brought you the freshest seafood, certified Angus steaks and great Cajun food. Well known for our "wimpy" burgers, seafood gumbo, jambalaya, razor clams, oyster shooters, wilted spinach salads and Louisiana steaks to name a few.

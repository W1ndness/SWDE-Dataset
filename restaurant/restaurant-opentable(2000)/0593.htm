Ming Court

writes...
  • Ming Court has been serving the area’s finest Oriental cuisine for over 20 years. The extensive menu features authentic Dim Sum, fine Sushi, and a vast selection of innovative Wok & Char-Grill dishes, from the finest seafood and steaks, to the most popular Chinese dishes and even traditional family style cuisine. Ming Court is located within walking distance to the Orange County Convention Center and is surrounded by a beautiful Koi pond and gardens. The recipient of over twenty dining awards, the restaurant also has a variety of private dining rooms and live Oriental music is played nightly. For large events, Ming Court can accommodate up to 400 guests for a banquet and up to 600 guests for a reception. Ming Court is mostly about really great food at really great prices, or as the locals like to say, it’s the reason chopsticks were invented!

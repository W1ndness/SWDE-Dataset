Ristorante Panorama

writes...
  • Ristorante Panorama features authentic contemporary Italian cuisine in a dramatic setting of hand painted murals, marble floors & torch lighting. Housing the largest wine preservation "Cruvinet" system in the world, the wine bar offers over 120 wines by the glass & in tasting flights daily & has been awarded Wine Spectator's "Best of Award of Excellence," Philadelphia Magazine's "Best of Philly" & Sante' Magazine's "Wine Hospitality Award For Fine Dining." at Panorama, every day is an opportunity for an individualized wine tasting to accompany homemade pastas, tender veal & seafood specialties. Panorama is located in the heart of Old City Philadelphia in Luca Sena's charming luxury boutique hotel, The Penn's View Hotel at Front & Market Streets.

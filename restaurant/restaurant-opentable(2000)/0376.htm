Scarpetta - Fontainebleau Miami Beach

writes...
  • RATED FOUR STARS BY THE MIAMI HERALD "I have long been asked where to find truly phenomenal Italian food in Miami -- an authentic, sophisticated, soulful eatery where the most basic ingredients achieve transcendence. This is it." Victoria Pesce Elliott, Food Critic, Miami Herald.- Scott Conant, formerly of L’Impero and Alto, is at the helm of Scarpetta™, Fontainebleau Miami Beach’s Italian restaurant and an outpost of his recently opened restaurant of the same name in New York City. The restaurant features ocean and poolside views, a wraparound veranda as well as casual dining areas and a cocktail lounge. Scarpetta regional menu comprises clean, crisp flavors prepared in layers to reflect the quality of locally sourced ingredients. Known for coaxing the most sublime flavors out of the simplest ingredients, Conant relies on Florida’s bounty of farm-fresh and organic produce to craft menus of surprising complexity

Agave - Las Vegas

writes...
  • Agave redefines the conventional Mexican dining experience by combining the radiant flavors and handcrafted care of traditional Mexican cuisine with a modern twist. Executive Chefs Matthew Silverman and Juan Saldana collaborated to create an authentic Mexican menu with a contemporary polish all presented in a traditional Mexican setting. The restaurant's dynamic architecture is reminiscent of a palapa; a structure found in the tropical resorts of Mexico, complete with a thatched roof and vibrant colors. Agave takes it name from the plant used to make tequila in the region of Tequila, Mexico. The restaurant features a bar which boasts over 80 fine Agave tequilas and encourages guests to sample the distinct flavors of each.

Agave is NOT your typical Mexican restaurant. For those looking for a vibrant and architecturally stimulating environment that takes Mexican cuisine to a new level, look no further. Known to Las Vegas locals and tourists alike for their award winning margaritas, colorful patio, and Mexican cuisine that goes well beyond “burritos smothered in cheese.” Agave provides an event space with amenities capable of creating an exciting and memorable experience. Designed to accommodate anything from intimate gatherings to large receptions, Agave can be sectioned to create a unique space suitable for any event. From a multi-level dining room anchored by a central bar to a large and inviting patio, indoor/outdoor access is a main attraction of Agave’s event space. Agave even has an authentic “street taco cart” that can be used on the patio or offsite to further enhance any gathering. With the taco cart, guests can create their own unique tacos with a variety of salsas and authentic garnishes.

Cafe Moustache

writes...
  • Neighborhood French Bistro serving lunch, dinner & Sunday brunch.

    French Country Comfort food comes to Houston from owners Elizabeth Abraham & Manfred Jachmich [formerly of SoVino Bistro & Wine Bar]. Join us and let us help you experience the espirit of Café Moustache: life is at its fullest when experienced around a table brimming with delicious food, good wine, intriguing conversation, family and friends.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: French
  • Neighborhood: Midtown / Montrose
  • Cross Street: Whitney (between Montrose & Taft)
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://cafemoustache.com
  • Email: elizabeth@sovinowines.com
  • Phone: (713) 524-1000
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Friday: 11:00am - 2:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Thursday: 5:00pm - 10:00pm, Friday - Saturday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm

    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Manfred Jachmich
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Beer, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Area, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Complimentary valet parking and street parking available at dinner and brunch. Lunch is free parking in our parking lot. We can arrange complimentary valet for your lunch events upon request.
  • Private Party Facilities: Private wine room elegantly designed and decorated, seats 15 people. We also have an area that we reserve for cocktail parties which accommodates 30-75 people. We also allow booking of the entire restaurant for parties of 70-200 people.

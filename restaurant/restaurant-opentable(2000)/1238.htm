Caffe Boa on Mill

writes...
  • An original, casual, Euro-style bistro/wine bar! Located in the registered historic "Casa Loma" building in the heart of old town Tempe's Mill Avenue District. The ambiance includes hardwood flooring, original brick walls, and two large "European Garden" terraces for people watching. Wine Spectator has awarded Caffe Boa an Award of Excellence for the wine list which features over 450 bin selections, many wine flights, and over 50 by the glass choices. Outstanding and award winning cuisine from Italy, Croatia, and all around the Mediterranean has made Caffe Boa a Valley of the Sun favorite for over 15 years. Organic fare and is the focus with over 85% of the menu made from certified organic ingredients. Favorites include Agnolotti Boa (Raviolis filled with Portabella mushrooms), Cajun accented Voodoo Penne, and our fresh daily Fish specials. Come see why Caffe Boa is a Valley tradition!

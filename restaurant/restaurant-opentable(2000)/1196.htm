Laudisio Restaurant

writes...
  • Laudisio Italian Restaurant is a Boulder tradition! Family owned and operated for 20 years , Laudisio uses the freshest local ingredients available, to best utilize and reflect the beauty of the Boulder County gardens, waters and farms. Our chefs create new, bolder tastes for today's palate, with innovative and exciting daily lunch and dinner features, including gluten-free menus! The award winning Laudisio cellar offers a wide variety of wines to accompany any choice from the menu. Smooth, upscale and contemporary, but still warm and comfortable, the Laudisio Dining Room makes this Boulder restaurant the ideal place for a great daytime business lunch, intimate, sophisticated evening meal, or just a casual affair with friends! Come try our Award Winning Wine Lists

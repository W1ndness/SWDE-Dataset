Brasserie 214 at The Inn at New Hyde Park

writes...
  • Brasserie 214 is a unique European style restaurant found within the Inn at New Hyde Park. It features cuisines from Belgium, France, Tuscany, Germany, Holland, and Scandinavia. The Brasserie's famous oval bar offers live music on weekends and has cozy booths where customers can enjoy the extensive European beer selection.

  • Cuisine: European
  • Neighborhood: New Hyde Park
  • Cross Street: South 2nd & South 3rd Streets
  • Price: $30 and under

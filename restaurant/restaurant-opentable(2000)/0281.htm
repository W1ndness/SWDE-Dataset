Nikolai's Roof

writes...
  • Nikolai’s Roof is a AAA Four Diamond restaurant and the epitome of fine dining at its best. Delight in the option of a four, six or eight-course tasting menu with wine pairings or an innovative ala carte menu. Enjoy intense flavors and tastes, fresh ingredients, and an elegant ambiance with a view overlooking Atlanta's starlit skyline.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: French, Fusion / Eclectic
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Cross Street: Harris Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.nikolaisroof.com
  • Email: nikolaisroof@hilton.com
  • Phone: (404) 221-6362
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Tuesday - Saturday: 5:30pm - 10:30pm.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Carte Blanche, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Olivier De Busschere
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Chef's Table, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Area, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (bar only), View, Wine
  • Public Transit: MARTA - Peachtree Station
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Private parking garage located under the hotel which offers valet or self park. Validated parking in hotel garage.
  • Private Party Facilities: Nikolai's Roof can accommodate private parties of 16 to 40 guests.
  • Private Party Contact: Daniel Rudiger (404) 221-6362
  • Entertainment: The Point of View lounge adjacent to Nikolai’s Roof offers classic cocktails, a cozy ambiance and a small plate menu. The only thing smoother than dinner and drinks at Nikolais’ is the cozy embrace of the Point of View Lounge – where the mood is always comfortably stylish and undeniably cool. Open 5 p.m. until Midnight Tuesday through Saturday.

22 Bowens Wine Bar and Grille

writes...
  • Welcome to 22 Bowen's Wine Bar and Grille, located on the waterfront in the heart of historic downtown Newport, Rhode Island. Serving prime beef, New England's finest seafood, 35 wines by the glass and over 650 labels on our wine list, we invite you to visit us and enjoy "a little bit of tenderness" at one of Newport's best.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Steakhouse
  • Neighborhood: Newport
  • Cross Street: America
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.22bowens.com

  • Parking: No
  • Private Party Facilities: Welcome to 22 Bowen’s Wine Bar and Grille, one of Newport’s finest waterfront restaurants, located in the heart of historic downtown Newport.

RH Restaurant & Bar

writes...
  • The French Southwest Perigord region is famed for its beautiful farm products such as mushrooms and truffles, walnuts, duck and foie gras, and Bayonne ham. Continuing to work with the local farmers, Michelin Star-rated Chef Sebastien Archambault is excited to bring authentic Southwest French cuisine to the Sunset Strip for the first time! Sebastien has created a unique menu, which features the freshest seasonal ingredients, in one of LA’s most open kitchens. From one of the communal or private tables, one can follow the progress of their dishes literally from cooler and pantry to table. With a cocktail list that not only includes the classics, RH has created its own authentic cocktails that keep in tune with the philosophy of the restaurant – the freshest, seasonal ingredients available.

RH located at Andaz West Hollywood, a sleek re-invention of the former Hyatt on Sunset Strip, RH is a nod to the hotel’s storied past and stands for its ‘70s nickname “Riot House”. While this moniker may conjure up images of its star-studded heritage – an era when the hotel was known as a hangout for hard-partying rockers – today the new hotel and restaurant are all about simple elegance. In that spirit, RH embodies conviviality in a refreshingly relaxed environment. Transcend the ordinary restaurant experience with one of LA’s most exciting culinary settings. With views into RH’s open kitchen, watch as the choreography takes place while RH’s culinary team transforms fresh seasonal ingredients into finished dishes. Since Executive Chef Sebastien Archambault of RH at Andaz West Hollywood first opened the restaurant in January 2009, there’s been great buzz about the simple, yet deliciously refined food, the vibrant space and the warm, friendly service at this neighborhood destination.

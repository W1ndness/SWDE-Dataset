  • Sip"s inviting dining interior features comfortable leather chairs, inviting, personal living rooms, and a 23 seat wine bar designed to create an ambiance reminiscent of wineries found along the California coastline. Detailed, classic accents, fire light and down tempo music combine with the perfect glass of wine for a soul warming experience. Outdoor diners enjoy a patio surrounded by a regal stone fireplace for a cozy feeling year-round.

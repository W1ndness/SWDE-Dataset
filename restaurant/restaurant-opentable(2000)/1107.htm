JD Hoyt's

writes...
  • J.D. Hoyt's is a roadhouse, located in the booming warehouse district of old Minneapolis. A cozy spot with an extensive menu, full bar and a wine list of extraordinary value. The featured entrees are prepared on their custom designed charcoal grill. This giant grill sears your order to juicy perfection. JD Hoyt's is proud to serve large portions of food you'll crave. The favorite dining spot of athletic organization, professional and collegiate, male and female. Open seven nights a week, with Sunday Brunch 10a.m.-2p.m. and now open Saturday mornings 10a.m.-2p.m. & All Twins day games 10a.m.-2p.m. Locally owned, Nationally known. Best staff in the city. Check out the "New" patio, just added for your enjoyment. Complimentary Valet parking Tuesday - Saturday nights(Limited Valet parking on Event Days)

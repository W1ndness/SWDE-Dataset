Nougatine at Jean Georges

writes...
  • Nougatine offers the signature dishes of Chef Vongerichten in a more casual but lively setting.
    Nougatine has a beautiful room overlooking Central Park and an open kitchen.
    The bar is located in Nougatine, which provides a lively atmosphere to the room.
    Nougatine is located adjacent to the Jean Georges four star formal dining room.
    We offer a prix-fixe lunch for $26.00 Monday-Saturday from 12pm-3pm. Pre-theater and post theater $38 prix-fixe menus are available Sunday-Friday evenings from 5:30pm-6pm, and 10-11pm.
    Our terrace is now open for outdoor dining. (Weather permitting.)

  • Farm 2 Bistro - A Comfy home away from home Green Bistro Farm 2 Bistro Supports a Green Philosophy Through its Commitment to Local Vendors This restaurant features local produce, naturally raised meats, sustainable seafood and eco-friendly design in a warm and welcoming environment. (Rockaway & Nutley New Jersey) - Farm 2 Bistro, located at 177 Franklin Ave in Nutley NJ opened in August 2008 and the Second Farm 2 Bistro in Rockaway, NJ opened 02/01/2010 located at 100 Hibernia Ave, offers guests and locals a fresh, exciting downtown NYC dining experience with local country fresh vittles. With Michael K Madigan Jr. as the Executive Chef and Owner-founder of Farm 2 Bistro restaurants focuses on serving the freshest locally sourced products whenever possible as well as naturally raised meat and sustainable caught seafood. Breakfast lunch and dinner are served up fresh and innovatively and gently priced for the quality delivered

Bonsoirée

writes...
  • Bonsoirée is an intimate 26 seat Fine Dining BYOB featuring French/Japanese Contemporary cuisine owned by Chef Shin Thompson. Ask about the beautiful outdoor patio that seats up to 20 people (weather permitting). Bonsoirée features a five, seven, and 13 course tasting menu.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary French, Contemporary American, Japanese
  • Neighborhood: Logan Square
  • Cross Street: Fairfield
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.bon-soiree.com
  • Email: shin@bon-soiree.com
  • Phone: (773) 486-7511
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Tuesday - Saturday 5pm-10pm, Sunday 5pm-9pm Note: Saturday, Underground invitation only dinners
    Sunday, No menus- Exclusive menu based on fresh seasonal produce
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Shin Thompson
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: No
  • Additional Details: BYO Liquor, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies)
  • Public Transit: Western Blue Line - Walk three blocks west on Armitage. California Blue Line - Walk three blocks south on California, turn left on Armitage, walk 1/2 block.
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: Street parking easy.
  • Catering: Bonsoirée specializes in full service custom catering for parties of 20-250 people. Anything from cocktail parties to weddings, the team of Chef's at Bonsoirée will design a menu to fit your needs.
  • Private Party Contact: Shin Thompson (773) 486-7511

El Gran Inka - Aventura

  • Excellent taste, amazing service and the freshest seafood -- El Gran Inka will not only give you the a taste of the traditional foods of Peru but it will allow you to experience a unique gastronomic-cultural experience. All Ceviche and other signature dishes like clams, shrimp and stone crabs are made to order from our Raw and Cerviche Bars. After one visit to our restaurant you will have a new appreciation for Peru and want to visit our other locations in Costa Rica, Guatemala, and El Salvador.

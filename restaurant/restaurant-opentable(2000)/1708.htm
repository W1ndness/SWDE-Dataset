The Cafe Carlyle

writes...
  • The Cafe is open from Monday through Saturday. Decorated with Vertes Murals, this legendary cabaret is an intimate venue in which to enjoy superb French culinary creations. All reservations are for dinner and a show.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: French, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Upper East Side
  • Cross Street: Madison and 76th Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $50 and over
  • Website: http://www.thecarlyle.com
  • Phone: (212) 744-1600
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Monday - Saturday: 6:.0 p.m. - 11:00 p.m.
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: James Sakatos
  • Dress Code: Jacket Required
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Beer, Cafe, Entertainment, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Wine

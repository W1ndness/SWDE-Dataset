ZEN on Ten

writes...
  • Located in Midtown, Atlanta on Northside Drive and Tenth Street, Zen on Ten Asian Bistro and Sushi Bar offers the finest Thai, Chinese, Asian Fusion and Japanese Cuisine in the city. Enjoy the warm, cozy, and inviting atmosphere of our dining room or relax and meet a new friend at our sleek and elegant bar. We're quickly being recognized as serving some of the freshest and most creative sushi in Atlanta. You won't find more authentic and flavorful Thai food anywhere in the area and our exotic drink menu will please even the most sophisticated of taste buds. Zen on Ten is the perfect spot for your next date, business lunch or party. Come to Zen on Ten and relax in our contemporary and trendy ambiance, while satisfying all of your senses.

Iruna

writes...
  • Iruna is a casual Basque influenced Spanish restaurant. Imbued with the flair of northern Spain and the Pyrenees mountains, Iruna brings the flavors and culture of Spain to Old Town Scottsdale. The romance of Hemingway fused with local produce, seasonal menu, and well-crafted drinks pair with a carefully selected wine list to provide a unique and unparalleled dining experience. The food, service and ambiance synthesize the industrial and hand crafted feel of contemporary Spain.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Spanish
  • Neighborhood: Scottsdale
  • Cross Street: Scottsdale Rd.
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.irunaaz.com
  • Phone: (480) 398-3020
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday - Thursday: 4:30pm - 10:00pm Dinner: Friday - Saturday: 4:30pm - 12:00am Happy Hour Daily 4:30-6:30pm & Reverse Friday and Saturday 10:00pm-Midnight

  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Smoking Area, Smoking Area (patio only), Wine
  • Public Transit: The Old Town Scottsdale Trolley makes it's stops in close proximity to Iruna and the many shops in Old Town Scottsdale.
  • Parking: Street Parking

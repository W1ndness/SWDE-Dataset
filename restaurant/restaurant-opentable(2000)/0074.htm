Birraporetti's - Friendswood

writes...
  • Birraporetti’s is a collection of restaurants that have served guests for more than thirty years. In 1974 Birraporetti’s flagship restaurant opened in Houston Texas at the River Oaks shopping center with the slogan "A Great Italian Restaurant...a heck of an Irish Bar!” Today we have three locations-- downtown Houston within walking distance of all the theaters, Arlington Texas, just blocks away from the new home of the Dallas Cowboys and our newest location in Friendswood Texas. Our menu emphasizes traditional Italian foods including pizza, ravioli, lasagna and fettuccine alfredo to name a few. Historically, Birraporetti's has been perceived as an Irish Bar and restaurant which offers large portions and good service at affordable prices. We emphasize freshly prepared Italian specialties as well as more innovative dishes, all presented with flair. The service is personable and responsive and the atmosphere is contemporary and comfortable.

Caliterra Bar & Grille

writes...
  • DINE. DRINK. DISH. At Caliterra, the hotel's signature restaurant. The newly revitalized menu by Chef Julia Brant presents New American cuisine where every dish is born of a simple philosophy: create classic and comfortable foods with the freshest local produce available. The menu boasts a flavorful mix of regional favorites including the freshest New England seafood. A balanced wine list offers an outstanding selection of premium American and Italian varietals. Warm and welcoming, the restaurant is an attractive dining destination with a spacious dining room and a sought-after private dining wine room which can be reserved for private dining.

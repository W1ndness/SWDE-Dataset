Rumi

writes...
  • Rumi has become a neighborhood favorite: between the extraordinary fusion of fresh and locally grown California ingredients and outstanding table service, your lunch or dinner at Rumi is sure to be a delicious and satisfying dining experience. Rumi’s ingredient-driven seasonal menu features local and sustainable food and wine from Northern and Central California with flavors and influences of the Southern Mediterranean and the Western reaches of the "Silk Road."
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Fusion / Eclectic, Mediterranean, American
  • Neighborhood: San Carlos
  • Cross Street: Brittan Aveue
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under

  • Public Transit: Convenient to San Carlos CalTrain station (599 El Camino Real) and SamTrans bus station (corner Of El Camino Real and Brittan Avenue.
  • Parking: Street Parking

  • Catering: Rumi Restaurant is committed to using local and sustainably raised foods. Our menu changes often to reflect seasonality of ingredients. Catering menus will be customized for your event with the help of our Chef and Manager.

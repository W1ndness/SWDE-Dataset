Hannah's off The Square

writes...
  • Hannah’s off the Square is a casual fine dining restaurant nestled in the heart of Denton. Established in 2001, Hannah’s was originally located at 209 W. Hickory St. with a quaint 40 seat dining room. Hannah’s moved to its current location at 111 W. Mulberry St in July of 2004 and boasts five unique dining areas. A lounge with bar seating, a 50 seat private function room, two main dining areas and a beautiful outdoor courtyard provide an inviting and comfortable atmosphere in which to enjoy food and wine that match the eclectic spirit of Denton, Texas. The culinary expertise of Chef Sheena Croft, a vast and diverse selection of wines and attentive service makes Hannah’s off the Square a memorable dining experience for every occasion.

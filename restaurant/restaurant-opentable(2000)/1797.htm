Jubilee

writes...
  • Sutton Place's favorite kitchen for the past 16 years, Jubilee brings elegance to casual dining in a quaint and vibrant atmosphere. Famous for serving a delicious variety of mussels, other specialties include striped bass, sea scallops, steak frites and French cuisine essentials such as steak tartare, escargots and profiteroles. Available for private parties.

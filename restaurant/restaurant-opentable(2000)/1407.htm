Padri Restaurant

writes...
  • Resembling an old Tuscan farmhouse with old oak trees silhouetting the rolling hills of old Agoura. Padri Restaurant and Martini Lounge captures the true essence of Tuscany Italian cuisine stepped in tradition. Padri offers soft pillow back booth seating in a romantic yet handsome atmosphere. Low romantic lighting sets the mood for an enjoyable and relaxing meal. In our candle lit Martini Lounge you can sip eclectic martinis or smoke a cigar in our outside patio. Padri offers live music Wednesday and Thursday with DJ Friday and Saturday night, so put on your dancing shoes and join the crowd.

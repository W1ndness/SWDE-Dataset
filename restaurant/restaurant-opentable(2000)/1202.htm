McCormick's Fish House - Denver

writes...
  • McCormick’s Fish House and Bar sits adjacent to the historical Oxford Hotel in the heart of “Lodi”. Known for an incredible fresh list of 30-40 species of seafood, we also offer prime cut steaks, poultry, salads, and pasta. McCormick’s lively front bar hosts the best Happy Hour in town with food items for $1.95 and generous portions at that! In addition, McCormick’s operates the Cruise Room, an art deco martini bar. Opened in 1933, the day after prohibition was repealed; it is the place in Denver to experience a classic martini in unforgettable surroundings. McCormick’s also serves a great breakfast 7 days a week and the largest Sunday Brunch menu in Denver. McCormick’s Catering can create a memorable event as well, for groups up to 250 guests in one of our private dining rooms or in the hotel’s historical ballrooms and conference rooms. For private parties you can contact our catering department at 303-628-5532. Voted “Best Seafood” by AOL City Guide, 5280, and Westward.

  • Private Party Facilities: For smaller parties of 10-40 guests, we have 3 beautifully appointed rooms off our main dining area.

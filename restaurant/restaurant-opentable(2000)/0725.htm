  • In the Heart of Downtown Nashville, The Merchants offers contemporary cuisine in a beautiful historic setting. The grill offers casual dining with a street side view. On the second floor, our romantic atmosphere is the perfect place to enjoy popular dishes with a southern flare. Our third floor is available for private functions. Complimentary valet parking. Reservations recommended.

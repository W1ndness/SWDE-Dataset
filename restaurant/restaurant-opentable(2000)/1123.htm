Sapor Cafe

writes...
  • SAPOR (the word) is Latin for taste or flavor. Sapor Café/Bar is a place to savor some of the most pleasing tastes & flavors from all over the world. Our menu changes seasonally & always includes our thoughtful interpretation of authentic foods & cuisine - from near & far, both historic & new - that are interesting & above all, DELICIOUS. Whatever the dish or preparation, Sapor works to create & express a satisfying balance of fresh flavors with contrasting ingredients, textures, temperatures & colors. Ban apetit, qing-chi, shalom, ENJOY! - Julie Steenerson, Co-owner & Tanya Siebenaler, Co-owner/Executive Chef

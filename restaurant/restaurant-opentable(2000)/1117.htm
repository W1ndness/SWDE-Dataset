Pittsburgh Blue

writes...
  • A steakhouse for people who work hard and want to enjoy the rewards, Pittsburgh Blue gives them their money's worth and more, with dry-aged steaks selected by master butchers, hand-trimmed chops, and day boat-fresh fish; deep booths and a deeper wine list; a bar that makes everything better; and the kind of service everyone deserves but hardly ever receives. Open for lunch & dinner seven days a week, Pittsburgh Blue serves a full menu in the bar. Come in a stretch limo or stretch pants - there's no dress code here. As refined as you WANT. As comfortable as you HOPE. As economical as you NEED! Tender Prices For Tough Times … $8.95 to 11.95 Sunday thru Thursday nights

Café Boulud Palm Beach

writes...
  • Café Boulud – Palm Beach, at the heart of the historic Brazilian Court Hotel, is steps from Worth Avenue and stretches of white sandy beach. Tucked away on a tree-lined street, the charming 1920's Spanish style buildings overlook our lush tropical garden courtyards. The menu parallels that of our award winning sister restaurant in Manhattan and reflects the chef's four culinary muses: traditional French cuisine, seasonal market dishes, the best of the vegetable garden and our chef’s interpretation of world cuisines. Complementing the Mediterranean hued main dining room are a lively lounge and bar and delightful out door dining terrace. For your private and corporate events visit our two private dining rooms: The Ballroom (seated events for up to 90 & receptions for up to 120) and The Conservatory (seated events for up to 50 and receptions for up to 80). Cafe Boulud’s Chef de Cuisine Zach Bell has twice been nominated “Best Chef in the South” by the James Beard Foundation.

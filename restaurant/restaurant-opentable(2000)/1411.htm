Parkers' Lighthouse

writes...
  • At Parkers' Lighthouse, discerning diners will find first class surrounding and award winning cuisine. Seafood is the main attraction at the unique restaurant located in a dramatic, free standing, actual working lighthouse with an outstanding waterfront located at the point of Shoreline Village in Long Beach. The menu also includes steaks, pasta, chicken, sushi and fresh salads.

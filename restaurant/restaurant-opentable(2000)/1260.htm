McCormick & Schmick's Seafood - Phoenix

writes...
  • Located in the prestigious Esplanade, offering a wide variety of fresh fish flown in daily from around the world, as well as prime steaks, poultry & vegetarian dishes. The main dining room's interior features handcrafted mahogany woodwork, stained glass masterpieces, English racing-car-green carpeting, brass fixtures, Tiffany-style lamps and an impressive selection of artwork. McCormick & Schmick's has six "snugs", for the ultimate in dining privacy. The comfortable bar setting is a great social gathering place with a lively atmosphere and offers a large selection of regional American and international wines, draft beers and single malt scotches. The bartenders' skill of traditional "mixology" is shown by their hand-shaken cocktails with fresh-squeezed juices. Our private dining Rockefeller Room seats from 10 to 65 guests, perfect for social gatherings, business meetings and holiday family events. Our professional catering team will assist you to customize your menu.

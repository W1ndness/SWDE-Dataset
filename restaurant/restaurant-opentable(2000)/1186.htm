Hearthstone Restaurant

writes...
  • Located in a historic, 120-year-old Victorian home in the heart of Breckenridge, the Hearthstone Restaurant, has been a locals' favorite since 1989 for wild game, fresh seafood, and hand-cut steaks all creatively prepared by our talented culinary team. Our extensive and reasonably priced wine list has received the Wine Spectator's "Award of Excellence" annually since 1997.
  • Dining Style: Fine Dining
  • Cuisine: American, Contemporary American, Continental
  • Neighborhood: Breckenridge / Frisco / Copper
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://www.hearthstonerestaurant.biz/default.aspx
  • Email: hearthstonerestaurant@stormrestaurants.com
  • Phone: (970) 453-1148
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner from 5pm (seven nights a week) Happy Hour 4pm to 6pm DAILY
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Michael Halpin
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar/Lounge, Entertainment, Farm to Table, Fireplace, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Private Party Facilities: For parties of 20 people or more dining at the Hearthstone Restaurant, we have created several unique menus. These menus accommodate a variety of different price ranges, and we are pleased to customize them to suit any occasion. To book a large party or special function, please contact Jen Cawley.
  • Private Party Contact: Jen Cawley (970) 453-1023

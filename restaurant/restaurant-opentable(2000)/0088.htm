Post Oak Grill - Sugar Land

writes...
  • The Post Oak grill has been serving Houston with the highest quality food, wine and service since 1989. The Rios family has owned and managed restaurants in Houston and Austin for over 20 years. We opened the Post Oak Grill of Sugar Land in 2006 and we are honored to present our newest location of the Post Oak grill in Sugar Land at Lake Pointe. We welcome you to enjoy the subtly delicious flavors of our menu which have maintained the Post Oak Grill as a distinctive destination for the perfect dining experience. Make your reservation today and join us for a wonderful culinary adventure featuring the finest center-cut steaks, chops, fresh seafood and international wine list. Ciao, The Rios Family

Red & White Wine Bistro

writes...
  • It’s all about the food as the Houston Rockets blast off for the 2009-2010 season. Hold on for a culinary adventure as Levy Restaurants takes your taste buds on a delicious ride. The Red & White Wine Bistro, located on the Arena’s lower suite level, features vaulted ceilings and hardwood floors. Guests can indulge in the very best contemporary cuisine, including our extensive dessert experience. The bar is flanked by two 1500 bottle wine towers offering an array of popular vintages and little-known treasures from around the world.

    Each game the Red & White Wine Bistro offers a spectacular “Chef’s Table” buffet featuring a variety of world cuisines, an assortment of delectable desserts, and other special items for $39.95. Kids age 6 thru 12 eat for half price $19.95. Adjacent to the Red & White Wine Bistro is our VIP Banquet Room, which is the premier location in Houston for your guests to enjoy delicious cuisine and cocktails for a private party or before the big game.

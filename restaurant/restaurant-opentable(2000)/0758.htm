SWB @ the Hyatt Regency Austin

writes...
  • Situated on the second level of Hyatt Regency Austin’s gorgeous atrium, SWB offers regional cuisine that will delight your taste buds with fresh seasonal ingredients. Sophisticated diners appreciate our contemporary décor, set against a wall of glass that overlooks the spectacular Austin skyline. Dine with a large group at our fun community table or enjoy the comfort of your own private table. Reward yourself with tempting dishes like savory smoked pork ribs or Texas steaks grilled to perfection. Discover an upscale Southwestern-style dining experience designed to please all your senses.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Southwest, American
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Cross Street: Barton Springs & South Congress
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://austin.hyatt.com
  • Email: swbrestaurant@yahoo.com
  • Phone: (512) 480-2035
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Monday - Sunday: 11:00am - 4:00pm
    Dinner: Monday - Sunday: 5:00pm - 11:00pm
    Breakfast: Monday - Sunday: 6:30am - 11:00am
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Diners Club, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Javier Ortiz
  • Dress Code: Business Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Beer, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Takeout, View, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access
  • Parking: Hotel Parking
  • Parking Details: Parking is complimentary for all patrons of SWB restaurant. The Hyatt Regency Austin also offers full valet parking for an additional charge.
  • Private Party Facilities: We offer a semi-private dining area to accommodate up to 25 guests. We also cater to parties up to 50 guests and offer an array of custom menus for your dining needs. Birthday parties, wedding rehearsal dinners, weekend brunch and holiday parties are always welcome. Contact us directly to have all details arranged and handled for you.
  • Private Party Contact: Mark Kosior (512) 480-2035
  • Entertainment: Marker 10 spirits & cuisine offers live entertainment Wednesday through Friday from 5pm to 8pm. Many of SWB's tables have prime seating to view the entertainment below and the enjoyable and relaxing music can be heard throughout our open air location on the 2nd floor of our atrium.

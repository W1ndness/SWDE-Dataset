Abe & Arthur's

writes...
  • Abe & Arthur's is a Contemporary American Bistro designed by layering the texture of a classic New York locale, the glamour and glitz of a big night out, and the familiarity and easy elegance of a neighborhood bistro. A place equally suited for after-work drinks or destination cocktails. Classic New York meets contemporary, casual, New York chic.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Contemporary American
  • Neighborhood: Meatpacking District
  • Cross Street: 9th Avenue & Washington
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.abeandarthurs.com
  • Phone: (646) 289-3930
  • Hours of Operation: Dinner: Sunday & Monday: 6:00pm - 10:00pm Tuesday & Wednesday: 6:00pm - 11:30pm Thursday through Saturday: 6:00pm - 12:30am Brunch: Sunday: 12:00pm - 3:30pm
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Franklin Becker
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Full Bar, Late Night, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Weekend Brunch
  • Public Transit: 1, 2, 3, A, C, E & L trains to 14th Street. Walk west on 14th Street to 9th Avenue.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Valet is located on 14th Street in front of Abe & Arthur's

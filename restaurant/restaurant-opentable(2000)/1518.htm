The Cockeyed Gull

writes...
  • The Cockeyed Gull on Peaks Island looks west across Casco Bay at the Portland cityscape and MT Washington, The Gull specializes in casual fine dining and glorious sunsets, our cuisine is French and Asian influenced yet strongly American in presentation. The Gull offers a full selection of Liquor, Wine and Beer.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Steak
  • Neighborhood: Peaks Island
  • Cross Street: Epps Street
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $30 and under
  • Website: http://cockeyedgull.com
  • Email: info@cockeyedgull.com
  • Phone: (207) 766-2800
  • Hours of Operation: OPEN DAILY 11 AM to 10 PM
  • Payment Options:
    Discover, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Mrs. Kim Chon Ye
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Full Bar, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Takeout, View, Wine
  • Public Transit: Ride the Casco Bay Lines making 16 round trips daily (14 Sundays); make sure you use family ticket books for groups and senior/handicapped discounts!
  • Parking: Street Parking
  • Parking Details: From the Peaks Island Ferry Terminal walk up the hill to the first stop sign then turn left on Island Avenue for 200 feet on the left just before Plantes Marina.
  • Catering: Catering is available and the facility may be reserved for private events.
  • Entertainment: The Gull occasionally offers live entertainment but normally offers a wide variety of classic rock, country and more.

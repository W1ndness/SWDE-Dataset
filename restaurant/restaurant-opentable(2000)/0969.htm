Palio d'Asti is the place to party! With a full time, dedicated Event Planning Department, Palio d'Asti is the answer to all of your Special Event needs. Palio d'Asti offers event space for breakfasts, luncheons, all day meetings, cocktail receptions, and dinners. Our multi-functional venue can accommodate groups of 10 to 300+. Planning 350+ events a year we've accommodated every industry and format imaginable. Events ranging from client appreciation events, business meetings, company and product launches, pharmaceutical dinner programs, holiday parties, as well as social celebrations such as reunions, weddings, graduations, bar/bat mitzvahs, birthdays and anniversary celebrations are all planned with the same level of service and attention to detail. At your request our experienced event coordinator, Abby Leutholdt, will be happy to send you a complete information packet. You can also arrange a time to meet and view the space in person.

Landmarc [At the Time Warner Center]

writes...
  • Chef Marc Murphy and his wife, Pamela Schein Murphy, bring their downtown neighborhood bistro uptown to the Time Warner Center. Landmarc offers contemporary bistro dishes as well as a famously well-priced wine list. Landmarc only takes reservations for parties of 6 or more. For parties of 11 or more or assistance making reservations, please contact the restaurant directly.
  • Dining Style: Casual Dining
  • Cuisine: Contemporary French
  • Neighborhood: Columbus Circle
  • Cross Street: 59th Street / Columbus Circle
  • Price: $31 to $50

  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Beer, Delivery, Full Bar, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Takeout, Wine
  • Public Transit: 1, A, B, C & D trains to 59th Street/Columbus Circle.
  • Parking: No
  • Catering: Drop-off catering and fully staffed, off-site catering available. Please contact catering@landmarc-restaurant.com for more information.

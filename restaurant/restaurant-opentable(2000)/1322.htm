Cask n' Cleaver - Riverside

writes...
  • The Perfect Place for Steak & Wine Lovers! In the mood for a juicy steak, fresh seafood, mouth-watering burgers or just a great salad? The Cask is the perfect place for lunch, dinner or just cocktails and appetizers at the bar! Steaks are the thing at the Cask ‘n Cleaver. Only mid-western corn-fed beef is served. All steaks are carefully aged to insure tenderness and flavor, and then custom-cut and charbroiled to perfection. Prime Rib is of the same great quality, slow-cooked and seasoned with the Cask’s own special blend of herbs and spices. Fresh Seafood, chicken, burgers, sandwiches, salads and Cask’s famous decadent desserts complete the experience. Looking for a place for your next gathering? The Cask is the perfect place for Banquets. Meetings, Celebrations & Parties. Lunch or dinner, big or small. Specializing in both social and corporate events: Service club meeting, staff parties, banquets, receptions, rehearsal dinners, holiday celebrations, anniversaries & birthdays.

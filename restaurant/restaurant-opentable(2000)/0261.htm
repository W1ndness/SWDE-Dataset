Chef Rob's Carribbean Cafe

  • Chef Rob's Caribbean Cafe. An Authentic Caribbean Cafe with a cool relaxed vibe. Great drinks, delicious food & fun times. Established in 2006 by a Jamaican, Culinary Institute of America graduate. Chef Robert Gayle has demonstrated his superior talents & his creative abilities for more than 15 years to some of America’s most recognizable celebrities including Russell Simmons, P. Diddy, L.A. Reid, Jennifer Lopez, Akon, Lethon Flowers Dikembe Mutombo.

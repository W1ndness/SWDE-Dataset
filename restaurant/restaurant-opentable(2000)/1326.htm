Charlie's Malibu

writes...
  • Longtime Malibu resident Charlie DiLorenzo has achieved her dream of opening a "dining room for the neighborhood" with the opening of her namesake restaurant. With a degree in design and architecture from the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising, Charlie lends her sense and sensibilities to this stylish yet casual restaurant and lounge, which features neutral tones and natural woods highlighted by striking leopard print chairs and a hand-blown glass chandelier. The menu satisfies all tastes with classic steaks, seafood and pasta, accompanied by a smart bar program offers cocktails and an extensive wine and beer list. Join us everyday for our happy hour "Charlie's @ 5:00" from 5-7 p.m. for featured drink specials, or sample small bites from our new Lounge Menu, served in our cozy lounge everyday until late night.

Located on the PCH within walking distance of the Malibu Pier, Charlie's offers incredible steaks and seafood, with a commitment to organic, locally grown ingredients. We host events from 2-100 guests in a variety of spaces throughout the restaurant. Our lounge offers casual seating for up to 20 guests and the dining room opens up for maximum flexibility. Our Private Dining Room, accommodates up to 20 seated guests. The adjacent upstairs Loft Lounge is perfect for intimate private events of up to 35 seated guests (60 reception style) with its own bar and separate entrance if needed. Our event menus are designed for flexibility and don't require pre-selection. We are comitted to the guest experience and believe your guests should order at the event. Our Chef & our Event Manager would be happy to custom design a menu to meet your needs and budget. Come join us for an unfogettable evening!

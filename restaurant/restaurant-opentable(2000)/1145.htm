Ice Kitchen Restaurant

writes...
  • Continental fusion does more than incorporate diverse flavors and culinary techniques. Its intent is to bring people together to sample, share and experience a truly unique culinary concept that pushes beyond expectation. Offering an extensive menu of small and large plates, our award winning chef has worked tirelessly to create a variety of dishes, that transcend nationality and convention. With creations that are ideal to share amongst large parties, small groups or enjoy for a more intimate occasion, our dedicated staff strive to bring people together and create a truly exceptional culinary experience. We guarantee the highest quality and the critics agree. In August 2009, just four months after opening, IceKitchen was awarded “Best Small Plate” by St. Louis Magazine. IceKitchen also offers the most extensive martini and vodka catalog in St. Louis. As you indulge in one of our signature cocktails, the Ice Bar provides a perfect place to “chill” and enjoy live music.

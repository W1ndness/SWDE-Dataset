Caffe di Fiore

writes...
  • Caffe di Fiore, a family-oriented, unique restaurant and gathering place for many of the well-informed locals offers a fusion of Italian and Mexican, not to be confused with Tex-Mex, cuisine. Here, we provide not only excellent entrees, coffees and desserts, but also a very comfortable and home-like atmosphere, where families can relax and really enjoy their dining experience. The lounge area is unique, with a fireplace and complimentary Wi-Fi, a great place to bring your laptop and get a little work done away from the office, or just connect with friends. With a great assortment of organic and fair trade certified teas and coffees, home made Italian gelato, and international breakfast, lunch and dinner always prepared to order, live music on Saturday nights, and the fact is a BYOB restaurant, why not stop by for a visit and try the delicious cappuccino or one of our blooming teas?

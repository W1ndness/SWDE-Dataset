  • Forge Club is a premiere North Shore private dining club that recently opened its door to the public. Guests will enjoy outstanding cuisine and old world service in a charming unique environment. The extensive menu includes contemporary favorites, tableside preparations, and seasonal specials that are synonymous with the excellent reputation of the Forge Club. The Club’s full premium bar boasts its own special, more causal menu. There is piano bar entertainment on Friday and Saturday evenings. The Forge Club is open for dinner Tuesday through Sunday.

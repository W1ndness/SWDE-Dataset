Acqua Restaurant & Wine Bar

writes...
  • Acqua is comfortably located on the Western shore of White Bear Lake and offers seating in two relaxed dining rooms, on the sheltered deck overlooking the marina, the spacious lakeside patio or up at the bar. The menu is Italian driven and Acqua Restaurant always strives to source out the highest quality local ingredients. The restaurant focuses on maintaining a warm, clean and comfortable atmosphere while offering knowledgeable yet relaxed service.

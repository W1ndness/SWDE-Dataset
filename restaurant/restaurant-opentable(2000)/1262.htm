Montana Avenue

writes...
  • Montana Avenue, the exciting new concept from Fox Restaurant Concepts, will feature Regional American Cooking. The menu, a delicious blend of updated comfort foods, will take its cue from the rich culinary traditions of Colorado, New Mexico, Arizona, and California. A well-chosen wine list will reflect a boutique selection from some of the finest American wineries and a unique specialty cocktail menu will feature one of a kind creations. A blend of rich traditional materials and contemporary styling sets the mood with massive wood beams anchoring a bright window-lit dining room, custom crafted wood tables and ultra sleek chairs with cowhide seats. The beautifully appointed patios will feature fireplaces with one fireplace passing through to the intimate private dining room. Montana Avenue's open exhibition kitchen will feature a beautiful glass partition etched with a spectacular Joshua Tree design that will allow guests to watch the action in the kitchen.

Canyon Ranch Grill - Miami Beach

writes...
  • Delight your senses and nourish your body at Canyon Ranch Grill™ – a stunning oceanfront restaurant serving the award-winning cuisine created at world-famous Canyon Ranch health resorts. Whether you prefer the outdoor terrace or the chic indoor setting, you will dine on innovative dishes featuring the finest locally grown ingredients, natural produce, and sustainable meats and seafood – organic whenever possible. Each selection blends culinary excellence and nutritional wisdom – savor Hearth-Fired Crab Benedict with Creole Sauce, Peruvian Ceviche, Grass-Fed Beef Tenderloin and delectable desserts such as Hibiscus Crème Brûlée. Excellent organic wines, beers and spirits are available to complement any meal. You can even enjoy a wine-tasting or watch our talented chefs at work in the exhibition kitchen. Every experience at Canyon Ranch Grill is a sensual joy and a revelation in healthy eating.

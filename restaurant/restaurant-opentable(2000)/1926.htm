The Stone Turtle

writes...
  • Bringing a sense of whimsy to fine dining, The Stone Turtle offers guests an unparalleled dining experience on Long Island. Executive Chef Gregory Baumel brings an imaginative take on comtemporay American cuisine, everyday food with a twist, crafted with only the season’s freshest ingredients, many of which are locally sourced. A brightly colored décor – brought to life with light yellow and olive shades of green – is met with exposed brick and wood. Sunlight streams through the floor-to-ceiling windows that line an entire wall of the restaurant, shining light onto the one-of-kind graffiti mural that stands opposite the bar. Classic rock, blended with Motown tunes and iconic Frank Sinatra sounds, cultivate a lively atmosphere that is at once festive and casual.

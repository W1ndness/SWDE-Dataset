Takami Sushi & Robata Restaurant

writes...
  • Takami Sushi & Robata restaurant offers only the highest quality sushi grade fish. Featuring 3 kitchens, Sushi, Robata and Asian Fusion. Takami is the only restaurant located in Downtown LA offering outside dining 21 floors above the city with 360 degree views of Los Angeles. Full bar along with extensive wine and sake list. Happy Hour is offered 7 days a week in our lounge.
  • Dining Style: Casual Elegant
  • Cuisine: Japanese, Sushi, Asian
  • Neighborhood: Downtown
  • Cross Street: Wilshire & Flower
  • Menu: View menu on restaurant's website
  • Price: $31 to $50
  • Website: http://www.takamisushi.com
  • Email: Leonard@takamisushi.com
  • Phone: (213) 236-9600
  • Hours of Operation: Lunch: Mon-Fri: 11:30AM - 3PM, Happy Hour 4:30PM to 6:30PM Mon-Fri, Sat-Sun: 5PM to 6:30PM
    Dinner: Mon-Thu: 5PM - 11PM, Fri-Sat: 5PM - Midnight
    Sunday 5PM to 10PM
  • Payment Options:
    AMEX, MasterCard, Visa
  • Executive Chef: Takashi "Stan" Ota
  • Dress Code: Casual Dress
  • Accepts Walk-Ins: Yes
  • Additional Details: Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Counter Seating, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, View, Wheelchair Access, Wine
  • Public Transit: The Metro station is located 1 block south at 7th & Flower.
  • Parking: Valet
  • Parking Details: Parking is located in Lebanon St. in between Figueroa & Flower off of Wilshire. Valet starts at 5PM nightly and is $7 and payment is made in advance. For lunch, we validate for 90min at the parking structure located at the Northeast corner of Figueroa & Wilshire. Parking is $5 with validation.
  • Catering: Will do off-site to go orders for groups of 30-80 guests. Set up with Director of Special Events.
  • Private Party Facilities: Kimono Room or patio can be manipulated to become private. Elevate Lounge is available for social & corporate events for groups of 50 to 400 guests. Elevate Lounge's Private Club can be used for private events all evening on weekends for up to 120 guests. Includes private entrance, bar, dance floor and patio. We are offering a Holiday Dine LA menu from now until December 30th. Lunch is $16 and Dinner is $26. Also, please visit our website for menu information under Holiday Menus.
  • Private Party Contact: Melissa Summers (213) 236-9600
  • Entertainment: After dining, visit Elevate Lounge for dancing and cocktails. Elevate is open to the public Thursday-Saturday from 10PM to 3AM. Friday & Saturday cover is $20 per person. Having dinner in Takami does not guarantee entry into Elevate. For bottle service/ table reservations, please contact Greg Omotoy at greg@elevatelounge.com. There is also complimentary taxi transportation to Staples Center & The Music Center when dining at Takami.
  • Special Events & Promotions:
    Takami's outdoor lounge is the perfect setting for corporate happy hours and small social gatherings for groups up to 40 guests. Reservations must be made in advance to reserve outside lounge area.

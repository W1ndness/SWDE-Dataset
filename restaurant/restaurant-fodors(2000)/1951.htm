Alec's by the Sea

  • Address: 131 E. Chance a la Mer Blvd., Ocean Shores, WA | Map It
  • Phone: 360/289-4026
  • Price range: $-$$
  • Cuisine: American
  • Location: Ocean Shores
Write a review | Avg. member rating: 1.0/5

Fodor's Review:

Some of the region's best seafood is served at this elegant restaurant conveniently set between town and beach. The best dishes are made with creatures caught locally, such as razor clams and Willapa Bay oysters. For a light meal, try the garlic bread served with bouillabaisse, or one of the salads. Steaks and burgers are also on the menu.

  • Credit Cards: AE, D, MC, V

Member Reviews and Ratings

Reviewed by RTurley from Ocean Shores on 4/30/10
Do not be fooled by Fodor's review, I can only think that they sent a Micky D "cook" to sample same. I have tried them 3 times now and they are out of my book for good. This afternoon I went in for a simple hamburger and got served one that would delight a cobbler if looking for new sole material. Indifferent service and the airhead cashier caped all by trying to short change me by $10 bucks. Stick to the bar, avoid anything from that kitchen.

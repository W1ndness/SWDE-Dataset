Mo's

  • Address: 1436 Bay St., Florence, OR | Map It
  • Phone: 541/997-2185
  • Price range: ¢-$
  • Cuisine: Seafood
  • Location: Florence
Write a review

Fodor's Review:

Two things you'll always find at Mo's are clear bayfront views and a creamy bowl of clam chowder. This coastal coffeeshop-esque institution has been around for more than 40 years, consistently providing fresh seafood and down-home service.

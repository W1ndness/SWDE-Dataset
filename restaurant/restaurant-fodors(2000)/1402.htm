Blue Mountain Bistro

  • Address: 1633 Glasco Tpke., Woodstock, NY | Map It
  • Phone: 845/679-8519
  • Price range: $$-$$$
  • Cuisine: Mediterranean
  • Location: Woodstock
Write a review

Fodor's Review:

A barn with rough-hewn beams and siding from a corncrib makes for a cozy yet elegant atmosphere. The zinc tapas bar serves duck liver pâté, Moroccan carrot salad, and other goodies. Local wisdom holds that you can't get a bad meal here. Try mushroom panzerotti (a pizzalike tart), jumbo scallops with wild-mushroom risotto, or moules Marseillaise, and see if you agree. Herb gardens border the outdoor patio.

  • Keep in mind: Reservations essential.
  • Credit Cards: AE, D, DC, MC, V
  • Closed: Closed Sun. and Mon.

Product Description

Fallen Angels: Soldiers of Satan's Realm traces the history of the belief in fallen angels in Judaism, Christianity and Islam. Bamberger examines the fall of Satan, the creation of Lilith, and many other intriguing myths and superstitions. His presentation also reveals a basic divergence between Judaism and Christianity in their respective attitudes toward the devil. The concluding chapter of the work deals with the return of the devil to prominence in contemporary religious thought and shows how Judaism seeks its own solution to the problem of evil. Paperback.

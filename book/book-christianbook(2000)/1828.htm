Product Description

Out of the crucible of the emerging democracies from the old Soviet Union, Larry Stout has created a model that has assisted in developing leaders in government, business, finance, education, and even the church. Stout takes the reader through a step-by-step process, with insightful and provocative questions at each stage, designed to simply and clearly guide a path toward leadership that makes areal impact on others. This model is called Ideal Leadeship and focuses on six areas: vision, values, wisdom, courage, trust, and voice.

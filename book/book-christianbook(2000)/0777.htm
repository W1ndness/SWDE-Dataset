Product Description

When God Takes Too Long examines the mysteries of God's timing and shows how you can thrive in the midst of one of life's greatest frustrations ... waiting. If you've been discouraged by a life of waiting, Bentz offers you a chance to alter not only your perspective on waiting but also your actions in response to it, transforming your frustration into a positive force that will enrich and change your life.

Included:
  • A copy of the book
  • A DVD containing 6 video lessons from author Joseph Bentz and a printable leader's guide.

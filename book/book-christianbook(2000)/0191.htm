Product Description

A childrens boadbook for 1-3 years old. It is brightly illustrated. Each page has a little story of all the children around the world and it tells how much Jesus Loves all the children. Debbie Anderson's whimsical illustrations and kid-like style is the perfect way to introduce your toddler to Jesus. Each of these fun books is designed to be sung to a familiar song and at the same time reinforce the simple biblical truths that Jesus loves and cares for us.

Product Description

The story of Christmas comes to life with this simple retelling of the best-loved Bible story of all. Read about Mary and the angel, Joseph and his dream, the journey to Bethlehem, the baby in the manger, the angels singing in the skies above Bethlehem, the visit of the shepherds and wise men, and d the gifts they brought to the newborn king. Creative and colorful pop-ups and flaps enliven every page in this festive book. The traditional story is exquisitely illustrated to make this book a gift to treasure for all the Christmases of childhood. Recommended for ages 3 to 6.

Warning: Choking Hazard. Contains small parts--not intended for children under 3 years old.

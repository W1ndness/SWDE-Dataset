Product Description

Author/Artist Review

Author: Todd Hilton
Located in: Covington, Georgia
Submitted: October 11, 2006

    Tell us a little about yourself.  I am truly blessed to have a loving wife Melanie, and 4 Miracles, Connor, Evan, Averi, and Bailey. I hold an Ed S degree in Educational Administration and Supervision and have been an educator for the past 10 years. Prior to that I was a Youth minister in the United Methodist church for 7 years.

    What was your motivation behind this project?  To develop a ministry that will inspire families to pray together.

    What do you hope folks will gain from this project?  I pray that this book will be a stepping stone in the development of a healthy family prayer life. Praying with your MIRACLE before his or her birth will create a bond that will never be broken.

    Who are your influences, sources of inspiration or favorite authors / artists?  I find great strength through listening to Dr. Dobson and Focus on the family. I found great wisdom in John Trent's book "Be There", and John Ortberg's "If You Want to Walk on Water, You've Got to Get Out of the Boat".

    Anything else you'd like readers / listeners to know:  Prayer is so simple.It is like quietly opening a door and slipping into the very presence of God. There, in the stillness,to listen for His voice, perhaps in petition, or only to listen, it matters not. Just to be there,in His presence, Is Prayer.

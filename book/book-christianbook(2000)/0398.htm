Product Description

The Blurry Rules Book is all about ethics. Explain THAT to an 8- to 12-year-old girl! Author Nancy Rue approaches the subject by talking about those gray areas and hard-to-answer questions that aren't so clearly addressed in the Ten Commandments. Kids clearly understand "Thou shalt not kill", but what about "Thou shalt not hate the guts of the girl who deliberately snubs you?" Sure "thou shalt not steal," but what if I catch somebody else doing it? There are no easy answers to questions or situations like these, but The Blurry Rules Book offers great advice about such issues. Young girls will discover that although there may not always be an easy answer or a concrete rule, there's always a God answer. As in other It's A God Thing! books, each chapter features special sections to help girls apply the message to their own lives, then journal and talk to God. This is the non-fiction counterpart to Ask Lily. Recommended for ages 8 to 12. A companion book to Ask Lily.

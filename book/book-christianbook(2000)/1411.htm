Product Description

If you've ever had to dial 911 before, you know what an emergency feels like. You need help and need it quickly. "Emergency Prayers" is written to be your spiritual lifeline. When you don't know what to pray in a crisis, let these prayers guide you. Filled with Scripture, each prayer addresses specific needs, from temptation to spend unwisely, wanting to have a child, needing deliverance from addiction to facing bankruptcy. Let this prayer book lead you in prayers for yourself or others in need.

Publisher's Description

From pastor Wendell C. Hawley comes a timeless collection of prayers to bring you—and your church—closer to God. Drawing upon years of experience shepherding a congregation, he offers eloquent prayers to help the people of God bring their unique joys and struggles, problems and praises, before the throne. A Pastor Prays for His People contains a year’s worth of wise, thoughtful, and loving prayers for Sundays and special occasions (including holidays, weddings, funerals, children’s services, and church meetings). Whether you pray in public on a regular basis or simply want to deepen your private prayers for the church, A Pastor Prays for His People will help you share the gift of prayer with others.

ChristianBookPreviews.com

Wendell C. Hawleys pastoral prayers, collected for his book A Pastor Prays for His People, records a pastors deep-felt love for his congregation. Encouraged by his parishioners to publish his weekly pastoral prayers, this volume preserves those petitions, intercessions, and exaltations.

In his foreword, Hawley explains that he has not always felt at ease with formal, written prayers. As his career and faith matured, however, he found more and more comfort and encouragement from prayers of old, and began delighting in the richness and depth of prayer, and especially those prayers preserved in the Puritan tradition.

The collection is divided by months, and some prayers reflect holidays or special occasions on the church calendar. Some prayers reach out to God for strength in the midst of trials; others lift praise to God for his loving-kindness. All lift the readers hearts to the all-loving, all-knowing, all-capable God. Prayer Number Forty-One implores, Gods bank account is more than sufficient to meet our every situation, for he is able to do exceedingly abundantly, surpassing above all that we can ask or think (Ephesians 3:20).

Different than a series of devotions, and not a coffee-table collection of poems, Wendell C. Hawleys collection of prayers is a wonderful resource for those times when we just dont know how to pray for our own or a loved ones situation, or for those times when our words just dont seem adequate to express our thoughts and feelings. Every prayer in this collection reflects the pastors deep love for his congregation, and his certainty of Gods love, as well. Recommended for anyone who needs or wants to supplement his or her prayer life with one pastors best prayers, as written for his people.  Dr. Jan Reber, www.ChristianBookPreviews.com

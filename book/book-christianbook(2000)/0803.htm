Product Description

We are called to live a life obedient to God. But what does that look like? And how can we live in a way that is pleasing to him? The answer is found in faith. Long considered the author of faith, Abraham sets the standard for what it means to be obedient. The book of Genesis holds the remarkable story of Abraham, a man who would give birth to a nation and make the way for our saviour.

Be Obedient: Genesis 12-25 has now been updated with study questions and a new introduction. N this book you will find insights and wisdom on pursuing an obedient life from the life of the great patriarch Abraham.

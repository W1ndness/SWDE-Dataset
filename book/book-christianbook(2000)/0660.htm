Product Description

We Are Baptists: Studies for Older Elementary Children is the third volume in a series of lessons designed to establish a foundational understanding of what it means to be Baptist. These fourteen flexible, Scripture-based sessions can be shortened into a six-week program or fully explored for in-depth study for congregational or small-group use. Each session addresses a single theme, allowing participants to learn more about Baptist identity and beliefs. Themes focus on: Soul Freedom, Discipleship, Believers

Product Description

A favorite Easter picture book has been reformatted into this hardcover edition. When children think of Easter, they may think of fresh new grass, baby animals, baskets full of candy and brilliantly colored eggs. But The First Easter retells the whole story of the very first Easter. Children will understand that Easter celebrates Jesus and all that He did for us. Recommended for ages 4 to 8.

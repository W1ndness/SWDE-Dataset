Product Description

In this book Rev. Kenneth E. Hagin explores the Scriptures and reveals how you can learn to use your mind and thoughts in a positive way. You will be able to tap into the powerful connection of thinking, believing, and confessing. Renew your mind and line up your thinking with the Word of God and learn to distinguish the source of your thoughts with this book. By reading this you'll be able to follow scriptural steps to walk in victory in every area of life.

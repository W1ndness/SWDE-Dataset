Product Description

Based on the scriptural text of Luke 8:1-3 about "certain women" who were with Jesus, Certain Women identifies 12 evident qualities of the certain women within those three verses. Written with powerful words and imagery, it reminds readers that just as Jesus called His 12 disciples, these certain women also had an invitation from Christ and so do the women of today. Certain Women is a biblical, historical, faith, and character-nurturing book written out of a desire to see the relationships among women strengthened. Trade Paper.

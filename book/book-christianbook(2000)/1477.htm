Product Description

"As our lives become marked by increasing complexity, there is something very refreshing about simplicity. For every dozen books that leave us asking, 'What did that mean?' we might find one that is clear, concise, immediately understandable, and obviously applicable. This book passes these tests of readability ..."
- Alistair Begg, Senior Pastor, Parkside Church

Basics for Believers presents a foundational review of the Christian faith, covering areas such as theology, the outworking of the Christian life, spiritual disciplines for godly living, and others. The 52 chapters are easily digestible and include a Scripture-based "action step" and resource for further reading. Basics for Believers is great for the new believer as well as for Christian who needs some clarity in the essentials of the faith and in their application.

Publisher's Description

Just as athletes must master certain physical skills to dominate their sport, Christians must master certain basic principles of the faith to reach spiritual maturity. But because many of us don't have a good grasp of the fundamentals, we find our spiritual lives stagnant. With 54 brief topical discussions, Basics for Believers includes subjects like the Trinity, life goals, worship, budgeting and evangelism - making the basics of Christianity accessible and understandable for everyone.

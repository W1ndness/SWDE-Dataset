Product Description

Prayer packs a punch-and this practical guide offers both solid biblical reasons to pray, along with specific prayer starters in 21 key areas of every mother's life. Chapters address topics such as children, home, church, career, marriage, and goals and include scripture and the life experiences of historic and contemporary mothers. Concise and quick to read, yet packed with spiritual punch, Power Prayers for Mothers makes an ideal gift for any mom.

Product Description

A young couple's struggle with infertility...September 11, 2001...Nazi Germany. When life hurts, how do we make sense of tragedy and pain? It's easy to say "God is good" when things are going well, but do we really believe that God is good all the time? In God is Good: Discovering His Faithfulness in Faithless Times, author Tony Beckett shares real-life experiences from people who felt like they had no way out. He also provides biblical illustrations such as the first century's Paul and Silas, who learned to trust the faithful God even in the darkest of times. If you feel imprisoned by your circumstances, this book can show you how to have fresh hope, turn worry into worship, and gain new perspective in spite of all that's going on around you. Let God is Good help you face life's most difficult challenges, learn to live beyond the immediate, and best of all, experience the life-changing power of God's love and faithfulness.

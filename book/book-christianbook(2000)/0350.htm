Product Description

What makes a guy a real...guy? Think God has something to say about what it means to love as a young man? Think God's view on growing into manhood might differ from our culture's view? (Do you really need the answer?) Guys is a 10-session curriculum that deals fearlessly with what it means to be a young man in Christ in today's world. In this volume, you'll explore the difference between tough and strong, dreams, dating relationships, sexuality issues, anger and why becoming a hero has nothing to do with money, status, or power - it's about sacrifice and service.

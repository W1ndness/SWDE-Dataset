Product Description

Outsold only by the Bible, the allegorical tale of Christian in Pilgrim's Progress has encouraged believers for more than 300 years. Now continue the journey with his beloved wife, Christiana! Illustrated with period woodcuts and KJV Scripture, Jim Pappas's updated edition allows you to easily follow the spiritual adventures of one of Western literature's most famous couples!

Christiana returns us to the mythical realm of Pilgrim's Progress with greater heights of power and clarity through this new enhanced version. Although this is perhaps the most adventure-filled and user-friendly adaptation ever penned, it is totally unabridged and--excepting certain amplified scenes--remains faithful to Bunyan's original story line.

As a parable that echoes the magical realism of Alice in Wonderland, Christiana ranks up there with the best classic fairy tales. The language adaptation by Jim Pappas Jr., is easy to read while retaining an Old World quality reminiscent of the King James era. Filled with illustrations, Pilgrim's Progress Part 2; Christiana is an excellent means of introducing the faith to young children as well as new believers with a love for fantastical folktales. Recommended for ages 9 and up.

Author Bio

Rowan Williams, the current Archbishop of Canterbury, is the spiritual leader of the world's one hundred million Anglicans and a renowned theologian and writer. He has served as a lecturer at Cambridge University and as the Lady Margaret Professor of Divinity at Oxford University. He is the author of many books, including Ponder These Things, The Dwelling of Light, and On Christian Theology.

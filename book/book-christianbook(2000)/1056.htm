Product Description

Based on Hutchens's childhood adventures near Indiana's Sugar Creek, these beloved tales have delighted preteens for over 50 years. Now your kids can enjoy the faith-building escapades of Bill Collins, Dragonfly, and the rest of the gang! This collection includes Green Tent Mystery, The Bull Fighter, Timber Wolf, Western Adventure, The Cat Killer, and The Colorado Kidnapping. Approx. 100 pages each, six softcovers from Moody.

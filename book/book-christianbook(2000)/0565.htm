Product Description

In Wild Animals of Africa Neil Wayne Northey, author of Old Homestead Tales, brings the animals on the Broad Plain to life. Some of these creatures are strange looking, act strange, and eat strange food! We may meet strange and different people. The author advises us not to judge too soon by what we see and hear. Many lessons can be learned from Gods animal creation. Recommended for ages 8 to 12.

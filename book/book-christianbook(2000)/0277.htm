Product Description

Written by a Christian education specialist and a Scripture study expert, Effective Bible Teaching promotes just that. Wilhoit and Ryken show you how to master a biblical text and discover its relevance to everyday living. They demonstrate how to detect and develop the ''big idea'' of each passage and to allow the text itself to suggest creative teaching methods. Teachers will learn to improve classroom skills, stimulate class discussion, motivate students, and use their imaginations. 256 pages, softcover from Baker.

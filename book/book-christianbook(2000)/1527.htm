Author Bio

Dr. Neil Gilligan currently lives in Bellevue, Washington and is single. He operates in the spiritual gifts of healings, miracles, prophecy, discernment, deliverance, evangelism and teaching. Neil is received his academic training DPM and MPM (Doctorate of Practical Ministry, and Masters of Practical Ministry) from Wagner Leadership Red Deer, Alberta, Canada; and his MCS (Masters of Christian Studies) from Regent College, Vancouver, BC. Neil is on the leadership team with Open Heart Ministry’s Bethesda Fellowship. Neil is a former professional Ballroom Dancer so as a trained dancer Neil is a natural as an evangelist for "How beautiful are the feet that bring the good news", and he is destined to win a multitude for Jesus Christ (Romans 10:15; John 4:34-42).

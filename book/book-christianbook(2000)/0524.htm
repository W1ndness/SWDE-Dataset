Product Description

On January 12, 2010 when a 7.0 magnitude earthquake struck the island nation of Haiti, Compassion International employee Dan Woolley had just returned to his hotel from a day of work. For the next 65 hours Woolley was trapped in the hotel's rubble with multiple injuries. In Unshaken: Rising from the Ruins of Haiti's Hotel Montana, Woolley shares his harrowing and emotional journey of survival. With color photographs and reflections from Woolley's wife Christina, Unshaken is a story of faith and perseverance you won't soon forget.

Author Info

Dan Woolley is an interactive strategies director for Compassion International and creator of the website www.MyKidsWeek.com. He earned his bachelor's degree in English and international studies from Azusa Pacific University and has spent his career working with nonprofit organizations. After his rescue from beneath Haiti's Hotel Montana, Woolley appeared on the Today show, NBC Nightly News, Inside Edition, and Larry King Live. He and his wife, Christy, reside in Colorado Springs with their two sons, Josh and Nathan.

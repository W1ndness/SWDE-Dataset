Product Description

What do Muslims, Jews, Hindus, Buddhists, and other groups believe about the great doctrines of faith: sin, forgiveness, salvation, the nature of the divine, ethics, and the future life? Divided into seven sessions, Stroble's study helps Christians understand and sympathize with the practices of their neighbors. 120 pages, softcover. Abingdon.

Product Description

Every system on Earth has been affected by human actions. Despite God's charge of the Earth's well-being to human stewardship, the planet is beleaguered by pollution, exploitation, and neglect. Martin and Margot Hodson--a scientist and a pastor--take readers on a journey of hope and illustrate practical enthusiasm for a better way of living. Drawing on stories of people who have found positive ways to respond to the state of the Earth, they show how understanding God's concern for his whole creation can inspire you toward a Biblical and responsible lifestyle.

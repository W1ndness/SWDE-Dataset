Synopsis:

Soon to be a major motion picture starring Academy Award winners Robin Williams and Cuba Gooding, Jr., a timeless love story from the bestselling author of "Somewhere in Time". The film is scheduled for an October 1998 release. First published in hardcover by Putnam in 1978, this novel is back in print after 20 years.

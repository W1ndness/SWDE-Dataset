Synopsis:

Mentored by a mysterious multi-millionaire whom he meets by accident, a golfer learns how to develop self-control and focus to overcome his pattern of failure. The story uses golf as a metaphor for life. Like THE INNER GAME OF TENNIS, his book will reach out to all those who want to enter the zone of victory on the green or in the business world.

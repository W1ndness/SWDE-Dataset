Synopsis:

U-boat radio operator Wolfgang Hirschfeld kept a secret diary throughout World War II....In this book, Geoffrey Brooks makes full use of Hirschfeld's vivid details and colorful anecdotes to provide an authentic picture of the war at sea as experienced by U-boat crews.

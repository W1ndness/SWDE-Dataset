Synopsis:

From every corner of the U.S. comes a growing concern with the problem of violence -- in the home, in the workplace, in the schools, in both rural and urban areas. Bearing Witness: Violence and Collective Responsibility is an extensive study of the causes of violence and a well-thought-out proposal to stem its growing. The book looks at how childhood violence is reenacted in adulthood; why our prisons are bursting at the seams; the connection between violence and pornography; and much more.

Synopsis:

A startling debut novel about a man who lives to be 256 years old... 1758: Matthew Zela flees Paris for Dover, having witnessed his mother's murder & his stepfather's subsequent execution. The extraordinary story of Zela's life follows. Spanning two-&-a-half centuries, John Boyne's mesmerizing fiction encompasses 1920s' Hollywood, the French Revolution, the Great Exhibition, the Wall Street Crash, the formation of the modern Olympic Games & much more, painting an ingenious portrait of a life lived selflessly.

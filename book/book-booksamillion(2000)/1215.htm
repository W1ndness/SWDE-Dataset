Overview

What We're Reading Now:
This books draws you closer to God and helps you open your heart in new ways.
Alicia Permenter, Assistant General Manager

In the heart of every person lies an inconsolable longing. Men often know it as the hunger for adventure. Women tend to feel it as a thirst for intimacy. This longing is the secret of our lives. It tells us who we are, what our life was meant to be. If you have ever felt those deep yearnings, we have some really good news: this is what the Gospel truly offers. Life is more than chores and Christianity is more than duty. It is a Sacred Romance - a love story set in the midst of a life and death battle. Chuck Swindoll called The Sacred Romance "profoundly significant," a message that "will show you how to rest securely in God's love." Dan Allender says that "The Sacred Romance invites us to see what God is doing behind the scenes of our lives to woo us to himself." Discover what hundreds of thousands have found in this book that will release your heart in deep and wonderful ways.

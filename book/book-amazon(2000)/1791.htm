Editorial Reviews

Product Description

Together with the bestselling book, A Framework for Understanding Poverty, this workbook is the perfect complement to the Framework workshop. If you're an elementary or secondary teacher or administrator, you'll find the practical real-world tools you need to improve your effectiveness in working with children from all socioeconomic backgrounds.

About the Author

Ruby K. Payne, Ph.D., career educator and founder and president of aha! Process, Inc. is an expert on economic class differences. With a special emphasis on the impact of socioeconomic class upon learning, she has written or co-authored more than three dozen books--including the bestselling A Framework for Understanding Poverty--and has published more than 3 dozen books and videos. When she's not speaking at one of many sold-out seminars a year, she makes her home in Texas.

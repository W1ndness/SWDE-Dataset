    Reading Group Guide

    After you've defeated the Pokémon League Elite Four and Champion, your adventure is over...or is it? Now you can explore the sprawling Kanto region east of Johto and track down all the Pokémon in the National Pokédex using the Kanto Adventure Walkthrough chapter. There's a ton of new events and Pokémon out there, so go check it out.

    With the Safari Zone Guide chapter, catch Pokémon from every region at the Safari Zone! Once you've entered the Hall of Fame and received the National Pokédex from Professor Oak, it's a whole new game at the Safari Zone. Now you can use the Object Arrangement function, and Pokémon from different regions appear in Safari Zone areas.

    Use the Battle Frontier Guide chapter to learn about the five different ways to enjoy your battles. The Battle Frontier has five attractions devoted to Pokémon battling, and you can try them in any order you want. Defeat each Frontier Brain and you'll receive commemorative prints.

    In the Pokégear Radio and Phone Data chapter, discover all of the different programs you can listen to on the radio. You can also read about the best times to call other Pokémon Trainers to request a rematch; sometimes they'll call you, too!

    Master the different battle formats with the Pokémon Battle Primer chapter. Each battle format has its own rules. Once you understand those rules, you'll have a better grasp of how to train Pokémon for each format. Learn different battling styles and you'll learn how to win!

    Can you achieve every Pokémon Trainer's greatest dream? The National Pokédex is capable of recording Pokémon from every region. With the help of the National Pokédex Completion Guide chapter, see if you can catch each and every Pokémon to complete this ultimate Pokédex!

    The National Pokédex chapter contains complete statistics and information (including basic data, how to obtain, evolution, and moves it can learn) for all 493 Pokémon!

    Give your Pokéwalker your all using the Pokéwalker Guide chapter. You and your Pokémon can go on a Stroll whenever you like. Learn how to use the Pokéwalker, and send your Pokémon to it to walk around and have all kinds of fun!

    The Adventure Data chapter provides complete lists of all Pokémon Moves, Pokémon Abilities, Pokémon Natures and Characteristics, Items, Apricorns, Accessories, Berries, and Seals, plus details on how and where to meet powerful Pokémon and more!

  • If you are a transfer student watch out. I transferred from MTSU and was told at admissions that all my credits would transfer and my tuition and books would be covered with my grants and loans. Well boy was that wrong. Four of my classes that I was told would transfer did not and they didn't bother telling me that until after I was already enrolled and attending. I even had to figure it out for myself because one of the classes was on my schedule for an upcoming term. When I asked them why it didn't transfer they said it was because the description of the class didn't match theirs exactly. Also financial aid is a joke. Nobody knows nothing about nothing when it comes to that. At the end of my first term my account showed that I owed the school $500.00 when I knew for a fact I had enough in grants and loans to cover it. They said you have to specifically ask them to max out your loans or they only ask for what THEY think is necessary. This happened to just about everyone I know there. So, just be careful, check and double check what they tell you before you commit, and after you do you have to stay on them about your money like they are a bunch of two year olds.

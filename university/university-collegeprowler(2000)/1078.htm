Financial Aid

B+

My thoughts about my school's tuition and financial aid process is good. The only thing I don't really like is that I have to be paying monthly until I finish school. According to Financial Aid office, both of my parents are making enough money. In reality, they really don't include the bills they both have to pay. My mother works to help my father and when she can, she helps me with a few bucks here and there. But I believe the education I'm receiving is worth the money. Even though it's tough, but it is also worth the tuiton I paying for. If I get to recommend financial aid office at my school, I will recommend it.

Work Hard!

A

All of the professors are pretty nice and willing to help. Since it's a small school, you pretty much know everybody anyway. The curriculum can be pretty rough at times, but you can get by that easily if you stufy often. Registration is easy. Workload can sometimes get out of hand when taking multiple classes, but not everything is going to be easy all the time.

Long Walks

B

The school is small therefore the parking lot is very small. It's reserved for administration only. Therefore students have to park in a separate parking deck. The only thing is, is that they chose the furthest parking deck possible from the school (we are on a hospital campus). So, to get to school, you must hike throughout the entire hospital and the partly outside...rain or shine, winter or summer.I'd estimate it at about 1/2 mile distance. I have to get there 20 min early just to walk to class...

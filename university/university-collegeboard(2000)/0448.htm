What the School Says...

Assumption College is a Catholic, coeducational institution that offers a classic liberal arts and sciences education with strong business and professional studies programs. Our 2,150 undergraduates choose from 39 majors and 44 minors, and thrive in small classes. The faculty are active scholars who mentor and challenge students to achieve their full potential. With 90 percent of the students living on campus in guaranteed housing for all four years, the campus is lively seven days a week.

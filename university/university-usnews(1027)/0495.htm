The Basics About Southern Illinois Edwardsville

Southern Illinois Edwardsville is a public institution that was founded in 1957. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 11,144, its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 2,660 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. Southern Illinois Edwardsville's ranking in the 2011 edition of Best Colleges is Regional Universities (Midwest), 61. Its in-state tuition and fees are $8,401; out-of-state tuition and fees are $.

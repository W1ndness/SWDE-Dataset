The Basics About SUNY Purchase

SUNY Purchase is a public institution that was founded in 1967. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 4,077, its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 550 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. SUNY Purchase's ranking in the 2011 edition of Best Colleges is National Liberal Arts Colleges, 187. Its in-state tuition and fees are $6,475; out-of-state tuition and fees are $14,375.

Mission (as provided by the school)  

Located 25 miles north of Manhattan in Westchester County, Purchase College sits on a 500-plus acre estate and boasts distinguished programs in the School of the Arts, the School of Liberal Arts & Sciences, and the School of Liberal Studies & Continuing Education. Purchase offers a unique education that combines programs in the liberal arts with conservatory programs in the arts in ways that emphasize inquiry, mastery of skills, and creativity. Our graduates contribute to the arts, humanities, sciences, and society.

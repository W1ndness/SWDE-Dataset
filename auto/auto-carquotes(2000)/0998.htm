What You'll Like

Whether hauling equipment to a job site, working a farm or towing a boat, the Silverado 2500 and 3500 are built for tough work. Available with short or long beds, with two- or 4-wheel drive or with dual rear wheels and with a few different engines, the Silverado can be set up do just about anything you ask of it. The 2500, even simply configured, is capable of towing over 10,000 pounds and is priced starting around $25,000.

A vehicle’s warranty can significant impact your maintenance costs after you drive off the dealer’s lot, and it’s important to understand the different parts. Typically, a new car warranty includes a Basic warranty, which covers everything except the wear items such as brakes and tires; and a Drivetrain warranty, that covers all the parts that make the car move, such as the engine and transmission.

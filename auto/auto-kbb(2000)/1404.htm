MSRP

Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) or sticker price, is the price recommended by the manufacturer for this vehicle. This is a reference point for negotiations with the dealer. Most vehicles typically are purchased for less than the MSRP.

Invoice

Invoice price, or dealer Invoice, is the price a dealer pays the auto manufacturer for the vehicle, including destination charges.

Fair Purchase Price

kbb.com's Fair Purchase Price is the amount consumers typically are paying for this vehicle. It is based on actual new vehicle transactions from across the country and adjusted regularly as market conditions change.


Fair Purchase Price may not include options, packages or upgrades. For more exact price, choose the options you want.

    Volvo S80 T6 4dr AWD Sedan

    VITAL STATS

    • Market Price:
      $40,477
    • Invoice Price:

    • Estimated Monthly Payment:

    • Basic Warranty:

    *Monthly payment estimate based on 0% down, 5% interest rate for 60 months; taxes, dealer delivery and extras not included.

    The Volvo S80 is a modern, good looking, quick and powerful sedan with a surefooted stance and solid performance. New for 2011 the engines receive horsepower increases, the 3.2 liter engine to 240-hp, and the turbocharged 3.0 liter to 300-hp. The V8 is no longer available.

    The front-wheel drive S80 is available with a 240-hp 3.2-liter inline six-cylinder engine or a 230-hp 3.2-liter PZEV engine. The T6 is available with all-wheel drive and a 300-hp turbocharged 3.0-liter inline six. Both engines are matched with a six-speed Geartronic automatic transmission. Safety features include ABS, traction control, stability control, and six airbags. Adaptive cruise control, collision warning system with pre-braking, and blind-spot information system are optional, as well as active bi-xenon headlamps that follow the road around curves. Also included in the safety roster is the Personal Car Communicator, and an electronic key fob.

    For 2011, the Volvo S80 is a carryover from 2010 with the addition of more horsepower.

    Kia Forte SX 4dr FWD Hatchback

    VITAL STATS

    • MPG:
      22 City / 32 Hwy
    • Invoice Price:
      $17,520
    • Estimated Monthly Payment:
      $347*
    • Basic Warranty:
      60 months/60,000 miles
    *Monthly payment estimate based on 0% down, 5% interest rate for 60 months; taxes, dealer delivery and extras not included.

    The Forte is Kia's offering in the competitive Compact market segment. Attractive styling, pricing and feature content are what make this car standout.

    The Forte is available in sedan or hatchback body styles. Both feature Kia's distinctive family grille. The Forte is available in three trims; LX (sedan only), EX and SX. The LX and EX trims both feature a 156-hp 2.0L four-cylinder and a five-speed manual, or available four-speed automatic transmission. The SX features a 173-hp 2.4L four-cylinder and a standard five-speed automatic (sedan) or six-speed manual (hatch) transmission. The Forte comes nicely equipped with standard items such as; traction control, stability control, side and side curtain airbags, anti-lock brakes with brake assist, Bluetooth and a tire pressure monitoring system. Automatic climate control, leather seats, power sunroof, heated seats and a trip computer are all available depending on trim.

    The Kia Forte receives a new hatchback body style for 2011.

    Ford Transit Connect XLT Wagon

    VITAL STATS

    • Invoice Price:

    • Estimated Monthly Payment:

    *Monthly payment estimate based on 0% down, 5% interest rate for 60 months; taxes, dealer delivery and extras not included.

    Ford continues to offer the award winning Transit Connect in the small commercial van segment. Combining flexibility, functionality, and reliability, the Transit Connect presents a unique and versatile answer to the business owner who needs a vehicle for delivery, service, or small work crews.

    Available as a van or wagon, the front-wheel drive Transit Connect is powered by a proven 136-hp 2.0-liter Duratec four-cylinder engine, matched with a four-speed automatic transmission. Sliding rear and split-opening back doors are available with or without windows. The two-seater van is available as an XL or XLT and the five-seater wagon comes in XLT and new XLT Premium trims. Standard features include front and side airbags, anti-lock brakes, front cloth bucket seats, a low maintenance vinyl floor behind the front seats, and full 180° opening back doors. XLT trims add power locks, windows, exterior mirrors and remote keyless entry. Roll Stability Control® is optional on vans, standard on wagons. The new XLT Premium Wagon includes vented third-row windows and upgraded audio. Ford Works Solutions include Mobility Packages, Taxi Packages, a CNG/LPG engine Prep package, and In-Dash Computer and interior shelving options.

    The 2011 Ford Transit Connect is a carryover from 2010.

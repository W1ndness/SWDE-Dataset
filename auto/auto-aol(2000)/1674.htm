    The Lexus IS-F is a true performance sedan, purposely designed to run with the likes of the BMW M3.

    Lexus take the IS and stuffs a huge 5.0-liter V8 under the hood that produces a ridiculous 416-hp. All that power is sent through a 8-speed sequential shift transmission with steering wheel mounted paddle controls. Further enhancements include massive 19" forged alloy wheels with performance tires, dual exhaust and Brembo brakes. Combine all that with the luxury of power, heated, leather sport seats, and you have yourself a true driver's car. Safety features include traction control, stability control, four wheel anti-lock brakes, and a full compliment of airbags - including driver and passenger knee airbags.

    The IS-F receives minimal changes for 2011.

    3.7 of 5 Review of P90 11/8/2010
     A customer from Lansdowne, VA  
    I like the P90 just fine and especially the movable monitor screen, but I have found the quality of the photos better when viewing on the camera than when viewing after downloading them onto my new computer. But that's probably a computer issue. Nevertheless, my friend recently purchased a new Sony digital camera, and the quality of her photos is far superior, as we took the same photos and compared. Wish I'd gotten the Sony, for all the money that I spent on the P90.

THE ULTRA-STYLISH ULTRA-ZOOM.
With its 15x wide-angle zoom and one-touch HD movie recording feature, the SP-600UZ is one seriously sophisticated camera. But don’t let its big capabilities fool you. The SP-600UZ is truly compact, making it the perfect travel companion for weekend getaways, and everyday snapshots.

15X WIDE-ANGLE ZOOM (F3.5–F5.4, 28–420MM EQUIVALENT).
This 15x zoom lets you get incredibly close to your subject while the 28mm wide-angle lens lets you zoom back out for panoramas and group shots.

HD MOVIE 720p.
A simple, one-touch button allows you to record movies with amazing clarity in high-definition, right from your camera.

DUAL IMAGE STABILIZATION.
The 2-in-1 anti-blur solution. Sensor-Shift Image Stabilization, which keeps your shot steady by compensating for camera shake, combines with Digital Image Stabilization to capture crisp, clear images in any situation.

AF TRACKING.
Automatically tracks unpredictable, fast-moving subjects like kids and pets. Continuously adjusts the focus and brightness so you can easily capture a great shot of your subject in motion.

CREATIVE ART FILTERS.
Give your photos a distinct look and artistic feel by applying one of four different Magic Filter effects, including Pop Art, Pinhole, Fisheye and Drawing.

    5 of 5 Great Camera 8/14/2010
     Jerry from Mifflinburg, PA  
    I've had this camera for about 3 weeks at this point and I'm extremely happy with my purchase. This camera is a step above a regular point and shoot but not quite as complicated as a SLR. It's not as portable as our old sony camera, but takes fantastic shots with a 15X zoom. We bought it for our kids sporting events to get closer to the action but we are using everywhere. The pics are clear and in focus and it is very easy on batteries which was quite a suprise. This is a great camera you won't be disappointed.

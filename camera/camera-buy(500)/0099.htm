4x WIDE-ANGLE OPTICAL ZOOM.
All the capabilities you need in one compact lens. The 4x optical zoom gets you close to the action while the wide-angle lens lets you capture breathtaking panoramas.

AF TRACKING.
The camera automatically tracks the movements of energetic photo targets like kids and pets and continuously adjusts the focus and brightness to capture them sharply with ease.

INTELLIGENT AUTO + SHADOW ADJUSTMENT TECHNOLOGY.
An Intelligent Auto + Shadow Adjustment Technology function identifies the scene you’re shooting, such as Portrait, Landscape, Night + Portrait, Macro or Sport, and adjusts the settings to optimize the results.

MAGIC FILTER.
Add a little extra dimension of drama or enchantment by applying a filter effect, such as Pop Art, Pinhole or Wedding.

ABOUT FACES.
Even large groups—up to 16 people at a time—will be in focus and properly exposed via the camera’s Advanced Face Detection feature.

STYLISHLY SIMPLE.
Even with all of its jam-packed functions and features, the camera sports a super-slim body with a premium dark grey, pearl white or magenta finish, and a sleek, ergonomic design with a 2.7” high-resolution LCD.

DIGITAL IMAGE STABILIZATION.
With high ISO and faster shutter speeds you can capture sharp, blur-free images even when your subjects are on the move.

MOVIE MAKERS.

Features
Achieve superior image quality with a high-resolution, 12-megapixel CCD. Make large prints, or even crop, without losing any detail at all.
You’ll never leave anyone out. With its 4x wide-angle optical zoom (26mm) you can get dramatically close to the action, and capture everyone and anyone in sight. Perfect for all those parties and reunions that you want to remember forever.
See exactly what you want to shoot on the bright 2.7" high-resolution LCD. Use the LCD’s Backlight Boost Button to clearly view your memories in direct sunlight
xD-Picture Card compatibility is complemented by an optional adapter for microSD memory cards, resulting in the ability to store and share your images on mobile phones, PDAs and HDTVs.
AF Tracking lets the camera automatically track moving subjects so your photos are sharp and clear byu continuously adjusting the focus and brightness.
The camera can track up to 16 faces with a technology called Advanced Face Detection, and then it automatically sets the focus and exposure to optimize the results of all your large-group photo efforts.
Intelligent Auto + Shadow Adjustment Technology automatically adjusts the settings based on what the camera assesses you are trying to shoot and keeps both the subject and the background clear and in focus.
Magic Filters give you the flexibility and creativity to be artistic with your photos by applying filter effects such as Pop Art, Pinhole or Wedding.
Digital Image Stabilization uses higher ISO and faster shutter speeds to capture sharp, blur-free images, even when your subjects are on the move.
Shoot your own movies with sound and then replay them on the big screen or on the Web. You can also upload your VGA movies to YouTube with the included, user-friendly Olympus Master™ 2 software.
20 shooting modes to choose from, makes it easy to take great photos in a variety of scenarios and lighting situations. Simply select the desired mode, and let the camera work its magic.
High ISO lets you minimize blurry images caused by movement and capture clearer, brighter images in low light.
Perfect Fix makes perfect pictures possible by activating two different editing features to deal with red-eye and lighting right in the camera – no software necessary.
Shoot like a pro—with the In-Camera Help Guides that give you brief descriptions of all the camera’s settings and features right on the LCD.

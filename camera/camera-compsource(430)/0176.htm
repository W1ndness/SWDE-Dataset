    I bought this camera because I keep dropping or losing my really good ones, but I like to have a camera on me whenever I go out so that I can get pictures if I need them. For serious photo people, this is not even close to the right camera to get, but I suppose it would be great for kids or vacations, since it's easy to use and takes decent pictures. There is no easy way to manually switch modes, so the camera just decides which mode you want to use automatically upon aiming the camera. Also, my nice rechargeable Kodak batteries do not fit into its battery space, which is really frustrating.

    However, as I said, it is a good camera for casual use such as in the case of vacationers, children, and teenagers taking pictures of themselves and their friends to put on facebook. So, this is your average camera for the average customer, but if you want to seriously work on photography and that kind of thing, I wouldn't really recommend this.

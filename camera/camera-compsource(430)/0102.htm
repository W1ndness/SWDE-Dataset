Pictures are not properly set up by the camera, and are often taken incorrectly. Previewing the pictures on the camera show rich vibrant color, but transferring them to a computer show them to be dull, with improper color representation. Video is no better. It looks to be captured via motion jpeg format. Framerates are bad, colors look as dull and misrepresented as in stills. Also, no optical zoom while shooting video. Just everything wrong going on here.)

    A few nice things that I really can't complain about is the build quality is superb. Also, the all interenal optics is great so if you are storing it in a purse or pocket, the lens will not protrude accidentally causing harm to the camera. This cannot make up for the fact that a) it's primary function, taking pictures is severly flawed, and b) Its main selling point, the huge multitouch OLED display looks horrific and does not Function properly.

    In April I purchased a Nikon S70 camera in preparation for a two-week trip to South Korea and Japan. Not wanting to lug along a large format camera, I thought the Nikon S70 would be the perfect size. Besides, what better brand to purchase than a Nikon? Unfortunately, it proved to be an exceptionally poor camera. Had I read the reviews posted on amazon.com prior to purchasing the camera, I would have chosen something else. Being a somewhat experienced photographer, I thought I could make good use of the Nikon S70, despite the negative reviews. Of the 1000+ photos I took, more than 75% were fuzzy, out of focus, poorly lighted, or had to be deleted. It was a once-in-a-lifetime trip and I'd hoped to come home with some good photographic memories. Needless to say, I was greatly disappointed. I've returned the camera to amazon.com. While the size of the camera was what I was expecting, the sensitivity of the touch screen and certain other features force you handle the camera with great care to prevent changing various settings or accidentally taking an unwanted photo. The lack of sharp focus was the worst issue with the camera. It seemed confused under certain lighting conditions. Apparently Nikon has discontinued this particular model, which is a good decision. Nikon's larger and more expensive cameras are among the best on the market, but this little device is a total disaster. Do not purchase one under any circumstances.

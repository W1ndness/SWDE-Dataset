    Features
  • 12 MP means you can make stunning prints up to 30 × 40 inches
  • 3X optical zoom (35 mm equivalent: 32-96 mm) gets you closer to your subjects without reducing image quality
  • 2.7 in. bright LCD - Relive moments and view pictures with brilliance and clarity
  • Press the Share button and tag it to KODAK Gallery, YouTube, FACEBOOK, FLICKR, and ORKUT sites, plus e-mail too
  • Kodak's Smart Capture & Kodak's Face Recognition feature
  • HD picture capture - Take stunning HD pictures in 16:9 format
  • Shoot video with ease - Record life in motion with continuous VGA video featuring audio capture and on-camera editing
  • Never miss a moment with AA batteries - Your camera can always be ready to go using convenient AA batteries
    Specifications
  • Sensor type: 1 / 2.3 in. CCD
  • CCD total pixels: 12.5 MP (4102 × 3057)
  • Effective pixels: 12 MP (4026 × 3026)
  • Zoom: 3X optical, 5X advanced digital zoom
  • Lens: 32-96 mm (35 mm equiv.)
  • Lens protection: built-in mechanical lens cover
  • Image stabilization: no
  • Shutter speed: 1/8-1/1400 sec.
  • Viewfinder: no
  • LCD: 2.7 in. indoor/outdoor color display, 230K
  • Storage: 32 MB internal memory available, SD/SDHC card expansion slot
  • Still format: JPEG/EXIF v2.21
  • Video Format: Motion JPEG-A, AVI with audio recording during capture
  • Video Quality: VGA (640 × 480) at 30 fps
  • I/O interface: A/V output (NTSC or PAL selectable), digital USB 2.0 high speed
  • Power: 2 AA Ni-MH batteries, 2 AA lithium batteries, 2 AA alkaline batteries
  • Dimensions (W×H×D): 3.7 × 2.4 × 1.2 in.
  • Weight: 172 g

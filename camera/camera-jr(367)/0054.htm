Optical Zoom / 4x Digital Zoom / 3.5" LCD Screen / Still and Movie modes / SD and SDHC storage / USB and AV ports / PictBridge / Sound Record
With a User-friendly menu with a choice of 31 languages, high-resolution still and movie modes, 5x Optical zoom, and anti-shake technology, the FujiFilm FinePix Z800EXR 12-Megapixel Digital Camera is a magnificent Point-&-Shoot camera to use and own everyday and on trips far and near.

The 12-megapixel FinePix Z800EXR Digital Camera combines beautiful styling, with a generous 3.5" Wide (16:9) High Resolution Touch Screen offers s the latest digital imaging technology wrapped up in a sleek, sophisticated package.

The next generation EXR sensor in the FinePix Z800EXR incorporates “Phase Detection pixel built-in CCD" to realize Hybrid High Speed Auto Focus. It has the ability to intelligently decide between the two focusing systems on the camera. For example, when a bright, high-contrast subject is positioned in the center of the frame, Phase Detection AF works fast and accurately; and in dark scenes, Contrast AF is employed to work accurately. The Z800EXR uses both Phase Detection AF and Contrast AF to offer fast auto focus speeds in all scenes.

With a newly enhanced gyro sensor, the new Image stabilization system promises further improved image quality with reduced blurring and noise in still pictures and even when shooting movies at the full 5x Optical zoom or in very low-light. The new sensor is able to overcome handshake and can compensate for both sharp and wide hand movements, therefore increasing ISO to freeze action is no longer so necessary, meaning beautifully sharp low-light pictures can be achieved at 400 ISO, rather than 800 or 1600 ISO at night scenery.

Fujifilm’s Face Recognition Technology gives you the ability to pre-program 8 people into the camera. Got a pet? This camera has Pet Detection. One Touch provides the ability for one touch focus and shooting for easy photo taking. Beyond stills, shoot HD movies at 720p and check out this camera's 360-degree panorama mode.

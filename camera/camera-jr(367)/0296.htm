Amazing things come in this small package. The Sony Cyber-shot® TX5 has it all, from a stunningly thin 3/4" body to advanced technologies that make it easy to shoot gorgeous photos and videos. Aside from its remarkable intelligence, the TX5 is also built extremely tough. Its airtight construction is waterproof, shockproof, dustproof, and freeze-proof. Just slip it in your pocket and you're prepared to capture the world in style, no matter where you are.
Intelligent Sweep Panorama Mode reaches beyond the traditional wide-angle lenses to let you capture expansive, breathtaking shots of landscapes or skyscrapers up to 258°. Just press the shutter and shoot the scene with a sweeping motion. The camera automatically stitches up to 100 pictures together for you. No more fussing with tiny buttons. Just tap the large, 3.0" (diagonal) wide touch screen display to review your photos and videos in vivid color. It even works underwater, so now you can enjoy shooting breathtaking photos on land and underwater. Shoot and share memories on your PC, Mac and the Internet with ease. In 720p MP4 movie mode, you can Record 1280 x 720 HD movies at 30 FPS for high quality, yet small File sizes - perfect for sharing on websites like Photobucket and YouTube (computer with internet connection required).
Just 3/4" thin, the pocket-sized TX5 is also waterproof to a depth of 10 feet, shockproof from falls up to 5 feet, completely dustproof and can operate at extreme temperatures (14°-104 °F). The TX5 is also highly intelligent, with incredible performance for dimly lit underwater scenes and Intelligent Sweep Panorama mode that works even in icy climates. It's a stylish and functional addition to all your outdoor adventures.

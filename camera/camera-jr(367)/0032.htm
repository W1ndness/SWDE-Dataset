Video requires the latest version of Flash Player
Samsung NX10 is a new type concept camera made possible by Digital-Camera capabilities and advanced technologies. It's a compact camera that allows Lens interchangeability. A 15.1 Total CMOS sensor delivers 14.6 Megapixel Resolution for detailed stills. Shoot movies, too, at 1280x720 HDTV resolution. The camera comes with a 3x Zoom lens (18mm to 55mm) and has a 3" LED screen. It also has an Optical viewfinder. Innovatively designed without a mirror box and pentaprism, the Samsung NX10 is compact and lightweight. It delivers the performance you'd expect of an SLR but isn't an SLR. With the NX10’s AF function you can capture life's most fleeting moments instantly …before they pass you by. You’ll always be prepared to take great photos to share with family and friends.

Unlike Optical viewfinder that only shows images reflected from the mirror with limited information, electronic viewfinder of Samsung NX10 can convey additional OSD information on screen. Since camera settings can be applied to the preview image instantly, what you see in the electronic viewfinder will be identical to the picture you take. Select automatic and manual modes, the Samsung NX10 is the camera anyone can use, appreciate, and grow with.

3.5" Touch screen / 4x optical zoom / Exmor R CMOS / 1080i AVCHD Movie / 3D Sweep Panorama / DSLR-quality pictures / Background Defocus /MS DUO & SD media

Video requires the latest version of Flash Player

Photographic brilliance meets styling design elegance in the Sony's DSC-TX9. Capture impressive landscapes with iSweep Panorama and in 3D and get clear and crisp images in low light thanks to Sony's "Exmor R" CMOS sensor. You'll also be impressed by Superior Auto, Background Defocus and Full HD 1080/60i AVCHD movies. Enjoy it all on an easy to view 3.5" touch screen.
  • Supplied Accessories: Rechargeable Battery (NP-BN1), Battery Charger, A/V Cable, wrist Strap, Paint Pen, CD-ROM, Multi Output Stand

    Features
  • 12.2MP "Exmor R" CMOS sensor for stunning low-light performance
  • 3.5" Touch screen for easy focus selection and photo viewing
  • Carl Zeiss Vario-Tessar 4x (25mm) Optical Zoom lens
  • 1080i AVCHD Movie records high-quality HD movies
  • 3D Sweep Panorama - incredible views to enjoy on a 3DTV
  • iSweep Panorama Mode captures stunning panoramic images
  • Sweep Multi Angle for 3D-like view on the camera's LCD
  • Superior Auto mode - DSLR-quality pictures automatically
  • Background Defocus highlights your subject like a DSLR
  • Fast capture with 10fps at full 12.2MP Resolution
  • HDMI (with stand) output for easy connectivity
  • TransferJet technology allows easy wireless sharing
  • Optical SteadyShot® image stabilization
  • Face Detection technology captures faces naturally
  • Motion Detection suppresses blur in people & other subjects
  • Card slot for Optional Memory Stick® Duo media & SD media

    Specifications
  • Imaging Sensor: 1/2.3" (7.81mm) "Exmor R" CMOS Sensor
  • Processor: Bionz
  • Effective Picture Resolution: 12.2MP
  • Lens Type: Carl Zeiss Vario-Tessar
  • Aperture: iAuto(F3.5-F6.3(W)) / Program Auto(F3.5-F6.3(W))
  • Optical Zoom: 4x
  • Focal Length (35mm equivalent): Still shots: 25-100mm / Movie 16:9: 27-108mm / Movie 4:3: 33-132mm
  • LCD Type: 3.5" Xtra Fine TruBlack
  • ISO: Auto / 80 / 100 / 200 / 400 / 800 / 1600 / 3200
  • AF Modes: 9 points AF / Center Weighted AF / Spot AF/ Flexible Spot AF (touch)
  • Media: 32MB internal Flash memory, optional MS DUO/PRO/PRO HG & SD/SDHC/SDXC media
  • Still Image Size: Up to 16:9: 9M( 4,000 x 2,248), 4:3: 12M (4,000 x 3,000)
  • Video Mode: Up to AVCHD 1920x1080 (59.94i), 1440x1080 (59.94i, Interlace) MP4
  • Interfaces: HD Output: HDMI / Component (1080i) / USB Port(s): Yes - USB2.0 Hi-Speed / BRAVIA Sync: Yes
  • Battery Type: Lithium ion N type 3.6V
  • Battery Capacity: Approx. 115 minutes / Approx. 230 images
  • Dimensions (Approx.): W x H x D: 3 7/8" x 2 3/8" x 23/32"
  • Weight (Approx.): Body: Approx. 4.7oz. (133g)

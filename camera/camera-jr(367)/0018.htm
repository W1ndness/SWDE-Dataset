3x optical zoom / 3.0" LCD / Easy Shooting Mode, Smile Shutter / Face Detection / SteadyShot® image stabilization / AA battery operation

Sporting extremely easy-to-use controls and a large, 3.0-inch (diag.) screen, the DSC-S2100 makes taking and viewing pictures easy. Get great shots from greater distances with 12.1-megapixel Resolution and 3x zoom. It even features the convenience of AA Battery operation. Plus, enjoy the convenience of AA battery operation and features like Easy Shooting Mode, iAuto and SteadyShot® for capturing great images in various conditions.
  • Intelligent Auto (iAuto) mode thinks for you, recognizing scenes, lighting conditions, faces, and adjusts settings resulting clear images, faces with natural skin tone and less blur
  • Intelligent Scene Recognition (iSCN) Mode automatically detects nine different types of scenes and, within just 1/30th of a second, selects the appropriate camera settings: Backlight, Backlight Portrait, Twilight, Twilight Portrait, Portrait, Landscape and Macro
  • SteadyShot Image stabilization compensates for camera shake and helps prevent blur by capturing two photos at high shutter speeds and combining them into a single clear image
  • Smile Shutter technology captures a smile the moment it happens
  • Face Detection technology detects up to eight individual faces and adjusts focus, exposure, and Flash to help deliver crisp, properly lit images of family and friends
  • Easy Shooting Mode, easy-to-understand basic instructions are displayed on the LCD screen and the camera switches to all automatic settings, removing several function choices and allowing you to focus on just your subject
  • 3.0" clear photo LCD (230,400 pixels)
  • Microphone/Speaker: mono
  • Imaging Device: 1/2.3 type (7.79 mm) Super HAD™ CCD
  • Approx. 12.1 Megapixels
  • Recording Media: 6MB internal Flash Memory; Optional Memory Stick® Duo Media, Memory Stick® PRO Duo Media, SD Media
  • Battery Capacity: Approx. 85 minutes / Approx. 170 images
  • Battery Type: AA
  • Flash Effective Range: ISO Auto: Approx. 0.5 - Approx. 3.3m
  • PictBridge Compatible
  • Movie Mode(s): VGA (640 x 480, 29.97fps, Progressive) / QVGA (320 x 240, 29.97fps, Progressive)
  • Red-Eye Reduction
  • White Balance: Auto / Daylight / Cloudy / Fluorescent 1 / Fluorescent 2 / Fluorescent 3 / Incandescent / Flash
  • 35mm Equivalent: 35-105mm
  • Focal Length: 6.2-18.6mm
  • Focus: 9 points / Center Weighted AF
  • Optical Zoom: 3x
  • Shutter Speed: iAuto (1/4- 1/2,000) / Program Auto (1" - 1/2,000)
  • USB 2.0 Hi-Speed
  • ISO: Auto / 100 / 200 / 400 / 800 / 1600 / 3200
  • Supplied Software: Picture Motion Browser Vers. 5.0 + USB Driver (not supported by Macintosh Operating Systems), PMB Portable 1.1
  • Operating System Compatibility:
  • Windows 2000 Pro SP4/XP Home & Professional SP3/7/Vista SP1; Mac OS 9.1/9.2/OS X (10.1-10.6)
  • Unit Dimensions: 3-7/8" W x 2-1/2" H x 1-1/8" D, Weight: 4.8 oz. (body only)

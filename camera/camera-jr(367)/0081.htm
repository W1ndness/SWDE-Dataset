2.4" screen / 3x optical and 8x digital zoom / SD card support up to 8GB / Anti-shake / PictBridge ready / Movie mode / Face and smile detection
Vivitar's Vivicam T324N Digital Camera has a high Resolution and a large 2.4" screen, combined with great choice of features. Resolution of 12.1 Megapixels together with high quality 3x Optical Zoom Lens allows you to capture every detail and produce large prints. Innovative anti-shake, red eye reduction and Face/Smile detection functions and a large preview screen make it a very tempting choice for any amateur photographer.

  • Specifications:

  • Lens: Auto focus Zoom 3X 36-108mm 1:2.8-4.8
  • ISO: Auto/100/200/400
  • Exposure compensation: -3.0EV ~ +3.0EV (in 1EV steps)
  • White Balance: Auto, Daylight, Cloudy, Incandescent, and Fluorescent
  • Scene: Auto/Night Portrait/Night Scenery/Portrait/Scenery/Sport/Party/Beach/High Sensitivity
  • LCD Display: 2.4 Inch TFT
  • External Memory: SD card (up to 8GB, not included)
  • Flash Mode: Built-in: Auto / Force flash / Fill Flash / Off flash / Red eye reduction
  • Self Timer: 2, 5, 10 seconds
  • File Format: JPEG / AVI
  • Software: PhoTags
  • PC Interface: USB 2.0
  • Power Source: 3 x AAA batteries
  • Zoom: 3X Optical Zoom; 8x Digital Zoom
  • PictBridge

  • Supported OS: Window XP/ Vista/ Mac OS X (10.4.6 or higher)

CMOS Sensor / 24x optical super-zoom lens / 25mm ultra-wide angle lens / 3" Rotating LCD / Electronic Viewfinder / Full HD 1080i video / SDHC/SDXC memory
The super-zoom compact for travel and nature photography: the Leica V-Lux 2 is the perfect camera for adventurers, globetrotters and nature-lovers who want to discover more of the world. Thanks to its powerful 24x Optical super-zoom Lens with an enormous equivalent focal-length range of 25 to 600 mm, it is extremely flexible. The 3" LCD display can be twisted and turned in almost any direction and makes it easy for photographers to compose pictures from even the most unusual angles. It captures images of outstanding quality and features full-HD-video function plus amazingly fast 11 images per second in continuous shooting mode. The V-Lux 2 is the ideal tool for globetrotters with a passion for exploring the parts of world that are still off the beaten track.
  • Box Contents: Camera with lens cap, Battery Charger (Leica BC-DC 9), Battery (Leica BP-DC 9), Lens hood, Carrying strap, AV cable, USB cable, CD

    Features
  • Compact design - Characterized by a compact design and extremely simple handling, the V-Lux 2's Integrated handgrip and lightweight allow photographers to shoot one-handed with ease.
  • Ultra-wide focal length range - Just like every other Leica lens, the Leica DC Vario-Elmarit 1:2.8-5.2/4.5-108 mm ASPH. on the V-Lux 2 is a true optical masterpiece. The equivalent focal lengths of this 24x super zoom for photo and video range from a super wide-angle 25 mm to an extreme telephoto of 600 mm in 35mm format.
  • Fold-out LCD display - The 3'' LCD screen that can be twisted and turned in almost any direction making it easy to compose pictures taken from the most unusual or challenging angles.
  • Continuous shooting at high speed - With 11 frames per second at the full Resolution of 14.1 MP or 60 frames per second with a resolution of 2.8 MP, the Leica V-Lux 2 is one of the world's fastest consecutive shooting cameras.
  • Videos in Full-HD-Quality - Another highlight of the V-Lux 2 is its ability to shoot videos in 1080i-AVCHD Full HD.
  • Software - Adobe Photoshop Elements 8 and Adobe Premiere Elements 8
    Specifications
  • Sensor: 1/2.33" CMOS with 15.1 MP (effective 14.1 MP)
  • Lens: Leica DC Vario-Elmarit 4.5 - 108 mm f/2.8 - 5.2 ASPH. (wide-angle f2.8 - f8, telephoto f5.2 - f8 ), corresponds to 25 - 600 mm, 35 mm equivalent
  • Zoom optical/digital: 24 x/4 x
  • Monitor: 3.0" TFT LCD
  • View finder: Color LCD
  • Microphone/speaker: Stereo/mono
  • Built-in Flash: Yes
  • External Flash Connection: Hot Shoe
  • Recording Media: SD/SDHC/SDXC
  • Recording File Format: RAW/JPEG, RAW/JPEG + Quicktime, AVCHD/Quicktime Motion JPEG
  • Resolution: Still up to 4320 x 3240; Movie up to Full HD 1920 x 1080, 60i
  • Interface: USB, Mini HDMI, A/V, Audio Out, 14-pin Dedicated jack, 2.5mm MIC/Remote
  • Dimensions (WxDxD): 4 5/6 x 3 1/6 x 3 5/7 in.
  • Weight: 18.34 oz.

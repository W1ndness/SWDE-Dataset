5x Optical Zoom Leica DC Vario / 3" LCD / AVCHD Video Compatible / Dolby Stereo Digital Creator / SD - SDHC - SDXC Storage Slot
[DMC-FX700 packs a host of advanced functions in a slim, stylish body. The DMC-FX700 is now truly a hybrid camera that records high quality 1920x1080 full HD movie in AVCHD. The 14.1-megapixel DMC-FX700 also features F2.2 super bright 24mm ultra wide angle LEICA DC VARIO-SUMMICRON Lens with 5x Optical Zoom to make the camera even more versatile from shooting dynamic landscapes to the moving subject in low-lit situations such as indoors or at night.

The iA (Intelligent Auto) mode now comprehends Motion Deblur mode and Intelligent Burst mode. The Motion Deblur mode is available by updating the motion detection (Intelligent ISO Control) and Brightness control (Intelligent Exposure) to gain as high shutter speed as possible. Combined with the outstanding brightness of the lens and the POWER O.I.S. (Optical Image Stabilizer), the DMC-FX700 thoroughly prevents any kind of blur - whether the blur is caused by a handshake or the subject's motion. The new Intelligent Burst mode determines the frame rate in the burst shooting according to the speed of subjects motion. The POWER O.I.S., Face Recognition, Face Detection, AF Tracking, Intelligent ISO Control, Intelligent Scene Selector and Intelligent Exposure work appropriately just by entering the iA mode for any User to take beautiful photos with maximum ease.

  • Lens Focal Length - 4.3-21.5mm (24-120mm in 35mm equivalent or 29-147mm in 35mm equiv. in movie recording)
  • Optical Image Stabilizer - Power O.I.S. (Off / Auto / Mode1 / Mode2)
  • Maximum Resolution still 4:3 4320x3240 / 16:9 4320x2342

  • AF Metering Face / AF Tracking / 11pt / 1pt HS / 1pt / Spot

  • Exposure modes: Program AE, Aperture Priority AE, Shutter Priority AE, and Manual
  • Still Image Scene Mode: Portrait, Soft Skin, Transform, Self-Portrait, Scenery, Panorama Assist, Sports, Night Portrait, Night Scenery, Handheld Night Shot, Food, Party, Candle Light, Baby1, Baby2, Pet, Sunset
  • Additional Still Image Scene Modes: High sensitivity, Flash Burst, Starry Sky, Fireworks, Beach, Snow, Aerial photo, Pinhole, Film Grain, High Dynamic (Standard, Art, B&W), Photo Frame
  • Movie Scene Mode Portrait, Soft Skin, Transform, Self-Portrait, Scenery, Low Light, Food, Party, Candle Light, Sunset, Beach, Snow, Aerial, Pinhole, Film Grain, High Sensitivity, High Dynamic (Standard, Art, B&W)
  • Recording Media Built-in Memory, SD Memory Card, SDHC Memory Card, SDXC Memory Card
  • Interface - AV Output (NTSC), mini HDMI, USB 2.0 High speed, DC Input (requires Optional DC Coupler) / VieraLINK compatible

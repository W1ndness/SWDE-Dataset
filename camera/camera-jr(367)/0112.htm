3x Optical Zoom / 2.5" LCD / Digital Image Stabilization / Face Detection / MPEG4 movie recording
Why choose whether you want to shoot still pictures or videos? The Samsung SL30 Digital camera lets you do both. It is an excellent camera for anyone. It's a fully automatic Point-&-Shoot camera with lots of advanced features that assure your results will be great. A 2.5-inch Intelligent LCD screen makes taking and reviewing photos easier than ever. Save to Optional SD SDHC MMC cards for easy Storage and sharing. The silver Samsung SL30 10 Megapixel Digital Camera is a great camera and a fantastic buy for any photo opportunity. This simple camera lets you choose when and what you want to shoot and makes certain the results are enjoyable.

This slim SL30 beauty exudes style, but still packs powerful features. 10.2 Megapixels gives you the high quality your memories deserve and a 3x Optical Zoom means you can get close enough to get a great shot. Once you have the shot, make sure it comes out sharp and clear. Digital Image stabilization reduces the blurred effects of shaky hands. Available in hot colors, the SL30 makes taking stunning photos, fun.

Features:

  • 10.2MP - High Resolution images, outstanding quality. The SAMSUNG SL30 delivers nothing short of brilliant images with a true-color filtered 10 megapixel CCD.
  • 3x Optical zoom - With a 3x optical zoom, you'll get up close and Personal with every subject. Plus, a slim, stylish design makes the mounted inner zoom Lens easier to use and carry. A special coating on both sides of the lenses prevents false color and Distortion while delivering outstanding picture quality.
  • 2.5" LCD screen - It's easy to take pictures with the large 2.5" LCD screen. And with improved contrast and color reproduction you'll be able to take and Edit your photos effortlessly.
  • Digital Image Stabilization - Get brighter and more natural pictures with DIS technology. With DIS the effects of image blur are reduced in lower light conditions.
  • Face Detection - With the Face Detection feature, you'll take portrait pictures easily. The camera detects faces in a scene and then automatically focuses and optimizes the Brightness of the face for beautiful portrait pictures.
  • MPEG4 movie Recording - Record video at a resolution of 800 x 592 (SVGA) at 20 fps. The SL30 also features helpful in-camera Editing controls, which lets you pause and then resume filming without having to save each clip as an individual file.
  • Effective Pixel - approx. 10.2 megapixel
  • ISO Equivalent - auto, 80, 100, 200, 400, 800, 1600
  • Media - internal memory: approx. 11 MB Flash memory external memory: SD card (up to 2 GB guaranteed), MMC plus (up to 1 GB guaranteed), SDHC (up to 8 GB guaranteed)
  • File Format - still image: JPEG (DCF), EXIF 2.21, DPOF 1.1, PictBridge 1.0; movie clip: AVI (MJPEG); audio: WAV
  • Image Size - up to 10 M: 3648 x 2736 pixels
  • Dimensions (WxHxD): 3.7" x 2.47" x 0.99"
  • Weight: 4.23 oz. (without Battery and card)

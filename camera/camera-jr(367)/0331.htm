Casio's EX-G1 is designed with style and endurance to last the elements of the ages, and the lifestyle elements of ENDURANCE - Shock, Dirt, Humidity, Temperature, Snow, Water proof.
BR> Life can be an adventure. Capture every thrill with the Casio EX-G1. The EX-G1 is designed with shock-resistant elements throughout the camera, both inside and out, creating a smart look in a slim-line camera with functional beauty. Capturing high-resolution 12.1 effective Megapixel images, the EX-G1 features modes such as interval shooting and multi-image capture.
  • Interval Shooting - With the EX-G1, users can take powerful time-lapse photography, the kind that is usually almost impossible to capture. The shooting interval can be set at 10 seconds, 30 seconds, 1 minute, or 3 minutes, and the capture time for movies may be set at 3, 10, 20, or 30 minutes, enabling users to get great shots when an event changes over time such as in nature, science, and even sports
  • Waterproof - The EX-G1 can be used at a water depth of three meters (10 feet) for 60 continuous minutes. Water will not penetrate the camera even when directly sprayed with jets of water from every direction
  • Shock Resistance - Passes drop test including multiple falls from a height of 2.13 meters (7 feet)
  • Freeze Proof - Camera can be used at temperatures as low as -10 °C (14 °F)
  • Dust Proof - The EX-G1 is impervious to dust, with performance corresponding to IEC/JIS Protection Class 6 (IP6X) dustproofing
  • Specifications
  • Number of Effective Pixels: Approx. 12.1 megapixels
  • Image Sensor: 1/2.3" square pixel CCD
  • Lens: 3 x Optical zoom, 4x Digital zoom, HD Zoom Maximum 18.7x (11 lenses in 9 groups, including Aspherical lens)
  • Lens Focal Length: F3.9(W) - F5.4(T) / f=6.66 - 19.98mm (Approx. 38 - 114mm equivalent to 35mm film)
  • Monitor: 2.5-inch TFT Color LCD
  • Still Images: Up to 12M(4000x3000)/3:2(4000x2656)/16.9(4000x2240)/8M(3264x2448)
  • Movies: Up to WIDE (848X480)/STD (640x480)/LP (320x240)/YouTube (640x480)
  • File Format: Still images - JPEG (Exif Ver. 2.2), DCF1.0, DPOF; Movies - AVI format, Motion JPEG, IMA-ADPCM (monaural); Audio (Voice Recording) - WAV format (monaural)
  • Recording Media: 35.7MB Built-in flash memory, microSD
  • ISO Sensitivity: Auto/ ISO64/ ISO100/ ISO200/ ISO400/ ISO800/ ISO1600/ISO3200
  • Flash Mode: Auto, Flash off, Flash on, Soft Flash, Red eye reduction
  • Recording Functions: Snapshot, Marco, Self-timer, Dynamic Photo, Continuous shooting (Normal Speed CS/High Speed CS), BEST SHOT, AUTO BEST SHOT, Make-up mode, Landscape Effect, Auto shutter, Face Detection, Interval mode, Movie (Wide Movie, Normal Movie, Prerecord Movie (For YouTube and Interval), Voice Recording
  • Terminals: USB/AV port (Hi-Speed USB compatible)
  • Power: Rechargeable Lithium ion Battery (NP-80) x1
  • Dimensions (WxHxD): 4.07 x 2.53 x 0.78 in. / 103.5 x 64.2 x 19.9 mm
  • Weight: Approximately 5.43 oz. / 154g

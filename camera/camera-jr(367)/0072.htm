10.2 MP/ 2.7" LCD / 24mm wide angle / 5x optical zoom / f/2.4 bright G lens / "Exmor R" sensor / Sweep Panorama / HD Movie / iAuto / Intelligent AF

The Sony WX1 features a 10.2 Megapixel "Exmor R" CMOS image sensor that brings out the full resolving power of the camera's G Lens to deliver extremely fast speed, high resolution, and stunning low-light sensitivity with improved image clarity and drastically reduced grain - 2x better than traditional sensors of equivalent size and pixel count. In addition, the combined "Exmor R" CMOS sensor and Bionz imaging Processor delivers extremely fast 10fps, Anti Motion Blur, Hand-held Twilight and 720p movie modes.
  • Supplied Accessories: Rechargeable Battery Pack, Battery Charger, Multi Connector Cable USB/AV, Wrist Strap, CD-ROM Software, Power Cord
  • "Exmor R" sensor for stunning low-light performance
  • Premium f/2.4 bright G lens for ultra sharp pictures
  • Versatile 24mm wide angle and 5x Optical zoom
  • Sweep Panorama Mode captures breathtaking panoramas
  • Fast capture ten photos with 10fps at full 10.2MP
  • Capture your videos in HD Movie mode (720p) MPEG4
  • Large 2.7" display (230K pixels) with easy operation
  • Optical SteadyShot® image stabilization
  • iAuto detects 8 scenes and optimizes camera settings
  • BIONZ image processor for high resolution and fast performance
  • Intelligent AF reduces shutter time lag as low as 0.3-sec.
  • Smile Shutter technology automatically captures a smile
  • Face Detection technology captures faces naturally
  • Megapixel: 10.2MP
  • Imaging Device: 1/2.4 type (7.79mm) "Exmor R"
  • Optical Zoom: 5x
  • Focal Length: 4.25-21.25mm (35mm Equivalent: 24-120mm)
  • Lens Type: Sony's G-lens
  • Lens Construction: 6 elements in 5 groups (including 5 aspheric elements)
  • LCD: 2.7" Clear Photo LCD 230K
  • Storage Media: 11MB internal Flash Memory, Optional Memory Stick® Duo Media, optional Memory Stick® PRO Duo Media
  • Still Image Mode(s): Normal (JPEG) / Burst (JPEG) / Bracketing (JPEG) - AE (Plus / Minus 0.3EV / 0.7EV / 1.0EV)
  • Movie Mode(s): 1280 x 720 (Fine) (Approx.30fps, Progressive); 1280 x 720 (Standard) (Approx.30fps, Progressive); 640 x 480 VGA (Approx.30fps, Progressive)
  • ISO: Auto / 80 / 100 / 200 / 400 / 800 / 1600 / 3200
  • Image Stabilization: Optical
  • Battery Type: Lithium ion / InfoLITHIUM G type 3.6V
  • Dimensions (Approx.): 3 9/16" x 2 1/16" x 25/32"
  • Weight (Approx.): Approx. 5.3oz

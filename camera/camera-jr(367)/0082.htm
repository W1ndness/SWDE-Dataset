5x Optical, 6x Digital Zoom / 2.7-Inch LCD / SDHC Slot / Up to 4000x3000 Still Resolution / Includes pouch, digital keychain, & mug
Colorful and stylish, this compact camera can give you the style with its full metal high quality body. The Finepix Z70 uses a Fujinon Lens, the high precision optic lens from Fujifilm has received positive praises, used in various industries - satellite in outer space, TV/film, security and compact cameras. The technology provides great 5x Optical zoom. The Z70 is Able to capture both photos and movies in High-Definition 16:9 format, the FinePix Z70 lets you discover the impact of full-screen HDTV image display. Photo Shooting lets you capture high-resolution images in 16:9 format (4,000 x 2,248 pixels, 1,920 x 1,080 pixels); Movie Shooting - Capture high-resolution images in 16:9 format. 1,280 x 720 pixels [HD] (max. Recording time: 12 min.) / 640 x 480 pixels / 320 x 240 pixels. Wow!

  • Lens Fujinon 5x Optical zoom lens equivalent to 36 - 180mm on a 35mm camera; Digital zoom Approx. 6.3x
  • Focus distance (from lens surface) Normal: Wide: Approx. 60cm / 2.0ft. to infinity Telephoto: Approx. 1.0m / 3.3ft. to infinity Macro: Wide: Approx. 9cm - 80cm / 3.5in. - 2.6ft. Telephoto: Approx. 1.0m - 3.0m / 3.3ft. - 9.8ft.
  • Sensitivity Auto / Equivalent to ISO 100 / 200 / 400 / 800 / 1600
  • Exposure control TTL 256-zones metering Programmed AE
  • Shooting modes Natural Light, Natural Light & with Flash, Portrait, Landscape, Sport, Night, Beach, Snow, Sunset, Party, Museum, Flower, Text, Digital Image Stabilization, Auction, Successive Movie, SR AUTO (Scene Recognition Auto), Auto, Program, Night (Tripod), Portrait enhancer
  • Shutter speed 1/4sec. to 1/2000sec., (with mechanical shutter)
  • Focus Auto focus, TTL contrast AF, Tracking AF
  • White balance Auto, Fine, Shade, Fluorescent light (Daylight), Fluorescent light (Warm White), Fluorescent light (Cool White), Incandescent light
  • Self-timer Approx. 10sec. / 2sec. delay, Couple Timer, Group Timer
  • Multimode Auto-flash
  • LCD Monitor 2.7-inch, Approx. 230,000 dots, TFT color LCD monitor; frame coverage approx. 96%
  • Movie recording 1,280 x 720 pixels / 640 x 480 pixels / 320 x 240 pixels (30 frames/sec.) with Monaural sound
  • Photography functions SR AUTO (Scene Recognition Auto), Face Detection (with Auto Red-eye reduction, on, off), Framing guide, Frame No. memory, Silent mode, FinePix color
  • Playback functions Face Detection, Edit FOR BLOG, Image Search, Crop, Image rotate, Slideshow, Multi-frame playback (2/5/9/100 frames), Sorting by date, Voice Memo, Movie Edit, Mark Upload
  • Video Output NTSC / PAL selectable
  • USB 2.0 High-speed
  • 13MB internal memory / SD SDHC slot for Optional memory cards - 4GB SDHC card advised for practical still and movie use

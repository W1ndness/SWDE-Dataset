CMOS sensor for high speed & low light / 10x Wide-Angle Optical Zoom / 3" LCD / 4-way VR IS System / 1080p HD Movie / SDHC/SDXC memory / Li-ion battery
Fast, responsive and versatile, the Nikon COOLPIX S8100 with advanced CMOS sensor technology and incredible 10X Zoom will capture fast-moving subjects in still images and Full HD (1080) movies. High-speed operation, exceptional low-light performance, outstanding image quality and sophisticated features mark the 12.1-megapixel S8100. The Optical 10x wide Zoom- NIKKOR ED glass Lens covers 30-300mm, and the camera's speedy start-up, fast autofocus, quick shutter release, superior low light performance and high-speed continuous shooting (five shots in one second!) ensure capture of all your memorable moments. The S8100 also offers Optical VR Image Stabilization, high ISO sensitivity to 3200, a new Flash control system, movie Recording with Stereo Sound and a 3-inch Ultra-High Resolution (921,000-dot) Clear Color Display for bright, sharp image viewing and sharing.
  • Accessories: Strap, EN-EL12 Rechargeable Li-ion Battery, Charging AC Adapter, USB Cable, Audio Video Cable, Software Suite CD

    Features
  • 12.1 -megapixel CMOS sensor for high speed operation and exceptional low-light performance
  • 10x Wide-Angle Optical Zoom-NIKKOR ED Glass Lens
  • 4-way VR Image Stabilization System - Optical VR Image Stabilization, Motion Detection, High ISO up to 3200, Best Shot Selector (BSS)
  • 3-inch Ultra-High Resolution (921,000-dot) Clear Color Display
  • EXPEED C2 continues to expand the possibilities of photography with improved levels of image quality, fine detail and Processing speed
  • Smart Portrait System
  • Full HD (1080p) Movie with Stereo and HDMI Output
  • Ultra-fast start-up, autofocus and shooting

    Specifications
  • Image Sensor: 1/2.3-in. type CMOS
  • Effective Pixels: 12.1 million
  • Lens: 10× optical zoom, NIKKOR ED glass lens
  • Lens Focal Length: 5.4-54.0mm (equivalent to that of 30-300mm lens in 35mm)
  • Monitor: 3.0-inches Wide Viewing Angle TFT LCD
  • Storage Media: SD/SDHC/SDXC Memory card
  • Storage File System: DCF, EXIF 2.2, DPOF compliant
  • Storage File Formats: JPEG, WAV, MOV (Video:MPEG-4 AVC/H.264, Audio: AAC stereo)
  • Size: Still image up to 4000 x 3000 (12M); Movie up to Full HD 1920 x 1080p / 30fps
  • Built-in flash Control: TTL auto flash with monitor preflashes
  • I/O terminal: A/V output, USB, HDMI mini (Type C)
  • Power Sources: One Rechargeable Li-ion Battery EN-EL12 (supplied)
  • Battery Life (shots per charge): Still pictures : Approx. 210 shots; Movies: Approx. 1 h 20 min (HD 1080p)
  • Dimensions (HxWxD): 2.4 x 4.1 x 1.2 inches / 59.2 x 104.0 x 29.9 mm
  • Weight: 7.4 ounces / 209g

Attention adventure-seekers: this is the go-anywhere, do-anything camera you’ve been waiting for. It snaps stunning photos and records HD movies. Hurtling down a rugged trail, plunging underwater, balancing on your snowboard, the Stylus Tough-8010 is engineered to thrive in any conditions. A revolutionary system of waterproof seals and gaskets keeps water out so you can take pictures up to 33 feet underwater, or boldly enter that winter wonderland without worrying about your camera. The STYLUS TOUGH-8010 is winterized to perform at below-freezing temperatures.

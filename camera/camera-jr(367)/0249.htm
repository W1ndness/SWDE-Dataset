12.0 Megapixels / 4x Optical Zoom / 3-inch TFT LCD With Touch Screen Control / Image Stabilization System / 720p HD Movie Mode / Smart Portrait System
Integrating sleek design and intuitive touch operation, the new COOLPIX S4000 Digital camera is all about the User experience. Whether it’s Recording HD movies or exploring creative possibilities, the COOLPIX S4000 places the camera’s controls at the touch of a finger. The COOLPIX S4000 incorporates a new 3.0-inch, high-resolution touch-panel 460,000-dot Clear Color Display LCD which makes it easy to compose, review and share pictures. Advanced touch features include Touch Shutter, allowing users to select their subject with a single touch on the screen to adjust Focus and exposure, and to automatically release the shutter, as well as Subject Tracking to automatically follow and focus on a subject selected by touching the screen.
The COOLPIX S4000 features 12-megapixel resolution; a 4x wide-angle Zoom-NIKKOR glass lens; ISO 3200; HD movie (720p); 4-way VR Image stabilization System; Scene Auto Selector Mode; Nikon’s Smart Portrait System and a sleek design.
Nikon COOLPIX cameras incorporate Nikon’s world-class NIKKOR lenses, delivering accurate detail and brilliant color, as well as Nikon’s EXPEED digital image Processing concept, which helps to ensure high-quality pictures with stunning color and sharpness. A new enhancement in select camera models is Advanced Flash Control, which creates a more natural-looking appearance in low-light settings. Other COOLPIX features include Vibration Reduction (VR) Image Stabilization, which minimizes blur that may appear in pictures, even in low-light or unsteady conditions while shooting handheld; and Scene Auto Selector, which automatically recognizes the shooting situation and adjusts camera settings accordingly.
  • 12.0 Megapixels for up to 4000x3000 pixel still picture resolution
  • 4x Optical zoom - 4.9-19.6mm (35mm Format equivalent: 27-108mm);
  • 3-inch TFT LCD with touch screen control and anti-reflection coating - Sensitive Response Touch Panel display
  • Retouch and Draw functions for quick, convenient in-camera enhancements and personalization to any image
  • 4-way VR Image Stabilization System - Electronic VR Image, Motion Detection, High ISO up to 3200, Nikon's original Best Shot Selector (BSS)
  • Shoot up to 720p HD movies - 720p HD movie recording at 24 FPS for easy in camera playback or on your computer
  • Smile Timer and Blink Proof makes sure you’ll always catch the decisive moment when your subject smiles and Blink Proof function prevents blinking
  • EXPEED Image Processing; Smart Portrait System
  • Internal Memory - Approximately 45MB
  • Storage Media slot for Optional SD and SDHC memory card media (4GB recommended for practical camera use)
  • Battery: Nikon EN-EL10 Lithium-Ion Battery
  • Approx. Dimensions: Height: 2.2" x Width: 3.8" x Depth: 0.8"
  • Approx. Weight: 4.7 oz
  • Includes: Rechargeable Li-ion Battery, Charging AC Adapter, USB Cable, Audio Video Cable, Stylus, Strap, Software Suite CD-ROM

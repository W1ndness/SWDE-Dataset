4x optical zoom / 3.0" LCD / Smile Shutter / Face Detection, SteadyShot® / Image stabilization / Intelligent Auto (iAuto)

The DSC-W330 captures photos with phenomenal clarity through its 28mm equivalent wide-angle 4x Zoom lens. Take impressive, 14.1-megapixel photos with this compact Digital camera. Plus, everything is made simpler with a large, 3-inch (diag.) screen and advanced features including Easy mode, iAuto and SteadyShot® image stabilization. And don't miss the handy in-camera retouching feature that corrects red-eye, trims photos and more.
  • Intelligent Auto (iAuto) mode thinks for you, recognizing scenes, lighting conditions and faces, and automatically adjusts the settings for you
  • Built-in SteadyShot® image stabilization compensates for camera shake and helps prevent blur by capturing two photos at high shutter speeds and combining them into one clear image
  • Simply press the Smile Shutter button and when your subject smiles, a photo is automatically taken
  • Face Detection technology detects up to eight individual faces and adjusts focus, exposure, and White balance to help deliver crisp, properly lit images of family and friends
  • In-camera retouching is the quick and easy way to enhance your photos on the go; you can trim, correct red eye and use unsharp masking functions
  • 3.0" Clear Photo LCD (230,400 pixels)
  • Microphone/Speaker: mono
  • Imaging Device: 1/2.3 type (7.76mm) Super HAD™ CCD
  • Recording Media: 28MB internal Flash Memory, Optional Memory Stick® Duo Media, optional Memory Stick® PRO Duo Media, optional Memory Stick® PRO Duo (High Speed), optional Memory Stick® PRO HG-Duo, optional SD media
  • Battery Capacity: Approx. 115 minutes / Approx. 230 images
  • Battery Type: Lithium ion / InfoLITHIUM N type 3.6V
  • Flash Effective Range: ISO Auto: Approx.0.3-Approx.3.5m
  • PictBridge Compatible
  • White Balance: White Balance Mode: Auto / Daylight / Cloudy / Fluorescent 1 (White Fluorescent Lighting) / Fluorescent 2 (Natural White Fluorescent Lighting) / Fluorescent 3 (Day White Fluorescent Lighting) / Incandescent / Flash. Underwater: Auto / Underwater 1 / Underwater 2
  • 35mm Equivalent: Still shots: 28-112mm / Movie 4:3 : 28-112mm
  • Focal Length: 4.7-18.8mm
  • Optical Zoom: 4x
  • Shutter Speed: iAuto (2" - 1/1,600) / Program Auto (1" - 1/1,600)
  • Audio/Video Output(s) via Multi-Use Connector
  • USB 2.0 Hi-Speed
  • ISO: Auto / 80 / 100 / 200 / 400 / 800 / 1600 / 3200
  • Operating system Compatibility: Windows 2000 Pro SP4/XP Home & Pro SP3/7/Vista SP1; Mac OS 9.1/9.2/OS X (10.1-10.6)
  • Supplied Software: Picture Motion Browser Vers 5 + USB Driver (not supported by Macintosh Operating Systems); PMB Portable Vers 1.1
  • Unit Dimensions: 3-7/8" W x 2-1/4" H x 11/16" D, Weight: 4.0 oz.

8x Optical Zoom / 2.7" LCD / SD - SDHC - SDXC Slot / Shoot Still Pictures and HD Movie Mode / USB / Intelligent Auto / Auto Focus and Exposure
Panasonic DMC-FH20 14 Megapixel Lumix Digital Camera is an easy way to get Professional results from all your pictures. It has Intelligent Auto mode that lets you choose scenes and the camera automatically adjusts for what you're shooting. A really nice touch is you can also shoot movies with this camera in widescreen mode up to 1280x720 for view on your TV. For a camera that's simple and smart, the DMC-FH20 is a great buy.

Great Camera!!

Reviewed By: TheDude on 6/28/2010
Tech Level: somewhat high - Ownership: 1 month to 1 year
This user purchased this item from Newegg
Pros: * Fast startup
* Auto-smile detection
* Great picture quality
* Easy to use
* Takes SD/SDHC Memory Cards!!!
Cons: * Be careful with the door for the battery/memory
Other Thoughts: I tend to over research when shopping, so I've been looking at so many different cameras in this price range. I'm replacing an extremely old Canon Powershot that died on me.

This camera is amazing!! Start up time is very fast, so it's easier to catch spontaneous moments. The quality great, even the indoor pictures look great!

Love the panoramic shot. Just hold the button down and turn the camera. It'll automatically create the panormatic image.

Smile detection is fun to play with, blink warning is a great thing to have, but the fact that Sony finally allows memory besides their own was a great plus!!

I bought a 4GB SDHC class 6 card.

I would definitely recommend this to anyone looking to buy a camera that easily fits in your pocket and takes great pictures. I'm sure it's not as great as a professional camera...but I'm just looking to take good pics!

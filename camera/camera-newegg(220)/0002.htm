Pros: Fantastic pictures! the G11 is a great camera right out of the box. (very little delay between pressing the button and capturing the picture, which is my biggest pet peeve with point and shoots)

I have to congratulate Canon for having the guts to LOWER the megapixel count from the previous version of this camera. (the G10)

In the case of the G11 less megapixels = better pictures, especially in low light and indoor shooting situations. The high iso performance of this camera is just fantastic. pics are very usable at 1600, even workable at 3200, though some noise is expected there.

The optics are very good, and the menus in the camera are very consistent across all canon products. I picked up on everything immediately and was comfortable with the manual controls without even cracking the man
Cons: I feel guilty leaving my DSLR at home.
Other Thoughts: I purchased the G11 as a backup and carry-around camera for when I don't feel like lugging around my big ol' canon 40d.

I love the manual controls, they're more intuitive and easier to access than the controls on my 40d, with an added bonus that I can hand this camera off to anyone and they aren't intimidated by it at all. It also attracts way less attention than my big camera.

When I was researching a new camera, it was kind of a tossup between this camera and the S-90. They both have the same sensor, and most other parameters are on equal footing. If you can handle the extra $100 and the slightly bigger footprint, the G11 is a fantastic camera.

Introduction

Experienced amateur photographers looking for a premium point-and-shoot camera will find it with the PowerShot G11. With robust features like a 28mm wide-angle lens, a 2.8-inch Vari-Angle PureColor System LCD and optical image stabilization, every image will have a professional look. For your convenience, the LCD screen can swivel out and rotate for hassle-free self-photography. In addition, you get a 10.0MP sensor, noise reduction technology and Smart AUTO that selects the proper settings for your camera for perfect pictures every time.

Other innovations include 35-person face detection, 26 different shooting presets for any condition and panorama stitching. With the RAW shooting mode the G11 opens advanced editing possibilities by allowing you to transfer the image to a PC without losing quality. For those moments that require movement, the G11 can also record in 640 x 480 video. Share the fun on an HDTV with the mini-HDMI connection.

Distinguishing the G11 from other cameras is the DIGIC 4 Image Processor that automatically finds the right settings based on an internal database of thousands of photographs. The result is less blur, intelligent contrast correction and reduced noise in your images. Take the next step to professional photography with the PowerShot G11 today!

Highlights

10.0 Megapixel Image Sensor
The Canon PowerShot G11 features an amazing 10.0 megapixel effective CCD to provide profound image detail with unparalleled richness and depth, and the ultimate flexibility in enlarging and cropping shots.
5X Wide-angle Zoom Lens
Equipped with a Canon 5X wide-angle zoom lens, the PowerShot G11 handles both wide-scene and close-up shots. Get the whole family and lots of friends into one shot at the 28mm wide-angle end, or take extreme close-ups via up to 20X zoom capability (5X optical zoom multiplies 4X digital zoom).
DIGIC 4 Image Processor
Canon’s state-of-the-art DIGIC 4 image processor increases the speed and performance of the PowerShot G11, and enables other advanced features such as Face Detection, Servo AF, Face Detection Self-Timer and Intelligent Contrast Correction for greater flexibility.
High-quality 2.8" Wide Vari-angle LCD Screen
The Canon PowerShot G11 features a wide 2.8-inch LCD to display large pictures whether shooting, reviewing or showing off images. A Night Display feature is added for easy viewing in low light. With the vari-angle design, swivel the LCD screen 180° to the front of the camera and display either a mirror image or a non-reversed image so you can shoot from just about any position.
Image Stabilizer
Canon Optical Image Stabilizer technology ensures awesome-steady shooting with phenomenal picture clarity even when you are in motion.
PictBridge Support
PictBridge technology makes photograph printing easier than ever. Simply connect your Canon PowerShot G11's to any PictBridge-compatible printer, select your image and print settings and start printing.
